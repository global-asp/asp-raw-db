page_text:

P1	Ungbo orbala mi dei?@  @@  Un@  li no.@  @@  Un@  lin ungbo duu no.@  @ @@@ 
P2	Ungbo orbala me dei?@  @@  È@  do ofe ate?@  @@  Ei ei, elia@  rule kwi efu ejefu.
P3	Ungbo orbala mi dei.@  @@  E@  defu oji oli ku ma kor@  outakada nyuu le?@  @@  Ei ei, e defu ofe mgbo ku@  ma kor outakada jor le.
P4	Ungbo orbala me dei?@  @@  E@  du gbo oli kuma nwuwu le?@  @@  Ei ei, e defu ukpologu unyi.
P5	Ngbo orbala me dei?@  @@  E@  du gbo ojeta le?@  @@  Ei ei, eguwu alu oowe kekele.
P6	Ngbo orbala me dei?@  @@  E@  du gbo agbaa mi?@  @@  Ei ei, e defu akpati.
P7	Ngbo orbala me dei?@  @@  E@  defu  ukpologu unyi?@  @@  Ei ei, e dubi ukpo alugbona.
P8	Ugbo orbala me dey?@  @@  Oun dei!@  @@  imaja efu@  ngbo ku ma ko ortakada joo.
