title:	Haasumi bee na'i
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu

lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	No nai Haasumi

page_text:

P1	Ɗo'o ɗum Haasumi. Haasumi e nodda ɓoosaaru maako.
P2	Haasumi bee ɓoosaaru maako no nduroyay na'i. Naange nyaaɗi.
P3	Haasumi bee ɓoosaaru maako nanii ɗonka bee weelo.
P4	Sey ɓe ndilli ɗaɓɓutugo ndiyam.
P5	Haasumi yejjitii to na'i maako ndillirno.
P6	Haasumi sey fuɗɗi woyugo.
P7	Sey mo wi'i ndikka mo hoota bee ɓoosaaru maako.
P8	Sey na'i ngarti feere maaji! Haasumi bee ɓoosaaru maako nani belɗum.
