title:	Musana Ni Maadhi
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Genevieve Terblanche, Ingrid Schechter, Lauren Rycroft, Melany Pietersen, Sandy Campbell and Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Osobola okukoba ebibi bya amaadhi no omusana. Kiki ekisinga maani? Soma olugero luno ozuule.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala, Musana
  ni Maadhi baaghakana. “Ndi wa maani,” Musana
  bwe yakoba. “Nkusinga amaani” Maadhi yakoba.
P2	Olunaku olwairaku, Musana
  yaghuunha munkyo inho. Yali mukaalaamu
  era mughagufu olunaku lwonalwoona. Abantu baayoza engoye dhaibwe dhonadhoona. Tidhalwagho kukala. “Ninze asinga maani.” Musana bwe yeghaana.
P3	Olunaku olwairaku, Maadhi
  yaghuunha munkyo inho. Gaagwa gaadhabaadha olunaku lwonalwoona. Abantu baidhuza na amaadhi buli bbuli entono ne enene. “Ninze asinga maani”Atyo Maadhi bwe yakoba.
P4	Mu Naisana, Musana yazuuka munkyonkyo buli lunaku, yayakira ilala na amaani.
  Abantu bonaboona baatabulwa. Buli  bisimbe
  ne ebisubi byakala. “Abantu baawulira enkalamata ne enhonta. Ebisolo byali bizira kya kulya.
P5	Buli kifo kyali kiyokya. Abantu baanoonia obwiga obutono okweghuga.
P6	Mu Namusebuko, Maadhi yaidha na maani mangi.
  Yagwa munkyo. Yagwa mu ntuntu, eigulo ni mu bwire. Maadhi yaidhula buli ghantu
P7	“Bwe gaagwa, embeera yafuuka inhinhogovu era ya butyerezi. Abantu nga bali
  na kweghemberera mu makooti na makuufiira.
P8	Abalimi bakyamuukirira, baasimba
  ebilime bingi ebyemere. Baasuubira amakungula aga amangumangu kubanga ebisimbe byakula mangumangu.
P9	Oluvainhuma lwe ekiseera,
  buli muntu yetamwa Maadhi. ”Buli kifo kyali
  kiidhuvu. Ebisolo mu nsiko byali bilina
  kunoonia bifo bikalu okweghogoma."
P10	Olunaku olwairaku, Musana
  yaidha aye yali munafu. Maadhi yaidha aye tiyagwa. Embeera yali ya bwinhogovu inho.
P11	Buli muntu yawulira
  obwinhogovu obubitirivu. Ezira yayenda mbeera eno.
P12	Abantu beebuzagania ku
  Musana ni ku Maadhi. Baayenda
  okutegeera aani asinga maani. Ologhooza ki? Ani asinga maani?
  Musana oba Maani?
