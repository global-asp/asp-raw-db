page_text:

P1	Her name is Mrs. Elena. She is kind and generous to everyone. She always has a smile on her face and is willing to assist others. She appears to enjoy animals.
P2	Mrs. Elena is the mother of two children. She instilled in them the importance of being nice and polite to everyone. She also stated that animals should be loved as well.
P3	She also teaches children in her neighborhood. Mrs. Elena instructs them on how to read, write, and solve problems. They are eager to learn because they want to fulfill a dream. Mrs. Elena's words have inspired them.
P4	Mrs. Elena's family is proud of her. Mrs. Elena is beaming at them. Her children proudly stated that their mother is a very nice woman. They are grateful that she is their mother. They used to say that to their friends all the time.
