desc:	Wamusota ayega okwewuga aye kumpi adhimire. Ekyomukisa omulungi wankudu aidhi ekyokukola.

page_text:

P1	E'ira ghaligho abemikago babiri, Wamusota ni Wankudu.
P2	Lulala Wamusota yakoba Wankudu ati,  tuje twewuge, "Kale, aye nze tidhi kwewuga," Wamusota yairamu, "nja kukusomesa," Wankudu yairamu.
P3	Bajja kukyewugiro."Nani anasoka okwika mu madhi?" Wankudu yabuuza. "Iwe sooka nze ndhidha kukwetegereza," Wamusota bweyamukoba.
P4	Wankudu yaika mukyewugiro. Wamusota yetegereza nga mukagwawe bwali kukola.
P5	Ekyavaamu, Wamusota yaigira mu kyewugiro eno nga Wankudu bwamulagirira.
P6	Wkundu ni Wamusota bewugira mu maadhi amampimampi age kyewugiro.
P7	Batandiika okulera ku maadhi.
P8	Bwebaali balikule kumaadhi, Wankudu yatandiika okuwuga ekyenhunmahuma. Wamusota bweyayenda okukola nga Wankudu, tiyasola era yatandiika okudimira.
P9	Bweyetegereza nga Wamusota alikubulirayo,Wankudu yayanguku mangu okwirukirira obulamu,bwa Wamusota.
P10	Wankudu bweyuafuluma mu maadhi ati, yagalamiriza Wamusota okugezaku okufulumya amaadhi mukagwawe geyali amize.
P11	Ekyavaamu, Wankudu yazesa enkola ya nhuna namunwa okutolamu amaadhi.
