desc:	Different animals have different types of teeth (and some have no teeth). What happens to our teeth as we grow?

page_text:

P1	Gonya elina amannyo amanene.
P2	Empologoma nayo elina amannyo amanene.
P3	Ente elina amanyo magumu.
P4	Ebinyonyi tebilina mannyo.
P5	Emese zirina amannyo amanene ag'omumaaso.
P6	Lukwata elina amannyo amasongovu.
P7	Nali nina amannyo mangi.
P8	Naye kati,  amannyo gange simangi.
