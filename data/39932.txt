page_text:

P1	Long ago there was a king which was a Leopard. The leopard was a great King to the kingdom
P2	One day he told the kingdom that there will be a competition of throwing an arrow.
P3	He came with it to show them and he said,"the one who throw it very far he will be  the next King". Everyone was happy to be the next King.
P4	Elephant tried throwing very far, but he couldn't do it
P5	Secondly,Rhino tried to throw it but he failed
P6	Thirdly, Monkey tried to throw and he failed too
P7	Lastly  the Impala came and he was surprised to see all the kingdom ,so he asked what was happening,they told him and he said,"I want to try"
P8	He tried to throw and he won
P9	The King said,"you will be our next King Impala". The Impala was happy but everyone was sad.
