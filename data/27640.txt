desc:	Emboozi eno edwadwamiriza era yaiza mu bazaire nabaana amaani abo abadhuuba okutandiika okusoma.

page_text:

P1	Olwabbalaza lwabiita, Nakiranda yatandiika okusoma.
P2	Nakiranda ya ingira mu kibiina ekiyaaka era yayagaana emikwano emiyaaka.
P3	Nakiranda ayendha obutabo bwe bifaanani.
P4	Baaja kuluya mu misomo egigheraku.
P5	Baasoma amaloboozi ge nhuguta ni nhukuta.
P6	Baawumula mu oluvainhuma lwo lusoma. 
Nakiranda alambala kumpi noomusomesa.
P7	Baaja mu lusoma lw'okulambuula.
P8	Nakiranda asoma ni mama ghe ghaka.
