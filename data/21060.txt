desc:	Enjala yagwa nyingi era mu maka ga Mulongo nga temuli kaakulya. Baasanga olusuku olutaalina nynyini lwo. Nnyini lwo ya basasuza omuwendo oyinene.

page_text:

P1	Enjala yagwa nnyingi era mu maka ga Mulongo nga temuli kaakulya.
P2	Lwali lumu, Mulongo yakulembera banne nebagenda mu kibira banoonya emmere.
P3	Baasanga Olusuku olutaalina nnyini lwo.
P4	Awo ezzike eddene nelibika, lya baleeka nebasigala.
P5	Obudde bwe bwakya, wazike kwe kumugamna nti "Nfumbira mutabaniwo"
P6	Akawungezi, wazzike kwe kumugamba nti"Enkya nsaga onfumbidde muwalawo.
P7	Abazadde baakweka abaana baabwe mu muti.
P8	Wazzike kwe kugamba nti"Enkya oteekwa okunfumbira balo"
P9	Akawungezi ako, Wazike kwe kugamba omukyala yeefumbe!
P10	Wazzike bwe yamala okulya kwe kwebaka wansi womuti natandika okufuluta.
P11	Bwe yazuukuka, yatunula mu muti waggulu. Bonna yabalaba, kwe kunyiiga ennyo
P12	Baalimba wazzike ne bakka wansi.
