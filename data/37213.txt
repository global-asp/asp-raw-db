page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,there lived an old tortoise.He was selfish,greedy, and a stubborn tortoise.He never shares his stuffs with anyone.There was a family of tortoises lost and looking for there way home.
P2	They met the old tortoise.They introduced their themselves by naming there names.The dad's name is Tom.The mum's name is Lily.The boy's name is Jeffrey.The girl's name is Dawn.The old tortoise did not care about them.
P3	They did not notice him,but they were happy he would help them find home.The old tortoise told them that there is no home you are going to be on this island forever.The family were sad because they would not be able to go home.
P4	They missed their family.They were unhappy.The old tortoise did not consider them.They asked him why he was doing this.He said he does not have a family,he only depends on himself and his time of death will soon come.
P5	The family told him how a family is they told him about love for a family.It touched his heart and he felt comfortable.He changed his mind and decided to help them find their home.
P6	Thy struggled and went through battles but still they succeeded in finding home.The time for the old tortoise had come.He fainted.After a while he recovered and saw what family actually means,the love they share for each other.
P7	He found his own love and thanked them for showing him what a family really means to him.He appreciated them and left the past to focus on the future.
