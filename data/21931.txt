title:	Utjhontjhwani usindisa izuba
author:	Phephelaphi Mnguni and Sponono  Mahlangu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiNdebele
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Indawo yomile utjhontjhwani womele amanzi khulu. Waya emlanjeni wayokusela bewakhamba. Utjhontjhwani usindisa izuba.

page_text:

P1	Kusehlobo ilanga liyatjhisa, ehlathini elimileko.
P2	Utjhontjhwani bekanganawo amanzi amalanga amanengi.
P3	"Ngifuna ithosi lamanzi, nanyana lingehla ekarini," kuzililela utjhontjhwani.
P4	Kodwana nombethe sewomile.
P5	"Nangingawatholiko amanzi, ngizokufa," kulila utjhontjhwani. "Kufanele ngiye le emlanjeni engizwe ngawo."
P6	"Umlambo uzele bewunamandla. Uzakukhukhula," kuyelelisa umlahlanja.
P7	Utjhontjhwani besele omele amanzi khulu.
P8	"Ngizokufa nangingaseli amanzi." Wakhamba, wayokufuna amanzi emlanjeni.
P9	Wakhamba hlangana notjani naphezu kwamataka wemithi eyomileko.
P10	Wakhamba waze wezwa ukuphahlaza kwamanzi. Kwakumlambo! Wezwa amagagasi.
P11	Utjhontjhwani  waginya umthamo omude wamanzi amakhaza.
P12	Bekathabe khulu azange asabona negagasi nelizako.
P13	Utjhontjhwani walinga ukubambela etjani obuthayako budlula ngakuye.

Kodwana amanzi amkhukhula.
P14	"Ngibawa isizo. Ngiyabawa ngisizani!" warhuwelela.
P15	"Rhaba, ukhwelele," kutjho izuba elimhlophe.
P16	Libambelele etakeni ngomlomo walo.
P17	"Ngizokuhlala la ngize ngikwazi ukuthokoza uzuba," utjhontjhwani uthatha isiqunto. 

"Ngizokulinda abe abuye azokusela amanzi."
P18	Ngelinye ilanga asalindile, abesana ababili beza emlanjeni.

Bebaphethe amaregere wokudumuza.
P19	"Kunezuba elikhulu elimhlophe eliza njalo lizokusela amanzi," kutjho omunye wabesana.
P20	"Lizokuba kukudla kwethu kwantambama namhlanje," kutjho umsana.
P21	"Angekhe ngivumele abesana babulale izuba elimhlophe," kucabanga itjhontjhwani. 

"Kodwana ngimncanyana nje ngingenza ini?"
P22	Ngaso lesi isikhathi izuba elimhlophe laphaphela phasi.
P23	Izuba elimhlophe belizokusela amanzi.
P24	Utjhontjhwani waba nombono.
P25	Weqela phezu kwenyawo lomsana omunye. Utjhontjhwani waluma khulu umsana ngendlela abe angakghona ngayo.
P26	Umsana weqa.
 
Warhuwelela, "Atjhu!"
P27	Izuba elimhlophe layelela belaphapha liphephile.
P28	Kungiyo idlela utjhontjhwani athokoza ngayo izuba ngombana nalo lamsindisa.
