desc:	Olufumo luno lugema ku lunaku lwe nasooka okuja ku isomero nobukyamufu bwe nali nabwo olwo kuja ku isomero.

page_text:

P1	Maama yansitusa munkyonkyo ku lwabbalaza nsobole okwetegekeera eisomero.
P2	Bwe namala okufuna ekyenkyo, naafuna ensawo yange naaja ku isomero.
P3	Nayagaana omusomesa ku mulyango. Yaanhandhula ya abaana.
P4	Twaingira mu kibiina twasoma paka saawa 5:30 edh'enkyo twawumula muuku.
P5	Oluvainhuma lwe daakiika 30, ekide kyakubwa "nda-nda-nda." Twaingira.
P6	Mu saawa ndhala, ebiseera bye ekibiina byawaaku baatutwala mu kye misana.
P7	Oluvainhuma lwe kyemisana, twairayo mu kibiina. Omusomesa yaatugha ebyokukolera ghaka.
P8	Oluvainhuma, maama ni bbaabba baannona ku isomero baanvuga paka ghaaka.
P9	Bwe twatuuka eka, maama yankoba ntoolemu yunifoomu ndhiyoze.
P10	Naakola ebyaghaka. Naalya ekyobwire.  Naatendhuka. Kino kyafuuka kyabuliidho.
