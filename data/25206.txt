desc:	Akibiidhi ekiwuga ti kyenda kuwulirisa mulimi!

page_text:

P1	Kino kiseera kya bisolo kuwumula.Aye owundi tikiriza.
P2	Omulimi yaakoba embiidhi okuja mu nhumba yaayo. Yaadheema.
P3	Omulimi yaakoba Tilisa asindiikesindiike embiidhi. Yaadheema.
P4	Omuliro tigwayokya Tracy olwo kudhema okugisindiika.
P5	Amaadhi amateefu gaalemesa omulimi okuzikiza omuliro.
P6	Omulimi yaakoba Maliko okunhwa amaadhi amateefu.
P7	Maliko yaakoba, "Ngya kunhwa amaadhi. Ndi kuwulira enhonta."
P8	Amaadhi amateefu gaalira, "otanhwa. Nd'a kuta omuliro ku luya."
P9	Kafeero yaewowoigana, "Busa toidha kwokya Tilisa."
P10	Tracy yalira,"Aye otandokya.Nda kusindiikiriza embidhi.'
P11	Kibiidhi yaanhiiga. "Nd'a kuja mu ndhu."
P12	Kibiidhi yaaja mu ndhu nga mwetefu tefu okuwumula.
