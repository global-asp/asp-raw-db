title:	Dondu bee ciilal
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu and Yusufa  Hammadu
artist:	Isaac Okwir
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ɗume ɓurii ƴoyre

page_text:

P1	Naane dabbaaji ladde e njooɗodii jam.@@Ciilal bee dondu ɗum soobiraajum.
P2	Sey weelo waɗi.@@Dabbaaji ngalaa ko nyaamata.
P3	Dondu bee ciilal sey ndilli ɗaɓɓutugo nyamdu.@@Ɗi tawi kusel, sey ciilal fuɗɗi nyaamugo feere muuɗum.
P4	Nde dondu yi'i ciilal e nyaama kusel feere muuɗum, sey ndu nanngingal.@@Dondu ƴami, "Mi warumaa na, koo mi acca?"
P5	Ciilal tori, "Useni, taa' waram! Taa' tallam e waana!"
P6	Dondu jaabi, "Mi tallataama e waana."
P7	Ammaa dondu foondi tallugo ciilal e waana.
P8	Dondu sey talli ciilal e waana.@@E ndu dari e gaɓɓuɗe waana kan ngam yi'ugo ɓanndu ciilal.
P9	Ammaa ciilal we''iti biyeeli muuɗum, fuɗɗi fiirugo.
P10	Ciilal hoɗi dow lekki.@@Dondu bee ciilal ngi'ootiri.
P11	Ciilal wi'i, "Mi hisi ngam mi ɓuriima ƴoyre."
P12	Dondu jaabi, "A hisi ngam mi accuma."
