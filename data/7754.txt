title:	N'wavhundlani Na N'wahawu
author:	Lorde Urias Cândido
artist:	Reginaldo Vicente Manhice
lang:	Xitswa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Rabbit invites his friend Monkey over to play

page_text:

P1	Kaleloko ka masiku kama wa ndlala,  n´wavhundlani na n´wahawu vawahi vanghana. Siku ginwe n´wavhundlani ano alakanya aku ramba a munghana wakwe kasi kuya hlakana.
P2	N´wavhundlani ati ngalu: Ahi hlakani hi ku bikana. N´wahawu ano hlamula aku: Hingazvimahisa kuyini ke?
P3	N´wavhundlani ano hlamula aku: Aku sangula kulaveka hi dzivela a nzilu.
P4	Hicela mati,  lomu ka bota hi kungeta.
P5	Nzita sangula himina nzinghena,  loku nziba a tapa ya bota ku laveka aku uvhulula nzitahuma.
P6	Hiku maha loko n´wavhundlani ano ngena hikuranga. Loko a mati masangula aku kufumela.
P7	N´wavhundlani ano gongonza a tapa,  makumu n´wahawu ano vhulula a mbita.
P8	Tsena lezvaku loku kucikele a khati ga n´wahawu,  n´wavhundlani ano teka a ribze ga hombe atlhateka a henhla ka bota,  a thlela a tshamela a tapa.
P9	Loku a mati masangulile kuvila,  n´wahawu ano gongonza a tapa,  tsena lezvaku n´wavhundlani anga vhumelangi aku vhulula a mbita.
P10	Hikwalaho,  n´wahawu anofa kwalomo. Kani lezvi n´wavhundlani a gile munghana wakwe!
