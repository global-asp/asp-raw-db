P1	There was a young boy named Prosper,who lived in a village of Eastern  Cape
P2	Prosper lived with his mom, dad, sister, two brothers and his granny.
P3	One day Prosper  was sent to go and fetch  water  from  a borehole.
P4	He decided to  take his  brother  with him.
P5	When  he  got there he realised  that he's brother Faith was not behind him
P6	Prosper  became  very scared  because  the village was far away
P7	Where could he find  his brother
P8	He decided to  look  in the wilderness to find his brother
P9	Oh no Prosper  went the wrong  way, what can he do now?
P10	Now he is stuck in the wilderness, with nowhere to go
P11	He decides  to  climb a tree to see  where his home is from  above
P12	But  unfortunately it doesn't help.
P13	Its slowly becomes dark and he is surrounded by  eagles.
P14	Can he find his way home  before  midnight, we will have to see.
P15	Read the next book to find out.
