desc:	The story of a kind girl and a greegy brother.The results may be bad and teaches a lesson. It tells us that you may be twins but not the same behaviour.

page_text:

P1	One day Jane was playing in the sitting room,"there came JabJabuu with a big smile, He grabbed her yoyo and ran away through the gate.
P2	Their parents promised to buy them nice toys.During their birthday they were given gifts by many people
P3	It was a nice day, family and friends were invited to rejoice with them.Jabu was given a toy car with batteries and Jane was given a colourful yoyo.
P4	Long time ago there were twins who looked identically.Their names were Jabu and Jane.They liked to play with different toys.
