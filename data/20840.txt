page_text:

P1	Kela habayeho umukobwa waliwitwa Emile. Emile ntabwo yunva.
P2	Buli ubwo nyina yamubwilaga ngo agile icho yakola yalangaga.
P3	Umusi gumwe nyina yaladwaye nuko alamusaba ngo aje kwanyilakulu kumuzanila umuti gwo kunwa Emile alanga. Kubelaa ntabwo yunvaga yashijimo kuja kwoga alikuja kwanyilakulu.
P4	Bwalije nuko nyina atangila kugila ubwoba.
P5	Nyina yakuye abokuchalo kumuyamba gushaka Emile.
P6	Yali yilalikiliye kubela ko halihaliho igisimba chadyaga abakobwa batoya nijolo.
P7	Abakobali bali kumushaka we yali yibeleye mukidiba alikwoga. Ntabwo yabonye umwijima.
P8	Isaha yukudyayigisimba yalageze nuko gitangila kuja gushaka ichokudya. Uko chalikili kugenda chatangiye guhumulidwa agakobwa gatoya. Abantu bichalo balibatanye kwegela ikidiba
P9	Emile yabonye igisimba gitangiye kuza nuko atela indulu. Abantu bichalo bunvise  agakobwa kali gutela indulu nuko balegeleze
P10	Abantu basanze Emile akili mumazi. Yali afite ubwoba bwinchi chane. Kuvana ugomusi yamenye kubahiliza nyina.
