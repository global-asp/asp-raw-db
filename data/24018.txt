title:	My first day at school

desc:	This is a story about a childs first day at school.

page_text:

P1	I woke up early in the morning to prepare for school early enough.
P2	I showered and brushed my teeth.
P3	I dressed and had breakfast with my father at around 8am.
P4	I got into the school bus which took me directly to school.
P5	When I got to class, the teacher welcomed me and I sat down.
P6	At 11am it was time for break and I enjoyed playing with other children.
P7	We had to go back to class for more lessons as we waited for lunch time.
P8	At lunch time, we ate our food, and then we all went home.
P9	When I got home, I greeted my mum and we did my homework together.
P10	Then we had supper, prayed, and went to sleep to be ready for day 2.
