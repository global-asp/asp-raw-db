desc:	There is a drought and Mulongo and his family are searching for food. They find a plantation and the owner lets them eat his crops at a very high price.

page_text:

P1	There was a drought. Mulongo’s family had no food.
P2	One day, Mulongo led them into the forest for food.
P3	They found a banana plantation but there was no owner.
P4	Suddenly, a big ogre arrived!

He let them stay.
P5	Then one morning he said, “Cook your son for me.”
P6	In the evening, the ogre said, “Cook your daughter tomorrow.”
P7	The parents hid their daughter in a tree.
P8	The ogre said, “Tomorrow you must cook your husband.
P9	That evening, the ogre asked the wife to cook herself!
P10	After eating, the ogre lay under the tree snoring.
P11	The ogre saw all of them alive. 

He was angry.
P12	They tricked the ogre and came down from the tree.
