page_text:

P1	It was time to go to@ kindergarten I told my dad to wake up or I will be late
P2	These are my friends Emily Jake Rosie and Jane
P3	We are best friends forever we play soccer outside and play on the swing and the slide
P4	And sometimes the 2nd graders try to let us learn about what's in the sky
P5	We make funny drawings that's what best friends do I love playing with my friends
P6	We read books outside and inside we show our books and we even try to create books
P7	We try to learn animals it is so much fun to learn with your friends because they are going to say the next animal and then we will understand it so that when we become older then we will know the animals
P8	We learned about eating fruits is healthy so that we won't be picky eaters when we grow up
P9	On summer vacation we went to the lake it is so much fun we are best friends forever and you are our friend too
