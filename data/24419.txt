desc:	Ogu mwana nakunda kulebera dole ye. Knadi dole ye etura eri munywanyi oshemerierwe.

page_text:

P1	Ogu ni Azizi. Azizi ni dole yange.
P2	Ninkunda Azizi ahabwokuba atula na mwenya.
P3	Kunkuheza kwimuka, ninaba aha misho. Obwo nza kuronda Azizi.
P4	Nafuna Azizi nimushushula eishokye.
P5	Naba nindya kyantsya nindisa Azizi.
P6	Naba nindya za swiiti Azizi nimuhereza ho emwe.
P7	Konka Azizi takulya swiiti zeye. Nenyebuza ahabwenkyi!
P8	Nintambula emumbuga ndikutolatola obumuri.
P9	Nimpeleza Azizi akamuli. Konka tankusima.
P10	Ninyoza Azizi mama atakanabise.
P11	Ninjweka Azizi omu'redi ahabokuba ninkunda redi.
P12	Nabaninza kubyama, Azizi nimuteka omwo nyowe.
