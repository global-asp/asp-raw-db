page_text:

P1	Ngonza ebibira!
P2	Kya kataano ekihoire nka genda mukibira kya kibale.
P3	Emiringo yemiti eyinarozere niyo eno omukindo,  omuvule,  na ebony.
P4	Nkorora ebisoro binu ebikuya,  enkerebe,  enkende nebindi bingi muno.
P5	Nkatunga nomugisa gwakukora ebinyonyi ebitali bimu nka entuuha ebihungu na kabyasi.
P6	Nkasemererwa muno kurora enjojo nkoota muno baituina akakira kataito.
P7	Nkarora ebintu ebitale bimu ebsoro,  ebinyonyi nebimera.
P8	Ekibira kya Kibale kiikaro kirungi kyo kubungamu. Ngonza ebibira!
