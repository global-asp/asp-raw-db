page_text:

P1	My mother and I love to go shoe shopping together.
P2	She loves the Lord and she encourages me to pray every day when I wake up.
P3	My mother loves my cousin too. He comes to visit every weekend.
P4	Mother loves to take me along with her to the market.
P5	At night, my mother always tucks me into bed before I sleep.
