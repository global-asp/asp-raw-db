id:	34253
title:	Vhana vha phula
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34253.png
desc:	Kale kale ho vha hu na muṱa u re na vhana vhararu vha thetshelesaho vhukuma. Thaidzo yo vha uri vho vha vho itwa nga phula.

page_text:

P1	Kale kale, ho vha hu na muṱa wo takalaho.
P2	Vhana vho vha vhe madele mapfani. @@Vho vha vha tshi thusa vhabebi vhavho hayani na masimuni.
P3	Fhedzi vho vha vha songo tendelwa uri vha sendele tsini na mulilo.
P4	Vho vha vha tshi fanela u ita mishumo yavho yoṱhe vhusiku.
P5	Nga ṅwambo wa uri vho vha vho itwa nga phula!
P6	Fhedzi muṅwe wa vhatukana o vha a tshi tama u bvela nnḓa ḓuvhani.
P7	Ḽiṅwe ḓuvha lwonolwo lutamo lwa mbo ḓi hulela. @@Mukomana wawe na khaladzi yawe vha mu kaidza.
P8	Wa sa ḽipfa u vhudzwani u ḓo ḽi pfela vhulaloni! @@A mbo ḓi noka ḓuvhani ḽi fhisaho.
P9	Vhana vha phula vha lila musi vha tshi vhona ṅwana wa havho a tshi noka.
P10	Fhedzi vha luka maano. @@Vha dzhia phula yo nokaho vha vhumba tshiṋoni.
P11	Vha dzhia ṅwana wa havho a re tshiṋoni vha mu isa kha thavha ndapfu-ndapfu.
P12	Musi ḓuvha ḽi tshi ṱavha, a fhufha, a tshi khou imba nga matsheloni.
