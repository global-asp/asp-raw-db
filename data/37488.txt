P1	The quiet horse's friends were going to try to make her talk.
P2	Cat said to chicken how do we make her talk. Chicken said i dont know.
P3	The quiet horse got such a big fright because of her friends she was going to cry. But still had a big smile on her face.
P4	That night they had a good supper.
P5	Her friends had an amazing idea. They are going to spy on the the people  so she will laugh. It  worked she laughed and laughed.
P6	She was so happy now she could make so much noise they had so much fun.
P7	The people  where so angry.
P8	The End.
