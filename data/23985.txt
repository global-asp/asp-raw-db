title:	Omugabe akatungyira omuhara we Wiwo Omushaij
author:	Lucky Atuhaire
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Wiehan de Jager, Caroline Lentupuru, Laura Libertore and Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Runyankore
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Emboozi negamba aha mugabe owayangyirire omuhara we okwerondera omushaija ahamuheru enyameishwa yahendera emutwire.

page_text:

P1	Hakaba hari ho Omugabe barikumweta Opale na omugabekazi murungi. Bakabaine omuhara omwe murungi.
P2	Eizooba rimwe omugabe akagambira omukaziwe ati muhara weitu yakura twine kumurondera omushaija murungi owa omukyaro.
P3	Noha orashwere omwishkyi wanje omugabe yayebuza. yatekateka aha kigyezo kyokuhereza abashaija bona abarikwenda okushwera muhara we. Naza kubatekyera amaizi agarikwotsa. Ora ganywe nuwaramushwere.
P4	Eizooba erya kuratsire ho, omugabe yataho ekirango. Buri mushaija muto atashwaire, nindonda orashwere omwishikyi wangye.
P5	Kubahurire ekirango, abashaija boona bashemererwa munonga bayetereza
P6	Omushaija omwe yagambira ishe ati omugabe yayeta abatsigazi omukyikaari kurondera omuhara we omushaija. Ninye kujenda kwemera omumaisho ga omugabe. Ninkyi hamwya nti ninkugarika na omwishikyi
P7	Aha eizooba eryo orunyerere rwa abashaija bajwaire gye barebeka aha kyikaari ba shemereirwe
P8	Omugabe yabakyira. Yabajira nti oranywe amaizi genyungwu agarikwotsia nuwe aratware omuhara wangye.
P9	Abashaija bakabali binji. Abamwe bajezaho kwonka baremwa kujenda na omunyiginyakazi.
P10	Eizooba eryakuratsireho, akame kaija. Bakakyira bakaha nahokushitama.
P11	Omugabe yamugyira nti Okutwara ohumahara wangye oyine kunywa amaizi agarikwotsya rumwe. Wakame yamugira nti ekyo tikyi kundema.
P12	Wakame akayine amagyezi maingyi. Yatandika kutebeza omugabe emboozi enuzire amaizi gari ku hoora.
P13	Omugabe nabahabuzi be bakabashemeirarwe bakugyira ngu nuwe ogu omushaija owutubeire nitwenda.
P14	Omugabe na abahabuzi be bakatangara kureeba wakame eri kunywa amaizi agarikwotsya.
P15	Ahamuheru, Wakame yatwara omunyiginyakazi wena ashemereirwe kwonka omugabe yaguma asisirwe kubi ahabwokuba omuhara we akagyenda na enyamishwa.
