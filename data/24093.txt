desc:	Aku mwana wa mululu ayiga esomo ekambwe.

page_text:

P1	Lunaku lumu. Mama yali afumba. Nayita, "Abena, Ajua, Akwasi, Aku, emere eyidde! Muleete amasowani!"
 
Maama yawa buli omu supu. Nabawa ne ku nyama. nabagamba nti, mugende mutule mulye"
P2	Aku natunulira fufu we. Natunulira supu we. Natunulira enyama ye. Nateeka esowani ye wansi mubunyiivu nabuuza,"Aku, olina kikyamu ki?"

"Enyama yange yesinga obutono," Aku nakaaba.  Naye gwe asinga obuto," Maama nagamba.
P3	Aku nakabira okusingako. Aku neyekauska kutaka. Maama yali tayagala Aku kukaaba. Nayita enyo, "Abena, Ajua, Akwasi, muleete emere yamwe mumangu!"

Abaana nebaleta emere yabwe. "Kati Aku londa ko enyama gyoyagala," maama na'agamba.
P4	Aku yamwenya musanyuffu. Natunulira enyama ya Akwasi naloowoza, "Enyama ya Akwasi nga nnenne."

Natunulirira ddala enyama y'Ajua. "Eya Ajua nga neneko," nalowooza.

Natunulirira ddala enyama ya Abena. Yeyali esinga obunene. Na'agamba maama we nti yali ayagala nyama ya Abena.
P5	"Abena, enyama yo giwe Aku otwalemu eyiye," maama namugamba. Abena teyali musanyuffu. Teyayagala kunyiiza maama. Enyama ye nagiwa Aku.

"Mmmm, eno enyama ejja kuba mpoomu," Aku nagamba. Natuula ku katebe nalya nga musanyuffu.
P6	Pii!Pii! Omusajja wa ice cream yeyali wabweru. Yavugga nga engali ennene nakayembe akatini

Aku yayagalanga ice cream.  "Abena, leta ensawo yange," maama nagamba. Abena naleeta ensawo ya'maama.
P7	"Ice cream nya bambi'" maama nagamba omusajja wa ice cream. Nasasula nawa buli mwana ice cream.

"Emu, biri, Satu, nya," Aku nabala. Natunulira ice cream we nga mutini. Natunulira owa Akwasi, nga owuwe amusinga ko obutini.
P8	Natunulira nyo ice cream wa Ajua. Yeyali asinga omutini mu zona. Nasanyuka. 

  Ice cream wange yasinga obunene.  Naloowoza.

"Lindaaako, nina okulaba ice cream wa Abena naye'", nasembera kumpi ne Abena. Nagula akamwa nakagaziya nakaaba.
P9	"Aku, kiki?" maama nabuuza.

"Ice cream wa Abena akirako owange obunene," nakaaba. Maama nagamba, "Aku oli muwala wamululu"

Aku nakabira ddala okusingako. Neyekanyuga mu ttaka. Maama teyali musanyuffu. Teyayagala Aku akaabe.
P10	Maama nagamba Abena, "Bambi ice cream wo muwe Aku otwalemu owuwe."

Abena tekyamusanyusa, naye teyayagala kujemera maama. Ice cream yamuwa Aku. Amaaso ga Aku negayakayakana ne Sanyu. Yatunulira ice cream omunene namweenya.
P11	"Mmmm, ono ice cream afanana bulungi," Akuu nagmba. Nagulawo omumwa nalumako.
"Ajish!" Nawogana nawandula ice cream.

"Olubadde?" Maama nabuuza. Ono ice cream akaawa," Aku nawoloma.
P12	Maama natwala ice cream wa Aku namulumako. Nasunguwala. "Waliwo ekikyamu naye. Yayonnonese," nagamba.

Maama nayongerako, "Aku ngo'labye, tojja kusobola kufuna mulala. Kati olaba nti kibi okubera no mululu?"
