page_text:

P1	There was a little girl with named Mia playing with her sister lia.
P2	One fine day she was teaching her sister.Then they ate fruits.But after sometime they got bored.
P3	It was time for Mia to go to school.She said bye to her Lia and left
P4	After school Mia and her friend were walking.
P5	Mias friend asked her to come to her house to play.Mia thought that if she went to her her friend's house lia would be home alone.Mias friend started climbing the tree.
P6	So she told her friend that she cannot come and lied to her friend for her sister lia.
P7	After reaching home she and her sister started playing as usual.
