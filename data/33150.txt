P1	Zacchaeus wanted to see Jesus, but he was too short.
P2	He ran ahead and climbed up into a tree.
P3	Jesus looked up and said, "Zacchaeus, come down."
P4	Zacchaeus came down quickly.
P5	Zacchaeus welcomed Jesus to his house.
P6	Zacchaeus said, "I will give half my possessions to the poor!"@@Then Jesus said, "Today salvation has come to this household."
