page_text:

P1	Ndimakonda masewero nthambo. 


I enjoy skipping.
P2	Ine ndili ndekha. 


On my own.
P3	Ndimakondanso masewero a nthambo ndi anzanga. 


I enjoy skipping with my friend too.
P4	Ndimakonda kuwerenga nthano zabwino. 


I enjoy reading interesting stories.
P5	Ndimakonda kuyenda mu dambo ndi anzanga. 


I enjoy walking in the field with my friends.
P6	Ndimakonda kutsuka mbale za amai. 


I enjoy washing dishes for my mother.
P7	Ndimakonda kulemba malembo akulu akulu amene sabvuta kuwerenga. 


I enjoy writing big letters which I can read easily.
P8	Ndimakonda kudya zipatso zosiyana siyana. 


I enjoy eating different fruit.
P9	Ndimakonda kugona pa bedi lalikulu. 


I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.
P10	Ndimakonda kumvetsera ku nthano zabwino za amai. 


I enjoy listening to my mother's interesting stories.
P11	Ndimakonda kucita zonse izi.  


I enjoy all these things.
