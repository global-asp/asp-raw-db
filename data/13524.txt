title:	Soccer Game is  Good
author:	Hasahya Joan

desc:	This is a story about a group of young people who enjoy playing soccer. Their coach has a big surprise for the team!

page_text:

P1	I love soccer so much,  what about you?
P2	Maliki is teaching us soccer,  are you interested too?
P3	Let us go to the field.
P4	Maliki is the best payer!
P5	After the game,  coach told us that she has a surprise for us.
P6	But she will only let us know tomorrow.
P7	The next day we all came early to the field. We all love surprises.
P8	Maloki has new soccer kits and balls for all of us! What a great surprise!
