desc:	A monkey who through his bravery ends up providing food for his village

page_text:

P1	There once was a monkey who lived in a beautiful village in the middle of the woods.
P2	Everyone lived in harmony and In beautiful green trees but there was a problem. There was no bananas in the trees except for in one very tall tree.
P3	One day the brave monkey decided that he was going to climb the tree and get some food for the village. He was scared and struggle up the tree. He started to slip and fell but didn't give up he tried again.
P4	He finally reached the top and picked as many bananas as he could. The brave monkey slid down the tree and took them to the village.
