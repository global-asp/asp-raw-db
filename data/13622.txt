page_text:

P1	Abawo khaale akho,  siimiyu syarula,  bisolo byaalya bunyaasi bwoosi khuungo byaamalawo. Bwaarama bubwaalaama khubisaala khungaaki.
P2	Lwabawo lutweeela Wanambusi waabona Namuunyu abira ni kumubano waamuloomba akiwe,  akwiisile bisaala,  alye imbowa.
P3	Namuunyu aamuwa,  wakwiisa bisaala waalya imbowa,  wekura,  ne weebibilila hesi aalekha kumubano tsana.
P4	Namuunyu khuukobola khumureeba kumubano tsena,  Wanambusi wapang'ana busa nga sasikhebulila hesi akulekhuula ta.
P5	Aaloma aryo nga ali khuhunyilisa ni khutakhula aha ni naaho. Nikhukhwoola ari asihentsa kumubano ukwo.
P6	Namuunyu kila alomela Wanambusi ari,  "Khukhwaama nga nutibisile kumubano kukuumba bya khulya,  kane ikhulye wamweene,  ndyatsake ni babaana boowo."
P7	Khukhwaama ulwo,  Namuunyu s'aaberera Wanambusi ta.
P8	Wanambusi naye nasiluno asihentsa kumubano kwa Namuunyu.
