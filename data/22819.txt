page_text:

P1	Ishima nende Ifisi basolana.
Benya amachero okhurula khumukhalachi owefina.
P2	Omukhalachi napara, "Lishene lilalia amatumwa. Ifisi ilalia efiayo fianje."
P3	Okhumalirikha omukhalachi nababolera, "Khaabe amachero khubakofu."
P4	Abakofu bosi baria. Shibahana amachero tawe.
P5	Abakofu babolera Ishima nende Ifisi, "Omuse kuno nomutinyu."
P6	Abakofu babola, "Khaabe amachero okhurula khumukhasi omutakha."
P7	Omukhasi omutakha nakhalaka omuse. "Euma emiandu echiokhukosia."
P8	Nabolera Ifisi, "Waribwa mbu oli omukhongo, lekha obusolo."
P9	Ifisi niibola, "Obolere abwene. Shindenya tsisoni tawe."
P10	Nabolera Ishima, "Oli omuchesi lekha obusolo." Ishima niifuchirira.
P11	Omukhasi nababolera, "Mufuchirire okhulekha obusolo. Nobulayi mulekhanire."
P12	Omukhasi omutakha namala emilekano chiabo.
