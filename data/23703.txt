page_text:

P1	Ezobarimwe enyamishwa zikachwamu kukora embaga bitu eketari yaburyomwe. emba
P2	wakame akana' yetebekanisiza omukoro. bitu yashanga ngu embaga eketari yaburyomwe. ekerye' nyamishwa ezine amahembe
P3	Hariho wakame ahiyagire yatunga amahembe. nabwanyima yaga teka ahamutwe haza yaza omumbaga
P4	wakame akarya, yanywa, zazina omumbaga
P5	Ekyokikamurusya  nabyanyima yagwa ahansi omumushana. Bitu wakame kuyabire abyamire amahembe gariga ahamutwe.
P6	Nabwanyima enyamishwa za shanga ngu  aka'tari ekika kyenyamwishwa ekibabirenibenda. bakanyurira amatu ga wakame mpaka obugabire mahango
P7	nabwanyima biruka. niye'nshonga ahabwenki obume bwine amatu mahango!
