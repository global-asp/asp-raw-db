P1	Once upon a time there was a silly girl named Milly.  She just romed here and there in the town.
P2	Everyone said that she is an alien. But she didn't listen them.
P3	Then her mother a said that she will go school. From that day she started going to school. Her teacher said that we should not do like that she did before.
