P1	Once upon a time in the animal kingdom, lives a creepy ant who has no friends nor enemies. He lived alone in the forest because he was too afraid to make friends because of his size .he lived in the forest for years without any friends.
P2	Until one day on his way to his farm he met a lizard who was so sad and worried. After some moment of gaze, the ant decided to know what the problem was and doing so he greeted 'hello lizard' hello ant replied the lizard '.
P3	Why are you alone in this lonely forest asked the ant.i am very hungry and tired replied the lizard. Being a kind person the ant requested to treat the lizard to a meal at his hut.whe they got there the ant prepared a delicious food
P4	And served the lizard. After few moments of silence and eating the two animals engaged in a little discussion full of jokes and stories after much talk the lizard asked to leave because its getting late so seeing him off the ant made friend
P5	With the lizard and that was how the creepy ant got himself a friend after so many years of loneliness. @               @@   THE END
