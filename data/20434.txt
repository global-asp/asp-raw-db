desc:	Kupitia hadithi hii

page_text:

P1	Chura na Nyoka ni marafiki.
P2	Chura anasema, “Njoo nami tumtembelea mamangu.”
P3	“Asante. Nani atachunga makao yetu?” Nyoka anajibu.
P4	“Nitachunga makao yenu,” Kima anasema.
P5	Chura anaweka mchwa katika mkoba kumpelekea mamake.
P6	Chura na Nyoka wanaondoka asubuhi.  Wanaingia msituni.
P7	Wanakuta genge la kindi. Nyoka anaogopa.
P8	Chura anamlinda rafiki yake.
P9	Wanahisi njaa sana wanapofika kwa Mamake.
P10	Wanaoga na kujitayarisha kwa chakula.
P11	“Tafadhali Nyoka, keti wima,” Chura anasema.
P12	Nyoka anajaribu kuketi wima, lakini yeye si chura.
P13	“Siwezi kuketi wima kama chura,” Nyoka anasema.
P14	Chura hamsikilizi.
P15	“Hunielewi. Ninaenda zangu nyumbani.”
P16	Nyoka amekasirika. “Nilidhani Chura ni rafiki yangu.”
