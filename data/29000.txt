id:	29000
title:	Embaluwa emponiiza
author:	Julius Bamwagala and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/29000.png
desc:	Olufumo luno lugema ku muguna omulwaire era ateekwa okusigala nga taja ku isomero. Embaluwa dha muganhulo inho kuba dhimukuuma nga muntu ikaikana.

page_text:

P1	Saasira eyabita nafuna embaluwa kamaala.
P2	Ku lwebbalaza omusawo ghange yaghandiikira omusomesa ghange embaluwa.
P3	"Naisanga mulwaire. Ateekwa kusigala ghaka."
P4	Kulwokubiri nafuna nafuna embaluwa okuva mu bana kibiina banange.
P5	"Naisanga tukwendeza kuwona amangu!"
P6	Ku lwokusatu nafuna embaluwa okuva ye mukagwa ghange keenekeene.
P7	Nakukubiire akafaanani kano. Bukwenda buva ye Duku.
P8	Kulwokuna nafuna embaluwa okuva ye maama ghange.
