title:	Kiti na Imbwa vanogadzira mifananidzo yavo
author:	Lilian Chaminuka
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	ChiShona
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31757.png
desc:	Kiti na Imbwa ishamwari. Vanowirirana. Vanotamba vese nguva dzose.

page_text:

P1	Uyu ndi Kiti.@@Uyu ndi Imbwa.
P2	Kiti na Imbwa vanoda kuita mufananidzo. Kiti na Imbwa vanoda kuisa ruvara. Kiti na Imbwa vanoda kugadzira mufananidzo.@@Imbwa ndiye anotanga.@@Imbwa anotora penzura ne bepa. Bepa racho riri sikweya.
P3	Pekutanga Imbwa anogadzira denderedzwa.@@Denderedzwa ndere muviri waKiti.@@Aah, zvakareruka izvi!
P4	Imbwa anogadzira magonyatatu (triangles) maviri pamusoro pedenderedzwa rekutanga.@@Magonyatatu aya ndiwo nzeve dza Kiti. Anobva agadzira rimwe gonyatatu dema mukati medenderedzwa.
P5	Apedza anogadzira maziso netsiye dzaKiti.@@Maziso aKiti madodzi (dots) maviri.@@Tsiye mitsetse miviri.@@Aah, zvakareruka izvi!
P6	Apedza, Imbwa anoisa muromo waKiti.@@Muromo wacho kamutsetse kadiki.@@Vhudzi rinotaridzika sa 'w' paanoriisa mumusoro.@@Mabvudzi matatu kuruboshwe nemabvudzi matatu kurudyi.
P7	Mufananidzo wavepedyo nekupera.@@Imbwa anoisa maoko nemakumbo aKiti.@@Zvigunwe tumadenderedzwa tudiki. Obva aisa muswe waKiti. Muswe wacho mutsetse wakareba.
P8	Kwekupedzisira Imbwa anonyora mitsetse mumuviri waKiti obva aisa ruvara rwakaita se ranjisi (orange).@@Mufananidzo wapera.@@Aah, zvakanaka izvi!
P9	Zvino yava nguva yaKiti yekugadzira mufananidzo wa Imbwa.
P10	Kiti anotanga nekugadzira denderedzwa.@@Denderedzwa ndiro muviri wa Imbwa.@@Aah, zvakanaka izvi!
P11	Apedza Kiti anoisa nzeve dza Imbwa pamusoro pedenderedzwa. Anoisazve kadenderedzwa katema mukati medenderedzwa rekutanga.@@Kadenderedzwa katema ndiko mhino. Anobva anyora kamutsetse pamusoro pemhino.
P12	Apedza izvozvo, Kiti  anoisa maziso netsiye.@@Maziso madodzi maviri.@@Tsiye mitsetse miviri.@@Aah, zvakanakisa izvi!
P13	Kiti anoisa muromo wa Imbwa.@@Muromo wacho kamutsetse.@@Muromo waImbwa unoita sa 'w'. Kiti anobva aisa tumadodzi twakatenderedza muromo wacho.@@Tumadodzi tutatu kuruboshwe tumwe tutatu kurudyi.
P14	Mufananidzo wavepedyo nekupera.@@Kiti anoisa maoko nemakumbo.@@Maoko nemakumbo anogadzira nemitsetse obva aisa zvigunwe zva Imbwa.@@Zvigunwe tumadenderedzwa tudiki. Apedza Kiti anoisa muswe.@@Muswe wacho unenge ruva.
P15	Pekupedzisira Kiti anoisa tuma denderedzwa tuviri mumuviri wa Imbwa obva aisa ruvara rwehuroro (yellow).@@Mufananidzo wapera zvino.
P16	Chisara Kiti.@@Chisara mufananidzo waKiti.@@Chisara Imbwa.@@Chisara mufananidzo waImbwa.
