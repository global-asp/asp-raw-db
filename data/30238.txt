desc:	Emboozi ya Nabwonso

page_text:

P1	Nabwonso yazaalibwa mumaka makomba.Yali kimoimo era nga agonzebwa inho.
P2	Ezira kye yaghanga kwega kukola kubanga bazaire be bali bakombe.
P3	Okumala emyaaka ikumi ne etaanu bali mufaaku era nga buli kintu bakimugha.
P4	Lulala bazaire be baafuna akabendhe baafa.Nabwonso yaghoghoola.
P5	Yaja okuba ni dhaadha ghe ayenga timukombe nga lata ghe.
P6	Tiyali musangaalufu kubanga yali asubwa bazaire be.
P7	Yaloba okukola emirimo ghaka era yalambalaga inho bulidho.
P8	Yakoba ati taghanga kukola mirimo kuba yali mwaana gha mukombe.
P9	Lulala dhaadha ghe yasungughaala yamubinga ghaka.
P10	Nabwonso yaja kungira yaadhagira eyo.
P11	Yeidhusa obutaghuliriza dhaadha ghe era yevuma amalala.
