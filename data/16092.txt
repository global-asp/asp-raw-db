page_text:

P1	Lona nguPalesa. Igama lakhe lichaza ukuthi 'imbali' ngolimi lwakhe lweSetswana.
P2	Ekuseni uPalesa uxoxa nesihlahla samawolintshi. "Sihlahla samawolintshi, sicela usiphe amawolintshi amaningi."
P3	UPalesa uya esikoleni. Endleleni uxoxa notshani uthi,  "Tshani, sicela uhlale uluhlaza usisize ungashi."
P4	UPalesa, ekuhambeni kwakhe, ubona izimbali zasendle. "Zimbali, siyabonga ngemibala yenu emihle neqhakazile."
P5	Wafika esikoleni uPalesa waxoxa nesihlahla esincane. "Sihlahla, ngicela ukhule ube namagatsha amakhulu ukuze sihlale ngaphansi komthunzi wakho."
P6	UPalesa uxoxa nothango oluzungeze isikole sakhe. "Sicela uhlale uqinile, uvimbele izigebengu zingangeni ngaphakathi esikoleni sethu."
P7	Ngesikhathi ebuyela ekhaya, wavakashela isihlahla samawolintshi. "Ngabe amawolintshi akho asevuthiwe?" kubuza uPalesa.
P8	"Amawolintshi aseluhlaza," kucabanga uPalesa. "Usale kahle, ngizokubona kusasa sihlahla samawolintshi. Mhlawumbe kusasa uzobe usunalo iwolintshi elivuthiwe ozongipha lona!"
