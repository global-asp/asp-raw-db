title:	Sei Ajao asina kuvigwa

desc:	Ajao muremwaremwa here kana kuti ishiri kana kuti imbeva?

page_text:

P1	Muremwaremwa anonzi Ajao aiva akarara mumba make achirwara. Paiva pasina anomubatsira.
P2	Ajao akabva ashaika.
P3	Vavakidzani vakati, "Ajao ashaika. Tinofanirwa kudana hama dzake dzimuvige."
P4	Vavakidzani vakaenda kunodana shiri vachiti, "Hama yenyu yashaya."
P5	Shiri dzakauya dzikaona Ajao. Dzakati, "Iyi mhuka haisi hama yedu."
P6	"Isu mhuri yedu ine manhenga asi Ajao haana. Saka, haasi umwe wedu." Shiri dzakabva dzaenda.
P7	Vavakidzani vakaunganazve vachitaurirana. Vakabvumirana, "Shiri dzataura chokwadi. Ajao haana manhenga. Saka haasi hama yadzo."
P8	Umwe wevavakidzani akati, "Anogona kuve ari wemhuri yembeva. Anoratidzika sembeva."
P9	Vavakidzani vakaenda kunodana mbeva vachiti, "Hama yenyu yashaya."
P10	Mbeva dzakauya dzikaona Ajao dzikati, "Iyi mhuka haisi hama yedu."
P11	" Mumhuri yedu tese tine miswe. Ajao haana muswe."  Mbeva dzakabva dzikaenda.
P12	Hapana hama dza Ajao dzakawanikwa kusvikira nanhasi haasati avigwa.
