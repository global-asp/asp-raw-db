P1	Today is Hlelo's big day she is ready to go to school. Anyone can tell from that smile that she is very excited for her big day.@@She first has breakfast and then ready to go
P2	She is so excited to learn how to write and read. She first learns to write alphabets.@@She has been practicing this for some time now and she is very excited that she can do it at last
P3	Finally the school is out and the girls are having fun while waiting for the parents to come and get them
P4	When she gets home she cannot wait to read all the books she came back with from school.
