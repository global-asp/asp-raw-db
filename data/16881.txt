page_text:

P1	UBheni libhontjisi elincani elizotho (elibrawunu) ngombala.
P2	UBheni bekanesifiso sokobana akhule abesithelo somuthi  omkhulu wamabhontjisi.
P3	Umma kaBheni wamphuthela ibhotjisi  elinguBheni ngetjhila lekoteni,  bese wamfaka ngebhodlelweni.
P4	Wamthela ngamathosana wamanzi kuze angomi,  wambeka lapha kunomkhanyo khona.
P5	Umkhanyo lo bekumkhanyo welanga,  lapho uBheni bekafumana khona umtjhiso.
P6	Ngebanga lomtjhiso, amanzi, kunye nokuphuthelwa ngetjhila lekoteni,  uBheni umbala wakha watjhuguluka.
P7	Umbala wakhe waba mhlophe begodu wamila nomsila omhlophe.
P8	Qobe malanga unina wayemthela ngamathosana  wamanzi kuze angomi.
P9	Ngelanga lesithandathu,  uBheni bekasele alihlaza kotjani ngombala begodu anendlebe ezihlaza.
P10	Iindlebe zikaBheni ezihlaza kotjani zacala la umkhanyo welanga lapho uvela  ngakhona.
P11	Kanti ke,  ukutjhuguluka kukaBheni bekukukhula kwakhe abasithelo somumuthi wamabhotjisi.
P12	Indlebe ezihlaza kotjani bekumakari,  umzimba olihlaza beku litaka bese umsila omhlophe bekumrabho.
P13	Izitho ezintja lezi,  zenza uBheni abesithelo somuthi omkhulu wamabhontjisi.
