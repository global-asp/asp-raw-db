desc:	Oscar the Shark has a problem with his eyes – he can’t see well enough to catch fish. His friend the seahorse encourages Oscar to eat his carrots

page_text:

P1	Oscar has a problem with his eyes. He cannot see any fish.
P2	But he will not eat his carrots.
P3	Johnnie the seahorse says,  "Oscar,  eat your carrots!"
P4	But Oscar does not like the taste of carrots.
P5	"Carrots will help you to see better," says Johnnie.
P6	Oscar goes to the doctor. Oscar and Johnnie wait for Dr Shakes.
P7	Dr Shakes says,  "Glasses will help you to see better."
P8	Oscar does not want to wear glasses.
P9	But the glasses help Oscar see the fishes. Now he eats his carrots with fish!
