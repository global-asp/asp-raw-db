title:	Magurub ge ǀnūga ra mā
author:	Mattheus  Goraseb

desc:	!Gûǁkhāsis di ! gâiban nîra ǁîn tsîna ǀama gab ge Maguruba hoa ǀgurunab nî ǀnūga mā

page_text:

P1	ǀGuian ge ǀguruna ǀnūna ūha tama ge hâi.@@Hoan ge ǂhanas ǀguisa gere hî.
P2	Khoen ǀguin ge ǀnūna ge ūhâ i, Magurub ge mā nina.
P3	ǀGuitsēb ge Maguruba mâ ǀguru-i hoa-eb nî ǀnūna mā, ti ge mîǀgui, ǃgûn nî ǃkhaisab ge ǂhâba hâ i amaga.@@ǁNāsab ge aiga ge mîba ni.
P4	ǀGuiǂâixasen ge ǁîna, "o, kaise nî !gâi ǀnūna ūhâsa," ti ge mî.@@ǁȊn ge ǁnae tsî gere ǂnā.
P5	ǂHanas ge kaise ge !gom!gâxa i, tin ge gere ǁgam.@@Tsûban ge gere hōǃâ.
P6	Tsēs ge hā, on ge ǂgui ǀguruna Magurub di oms ǁga ge dāǁnâ.@@!Garo!naigu, xamn, ǂkhoan, durun, ǁnâregu tsî ǀgui-anin on ge ge mâsaogu.
P7	Mâ ǀgui-i hoa-i ge haka ǀnūga ge māhe.@@Xawe ǀgui-anin ge ǀgamkha ge māhe.
P8	ǀNūgan ge hōtoas khao !gâ i ge xū-i hoa-e ge ǀkhara.@@ǀNîn ra dâb xa ǂnā, hîan ge nauna gere !khupusen.
P9	!Gû tsîn khoena gere ǁgau.@@ǁKhawa da ǂhana tide, tin ge gere ǀgaiaǁgoro.
P10	Tusôab ge ǀuniga ge saogub !nâ mâ i. Ob ge Maguruba ge dî bi, "ǀNî khoe-e sa khao!gâ mâ?" ti.@@Ob ge, "Hîî, tita ge a ǀuniga," ti ge !eream.
P11	Ob ge Maguruba gere ǂâi, "O, !gau go ǀnūga ǀkha tare-e ta nî dī, ǁîga ǀunigao?"@@Tsîb ge Maguruba !gau ge ǀnūgu hoaga tusôaba ge mā.
P12	"Nēs ǂamab ge kaise ge dâ tsî naun ǂamai ta ge tita !garise nî !gû," ti ge mî.
P13	O xawe tusôab ge oao, ob ge ǀaoba Maguruba ge sari tsî, "Tita ona ǀnūna mā re," ti gere ǀkhoma.
P14	Ob ge Muguruba hoaraga ǀnūna ta ge go māǂui.  "Mâpats go hâ i?" ti.@@Ob |oaba ra !eream, "ǁOmxūsen ta ge go," timi.
P15	Magurub ge ǁnara isib ganube ǀnūna ūhâsao, i ge ǁaus khamakō-e a ǀkhai.
P16	Magurub ge ǀaoba kōǀî tsî, "ǁGabaǁaegus," ti ge mî.  ǀNū-i ǀkhai, ti.  ǀAob ge ǀnūose ge gababēsen.@@ǁNātsēsa xub ge ǀaob kaixuse ǁom tama hâ.  Saogub !nâ mâsab ǂguro mâsab ra ǃâunǀkha amaga.
