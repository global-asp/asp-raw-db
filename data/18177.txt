title:	An Be Kena Nɛ Baa Tre Pendo Na
author:	Chief A-R. Adam (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Gonja
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18177.png
desc:	Kena nɛ kumo nyɛmpeana kikɛ bee sha ga na be asheŋ.

page_text:

P1	Ndalo nde.
P2	Ndalo bee sha ebuku be kekraŋ ga.
P3	Pendo nde.
P4	Pendo bee sha debelbi be keji ga.
P5	Ndalo baŋ koso sukuu mo e naa kpa Pendo a yɔ kejito.
P6	E bee sa Pendo nchu lela nna.
P7	Mo tuto bee sa Pendo aboyu nna.
P8	Karechɛ kikɛ e bee nyaŋ Pendo be kenyipo nna.
P9	Pendo kɔ kenyipochu ga.
P10	N tuto bee fa Pendo be kenyipochu na nna.
P11	Ndalo bee nuu kenyipochu pɔpor kacha kama nna.
P12	Amoso e bee kaŋɛ nna,  "Pendo mee chɔrɔ fo ga."
