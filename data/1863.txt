page_text:

P1	Ubaba uthanda iphalishi lempuphu.
P2	Mina ngithanda ama-cornflakes.
P3	Ubaba uthanda ikofi.
P4	Mina ngithanda ubisi.
P5	Ubaba uthanda ama-apula.
P6	Ngithanda amawolintshi.
P7	Ubaba uthanda isinkwa kanti mina ngithanda isinkwa esigazingiwe.
P8	Kodwa sobabili sithanda amagwinya.
