page_text:

P1	Chibuzo and Ike had some delicious  lunch with father.
P2	Then they left with a basket of food to eat after work at the farm.
P3	On their way, a chimpanzee jumped in front of them. They were startled.
P4	The Chimpanzee growled at them. The basket dropped from their hands.
P5	They picked it up but the Chimpanzee snatched it out of their hands.
P6	The chimpanzee  ate it all up. The boys were really sad.
P7	Suddenly a dog come out from the bush and attacked the chimpanzee.
P8	The boys returned home sad, father was angry at the chimpanzee he assured the boys he would make it up to them.
