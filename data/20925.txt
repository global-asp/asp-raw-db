title:	Ensi Yaife
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Herminder Ohri
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words

desc:	Olugero lufa ku kutonda ensi na abantu.

page_text:

P1	Katonda yayenda omuntu
  okulabirira ebitonde bye.
P2	Wampologoma yaaghologoma, “Ninze
  Kyabazinga, nsobola okukikola.”
P3	Wandhovu yaalenkaana, “Ndi
  munene. Ntuukaana no omulimo ogwo.”
P4	Wabulenya yaakoba, “Mpuga
  naalabirira ebyenhanda. Ninze omutuufu.”
P5	Wansaka yaaloghooza ati
  amaino ge amooji naagamufuula omutuufu.”
P6	Wanzu yaaseka, “Nguluka
  naabona buli kantu. Ninze asaanira.”
P7	Katonda yakoba, “Ndidha
  kutonda omuntu. Aidha kukola omulimo ogusingaku.”
P8	Yaatonda omusaadha no omukazi
  ne ensusu endhirugavu edhimyesunkana.
P9	Abandi baali na mibiri myeru, amaiso ga bululu
  ne enviiri dha luzaabbu.
P10	Abandi baali na nsusu dha kyenvu,
  enviiri ndirugavu na amaiso ga
  ndali.
P11	Abandi baali na nsusu dha kitakataka,
nviiri ndiugavu na amaiso mairugavu.
P12	Wampologoma yaaghologoma, “Nsobola
  okubaita bonaboona. Ndi wa maani.”
P13	Bawansolo abandi baaloghooza
  bati abantu baboneka ngeri era bandhaghulo.
P14	Wansaaka ni Waike baasekerera abantu, “Munaaghuga mutya?”
P15	Katonda yaamwenha, “Abantu
  bali nookulabirira ebitonde byange.”
