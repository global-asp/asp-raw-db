page_text:

P1	Aroko and Akin are having breakfast with their father.
P2	Their father sends them on an errand to deliver food to their sick neigbour.
P3	A Baboon attacked them on their way.
P4	The Baboon ate all the food in the basket.
P5	They came back home and told their father about the Baboon.
P6	Their father sent them on their way with a trap for the Baboon.
P7	Baboon came again and forcefully took the basket from the boys.
P8	A dog jumped out of the basket to attack the Baboon.
