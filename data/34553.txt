P1	The sun shines daily, but sometimes it smiles too brightly. @@Remember to keep these sun safety rules to keep you safe.
P2	When the sun shines, you sweat! @@Stay hydrated. Drink lots of water.
P3	Use an umbrella. An umbrella keeps you safe; under the sun and in the rain.
P4	The sun rays can hurt your skin.@@Remember to use some sunscreen...
P5	... Or use a hat, especially if you do not have an umbrella.
P6	Use Sensible clothing to protect your skin.@@ Clothing that will let your skin breathe. Yes, your skin breathes too.
P7	Pamper your eyes. Use eye glasses to shield them from the harsh rays of the sun.@@If you do all these, your skin will be healthy and happy.
