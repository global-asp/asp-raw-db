desc:	Shifu wesigodzi wabobunjwa labanyenti walahlekelwa luphondvo lwakhe lolukhetsekile lakalushaya nakacala lidzili lesikhatsi sentfwasahlobo. Jojo lomncane uhamba afuna luphondvo lolunyamalele

page_text:

P1	"Nginenkinga lenkhulu kakhulu," kukhala Shifu Lomfisha kanye naloyindzilinga.
P2	"Ngilahlekelwe luphondvo lwami lweNtfwasahlobo," kusho Shifu Lomfisha kanye naloyindzilinga.
P3	"Ngitalutfola luphondvo lwakho lweNtfwasahlobo," kusho Jojo lomncane. "Ngitawubuka ekhaya laBabe Caladze."
P4	BoJojo lomncane naBabe Caladze babuka yonkhe indzawo kodvwa abamange balutfole luphondvo lweNtfwasahlobo.
P5	"Hamba uyobuka kutsi Make Calantsatfu unalo yini luphondvo lwesipulingi," kusho Babe Caladze.
P6	Jojo lomncane wema ekhaya lakhe kute atsele ingilazi yemanti.
P7	Jojo wavakashela Dudu Dayimane wase uyambuta kutsi utawuhamba naye yini.
P8	"Akusiko khashane kodvwa ngitawuphatsa kudla lesitawuhamba nako," kusho Dudu.
P9	BoDudu naJojo bema kute badle babuye baphumule ekucaleni nje kwentsaba yacalantsatfu.
P10	Ngalesikhatsi basemkhatsini nabakhuphuka intsaba, babona bulwembu lobungakavami.
P11	BoJojo naDudu befika ekhaya laMake Calantsatfu.
P12	"Shifu akalushiyanga lapha luphondvo lwakhe lwesipulingi," kusho Make Calantsatfu.
P13	Make Calantsatfu bekati lapho bebangalutfola khona luphondvo lwesipulingi.
P14	Bekusebusuku ngalesikhatsi befika ekhaya laShifu Lomfisha naloyindzilinga.
P15	Make Calantsatfu wakhomba kuShifu. "Ulugcokile luphondvo lwakho lweNtfwasahlobo enhloko."
P16	Shifu Lomfisha naloyindzilinga wanika Jojo lomncane luphondvo lweNtfwasahlobo kutsi alushaye. Bebajabulile.
