desc:	This is the story of a man who didnt want to live near other people. The man and his wife moved to a remote house

page_text:

P1	Akhalipo mwamuna m'bodzi omwe akhafuna lini kukhala pabodzi na andzace. Napo na mkazace kuyenda m'mii mwa anyapafupi wa nyumba yace.
P2	Ntsiku inango iye adayenda kumanga nyumba kuthengo kutali na nyumba na wanthu andzace.
P3	Iye akhambacoka ntsiku zinango acimbabwerera usiku.
P4	Tsono bzidawoneka ntsiku ibodzi,  mpsakuti mwamuna ule adadokedwa kwene-kwene.
P5	Ntsiku imweyo thika nakuti akhawona bzomwe mwamuna ule akhacita ndipo thika ule adayendambo kukagogodza msuwo wa nyumba yaceyo.
P6	Na tenepo mkazace ule acimupumpsa kuti ni bayace na tenepo iye adafungula msuwoyo,  ndipo thika ule adamugwesera pantsi mkaziyo acimudyeratu.
P7	Mwamuna ule pomwe adadzafika pamui pale adadzawona mkazace wadyedwa na thika. Pamwepo iye adasaya nyakubvundza.
P8	Bzentsenebzo bzidacitika na thangwe rakusaya kukhala pafupi na andzace.
