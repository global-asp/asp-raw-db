title:	Isihlahla sokuthula
author:	Zanele Zuma
artist:	Tshiamo  Msimanga, Tumi  Mofokane and Mpumelelo  Mlangeni
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/33782.png
desc:	Sonke siyayidinga indawo esizizwa sinokuthula uma sikuyona. (Indaba nemifanekiso kuqanjwe engqungqutheleni yokufundisana eyayigqugquzelwe abasebenzi bezengqondo baseSophiatown eGoli ngamaholide kaJuni 2019).

page_text:

P1	Kwakukhona intombazane igama layo kwakungu-Ayanda. @@Kwakunguyena yedwa ingane ekhaya kubo.@@Abazali bakhe babefuna isehlukaniso.
P2	U-Ayanda wakhetha ukubaleka ekhaya.@@Wabalekela ehlathini ngoba wayengazi ukuthi angayaphi.
P3	Impisi yamxosha u-Ayanda, wagibela esihlahleni.@@Impisi yazama ukumluma wagibela phezulu esihlahleni.
P4	Esephezulu wabona ilanga lilihle liqhakazile.@@Walithanda kakhulu, wase ekhetha ukuhlala lapha ehlathini.
P5	Zonke izinsuku wayegibela esihlahleni axoxe nelanga.@@Ilanga lase liphenduke umndeni wakhe.
P6	Ngelinye ilanga wabona indoda ngaphansi kwesihlahla.@@Le ndoda kwakungubaba wakhe.@@U-Ayanda wehla esihlahleni. Ubaba wakhe wachaza ukuthi kungani wayeze lapha.
P7	Wathi, "Kudala ungakazalwa, mina nomama wakho sasihlangana kule ndawo. Ibaluleke kakhulu kithina."@@"Manje ngigibela kulesi sihlahla uma ngifuna ukuthula lapho sixabana nomama wakho," kusho ubaba ka-Ayanda.
P8	Emva kwesikhashana, umama ka-Ayanda naye wafika ehlathini.@@Wayezoxolisa kubaba ka-Ayanda.@@Lesi kwakuyisihlahla sabo sokuthula.
