page_text:

P1	Sandra was walking next to the River Nile with her baby.
P2	She slipped and fell into the river!
P3	She was very frightened when she could not see her baby.
P4	She shouted for help to rescue her baby because she could not swim.
P5	Unfortunately no one came to her rescue and she lost her baby for good.
