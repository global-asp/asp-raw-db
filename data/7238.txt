title:	Ãyĩkõrú Ꞌbã Wárágã Kî
author:	Barnabas Delu
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Aringati
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7238.png
desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school. Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Sãbátũ ájệ rĩ sĩ má ịsụ́ wárágã kí ũꞌbí ru.
P2	Ụ́ꞌdụ́ ãlu sĩ,  dãkítárĩ mádrị̂ sĩ wárágã ímbápị mádrị̂ nî.
P3	Ãyĩkõrú ꞌi ãyánĩ rú. Lẽ ꞌbã uꞌá lị́cọ́ gá.
P4	Ụ̃ꞌdụ́ ị̃rị̃ sĩ,  má ịsụ́ wárágã kĩlásĩ mâdrị̂ drị́.
P5	Ãyĩkõrú! Mí ádrí gbõrú.
P6	Ụ́ꞌdụ́ na sĩ,  má ịsụ́ wárágã mâ wọ̃rị́ mâ lẽlé ambamba rĩ drị́.
P7	Ágbã íꞌdá ꞌdĩ mínî. Lẽtáŋá angájó Dụ̃dụ́ drị́.
P8	Ụ́ꞌdụ́ sụ sĩ,  má ịsụ́ wárãgã angájó mâ ãndrẽ drị́.
