title:	Moseme O Itsheng Wa Akai
author:	Kopano Makhubela
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Sepedi
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Kanegelo ya ngwanenyana le metshe wa gagwe. Metshe wo omo tshireletsa gare ga dilo tsa mehuta ebile ka thuso ya nonyana wa mo thusa go hwetsa tsela ya go boela gae morago ga gore a lahlege.

page_text:

P1	Ge Akai esale ngwanenyana,  mmagwe obe a morobatsa mosemeng omo botse. Mmane wa Akai odirele moseme wo ka mahlare a mopalema.
P2	Moseme obe onale mmala ya go kganya pinki le eme tala. Obe osa swane le me mmetse ye mengwe yeo mamago Akai abego anale yona ka ntlong.
P3	Naga ye ebe ileng kgaufi le gabo Akai ebe e omile,  efisa ebile etletse ka maswika. Gobe go tletse diphepheng,  digokgo le dinoga tse dintshi.
P4	Eupsa Akai obe asele kotsing ya go longwa ke dobopiwa tse kotsi. Mmagwe orile,  moseme wa gagwe omo sireletsa gare ga mathata a mehuta.
P5	Akai ebe ele ngwana wa go hlalefa. O ile a lemoga mo sediba sa kgaufi sebe sele kgona.
P6	Ebile obe a tseba mo koko Manyatta a bego a dula kgona. Gantshi obe a eya go isphina ka go nwa maswi a kamela le koko wa gagwe.
P7	Ka le lengwe letsatsi,  Akai gase aba le mahlatse. Oile a tloga gae a eya ga koko Manyatta,  eupsa gase a fihla fao. Oile a lahlega mebotong. Akai obe a fsega kudu.
P8	Oile a dula fase ga sehlare sa edome a emela thuso. Ka morago ga nako o ile a robala gomme a bale toro.
P9	Toro e tsamaile ka tsela ye: Obe a dutse godimo ga moseme wa gagwe. Mosadi wago swana le koko wa gage obe a molebeletse. Mosadi yo a myemyela a mofa sekotlelo se se kgolo sa maswi a kamela. Eno re ge Akai a tlesa matsogo go amogela maswi,  a tsoga.
P10	Akai oile a bula mahlo a gagwe ka go neneka. Orele ge a lebelela godimo,  a bona nonyane e eme godimo ga lehlaka emo ukametse.
P11	Ge Akai a emelela,  nonyana ya mmala o motala e ile ya hlaramotsa mafego a yona ya eta tsela pele. Akai o ile a latela nonyana.
P12	Akai o ile a fihla lefelong fao tsela ebego ele tse pedi tsa go fapana. Nonyana e tala e ile ya wesa tsitlu ya moseme wa go swana le wa Akai.
P13	Erile ge a thopa tsitlu ya moseme,  Akai a bona dikgato a tseba gore ke tsa mama gagwe. Ese kgale a bona se ba kgago meetse gona.
P14	Lapa la gabo Akai le ile la opela le go bina go mo amogela gae. Ba ile ba hlaba pudi,  ba ye besa gomme ba keteka ge ngwana wa bona a boile a bolokegile.
P15	Akai o ile a dula godimo ga moseme wa gagwe gomme a ja seripa sa nama ya go beswa.
