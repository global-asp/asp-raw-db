desc:	It is a story about a girl called Mary

page_text:

P1	Mary wakes up early in the morning, brushes her teeth, wears her uniform, takes breakfast and goes to school.
P2	In school, Mary sweeps their classroom with her friends. She also wipes her desk clean.
P3	This is Mary's classroom. Her teacher is Mrs. Marion. She is a jovial teacher and Mary loves her so much.
P4	Mary is the class monitor. She brings the books from the office and gives them to the pupils to read.
P5	It is break time. Mary and her friends are in the field playing football. It is healthy to play.
P6	It is lunch time. Mary and her friends are going back home. Back at home Mary helps her parents in house chores.
P7	At home, Mary and her whole family sits in the dining room and eats their meal after a prayer by their grandmother.
P8	Mary likes cleaning the house and washes the dishes after lunch. Mary ensures that the kitchen is clean always and all pest are chased away.
P9	Mary and her brother feeds their animals with the leftovers. They do this during their free time.
P10	It is now night. Everyone is tired and feeling sleepy. Mary and her brother are in their bedroom ready to sleep.
