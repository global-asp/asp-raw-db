desc:	Empndhiika eyamadandha ku mukwano nokufunha mu okuva mubulo.

page_text:

P1	Eira  einho,  Mazima  ni  Kasiisa  baba  gho  nga  bagabanha  emere.
P2	Mazima  yaakoba,"Tulimire  ghalala  nga  bwe  tulirira  aghalala."
P3	Mazima  yakoba  Kasiisa, "Iwe  lima  ndisimba."
P4	Mazima  yakobaK   Kasiisa,  "Iwe  simba  nze  ndiduba."
P5	Obulo  bwe  bwatuka,  Kasiisa  yakoba  Mazima  akungule.
P6	Mazima  yakoba, "iwe  kungula,  nze  ndita  mukyagi."
P7	Mazima  yali mugayavu.Agho Kasiisa yagha Magezi  akalo kalala akakese.
P8	Mazima  yatwala  akala  akakese.  Yayagana  enkoko.
P9	Enkoko  yalya  obulo  bwonabwona.  Yagha  Magezi  eigi.
P10	Mazima  yayagana  abaana.  Bamenha  eigi.
P11	Abaana  bagha  Magezi  omuyembe.
P12	Mazima  yayaganha  endegeya.  Endegeya  yalya  omuyembe  yamugha  eryoya.
P13	Enhanda  yatwala  eryoya,  yamughaku  amadhi.
P14	Mazima   yakozesa  amadhi  yazikiza  omuliro  kumanda.
P15	Omwokyi  waamanda  yaagha  Mazima  empasa.
P16	Mazima  yayagana  abatunzi  be  mamba.  Bamenha  empasa.
P17	Mazima  yaafunha  omutwe n'omukira ogwente  enhume. Bona Mazima kyakoze.
P18	Agho  yaleekanha,  "Enhume  yakabaka  eremeire  mubityanka!"
P19	Abantu  baidha  okuyamba. "sika  omukira  n'omutwe," Mazima  yakoba.
P20	Mazima  yakoba  abantu, "mumpe  ente  dhaimwe  mpe  kabaka."
