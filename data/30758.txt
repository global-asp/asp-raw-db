title:	Endhovu ni maaya

desc:	 Maaya yesiga mwine Wandhovu nga taidhi Wandhovu kiki kyalogholeza baana be.

page_text:

P1	Maaya ni Wandhovu baali  ba  mukwano.
P2	Maaya  yali  aliisa  abaanabe   bukalamu.
P3	Obwana  bwa  wandhovu bwali  tibufuna  mmere  emala.
P4	Wandhovu  yayenda  obwana  obunene  obwa   maaya   bube  bubwe.
P5	Maaya  bweyali   azirayo  eka,   wandhovu  yaatwala  obwana  bwa  maaya.
P6	Maaya  bweyaira, yaabuuza  abaana  be webali.
P7	Maaya  yayagaana  abaana  be  ni  wandhovu.
P8	Wandhovu  yaamukoba  ati  abaana  ti  babe  buti.
P9	Maaya  yeebuuza  kiki  kyabe  akole.
P10	Yaaayeta  ebisolo  byonabyona  mu  lwekobaano.
P11	Ebisolo   byeidha  mu  lwekobaano.
P12	Wampologoma  yaakoba  wandhovu  ni maaya  boogere.
P13	Ebisolo  ebisinga  byatya  okusalira  wandhovu  omusango.
P14	Aye  akadhaadha  kakoba   kati  abaana abafaanana  maaya  babe   ba  maaya.
P15	Akadhaada  kaalumukira  mu  nkompe.
P16	Wandhovu  yanhiiga  inho. yairayo  eka  ni  obwana  bwe.
