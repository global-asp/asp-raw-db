desc:	Ikamunit apese na obu odekakin omam abeyit alosit osomero konye obu itosom ebaluwa atijenikin eke mwalimu. Itodikit ooni  ajokis na ibaluwan.

page_text:

P1	Last week I received a lot of letters. Osabiti je abu adum ibaluwan lu ipuu noi.
P2	On Monday,  my doctor wrote a letter to my teacher. N'abalasa obu eka mukian iwadikaik eka esisianakinan ebaluwa.
P3	Thabisile is sick. She must stay at home. Ebala ebe,  edeka Itabisa. Ebeyit ngesi asalakin ore lolo.
P4	On Tuesday,  I got a letter from my class. Edol na'eyareyit mam ngesi elosit osomero,  obu ake tukot iwadikaik ngesi ebaluwa.
P5	Get well soon,  Thabisile! Ebala ebe,  ongaleu atipe Itabisa!
P6	On Wednesday,  I got a letter from my best friend. Na'ewuniyet,  abu adum ebaluwa ane jai apaperoka.
P7	I drew this picture for you. Love from Dudu. Enomit engo aputosia nu anukon. Amina engo jo noi.
P8	On Thursday,  I got a letter from my mother. Na'ewongonet abu bobo adum eciye baluwa ane jai toto narai amame ngesi ore.
P9	On Friday,  my teacher sent a letter asking if I was getting better. Na'ekanyet abu adum ebaluwa ane jai emwalimu ka ekoto ajenun eka ejautene.
P10	I replied that I was getting better and I could now play with my friends. Abu abongok ebe atamak engo ido ageu abolya kede ipapero ka.
