page_text:

P1	Sabe sihlala nogogo begodu nomma.
P2	Abomzala bami, uChristina, uThandi, uJunior noKghari bebahlala nathi.
P3	Ugogo bekasifundisa imidlalo.@@Bekusikhathi esimnandi sidlala kamnandi.
P4	Kwathi kusesenjalo, uJunior wakhamba waya eMalawi, nogogo naye waya eMalawi.
P5	Ukghari banoChristina nabo bakhamba baya eMalawi.
P6	Afeke! uThandi walandela naye waya eMalawi.
P7	Khona kwaba buhlungu nokho.@@Akekho umuntu owasalako wahlala nathi.
P8	Khona akunamraro khulu ngombana umma yena usesekhona uhlala nami.
