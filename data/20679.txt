desc:	Le ndaba ikhuluma ngezinombolo nemibala.

page_text:

P1	Imibala eyisikhombisa yothingo lwenkosazana.
P2	Owokuqala obomvu.
P3	Ufake ihembe elibomvu.
P4	Owesibili osawolintshi.
P5	Yini esawolintshi kulesi sithombe?
P6	Owesithathu osaphuzi.
P7	Yini ephuzi kulesi sithombe?
P8	Owesine oluhlaza satshani.
P9	Yini eluhlaza satshani kulesi sithombe?
P10	Owesihlanu oluhlaza sasibhakabhaka.
P11	Yini eluhlaza sasibhakabhaka kulesi sithombe?
P12	Owesithupha umbala oluhlaza ngokujiyile (indigo).
P13	Yini enombala oluhlaza ngokujiyile kulesi sithombe?
P14	Owesikhombisa osabukhwebezane ngokujiyile (violet).
P15	Umbala wokugcina uyedlula yonke imibala ngobuhle. Wena uthanda umbala onjani?
P16	Yimiphi imibala eyisikhombisa yothingo lwenkosazana?
