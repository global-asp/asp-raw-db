desc:	Umngane wami okhulu uSpot.Uthanda izinto eziningi.

page_text:

P1	Lona nguSpot inja yami.
P2	USpot uthanda ukudlala ngebhola.
P3	Emva kwalokho uSpot ukhathele.
P4	USpot uthanda ukukhonkotha.
P5	USpot uyathanda ukulala phansi.
P6	USpot uthanda ukudlala nezinye izinja.
P7	USpot uthanda ukudlala namakati.
P8	USpot uthanda ukugijimisa abantu.
P9	Kodwa ukudlula konke uSpot uthanda ukudla!
