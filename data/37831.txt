page_text:

P1	On a lovely Saturday morning, Isabella woke up and it was her 8th birthday. @@Mommy and daddy took the cake to Isabella.
P2	Isabella sat up on her bed rubbing her eyes as mommy and daddy sang happy birthday to her.
P3	Isabella was smiling as she got ready to got to school.
P4	Now they are on their way to school.
P5	Isabella got out at the school gate and waved goodbye. @@"Have a nice day at school, Isabella, and enjoy your birthday," said mom.
P6	Isabella played with her friends on the playground. @@Everybody said happy birthday jumping up and down.
P7	When the school bell rang, everybody lined up in front of their classrooms waiting for their teachers.
P8	They walked with the teacher to the classroom. @@Inside the classroom, they put their bags in the passage on their hooks.
P9	The learners sat at their tables. @@They started doing mental mathematics.
P10	Everybody was busy with a mathematics activity.
P11	The teacher was busy with small group teaching.
P12	Next period, was reading time. @@Everybody sat on the mat and listened to the story.
P13	After the story, the bell rang for lunch time.
P14	Firstly, they went to the bathroom to wash their hands.
P15	When they went back to class, there was a surprise for Isabella.
P16	They sang happy birthday to Isabella.@@They danced and screamed with laughter.
P17	What a nice surprise Isabella had on her birthday! @@She experienced the love of her presents and friends at school.
