title:	Bakame yongera gushuka  Nzovu
author:	Julius  Bazanyamaso
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Rufumbira
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22762.png
desc:	Muri iyi nkuru turamenya uko Bakame yiruka chane nuko yabeshyabeshye Nzovu yifashishije agakino kabi chane.

page_text:

P1	Nzovu na Bakame byari nshuti magara.
P2	Baje kubona urwuri n'amazi by'inyamaswa byabo.
P3	Bakundaga gukina umupira w'amaguru.
P4	Nzovu asinda ibitego byinshi kurusha Bakame. Ntibyashimisha Bakame.
P5	Nuko Bakame abaza Nzovu ati, "Niki kikugira umukinnyi mwiza?" "Amaguru manini yanjye."
P6	Bakame  na Nzovu barushanywe mu kwiruka. Bakame aratsinda.
P7	Nzovu yabajije Bakame ati, "N'iki kikugira umwirutsi wihuta chane?" "Utuguru duto twanjye."
P8	Nzovu arabaza ati, "Ni gute nshobora kubona utuguru duto?"
P9	Bakame aravuga ati, "Hagarara mumuriro."  Nzovu yarabikoze. Aratabaza agira ati, ''Ndimondashya!''
P10	Bakame  abwira Nzovu ati, "Ubu ni bwo buryo bwonyine  amaguru yawe ashobora guhindukamo mato."
P11	Nzovu yavuye mumuriro aryama hasi.
P12	Mu gihe cy'iminsi myinshi, Nzovu ntabwo yashoboraga guhagarara.
P13	Amaze gusa n'ukize, asubira imuhira mumulyango wabo.
P14	Kame yarashavuye. Asaba Nzovu imbabazi.
