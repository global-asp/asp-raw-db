page_text:

P1	Bara durii, Warabesaafi Illetin hirriyaa turan. Lamanu hiyyeesota garuu Illetin harreefi gabate harreen bishaan ittin dhuguu qabdi.
P2	Ganama tokko, Illetin gabateen isaa xiqqoo waan ta'eef, gabatee warabeesa isaa gudda akka ergisuufi gaafate. Warabeesis nikeenef.
P3	Illettinis gabatichan Harre isaa bishaan obase fudhaate galee. Torbee lama boodas, warabeesi gabate isaa dhaqna dhiqachuuf waan barbaadef gara mana Illetti deeme.
P4	Illetinis gabate isaa duraa fudhate kan Warabeesa waalin kennefi, "Gabaten kee mucaa godhate kanafuu lamansa fudhuu," jedhe. Warabeesis gammade fudhate.
P5	Yeroo booda, Illetin Harree isaa bishaan obasuuf Warabessan gabate gafaate. Warabessis gabatee 2 waan qabuuf, gabatee isaa guddaa kennef. Illetinis gabate Warabeesa isaa guddaa fudhaatee gale.
P6	Warabessis gabaten isaa yommuu dhaluu Illetin naaf fidaa jedhee eege. Garuu gabate isaa waan jala tureef gara mana Illetti fudhachuuf deeme. Illetinis, "Garaa mana kee dhufaan jiraa oduu gadisisa sitti himuufi. Gabateen kee bulti lama duraa du’ee. waan lubbuu qabuu ni dhalata nidu’aas," jedhe Warabeesan.
P7	Warabessi amanu dadhabee, "Waan kana bineensonni gurgudo murtoo nuf hakennani," jedhe. Arba, Gafarsa, Roobbif Satawaanis Waamamani.
P8	Illetinis waan ta’ee akka ibsuuf gaafatame. "Dura gabaten ani warabeesa irraa ergifadhe mucaa dhalee. Anis mucaa isaa waalin nankennefi. Gabaten yeroo lamaffaa irra ergifadhe garuu osoo mucaa hin godhatin du’ee. Waan lubbuu qabu hundu nidu’aa," jedhe. Bineensonni hundii yaada kanan waali galan. Warabeesis haala kanan gabatee isaa isaa guddaa dhabee.
