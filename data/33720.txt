P1	This is my family. @@My uncle, my cousin, my mother, my two sisters (who always dress the same), my brother, and me!
P2	This is my family on the bus going to see animals. @@On the bus was my aunty, my mother, my brother, my two sisters (who always dress the same), and me!
P3	We saw animals from the bus.
P4	We saw a giraffe.
P5	We saw a springbok.
P6	We saw a lion.
P7	We saw an elephant.@@We laughed and laughed at the elephant, because...
P8	...that elephant pooped in a tree!
