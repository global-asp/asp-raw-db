P1	Oscar ran into camp.
P2	He heard the dog barking and looked.
P3	'Auwwwhhhh!' Oscar fell back.
P4	"Father,  help!"
P5	"A big snake! There. It will bite my brother."
P6	Oscar's father beat the snake to death.
