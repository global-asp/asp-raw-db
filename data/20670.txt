title:	Ensi Yaife
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Herminder Ohri
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words

desc:	Olugero lufa ku kutonda nsi na abantu.

page_text:

P1	Katonda yayenda omuntu
  okulabirira ebitonde bye.
P2	Wampologoma yaghologoma, “Ninze
  Kyabazinga, nsobola okukikola.”
P3	Wandhovu yalenkaana, “Ndi
  munene. Ntuukaana no omulimo ogwo.”
P4	Wabulenya yakoba, “Mmpuga
  naalabirira ebyenhanda. Ninze omutuufu.”
P5	Wakibbalo yaloghooza ati
  amainho ge amooji naagamufuula omutuufu.”
P6	Wanzu yaseka, “Nguluka
  naabona buli kantu. Ninze asaanira.”
P7	Katonda yakoba, “Ndidha
  kutonda omuntu. Aidha kukola omulimo ogusingaku.”
P8	Yatonda omusaadha no omukazi
  ne ensusu edhilugavu edhimyensukana.
P9	Abandi baali na mibiri myeru, amaiso ga bbululu
  ne enviiri dha luzaabbu.
P10	Abandi baali na nsusu dha kyenvu,
  enviiri ndirugavu na amaiso ga
  ndali.
P11	Abandi baali na nsusu dha kitakataka,
  nviiri ndirugavu amaiso mairugavu.
P12	Wampologoma yaghologoma, “Nsobola
  okubaita bonaboona. Ndi wa maani.”
P13	Bawansolo abandi baloghooza
  bati abantu baboneka ngeri era bandhaghulo.
P14	Wakibbalo ni Waike baasekerera abantu, “Munaaghuga mutya?”
P15	Katonda yamwenha, “Abantu
  bali nookulabirira ebitonde byange.”
