desc:	Ngabe eminye imisebenzi ibaluleke ukwedlula eminye?

page_text:

P1	Laba bantu abavumelani.
P2	Uthisha uzwakala ethi, Othisha babaluleke kakhulu, niyasidinga ukuze nifunde.
P3	Umakhi yena athi, "Abakhi nabo babaluleke
  kakhulu. Nidinga izindlu."
P4	Umbazi naye athi, "Ababazi babaluleke kakhulu. Nidinga ifenisha."
P5	"Odokotela babaluleke kakhulu. Nizolashwa ngubani?"
P6	"Abalimi babaluke kakhulu. Nizokuthathaphi ukudla?"
P7	Umfundi wezwakala ethi, "Nathi bafundi sibaluleke kakhulu."
P8	"Nonke naba ngabafundi kuqala!" Ngakho-ke, bonke babukana, bavumelana.
