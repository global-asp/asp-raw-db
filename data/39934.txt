page_text:

P1	Once upon a time in a jungle of all the animals there King tiger and Queen tigress and all the other jungle animals  King tiger was a carnivore and killed his people without mercy. One day he got animal to catch and became very angry
P2	So while sleeping empty stomach a plan crept into his heard. And he announced that he was sick so whiles in his room he wants the animals to come over and visit him because he would die soon
P3	So one by one every animal was killed but he was still not satisfied with that so he killed his wife and kids but still he wasn't full so went sleep on top of a big tree looking for other animals but unfortunately a Hunter killed him
P4	That was the end of the wicked King tiger
