page_text:

P1	There was a beautiful girl who was named “ Chidiebube “ and she was a very happy girl.
P2	She also had a younger brother who was named “ Joe “ and he also very handsome.
P3	She loved looking at the clouds and also loved looking at the passing aircrafts and flock of birds in the sky.
P4	Her uncle was the one who took her to school everyday.
P5	But one day the taxi they were in got involved in an accident her uncle came out alright but Chidiebube came out severely injured.
P6	She also loves eating fruits because when she eats it she feels good.
P7	By the time she was better she lost her eyesight but she still lives happily ever after.
