page_text:

P1	Hobaneng ngoana alla?
P2	Ke nahana o lapile.
P3	Hobaneng moshanyana a koatile?
P4	Ke nahana o hlolohetse ntate oa hae.
P5	Hobaneng ngoanana a thabile?
P6	O na le mose o mocha.
P7	Hobaneng ntate a moholo a ts'eha?
P8	O balile ntho e soasoang.
P9	Hobaneng 'M'e a bososela?
P10	Bana bahae ba bala ba thotse.
P11	'M'e eoo,  o ikutloa joang?
P12	Ke nahana hore o ikutloa ale litlhong?
