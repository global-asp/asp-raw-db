desc:	Lebo has grown out of all her clothes and they are now too small for her. What will she do with them?

page_text:

P1	Logose alanga ati,  ''maama nja obone. Engoye jinonga josi joosi ndidiri huuse'' Nyina ng'aloma ati,  ''leha mbone.
P2	Logose nga alomaati'' bona ehirinda hyange hyene. Nyina nga aloma ati,  yee pohiri Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira ehirinda hihyo.
P3	Logose aloma ati,  bona ejiini jange. Ndidiri nyene. Nyina nga aloma ati,  ''Hitufu pojiri. Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira ejiini jijo.
P4	Logose aloma ati.'' Bonna T-shirt yange. Ndidiri nyene'' Nyina nga aloma ati ''Hitufufu pojiri Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira T-shirt yiyo''.
P5	Logose aloma ati, ''bona omujoozi gwange Mudidiri mwene'' Nyina nga aloma ati'' Hitufu pohiri Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira omujoozi gugwo''.
P6	Logose aloma ati, '' bona ekawuti yange ey'efula. Ndidiri nyene'' Nyina nga aloma ati,  Hitufu pohiri Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira ekabuti yiyo eyefula''.
P7	Logose aloma ati,  bona esokisi jange, ndidiri nyene'' Nyina nga aloma ati, '' hitufu pohiri Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira esokisi jijo''.
P8	Logose anga aloma ati,  ''bona takizi jange ndidiri nyene'' Nyina nga aloma ati, '' Hitufu pohiri, Namiisi aɳanga ohuɳira hitengi jijo.
P9	Logose aloma ati,  ''ɳahani oli n'engoye nyingi nyene. Namiisi nga aloma ati, ''Bbee mbul. Engoye jino josi mbala house!
