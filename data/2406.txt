title:	Ekijanjaalo Ekimera
author:	Rebecca Kuteesa
artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini
lang:	Luganda
level:	First words

desc:	Simba ensigo y’ekijanjaalo olabe ekibaawo ng’ogifukiridde era n’ogirabirira bulungi.

page_text:

P1	Omulandira gwe gusooka okumera.
P2	Gumerera mu ttaka.
P3	Ekikolo kye kiddako okumera. Kitutunuka kyolekera enjuba.
P4	Ebikoola ebisooka bifuluma.
P5	Ekimera ky'ekijanjaalo kikula ne kiwanvuyira ddala.
P6	Oluvanyuma kimulisa (kireeta ebimuli). Ebimuli bifuukamu ebijanjaalo.
P7	Mu kijanjaalo mubeeramu ensigo nnyingi.
