title:	Abakazi Abomururu

desc:	Enjara bweraba nee kuruma

page_text:

P1	Obusumi obweira muno,hakaba haroho omusaija ni baweta Kato.Akaba naikara Kabuusu town.Yaikara ne mbwa yaaye mu kajju aka taito
P2	Kiro kimu Kato akarwara muno.Akaba tayne muntu weena kumuyanba.Bweyakizire,akasara ho kusweera omukazi eyaikaraga haihi nawe
P3	Akeeta abanywani baaye  naa boowayee kuwijja hamukoro.Kato akasemererwa muno habwookuba akaba naateekereza nti a funiire omuyambi.
P4	Omukazi no musaijja bakaleetra ebintu biingi.binu bikaba ebiibo byoo buro,emikeeka,ebitooke,ebinyoobwa,nebisembo ebiindi ebyomuka.
P5	Embagga eekahwa abagenyi bona bagaruka mumaka gaabwe.Kato akaba yetegekere  kutandika obwomezi obuhyaka no mukazi we ne mbwa.
P6	Ekiro eyagarukireho,Kato akaha mukaziwe ebyenjju ebikunuliira,baytu yayanga kulya.
P7	Hati Kato weyagenzere kuhaiga,abakazi boona balya ebyenjju eebikunuliira byoona.
P8	Kato akaruga kuhaigga enjara nemuruma.Yasaba bamuhe ebyenjju.Mukaziwe yagamba nti ebwa ebiriire byoona.
P9	Ekiro ekyagarukireho,Kato akagenda ha farm ye.Bweyagarukire muka,a kasanga mukaziwe aliire enyama yoona.Nembwa akaba a tagihaireho.
P10	Ekiro ekindi,Kato akagenda kubungira munywani waye kandi bweyagarukire,omukazi akaba aliire ebinyobwa byoona.Ekiibo kikaba kitayina kintu kyona.Kato aka biihirwa mukazi we.
P11	Kato yatekereza,"Haroho ekizibu noomukazi onu."Ya saraho kukozesa oburogo.Akateeka amatta mu nsoha yo burogo.Ya teeka ensoha hansi wekitabu,ya genda kuhayiga.
P12	Omukazi akarora ensoha,eyijjuwiire amatta.Murundi gumu,yahimba ensoha ya giteeka hamunwa gwaye.Yanywa amatta goona embwa nemurora.
P13	Ebyeembi,ensoha ekakwatira hamunwaa gwe.Akagezaho kugihamu baytu byayanga.Ya Toka,yaguruka hansi nahaiguru.Baytu ensoha yalemeramu.Mbwa yarora binubyoona.
P14	Hati embwa yairuka kuseera kato.embwa yaboigora muno,nokuguruka yaguraka guruka.Kato hati yamanya nti haguwireho ekizibu omuka.
P15	Bayruka bombi omuka.Kato akewunya kusanga omukazi we ne nsoha emulemyre mukanwa.Yamuturira neyewunya.
P16	Kato akakwata haytama lyoomukazi we ensoha yagwa hansi omurundi gumu.Omukazi akakwatwa ensoni nyiingi.Yasaraho agaruke owaabazaire be.
