page_text:

P1	Numva ibintu byinshi mu mutima wange.
P2	Ndishima iyo nyogokuru aducira imigani nimugoroba.
P3	Ndatuza iyo nkina n'inshuti yange.
P4	Ndababara iyo data avuze ko adafite amafaranga.
P5	Numva nkunzwe iyo mama ampobeye.
