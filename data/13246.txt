title:	Tingi Ne Mombe
author:	Irene Pholo
artist:	Ingrid Schechter
lang:	ChiShona
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Grandmother saves Tingis life.

page_text:

P1	Tingi nanga a tshigara na mbuya wake.
P2	Tingi na mbuya wake nanga wa tshifvuya mombe.
P3	Ringwe zuwa ku ka uya masoja.
P4	Masoja iyayo ndo ku tora mombe i dzodzokure.
P5	Tingi na mbuya wake waka thiza wa tshino zwiwiga.
P6	Wka zwiwiga ku masango ku swikira hu siku.
P7	Masoja iyayo ndokudzoka.
P8	Mbuya wa Tingi,  waka wiga Tingi pasi kwe mithi.
P9	Mungwe wa masoja a ka tshweta gumbo rake pamusoro wa Tingi,  kasi Tingi a ka nyarara a singa tshemi.
P10	Pananga wasoja ayenda ndo mbuya wa Tingi na Tingi waka buqa mumasango iyayo.
P11	Waka yenda ku mba kwawo wa ka vhunduka waka qaro ne tsitsi.
