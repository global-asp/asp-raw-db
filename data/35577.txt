page_text:

P1	Amina is a very clever girl but her parents did not put her in school.
P2	She wished and prayed for God's help to enable him go to school.
P3	She had three friends who lived in the same village.
P4	The three friends were, Halima, Fatima and Khadijah.
P5	One day, one of their friends , Khadija was worried about Amina's stay at home.
P6	Khadija told them that she was going to tell her uncle.
P7	Each of Amina's friends told them their parents. They  helped her go to school.@Amina became pharmacist.
P8	Amina, Fatima, Halima and Khadija helped other girls to also learn. The village also had a school built by the four friendship.
