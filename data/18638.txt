title:	Kanyenyene Mbokakavwuna Nziba
author:	Edith Sikota Habwanda
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	ChiTonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18638.png
desc:	Nyika yayuma. Kanyenyene kalaanzala anyota. Kaunka kumulonga kuyakunywa meenda. Ono katolwa ameenda. Nziba yafwutula Kanyenyene. Ino Kanyenyene kalumba buti Nziba?

page_text:

P1	Izuba lyakabala kapati Cilimo eeco. Mucisaka mwakayuma.
P2	Kanyenyene tiikakajisi meenda kwamazuba manji.
P3	"Ndiyanda dosi lyameenda naanka kuti lyandilokela buyo kupepe," wakaamba Kanyenyene.
P4	Ono amume buyo wakaliyumide.
P5	"Naa nsejisi meenda ndilafwa," wakoompolola Kanyenyene. "Ndeelede kuya kumulonga ngondakamvwa."
P6	"Mulonga wazula alimwi ulaanguzu. Ulakutola," mbwaakakacenjezya Lukobo.
P7	Pele Kanyenyene kakalaanyota kapati. "Ndilafwa naa ntanywi meenda." Aboobo wakajoka kuyakuyandaula mulonga.
P8	Wakainda mubwizu bwakazambene aatalaamitabi yakayumide.
P9	Wakeenda limwi wakamvwa meenda asansa. Ooyu wakali mulonga. Wakaamvwa mayuwe aameenda.
P10	Kanyenyene kakamena nkudyu yameenda atontola.
P11	Kakakkomana kapati cakuti kunyina nokakabona yuwe nkolyalikuboola.
P12	Kanyenyene wakasola kujatilila kubwizu bwakali kwiimpauka,  kabwiinda awo mpokakabede.    Pesi kakapyeelwa ameenda.
P13	"Amundigwasye! Amundigwasye!" Kanyenyene kakaita.
P14	"Fwambaana,  tanta awa," mboyakaamba Nziba kaijisi katabi kumulomo.
P15	"Ndilakkala aano kutegwa ndilumbe Nziba." Kanyenyene kakayeeya.    "Ndilalindila kusikkila iboole kuzikunywa meenda."
P16	Buzuba bumwi kakalindila,  kwakaboola basankwa bobilo kumulonga. Basankwa aaba bakajisi malekani.
P17	"Kuli nziba mpati ituba iboola kunywa meenda," mbwaakaamba musankwa umwi.     "Njotutiilalile masiku asunu."
P18	"Nsekwe kulekela basankwa kujaya Nziba iituba," mbokakayeeya Kanyenyene. "Ono ndaceyesya. Inga ndacita buti?"
P19	Ndilyona buyo,  Nziba iituba yakaboola ansi kuzwa mumusamu kutegwa inywe meenda.
P20	Kanyenyene kakayeeya cakucita.
P21	Kakasotokela acituta camusankwa omwe. Kanyenyene kakaluma musankwa uulya acanguzu.
P22	Musankwa uulya wakasotoka. Mpoona wakoomba kati,  "Acuu!"
P23	Ilya Nziba yakanyongana mpoona yakauluka. Mboyakafwutuka oobo.
P24	Mbombuboobo Kanyenyene mbokaalumba Nziba akukafwutula nkoyakakafwula.
