desc:	I describe my different feelings at different times and with different people.

page_text:

P1	Ngoo yakwa ĩĩw'a syĩndũ mbiingĩ mũno.
P2	Nĩw'aa muyo ĩla sũsũ ũkũtweea ngewa mawĩoo.
P3	Nĩw'aa makothe ĩla ngũthaũka na mũnyanyawa.
P4	Nĩw'aa naĩ ĩla tata wasya ndena mbesa.
P5	Nĩw'aa nĩmwende ĩla mwaitũ angumbatĩla.
