desc:	Abasore babiri bemeye gucumbikirwa no kugaburirwa ninda

page_text:

P1	Kera habayeho abasore babiri baza kubura inka zabo. Bashakishije izo nka zabo ahantu hose, kugeza  bubiriyeho.
P2	Bumaze kwira, babonye urumuri mu nzu. Bemeranya kujya kuhasaba icumbi.
P3	Bagiye kuri iyo nzu barakomanga. Urugi rurakinguka barinjira. Abo basore ntibagira uwo babona muri iyo nzu. Gusa bumvise ijwi ribasuhuza.
P4	Iryo jwi bumvise ryarababwiye riti: "Ndi inda. Nimupfundure isafuriya murye, hanyuma mupfundure ikibindi munywe amarwa."
P5	Nuko inda yambara uruhu rwayo irisohokera.
P6	Abasore bamaze kurya no kunywa barashimira. Hanyuma basohoka mu nzu baragenda.
P7	Bakigenda baza kugira igitekerezo kibi. Baravugana bati: "Oya, ntibyashoboka ko twarya ibiryo twagaburiwe n'inda." Ako kanya bahise bafata umwanzuro wo kugaruka bagakandagira iyo nda bakayica.
P8	Bagarutse mu nzu bakandagira ya nda. Ariko inda yari yagiye kare; ndetse n'inzu nayo yahise izimira! Bisanze bahagaze hanze hadatwikiriye.
