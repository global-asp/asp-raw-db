page_text:

P1	This is Maggy's cat. The cat's name is Doris.
P2	What a naughty cat!
P3	Everybody loves this cat. They play with Doris too.
P4	Maggy enjoys watching Doris resting with mother cat. Doris has two brothers.
P5	Maggy loves cats! But cats do not like to be hugged.
P6	Maggy's friend and her family came to visit Doris the cat. They brought a present for Doris. Maggy is so happy about this.
