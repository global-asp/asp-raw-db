desc:	Ensuugha ya natabo enhungi eeyatiike

page_text:

P1	ono nhi natabo.she bagandha be abaleenzi mukaaga.
P2	bazaaile be bamugha akasuugha akatoono.akeendha inho.
P3	lwali lulala mugandaghe omuleenzi yamenha akasuugha.
P4	natabo tiyasobola kubonha kasuugha ke.yalila ela yaalila.
P5	natabo yalumuka gho.yanhiinha omutti omughanvu.
P6	bazaaile be bamwaaganha."ika ghansi,"bamukooba.
P7	bagandhabe abaleenzi beemba,"bambi ika ghansi."
P8	natabo akokolakola obusosolo o'lwolweemba.
P9	awo mukwaano gwa natabo aidha."baambi ika ghansi,"ayeemba.
P10	mukwaano gwa natabo ayeemba okutuusa bweyaika ghansi.
P11	mikwaano gye gyataambula naye okujja ghaka.
P12	bulimuntu yasanhuka nga natabo alighaka.Natabo afuna ensuugha eyiindhi.
