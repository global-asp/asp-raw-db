desc:	Imbwa yasanze amagi yingona mu kibaya cyinyinja irayatwara

page_text:

P1	Umunsi umwe,Imbwa yasanze amagi ku nyanja
P2	Nuko ifata amagi icumi iyashyira mu mufuka wayo irayajyana.
P3	Ayo magi irayajyana,irayamenya iyarindira mu bushyuhe.
P4	Ingona iza kubaza Imbwa iti, "waba wamboneye kumagi yange?".
P5	Uko niko  iyi Ngona yabajije nizindi nyamanswa zose.
P6	Muri icyo gihe,yamagi yarimo guturaga rimwe kurindi.
P7	Imbwa inanirwa guha ibidyo bihagije ubwana bw'ingona bwa turazwe.
P8	umunsi umwe, Ingona iza kwunva guhinda kuva mu nda z'ubwana bwayo
P9	Nuko yinjira munzu y'imbwa iyikubitisha umurizo
P10	Ingona irangije irayirukankana zimanuka mu muhanda
P11	Hanyuma Imbwa igeze aho irahagarara ibwiri Ingona iti, "mbabarira"
P12	Ingona irayibabara maze ihita ijya kwoga n'ubwana bwayo
