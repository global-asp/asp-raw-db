P1	Ndi hwemba.
P2	 Ali hu hubbira abaatu jamba.
P3	Ndi mu huhumba amaŋi.
P4	Ali hwegolola.
P5	 Alaanga.
P6	Ndi hufugira.
P7	Ali huŋulirisa.
P8	Ndi hwebbomba.
P9	I am skipping.
