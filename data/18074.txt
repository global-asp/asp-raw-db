desc:	ikeny lokut loatuman lo anyami erot

page_text:

P1	Ache kolong'it alosi ayong nawi,  abu atorukau lokut lotiatuman.
P2	Napei achaki amoru  anapandil kona,  dio kirema tama, "Akoro,  akoro akoro!"
P3	Toporo lokut kidiama. Nakaneni,  abu ayong epung'ar ewapak etor kiding ng'inyia.
P4	 Todol ni adoo kidiama ekitoe lotikoonon. Dio atachak nabo. Apak naga tachakun kwap.
P5	Atokopu ayong ikeny lokut kang.
P6	Ainak Erot: lo anyameene ibore daang nicha aanyuni ng'esi. Talakar ng'esi ikobari lokut.
P7	Apolot akou alokut,  nabo agogong noi ekoni epanga kori aep.
P8	Ayakar lokut atumes na ikoni na amesek. Arai lokesuburakinan noi!
P9	Nyepalag aanyun lokut. Eperenete nakwaare naduon  naebokito katen aekitoe nakoonok. Imujeene ituan ibore ni emina. Ng'esi anyamia Erot lokut.
