P1	My name is Subrata Roy Chowdhury. I have become old and have many parameters like sugar, blood pressure, etc. In 2020 I got the first dose of COVID vaccination. The next day my hip started paining. So I bought a strong  stick for myself.
P2	Then in 2021 I began to forget things. The doctor said that it is because of the hip pain. Then, at last, in 2022 I ended up breaking my femmer bone. The orthopaedic said that I need one year bed rest. I am very stubborn, after two days I
P3	Became restless, started moving my leg and sitting on the bed whereas the orthopaedic had told to lay down. In future what can happen, I don't know, but he has become a small baby boy!
