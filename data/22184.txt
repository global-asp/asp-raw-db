desc:	Who is cleverer - the raven or the fox? (This story was developed at a teacher workshop in Adwa

page_text:

P1	Long ago, wild animals lived together.  

Raven and Fox were good friends.
P2	There was a famine. The animals had nothing to eat.
P3	Fox and Raven went in search of food.

They found some meat. Raven started eating alone.
P4	Fox saw Raven eating meat alone. She grabbed the bird. 

"Shall I kill you or not?" asked Fox.
P5	"Please, do not kill me! Specially, do not throw me off the cliff," begged Raven.
P6	Fox replied, "I will not throw you off the cliff."
P7	But Fox had decided to throw Raven off the cliff.
P8	Fox threw Raven off the cliff. 

She stood at the edge of the cliff to check for Raven's body.
P9	Meanwhile, Raven had turned in the air and started flying.
P10	Raven landed in a tree.

Fox and Raven saw each other.
P11	"I escaped because I am clever," boasted Raven.
P12	"You escaped because I let you escape," replied Fox.
