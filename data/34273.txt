page_text:

P1	The farmer and his wife are farming.
P2	They decided to rest and have some fun.
P3	The farmer has been working all day,  he is now tired.
P4	They took another break from work.
P5	The farmer saw a rabbit.  He threw his hoe at it but missed. The dog ran after it.
P6	His hoe is broken! They only have one left! They are sad!
P7	The farmer wondered where to get another rabbit.
P8	He remembered  that he had a chicken at home but it would be tireing to hunt it so he will ask his neighbor to help him catch it.
