desc:	A story for toddlers about a cow with daytime identity as a soft toy

page_text:

P1	Eino ekhafu. Ekhafu ili bulai po!
P2	Khwama echuli khwola kumusi ekhafu yanga'ali.
P3	Epurusia ikaiti.
P4	Namwe yipa kumpira.
P5	Musilo mwosi,  ekhafu yakhuukia!
P6	Eonia bulamu.
P7	Yipania busiku.
P8	Nono nofunikha chimoni lukali,  Mala wikombe kamalayi -.
P9	Ekhafu enyala yakhukendelakho wesi!
P10	The End.
