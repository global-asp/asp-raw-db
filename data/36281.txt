page_text:

P1	Edounio ikoku be Achimili Alorokon . Arai apakeng’ emarabut kaa toto keng’ aturkanait nasonyokait alokatilo, akwap angamanat.@@Abu Achimili topolou todol ngikar luelosio losukul.
P2	Apot ngikauriak keng’ towotokis katilu, sodi kisiekinia Achimili eskul.@@Alosukulaa , aminasi ng’iketatamk Achimili kotere arai ng’esi ikoku niamina akiswam noi, pee ecanene ng’ide luce. @@Apei kwaariaa, abu eketatam epei king’it ng’esi
P3	“Achimili, koterenyo iminar iyong’ akiswam akidwang’ ngide luce?”@@Tamaa Achimili kane, Asakiang’ atoluwor akamian ng’ikeny luapolok luitorenete kidiami ama.
P4	Kotere, kalimokini kolong’ apakang’ tamaakane, “ikoku, kiricak jik akiswam, ng’esi eng’auni akiyar kon moi monan.”
