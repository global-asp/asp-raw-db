page_text:

P1	My name is John.
P2	I have an older brother called Ken.Â  Ken loves me and I love him too.
P3	We walk to school together in the morning.
P4	When the bell rings for break time,  Ken helps me to put on my games uniform.
P5	Often I play with Ken and his friends during break. When the bell rings at the end of the day,  we hurry home.
P6	After we reach home,  we go to swim in the river.
P7	At bedtime Ken reminds me how to brush my teeth.
P8	During the weekend,  we go to play football in the field.
P9	I love my brother very much.
P10	My mother loves watching her children play football.
