id:	8467
title:	Eger Lo Aiunia Imukunyo Akuuri
author:	Kholeka Mabeta
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8467.png
desc:	The land is dry and Little Ant is very thirsty. He goes to the river for a drink and is swept away. Dove saves Little Ant’s life. How does Little Ant thank Dove?

page_text:

P1	Arai apaaranit nakariran ng'irwa lu akamu anamoni anakoonon.
P2	Ang'opikinito Imukunyo ng'akipi ngirwa lukaalak.
P3	"Asaki ayong ekabelit a ang'akipi,  tani kechakun anakuyen."
P4	Nabo dae akutasi tani ng'akop.
P5	"Pekamat ayong ng'akipi atoani," kubaloo Imukunyo. "Alosi ayong nag'olol na apupi."
P6	"Kileleyari iyong ang'olol," kikany Ekunyuku lokooson ng'osi.
P7	Anyarinut Imukunyo akure lokojokon noi. "Atoani ayong pekarmat ng'adio kipi."
P8	Torotok Imukunyo asakar ang'olol.
P9	Toped ng'inyaa lukoonok ka kidiama ng'akuii nakoonok.
P10	Tani ni eiraria ng'itoroi ang'olol ekulaate.
P11	Totuk Imukunyo ng'akipi nakalimok nikooyen.
P12	Alakara ng'esi kotere amam etoro loapolon abunit.
P13	Kitetemiki Imukunyo akikod ng'inyia lu elebolebosi anakipi. Kaneni apotu ng'akipi toleleyasi ng'esi.
P14	"Keng'araki idio toan. King'arakinae kare."
P15	"Todoka atipei," Tolimok Akuuri nakwaan ng'esi ekodit ateni anakituk.
P16	"Nyarotokini mati kabuak Akuuri alakari. Edaruni ayong ng'esi ebong'uni akimat ng'akipi."
P17	Apei kwaar idarounit,  apot ng'ide lusapa ng'iarei potu nang'olol ng'apandilio.
P18	"Eyei Akuuri nakwaan na ebunene neke akimata ng'akipi," tolimu idia ipei. "Kiari ng'oni ng'esi atanyama ebong."
P19	"Nyemiekini ayong ng'ide luku taara Akuuri nakwaan. Nabo adit ayong,  ngo asubakini ayong."
P20	Napei kona topor Akuuri nakwaan kwap ekitoe akimat ng'akipi.
P21	Ayakar Imukunyo atamet.
P22	Toporok edia epei nakeju dio tokony ng'esi lokojokon.
P23	Topor edia. "Oi takoi!" towanik.
P24	Tobul Akuuri toporo niajokon.
P25	Kong'ina ng'esi abuakinia Imukunyo Akuuri ajokis kotere aiun ng'esi.
