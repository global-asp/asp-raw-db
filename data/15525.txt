title:	Enkerai Nayawua Eseriani
author:	Susan Nashipae
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Maa
level:	First sentences

desc:	 Enkerai nayaka oolmurrani le nkae murrua eseriani.

page_text:

P1	Netii ena tomononi nanapita enkerai enye entim alo aitayu irganayio.
P2	Ore pee ebaya ninye etim netum olchani oata irganayio ootoo.
P3	 Neiragie ninye enkerai enye nairura ti abori olchani neilepaki taa ninye olchani.
P4	Ore egira olmurrani loingua enkae murrua aim ine neingasia ninye oleng pee edol ina kerai neikilikwan nejo,  "Koree pasa ngotonye ena kerai."
P5	Ore taa pee ejo ninye airugo neinyoyie enkerai  oltoilo lo masaa naishopito  o laduo murrani te murrt enye.
P6	Neisho ninye enkerai edalare imasaa enyena egira ninye enkerai akweni.
P7	Neigor ninye ngotonye enkerai abori ainguraa ajo kanyio nagira aitakweni enkerai enye nedol oltungani lemeyiolo ninye piipi otashare enkerai.
P8	Neirutt ninye oleng ometabaa ana neitiaya ninye orbene obore irrganayio.
P9	Neigorr ninye olmurrani ina tomononi nejoki,  ''Mintoki airrut amu  kagira ake adalare ena kerai ino sidai.''.
P10	Ina taa pee edou ninye entomononi to olchani.
P11	"Neitayu ninye olmurrani emaasai enye nabo  neisho ninye enkerai,  nejoki nena enkishoroto ino."
P12	Nejoki ninye ina tomononi,  ''Iwaa enkerai ino orbene lino iwa ang nijoki orpayian lino pee eidurr emurrua natii eseriani amu aishoo enkeraai enye eseriani.
