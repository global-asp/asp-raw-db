page_text:

P1	Nnhingirira bambi.
P2	Ndi mughala.
P3	Ndi na katabo kange akayaaka.
P4	Lengera  akatabo kange akayaaka.
P5	Muganda ghange yeeyaziike akatabo kange.
P6	Ni muganda ghange omulenzi yeena akeeyaziike.
P7	Ate buli muntu ayenda okusoma akatabo kange.
P8	Buti  kankasome akatabo kange nkamaleku.
