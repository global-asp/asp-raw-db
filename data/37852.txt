desc:	UNita uyajinga

page_text:

P1	UNita ujonge ezantsi, iinwele zakhe ezinde zirhuqa phantsi. @@Imithi, ingca, nayo yonke into igqwethekile.
P2	Iinyawo zakhe zixhokonxa isibhaka-bhaka. @@UNavi omncinci uhamba-hamba apha. @@Uthi kuye, "Ndakhe ndakubona apha ngaphambili. Uphinde wajonga  ezantsi! Wenzela ntoni?"
P3	Iinyawo zikaNavi zajikeleza ngokulula emoyeni. @@UNita uzama ukusithela ngapha kweenwele zakhe.@@"Ku-u-u-nz-i-i-ma uku-th-e-e th-a-a," watsho uNita. @@"Andifani nabanye. Andiyonxalenye."
P4	UNavi umbamba ngesandla. Ufuna ukumnceda aqonde. @@Bagwencelela kwindawo uNavi athanda ukuhlala kuyo abone yonke into. @@Apho phezulu babona izinto ezininzi.@@Kweso sikhundla, bathi ntsho kubantwana abadlala pha napha.
P5	Abo bantwana abafani tu.@@U-Abe uyingqukuva.
P6	UChi unamachokoza ebusweni.
P7	ULala mde kakhulu.
P8	UBambam ubonisa ukundweba nokukhululeka.
P9	ULulu uyazifundela ethe cwaka.
P10	Jonga iinwele zikaFreya ezidlaka-dlaka.
P11	Yena uSid unxiba izipekisi kuyo yonke indawo.
P12	Mna, ndibhitye ndingamathambo.@@Wena unguwe. Awuwedwa.@@Wonke umntu unendawo emangalisayo, uyabona.@Oko kukwenza ufane, njengam.@@Ilizwe lona lingumdlalo omkhulu. Ukuze sikwazi ukudlala, asinakho ukufana sonke.
P13	UNita waziva emi ngendlela efanelekileyo, enkosi kumhlobo omtsha amfumeneyo. @@Akukho mnandi ukujonga ezantsi. @@Ngoku udlala nomntu wonke.
