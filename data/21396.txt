page_text:

P1	Fana and her family live in a busy town.
P2	Fana has a cat, two hens, a goat and a dove.
P3	One day, Fana saw other children throwing stones at doves.
P4	Fana ran towards the children. 

She shouted, “Stop throwing stones.”
P5	Fana took the two doves home to look after them.
P6	She fed them and told her family how she saved them.
P7	Fana and her parents took the doves to the clinic.
P8	Fana tells her friends, “Animals are our friends. We protect them.”
