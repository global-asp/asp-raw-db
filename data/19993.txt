title:	Nyathi ma nyakare
author:	Joseph Alfred Owino Ogot and Owino Ogot
artist:	Meghan Judge
lang:	Dhopadhola
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Kigana ma fonjo wan kwoŋ teko ma rwenyo gi nono kosa peco mawan kelo.

page_text:

P1	Aneno dhano to akaŋasa go tek tek.
P2	To aneno dhako ma yac ma omako iye.
P3	Jo calo mawan jo jolo dhako no to doŋ both wan.
P4	I dyer wor dhako no okoko ni, "Nyathi! Eno nyathi wok."
P5	Dhako no to nywolo punda.
P6	Ji jo penjere ni, "Wa no tim nedi?"
P7	Ji jo ŋweco kwoŋ go. To dhako no paro ni, "A la timere nedi gi nyathi me?"
P8	Rumacen, dhako no oyere to maro nyathi pere.
P9	Nyathi no odongo, obedo ma dwoŋ, ma odhyero kir min tiŋo.
P10	Min obedo gi gut. Nyathi pere bende omako nge to gweyo go.
P11	Oŋweco woko munyo oneno min opodho piny.
P12	Nyathi punda odoŋ kende, kec oneko, ool, to nindo kwalo.
P13	Munyo nyathi punda ocew, oneno jal moro ocungo dho bothe.
P14	Jalo otiŋo go to dok gine peco pere.
P15	Nyathi punda otiŋo jalo i gongo munyo otegino.
P16	Ndir acel, jo idho wi got moro kir malo, jo ol to jo podho nindo.
P17	Munyo nyathi punda ocew, onwaŋo ni nitye kende kendo.
P18	Nyathi punda obedo gi gut. Owotho ka gi kaca rango min.
P19	Nyathi punda oneno dhano moro to penjere ni, "Meno
won ki mama?"
P20	Wok i ndiri no nyahti punda gi min jo bedo ka nyacel. Tiŋo min to wotho gi ne.
