desc:	This is a counting story. Work out how many cupcakes Ben and Thabo left for Granny.

page_text:

P1	Ben nende Thabo bali basoleli babili be njala.
P2	Kukhu khang'ona chikeki chinditi likhumi. Akhachira khusaani.
P3	Ben abukula abukula ekeki enditi endala. Thabo abukula chikeki chinditi chibili.
P4	Ben abukula chikeki chinditi chine. Thabo abukula chikeki chinditi chibili.
P5	Chikeki chinditi chinga nicho Ben kalia? Chikeki chinditi chinga nicho Thabo kalia? Chikeki chinditi chinga chiramile kukhu?
