page_text:

P1	Amahoro ni ikintu k'ingenzi mu buzima bwacu bwa buri munsi.
P2	Umuntu udafite amahoro nta kizima abasha gutekereza cyangwa ngo akore.
P3	Iyo nta mahoro ufite, uta ikizere cyo kubaho ukaniyumvisha ko ntacyo wakora ngo gishoboke.
P4	Amahoro ni umuzi w' ikizere cyo kubaho, ubukungu n'iterambere rirambye.
P5	Umuryango ufite amahoro ntakintu cyabasha kuwuhungabanya mu mibanire y'abawugize.
P6	Inshuti zifite amahoro zirakundana, kandi zikabana nta nkomyi.
P7	Igihugu gifite amahoro nta ntambara ikirangwamo kandi ibyo abanyagihugu bifuje, bashyira hamwe bakabigeraho.
P8	Amahoro abe kuri mwe mwese!
