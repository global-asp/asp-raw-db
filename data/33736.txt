desc:	About the house and garden that this young writer had to leave behind when she left her home in the Congo. (Story and illustrations created at a June 2019 holiday workshop

page_text:

P1	This house is in Congo. @@I like this house.
P2	We had our own bathroom.@@We had our own toilet.
P3	We had a garden. @@I was planting my own garden.
P4	My mother told me, "My mother taught me to plant. Now I am teaching you."
P5	I planted tomatoes and spinach. @@I did it alone.
P6	I would take tomatoes to my mother.@@She would say, "Thank you."
P7	We left that house in Congo.
P8	We have no garden here in South Africa.
