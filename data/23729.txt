title:	AYOM KI NYANG

page_text:

P1	Chengu moh, Nyang ubedo chok ki nam icheng. Ayom ubino kangere kiyaga gumote.
P2	Ayom upenyu gang a nyang. Nyang ugamo  "Abedo i nam" Nyang uyomo i Ayom ki gameh. Ayom upenyo kidojo.  "Kwo i nam bedo nining? beh?"
P3	Nyang ugamo, "Kwo i nam beh.  Ibedo kwene?"  Ayom ugamo, "Abedo iwi yat.  Achamo labolo, muyeme ki pot yat.  Ichamo ngo"
P4	Nyang ugamo, "Achamo riyu" Nyang no tika tamo yo me mako ki chamo ayom. Upenyo Ayom  "Iromo kwang"
P5	Ayom ugamo, "Matwala, pangeyo kwang" Nyang uwagu ni "weka fonyi kit ma kwang."  Ayom ugamo "Pamiru ngeyo kit ma kwang"
P6	Nyang ubolo Ayom  "Wa dogo lurem. Pey lwo an. Bin, ibed i ngeya."  Ayom ubedo i nge Nyang muchago kwang.
P7	Ma gu oh i dye nam, Nyang upenyo Ayom, Imaro bedo I nam, itye nining? itye maber"  Ayom ugamo ni  "Pe rach gire, atye maber, iya yom"
P8	Nyang uwago bot Ayom  "Kom nera lit. Peh wah no yat me konye"
P9	Ayom no lwo tutwal, kome uchago yenge. No tika tamo yo me lwi ki wang Nyang.
P10	Ayom utamo. Uwago bot Nyang, "Pey pa. Abi miyu chwinya bot neri. Riyu pah Ayom romo konye."
P11	Chwiny Nyang udogo yom "Iromo miyu chwinyi bot nera?" Ayom ugamo ni "Aromo doh. Wan lurem"
P12	Ma fud cha ma dwong fud po karo. Ayom uwagu ni  "Oh, wiya uwil ikom chwinya, awego iwi yat. Wa chet ka gamo neh"
P13	Nyang upenyo  "Lok adah? Ibi kelo chwinyi ki iwi yat?"  Ayom ugamo  "abi kelo doh, beh abi chero kedi ka neno neri"
P14	Nyang uye kiyaga ukwango wanga u oh i dog nam. Ma gu oh iwi ngom, Ayom upye ki inge Nyang.
P15	Ma dong Ayom u oh iwi yat, Ayom udango ni  "Afoyo me nyuta nam, Nyang, iromo chero dong"
P16	Nyang ukejo kiyaga uwajo ni  "Ibola? itye ka binu ki chwinyi? Ayom ugamo ni  "No aromo bedo ingeyi pi yen no atye ki chwinya"
