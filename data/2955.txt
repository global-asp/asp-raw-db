desc:	This story is about the different sorts of books my family reads.

page_text:

P1	Muganda wange ali kusoma ekitabo ky'eisomero.
P2	Mama wange ali kusoma Baibuli.
P3	Mwaninhaze ali kusoma ekitabo ekyebyokufumba.
P4	Baaba wange omuto ali kusoma mawulire.
P5	Daadha ali kusoma katabo ka mawulire.
P6	Mwaninhaze omuto ali kusoma katabo ka bifaanani.
P7	Ndi kusoma kitabo kino.
P8	Ndi kumpi kusobola kusoma buli kintu!
