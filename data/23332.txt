title:	Ebizike bisiru
author:	Lule Eriah
artist:	Natalie Propa
lang:	Luganda

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Waliwo enjala Mulongo nefamileye ne batandika okunonya emmere.

page_text:

P1	Waliwo enjala  fulula biswa  Mulongon ne famile ye tebalina mere
P2	Lwali lumu  yabatwala mukibira  okunonya ekyokulya
P3	Basanga olusuku lwa  matoke naye nga teluliko nyini lwo
P4	Oluvanyuma  ezike eddene lyatuka  

Nelibaleka nebabere wo
P5	Amakya agamu nelimugamba  nti afumbe mutabani we
P6	olwegulo  ezike nera nelimugamba  fumba muwala  wo enkya
P7	Abazadde  nebakweka mula wabwe muti
P8	Enkeera neligamba omukazi  enkya nfumbira bawo
P9	olwegulo olwo nelimugamba  yefumbe
P10	Nga limaze okulya  lyebaka nelifulutira wansi womuti
P11	Ezike lyabalaba  nga balamu 

Nelinyiga
P12	Bali tega  akakodiyo  nebaka wansi  okuva kumuti
