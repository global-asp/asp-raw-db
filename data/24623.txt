title:	Omushuma Kakulu
author:	Mark Byamugisha
artist:	Mango Tree, Wiehan de Jager, Lerato Khuzwayo, Salim Kasamba, Saulsville, Magriet Brink and Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	Runyankore

desc:	Kakulu nomusheija wa Bendo village kuguga kare. Yatakwenda okuhinga nokukora ekintu kyona. Bakamukwata  omumisiri eya Mukasa.

page_text:

P1	Kare munonga, habireyo
  omushieja, nibamweta Kakulu.
P2	Akaba natuura n’omukazi
  we n’abaana be bakaaga.
P3	Kakulu akaba nayimuka
  kare munonga omukasheshe akuruga ‘omuka.
P4	Omukazi we akatura nayebuza
  aha kuba naza.
P5	Akaba naaza omu misiri gyabantu kand’ akwiba ebyokulya.
P6	Ab’ekyaro baka manya ngu hiine akwiiba
  ebyokurya byaabo.
P7	Bakaroopa omushango ow’omukama
  w’ekyaro
P8	Y’abeeta omu rukiiko
  kandi basharamu okubarakwate omushuma.
P9	Omukasheshe bakora akakodiyo.
P10	Engoma z’ekyaro baziteera
  okweeta abantu b’ekyaro okwiija omu rukiiko.
P11	Bagyenda ow’omukama kandi
  Kakulu babeire bamusibire omumeisho.
P12	Omukazi we n’abana batabdika
  okura n’okubashaba ngu bamurekye agyende.
P13	Omukama y’amubinga kandi
  yatakwenda kumusasira.
P14	Kakulu n’eka yeeye
  baruga omu kyaro kandi babateine
  kugarukayo.
