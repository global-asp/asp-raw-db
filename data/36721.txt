P1	Once apon a time a old woman was sitting on the couch when she saw a angel.
P2	So she was so afraid but the angel said said don't be scared for you are going to have a grand son She spreader her arms.
P3	The angel made her move to another spot.
P4	She was sent to a another place so she set.
P5	The angel sent her back and she was happy the end.
