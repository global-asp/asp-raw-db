P1	Once upon a time were two opharns named Thabo and Jack.They grew up with no parents their parents died when they were young at the age of six years .They played soccer together and gathered food for eating .
P2	When Thabo used to see kids with their parents he used to cry and ask God is this really what you had prepared for us in life as he cried but Jack  said â€œyou have to be strong ".Sometimes they had to sleep on an empty stomach .
P3	It was hard for them to get a good life because they did not have money .People always laughed at them .They worked in their neighbours garden for some money to buy food.
P4	One day a woman heard about them and took them to an orphanage .They lived the and got food , education and all things kids had got.
P5	After a year a man and a lady arrived at the orphanage with  a donation  for the kids. And saw Thabo and Jack and she liked them she had no kids so she decided to adopt them .
P6	She went with them home and prepared rooms for them and cooked a meal for them .Then the lady got them admitted in a school they got used to their new parent's and house .They got a good life and lived happily ever after.
