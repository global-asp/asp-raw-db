desc:	Child thinks of travelling and wonders what is the best means of transport.

page_text:

P1	There are many things of travelling.what i am going to choose.
P2	Can i travel with an airplane?
P3	The airplanes are flying on the sky.I am scared of flying.
P4	Maybe i can travel with the helicopter.
P5	But the helicopters and airplanes are the same.
P6	Maybe its better if i use a train.
P7	But the train stop is very far.
P8	Or maybe i can ride on bus?
P9	But our has only got one bus.
P10	Maybe i can ride on a truck.
P11	But the trucks are carrying heavy loads.
P12	Can i ride on a bicycle?
P13	But my father is he going to borrow me his bicycle?
P14	Maybe i can try the boat?
P15	But i do not like boat!
P16	Oh!Now i know what i am going to travel with.I will travel with tractor.
