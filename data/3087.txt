desc:	This story talks about the different kinds of houses that we live in.

page_text:

P1	Mzi ekalaa nyumba ya musonge.
P2	Thulani ekalaa nyumbani ya kona inya sianene.
P3	Nyumba ii ni ya kona inya iteanene.
P4	Wite wa nyumba ii ni wa kona itatu.
P5	Mundu uu eaka nyumba yake.
P6	Nyumba ii ni nzeu.
P7	Nyumba ii ni nguu.
P8	Kethiwa no nekalie nyumba ii.
