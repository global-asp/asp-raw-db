desc:	Kigana kwoŋ dhano ma oneno nyaliŋ liŋ pa Tho to ku konyo go.

page_text:

P1	Cango,  cango oyido niye ŋat m'ilwoŋo ni Hupapa. Oyido bedo yo Rukul koro.
P2	Ndir acel to Tho bino lyewo go.
P3	Lworo omako Hupapa munyo oneno wendo nger no.
P4	Tho owaco ri go ni,  "A omin kidho neno were perin." Hupapa oywakere ri go ni,  "Fodi akiyikere." Tho to ridho ri Hupapa ni hongo pere otundo.
P5	Hupapa oyiko cemo ma ber swa to orweyo Tho.
P6	Tho ocemo to yeŋ to podho nindo. Hupapa okwalo papila pa Tho to ndiko nyinge piny ma cowe.
P7	Munyo Tho ocew,  ofoyo Hupapa swa gi rweyo go. To cako lwoŋo nyingi ji cako gi ma piny icowe.
P8	Silwany ma rac,  Hupapa oyido otyeko kowo nyinge i ma cowe.
