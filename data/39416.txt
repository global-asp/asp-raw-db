title:	Swinki ya ga Keke
author:	Oteng Nkanokang
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Setswana
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/39416.png
desc:	KeKe o iswinka mo swinking ya gagwe e a e ratang. A ya godimodimo. O tshogela gore o tloga a fofela ko ngweding.

page_text:

P1	Keke a palama mo swinking ya gagwe. O ne a rata swinki ya gagwe. E ne e le swinki e di phalang tsotlhe
P2	O ne a ikgorometsa ka bonya. Go ya pele le morago fela jalo . Fa a ntse a ikgarametsa, swinki ya gagwe ya tsamaya ka bonako thata. Go ise go ye kae ke fa a le ko mankalekaleng.
P3	Keke a leba fa fatshe. O ne a le ko mankalekaleng. "Heelang" a goeletsa. "Bona gore ke ko mankalekaleng jang"
P4	O ne a itumeletse go bo a le ko mankalekaleng. O ne a kgona go bona motse otlhe. O ne a tloga a kgoma dinonyane tse di fofang mo masimong.
P5	Morago ga lebakanyana keke a batla go fologa. Swinki e ne e gana go ema. "Ijoo, Ijoo, Ijoo," a goeletsa. "Ga ke itse gore nka emisa jang swinki e!"
P6	Swinki ya ya hela jalo ka mokgwa o le mongwe. Godimo le tlase, godimo le tlase. Keke o ne a tshogile. "Swinki e e nkisa kae? ke ka nna ka fofela ngweding. Ke tshwanetse ka fologa!" a lela.
P7	Keke a tlogela dithapo tsa swinki. A wela ko tlase, tlase,  tlase.
P8	"ijoo, Ijoo, Ijoo!" ga goeletsa bananyana ba bangwe. Keke a wela fatshe. A golafala mo tlhogong. "Iiiii, Iiiii, Iiiii,"a lela.
P9	Bananyana ba banngwe ba ne ba tshwenyegile thata. Keke o ne a sa itse o ka reng. "Iiii, Iiii, Iiiii,"a lela
P10	Bananyana ba bitsa morutabana wa bone. "Mma Mpho, ka tsweetswee tlaya kwano!" ba bitsa. "Keke o wele mo swinking. O golafetse mo tlhogong."
P11	Mma Mpho a ragogela kwa swinking. A tsholetsa Keke a bo a mo atla mo tlhogong. Keke a nyenya, "Go botoka jaanong, ga nkitla ke palama swinki e ke le nosi" a bua jalo. "Ga ke sa batla go ya ngweding."
P12	"Nako e tlang ke tla swinka le wena, Keke," ga bua jalo Mma Mpho. "Re tlaa ya ngweding mmogo!"
