page_text:

P1	Nhendha amaadhi.
P2	Nnhumirwa okuzaanhisa amaadhi.
P3	Amaadhi gali kugwa.
P4	Amaadhi agagwa gatugha amaadhi.
P5	Nnhumirwa okuzaanhira kuliya.
P6	Aye amaadhi gali kutoonha.
P7	Nhumirwa okuzaanhira kuliya mu maadhi.
P8	Enviiri dhange mbolu olw'okuzaanhira mu maadhi. Bbaabba ansiba enviiri.
