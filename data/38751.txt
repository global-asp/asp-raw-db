desc:	Mary at home is a Story written by Johnmark of Gatimbi library. It talks about Mary a young respectful and obedient girl. Mary has a friend called Dora. At the end Mary celebrates her birthday with her friends

page_text:

P1	Mary is a hardworking girl.She respects her parents.After finishing her work Mary went and ask her parents to excuse her to go  and play with her friends.Because Mary was obedient her parents allowed her.Mary left to pray with her friends.
P2	After a while Mary came back home crying to report to her parents.Her parents told her friends to be careful when they play with her because she is young.Mary's mother asked her to forgive her friends.She forgave them and went away.
P3	Mary decided to go and play on the mango tree.She climbed the tree and sat at the top.
P4	After some few minutes her parents who were watching from far followed her and told her to climb down the tree.Mary obeyed them and decided to climb down.
P5	Dora the friend of Mary came to look for Mary so that they can go and play.Dora called Mary and waited her to climb down from the tree.
P6	As they were going,Mary reminded Dora that it was her birthday.So Dora decided to go and help Mary to go and prepare for the birthday.They went and also invited their friends.
P7	When Mary and Dora arrived home they were surprised to get their friends and their parents waiting for them.They sang for Mary a happy birthday song as they danced, ate and drunk.Mary was given a gift by her grandparents and she was happy.
