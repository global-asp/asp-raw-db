title:	Timonjero Steals
author:	Hellen Wambui
artist:	Melany Pietersen, Michael Omadi and Selina Masego Morulane
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15177.png
desc:	The story is about a girl who steals money in order to buy sweets.

page_text:

P1	There was a girl called Timonjero.
P2	Timonjero liked sweets but did not have money to buy any.
P3	She stole money and bought sweets. She hid to eat them.
P4	Timonjero heard her sisters asking,  "Who took the money we saved?"
P5	Timonjero was afraid of her angry sisters. She ran away.
P6	She met an ugly animal. It said,  "I will eat you."
P7	Timonjero was so scared. She got into the boat to run away from the ugly animal.
P8	Mother asked,  "Why did you steal?"  Timonjero said,  "I am sorry."
P9	Timonjero was forgiven. She went to play with her friends.
