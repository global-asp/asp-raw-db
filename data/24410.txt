desc:	Judy and Mother are responsible in their family. They do housework and always work hand in hand helping each other.

page_text:

P1	Judy and her mother wake up early in the morning and prepare breakfast for the rest of the family.
P2	They collect the utensils they used and Judy washes and rinses them.
P3	Judy and her mother go to the market to buy groceries to prepare for lunch.
P4	They arrive home and start cooking food for the family. The other children are helping too.
P5	Food is ready and everyone at home sits down at the diningroom table to eat and enjoy.
P6	After eating, Judy clears the table and gives everyone water or milk to drink.
P7	While her mother clears the table, Judy sweeps the dining room to make it tidy.
P8	Judy sings as she washes the utensils.
P9	After washing up, Judy goes out to play with her siblings.
P10	Her mother sews a mat.
