page_text:

P1	This is my school, it has eight classrooms, one staff room which is divided in to two, one for the teachers and the other for the headmaster my school is good.
P2	We have fifteen teachers in my school, my best teacher is MRS. Juma, she is a good teacher, she cares and loves us so much.i love MRS juma.
P3	During break time, we play in the field. It is big, we have ropes and balls that we use to play. Break time is my favourite  time.
P4	This is our school library. It has a lot of books. During our free time. We come to read in the library. It has rules. It is fun to read in the library.
P5	Our school environment is clean, every morning we pick up the dirts  in the compound. Mr Macharia is in charge of the environment. He is very harsh. All the students are scared of him.
P6	We are five hundred pupils in my school. Class six has the highest number of pupils, our school performance is encouraging too. Last year we were number two in  my district i love my school.
P7	My school is very good. It is the best in town, am proud of my school.
