desc:	Emirimo edhindi dhiwula edhindi? Oga dhoonadhoona daagayaga.

page_text:

P1	Abantu bano baghakana.
P2	"Basomesa n'abawula. Mutwetaaga okusoma," omusomesa bwakoba.
P3	"Bazimbi naabawula. Mwetaaga endhu," omuzimbi bwakoba.
P4	"Babaadhi naabawula. Mwetaaga ebibaadhe," omubaadhi bwakoba.
P5	"Basawo naabawula. Mutwetaaga okuba abalamu."
P6	Balimi naabawula. Mutwetaaga lwa mmere."
P7	"Abasomi naabawula!" omusomi bwakoba.
P8	Kuba mwenamwena mwali basomi okusooka!" Buti buli muntu aikiriiza.
