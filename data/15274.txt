desc:	What do you enjoy doing?

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping with my friend.
P2	I enjoy writing letters on the board. Ninafurahia kuandika herufi kubwa ninazoweza kusoma kwa wepesi.
P3	I enjoy reading books with pictures. Ninafurahia kusoma hadithi zinzovutia.
P4	I enjoy eating different fruit. Ninafurahia kula matunda tofauti.
P5	I enjoy helping at home. Ninafurahia kumwoshea mama vyombo.
P6	I enjoy listening to my mother's stories. Ninafurahia kumsikiliza mama akisimulia hadithi za kuvutia.
P7	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Aah! Ninafurahia kufanya hivi vyote.
P8	I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?
