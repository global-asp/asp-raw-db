P1	Once upon a time, a girl was born on April 30, 2001. She is the the 4th child in a 5 siblings. She was name as Carelia.
P2	She was adorable, kind and naughty girl. She was close with her little sister and she has so many friends.And the time passes by, she is slowly changing.
P3	From being adorable to being grumpy, from being naughty to serious type of person. The experience the she encountered were the one that changed her. No one can stay her fo a long time until she met these four girls who stay beside her.
P4	And she became teenager, she loves collecting bags, shoes, and fashion clothes. It made her happy.
P5	She is now a college student who dream to finish her study. Find a job that has high salary in order to support her parents. Her dream is to become a successful business woman some day. That's why she is doing her best to achieve her goals.
