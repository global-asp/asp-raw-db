title:	Petros yaboona omunywani
author:	Shiba Atukunda
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Runyankore

desc:	Ekyigano kirungi kirikukwata aha mukago ahagati yomwojo nana embwa.

page_text:

P1	Petros akaba ari omwana wamagyezi. Akaba ari omwana omwe wenka.
P2	Eizooba rimwe, Petros akaza kuzaana nabanywanibe.
P3	Akabwa kato kakaba kakwatiire omukyina. "Ninza kukuyamba, "Petros yagamba.
P4	Embwa yamoka, yaziina, yarigasa Petros namashemererwa.
P5	Munywani wa Petros yabiinga embwa. "Rekyeraho! Rekyeraho!" Petros yagamba.
P6	Embwa yeiruka. Yayeshereka aboojo babi.
P7	Banywani ba Petros bamushanga narira. Bagyira, "Leka turonde embwa yawe."
P8	Petros akaba ateine matsiko. Banywanibe bagyira, "Niruza kugyiboona."
P9	Baronda burihamwe. Petros yagyira, "Nebaasa kuba eri aha mugyera."
P10	Embwa ekaba etinire aboojo. Petros yayeta, "Otaguruka!"
P11	Embwa yeiruka erikuza owa Petros. Bakaba bashemereirwe hamwe.
P12	Banywanibe bagyira, "Tusasire. Nayo nezakuba munywani weitu."
