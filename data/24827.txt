desc:	Omughala aguya dhaadha ghe. Bwamala okulya emere. Dhaadha ghe ategeera atya amazima?

page_text:

P1	Abaana batyaime nga bagabulwa emere nga baja okulima.
P2	Abaana bagemye embago. Baja kulima.
P3	Abaana ni dhaadha ghaibwe babeikereire embago. Omughala owundi iye airayo.
P4	Omughala aiwula emere  yagilya ku bainhe yenka nga bazirawo.
P5	Dhaadha gh'abaana afumbawo emere eyindi.
P6	Dhaadha gh'abaana asandhukula ku mmere.
P7	Dhaadha abuuza aliire emere era abaana beegaana boonaboona.
P8	Dhaadha ghaibwe yasiba akaguwa kumwiga bakabiteku olwo azuule aliire emere.
P9	Omughala alina akateteeyi ka kathungwa neeyasooka okutambuliraku yaasala.
P10	Omulenzi n'omughala owa kateteeyi ka kilagala bawakana abe eireku.
P11	Omughala mu kiteteeyi kya kilagala yairaku era entiisa yaamugema yaagwamu.
P12	Omughala omulimba agwa mu maadhi era alemererwa okutambulira ku kaguwa.
P13	Dhaadha omukazi n'omulenzi, bainhulula omughala mu mwiga.
