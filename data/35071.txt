title:	Mpikane yacikuti cilu cangu
author:	Ahmmardouh  Mjaya and Lyna  Tchale
artist:	Angie and  Upesh
lang:	Ciyawo (Malawi)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ana pana lyuŵa limwapikanile yacikumsalila cilu cenu?

page_text:

P1	Lelo ganjawula ku sukulu.@@Lelo lyakupumula.
P2	Lelo ngandolela kanema.@@Kwangali magesi soni.
P3	Ana cindende cici?@@Lelo cimbikanile yacikuti cilu cangu!
P4	Candanda cindame jiii kuti mbikane yacikuti cilu cangu.
P5	Elo sambano ngukupikana kupumula kwangu.@@Ngupumula cenenepe.
P6	Soni ngukombola kupumula mwakusinganga ssssssssss!@@Kwisa soni mwangali lisengwe mmmmmmmm.
P7	Sambano ngupikana kugunda kwa mtima!@@Titii tii tii.
P8	Ana mpaka ngombole kuwutendekasya mtimawu kuti ugunde mwakuwutuka ni mwakunyanyika?@@Elo pakuguluka mwinani ni kutula pasi kwakwana makumi gawili.
P9	Sambano mnole mtima wangu ukugunda mwakuwutuka.
P10	Naga mkutwinicisya yala yangu pa mtasi wa pamalumbiko ga ligasa lyangu, ngupikana katute ka myasi jangu.
P11	Ngupikana kuseka kwangu.@@Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Ngupikana kulila kwangu.@@Mamanee! Mamanee!
P13	Ngupikana naga namsyene ndili mkukwata myala mwangu.@@Pwa, pwa, pwa.
P14	Ngumupikana m'citumbo mwangu muli mkuguluma!@@Gululu, gululu
P15	M'citumbo mwangu mukuŵeceta, "Mundisye!"
P16	Mbula syangu sikupikana kununjila kwa yitumbuwa yakuteleka mama ku kicini.@@Sambano ngusaka kupikana majeje gangu gali mkutawuna yitumbuwayo!
