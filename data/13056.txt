page_text:

P1	Ingabe untwana ulilelani?
P2	Ngidlumbana kobana ulambile.
P3	Kubayini umsana lo adanile?
P4	Ngidlumbana kobana uhlulukele uyise.
P5	Kubayini umtazana lo athabile?
P6	Unerogo elitjha.
P7	Ingabe ikhehla leli lihleka ini?
P8	Ufunde ihlaya elihlekisako.
P9	Kubayini udade lo amomotheka?
P10	Abentwana bakhe bafunda ngokuthula.
P11	Ngimiphi imizwa kaDade lo?
P12	Ngidlumbana uzizwa aneenhloni.
