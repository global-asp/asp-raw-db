title:	Mbolera Bulano...obusangafu Nende Eshibeera!
author:	Catherine Were

desc:	Tsinyali tsiyunjire khu buulili nende efindu fichiranga nikhusangala nende nikhubeerera.

page_text:

P1	Omwana aliranga mbushi?
P2	Embara mbu aliranga Okhubera inzala.
P3	Omwana omusiani wuno yeberere mbushi?
P4	Embara mbu auliranga eshiyungo shia samwana.
P5	Omwana omukhana wuno asangalire shi?
P6	Ali nende lirinda liyiakha.
P7	Omukofu wuno atsekhanga mbushi?
P8	Asomere ingatso indayi.
P9	Omukhaaye wuno amwenyeranga shi?
P10	Abaana baye basomeranga mumwoyo.
P11	Omukhaye wuno auliranga arie?
P12	Embara mbu netsisoni.
