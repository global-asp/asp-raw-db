desc:	Talks about the activities done during the school holidays.

page_text:

P1	It is now time for holidays. Every pupil is eager to go back home.
P2	During holiday time,  we do a lot of work and play.
P3	Opio and Asio wake up very early to feed the chickens,  pick eggs and collect firewood.
P4	Opio and his father take the cows to graze.
P5	Mother,  Opio and Asio go to visit their grandmother in the village of Apokor.
P6	They go to the bus stop and wait for the bus to take them.
P7	Grandmother is very happy to see her grandchildren! They bring her some gifts too.
P8	Grandfather is also very happy to see his grandchildren!
P9	Grandmother is preparing lunch for her visitors.
P10	Asio and Opio are served fish and rice for lunch.
P11	Late in the evening,  Opio goes to play football with his friends.
P12	Asio joins the reading club in the evening too.
P13	On the last day of the holiday,  Opio and Asio join the children's party. They meet new friends.
