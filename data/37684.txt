title:	Kanyenyeene kafwutula Nziba
author:	Edith Sikota Habwanda and Eunice  Mukonde-Mulenga
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	ChiTonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37684.png
desc:	Nyika yayuma. Kanyenyene kalaanzala anyota. Kaunka kumulonga kuyakunywa meenda. Ono katolwa ameenda. Nziba yafwutula Kanyenyene. Ino Kanyenyene kalumba buti Nziba?

page_text:

P1	Izuba lyakabala kapati cilimo eeco. @@Mucisaka mwakayuma.
P2	Kanyenyeene tiikakajisi meenda kwamazuba manji.
P3	"Ndiyanda dosyi lyameenda nokuba kuti lyandilokela biyo kupepe," kakaamba Kanyenyeene.
P4	Ono amume buyo wakaliyumide.
P5	"Kuti naa nsejisi meenda ndilafwa," kakoompolola Kanyenyeene. @@"Ndeelede kuya kumulonga ngondakamvwa."
P6	"Mulonga wazula alimwi ulaa nguzu. Ulakutola," oobu mbwaakakacenjezya lukobo.
P7	Pele Kanyenyeene kakalaa nyota kapati. "Ndilafwa naa nditanywi meenda." @@Aboobo kakajokela kuyandaula mulonga.
P8	Kakainda mumbwizu bwakazambene atalaa milabi yakayumide.
P9	Kakeenda limwi kakamvwa meenda asansa. Ooyu wakali mulonga. @@Kakamvwa mayuwe aameenda.
P10	Kanyenyeene kakamena ngudyu yameenda atontola.
P11	Kakakkomara kapati cakuti kunyina nokakabona yuwe nkolyaali kuboola.
P12	Kanyenyeene kakasola kujatilila kubwizu bwakali kwiimpauka, kabwiinda awo mpokakabede. @@Pele kakapyeelwa ameenda.
P13	"Amundigwasye! Amundigwasye!" Kanyenyeene kakaita.
P14	"Fwambaana, tanta awa," Nziba yakaamba kaijisi katabi kumulomo.
P15	"Ndilakkala aano kutegwa ndilumbe Nziba." Kanyenyeene kakayeeya. @@"Ndilalindila kusikila iboole kuzyikunywa meenda."
P16	Bumwi buzuba kakalindila Kanyenyeene, kwakaboola basankwa bobilo kumulonga. @@Basankwa aaba bakajisi malekani.
P17	"Kuli nziba mpati ituba iboola kunywa meenda.  Njotutiilalile masiku aasunu," wakaamba musankwa umwi.
P18	"Tandikoolekela basankwa aaba kwiijaya Nziba," kakayeeya Kanyenyeene. @@"Ono ndaceesya. Ino ndacita buti?"
P19	Ndilyona biyo, Nziba iituba yakaselemuka ansi kuzwa mucisamu kutegwa inywe meenda.
P20	Kanyenyeene kakayeeya cakucita.
P21	Kakasotokela acituta camusankwa omwe. @@Kanyenyeene kakamuluma canguzu musankwa ooyu.
P22	Ooyu musankwa wakasotoka. @@Mpoona wakoomba kati, "Accuu!"
P23	Niyakamvwa koompolola, Nziba yakanyongana mpoona yakauluka. @@Oobu mboyakafwutuka.
P24	Mbombuboobo Kanyenyene mbokaalumba Nziba akukafwutula nkoyakakafwula.
