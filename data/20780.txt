desc:	Lynne wenta umsebenti lomnyenti kakhulu alungisa tintfo telidzili lakhe lekugubha lusuku lwekutalwa kwakhe

page_text:

P1	BoLynne, Mkhulu Karel na-Anton bebalungisa tintfo telidzili lekugubha lusuku lwekutalwa kwaLynne.
P2	Bo-Anton naLynne batawubhaka likhekhe. Mkhulu Karel ufundza iresiphu.
P3	"Asihlanganise letitsako endishini lenkhulu," kusho Lynne.
P4	Anton wahlanganisa titsako wabanga kungcola.
P5	"Likhekhe livakala lisindza nyalo," kusho Mkhulu Karel.
P6	"Bangani bakho sebafikile," kusho Mkhulu Karel.
P7	Anton bekacabanga kutsi sipho sekucala sitawusindza ngobe besisikhulu.
P8	Bekungumcamelo kantsi futsi bewusindza kancane kakhulu.
P9	Anton wacabanga kutsi sipho lesilandzelako sitawuba lula ngobe besisincane.
P10	Bekulidvwala lelipendiwe kantsi belisindza kakhulu.
P11	Bantfwana batsi 1 ikhilogremu yematje isindza ngalokufanako na-1 ikhilogremu yetinsiba.
P12	Likhekhe laLynne lasha.
P13	Lynne weva buhlungu kakhulu.
P14	Anti Mavis wacela Anton kutsi acagele kutsi sipho sakhe sisindza kangakanani.
P15	"Sisindza njengelikhekhe lelishile," kwasho Anton.
P16	Bonkhe batsi, "Lusuku loluhle lwekutalwa, Lynne."
