desc:	Alake yintfombatane lehlahlambile letsandza tivakashi nalekwatiko kutenta titive temukelekile.

page_text:

P1	Nco, nco!@@Kunemsindvo lomkhulu emnyango longembili.
P2	Alake ujabule kakhulu.@@"Ngubani loyo?" abuta.@@"Make, kunesivakashi!"
P3	"Kulungile," kuphendvula make wakhe. @@"Kungenteka kutsi lowo nguNkt Hassan. Ngicela umvulele umnyango."
P4	Alake uvula umnyango amoyitela. "Siyakwemukela make, ngicela ungene. Make utawuba nawe masinyane."@@Nkt Hassan umoyitelela Alake.
P5	"Ngingakwentela lokunatfwako?" kubuta Alake.@@"Ngikahle, Alake lotsandzekako, ngiyabonga," kuphendvula Nkt Hassan.
P6	"Sawubona, Nkt Hassan," kusho make wa Alake. "Ngiyacolisa ngekukulindzisa."@@"Akunankinga mngani wami, Alake unginakekele kahle."
P7	Make wa Alake uyamoyitela.@@"Yebo, Alake ukwati kakhulu kwemukela tivakashi futsi ngiyatigcabha ngaye," asho kuNkt Hassan.
