title:	Tamara atameka osikola
author:	Hamukoto  Efraim
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Oshindonga
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31508.png
desc:	Okutameka ootundi osho onkatu onene monkalamwenyo kehe yomunona.

page_text:

P1	Tamara okwa li okakadhona koomvula ntano hoka kwali hakazi pwamwe naamwayina yako. @@Omukunda gwawo ongwali guli popepi nomuthitu ngoka gwali guna iinamwenyo hayiilyana.
P2	Tamara okwali omuvuliki. @@Okwali hato nateli oonzi niikombo.
P3	Tamara okwali ehole oku pulakena kuudhila ngele tawu sheketa, ethimbo ta tonatele iinamwenyo. @@Uudhila ngele tawu sheketa, oha tula mo iitya ye mwene.
P4	Tamara okwali omunona omushona noku Tameka osikola. Aakuluntu ye okwa li owala yapitika omu mwaina kadhona ngu omukuluntu, Nashipae na Tasieku, ya tameke osikola. Opwali pu na oshi nano shoo kilometa omulongo.
P5	Nashipae na Tasieku okwali hayayi naanona ooyakwawo yopomukunda ongula no komatango sho taya galuka. Okwali haya umbata iikulya muumbaki.
P6	Tamara okwa pula meme gwe, "Unake teyi kosikola?" @@Meme gwe okwa yamukula, "Oto tameke nziya, kaholike kadhona kandje."
P7	Esiku limwe, ohauto oyeya po nomunenhentu opamwe naa kadhona yaali. @@Omunenhentu okwa kwashilipaleka  kutya aanona ayehe ohaya tameke osikola poomvula dheya opalela.
P8	Aantu okwali yi itayela kutya ngele, okanona ota kavulu okupititha okwaako kwe kombanda yomutse ihe ta kwata okutsi kwe, nena ota shi ulike kutya okanona oka gwana oku tameka osikola.
P9	Omunenhentu okwa pula, "Ouna oomvula ngapi?" Tamara okwa yamukula, "Ondi na oomvula ntano." @@"Atuya tutale ngele otovulu oku tameka osikola," omunenhentu osho ati.
P10	Omukadhona gumwe okwa kutha okwaako kwa Tamara koko lulyo nokweku tula kombanda yomutse gwe nuukeka. @@Tamara okwa kwata okutsi kwe kwoko lumoho.
P11	Tamara otavulu okuya kosikola ya yooloka kwaa ndjoka hakuyi Nashipae na Tasieku. @@Okwali osikola ompe noyali popepi negumbo.
P12	Tamara okwa li anyanyukwa noonkondo. @@No kwa li adhiladhila, "Kutya ota vulu oku lesha noku nyola ngaashi  Nashipae na Tasieku."
