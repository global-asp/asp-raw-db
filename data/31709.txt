page_text:

P1	Bee kinal am...
P2	E mi nana paɗe e luuɓa.
P3	E mi nana urngol pinndi belngol.
P4	Bee gite am...
P5	E mi yi'a ko fiirata dow.
P6	E mi yiʼa kuleyel bonngel.
P7	Bee honnduko am...
P8	E mi nana ndiyam lammuɗam ɗam maayo teeku.
P9	E mi nana leemuure welnde.
P10	Bee junngo am...
P11	Mi jogake junngo ma.@@En ndillu!
