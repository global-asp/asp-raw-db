page_text:

P1	N sɔlɔ ʊlɔsǝlaka.
P2	Amʊnɖǝnɖǝn.
P3	N sɔlɔ ʊlɔsǝlaka n'ambasoro gboo.
P4	N sɔlɔ apǝrǝ ɩsoŋsono kʊtʊr.
P5	N sɔlɔ gʊyeeyee n'ambasoro.
P6	N sɔlɔ anyanʊraʊka na shee amaŋana.
P7	N sɔlɔ ma naa ŋɔ̂n n'ɩbii babʊmbɔnɔ mbaɖee kǝ ba sɔm ʊtʊr na ma.
P8	N sɔlɔ ɩyupi baa ɩpaŋa nɩ kʊjɩʊ.
P9	N sɔlɔ gaɖo abʊmbɔnɔ laŋ kʊɖaʊ.
P10	N sɔlɔ apǝrǝ ɩsoŋsono nɖee amaŋana a na cǝ ma.
P11	N sɔlɔ na lee alesʊra ɖe ɩŋunii.
