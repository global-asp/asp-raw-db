title:	ukuhlangana kwabagijimi emzansi
author:	zesule hlongwane

desc:	Lendaba ikhuluma ngabafana abazungela umhlaba wonkebehambisa umlayezo ngobunye. Endleleni besahamba kunabantu abahlanganyele nabo aili abagijima.

page_text:

P1	Kadoga kanye no Juma abangani abakhulu abathanda ukugijima ndawonye.Bahlezi begijima nsuku zonke ndawonye.
P2	Ngolunye usuku esikoleni bebefunda nge balazwe.''Asigijime sizungeleze umhlaba.Asigijimeni,ngokuhlanganyela namanye amazwe!'Asambeni,singayenza lento!Mzansi omunye''kubonga Kadogo
P3	Bakhetha ukuqhubeka ngokuhlanganyela endleleni yabo.Bebephethe amathoshi abo baqala bagijima.Umhlanganyelo waqala esiqalweni sezwekazi elisemazansi. lapha edolobheni lase Kapa,Eningizimu Afrika.
P4	Kusuka eningizimu Afrika baya kugu olusentshonalanga. Bagijima baya eNamibia,Angola,DRC,Congo kanye nase Camerroon. Abagijimi bashesha bahlanganyela namanye amazwe.Ukukhula kweqembu labantu abaphumule eNigeriya, lapha koAbuja.
P5	Iningi labagijimi kusukela entshonalanga ye Afrika lahlangana nabangani ngenkathi lindlula eNigeriya.
P6	Isivunguvungu ezweni lase Mali lenza kwaba nzima kubagijimi.
P7	Ogwini lwaseGuinea kwelase Conakry bekunomoya abagijimi bageza olwandle.Babe sebekhetha uku gijima eMorocco  ngokusebenzisana ne Senegal kanye ne Mauritania.
P8	Lapha eCasablanca,aba gijimi bajabula bedlala olwandle.Banza abangani abaningi.Okulandelayo,benza ukuthi banqamulele enyakatho yeningizimu Afrika.
P9	Bagijima ngokusebenzisana kwelase Algeria kanye naseLibya,baya bayokuma Egypt bevakashela ama phiramidi.Babuye bajika base beya eMpumalanga belandela umvula wase mpumalanga.
P10	Bagijima beqa amapaki kazwelonke.Ubuningi babantu abasha bahlanganela nabo eKhampala nase Uganda.Iqembu lagijima laya eKenya,lase libhekisa ikhanda ngasogwini lwase dolobheni lwase Mombasa.
P11	Mabefika ngaso lwandle,bahlala khona base badla ukhukhunathi welayisi kanye nenhlanzi.UJuma wathi,'' asithatheni ithoshi lethu silikhweze eKilimanjaro kwelase Tanzania''.
P12	Ngaleso sikhathi, uJuma bekade esekhathele.Mabesuka eKenya,uJama wawela phansi.Abagijimi bayeka umjaho wabo.Bonke abantu basondela bezonceda uJuma.
P13	UJuma wandlulisa ithoshi ku Kadogo.Wase ethi''Ungayithatha ithoshi uyibambe ukhweze iKilimanjaro.Asikhanyise ilangabi kubunye be Afrika.''
P14	Mabeqeda ukukhanyisa ilangabi elikhulu eKilimanjaro,iqembu labagijimi labhekisa amakhanda abo kwelase Ningizimu Afrika.Abantu bajabulela abagijimi,''Ningama qhawe.Sisonke kulento''.
P15	Bayeja ukubhukuda kwelase Lake eMalawi,base bephumula ukuze bezoya kwelinye izwe.UKado wathi kuJuma,''Sigijime ezweni lobaba eNingizimu Afrika saya ukuyokhomba enyakatho ye Afrika.Manje sise ningizumu futhi.''Uhambo lwethu luphelile.''
P16	Abagijimi ekugcineni baphatha amathoshi baya kahle eZimbabwe,babona  zasendulo edolobheni kulelo lizwe.Izixuku zahlanganyela endaweni ebalulekile,bejabulela ukwamukela abagijimi.''Siyenzile!''kusho uKadoga.''Umjaho onjani lo!''kuhleka uJuma.
