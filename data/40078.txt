P1	A long time a go there lived a man called Komu.He a was very poor man.@He lived in a village called Matumaini. Although he was poor,he liked to help people.
P2	In that village there were thieves who liked stealing from the villagers.Komu did not like this bad habit.He decided to help his fellow villagers catch the thieves since he had four trained furious dogs.
P3	One day as he was sleeping,he heard screams from his neighbor's house!He rushed out without wasting time because he wanted to help his neighbor.He  realized that the thieves had come to steal cattle from his neighbour's stalls.
P4	He called his four trained dogs and started chasing the thieves.Luckily the dogs caught up with the thieves and all the stolen animals were recovered.The owners were very greateful to the poor man for the action he took to save their animal
