title:	Petros yatunga omunywani

artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Rutooro

desc:	Oruganikyo orurungi ha bunywani bwo omwojjo ne embwa egonzi.

page_text:

P1	Petros akaaba ali omwana mugezi.Akaba nowu omwana wenka omuka.
P2	Kiro kimu, Petros akagenda kuzaana na ba nywani be.
P3	Akabwa kaba karemereirwe. ''Ninyija ku yamba,''Petros yagamba.
P4	Embwa yabwegora, yaziina kandi yakooba Petros nokusemerwa kwingi.
P5	Abwanywani ba Petros babiinga embwa. ''Murekeraho!Murekeraho!Petro yatooka.
P6	Embwa ekairuka.Ekeisereka ha bwojjo baabi.
P7	Banywani ba Petros ba musanga naara.Bagamba,''Katukuserure embwa yaawe.''
P8	Petros  akaba ataine okunihira.Banywani be bagamba,''Ni twija kumutunga.''
P9	Bakaseera buli hamu.Petros yagamba,"mpaka ali ha mugeera.''
P10	Embwa ekatiina abwojjo.Petros yayeeta, ''Otaguruka!''
P11	Embwa yeruka wa Petros. Bakasemererwa kuba hamu.
P12	Banywani be ba bagamba,''tuganyire.'' Nawe naija kuba munywani weitu.
