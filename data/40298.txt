desc:	En fuu en anndi fitina hononnde ɓowɗi jemma. Kuungel pamarel

page_text:

P1	Kong! Kong!
P2	"Moye woni ɗo'o," mi ƴami nder ɗoyɗoyde.
P3	"Ɗum min," hononnde nyaaɗnde nootii.
P4	"An moye?" mi ƴami.
P5	"Mi wi'i-maa, ɗum min f-e-e-r-e-y-e-l-a-m," hononnde nden wi'i.
P6	"Mi anndaa goɗɗo bi'eteeɗo, 'f-e-e-r-e-y-e-l-a-m'," mi jaabii. @@Mi raari sange am mi tawi e tirii to keeri leeso am fuu.
P7	"Toh, innde am Ɓow." @@Hononnde nden ɓeydi nyaaɗugo. Nde ɓeyditi ɓaddaago noppi am.
P8	"Moye woni Ɓow?" Mi ƴami e mi raara ley sange am.
P9	"Ɓow Ngu," haa ragare hononnde nden laɓɓini.@@Mi nani ŋatannde dow nofru am.
P10	"Kash!" Nden mi diwi mi fuɗɗi ɗaɓɓutugo. @@"Hannde bo a yoɓay!" mi ŋawtii.
P11	Nder suno, mi diwi gila leeso nden mi huɓɓi hiite.
P12	Gila ɗon mi anniiyii honugo Ɓowɗi!
