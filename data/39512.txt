page_text:

P1	One day Frank and Joyce were going to their  friends house, they used the high way they saw a red lorry passing by the person driving the lorry was on the phone then Joyce said "I thought it was wrong to be on the phone while driving."
P2	"I thought so too " and then they saw a green car which was driving at high speed " ooo no that car could hit someone !"Joyce said with fear Frank  told her that "no need to worry we are on the side walk "they continued with their walk
P3	"ooo no the red lorry hit the green car"  Joyce said , they had a head to head collusion, after wards they heard a siren of police cars and ambulance Joyce was scared while Frank was shocked and scratching his head
P4	After a minutes the police cars arrived and Joyce along with her brother were relieved but Frank was still shocked and scratching his head  they saw other cars moving the lorry and the car away from the road
P5	The driver In the green car was barely injured but the person in the lorry was sister to death we  stood there for a while and then we went to the place we were going to  , tomorrow we went to school and told pupils about Safety of the road
