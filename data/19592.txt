desc:	Omusiani omulangu anyola eshinyasio.

page_text:

P1	Inyanga yindi mama yakula efiamo fiaukhane.
P2	"Khunyala khulia lina efiamo fino?" Khwareba mama. Mama yakalusia,  "Khulafiria ingolobe."
P3	Omusiani wefu Wakhulia nomulang'u. Yakonyerera buli eshiamo nalia efinji.
P4	Omusani wefu omutiti yayula,  "Lolakho Wakhulia akholele! Wakhulia nowokhwienyera."
P5	Wakhulia yasinyisia mama.
P6	Kata fwesi khwasinyikha. Nabutswa Wakhulia shiyesundukhakho tawe.
P7	Musiani mutiti yareba mama,  "Otsia khwelesia Wakhulia eshinyasio?"
P8	"Wakhulia waleitsulisia," mama yamubolela.
P9	Wakhulia yachaka okhusalira.
P10	"Inda inzuna," Wakhulia yamonyera mama.
P11	Mama yamanya eshichira Wakhulia nasalira. Yapara,  "Efiamo fya Wakhulia yalia nefiamulelera okhusalira inda."
P12	Olunyuma,  Wakhulia yasaba eshileshero,  "Shendivakho omulang'u khandi tawe." Khwasubira eshilamo she.
