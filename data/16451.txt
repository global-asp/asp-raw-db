page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping. Cilandikonda kusotooka a mukalo.
P2	Alone. Endikke.
P3	I enjoy skipping with my friend too. Cilandikonda kusotooka mukalo abeenzuma.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Cilandikonda kubala twaano tubotu.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Cilandikonda kwendeenda mumbewu abeenzuma.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother. Cilandikonda kusanzya mitiba yaba maama.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters which I can read easily. Cilandikonda kulemba mabbala aabalika kabotu mapati.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruits. Cilandikonda kulya michelo yindene-yindene.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Cilandikonda koona abulo bupati.
P10	I enjoy listening to my mother's interesting stories. Cilandikonda kuteelela twaano tubotu twabamaama.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy skipping,  reading,  writing,  eating fruits,  walking with friends,  washing utensils and listening to my mother's interesting stories!Ooh! Cilandikonda kucita zyoonse ezi.
