desc:	Neo o lahlile fariki ea hae ka kotsi. Ho etsahetse eng ka morao ho moo?

page_text:

P1	Neo a palamisa fariki baesekele.
P2	A chaise lejoe tseleng ka baesekele. Fariki ea oela fatše. Ea lla,  eare,  "Hooeeeee! Hooeeeee!"
P3	Tsotang a e thola. A re,  "Haai,  fariki eo,  ebe kea mang?"
P4	Tsotang a e lelekisa a ba a e tšoara.
P5	A isa fariki Moreneng. Morena a re a e boloke.
P6	Neo a utloa lehonotho la fariki,  "Hmmm,  hmmm,  hmmm!"
P7	A be a pota a thaba ha a bona fariki ea hae.
P8	Neo a re ho fariki,  "Haree hae. Haresatla palama baesekele hape."
