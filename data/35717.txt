page_text:

P1	It was Christmas day. My parents told me we will visit Mombasa to see the ocean.
P2	I woke up early in the morning and wore my best dress. I was very happy.
P3	On the way to Mombasa, I saw wild animals. There was lion, Elephant, giraffe, zebra, gazelle and rhino.
P4	"Mom,Dad, wilI i swim in a big ocean? I asked.@"Yes. I know you will enjoy" Dad said.
P5	When we reached Mombasa, we went to the beach.  I saw children playing many games.
P6	I wore my swimming costume and jumped to the ocean.
P7	It was the best Christmas .
