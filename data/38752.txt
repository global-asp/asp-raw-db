page_text:

P1	Elvin loved his dad's donkey as his pet.@One Saturday morning, his dad decided to take the donkey to the market because it was aging. He requested Alvin to accompany him to the market.
P2	The Market was far away from Elvis' home. Elvis became tired as the journey was long. Elvis' dad told him to sit on the donkey to ride him. Elvis was excited to be rode by the donkey.
P3	The distance to the market was quite long and tiresome. Elvis' dad got tired too. He asked Elvis to allow him ride the donkey to get some rest. Elvis was obedient to his parents. He excused his dad have some ride .
P4	The sun was too hot since it was afternoon. Elvis and his dad had not yet arrived in the market. Elvis was very exhausted after a short period of his dad's ride. Their donkey was strong enough to ride the two of them. Elvis joined his dad.
