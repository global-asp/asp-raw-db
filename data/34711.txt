title:	Marafiki zangu
author:	Keziah Badi
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Kiswahili
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Kwenye kitabu hiki

page_text:

P1	Jina langu ni Daudi.@@Nina miaka tisa.
P2	Rafiki yangu mmoja ni mvulana. @@Jina lake ni Robert.
P3	Robert pia ana miaka tisa.@@Yeye anapenda kucheza mpira.
P4	Robert ana paka anayempenda.@@Paka huyo ana rangi nyeusi na nyeupe.
P5	Robert anaishi na baba, mama na dada yake.
P6	Tunapenda kucheza pamoja.
P7	Nina pia rafiki msichana.@@Jina lake ni Emy.
P8	Emy ana miaka minane.@@Yeye anapenda kuogelea.
P9	Mimi huogelea na rafiki yangu siku ya Jumapili.
P10	Emy husoma vitabu vingi sana!
P11	Emy hana dada wala kaka, lakini, ana kuku.
P12	Emy na Robert wanapenda kucheza mpira wa miguu.
