page_text:

P1	"Carlos, tokuliisa embwa  ebisusu! Maria akagamba. "byaitu embwa enjara ekkugiruma," Carlos akagarukiramu. "ejya tugye kuliisa ebisoro. Enjara ekubiruma," Maria akagamaba Carlos.
P2	Carlos bworaba Omaziire okwo leka tukole ha bisoro bindi
P3	"Habwokuba oina ebisusu naiwe, leka tugende tuliise n'enkoko," Maria akagamba. Carlos yanaga ebisusu mu maiso g'enkoko.
P4	Enkoko zaabirya byona
P5	"Hati leka tuhereze ebinyonyi oburo." Maria yagamba Carlos okunagira ebinyonyi oburo bwona. Ebinyonyi bya gwa hansi byona byona.
P6	ebinyonyi bikasemererwa nga bituroozere
P7	"Maria, twayebwa embwa. Nayo tekalire." Carlos yagamba. "baaitu kiki kyetugya kuliissa embwa?" yaakahaguza.
P8	Emere yaaye eli mujyokoni
P9	Embuzi yagamba, "meee. meee." biboneka bikuraamukya Carlosss na Maria. "hati tuukusobora kuugenda kuulisa wakame?" Carlos yaakahaguza
P10	Embuzi yaagarukiramy meee. meee.
P11	" katuubihe biikora bya green. ha esomero ba twegesa  wakame zenda muno carrots." Maria akagamba. wakame zenda muno namaizi.
P12	ebinyonyi byasoronkana haitaka
P13	Nkutekereza embwa eri kurungi hati. Embwa Yaibokora nkekwoleka ngu unjara egiruma muno
P14	"ha kuba tekalire". Carlos yaalemeraho.
P15	Maria na Carlos ba gya kufuna emeere ye'embwa mu jyokoni. "Whaf. Whaf. Embwa yabokora ne semereirwe. Maria bweyaiguraho oluuigi, embeba za torokamu.
P16	"Carlos, embeba oziiboine?" Maria yamuhaguza.
P17	Maria ya cuura, "Embeba zaa sansa ebinyobwa." "Emeba zikusobora kulya ebinyobwa?" Carlos akahaguza.
