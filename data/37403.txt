title:	Ʊɖǝn a ɖa ɖaa amʊ ma aa ?
author:	Djabarou Chabi AKPADO
artist:	Jess Jardim-Wedepohl
lang:	Anii
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37403.png
desc:	Mbʊpá njala nnɖe n tɩ lǝ atʊ ɖaa abɔrɩ ɖǝn a na yɛɛ agʊsʊ̀rá nɖulinya nɩ ma.

page_text:

P1	N tɩ taba aŋgʊlɔ, ɩ ta jɩ̂ wàà, ɖulinya kaaɖu gaɖǝn nɩ
P2	aŋgʊsʊ̀rá gʊ kɔɔ ɖa, gʊ ɖoo amʊ ma.
P3	Na lǝ bʊlei ɖaa amʊ ma,
P4	awʊɖa aŋʊrʊ ɩpǝlǝpǝlǝka ɖaa amɩja ma,
P5	naa akɔɔ wʊɖa amɩnyine na amatɔ kasʊ̀rá
P6	anyɔnna aŋgɩlɛʊ.
P7	Naa asǝra na lee bʊkɔ mbʊɖee n na lee ma,
P8	na k'a sǝ̂ra alee bʊkɔ mbʊɖee kǝ ma sǝ̂ra alee ma na.
P9	N kiɖe akiɖe bʊɖu bʊŋunii na yɛɛ aŋgʊsʊ̀rá. N ce gaɖukamakamaka akiɖe àyó asir akal
P10	akiɖe àlaŋ n'atǝn,
P11	anyɔnna bʊyɛ bʊŋunii.
P12	Kǝ ma ŋǝ aŋgʊsʊ̀rá na.
P13	Amʊnɖǝnɖǝn n ɖa ka ɖaa amʊ ma, ɖulinya nɩ, aŋgʊsʊ̀rá gʊ mana oo ! Ɩjá nɩ !
P14	Ama k'ɩ lee ashee amʊ kanɖǝnɖǝn na. K'á ŋǝ gǝŋ aa ? Akɩ gboo akʊʊsʊ̀rá gʊ mana.
