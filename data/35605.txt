P1	She is brilliant and loves going to school.
P2	She always comes first in her class.
P3	Her aunty loves her very much.
P4	She advices and mentors Aisha and her friends often.
P5	She likes children and they love her.
P6	Aisha loves rice and beans.
P7	She also likes wearing her hijab.
