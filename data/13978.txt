P1	This is Papa's dog. It is called Tommy.
P2	Papa loves his dog. He feeds it every day with meat and ugali.
P3	He made a small kennel for Tommy. He makes sure that Tommy is well before going to school.
P4	Sometimes, Tommy goes to the road and barks to the vehicles,  "bow-bow-bow."
P5	Tommy is friendly to all people and every one loves it.
P6	Tommy likes playing with Papa's cat.
P7	Papa bought a red ribbon for Tommy. Tommy looks smart.
P8	The End.
