desc:	This story is about a little girl called Zamu

page_text:

P1	Zamu's mom bought her a game, it is called The Princess. Zamu liked the game a lot, and she always played it.
P2	Zamu gave the princess in the game a name, she called her Anny. Anny had a palace in the game. Anny go to town and do shopping inside of the game. Anny wears princess dresses and beautiful shoes.
P3	Zamu asked her mom to buy her princess clothes and shoes. Zamu made her own palace in the living room, because she wanted to be just like princess Anny in the game.
P4	Zamu liked Anny a lot and was playing the game every day. Zamu started to feel like a real princess.
P5	She washed Anny and changed her clothes. Zamu gave Anny food and made Anny dance also. Zamu was happy to look after Anny inside of the game.
P6	One day Zamu was drinking juices while playing the game. The juice spilt over the game and the game stopped working. Zamu was sad because she cannot see the princess anymore. Zamu stopped feeling like a princess.
P7	The next day, Zamu got doll from her mother that was dressed as a princess. Zamu named the doll Anny. Zamu was happy with the doll and started to live like a princess again.
