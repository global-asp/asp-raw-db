P1	Once upon a time, there lived a boy called Joseph.@@Joseph liked football. @@He could kick the ball using either of his feet.
P2	Joseph plays the ball using his head.
P3	Joseph controls the ball using his chest.
P4	Joseph dribbles the ball using the outside of his foot.
P5	Joseph controls the ball using his thigh.
P6	He dribbles the ball using the inside of his foot.
P7	He traps the ball using his instep.
P8	Joseph volleys the ball using his instep.
P9	The keeper stops the ball using his hands.
P10	Joseph does not score!
