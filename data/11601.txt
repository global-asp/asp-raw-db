page_text:

P1	Altine bittiiɗo oo,  Faati fuɗɗi no yaha janngirde.
P2	O naatii nder janngirdu heyru nden o waɗi higiraaɓe heyɓe.
P3	Faati no yiɗi dewte ɗe nate.
P4	Janngooɓe mburtake faa ekkitoo janndeeji gooɗom.
P5	Ɓe anndii sawto alkule.
P6	Ɓe powtii caggal jannde. Faati waalii hadde jannginoo debbo on.
P7	Iɓe ngaɗa duu juurowanɗe janngooɓe.
P8	Faati no jannga wuro hadde inniiɗum.
