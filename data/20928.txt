desc:	Kayungu kwo Natabo kesi agana nabi kandikere

page_text:

P1	Uno  niye Natabo.Ali ni bayaya bewe baside mukaga.
P2	Basali bewe bamuwa kayugu.Aba akagana nabi.
P3	Lunaku lwogene bayaya bewe basinde bakadisa.
P4	Natabo aba kali kuduya kayungu kewe nda.Alila,alila.
P5	Nataba eluka.Aza anina gusala guleyi.
P6	Basali bewe bamuduya."yeeka anesi,"bamulomela.
P7	Bayaya bewe basinde bamwibelia,"yeeka anesi."
P8	Natabo akolamu mwinuyowo mu mwenya.
P9	Awo umusale bwo Natabo naye enza."yeeka anesi,"naye eiba.
P10	Umusale wo Natabo eiba mpaka eeaka anesi kwama kugusala.
P11	Basale banza mpaka ingo bwosei
P12	Buli muntu ajaguza kubanga Ntabo agobole ingo.Natabo afuna kayungu kashaka!
