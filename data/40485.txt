title:	No nyuungel hisniri poola
author:	Abdulkariimu  Dawda and Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences

desc:	Ndiyam walaa nder laynde sey nyuungel nani ɗonka waanee. Sey ngel dilla maayo ngam yarugo ammaa ilaagol hoosingel.

page_text:

P1	Nde ceeɗu waɗi, ndiyam walaa nder laynde.
P2	Balɗe ɗuɗɗe, nyuungel heɓaayi ndiyam.
P3	Nyuungel woyi, "Sey mi heɓa baadel ndiyam, koo simtooyel diga to haako."
P4	Ammaa, koo sammam walaa.
P5	Nyuungel woyi, "To mi heɓaayi ndiyam, mi maayay."@@"Mi nani e woodi maayo, sey mi yaha."
P6	Jiire wagginingel, "Maayo e heewi tep, ilaagol maajam yoolete."
P7	Nyuungel nanii ɗonka waanee, "Mi maayay to mi yarataa ndiyam."@@Ngel dilli ɗaɓɓutugo maayo.
P8	Raa laawol ngol nyuungel tokki.
P9	Ngel saalii daƴƴe geene e lise leɗɗe jorɗe.
P10	Ngel yahi haa ngel nani saa'o ndiyam.@@Ɗum maayo! Ngel nani ƴuuge ndiyam.
P11	Nyuungel yari ndiyam pewɗam ɗuɗɗam.
P12	Ngel nanii belɗum, ngel nanaayi ƴuugere ndiyam e wara.
P13	Nyuungel haɓdi nanngugo geene beeyuɗe dow ndiyam.@@Ammaa ndiyam ilningel.
P14	Ngel noddi, "Mballeeyam, mballeeyam!"
P15	Poola ndanewa jogii lisal wi'i, "Yaawu, ƴeengu."
P16	Sey poola hisni nyuungel yoolaago.
P17	Nyuungel numi, "Mi munyay ɗo'o, sey to poola so''ake ngam yarugo ndiyam, nden mi yettanga."
P18	Nyalaande feere, e ngel reeni, ndaa ɓiɓɓe ɗiɗo bee piɗirɗum maɓɓe.
P19	Gooto wi'i, "Poola mawnga ndanewa e yara ɗo'o."@@Oya wi'i, "En kirtotonga hannde jemma."
P20	Sey ɓe cuuɗii nder baafe e ɓe ndeena poola.
P21	Nyuungel wi'i, "Mi yiɗaa ɓe mbara poola. Ammaa mi pamaro, ko mi foti waɗugo?"
P22	Sey poola jippii diga dow lekki ngam yarugo ndiyam.
P23	Ɓiɓɓe e taskano fiɗugo poola.
P24	Sey nyuungel heɓi dabare.
P25	Ngel ƴeeŋi koyngal ɓiɗɗo, ngel ŋatimo bee semmbe maagel fuu.
P26	Ɓiɗɗo diwi wulli, "Wayyoo!"
P27	Poola ndanewa sey riggii, fiiri, nga hisi.
P28	Non nyuungel yettiri poola ndanewa ngam hisnugongel.
