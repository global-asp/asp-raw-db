page_text:

P1	One beautiful morning a baby girl was born
P2	Her parents decided to name her blue
P3	she was given this name because of her beautiful blue eyes
P4	when years went by,she grew more beautiful
P5	she was so kind and she had a wonderful heart
P6	But there was one problem she didn't have friends.They bullied her
P7	But one day something happened,the sky opened and Blue became a angel.
