page_text:

P1	Ngenye imini,  izilwanyana zathatha isigqibo sokwenza itheko. Kodwa kwakungangeni zonke izilwanyana. Yayilitheko lezilwanyana ezineempondo zodwa.
P2	Umvundla waye wazilungiselela ukuya ethekweni. Kodwa wafumanisa ukuba asilolika wonke-wonke. Yayilele zilwanyana ezineempondo zodwa.
P3	Umvundla waya ndaweni ithile wafumana mpondo zithile. Waye wazibeka kuye entloko waze waya ethekweni.
P4	Ngexesha esethekweni umvundla waye watya,  wasela,  wade waxhentsa.
P5	Lento yamenza wadinwa waze walala elangeni. Kodwa ngelixesha aleleyo,  iimpondo zaye zawa!
P6	Ke ngoko,  zonke izilwanyana zaye zafumanisa ukuba umvundla lo akasiso esi silwanyana bebecinga ukuba siso. Ke ngoku,  zaye zamtsala ngeendlebe zade zanwebeka kakhulu.
P7	Waze wabaleka. Yiyo lento imivundla ineendlebe ezinkulu!
