page_text:

P1	Apwiya ti mukukhula aka; khiivo enrowa okithowaka.
P2	Awo annikihoola mapuro ari munyepu.
P3	Annikihoola mapuro ari maasi oowuuraamela.
P4	Awo annivaha ikuru okumi aka. Mwaha wa nsina naya annikihoola iphiro sa exariya.
P5	Hata miyo kiviraka mapuro oowoopiha okhwa,
P6	nkinoova etthu yootakhala, okhala wiira nyuwo muri ni miyo.
P7	Owerya wanyu ni ekopo anyu sinnikikurunuxa murima.
P8	Nyuwo munnikilokiherya yoolya ohoolo wa awanani aka.
P9	Munnikittikitthela makhura mmuru, nave aasareya mukahini mwaka enayeeheya.
P10	Veekeekhayi sooreera ni oreera murima sinookihoola mahiku ootheene a ekumi aka.
P11	Nave kinookhala mpaani mwa Apwiya mahiku manci-ene.
