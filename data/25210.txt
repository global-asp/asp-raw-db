desc:	Kiki kyonhumirwa kukola? Abantu abendhawulo banhumirwa okukola ebintu ebyaghukana.

page_text:

P1	Nnhumirwa okutuuma omuguwa ni mukwano gwange.
P2	Nnhumirwa okughandiika enhuguta 5 ku lubaawo.
P3	Nnhumirwa okusoma ebitabo ebirimu ebifaananie.
P4	Nnhumirwa okulya ebibala ebyendhaghulo.
P5	Nnhumirwa okuyambaku maama mu ifumbiro.
P6	Nnhumirwa okuwulirisa enfumo maama dhankobera.
P7	Nnhumirwa okulambaala ku buliri obunene.
P8	Nnhumirwa okukola ebintu bingi. 
Iwe onhumirwa kukola ki?
