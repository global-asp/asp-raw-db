page_text:

P1	Henrietta!!!!! Screamed Timmy. There's  a big monster with spotty black and white fur coming at me! He yelled. Henrietta screamed and ran to tell dog.
P2	Henrietta told Charles. Charles was so surprised he leapt out of his skin and dropped his collar on the ground.
P3	They all screamed the loudest scream ever and ran away screaming their heads off, running off to tell horse.
P4	They told Fred all about their problem. Fred neighed the loudest neigh in the whole world ever!!!!!
P5	They all  ran around  clucking,  barking, neighing and squeaking as they ran off to tell donkey.
P6	They told Donald about the monster.  He brayed and his eyes widened.
P7	MOOOOOOOOOO! went the noise. Aaaaaaaah! MONSTER!!!!!!! they screamed as they ran around in circles, waving red flags everywhere.
P8	A cow appeared. It was their friend Leon. Leon!!!! Why would you scare us like that?! They asked. Dunno said Leon. Just can. And everybody laughed.
