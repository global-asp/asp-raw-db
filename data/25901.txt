page_text:

P1	Omughala bamweta Noola. Doli ghe yamutuuma Sheila.
P2	Noola ayenda inho doli. Noola azaanha ni Sheila buli lunaku. Asula ni doli ghe buli bwire.
P3	Lwali lulala, Noola yali tali kubona doli ghe. Yanhonia buli ghantu nga tabona Sheila. Noola yeralikirira inho.
P4	Noola yali tategaire ati maama ghe  yali atwoire Sheila okumunaaza. Yaghanika doli kuliya  okukala.
P5	"Tilikubona doli ghange," Noola yalira. "Oteralikira," maama ghe yamukoba.
P6	Eigulo elyo, maama Noola yamugha doli. Doli yali alikunhirira. Sheila yaboneka oti muyaaka.
P7	Noola yali musanhufu inho. Yabuka buuka. Yakina era nga bwaseka.
P8	Era yeyanza maama ghe.
