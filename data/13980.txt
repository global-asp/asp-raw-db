page_text:

P1	This is Otoyo. He is eight years old. He is in standard three.
P2	He has a younger sister in standard one. He likes her so much.
P3	He plays with her and their friend Akinyi during the weekends.
P4	He lives with his parents and his grandparents. He does his duties very well.
P5	This is Otoyo's school. It is called Umoja primary school.
P6	In class he works hard. He respects his teacher. He always top in his class.
P7	His father is a game warden and he takes them to the animal park during holidays.
P8	On Sundays,  Otoyo accompanies his parents to the church. He likes singing. He teaches his friends how to sing too.
P9	Otoyo is a good boy. Who would not wish to be like him? The End.
