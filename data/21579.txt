page_text:

P1	NgeLisontfo, Jimmy Zogba washaya ligoli leliwinako.
P2	NgeMsombuluko kwefika Licembu leCool Cat litichenya.
P3	"Hheyi wena Rafiki, tiphi tinelwe takho telidvuba. 

Tinwele takho nguletetayelekile nje sengatsi awunandzaba."
P4	"Hheyi wena Rafiki, iphi ithathu yakho mbumbulu?

Sikhumba sakho siyadzinana. Nawe uyadzinana."
P5	"Hheyi wena Rafiki, buphi bukhatikhati bakho?

Ematinyo akho agwegwile, ngiso sizatfu leso."
P6	Ngalelo langa, Rafiki waya ekhaya ahamba kancane.
P7	"Anti ngicela wente tinwele tami tibe lidvuba?"

"Angikwati kukuhhula ngikwenete tinwele telidvuba. Ecinisweni angeke sicale nekucala."
P8	"Sikhumba sami siyadzinana, Sisi. Angangidvweba yini ithathu mbumbulu?"

"Awuyidzingi kutsi ikwente ubukeke unguwe."
P9	"Mkhulu, ngifisa sengatsi benginebukhatikhati."

"Bukhatikhati, mntfwanami, abusho lutfo."
P10	Ngemphelansontfo lelandzelako, Santi Ramires washaya ligoli leliwinako.
P11	Ngemsombuluko, Licembu leCool Cat lefika litichenya.

"Hheyi Rafiki! Ubukeka umuhle!"
P12	Rafiki wanyikitisa emahlombe.

"Ngibukeka njengami. Lesitayela lesi sami."
