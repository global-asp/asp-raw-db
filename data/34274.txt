page_text:

P1	Sun and moon had a fight. So it rain and poured, everywhere was flooded.
P2	Sun and Moon had to stop their fight to solve the flood chaos.
P3	Sun and Moon helped the animals out of the flooded house.
P4	The Sun chatted with the Moon.
P5	The sun bade goodbye to the water.  The sun bade good bye to Moon.
P6	The rainbow stretched in the sky.
P7	The children skipped and played.
P8	The animals said hello to the moon.
