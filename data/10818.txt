page_text:

P1	Nna le dienaso yaka re lebelela dinonyana tsa ka sebakabakeng.
P2	Nna le dinonyana tsa ka re hlakane le tshipansi mo sehlareng.
P3	Nna le tshipansi yaka re raloka le thutlwa yaka godimo ga thaba.
P4	Nna le dithutlwa tsaka re dira dibolo tsa tshwene ka letsopa.
P5	Nna le tshwene yaka reja dipanana le Gorila ka matsatsi.
P6	Gorila yaka le tshwene di tshaba Khudu ka tsela ka moka.
P7	Nna le Khudu reya gae renanya le noga.
P8	Nna le noga re rata go otsa lego opela dikosa kgaufi le seokodiba.
P9	My entire family and I live together in great happiness! Nna le lelapa laka kamoka redula bohle ka lethabo le lekgolo.
