title:	Mbe'a nyaamunga haako
author:	Jibrilla  Eggi and Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40497.png
desc:	Ɗum habaru dow ɓiɓɓe ɗiɗo ɓe ɗuuɗaa nanugo ko maama wiataɓe

page_text:

P1	E woodi ɓiɗɗo gorko, bi'eteeɗo Saabaana, e mo jooɗii wuro maama maako. Innde soobaajo maako Umar.@@Saabaana bee Umar ɓe ngiɗi fijugo bol.
P2	Nyalaande fuu, to ɓe ngartii janngirde, e ɓe pija bol.@@Ngesa haako maama Saabaana e haade.
P3	Yaake feere, to ɓiɓɓe e lata bol bee semmbe, sey nga nasta ngesa maama.@@Maama wi'iɓe, "To on koosoyi bol nder ngesa, taa' ngejjitee maɓɓugo kowaagol."
P4	Nyalnde feere, e ɓe pija, sey bol nasti ngesa. Ɓe njahi hoosugo bol, sey ɓe ngejjiti maɓɓugo kowaagol.@@Mbe'a sey nasti ngesa, nyaami  haako maama fuu.
P5	Sey maama yahi ngesa ngam ittugo haako hiraande.@@Sey mo tawi kowaagol e maɓɓitii, kummbi fuu timmi.
P6	Maama sey ƴamitii, "Ɗume ɓiɓɓe ɓen ngaɗiiyam hannde?"@@Mo noddi semmbee, "Saabaana!" Saabaana doggi wari, mo wi'i, "Maama am?"@@Maama ƴamimo, "Noye kummbi am waɗi?"
P7	Saabaana hultorii, fotaayi wolwugo. E be ndari nder ngesa, sey Umar wari.@@Maama ƴamimo, "Umar, ɗume annduɗa dow haako am?"
P8	Umar jaabi, "Aa'a maama, walaa ko mi anndi dow kummbi maaɗa."@@Maama wi'i, "On ngejjiti ko mi wi'i'on na? Onon ɗiɗo, on maɓɓaayi kowaagol."@@Ɓe njoftidi, "Aa'a maama."
P9	Ɓiɓɓe puɗɗi felootirgo haa ɓe njokkiri.@@Nde maama naniɓe sey noddiɓe.
P10	Maama ƴamiɓe ko waɗi ɓe njokkirta.@@Sey ɓe mbiitimo goonga dow kummbi maako.
P11	Ɓe mbiiti maama, kamɓe ngejjiti maɓɓugo kowaagol.@@Saabaana e Umar nani monee, ɓe tuubi ɓe mbi'i ɓe meetataa.
P12	Maama enɗiiɓe. Mo wi'i, "Mi yeloo, on ɓeydi hakkiilo. Taa' meetee alee kowaagol maɓɓaaki."
