title:	Iliibwa
author:	Cornelius Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Cathy Feek, Vusi Malindi, Mlungisi Dlamini and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Ekigete kyogera ku nkula na migaso gya iliibwa.

page_text:

P1	Eiliibwa! Eiliibwa! Eiliibwa liwooma!
P2	Kale liwooma inho!Liwoomera ilala!
P3	Ate bwolirya,  lileeta amagezi!
P4	Eliibwa likuza abaana! Lileeta amaanhi!
P5	Nze maama owange ampa bwa iliibwa!
P6	Buti ndi mugimu! Ndya bwa bulamu!
P7	Ndi na amagezi! Ndya ne empambo!
P8	Bwoba ne ekilyo! Olyanga ne eitundwe!
P9	Alini gha asimba! Simba bwa mpambo!
P10	Eiliibwa bwe likula! Olyaku ne eitundwe!
