page_text:

P1	Mummy cooked food in a cooking pot. She locked it up in the kitchen so that she could catch up with her customers at the market.
P2	Ann and her two brothers came back from Ntuuse 
Primary School. Ann was in Primary Four, Arnold in Top Class and Junior in Baby Class.
P3	Junior cried of hunger but Ann told him that they should all wait for mummy to come back from the market. Junior sneaked into the kitchen and ate the food.
P4	When mummy returned, She was so happy to see her children at home. She opened the kitchen so that she could serve them food. She found grains of rice allover the kitchen.
P5	She called all her children to the kitchen and asked them about the food. Junior was the first to deny but his shirt was stained with soup.
P6	Mummy served the remaining food and purnished junior by denying him lunch. She told him never to do it again
