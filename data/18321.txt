id:	18321
title:	Mukapu
author:	Ramikosi Gudani
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenda
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18321.png
desc:	Mmawe a vhaho baba vha kho bika mukapu wa matsheloni.

page_text:

P1	Zama u kho ḓi lugisela u ya tshikoloni.
P2	Baba vha kho bika mukapu wa farisa thumbuni.
P3	"Mmawe vha bika mukapu wa u ḓifhesa!" hu amba Zama.
P4	Mukapu u ṋea Zama maanḓa a tshikolo.
P5	Mukapu wa baba wo luga.
P6	Baba vha shela mafhi,  nga lufuno.
P7	"Baba,  ndi pfa unga mukapu hoyu u kha ḓi ṱoḓa swigiri," hu amba Zama.
P8	"Mukapu wa mmawe u ḓifha u fhira wa baba," hu humbula Zama.
P9	"Baba ndi kho humbela u engedzelwa swigiri?" ndi Zama a tshi humbela.
P10	Baba vha muengedzela swigiri.
P11	"Ehe! Baba! Vho shela muṋo mukapuni wanga!"
P12	"Baba,  mmawe vha vhuya lini hayani?" ndi Zama a tshi vhudzisa.
