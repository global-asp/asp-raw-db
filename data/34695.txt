page_text:

P1	UThami uvuka kakhulu zonke izinsuku.
P2	Umama kaThami uyamsiza ukulungisela ukuya esikoleni.
P3	Isikole sikaThami sikude nasekhaya lakhe. Uhamba nabangane bakhe uma eya esikoleni.
P4	Ngelinye ilanga, umama kaThami wavuka waya emsebenzini. Wavuka sekudlule isikhathi sokuya esikoleni. Abangane bakhe bamshiya, baya esikoleni.
P5	UThami wayehamba yedwa edlula ehlathini.@UThami wezwa umsindo ongajwayelekile eduze kwezihlahla.
P6	Wezwa amazwi emuva kwamahlathi. Waqala wagijima. Wagijima waze wayofika esikoleni.
P7	UThami wangena egijima ekilasini lakhe. Uthisha wambuza kungani engena egijima.@Wachazela uthisha ngakuzwile emahlathini ngesikhathi ehamba yedwa.
P8	Uthisha wabe esexwayisa abafundi ngobungozi bokuhamba wedwa emahlathini.@Kusukela ngalelo langa, uThami usevuka ekuseni kakhulu alungise bese ehamba nabangane bakhe.
