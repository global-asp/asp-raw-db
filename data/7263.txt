page_text:

P1	A walk in Zanzibar,  what do you see? Shops selling sweets,  in very narrow streets.
P2	A swim in Zanzibar,  what do you see? Changu,  Kibua,  Tasi,  Pono,  Jodari na Kambisi.
P3	A drive in Zanzibar,  what do you see? Animals in a jungle,  watching at an angle.
P4	A dance in Zanzibar,  what do you see? Swahili,  English and Arab,  all dancing Taarab.
P5	A drink in Zanzibar,  what do you see? Miwa,  madafu juicy,  coffee and tea are spicy.
P6	A trip to Zanzibar,  do you wish to go back?
