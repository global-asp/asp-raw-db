title:	Mukagwa gwe ntabona

artist:	Benjamin Mitchley, Catherine Groenewald, Rijuta Ghate and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Bamukenkede babiri bakulira mu kyalo ghalala okuva mubuto bwaibwe. Owundi ava mu isomero ate nowundi aja yenka yabuula ku ku mukenkede we.

page_text:

P1	Buli bwenasitunga  kunco nayangughanga okulya ekyenco.
P2	Nayambalanga bulawuzi yange eya bululu nalumukanga okuva  mundhu okunonhiaku mukenkede wange muthon.i
P3	Wenabona muthoni,  twerabiranga ebintu ebindi.
P4	Muthoni ninze nga  tuzaana kyekweko. Nga nekweka einhuma womuti omunene, nga muthoni aira inhuma wa nhumba edheisubi.
P5	Muthoni ninze twaja mukyalo twembiriri. Twaja okuwerera enku. Twaja kw'isomero twembiriri.
P6	Twesubiiza twidha kusoma inho tufune embaluwa.
P7	Aye lwali lulala, Muthoni tiyaidha kw'isomero. Nayemerera ku mulyango ogwe kibiina kyaife nga mmulinda aidhe. Aye tiyaidha.
P8	Bwenairayo eka olunaku olwo, Nakoberaku maama ebya mukagwa wange muthoni.
P9	Naja n'emisomo jange mumaiso,aye nafiirwa inho muthoni.
P10	Buli bwenajanga kw'isomero, Nanoonhianga muthoni buli wantu woona woona.
P11	Buti ndi mukazi, aye mukenkede wange yambulaku.
