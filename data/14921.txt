page_text:

P1	Tingi yaikaraga nanyinenkuru.
P2	Barolerraga ente zaabu hamu.
P3	Kiro kimu abaserukali.
P4	Baija batwara ente zoona.
P5	Tingi nanyinenkura bakairuka bayesereke.
P6	Bakesereka kuhika itumbi.
P7	Abaserukali bakamara bagaruka.
P8	Tingi nanyinenkuru bayesereka hansi mubikoora.
P9	Omuserukali omu yarubata Tingi baitu atateere nduuru akaculeera' chi'.
P10	Tingi nanyinenkuru obubahuliire abaserukali bagenda bakuta kuta barugayo.
P11	Bokasooba mpora bataaha.
