page_text:

P1	Kakyo nomwishiiki ari kurunga omuri Eastern Uganda.
P2	Erizooba yafuuna omuywani owubakweeta Nankya.
P3	Nankya naruuga Masaka omuri Central Uganda.
P4	Bakashaaga boona bakukunda okuzaana.
P5	boona abaishikyi nibakunda okwibiira.
P6	Kakyo akeeta Nankya omuka owaabo kuzaana kandi kyaba ekyaburiizoba.
P7	Baguuma nibazaana hamwe burizooba.
P8	Baguuma nibibiira buri muheeru gwe esande.
P9	Kandi okuywaana kwabo kwagumaho okuhitsya obu bakuziire.
