P1	Their was a girl named Joslyn who had a backpack, she would take her backpack everywhere. One day Joslyn couldn't find her backpack."I'll never find my backpack" Joslyn said. Joslyn was sad.
P2	But Joslyn knew that if she thought about it she would find it. So she thought and thought and thought. And she knew where her backpack was!
P3	Joslyn was so excited to see her backpack again!
P4	The end
