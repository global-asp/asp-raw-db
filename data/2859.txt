title:	Obusiro Nende Obwangu
author:	Catherine Were

desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	Eshisiro neshi? Ingokho nomba libaa?
P2	Eshiangu neshi? Eshimuli nomba litumwa?
P3	Mwana shina omusiro?
P4	Niwina omusiro,  inzofu nomba papa?
P5	Nishina eshisiro okhushila,  iratili ya amachina.
P6	 Nomba akhapimo khe isimiti?
P7	Nishina eshisiro? Iratili ye isukari?
P8	 Nomba iratili ya mabaa?
