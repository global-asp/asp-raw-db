title:	Education of a girl -child is wisdom
author:	Sumayya ShafI.  U.
artist:	Rob Owen and Jesse Breytenbach

desc:	This story teaches that parents should ensure to send theiron children to school now matter what and they should encourage their children to be good in the society.

page_text:

P1	There was a woman who was a drop out amongst her friends. She lacked the basic things of life,  like food, good clothes and good shelter. She always faced marital problems with her husband. She had early marriage. She has 3 daughters.
P2	One day, her school friend came to visit her from city,  her school friend observed all the things that is happening between her and her husband.
P3	Her school friend asked her, "Did your daughters go to school?" she said "no because we don't have the money for the responsibilitily of their school". When her friend prepared to go back to the city, she took the first daughter .
P4	Her daughter had already finished her secondary school, when they went back to the city, she put her in the university, she studied law, when she was in the university she became the class president because she was brilliant.
P5	When she wrote her final year, she was honest and truthful, at last she became an activist for human rights, she helped her sisters with their studies, and she helped her mother with their shelter.
P6	,,It is said that if you educate a girl , you educate the nation, , the other daughter became a lecturer, and the last one became a newscaster on NTA Abuja channel.
P7	If mothers are not educated they should use the opportunity to put their children in school because education of a girl is wisdom in the socity.
