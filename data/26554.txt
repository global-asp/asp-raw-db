title:	Magulu Agha Ebigere

desc:	Magulu asalawo okugha ba wansolo boona boona amagere basobole okuyumirwa okutambula nga abantu.

page_text:

P1	Ebiseera ebyeira, ba wansolo baali bazira bigere. Boona boona baali b'eghalula bweghalule.
P2	Abantu bonka naabaali n'ebigere, Magulu bye yabagha.
P3	Olunaku lulala, Magulu yasalawo okugha ebigere buli wansolo. Yayendha ba tambule.
P4	Ba wansolo baakoba,"Kiidha kuba kirungi inho okuba ne ebigere." Baayeemba era baakina."
P5	Ba wansolo baakoba, kyali kizibu okweghalula. Baalumizibwa nga.
P6	Olunaku bwe lwatuka, ebinonhi ebisinga byaja yendhu ya magulu.
ba wantwiga, ba wampologoma, ba wandhovu, ba wandhuyu, ba wangoina ne ba wabinonhi ba yemerera mu lunhiriri.
P7	Buli wansolo yagheebwa ebigere bina. Ebinonhi byagheebwa ebigere bibiri.
P8	Buli kintu kyali kyandhawulo bwebaafuna ebigere. Abaandi baakina olweisanhu abaandi baagwa ghansi.
P9	Baaja okulaga abantu.
Ba wansolo baakoba, "Tituja kwiramu kwekulula."
P10	Eyasembayo mu lunhiriri yali waigongolo. Magulu yabuuza, "Eriyo owuundi oluvainhuma lwaiwe?"

waigongolo yaira mu, "Mbe, ninze asemba yo."
P11	Mugulu yalowooza, "Naasembayo. Nnakola ntya ebigere ebindhi?"
Mugulu yagha Wa igongolo ebigere byona byona ebyari bisigaire wo.
P12	Wa igongolo yali musanhufu inho. Yakoba, "Ndha kutambula mangu okusinga ku abandhi."
P13	Oluvainhuma lwa Waigongolo okuvaawo, wamusota yaidha ku nhumba ya magulu. Ya koba Magulu, "Muna mpa ku ebigere."
P14	Magulu yakoba, "Ngabye ebigere byonabya. Obaire gha?"
Omusota gwairamu, "Natendwike ino."
P15	Magulu yalingalinga abone oba yali esigaire yo ku ebigere. Tiyayagana yo waire mulala.
P16	Mugulu Yakoba Wamusota, "Bambi. Ezira bigere bisigaire yo." wamusota yeghalula okwirayo eka nga azira bigere.
Okuva kulunaku olwo, wamusota tatenduka ino. Alindirira kusooka ku lunhiiriri afune ebigere.
