desc:	O se itlhaganele go tsena mo ntweng! Ntwa ga e na mosola!

page_text:

P1	Khudu le Moshe ba tlhoka madi.
P2	Ba dira dijana go rekisa kwa marakeng.
P3	Khudu o bona ntwa kwa marakeng.
P4	Moshe le Moulu ba a lwa.
P5	“Tlogela tsala ya me!” Khudu a goa.
P6	Moulu a loma Khudu mo nkong.
P7	Nko ya gagwe e robegile. E botlhoko.
P8	Khudu o robegile nko gonne a tabogetse ntwa.
