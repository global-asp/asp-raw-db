P1	Caame papa musarek.
P2	Caame papa kawanik.
P3	Papa yachama ikaawa.
P4	Acaame ani ceeko.
P5	Caame papa.
P6	Acaame ani mucuŋŋwanik.
P7	Caame papa mukatiyanteet. Acaame ani mukatiyanteet nyo kace pel.
P8	Cee caame acek tuway kakyonteet nyo mac kumway.
