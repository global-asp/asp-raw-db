desc:	USihle uzokukghona njani ukuyamisa abantu neenlwana ngesikepe sakhe esincazana.

page_text:

P1	USihle unesikepe esincazana. @Silisizo lokuyama emlanjeni ozele amanzi.
P2	USihle ulinga ukusiza umndeni oneembuzi ngokuwuyamisa emlanjeni.
P3	Abesana ebebathiya iimfesi nabo babawa ukusizwa ngesikepe.
P4	USihle wathi "kulungile, izani nani besana nizokukhwela".
P5	Iinlwana zithe nazibona isikepe, nazo zathi "Ekhu! sibawa usiyamise nathi".
P6	Umndeni oneembuzi, abesana neenlwana bayanela ngesikepeni esincazana.
P7	Phakathi nomlambo, kwavela indlovu yathi "Nami ngiyakhwela la, angisali."
P8	Sathi "phekghu!" isikepe, boke bathi "tlum!" ngemanzini ngebanga lendlovu.
P9	Isikepe sazala amanzi, indlovu yalemuka bona yonile. Yadana khulu ngalokho.
P10	USihle wathi "Ngizaniyamisa ngeenqhema zenu", wathoma ngomndeni oneembuzi.
P11	Walandela ngesiqhema sabesana ebebathiya iimfesi, naso wasiyamisa.
P12	Akhange azilibale iinlwana, wagcina ngokuyamisa isiqhema sazo.
P13	Nendlovu yayama ngokwayo. Nangambala isikepe esincazana silisizo elikhulu.
