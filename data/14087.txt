title:	Umgcini Nesikhungo Esikhethekile
author:	Margaret Nokuthula Zondi
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	isiZulu

desc:	Indaba imayelana nokufika kwezilwane ezihlengwa zisengabantwana esikhungwini sezintandane. Lezi zilwane ezincane zinakekelwa ngumuntu okhethekile nethimba lakhe.

page_text:

P1	Lena yindaba yomgcini nesikhungo sezilwane eziyizintandane.
P2	Inyanga edlule kwakuyinyanga ematasatasa esikhungwini. UNomusa ononyaka waba ngowokuqala ukufika. Wayezacile futhi elusizi.
P3	Kodwa ngokukhulu ukushesha uNomusa wavele waqina. Waqala ukudlala nezinye izilwane.
P4	Ngelinye ilanga ngayo leyo nyanga,  kwathi cababa indiza enophephela emhlane kuleso sikhungo.
P5	Kundiza enophephela emhlane kwakunendlovu encane phansi kwengubo yokulala enkulu.
P6	UNdiwa wayengaphansi kwengubo yokulala. Wayenezinsuku ezinhlanu ezelwe. Umgcini wamncelisa ibhodlela.
P7	Ngobunye ubusuku,  wonke umuntu wavuswa ngukufika kuka-Ambiya.
P8	U-Ambiya wayenezinyanga ezinhlanu. Wayebuthakathaka emva kohambo lwakhe olude.
P9	Ngenxa yentamo yakhe ende,  umgcini kwakufanele aliphakamisele phezulu ibhodlela ukuze akwazi ukumncelisa.
P10	Izintandane ezagcina ukufika ngaleyo nyanga kwakungamawundlu ayelambile oKopi,  noKepi,  noKeji.
P11	Umgcini nabasizi bakhe babezithanda zonke izilwane,  ngisho nalezo ezigangile.
P12	Abantu bakulesi sikhungo basebenza kanzima. Bathemba ukuthi ngelinye ilanga izintandane ziyokwazi ukuzinakekela ngokwazo. Uma kunjalo ziyokwazi ukubuyela endle.
