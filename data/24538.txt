page_text:

P1	One day a family went to the market to buy things for Christmas.
P2	They were five people. Mother, father, brother, sister and their helper.
P3	Their son John and the helper, Mercy, carried the baskets.
P4	Daddy was carrying Joy the young sister and Mummy was carrying her bag.
P5	When they reached the market, they bought many things.
P6	They bought things like vegetables, fruits, meat, matooke among others.
P7	Young Joy started crying for everything, sweets, toys, everything.
P8	Daddy bought her a  big doll and she was happy.
P9	John also demanded a ball and they bought it for him.
P10	They also bought the helper a nice dress and she was very happy.
P11	Everyone went home very happy.
