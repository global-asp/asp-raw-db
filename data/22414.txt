title:	Kán wùn Kuúrá
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh and Mbinkar Emmanuel Keelen
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	A dzə̀ yiì tìnén la nsaʼ Kán wùn Kuúrá.

page_text:

P1	Kán wùn Kuúrá-aà biʼ mbìʼ. Áwune dù e yèn loóyà.
P2	Loóyà nèn kwàʼtì jii, "Kán lo e yi ŋgwàsáŋ wom, é kuúrá kfər nàʼ wom."
P3	Loóya suúy e kán wùn kuúrá jii áwune du e yèn á ŋgày sé wìr seé ntèʼ.
P4	Ŋgày sé wìr si nèn dù a fàn fo tinén nsaʼ e yì koó kán kìn Kuúrá.
P5	Ŋgày sé wìr si yòŋ e Kán wùn Kuúra ji nsaʼ yee shoon tàv á feyì.
P6	Áwune nèn yòŋ e Kán wùn Kuúrá jii, áwune du e yèn a wiíy woo ŋgaà kitàn.
P7	Wiíy woo ŋgaà kitàn və̀y biímé ji wun-éè tínén a nsaʼ yev.
P8	Wù yòŋ Kuúra e sùúy jii, "À fàŋ, a dzə̀ ntav, a wíri ghvə́m wo."
P9	Kuúrá bínsí jii, "À suuy rú. M lo e màʼtì mbìʼ və̀n."
P10	Wù yòŋ e kán jii, "À kér vifií é ŋàŋ fo lò a taŋnén bèn." Kán biímé.
P11	Wù suúy jii, "Mo ven biimen ne ji kitáŋnèn ki wàá may, wìr làysìn ri jay wìr."
P12	Á bèy wiíy woo ŋgaà kitàn və̀y nèn màyìr kinyo kìy.
