desc:	Ekitontome kyogera ku Nsujju ne migaso gyayo.

page_text:

P1	Ensujju -Ensujju Ensujju-ewooma!
P2	Kale ewooma nnyo-ewoomera ddala!
P3	Ate bwogirya-ereeta amagezi!
P4	       Nali simanyi, nti-ensujju nnungi!        Naye bwogiryako-kiyitirira!
P5	Ensujju ettukuza! Ereeta amaanyi!
P6	Kunva z'ebinnyebwa! Wamma wekomba!
P7	Nze maama owange- ampa ensujju!
P8	Kati ndi mugimu ate ndi mulamu!
P9	Nina amagezi. Nina amaanyi!
P10	Bwoba togirina genda ogigule!
P11	Olina wolima? Keera osimbe!
P12	Ensujju bwe kula,  olyee!
