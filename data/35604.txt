page_text:

P1	My cat's name is Baby. She is fantastic.
P2	She lives in our house.
P3	She can change the colour of her body anytime she feels like.
P4	She likes eating fish, meat, bread and rice.@@She drinks water and milk too.
P5	I love Baby. She does not steal like the other cats I know.
P6	Baby misses me when I go to school.
P7	She likes staying around me.
P8	I really love having her in my room.
