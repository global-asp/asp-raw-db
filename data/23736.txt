page_text:

P1	Obwiira hakaba hariho omutsigazi muto bakwemweta kumbana
P2	kuyabire emyaka abiri netano yakundana no mwichi bakumweta Zangane
P3	Baba hamwe emyaka eshatu. konka akaba ataine sente zi kumara kumushwera.
P4	Ezoba rimwe ya shanga ayine enda, ahabwokuba tibarayekumire.
P5	Akerere kazarwa, baketa Kheri
P6	konka kumbana akaba naki shoma. kandi Zangane akaba ayine omurimo hare, mbwenu bakaba beine obutatura hamwe
P7	kuhahwireho obwire, zangane ya fuuna omukundwa omusya. Akaija kutura nawe nana omwana we
P8	Kumbana aka chweka omutima. Akara yara ahabwokuba bakamutanisa na muhala we.
P9	Obwire bukarabaho na kumbana ya funa omukundwa omusya nawe. Bakatura hamwe  kwonka tibara funire baana.
P10	Hati kheri tarakunzire ishe omustya nakakye, akaba atashemereirwe, kandi akaba nakira kumuteera.
P11	kuya hikize emyaka ekumi nebiri, nyina ya sharaho kumugara owa ishe kimuzara
P12	Reru kumbana na kheri bagaruka ba shemererwa,
