page_text:

P1	UJabulani bekathanda ukuyohlala ngehlathini neenyoni.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga abantu bathoma bakapa imithi ngehloso yokwenza inkuni.
P3	Ngelanga elilandelako abantu abanengi babonakala sele bakapa imithi ehlathini laka Jabulani. UJabulani wazwakala selasithi: "asihlonipheni amahlathi ngombana asilethela umoya ohlanzekile begodu amakhaya weenlwane."
P4	Abantu bathula bamulalela uJabulani bazwakala sele bamubuza ukuthi "Jabulani senzenjani ngombana sifuna iinkuni? UJabulani wathi "asitjaleni imithi eminye,  singayikapi kwaphela." Abantu bajabula babona kumbono omuhle khulu,  ngelanga elilandelako bathoma batjala imithi eminqani."
P5	UJabulani wathaba khulu ngokutjalwa kwemithi eminqani.
P6	Woke umuntu besele azithabisa ngemvelo begodu besele bayithanda babe bayihlonipha bangasakapi imithi kwaphela kodwana bayitjala nokuyitjala.
