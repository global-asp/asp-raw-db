page_text:

P1	——Ngwenya naMfene bebabangani labakhulu.Ngwenya bekahlala Emfuleni.Mfene ahlala esihlahleni dvute nemfula.
P2	Njalo Ngwenya abedla tinhlanti phansi emfuleni.Mfene adla titselo etulu esihlanhleni.
P3	Wagula kakhulu umntfwana Ngwenya.Gogo Ngwenya watsi abatfole sibindzi lesincane semfene kutomelapha.
P4	Ngwenya wamema Mfene ekhaya lakhe lelihle, ngobe angakwati kubhukusha wamema emhlane wakhe lomubi.
P5	Ekhatsi nemfula Ngwenya wacocela Mfene kutsi ugulelwa ngumntfwana, kute aphile udzinga sibindzi lesincane semfene.
P6	“Hhawu, mnganami, ngabe usho singakesuki, phela sibindzi sami sihlala esihlaleni,” kusho Mfene.“Asijike sisilandze.”
P7	Nembala bajike.Mfene azubele esihlahleni atsi nya.Ngwenya walindza.
P8	Ngwenya walindza sikhatsi lesidze, kepha do! Mfene kubuya.
