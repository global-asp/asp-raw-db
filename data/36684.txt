page_text:

P1	Once apon a time a man named zoyer was a poor man but was starborn. @ one day he saw a woman and her son did not have anything to eat he promised her that he at the end of the day he would bring food.
P2	He walked as quickly as he could.
P3	But he stopped to a giraffe it looked so dileash  that he even thought to cheat the woman and her  son.
P4	He said to himself maybe I can kill it and run to eat it for myself.
P5	But things did not go well for he chaced it and had not yet killen it.
P6	He found its  wicknes but he fell in to a sticky bush and had broke his leg.
P7	He was able to get up and he found the giraffe but he also saw his stick broke his favorite robe and the giraffe ran away
P8	He was empty handed but everyone was sharing their foods and the king gave the woman and her son food and  she forgave zoyer the end.
