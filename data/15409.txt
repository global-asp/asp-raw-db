title:	Talking Tree

lang:	English

desc:	The talking tree complains to Kiki about human destruction.

page_text:

P1	One day Kiki sat under a tree looking very sad and worried.
P2	Suddenly he heard a voice coming from behind him. He turned his head and found the tree smiling at him. He jumped away with fright looking suprised.
P3	Then the tree started to cry. Kiki got worried and asked the tree why it was crying. "Why are you crying talking tree? The tree answered, "because people kill us by cutting us down.
P4	Kiki felt sorry for the tree and promised to protect the tree and its relatives from their enemies.
P5	Kiki and the talking tree became friends. Both Kiki and the taliking tree were happy to become friends.
P6	Kiki introduced his wife and children to the talking tree. They all became good friends. During free time Kiki and his family would spend time talking to the talking tree.
