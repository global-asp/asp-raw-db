desc:	Ekibala kirungi inho mwife. Abantu

page_text:

P1	Ebibala, ebibala, ebibala.
P2	Ebibala bighooma inho okubilya.
P3	Abantu balya ebibala eby'endawulo.
P4	Omuntu asobola okulya emiyembe, owundi yalya enaanansi.
P5	Twena tunhumirwa okulya emithungwa.
P6	Abaana benda inho amapeera.
P7	Ebinhoni bilya nsaali.
P8	Oooh, ooh! ebibala birungi ku bulamu. Abantu, ebisolo n'ebinhoni byenda ebibala inho.
