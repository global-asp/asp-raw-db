title:	Syana Sya Mũvũa
author:	Anna Kula
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First sentences

desc:	Once there was a family with three very obedient children. The problem was they were made of wax

page_text:

P1	Ĩvinda ya tene na tene,  vaĩna musyĩ wekalaa na ũtanu.
P2	Mayaũkitanaa ene kwa ene. Nimatetheesyaa asyai moo musyĩ na mĩundani.
P3	Ĩndĩ mayanengetwe mwanya wakũthi vakuvĩ na mwaki.
P4	Maĩlĩte kũthũkũma wĩa woo w'onthe ũtukũ.
P5	Ĩndĩ mwana ũmwe aĩ na mea ma kũthi nza kwĩanĩkĩa kyeni kya sua.
P6	Mũthenya ũmwe nĩwethĩiw'e na mea asu ma kũthi nza muno. Ĩndĩ aamwanainya nĩmamũkanisye.
P7	Ũũi! Mwana ũsu ndaatetheka. Nĩwanyinyũkie nũndũ wa ũvyuvu wa sua.
P8	Syana sya mũvũa nĩsyakwatiwe ni kyeva nũndũ wa kwona mwanaainya ainyinyuka.
P9	Ĩndĩ nĩmethĩiw'e na mũvango. Nĩmosie mũndo wa mũvũa ula wanyinyukie maseovya kasũni.
P10	Nĩmosie mwanaainya kasũni mamũlĩsya ĩũlu wa kĩĩma kĩtũlu.
P11	Na sua ĩkuuma nĩwolũkile athi kũu akwina kyeninĩ kya kĩoko.
