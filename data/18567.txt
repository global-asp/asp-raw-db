title:	Kuitalifisa Kwa Kana Ka Tou

desc:	Kana ka Tou nakana nilipuzo zeng’ata zalika kaufeela

page_text:

P1	Makande akuli Tou ina ni mulomo omutelele azibahala kwa batu kaufeela.
P2	Kono muzibe kuli mulomo ho nesi omutelele inge kamomuubonela kacenu cwana hape neinunile maswe.
P3	Lizazi leling'wi kana ka Tou kapepwa. Nakana nibutali ahulu nikulata kuziba kaufeela zeneli kazungulukile. Nakabuzanga folofolo kaufeela yakabona.
P4	Akobona Tutwa kakomoka ahulu mwaminano yakona mane kabuza Tutwa kuli,  "Cwale wena Tutwa king'i unosi auna nimulala omutelele?"
P5	Inge kasakomokile kakopana hape ni Sembele. Kamubuza kuli,  "King'i lunaka lwahao alushengile cwalo?"
P6	Hape mane kayokopana nikubu nikuibuza kuli,  unitaluseze,  "King'i meto ahao afubezi inge mali?"
P7	Ababonana ni Kwena kamubuza kuli,  "Kono kuli wena Kwena ucanga busihu?"
P8	"Utaza lilata." Bahalifa bomaye ka Tou. "Akipuzo yeho." Akautwa cwalo kanyema. Kana ka Tou kananuwa nikuzwa inge kanyemile.
P9	Onafo kanako yenyinyani,  kwataha Mbande kukona nikukabulelela kuli,  "Unilatelele,  luye kwanuka uyobona zeica Kwena."
P10	Kakusasinya nako kana ka Tou kalatelela Tou kwa nuka.
P11	Kafita mwabucwani bonobwizi kwatuko ni nuka nikuyema kwapata ya nuka. Nibata kuiponela kacenu. Ikayi kwena?
P12	"Mucwani," ahala linzwi mwa pako ya licwe ya nuka.    "Luiketile," kahalaba kana ka Tou. "Amunibulele zeicanga kwena busihu," kabuza.
P13	"Sutelela nikutaluseze." Kautwa lizwi leo kuzwa mwapako yalicwe. "Sutelela bukaufi kuli uiponele."
P14	"Kasikananela kale kaswaseya mwanu ya Kwena!"    Ngo yakona kaufeela yafelela mwahanu yakwena. He-he-he-he-eee,  "Kaceku usico sakwena yaseya Mbande inge ikutela kwa kota."
P15	Kwaba siyongili sakwiposhola mwana ya Kwena. Kwena yapanela ngo ya Tou katata inge ihowa kakutundeka.
P16	Bakeng'isa yakuhowa ngo yaka Tou,  yakala kunamuluwa. "Pwaa!" Kayowela ko kana ka Tou.
P17	Bakeng'isa akuiposhola mwa meno aKwena,  Kwena yanyema inge ikutela mwamezi atungile.
P18	Akali katalimele ngo yakona kana ka Tou kakomoka kubona ngo inge inamuluhile. Kapalelwa nikubona kweneyifelela ngo yakona.
P19	Ngo yaba yetelele ahulu nikusabisa kuli mane nakakona nifula miselo yeli mwalimu zali kota.
P20	Bakeng'isa kunamuluha kwango yakona kakala kuisebelisanga nikuiselanga mezi famukokoto. Kuzwa liza leo liTou yaba ni lingo zetelele.
