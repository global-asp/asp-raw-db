title:	Kon-Iyôn man Atii
author:	Terkule Aorabee
artist:	Joe  Werna
lang:	Tiv

desc:	Hwev doo ga. Doo u lun a iwuese hen mba wasen we.

page_text:

P1	Kon-Iyôn lu or-sule u dedoo. @@A maa ya na man wuna u kwaghyan shin nya.
P2	A mar ônov kpishi. @@Ve cii ve eren tom taveraa. @@Ve lu a kwaghyan kpishi.
P3	Atii wan ndor vea Kon-Iyôn. @@A wa iya sha kon. @@Lu a kwase man mbayev uhar.
P4	Atii wa hwev kpishi. Un man tsombor na cii ve zamber a zamber. Ve gema ve fa imo gberen kpishi.
P5	Iyange igen wura nôô kpishi. Kon-Iyôn due a iyiav nav tsen.@@Atii kua tsombor na va u va zamber.
P6	Kon-Iyôn kaa a atii er, "Me nau kwaghyan wam ga. U wa hwev gande. Yem za kaha sule."@@Atii gba mi zamber.
P7	Atii vea tsombor na cii tôô icam, "Iyôn u ngu Tor! Iyôn u ngu Tor! Iyôn u ngu Tor!"
P8	Kwagh la doo Kon-Iyôn. A na Atii Kwaghyan Kpishi. @@Atii mba ye kwaghyan yô, ve purugh ve za tema sha kon.
P9	Atii gba gberen imo hiin Kon-Iyôn, "Man tor lu tyough gbenee? Man tor lu tyough gbenee? Man tor lu tyough gbenee?"
P10	Kpen nan kpa ijen num ve. Mbatii shi va tôô icam, "Iyôn u ngu Tor! Iyôn u ngu Tor! Iyôn u ngu Tor!"
P11	Kon-Iyôn nyume ve kwaghyan. Kon-Iyôn kaa er," Ne mba a inja ga! Ne lan kwagh yum!" @@Kpa Atii zamber, "Se vaa afanyô."
P12	Kon-Iyôn zungwe ve mhôônum, na ve kwaghyan. Ve purugh ve hide sha kon.@@Hanmô shie ka ve waan icam ve wuese Kon-Iyôn.
