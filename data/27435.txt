page_text:

P1	Maganduli kifo kikalamu okumpi ni Mutaata era kiri ne ebizimbe bigonoka omboneku. Eriyo enguudo nningi era eliyo ebifo bingi eby'okuja mu kukyakala e Mutaata.
P2	Zuluma ali no ogutwe ne ensaya mpanvu. Ali na agaiso, ti mulungi, ate mukaire. Bwolinga ku Zuluma obona omwisi.
P3	Zuluma ayendha kusaba Zahara amubayize, aye taidhi ngeri ya kumukoba. Asalawo kuja mu nhumba ya Zahara ayogere naye.
P4	Zuluma asaba Zahara amubayire. Zahara akoba, "Mbe nze tiidha kukubayira kuba oli mukaire inho ate nnina mukenkedhe wange ayetebwa Amaza. Ndidha kumufumbirwa."
P5	Zuluma aja ku musawuzi afune akalezi akanaaleetera Zahara okumufumbirwa. 
Omusawuzi akoba, "Nnhendha Zahara ni Amaza gwendibayiza. Kale tisobola kukola ekyo."
P6	Zuluma tafuna bulezi era anholwa no okunhiiga kuba agonza Zahara kufabufe.
P7	Zahara tiyafumbirwa Zuluma. Era talimufumbirwa.
