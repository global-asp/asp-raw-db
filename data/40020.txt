page_text:

P1	Today joe was going to the zoo to meet the animals at school and the teacher is going to tell the class what animal do you like
P2	Joe start putting on his clothes ready to go to school
P3	When he was done putting on his clothes he was thinking about the wonderful food daddy is creating
P4	Joe came in the kitchen to see what dad was making for dinner
P5	Joe said dad what are you making? Dad said it was oatmeal oatmeal is very healthy because you put fruits inside of it so that's why sometimes oatmeal is for dinner but be careful it's hot
P6	But I don't like oatmeal said Joe come on! Give it a bite you never know it might be good
P7	But Joe don't want to eat it daddy wanted to tell him a story so that he can eat it
P8	If you eat oatmeal you're going to be strong like the elephants and tall like the giraffes
P9	I'm going to be strong and tall but if I'm tall I'll be able to reach stuff but I don't know if I can fit in the school
P10	He gave it a taste and then he liked it wow this is real good!!! I love oatmeal! I told you so Joe will eat oatmeal sometimes and the afternoon or maybe not or maybe on the day of school
