page_text:

P1	Empungu ekaba eine eiriho, yatambura orugyendo rureingwa erikuronda amaizi, konka tiyagabona.
P2	Yasharamu kuguruka ngu egaruke omukiti ahuyabiire netuura. Kikagitwarira obweire buringwa ahabw'okugira ngu ekaba eteine manyi.
P3	Kuyabire neguruka, yareeba amaizi omunyuungu omumbuga y'omuntu.
P4	Amaizi gakaba garimakye, gari ahansi omunyuungu. Omunwa gw'empungu gukaba gutakahika amaizi. Ekatandika kuronda oku eranywe ag'amaizi.
P5	Ekasharamu yaronda otubaare. Yanaga akabaare kamwe omu'maizi omunyungu, otweizi tukye twayatika.
P6	Empungu yayashama yatunywa.
P7	Yaronda agandi mabaare aguyagumire nerekyera omu'maizi, obwe erikuganywa kuhisya obu yahagiire.
