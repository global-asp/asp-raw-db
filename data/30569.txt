page_text:

P1	Nninze Juliet. Ndhi wamyaka munaana egy'obukulu nnhendha inho okukola emirimo gyangee ejaghaka. Ndhikweya enhumba yaife. Ndhagaine Empube ibiri mu dhokooni. Mwanhinaze yatiire empube yaalumuka wo asobole okwetaasa okumpi n'embwa.
P2	Ono ni maama wange. Erina rye nhi Mariam. Nnhendha inho okumugherekera ku mu katale buli lwa saabbiti. Asubulira mu Katale e kamwokya.
P3	Kano n'akatale ka kamwokya. Maama wange alimu n'omudaala. Atera kutundha mithungwa ate ebiseera ebindhi matooke. Aghandi yaatundha eiva. Ali ni Mwami Okero atera okutambukiza ku maama omudhaala.
P4	Eriyo abantu banji mu katale e kamwokya. Abandhi baidha kugula bintu abandhi baidha kutunda. Akatale ka Kamwokya kaagabizibwa mu ebiketezo okusinziira ku biki ebitundhibwa. Nsazeewo okutambula ku mu katale nhi maama wange.
P5	Lino ne Isamba lya maama wange. Atera okulirima. Ghano ghaatoola e iva lya tundha mu katale. Ali kundhegeresa engeri y'okunhoga mu e iva.
P6	Maama wange yaangulira akabuzi akato. Nakatuuma erinha lya popi. Mwanhinhaze yali aziramu kwendha mbuzi aye kwamwidhamu nga maama amaze okukangulira.
P7	Mukaseera akatono embuzi yakula. Yazaala obubuzi obundhi bubiri. Naghaaku mugandha wange kalala.Ntera okuliisa embuzi dhaife n'esubi ni mugandhawange.
P8	Maama wange yali musanhufu okubona nga popi yali ezaire abubuzi obundi bubiri. Yatuundha embuzi eibiri yaatusaasulira ebisale ebye isomero. Maama atwendha inho era tayeendha twizibwe ghaka okuva ku isomero lwa bisale bya isomero. yakozesa empiya edhiindhi okutugulira mu emere ni indifoomu. Maama waife atulabirira. Mubutuufu tumwendha inho.
P9	Maama waife yeena yatwendha inho era yaatulabirira bulungi nga omuzaire ow'obuuvunaanizibwa bwa teekwa okuba.
