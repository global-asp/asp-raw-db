desc:	Olufumo luno lugema ku kiki Kaseenhawuuzi kyakola era nibwekifuukamu ekintu ekyendhawulo.

page_text:

P1	Obuwuuka buno bwabiika amagi ku makoola.
P2	Twabuleka bwalyaku ku makoola.
P3	Bwageedha era bwasavughala.
P4	Kakubanta yaakola obuyumba bwa katetema.
P5	Mu buyumba bwa katetema, obuwuuka bwafuuka katetema.
P6	Abantu abandi baaya kaseenhawuuzi olw'emiganhulo edhindi.
P7	Munsi ya China, baaya ebika nkumi na nkumi ebya kaseenhawuuzi.
P8	Bakola engoye dha wuuzi okuva mu buyumba bwa katetema.
