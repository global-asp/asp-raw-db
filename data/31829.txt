title:	Ebibusi bia Selemeng'
author:	Gladys Nyachieo
artist:	Marleen Visser and Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Selemeng nigo abwate ebibusi ebinge. Ebibusi birenga Selemeng abwate?

page_text:

P1	Selemeng' nigo anchete ebibusi. Ebibusi nebinge bire mwaye.
P2	Selemeng' nabwate ekebusi ekemo ekemwamu. Ekebusi ekio nigo gekoria enyema enyinge.
P3	Selemeng' nabwate ekebusi ekemo ekenoru. Ekebusi ekio nigo gekori kera egento.
P4	Ekebusi kiagatato kiaminyokire omote igoro. Ekebusi ekio giakwamire omote igoro.
P5	Selemeng' orinire omote igoro. Okwamire omote igoro  amo n'ekebusi kiagatato. Ng'ina Selemeng' nkobakonyare goika korwa omote igoro.
P6	Selemeng' nigo abwate ebibusi bibere ebioro. Bikorara ase omobaso igoro rituko rigima.
P7	Selemeng' nigo abwate ebibusi bitato. Nigo bikobwata chimbeba chikoni botuko bokia.
P8	Selemeng' nabwate esese eyemo. Nigo ekerokua Lirafi. Lirafi tebwati basania. Nigo ebeete.
P9	Ebibusi bia Selemeng' tibiancheti Lirafi. Nigo bikoyeminyokia korwa nyomba. Lirafi yatamire.
P10	Selemeng' onyorire Lirafi nakoyereta inga. Ebibusi biabeire. Tibitageti korora Lirafi nainde.
P11	Selemeng' ebibusi birenga abwate? Nigo abwate ebibusi bitano nabitato.
P12	Chisese nirenga Selemeng' abwate? Nigo abwate esese eyemo. Lirafi ng'aiere bono?
P13	Ebibusi birenga  obwate inka? Chisese irenga obwate inka?
