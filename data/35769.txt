desc:	This is a story about Moi the happy and kind cow.

page_text:

P1	Moi was always happy.@Because she had many good friends.
P2	Her friends called her Jolly,@Because Moi loved to Jolly.
P3	Her neighbors called her Kindness,@Because Moi was always kind.
P4	One day Moi went to town,@To see what was around.
P5	Oh Hoi! Moi met Poi,@And fell in love with Poi.
P6	Together Moi and Poi went home,@To live happily ever after.
