title:	Akabaga Ka Mazalibwa Gange

desc:	Kakwata ku mwami Wakamyu eyajaguza amazalibwa ge nga wayiseewo emyaka abiri mwetaano.

page_text:

P1	Amanya gange nze Wakamyu. Mbeera ku kaalo akatono akayitibwa Kasanja.
P2	Amaka gaffe gaali maavu nnyo,  nga tetusobola kuzimba nyumba nnungi.
P3	Twalyanga ekibu kimu buli lunaku.
P4	Buli kumakya,  taata yantumanga mu nnimiro. Okugobanga ebinyonyi ne'bisolo ebirya ebimera.
P5	Mu kiseera kya makungula,  twafuna emmere nyingi ddala.
P6	Taata yasanyuka nnyo era nansubiza okuntegekera akabaga ka'mazalibwa.
P7	Wayitawo emyaka abiri mwetaano nendyoka nfuna akabaga ka'mazalibwa gange akasooka.
P8	Muliranwa waffe mwami Wankoko yasanyuka nnyo,  yalowooza kubaana be," Ndowooza njakugenda nabo."
P9	Ku makya ku maliri,  taata yatuzukusa tutandike okutegeka eby'okulya kubanga twayita abagenyi bangi.
P10	Amangu ddala,  twatandika okugabula abagenyi baffe.
P11	Oluvanyuma lw'okulya emmere,  abagenyi twabagabula keeki.
P12	Oluvanyuma lw'okulya keeki,  mikwano gyange bantonera ebirabo bingi.
P13	Kunkomerero,  mwami Wambuzi yaggulawo amazina anti ye muzinyi wokukyalo anywa mu banne akendo.
