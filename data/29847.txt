desc:	Mwami Kiio ali ni puusi endhabuufu yebeeta Safi.

page_text:

P1	mukyalo kyange, ghaligho Mwami kiio.mwami kiio ali ni puusi yebeeta Safi.
P2	mwami kiio bwaaja okupakasa,aleka olwigi nga lwigule.
P3	safi atyama mu mulyango okukuuma enhumba.
P4	embwa yamulilwana ghe bweidha okumpi, safi agikanga.
P5	Embwa elumuka gho.
P6	milirwana bw'akonkona kulwigi, Safi mw'etefutefu okukwadhula.
P7	Mulilwana acuuka era yalumuka
P8	safi bwaba endhala, aigalagho olwigi.
P9	safi alya emere ye.
P10	Oluvanhuma, Safi abukira mu ntebe,"tulya! tulya!tulya!"
P11	Mwami kiio bwaira, Safi y'edhumuula mu sanhu era ya 
musangaza ghaka
P12	Mwami kiio ni Safi batyama wansi, era basoma amaghulire.
