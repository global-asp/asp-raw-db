desc:	Nhu wewhola weTurkana unotidiya nekudziwa kwenti wemalala.

page_text:

P1	Letjani ndimubudze nekwenti wemalala.
P2	Mutjilimo nti woyu unotjila kose kumwe kwawoma.
P3	Ntuzi wenti wemalala unogala utonholela.
P4	Mitjelo yenti wemalala inodliwa.
P5	Tohingisa mahhani onti wemalala pfulila dzin'umba.
P6	Tothama mitshwayilo nemacansi tihingisa mahhani onti woyu.
P7	Mhudzi dzonti wemalala dzobesa moto.
P8	Nobona kuti nti wemalala unokudziwa kungapani?
