title:	Chicken and Millipede
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18289.png
desc:	Chicken and Millipede have a very competitive friendship. One day Chicken gets so cross about losing that she does something very silly.

page_text:

P1	Kuku na Jongoo walikuwa marafiki.
P2	Walienda kucheza mpira wa miguu.
P3	Kuku alikuwa golikipa.
P4	Jongoo alilifunga bao.
P5	Kuku alikasirika sana.
P6	Aliamua kummeza Jongoo.
P7	Kuku alipoondoka wanjani,  alikutana na Mama yake Jongoo.
P8	Jongoo aliyemezwa alilia,  "Mama nisaidie. Nimemezwa."
P9	Jongoo alikuwa na ladha mbaya sana. Kuku alihisi kutapika.
P10	Kuku alikohoa kumtema Jongoo.
P11	Jongoo alipotemwa,  alitoroka na kwenda zake.
P12	Tangu siku hiyo,  Kuku na Jongoo walikuwa maadui.
