title:	Utitshalakazi Uakinyi

artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	isiXhosa

desc:	Ibali elifutshane elilula elingotitshala ongumangaliso nezinto asifundisa zona.

page_text:

P1	Lo nguTitshalakazi wam. Igama lakhe nguTitshalakazi Akinyi. Uyandithanda kakhulu.
P2	UTitshalakazi Akinyi usifundisa ialfabhethi kunye noonobumba. Ndiyakwazi ukubiza amagama athi: a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	UTitshalakazi Akinyi usifundise le mibala: bomvu,  bhlowu,  luhlaza,  tyheli,  nomnyama.
P4	Xa kufike ixesha lokulala uyasiculela side silale. Usiculela ingoma yokusilalisa.
P5	Le ngoma yokulalisa ihamba ngolu hlobo: Thula bhabha,  thula mntwana Thula bhabha,  mus'ukulila Umama uyeza Uza neelekese.
P6	UTitshalakazi Akinyi wazi amabali amaninzi. Usibalisela ibali elahlukileyo kusuku ngalunye lweveki.
P7	NgoMvulo,  usibalisela amabali ngamadoda amangalisayo.
P8	NgoLwesibini,  usibalisela amabali ngamakhosikazi amangalisayo.
P9	NgoLwesithathu,  usibalisela amabali ngezithuthi.
P10	NgoLwesine,  usibalisela amabali ngezolimo nemfuyo.
P11	NgoLwesihlanu,  uTitshalakazi Akinyi usiyalela ukuba sibalise amabali wethu eklasini.
P12	UTitshalakazi wethu ngoyena titshala ubalaseleyo kulo lonke ihlabathi! Xa ndikhulile ndifuna ukuba njengoTitshalakazi Akinyi.
