P1	izolo ntambama, uSipho nomndeni wakhe baye iZoo. @ @@abantu abaningi bebefuna ukubona izingwe. @@uSipho akathandi izingwe, kodwa umfowethu, uThandi, bewufuna ukuthatha izithombe nabo.@@uSipho uthanda obhejane
P2	uSipho nomama wakhe baye ukubona ezinye izilwane zasendle ezinye. Babone indlovu yinye nezindlulamithi ezimbili.@@bekuwusuku olushisayo, umama kaSipho umuphe imali yokuthenga ubisi.@@ngendlela wakhe, uSipho ubone abantu abaningi abagijimayo. "Inyoka ibaleke eZoo." kusho Intombazane eyahambayo.
P3	bewulahlekile uSipho. bewuqala ukukhala.@@indoda imbuze "uyaphi?@@Ngiya esangweni elibili. @@Yena uphendule@@Jika kwesokudla. Kusho indoda@@uSipho umbonge bese ujike kwesokudla.
P4	uSipho ubone umama bewuseduze nendlulamithi. @@uSipho ugone umama.@@baye ukufuna ubaba nomfowethu bese bahambe ngemoto entsha ukuya erestorenti.@@umndeni badle chakalala futhi baphuze amahewu baze basuthi.
