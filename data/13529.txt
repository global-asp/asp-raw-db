page_text:

P1	Cow,  Goat,  and Dog went to town in a taxi. They were going shopping.
P2	Cow bought a TV.
P3	Goat bought a blanket.
P4	Dog wanted to buy new shoes. But he did not have enough money.
P5	The three friends went home in a taxi.
P6	Did you see Cow standing in a field doing nothing? It is because she is thinking about her TV.
P7	Did you see Goat running away from cars? It is because he is worried that someone might steal his blanket.
P8	Did you see Dog running towards cars? Now he has enough money to buy his shoes. He wants to go to town.
