title:	Keeper Ni U Wubsigu Yiri
author:	R.Y. Tongo and J. Salifu  (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Mampruli
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Soalini wa tɔasirila bunkɔb kpiibi seesi ban daa pisi n zi n kyɛaŋ wubsigu yiri ni. Bunkɔb bisi wa daa nyɛa wubsigu ni gɔahabu ninvug suŋŋu saani ni u suŋ suŋni ba.

page_text:

P1	Sɔlimni wa gyannila Keeper ni u bunkɔb bisi sheeba ŋɔn wubisi sheeba ni ŋɔn wubisiri la gɔari shea kan gaari la Keeper ni ban sunŋini u nimbea daa mɔaiyani.
P2	Nomsa ŋɔn daa nya yuun yinni la n daa danŋi paari na. U daa kyanni na ni bɛnkoom turinkaa.
P3	Keeper ni bandaa suŋni u la suhuri daabu pɛligi n-basi Nomsa. Amaa di daa bu yuui ka Nomsa nya yaa. U daa pili ndemni la u taaba.
P4	Dabishɛa yaasa alikɔbta n-daa zaani mayankalesi.  
Alikɔbta maa da nyala bubila ka pli kuntu titaari.
P5	Ndiwa daa bɛla kuntu maa tiŋni. Uda nyala yuumaanu bidibga. Keeper daa diisiri Ndiwa ni koliba ni.
P6	Kpibga ŋɔnpasi ata la noori nyala Malea. U da nyala gɔaya yoobu.
P7	Di daabuyui ka u labigi Malea zɔa.
P8	Yuŋu yinishea, sokam daa niaya goom ni ni banya Amiba paari na.
P9	Amiba daa nyala gɔayanu. Di daa wummu paam u kyan wa-ala maa.
P10	U nangowalli maa zugu, Keeper daa wukki koliba maa saazugu ni u nyulisi Ambia. Kpibsi sheeba ban daa yooli n kyana gɔari Kurugu la nyala Kopi, Kepi ni Keji. Ba daa nyala bakwai ayi dima.
P11	Keeper ni u sungniba maa wuri bukɔbri maa zaa hali ni bandaka laasabu la.
P12	Mayenkale niribamaa daa tumiya pam. Badaa mari tama ni dahansheli kpibsi maa ningɔasi bamansi. Ka bunkɔbri maa labi ba besigu daziya.
