page_text:

P1	Hi! I am princess, I live in that big castle with my family.
P2	I am Cece, from a small village not far from here.
P3	Can we become friends because I do not really have friends around here?
P4	Yes, I would love that. I was going to my favorite place, do you want to join me?
P5	Yes please.
P6	Cece and Princess: Walking to Cece's favorite place.
P7	We are here!
P8	This is a beautiful place Cece, why do you love it so much?
P9	Well, the river is peaceful and I get to learn about many animals sounds.
P10	That is wonderful. What sound does the crocodile make?
P11	Grunt, grunt
P12	That's scary.
P13	Well, this is enough for today, tomorrow we will learn about the forest
