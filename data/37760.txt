P1	It was on a beautiful Monday morning, when Tshamano's mother accompanied her to bus stop, where she is normally fetched by her school bus. She was just left alone, after her mother rushed to work.
P2	Her friends arrived and find Tshamaano sitting on the bench. Upon theirarrival,Tshamaano was more relieved.@After several arguments and quarrels, they took Tshamano's lunch box.
P3	Poor Tshamaano was very sad and as a result, she threw herself on the road and a speedy truck trampled on her and also hit another car. Tshamano was confirmed dead on the scene.
