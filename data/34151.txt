page_text:

P1	One sunny day, on top of the hill lived a clever mongoose and a a red rooster. The mongoose was pretty but full cunning manners.
P2	One morning, a mongoose was very hungry. She then to a walk to seatch for food. While she was walking, she noticed a rooster sitting on top of the big tree.
P3	The rooster was so excited to hear the news. He climbed down the tree. When he got on the last branch of the tree, he thought. Something was not right.
