desc:	Ajoh alamanyanga

page_text:

P1	Lego yali inzukha ikhongo. Yali niyamenya mushikwenyi shia Sinyare.
P2	Apiyo, Ajoh nende Atieno batsia okhurenya tsikhwi.

Kukhu abawa amachero.
P3	Bosi bataru bakasia eshiakhulia. "Ilaba inyanga indabi."
P4	"Recheresiekho, Lego amenyanga hambi ano." Apiyo yetsulisia abashie.
P5	Atieno yenya ameno ka Lego.

Apiyo namubira, "Kukhu yakhukania."
P6	"Ndenya ameno ka Lego keitsahabu." Ajoh shiyateba tawe.
P7	Lego shiyanyola amenoke keitsahabu tawe.

Yesumirisia okhutira omwifi.
P8	"Hsssss!" Abakhana bahulira omwoyo. Lego yenya okhumuluma owali niyebere.
P9	Lego yabaheresia amarebo. "Owali niyebere shiyakhembere obulayi tawe."
P10	Apiyo nende Atieno babira amarebo. Beemba obulayi.
P11	Ajoh shiyanyalirwa okhuimba  tawe.

Lego yamanya omwifi ni wina.
P12	Lego yamumira Ajoh!

"Alamanyanga yakhahulire amachero ka kukhu."
