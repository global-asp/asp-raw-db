title:	Cat and Dog and the Egg
author:	Elke and René Leisink
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20150.png
desc:	Cat and Dog are best friends. They see an egg on the grass. What will they do with it?

page_text:

P1	This is Cat.

This is Dog.
P2	Cat and Dog walk. 

They walk in their village.
P3	Then they see an egg. 

The egg is in the grass. 

The egg is alone in the grass. 

The egg is all alone.
P4	Cat and Dog walk to a bird. 

They ask the bird, "Is this your egg?"
P5	But the bird says, 
"No, that is not my egg. Ask the owl.  
Maybe it is his egg."
P6	Cat and Dog walk to the owl. 

They ask the owl, "Is this your egg?"
P7	But the owl says, 
"No, that is not my egg. Ask the goose.  
Maybe it is her egg."
P8	Cat and Dog walk to the goose. 

They ask the goose, "Is this your egg?"
P9	But the goose says, "No, that is not my egg. 
Ask the two ducks. Maybe it is their egg."
P10	Cat and Dog walk to the two ducks. 

They ask the the two ducks, "Is this your egg?"
P11	But the two ducks say, "No, that is not our egg." 

Then the egg breaks.
P12	There is a lizard in the egg. 

A baby lizard.
P13	The baby lizard says, 
"Where is my mother? Where is my father? 
Where are my mother and father?" 

Cat and Dog bring the baby lizard to his mother and father.
P14	Bye bye Cat. 

Bye bye Dog. 

Bye bye baby lizard.
