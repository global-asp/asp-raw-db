desc:	An invitation to celebrate nature with the characters in the story. Will you take it? This story was child created at the ASb Storymaking Workshop at Oakbridge School

page_text:

P1	Sarah loves to count clouds. How many clouds can you see?
P2	Chidubem and Chibuzor are crying, their cloths are dirty.
P3	It is raining heavily, we run for cover when it rains.
P4	The frog is unhappy because there are no flies to eat.
P5	This rabbit likes enjoyment.
P6	Lola is dancing in the rain. It makes her happy.
P7	The rainbow is out at last, Nkechi is excited.
P8	Nkechi celebrated the rainbow and went home to eat some oranges.
