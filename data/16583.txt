title:	Engoho Ni Waga
author:	Bukheye Mulongo Christopher
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/16583.png
desc:	Olugano lw’Engoho ni Waga olw’edaala eryo eryohune (4) ni hulwihiise hu daala edaayi (1).

page_text:

P1	Engoho ni Waga bali bamuhago.
P2	Batiina ohusamba omupiira.
P3	Engoho nj'eyali epambi y'e goolo.
P4	Waga gateeba!
P5	Engoho esunguŋala.
P6	Engoho ya mira Waga.
P7	Engoho yaganana ni nyina ya Waga.
P8	Nga Waga alira ati,  "Maama njede!"
P9	Engoho yeŋulira n'erwaye.
P10	Waga abulamo jamu.
P11	Engoho ya haasa Waga nga Waga gedulumiraŋo.
P12	Engoho ni Waga sijendana.
