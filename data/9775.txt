title:	Doli Na Manyinge Azizi
author:	Nyadhi Eric Juma
artist:	Natalie Propa
lang:	Dholuo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/9775.png
desc:	Nyathi mawinjo maber ka orito doli mare.   To kendo doli ne no osiko ka omor.

page_text:

P1	Maniendi en Azizi. Azizi en doli mara.
P2	Ahere kinde duko nimar kinde duko osiko ka obunjo.
P3	Bang' ka achiweo,  alwoko wang'a bang'e amanyo. Kama Azizi nitiere.
P4	Banye ka ayude,  aywoyone wiye.
P5	Sama ametho,  ko ametho kanyakla,  kod Azizi.
P6	Kata sama achamo nyami bilie,  ko amiye moro.
P7	To kata kamano,  en ok ocham mare. Kendo mano kawa kod wuoro!
P8	Samoro awuotho e aluora ka apono maua.
P9	Bang'e amiye,  kata kamano,  ok ogo,  "Erokamano."
P10	Alwoko Azizi maer ahinya kapok mamana olwoka.
P11	Bang'e arwake nanga makwar mimar ahero range makwar.
P12	Bang'e ka adhi nindo,  adhi kod Azizi kendo apiele e batha.
