page_text:

P1	Linda  was  a young smart girl living with her parents.. They lived on a farm name Duka at Berlin.
P2	Her mother always  told  her about  the importance of education. If you are  educated you can go  anywhere, education open  all the  doors. Education has no limits.
P3	Linda was going to school. The school was too far  and walked by foot, there was no transport.
P4	Linda  was doing her higher school study  when she notice  young girl left the school and married. Linda was  worried.
P5	Linda's mother  told  Linda that"there is a law that was  amended, the girls at age of fifteen must leave  the school and married. "
P6	Linda study at the university. Linda studies the law. Linda graduate  and become a lawyer.
P7	First thing  she open the case to change the law  which abuse the women and the girls
P8	Linda won the case  and the law change. Education opens all the doors. Linda was smart  and bright.
