title:	Endogoyi  nente  eriima

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Olufumo olwente eriima enkozi einho nendogoyi engezi.

page_text:

P1	Omulimi  yali  n'ente  eriima  enkulu.
P2	Era  yali  n'endogoyi.  Ente  eriima  n'endogoyi  bali  bamukwano.
P3	Eigulo,  ente  eriima  yakoba  endogoyi, "nkoye  inho  olwalero."
P4	Ente  eriima  yakoba  endogoyi, "Tighumwireku  lero."
P5	Endogoyi   yairamu,  "nkoze  okusinga."
P6	Olunaku  olwairaku,  ente  eriima  yakoba, "lero  lubaire  lunaku  lubi."
P7	Endogoyi  yaakoba,  "Omulimi  bwanaira,  'moora!'  Aidha  kukuleka."
P8	Enkyo eryairaku, ente eriima yaliira,'Yamoola!'  Omulimi muleke aghumure.
P9	Omulimi  yaatwala  endogoyi  okulima.
P10	Endogoyi  yakola  inho  olunhaku  lwonhalwona.  Yalinkoghu  inho  beyaira
P11	Endogoyi  yakoba,  "Omulimi  akobye  agya  kwita  afune  emamba.
P12	Ente  eriima  yakoba, "Ndakola  idho. mpulira  bulungi  kati"
