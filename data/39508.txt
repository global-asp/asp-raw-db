Amara is a little girl who loves all animals big and small.  She one day met two doves who were injured   and she took them home. What
do you think happened next ?

page_text:

P1	There was a girl called Amara ,she loved all animals big and small  but not that many people in her community loved animals as much as she does.
P2	Her parents owned a farm which had many animals such as goat , cat, chicken and doves she loved all of them and took good care of them .
P3	One day her and her friends Lily and Bobby were heading to the market Amara said "I hope l will  get the tomatoes my mother sent me ...."before she could finish she was interrupted with Bobby Bobby shouted "ooo no look to injured birds."
P4	Amara and her friends took them back home to Amara's house her mother asked  her "where are the tomatoes I sent you to get ?" She answered while looking down on the floor "mother I didn't get them sorry but I got two injured birds.
P5	Her parents  ware happy   her father said "tomorrow we will take the doves to the vetenary"she couldn't sleep all night long but morning  reached and they took the doves to the vet the vet said "it is only a small  injury take this medicine
P6	and spread on injured part " they went home and tomorrow she went to school and spoke about the  awareness of taking care of animals.
