id:	31808
title:	Katse le Ntja ba a taka ba tlotsa  ka mebala
author:	Thelma Tshesane
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31808.png
desc:	Katse le Ntja ke metswalle e makatsang ebile ba itumella ho etsa dintho kaofela ba le mmoho. Ba a taka ebile ba kenya mebala.

page_text:

P1	Enwa ke Katse.@@Enwa ke Ntja.
P2	Katse le Ntja ba batla ho taka.@@Katse le Ntja ba batla ho  tlotsa ka mebala.@@ Katse le Ntja ba batla ho etsa setshwantsho.@@Ntja o a qala.@@O nka potloloto, o nka le pampiri. Pampiri e kgutlonne.
P3	Ntja o qala ka ho taka sebopeho sa lehe.@@Sebopeho sa lehe ke mmele wa Katse.@@Kgidi! Ho bile bonolo.
P4	Jwale o taka dikgutlotharo tse pedi hodima sebopeho sa lehe.@@Dikgutlotharo tse pedi tseo, ke ditsebe tsa Katse hape o taka kgutlotharo e ntsho ka hara sebopeho sa lehe.@@Kgutlotharo e ntsho eo ke nko ya Katse mme o seha  mola ka hodima nko.
P5	Jwale o taka mahlo le dintshi tsa Katse.@@Mahlo ke matheba a mabedi.@@Dintshi ke mela e mmedi.@@Kgidi! Ho bile bonolo.
P6	Jwale o taka molomo wa Katse.@@Molomo ke mola.@@O shebahala jwale ka tlhaku ya "w" hape o taka ditedu tsa  Katse.@@Ditedu tse tharo ka lehlakoreng la letsoho le  letshehadi le ditedu tse tharo  ka lehlakoreng la letsoho le letona.
P7	Setshwantsho se se setla fela.@@O taka matsoho le maoto a Katse.@@Matsoho le maoto ke mela hape o taka le monwana wa leoto wa Katse.@@Menwana ya maoto ke di matheba hape o taka le mohatla wa Katse.@@Mohatla ke mola o molelele.
P8	Qetellong o taka metsero e  itseng mmeleng wa Katse mme a kenya mmala o lamunu.@@Jwale setshwantso se feletse.@@Kgidi! Mosebetsi o motle.
P9	Jwale ke sebaka sa Katse. O nka phensele, o nka le pampiri. Pampiri ke kgutlonne.
P10	Sa pele Katse o taka sebopeho sa lehe.@@ Sebopeho sa lehe ke mmele wa Ntja. @@Kgidi! Seo se setle.
P11	Jwale o taka dibopeho tse pedi hodima sa lehe.@@Dipobeho tseo tse pedi ke ditsebe tsa Ntja hape o taka sedikodiko se setsho ka hara sa lehe.@@Letheba le letsho ke nko ya Ntja mme a seha mola hodima nko.
P12	Jwale o taka mahlo le dintshi tsa Ntja. @@Mahlo ke matheba a mabedi.@@Dintshi ke mela e mmedi.@@Kgidi! Seo se phethahetse.
P13	Jwale  o taka molomo wa Ntja.@@Molomo ke mola.@@Sebopeho hape ke tlhaku ya "w" ebile o taka matheba a itseng ho potoloha molomo wa Ntja.
P14	Setshwantsho se batlile se feleletse.@@O taka matsoho le maoto a Ntja. @@Matsoho le maoto ke mela hape o taka le monwana wa leoto la Ntja.@@Menwana ya maoto a Ntja ke  matheba hape o taka mohatla wa Ntja.@@Mohatla o shebahala jwale ka palesa.
P15	Qetellong o taka matheba a mang mmeleng wa Ntja mme a kenya mmala o mosehla.@@Jwale setshwantsho se feleletse.@@Kgidi! seo ke mosebetsi o motle.
P16	Sala hantle Katse.@@Sala hantle setshwantsho sa Katse.@@Sala hantle Ntja.@@Sala hantle setshwantsho sa Ntja.
