P1	Her name is Lila. She has a disability to see.@@Her name is Lila. Born with disability to see. @@So she use her hands to see. If she would use her sense of touch she would be able to see through her imagination.
P2	Despite of her condition, she still have a dream and that is to see the world.@@"One day, just for one day let me see the beauty as well as the ugliness of the world and I will be contented for the rest of my life" she said.
P3	"You're still lucky. You're fine and you will choose to smile" she said to herself.@@Everytime she would feel sad, she would just let herself be with her animals and play with them.
P4	The other boy heard what Lila said.@"You don't have to feel sad because from now on you will have me. I will become your friend and I will help you see the things you wanted to see" the boy said.@@"Thank you" Lila said politely.
P5	She would just smile every time she would remember what the boy said to her. @Because now as she opened her eyes, the tears vanished that's keeping her from opening her eyes however as the tears vanished as well as how the boy vanished.
P6	"Now, I would be able to help others completely" she said with a hint of sadness. @@So she worked diligently in the community to have money and save up.
P7	As a fruit of her hardwork she able to explore and see the world with her eyes. But then, she saw a man. @@"Impossible" she said unbelievably.@@But when she looks closely, she knew.
