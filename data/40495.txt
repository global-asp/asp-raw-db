title:	Salaamatu e seyi
author:	Abdulkariimu  Dawda and Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ɓinngel nanii belɗum ngam heɓugo paɗe artuɗe.

page_text:

P1	Salaamatu walaa paɗe.@@Higooɓe maako fuu e ngoodi paɗe.
P2	Salaamatu wi'i daada maako, "E ɗum saɗi fijugo bannda paɗe."
P3	Daada maako sey wi'i, "Salaamatu, mi yi'i ndikka keɓaa paɗe."
P4	Fajiri cub, daada ummini Salaamatu.
P5	Salaamatu bee daada maako njahi luumo.
P6	Ɓe njahi to ɗum soorata paɗe.@@Daada sey soodani Salaamatu paɗe.
P7	Jemma, Salaamatu e numa paɗe maako kese.
P8	Salaamatu foondi paɗe maako.
P9	E mo numa no mo hollirta soobiraaɓe paaɗe maako kese.
P10	Salaamatu faɗɗii paɗe maako kese fahin.@@Mo wami nder suudu.
P11	Daada wi'i, "Taa' faɗɗa paɗe to a ɗaanoto."
P12	Salaamatu nanii belɗum soosey ngam heɓugo paɗe artuɗe.
