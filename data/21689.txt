title:	Unkawu Ngomnyaka Wendlala

desc:	Singahlalisana kuhle nxa sisabelana labanye singancitshani.

page_text:

P1	Ngalowo mnyaka izulu alizange line. Umhlaba wawome qha!
P2	UNkawu wasuka ekhaya waydinga amanzi lokudla.
P3	Wedlula izintaba lezindonga.
P4	Wafika endaweni okuthiwa yiThikolo.
P5	UNkawu wathaba kakhulu efika kulindawo.
P6	Wadla izithelo wazimuka! Kodwa waba lesizungu ekhumbula abanye bakhe.
P7	Ngakho-ke wabuyela ekhaya.
P8	Abanye onkawu bathaba bembona efika.
P9	"Ingaphi indawo le elezithelo?" bambuza.
P10	"Ngizahamba lani," waphendula uNkawu.
P11	Abanye onkawu bakuthanda eThikolo. Bakhetha ukuhlala khona kokuphela.
P12	Onkawu base Thikolo kwabakhathaza lokhu.
P13	Bathi, "Onkawu bemzini bazakudla izithelo zethu zonke."
P14	Onkawu base Thikolo bayahlasela onkawu bemzini.
P15	"Kungani lisilwa?" wabuza omunye wabadala. "Kulezithelo eziseneleyo sonke."
P16	Lokhu kwakuliqiniso. Onkawu bonke bakhetha ukuhlalisan kuhle ngokuthula.
