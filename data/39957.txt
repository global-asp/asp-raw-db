desc:	The crocodile was taught a lesson by hare this means that you should be very kind to others.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there was  only one dam in the forest.A big and fierce crocodile live in the dam. All the animals feared him because of his appearance. The animals also feared going into the dam.
P2	"We are thirsty" animals said to the lion. King lion thought what to do but the crocodile was very fierce and huge.The animals told lion that hare was very clever. "Can you please think how we will get the water from the dam?" Lion ask hare
P3	Hare thought and thought at last he got a solution,he went to his king and told him that he had a plan, hare told him about the plan. ' it is a good plan,' said the king."But be very careful of the crocodile,"  The hare was very scared.
P4	He bravely went to the dam ' good morning crocodile" hare cheerfuly said." I know you are very brave."the crocodile said.Hare laughed very loudly,"What is so funny?why are you laughing?"The crocodile asked.
P5	The animals agreed that the animal with the longest mouth will be the king.Crocodile was very excited,ok measure mine, the Crocodile thought that he would be a king,Hare had a rope,he tightly tied crocodile's mouth with the rope.
P6	"Everyone can now drink this water"said Hare. Crocodile could not answer or talk to the animals who come to drink water from the dam.They were very happy that they sang many songs that hare was the bravest animal in the kingdom.
P7	The crocodile asked for forgiveness and was forgiven. All the animals lived happily in their kingdom.They decided to be very helpful and polite to others.
