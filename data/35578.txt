page_text:

P1	Delu is a girl from Dakuma village. She hawks nono(cow milk) from morning till evening.
P2	One day, as she was hawking, a man invited her to bring nono pretending that he was going to buy it all. As Delu entered the house,the man grabbed her, locked the door and raped her.
P3	Even though she reported the incidence to her family, nothing was done. The girl child needs to be safe and protected from any form of violation.
P4	Girl child violation could be rape or any physical and emotional harm. We must all speak out.
