page_text:

P1	UTjhontjhwani omncani uthanda khulu ukufunda. Ngamalanga bebahlala barhuga uTjhontjhwani.

"Abotjhontjhwani abafundi. Abotjhontjhwani kufanele bondle. Abotjhontjhwani babuthelela ukudla abakufunako."
P2	UTjhontjhwani omNcani ubefunda ilanga loke, afunde, afunde ilanga belitjhinge.
P3	Abanye abotjhontjhwani babuthelele ukudla abakutholako bese bakubulungela ubusika ngaphasi komhlabathi.
P4	Unina noyise kaTjhontjhwani omNcani bese ibahlanganisa ihloko indaba le. UTjhontjhwani azange imphathe kuhle indaba le.
P5	Ngesikhathi sesiruthwana lokha amakari nakathoma ukuwela phasi, abotjhontjhwani kufanele babuthelelele ukudla ngaphasi komhlabathi.
P6	INdlovukazi ifuna ukudla kuzale esitorweni, ngalokho boke abojhontjhwani kufanele basebenze.
P7	UTjhontjhwani omNcani wathoma ukurhuwelela ngalokhu afunda ngakho.

"Irestjurente yinto esiyidingako, indawo lapha abantu badlela khona. Itjho njalo incwadi engiyifundako."
P8	Abotjhontjhwani bathoma ukukhamba ngamunye ngamunye. Bakhamba nje baya lapha ilanga litjhinga ngakhona.
P9	UTjhontjhwani omNcani wathi, "Naku okhunye!"
P10	Abotjhontjhwani bakhamba ngamunye ngamunye baphethe ibhega, baphethe ibhansi.
P11	Lokha nababuyako bathole iNdlovukazi ithabe khulu. Iintoro bezizele swi. Bathaba khulu.
P12	Unina noyise wabotjhontjhwani wagona abantwana babo.
P13	Umntwana wakwabo omntazana walotjhisa uTjhontjhwani omNcani

"Njeke ngiyazwisisa. Ngendlela uthanda ukufunda incwadi ngakhona, kungenza bona ngifune ukuqalaqala nami."
