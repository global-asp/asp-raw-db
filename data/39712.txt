P1	Daniel and David are twin brother, there always play together, wears the same type of clothes, eat together with their father and goes to the farm together.
P2	On day, their father asked them to go to the farm to harvest some fruits crops, there where so happy to go together. On their way, they tell theirselves story's, jokes, riddles,  and soon reach
P3	Where their are going. On their way from the farm, there saw a monkey that scared them and they drop their fruit crop basket and hold on to each other in fear.
P4	There where terrified!. The scary monkey took their fruit basket and eat all the fruits, right in front of them, as the twin where Extremely frightened,
P5	There watch the monkey eat all the fruits. They went home and told their father everything that happened. Their father caution them and ask them to go inside and rest
P6	The scary monkey keep on frightening them until one day, the twin deviced a plan and timed the usual time the monkey use to attack them on the road. Luckily as planned the monkey
P7	Came as planned, they cover the basket and hand it over to the  scary monkey, as it becomes usual of it collecting their friuts crop basket, thinking is the friuts crops inside the basket
P8	Immediately, the monkey collect the basket and was about to open it, an angry dog jumped out of the basket and chased the monkey away. The twin became happy as their plan works.
P9	THE END
