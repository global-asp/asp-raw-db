page_text:

P1	Obwakale hakaba haliyo omushejja muto baramweta Kumbana.
P2	Ahamweka 25, yakunda omushejji baramweta Zangane.
P3	Bakekunda obwele bwe emwenyaka 3, nkoka akaba atine ssente nzo kumutansa nali kumushwera
P4	Enzoba lemwe yagira endda, ahabwokuba tebalankonzensi akampila.
P5	Banzara omuhala, bamweta Kheri.
P6	Nkoka Kumbana akaba akyali mweshomwero, leero Zangane aine omulimo mu tawnu hale hoo, kyatuma batatura hamwe.
P7	Ahanyima, Zangane yafunna omushejja mussa. Batula hamwe na muhara weye.
P8	Kumbana akawa amatsiko. yachura ahabwokuba bakamutwala hoo muhala weye.
P9	Obwile bwagenda, Kumbana yafunna omukazi omwusa. Batura hamwe kwoka tabanzala abaana.
P10	Hatiya Kheri, talakunzere taata omunsya. yanyiga munoga, kandi akaaba alamu teela.
P11	Aha mweka 12, nyena yasharaho kumwohereza oweshe kutula nawe.
P12	Obwe nali ahayima Kumbana na Kheri bashemeregwa hamwe.
