desc:	Omuhara muto bakakohereza owa shwenkuru weye ahuya bugana enyameishwa omu ihamba.

page_text:

P1	omuhara akasindikw'a bazire be owa shwenkuru weye aratuura enyima y'ekibira. Y'agenzire namashemererwa.
P2	okuya hikire hihi nekibira yashanga embuzi eyamuhabwire kugenda nobwegendereza ahabokuba hariyo orukungana rwenyameishwa.
P3	okuya hikire ahagati yekibira, yashanga enyameishwa aha muhanda yeirkua munonga arikutera endulu.
P4	kuya shohwire ekibira akashanga enyonyi zikuyomba. akeishiki kiruka arikuyomba. shwenkuru yamuhurira.
P5	shwenkuru yagambira mukyala we ngogwe nomwijjukuru waabo. yarahuka ku mubona nembwa yeeye. bamushanga aruhire.
P6	kuba hikire omuka, yatahamu ya ramusya aband'abaana kuyamazire, ya rye'mere. kuyaherize, ya bagambira ekya bire ho amuhanda gweye.
P7	kuyaherize kubagambira ekibeireho hamwe nokuffuna amagezi gokutambula nondijjo muntu, yagenda kuzana na abaana'bandi  namashemererwa.
P8	Taragarukiiremu kuraba'mukibira wenka.
