title:	Ẹdjẹnẹkpo v' Akora
author:	Eruotor Grace
artist:	Isaac Okwir
lang:	Urhobo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/35199.png
desc:	Leopard and Antelope take an oath to be friends. But their friendship is doomed from the start. (This story was originally narrated by Magabi Enyew Gessesse. The English version was written by Elizabeth Laird.).

page_text:

P1	Ẹdjẹnẹkpo guọnẹ akora@  mhẹ tobọ
P2	Akora kọjẹ kẹ Ẹdjẹnẹkpo
P3	Ẹdẹvo vwo te, Ẹdjẹnẹkpo ko se akora, yarhe rere em’ ugbeya.@  Mẹvwẹ riobo whọria aa. Mẹvwo@  wẹ kasa di’ ọvhreghrẹ ẹ
P4	Akora da rhọvwe phiho,Ẹjẹnẹkpo kọtare nẹ, gbe@  ja ri’ọvwọ rugbeyan kugbe. Kowhokowho r’ ọfa ọvwọ na, ọmọ@  rọye ko gwẹ
P5	Kaye rhiọvwọ rugbeyan kugbe. Aso vwo te,Akora da pherẹ otọ r’urhe.@  Ẹdjẹnẹkpo da pherẹ agho r’urhe@  obenu
P6	Akora kọre rhuoma,@  ẹdjẹnẹkpo da dọn.
P7	Ẹdjẹnẹkpo kọ guọnẹ akora wheria. Kọtare nẹ, ọye@  gbe brudu emu vuọvo oo, ifọkiridie,ọye@  vw’ọmọ vuọvo oo
P8	Ẹdjẹnẹkpo ko vwo wotọ rọ se vwo@  mhẹ akora, ẹkẹvuọvo, aghọ rẹ urhe na@  da nyọrẹ ẹdjẹnẹkpo
P9	Akora ko guegue ri, ode kperi nẹ Bẹh!  Bẹh!
P10	Ẹdjẹnẹkpo kọ rhoma rẹ akora, nẹ@  ọchọye uko. Ọnẹ ọgba karo@  phihọ ọvwọ rugbeya raye rere.
P11	Akora kọ kpahe vwọkẹ ne, ộke-ộvo esẹ rọye eye rie ọvwọ yena@  kugbe ekpuyovwi roma rọye.
