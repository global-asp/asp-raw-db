title:	Entama eyatamirwe amagara ge tawuni
author:	AMUMPEIRE SHANKLINE
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi
lang:	Rukiga

desc:	Buri mbera eyoreshagamu

page_text:

P1	Entama ekaba erataha omu tawuni. Yatamwa embera ze tawuni lero yasharamu kuza omukibira. Ahamuhanda yabugana empiisa owa beire amazire ebiiro bwigyi atakarire.
P2	Yaramusyaempiisa yamuramusya ogundi murundi nogundi, "Mwene mawe mpiisaoryota?" Empiisa yamugarukamu anti, "Otandamusya munoga. Gyira ekyiwakora kumaraho egyijjara yagye."
P3	Entama kuyahurire ekyi, yatandika kwiruka. Aho naho yahika omukyijju kye entare. Obwe empiisa ekaba emwirukyireho. Empiisa yahika omujju ye entare lero yayemerera orikushuka omuchuchu omumeisho ge entare.
P4	Entare yamugyira ati, "Titurabasa kugyira obusigye omujju zeeitu? Ori kushuka omuchuchu omumeisho geitu!"
Empiisa yamugarukamu ati, "Omukama,nabatakyimanyisinze kukujjoga! Kwiyaho nyirukyire ahamere yagye!"
P5	Entare yamugyira ati, "Niwe oriemere yagye, Empiisa. Nakwirukaho? Wayereta. Otanyekwasa bwanyima."
P6	Lero entare yabuza entama, "Habwekyi oyijjire omukyishaka?" Entama yagarukamu, "Nyijjire ahabwokuba ryo omufumu."
P7	Entare yagyira, "Tebekanissa omubazi gwebishaka nku nyoreka ngu buzima ori omufumu wamaziima." Entama yamugarukamu ati, "Omubazi gwagye ngurasera."

Entare yagyira, "Ekyotikyizibu, buri sente zona. Ntihariho ekyirandema."
P8	Entama yagyira, "Omubazi nokuttu kwe'mpiisa." Entare yasharaho okutu kwe'mpiisa lero yakuha entama. Entama yata okutu kwe'mpiisa omu jaaga yo'bwokyi lero yaha entare.
P9	Entare yarya okutu kwe'mpiisa lero yashaga kunuzire. Yabuza Entama, "Ndabasa kurya okutu kwakabiri?"
P10	Entama yagyira, "yego, kyirabasika mukama wagye." Lero entare yasharaho okutu kwe'mpiisa okwakabiri lero yakuha entama. Entama yakuta omu jaaga yobwokyi lero yaha entare.
P11	Lero entare yabuza entama, "Kyirabasika kubona oruhu?" Entama yagarukamu ati, "Ndatekateka kyirabasika mukama wagye." Empiisa kuyakyihurire yantina kuffa lero yiruka. Entare yamwirukaho.
P12	Entama yagyira, "Aga gabagarigo amagara gekyibira, kyikaboneire nagaruka omuka,"
