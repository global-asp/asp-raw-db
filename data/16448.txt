title:	Shibvə̀m Shee Azizi
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh
artist:	Natalie Propa
lang:	Lámnsoʼ

desc:	Wan və̀n-eè koŋ fó a leéy fo shibvə̀m sə́ shí

page_text:

P1	Və̀n dzə̀ Azizi. Azizi dzə̀ shibvə̀m shém.
P2	M koŋ Azizi,  bìʼ fo wù lo,  wù wàa ŋwàŋ ghán sidzə̀m.
P3	M lo e yuv,  su sí yém,  m nèn tàʼ fo Azizi dzəə̀n.
P4	M yén Azizi m nèn sàa kitú kfə́.
P5	M lóo yikír kijàvndzə,  m wàa yiír Azizi.
P6	M lóo nyoŋ shwìir,  m fò wùmòʼ e Azizi.
P7	Yii bèy,  Azizi yòʼ lo,  wù nyòŋ və́ə woo shwìir. A kí jivír mo.
P8	M lo,  m wàa ghan,  a kaarí gháa lav,  a keŋ vibuushì.
P9	 M fó kibuushì e Azizi,  Yii bèy,  a yòʼ dzə̀ féyì wù yòŋ jii,  "beerì wò."
P10	M lo,  m sù Azizi,  wù ràn,  e bo yèwor yiì a sù mo.
P11	M lo,  m màʼ ndzə̀y se baàr sí e Azizi wùn,  bìʼ fo m yii koŋ nshòm ye baàr.
P12	M du,  lóo neèm ji m buúné,  m neèr Azizi e mǒ ghaŋ.
