page_text:

P1	Audu woke up hungry.
P2	He went into the kitchen to get some food.
P3	He had nothing to eat because the pot was empty.
P4	He was so hungry, he cried and ran to Mama.
P5	He wanted Mama to give him some food.
P6	Mama said, "Audu, you were supposed to fetch some water but you didn't. I couldn't cook any food without water."
P7	Audu fetched some water and Mama cooked a wonderful meal for him.
