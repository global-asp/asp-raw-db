desc:	Tortoise and Hare have work to do. Will they work together?

page_text:

P1	Long time ago, Rabbit, Tortoise and Hare ran in a competition.
P2	Other animals watched the race.
P3	Rabbit was defeated. Although Tortoise won the race, he did not boast.
P4	Tortoise and Rabbit remained friends.
P5	King Lion sent Tortoise and Rabbit to the neighbouring kingdom for work.
P6	Tortoise and Rabbit left to do as King Lion ordered.
P7	King Lion waited for Tortoise and Rabbit to return.
P8	The road they used was full of stones and thorns.
P9	Tortoise and Rabbit walked and walked. 

They rested and talked.
P10	They planned to help each other to reach the other kingdom.
P11	Rabbit carried Tortoise over thorns.
P12	When they reached the river, Tortoise carried Rabbit across.
P13	Tortoise walked very fast. 

Rabbit held on his back.
P14	Tortoise and Rabbit discussed with the other king as their king wanted.
P15	They returned home very fast. 

Tortoise and Rabbit were bigger friends!
