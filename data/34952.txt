title:	Mphaka ndi Galu ajambula ndi kupenta
author:	Ausward  Siwinda and Peter  Msaka
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Chichewa
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Mphaka ndi Galu ndi abwenzi. Amasangalala ndipo amachitira limodzi zinthu zosiyanasiyana.

page_text:

P1	Uyu ndi Mphaka.@@Uyu ndi Galu.
P2	Mphaka ndi Galu akufuna kujambula.@@Mphaka ndi Galu akufuna kupenta.@@Mphaka ndi Galu akufuna kupanga za luso.@@Galu ayamba.@@Watenga pensulo ndi pepala. Pepalali ndi la sikweya.
P3	Poyamba Galu ajambula chithunzi chozungulira ngati dzira.@@Chithunzi chozungulirachi ndi thupi la Mphaka.@@Aaaa, ndizophweka!
P4	Kenako, ajambula mathilayango awiri pamwamba pa chozunguliracho.@@Mathirayango awiriwa ndi makutu a Mphaka ndipo ajambula thirayango ya kuda mkati mwa chithunzi chozungulira chija.@@Thirayango ya kudayi ndi mphuno ya Mphaka ndipo ajambula mzere pamwamba pa mphuno.
P5	Tsopano ajambula maso ndi nsidze za Mphaka.@@Masowa ndi madontho awiri.@@Nsidze ndi mizere iwiri.@@Aaa, ndi zophweka.
P6	Kenako, ajambula kamwa la Mphaka.@@Kamwalo ndi mzere umodzi.@@Ukuwoneka ngati chilembo cha 'W', ndipo kenako ajambula ubweya wa Mphaka.@@Ubweya utatu mbali ya kumanzere, ndipo utatu kamanja.
P7	Chithunzichi  chatsala pang'ono kumalizidwa.@@Kenako, ajambula manja ndi miyendo ya Mphaka. @@Manja ndi miyendo ndi mizere, kenako ajambula zala za Mphaka.@@Zalazi ndi kanthu kozungulira, kenako ajambula mchira wa Mphaka.@@Mchira ndi mzere wawutali.
P8	Pomaliza ajambula mizere yoyera ndi yakuda pa thupi la mphaka. Ndipo apaka utoto wa orenji.@@Chithunzi chamalizidwa.@@Ooo! Chikuoneka bwino kwambiri.
P9	Tsopano ndi nthawi ya Mphaka.@@Iye atenga pensulo ndi pepala.@@Pepala ndi la lekitango.
P10	Poyamba Mphaka ajambula chithunzi chozungulira ngati dzira.@@Chithunzi chozungulirachi ndi thupi la Galu.@@Oooo! Zili bwino!
P11	Kenako ajambula tinthu tiwiri takuda pamwamba pa thupi la galu.@@Tinthu tiwiri takuda ndi makutu a Galu, kenako ajambula kanthu kakuda kozungulira mkati mwa thupi la Galu. @@Kanthu kakuda kozungulira ndi mphuno ya Galu, kenako ajambula mzere pamwamba pa mphuno.
P12	Tsopano ajambula maso ndi nsidze za galu.@@Nsidze ndi mizere iwiri. @@Ooo! Zili bwino!
P13	Kenako ajambula kamwa la Galu.@@Kamwa ndi mzere umodzi.@@Kaonekedwe kake kali ngati chilembo cha 'W', ndipo ajambula madontho kuzungulira kamwa la Galu.@@Atatu kumanzere ndi atatu ena kumanja.
P14	Chithunzi chatsala pang'ono kumalizidwa.@@Ajambula manja ndi miyendo ya Galu.@@Manja ndi miyendo ndi mizere, kenako ajambula zala za Galu.@@Zala ndi tinthu tozungulira, kenako ajambula mchira wa Galu.@@Mchira ukuoneka ngati duwa.
P15	Pomaliza, ajambula tinthu tozungulira takuda pa thupi la Galu ndipo apaka utoto wa chikasu pathupi.@@Chithunzi chatha kujambulidwa.@@Oooo, chikuoneka bwino.
P16	Ndapita Mphaka.@@Ndapita chithunzi cha Mphaka.@@Ndapita Galu.@@Ndapita chithunzi cha Galu.
