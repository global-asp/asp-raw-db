page_text:

P1	ULinda wayeyi ntombazana encinane eyayihlala nabazali bayo ihlakaniphile. Babe hlala ezifama kwa Duka eBerlin.
P2	Umama wakhe wayesoloko emxelela ngokubakuleka kwemfundo. Wayesithi xa ufundile akukho ndawo ungena kuya kuyo. Imfuno imvula yonke iminyango, uyakunqandwa zinkwenkwezi.
P3	ULinda waqalisa  isikolo. Isikolo sasi kude ehamba ngenyawo kwakungekho zithuthi.
P4	ULinda  wafunda wade wakumabanga a phezulu apho abenengqwalasela yokuba amantombazana ayasiyeka isikolo aye kwenda. Yamkhathaza lento
P5	Umama kaLinda wambalisela ukuba   kukho umthetho owamiswayo ukuba amantombazana xa eneminyaka elishumi elinesihlanu  mawa yeke isikolo  aye kwenda.
P6	ULinda wakufunda  edyunivesithi apho  afunde le ezomthetho. Uphumelela wabanesidanga sobugqwetha
P7	Into wayenzayo  wayokuvula  ityala lokuba  kutshintshwe  umthetho  oxhaphaza abantu base tyhini.
P8	ULinda  waliphumelela  ityala  watshintsha  umthetho. ULinda, imfuno yamvulela onke amacango wabubonakalisa ubukrelekrele  nokukhalipha kwakhe.
