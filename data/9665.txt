page_text:

P1	Omusajja ayitibwa Kato yabeeranga ku Kyalo Bunghumu. Yabbanga ebisolo bya bantu.
P2	Olunaku lumu yabba embuzzi ku kyalo ekirirannye wo nagisala nagiteereka mu nyumba ye.
P3	Amakya agaddirira abantu bakizuula nti Kato yali abbye embuzzi. Nebatandiika okumunonnya.
P4	Bwe batuuka mu makage bamusaanga ayokya nnyama.
