desc:	Noonia ebyobukombe byoyenda byona byona byoyenda mu itaka.

page_text:

P1	Abantu kamaala mu kyalo kino baali baghangalire mu bwavu.
P2	Omukungu Kiyimba na bakulembeze abandi baabalabula, "Mu noonie obukombe mu itaka." Aye ti bafaayo era ti bawuliriza bigambo.
P3	Akalenzi akato kebeta Mutaka kaali ku kyalo kuno. Mutaka yavuunianga ebinhonhi kamaala. Yali abiryanga byona byona. Tiyasuulanga mani na byenda.
P4	Lwali lulala Mutaka yagema Eighemba ku muti. Eighemba bwelya bona Mutaka nga aligemye, lya lira,"Mukwano, ndeka era nkukobere kyosanire okutegeera."
P5	Mutaka yakoba,"Nkobera. Ti dhakulya!"
P6	Eighemba lya yogera nga bwericuusa langi yalyo okuva mu kicangacanga oku ira mu Nakasooma. "Leka nje ku muti gule era nnhongere okukobera ebindi kamaala."
P7	Mutaka oyo yaleka e Ighemba lya bulukira ku muti. Bwe lyatuuka eyo, lya sekerera Mutaka. "Obukombe buli munze!" Eighemba lyayeta.
P8	Mutaka yakoba,"Olikobaki?" "Munda munze zaabbu!" Eighemba lyairamu. "Obaire musiru inho okundekula. Osubiibwa obukombe kyodho," Eighemba lya saaliza Mutaka.
P9	Eighemba lyania ensabira edhifanaana oti zaabbu. Lyabbuluka okuja ku muti omuleyi. Mutaka yatandiika okutakula ghansi mu muti.
P10	Mutaka ya kunga abana kyalo,"Mwide tuje, tutandiike okunonia ebyo bukombe mu itaka."
P11	Paka no lwaleero, Mutaka era na bekyalo bakaatakula itaka nga banoonia zaabbu.
P12	Eighemba lisigala ly'embera ku muti,"Obukombe, buli munze!"
