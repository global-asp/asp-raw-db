title:	Maguru ta gandja omagulu
author:	Hamukoto  Efraim
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Oshindonga

desc:	Maguru okwa tokola opo agandjepo omagulu kiinamwenyo ayihe

page_text:

P1	Konalenale iinamwenyo ka ya li yi na omagulu. @@Oyali hayi kokaya.
P2	Aantu oyo ayeke ya li yena omagulu, ngoka kwali yega pewa kuMaguru.
P3	Esiku limwe, Maguru okwa tokola opo agandje omagulu kiinamwenyo ayihe. Okwa li ahala yi ende. No kwe yi lombwele kombinga yasho.
P4	Iinamwenyo oya ti, "Otashi ka kala oshiwanawa ngele tuna omagulu." @@Oyiimbi noku nyanda.
P5	Iinamwenyo oya ti, "Oshali oshidhingu oku kokaya," noyali hayi uvu uuwehame.
P6	Esiku sholya thiki, iinamwenyo oyindji oya yi kegumbo lya Maguru.@@Oonduli, oonkoshi, oondjamba, uulimba, oongandu, osho wo uudhila nawo owayi mo mukweyo.
P7	Oshinamwenyo kehe oshali shapewa omagulu gane. @@Uudhila owali wapewa omagulu gaali.
P8	Opwali eyooloko konima sho yamono omagulu. @@Iinamwenyo yii mwe oya ndanisa kenyanyu, yimwe oyuuvu nai.
P9	Oya yii yekulukile aantu, iinamwenyo oya ti, "Ita tu ku kayawe."
P10	Ngoka ali gwa hugunina momukweyo okwali ongongololo. Maguru okwa pula, "Opuna sha we gumwe konima yoye?" @@Ongongololo oyayamukula. "Ahowe, ongaye gwahugunina."
P11	Maguru okwa dhiladhila, oye gwashuguninwa, oshike ndina okuninga no magulu ngoka gahupapo. @@Maguru okwa gandja omagulu agehe ngoka gahupapo.
P12	Ongongololo oya li ya nyanyukwa nookondo, no yati, "Otandi endelele unene ndivulithe yakwetu."
P13	Konima sho ongongololo yayi, eyoka olye ya kegumbo lya Maguru. No kwa ti kuMaguru, "Kwathelandje upendje omagulu."
P14	Maguru okwati kuye, "Onda gandjapo omagulu agehe. Owalipeni?''@@Eyoka olya yamukula, "Onda kothelela."
P15	Maguru okwa talatalangele opuna omagulu gakalapo. @@Ina monaposha.
P16	Maguru okwati keyoka, ombili. Inapu hupa omagulu, eyoka olyayi kegumbo talipwedha. Oku za tuu esiku ndjoka, eyoka ihali kotha we unene. Olya tega olyo lyininge lyotango momukweyo opo lipewe omagulu.
