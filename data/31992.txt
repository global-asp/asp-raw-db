title:	Ɓinngel Ɓurdungel Yiɗeego
author:	Abdulaahi Aadamu Bappa and Dayyibu  Abdullahi
artist:	Brian Wambi

desc:	Payanngel

page_text:

P1	O'o woni Deelu. @O woodi sakiraaɓe worɓe joweego'o.
P2	Danyooɓe maako kokkiimo payanngel leydi. @E mo yiɗingel.
P3	Nyalaande feere sakiraaɓe maako pusingel.
P4	Deelu yiʼaayingel. @E mo woya semmbee.
P5	Deelu doggi ƴeengi lekki mawki suuɗii.
P6	Danyooɓe maako kebtimo mbiʼimo, "Jippa!"
P7	Sakiraaɓe maako ngimanimo mbi'imo, "Jippale!"
P8	Deelu wartiri gimol maɓɓe jalniiɗum tan.
P9	Sey soobaajo Deelu waroyi yimanimo, "Waɗu munyal, jippale!"
P10	Soobaajo on ɓeydi yimugo haa Deelu jippoyii lekki.
P11	Deelu bee soobaajo mum ɓe kootidi.
P12	Koo moye e seyorii wartugo Deelu. Deelu heɓi payanngel kesel.
