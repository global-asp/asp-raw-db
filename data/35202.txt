desc:	Every day is a new beginning for Fati

page_text:

P1	Fati omotete r’ ọmọtẹ@  rọ jugbonẹ ki roju.O ph’ẹguọnọ@  kpai ewhẹ mamọ,obo ravuẹrẹ@  ọye oruẹ.
P2	Ẹdẹvo vwo te, ode@  nene ọse rọye kp’ẹgwha dara gbẹbe, idjighere@  ọnye ọvẹ ọsẹ rọye roro
P3	Ọke rẹ owhian vwọben Fati nu, ọda nẹ ọsẹ@  rọye nẹ, mise rovwoma taye vwẹ otọ@  r'urhe r' imagoro na? Ọsọ rọye@  da vuẹ nẹ ee, ẹkẹvuọvo,@  biko, who yer' enu rurhe vuọvo@  nonẹẹ.
P4	Fati de kidia otọ@  rẹ urhe r’ imagoro na, ode nenu, ọ damrẹ imagoro ọvo, ive, erha re vwareku
P5	Fati de nẹ ọsẹ rọye. Owhiowhian@  yanra, onasa rẹ Fati evha@  aa. Ememerha, Fati ko nyere enu rẹ urhe@  r’ imagoro na. Ọda tanẹ, mi ke kidia@  etinẹ.Ọda tane, etinẹna nyovwiri kẹ oma erovwo@  ve imagoro erarọ. Ko kidia, kọ rar’ imagoro na.
P6	Ọvwọ rare nu, Fati de@  wontọ rhe. Ọ da rhoma mre urhe ọfa.@  Urhe nana o vwẹ eravhọ obiebi evo re jẹ noma@  rọye cha.
P7	Enyọ ọye evu r’ urhe na ! Fati vwẹguọnọ@  k’ ame r’enyọ mamọ !@  Ẹkẹvuovo, o’ vwẹguọnọ k’ enyọ ọọ. Fati de nenu, ọda mre ọnyọ@  ọvo.
P8	Fati ko rorori nẹ, ọnyọ vuọvo@  ọyevuretiyi. Ọda tanẹ, oke ruvwe@  emuovọ oo mide nyere enu@  r’ urhe na ra guọnọ enyọ. 
P9	Fati de n ‘ọsẹ rọye,@  O nẹ asa ri Fati evwa@  aa, Fati de nyer’enu r’urhe na, ọda whiẹ@  ame r’ enyọ n’ unu r’ urhe na.
P10	Fati da tanẹ, mi@  ke kidia etinẹ, etinẹna nyovwiri kẹ oma@  erovwo ve ame r’enyọ rarọ
P11	Fati vwọ rarẹ ame r’eyọn na@  nu, o de wotọ rhe.O da mre ne eyọn@  na vwa buebu mamọ! BZZZ! BZZZ! BZZZ!@   Fati da jek’eyon@  na
P12	Fati kọ jẹ brẹ ọsẹ rọ@  ye ra. Kọ wẹn vuevue. Ọsẹ@  rọye da nọ nẹ tivọ@  wo nẹcha? Fati gbe kpavwhe kẹẹ.
P13	Kọ tare nẹ, nobonẹ@  ọsẹ mẹ, ni gh’ imagoro,@  whọ rare? Ọsẹ r’ọye da se, ọda tanẹ, Fati,ukpo tere
P14	Fati kidia obuko@  r’ Ọsẹ r’ọye evu r’ idjighere. Osio kọrhọ ọke@  ri Fati v’ọsẹ rọye vwo@  kpore
P15	Ọsẹ r’ Fati jẹ idjighere na ga nọ. Idjighere@  na da jẹ@  ruẹ ẹghwa. Kproghogho! 
P16	Idjighere na de she tio@  owhọn r’ Fati.
P17	 
P18	Ọke raye vwo tuwevwi, oni v’ oni rode r’ Fati da naye ekpẹ@  r’ ovwian obẹ ẹghwa.Ọsẹ r’ Fati vuẹaye nẹ Fati@  yere enu r’ irhe, Fati vuaye@  ne ọsẹ rọye jẹ idjighere ga nọ
P19	Oni vẹ oni rode  r Fati de yere. Aye da vwerote owhon r’ Fati.
