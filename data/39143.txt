EGUSI SOUP well explain For beginner

page_text:

P1	Dis recipe go show you how to prepare Nigerian Egusi soup wit melon seeds@Ingredient's@Graded melon @Palm oil@  Cow meat ,fish ,stock fish,maggi ,pepper, water ,achi ,salt and vegetables @Method@Poor 2 litters of water in side pot add meat
P2	Add fish, stock fish, place on fire to boil add Red oil allow to boil
P3	And egusi allow to boil well
P4	Then add meggi, Salt
P5	Add achi to ticking it
P6	Then add your vegetable wait to boil before bringing it down from fire
P7	Allow for some seconds
P8	Enjoy with your EBA or garri
