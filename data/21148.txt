desc:	Byantaka alikuwa na chungu kidogo. Alihitaji kikubwa. Alitumia akili yake kukipata.

page_text:

P1	Byantaka alikuwa na ng’ombe na chungu kidogo cha maji.
P2	Alienda kwa jirani yake kuomba chungu kikubwa.
P3	Byantaka aliweka chungu kidogo ndani ya kile kikubwa cha jirani yake.
P4	Alivibeba vyungu vyote akavipeleka kwa jirani yake.
P5	Byantaka alimwambia jirani, “Chungu chako kilizaa.”
P6	Byantaka alirudi kwa jirani kuomba chungu tena.
P7	Hakukirejesha. Jirani aliamua kwenda kumwuliza.
P8	Byantaka alisema, “Kilikufa. Nilikuwa najitayarisha kuja kukwambia.”
P9	Jirani alipiga kelele, “Sijawahi kusikia chungu kikikufa!”
P10	Byantaka alijibu, “Rafiki yangu, kila kitu kinachozaa lazima kife.”
P11	Jirani alienda kortini kumshtaki Byantaka.
P12	Jaji alisema, “Kila kiumbe hai kinachozaa, lazima kife.”
P13	Byantaka alipata chungu kikubwa naye jirani yake akakipoteza.
