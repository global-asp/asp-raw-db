P1	JABULANE ABENGUMFANA LOYITSANDZA KAKHULU IMFUYO YAKUBO.
P2	JABULANE ABEVUKA EKUSENI APHE IMFUYO YAKUBO KUDLA,  IMFUYO YAKHABO JABULANE IDLA TJANI, EMACEMBE BESE INATSISWA EMANTI.
P3	IMFUYO YAKHABO JABULANE ILUSITO KAKHULU EKHABO JABULANE NGOBA BALIMA NGAYO BAPHINDZE BAKHELELE NGAYO EMANTI.
P4	EKHABO JABULANE KUNENKUNZI LETEYISA TONKHE TINKUNZI ENDZAWENI YINGAKO JABULANE AYITSANDZA KAKHULU.
P5	NGALELINYE LILANGA JABULANE WABUYA ESKOLENI WATFOLA IMFUYO YAKUBO INYAMALELE INTJONJTJWE MAZIMUZIMU, JABULANE WAHLULEKA NGISHO KUDLA.
P6	JABULANE WAVUKA EKUSENI NGAKUSASA NABABE WAKHE BAHAMBA BAYOFUNA IMFUYO BAFIKE BAYITFOLA EHLATSINI.
P7	JABULANE WAJABULA KAKHULU NABATFOLA IMFUYO YAKUBO BASE BAYAYICHUBA BABUYELA NAYO EMUVA.
P8	BABE WAJABULANE NAJABULANE BAHLABE INKHOMO LENKHULU NGOBA KUTA KHISIMUSI.
P9	IMBONGOLO YAKHABO JABULANE BEYITIYELA YONA KUYONATSA EMANTI BEYINGADZINGI JABULANE KUTSI AZE AYIYISE EMFULENI.
P10	IMFUYO YAKHABO JABULANE YAYIVANA YONKHE,  JAMLUDI ABEKUTSANDZA KULIMA NGOBA ABEZE AHLEKELELE.
