desc:	Hare is playing tricks again! Read the story and find out.

page_text:

P1	Hare met Elephant carrying a pot of honey.
P2	Elephant carried Hare on his back. Hare ate Elephant’s honey.
P3	They found a big buffalo drinking water in a river.
P4	Hare wanted to know who was stronger, Buffalo or Elephant.
P5	Hare said he had an idea how to find out.
P6	Hare tied one end of a rope around Elephant’s waist.
P7	Hare tied the other end around Buffalo’s waist.
P8	Hare hid in a tree and shouted, “Pull!”
P9	Buffalo and Elephant pulled. Elephant pulled Buffalo.
P10	Buffalo pulled Elephant. Neither could win.
P11	Hare cut the rope, closer to Buffalo.
Elephant fell over!
P12	Hare praised Buffalo saying, “You are stronger.”
