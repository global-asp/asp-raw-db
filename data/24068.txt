page_text:

P1	Chat akazarwa alyempumi.Akaaba atakarebagaho abazeire be ninga beine nyina. Akaaba abanza kubakwata aha meisho kubamanya.
P2	omurundi gumwe, Chat akabanatambura omu musiri.
Akakunda akahoho ke'bimuri.
Akaba nakunda ku kwata ebirabyo.
P3	Chat akabanayegonba kureeba ebimuri."biboneire" akagamba
P4	Burizooba, Chat akaba aza omumusiri. kandi akaba namanya omuhanda.Erizooba  yareeba omu bikyu.
P5	Yahura ekunba nomurabyo,"nyine kwiruka omunju, Chat akatekateka".
P6	Enjura akatandinka kugwa. Chat yateera,yagwa.akatera omutwe gwe ahibare.
P7	Chat kuyimukyire,yashanga abantu bamwetorire."habahokyi?"Chat akabuza.
P8	"ogwire ahansi wateraho omutwe gwawe,"taata we yamugarukamu. "Katusiime mukama ahabwokuba origye,"Maama we yagarukamu.
P9	"Taata na maama?" Chat aketa.Aketa namaziina gabaine nyina."mudentere ekyimuri".
P10	Murumuna we akagaruka na ne'ekyimuri.Chat yakyikwata."kyiboneire,"
P11	Abowabo bakarebana."Chat.noreeba eyimuri?"maama we akabuza.
P12	Chat akazana nekyimuri akyikwise omungaro ze."Mwena muboneire nkekyi ekimuri" ya mwenya.
