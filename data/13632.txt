P1	One day,  Death visited a man called Hupapa,  whoÂ lived in the village of Mugulu.
P2	Hupapa was scared to see the visitor.
P3	Death told Hupapa that his turn to see God had come. Hupapa told Death that he was not ready, but Death insisted claiming it was Hupapa's turn to see God.
P4	Hupapa served Death with a delicious meal.
P5	After the meal, Death fell asleep and Hupapa picked death list and wrote his name at the bottom.
P6	When Death woke up, he was happy with Hupapa's hospitality and opted to begin with the names at the bottom.
P7	Unfortunately, Hupapa had his name at the bottom.
