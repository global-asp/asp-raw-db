page_text:

P1	Eira  einho, ghaaligho  omusaadha  ng'ayetebwa  Kato.Yali mu kibuga  kabuusu.Yali n'embwaye mu  kayumba  akatono.
P2	Lwali lunaku lulala,  Kato  y'aba  mulwaire  inho. Tiyali na muntu yenayena ku muyamba. Bwe  yaisuuka, yasalagho okubaiza  omukazi  eyali  okumpi  agho.
P3	Yayeta  bakagwa be  n'abekika  kye, okubaagho ku  mukolo ogw'okubaiza. Kato  yali  musaamaavu  inho kubanga  yadhuuba  nti  alikuja  kufuna  omuyambi.
P4	Ebintu bingi  by'aleterwa  omulya ni  mukagwa  we. Bino  byali biibo  bya  bulo, emikeeka, amatooke, amaido, n'ebirabo  ebindi eby'amaka.
P5	Omukolo  ogw'okubaiza  olwawa, abageni  bonabona bairayo  mu  maka  gaabwe.Kato  yali  mwetegefu  okutandika  n'omulya  n'embwa  ye.
P6	Olunaku olwairirira, Kato  yagabula  omulya we  amenvu, aye  yaloba  okugalya.
P7	Kato  bwe  yaja okuiiga, omulya  yalya  amenvu  gonagona.
P8	Kato  y'aira okuva mu  kuiiga  ng'endhala  eri kumuluma. Y'asaba  amenvu. Mukenkedewe  yakoba ati  embwa  egaaliire  gonagona!
P9	Olunaku  olwairaku, Kato  yaja ku  nimiro  ye.Bwe  yaira  eka, yayagaana  nga omulyawe  aliire  emamba yonayona. Tiyaghaaku waire  na mbwa.
P10	Olunaku  olundi,Kato  yaja  yagenighalaku  ye  mukagwawe  bwe  yaira, omulyawe  ng'aliire  amaido  gonagona. Ekiibo  kyali  ky'erere. Kato  yanakughalira   omulyawe  omuyaka.
P11	Kato yadhhuba,"Ghaligho ekikyamu ku mukazi  ono."Y'asalagho okukozesa eirogo. Yata  amata mu kasugha akadaagedaage kaaku, yakata mu kunkuliisi  yaja  okuiiga.
P12	Omulya  yabona  akasugha,nga kaali  kaidhuvu  k'amata. Amangu  ago, y'asitula  akasugha  yakata  ku munwa. Yanwa  amata  gonagona  ng'embwa  emulingiriire.
P13	Eky'omukisa  omubi, akasugha  kaalemera  ku  munwa gwe. Yagezaaku  okuketoolaku  aye nga busa. Yalekaana, yatumira  ghaigulu  ni  ghansi. Aye  akasugha  kaasigala  nga  kegemeireku. Embwa yalingirira  bino  byonabyona.
P14	Agho embwa  y'alumuka  okunoonia  Kato.  Embwa yasama  yasama, yatuumira  ghaigulu  ni  ghansi.Kato yakizuula nga  ghaligho  ekikyamu  eka.
P15	Baalumuka  bairayo  eka. Kato  yasamaalirira  inho okagaana  omulyawe  ng'akasugha  kamweripiire  ku  munwa. Yamulungirira  yewunia.
P16	Kato  y'agema  kw'itama  lya  mukagwa we  akasugha kaagwa  ghansi  amangu  ago. Omulya  yaswaala  inho. Y'asalagho  kwirayo  ka  y'abazaire  be.
