desc:	A miracle of Jesus from Matthew 14 in which he solves a big problem using something very small.

page_text:

P1	Jesus' disciples said to him, "Send the crowds away so they can buy food."
P2	"You give them something to eat," Jesus said.
P3	They said, "We have only five loaves and two fish."
P4	"Bring them here to me," Jesus replied.
P5	Jesus gave thanks and the disciples gave the food to the crowds.
P6	Everyone ate and was satisfied. Those who ate were more than five thousand.
