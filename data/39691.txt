desc:	This story is about a woman and a fairy.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a grand mother whose name is Rose. one day Rose she was she was inside in his house.
P2	Suddenly she  saw a fairy and she surprised to see the fairy and she said what you want?
P3	The fairy said I want to give you a new life.
P4	A grand mother said what kind of life you want to give me? Grand mother asked. Fairy said I want to give you a good place to started you new life. fairy said.
P5	The fairy stretched her arm and she said god give this woman a new life. And suddenly a woman disapear and she is in a good place.
P6	The woman said I want to go back home. The woman said.
P7	The fairy said I will get you back home.
P8	And the fairy said god get this woman back home now..
P9	And the woman she is in her house..
P10	Don't  forget about this story..
