title:	Okughabulwa okucaamu

desc:	Abasaadha babairi baghabulwa mu bucaamu omulamuzi. Banoonia okumanha okuva eri abakulu ku kyalo era oluvainhuma baba basanhufu.

page_text:

P1	Baakola buli kintu kulwaibwe.
P2	Baaduuba okuba n'endogoyi.
Yandibeese ebintu byaibwe.
P3	Kombe yakoba, "Tugule endogoyi."
Basanhufu.
P4	Bbaabba gha kombe bweyafa, Kombe yafuuka mukombe.
Yerabira Idhuma
P5	Kombe yayenda emamba y'endogoyi okugha embwadhe.
Idhuma yayenda Ndogoyi.
P6	Omulamuzi yaakoba, "Buli mulala kwimwe atwaleku olwawu lwayo."
P7	Kombe yaita endogoyi.
Idhuma yeetwika ebintubye kabiri.
P8	Kombe yayenda kwoca nhumbaye azimbe eyindi.
P9	Idhuma yakoba, "Eyange yoona eidha kuya."
Omulamuzi yaikirz ekyaa ni kombe.
P10	Enhumba ya Idhuma yaya.
Omulamuzi yaakoba, "Kombe yayoca nhumbaye yonka."
P11	Idhuma yabulwa enhumba, ghaire ndogoyi. Yaasula ghansi gh'omuti.
P12	Idhuma yaakola inho. Yali ni empande enungi.
P13	Batabane ba kombe baalya empande dha Idhuma.
P14	Abalenzi baakoba, "Koba bbaabba ghaife akusasule."
P15	Idhuma yaakoba, Tyenda sentedho. Nnhenda mpande dhange."
P16	Omulamuzi yaakoba Idhuma, "Salamu batabanebo. Otwale empandedho."
P17	Idhuma yaaloba. Kombe yaalira. "Abakaire basalewo."
P18	Abakaire baakoba, "Waita endogoyi. Wayoca enumbaye."
P19	Baakoba Idhuma, "Okwita mutabane gha kombe kyona kibi."
P20	Kombe yaagha Idhuma ekitundu eky'ebintubye.
Baasigala basanhufu.
