desc:	Some animals have sharp teeth for biting. Other animals have flat teeth for grinding. Some animals don’t have any teeth. What about you?

page_text:

P1	A crocodile has sharp teeth.
P2	A lion also has sharp teeth.
P3	A shark has very sharp teeth!
P4	A cow has strong teeth for grinding food.
P5	A bird does not have teeth. How does it eat?
P6	Rats have long front teeth.
P7	I used to have front teeth.
P8	My front teeth fell out!
