P1	Ceng moko obedu kudie ceng, wabe do ka chamu kwen. Wayira owacu nia obedu ceng moko magicidhui soku. Ecidu ka wilo rec. Ebielo nyathin I ngeye, ebeneno rec. Karaba nyathin ma ebielo utingo rec moko u turo ie uwok aria. Jalwor rec karabe ubeneno kite ma nyathin ube tura rec eca, egam e wacu mi wayira uting rec ne ire,  wayira owacu egam eca ko woth mi dok in pacu. 
P2	E wok idhukulu enwago kude rec ma upodhu kud i mutukari pa jukuloka,  etingu rec ecade edok kude ipacu,  Etedu rec ne ma e be came,  ewacu enirombu pareodhu.
