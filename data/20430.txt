title:	Hojii Isaa Kamtuu Barbaachisadha?
author:	Mulualem  Daba
artist:	Gill Bond and Neo
lang:	Afaan Oromo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20430.png
desc:	Hojiin tokko isaa kan biraa ni caala?

page_text:

P1	Namootni baay'ee wal falmu.
P2	Barsiisootni, "Barsiisumman baay'ee barbaachisadha. Barsiisuf barbaadamna," jedhu.
P3	Warri mana ijaaranis, "Ogumman mana ijaaru baay'ee barbaachisadha. Mana hojjechuf barbaadmna," jedhu.
P4	Warri meesha mana hojjatanis, "Ogumman meesha mana hojjechu baay'ee barabaachisadha," jedhu.
P5	Doctoronnis, "Doctorumman baay'ee barbaachisadha. Fayyaa keessan eeguf barbaadamna," jedhu.
P6	Qotee bultoonnis, "Qotee bulaan barbaachisadha. Midhaan nuyutu oomisha," jedhu.
P7	Barattonis, "Barachuun baay'ee barbaachisadha!" jedhan.
P8	"Sabaabni isaas hundii keessanu duraa barattota turtan." Amma Namonni hundinu waali galaan.
