page_text:

P1	Hakaba haliho omusigazi muto bakumweta Kumbana.
P2	Kuyahikire emyaka abilinetano, yajira rukundo nomwikyi wubakweta Zangane.
P3	Basigara hamwe obwire bwemyaka eshatu, konka akaba ataine sente zikumala kumushwera.
P4	Eizooba limwe yashanga aine enda, ahabwokuba tibalayekumile.
P5	Akelere kazalwa, bakeeta Kheri.
P6	Konka Kumbana akaba nakishoma, Zangane akaba aine omulimo hale ye tawuni, bwenu batakutula hamwe.
P7	Bwanyima yobwile,Zangane yafuna omukundwa omusya. Yija kutula nawe hamwe nomwana.
P8	Kumbana yacweka omutima. Akaara ahabwokuba bakamwihaho omwaana we.
P9	Obwile bwalabaho, na Kumbana yafuna omukundwa. Batula hamwe, konka bakaba batine baana bona.
P10	Hati Kheri akaba atakukunda ishe we omusya. Yajira enaku, kandi alikumuteera.
P11	Kuyahikize emyaka ikuminebiri,nyina yasharaho kumusindika owa ishe wenyini.
P12	Kumbana na Kheri bagaruka bashemererwa.
