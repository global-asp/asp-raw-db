page_text:

P1	Lihle loves to spend time in her day reading and her favourite book is ‘Jamela’s Box’
P2	Lihle loves to spend time in her day learning her a b c’s so she can learn to write her name!
P3	Lihle loves to spend time in her day playing with her sister Nikhanye and they skip and laugh together!
P4	Lihle loves to eat some fruit after a long day of playing and reading. Lihle has a big smile on her face because she had a great day!
