desc:	Isobho likagogo Mampinga owayehlala yedwa emzini wakhe.

page_text:

P1	Wake wezwa ngendawo ebizwa ngokuthi iNkangala?
P2	Unkosikazi Dubula kanye nomyeni wakhe bahlala eNkangala. Banabantwana abahlanu.
P3	Abafana ababili nguSobuka kanye noNjalo. Kanti amantombazane  nguNtando, uChika kanye noSabu.
P4	Ugogo Mampinga umama omdala osekhulile, ohlala @endaweni yaseNkangala.
P5	Umndeni wakwaDubula uyaye uvakashele ugogo uMampinga ukuze amsize.@ Bayamuthanda kakhulu.
P6	Ugogo uMampinga ubaphekela isobho elimnandi, elakhiwe ngobhontshisi kanye nezitshalo.
P7	Njalo uma befika kagogo, abafana ababili bayahamba bayomkhelela amanzi.
P8	uma sebeqedile, bonke babeye bahlale phansi kwesihlahla bathokozele isobho likaGogo uMampinga.
