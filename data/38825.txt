title:	My Egg

desc:	This story is about a hen looking for the safest place to lay her eggs. The story words include repeated questions

page_text:

P1	Where can I lay my egg? Can I lay it in a tree?@@Cluck! Cluck! Cluck! It will fall and break!
P2	Where can I lay my egg? Can I lay it on the roof?@@Cluck! Cluck! Cluck! It will fall and break!
P3	Where can I lay my egg? Can I lay it in the garden?@@Cluck! Cluck! Cluck!  It will get wet when they water the plants!
P4	Where can I lay my egg? Can I lay it in the pool?@@Cluck! Cluck! Cluck! It will rot!
P5	Where can I lay my egg? Can I lay it in the yard?@@Cluck! Cluck! Cluck! They will take it and eat it!
P6	I have been looking for a place to lay my eggs. I am tired.@@I see a nest there. I will lay all my eggs in it!
P7	This place is safe.@@I will nurse the eggs right here until they  hatch!
P8	Now, I have six beautiful chicks.@@I am very happy.@@I count them every day!
