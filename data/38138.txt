title:	The jungle challenge.
author:	Victoria Tanaka
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38138.png
desc:	This story is about a a king who was very old and decided to make a challenge to see who will be king. The impala wanted to join the challenge but the animals made fun of him. Will impala be able to win the big challenge?

page_text:

P1	Once there was a leopard king who was very old and he called everyone in the jungle for competition to see who will be the king.
P2	He told everyone he has to retire. Everyone was sad because they had to have another king. So the challenge was about to start.
P3	The first challenge was to see who can throw the bow very high.He also told them to get started. The impala also wanted to join the challenge  but the animals made fun of him.
P4	He told them that the impala  must also  join the challenge. The Elephant started throwing the bow but it was not very far.
P5	The elephant wasn't happy at all. The next animal was warthog he threw the bow but it was not very far again. So warthog was not happy. The king was very sad because no one was winning.
P6	The next animal was Gorilla he threw the bow as far as possible but it was not very far enough again. All the animals wanted to give  up but impala came in front and decided to do the challenge.
P7	The king agreed and impala threw the bow very far and all the animals were surprised. Impala was very happy.
P8	Now the king said impala is the new king of the jungle. All the animals were very angry.
P9	The old king said to the other animals you should not judge others by their look. The animals were sorry and said they will never do it again.  THE END.
