page_text:

P1	There is a girl called Maryam. Her parents are poor.
P2	She lives with her parents and siblings.
P3	She is ten years old. She admires children going to school.
P4	Her parents do not send her to school. They want her to marry.
P5	Maryam is determined to sell in order to get money for her fees.
P6	One day, she met a young man. He promised to buy all the Banana.
P7	Maryam happily followed. There he wanted to rape her.
P8	Maryam shouted and screamed. Neighbours came and rescued her.
P9	The neighbours contributed money for her school. The rapist was jailed.
P10	She successful graduated. She became the first architect in her State.
