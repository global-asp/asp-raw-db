page_text:

P1	Naira atwala embiidhiye ya givuga ku igaalilye.
P2	Yeekontola kwibaale mu ngira. Embiidhi egwa ghansi. Erira. Embiidhi yaakoba,"ooooeeeeee,ooooeeeee!"
P3	Kyotya ajagaana. Yakoba,"eeeee!, eno embiidhi yaani?"
P4	Kyotya agibinga, paka bweyagigema.
P5	Yagema embiidhi eno yaagitwala ewa isentebe ogh'aigombolola. Isentebe ogh'aigombolola amukoba agirabirire.
P6	Naira abitagho ku kagira. Awulira nga embiidhi erira,"mmm,mmm,mmmm!"
P7	Naira aingirayo. Musanhufu okubona embiidhiye.
P8	Naira akoba embiidhiye,"Idha,tuje eka. Tituja kuvuga igaala kayiri."
