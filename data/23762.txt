title:	Umviriza umubirii wanjye
author:	Davis  Rukundo
artist:	Angie and  Upesh

desc:	Ese wari wabasha kumva icyo umubiri wawe ukubwira.

page_text:

P1	Uyu munsi ntabwo bujye ku ishuru. ni ikonje.
P2	Uyu munsi ntabwo ndi burebe TV. Nta muriro uhari.
P3	Icya mbere, ngomba gutuza. Mu gihe ndi kumva umubiru wanjye.
P4	Yego, ubu ndi kumva uguhumeka kwanjye. Ndahumeka imbere n'inyuma, imbere n'inyuma.
P5	Yego, ubu ndi kumva uguhumeka kwanjye. Ndahumeka imbere n'inyuma, imbere n'inyuma.
P6	Kandi nshobora kongeza urusaku rw'uguhumeka kwanjye. Ssssssssss! n' uburyohe. Mmmmmmm
P7	Ubu, nshobora kumva gukubita k'umutima wanjye! Doodom, doodoom, dooo doom.
P8	Ese nshobora kongeza umuvuduko w'umutima wanjye cyangwa kuwukomeza? Yego, ndakoresha imbaraga incuro makumyabiru.
P9	Ubu, umutima wanjye urabubita vuba.
P10	Ninshyira intoki zanjye ku bujana bwanjye, ndibubashe kumva umutsi wanjye.
P11	Ndabasha kumva uguseka kwanjye. Haha, haha, haaha, haaa!
P12	Ndabasha kumva uburira kwanjye. Boohoooo hooo!
P13	Ndabasha kwiyumva nkoma amashyi. Kaci Kaci
P14	Ndabasha kumva igifu cyanjye! Guddu, guddu, guddu.
P15	Igifu cyanjye kirumo kunsaba kukigaburira.
P16	Amazuru yangjye ari guhumurirwa na keki mama ari guteka mu cyikoni. Ndashaka kumva akananura kanjye kari kuzihekenya.
