desc:	Sally loves to play with sand. This story tells what happens to her on one of her sand playing escapades.
Its prose in poetic form to help build reading for beginning readers.

page_text:

P1	Sally is a girl. @Sally likes to play.@She plays on sand.@Sand sand sand. @She throws sand in the air.@Sand gets in her hair
P2	Is it not silly.@@Sally throws sand in the air and gets some in her hair.@@She gets some in her eyes too.@Sand is in her ears too.@@She now sneezes "atchoo!"@Sand is in her nose.
P3	Sally is now crying.@She threw sand in the air.@Sand is in her hair.
P4	Sand is in her eyes.@@Sand is in her ears.@@Sand is in her nose too.@@She sneezes atchoo!
