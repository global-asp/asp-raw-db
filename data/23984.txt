title:	ebirikuriga omunjara

desc:	Enjaara nebasa kureta okwiba na mazaara ekyintu ekyitagambwa fulve.

page_text:

P1	Ira niira, ahakaba haruho enjara omukicweeka kya Fommbina. Abantu bagyemisibwa kuhinga ebikungu kuronda ebicori
P2	hakaba hariho omusheija atari kubasa kugyemura eka'ye. Izooba rimwe akagambira omukazi we "reeka nze kuronda ebyokurya" yagyenda!
P3	kuyabire atariho, omukazi we atakibasa kurinda. Yateeka ekyozi kyiyihire owa nyiina kuha abaana.
P4	Eiba akagarika kyikyerezi ataine ekintu kyoona kye'ka ye. omukazi we yamwakira yamubuza ati "hariho ebyokurya byabana?" yamugarikamu ati "ngaha" omukazi yamuhereza ahakyozi konka tikyamwigutsya.
P5	omusheija yabuza, "ebyozi wabihahi? omukazi we yamugarikamu mawe nabibika ahakibati kyekyishengye kye.
P6	shaha mukaga zekyiro, iba yaimuka. yasharamu kuza owa nyinazara kwiba ebyozi. yatunga idaala.
P7	yahanika idaala, yatemba ibaati ryo'mukaikuru.
P8	omusheija yatandika kutambura ahaibati kuza kweihaho ebyoozi.Ahonaho yarengyera omu ibaati norwari rwingyi.Pararam!
P9	yeimuka ho juba "salaamu alaykum,mukaikuru turyabatu bomwiguru. ndyomwe ahabashatu abandi baryonibija. omusheija yagamba.
P10	omukaikuru yagira obwoba bwingyi munonga.Yatina!
P11	yairuka kushohora orwigyi konka omusheija yamutanga kushohora.
P12	bombi beiruka nibaronda obuyambi, omusheija yairuka naza oburugwa izooba n'omukaikuru yeiruka naza oburengyerwa izooba
