page_text:

P1	Luka 18:1-8@@Jezu adauza mfananizo ateweri wace kuti ambapembe nthawe zentse, ndipo aleke kuneta:@@Mumzinda winango mukhana mtongi m'bodzi. Iye akhagopa lini Mulungu. Ndipo akhalibe basa na wanthu.
P2	Mumzindamo mukhana mantsiwa omwe akhabwera ntsiku zentse acimbadzamukumbira kuti: "Ndiyeruzenimbo pakudzatongiwa mulandu wangu ine-pano na nyamadulanthaka wangu."
P3	Ndipo mtongi ule akhalamba kumutawira kamangu.
P4	Tsono iye ntsiku inango adadzakumbuka yekha kuti: "Mkaziyi ali kundinesa kwene-kwene, lekani ndimuyeruze mulandu waceyu kuti aleke kubwera-bwera pomwe kudzandinesa."
P5	Jezu adalewa kuti: "Bvesesani bwino bzomwe walewa mtongi wakuipa mtimayu. Kodi Mulungu angadzalekembo kucita cirungamo kuna wanthu wace omwe ambamupemba usiku na masikati? Kodi iye angadzacedwe kuwathandiza?"
P6	"Ndiri kukuuzani kuti an'dzawayeruza na kam'kulumize. Tsono, kodi Mwana wa Munthu akadzabwera, an'dzagumana wanthu ana cikhulupiriro pantsi pano?"
