desc:	Luno lugero lwa mulenzi ali kwetaaya mu maka agage niba wansolo!

page_text:

P1	Ekimenke kyange ni Nze tuseka no obunhonhi bwange ghaigulu mu bire.
P2	Ebinhonhi byange ni Nze twesuuba ni Wansude mu muti.
P3	Wansude wange ni Nze tuzaanha ni Wantwiga wange mu nsozi.
P4	Wantwiga wange ni Nze tuwumba obupiira bwa Wankembo okuva mu itoosi.
P5	Wankembo wange ni Nze tulya amenvu ni Wairike buliidho.
P6	Wairike wange ni Wankembo batya Mwami Wankudu mu ngeri yonayoona.
P7	Mwami Wankudu ni Nze twilayo eka kasoobo ni Mukyala Namusota.
P8	Mukyala Namusota ni Nze twenda okusiisa no okuyemba enhembo ku mwaalo.
P9	Amaka gange gonagoona ninze tuba ghalala bulaala!
