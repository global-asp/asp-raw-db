title:	Akidodok Nabworok
author:	Lolupe teachers
artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/13522.png
desc:	Erono akirok ngakiro napenyepedorosi.

page_text:

P1	Arai akidodok ka ayole ng'ipaalon.
P2	Amalimali eleu lo ayole.
P3	Abworok eleu lo akidodok.
P4	Abu ayole tama napaekeng kitukulau ng'akipi.
P5	Kipung'ar akidodok nawi akisub ng'una.
P6	Dio akidodok kitukulau ng'akipi.
P7	Tachakakin ng'esi nakipi na akulate.
P8	Tonoma ng'akipi eleu keng kilebeleb.
P9	Abu akidodok tobworokok noi!
