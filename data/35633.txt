page_text:

P1	There lived a newly wedded girl called Aisha.
P2	After the wedding, her father allowed her younger brother to go with her.
P3	A week later, Aisha asked Usman to serve food from the pot.
P4	Usman brought the food with two pieces of meat.
P5	Aisha asked, "did l ask for meat?'
P6	She started beating and abusing Usman.
P7	The next morning, their father came and saw how badly wounded Usman was.
P8	"Why did you do this?" He asked father. "I will never allow you to live with anyone again," he continued.
