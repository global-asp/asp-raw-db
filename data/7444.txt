page_text:

P1	Thandi woke up happy and she wanted to play before going to school.
P2	Grandma says to Thandi "you will be late for your bus Thandi" Thandi replies "I will go now grandma" Thandi leaves 5 minutes later.
P3	Thandi goes to the bus stop too late. The bus has left.
P4	The old man asked "why are you crying child" Thandi replies "I missed my school bus" The old man says "I will walk you to school."
P5	The old man askes Thandi "Is that your school." Thandi replies "Yes it is. Thank you" The old man leaves her at school.
P6	Thandi tells her teacher and friend's about she missed her bus and the nice,  old man who helped her.
P7	THE END.
