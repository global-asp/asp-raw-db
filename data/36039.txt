desc:	Le ndaba isinikeza umqondo ngokusebenzisa amaqhezu njengoba ineresiphi yokwenza ujusi kalamula.

Ukubhalwa kwayo kwakhuthazwa yindatshana ebhalwe ebhukwini elithiLets make lime juice

page_text:

P1	Nanku ujeke kajusi kalamula.@@Iyabanda futhi inoshukela.@@Siyisishiyagalombili lapha. @@Singawuhlukanisa kanjani ujusi ukuze sonke sithole ngokulingana?
P2	Singakala izingxenye eziyisihiyagalombili kujeke. @@Singasebenzisa intambo ukukala. @@Sika intambo ilingane nojusi okujeke.
P3	Goqa intambo ngokulingana kayisishiyagalombili.@@Qopha izingxenye ezilinganayo kujeke. @@Manje sesingathela izingxenye eziyisishiyagalombili ezilinganayo zikajusi.
P4	Siyabelana ujusi kalamula. @@Uma usuphelile, sifuna ukuphuza omunye!@@Uwenza kanjani ujusi kalamula?
P5	Ujusi kalamula@@amanzi – 7 izinkomishi@ushukela – 2 izinkomishi@ujusi wamalamula – 1 inkomishi@@Kuzofanele ukhame olamula abavuthiwe ababalelwa kwabayi i-7.@@Sebenzisa ujeke omkhulu noma ibhodlela eliyisilinganiso samalitha amabili ukuhlanganisa izithako.
P6	Indlela@@1.	Hlanganisa ushukela nojusi kalamula kanye nezinkomishi ezimbili zamanzi abandayo.@@2.	Goqoza kujeke noma uxukuze ibhodlela kuze kuncibilike ushukela.@@3.	Thela izinkomishi eziyisihlanu zamanzi abandayo.
P7	Nayi enye indlela yokubhala irecipi:@@1/10 ujusi kalamula@2/10 ushukela@7/10 amanzi@@Ujusi kalamula ozwayo uyingxenye eyodwa kajusi wonke usuphelele. @@Zingaki izingxenye kujusi usuphelele?
P8	Isithombe sibonisa uhhafu kalamula. @@Zingaki izingxenye zohhafu kalamula?@@Zingaki izingxenye kulamula ophelele? @@Ingxenye iyinye iyiqhezu likalamula ophelele.
