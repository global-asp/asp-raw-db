page_text:

P1	Mr Saucepan and Mr Pot were good friends. They lived happily for many years.
P2	One day Mr Saucepan told Mr Pot to go and visit their friend Mr Plate.
P3	Mr Pot said"I cannot walk,  am weak, if I walk I die."
P4	Mr Saucepan convinced Mr Pot and at last he accepted to go.
P5	On their way,  Mr Saucepan slid and fell onto Mr Pot. Mr Pot broke into pieces.
