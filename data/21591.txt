desc:	The story is about a clever hare that tricked two fat animals in a tug of war.

page_text:

P1	A hare is a clever animal.
P2	A hare tricked two, big, heavy animals.
P3	He went to Mr Elephant and told him i want to have a tug of war with you.
P4	He also went Mr Hippopotamus and told him, "I want to have a tug of war with you."
P5	The hare reached the venue first.
P6	The elephant and the hippo also arrived but he made sure that the two animals don't see each other.
P7	The big animals started pulling and pulling but nothing could move.
P8	The rope broke and each fell down.
P9	On realising that, what each of them was pulling from either sides was very heavy than what was expected, so each decided to go and see a heavy small so called hare, but each was surprised to see that each was pulling a very big fat animal and the hare had disappeared long time.
