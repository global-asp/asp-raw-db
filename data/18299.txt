title:	Khonani
author:	Ramikosi Gudani
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenda

desc:	Iḓani

page_text:

P1	Nṋe ndi pfi Simo.
P2	Ndi na khonani nṋa.
P3	Madzina avho ndi Zizo,  Lele,  Sisa,  na-Ayanda.
P4	Khonani yanga Zizo u takalela bola.
P5	Khonani yanga Lele u takalela u bambela.
P6	Khonani yanga Sisa u takalela u tamba mudzumbamo.
P7	Khonani yanga Ayanda u takalela u vhala.
P8	Nṋe? Ndi takalela zwoṱhe zwine vha funa u ita.
P9	Ndi tamba bola na Zizo.
P10	Ndi bambela na Lele.
P11	Ndi tamba mudzumbamo na Sisa.
P12	Iḓani khonani,  inwi ni funa zwifhio?
P13	Iḓani,  ni tambe bola na riṋe.
P14	Iḓani,  ni bambele na riṋe.
P15	Iḓani,  ni tambe mudzumbamo na riṋe.
P16	Iḓani,  ni vhale na riṋe!
