desc:	You should never climb a tree alone. Always appreciate your parents

page_text:

P1	One sunny afternoon Linda went to her grandmother and father she asked if they can make for her a birthday party.They did not answer. She went to go and play soccer with the other kids .
P2	While she was playing she felled and she cried and cried. Her grandmother came to come and see what is happening. She saw Linda was crying.
P3	She went to take a walk in the jungle. While she was walking she saw a tree. She climbed the tree higher and higher.
P4	She was high up and she couldn't get off she called and called that's when Nomusa,grandmother and her father came.
P5	They helped her get off the tree.She hugged Nomusa and said she'll never do that again
P6	She went home with Nomusa they walked and laughed and laughed.
P7	When she came home she saw a beautiful party there was cake,colddrink,balloons  and partypack she said thank you to everyone. Everyone said happy birthday.
