page_text:

P1	Ethresteni engihlala kilo kuhlala uNomzana uKio. @@UNomzana uKio unokatsu ibizo lakhe nguSafi.
P2	Lokha uNomzana Kio nakaya emsebenzini, utjhiya umnyango uvuliwe.
P3	USafi uhlala ngemnyango agade indlu.
P4	Inja kamakhelwana nayitjhidelako, uSafi uyayisabisa ibaleke.
P5	Inja iyabaleka.
P6	Umakhelwana nakakokoda emnyango, uSafi uzilungiselela ukumnghwara.
P7	Umakhelwana uyajika abaleke.
P8	USafi nakalambileko, uvala umnyango.
P9	Usafi udla ukudla kwakhe.
P10	Nakaqedako, uSafi weqela esofeni alale, "Phurr! Phurr! Phurr!"
P11	UNomzana Kio nakabuyako, uSafi uyazilula  amthabele amdidibeze bese uyamamukela ekhaya.
P12	UNomzana Kio banoSafi bahlala phasi, omunye nomunye wabo ufunda iphephandaba.
