desc:	The story describes  how huts are made and where they are found mostly.

page_text:

P1	A hut is made using poles,  grass and mud.
P2	A hut has got one door and two windows.
P3	Huts are very good to stay in during dry seasons because they are cool.
P4	Huts are also good to stay in when it is raining because you do not hear noise like on iron sheets.
P5	Huts are very easy to make.
P6	Every village in Uganda has got huts.
P7	BUT our village Nandahabi has got the most beautiful huts.
