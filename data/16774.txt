page_text:

P1	U Snara wayelelesa khulu unina wathukuthela khulu!
P2	Unina likaSnara wabona bona iksasa lendondanakhe liyatjhabalala,  wambophelela ngendlini.
P3	Wabiza amapholisa bona azomthusela ukuze alise iindakamizwa nokulelesa.
P4	Ekseni uSnara wavukela eskolweni nomnganakhe UBongwe.
P5	USnara bekazimisele khulu nakuphuma isikolo asale enze imisebenzi etjhodako notitjhere.
P6	USnara unebhudango lokuba nerhwebo.
P7	USnara wafunda iincwadi zakhe kuze aphumelele epilweni.
P8	USnara wafunda bewaphumelela wabanerhwebo.
