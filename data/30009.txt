page_text:

P1	Eno nhimiro ya maama. Nnene era nnungi. maama asimbamu ebirime bingi.
P2	Guno muti gwa muyembe mama gwe yasimba. Emiyembe myenvu ate minhulugufu. Gindeetera amairu.
P3	Nze ni maama twaja munhimiroye okufuna ku duuma ow'okwokya. Ebiseera ebindi maama aja mu katale yaatunda duuma.
P4	Enhimiro ya maama erimu  amaliibwa amanene n'enhaanha. Waliwo oguliibwa ogubba gwenhumirwa okulingirira.
P5	Ente da balirwana dhiri kulya amakaba g'embooli da maama.Maama aida ku nhiiga inho bwanakitegeera.
P6	Maama era yasimba ebikaadho.Nze ni mukwano gwange twenda inho okuzaana kyekweko mu bikaadho.
