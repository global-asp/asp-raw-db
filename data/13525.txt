page_text:

P1	Sane at home. Sherry ali engo.
P2	It is Sane's birthday. Oludaalo luno njo lubbasaliyeho Sherry.
P3	Sane at the bus stop. Sherry ali ebbasi aŋajemelera.
P4	Sane in the bus. Sherry ali mu bbasi.
P5	Sane at the market. Sherry muhatale.
P6	Ooh! Many fruits. Ooh! Ebibala bingi.
P7	Sweet apples. Emiyembe ejiŋoma.
P8	But where is mother mother? Naye maama aliŋena?
P9	I went to buy a birthday present for you. Njiye ohuhugulira ehirabo hya mazalibwa.
P10	Thank you mum! Webale maama!
