desc:	Its the story of my life as a baby. I love my life because its full of sweet memories

page_text:

P1	My life is good because I eat well and I  am growing gracefully. I travel as often as I want to and I go to places of interest.
P2	I have a beautiful doll, I like to play with my doll a lot. The name of my doll is Regina, I love and adore her so much because she makes me happy. Each time I return back home from sc welcomes me with a warm hug and kisses. I love my doll.
P3	I love to go to bed with my doll. I have a grandmother that calls me pet names like " baby girl" I love that name so much and I also love my grandmother so much. The name of my grandmother is Azuka, she is light in complexion.
P4	My life is full of beautiful memories of my adventures. I love the people in my life, they make life beautiful for me. I love my life so much, it is full.of adventures and fond memories.
P5	My life is godly because I go to church not just on Sunday but when necessary.  I love to go to church to worship with other brethren. The name of my pastor is Pastor Abraham.
P6	My pastor preaches like the Abraham in the bible. He is tall and very handsome. He is from the southern part of the country and speaks yoruba very well.  He also speaks Engliah fluently.
P7	I also love my grandfather,  my grandfather is a Fisher man, he hails from Rivers State of Nigeria.  My grandfather speaks okrika very well and lives very close to the rivers. I love the sight of the rivers.
P8	I have lovely sisters and brothers, I  like to play and eat with them. The names of my sisters are: Sarah, Ruth, Azuka and Babyoku. We go to  church together and play together. I love my sisters very well.
