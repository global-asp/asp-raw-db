desc:	The story is about a mango tree that has been named Kapopo by children who like its sweet fruits.

page_text:

P1	Kapopo the mango tree lies along the road. It is on the way to Jamuui Primary School.
P2	The mango tree is short. Children can easily reach it.
P3	They also climb on the tree to pick mangoes. They call it kapopo as it has sweet mangoes.
P4	They go to graze near the tree during weekends. They relax there.
P5	The children pick mangoes on their way to school.
P6	Birds also go to the tree to eat mangoes. Others eat ones that fall on the ground.
P7	Vehicles pass near the mango too. Passengers scare the animals around. They all run away.
P8	Children go for swimming at the river. It is opposite the mango tree. They carry their bicycles as well. It is interesting.
P9	School pupils like playing near the mango tree. They often play on there way home.
P10	Kapopo is also near Maguni forest. It is few meters away. Few people pass through the forest.
P11	The school farm borders the mango tree. One can see it from there. Many crops are grown around, potatoes, tomatoes and carrots.
