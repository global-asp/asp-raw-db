desc:	Nyere yekukhaba bhora yakupuwa kamurumbwana senthaka.

page_text:

P1	Makore ekudhaya maningi kwakanasirwa nyere yekukhaba bhora.@@Pazvakazikanwa kuti kune nyere yebhora vanthu vakatakana maningi veiida veshe.
P2	Pakatorwiwa hondo hurutu vanthu nenyika dzeshe veipenya pane nyere iyana. Nyere yainga yarashika. @Makore mazhinjitu akadarika pasina waiziya kuti iri paini. Kwaizwi yaizoonekwazve kana kwabarwa mukhabi mukuru webhora.
P3	Pasina mukuwo wakareba pakazobarwa kamurumbwana kaizwi Nyasha. @@Nyasha kakabarirwa mumphuri yakaromboka.
P4	Baba vaNyasha vakatamika kacho kadoko. Vasati vatamika vakakahina nyere iyana yaipenywa pashi reshe.@Pakuamba Nyasha akana zvakakatomboziya. Kaitoti nyere iyi aina mushando weinawo.
P5	Pakakazokura kuita jaha, Nyasha kakaqaya kukhabawo bhora nevangani vako.@@Kaishamiswa ngekunyunya nekumwisa kwakaiita vangani vako mubhora.
P6	Apapo ndipo pakakazonyumwa kuti nyere iyana yaiya nesimba pakukhaba bhora. Kakazotoqabanga kuti kaishandise kuita mare ndiyo.@Kaizobourira mai ako chitoro. Kaizofundisazve hunun'una nehukunda hwako chikora.
P7	Kazonasa kukura Nyasha kakatozoita mukhabi mukuru webhora.@@Vanthu vamweni vakagqina ngekuti Nyasha amunthupi ngemakhabire ake ebhora. Vamweni vaiti mudzimu vamweni vakati ingirosi.
P8	Kuguma nanyamashi apana unoriyarwa Nyasha mukhabi mukuru webhora pashi reshe.
