page_text:

P1	She keeps him company as he looks after his animals.
P2	As they go home, they talk about what child they will have.
P3	The husband argues when his wife begins to imagine if she will have twins.
P4	The wife realizes her husband imagines that she is carrying one child.
P5	She visits the midwife to know when she will give birth.
P6	After a while, she gives birth to twins and she holds them with joy.
P7	In the future they will expand the family with four more children.
