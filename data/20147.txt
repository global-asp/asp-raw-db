title:	Little Jojo's Long Tall Tale
author:	Leo Daly
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	English

desc:	The Chief of the Village of Many Shapes has lost the special horn that he blows to start the springtime party. Little Jojo goes in search of the missing horn

page_text:

P1	"I have a very big problem," cried Chief Short and Round.
P2	"I have lost my spring horn," said Chief Short and Round.
P3	"I will find your spring horn," said Little Jojo. "I will look at Tata Rectangle's house."
P4	Little Jojo and Tata Rectangle looked everywhere but they could not find the spring horn.
P5	"Go and see if Mama Triangle has the spring horn," said Tata Rectangle.
P6	Little Jojo stopped at his house to pour a glass of cold water.
P7	Jojo visited Dudu Diamond and asked if she would go with him.
P8	"It’s not far but I will pack some food to take with," said Dudu.
P9	Dudu and Jojo stopped to eat and rest at the bottom of the triangle mountain.
P10	When they were half way up the mountain, they saw a strange spider web.
P11	Jojo and Dudu arrived at Mama Triangle’s house.
P12	"The chief did not leave his spring horn here," said Mama Triangle.
P13	Mama Triangle knew where they could find the spring horn.
P14	It was evening when they arrived at Chief Short and Round’s house.
P15	Mama Triangle pointed at the chief. "You are wearing your spring horn on your head."
P16	Chief Short and Round gave Little Jojo the spring horn to blow.  They were happy.
