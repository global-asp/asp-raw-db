title:	Wambwa aja ku mwezi

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Luno lufumo lugema ku mbwa eyanda okuja ku mwezi. Enonia omuntu agidhuna okutuuka yo eya nga buli muntu agisekerera. Eyagaana oscar era bonbi bazimba emeeri yomwibbanga balambule ku mwezi.

page_text:

P1	Omwezi omubba ogutangala gwafuluma mu igulu erirugavu eryobwire. akabwa kali katyaime wansi wa munhenhie,kalota ku lunaku lwe kali kyala ku mwezi.
P2	Wambwa yali munhomwa, wa kivuuvu, ni amatu mabba ni ekirevu kibba kirugavu era kya kivuuvu.
P3	Bwali ku nco ku kyenco wambwa yakoba inhinha; "maama, osobola okunhamba wagya ku sitenseni ya gaali mwosi wangulira ku tikiti nje ku mwezi?" mama gha yo yaseka,"oh wambwa omwagalwa,ntire oli mutono okuja ku mwezi."
P4	Bwali ku nco ku kyenco wambwa yakoba maama we"maama, nkwegayirire osobola okuja ku sitenseni ya gaali mwosi ongulire ku tikiti ngye ku mwezi?" maama ghe yaseka, "Oh wamwa! oli mutono okuja ku mwezi.
P5	Nga wambwa akali ku isomero omusomesa ghe yabuuza ekibiina ki kyebadhuuba okuba nga bakuze. wambwa yawanika omukono gwe yakoba" madamuTeddy, nnhenda kuja ku mwezi." ekibiina ni madamu Teddy baaseka;"oh wambwa omulungi! ntire nti ebikule bidha kukugema!"
P6	Ti mwetegefu kubiva ku, wambwa yasaba Naikulya."Naikulya bu butuufu nnhendha kuja ku mwezi. osobola okunhamba?" wambwa yabuuza. naikurya yaseka inho, kata aganguke wansi okuva ku ntebe."oh wambwa omulungi! ntire nti ekirevu kyo ekibba tikibite mu elementi yo.
P7	wanbwa yanolwa ihno. Mu byona byona kye yayendha ni okuja ku mwezi. Yalowoza at singa bakagwa be tibamuyamba,aidha kunoonia omuntu owundi asobola. awo yava eka ya tambula okuja ewala ni awagazi afunhe omukwano ogwendha omwezi.
P8	Wambwa yaserengeta okuva ku lusozi yabita mu kibira, yasala olutindo olukaire okumaliririza yaseyeeya mu mwiga. yabona anhumba. yali ya oscar.
P9	"Oscar, ndi musanhufu okukubona!" wambwa yakob. "nnhenda kuja ku mwezi! maama akoba mbu ndi mutono, madamu Teddy yakoba mbu ebikuule biidha kungema ni naikula yakoba mbu ekirevu kyange kyali kinene! kiki kye mbe nkole buti?"
P10	Oscar yamwenhera wambwa era yamukoba,"oh wambwa omulungi! tewewalikirira,twidha kuna ku mwezi twembi."wambwa yasanhuka.
P11	Oscar yaja einhuma we nhumba ye era ya waluza yo ebokisi emba. "oli mwetegefu okukola emeeri yo mwibbanga wambwa?" oscar yaseka.
P12	Wambwa yali mucamukirivu yabukira mu ibbanga,"okutakoma ni okuswika gho!" ya lira.
