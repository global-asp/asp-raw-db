desc:	Kigana me obedo kwong Ochulu gi peacho pere. Ngeri mago tiyo tektek kodi ngeri mago miyo nyithindho pere dongo ingeri ma otire to nyithindho pere ju dongo maju kineni itichi bende. Jubedo gi siemi pa pecho.

page_text:

P1	Me baba Ochulu, go oli, baba Ochulu obedo jatichi matek. Chuka mama Ochulu otho baba Ochulu rango chemo ri nyithindhi Ochulu.
P2	Baba Ochulu bedo gi Ondieki maja woro kodi ji ma pecho pere,Ondieki maja woro tere kwalo chemo pa Baba Ochulu, ka Baba Ochulu kadho rango chemo Ondieki nindo, Ondieki maja woro nigi lithiroki aka go rachi.
P3	Ndelo acheili Ondieki majaworo ochamo nyathi Ochulu munyo Baba Ochulu oyido ongoye, Baba Ochulu owinjo marachi aka omako nge swa. Otimo ryeko ri Ondieki ni okadhi okwali chemo pa Simbwori, Simbwori omako Ondieki majaworo to chamo go.
P4	Mama Ondieki ongwecho iluli, go okadho bedo gi ogwange mani paka oyido go kinyali bedo gi Baba Ochulu doko. Rupiri go obedo gi bwoki ru gima nwango otimere.
P5	Me pecho pa Baba Ochulu, junitye ioti. Baba Ochulu kuro nyithindho pere kwong teko isawa pere mago nitye nono aka go fwonjo nyithindho pere geri ma rango chemo,jo nigi kineni itichi bende. Pecho pa Baba Ochulu nigi siemi.
