page_text:

P1	Anansi nolububi olutofu.
P2	Yachama efiokhulia, halali shatekhangata.
P3	Anansi achenderera Eshimuna. Asaya balie bosi eliani.
P4	"Khonye okhufuluka," Eshimuna shaboola. Anansi shiyenyata khulwa obutofu.
P5	"Ndalakalukha khale," Anansi yoboola.
P6	Abohanga obuusi khushilenje nende khunyungu eyeshimuna.
P7	"Oyiniekho obuusi nefiakhaya," Anansi yaboola.
P8	"Lishene, khulie fwesi amahenjera?" "Khukhonye okhukhola emilimo."
P9	"Ndalakalukha," Anansi yaboola. Yaboha obuusi muno.
P10	"Injiri, khulie amapwoni fwesi?" "Khonya okhukakasia," Injiri yaboola.
P11	"Ndalakalukha," Anansi yaboola.
P12	Mukhumalirikha, buli eshirenje sha Anansi shabohwa khunyungu.
P13	Anansi yakhweswa eshilenje esho 2. Khandi eshilenje esho 3.
P14	Efilenje fiosi 8 fiakhweswa. "Leshe okhukhwesa!" Anansi yalira.
P15	Abulaho ouhulira Anansi. Obuusi bukhalikha lulala shalulala.
P16	Neshichiranga tsimbubi tsiosi tsili nefilenje efinyere.
