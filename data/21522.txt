title:	Koko E E Fofang!
author:	Book Dash
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	Setswana
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mo polasing nngwe e nnye gaufi le motsana mongwe

page_text:

P1	Mo polasing nngwe e nnye gaufi le motsana mongwe...
P2	go ne go le kokwana e bidiwa Teise.
P3	"Fa ke gola, ke batla go fofela kwa godimo, kwa godimo thata kwa marung," Teise a rialo.
P4	Dikoko tse dingwe tsotlhe tsa mo tshega.
P5	"O a tsenwa wena," tsa mo raya jalo. "Re ka se tlhole re tshameka le wena."
P6	"Teise, rotlhe re kgona go itaaganya diphuka mme dikoko ga di kgone go fofa," Mama a mo raya jalo.
P7	Teise a gana go utlwa.

O ne a ithuta a le nosi go fofa letsatsi le letsatsi, a itaaganya diphuka.

Pha, pha, pha, o ne a itaaganya diphuka mme a sa kgone go tlhatloga.
P8	Fa a ntse a ithuta, a ipona a fofa a le kwa godimo kwa loaping a lebile dikoko fa fatshe.

O ne a ipona a fofa a feta dithaga le dipeolwana.

"Banna!" dinonyane di ne di tla bua jalo. "Koko e e fofang!"
P9	Ka jalo …go ne go utlwala Pha, pha, pha, letsatsi le letsatsi Teise a itaaganya diphuka.
P10	O ne a kgona go tlhatloga go sekae mme a bo a wa gape.
P11	"Ke ka se kgone go fofa!" Teise a raya Mama jalo a lela. "Ga ba ake."
P12	"Teise, ga o tshwane le dikoko tse dingwe. Ga di batle go fofa mme wena o a batla! O ka kgona go fofa," Mama a mo raya jalo.
P13	Letsatsi le le latelang Teise a palama mo godimo ga hoko ya dikoko mme a itaaganya diphuka a re pha, pha, pha.

A fofela mo phefong mme a itaaganya diphuka … a itaaganya diphuka … a itaaganya diphuka mme a re...
P14	RUTHU!
P15	Dikoko tse dingwe tsa mo tshega. "Ha ha ha! Re go boleletse! Dikoko ga di kgone go fofa!"
P16	Letsatsi le le latelang Teise a palamela kwa godimo le go feta, kwa godimo ga rantabole.

Pha, pha, pha, Teise a itaaganya diphuka.
P17	A fofela mo phefong mme a itaaganya diphuka … a itaaganya diphuka … a bo a itaaganya diphuka mme…
P18	A nna a fofa!

Phefo ka fa tlase ga diphuka tsa gagwe ya nna maatla mme a fofela kwa godimo-dimo!

Dinthaga le dipeolwane tsa re, "Ijo! Koko e e fofang!"
P19	Mme dikoko tse dingwe tsa batla go tshwana le ene.
P20	Tsa re, "Teise, o re makaditse!"
