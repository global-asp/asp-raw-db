P1	It was sunny that day, a little girl named Aiza was looking up in the sun and in some few clouds. In her mind she was dreaming what if those clouds are in different colors, soft, creamy and fruity
P2	Sweet Fruits? Said the fairy. This is a forbidden forest and all the fruits here are for the animals.@We are sorry for coming here, i just dream of making something that is soft like the clouds and fruity that will be for everyone.
P3	She went to meet her friend to stroll around the forest, went  the forest fairy named Cream appeared before them.@The fairy asked; what are you doing here in the top of the tree? We are want to get some sweet fruits; she replied
P4	you have good intentions, so i will share you all the delicious fruits in my forest.@You can come back and gather some fruits.@but you should also share the fruits with the forest animals.@They thank the fairy, and ask her father to help he
P5	With the help of some of her friends they continued to gather  some fruits and as promised they also share it with the animals.
P6	Her friends help her gather  fruits and she started to find the formula of her dream of creating something that is soft as the cloud, sweet, fruity  food that can help ease out the summer heat
P7	With the help of her mother they finally formulated the delicious  food she dreamed about, and she remembered the forest fairy named Cream...she decided to call the fruity cold dessert ICE CREAM
P8	She let her brother taste it, and he like it very much
P9	Then to their friends
P10	And to the village. Until it was known everywhere, And it was flavored with every fruits, nuts, beans you can imagine
