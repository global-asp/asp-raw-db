page_text:

P1	Onyimowo loves the colour red because it is part of the rainbow.
P2	Her mother is cooking rice for a party.
P3	Onyimowo cannot wait to eat up her breakfast.
P4	The party is on, they are all having fun.
P5	After the party, Mother is sunning the cloths on the line.
P6	Onyimowo loves to dance in the wind.
P7	Onyimowo has drawn the lines of the rainbow. Where are the colours?
P8	Now Onyimowo is holding a plate of blue fruits. "I love fruit." She says.
