page_text:

P1	Izoba rimwe, hakabahariho omushija, omukazi na'bana babo.

Nyina wa'bana akana teka emere birizoba. akana bihurira emere agiteka aha meza nabwanyima balya
P2	Ishebo akana baganisa enyama. akagambira mukuru wabo ati,"nkuzire kurya enyama. enyama eyawe  ngigi. linda obanze wakura."
P3	Nabyanyima agambira ondizho ati,"nkuzire kurye'nyama.eyawengigi. linda obanze okure

Burikachweka kenyama kakeyongera kukyendera. eki kikanikikorwa ahabana boona. Omwana omuto akatunga akachweka akache munonga
P4	Ishebo akabika enyama empango bitu zikaziri ezeye nano'mukaziwe. bitu abana bakahunama nikwokurya emere yabo
P5	Bwanyima ye mwakamwingi abana bakakura abazire bahwa amani haza abana bareberera nyina wabo nan'ishe
P6	Ezobarimwe barikurya, omwana omukuru akihura enyama nkaburizho.buti akahereza' ishe akachweka kenyama akakye  nanyina'bo
P7	Yagira'ati," mukarya eyanyu, hati nakeirekitu. tukuzire ekikumare."


Akihurira banyanya be enyama'nyingi
P8	Ishebo yijuka ekiyabire akora abana bakiri baato. nyinabo n'ishebo bikirizana ngu kikakitikire. bakashba okusasirwa. Abana baraganisa nkoku batarikora enshobi'egi nabana baabo
