desc:	Pa munzi wina panali kunkala Daisy. Daisy anali kankuku kakangono kamene kanali kunfunisisa kuuluka. Nkuku zonse zinamuseka Daisy pamene anayesesa mobwezerabwezera kuyeselela kuuluka.

page_text:

P1	Kalekale pafamu pena panali mudzi wina waung'ono.
P2	Panali kukhala kankhuku kakang'ono kochedwa kuti Daisy.
P3	"Ine ndikakula, ndifuna kuuluka m'mwamba, m'mwambamu mlengalenga," anatero Daisy.
P4	Koma nkhuku zonse zinamuseka iye.
P5	"Iwe ndiwe wosokonezeka," iwo anatero. "Ife sitidzasewera nawenso."
P6	"Daisy, ife tonse tingapukuse mapiko athu, koma ndicovuta kwambiri kuti nkhuku ziuluke," Amai ake anamuuza.
P7	Koma Daisy sanaleke kuyesa. Tsiku lililonse amayeserera payekha, kupukusa mapiko ake. @@Puku, puku, puku, anapukusa mapiko ake koma sanakwanitse kuuluka.
P8	Pamene anali kuyesetsa, anayerekezera kuti anali kuuluka mu mlengalenga ndi kupenya nkhuku pansi. @@Iye anaganiza kuti amauluka pamwamba kuposa mpheta. "Taonani!" Mbalame zinatero, "Nkhuku imene ikuuluka!"
P9	Conco, tsiku lililonse Daisy amapukusa mapiko ake, puku, puku, puku.
P10	Iye amati akakwanitsa kunyamuka anali kugwera pansi.
P11	"Sindidzakwanitsa kuuluka!" Daisy anadandaulira amai ake. Anzanga ena onse akunena zoona.
P12	"Daisy, ndiwe wosiyana ndi nkhuku zina. Izo sizifuna kuuluka, koma iwe ufuna kuuluka! Iwe ungakwanitse kuuluka," anatero amai ake.
P13	Tsiku lotsatira Daisy anakwera pamwamba pa khola ndipo anayamba kupusa mapiko ake puku, puku, puku ndipo anapukusabe mapiko ake ndipo potsirizira pake…
P14	PHWEE…
P15	Nkhuku zina zonse zinaseka kwambiri. "Ha,ha, ha! Tinakuuza! Nkhuku sizingauluke!"
P16	Koma tsiku lotsatira, Daisy anakwera pamwamba kwambiri, pamwamb panyumba. @@Puku, puku, puku, Daisy anapukusa mapiko ake.
P17	Iye anauluka m'mwamba ndi kupukusa mapiko ndi kupukusa mapiko ndipo potstira pake…..
P18	Iye anapitiriza kuuluka! Mpweya pansi pa mapiko ake unakula ndipo anauluka m'mwamba ndi m'mwamba! Mpheta ndi anamzeze anati, "Codabwitsa! Nkhuku ikuuluka!"
P19	Ndipo nkhuku zina zinafuna kukhala ngati Daisy.
P20	Nkhuku zinati, "Taonani Daisy ndiwe wodabwitsa!"
