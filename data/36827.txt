P1	This is the photo of second world war.This shows the loss of lives caused by the second world war.
P2	This is also the photo after second world war. This picture shows the loss of property.
P3	It is another effect of the war . War damages the environment very badly by destroying the natural resources.
P4	War causes the loss and misuse of the economy. For example:US spends billions of dollars annually for wars and weapons which can be used for better causes.
P5	Due to the war,many people became homeless and helpless which is very bad.
