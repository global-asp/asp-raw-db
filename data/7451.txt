desc:	Lo mbongo ungentombazana encinane ezimisele ukuba ngumdanisi ophum izandla ngenye imini. Abanye abantu abafana notitshalakazi wakhe abayithandisisi ncam le nto yakhe

page_text:

P1	Ndidanisa xa ndonwabile,  Ndidanisa xa ndibuhlungu,  Ndidanisa xa ndindedwa,  Ndidanisa xa ndicaphukile.
P2	Utitshalakazi wam uthi,  "Hlala phantsi uzole! Sukudanisa imini yonke."
P3	Kodwa umama wam yena uthi,  "Ukudanisa kwenza ube sempilweni,  Ukudanisa kwenza ube mde,  Ngenye imini uza kuba ngumdanisi,  Kwaye uza kubamangalisa bonke!"
P4	Kwaye mna ndithi,  "Ngenye imini ndiza kudanisa kwaye abantu baza kubhatala xa befuna ukundibona nditsiba-tsiba kwaye ndizibhija-bhija." 'Udanisa kakuhle KAKHULU',  baza kutsho.
