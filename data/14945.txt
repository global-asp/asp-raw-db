title:	Miringoki Eyamaino?
author:	Mugasa Nathanael Erisa
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Rutooro
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/14945.png
desc:	Ebiroso ebindi biine amaino agatoire agokuruma. Ebisoro ebindi biine amaino agaterekerire agokucankunya. Ebisiro ebindi tiibina maino. Kandi iwe?

page_text:

P1	Enzigamira ine amaino gasongoire.
P2	N'entale nayo ine agasongoire.
P3	Ensamaki enkoto ine agasongoire muno! Habwaki ebisoro ebindi biine amaino agasongoire?
P4	Ente ine amaino agagumire ha bwokucwacwana.
P5	Ekinyonyi tikine maino. Ekiyonyi kilya kita?
P6	Embeba ine amaino makoto agomumaiso.
P7	Nkaba nyina amaino.
P8	Amaino gange agomumaiso gakaruga yo! Oine amaino aingaha?
