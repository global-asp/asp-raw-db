desc:	Luukano luno luwaamba khu mukhaana umukyeekhe uuli umulwaale

page_text:

P1	Isapiiti iyabirire nafunile tsibarwa tsingali.
P2	Khu palatsa umukaangi wase aaronela umuleekeli wase ibarwa.
P3	Nafuuna mulwaale. Aakha arame ingo.
P4	Khu lwa khabiri nafuna ibarwa khukhwaama isi baasomi bashaase.
P5	Uwumbuutse khaangu,  Nafuuna.
P6	Lwa khataru nafuna ibarwa khukhwaama isi musaale wase naabi.
P7	Nakhuupile iwe shifaani shino. Ni khuukana khuukali khukhwaama isi Dada.
P8	Lwa khane nafuna ibarwa khukhwaama isi maayi.
