page_text:

P1	Uyu ni Khalai. Afite imyaka irindwi. Izina rye risobanura 'umwiza.'
P2	Khalai arabyuka akavugana n'igiti cy'icunga. Akakibwira ati: "Mbabarira wa giti cy'icunga we, ukure vuba maze uduhe amacunga  menshi ahishije."
P3	Khalai ajya kw'ishuri. Mu nzira avugisha icyatsi. "Mbabarira wa cyatsi we,ukure utohagire kandi ntiwume."
P4	Khalai abona indabo. Akazibwira ati: "Mumbabarire mwa ndabo mwe, komezemurabye mbashyire mu musatsi wange."
P5	Kw'ishuri, Khalai avugisha  igiti kiri mu busitani bw'ishuri. "Mbabarira wa giti we, mera amashami manini  tuge twigira mugicucu cyayo."
P6	Khalai avugisha uruzitiro  ry'ishuri rye. "Mbabarira ukure ukomere uturinde abantu babi."
P7	Iyo Khalai agarutse mu rugo avuye ku ishuri, asura cya giti cy'icunga. Akakibaza ati: "Amacunga ntiyari yera?"
P8	Khalai akiruhutsa ati: "Amacunga yawe aracyari mabisi." Akibwira ati: "Nzakubona ejoWenda uzaba unfitiye icungaryeze!"
