desc:	Eriyo eyaibbire emere omuntamu era buli muntu yegaine.

page_text:

P1	Omaama yasumbire emre omuntamu. Yagisibiriirye aghaigulu omujokooni okusobola okukwatagania abaguzi be omukatale.
P2	Okagoya n'aboonyoko be ababiri bairire okuzwa ku Mayirikiti primary school. Okagoya yaali mu kibiina kyakuna, Andereya mu Top class n'Owaiswa mu Baby class.
P3	Owaiswa yakungire enzala aye okagoya yamukobere  ati bonabona bali na kulinda mughaabu kwira mu katale. Owaiswa yakukuukire omunyumba n'alya emere.
P4	Omaama olwairire, yaali musanyufu okubona abaana b'eika. Yaigwiregho ejokooni okubagha emere. Yayagiirye bukunkumusya bwa mutyere omujokooni yonayona.
P5	Yeetere abaana bonabona omujokooni n'ababuulya emere. Owaiswa niiye eyasookere okwegaana aye esaati ye yabbaire eizwire osupu.
P6	Omaama yagabire emere eyasigairemu naagha owaiswa ekibonerezyo ky'okumwima kyamisana. Yamukobere obutairayo tete.
