desc:	Ikwena yali ne inzala iswekha mumatsi ikhaba efiokhulia.

page_text:

P1	Yaliwo khale ikwena yali inzala muno.
P2	Yakhaaba efiokhulia kaala khandi iwolere.
P3	POW! Ikwena ikamula shiokhulia!
P4	Mufise fino shiili inzala ta,  na ili nende obusangafu.
P5	Okhula lwo ilaulira inzala khandi.
