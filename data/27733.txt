page_text:

P1	Obukoola butandiika okulaga, Akasimbe akatono katandiika okumera.
P2	Okutuka okumpi n'endhuba, okutuka ghaigulu paka nga kiweire.
P3	Kati muti munene, amakoola mangi era gwa kiragala, gusansanhia amasaga gaagwo gu sobole okubonebwa.
P4	Ensaga ngumu saawa eno, nnwevu era neesimbu. Omuti gwendha ku ebibala kati, ensaga dhitukaine.
P5	Ebimuli bisooka byaidha, male endhuki dhaidha. Oluvainhuma lwe dhoki ebibala byata kumuti.
P6	Emiyembe ginogebwa ne mikono egidhugumira, era jaatebwa mu biibo ebinene okulibwa abo okukola ebisiiga.
P7	Omuyembe ogudhuudhuula guno omumyufu, gufaanana nga mulungi era gwakanhogebwa.
P8	okugulumaku obulume oti, guwoomerera, gudhuudhuula, gwinhogoga. Okuwoomerera okutono okwo kuleeta okusologoka.
P9	Omuyembe gwange guweire gho, nagulire mangu inho, Ndinookufuna oguundi ate ti noogunasembayo!
