page_text:

P1	Awo' lwatuuka nga  wabagho kyabazinga eyali ni mughala we omubalagavu. Abasadha bamusaba okubafumbirwa aye kyabazinga yadhema. Kyabazinga yakoba nti mughala  we yali alina kufumbirwa musadha mugaiga yenka.
P2	Lwali lulala omusadha yaingira mu lubiri lwa kyabazinga nga'musaba aghase omughal ono omubalagavu. Kyabazinga yamubuuza kki kyeyali agyo'muwa  mumughale we, ero'musadha yamukoba nti yaili alina zaabu ne'nte ningi inho edho'kughayo ngo'mwandhu.
P3	Kyabazinga yasanuka inho era yayeta mughala we. Omughala bweyaidha yasiima omusadha ono omugaiga era kyabazinga yasalagho okumughaba.
P4	Omughala yafumba era omusadha  yagya okweta abe'kiika kye okwiidha okubona mugole we. Abekiika  kye baidha okulye'kyeigulo. Aye omusadha  bweyali   aingira munhumba, yabona nga mukazi we timusanuffu.''kayi kki mukyala wange? omusadha yabuza.'' Tifumbangaku mubulamu bwange'', mukhazighe yamwiramu.
P5	Omusadha yeinamirha  inho. Yakoba mukhazi we nti baidha kulya ekyo kyona kyona kyannaba nga'fumbye. Omughala yagya mwifumbiro era yafumba emmere embi inho.Emere bwebagireta kumeza, abageni bagyo'gyiryaku nga tegamulamu naire. Omusadha omugaiga yagema omughala yamwiza yo ewa Kyabazinga, era kyabazinga yamwiriiza ente dhe nni zaabu we.Kyabazinga yeinamira inho yaizayo mughala we.
