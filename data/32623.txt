title:	Mutesi Intangarugero
author:	Patrick  Munyurangabo
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First sentences

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32623.png
desc:	Mutesi ni umukobwa wintangarugero mu rugo iwabo ndetse no ku ishuri aho yiga ahorana umurava.

page_text:

P1	Murumuna wange aryama atinze cyane. Mbyuka kare, kubera ko ndi umunyamurava!
P2	Ni ngewe ufungura imiryango y'inzu bwa mbere.
P3	Mama arambwira ati: "Uri inyenyeri y'igitondo."
P4	Ndiyoza, nta bufasha na buke nkenera.
P5	Nshobora kwihanganira amazi akonje n'isabune y'ubururu yo kumesa.
P6	Mama anyibutsa koza amenyo buri gitondo @@Nkamusubiza nti: "Sinshobora kubyibagirwa!"
P7	Nyuma yo koga, nsuhuza sogokuru na masenge, nkanabifuriza umunsi mwiza.
P8	Hanyuma nkiyambika, nkabwira mama nti: "Ndi munini, narakuze ubu."
P9	Nshobora gufunga ibipesu nkanafunga inkweto zange.
P10	Nkora uko nshoboye ngo musaza wange muto amenye amakuru yose yo ku ishuri.
P11	Mu ishuri mparanira kuba intangarugero muri byose.
P12	Nkora ibi bintu byiza byose buri munsi. @@Sinjya ntezuka gukora ibyiza. Ariko ikintu nkunda cyane ni ugukina.
