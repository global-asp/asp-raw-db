P6	I climb the mango tree and pick the ripe mangoes.
P7	I pick as many mangoes as I can.
P8	My mother, stands under the mango tree to collect the mangoes.
P9	When I get tired, I climb down the Mango tree to eat the mangoes.
P10	When I go to school on Monday, I will share the mangoes with my friend Sarah.
