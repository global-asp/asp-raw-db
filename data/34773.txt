page_text:

P1	Mfene naNgwenya bebabangani labakhulu. Bacitsa lusuku baticocela, batihlekela.
P2	Mfene abehlala etulu esihlahleni, Ngwenya ahlala phasi emfuleni.
P3	Langa limbe wagula umntfwana Ngwenya, watsi gogo Ngwenya abatfole sibindzi semfene kutomelapha.
P4	Ngwenya wacela Mfene kutsi amvakashele kakhe, ngobe angakwati kuhlamba wammema emhlane Ngwenya.
P5	Endleleni Ngwenya wacocela umngani kutsi ugulelwa ngumntfwana manje kufanele atfole sibindzi semfene kutomelapha.
P6	Mfene kwametfusa loko, watsi kuNgwenya ,‚Äùhawu ngabe ushilo mngani, sami sibindzi ngisishiye esihlahleni.
P7	Mfene wazubela esihlahleni, Ngwenya wabatse uyamncenga wahleka Mfene kutsi bengitsi ungumngani kantsi ungumbulali.
