desc:	Kaza da shanshani suna takara kullum tsakaninsu. Wata rana kaza da ba ta ci nasara ba

page_text:

P1	Kaza da shanshani abokai ne. Amma kullum suna takara tsakaninsu. Wata rana,  sai suka niyyar wasan ƙwallon ƙafa don sanin wa ya fi kwaninta tsakani su biyu(2).
P2	Sai suka jika filin ƙwallon ƙafar kuma suka fara wasan. Kaza tana da gaugawa amma shanshani ta fi ta gaugawar. Kaza ta harba ƙwallon nesa amma kuma in shanshani tana aika ƙwallon neza ƙwarai fiye inda na kazar zai je. Ganin haka,  sai kaza ta fara hassala.
P3	Ƴan takarar biyu (2) sun yi niyyar su je bugun da kai sai mai tsoron gida. Da farko shanshani ita ce mai tsaron gida wato gola kenan. Kaza ta saka ƙwallo ɗaya rak cikin raga. Sai aka juya,  kaza ta zama ma gola mai tsaron gida.
P4	Shanshani ta harba ƙwallo,  kuma ta saka a raga. Shanshani tana gwaninta kuma tana saka ƙwallon. Shanshani ta saka ƙwallon har da kai. Shanshani ta saka ƙwallon biyar (5).
P5	Kaza ta hassala da rishin nasarar da ta yi. Kaza ba iya wasa ba. Shanshani ta yi ta dariya saboba abokiyarta ta hassala wajen wasa.
P6	Sai kaza ta buɗe babban bakinta ta hadiye shanshanin.
P7	Lokacin da kaza tana shigowa gida,  sai ta gamu da uwar shanshani. Sai ta ce muta,  "ba ki ga ɗiyata ba?" Kaza ba ta amsa mata ba. Uwar shanshani ta damu.
P8	Sai uwar shanshani take ji wata ƙaramar murya tana cewa: "Ki agaje uwata!" Shanshani take faɗi. Uwar shanshani ta dubu kewayenta da kyau. Muryar nan da cikin kaza ta fito.
P9	Uwar shanshanin ta yi ihu "Ki yi amfani da duk azirinka ɗiyata!" Shanshani ta saki wani wari. Sai kaza ta fara jin wani ciwon ciki.
P10	Kaza ta yi gyatsa. Kuma ta yi kaki. Ta yi attishewa kuma take ta tari,  take tari. Shanshani bai ciyuwa!
P11	Kaza ta yi ta tari sai ta kako shanshanin da yake cikin cikinta. Shanshanin da uwar sun tafi sun ɓoye cikin wani icce.
P12	Tun daka shi kaza da shanshani ba su shiri.
