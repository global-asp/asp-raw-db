desc:	Ms Phone is quite a character! Can you imagine having five phones

page_text:

P1	She is called Ms Phone. Look at her!
P2	She has five cellphones. When she wakes up,  she arranges her cellphones on the table.
P3	The yellow one is for communicating with family.
P4	The white one is for communicating with people at work.
P5	The red one is for communicating with those who owe her something.
P6	The pink one is for communicating with her best friends.
P7	The purple one is for communicating with people who need her help.
P8	Ms Phone likes people to be happy. She does not like fighting.
P9	She lets the children watch television at her shop.
P10	She visits the sick and the elderly in their homes.
P11	When she passes,  there are shouts of "Ms Phone! Ms Phone!"
P12	Then she feels happy and says,  "I am Ms Phone. Here,  look at my phones!" That is Ms Phone and her five cellphones.
