page_text:

P1	Kushushu kakhulu. Umama uxakekile endlwini, uthi masiyokudlala ngaphandle.
P2	￼￼"Masiyeni emlanjeni siyokudlala ecaleni kwamanzi," watsho uZoleka.
P3	Safika emlanjeni. Sakhulula zonke iimpahla zethu.
P4	Sadlala, sadlala emanzini.
P5	￼"Masiqubheni!" watsho uXolani. Saqubha, saqubha, salibala ngexesha.
P6	Ilanga latshona, saziva sigodola. Zaziphi iimpahla zethu?
P7	Sakhangela phantsi kwemithi nasemahlahleni. Sakhangela kwindawo yonke.
P8	"Imazi yenkomo itya isikipa sam!" wakhwaza uThozama.
P9	"Enye itya isiketi sam!" watsho uZoleka.
P10	Safika umama esilindile. Ngokukhawuleza, iimpundu zethu zaba shushu.
