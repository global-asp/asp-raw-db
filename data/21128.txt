desc:	Eyakar agirokin lokookeng lobe akimileun

page_text:

P1	Alomito kolong Agirokin ka Akimileun ka ng’itung’a aapei.
P2	Agirokin ng'esi arai itokeng Akimileun.
P3	Ang’oana Akimileun noi.
P4	Arai na eng’oya Akimileun taar ng’itung’a ka aukor ng’akais ka ng’ikito.
P5	Na esubia Akimileun ng'inyas ng'ulu, dio Agirokin kikilau etoil aloapaolon.
P6	Nakaneni abu Akimileun nyiira ng’akiro aitokeng.
P7	Sodi ng’itunga toloto kigoronokis ekapolon.
P8	Abu ekapolon kirita Agirokin ka Akimileun kiboyoto anikalonon alotung’a.
P9	Nyepalikina Akimileun anomeenen ng’ikito esaa lo eng’oya.
P10	Apotu ng’itung’a tobong’o lokapolon nabo akiburare.
P11	Abu ekapolon kirita Agirokin ka Akimileun kiboyoto alokidiama adis.
P12	Neni dae, ering’a Akimileun eari ka akinom apak na eng’oya.
