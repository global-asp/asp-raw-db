desc:	Emilimo egindi gisingaku giinaajo? (Kiliyo ni ku luse 3).

page_text:

P1	Abantu bano bali kughakana.
P2	“Basomesa naabasinga. Mutwetaaga okwega,” omusomesa bwakoba.
P3	“Bazimbi naabasinga. Mwetaaga amayumba,” omuzimbi bwakoba.
P4	“Babaadhi naabasinga. Mwetaaga bibaadhe,” omubaadhi bwakoba.
P5	“Basawo naabasinga. Mutwetaaga okuba obukalamu.”
P6	Balimi naabasinga. Mutwetaaga
  lwa mmere.”
P7	“Beega naabasinga!” Omwega bwakoba.
P8	Kuba mwenamwena mwali
  beega okusooka!” Buti buli muntu
  akiikiriza.
