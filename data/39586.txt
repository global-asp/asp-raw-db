title:	Anyinya a ken Debre Birhan
author:	Terkule Aorabee
artist:	Yirgalem  Birhanu
lang:	Tiv

desc:	Kwagh-hir ne tese se er anyinya yange vihi isima sha ican i ve lu yan ke Debre Birhan ken Itiopia nahan ve mough num a mba eren ve atsan ve gema u uma vev.

page_text:

P1	Chalew ka nyinya u ulugh kyekye. @@Ka a tôô ikyav mbi yuhwan sha igbudu mba Debre Birhan. @@Ka a er tom hii pepe zanzan aikie.
P2	Chalew zough a shien u memen kuman un ga. @@Zough a kwaghyan ka kom un ga. @@Or hendan ikyekye ka a shi gbidyen un er a yevese fefa.
P3	Iyange i gen yô, mba hendan anyinya a ulugh akeke zua u tsan ku. @@Anyinya a ulugh akeke zua a ian i yan gwer gwer nongo hen jiir imôm. @@Ve hii u timen sha atsan a ve.
P4	"Mo shidi wam la nyôrom ga. Ka atsulem gbang a tôndom avav iyol," nyinya ugen kaa nahan. @@"Mo kpa sha zwa la," ugen tsôrough.
P5	Tse nyinya ugen kaa er, "M ngu nôngun u kpen nguhar sha ukusa mba manden mo shin apande a ngahar." @@Anyinya kpishi kpegher sha tyo.
P6	"Ka se er tom taveraa kpa ior asev ve nzughur a vese. Ve kpishi ve fa u nengen sha vese ga," Chalew kaa nahan. @@Anyinya cii rumun.
P7	Chalew yôô er, "Se soo mkpeiyol. Se gba nen num !" Anyinya cii ger! @@Ve rumun u zan maape môm ga er  saa mbahendan ve rumun u geman anza.
P8	Mbahendan akeke fa kwagh u vea er ga. @@Môm ken mbera ta ihiagh er, "Me faityô u yamen nyinya u genegh." @@Kpa mbagenev ken a ve soo anyinya ve u zan hemen a ityom ve.
P9	Mbahendan akeke mbela zua mtem sha u tôvon sha num gban u anyinya la. Ve nyiman kwagh shie gôgônan.@@"Kam er anyinya ne nga a ishô nahan," or ugen ôr nahan. "Ityôkyaa i ve soo mkpeiyol yô ka nyi jimi?" u gen pine ve her.
P10	Num gban u anyinya ne samber hii hen gar u Debre Birhan tser tar u  Itiopia. @@Anyinya kuma milien utar cii venda u eren tom. @@"Kuma se nahan! Er nen a vese sha inja," ve kaa nahan.
P11	Gande mba hendan akeke tsô ve gema anza. @@Ve rumun er a eren a anyinya cii sha inja shi a na ve mkpeiyol kpaa. @@Ve hii u wan ve ikyo shi eren ve twer kpaa.
P12	Kule kule tsô anyinya hingir u zuan a mkpeiyol. @@Ve zua a kwaghyan man m-mem  u kuman ve. Shidi kpaa nyôr ve vough. Akôv a shin apande a ve kpaa lu guda. @@Orvesen Chalew hingir icuwan nyinya hen a ve.
