page_text:

P1	Imbwa na Nyiramuhari byakoreraga Sekamana.

Zamuhigiraga ku manywa.
P2	Nijoro zikararira urugo rwe.
P3	Buri mugoroba Sekamana yaryaga inyama.

Naho Imbwa na Nyiramuhari akaziha amagufa gusa.
P4	Umunsi umwe Nyiramuhari abwira Imbwa ati, "Tugomba kwanga agasuzuguro ka Sekamana. Reka tumutoroke twigendere."
P5	Ariko kubera ko Imbwa yubahaga Sekamana ikanamutinya, yahisemo kuhaguma. 

Naho Nyiramuhari irigendera.
P6	Mu mwanya muto, Sekamana  aza kubona ko Nyiramuhari yigendeye. Abaza Imbwa ati, "Wa muhigi wanjye w’intwari ari he?" Nuko Imbwa imubwira uko byagenze.
P7	Kuva ubwo Nyiramuhari ntiyagarutse mu rugo.

Ubu Nyiramuhari asigaye ahiga wenyine akarya inyama yihigiye.
P8	Naho Imbwa yo n’ubu iracyarya amagufa.
