title:	Lifudu Nitye Gi Cal Abiryo

artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Dhopadhola

desc:	Me kigana ma cako fonjirok kwoŋ kwan gi cal.

page_text:

P1	Lifudu nitye gi cal abiryo.
P2	Mar apena obedo ma obokere.
P3	Opio oruko sati ma obokere.
P4	Mar aryo obedo ma nya micungwa.
P5	Aŋo ma nyamicungwa i cal me?
P6	Mar adek obedo ma nyatongweno.
P7	Aŋo ma nyatongweno i cal me?
P8	Mar aŋwen obedo ma nyabokependi.
P9	Aŋo ma nyabokependi i cal me?
P10	Mar abic obedo ma nyapolopolo.
P11	Aŋo ma nyapolopolo i cal me?
P12	Mar aucel obedo ma nyabululu.
P13	Aŋo ma nyabululu i cal me?
P14	Mar abiryo obedo ma nyako.
P15	Nyako obedo cal ma ber loyo. I maro cal mene?
P16	A kwan raye cal abiryo ma lifudu nitye gine.
