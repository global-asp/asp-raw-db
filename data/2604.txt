desc:	Mr. Fly and Mr. Big head were two great friends. Then one day they argued about who could swim across the river.  They decided to go out and swim to prove who was right.  Mr. Big Head failed to swim. Mr. Fly laughed so much that his mouth became wider and wider…

page_text:

P1	'Di Onyukunyu 'i.
P2	'Di drikulu 'i.
P3	Onyukunyu pi ndra drikulu be agyini. O`du alu,  mu ki aci si tualu.
P4	Onyukunyu pi ndra drikulu be agyini. O`du alu,  mu ki aci si tualu. "Mi econi mbu yi `di ma drile si ku." "Ma eco ra! Mi eco ni mbu ku mi `!"
P5	"Imbu te nga!" Onyukunyu `yo. "Yo,  imbu ra ka," Drikulu `yo. Coti onyukunyu sa yi dria si hawu.
P6	Drikulu ni leria mburia,  e`de yi alia dri be kulu. Eri ma dri ni nzi tu ri si,  e`do `de yi etia.
P7	Onyukunyu gu "He hee!" Gu 'i kai ra fe eri ma ti e`do 'i asi e`dozu kala aluria risi kpere kala aziria!
