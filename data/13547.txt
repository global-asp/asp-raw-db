page_text:

P1	Sonke siyazi ukuthi indlovu inomboko omude,  ikhala layo phela.
P2	Kodwa emandulo umboko wendlovu wawumfishane futhi unonile. Njengesicathulo maphakathi nobuso bayo. Uyikhala nje elijwayelekile.
P3	Kwazalwa inkonyane lendlovu elalithanda ukubuza imibuzo. Lalinombuzo esilwaneni ngasinye ababephila nazo.
P4	Lalilangazelele ukwazi ngendlulamithi. ''Kungani unomqala omude?'' labuza indlulamithi.
P5	Lalilangazelele ukwazi ngobhejane. ''Kungani unezimpondo ezicijile?'' Labuza kubhejane.
P6	Kanti lalilangazelele KAKHULU ukwazi ngengwenya. ''Ingwenya idlani ngesidlo sakusihlwa?''.
P7	Kodwa ngokushesha igwababa elihlakaniphile lathi,  ''Ngilandele siye emfuleni. Kulapho uzobona ukuthi ingwenya idlani ngokudla kwakusihlwa.''.
P8	Nangempela inkonyane lendlovu lalilandela igwababa behlela emfuleni.
P9	Laziminyeka encemeni labe selima osebeni lomfula. Labuka emanzini. Yayikuphi ingwenya?
P10	''Sawubona, '' kusho itshe ngasosebeni lomfula. ''Yebo, '' kuphendula inkonyane lendlovu. ''Mhlawumbe ungangitshela ukuthi ingwenya idlani ngesidlo sakusihlwa?''.
P11	''Gobela phansi ngizokutshela, '' kuphendula itshe. ''Yehla futhi,  futhi.'' Ngokunjalo inkonyane lendlovu lagoba laze.
P12	''Ngqi!'' Ikhala layo labe selise mhlathini wengwenya. ''Ingwenya izodla wena ngokudla kwakusihlwa!'' Kuswininiza igwababa.
P13	Inkonyane lendlovu lazabalaza ngemilenze yalo yangemuva eqinile,  ladonsa,  ladonsa,  ladonsa. Kodwa ingwenya yangalidedela.
P14	Ikhala lenkonyane lendlovu lanwebeka,  lanwebeka,  lanwebeka laze. ''Didli!'' lawainkonyane lendlovu lashayeka ngomhlane. Ingwenya yashibilika yaphindela emanzini.
P15	Inkonyane lendlovu labuka ikhala lalo. Kodwa langakwazi ukubona lapho ligcina khona!
P16	Labe selilide kangangoba lase likwazi ukukha isithelo egatsheni eliphezulu esihlahleni. Kusukela ngalelo langa,  zonke izindlovu zinomboko omude olusizo kuzona.
