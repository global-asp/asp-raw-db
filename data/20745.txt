title:	Kubala Imboga

lang:	Rufumbira

desc:	Imbozi yo kubala no gusangira. (Ikigitabo chashobonzwe nu bufasha bwa Oppenheimer Memorial Trust).

page_text:

P1	Nyina wakama yabaga ali kwanura imboga ichumweru chose. Uwimana, Uwera na Tuyishime bayambaga nyina wa kama buli kuwa mukaga mugitondo. Uyumunsi abana bagiye kuyamba kubala nogupakila izi mboga. Nuko ise wa kama azijane kuzigurisha mugatare.
P2	Imodoka ishiraga abana kurugi dwu mulima. Ahobashobora kuleba ikirundo chi mboga kirunzwe uruhande dwi modoka yase wakama.
P3	basanze nyina wa kama abalindiye ku mudyango nuko alabalamutsa. Arababwira ati bifuza kubara imboga nuko bakazishira mubudeyi. Imboga 12 muli buli kadeyi. Aravuga ati afite ubudeyi 20. arongera arvuga ati babiri nugupakila ubudeyi bulindwi nuko umuntu umwe agapakira butandatu
P4	Nuko abana bahagarara uruhande dwikirundo chi mboga batangira gutesha uko barabara izimbogo. uwimana aravuga ngo we arabara muli ibiri, Uwera we aravuga ngo arabara muli enyenye maze Tuyishime we aravuga ngo arabara muli itatu itatu
P5	Mukanya gakeya chane abana baba barangije kuzuza ubudeyi 20. Mwakoze chane, nyina wa kama yarabashimye ageze aho alabawira ngo imboga zisigaye bazikunganyirize ingurubeze kubera yuko nazo zizikundaga
P6	Umulimo gusigaye nukwandika ibeyi kulibuli kadeyi nuko balangiza bakayamba se wa kama kushira izi mboga mu modoka.
P7	Tulibugufi kulangiza, Leka tulebe nimboga zingahe zodushobora gushira kuli buli luhande dwi modoka
P8	Mukanya ubudeyi makunyabiri byose bwari bumajije kuzula mumodoka. nukomaze ise wakama avuga imodaka agenda ari gutekereza icho yakolesha izo sente agurishije izo mboga zose
P9	Basubiye murugo, abana bafuhiriran izindi mboga. Aho isaha itandatu zagereye, bose bali barushe. Nina wa Kama arbambira ati abafitiye igihembo
P10	Nuko aja munzu kuzana igihembo chabo. yavuye munzu afite imifuka ilemeleye.
P11	mumifuka ye yakuyemo imiyembe. Nuko alababwira ngo nibasangire bagabanire hagati.
P12	Mbele abana babaze imiyembe muli ibili. Balabaze bati, 2,4,8,10,12,14,16 nuko hasigala umuyembe umwe.  Zose hamwe zali imiyembe 17
P13	Barangije bagaba imiyembe mohagati hasigara imiyembe ibiri bayijana mulugo
P14	leka tugabane iyimiyembe muhagati. Mbesi buli muntu alabona imiyembe ingahe?
P15	Murako kanya ise wa Kama aragaruka avuye mugatalere ali guseka mumodoka ye nta mboga zirimo
P16	Ibyasobanuwe
