page_text:

P1	Arai Dongila echom loti apolon. Areit luche daang.
P2	Arai ng'esi ekatukon ang'ichomin a luche.
P3	Apotu ng'ichomin luche nakis ng'esi aworu na apolou.
P4	Sodi ng'esi tama, "Toenikisi nakou kang."
P5	Kichikak, "Kiwapa ng'akiro kang. Nyikitepega ayong!"
P6	Apotu kechi tonupa ekatukon kech. Na eporio dio toporo.
P7	Nakaneni, erijakinit aworu akou keng noi.
P8	Abu ng'esi tatap akou niapipil. Tatapa luche dang.
P9	Tama, "kiwani ibore en,"  tolimut luche kwanapei.
P10	"Kerijakinit ibore en!" Tolimut luche kwanapei.
P11	Tachakun Dongila alokipetot, tachakun luche dang.
P12	Towan ekatukon totoan. Towanas luche nabo apotu toyara.
