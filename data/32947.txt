title:	Umqasa wenzela indlovu amaqhinga,godu
author:	Busisiwe Nombuso and Slindile Immaculate Mtsweni
artist:	Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Endatjaneni le sifunda bona kubayini umqasa ugijima khulu begodu kuzenjani bona wenzele indlovu iqhinga ngehlaya elibuhlungu.

page_text:

P1	Indlovu nomqasa bebabangani abalungileko.
P2	Batholele iinlwana zabo amanzi notjani.
P3	Bebathanda ukudlala ibholo erarhwako.
P4	Indlovu yabetha amagondelo adluleleko.@@Umqasa azange uthabe.
P5	"Yini ekwenza bona wazi ukudlala ibholo?" kubuza umqasa."Milenze emikhulu."
P6	Umqasa nendlovu bareyisisa.Kwathumba umqasa.
P7	Yini ekwenza bona ukwazi ukugijima kangaka?,kubuza indlovu,"Milenze yami"
P8	"Ngingayithola njani imilenze ematsikana?",kubuza indlovu."Ngizokukhombisa"
P9	"Jama emlilweni",kutjho umqasa.Indlovu yajama,yalila,"Ngiyatjha!"
P10	"Ngiyoyodwa indlela yokwenza imilenze yakho ibematsikana", kwatjho umqasa.
P11	Indlovu yatjhida emlilweni. Yalala phasi.
P12	Kwadlula amalanga amanengi, indlovu ingakgoni ukujama.
P13	Yathi bona ibencono, yakhamba yaya ekhaya emndenini wayo.
P14	Umqasa wazisola khulu. Wabawa indlovu bona iwulibalele.
