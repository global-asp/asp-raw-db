desc:	Indatjana le imayelana nendoda encani yoburotho. Indoda le iphunyurhe epanini kagogo ebeyipholiswe ngaphandle yabaleka. Bazamile ukuyigijimisa kodwana babhalelwe. Ekugqineni indoda le ithole ukuphumula.

page_text:

P1	Ugogo wavuka ekuseni wazibhagela uburotho wathi asabupholisile bona bazokudla banomndenakhe kwazwakala iphimbo lisithi "vuka sikhambe" kweqa indoda encani yoburotho yalandela iviyaviyani.
P2	Umndeni kagogo waphuma woke wagijimisa indoda encani yoburotho. Indoda Â yazwakala ithi "hehe! Angekhe ningibambe."
P3	Kwathi kusenjalo bahlangana nepera yabagijimisa kodwana akhenge ibathole.
P4	Bathi basaphumula kwavela ujakalasi wabavusa wathi vukani nginikhombise indlela.
P5	Ujakalasi wabasiza bayama umlanjana.
P6	Nabaqeda ukuyama umlanjana iviyaviyani lathi ngizakusa lapha kukuhle begodu kuphephileko.
P7	Indoda yoburotho yafika endlini ehle yoburotho beyathola nomma omuhle woburotho bahlalisana kamnandi ngokuthula.
