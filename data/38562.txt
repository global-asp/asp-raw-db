desc:	The story is about two girls who are sisters.
One is playful while another likes doing constructive work.

page_text:

P1	Auma and Akinyi are sisters.@They love each other, they do their things together.@They are in their home jumping the rope.
P2	Akinyi's hobby is reading storybooks.@She likes reading when Auma is away.@She is lying on the ground enjoying reading her storybook.
P3	On week days Akinyi goes to school.@She likes to study and enjoys writing on the blackboard.@She has gone to write letters on the board.
P4	On weekends Akinyi goes to the market.@She likes helping her parents to sell fruits.@She is already at the market selling the fruits.
P5	Akinyi and Auma are friends.@Akinyi spends most of her time doing good work while Auma only likes playing.@Akinyi is a good girl.
