P1	Toyoyono woke today was Monday and the first day back at school but he didn't have a uniform for school so him and Mama went to the market
P2	He found so many interesting things they could buy there but they were only looking for school uniform
P3	Toyoyono want to be a seller when he grows up
P4	We need a school uniform mama said I'll give you $5 so you can pay for it yourself and if you have any extra money you can buy something you want
P5	He wore at the school uniform to school
P6	He did have extra money and bought a book for school he also bought a ruler his dad was making him lunch
