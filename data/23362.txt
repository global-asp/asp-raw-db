title:	umusi wigisirani
author:	Kagame Bruce
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Rufumbira

desc:	Umugani mwiza wa bahugu babiri bafashwe nabagirabi bashaka umutango.

page_text:

P1	Njeninshuti yanje Kiki ndusubiranga murugo namaguru.
P2	Umusi umwe, umugabo yarandukurikiye.
P3	Yarafite amenyo yumuhondo, nimiburu mumaso.
P4	Twaramurebye, nuko aradukabukana.
P5	kiki yashoboye kumuchika.
P6	Bantereye mukimodoka.
P7	Banshize mugisenge kirimo umwijima. Amaboko yanje yarahambiwe.
P8	Umugabo yarimbwiye, "Irya ibibyokurya ururugendo nirurerure."
P9	Narariye, umugabo arigutumura itabi.
P10	Mukanya, abandi bagabo baje bariukurura inshuti yanje Kiki.
P11	Twunvishe barigutongana mukindi gisenge.
P12	Kiki yarambiye, "Dufite gutoroka. ababyeyi bachu ntabo babishura"
P13	Twakuruye urubaho mudirisa mpaka tubonye urumuri.
P14	Kiki yaruriye, yiruka gushaka ubuyambyi.
P15	Abagabo baje mugisnge vubavuba. Ubakuriye arakaye
P16	Basubiye muri chagisenge bakomezamo gutongana.
P17	Nunvishe ijwi rimbira, "witinya. Abasirikare barihano."
P18	Baribarikuyomba urwari rurihejuru, nunva bateye amsasi.
P19	umusirikare yarambwiye "ubunta kibazo, burikimwe kiri mu nteko.
P20	Kuva ugo musi, nje na Kiki turigengeserezaga.
