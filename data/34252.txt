id:	34252
title:	Mukhwama wo no amba
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34252.png
desc:	Vhasidzana vhaṱuku vhararu vha ṱangana na ḽidyavhana ḽine ḽa ḓiita u nga ḽi khou ṱoḓa thuso ya u wana khuni.

page_text:

P1	Kale kale ho vha hu na ḽidyavhana ḽa musadzi. Musi ḽiṅwe ḓuvha ḽi tshi khou tshimbila tsini na mulambo, ḽa ṱangana na vhasidzana vhaṱuku vhararu.   @@Ḽidyavhana ḽa vhudzisa vhasidzana, "Ndi nnyi kha vhoiwe ane a funwa nga vhabebi vhawe vhoṱhe?"
P2	Muṅwe wa vhasidzana a ri, "Mme anga vha a mpfunesa vhukuma." Muṅwe a ri, "Khotsi anga vha a mpfunesa nga maanḓa." @@Fhedzi wa vhuraru a ri, "Nṋe ndi funwa nga vhabebi vhanga vhoṱhe."
P3	Ḽidyavhana ḽa vhudzisa musidzana ane a funwa nga vhabebi vhawe uri a ḽi thuse u hwala khuni. @@Nga zwenezwo musidzana a sala ḽidyavhana murahu.
P4	Vho tshimbila tshikhalanyana. Musidzana a vhudzisa ḽidyavhana, "Khuni dzau dzi ngafhi?" @@Ḽidyavhana ḽa fhindula, "Dzi tsini na heiḽa miri!"
P5	Musi vha tshi swika kha yeneyo miri, musidzana a dovha a vhudzisa, "Khuni dzau dzi ngafhi?" @@Ḽidyavhana ḽa fhindula, "A dzi ho kule!"
P6	Vha ṱangana na munna musi vhe nḓilani. A vhudzisa ḽidyavhana, "Ṅwana wau u pfi nnyi?" @@Ḽidyavhana ḽa fhindula, "U pfi mukhwama u ambaho."
P7	Musidzana a sima luimbo. @@A imba, "A thi pfi mukhwama u ambaho. Dzina ḽanga ndi Kaamungei!  Oh! Mme anga, vhane nda vha funa. Oh! Khotsi anga, vhane nda vha funa. Oh! Khavho yanga, ine nda i shumisa u nwa mafhi!"
P8	Musi munna a tshi pfa zwe musidzana a zwi imba, a mu tshidza nahone a mu humisela murahu kha vhabebi vhawe. @@Ha mbo ḓi vha u fa ha lungano!
