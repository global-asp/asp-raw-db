page_text:

P1	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ahubeghi m pusi m.@  @@  Enweghi  ebe@  ọbụla m nwere ike ihu pusi@  m.
P2	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @  Ọ nọ n'okpuru akwa?@  @  Mba, osi n'ime ụlọ agbapụta.
P3	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ọ nọ n’elu kọbọd?@  @@  Mba, Ọnọ n’okwuru@  shelfu?
P4	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ọnọ n’azu oche?@  @@  Mba, ọ nọ na-mbara@  ezi.
P5	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @  Ọ nọ n'akụkụ ebe ana-etinye@  ihe rụrụ uṅyi?@  @  Mba, osi na mpio ụlọ amapụ.
P6	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ọ nọ n’ime nkata?@  @@  Mba, Ọ nọ n’ime@  igbe.
P7	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ọ nọ n’azụ ụlọ?@  @@  Mba, Ọ nọ n’azụ@  akwa mgbochi.
P8	Ebee ka pusi m nọ?@  @@  Ebea ka Ọ nọ.@  @@  Ọ zoro n’ime kọbọd a!
