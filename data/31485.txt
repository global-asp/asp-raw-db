title:	Kalabushe kuuyunga unene
author:	Djaupyu  Siteketa
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Rukwangali
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31485.png
desc:	Kalabushe munambili nokuhara kudiva yuma age hena kwa hara kuuyunga.

page_text:

P1	Siruwo saka pita nare, kwa kere mukadona gedina Kalabushe. @@Kalabushe nga uyunga unene. Nampili ngomu vamu rondwere ozina asi gaha uyunga unene, Kalabushe kapi ga purakenene.
P2	Zinagona zaKalabushe kwatungire mesinya mwina lyomuramba lyomomukunda gwaKalabushe. @@Ezuva limwe ga verere po. Ano kwato gumwe ogu gamu tekwire.
P3	Ozina va Kalabushe kwa kere vana lipyakidire unene. Konyima zosiruwo kongurova yipo gaya twere Kalabushe nondya kozinagona ava va verere.
P4	Apa ga zire, Kalabushe ga gwanekere naSinson. Sinson kwa kere embungu eli ngali lisintunuka morupe romuntu.
P5	Sinson kwapulire Kalabushe, "Yinke ono simbi?" Kalabushe kwa limbwilire, "Ame kuna simbi nyama, mauta nomasini." @@Ozina vaKalabushe vamu rondwere asi age gaha tanta asi yinke ga simbire.
P6	Kalabushe simpe hena ga uyungire, "Ame kwa kutwara nondya konanegona vana kuvera."@@Sinson kwa lipehere konomurungu dalyo kegazaro lyozo nyama ga simbire Kalabushe.
P7	Sinson kwa dukire usimbu komeho zaKalabushe koko kembo lyozinagona vendi.
P8	Makura age taka mina ozinagona vaKalabushe nokulikudumika monguwo zawo.
P9	Apa ga kasikire Kalabushe, embo kwa kere tete sili unene. Age kwa zire monda nokuzigida, "Nane, kupi muna kara?"
P10	Kalabushe kapi ga zuvhire ezwi lyozinagona. Makura age kwa zire monda zonzugo omu ngava rara ozinagona. @@Yamu tetukisire apa ga mwene muntu ogu gali kudumikire monguwo zonene.
P11	Kalabushe ga pulire, "Nane, morwasinke matwi geni gana nenepa ngoso neina?"@@Sinson ga limbwilire konhi zonguwo zonene, "Yipo nikuzuvhe nawa-nawa."
P12	Kalabushe simpe ga pulire hena, "Nane, Morwasinke meho geni gana nenepa unene ngoso neina?"@@Sinson kwa limbwilire, "Yipo nikumone nawa-nawa."
P13	Kalabushe moruhura ga pulire, "Nane, morwasinke kana keni kana nenepa unene ngoso neina?"@@Sinson ga limbwilire, "Yipo ni vhure kukulya po!" Alyo makura kwa vatukire mombete nokuya mina Kalabushe.
P14	Kalabushe simpe ga twikilire kuuyunga apa gakere mezimo lyaSinson. Ga pulire mapuro gomanzi.
P15	Kouhura, Sinson gaya rorokere komapuro gomanzi gaKalabushe yipo lya tokwere limu fizemo.
P16	Kalabushe nozinagona vanamukunda vava poperere.@@Kutunda ezuva olyo, Kalabushe kapi hena ngauyunga unene kwava gadilire kudiva.
