desc:	Kuhusu namna Sokwe aliepuka kuliwa na Mamba.

page_text:

P1	Sokwe anamkuta Mamba kando ya ziwa.
P2	Sokwe anauliza, “Unaishi wapi?” Anajibu, “Ziwani.”
P3	Mamba anauliza, “Unaishi wapi?” Anasema, “Kwenye miti.”
P4	Mamba anauliza, “Unaweza kuogelea?”
P5	“Siwezi,” Sokwe anajibu. Mamba anasema, “Nitakufunza."
P6	Mamba anasema, “Sisi ni marafiki. Usiogope.”
P7	Mamba anasema, “Mjomba ni mgonjwa. Anahitaji
nyama.”
P8	Sokwe ameogopa sana. Je, Mamba atamkula?
P9	Sokwe anataka kumtoroka Mamba.
P10	Sokwe anakuwa na wazo.
P11	“Nitautoa moyo wangu umpatie,” Sokwe anaahidi.
P12	“Moyo wangu uko kwenye mti,” Sokwe anasema.
P13	“Utauleta moyo wako?” Sokwe anajibu, “Ndiyo.”
P14	Mamba anaogelea kutoka majini. Sokwe anakimbilia miti.
P15	“Wewe si rafiki yangu. Ulinidanganya!” Mamba anasema.
P16	“Hatuko marafiki. Unataka kunila! Sokwe anajibu.
