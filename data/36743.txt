P1	Once apon a time a boy asked how can you be energetic.
P2	The teacher answered first I eat fruits from I o'clock to 2 o'clock.
P3	I read books from 4 o'clock to 7 o'clock.
P4	I play skip rope from 2 o'clock to 4 o'clock the end
