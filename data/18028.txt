desc:	Mma ga a gona gomme tate o apea motepa wa go fihlola.

page_text:

P1	Zama o itokišetša go ya sekolong.
P2	Tate o apea motepa wa fihlilo.
P3	"Motepa wa mma ke wo mobose kudu!" gwa bolela Zama.
P4	Motepa o fa Zama maatla a kua sekolong.
P5	Motepa o lokile.
P6	Tate o tšhela maswi,  ka lerato.
P7	"Tate, ke nagana gore motepa wo o hloka swikiri."
P8	"Tatso ya motepa wa mma e kaone go phala tatso ya motepa wa tate."
P9	"Tate,  ke kgopela swikiri gape hle," gwa kgopela Zama.
P10	Tate a fa Zama swikiri gape.
P11	"Iyo! Tate! Le tšhetše letswai ka motepeng wa ka!"
P12	"Tate,  na mma o tla boa neng gae?" gwa botšiša Zama.
