title:	Wakaduuyu Owobungodera

desc:	Langi mu kitabo ekyEnfumo. Wakaduuyu kabiri ali kukola obungodera.

page_text:

P1	Lulala,Wakaduuyu yafuluma okwagala emere. Yaayagaana Wandhovu eyali n'esugha ey'omudheenene ogw'endhuki. Wakaduuyu yaasaba Wandhovu ati: "Ndhuna ombeekeku. Nnafiighe."
P2	Wandhovu yaaganha okubeekaku Wakaduuyu. Wakaduuyu yeebeesa ku mugongo gwa Wandhovu yeegema yeekalangula. Butereevu yaatoolera okunhwa omudheene gwa Wandhovu!
P3	Omudheenene gwatoolera okutoonha ku mugongo gwa  Wandhovu. Wandhovu bwe yabuuza ekyo kye kyali, Wakaduuyu yamwiramu ati kyali kiri kuva mu ibwalye.
P4	Baatuuka ku mwiga baayagaana embogo enkologho nga eri kunhwa amaadhi.
P5	Wakaduuyu yaayenda okumanha awula maani ku Wambogo ni ku Wandhovu.
P6	Baatoolera okutakana. Nga Wandhovu akoba ati naawula maani, nga ni Wambogo akoba ati mwene naawula maani.
P7	Wakaduuyu yaakoba ati ali n'engeri yaaghanga okuzuulamu oyo awula maani. Agho yaabakoba bamuteegereku agho.
P8	Wakaduuyu yaira n'omugugha omuleeyi. Yaasiba oluuyi olundi olw'omugugha mu nkende ya Wandhovu era yaamutoola gha mwiga.
P9	Wakaduuyu yaasiba oluuyi olundi olw'omugugha mu nkende ya Wambogo.era yaamuleka kumpi n'omwiga.
P10	Wakaduuyu yaalumuka yeegisa mu muti. Agho yaaleekaana ati: "Mwetegeke! Ndala, ibiri, isatu! Sika!"
P11	Wambogo ni Wandhovu baasika. Wandhovu yaasika Wambogo.
P12	Wambogo yaasika Wandhovu. Ghaabula aghangula mwine.
P13	Agho Wakaduuyu ghe yeegisa, yaja mu maiso n'okunhwa omudheeene gwa Wandhovu.
P14	Oluliira olwokumalagho omudheenene, yasala omugugha agho okumpi ni Wambogo.
P15	Wandhovu yaagwa ere yeekuba ku muti  omukalangufu
P16	Agho Wakaduuyu yaaja ye Wambogo yaamusaaka yaamukoba ng bwali ow'amaani okuwula Wandhovu.
