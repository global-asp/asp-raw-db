desc:	Anyazia nɛ kɔɔ we mi na ko nɛ a suɔ wawɛɛ he.

page_text:

P1	Ndalo ji nɛ.
P2	Ndalo suɔ womi kanemi.
P3	Pendo ji nɛ.
P4	Pendo suɔ karɔti kpemi.
P5	Mother woke up Akai the following morning. Kitonyou akimat Akai nataparac nace.
P6	Ndalo ngɔɔ Pendo kɛyaa nga kpemi ke akpa sukuu.
P7	Akai and her mother went to the market. Toloto Akai ka itokeng ni imucuo usese.
P8	E haa Pendo nyu mua.
P9	Akai and her mother went into a shoe shop. Akai itokec to lomasi.
P10	Paa haa Pendo ngwia.
P11	E ngaa Pendo nyɔnyu daa ligbi.
P12	Ana anyɔnyu fuu ngɛ Pendo nyɔ mi.
P13	Papa jua Pendo nyɔnyu ɔ.
P14	Ndalo nuu na nyɔnyu ɔ daa ligbi.
P15	E de ɔ ke,  "Pendo mo tsumi!"
