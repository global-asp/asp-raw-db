desc:	A story about a much loved family cow.

page_text:

P1	Jina langu ni Ndalo.
P2	Baba yangu humfuga ng'ombe aitwaye Pendo.
P3	Pendo hupenda kula nyasi siku nzima.
P4	Wakati mwingine baba humpa Pendo nafaka.
P5	Pendo pia hunywa maji mengi.
P6	Mimi ninampenda Pendo.
P7	Baba humkama Pendo. Sisi humtunza Pendo naye hutusaidia.
P8	Pendo hutupa maziwa mengi.
P9	Mimi hunywa maziwa mengi.
P10	Baba yangu huuza maziwa na hutumia pesa hizo kuninunulia vitabu vya hadithi.
P11	Mimi hupenda kusoma hadithi.
P12	Je,  umefahamu kwa nini ninampenda ng'ombe wetu Pendo?
