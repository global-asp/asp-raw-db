P1	Once apon a time there was a hunter . Who lived near a jungle
P2	The Hunter had the power to change into any animal of his choice
P3	One day there was an elephant . He wanted to know the Hunter's secret
P4	So then the elephant disguised him self  as a man and went to meet with the Hunter.
P5	At night the elephant was with the Hunter. They drank, and drank,and drank .
P6	Until then the man asked the Hunter about his secret and the Hunter said"my secret is turning into any animal"
P7	The next day the elephant turned into a tiger then the Hunter turned into a cheetah then the elephant turned into a leopard then the Hunter turned into a fly and the elephant could not see him
P8	Then the Hunter hit the giraffe and took it home and made a braai.               The end@                       By: altor             Jonathan Mushonga
