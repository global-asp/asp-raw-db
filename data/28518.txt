desc:	Abantu abandhi baaya ebisolo mumaka gaibwe aye tibafayo kulabilira bisolo okwonhona emere yabantu abandhi. Bulimuntu atekwa okuba nobuvanhizibwa ku buli kintu kye.

page_text:

P1	Mudde nhi Kiza ba mulinhano.
P2	Mudde alina ente nhingi kwilundhiro lye.
P3	Kizza alina ebimera ebyendhaghulo mu nhimiro ye.
P4	Kizza yabonha ente.yadhigema yadhisiba.yalabula Mudde abe mwegendhereza ku bisolo bye.
P5	yamusubiza okumutwala mu bobuyinza,singa ente dhiiramu okulya emere ye.
P6	lulala olweigulo ente dha Mudde dhailayo mu nhimiro ya Kizza.era dhaalya ebimera bye byonhabyona.
P7	kulunho Kizza tiyalindha.yalumuka mangu okutegeza abobuyinza.abobuyinza batyaamya olukio.Mudde bamusasuza eseente nhingi inho.
P8	ab'obuyinza batyaamya olukiko lwekyaalo.Mudde bamusasuza sente nhingi inho ol'obugayavu bwe.
P9	abatyamye bekyaalo bayega okufaayo kubisolo byaibwe.
