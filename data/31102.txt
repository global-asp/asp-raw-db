title:	Omugeere yayagiirye omukagwa
author:	Mpala Paul
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Lulamoogi
level:	First words

desc:	Olufumo olughoomu olukwatagana okumukwano omuyaaka aghakati gha omwisuka no ombwa.

page_text:

P1	Omugeere yabbaire musomi mugezi. Yaali mwana mukulu.
P2	Lwali lumo, Omugeere nayabaku okuzaanya ne emikwano gye.
P3	Ombwa omutomuto yatiiremu. "Ngiza kukuyamba," Omugeere yakobere.
P4	Ombwa yasamire, yakinire era nakomba omugeere neisanyu.
P5	Emikwano gya Mugeere gya gudirye ombwa. "mulekere!" Omugeere yabaasire.
P6	Ombwa yapyatiregho. Yekwekere okubaisuka ababbiibbi.
P7	Abemikwano bayagiirye omugeere akunga. Nibakoba, "katunoonie ombwawo."
P8	Omugeere yaali abula suubi. Emikwano gye gyakobere, "aiza kumwagirya."
P9	Banoonarye buli ghantu. Omugeere nakoba, "ayinzya okubba nga ali ku mwiga."
P10	Ombwa yatiire abaisuka. Omugeere nayeta, "totuuma!"
P11	Ombwa yapyatire egyomugeere. Baali basanyufu okubba aghamo.
P12	Abemikwano bakobere, "otusonyiwe. Eiza kubba mukwano gwaisu twena."
