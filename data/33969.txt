title:	Kumusha kunei?

desc:	This is a brief story of what a child who has never been to the rural areas should expect to see in the rural areas.

page_text:

P1	Kumusha kune hari. Hari dzinobikirwa kana kuchengeterwa mvura kana maheu kana doro.
P2	Kumusha kunobikwa chingwa nemarasha. Chingwa chacho chinonzi chimodho.
P3	Kumusha kunomera hohwa. Hohwa hunonaka kunge nyama.
P4	Kumusha kunorimwa nzungu. Nzungu dzinokangwa kuti dzigadziriswe dovi.
P5	Kumusha kunokamwa mukaka wemombe. Mukaka wakakora unonaka nesadza.
P6	Kumusha vanowurutsa chibage kuti chibve marara. Vanoendesa chibage kuchigayo kuti chiite upfu.
P7	Kumusha kune miti yakawanda inobereka vana vanoshamisa. Muti uyu unonzi musekesa.
P8	Kumusha kune nzira dzetsoka. Nzira dzekumusha dzinoenda kutsime, kudanga, kurwizi kana kuvavakidzani.
P9	Kumusha kune dzimba dzakapfirirwa nehuswa. Kupfirira zvinoreva kuvhara denga kuti mvura isapinde kana kuchinaya.
