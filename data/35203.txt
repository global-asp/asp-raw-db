desc:	Every day is a new beginning for Fati

page_text:

P1	Fati omotete r’ ọmọtẹ@  rọ jugbonẹ ki roju.O ph’ẹguọnọ@  kpai ewhẹ mamọ,obo ravuẹrẹ@  ọye oruẹ.
P2	Ẹdẹvo vwo te,Fati@  v’oni rọye kugbẹ ọsẹ rọye@  de kp’irhe
P3	Fati ph’ẹguọnọ rọvwọ@  ch’oni v’ọsẹ r’ọye uko mamọ,@  ẹkẹvuọvo, ewhian v’ ughe v’otọ. Emevra@  ve ewerin kugbẹ ọren evetiyi@  kẹ.
P4	Oni rọye tane, Fati, kerhọn@  wẹ,yanra wọnra gwọnirhe she
P5	Ẹkẹvuọvo,noma wẹn@  so.
P6	Oni vẹ ọsẹ r’ Fati phuvhẹ@  re kidia ọvuọvo o
P7	Aye mhwẹ irhe ride v’itutuno kugbe
P8	Fati ji phuvhe,@  o ji mhe irhe itutuno kugbe
P9	Fati ko nenu.Ọda@  mrẹ ọmọvra ọvwavware ọvo enu rẹ ato.
P10	Fati kọ rhoma notọ, ọda mrẹ@  irhirhi obiebi ọvo
P11	Fati kọje mrẹ ẹbe oyoyovwi ọvo ro no wrowro,@  oda vwobọ rọye te
P12	Fati da ta nẹ ẹbe nana họhẹ@  ebe rẹ, siẹvuọvo, ẹbe oyoyovwi na de kpokpoma! Fati da tanẹ, ọnana@  d’ẹbe oyoyovwi rẹ. Onana ọrọdekọ@  r’urhuoma ẹbe
P13	Fati ko bunu, ‘Eii!’,ọda jẹ br’oni v’ọsẹ rọye ra
P14	Fati ko bunu, nẹ ọrọdekọ! ọrọdekọ!, ọsẹ rϙ ye nẹ k’ọrhọ? Fati@  de je kẹ
P15	Ọse rọ ye da vwẹ urhe vwọtuẹ@  ọrọdekọ na, ẹkẹvuọvo, ọrọdekọ na da jẹ@  kpo
P16	Ọse rọ ye da tanẹ, emuovwo gbi ke ruooo,@  aye da’yan br’oni rọye, dara vuẹ@  kpahẹ ọrọdekọ na
P17	Oni rọye datanẹ me ghoghore nẹ ọrọdekọ na@  rhovwo wẹ rẹ
P18	Ẹkẹvuọvo, nomaso ẹdẹfa
