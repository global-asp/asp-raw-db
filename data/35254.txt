P1	Love from a distance.@@Don't get too close!
P2	Sneeze or cough inside your elbow.@@Don't spray your germs!
P3	Stay away from others if you are sick.
P4	Soap with water kills the coronavirus.@@Wash your hands often.
P5	Wash your hands for 20 seconds.
P6	Greet with your elbow.@@Greet with your toes.@@Greet with a wave.
P7	Wear a clean mask every day.@@Cover your nose and mouth.
P8	Don't give this coronavirus any chance to spread!
