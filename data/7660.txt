page_text:

P1	Joy played all the day.
P2	Her mother told her to wash.
P3	She picked up her tooth brush.
P4	Her mother put water in basin for her.
P5	She dried her face.
P6	She joined her sister to sleep.
P7	In the morning she brushed her teeth.
P8	Her mother helped her dress up.
P9	She tied her shoes.
P10	Joy goes to school.
P11	Joy is in class.
