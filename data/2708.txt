desc:	In this story we look at the different kinds of teeth animals have and what happens to our teeth when we start to grow up.

page_text:

P1	Eyio ma si yi `wara.
P2	Kami ma si vini `wara.
P3	Ti ma si ci.
P4	Aria yi ma si yo.
P5	Edro yi ma si andraleru `diyi `wara.
P6	Kasurubana yi ma si ci aci.
P7	Ma si yi oku tre.
P8	Te curu`do ma si ovuni tre tu ku.
