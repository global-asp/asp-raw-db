title:	Cats and dogs in the house
author:	Farida Abdullahi

page_text:

P1	Mallam Jungudu has a cat called Minos. @@Minos lives in the house with her children.
P2	Mallam Jungudu also has a dog called Danger. @@He also lives in the house.
P3	Minos and Danger sometimes eat together.
P4	There are many lizards in the house. Minos loves eating them. Danger loves pursuing them too. @@Sometimes Danger helps Minos to catch lizards.
P5	One night, a thief came into the house. He stole some items and tied them in a big cloth. @@Danger saw him. He barked and barked but the thief had a big stick. Danger was afraid of the stick.
P6	Minos and her son named Manny heard Danger barking. @@They woke up and went to see.
P7	The thief saw the cats coming. He was afraid of cats. @@He dropped the stick and the items and ran.
P8	In the morning, Mallam Jungudu and the family saw the items the thief left. @@They we happy that the cats and the dog lived in the house.
