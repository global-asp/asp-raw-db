page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, Ladi and Ummi lived in a village where girls were not allowed to go to school. Ladi and Ummi choose to go to school and read their books.
P2	They finished their  primary and secondary education, and went to the same University and graduated.
P3	They got jobs in the City, later went back to their village, and built class room in their community.
P4	They also gave poor women money to start trade, with. They became famous and every parent in the village wanted girls to be like Ladi and Ummi.
