desc:	Lwali lulala omwana yaalimba ise. Ebyavaamu ti byali bilungi. Ologhooza okulimba kulungi?

page_text:

P1	Omwana wange mulimba.
Namutuma okulima yaannimba.
P2	Omwana wange mulimba.
Namutuma ku ntuuma yaatuuma.
P3	Omwana wange mulimba.
Namutuma ku naikonto yaankoota.
P4	Omwana wange mulimba.
Bwe yaja mu bikaadho yaadooka.
P5	Omwana wange mulimba.
Bwe yabona kyapati yaadhikyapa.
