page_text:

P1	Fana n'umudyango we batuye muri Debre Birhan,umugi utuwe cyane muri Ehiopia. Fana n'umunyeshuri womukiciro 3. kandi afite umutima mwiza kandi n'umuhanga cyane.
P2	Fana akunda amatungo. Afite ipusi, inkoko 2, ihene n'inuma
P3	Umunsi umwe,Fana yarimwo gukina kwi shuri mu kibuga n'ishuti ze ubwo  yabonye abandi bana barimwo gutera inuma amabuye.
"kubera iki barimwo kuyikubita?" aribaza.
P4	Yahiye arekera aho ukina nuko yirukanka abasanga
Mumizi yambare inshuti ze ntizamenye icyari kibaye maze nabo baramukurikira. "Murekere aho kujugunya amabuye vuba" avugira hejuru n'umujinya.
P5	Ba bana bahise biruka.
Fana afata inuma ebyiri bari bakomerekeje. Abona ibisebe ku mababa yazo. Ahitamo kuyatahana iyiteho
P6	Arayagaburira. Mu mugoroba abwira umudyango we ibyo yabonye n'ibyo yakoze uwo munsi n'ukunutu yakijije za numa
P7	Nuko mugotondo Fana n'ababyeyi be  bajya kwa muganga bagura imiti yaza numa.
 Hashize iminsi,za numa zirorehedwa na byabisebe birakira. Fana arishima cyane
P8	Fana burigihe yabwiraga inshuti ze ati, ''Nkunda inyamanswa n'amatungo. Ni byiza kandi bigira urukundo. Niyo mpanvu dukwiriye kuzirinda no kuzirokora.
