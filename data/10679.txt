desc:	Abebusi babu Mukhwana nende Mulongo bafwa babalekha okhuikhabira efiakhulia abeene. Balindwa obubi muno nende khotsa wabu kho niberukha batsia khumenya mwibina. Bali nende oburi okhubera bali nibaulira mbu amanani kali nikamenya mufikulu. Mukhwana yarulanga elwanyi okhuyiima na Mulongo yekhalanga mwibina. Mukhwana yabolera omukhana wabu mbu alatekha inyama tawe khulwa amanani kachama isami ye inyama itekhwa. Mulonga alakhola nga abolerwa? Soma olole shiokheleshera Mukhwana nende Mulongo.
