P1	There lived a poor man in Runogone village called Juma.He had a friend known as Kora. Kora was a good man to Juma and all the villagers liked him. @Juma had a very big farm but he did not plant anything.
P2	Every day Juma begged for food from the villagers but no one gave him food except Kora and two old women.
P3	"Go and look for job before you die of hunger" Kora told his friend Juma.@ "I have been looking for a job but I can not find any"Juma answered. "Stop being lazy my friend" Kora said to Juma. The next day Juma went to look for  farming job.
P4	Juma harvested ten sacks of beans and twenty sacks of maize. He sold some beans and maize at the market and get got a lot of money.From that day,Juma became a hard-working farmer and he never went hungry again.
