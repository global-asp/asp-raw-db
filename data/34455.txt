desc:	Ingabe Ajao lilulwane uyinyoni nobe uligundvwane?

page_text:

P1	Ajao lilulwane, bekalele endlini yakhe agula kakhulu. @@Bekute muntfu lomnakako.
P2	Ekugcineni, Ajao wafa.
P3	Bomakhelwane batsi, "Ajao ufile. Kufanele sibite tihlobo takhe kute tite titomngcwaba."
P4	Bahamba bayobita tinyoni batsi, "Sihlobo senu sifile."
P5	Tinyoni tefika tase tibona kutsi ngu Ajao. @@Tatsi, "Lesilwane asisiyo incenye yemndeni wetfu."
P6	"Bonkhe emndenini wetfu banetinsiba kepha Ajao ute. Akasuye lomunye wetfu." @@Tinyoni tahamba.
P7	Bomakhelwane babonisana. @@Bavumelana, "Tinyoni ticinisile. Ajao ute tinsiba. Ngako, akasiso sihlobo sato."
P8	Lomunye wabo watsi, "Kufanele kutsi ungumndeni wemagundvwane ngobe ufanana nawo."
P9	Bahamba bayobita emagundvwane batsi, "Lomunye wemndeni wenu ufile."
P10	Emagundvwane efika ase abona kutsi ngu Ajao. @@Atsi, "Lesilwane asisiyo incenye yemndeni wetfu."
P11	"Bonkhe emndenini wetfu banemsila. Ajao ute umsila." @@Emagundvwane ahamba.
P12	Kute tihlobo ta Ajao letatfolakala futsi akazange angcwatjwe.
