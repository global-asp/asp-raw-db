desc:	Omusiani akhaba buli habundu akhapao khakhe. Alakhanyoola?

page_text:

P1	Engosiye akhapao khanje. Ne ekhairwe okhukhanyola.
P2	Ekhabire inyuma wo muliango. Shikhabereyo tawe.
P3	Ekhabire khumuseretsa. Ekhanyolirekho ta.
P4	Ekhabire mulwalo weshitali. Khali hao,  khabulao.
P5	Ekhabire mukari welisanduku. Khali hao, khabulaho
P6	Ekhabire mushilama mwosi.
P7	Ekhabire ano,  alia nende buli abundu,  senyolirekho abundu wosi tawe.
