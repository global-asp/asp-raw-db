page_text:

P1	Makhe ndikubalisele ibali lam.@@Ndiqala njengentlantsi encinci.
P2	Ndiyanwenwa xa umoya undiqhuba.
P3	Singabahlobo benene, mna nawe.
P4	Ndipheka ukutya kwakho.
P5	Ndiyakhanyisa ubusuku bakho.
P6	Ndinamaqhinga amaninzi.
P7	Ndiyinxalenye yosapho lwakho.
P8	Kodwa lumka xa undimemela kwikhaya lakho. @@Andithandi ukushiywa ndodwa.
