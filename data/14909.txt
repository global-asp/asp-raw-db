title:	Butandwa Eyakabi
author:	Musinguzi Moses
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Rutooro
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/14909.png
desc:	Abaana babiri barora butandwa ye kiroole nakamotoka akataito.

page_text:

P1	Kiro kimu nyowe namunyaanya omwisiki tukaba niturubata haihi noruguudo.
P2	Ekimotoka kikaija nikisirimuka hakasozi.
P3	Akamotoko akataito kaali nikairuka enziriga nikatemba harusozi.
P4	Omuvugi wekiroole akaba nabaliza hasiim. Atakurora muruguudo.
P5	Ekiroole kikatomera akamotoka!
P6	Ambulensi ya poliisi yaija kuyamba.
P7	Nebimotoka bibi ebikooto byaija. Ekimu kyatwaraekimoyoka. Endi yatwara akataito.
P8	Tukarora buli kintu. Tukahunirra muno.
