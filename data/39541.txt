page_text:

P1	Hi, my name is Kaftsui and I am from Kannauj, India. Known as the perfume city for it's perfumes. Also, I have a wonderful family, a super family! Let me take you to my house to tell you everything in person. Let's go!
P2	This is my papi. He works as a restaurant owner. He works very hard for our family and loves us so much! I love him so much! My mami helps him too! She is also the same except she's a bit smarter than him. I love her too!
P3	My twin sister, Saftsui and my little brother, Didieni are so nice! We love each other no matter what! But Didieni and my parents find it hard to tell me and my sister apart.
P4	So that's why I think I have a super hero family. It might seem ordinary to you, but I have the best super hero family ever! Although I wished we had super powers?! Bye reader! Also, try to go to Kannauj, India for perfume.
