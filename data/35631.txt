page_text:

P1	Titi is a beautiful girl.@@She lives in Opobo village.
P2	Titi lives with her grandmother.@@She has been longing to visit her parents.
P3	Grandmother decided to take her to Bausa. @@Titi was happy to see her family and friends.
P4	One day, Grandmother sent Titi to the market.@@She gave her some money.
P5	On her way to the market, Titi saw Donkey, the trickster.
P6	Donkey saw Titi with money and asked her to clap her hands.@@ Titi did so and the money fell down.
P7	Donkey, the trickster, quickly took the money and ran away.
P8	Titi called for help. @@People helped her to get back the money from Donkey.
