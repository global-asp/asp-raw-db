desc:	Dont be too quick to enter a fight! Colour-in storybook. (Also available at Level 3.).

page_text:

P1	Kudukarea-a kǝnyeri-a kunguna mǝradǝzana.
P2	Rokko kulo baregada yadǝm kasuwun ladoro.
P3	Kudukarea kasuwun luwala curo.
P4	Kǝnyeri-a Jibda-a zǝgadin.
P5	Kudukareaye Yiljinnaro, "Sawaniya kolle," wono.
P6	Jibdaye kǝnza Kudukareabe timin jigǝroga.
P7	Kǝnzanzǝ namgata dǝga sarjin.
P8	Kǝnza Kudukareabe dungokti dalil azalzǝ dungoktǝdǝ luwalaro kargawo.
