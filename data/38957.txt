P1	Bruno and Ella, are best friends since they were in kindergarten. They love to play football together during their free time. Since school just ended, they got a lot of time to play together.
P2	Nina, a football coach conduct a meeting. She suggest a try out for her football team. Since Ella is a girl she ask Nina if she can take the try out. So, Nina allow Ella and her friend Bruno participate on the try out.
P3	The next day, Ella and Bruno went to the football field. Ella show's her skills in handling the ball. Nina was impress with Ella's energetic tricks together with the support of Bruno.
P4	The elemination round was successfull. It is now time to announce the names for those who are chosen during the try outs. Both Ella and Bruno are excited for the result. They are confident that their names will be written on the list.
P5	Nina was so happy that she was able to select the best members for her football team. All the members were happy as they receive their new uniform. The team is ready now to play for a competition. Hope they can get a trophy.
