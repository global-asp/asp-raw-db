page_text:

P1	Eizoobarimwe hakaba hariho omwishikyi owubarikweeta Emily. Emily akaba ayine ekyeezo.
P2	Mama weye kuyabeire amushaba kumukorera ekintu kyoona akaba ayanga.
P3	Eizooba rimwe mama weye akaba arweire. Yamushaba ngu agende owa nyinenkuru agyire omubazi oguyamuretera obundi akagunywa abaho kurungyi. Kwonka okugira ngu Emily akaba ayine ekyeezo, akasharamu yaaza kwooga omunyanja kuyabeire ariyo naaza owa nyinenkuru.
P4	Bukeira nyina yatandika kwerarikirira.
P5	Mama weye akeeta abantu bomukyaaro kumuyamba ahakuronda Emily.
P6	Akaba ayerarikiriire ahakuba hakaba hariho kifaaru eyabeire nerya abaishikyi bato omukiro.
P7	Abantu kubabeire nibaronda Emily, akaba akiriyo nayeshemezamu omwiziba.
P8	Bukaba buri obweire bwakyakiro kya kifaru, yatandika kuronda ekyokurya. Kuyabeire netambura  yanunkiriza akaishikyi kato. Abantu bomukyaro bakaba batandika kwirira akaziba
P9	Emily akareeba kifaaru neija aho naho yatandika kuteera orwaari. Abantu bomukyaaro bakahurira akaishikyi kato nikateera orwari nikwe batandika kweiruka barikuza ahi bahureire orwari.
P10	Abantu bomukyaaro bakashanga Emily akiri omu kaziba,ayine obwoba. Kandi kuruga obwe akeega okuhurikiza kandi akateeka ekitinisa omuri mama weye.
