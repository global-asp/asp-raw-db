page_text:

P1	Namu and her brother are ready for school.
P2	Namu carries her brother on her bicycle.
P3	Their friends cheer for them as they ride into school.
P4	While they are in the classroom,  a monkey arrives outside. The monkey rides away on Namu's bicycle!
P5	The children chase the monkey.
P6	The monkey leaves the bicycle under a tree.
P7	The monkey swims to an island in the middle of the lake. He climbs high into a tree.
P8	The monkey waves goodbye! The children wave back. Namu has her bicycle again.
