title:	Bhazi Hombe reBhuruu

page_text:

P1	Mumusha waigara Ebei maive ne bhazi rimwe chete. Raive bhazi hombe rebhuruu. Raiita ruzha rwakanyanya.
P2	"Mangwana tichaenda kudhorobha," vakadaro amai va Ebei. "Tichanotenga hembe dzako dzekuchikoro."
P3	Ebei akafara zvikuru. Vaizoenda ne bhazi hombe rebhuruu. Haana kurara husiku uhu nomufaro.
P4	Pakauya amai vake kuzomumutsa, Ebei akange atopfeka kare.
P5	Ebei na aimai vake vakafamba kuenda kuchiteshi chemabhazi. Vakamirira bhazi hombe rebhuruu. Asi bhazi harina kuuya.
P6	Vanhu vakatanga kusvika pachiteshi chemabhazi. Vakanyunyuta noku nonoka kwebhazi. "Bhazi riripi ko?" vakabvunza.
P7	Ebei akashushikana. "Tichatadza kuenda kudhorobha," akafunga kudaro. "Ndichatadza kuwana hembe dzangu dzechikoro."
P8	Vamwe vanhu vakasviba mwoyo vakadzokera kumba. Ebei akachema. Haana kuda kudzokera. "Tichamirira kwechinguva," vakadaro amai vake.
P9	Pakarepo vakanzwa ruhza. Vakaona huruva mudenga. Bhazi rakange rave kuuya!
P10	Asi bhazi rakange risiri rebhuruu. Rakange risiri hombe. Raive tsvuku uye raive diki. Vanhu vaisada kupinda mubhazi iri.
P11	"Pindai! Pindai!" akadanidzira mutyairi. "Nhasi tanonoka," akadaro.
P12	Ebei na amai vake ndivo vakatanga kupinda. Vanhu vese vakapinda mubhazi diki tsvuku.
P13	Ebei akatarisa panze nepafasitera. Aka ona vanhu vakawanda pa chiteshi chema bhazi.
P14	Vanhu vakawanda vaimhanyira bhazi. Asi vakanga vanonoka. Bhazi tsvuku rakanga razara. Rakasimuka kuenda kudhorobha.
P15	"Ko ririkupi bhazi hombe 
rebhuruu?" vakabvunza amai va 
Ebei. "Rakafa," akapindura mutyairi. "Tirikurigadzirisa. Richauya mangwana."
P16	Ebei haana kuira hanya neruvara rwebhazi. Haana kuita hanya nekukura kwaro. Chero bhazi raienda kudhorobha!
