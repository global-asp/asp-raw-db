desc:	Have you ever seen a cow with only one horn and no tail?

page_text:

P1	Heeeee! There is a beautiful cow at home.
P2	It has only one horn,  and no tail.
P3	It is fat,  with a big stomach.
P4	It is a powerful cow.
P5	The shepherd loves this cow the most.
P6	When the other cattle graze,  this one eats alone.
P7	This cow is a fighter. Other cows leave it alone.
P8	If you met such a cow,  what would you do?
