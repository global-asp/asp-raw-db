page_text:

P1	Eira einho, Wampiti ni Wakayima baali ba mukwano inho. Bombi baali baavu ayenga Wakayima yali ni endogoyiye. Yali ni ebbaafu nga ntono, yeyakozesanga  okuliisa endogoyiye.
P2	Lwali lunaku lulala mu kasana, Wakayima yasalagho okugha endogoyiye amaadhi. Ni olwensonga eyo, Wakayima yali tasobola kuja kuleeta maadhi mu kabbaafu ako akatono. Agho yaaalagho okweyazikwa enene ku Wampiti. Wampiti yamugha ebbaafuye olwo okuba baali ba mukwano.
P3	Oluvainhuma olwo okufuna amaadhi okugagha endogoyi, Wakayima yaja eka ni ebbaafu ya Wampiti. Oluvainhuma lwa wiiki ibiri, Wampiti yaleka engoyede yaaja mu nhumba ya Wakayima okunona ebbaafuye.
P4	Wakayima yakoba,"Ebbaafuyo yazaala ento aye olwo okuba tuli ba mukwano, ndi kukugha dhombi." Wampiti yali musanhufu era yaikiriza ebbaafu dhombi.
P5	Mu nnaku ntono dhiti, Wakayima yayenda okuyuwirira endogoyiye amaadhi era yaaja ewa Wampiti okumuyamba. Wampiti yaloghooza,"nga bwenja okufuna ebbaafu eye enswiso, ndha kumugha ebbaafu yange!" Wakayima yatwala ebbaafu ya Wampiti enene yaaja okuyuwirira endogoyiye. Oluvainhuma yamala yaaja ni bbaafu ya Wampiti.
P6	Wampiti yalinda anone ebbaafuye ewa Wampiti aye Yali ayenze okugigha akaseera ezaale ento eyindi! Wampiti we yaja ewa Wakayima okunona ebbaafuye, Wakayima yamukoba,"Wampiti, mukwano gwange, mbaire nnaatera okwidha okukukobera amawulire amabi. Ebbaafuyo yafa enaku ibiri einhuma. Ate oidhi nti buli kintu ekibaagho kifa!"
P7	Wampiti tiyaikiriza mawulire gano. Yaakoba,"Nteekwa okweta ensolo enene okusala omusango guno." Agho Wampiti yayeta ensolo enene mu lukiiko. Endhovu, Embogo, Envubu ni entwiga dhatuuka mu maka ga Wakayima okusla ensonga eno.
P8	Wakayima yabuuzibwa okwinhonola ekyali kigwiregho. Yakoba,"Olwa sooka neeyazikwa ebbaafu ya Wampiti, yazaala ento eyindi naamugha ibiri. Mu butamanha, omulundi ogwo okubiri neeyazikwa ebbaafu ya Wampiti, yafa nga tezaire. Buli kinti kibaagho kiri noi okufa." Ebsolo dhoonadhoona dhaikriza enhinongola ya Wakayima. Wampiti yaafiirwa ebbaafuye olwa Wakayima.
