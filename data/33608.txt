desc:	This folktale from the Nyungwe people of Mozambique reminds us of the importance of having older people in our communities.

page_text:

P1	Yahipadduwa mudhidhi mwina wi nikuru na azombwe ahilamulela wapamo anddimwa etene a m'muruddani wila awene attiye okwiri.
P2	Muzombwe m'modha bamuttawiya vina banvita babe.
P3	M'malele esiwene, nikuru na azombwe baroma wapamo anddimuwa etene.
P4	M'bwenyi dhawene, nowa elofiya m'muruddani vina bivolowa mbani mwa mwana. Nona, musolroya wabudduwele otakulu wa mulomo wa mwanaya.
P5	Ababe ahitagiya malre etene wila ahibwilricemo mbwenyi kawodhile,
P6	mbwenyi anziwa wi agapa nowaya anompa vina mwanaya.
P7	Muzombwe wanvitile babe wahidhowa bamwaddela dhotedhene dhapadduwile m'muruddani. @@Munddimuwa bamwakula, "Ejo kaiye elobo yowopiya. Enowodheya! Kamuttiddeni nanciddwe mumwike osongolro wa mwanaya."
P8	Nona, muzombwe badhowa basoriya malre ninga mwamwaddele babe. Vina nowa edha, ewona nanciddwe elobudduwa!
P9	Mwana olovuneya m'makattamiyoni mwaye.
