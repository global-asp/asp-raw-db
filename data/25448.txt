desc:	Bbaaba wa Musana yatamiranga buli bwire. Bwire bulala

page_text:

P1	Musana yali aba ni ise Walujo.
P2	Walujo yaja agunhwa buli  lunaku.
P3	Walujo yaira eka nga atamiire.
Yayeta, "munhambe! Empiti eri kundhita."
P4	Abantu  baidhanga balumuka. Aye  tiyali yo mpiti.
P5	Walujo  yakolanga  kino  buli  bwire.
P6	Buli  kisera  Walujo  bweyalekana, Musana  yasituka.
P7	Bwire  bulala, empiti  yeelinda  Walujo.
P8	Walujo yalira, "munnhambe!"  Baakoba, "tukooye  okutubuzaabuza."
P9	Musana  yaalinda.  Bbaaba  ghe  tiyatuuka.
P10	"Bbaaba wange ali mu buzibu,"  Musana  yaakoba.
P11	Musana  yaalumuka kuluya.
P12	"Bbaaba" Bbaaba!"Musana yayeta.Empiti yali elikusika  ekigere kyabaaba ghe.
P13	Okuva  olunaku  olwo,  Walujo  yakyusa.  Yatandika  kukobera  baana  bugero
