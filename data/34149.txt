page_text:

P1	Ngivuka ekuseni ngilutsakasele lusuku lwami lwesikole ngobe ngiyati kutsi ngitovakashela kumtaponcwadzi.
P2	Itsi ingashaya insimbi ngigijimele kuba wekucala kuyovela etafuleni laSomtaponcwadzi ngiyoboleka incwadzi lengitoyifundza.
P3	Sihlala ematafuleni sifundze nebangani. Sijabulela  nekubhala umsebenti wasekhaya.
P4	Somtaponcwadzi unelutsandvo nesineke uyasisita ngelucwaningo lesitfunywe lona bothishela.
P5	Uyamema netihambeli titosifundzela nobe tisicocele tindzaba ngetincwadzi letinkhulu
P6	Thishela wetfu naye uvakashela umtaponcwadzi aboleke tincwadzi lestifundza ngemacembu ekilasini.
P7	Kunetincwadzi mahlaya ngetincwadzi tetifundvo lesitifundzako, lolwati lutfutfukisa tingcondvo tetfu.
P8	Ngabolwesihlanu siboleka tincwadzi lesitatifundza ngempelasontfo, ngemsombuluko sitiphindzisela kumtaponcwadzi.
