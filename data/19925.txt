title:	Tshwene Le Komelelo
author:	Lorato Trok
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Setswana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/19925.png
desc:	Tlala e kopantsha ditshwene.

page_text:

P1	Go ne go sena dipula. Lefatshe le ne le omeletse.
P2	Tshwene e e ne ya tsamaya go ya go batla metsi le dijo.
P3	E ne ya feta dithaba le melapo.
P4	E ne ya fitlha kwa lefelong le le bidiwang Tsaoge.
P5	Tshwene e ne e itumetse thata mo lefelong le.
P6	E ne ya ja maungo mme ya nona. Fela e ne e gopotse ditsala tsa yona.
P7	Jaanong Tshwene ya tsamaya tsela e telele go boela gae.
P8	Ditshwene tse dingwe di ne tsa itumelela go mmona.
P9	"Le kae lefelo le la maungo a makana?"
P10	"Ke tla lo isa kwa teng," ga rialo Tshwene.
P11	Ditshwene tse dingwe di ne di rata Tsaoge. Ba ne ba tsaya tshwetso ya go nna baagi ba moo.
P12	Fela ditshwene tsa Tsaoge di ne di tshwenyegile.
P13	"Ditshwene tse dišwa di tlile go fetsa maungo a tsona."
P14	Ditshwene tsa Tsaoge di ile tsa tlhasela ditshwene tse dintšhwa.
P15	"Ntwa ke ya eng?" ga botsa tshwene e kgolo. "Go na le maungo a mantsi go re lekana rotlhe!"
P16	Se e ne e le nnete. Jaanong ditshwene di ne tsa nna tse tshotlhe ka kagiso.
