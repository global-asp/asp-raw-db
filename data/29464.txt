page_text:

P1	Ananiiya yali Nabumbi omugayaavu.
P2	Ayenda inho emere, mugayaavu okufumba.
P3	Ananiiya acaalira Akadhuuyu era amukoba bagabane mu iva lye.
P4	"Iiii. Nnhambaku no okuyuga mu," Akadhuuyu kakoba. Aye Ananiiya mugayaavu.
P5	"Ndhakwiranga eira," Ananiiya akoba.
P6	Ananiiya yesiba edhiwuuzi ku mugulu gwe era ni nsaka ya akadhuuyu.
P7	"Sika edhiwuuzi nga eiva liyiire bukalaamu," Ananiiya akoba.
P8	" Wankembo, nsobola okulya ku ebidhandhaali byaimwe?" "Iiii. Tusalire ku."
P9	"Ndakwiranga," Ananiiya akoba. Ayongera okusiba obuwuuzi.
P10	"Wambidhi, nsobola okulya ku mbooli dho?" "Iiiii. Nnhambaku okutegeka."
P11	"Ndakwiranga," Ananiiya akoba. Ayongera okusiba edhiwuuzi.
P12	Mangu ago, amagulu goonagoona omunaana aga Ananiiya gaali gasibwa ku kibya.
P13	Ananiiya awuulira okusikibwa ku magulu abiri. Kabiri no ogwo okusatu.
P14	Amagulu goona goona omunaana gaali ku sikibwa! "Lekeraawo okusika!" Alira.
P15	Ezira awuulira Ananiiya nga alira. Eghiwuuzi dha kutuka lulala lulala.
P16	Amagulu ga Ananiiya gadhuumuka! Era Banabumbi  balina amagulu maleeyi inho.
