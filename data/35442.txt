P2	I love ti eat fruits. My favorite fruit is apple. They are helthy and will give us energy.
P3	I love to read book. It will give me knowledge. By reading, I can improve my English.
P4	I love to play with my friend name Jessy. Everyday, we will play at 5.00 p.m. We are best friend and also same school.
