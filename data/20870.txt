title:	Lioness And Ostrich

desc:	Lioness plans to take Ostrichs children.

page_text:

P1	Ostrich and Lioness were once good friends.
P2	Unlike Lioness, Ostrich fed her chicks well.
P3	Lioness’s cubs did not have enough food to eat.
P4	Lioness wished the fat chicks were hers.
P5	When Ostrich was not home, Lioness took the chicks.
P6	When Ostrich returned, she asked, “Where are my children?”
P7	Ostrich found her chicks with Lioness at her hut.
P8	Lioness said, “The chicks are mine now. Take the cubs.”
P9	Ostrich asked herself, “How can I get my children back?”
P10	She called all the animals for a meeting.
P11	The animals arrived for the meeting.
P12	Elephant asked Ostrich and Lioness to speak.
P13	Most of the animals were afraid to blame Lioness.
P14	Squirrel said, “Children that look like birds belong to Ostrich.”
P15	Then Squirrel ran into a hole.
P16	Lioness was very angry. She went home with her cubs.
