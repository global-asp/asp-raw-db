title:	Visiting Grandmother

desc:	Children visit their grandmother in the village and have a wonderful time.

page_text:

P1	Odongo and Apiyo waited for the school holidays to come.
P2	It was time for them to visit their grandmother.
P3	They drove past mountains, wild animals and farms.
P4	After a while, Odongo and Apiyo fell asleep.
P5	They found their grandmother resting under a tree.
P6	Grandmother danced and sang. The children gave her presents.
P7	Grandmother blessed Odongo and Apiyo in a traditional way.
P8	Odongo and Apiyo played with butterflies and birds.
P9	They climbed trees. 

They splashed in the water of the lake.
P10	They were tired. 

They fell asleep before finishing their dinner.
P11	They helped grandmother. 

Theycollected eggs and picked vegetables.
P12	Grandmother taught Odongo and Apiyo how to cook different foods.
P13	Odongo took grandmother’s cows to graze. 

They ate a neighbour’s crops.
P14	They went to their grandmother’s stall at the market.
P15	In the evening, they helped grandmother to count her money.
P16	Soon, the holidays were over. 

Grandmother packed food for their journey.
P17	The children did not want to leave. 

“Grandmother, come with us.”
P18	Odongo and Apiyo hugged grandmother. 

“Goodbye, grandmother,” they said.
P19	What is better, city life or village life?
