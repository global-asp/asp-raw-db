title:	Munywaani wange, muraabe wange
author:	Tracy  Kababiito
artist:	Thulani Mhlanga
lang:	Rutooro

desc:	Thabo o etsa segwera se sa lokang. Na dilo di ka fetogela Thabo?

page_text:

P1	Obusuumi bweyiira munno, mukyaaro kya Mphahlele, hakabba haliyo omusigaazi yeyikara ga nabazeire be
P2	Omusigaazi akabba naha bazeire be ekitiniisa nabantu bandi bonna mukyaaro. Ibaara lye lyaali Thabo Maloba.
P3	Balaaza  birro byokukoora mukyaaro nikwo abaana nibetekaniiza kugenda kusooma hanyuuma ya ruhumurro. Kiiro kimu, obuyalinagenda heisomero, Thabo akatangaana omwojjo owuybayeetaga Sparks. Bakabaaza ha rugudo bafooka banywani.
P4	Sparks yeyikaaraga narwana, nanyigiriiza  bandi nokwesaraaho kusooma. Akabba muntu mubbi rwagonzaga kusiiba sente nokwikara nabantu babbi abandi. Mpoora beitu nobwegendereza Sparks akasiisa Thabo. Thbo akabba takwenda kurorwa nka omudooma dooma naawe yatandiika kwerabya kubbi nka Sparks.
P5	Thabo akafokka munyigirizza kandi atakutiina bazaire be nabantu bandi yahaagga ekitinissa. Akafooka mutamizzi kandi atakusoboora kweroorera
P6	Thabo akafokka kintuntu kandi yatunga noburweire bwokwesweera. Akafokka mutaito munno kandi Sparks yamusekerezza atakyayenda kubba munywani we. Bazaire ba Thabo bakamuhaliriizakugenda mirwaaro kutunga obujanjaabi. Obuyamaziire obujanjabbi Yabba kurungi.
P7	Thabo akerooramu obwomezi bwe. Aketegerezza ngu akabba anyweine omuraabe. Akatandiika kukoora heisomeero ya sabba ekiganyiiro mwabazaire be na basoomesa.
P8	Sparks akahurra kubbi  habwengeeso za Thabo ennungi.Beitu akaswara habweeze embi kandi yacwaamu kuhindukka yafooka mutu murungi.
