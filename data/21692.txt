title:	Mageziifi Kasiira
author:	Mulualem  Daba

desc:	Oduu durii waae hirriya cimmafi dadhaba. Hirriyaan dadhaba haala midhaan qaba harkaa tokkotin waan keessa darbee agarsiisu.

page_text:

P1	Bara durii, magazifi kasiran wajjin waan argatan nyaacha jiraacha turan.
P2	Magaziin, "Akka wajjin nyaanu wajjiin maalif hin qootnu," jedhe.
P3	Magaziin, "Ati qoti ani nan facaasa," jedhe kasiratiin.
P4	Magaziin kisaratiin, "Yommuu ati facastu ani immoo nan arama," jedhe.
P5	Kasiiran yommuu midhaan gahuu akka Mageziin sasabu gaafate.
P6	Magaziin garuu, "Ati sassabi ani gumbittin kuusa," jedhe.
P7	Magaziin dadhaba dha.

Kanaafu Kasiraan miidhan harkaa qaba (haammara) tokko qofaa keenifi.
P8	Magaziin miidhan sana fudhate osoo deemu handaqootti dhufee.
P9	Handaqqoon sunis miidhan sana jala nyaate fiixe hanqaquu tokko keeniteef.
P10	Magaziin osoo hanqaaqu sana fudhate deemu, ijoolletti dhufe.

Ijoollennis hanqaqicha jala caabsan.
P11	Bakka hanqaquu, ijoollen magaziif mango kennaanif.
P12	Magaziin Parrootiiti dhufe.

Parrootin mango isaa jala nyaate baalli tokko kennef.
P13	Lagni immoo baalli sana jala fudhate bishaan kennef.
P14	Magaziin namicha kassala baasuf bishaanicha kennef.
P15	Namichi kassala baasu sun immoo Magaziif qootto kennef.
P16	Magaziin namoota foon gurguranitti dhufee.

Isaan immoo qootto isaa cabsani.
P17	Gaafafi eege Magaziif kennani.

Ilaala waan Magaziin keessa darbee!
P18	Isa booda Magaziin, "Sangaan mooti keenya dhoqee keessa lixe," jedhe iyyee.
P19	Namonnis isaa gargaruuf gadi bahani.

Magaziinis, "Eegefi gaafa isaa qabaa harkisaa," jedhe.
P20	Namoonnis yommu harkisan gaafaf eege qoofa baasani. 

Magaziinis namootan, "Horrii keessan fiida mootti keenyaf," jedhe.
