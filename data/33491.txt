desc:	Umama no baba basthala engadini. Umndeni usebenza ndawonye ukusthala emasimini.

page_text:

P1	Umama wasthala.Wasthala ingadi encane.Wasthala ingadi encane yama boontjies.
P2	Ubaba wastjala.Wa sthala ingadi encane.Ingadi encane yama blomu.
P3	Mama wahlakula kuzozo zombili izingadi.
P4	Baba wanisela zombili izingadi
P5	Mina nabazali bam sasebenza ndawonye engadin encane.Mina nabo dadewethu sesivunile izimbali nama boontjies.
P6	ingadi encane yama boontjies ikhiqhize ama saka amaningi wama bootjies.Ingadi encane yama blomu ikhiqhize ama saka amaningi wezi mbali.
P7	Umama wa sthala.Usthale ingadi enkulu.Usthale ingadi enkulu yama boontjies.
P8	ubaba utshalile.Usthale ingadi enkulu.Usthale ingadi enkulu yezimbali.
