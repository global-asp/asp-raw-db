page_text:

P1	Jane was going to school with her four friends named Yuki,Zakia, Lucas and Louis. On Hazel Street a man kidnapped them, they were terribly scared as they were kicking the man.He put them in a red truck.
P2	When he put them inside of the room, they saw two other boys,one was wearing a yellow t-shirt and the other one was wearing a blue t-shirt.They asked them what their names were and they said that their names were Daniel and Zack.
P3	On the other side of the room, they saw two girls and they asked them what their names were and they said that their names were Kenzy and Grace.
P4	The man came and said that it was time for lunch and he gave them pouring and disgusting soup.After he went and got a belt and began to hurt them.
P5	Jane's little brother and sister Demi and Justin realised that Jane was taking a long time at school and they were worried so they asked their parents and their parents asked Ms Kim, the headmistress and she said that she was not there.
P6	"Daddy,where is Jane?", said Justin,dad could not say that she was missing so he said that she was at Lucas' home. Justin knew that he was lying,he said,"I know you are lying daddy",then dad said that he was right
P7	Justin got dressed so he can go and search for Jane,he went for Demi to tell her that he was going to search for Jane.He decided to get a bag with a medical kit,water and food so he can feed her and if there are other children.
P8	He called Diana,Lee, Zendeya and Levi to come to the public park in Imtarfa so they can talk about this,when they arrived they saw him waiting for them and after a few minutes,they discussed that they were going to go together and search.
P9	After they found them they saved them and then the police arrested them because there was a woman and a man.
