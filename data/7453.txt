title:	Indlela Imbovane Eyalihlangula Ngayo Ihobe
author:	Siyamthanda Jiza
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7453.png
desc:	Eli libali elingembovane eyemka namanzi yaze yancedwa lihobe ekurhaxweni. Nayo yafuna ukulibuyekeza ngoncedo layo. Yalihlangula kumakhwenkwe awayefuna ukulitya.

page_text:

P1	Ilanga laligqats' ubhobhoyi kwihlathi elomileyo.
P2	Kwakusele kuziintsuku ezininzi imbovane encinane ingawaseli amanzi.
P3	"Ndidinga ithontsi lamanzi nokuba liwa egqabini."
P4	Kodwa nombethe wawusele womile.
P5	"Ukuba andiseli manzi ngoku,  ndiza kufa," yatsho imbovane encinane inyembezana. "Kufuneka ndiye kula mlambo bendive ngawo."
P6	"Umlambo uza kumka nawe," watsho unomatse okrelekrele eyilumkisa.
P7	Kodwa yayomile lunxano imbovane encinane. "Ndiza kufa ukuba andiseli manzi ngoku."
P8	Imbovane encinane yahamba isiya kukhangela loo mlambo.
P9	Yahamba yahamba idlula kwingca eyomileyo nakumahlahla awomileyo.
P10	 Yahamba yade yeva ukugwinkciza kwamanzi omlambo.
P11	Imbovane encinane yathatha ithamo lamanzi ixesha elide.
P12	Ngendlela eyayivuya ngayo ayizange ilibone iliza elikhulu lamanzi elalisiza.
P13	Imbovane yazama ukubambelela emcingeni owomileyo wengca owawudlula ngakuyo. Kodwa yemka namanzi.
P14	"Ndincedeni bantu. Khanindincede."
P15	"Khawuleza,  bambelela apha," latsho ihobe elimhlophe liphethe ikhuni ngomlomo.
P16	"Andinakuhamba ndingekalibuleli ihobe. Ndiza kulinda lide lize kusela amanzi."
P17	Ngenye imini isalindile njalo,  kwafika amakhwenkwe amabini emlanjeni ephethe iiketi zawo.
P18	"Kukho ihobe elikhulu elimhlophe elidla ngokuza kusela amanzi apha," yatsho enye inkwenkwe. "Siza kutya lona ngale ngokuhlwa."
P19	"Andinakuwavumela la makhwenkwe alibulale eli hobe limhlophe. Kodwa njengokuba ndimncinane kangaka nje,  ndingenza ntoni?"
P20	Kanye ngelo xesha ihobe elimhlophe labhabha lisuka emthini lisiya kusela.
P21	Imbovane encinane yavela necebo.
P22	Yatsibela unyawo lwenye yala makhwenkwe,  yaza yalithi nkxii kanobom.
P23	Le nkwenkwe yaxhuma. "Yhoo!" yakhwaza isitsho.
P24	Ihobe elimhlophe lothuka laza labhabha lisiya kwindawo ekhuselekileyo.
P25	Yaba ke ngolo hlobo imbovane encinane ibulela ihobe elimhlophe ngokuyihlangula.
