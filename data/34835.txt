page_text:

P1	Luwo le ti ugbo aduwa kpai iyeun.@  @@  I@  kwonyo owou alu ku ma la ro ajrna.
P2	Efu unyi aduwa@  le, a mone jor mo wéwé.@  @@  Ma@  ja keli.
P3	Luwo kwefu church.@  @@  Iye-un liin.
P4	Luwo kpo da go eju@  ona le.@  @@  E@  chane kpe be, “Ungbo chunyi walé?
P5	Taki chanei lulé.@  E funyi a lélà lii lé.@  @@  Taki ka kini, “ayi chunyi waan.@  Nyi wa de kékélé.
P6	Luwo lule to gba@  pee, I cheli kakini, unyi ki do gbaun@  le gbogba.@  @@  E@  kakini, aiyi chunyi waan. Unyi wa gbogbaan.
P7	I@  defu ule gbogbo. Taki unyi kékélé li le.@  @@  Taki kakini, a yi@  nugo chunyi wan. Unyi wa chi nekete@  ku ma du bubo.
P8	Luwo le togba. E fu@  unyi kekele ko oli dago ododa.@  @@  E@  kakini, a iyi chunyi wan. Unyi wa na@  mo li me jii ododaun.
P9	Ugbogbolo le, luwo fi iyeun@  le ki da waa, taki ruule@  nya kerebo@  @@  Iyeun kakini, “alo@  tunyi!”
P10	Ungbo chunyi Luwo@  le?@  @ @@@
