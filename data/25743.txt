desc:	Ani obishi loo loofɔlɔ ji Ajao

page_text:

P1	Ajao, aputupata ko, he baye be ko. Ekã hela saa mli yɛ eshia lɛ. Mɔ ko bɛ ni kwɛ enɔ.
P2	Ajao bagbo.
P3	Ajao henɔi lɛ kɛɛ, "Ajao egbo. Esa ni wɔyatsɛ ewebii lɛ ni amɛba koni amɛbafũ lɛ."
P4	Henɔi lɛ tee amɛyatsɛ loofɔji lɛ amɛkɛɛ, "Nyɛwekunyo ko egbo."
P5	Loofɔji lɛ ba ni amɛbana akɛ Ajao ni. Amɛkɛɛ, "Kooloo nɛɛ fataaa wɔweku lɛ he."
P6	"Wɔweku nɛɛ mli mɛi fɛɛ yɛ tsɛji. Ajao bɛ eko. Hewɔ lɛ, jeee wɔteŋ mɔ kome ni."
P7	Henɔi lɛ susu sane lɛ he. Amɛkpã gbee amɛkɛɛ, "Loofɔji lɛ asane ja. Ajao bɛ tsɛji. Hewɔ lɛ, jeee amɛwekunyo ji Ajao."
P8	Henɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome kɛɛ, "Ajao baafee obishii lɛ awekunyo. Eje amɛ."
P9	Henɔi lɛ tee amɛyatsɛ obishii lɛ amɛkɛɛ, "Nyɛwekunyo ko egbo."
P10	Obishii lɛ ba ni amɛbana akɛ Ajao ni. Amɛkɛɛ, "Kooloo nɛɛ egbeee wɔweku lɛ yi."
P11	Wɔweku nɛɛ mli mɔ fɛɛ mɔ yɛ lei. Ajao bɛ lei." Obishii lɛ hu tee.
P12	Anaaa mɛi pɔtɛɛ ni ji Ajao webii. Enɛ hewɔ anaaa afũ Ajao lɛ.
