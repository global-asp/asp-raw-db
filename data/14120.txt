page_text:

P1	UNamukuru nomntakwabo balungele ukuya esikolweni.
P2	UNamukuru uthwala umntakwabo kwibhayisekile yakhe.
P3	Abahlobo babo bayababulisa endleleni eya esikolweni.
P4	Ngeli lixa beseklasini,  inkawu iyafika ngaphandle. Inkawu imka nebhayisekile kaNamukuru!
P5	Abantwana baleqa inkawu.
P6	Inkawu ishiya ibhayisekile phantsi komthi.
P7	Inkawu idadela kwisiqithi esiphakathi kwichibi. Ikhwela phezulu emthini.
P8	Inkawu iyabulisa iphephezelisa isandla! Abantwana nabo bayabulisa bephephezilisa izandla. UNamukuru uyifumene ibhayisekile yakhe kwakhona.
