desc:	A wise old mouse teaches us that when your Empube enkuluwegeresa eti singa abalabebo beefuula okuba mikwanogyo

page_text:

P1	Eira einho, ba  wakapa ni  wampube baali  bagonzagania.
P2	Entangama bweyabita, empube dhatandhiika okufiirwa abandi ku boluganda.
P3	waampube  oomukulu yaakoba, "Kapa dhiri kutumalagho
P4	Bamaama bakoba abaana, "Mwegendereze. Mutafuluma."
P5	Empube dona dona dhaali dhegendereza. Dhasigalanga mu biinha.
P6	kapa edhiri endhala dhabuuza, "Empube dona dona dhirighaye?"
P7	Kapa dhasalagho okudhikyalira dhateekateeka embaga.
P8	Dhaakoba, "Muleke mulala ku baghala baimwe afumbirwe mutabane ghaife."
P9	Empube dhakoba kapa, "Mwendha kutulya."
P10	Oluvainhuma, baikiriza abaana bafumbirwagane.
P11	kapa dhairayo nga nsanhufu era nga dhikomba ensaya.
P12	Ku lunaku olwembaga, empube dhegendereza inho.
P13	Kapa dhatuuka nga dhirikwemba olwembo olwembaga.
P14	Olwembo lwali lukoba, "Mubageme mubalye."
P15	Empube enkulu dhainhama okwemba, "Mulumuke, Empube, Mulumuke."
P16	Empube enkulu dhaakoba kapa, "Tituli ba mikwano."
