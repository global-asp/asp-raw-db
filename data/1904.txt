desc:	Sentences that explore feelings and what makes people upset

page_text:

P1	Why is the baby crying?
P2	I think she is hungry.
P3	Why is the boy sad?
P4	I think he is missing his father.
P5	Why is the girl happy?
P6	She has a new dress.
P7	Why is the old man laughing?
P8	He read a good joke.
P9	Why is the lady smiling?
P10	Her children are reading quietly.
P11	What is this lady feeling?
P12	I think she is feeling shy.
