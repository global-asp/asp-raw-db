page_text:

P1	Lero lwamukaaga. lusu inho.
P2	Alice, Arthur ni Anabel beendha kwewuuga mu mwiiga.
P3	''Leka tutolemu egoye dhaife era twewuuge,'' Anabel akobye.
P4	''Twenhatwenha tubukire mu mwiiga,'' Arthur akobye.
P5	''ooh mubone onho! Leka twewuuge, okwewuuga munhonho!'' Alice akobye.
P6	Edhuba erikwiika, tuwulira obuwoomi. Egoye dheife dhirigha.
P7	Leka tudhinhonye. Ghansi aghemiti. Mu nsiko. Oba awantu ghoonghoonha.
P8	Oh! A ente! kiki ekyo ekigiiri mu kamwa?''Saati yange,'' Arthur alira.
P9	Oh katonda wange! Owundhi,'' Eyo saati yange!'' Alice akaabuka.
P10	Baana baange,''Kiiki ekibatuuseku?''
