title:	Shantani unzani webhola iyamakumbo
author:	Milton  Nyathi
artist:	Eden Daniels
lang:	ChiNambya
level:	First paragraphs

desc:	Shantani unsichana unoda kuzana ibhola lyamakumbo kuchikolo asi unkulwana wegapo lyabechinhulume lyebhola unoti ibhola lyamakumbo ndilyabanhulume. Shantani unopiwa unshaji wokuti ezanile banhulume. Wakazana zwakashantisa unhu wose.

page_text:

P1	Shantani wakabeyebela banhulume bezana ibhola lyamakumbo. Wakada kungwina kuti ezane nebo.@@Wakakumbila kunkulwana wabo unobazanisa ibhola kuti angawana mwenya wokuti ebezha ezana nabanhulume ibhola.
P2	Yoyo unobazanisa ibhola wakabhata munshana akati, "Pechikolo yopano banhulume chete ndibo banovumijwa kuzana ibhola lyamakumbo."
P3	Banhulume bakombuja kuti ende enozana ibhola lyamaboko. Bakombuja kuti ibhola lyamaboko ndilyabanhukaji lyabanhulume ndilyamakumbo.@@Shantani wakanyema.
P4	Izhuba linotobela, chikolo chakabeli nonzano unkulwana webhola.@@Unkulwana wabo ajakabeka shantilwa nokuti bamwe babazani bakabezana zwinonakilija bakabalwala.
P5	Shantani wakakumbila kunkulwana wabo eti, "Ndokumbila kungwina ndizane."@@Unkulwana wabo ajakabechinoziba kuti ete chini. Wakayeya kuti Shantani engwine mugapo.
P6	Unzano wakabeli unkukutu kasa.@@Apana wakagoja muchikamu chokutanga.
P7	Pakati muchikamu chechibili, Shantani wakapilwa ibhola. Wakalabuka nokukambija enda kumbhati webhola kugedi.@@Shantani wakabhakala ibhola nesimba akagoja.
P8	Bakabeyebela ibhola bakanakilijwa kasa bakashotoka.@@Kuva nelyo izhuba banhukaji nebo bakatanga kuvumijwa kuzana ibhola lyamakumbo pechikolo.
