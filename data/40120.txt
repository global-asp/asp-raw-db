page_text:

P1	I am Anna Paul from Mbeya Tanzania.My teacher is very supportive of my dream.When I grow up,I want to be a doctor.
P2	We all have wings that are meant to fly high.We are like the beautiful butterfly and need to spread our wings out there.
P3	Teacher Suzanne encourages all students to dream big and high and to let their wings fly.
P4	We all study hard to make sure we reach our goals in life and spread our wings across the world.
P5	We can achieve all our dreams when we work hard.We are limitless and can fly so high that not even the skies can limit us . Together let's us spread our wings near, far and high.
P6	We can become anything we want to achieve in life.Keep dreaming and work towards your dreams beautiful butterfly.
