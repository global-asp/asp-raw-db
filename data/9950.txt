page_text:

P1	Omanyire niyaaliyo omuhayire Omweene omuhayire?
P2	Oyu balomire ohuleeta omugayire Omugayire omunyaaha sosi omuhayire.
P3	Aye omuhayire nigahejire omugayire Omugayire omuhayire sigaweene munyaaha.
P4	Omuhayire omugayire galeeta muhayire.
