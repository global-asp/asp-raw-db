P1	Once upon a time there was a gredy Hyena who wants to be a king of the jungle. But he was so curious to be a king he decided to kill the former king who was a lion . Hyena was so afraid of alion. He conducted a meeting with his fellow Hyena
P2	He plan to kill the king of the jungle he said, " let's kill the king of the jungle and we can rule over the jungle." But one of them said, "who will kill the lion?" No one replied. They all went to look for the assassinate who can kill
P3	Lion . They assinate elephant to kill the lion. But the had a plan with his friend leopard. He wanted leopard to be a king of the jungle, because the lion had kill his family members. He vowed to kill lion. He went to the lion den at night.
P4	He went on and on up to the Lions den. He found lion sleeping. He wake him with anger and threatened to kill him. Lion beg for mercy. Elephant told the lion to give his throne to the leopard, lion agreed to what the elephant said.
P5	Elephant was very happy and he spared lion`s life . Leopard become king of the jungle. The End.
