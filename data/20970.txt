desc:	Odhiri nyako mathini bongi kwari mere to romo kodi ogwange ideiri luuli.

page_text:

P1	Junywoli pa nyako mathini judhiro go bongi kwari mere mayido bedo yucheni maluli go okadho kende munyo nigi seimi.
P2	Munyo go otundo chengini gi luuli go oromo gi ndeili mayido wotho kichari to luwo gi go munyo kangasa rupiri oyido nitye romo pa ogwange iluuli.
P3	Munyo go otundu ideiri luuli, go onwango ogwange kweth chengin gi royo togo ngwecho angwecha kanyo go koko.
P4	Munyo go owoki iluuli go oromo bende gi winyini kweth kanyacheili munyo ju koko. Nyako mathini mee ongwecho munyo medo koko. Kwari go owinjo go.
P5	Kwari go oluwo kodi dhako pere ni meno nyakwari go. Go okadho neno go mapiyopiyo gi gwoki aka onwango go munyo gooli.
P6	Munyo jutundo pecho go odonjo to motho nyithindho megi nyaka to chemo.Munyo ochowo chemo go oluwo kwong gima otimere rigo iroyo.
P7	Munyo go ochowo wacho gima otimere gi nwango ryeko ma kwero luwo iluuli mungoye dhano mani, go okadho tuko gi nyithindho wade gi seimi.
P8	Go kuluwo doko iluuli mugonye dhano mani.
