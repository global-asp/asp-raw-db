page_text:

P1	Nana and Amina live in Bama village.
P2	Nana goes to school while Amina stays at home.
P3	Nana asked Amina "why don't, you come to school?" Amina answered," because school is not important".
P4	Nana said, "my dear friend, education is light. A girl without education is like rice without stew".
P5	Amina took Nana's advice and started going to school.
P6	After some years, they both graduated from the university.
P7	Nana became a medical doctor and Amina a nurse.
