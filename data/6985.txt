desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	Ĩwá ãlu mụ ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P2	Kụ́rị ị̃rị̃ mụ kí ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P3	ꞌDãá ụ̃drụ́ na kí ãzíla ãriŋa sụ abe. Kí pírí mụ ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P4	Lẽza tõwú kí ĩzõgó ázíyá abe kí mụ ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P5	Ụ́drụ́gọ́ ázị̂rị̃ kí ị̃ꞌbị ãrõ abe ị̃yị́ agá.
P6	Ũndere úrômĩ kí mụ ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P7	Kẹ̃mị̃ ãlu amụ́ ị̃yị́ mvụlé.
P8	Ãꞌdi ụ̃rị̃ kóru sĩ mụjó ị̃yị́ mvụŋá trũ drị̃ gá nĩ yã?
