desc:	Ingaba ukhona omnye ofana nam ehlabathini?

page_text:

P1	Ndiyacinga ukuba ukhona ondim nakweyiphina indawo ehlabathini.
P2	Okhangeleka ngokufanayo.
P3	Nothetha ngokufanayo.
P4	Nonamafongqofongqo afana nawam kananjalo.
P5	Omnye onamehlo am kunye neendlebe zam.
P6	Nonokuhleka okunjengokwam kananjalo.
P7	Ongenza into endingayenza.
P8	Nongenako ukwenza endingenako ukukwenza.
P9	Ndiye ndakhangela kwaye ndakhangela yonke indawo ukuba ndifumane omnye ondim. Ndiye ndehla ndaya epakini kwaye ndakhangela emva kwemithi.
P10	Ndikhangele konke okundingqongileyo,  phezulu nasezantsi.
P11	Kubo bonke ubuso.
P12	Andikwazanga ukufumana omnye ondim nakweyiphina indawo.
P13	Kukho ondim omnye kuphela ehlabathini! Ndindodwa,  oh yinyaniso!
P14	Kodwa nje ingendim,  awuboni? Kukho omnye kuphela onguwe!
