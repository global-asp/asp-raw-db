page_text:

P1	Where is my cat?@@I can't find my cat.@@I can't find my cat anywhere.
P2	Where is my cat?@@Is he under the bed?@@No, he's running out the room.
P3	Where is my cat?@@Is he on top of the cupboard?@@No, he's on the bottom shelf.
P4	Where is my cat?@@Is he behind the couch?@@No, he's outside in the yard.
P5	Where is my cat?@@Is he next to the bin?@@No, he's jumping out the window.
P6	Where is my cat?@@Is he in the basket?@@No, he's in the box.
P7	Where is my cat?@@Is he outside in the yard?@@No, he's behind the curtain.
P8	Where is my cat?@@Here he is!@@He is hiding in this cupboard!
