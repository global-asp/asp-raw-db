title:	Esolo Jifuna Amagulu

desc:	Hamba asalaŋo ohuŋa esolo josijosi amagulu ko jaha nyumirwa ohugenda hyabaatu.

page_text:

P1	Ahale esolo si jaali n'amagulu.
P2	Hamba gaali aŋaaye baatu bonyene amagulu.
P3	Hamba gasalaŋo ohuŋa hiisi solo amagulu.
P4	Esolo jendire amagulu ko jahagenda era jahaduluma.
P5	Hyajiberanga higosi ohweŋalula.
P6	Oludaalo nirwolire, esolo jeŋalula jatiina ewa Hamba.
P7	Hamba gaŋa esolo n'enyuni amagulu.
P8	Esolo ejindi jahina atenga ejindi jegwira ŋaasi.
P9	Jaloma jiiti, "Sihuhigoboyeyo ohweŋolula nindi."
P10	Namugongolo njowasembayo hu lunjiriri.
P11	Hamba gaŋa Namugongolo amagulu gosigosi agaali ni gasigayeŋo.
P12	Namugongolo galoma ati, "Nja hugendanga n'esupiidi ohuhira ejindi."
P13	Namugongolon nigaŋwaŋo, Etemu yajire yegayirira yiiti, "Peeho amagulu."
P14	Etemu yaloma Hamba yiiti, "Ero lingoneye."
P15	Hamba gahayire amagulu aganayiŋe.
P16	Ohuŋwo hulwo, Etemu ehirindire ohufuna amagulu.
