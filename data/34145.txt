page_text:

P1	Ngempelasontfo sihlala sonkhe.Gogo asifundzise ngekutigcina siphephile.
P2	Gogo ucwayise ngekutsi sidlale khashane netigcobho tagesi, singalingi nekufaka iminwe kuto.
P3	Gogo utse singadvonsi tintsambo tagezi, ngobe singatselwa ngemanti labilako.
P4	Gogo utsi gesi nemanti nobe lokuyinsimbi akuhlangani, kuba ngumbane lomkhulu lesingete sasindza kuwo.
P5	Usicwayise nangekutsi singatsintsi lokusesitofini, ngobe kungenteka kuyashisa.
P6	Utse gogo singabasi imbawula endlini. Imbawula siyotsela egumeni siyishkye khona.
P7	Tintfo letisabuhlalu nobe tinkinobho utsi singatifaki emlonyeni nobe endlebeni kumbe emphumulweni.
P8	Longatitsatsi letecwayiso angalimala kabi ,angalale nasesibhedlele.
P9	Lokuhle kutsi naBhobi bekativela ngetakhe tindlebe.Phela Bhobi ngumagangane.
