desc:	Lendzaba imayelana nemntfwana labekhala. Unina bekangati kutsi ukhaliswa yini watama ngato tonkhe tindlela tekutsi amthulise wagcine sekathulile nangabe sekagezile.

page_text:

P1	Lona nguNkhanyeti, ulele embhedzeni utitfola uyedwa wase ucala kukhala.
P2	Nkhanyeti uyakhala ngobe akaboni muntfu edvutane naye.
P3	Make wakhe weva Nkhanyeti kutsi uyakhala, watsatsa libhodlela lakhe wamumunyisa kepha wangathula.
P4	Make wakhe wabona kutsi kuncono ayomgezisa, wase uyamgeza ngemanti lafutfumele kepha solo angathuli.
P5	Make wakhe wamfaka linabukeni kepha solo akathuli.
P6	Wamtsatsa wambeka emahlombe, watetema kodvwa bekasacala kuthula.
P7	Ngemuva kwekutsi sekamgezisile Nkhanyeti wagcina sekathulile asalele butfongo.
