desc:	Ixoxo kube libelihle ukufana nentikilish kube waye mamela.

page_text:

P1	Eminyakeni eminingi edlule, i-Lizard ne-Frog babengabangani. Ngelinye ilanga banquma ukuyofuna izintombi edolobheni.
P2	UFrog wayenomona ngesikhumba esikhanyayo nesikhanyayo seLizard.
P3	Uthe, "Wenzeni ukuze ubukeke muhle kangaka? Ngibuke, mubi kakhulu. Yini engingayenza ngesikhumba sami?"
P4	UMbizi wathi, "Lalelani! Beka amanzi ebhodweni uwafake emlilweni. Okulandelayo, wena"
P5	Kepha uFrog uthe, "Ngiyazi, ngiyazi. Qhubeka, mngani wami." Futhi wahamba.
P6	Ngakho-ke wafika ekhaya wabeka amanzi ebhodweni.
P7	Lapho amanzi eqala ukubila, waziphonsa kuwo.
P8	Isikhumba sakhe sashiswa futhi umsila wakhe waqhuma!
P9	Futhi esikhundleni sokuba ngumqali futhi abe muhle njenge-Lizard, wayembi kakhulu!
