title:	Mboju ƴoyrii nyiiwa fahin
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38249.png
desc:	Ɗum habaru dow no mboju waawiri doggugo boɗɗum bee no mboju yoyriri nyiiwa ƴoyre hallunde.

page_text:

P1	Nyiiwa bee mboju e ɗi coobiri soosey.
P2	Ɗi keɓani dabbaaji maaji durdude bee ndiyam.
P3	Ɗi ngiɗi fijugo bol.
P4	Nyiiwa ɓuri nastingo bol. @@Mboju seyaaki sam.
P5	Mboju ƴaminga, "Ɗume waɗi a waawi fijugo boɗɗum?"@@"Ngam koyɗe am mawɗe."
P6	Nyiiwa bee mboju ndoggi. Sey mboju daɗi nyiiwa.
P7	Nyiiwa ƴami, "Ɗume waɗi a waawi doggugo boɗɗum?"@@"Ngam koyɗe am cewɗe."
P8	Nyiiwa ƴami, "No mi heɓirta koyɗe cewɗe?"@Sey mboju wiʼinga, "Mi hollete."
P9	Mboju wi'ii, "Dara nder hiite." Nyiiwa waɗi non sey nga woyi, "E mi wula!"
P10	Mboju wiʼi nyiiwa, "Laawol ngo'ol tan waddantama koyɗe cewɗe."
P11	Nyiiwa wurtii hiite. E nga waali e leydi.
P12	Ɗum waɗii balɗe nyiiwa fotaayi daroo.
P13	Nde nga nani daama, nga hooti e iyaalu maaga.
P14	Mboju nani semtuɗum. Sey ɗum ƴami nyiiwa yaafuye.
