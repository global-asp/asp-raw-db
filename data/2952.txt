desc:	This story is about the different sorts of books my family reads.

page_text:

P1	Muganda wange asoma akatabo k'essomero.
P2	Maama asoma Bayibuli.
P3	Mwannyinaze asoma akatabo akalagirira okufumba.
P4	Kojja asoma olupapula lw'amawulire.
P5	Jjajja asoma magaziini.
P6	Muto wange asoma akatabo k'ebifaananyi.
P7	Nze kano akatabo ke nsoma.
P8	Mu bbanga ttono nja kuba nsobola okusoma buli kintu kyonna.
