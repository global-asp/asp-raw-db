title:	Ngendikolesya'kyi omwangenda?
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere and Jenipher  Kabugho
artist:	Sue Kramer, Catherine Groenewald, Marion Drew, Louwrisa Blaauw, Rob Owen, Vusi Malindi, Jesse Breytenbach, Natalie Propa, Wiehan de Jager and Mango Tree

desc:	Omwana mwalengekania olhughendo nikwa akayibulaya nimubere wahayi omubuya owerighendesya.

page_text:

P1	 Nimibere minene eyerikolesya omwalhughendo. Ngasombole wahayi kwehi?
P2	Ngakolesaye endege kwehi?
P3	 Esyondege sikahulhuka eyighulhu kutsibu!
P4	 Nokweryo mbino ngakolesaye erisu?
P5	 Aliriryo erisu ne’syondege bisosene!
P6	 Ngakolesaye egaliyomuki kwehi?
P7	Aliriryo egaliyomuki yikapakirira
  halihali.
P8	 Kutse,nokweryo, ngakolesaye ebasi?
P9	 Ekipindi kyethu kiwithe basi nguma nyisa!
P10	  Nokweryo ngendikolesya elore omwalhughendo.
P11	 Beitu esyolore sikakolesibawa eriheka emighughu eyirithohire.
P12	 Ngakolesaye egali omwalhughendo?
P13	 Beitu thatha wayi animwendi nyihererya egali yiwe?
P14	 Nokweryo ngendilengesya erikolesya obwathu?
P15	 Aliriryo singaliyikethera amathu kutsibu!
P16	Aaa! Nganasi ekyangendikolesya! Ngendikolesya
  egalieyikalima omwalhughendo.
