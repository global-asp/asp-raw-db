desc:	Bayyana amfanin dabbobi a cikin alumma.

page_text:

P1	Kalli dabbobin da mu ke kiwo a gida.
P2	Saniya tana bamu madara.
P3	Zomayye da mage abokan mutane ne.
P4	Doki yana haniniya ya ce, "Ehhehhh."
P5	A ƙasar Uganda ana bayar da shanu da awaki a matsayin sadaki.
P6	Kaza tana cewa, "Kuut-ƙuut."
P7	Ana amfani da kare a matsayin mai tsaro a gidajen mu.
P8	Manomi yana kula da dukkan dabbobi sosai.
