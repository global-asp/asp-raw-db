desc:	This storybook reminds us to recognise and value what is important in life. (This story was created at a holiday programme run by Sophiatown Community Psychological Services in Johannesburg

page_text:

P1	Once there was a girl called Linda. She lived with her two sisters, brother and mother. @@She was a very happy and active girl.
P2	She had a best friend called Stacy. @@They trusted each other and always shared their secrets.
P3	Every Tuesday, Linda and Stacy would go to the movies because free popcorn was served.
P4	After watching a movie, they would go to the park and listen to music.@@They would enjoy ice-creams.
P5	This was Linda's happy day. @@Watching a good movie, with free popcorn.
P6	Then music and ice-cream at the park with Stacy.
P7	Happy moments do not last forever. @@Learn to cherish your happy moments…
P8	…and the people you share those moments with.
