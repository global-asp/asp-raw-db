title:	Nganeavhutshilo ya dziṋawa
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Nina  Orange
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First words

desc:	Nganea ya zwifanyiso i re na mutaladzi muthihi kha siaṱari

page_text:

P1	Khonani yanga o mpha ṋawa dzi bvaho ngadeni yawe.
P2	Ndo ṱavha ṋawa mavuni.
P3	Ṋawa i a mela mavuni.
P4	Mbeu i alutshela nṱha ḓuvhani.@@Midzi i tsela mavuni.
P5	Mbeu i a phula ya vha nṱha ha mavu.
P6	Mbeu i a aluwa,
P7	ya aluwa.
P8	Nda ṱavha ṋawa dzo engedzeaho.
P9	Zwimela zwi a aluwa,
P10	zwa aluwa.
P11	Zwimela zwi bveledza dziṋawa.
P12	Zwimela zwi aluwa zwa ya nṱha-nṱha u fhira luvhondo lwanga!
P13	Musi dziṋawa dzo no oma, ndi a dzi kaṋa.
P14	Ndi a dzi kuvhanganya fhethu huthihi.
P15	Ndi bika dziṋawa na nyala tswuku na zwiṅwe zwidzangelwa.
P16	Hedzi ṋawa dzo no lugela u ḽiwa.
