desc:	Noha owa mareho ebisibu ebinwa aha gati ya akame ne ekyende?(ekitabo ekyokubaza kikatebezibwa Mohammed Kuyu omu Oromo. Kandi ekyorugereza kyikahadikwa Elizabeth Laird).

page_text:

P1	izooba rimwe hakaba hariho episi ne ekyende.
Ekyende na episi bakagiraho obu takirizana.baza owomuramuzi ku tereza esonga zabwo.
P2	Omuramuzi kuyaherize kuhurira  omusangoyatina kusara orubaza aha musago rwabo.
Yatekyereza "kunasarire episi neza kurya amatungo gange.kandi kunasarire ekyende,neza kurya bona ebibara byanje,nkorekyi?"
P3	omuramuzi akatekyereza kumara akire,yagira "omusango niguremera takagusara wenka.mugende owa abakuru bwe ekyaro".
episi ne ekyende baza owabakuru ahakyaro.
P4	Bagabira abakuru ah butikyirizana byabo.
Bwayima abakuru abahurikyiza omusango,nabo batina kusara omusango.
P5	kuriba hagire ekyende,episi nebasa kurya amatungo.kuriba hagire episi,ekyende akaba nebasa kurya ebisuma byabo.abakuru bagabira ekyende ne wakame igu omusango rwabo gugumire.
P6	Abakuru ba kijuka omukazi omu towuni.akaba atine ekyo ku ferwa,ninga amatungo nari ebisuma.
"nabasa kusara omusango ati ne nkyakutina.kyagabwa abakuru bwekyaro nabandi.Bagabira ebikoko bibiri kuza owa ye.
abakuru na abandi.ebikoko bibiri kuza oweye.
P7	Baza owa aha igu womukazi bamusaba kubasarira omusango.yayikiriza.
konka yabagira ngu bagabe omwe ahari omwe.
P8	okubaza omukazi aketa episi yagigira,"oyine ekitinisa,ekikoko kihango,omurwani.Aha bweki noyoba nka akakoko kakye.
P9	Omukazi omyoro akagabira episi ati,"kuri abantu bahurira obutikyizana, babanibaza kukusarira omusango.rekyeraho kutongana na ekyende".
"ego ohikire,"kyagabwa wakame."niza kwihayo emisango.Emareho obutikirizana".
P10	Omukazi aketa wakame yagira,
"orwomuyonzo kandi orimurungi.ahabwekyi no yoba nomurofa,arikunuka kandi arikurya ebizuzire?abatu nibaza kutekyereza kyi bahurira ngu noyomba na enyamiswa ibi.
P11	kubamazire,omukazi ya betta boona ya  bagira,
"bwena myikirizane mureke emisango.musemerire ku sasirana.
P12	Byona ebikoko basaba okusasibwa kandi batereza eobutikyirizana.
Abantu bwo'omutahuni bakahurira ogu omugane,basoberwa.omukazi akasara omusango orwa remire omuramuzi na abakuru be kyaro.
