title:	Muyobozi aciyobwedo caakwe caandene
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa and Maureen Musale

desc:	Caano cabana babanyama bakafutulwa nobasika ku ciyobwedo caba mucaala. Otu tunyama tulasaninwa akulanganizigwa a muntu waandeni ambungano yakwe yabagwashi.

page_text:

P1	Eci ncaano ca muyobozi aciyobwedo caakwe ca banyama bamucaala.
P2	Mwezi wakainda wali mwezi wakubutauka kunji kuciyobwedo.  Wakusaanguna kusika kuciyobwedo wakali Nomsa wakali amwaka omwe. Kakokede amana kaetezya.
P3	Pesi mukaindi kashoonto, Nomsa wakaba ansaana.  Wakatalika kusobana abanyama bamwi.
P4	Mubuzuba bumwi mumwezi ngoonya oyu, cipolopela cakasika aciyobwedo.
P5	Mucipolopela mwakali kana kamuzovwu kakavwumbidwe ipayi.  Izina lyakwe ngu Ndiwa.
P6	Ndiwa wakali amazuba osanwe.  Muyobozi wakasanina Ndiwa kubelesya ibbodela.
P7	Bumwi busiku, boonse bakabusyigwa mukusika kwa indyabuluba lya zyina lya Ambia.
P8	Ambia wakali amyezi yosanwe. Wakali mubombe akaambo kamusinzo mulamfu.
P9	Akaambo kansingo yakwe ndamfu, mweembezi wakaimikizya bbodela mujulu kuti amusaninine Ambia.
P10	Bamucaala bakacaalizya kusika mumwezi ooyu bakali bana basyuumbwa botatwe kabali anzala.
P11	Muyobozi abagwasyi bakwe balabayanda banyama boonse, kusanganya abaabo baputa.
P12	Ibantu baaciyobwedo balabeleka canguzu.  Bali abulangizi kuti bumwi buzuba bamucaala  baya kulilanganya.  Elyo aba banyama bayakujokela musyokwe.
