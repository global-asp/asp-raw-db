page_text:

P1	Tolu loves to watch her shadow @She is always excited to see her shadow join in her play@When the sun set, Tolu always feel sad@She always thinks her shadow has gone to sleep
P2	The sun rises with a smile @Tolu is excited, I will see my shadow today, she said,@She rushed out of the house to play, her brotger had some fun too, @Her shadow joined in the play.
