title:	Ijen

lang:	Tiv

desc:	There is a drought and Mulongo and his family are searching for food. Ijen ka i mgbegha ior ve nyôr ken zaiyol.

page_text:

P1	Icegh gba. Tsombur u Mulongo lu a kwaghyan ga.
P2	Mulongo kura ve yem a min ken ikyô u keren kwaghyan.
P3	Ve zua a sule u kôôkômbu kpa ve nenge a orsule ga.
P4	Ve va nenge a zege ijôv mbakuv. @@Ijôv la na ve ian.
P5	Sev mbu aven yô, ijôv kaa er, "Jiidi mo wan u nomso la!"
P6	Aikie yô, ijôv kaa er, "Kper pepe ne jiidi mo wan ukwase la!"
P7	Mbamaren yer wan kwase la kpa sha kon.
P8	Ijôv kaa er, "Kper yô u jiidi mo nomou!"
P9	Aikie yô ijôv kaa a kwase la er a jiidi iyol na!
P10	Ijôv ye kwaghyan yô man a yav a gba kôun.
P11	Ijôv gema nenge tsombur la cii lu uma. @@Ishima vihi un.
P12	Ve er un atseregh ve hungwa sha kon ve yem.
