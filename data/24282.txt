desc:	The Story is about the Hyena (Kura) and some robbers.

page_text:

P1	Wani daji ne a a kan hanya da kura a ciki
P2	Sai kura ta rinƙa kururuwa  a dajin don kada yanfashi su shigo
P3	Ana nan, wata rana yanfashin suka shigo dajin da daddare
P4	Amma suka kama barci. Ashe kurar ta hange su
P5	Sai kurar ta tsirgo daga bishiya, yanfashi kuma suka farka suka ranta ta kare
P6	Ashe da maciji a kusa da su a dajin
P7	Sai macijin ya sari ɗaya, ɗayan kuma ya gudu.
