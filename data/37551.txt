P1	Once upon a time there lived two friends  Peter and John. They became friends in second year. Peter was always jealous of John. Everyone likes John because he was caring, intelligent and helps other. Peter was opposite of John.
P2	He never speaks truth, never obey other people and always cheat in exams. As John was intelligent and talented he decided to make a device. He shared his plan with Peter.  Peter got jealous of him.
P3	He thought that if John would a make device than he will become famous. Soon John made a device. He hurriedly informed Peter about it.  Peter asked him to show the device. He came to Peter and showed him the device happily.
P4	He told that l am going to launch this device next week. Peter stopped him and told him that first we should check that could it work properly? John agreed to Peter. Peter asked him for his device.  John gave him the device and left.
P5	Peter took his passport, device, clothes and some other things and left the city. John called him after two days but he didn't show any response.  John waited for Peter but he didn't came back. Finally the day to launch the device came.
