page_text:

P1	Joy akazana ezobaryona
P2	nyinawe yamugiraa ngu ayozye emwendaye
P3	yaronda akakuba ka mino
P4	nyinawe yamutera amizi omu bafu
P5	ya simura amishogye
P6	nabwanyima yaza kubyama
P7	omukasheshe ya kuta amino gye
P8	nyina yamuyamba kubyama
P9	kuyamazire ya siba enkitizye
P10	nabwanyima Joy yaza ihishomero
P11	Joy ari omukibina
