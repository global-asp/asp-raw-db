P1	UMzi uhlala endlini efulelwe ngotshani.
P2	UThulani uhlala endlini eyisikwele.
P3	Lendlu iwunxande.
P4	Uphahla lwalendlu lingunxantathu.
P5	Lendoda izakhela indlu yayo.
P6	Lendlu yintsha.
P7	Lendlu yindala.
P8	Ngifisa ukuba bengihlala kulendlu.
