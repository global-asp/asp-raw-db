P1	Once apon a time there was a bunny named sly one day she saw a chicken there was no chicken in the farm so she did not recognize it she ran away.
P2	She told dog but he also did not recognize it so he ran away with sly.
P3	They also told horse but it was to late. For horse also already seen it when they arrived and they ran away.
P4	They told donkey and he ran away with the others.
P5	Every one started running around the farm.
P6	They told cow to help but she said thats just a chicken !They should the owner of the farm.He was so happy because it was the first chicken the end.
