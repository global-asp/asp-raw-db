desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Sipho acha khusiiro nende omukhana wabwe.
P2	Omukhana wabwe alingalanga chingubo mumaduka. Sipho yahaluya.
P3	Sipho alola esindu simujamisie.
P4	Sipho alola esindu simujamisie.
P5	Yekera ebilaro ebio biosi.
P6	Omukhana wabwe amukulira ebilaro ebiokhubaya omupira kwa makulu.
P7	Esikera sina bali lwangu okhwola ingo?
P8	Ikhabi indayi yola musiguri sio okhubaya omupira khubikha. Afwalire ebilaro biaye. Biyakha bia okhubaya omupira kwa makulu.
