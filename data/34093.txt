page_text:

P1	Eze was ready for school but Ada his sister wasn't ready.
P2	Ada quickly took her bath and joined her brother on the way.
P3	They got to school and learned lots.
P4	Ada loves to teach her friends.
P5	Eze and Ada eat at school too.
P6	Ada and Eze always remember to wash their hands.
P7	At home, they eat a lot of fruits.
P8	Eze loves to draw often too.
