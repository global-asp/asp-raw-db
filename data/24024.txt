page_text:

P1	Fana neka ye nibatura
  omuri Debre Birhan, omyanya ogurikukora munonga omu ihanga lya
  Ethiopia. Fana nashomera omukibina kya kashatu. Aine omutima murungi kandi ayine obwenje.
P2	Fana nakunda enyamishwa.
  Aine enjangu, enkoko ebiri, embuzi, ne enkombe. Nakira kumara obwire bwe nazana nazo
  Kandi nokuzigaburira ebyokurya.
P3	Eizoba rimwe, akabanazaana
  nabagyenzi be ahishomero,
  yabareeba nibarekyera amabare nibateera enkombe. 
  

  Yayebuza “Ahabwenki bariyo
  nibagyezaho kuziteganisa?”
P4	Yarekeraho okuzaana yazakureeba
  abo abana abibire nibaziteganisa. Omukubanza banywanibe tibaramanyire ekirikugyenda omumaisho, reero bamukurata. Fana yagamba munonga “Murekyeraho kurekyera amabare”
P5	Abaana bairuka. Fana
  yakwata enkombe eibiri azabeire zafutiire. Yareeba ebironda ahamapapa gazo. Yasharaho ktwara enkombe omuka akazireberera.
P6	Yazigaburira. Omumwabazyo yaganirira
  abomuka ebyamubeireho Kandi nockuyacungwire enkombe.
P7	Omukasheshe, Fana nabaziire
  be bakaza omwirwariro batunga omubazi gwokureberera enkombe.
  

  Kuhahwireho ebiro bikye,
  ebironda byenkombe byakira. Fana akashemererwa munonga.
P8	Fana nagambira bagyenzibe
  “Ninkunda enyamishwa. Enyamishwa nizituherwa Kandi ningyenzi zeitu. Nitugemwa kuzireberera tukareba ngu tizafa.”
