desc:	Palesa alaalanda ku fimuti

page_text:

P1	Uyu ni Palesa. Ishina lyakwe lipilibula 'iluba' mululimi lwakwe ulwa ciTswana.
P2	Uluceelo Palesa alanda kucimuti ca ma olenji. "We cimuti ca maolenji,  tupeele amaolenji ayengi twapaapata."
P3	Palesa aya kusukulu. Munshila aeba icaani ati,  "We caani,  ba gilini saana kabili wikooma."
P4	Palesa amona amaluba yaimenenafye. "Mwe maluba,  twatootela pa makaala yeenu ayabalabata."
P5	Pa sukulu,  Palesa alanda ku cimuti icishilakula.    "We cimuti,  imisambo yoobe ikakule pakuti tukaleebelengelamo."
P6	Palesa asosa ku lupango lwashinguluka isukulu lyakwe ati,  "Ukule uwakosa pakuti uleelesha abantu babipa ukwingila mukati."
P7	Ilyo Palesa abwelela ku ng'anda ukufuma kusukulu,  ayamona icimuti ca maolenji. "Bushe amaolenji yobe nayapya?" Palesa amwipusha.
P8	"Amaolenji tayalapya," efya asuka Palesa.    "Tukamonana mailo we cimuti ca maolenji," efyo asosa. "Limbi mailo ukakwata iolenji lyandi ilyapya."
