desc:	Mulandunshi basaca tabafikila panshita kufitesheeni makamaka ilyo tuli noobulendo bwa kuya kutauni?

page_text:

P1	Sam naipekanya ukuya kutauni muno luceelo.@ "Saca ileeisa fye pa 9 koloko," banyina baaseka. @Sam aipekeenye ninshi kwashala insa shibili!
P2	Sam nabanyina baafika kucitesheeni ca saca  nenshita ya 8:45 uluceelo.
P3	Abantu abengi bafika kucitesheni lilya 9 koloko ishilakumana.@Inshita iitali yapita ukwabula ukuti saca uyu ese. Abantu bonse baleeloleela.
P4	Sam aasakama. "Limbi saca naifwa," efyo atontonkanya.@"Limbi tatwaye kutauni leelo. Limbi ilaya lyandi ilya kusukulu talyashitwe."
P5	Lilya inshita yafika 9:45 abengi banaka babwelelamo kumayanda. Ukumoneefi, Sam atampa ukulila. "Twalaloleelako panoono," efya balanda.banyina
P6	Tapaapitile nshita, baumfwa icongo.@ @Saca yaisa!
P7	Saca afika pacitesheeni nenshita ya 10 koloko.
P8	"Nineeni! Nineeni!.Twacelwa lelo!" Namutekenya  efyo apuunda.
P9	Abantu baanina muli saca nookwikala.@@ Saca yaima pacitesheeni neenshita ya 10:10 uluceelo.
P10	"Ninshitanshi yalabako saca yakubwela nayo?" banyina Sam baaipusha. @Namutekenya aatile, "Saca ya makumbi-makumbi ifuma mutauni neenshita ya 2:30 akasuba."
P11	Sam aatontonkanya, "Twalaafika mutauni neenshita ya 11 koloko." @"Mwati twalaikalamo  neenshita iitali?" Sam aasakamana.
P12	1. Ninshitanshi Sam ayipekenye ukuya uluceelo?@2. Sam na banyina baloleele saca neenshita iitali shani?@3. Saca yaceelelwe nenshita iitali shani ukufika?@4. Bushe saca isenda inshita iitali shani ukuya kutauni?@5.Saca wa makumbi-makumbi nga afuma mutauni panshita iilingile, bushe Sam na banyina baleeya fika nshita nshi kung'anda?
