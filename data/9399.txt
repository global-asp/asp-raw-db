title:	Lerato, Thabo Le Khomo
author:	Matlotlisang Tenane
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/9399.png
desc:	Thabo and Lerato have to deal with a difficult cow. The cow is sitting next to the village water supply.

page_text:

P1	Motseng o mong hone ho e na le mohlankana ea bitsoang Thabo.
P2	Thabo e ne e le motsoalle oa Lerato. Matlo a bo bona a ne a bapile,  e le bahaisane.
P3	Ka tsatsi le leng baea selibeng hona motseng moo.
P4	Ha ba atamela,  ba bona khomo e bothile,  haufi le seliba. Ba lula fats'e,  ba rera ho bapala le eona.
P5	Eaba Thabo o qalella ho e tsisa khomo ha e lla. "Muuu, muuu,  muuuuuuu," ka lentsoe le phefa.
P6	Hofihlela e hetla khomo eoo,  ea basheba. Hanghang Lerato a ts'oha. Thabo a mohlokomela hore o ts'ohile.
P7	Eaba o re,  "haree ho eona relo e shapa. Etla baleha e be re khaa metsi." "Aa Thabo,  nna kea ts'aba, '' ke Lerato eoo ka lentsoe le thothomelang."
P8	Empa Thabo a moqobella hofihlela a ba a lumela. Ha bafihla,  baleka ho e shapa,  empa ea seke ea tsoha.
P9	'Me Thabo a re,  "Lerato,  lula mona. Ke tlalokha metsi." Lerato a lumellana le ena.
P10	Thabo a khelella ea pele.
P11	Thabo o ile a hooa a re,  "Joooo,  joooo,  joooo!" Eaba Lerato e na o oele ke lits'eho.
P12	Khomo eona eaba e ntse e re fela,  "Muuu,  muuuu,  muuuuuu!"
P13	Empa ka morao hore Lerato a ts'ehe,  a mathela hae,  hoea joetsa 'M'e oa Thabo hore khomo e hlabile Thabo. A fumana 'M'e oa Thabo antse a fiela lebala kantle.
P14	"'M'e 'M'e 'M'e Matha Thabo! Kho kho khomo e hlabile Thabo ka lenaka!" ke Lerato eoo ka ho hemesela hoa letsoalo.
P15	'M'e Mathabo a nyahlatsa lefielo,  eaba o ile ka potlako hoea thusa Thabo. Oena o nahana hore ho etsahetse eng ka morao ho moo?
