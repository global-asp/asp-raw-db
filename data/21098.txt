desc:	Hamba asalaŋo ohuŋa esolo josijosi amagulu ko jaha nyumirwa ohugenda hyabaatu.

page_text:

P1	Hamba gaali aŋaaye
  abaatu bonyene amagulu.
P2	Hamba gasalaŋo ohuŋa hiisi solo amagulu.
P3	Esolo jendire amagulu
  ko jahagenda era jahaduluma.
P4	Hyajiberanga higosi ohweŋalula.
P5	Oludaalo ni rwolire,
  esolo jeŋalula jatiina ewa Hamba.
P6	Esolo ejindi jahinanga
  ate ejindi jegwiranga ŋaasi.
P7	Jaloma jiiti, "Sihuhigoboyeyo
  ohweŋalula nindi."
P8	Namugongolo nj’owasembayo hu
  lunyiriri.
P9	Hamba gaŋa Namugongolo
  amagulu gosigosi agaali ni gasigayeŋo.
P10	Etemu yaloma Hamba
  yiiti, "Mbaye mwiro."
P11	Hamba gahayire amagulu
  aganayiŋe.
P12	Ohuŋwa hulwo, etemu
  ehirindire ohufuna amagulu.
