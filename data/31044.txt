desc:	Ebidhandhaali amandha byeidha ku mwiiga. Buli mutu tiyayendha kusala.

page_text:

P1	waaliwo ebidhandhaali n'akandha katono. bali bagya mukyaalo.
P2	baatuuka kumwiiga gwebayendha okusala. bayagaana nga mughaanvu nga gutambula mubwangu.
P3	batandhika okughakana mungeri eyo'kusala. ebidhandhaali byakoba,'sooka osala.''
P4	bawghakana okutuusa ebidhanddaali bwebyasala.
P5	eryandha bwelyasala, lyaagwa mumaadhi,''pshhhhhhh!'' ebidhandhaali byaseka okutuusa bwebyeyawuzaamu.
