desc:	The story of two thieves

page_text:

P1	Wata rana da makaho da mai ido sun je satar mangwaro
P2	Da suka hau bishiyar, sai ga mai gonar. Mai idon ya ce "ga mai gona"
P3	Sai suka sauka daga bishiyar suka ruga da gudu.
P4	Sai mai idon ya ce "a tare, a tare, makaho zai fa…óa ruwa!
P5	Wajen kuma babu ruwa, amma makahon ya toshe kunne da hanci
P6	Sai ya buga nutso, ya buga fuska a kwalta, ya kurkurje a fuska
P7	Sai ya ce "wayyo, Allah, na mutu na lalace."
