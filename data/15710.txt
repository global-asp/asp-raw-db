title:	Mfumu Ya Mbalame

desc:	Mbalame zinafuna mfumu koma zinali ndi vuto losankha mfumu. Anaganiza kuti mbalame yimene yidza culuka ku mbululuka mumwamba-mwamba ndiyamene yidzakala Mfumu.
Sika yinapambana mkangano

page_text:

P1	Kale-kale mbalame zinasonkana pa msonkano. Zinafuna kunkhala ndi mfumu ngati anthu ndi nyama. Kodi ndi mbalame iti izakhala mfumu?
P2	" Nkwazi,  ndiwolimba alinso ndicifundo." Inatelo imodzi mbalame. "Ayi,  alibe cipolomombo ndipo amveka wacisoni kwambiri." Inanena ina. "Ndiye bwanji nthiwatiwa,  cifukwa ndiwamkulu,  ali ndi liu lokweza ngati mkango." Anakuwa wina. "Ayi,  sakwanitsa ku mbululuka. Mfumu ya mbalame ifunika kukwanitsa kumbululuka."
P3	"Koma ndikhale mfumu ine," anatero nkhukutembo,  alikuzunguza mcila wake. "Ndineso wokongola." "Ndiwe wonyada iwe," anati kazizi. "ine maso anga ndiwakulu kupambana mbalame iliyonse. Ndikhale mfumu ndine." "Iyai,  osati iwe kazizi," anakuwa. "Iwe umagona kukacha."
P4	Ndiye nkhani ya kazizi sinapite kutali. Koma mbalame imodzi inali ndi ganizo. "Iye womwe akwanitsa kumbululuka kufika patali,  ndiye azakhala mfumu." Anatero. "Inde,  inde, "Anakuwa,  ndipo onse anapita ulendo kumbululuka m'mwamba.
P5	Baka anambululuka tsiku limodzi,  kupita pamwamba pa phiri lalitali mu dziko lonse. Nkwazi inambululuka masiku awiri kufika m'mitambo pamwamba pa ma phiri. Koma nombo anambululuka masiku atatu osaima,  iye analikupita ku dzuwa. Pamwamba pa mbalame pazonse,  nombo analira,  "ndine nili pamwamba,  ndine mfumu."
P6	Koma pamwamba pake nombo anamvwa kaliu kang'ono. "Titi titi titi! Ndine nafika pamwamba poposa,  ndine mfumu." Uyo anali timba,  kambalame kang'ono pazonse. Timba anagwirilira phiko la nombo pomwe anali kupita pamwamba mu mlenga-lenga.
P7	"Siuzanipambananso," anatero nombo ndipo anapita m'mwamba. Anambululuka mpaka kutha mpamvu. "Ndili pa mwamba pa mbalame zonse. Ndine mfumu yanu!" ananena mokuwa.
P8	Koma kucoka mu mapiko ka mbalame kakang'ono kanatuluka. "Titi titi! Titi titi! Ndine wamng'ono! Ndine mfumu yanu" Nombo analema kwambiri kuti apite pamwamba.
P9	Anayamba kubwelera pansi ndi kambalame kang'ono mu mapiko. Mbalame zina zinakalipila timba. Anamuyembekeza kuti apusule mangala yake onse. Koma ka mbalame kang'ono kanaona kukalipa kwao,  ndipo kanalowa mu nyumba ya njoka.
P10	"Iwe wa maso wakulu,  ulonde pomwepa akafuna kutuluka umgwire," anati kwa kazizi Kazizi anakhala pa nkhomo kuyanganira.
P11	 Koma pomwe dzuwa linatuluka,  kunathuma ndipo kazizi anagona. Ka timba kasunzila ndikuona kuti kazizi wagona. Mwazizizi kanatuluka ndikuthawa.
P12	Kazizi anamva manyazi cifukwa kambalame kang'ono kanathawa. Tsopano asaka zakudya utsiku cabe. Mdzuwa agona ndikubisala,  kucoka kwa mbalame zinzake zonse.
