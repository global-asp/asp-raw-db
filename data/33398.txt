title:	Kati Nenja bayadvweba bese bafaka umbala
author:	Mduduzi Benjie Shongwe
artist:	Elke and René Leisink

desc:	BoKati Nenja babangani. Bayahlekisa futsi bajabulela kwenta tonkhe tinhlobo tetintfo ndzawonye.

page_text:

P1	Lona nguKati.@@Lena Yinja.
P2	Kati Nenja bafuna kudvweba.@@Kati Nenja bafuna kufaka umbala.@@Kati Nenja bafuna kwenta umsebenti webuciko.@@Kucala Inja.@@Itsatsa ipensela itsatse neliphepha. Liphepha lisikwele.
P3	Inja icale idvwebe lokusalicandza.@@Lokusalicandza kungumtimba waKati.@@Oh, loko kulula!
P4	Ibese idvweba bocalatsatfu lababili ngetulu kwalokusalicandza.@@Bocalatsatfu lababili batindlebe taKati ibese idvweba calatsatfu lomnyama kulokusalicandza.@@Calatsatfu lomnyama ulikhala laKati ibese idvweba umugca ngetulu kwelikhala.
P5	Manje idvweba emehlo nemashiya aKati.@@Emehlo ngulamachashati lamabili. @@Emashiya yimigca lemibili.@@Oh, loko kulula!
P6	Ibese idvweba umlomo waKati.@@Umlomo ngumugca.@@Ubukeka ufana nangwaca 'w' ibese idvweba emadzevu aKati.@@Emadzevu lamatsatfu eluhlangotsini lwesencele kanye nemadzevu lamatsatfu eluhlangotsini lwesekudla.
P7	Ibese idvweba imikhono nemilente yaKati.@@Imikhono nemilente yimigca ibese idvweba lutwane lwaKati.@@Tintwane tindingilizi ibese idvweba umsila waKati.@@Umsila ngumugca lomudze.
P8	Ekugcineni idvweba imishi emtimbeni waKati bese yenta umtimba uba nembala lowolintji.@@Nyalo umdvwebo sewuphelile.@@Oh, kwate kwaba kuhle!
P9	Nyalo selitfuba laKati.@@Utsatsa ipensela neliphepha.@@Liphepha lingucalandze.
P10	Kwekucala Kati udvweba lokusalicandza.@@Lokusalicandza kungumtimba Wenja.@@Oh, loko kuhle!
P11	Ubese udvweba imifanekiso lemnyama lemibili ngetulu kwalokusalicandza.@@Lemifanekiso lemnyama lemibili tindlebe Tenja ubese udvweba indingilizi lemnyama kulokusalicandza.@@Lendingilizi lemnyama likhala Lenja ubese udvweba umugca ngetulu kwelikhala.
P12	Manje udvweba emehlo nemashiya enja.@@Emehlo ngulamacashati lamabili. @@Emashiya ayimigca lemibili.@@Oh, loko kuhle kakhulu!
P13	Ubese udvweba umlomo Wenja.@@Umlomo ngumugca.@@Ubukeka ufana nangwaca  'w' ubese udvweba emacashati latungeleta umlomo Wenja.@@Lamatsatfu eluhlangotsini lwesencele nalamatsatfu eluhlangotsini lwesekudla.
P14	Umdvwebo sewucishe uphele.@@Udvweba imikhono nemilente Yenja.@@Imikhono nemilente yimigca ubese udvweba lutwane Lwenja.@@Tintwane tindingilizi ubese udvweba umsila Wenja.@@Umsila ufanana nembali.
P15	Ekugcineni udvweba tindingilizi letimnyama letimbalwa emtimbeni Wenja bese ufaka umbala lophuti emtimbeni.@@Nyalo umdvwebo sewuphelile.@@Oh, kwate kwamangalisa!
P16	Usale kahle Kati.@@Usale kahle mdvwebo waKati.@@Usale kahle Yinja.@@Usale kahle mdvwebo Wenja.
