page_text:

P1	Fana n'eka ye nibatuura omu rurembo eribakweta Debre Birhan, omu Ethiopia. Aine omutiima murungi kandi aine obwengye.
P2	Fana nakunda enyamaishwa. Aine ho kappa, enkoko ibiri, embuzi emwe ne enkombe.
P3	Eizooba riimwe, Fana  akanazaana ahishomeero nabanywani be yareeba abaana nibateera enkombe amabaare. Yayebuuza; "abwenchi nibenda kushashiza enkombe?"
P4	Okuzaana yakureka yiiruka aha abaana bari ku naaga amabaare. Abanywani be bamureeba  nayiruka bagyenda nawe. "Mureeke kunaaga amabaare." yayomba.
P5	Abaana bairuka. Fana yakwaata enkombe ezikushaasha. Kuyareeba ziine ebironda, yasharaamu ku twara enkombe omukaa azirebeere.
P6	Yazigabuura. Omumwazyo, yagambira abomuka ekiyakora n'oku ya jjuna enkombe.
P7	Omukasheshe, Fana naabazeire be baaza omu eirwariro baboona omubaazi gwe enkombe ze. Bwanyuma yebiro bikye,  ebironda byakira. Fana yashemererwa
P8	Fana akira kugambira banywani be ati, "Ninkunda enyamaishwa, enyamaishwa zitwiniire omugasho, kandi nizitukunda. Nituteekwa kuzirinda nokuzikuuma."
