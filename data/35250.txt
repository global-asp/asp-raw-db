P1	Sara has a canoe to cross the river.
P2	Some women want to cross.
P3	Some men want to cross.
P4	They get in.
P5	Some animals want to cross.
P6	They get in.
P7	The elephant wants to cross.
P8	Everyone falls out!
P9	The elephant is sad.
P10	Sara takes the women across.
P11	She takes the men across.
P12	She takes the animals across.
P13	The elephant has to walk!
