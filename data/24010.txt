title:	empungu ne enkoko
author:	fatuma naleba
artist:	Mango Tree, Silva Afonso, Marleen Visser and Melany Pietersen

thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/24010.png
desc:	Olugero lunyoyola rwaaki enkoko ebera na abantu ate rwaaki empungu elaya abaana be enkko.

page_text:

P1	olutuka empungu ne enkoko jali miwakono nfenfe mu bisera ebyobwetavu beyamba
P2	ba zaaya bona,ba zaaya ne emisolo ga nfudu
P3	baali balina ente endiga embuzi ne nengomiya nga nyinji baali bazilund mu bisubi nga we ba zaanya
P4	olunaku lumu enkoko  ya  gends  neyewola ejirita ewa empungu
P5	enkoko yamawa abaana bayo naye yelabila ejirita mu kitffo ekilungi
P6	empungu ya ja okumchi ejirita ya, ga enkoko ate ji funa
enkoko ya noya ne noya empungu na yiga netaki oku kalya aboona be koona
P7	enkoko ya duka yo li omulawe ne genda ne bera na abantu perka na kati enkoko bera abantu ate empungu elaya abaana be enkoko
