desc:	A book about a cat teaching her human how to rest.

page_text:

P1	Harley was a fluffy grey cat. She had a huge belly which she would stretch out on hot days.
P2	When it rained she snuggled on the clean laundry.
P3	When the sun set in the evening she would drape herself on the stairs.
P4	When the morning came and the sun rose, she stretched on mat.
P5	Her human was very busy. She worked hard every day .
P6	Harley tried to show her human how to rest. She tried lying in the morning sun.
P7	She tried lying on her human's book.
P8	Finally, Harley decided to do her best! She tried lying on her human.
P9	After a few minutes, her human fell into a deep sleep and Harley was happy.
