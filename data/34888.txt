page_text:

P1	Pci ana thlata dՁdupa tubwa raghi.@@I ana nggata tuwar fugwum sakari kula-kula bing.
P2	Pci ni ata tՁra ata kՁra kutakir kՁga Terfa akwabu sakar sum susimar dՁdupa.@@Atar didiwur mbwarma ni tՁng ja shurՁr bier ka@akpupa ni ana tsa ra.
P3	Pci ana tՁra abwahila nfwa, gaagha sakar sum susimar tapchi.@@Ka tsa titila ata vi njinjabu akwa vi hughuri gyeli ni.
P4	Pci thlathata amta ata kՁri. Shangwuri tsuwa thlathlata ahyimbli.@@I ana gyelir shangwur kՁga pazhiyerima!.
P5	Shangwur ka kela, kuma zir rakka kuma ta ka hwi ya kwa nuni.@@Shangwur na sabul kuma a di sabul wa ta ka hwi ya kwa nuni.
P6	I thlata, pajirna thlatha ya wuthi shangwuri. Ya yiri yanka lukwa class.@@Makaratar ku kuri, ya livi.
P7	Yin wuti chini tsakwa takwa thira. E wuti shagwuri atha liya.@@Saka heni ksi.
P8	Chini thira azir dhamma.@@E pippi atha kra da nkirna, e wuthum chi aka lungu.
