title:	Ekyenco kyange ni bbaabba

desc:	Manha kiki bbaabba ni nze byetugonza okulya ekyenco.

page_text:

P1	Bbabba wange ayendha bug nagherere.
P2	Ngonza obukopo bwa aisi kurimu
P3	Bbaabba agonza  kawa
P4	Ate nze nnhendha inho amata.
P5	Bbaabba agonza ekibala kya apo.
P6	Bbaabba agonza kawa.
P7	Bbaabba agonza inho omugaati. nze nnhendha musale-sale.
P8	Twembi tugonza agakeeke aga bwito.
