P1	Bousso is a young girl of 17. She wears a veil and always dresses modestly.@She works at Solalma Couture in Kaolack central market.
P2	The young dressmaker is respected by the customers who come to the workshop.
P3	Bousso does her work well. She is a good timekeeper and her boss likes her.
P4	Before special holidays, Bousso works much longer hours at the workshop so that she can finish all the customers' orders.
P5	The young girl dreams that one day she will have her own dressmaking business with her name over the door: Bousso Fashion.
