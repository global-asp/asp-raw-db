title:	Fowru bee dawngal

desc:	Fowru e yiɗi fiirugo bana dawngal. Sey fowru haɓdi fiirugo

page_text:

P1	Naane, fowru bee dawngal ɗum soobirajum.@@E ɗum pijida saa'i fuu.
P2	Nyalaande feere sey fowru wi'i soobaajo muuɗum, "E mi yiɗunoo fiirudugo bee maaɗa dow asama."
P3	Dawngal hokki fowru liiliiji seɗɗa, sey ndu nyooti biyeeli. @@Ammaa fowru teddii, walaa no ndu fiirira.
P4	Fowru ƴami dawngal, "Taa jaɓii, foti mi jogoo wicco maaɗa piiriden?" Sey dawngal bee fowru piiridi dow asama.
P5	Dawngal fiiri, fiiri naaka ngal somi.@@Ammaa fowru wi'i, "Fiiru seɗɗa fahin, soobaajo am!
P6	Sey fowru nani dippitannde. Liiliiji wicco dawngal pettake junngo maaru!
P7	Liiliiji dawngal potaayi jogaago fowru fahin.@@Fowru sey diwi dow duule. Ndu numi duule njogotondu, ammaa ɗe njogaaki!
P8	Jooni e ndu yana bee semmbe, sey ndu wulli, "Mballeeyam!!"@@Ammaa walaa nanoowondu. Dawngal e fiira dow.
P9	Sey fowru yani e leydi.@@Ndu ummii bee koyɗe gewɗe e ɓoranɗe e ɓanndu maaru fuu.
P10	Daga ɗon fowru e laƴa, e ndu mari ɓoranɗe e ɓanndu maaru.@@Soobaaku fowru bee dawngal timmii.
