title:	Impanuka

artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Rufumbira
level:	First words

desc:	Umuhungu abona akamodoka nikimodoka kininibi bona impanuka.

page_text:

P1	nabonye ikimodoka kinini kili kuza hasi mumuhale
P2	akamodoka gato kali kali kuva hejuru mumuhale
P3	umuvuzi wikimodoka kinini yali ali kuvugila kusimu
P4	ntabwo yali ali kuleba mumuhale
P5	habaye impanuka nininchane
P6	imodoka yidwaliro niya basirikale zaje kuyamba
P7	ibimodoka bibili byalukululana byalaje bijana izimodoka
P8	naratinye chane
