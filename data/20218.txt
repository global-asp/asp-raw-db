title:	Ntegeka Eya Kasimizi  Mbbedde
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere

desc:	Olugero luno lwa kasimizi akaali ne entegeka embbedde

page_text:

P1	Kasimizi yayendanga inho
  okusoma. Olwisi nga Kasimizi bamuyeeya. “Busimizi ti busoma. Busimizi bulina kulya. Busimizi busima bwa mmere ye busiima.”
P2	Kasimizi iye nga asoma kumalaku lunaku, nga asoma-a era yasoma-a-a omusana kugwa.
P3	Busimizi obundi bwatwaala
  kye bwagaine bwatereka mu kyagi kye eifo.
P4	Inhina ni lata wa Kasimizi baakaalaama,
  era Kasimizi yawulira bubi inho.
P5	Mu biseera ebyokughaaghuza, Busimizi bukuumira emmere mu kyagi kye eifo.
P6	Inhemizi ayenda amasitoowa
  gaidhule emmere, kale Busimizi bulina kukola inhoku.
P7	Kasimizi katandiika okuleenkaana
  ku kifo kye kaali kasomyeku.
  “Kifo kya kuliiramu kyetwetaaga, ekifo abantu mwe baja ni balya.
  Kityo bwe kikoba mu kitabo kye nasomye.”
P8	Busimizi buja bukumba
  kalala kalala. Bukumba bwe buja
  mu musana oguli kugwa.
P9	Kasimizi kakoba, “Bona, ekifo
  kiikyo!”
P10	Busimizi buja bukumba
  kalala kalala, buli kusitula mbooli, buli kusitula sukaali.
P11	Ghe bwira Inhemizi
  asanhuka. Amasitoowa gaidwire. Basagamba kilalu.
P12	Kasimizi afuna okunhwegerwa
  kwa inhina ni ise.
P13	Mwanhina omuto amugema
  ku mukono, “Buti ndoghooza ntegeera. Engeri yoyendamu okusoma ekitabo, eneegombesa nze zeena okwenda okusomaku.”
