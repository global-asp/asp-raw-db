title:	The boy who ran away
author:	Ingrid Schechter
artist:	Megan  Barry
lang:	English
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22357.png
desc:	This story deals with the emotions in a family when someone does something wrong.

page_text:

P1	Gemo and Nelson were brothers.
P2	Gemo went to school every day, but Nelson did not.
P3	Their father went to work in the town.

Their mother worked in the garden.
P4	One day his father left some money on the table.

Nelson took the money!
P5	His father was very angry.
P6	So Nelson ran away.

He walked and walked all day.
P7	He sat under a tree all night.
P8	His brother Gemo found him under the tree, and took him home.
P9	"Why did you steal the money?" asked his father.
P10	"Why did you run away?" asked his mother.
P11	"I am very, very sorry," said  Nelson, and he returned the money.
P12	They hugged him and were happy again.
