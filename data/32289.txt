desc:	How the tortoise got a hard shell

page_text:

P1	Wääthɛɛr leŋ Aŋui tiɛl
P2	Yen ëya ee leŋ Arou.@@Aŋui kek Arou aake mäth.
P3	Aci Aŋui lɛ̈k Arou, "Lok wïcku miok ciɛc."
P4	Aŋui aci kat apɛi.@@Ku lɔk Arou buɔth cok amaath.@@Yen ë ci rioc.
P5	Wën cï kek war tem.@@Kek aci tiap.
P6	Wën tiŋ kek athien ciɛc.@@Go Aŋui riŋ tënë athien.
P7	Aci Aŋui lɛk Arou, "Lör nhial ku ben athien ciɛc la bɛi."
P8	Arou aci lɔ nhial amaath.@@Go Aŋui puɔ̈u riääk.@@Ku loi kɛɛu tënë Arou.
P9	Arou aci athien ciɛc gɔɔt.@@Ciɛc aci thiei yomic.
P10	Arou aci löny.@Go athien ciɛc löny ye guop.@Aŋui aci miok ë ciɛc nyian.
P11	Ciɛc aca Aŋui kac.@@Yen aci kat.@@Go ciɛc buɔth cok.
P12	Aŋui aci riŋ wïïr.@@Go ciɛc ŋot ke buɔth cok.
P13	Go athien ciɛc ku miok aci ril Arou kɔ̈u.
P14	Arou leŋ kɔ̈u a kuet yen rot tiit.@@Ku ler baai.
P15	Këc ciɛc aci Aŋui yiɛk guop kït.
P16	Arou kek Aŋui akëc ben mäth.
