title:	Ah! Bol-koyɗe
author:	Also Mohammed Sale
artist:	Onesmus Kakungi
lang:	Fulfulde Adamawa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40294.png
desc:	Maamiraawo suka pamaro lili-mo ngam o soodana-ɓe are gila saagooru. Nde o yahi

page_text:

P1	Mi yiɗi fijudugo e soobaajo am Chuma.
P2	Min soobiraaɓe masin.@@Kala nde gooto amin lilaa fuu, min njahday.
P3	Nyalnde wonnde, maamiraawo am lili-yam mi soodana-mo mannda e nebbam.@@Mi yahdi e Chuma.
P4	Nder yahdu amin, min ngi'i soobiraaɓe amin e pija bol-koyɗe.@@"Ah! Bol-koyɗe," mi wi'i.
P5	"Ah! Bol-koyɗe," mi wi'i.@@Min maa mi yiɗi mi fija.
P6	Mi wi'i Chuma, "En ƴamu-ɓe to ɓe njaɓii pijiden e maɓɓe. Mi yiɗi fijirde bol-koyɗe masin."
P7	Mi wi'i Chuma, "En ƴamu-ɓe to ɓe njaɓii pijiden e maɓɓe. Mi yiɗi fijirde bol-koyɗe masin."
P8	Ammaa mi jaabii, "En piju tawon! Maamiraawo accataa'en co"ooɗen."
P9	Min kawti e soobiraaɓe amin nder fijirde. Min laatii aynoowo saare.@@Saare amin waɗiraama kaaƴe mawɗe ɗiɗi.
P10	Mi haɓdii masin ngam mi nannga bol.@Soobiraaɓe amin keɓaayi nyaamugo yalla bol go'o.
P11	Min piji haa nokkuure nden heewi njaareendi.
P12	Nden ɓaawo ɗon, min njahi saagooru. Ceede maamiraawo am majji! Mi fuɗɗi woyugo. @Chuma hooli, "Accu woyugo. An yiɗno artiraa fijirde."
P13	Min kooti saare naa e mannda sakko nebbam.@Min fuu amin min tunnii nden e min kultori.
P14	Maamiraawo amin seyaaki nde min kooti saare.@Ƴamol aranol ngol o ƴami min woni, "Toye ngonnooɗon gila nden?"
P15	Min mbi'iti-mo goonga haala.@O waggini-min, "Kul on meetii fijoygo on dunŋanaaka."
P16	Min njahi min nyaamnoyi aguwaagaaji.@Ɓaawo ɗon, min njahi min ngiiwii.
