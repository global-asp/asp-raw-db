title:	Umjarho Phakathi Kuka Fudu No Nogwatjha
author:	Zanele Sibanyoni
artist:	Iinlwana zazihlala ngehlathini zoke zithabile., Umjarho kanogwatjha noFudu, Baphetha ngokobana baphalisane ngokugijima., UNogwatjha wathoma ngebelo watjhiya ufudu., UNogwatjha noFudu bebaphikisana ngokobana ngubani onebelo ukudlula omunye., Wathi nakafika ekupheleli wathoa uFudu kade afikile.Kwaba kuthumba kukaFudu., Emva kwesikhathi waphaphama wathi tinini ngebelo. and UNogwatjha wajima wadinwa babona noFudu angabonakali waphumula ngaphasi komuthi.Wathi asalele wadlula uFudu.

page_text:

P1	Iinwana zazihlala ngehlathini zoke zithabile.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga uNogwatjha nofudu bebaphikisana ngokobana ngubani onebelo ukudlula omunye.
P3	Baphetha ngokobana baphalisane ngokugijima.
P4	UNogwatjha wagijima ngebelo watjhiya uFudu emva.
P5	UNogwatjha wagijima bewadinwa wathola umuthi endleleni waphumula walala. Wathi nakasalele uFudu wadlula.
P6	UNogwatjha waphaphama ngemva kwesikhatjhana wagijima.
P7	UNogwatjha wathi nakasekupheleni wathola kukade uFudu afikile. Kwaba kuthumba kukaFudu.
