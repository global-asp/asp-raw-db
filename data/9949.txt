page_text:

P1	I live in a village in Uganda called Kiwangala. It is a very nice village.
P2	The children in the village like to go to school. We like our teachers.
P3	We like to to read at school. We like to count numbers. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5!
P4	We like to play with our friends. We play with our balls.
P5	We like to eat banana's in our village.
P6	We like to run after the pigs.
P7	We like to play with our friends.
