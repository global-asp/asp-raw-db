title:	Umhobholo
author:	Nomasonto Sithole and Cynthia Nxele
artist:	Melany Pietersen
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Ujeziswa kanjani umfana onomhobholo odle izithelo eziningi?

page_text:

P1	Ugogo usiphathele izithelo emakethe.
P2	Izithelo ziconsisa amathe.
P3	Umfowethu uVusi akakwazanga ukuzibamba. Waqala ngokudla ubhanana.
P4	Hhayi bo! Ubani olume izithelo wazishiya?
P5	Wathukuthela wafa ugogo. Wathetha!
P6	Mina no Jabu sisola ukuthi nguVusi.
P7	UJabu watshela ugogo ukuthi sisola uVusi.
P8	Ugogo wambuza uVusi,  wavuma ukuthi nguye.
P9	UVusi waqala ukungazizwa kahle.
P10	Watshela ugogo ukuthi uphethwe yisisu.
P11	Wagijimela endlini encane.
P12	Ugogo wasibuza ukuthi sifunde sifundo sini ngokwenzeke kuVusi?
