title:	Ensiri enhiiza

artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Twenatwena twidhi kerere ensiri yedhikola nga dhiri kuzingaana obwiire. Akaghuka ako katonioli aye kiri kinene!

page_text:

P1	Konkona! konkona!
P2	''Ani ali omwo?''mbuza mundolololo.
P3	''Ninze,'' yairamu mu iroboozi eighaghavu.
P4	"Nani,'nze'?"Ndikubuuza.
P5	'' Nkukobeire,nti n-i-n-z-e z-e-n-ka,''mu iroboozi irala elyo.
P6	''Tidhi oyo ayetebwa,'n-i-n-z-e-z-e-n-ka,''Ndiramu.Nkebera akatimba kange akensiri oba kakunikize bukalamu.
P7	''Kale,eriina lyange ninze Amos."Eiroboozi nga lirenkana okusingawo.Lilikumpi inho ne emotoka yange.
P8	"Amosi ki?"Mbuza eno bwe ninga mukatimba.
P9	"Amos nsiri,"eiroboozi lyairamu. Mpulira ekinhuma kukutu yange.
P10	"ki-kino"Ntuma ghaigulu ntandiika okunonia."Kino oidha kukisasulira!"Ndekaana.
P11	Mubusungu,Nva muburiri nataaku ekibona.
P12	Ndikulangirira olutalo ku Amosi nsiri.
