desc:	Ninde uzacha urubanza rwigisamagwe n inkende? (oyumugani ni Mohammed Kyu wamanje kuwucha mori Oromo. Ngasobanuwe murugyereza na elizabeth Laird.).

page_text:

P1	Mbere nambere habayeho igisamagwe ninkende.
Igisamagwe n' ikende bwabifite impalana,
Bagiye kumuramuza ngo abaramure.
P2	Umuramuzi amaze kunva urubaza rwabo, Yatinye yantinye kurucha.
yaratekerje. "Ninenga igisamagwe, kirandira inka. Kandi ninenga inkyende, irandir' ibigori! none, nakora iki?"
P3	Umuramuzi yaratekereje kumara umwanya, nuko aravuga, "ururubanza rurakomeye, ntanarucha njenyine. Mufite kuja mubakabwe bicharo.

Inkyende n' igisamagwe bagiye mubakambe
P4	Babwiye abakambwe ibyobatari guhuza.

Abakambwe nabo bamaze kunva urubanza rwabo batinya kuramura.
P5	iyo bashigikira inkyende, igisamagwe chari kibasha kurwa inka zabo, iyobashigikira igisamagwe, inkyende yari basha kurya ibigori bwabo. Abakambwe barabibwiye ngo urubanza rurakomeye ntabarushobora.
P6	mukanya abakambwe bibutse umutindikazi Wumugore womumugi, ntayarafite ichoguhomba, haba inka chagwa ibigori.
Abakambwe barangoreranye "Yaruramura ntabwoba" Bambiye inyamaswa ngariwe zisanga.
P7	Bageze murugo rwa wamutindikazi,  baranubwira ngobashaka ko abaramura, yareye.
Gusa yababwiye kumureba umwe kurumwe.
P8	Mbere uyumugore yamanje yakura igisamagwe nuko aravuga, "urinyaswa nini yimanzi, kandi yuba wha. Nugute wa tera imprana naka gakyende kari kubwerabwera?"
P9	Uyumutindikazi yabwiye igisamagwe, "ubu abantu bunvishe uru rubanza bagusuzugura, rekeraho kuyomba ninkyende."

"Kabbisa arashintsa," niko igisamagwe chavuze. "Ndicha uru rubanza, ndahagarika ubu butunvikana."
P10	Uyumuntindikazi yakuye inkyende arayibaza ati, "asaneza kandi uraboneye, nigute watera impalana niki kinyamwanda, kirikunuka, kiryaga nibibore? Abantu batekereza iki bunvishe ko ufite urubanza niki kinyamaswa kikinyamwanda?"
Hashize akanya ikyende yarravuze "urimukuri! ndacha urubanza."
P11	Baragije uyumutikazi wumugore yarabakuye bombi nuko aravuga, "Mwese mwemeye kwicha ururubanza. None mufite kubabarirana,"
P12	Inyamaswa zombi zasabanye imbazi nuko zikyemura iki kibazo.
Abantu bomurako gache ubwo bunvishe iyinkuru yuyumutindikazi  wawa chiye urubanza rwananiye umurazi nabakambe, barumiwe.
