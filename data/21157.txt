title:	Morwadi Yo O Rategang

artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Setswana

desc:	Pitsa e e rategang ya ga Natefo e thubegile. O utlwile botlhoko thata mme o tshabela kwa sekgweng.

page_text:

P1	O ke Natefo. O na le bokgaitsadie ba barataro.
P2	Batsadi ba gagwe ba mo fa pitsa e nnye. O a e rata.
P3	Ka letsatsi lengwe bokgaitsadie ba thuba pitsa e.
P4	Natefo ga a bone pitsa ya gagwe. O lela letsatsi lotlhe.
P5	Natefo o a tshaba. O palama setlhare se se telele.
P6	Batsadi ba gagwe ba a mo fitlhelela. “Ka kopo, fologela kwa tlase,”.
P7	Bokgaitsadie ba opela, “Ka kopo fologela kwa tlase.”
P8	Natefo o kgala pina ya bona.
P9	Jaanong tsala ya ga Natefo ya fitlha.“Ka kopo fologela kwa tlase,” a opela.
P10	Tsala ya ga Natefo a opela go fitlhelela a fologa kwa setlhareng.
P11	Ditsala di tsamaya mmogo go ya gae.
P12	Botlhe ba keteka gore Natefo o boetse gae. Natefo o fiwa pitsa e ntšhwa.
