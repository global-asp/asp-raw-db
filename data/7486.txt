page_text:

P1	Lesicabucabu besihlala ekhaya elijwayelekile.
P2	Kodwa ngesikhathi izilwane zasendle zidla amagatsha ezihlahla,  sazithola sekufanele sibaleke kuleyondawo.
P3	Sathi sisahamba sazithola sesidukelene nomndeni waso.
P4	Kuwona lowo mzwangedwa lashintsha izulu. Lanetha kwaba nezikhukhula.
P5	Saqhubeka nohambo lwaso ukuyofuna ikhaya elisha. Kodwa indlela yayiyinde futhi yesinda.
P6	Endleleni sahlangana nonogwaja. Unogwaja wasihleka ngoba sasibukeka sethukile.
P7	Unogwaja wasiyalela lapho ikhaya laso lingakhona.
