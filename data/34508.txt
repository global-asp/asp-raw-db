desc:	U-Afeefah uyintandane yobhejane ohamba ngobuhlungu benhliziyo. Silindele ukusindiswa.

page_text:

P1	I-Afeefah ubhejane abasha abamhlope.@@AbakwaRanger bamsindise ehlathini.
P2	Umama wakhe wabulawa ngabazingeli.@@Abagibeli bathathe I-Afeefah bayisa endaweni ephephile.
P3	Inhliziyo ka-Afeefah yaphuka.@@Futhi, wayesaba ukuthi abazingeli bayobuya bazothatha uphondo lwayo.
P4	Kancane kancane. U-Afeefah wenza abangane.@@Wayezizwa ephephile futhi edlala odakeni.@@Waphuza ubisi olunomsoco endlini engcwele.
P5	Ngelinye ilanga u-Afeefah wabuza umngani wakhe u-Aaminah, "Kungani bafuna izimpondo zethu?"@@U-Aaminah waqukula,  "Bakholelwa ukuthi izimpondo zethu zingumlingo!"
P6	"Kodwa izimpondo zethu azimlingo!' kuzikhalela u-Afeefah.@@"Cha, cha akunjalo. Izimpondo ziefana nezinwele namazipho." Kusho u- Aaminah.
P7	U-Afeefah no-Aaminah bangabokugcina bohlobo lwabo.@@Obhejane abamhlophe bacishe baphela.@@Cishe konke kusukile emhlabeni.
P8	Singasindisa obhejane nezinye izilwane ezicishe zaphela.@@Yini ongayenza?@@Thola okuningi!
