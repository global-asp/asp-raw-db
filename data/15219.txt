page_text:

P1	Empyo yange nanyowe niturora ebinyonyi yange mumwanya.
P2	Ebinyonyi byange nanyowe tyatangaana ekikuya kiri mumuti.
P3	Ekikuya kyange nanyowe twazaana nentwiga hakasozi.
P4	Entwiga zange nanyowe twakora emipira nenkede kuruga mwibumba.
P5	Ekende yange na nyowe twalyaga ebitoke nengagya bulikiro.
P6	Engagya yange nenkende bitiina enyamankogoto.
P7	Enyamankogoto nanyowe twataaha nitugenda mpora muno nenjoka.
P8	Enjoka nanyowe tugonza kusuliza nokuzina harubaju rwenyanja.
P9	Eka yange twikara mukusemererwa kwingi muno.
