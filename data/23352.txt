page_text:

P1	Okuboonabona nokwa buli kihangilwe. embwa nekinya ne' ebinya nibyo okuleebelaho ebihngirwe. bika nibigamba ahabizibu byabyo ekinya kyagila, "Nintooba kuchila iwe." Embwa yamugalika mu, "Tokandebile omushaha ezokulya." Ekinya kyagila, "Buli wendi kwenda kulya esheehera, zigulika. Nki za ahabisika aha bwo bwile bubi, bantela amabaale." Embwa ejila ekinya egumeho omushaaha zokulya.
P2	kubabile ni balinda ebyokulya neeka, ekinya kyaguma aha kisiika kili kuleeba. Abashaija kuba tandika kugaba ebyo kulya embwa yisha. Abantu bakajitela na omuti muhango yiluka eli ku mooka. Tela yilukile haale ahabwokuba eka eyine enjala. Yagalika bajiha akanyama kakye. Ekinya  kikaleeba buli kyimwe kyikiliza nti embwa nizi tooba munonga.
P3	naha bwekyo, buli kuleeba embwa, ekinya ni kyi chunda omutwe. Ekinya kikahendela ni kigila ngu ekyokulya  ekya omntu omwe niki basa kuba obutwa obwa ondisho. Otali hangusa ebizibu byawe aha bandi ahabwokuba nibabasa kuba baine ebizibu ebiku kyila ebyawe ba kaku shekyela
