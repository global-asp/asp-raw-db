page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there were three little boys and one girl. They all lived in one village called Nakwiga.
P2	They were studying at Busolwe Glory of God primary school.
P3	On their way to school,  they used to hear birds singing.
P4	One day,  three boys and one girl decided to dodge school and went for hunting in the nearby forest.
P5	The bird started singing like this,  You call me bad; I am not bad x4.
P6	Am good but if you think am bad I will be bad at the end. Then the children caught that bird and went with it at home.
P7	When they reached home they slaughtered and ate it.
P8	The following day they all died. Because its meat was poisonous.
