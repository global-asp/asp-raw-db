P1	It was very early in the morning.
P2	I woke me up to prepare for school.
P3	As I dressed up,  I thought,  "How will I look like in my school uniform?"
P4	My father escorted me to school.
P5	I joined other pupils in class. It was my first day in school. Â  I had my first lesson.
P6	The next teacher to come to class was teacher Mary. She was tall.
P7	When i grow up,  i want to be like my teacher.
P8	I loved going to school very much.
