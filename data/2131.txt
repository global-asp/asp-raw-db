desc:	Different animals have different types of teeth (and some have no teeth). What happens to our teeth as we grow?

page_text:

P1	A crocodile has big teeth.
P2	A lion has big teeth as well.
P3	A cow has strong teeth.
P4	Birds do not have teeth.
P5	Rats have big front teeth.
P6	Sharks have sharp teeth.
P7	I used to have a lot of teeth.
P8	But now I don't have many.
