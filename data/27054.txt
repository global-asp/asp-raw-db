title:	Ani omubbedde?

desc:	Eige limagama weriri lyalowooza nti ibbedhe okukira ku ebintu ebiri awo. Aye mu butufu Eige ibbedhe? Ani munene?

page_text:

P1	Eige lyalingirira ebilime obuniavu ni ebimunku ebiriraine."ndi munene" lityo bwelyakoba.
P2	"Mbe, Eige.toli mubbedhe,"Wakadada bwe yakoba. Ndi mubbedhe!"
P3	"Mbe,Wakadada,toli mubbedhe," Wamuyayu bwe yakoba.Ndi mubbedhe!"
P4	"Mbe, Wamuyayu.Toli mubbedhe," wakayima bwe yakoba," ndi mubbedhe!"
P5	"Mbe, Wakiyima.Toli mubbede," atyo nkula bwe yakoba, ndi mubbedhe!"
P6	"Mbe Wankula. Toli mubbede,"atyo wandhovu bweyakoba,ndi mubbede!"
P7	Awo Wandhovu yalinga ku igulu. Eigulu lyali lidwire munhenhie, inene ate igazi,igaziye ebyandha nebyandha mu bule nsonda." Mbe,tiri mubbede,Oba! tuyinza okuba badiri!"
