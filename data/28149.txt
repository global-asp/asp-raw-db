page_text:

P1	awo olwaatuka,omu kukyabazinga olyemyaka yasalagho okufunha mutabanhi omu amufuule mutabanhighe.abe omusika gha namulondo ye, kubanga yali azila mwaana gha buleenzi.
P2	bweyamala agho,yayeeta abantu kukyaalo  yalondha abaleenzi ikumi.beyagha ensigo dha dhuuma.yabakoba bagye badhisiimbe.nga ghabiseegho saasira ibiri alileeta amakungula amalungi,alifuuka mutabanhi ghe omuzaale era nga nhaliba gha namulondo ye.
P3	mumaka agamu,omuvubuka omu yasimba ensigo dhe,era nga adhifukirira bulilunhaku.yakola kyokyonha ekisoboka aye ensigo dhadheema okumera.
P4	mikwaanho gye,gya mughabula agye mukataale agule dhuuma gwa na ghaayo eghaka asoboole okumulondha."nze tisobola kukola ekyo,abazaile bange ba ndhegeresa okuba oghhamazima".omuvubuuka ayanhukula eri mikwaanho gye.
P5	ebiseera ne nhaka,byaabita,abaleenzi eikumi baagya mulubiri okulaga amakungulagaibwe.boonhabonha baajja nebikutiya bya dhuuma okutoola ku omuvubuka mumulala.
P6	wakolaki n'ensigo dhenakugha?kyabazinga alikubuuza omuvubuuka eyali n'engalo ensa.ogh'ekitibwa,nhasimba,nafukirira aye tidhaamera.kyabazinga yatwaala omuvubuuka yakooba ati,amazima nh'ekyobugaiga kyabazinga yeenha kyaalina okuba nakyo.ensigo dhenabagha dhaali nfuumbe,nga tidhisobola kumera onho omuvubuuka yenka nha ghangwiire afuuse mutabanhi ghan
