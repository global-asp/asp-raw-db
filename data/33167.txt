page_text:

P1	Delesi ya mkulu wanga niikulu kwambili kwa ine.
P2	Juzi yamkulu wanga niikulu kwambili kwa ine.
P3	Cola canga nicikulu.@@Koma sicikulu kwambili.
P4	Bande yakale niyocepa kwambili.
P5	Ici nicisote ca kadoli wanga.
P6	Ma sokosi anga akwana bwino.@@Koma kala siyamene.
P7	Izi ni nsapato zanga zasopano.
P8	Zinikwana bwino kwambili.
