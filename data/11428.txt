title:	Te Klein

desc:	Lebo has grown out of all her clothes and they are now too small for her. What will she do with them?

page_text:

P1	"Ma!" roep Lebo. "Kom kyk. Hierdie klere is te klein vir my!" "Laat ek kyk," sê Ma.
P2	"Kyk,  hierdie romp is te klein vir my," sê Lebo. "Ja,  dit is," sê Ma. "Jy kan die romp vir Nomsa gee."
P3	"Kyk,  my denimbroek is ook te klein vir my," sê Lebo. "Ja,  dit is," sê Ma. "Jy kan dit vir Nomsa gee."
P4	"Kyk,  my t-hemp is ook te klein," sê Lebo. "Ja,  dit is," sê Ma. "Nomsa kan jou t-hemp kry."
P5	"Kyk,  my trui is ook te klein," sê Lebo. "Ja,  dit is," sê Ma. "Nomsa kan jou trui kry."
P6	"Kyk,  my reënjas is ook te klein," sê Lebo. "Ja,  dit is," sê Ma. "Nomsa kan jou reënjas kry."
P7	"Kyk,  my kouse is te klein," sê Lebo. "Ja,  hulle is," sê Ma. "Nomsa kan jou kouse kry."
P8	"Kyk,  my tekkies is te klein vir my," sê Lebo. "Ja,  hulle is," sê Ma. "Nomsa kan jou tekkies kry."
P9	"Nou het jy sommer baie klere,  Nomsa" sê Lebo. "O nee,  ek het nie," sê Nomsa. "Hierdie klere is almal te groot vir my!"
