title:	Cirgu e Lelwa

desc:	Cirgu e lelwa kunake ngam waɗugo soobaaku. Ammaa soobaaku ɗum neeɓaayi

page_text:

P1	Koo ndeye cirgu e yiɗi nanngugo lelwa.
P2	Koo ndeye lelwa e sobtoo cirgu.
P3	Nyalaande feere, cirgu noddi lelwa, "Sey coobootiren. Ko nyaamaymi min, an a nyaamataaɗum. Naaʼ en haɓidooɓe."
P4	Lelwa yerdake. Cirgu sey wiʼi, "En kunoo en soobiraaɓe. To gooto men yewii alkawal, ɓinngel muʼum maayay."
P5	Ɗi kunii, ɗi coobira. Jemma, lelwa waaloo ley lekki, cirgu boo e dow lekki kin.
P6	Yeeso seɗɗa, lelwa tekki. Cirgu boo laafi.
P7	Cirgu nanii suuno nyaamugo lelwa tekkunga. Cirgu wiʼi, "Mi faalaaki alkawal ngam mi walaa ɓinngel."
P8	Cirgu diwi jippi ngam nanngugo lelwa. Sey cirgu ɗaggi hakkunde lice leɗɗe.
P9	Lelwa boo hultori. Nga diwi e nga woya, "Ɓey! Ɓey!"
P10	Cirgu torake wiʼii, "Soobaajo am, wallitam. En kunake gewuɗo alkawal fuu sooyay ɓiyum."
P11	Lelwa notii, "Hakkilaayi, danyooɓe maaɗa kunii, naaʼ an! An anndi."
