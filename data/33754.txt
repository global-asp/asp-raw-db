page_text:

P1	I live in a house.@@It shelters us from the rain, wind and sun.
P2	We have cups in our house.@@We use them to drink water, tea, juice and smoothies.
P3	We have flowers in our garden.@@They are pretty and smell nice too.
P4	My brother and I ride our bicycles.@@ We have fun doing this.
P5	If I had a horse, I would ride it to my castle.
P6	I love my cat!@@I feed it cat food.
P7	If I had a tortoise, I would feed it lettuce.
P8	I see trucks on the road near my house.@@Trucks carry different things around.
P9	I am a super hero.@@I have rainbow powers! @@I love bright rainbow colours!
P10	I can enter a submarine.@@I can see water animals.
P11	I can change shape. @@I can move easily.@@I can enter a mouse's home to visit.
P12	Superheroes like to eat cake at parties. @@Cakes are sweet!
