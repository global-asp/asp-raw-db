title:	Soobiraaɓe Famarɓe Ɗiɗo
author:	Abdoulaye  Adamou Bappa and Dayyibu  Abdullahi
artist:	Jesse Pietersen
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31942.png
desc:	Habaru no soobiraaku fuɗɗiri e no ɗum wonnorii ngam peerootiral allaadaaji.

page_text:

P1	Ndaa Ɓii Mbeʼa bee Ɓii Siiwo.  Koy ngarti soobahoy.
P2	Koy mburtii ladde koy pijoyi.
P3	Ko koy ngarti wuro jenngi.
P4	Ɓii Mbeʼa wiʼi inna maagel, "Mi heɓii soobaajo boɗɗo, mi nanii belɗum min pottay jaango maa fahin."
P5	Inna beʼel jaabii, "Kaay ɓinngel am mi seyake nde a wartii jam.  Oʼo naaʼ o soobaajo men. Taaʼ fottu bee maako jaango."
P6	Wakkati goʼo Ɓii Siiwo maa wiʼi inna maagel, "Mi heɓii sobaajo boɗɗo, mi nanii belɗum min pottay jaango maa fahin."
P7	Inna siiwo jaabii, "Kaay ɓinngel am mi nani mone nde a nyaamaayi Ɓii Mbeʼa. To on pottii jaango mbarungel nyaamaangel."
P8	Nde weeti Ɓii Siiwo yahi wuro Ɓii Mbeʼa.
P9	Ɓii Mbeʼa wurtaaki yaasi, ngel nyukkii nder suudu bee inna maagel.
P10	Ɓii Siiwo hoolii wiʼi, "En ndillu ladde pijoyen."
P11	Ɓii Mbeʼa nootii wiʼi, "Diga hannde naaʼ en sobaaɓe, e mi nanana inna am. Ɓikkoy mboɗkoy e nanana innaʼen maakoy."
P12	Ɓii Siiwo sunake hootii to inna mum.
