desc:	Arai omuk egemita huno agalihini huno omuk?

page_text:

P1	Itwa miji owon nya etarissa ihiji wati hulo (huno) olufaji egemita. @@Iyiyoi daŋ abotie (abotye) abotie jio (jyo) egiama hosi ana agalik to.
P2	Ojio Ettiyanani, egiama honyi ana agalik to. @@"Obeŋi dwo ettiyanak, ibiaŋata dwo itai ifwata isukul ittiyanari."
P3	Ojio Aduhoni, egiama honyi ana agalik to. @@"Obeŋi dwo aduhok, ibiaŋata dwo itai iwanata iko asukuli hune ittiyanarik kuya bwo ve asik huno ojotorik."
P4	Ojio Ahawotoni bau, egiama honyi ono agalik to. @@"Obeŋi dwo ahawotok bau, ibiaŋata dwo itai iwanata iko asaŋ (nasaŋ) hasik huno asik iko asukuli hatai."
P5	Ojio Akim (etamatani yani), egiama honyi ono agalik to. @@"Obeŋi dwo Akimihien (Akimihyen) iko etaŋwak, iŋwati dwo itai ette iko iye."
P6	Ojio aromoni, egiama honyi ono agalik to. @@"Obeŋi dwo aromok, ibiaŋata dwo itai iwanata iko angiria ono oŋiyei."
P7	Ilorissa eto (eito) Sukul jio jio egiama duri Sukul ana agalik to.@@"Obeŋi dwo aduri Sukul, obeŋi dwo ve ettiyanak, aduhok, Akimihien, aromok bwo ahawotok bau."
P8	Te ehalu, iruhi isi daŋ jio jio agalihini egemita daŋ.@@Ewak hohoi ettiyanak, aduhok, Akimi (Akimihyen), romok iko ahawotok bau.@@Ati itteri isata bu do daŋ aduri sukul!
