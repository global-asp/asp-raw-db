desc:	Kigana me mako kwoŋ pus ma ofonji kula maber.

page_text:

P1	I calo mawan nitye Jadwoŋ ma i lwoŋo ni Dibworo. Dibworo nitye gi pus ma akol. I lwoŋo ni Olonyo.
P2	Ka Jadwoŋ Dibworo kidho tic,  go weyo thigo ma ku cego.
P3	Olonyo bedo i dho ot to kuro ot pa Jadwoŋ Dibworo.
P4	Ka gwok pa jager obino,  Olonyo neno ma omoro amora waŋe.
P5	Ka gwok oneno ŋweco aŋweca ma doko ku ŋiyo cen. Aka doko ki dwoki.
P6	Jager ma olimbo,  nwaŋo Olonyo pa nende oyikere ayika ma gwaro go.
P7	Ka jager oneno ameno wire awira to ŋweco kadho. Aka doko ki dwoki.
P8	Ka Olonyo winjo kec neko,  dhiro thigo gi lwete to diyo matek.
P9	Go gik i ot to bedo piny to mwole. Nyaka to camo cemo.
P10	Ka Olonyo ocowo,  por i kom ma yom to nindo. Kenyo onyo iwinjo nyaka ka ywe ni,  "Purr! Purr! Purr!"
P11	Ka Jadwoŋ Dibworo odwoko,  Olonyo twenyere gi kisangala to jolo go.
P12	Jadwoŋ Dibworo kodi Olonyo jo bedo cegin gi mac to kis dhano soma papila.
