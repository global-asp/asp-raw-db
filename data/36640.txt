P1	One day in 1921 three weeks before Christmas, a couple and their four year old daughter moved in a house in the middle of the woods
P2	Three days after moving in the couple found  a drawing in their daughter's bedroom but they thought nothing of it and went to bed
P3	The next day the parents asked their daughter "why do you have a drawing of Santa in your room?"  "She told me to". "Who told you to do that?" "The spirit of the witch@"said Sam in a demonic voice
P4	The day of Christmas Eve came and the family had forgotten about what had all happened a few days ago .They all went to sleep at 9 p.m. Santa came and the spirit of the witch traveled through the potal of the drawing possessing santa
P5	Possessed Santa went to the kitchen and took the largest knife and gave it to Sam telling her to go kill both her parents
P6	After killing her parents she stabbed herself to death
P7	Three years later the family's bodies@ was discovered by a police crew
