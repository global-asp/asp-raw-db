page_text:

P1	Palesa nŋwala. O wuor la gbin aa puug o teng, Setswana antoa poone.
P2	Beege darwoo, Palesa toat tera leemu tee yee. "Leemu teega, tem te leemu berug-berug."
P3	Palesa n ne ya sukuu, o ne toat tera mug yee. "Muugo zuogen somma ka de kpii."
P4	Palesa n dol mug puut zi'i, o ne yee. "Puure, ya tuuma tuuma ne ya muolum soma wa."
P5	O n be sukuune, Palesa ne toat tera tee bila yee. "Teega, ke ka e yilla zuo yiem, ka te ne zi e ma'ahem ne kaalma."
P6	Palesa ne toat teet fulenfuut o sukuune yee. "Ya kpegem paa ka de ke ka nirvoa biega kpeet ya poone."
P7	Palesa n ne yi sukuu paa yinne na, o ne kaaga leemu tee la. "E leemu nan pu muoge?" Palesa n ne bueh.
P8	"Leemu la nan aa bun kaha," Palesa ne fabula ŋwala. "M sa'an nan nyee beog," o ne yela nŋwala. "Daansier e nan mar leem be'emer ne."
