title:	Purutanai unoti, "Ngatishambe nyara"
author:	Emmanuel Sithole
artist:	Nathi  Ngubane
lang:	ChiNdau

desc:	Purutanai une makore gumi. Mundawu mwaanogara mwaite dambudziko recoronavirus. Purutunai unophahlangura maqhinga ekunyisa ndiwo chirwere ichi.

page_text:

P1	Bhuku rineri rinokukumidzwa kune vana vadoko vemuAfrica
P2	Bizo rangu ndinozwi Purutanai. Ndine makore gumi ekubarwa.@@Ndinogara nemukunda wangu, Yayeyai, une makore gumi nematatu.@@Tinogara neabereki nekamunun'una kangu Reyai nembwa yedu Bhingo.
P3	Kubva payakaguma, coronavirus yakaphindura maponere edu.@@Coronavirus chiro chidoko maningi asi chinobaarwarisa zvemene mene. Munthu weshe unokona kurwara ndiyo.
P4	Ndinonyangadzwa maningi ngekuti andichaendi kootamba nevangani vangu.@Reyai unonyangadzwawo kuti aachakoni kugumbatirana nevangani  vakewo.@Yayeyai unochemawo kuti aachakoni kupa kana kupuwawo hwumafuta hwekununisa muromo ngendombi dzinthani
P5	Mai vedu vanoita mushando wekusuka nekutsvaira kuchibhedhlera. @Babawo vanotengesa miriwo nemichero kuzvitoro.@@Pavanobuda zuwa rega rega kuende kumushando Yayeyai ndiye unosara ari mukuwane pamuzi.
P6	Chigarisano chedu ndimbuya vachembera maningi vane chirwere chemwoyo.@@Ngendaa yecoronavirus inothyisa kuvanthu vechikuru, apana unotenderwa kukhwedera padhuze navo mukuwo wetinawo unowu.@@Atidi kuti vabatwe ngecorona.
P7	Mai vabva kumushando vanoenda koovaona nekuvapa michero.@@Vanobeka michero pamusiwo ngekuti avatenderwi kungena mukati memphatso munambuya.
P8	Mangwanani ega ega, Yayeyai unochera mvura pamugodhi.@Ngokuti vamweni avagqoki zvekumbonyesa muromo nemiro, Yayeyai unoshandisa dhuku kuzvimbonya.@Yayeyai unogezera nyara dzake kanyi. @Mai vanoobeka zvimushonga zvekuuraya coronavirus munyara dzedu.
P9	Madeekoni ega ega, mai vanokarakadzira veiti:@@"Gqokai zviputiriso zvemuromo nemiro peshe peshe. Shambai nyara kwekamukuwo kakati rebei. Mbonyai miromo nemiro dzenyu mweikotshora".@@"Tazvizwa eya mai!" Tinopingura.
P10	1. Shambai nyara ngemvura.@2. Shandisai sipho kushambe nyara.@3. Erengai kugume makumi mairi meishamba nyara.@4. Sukurudzai nyara ngemvura.@5. Omesai nyara dzenyu ngethaura.@6. Kana madaro chiziyai kuti nyara dzenyu dzachena.
