title:	The big man in the village

page_text:

P1	In this village, there is a big man everybody is afraid of.
P2	Some children don't go to school because of the big man.
P3	The children are also afraid of going to the hospital.
P4	People asked the doctor not to go close to the big man.
P5	Doctor asked why they don't come to the hospital?
P6	The people said they are afraid of the big man.
P7	The doctor said, "don't be afraid of the big man."
P8	The big man said, "I will not hurt you. I am only a gaint.''
