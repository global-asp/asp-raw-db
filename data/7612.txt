title:	Enva Z'ebinyebwa
author:	Ritah Katetemera

lang:	Luganda

desc:	Olugero luno lukwata ku maama eyategekera abomumaka ge enva zebinyebwa. Balya nebawomerwa nnyo.

page_text:

P1	Ebinyebwa,  ebinyebwa,  ebinyebwa.
P2	Maama wange afumba enva z'ebinyebwa.
P3	Enva z'ebinyebwa ziwoma nnyo. Nze mpomerwa nnyo enva z'ebinyebwa.
P4	Taata wange awomerwa nnyo enva z'ebinyebwa.
P5	Maama wange ye aziwomerwa nnyo.
P6	Enva z'ebinyebwa ziwomera nnyo ku ttooke. Anti bwozirirako obula okwekomba olwobuwomi.
P7	Maama atadde enva z'ebinyebwa ku kyoto,  ataddemu akatungulu,  omunyu,  obunzaali n'enyanya.
P8	Ataddemu omuliro mutonotono. Enva zitokota mpolampola. Zirese omuzigo era ziyidde bulungi nnyo.
P9	Maama,  taata,  nange twesunga okulya ettooke lyaffe nekinyebwa kyaffe ekibobedde obulungi.
P10	Maama ajula emmere,  buli omu amuwa enva nyingi. Tukoza ettooke lyaffe netuwomerwa nnyo.
P11	Ffena twawomerwa nnyo,  netwebaza maama nti webale okufumba nyabo.
