title:	Mamela Mmele Oaka
author:	Moeketsi Buku
artist:	Angie & Upesh
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2031.png
desc:	Kaofela ha rona re na le mmele e metle. U kile oa mamela oa hao mmele hore o re eng ho oena.

page_text:

P1	Tsatsing lena ha ke tlo ea sekolong. Ke letsatsi la phomolo.
P2	Tsatsing lena ha ke shebella TV. Ha hona motlakase.
P3	Ke tlo etsa joang? Tsatsing lena ketlo mamela mmele oaka.
P4	Pele ke tlameha ho thola haholo, ele hore ke mamele mmele oaka.
P5	Eea, hona joale kea ikutloa ha ke hema. Ke hemela kantle le ka hare.
P6	Hape nka khona ho hemela holimo haholo. Sssss! Hape, ka hemela fatsé. Hmmm!
P7	Hona joale ke khona ho utloa pelo eaka ha e otla. Tuluum, tuluum, tuluum!
P8	Na nka khona ho etsa pelo ea ka e otle kapele kapa haholo? Eea, kaho qhomela holimo le Hase ka makhetla a mashome a mabeli?
P9	Hona joale bona pelo eaka e otla ka pele. Tululuum,  tululuum, tululuum!
P10	Haeba ke beha menoana eaka leqaqailaneng. Keutloa, ho runya-runya.
P11	Ebile ke khona ho ikutloa hake tséha. Hahaha!
P12	Ke khona ho ikutloa hakella. Buuhuuhuu!
P13	Ke khona ho ikutloa hake opa liatla. Pa, pa, pa!
P14	Ke khona ho ikutloa ha mpa eaka ella kru, kru, kruu!
P15	Mpa eaka a ere, "Mphe lijo!"
P16	Linko tsaka li khona ho nkhella monko oa likuku tseo le phehiloeng ka kichining. Le hona joale, ke batla ho utloa mehlare eaka ha e hlafunya likuku tsena.
P17	Ho monate ho mamela mmele oa ka. Joale ke batla ho mamela mehlahare eaka ha e hlafunya.
P18	Le mala aka ha a sila likuku tse monate tseo 'M'e a li entseng.
