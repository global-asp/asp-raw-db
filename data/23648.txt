page_text:

P1	Obwa kale kale, enyamishwa nzikasharamu kukora embaga. Konka ekaba ery'eye biryamishwa ebyiine amahembe.
P2	Wakame ekaba neyetebekanisiza embaga konka yashanga embaga ekaba etari yaburyomwe. Ekaba eri 'ye nyamwishwa ebyabiire byiine amahembe.
P3	Wakame yasharamu kugyenda ahantu ebooneyo amahembe. Kuyahelize yagatekaho yaza omumbaga.
P4	Wakame kuyahikire omumbaga, yarya, yarya, yanywa hamwe yazina.
P5	Konka kuyahelize, yaruha yagwa omumushana. Kuyabiire ebyami, gamahembe galagala.
P6	Ebinyamishwa byakimanya ngu wakame ekaba etari omwe aharibo. leero bakulula amatu ngayo mpaka ngabire malingwa munonga.
P7	Kubaherize, bayiruka. Ekyo nikyo kyiku'ku shobololela ahabwaki wakame eyiine amatu malingwa.
