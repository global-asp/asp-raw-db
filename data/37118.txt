desc:	Ntsheketo wa wanhwana wa bofu loyi a tsakisiwaka hi ku jikajika exirhapeni. (Ntsheketo lowu wu tshuriwile eka Ndzeteladyondzo yo Tsala Mitsheketo eAbuja hi 2018

page_text:

P1	Kulani u tswariwe a nga voni.@@Mahlo ma yena a ma si tshama ma vona vatswari va yena, buti na sesi va yena. A tiva swikandza swa vona hi ku swi khoma.
P2	Siku rin'wana Kulani a jikajika exirhapeni.@@U rhandza nuhelo wo tsokombela wa swiluva.@@U rhandza ku khoma swiluva swa kona.
P3	Kulani u twa wonge a nga vona swiluva, kan'we ntsena.@@"Swi sasekile," a tibyela.
P4	Siku na siku, Kulani a fambafamba exirhapeni. A tiva na tindlela ta kona.@@Namuntlha, u tlakusile nhloko a languta ehenhla.
P5	Kulani a twa ku duma na rihati.@@"Ndzi fanele ku tsutsumela endlwini," Kulani a ehleketa.
P6	Mpfula yi sungula ku na.@@Kulani a rheta, a lahlekeriwa hi vulawuri a wa.@@A wela ehenhla ka ribye hi nhloko.
P7	Loko Kulani a pfuka, a ku ri na vanhu va n'wi rhendzerile.@@"Ku humelele yini?" a vutisa.
P8	"U wile na ku banana na ribye hi nhloko," tata wa yena a vula.@@"Hi khensa Xikwembu u kahle," ku vula mana wa yena.
P9	"Tatana, Manana?" ku vutisa Kulani. U tlhele a huwelela na vito ra sesi na buti wa yena. "Ndzi kombela mi ndzi kumela xiluva."@@Un'wana na un'wana a hlamala.
P10	Sesi wa yena a n'wi nyika xiluva.@@Kulani a xi khoma kahle. "Xi sasekile," u vula.
P11	Ndyangu wa yena va langutana.@@"Kulani, wa swi vona swiluva?" ku vutisa mana wa yena.
P12	Kulani a tlangisa swiluva evokweni ra yena.@@"Hinkwerhu misasekile, ku fana na swiluva leswi." A n'wayitela.
