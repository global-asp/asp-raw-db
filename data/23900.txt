title:	Okambishi nombwa taya thaneke nokumbapeka
author:	Hamukoto  Efraim
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Oshindonga
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Okambishi nombwa ookuume. Aayolithi nohaya tyapula okuningila iinima yawo pamwe.

page_text:

P1	Hano okambishi.

Hano okambwa.
P2	Okambishi nombwa oya hala oku thaneka. 

Okambishi nombwa oya hala okumbapeka. 

Okambishi nombwa oya hala okuhonga. 

Ombwa oyo ya tameke. 

Ombwa oya kutha opena nombapila. Ombapila oyili molupe losquare.
P3	Tango ombwa oya thaneke ongonga. 

Ongonga oyo olutu lo kambishi. 

Wow, shoka oshipu!
P4	Ta thaneke natango oohuke ndatu pombadha yongonga. 

Oohuke mbali odho omakutsi gokambishi natango okwa thaneke oohuke oondudhe meni lyongonga.

Ohuke ndjoka odudhe oyo eyulu lyo kambishi natango okwa thaneke omusinda pombanda yeyulu.
P5	Paife ota thaneke omeho neethile isho tho kambishi. 
Momeho gaali omuna uutsa. 

Eethile isho o dhina omisinda mbali. 

Wow! Shoka oshipu.
P6	Ta thaneke okanya kokambishi. 

Omusinda ogo okanya. Ota ka monika kafa ondada 'w' natango okwa thaneke ooxwiki tho kambishi. 

Ooxwiki ndatu kolulyo na ndatu kolumoho.
P7	Oku thaneka okuli po kupwa. 

Okwa thaneke omaoko nomagulu gokambishi. Omaoko nomagulu omisinda na tango ta thaneke ominwe dho kambishi. 

Ominwe odho gonga natango okwa thaneke omushila gokambishi. Omushila omusinda o le.
P8	Lwa shugunina okwa thaneke omayala kolutu lokambishi nokwa mbampeke olutu lo kambishi no lwala lwafa elemune. 

Oku thaneka okwa pwa.  

'Wow' Shika osha nawa!
P9	Paife olufo olo kambishi. 

Okambishi oka kufa opena no mbapila.

Ombapila oyili molupe lo rectangle.
P10	Tango okambishi oka thaneke ongonga. 

Ongonga oyo olutu lokambwa. 

Wow! Shika osha nawa.
P11	Paife okwa thaneke uudhidhiliko uyali pombanda yo ngonga. 

Uudhidhiliko mboka uyali owo omakutsi gokambwa. No kwa thaneke ongonga oduudhe meni lya o. Ongonga oduuthe oyo eyulu lyombwa.
P12	Paife okwa thaneke omeho neethile isho dho kambwa. 

Mo meho omuna uutsa. Eethile isho odho omisinda mbali. 

Wow, shoka osha nawa unene!
P13	Okwa thaneke okanya kombwa. 

Okanya oko omusinda. Ogwafa ondada 'w' no kwa thaneke uutsa wa dhingonoka nomulungu gombwa. 

Utatu kolumoho nuutatu kolulyo.
P14	Oku thaneka okuli po ku pwa. 

Okwa thaneke omaoko no magulu gombwa. 

Omaoko no magulu ogo omisinda, no kwa thaneke iipanyala yo kambwa. Iipanyala owo uungonga. 

No kwa thaneke oshila gombwa. Oshila ogwa fa ongala.
P15	No konima okwa thaneke uungonga uuludhe ko lutu lombwa no kwa mbampeke olutu no lwaala olushunga. 

Oku thaneka okwa pwa. 

Wow, shoka osha nawa unene!
P16	Kalapo nawa kambishi. 

Kalapo nawa ethano lyo kambishi. 

Kalapo nawa ombwa. 

Kalapo nawa ethano lyo kambwa.
