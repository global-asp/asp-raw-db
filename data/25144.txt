title:	Kyabazinga omubi
author:	Kyambogo  University and William Balyegisagha
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Buti oidhi lwaki embuzi tidhikiriza kudhighaluza!

page_text:

P1	Wambuzi yali kyabazinga wabisolo ne'binhonhi. Yayeta olukiiko.
P2	Wambuzi yabakoba ati naloose ekiroto.Bonabona bawulirisa.
P3	Ekiroto kyali kigema ku ndhala. Pusi yabuuza.Tunakola tutya?
P4	Enkoko ne mbaati bakoba "Tukuumire emere ewa kyabazinga."
P5	Kyabazinga Mbuzi yakoba,"Ati musibe oyo yenayena adhema"
P6	.Kiseera kyakulonda kyabazinga wundi, Balonda Pusi.
P7	Embuzi tiyakyenda. "Ninze Kyabazinga"
P8	Embuzi tiyatwala mmere. Ente yabuuza, Tukozeki?"
P9	Ebisolo ne binhonhi byaisa kirala. Banhigire Embuzi.
P10	Embwa yakoba "Nnalinga naye nga kyabazinga"
P11	Entama yakoba,"Nnaghanga abaanabe ebiviiri"
P12	Embiidhi yalira,"Bweyali kyabazinga, Nnalabiriranga  enimirodhe"
P13	"Embuzi yaloghoza nga yali kyabazinga byandha nabyandha?" Entama yabuuza.
P14	Ebisolo byaikirizigania Embuzi erinokutwala emere ewa'Pusi kyabazinga,
P15	Ebisolo byayagana Embuzi.Embuzi yakoba, "Nja kusigala nga ndi kyabazinga "
P16	"Tujja kukusiba era tukutwale ye kyabazinga omuyaaka."
P17	Ente yaghaluza Embuzi yamutuusa mu mbuga ya kyabazinga. Abandhi bacacanca.
P18	Eyo nensonga lwaki embuzi tidhiganha kutambula nga bali kudhighaluza.
