title:	Ɓikkoy ɓellere
author:	Mohammed Also and Naseh  Yusuf
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Woodunoo ɓikkoy saare wonnde kankoy tatoy ɗowtii-koy masin. Ɓilla ngoota ni koy tagiraama ɓellere.

page_text:

P1	Nyalnde wonnde, woodunoo saare seyiinde.
P2	Ɓikkoy saare nden meeɗayi kaɓa hakkunde maɓɓe.@@E ɓe mballa saaro'en maɓɓe nder kuwɗe saare e maa ngesa.
P3	Ammaa ɓe kaɗaama yahgo haade yiite.
P4	Kuuwɗe maɓɓe fuu jemma ɗe kuwetee.
P5	Ngam ɓe tagiraama ɓe ɓellere!
P6	Ammaa kadi go'oto nder maɓɓe haaɓii ngam nasta nder naange.
P7	Nyalnde wonnde haaɓi masin. @@Hamma e adda maako waggini-mo.
P8	Ammaa artii sakitake!@@O ɓolwi nder naange nyaaɗnge.
P9	Ɓikkoy luttukoy koy mboyi nde ɓe ngi'i minyiraawo maɓɓe e ɓolwa.@@Ammaa ɓe taskii dabare wonnde.
P10	Ɓe mooɓti ɓolwuri ndin, ɓe tagi sonndu.@@Ɓe koosi minyoo maɓɓe sonndu ɓe njaari-mo dow hooseere townde.
P11	Ɓe koosi minyoo maɓɓe sonndu ɓe njaari-mo dow hooseere townde.
P12	Nde naange wurtii, o fiiri, e mo yima nder naange beete.
