P1	Once upon a time on a hot sunny day a man named Abdullahi was strolling around the town till he met a tree that was magical. So he went back home and every morning when his family members are sleeping he would go out and eat under the tree.
P2	She was so surprised that she fainted and they rushed umulkulthum to the hospital and she woke up when they cured her  ,when they got home they had an extended family picture and lived happily ever after.
