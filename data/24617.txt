title:	My first day at school

desc:	This story is about the first time I went to school and how excited I was about going to school.

page_text:

P1	Mum woke me up very early on Monday so that I could prepare for school.
P2	After having breakfast, I got my bag and went to school.
P3	I met a teacher at the doorway. He introduced me to the other pupils.
P4	We entered class and studied till 11.30am which was breaktime.
P5	After 30 minutes, the bell rang "kling-kling-kling". We went back to class.
P6	Just within one hour, class time was done and we were headed for lunch.
P7	After lunch, we went back to class and the teacher gave us homework.
P8	After that, Mum and Dad picked me up from school and they drove me home.
P9	Once we reached home, Mum told me to take off my uniform and wash.
P10	I did homework, had supper and then slept. This became my daily routine.
