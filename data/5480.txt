title:	Langi Eya Kiragala
author:	Annet Ssebaggala
artist:	Sue Kramer, Rijuta Ghate, Padmanabha, Wiehan de Jager, Sandy Lightly, Benjamin Mitchley and Sean Whitehead
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/5480.png
desc:	Olugero luno lunyonyola obulungi bwa langi eya kiragala era nemigaso gyayo negeri gyetuyamba.

page_text:

P1	Langi eya kiragala nnungi nnyo.
P2	Langi eya kiragala ereeta obulamu.
P3	Ebikoola bye miti birabika bulungi nnyo. Bya kiragala.
P4	Munnimiro waliyo ebirime ebya kiragala.
P5	Ku luguudo,  ebitaala bwebyaka kiragala ebidduka byona ne bisimbula.
P6	Engoye zemizanyo nazo za Kiragala.
P7	Olujja lwewaffe lwonna lwa kiragala.
P8	Langi eya kiragala nnungi nnyo!
