page_text:

P1	Liso was a strong short boy who lived at Regu village.He loved playing ball.
P2	His mother made a plastic ball for him as he had no real ball.He always played alone at @his home.
P3	One day his mother bought him a big red ball.Liso was very happy for the gift.
P4	Liso played outside and Lelo saw him.Then Lelo came.
P5	Lelo asked to play with Liso.But Liso said "No".
P6	Mother said "Boys,it feels good to play with one another than playing alone"
P7	Liso played with Lelo and felt very good than when playing alone.
P8	Liso ended up calling other children to play with his ball.The new soccer team was formed.
