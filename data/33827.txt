page_text:

P1	Luwo anapita ku tchalitchi ndi mayi ake. @@Iye anali atagwidwa dzanja mu njira.
P2	Mkati mwa tchalitchi munali anthu ambiri omwe anasonkhana. @@Iwo anali kuyimba.
P3	Luwo anachoka m'tchalitchi.@@Ndipo mayi ake sadamuone iye.
P4	Luwo anaima panjira.@@Iye anayang'ana pansi nadzifunsa kuti, "Kodi nyumba yathu ili kuti?"
P5	Kotero iye ankatsatira njira.@@Iye adaona nyumba yaikulu, ndipo iye anati, "Ayi, sinyumba yathu, nyumba yathu ndi yaing'ono."
P6	Luwo anayenda pang'ono. Iye anaona nyumba pa nsanja. @@Iye anati, "Ayi, iyo sinyumba yathu. Nyumba yathu siili pa nsanja."
P7	Iye adapitiliza kuyenda. Iye anaona nyumba ya udzu. @@Ndipo iye anati, "Ayi, iyo sinyumba yathu. Nyumba yathu ili ndi makoma amatope."
P8	Luwo anapita patsogolo. Anaona nyumba yaing'ono ndi mtengo wa mango kunja.@@Ndipo iye anati, "Ayi, sinyumba yathu. Nyumba yathu ili ndi mitengo iwiri ya mango panja."
P9	Pamapeto pake Luwo anaona mayi ake akubwera kwa iye. Luwo anathamangira kwa mayi ake. @@Ndipo mayi ake anati, "Tiye kunyumba kwathu!"
P10	Kodi nyumba ya Luwo ndi iti?
