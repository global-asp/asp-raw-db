page_text:

P1	Kera habayeho ingona yari ishonje cyane bikabije.
P2	Yashakaga ibyo irya gahoro gahoro idasakuza.
P3	Hanyuma.
P4	Ifashe icyo irya
P5	Nyuma y'ibyo inzara yarashize ingona iranezerewe.
P6	Kugeza ubwo yongera kugira inzara.
