page_text:

P1	Sipho goes to town with his sister.
P2	His sister is looking at the clothes in shops. Sipho is bored.
P3	Sipho sees something he likes.
P4	The shoes are on sale.
P5	He tries them all on.
P6	His sister buys him soccer shoes.
P7	Why are they in such a hurry to get home?
P8	Luckily he got to the soccer field on time! He is wearing his new soccer shoes.
