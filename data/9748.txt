title:	Azizi the Doll

desc:	This child enjoys looking after her doll. And her doll is always a happy friend.

page_text:

P1	This is Azizi. Azizi is my doll.
P2	I like Azizi because he always smiles.
P3	After I wake up I wash my face. Then I look for Azizi.
P4	When I find Azizi I brush his hair.
P5	When I eat breakfast I feed Azizi.
P6	When I eat sweets I give one to Azizi.
P7	But Azizi never eats his sweet. I wonder why!
P8	I walk around the compound picking flowers.
P9	I give a flower to Azizi. But he never says,  "Thank you."
P10	I wash Azizi clean before my mother bathes me.
P11	I dress Azizi in red because I love the colour red.
P12	When I go to sleep,  I lay Azizi beside me.
