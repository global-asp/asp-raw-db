desc:	This story is about Bereket and his activities at home and at the library.

page_text:

P1	Bereket is one of the children in my village. His father supports the family by driving a taxi. Bereket helps his father by washing the taxi after he comes from school.
P2	When he finishes washing, Bereket goes to Ras Abebe Aregay Library and reads storybooks in Amharic. The librarian helps him choose.
P3	The storybooks that Bereket and the other children read are written by many people from across Ethiopia and Africa. These people feel happy when children read and play.
P4	Bereket and the other children clean and wash the materials in the playground. They feel happy and thankful when they leave the library.
