desc:	A little girl has to learn to be independent and go to school without her big sister. (This storybook was created on the Maker App at a workshop in Durban (February 2020)

page_text:

P1	My sister's name is Resego. @@She is three years older than me.
P2	Resego helps me to put on my school uniform in the morning.@@She walks with me to school every day.
P3	But today, my big sister is not feeling well. @@Resego is sick!@@What am I going to do without her?
P4	On the way to school, I meet Tumi and we walk together.@@This makes me feel a little better.
P5	At school, my teacher sings me a song.@@This makes me feel happy.
P6	At play time, Boipelo plays with me. @@I am feeling much happier.
P7	It's time to go home.@@I had a good day.
P8	And I will feel happiest when I tell my big sister all about today!
