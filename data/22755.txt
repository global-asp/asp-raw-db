title:	Lishere nende Inzukha

desc:	Lishere nende inzukha fiali abetsa. Batsia khuchendera mama wa lishere.

page_text:

P1	Lishere nende Inzukha fiali abetsa.
P2	"Itsa khutsie khuchendere mama wanje." Lishere liabolera Inzukha.
P3	"Wina oulakhulindira amatala kefu?" Inzukha yareba Lishere.
P4	"Ndalabalindira amatala kenyu." Lishene lieinia.
P5	Lishere liatolera tsiswa muloloti okhuirira nyinamwana.
P6	Lishere nende Inzukha fiarula mumabwibwi. Nifinjira mumutsuru.
P7	Fiabukana nende efimuna. Inzukha niyifiria muno.
P8	Lishere liashinjirira Inzukha omwitsa walio.
P9	Olwa fiola wa nyinamwana Lishere, fiali nifiulira inzala.
P10	Fiasaba nifikasiria okhulia efiakhulia.
P11	"Inzukha, nochama ikhala shirunji." Lishere liabolore omwitsa walio.
P12	Inzukha yatema okhwikhala shirunji obulanyala ta.
P13	"Shenyala okhwikhala nga lishere tawe." Inzukha yalikalusia.
P14	Lishere shiliairecheresia Inzukha tawe. Nilichaka okhusinyikha.
P15	Inzukha niyisinyikha. "Shiumbuliranga tawe. Nakhaleshe engalushe ingo."
P16	Inzukha yarula neliruma. "Lishere embaranga mbu oli omwitsa wanje."
