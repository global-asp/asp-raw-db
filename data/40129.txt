desc:	Wà n-kfàtɨ̀ na gha kùm ìtebtɨ̀ i bò Abula? (fɨtɨtɨtɨ̂ fêynfɨ̀  nɨ ghɨ ma ghɨ læ bàlɨ a ndô mɨ̀tɨ̂tɨ̀ nɨ̀ Las Abebe a Deble Bilan

page_text:

P1	À n-læ  nà ghɨ à ntè' ìlvɨ̀, wayndà ɨ̀lumnɨ̀ ɨ̀lvɨ̀ nà ghɨ, izɨyn i ŋweyni ghɨ Abula.@@Wù n-læ nà kôŋ wì sɨ nà jàŋà kèsa sɨ yàsì ŋwà'lɨ̀ ŋweyn. Wù n-læ nà yoli-à a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀, fi kelɨ chɨ̂nɨ̀ ìbɨ.
P2	Abula n-læ choŋ ɨkwo nà ŋweyn sɨ yuyn mɨfolɨ ɨ̀n tofɨnɨ ateyn. Nô mɨ ta nà ŋweyn nà leytɨ̀ ɨ̀kwo ɨ ŋweyn, a wù kɨ̀ŋ yeyn ɨ̀ lì.@@À nì nà sɨ ghɨ tèyn ta wù yɨ mìsì ɨfwo ɨ tofɨnɨ nâ vzɨ̀, a Abula na sɨ dɨm ŋweyn suynsɨ ŋweynsɨ
P3	Ghɨ chɨ̀tɨ kɨ̀ têyn, Abula fvɨ̀ sɨ-a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀. Wù n-læ faŋ nà sɨ dɨm kɨ achi a fɨblà ŋweyn suynsɨ ŋweynsɨ, wù nì kfa a ndo kɨ̀ aleŋ a likfa. @@Nà Abula ɨ yeyn keli iwo nâ yèynì yi nà fu nge' sɨ ŋweyn.
P4	"Wayn ɨlumnɨ̀ nɨ̀ ghès nɨn chî wì a dzɨ̀ ìjùn. Mɨ̀ n-yeyn na wù bu fi se' wi a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀. Wù fi choŋ kɨmɨ ɨkwo wom," iyeyn ɨ bè nà Abula sɨ lum ŋweyn.
P5	"Wu bê si wî ŋweyn na 'wà n-læ̂ se sɨ lèytɨ̀ sɨ̀ kfèynsɨ ɨkwo ɨ̀ va, wa lèyt ɨ̀ àntêynɨ̀ tɨfɨtɨ ŋwà'lɨ̀. Wù n-lum bu yaytɨ̀  wì ŋwà'lɨ̀ ŋweyn, tèyn a ɨ̀kwo nâ vzɨ̀ na ghɨ kɨ nô ale' a juŋ-à."
P6	Ɨtu' ɨ layn kfeynsɨ̀, Abula nà sɨ kɨŋ ɨkwo ɨ nà ŋweyn. Wù n-læ kɨŋ kɨ̀ ɨlwe' ɨ̀jɨ̀m mɨtɨ̀ faŋ tɨ̀ yeyn ɨ̀kwo ateyn. @@Tèyn wu kfà'tɨ̀ na yì kà' a yì ndu ɨwe vzɨ̀ a wu nà ba'sɨ.
P7	Bò ŋweyn ɨ yeyn ale' kì, a wù nà ndu ateyn.@@"Wayn wom, mɨ̀ n-kya ìwo zɨ̀ a wà n-ndu ɨ̀we bòm ìnyeyni. Lvɨ̀yn, kasi ndu ki ɨkwo ateyn a wa àntêynɨ̀ ŋwà'lɨ̀," bò ŋweyn ɨ̀ kæ bè.
P8	Abula nà ghɨ nɨ̀ ìkani, mɨ̀tɨ̀ ɨ̀ ndù ɨ̀ kɨŋ a ŋweyna ŋwà'lɨ̀sɨ̀. @@ Ɨ yeyn ɨkwo ateyn ta nà ŋweyn n-læ ley'tɨ̀  àfu.
P9	Ɨtu' ɨ layn kfêynsɨ, wu fi ndù sɨ̀ kɨŋ àntêynɨ̀ ŋwà'lɨ̀sɨ̀. Mɨ̀tɨ̀ afo ghɨ wi ta ghɨ̀ ley'tɨ̀ ateyn. @@Wu ndù sɨ bɨf sɨ bò ŋweyn na bòm ghà ta ghɨ bu fi lêm ɨkwo àntêynɨ̀ ŋwà'lɨ̀sɨ̀ -a.
P10	Bò ŋweyn ɨ bèynsɨ̀ na, "Wayn wom, wà kɨ̀ŋ sɨ̀ keli ɨ̀kwo sɨ̀ yuyn mɨ̀fôlɨ̀ ɨ̀n tofɨ̀nɨ̀ no sɨ̀ dvɨ̀-à?"@@Abula ɨ bèynsɨ̀ na, "Samo' a bà."
P11	Bò ŋweyn ɨ bè na, "Yvɨtɨ iwo zɨ̀ a ka mà bê. Wa jaŋ ŋwà'lɨ̀ syasɨ fì ndù a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀. Wà læ̀ yeyn ɨ̀mwa'tɨ àntêynɨ̀ ŋwà'lɨ̀. Ka wa n-kàsi ìbàm."@@"Mɨ̀tì, Bà, ijaŋi nɨn nto chwôsɨ-à. ijaŋi ŋwalɨ̀sɨ̀ nɨn boli-à!" Abula ɨ bèynsɨ̀ bè.
P12	Sɨ̀ a fɨ ntɨ̀mlɨ̀ fɨ̀lvɨ̀ tèyn, bò Abula ɨ chìmi moŋ. Wu kfàtɨ̀ na yì kà' a yì tutɨ ti-a wâyn ŋweyna.@@Ɨ kæ bè sɨ̀ Abula na, "Ghèsɨ̀và na jàŋ kɨ̀ a mo. Mɨ n-gàmtɨ̀ và sɨ keli igha' toynɨ̀ a ŋwà'lɨ̀."
