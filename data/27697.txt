page_text:

P1	Erina lyange ninze NAMAGANDA, nsoma mukibiina kyamukaaka.
P2	E isomero lyange balyeta Busoga pulaimare.
P3	E isomo lyange lyensinga okwenda lya kukuba bifaananhie.
P4	Madamu Namwase atusomesa Lungereeza na kukuba bifaananhie.
P5	Musa ni mukwano gwange asiinga. Ansinga obukulu.
P6	Nnhenda okuzaana omupiira ogwe ebigere ghemba nga tiri mu kibiina.
P7	Nga tumaze okulya ekyomusana, twira yo eka okuyamba ku abazaire baife.
P8	Nnhendha maama wange, Bamweta NAIGAGA.
P9	Buli lunaku, tuja naye mu katale okugula emere.
P10	Tugula obuse bwa bbando okuva ku kyuma kya Musomba.
P11	Nnhenda inho okulya embooli, omusomesa Fatuma yakoba nti dhamugaso ku bulamu bwaife.
P12	Waidha ni muganda wange omukulu, Ayendha kuzaana yenka.
P13	Mu kiwumulo tu kyalira e iterekero lye ebisolo okubona endhovu, empuuli ne etwiga.
P14	Tu simba dhuuma mu isamba lyaife. Maama wange ayendha okulya dhuuma.
P15	Bulinkyo Nzukuka mangu okuja ku isomero.
