page_text:

P1	Ku  kyalo  kyaife  kuliku  omusaadha  owe liina  Damba.
P2	Damba  yali  n'eiduuka  einene  inho  mu  katawuni    ka  Gayaza.
P3	Mu iduuka  lya  Damba  mulimu  bbando,  sukaali,  ebizigo,  omutyeere,  ebidhandhaali,  ne ebintu ebindi  bingi.
P4	Damba  ali no  omukazi  n'abaana  basatu.  Boona  bakola  ino  naamaani  mu  iduuka.
P5	Mu kiseera  eky'olughuumula,  abaana  bayambaku  abazaire  baibwe  okukola  mu iduuka  nga  bagheereza  abaguzi.
P6	Damba  ali ne  ebitabo  mwa ghandiika  dhatunze  buli  lunaku.
P7	Damba  ni  mukazi ghe  bali ne  enhumba  enhungi  n'emotoka.
P8	Baaya  embuzi,  ente,  enkoko,  n'obuduyu.  Obulamu  bwaibwe  bweyagaza.
P9	Damba  ni  mukazi  ghe  bafuna  sente  edhibamala  okulabirira  abaana  baibwe  n'okubatwaala  mu  masomero  amalungi.
