title:	Paalakyaanik
author:	Joyce Chemitai
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Sabinyi
level:	First words

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2923.png
desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school.  Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Kyanyoru ani palakyaanik luket wikitwe.
P2	Yoo ki kule,  kusilyi kanyoyinteet nyimwani palaakyeet kaneetinteet nyimwani.
P3	Myaanat thabisile yeme kupur ka.
P4	Yoo ki anyo anyoru palakyeet nyee kisilu leekook cee ceesumani tokoi.
P5	Sap lakwar no Thabisile!
P6	Yoo ki anyoru palakyeet nyee kisile corweemwani.
P7	Kwapurury piceet nyi?
P8	Yoo ki anyoru palakyeet nyee kisile yeyo nyimwani.
