desc:	Omughala anhaala ku buliri buli lukyeire aye awaayiriza muke we omuto Anna. Kale

page_text:

P1	Obwire  bwize  kabiri  era  enzikiiza  eizeemu.
P2	Bwemala  okulya nja  kubuliri ni  muke  ghange  Anna
P3	Ntya  buli  kiseera  kuba  buli  lunaku  nnhaala  ku  buliri.
P4	Buli  lwe  ndoota, nsitukiramu  gheene  agho  nga  nnhaire.
P5	Mangu  ago  nkyusa  ekifo  naasula  ewa Anna  aghakalu  nga ate  wasu.
P6	Munco, nsituka  mangu naaleka  Anna  mubuliri.
P7	Maama  bwabuuza  ani  anhaire  ku  buliri,  ndhiramu  inho   nti  ni  Anna.
P8	Kale  olwairirira  maama  yasalagho  okusula  ni Anna  mu  buliri.
P9	eky'akabi, kirala  ekyo  kyenakola era  nga  ghazira  wundi gha  ku  vunaana.
P10	Olumalirira  maama  yakitegeera   nti  Anna tanhaala  ku buliri   aye  ni  nze.
