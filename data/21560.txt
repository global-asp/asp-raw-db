desc:	Isitayela sikaRafiki ngesakhe yedwa. Kodwa uma ehlangana neqembu leCool Cat

page_text:

P1	NgeSonto, uJimmy Zogba washaya igoli iqembu lakhe elanqoba ngalo.
P2	NgoMsombuluko, iCool Cat Crew yayihambela phezulu.
P3	"Rafiki, unazo nje wena izinwele ezigundiswe okwedube?

Izinwele zakho zithule nje, kungathi awunandaba nazo."
P4	"Rafiki, uphi umdwebo wakho wasesikhunjeni ophucukayo?

Unesikhumba esibi kabi phela wena. Futhi umubi."
P5	"Rafiki, unalo nje izinyo elikhazimulayo? Phela wena unezingovolo."
P6	Ngalolo suku, uRafiki wagoduka ehamba kancane.
P7	"Anti, ngingakwazi ukugunda izinwele zami zibe njengedube?"

"Ngeke ngikugunde nje mina njengedube. Empeleni nje, angeke ngize ngilokothe mina."
P8	"Sisi, nginesikhumba esibi mina. Ungakwazi ukungidweba umdwebo ophucukayo emzimbeni?"

"Cha, Rafiki, wena awuwudingi umdwebo ofana nalawo."
P9	"Uyazi Mkhulu, ngifisa sengathi ngabe nginezinyo elikhazimulayo."

"Mzukulu, ubukhazikhazi abusho lutho."
P10	Ngempelasonto elandelayo, uSanti Ramires washaya igoli iqembu lakhe elanqoba ngalo.
P11	NgoMsombuluko, iCool Cat Crew yayihambela phezulu.

"Sawubona Rafiki! Ubukeka umuhle!"
P12	URafiki wavele wangakunaka nje lokho. 

"Ngiyilokho engiyikho, lesi yisitayela sami."
