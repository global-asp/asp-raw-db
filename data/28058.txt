desc:	Omughala ayagaana obuzibu okugemagana ni baine mu kyalo. Akyukira Endhuba emubeseegho. Olufumo luno lwatondebwawo ku musomo ogwali mu Abuja

page_text:

P1	Mu  kyalo  ekiri  kumpi n'enhandha,ghaligho  omughala  omuto. Eriina lye  yali  Kampi.
P2	Baaba  wa  Kampi  yali  muvubi  mumanhifu. Yali n'eryaato lye  eitono  ery'okuvubisa. Kampi  yaghoomerwa  okujanga  okuvuba n'Ise.
P3	Kampi  yayendanga  okwerorera  abalenzi  nga  basamba  omupiira. "Ka nzaanheku  niimwe,"yabegairiraku  olunaku  lulala.  Abalenzi  bamusekerera,"Ja ozaanhe  kyekweko n'abaghala."
P4	Abaghala  baakoba  Kampi,"Amagulu go maleeyi  inho."Kampi  y'anakughala.  Buli nco, Kampi  y'akinanga n'endhuba  yenka.  Endhuba  y'afuuka mukenkede we.
P5	Lwali  lulala  endhuba  tiyavaayo. Enkoko  empanga  tidhakolyoka, ebinhonhi  tibyayemba n'abaana tibaaja kw'isomero.  Waire Ise  tiyaja  kuvuba. Kampi  y'anakughala."Aligha  mukenkede  wange, Wandhuba? Lwaki leero  kuzimeevu?"
P6	Kampi  yaja  y'abaana  abandi  y'abakobera obunakughavu  bwe  yaliku. Aye  baamusekerera,"Ob'olyagho  mukenkedewo Wandhuba,  afiire. Oba  olyagho  ekulumwikeku." Kampi  y'akoba,"Endhuba  mukenkede  wange. Teyinza  kufa."
P7	Kampi  yanakughala inho y'alumukira  mu  nhumba. Y'asamba inho  omupiira gwa  mwanhinha.
P8	Kampi  y'aloghooza,"Ndidha  kuzaanha  n'omupiira guno  okutuusa mukenkede  wange, Wandhuba  bw'anaira." Y'asitula  omupiira  y'aluka  nagwo  ku luya.
P9	Kampi  bwe  yatuuka  ku  luya, y'ata  omupiira  ghansi. yaloghooza, "Nsobola  okuzaanha nga  bo."Yasamba inho  omupiira.  Gwe  dhuumba, gw'abulura  eyo mw'igulu eridwire ebireri.
P10	Nga  buli  muntu   bwe  yalinga ghaigulu,  omupiira   gw'abulira   mu bireri  ebizimeevu. Ghabaagho  akasirikiriro ghonaghona.
P11	Amangu  ago, ebireri  ebizimeevu  by'awagho  endhuba   y'aboneka. Obulamu  kabiri  bw'aira  ku  kyalo eky'ompi   n'enhandha.  Buli  muntu  y'ateekateeka  kye yakolanga   buli  idho.
P12	Kampi  tiyasobola  ku  kikiriza  ati  aiziiza  endhuba  ku  kyalo.Aye  okusingira  irara,  endhuba  yali  eize  mu  bulamu  bwe. Y'akoba  abaana,"Mukenkede  wange, Wandhuba  aize.!"
