title:	Mwarῖmῦ Njeri
author:	Kariha Nancy Wamaitha
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Gikuyu

desc:	A short and simple story about a wonderful teacher and what she teaches us.

page_text:

P1	Uyῦ nῖ mwarῖmῦ wakwa. Etagῦo Mwarῖmῦ Njeri. Ni anyedete mῦno.
P2	Mwarῖmῦ Njeri athomithagia ndemwa na a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Mwarῖmῦ Njeri nῖ athothomi atῦthῦmithetie marangi mῦtῦne,  bruu,  grini na mῦirῦ.
P4	Tῦgῖkoma Mwarῖmῦ Njeri nῖ atῦinagira karuῖmbo kega.
P5	Karῦῖmbo gaka nῖkῦ Mwarῖmῦ Njeri atuinagῖra:   Kŏŏma mwana Kŏŏma mwana Mami ni egῦka Kŏŏma,  akῦrekere Iria kŏŏma.
P6	Mwarῖmῦ Njeri atῦganagῖra tῦrῦgano tῦῖngῖ.
P7	Jumatatu nῖ atῦganagῖra tῦrῦgano twa andῦ njorῦa.
P8	Kerῖ,  Mwarῖmῦ Njeri atῦganagῖra ng'ano cῖa atῦmia njorῦa.
P9	Gatatῦ,  atῦganagῖra ng'ano cia ng'endo.
P10	Kanna atῦganagῖra ng'ano cia urῖmi.
P11	Jῦma,  Mwarῖmῦ Njeri atuῖraga tugane ng'ano ciitu.
P12	Mwarῖmῦ witῦ nῖῖ mwega mῦno. Ndaneneha,  gwenda gutwika ta Mwarῖmῦ Njeri.
