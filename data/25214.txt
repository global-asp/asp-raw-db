page_text:

P1	Isabirye ni Wakaduuyu baagonzaganianga. Nabirye nga alumwa olw'obugaiga.
P2	Isabirye yatwaala eisamba lya wakaduuyu. Yabinga wakaduuyu.
P3	Isabirye yaatunda ebirime byonabyona. Yaafuna empiiya mbitirivu.
P4	Isabirye yagolola eka ne ekiibokye ku mutwe.
P5	Isabirye yaisa ekiibo ghansi. Yaaja okweigama amaadhi.
P6	Amaadhi gaali mangi gaavaamu amataba, Isabirye yeekweka mu kiina.
P7	Ensega yaabona ekiibo yaakibwikaku na amaghagha.
P8	"Ekiibo kino kyange," Ensega bwe yakoba. 
"Kyange!" Isabirye yaawowoigana.
P9	"Ndagainemu esente  ni duuma  nga ti bibwike byaku." Ensega yaakoba.
P10	Omukulu owe ekyalo na abakaire baalwa okusalawo naani omutuufu.
P11	Oluvainhuma, baakoba, "Ensega ntuufu. Ekiibo kyayo."
P12	Wakaduuyu yairayo mu isamba. Isabirye yali azira sente ghaire mikwano.
