title:	Sundya's Smelly Mouth
author:	Ivan Higenyi
artist:	Jesse Pietersen, Catherine Groenewald, Karlien de Villiers, Marike le Roux, Alice Toich and Vusi Malindi
lang:	English
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/10269.png
desc:	The story is about a young boy who is abandoned by his fellow pupils at school because of his smelly mouth.

page_text:

P1	Sundya is a pupil at Buwesa primary school.
P2	Every day he goes to school and every one likes him at school but never brushes his teeth.
P3	He keeps laughing and smiling even when the other pupils are not.
P4	He shares a desk with other four pupils but they dislike and always shout at him.
P5	At play time,  all his friends leave him alone.
P6	Sundya is now worried because no one wants to be near him though he performs well in class.
P7	The teacher then advices Sundya to clean his teeth and mouth every day.
P8	Next day he comes back at school with the teeth so white and the mouth clean. Now every one is back to him like before.
