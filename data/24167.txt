title:	Endogoyi ne ente elima
author:	Nagimesi Arthur
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/24167.png
desc:	Olugero lwe ente elima enkozi ne endogoyi.

page_text:

P1	omulimi yalina ente elima
P2	yalina ne ndogoyi. ente elima ne ndogoyi zali zamikwano
P3	olwegulo, ente elima yagamba endogoyi, "nkooye nnyo leero"
P4	ente elima yagamba endogoyi, "sinaba kuwuumula ko leero"
P5	endogoyi yadamu, "nakoze nnyo okusinga"
P6	olunaku olwadako, ente elima yagamba, "leero lubade lunaku lubi"
P7	endogoyi negamba,"omulimi bwanajja, gamba, 'moo' ajakuleeka owumulemu
P8	enkeela, ente elima, yakaaba, 'moo' omulimi nagileka ewumulemu
P9	omulimi natwala endogoyi okulima
P10	endogoyi yakola nnyo olunaku lwona. yakoma wo ewaka nga ekooye
P11	endogoyi negamba, "omulimi agambye agenda kutta afune ennyama
P12	ente negamba, njakukola enkya. kati mpulila bulungi
P13	olugero lwe ente elima enkozi ne endogoyi engezi
P14	olugero lwe ente elima ne ndogoyi
