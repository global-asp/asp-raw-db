desc:	Obughanvu ne nfaanana eye bintu bya kasaayi ebya buliidho.

page_text:

P1	Maama alikwidhuza akalobo.
P2	Abongire ali kuzaanhisa akamotoka aka kasaayi.
P3	Deewo ali kuzaanhisa ebimuli bya kasaayi.
P4	Awute avaire omudhoozi gwa kasaayi.
P5	Endhuba eya kasaayi eri ghaigulu gha dhaadha omusaadha.
P6	Akapikipiki kali gha muti.
P7	Omunha ogwa kasaayi guli ku mubiri gwa Babalwa.
P8	Yingini eya kasaayi ey'omuliro eri aghali omuliro.
P9	ono ni shibba avaire omudhoozi gwa kasaayi.
P10	Ali kugezaaku okuvaala engaito dha kasaayi.
P11	Tidhimutuuka!
