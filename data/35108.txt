title:	Tamara ohambella esukulu
author:	Elias  Mangulama and Syned  Mthatiwa
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Ellomwe (Malawi)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/35108.png
desc:	Wambella esukullu tti ettilla emmodda mumoyoni wa mwana arive mutthene.

page_text:

P1	Tamara ari ni miyakha mitthanu nave akhalla nimodda na makholloaye.@@Mudziwa wari vakhuvuru na kokholla mmo mwari inama ddamuyakani.
P2	Tamara ari owiwellella wa makholloaye.@@Olliyeni achiha inkhosa ni ipuri.
P3	Tamara anaddivelliwa wiwellella ipallame nave achihaka iddiweto.@@Ipallame ddawipaka, olliyeni anahellamo mawu aye mu enyimbonddo.
P4	Tamara ari muttokottoko wittuwambella esukulluni. Makhollaye yalole aniwa allupalli, Nashipae ni Tasieku oya osukulu. @@Yari makilomita 10 orekamella.
P5	Nashipae ni Tasieku yarowa nayakhwiwa ovenyera owaniwa ohinase nave yahokollowa musikuwa.@@Yakusa yocha mumakontena atthokottoko.
P6	Tamara ahaakoha amenya, "Kinambelle lini oya osukulu?"@@Amenya yahakhulla, "Onoroyawo nanwene vava mwanaka waphama."
P7	Nllapo nimodda waphiya  kharo mwari amwene natthiyana apilli.@@Amwenayo yanonesesa wi mwana aphiya musinkhu waphaama ayeke osukulu.
P8	Athu yanakhullupillilla wi mwana atukhulla muonoaye oviriha vamuru nuphara naaru, yoyo ophiya vamulingoni woya osukulu.
P9	Amwene yamukoha, "Ori na miyakha kavi?" Tamara ahakhulla, "Kiri na miyakha ttanu."@@"None nave weyano ttuya osukuluni," amwene yahulluma.
P10	Mmoddha mwa atthiyana ayo akusa mnattha namachello na Tamara nuviriha vamuruni vaye.@@Tamara aphara naaru naye namazeleni.
P11	Tamara dduya esukulu emoddene ohi  ya Nashipie na Taieku.@@Yari epsya na vakhuvuru ni va waniwa.
P12	Tamara ahakallalla.@@Aganiza, "Nave kinolotthuwella olemba na owerenga mmo ariri Nashipae na Tasieku."
