title:	Isipho sakagogo
author:	Sponono  Mahlangu
artist:	Sibongile Mnkandla
lang:	isiNdebele

desc:	Umma usiza uThabo ukwenzela ugogo isipho esikhethekileko.

page_text:

P1	Ugogo uyeza uzokuvakatjha. Ngaso soke isikhathi nakezako uphathela uThabo amantongomani neenthelo zommango namkha isiphila begodu kwesinye isikhathi umphathela nekhabe.@@UThabo naye ufuna ukupha ugogo into ekhethekileko.
P2	Incwadi ingaba yihle, kodwana ugogo  uyakwazi ukufunda?@@Nange aya eentolo angamphathela into ehle?@@"Asimenzeleni isikhwama esikhulu," kutjho uThabo amomotheka. "Isikhwama esikhulu esiqinikelo ngetjhila elihlaza sasibhakabhaka lebhokadi."
P3	Umma ukhupha itjhila abalithenge esitolo. Undlala kuhle amaphetheni bewuthoma nokulisika.@@Kokuthoma, uthoma ngamahlangothi amabili amakhulu nabanzi.
P4	Ulandela ngamabili amancani ukobana ibe nejamo.@@UThabo uyawathatha uyawabhinca womabili namahlangothi amabili amakhulu. Uqalile lokha umma nakakhupha iphetheni godu.
P5	Usika kwangaphasi kuba ziintokana ezimbili ezide. Lokhu-ke ngikho okuzakwenza isikhwama siqine.@@UThabo uyawabhinca lokha umma nakathatha iphetheni godu.
P6	Usika  amabhande amane abanzi begodu namade.@@Mabili wawo azakuthungelelwa ndawonye ukwenza ibhande elilodwa. Lokhu kuzakwenza isikhwama siqine khulu.
P7	Ngemva kwalokho umma ukhupha umtjhini wokuthunga.@@Uhlanganisa iintokana zoke ukwenza isikhwama sakagogo.@@Usinikela uThabo, uThabo wamomotheka wathi, "Ngiyathokoza mma, ngesikhwama sakagogo esihlaza sasibhakabhaka!"
