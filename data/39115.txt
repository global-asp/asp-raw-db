title:	The fairy Girl
author:	Casey Jones
artist:	Abraham Muzee and Jesse Breytenbach

desc:	This is an inspirational story book for kids at the age of 6-8 it inspires them to never give up and never run away from their parents. This young girl sprouted tiny wings at the age of 20 then at 40 she grew full wings like a butterfly.

page_text:

P1	This story is of a young girl that grew to be a fairy she grew wings.
P2	She was 20 when she grew wings.
P3	She always annoyed her mother when she broke stuff when she was flying.
P4	Her mother worryed about her alot.
P5	She asked her mother if she could come inside.
P6	Her mother never approved of her.
P7	Her own mother couldn't even bare to look at her!
P8	When she came inside without her mother knowing and placed stuff she forgot her mother got upset.
P9	So when she comes in and asks her mother if what is wrong with her her mother would always say i don't know.
P10	She would beg her mother "mother please tell me the answers?"
P11	When she would try to even touch her own mother she would always run or flinch.
P12	She told her mother there was a spider to distract her.
P13	When her mother told her theres no spider she says "Yes there is mother look!"
P14	Then when her mother got distracted enough she ran away and broke through the wall of their tiny cottage home. And was never seen again.
