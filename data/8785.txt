desc:	Ó’dʉ́kó ‘dɨ̀ àfa zámvá gàá nɨ nɨ́ lě àvásɨ̀ ‘dɨ̀yɨ mà drìá.

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping rope.Álè báká ombʉ́zʉ́.
P2	 Alone.a'dúle rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too.Álè vɨ́nɨ báká ombʉ́zʉ́ mâ àgyipí yi pie rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories."Álè á'dɨ́kó ndrɨ̀zá'dìyi làzʉ́ àvásɨ̀."
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends.Álè acízʉ́ mâ àgyipí yi pie rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother.Álè òfazʉ́ yi òjɨ̀zʉ́ mâandríi nɨ́ rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily.Álè àfa mánɨ́ ecó làlérá 'dìyi òsɨ̀zʉ̀ 'wàrà 'wàrà rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit.Álè pat íéfɨ́ ndʉ́ ndʉ́ 'dìyi nyazʉ́ rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.Álè o'dú kozʉ́ kɨ̀tándà àmbóó nɨ mà drìá rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories.Álè mâ andríi nɨ erɨzuʉ́ á'dɨ́kó òlʉ̀ rɨ̀á rɨ̀ àvásɨ̀.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Ééé,  álè àfa 'dìyi 'yezʉ́ drɨ́á àvásɨ̀!
