title:	Ninafurahi - I Enjoy
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Kiswahili
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2106.png
desc:	Hadithi katika lugha mbili inayoelezea vitu vyote ambavyo msichana mdogo anafurahia kutenda.

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping. Ninafurahi kuruka.
P2	I enjoy skipping rope alone. Ninafurahi kuruka peke yangu.
P3	I enjoy skipping with my friend. Ninafurahi kuruka na rafiki yangu vile vile.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Ninafurahi kusoma hadithi za kuvutia.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Ninafurahi kutembea uwanjani pamoja na marafiki zangu.
P6	I enjoy washing dishes to help at home. Ninafurahi kumwoshea mama vyombo.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters which I can read easily. Ninafurahi kuandika herufi kubwa ambazo ninaweza kusoma kwa urahisi.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruits. Ninafurahi kula matunda ya aina tofauti.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Ninafurahi kulala kwenye kitanda kikubwa.
P10	I enjoy listening to my mother's interesting stories. Ninafurahi kusikiliza hadithi za mama za kuvutia.
P11	I enjoy doing all these things!Aah! Ninafurahi kuruka,  kusoma,  kuandika,  kula matunda,  kutembea pamoja na marafiki,  kuosha vyombo na kusikiliza hadithi!
