page_text:

P1	Maama yafumba emere muntamu.Yagisibiriza mwifumbiro okusoobola okwagaana ni bakasitoma be mu katale
P2	Naana ni baganda be babiri baira okuva ntuuse kwisomero.Naana yali gha kya kuna,Anodi gha nasale ni junior.
P3	Junior yalira olwe ndhala aye Naana yamukoba tulinde maama ayiire mu katale.Junior yeibiriza yaingira mwifumbiro yalya emere.
P4	Maama bwe yaira,Yasanhuka okubono abaana be ghaka.Yaigulawo efumbiro agabe emere.Yayagaana mutyere mwerere mwifubiro.
P5	Yayeeta abaana boona boona mwifumbiro yababuuza ku mmere. Junior neya sooka okwegaana aye nga saati ye etonieku supu
P6	Maama yagaba emmere eyasigaalawo era yakangavula Junior nga amwima ekyemisana.Yamukoba ati toirangamu.
