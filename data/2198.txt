desc:	A story for toddlers about a cow with daytime identity as a soft toy

page_text:

P1	Ino ni Ing'ombe. Ing'ombe inyirire!
P2	Esidere Ing'ombe yaabwene.
P3	Iburusia Indeke ye ikaratasi khulusia.
P4	Kose yuba omupira.
P5	Musiro Ing'ombe yamakana!
P6	Ionia obulamu.
P7	Nende okhulwania obusuku.
P8	Nabusa nodimba chimoni tata,  nende okhusaba elienya -.
P9	Inyala okhwicha okhukhu kenia wesi!
P10	Kawere.
