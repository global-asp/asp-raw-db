page_text:

P1	One  day, I and  my sister  went to the  farm  and  saw a baby laying  alone.
P2	My sister told me to pick up the baby and we went home.
P3	On reaching home, our mother asked "where did you get this baby from?"
P4	We answered her, "we saw the baby laying alone in our farm and we decided to bring her home to care for her so that she will be save."
P5	Our mother helped  us to bath and care for the baby.
P6	She layed the baby under a mosquito net.
