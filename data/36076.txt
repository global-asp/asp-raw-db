page_text:

P1	She lived unhappy and lonely life.The father could not provide all her basic needs.She was dirty,smelling and had an uncombed hair.
P2	Later the father fell sick.She watched her father struggle with the sickness but she had no help.He finally died.People came to mourn with her.She finally got a sponser.Finished her education and got a good job.
P3	In our village there lived a girl.Her name was mercy a pupil in class six.She was living with her father.Her mother was addicted to drugs and therefore aba ndoned  her when she was young.
P4	The father started drinking beer and even taking drugs.This made Mercy bitter with everyone.She started missing her mother so much.
