page_text:

P1	Omuhara muto akatumwa bazire beye owa shwenkuru weye ohuatwure enyima ye kibira akagenda ashemelirwe.
P2	Ahuya yikire hihi ne kibira ya bugana embuzi ehiya'muhabwere kwegyendeseleza ahakuba emu kibira mukaba murimu okubugana kwe'enyameishwa.
P3	Okuyahikire ahagati ye kibira, akashanga enyameishwa nyingi a'hihi nomu handa yiruka yiruka arikutera enduru
P4	Okuya rugire omu kibira ya'shanga enyonyi nyingi ziri kutera enduru. Omuhara yiruka arikutera endure. shwenkuru weye yamuhurira.
P5	Shwekuru weye ya'gamba nomukazi weye arikumugambira ngu ogwe nomweishukuru weitu. Agireyo kumureba zhuba ne'mbwa yeye yamushanga aruhire.
P6	kuya hikire omuuka yataha omunshu yabuza abandi baana yamara yarya ebwokurya. Yamara kurya ya bagambira eyaba kyabireho omu'muhanda.
P7	Yamara kubagambira ekikyabireho bamuhabura obutagaruka kuraba omu'kibira wenka. Yagaruka omuuka ashemerirwe kuzana nabaana bandi.
P8	Taragarukire kuraba omu'kibira wenka.
