title:	Abaana Be Iula
author:	Catherine Were
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Oluwanga
level:	First sentences

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2516.png
desc:	Khale yaliwo ifamili nende abaana bataru abeshiribwa. Efitinyu fiali mbu bali balongwa khurula khu iula

page_text:

P1	Khale lialikho litaala elala eliabandu bali nibamwenya mubusangafu.
P2	Shibakhupanan- gakho tawe. Bakhonyanga abebusi babu hango nende mumukunda.
P3	Ne shibafuchirirungwa okhusuta hambi no muliro tawe.
P4	Bakholanga mirimo chiabu chiosi mushiro.
P5	Ne mulala khu basiani yekomba okhutsia okhwora omubasu.
P6	Inyanga ndala,  yekomba muno muno okhukhola ario. Abasiani babu nibamukaya.
P7	Ne bachelewa! Yasalulukha mumubasu.
P8	Abaana be iula baberera muno muno,  olwabalola owashiabwe nasalulukha mulimba.
P9	Nibakasia liparo. Nibalonga liyoni okhurula khu iula yali niyisalulushe.
P10	Nibabukula omusiani wabu weliyoni,  nibamuira khu shikulu eshirambi.
P11	Ne olwa elimba liarula,  napurukha hekulu niyemba mubulafu bwe itsuli.
