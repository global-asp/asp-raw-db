desc:	A beautiful story about the friendship between two dogs and the challenges they faced. The storybook was developed at a story writing workshop held at Ras Abebe Aregay Library

page_text:

P1	Long ago, two dogs lived  peacefully in a dense forest.
P2	One day, they went out of their home in search of food. @@They walked in the forest.
P3	As the two dogs were searching, they saw a rabbit in a hole. @@One of the dogs told the other that he wanted to hunt it out.
P4	Then, he entered the hole. @@The other one waited for the rabbit at the entrance of the hole.
P5	The dog that went in to hunt the rabbit ate it alone.@@He lied to his friend that the rabbit escaped.
P6	"Where has the blood on your mouth come from? You have eaten it, haven't you? We are no more friends," said the other dog.
P7	The other dog went alone to search for food. @@He went for a long time.
P8	After searching for a long time, he found food and ate. @@The dog that lied followed the other one from a distance. He wanted to apologise to his friend.
P9	He asked his friend for an apology and it was granted. The friend said to him, "I have accepted your apology. Promise that you won't lie again." @@The other dog promised and they continued became friends again.
