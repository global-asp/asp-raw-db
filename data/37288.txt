title:	Zwipuka zwe ra zwi vhona
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Penuel  K
lang:	Tshivenḓa

desc:	Tshi malugana na lwendo lwa muṱa wahashu lwa u ya u vhona zwipuka. (Nganea na zwifanyiso zwo itwa kha wekishopo ya holodei nga Fulwi 2019

page_text:

P1	Hoyu ndi muṱa wahashu. @@Malume anga, muzwala wanga, mme anga, khaladzi dzanga mbili (dzine dza dzula dzo ambara zwiambaro zwi fanaho), mukomana wanga, na nṋe!
P2	Hoyu ndi muṱa wahashu u re ngomu bisini, ri khou ya u vhona zwipuka. @@Ngomu bisini hu na mmane wanga, mme anga, mukomana wanga, khaladzi dzanga mbili (dzine dza dzula dzo ambara zwiambaro zwi fanaho), na nṋe!
P3	Ro vhona ṱhuḓwa.
P4	Ro vhona ntsa.
P5	Ro vhona ndau.
P6	Ro vhona nḓou.@Ro sea vhukuma musi ri tshi vhona nḓou ngauri...
P7	… yeneyo nḓou yo vha i tshi khou ḓithusa kha muri.
