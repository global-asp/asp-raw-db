title:	Ikati nenja ziyadweba futhi ziyahlobisa
author:	Pumla Mdontswa
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	isiZulu
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Ikati uKitty nenja uBobby ngabangane. Bayahlekisa futhi bayathanda ukwenza izinto ezihlukahlukene ndawonye.

page_text:

P1	Yikati leli, nguKitty.@@Yinja le, nguBobby.
P2	Inja nekati zifuna ukudweba.@@Inja nekati zifuna ukuhlobisa.@@Inja nekati zifuna ukwenza imifanekiso.@@Sekuqala inja.@@Ithatha ipensela nephepha. Iphepha liyisikwele.
P3	Iqale idwebe isimo esimise okweqanda.@@Lo mfanekiso umzimba wekati.@@Kwaze kwalula bo!
P4	Emva kwaloko idweba onxantathu ababili phezu komfanekiso onjengeqanda.@@Labo nxantathu ngamadlebe ekati. Idweba omunye unxantathu omnyama phakathi emfanekisweni osaqanda.@@Lo nxantathu omnyama yikhala lekati. Idweba ke manje umugqa phezu kwekhala.
P5	Manje idweba amehlo kanye namashiya ekati.@@Amehlo amachashazana amabili.@@Amashiya imigqa emibili.@@Kwaze kwalula bo!
P6	Idweba umlomo ke manje.@@Umlomo ngumugqa.@@Kodwa ubukeka njengohlamvu 'w' futhi  bese idweba  izindevu zekati.@@Izindevu ezintathu ohlangothini lwesokunxele nangasohlangothini lwesokudla.
P7	Umfanekiso cishe usuphelile.@@Idweba izingalo nemilenze yekati.@@Izingalo nemilenze yimigqa bese idweba ubhonsi lwekati.@@Obhonsi yizindingilizi bese idweba umsila wekati.@@Umsila wona ngumugqa omude.
P8	Ekugcineni idweba imithende embalwa emzimbeni wekati. Ihlobisa ngokufaka umbala osawolintshi emzimbeni.@@Usuphelile umdwebo.@@Waze wamuhle!
P9	Manje ke sekuyithuba lekati ukudweba inja.@@Ithatha ipensela nephepha.@@Iphepha lingunxande.
P10	Liqale ikati lidweba. Lidwebe isimo esisaqanda.@@Lesi simo simele umzimba wenja.@@Hmm! Kuhle!
P11	Lidweba izimo ezimbili ezimnyama phezu kwalesi simo esisaqanda.@@Lezi zimo ezimbili ngamadlebe enja, liphinde lidwebe indingilizi emnyama phakathi kulesi simo esisaqanda.@@Indingilizi emnyama ikhala lenja. Manje lidweba umugqa phezu kwekhala.
P12	Selidweba amehlo kanye namashiya enja.@@Amehlo amachashazana amabili.@@Amashiya imigqa emibili.@@Hmm! Awuboni-ke, sekulungile.
P13	Manje lidweba umlomo wenja.@@Umlomo ngumugqa.@@Isimo futhi siwuhlamvu 'w'. Seliphinda lidweba amachashazana azungeza umlomo wenja.@@Amathathu ohlangothini lwesokunxele, amathathu ohlangothini lwesokudla.
P14	Umdwebo cishe usuphelile.@@Lidweba izingalo nemilenze kwenja.@@Izingalo nemilenze yimigqa, bese lidweba ubhonsi lwenja.@@Obhonsi yizindingilizi bese lidweba umsila wenja.@@Umsila ubukeka njengembali.
P15	Ekugcineni lidweba izindilinga ezimbalwa ezimnyama emzimbeni wenja. Lifaka umbala ophuzi emzimbeni.@@Nanko-ke umdwebo uphelile.@@Waze wamuhle!
P16	Usale kahle Kitty.@@Usale kahle mfanekiso wekati.@@Usale kahle, Bobby.@@Usale kahle mfanekiso wenja.
