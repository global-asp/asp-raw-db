desc:	Omusaadha omulagaadhavu tiyakuuma mmere bulungi

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala, yabaayo
  omusaadha eyayetebwanga Muyala mu kyalo Buyala.
P2	Tiyafangayo kweterekera mmere
  ya kulya mu biseera biidha, aye nga ajiyonoona.
P3	Ezira kyali kisigaire
  mu kyaji.
P4	Ebisimbe bya duuma bya nhuluka era bya kala.
P5	Kibuyaga yaafugha akasulya
  ake ekyaji kaagwa ere.
P6	Abaana baanhaagaana akamere
  akasigaire mu seepiki.
P7	Maama waibwe yaagwa
  ku itaka yaalira nga taidhi
  mmere eye ekye enkyo gheenaava.
P8	Lata waibwe atyama
  ghansi mu kisasi ali kuloghooza ku kyanaakola yebwidha.
