page_text:

P1	Eke ne'ekebusi.@@Eye ne'sese.
P2	Ekebusi nesese bikorigereria korwa etirisa.@@Bikorigereria korwa etirisa.
P3	Nari erio Ekebusi nesese bikorora ekegwankwa!@@Ekegwankwa ekio nigo keresitigamu.
P4	Ekebusi nesese nigo bitagete kobuata ekegwankwa.@@Ekebusi nesese biabwatirie ekegwankwa.@@Biabwatirie ekegwankwa.
P5	Ekebusi nesese bikobwatia ekegwankwa namagoro. Bigotara magega ya ekegwankwa.@@Korende ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango. Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango, esese neekebusi nigo bigotara ng'ora. Nigo bire ng'ora mono.
P6	Ekebusi nesese bikobwatia ekegwankwa neenyange. Bikogendia magega yekegwankwa.@@Korende ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango. Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango mono, ekebusi nesese nigo bire ng'ora. Nigo bire ng'ora mono.
P7	Ekebusi nesese bikobwatia ekegwankwa negari. Bikogendi magega yeekegwankwa.@@Korende ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango. Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango mono, Ekebusi Nesese nigo bire ng'ora. Nigo bire ng'ora mono.
P8	Ekebusi nesese bikobwati ekegwankwa nobwato. Nigo bigoterema magega yekegwankwa.@@Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango. Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango mono, ekebusi nesese nigo bire ng'ora. Nigo bire ng'ora mono.
P9	Ekebusi nesese nigo bikobwatia ekegwankwa nendege. Bikoiruruka magega yeekegwankwa.@@Korende ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango. Ekegwankwa nigo kere bwango mono, ekebusi nesese nigo bire ng'ora. Nigo bire ng'ora mono.
P10	Ekero ekio ekebusi nesese biatenena. Biatenena  nagoikaransa. Biakaransa inse yomote.@@Biakaransa inse yomote omonene.@@Ekero ekio...
P11	Biarora ekegwankwa ekemo omote igoro.@@Biarora ebigwankwa bibere omote igoro.@@Biarora ebigwankwa bitato omote igoro.
P12	Biarorire ebigwankwa emia eyemo!
P13	Kwaeri Ekebusi.@@ Kwaeri Esese.@@ Kwaeri ebigwankwa.
P14	.
