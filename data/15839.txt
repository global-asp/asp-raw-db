page_text:

P1	Injangwe yanjye irihe? Where is my cat?
P2	Ese iri munsi y'igitanda? Could it be under the bed?
P3	Ese iri hejuru y'akabati? Could it be on the top of the wardrobe?
P4	Ese iri inyuma y'intebe? Could it be behind the chair?
P5	Ese iri aho tujugunya imyanda? Could it be it be in the dustbin?
P6	Ese iri mu gitebo? Could it be in the basket?
P7	Ese yagiye hanze? Could it have gone out?
P8	Ngiyi hano! Here it is!
