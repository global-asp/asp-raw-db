title:	Enkyende erire omukira gwayo
author:	Twinamasiko Julius
artist:	Horácio José Cossa
lang:	Rukiga

desc:	Egyo wakame ya garukaho ogundi murundi! Oku wakame yarebize munywani wayo enkyende bakahendera bagikwasire bakagyisharaho omukira nabwanyima

page_text:

P1	Obweire bwenyimaho wakame ekaba eiri munywani waamani munonga we enkyende
P2	Eizoba rimwe wakame yangira,"renka tugyende tuntaburemu turonde ekiitu kyona ekyokurya."nanekyende yikiriza, kwoka gukaba guri omutego gwayo.
P3	Bakangyenda omumusiri gwebikyori batandika kugurya.
P4	kyonka omugyezi wakame akaba yagambire bakama bomusiri, kukwata ekyende bakagyoreka ku eteine magyezi.
P5	Omunyabwengye wakame akantandika kutera enduru,"abashuma! abashuma! ebikyori byanyu byona babitwara!" nawe yagurukagayo yiruka.
P6	Ahonaho bakama bagwe beinja bashanga enkyende erikugyezaho kwiruka.kyonka ekaba eyine omukira murigwa ngurikugyizibira.
P7	bakama bangwe bakamukuratira, nenduru,"yemerera! yemerera! twakurebire, ori ekyende."
P8	kyonka bakamukwata kandi batandika kumutera.
P9	Tarabasize  kwemere obuhurunzi yangira ngu,"naba ndi naa munywani wagye wakame.Niwe anyesire."
P10	kyonka bakama bagwe bamusharaho omukira," kandi bamugyira,gyenda kandi ogambire munywani  ngu twakushaho omukira."
P11	Rero bagyenda owenju ya wakame kandi baateka omukira gwe ekyende kugurya. Wakame yayeta ekyende ahakyamushana.
P12	Ahamuheru gwokurya wakame yabuza enkyende yaba nemanya eki yarya. enkyende etiinue magyezi yagarukamu," aaha tindikumanya."
P13	wakame yamugyira ngu yarya omukira gweye kandi yiruka. Kandi kuruga obwe baba abazigu!
