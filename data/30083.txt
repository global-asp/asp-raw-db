title:	Ebirime n'ebyaayo bya Dhaadha
author:	Juliet Namugaya
artist:	Ursula Nafula
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Omwaana ainhongola Enhimiro ya Dhaadha ghe.

page_text:

P1	Dhaadha ghange mulimi.Endhu ye kasolya ka bbululu.
P2	Akuuma ente ekamwa mu kisibo kye.
P3	Alundha n'embuzi.
P4	Embuzi eyindhi ndheru.Elya eisubi lya kilagala.
P5	Dhaadha alundha n'enkoko.Ghaligho n'empanga emyuufu.
P6	Ghaligho n'ebisonga kamaala.
P7	Dhaadha ghange alundha n'engyibwa mu kisibo kye.
P8	Ngonza engyibwa endhirugavu n'endheru.
P9	Ghaligho n'embwa ibiri ku kisibo kye.Dhigonza inho okuzanhira ghalala.
P10	Dhaadha ghange alima dhuuma ku nhimiro ye.
P11	Asimba n'emiti gya fakedho.
P12	Bwe nkyaala ku mu nhimiro ya dhaadha ghange,ndya amapaapaali.
P13	Ebigogo bya dhaadha bikali bito.Dhaadha aidha kundheeteera ku amenvu nga geenze.
P14	Enhimiro ya dhaadha yebunguluire emiti emighanvu.
P15	Ghaligho akasondha k'eidhoodho eryendhaghuulo.Dhaadha alikozesa ebitepere by'eidhoodho eibisi.
