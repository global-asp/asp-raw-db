page_text:

P1	Nazi izilwanyana zasekhaya.
P2	Inkomo iyakhala ithi,  "Mhu-u-u. Mhu-u-u." Inkomo ifuna amanzi.
P3	Ibhokhwe iyakhala ithi,  "Mhe-e-e-eh. Mhe-e-e-eh." Ibhokhwe ifuna amanzi.
P4	Ihashe liyakhala lithi,  "Yi-hi-hi-hi. Yi-hi-hi-hi." Ihashe lifuna amanzi.
P5	Ihagu iyakhala ithi,  "Hho-hho-hho-i-i-i-i." Ihagu ifuna amanzi.
P6	Isikhukukazi siyakhala sithi,  "ke-ke-ke-keeee,  ke-ke." Isikhukukazi sifuna amanzi.
P7	Inja iyakhonkotha ithi,  "Hawu-hawu-hawu." Inja ifuna amanzi.
P8	Umlimi uphendula uthi,  "Shhh! Nanga amanzi!"
