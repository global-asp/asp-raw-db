title:	Wantaama eyakoowa n'ekibuga
author:	Rebecca Ndhibogeza
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi

desc:	Buli mbeera yooyagaana mwene

page_text:

P1	Wantaama yabanga mu kibuga. Yakoogha ekibuga yaaslagho okuja mu nsiko. Mu kutambula, yayagaana Wampiti eyali taliire okumala enaku.
P2	Yalamusa Wampiti yairaganamu," Muganda wange Wampiti, oliyotya? Muganda wange Wampiti oliyotya?"  Wampiti yairamu Wantaama,"T'onamusa woiraganamu. Kolagho ky'oklagho okutoolagho endhala yange."
P3	Wantaama weyawulira ekyo, yatandiika oklumuka. Amangu ago, yatuuka ewa Wampologoma. Wampiti yali ali kumulumuukirira. Wampiti yatuukagho era yaamala yaakoma, yaakuba Wampologoma enfuufu mu maiso.
P4	Wampologoma yakoba Wampiti,"Titusobola kwagaana mirembe mu maka gaife? Osuula enfuufu mu maiso gaife!" Wampiti yairamu,"kyabazinga, mbaire titegeeza butakugha kitiibwa! Ndi kulumuukirira mmere ya kulya!"
P5	Wampologoma yaakoba,"oli mmere yange, yeene, Wampiti. Aye nkubinzeeku? oidhye yendi. Kabiri toonenia."
P6	Wampologoma kabira yakoba Wantaama,"Lwaki waidha mu kapanga?" Wantaama yairamu,"Naidha olw'okuba ndi musamize."
P7	Wampologoma yakoba," Tegeka obulezi obw'ekinansi ntegeere nga oli musamize." Wantaama yairamu," Obulezi bwange bwa bbeeyi." Wampiti yakoba,"Ekyo ti kizibu, oba bwa imeka. Ezira kintu kitasoboka kunze."
P8	Wantaama yakoba,"Obulezi bwa kutu ya Wampiti." Wampologoma yasala ekutu ya Wampiti yaagigha Wantaama. Wantaama yata ekutu ya Wampiti mu jaaga y'omubisi yaagigha Wampologoma.
P9	Wampologoma yalya ekutu ya Wampiti yajagaana nga ewooma inho. Yabuuza Wantaama,"kisoboka nze okufuna ekutu eyindi?"
P10	Wantaama yakoba,"Iyi, kisoboka kyabazinga wange."kale Wampologoma yasala ekutu ya Wampiti ey'okubiri yaagitwalira Wantaama. Wantaama yata ekutu eyo mu jaaga y'omubisi yaagigha Wampologoma.
P11	Agho Wampologoma yaakoba Wantaama,"Kisoboka okufuna oluwu?" Wantaama,"Kisoboka kyabazingz wange." Wampiti weyawulira ekyo yali mwereriikirivu okufa,ya lumuka. Wampologoma yeena yamulumuukirira.
P12	"We kiba nga eno n'embeera eri mu nsiko, kyandibaire kikalamu okwirayo eka," Wantaama akobye.
