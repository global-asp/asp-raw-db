desc:	Akaisiki akato bakakatuma owa isenkuru wako. Omumuhanda

page_text:

P1	akaisiki akato abazaire be bakakatuma mwa esi nkuru waye awyalara ga mukibira. Akaisiki kakagenda konka nokujaguza kwingi
P2	omyisiki yeyahikire mu kibira, yasanga embuzi, yamugamba nti wengendereze habyokuba haliyo orusoronkana rwebisolo by'mukibira
P3	omusiki weyakire hagati yekibira, yasanga ebisoro bingi omuhanda yairuka muno natooka
P4	weyaturikire omukibira, yasanga ebinyonyi bingi hamu nebitooka. Omyisiki yairuka natooka. Ise'kuru wamuhuura
P5	Isi'nkuru yabaza nomukazi we nti ogo myijukuru waitu. Yagenda yo mangu ne embwa ye kumurora
P6	webakire omuka, yaramukya burionmu na baana abandi emere. weyamazire, yatandika banyumiza ebya mubaireho omumuhanda.
P7	weyamazire kubanyumiza kandi bamuha amagezi aga kutagarukamu kulibata wenka omu kibira, yagenda nekujaguza kwingi kuzana naaba abandi
P8	Atakyore kulibata wenka mu kibira.
