page_text:

P1	Themba wavakashela esikhungwini setilwane,  nebatali bakhe,  silwane sekucala lasibona yindlulamitsi,  lenentsamo lendze.
P2	Lesinye setilwane lasibona Themba kwaba libhubesi. Phela libhubesi ngilo inkhosi lebusa tonkhe tilwane tasendle.
P3	Themba nebatali bakhe,  baphindze bahlala phansi babukela tindlovu nebantfwabato tidlala.
P4	Themba wetfuka kakhulu ngalesikhatsi batali bakhe bamkhombisa feleba wenyoka.
P5	Silwane lesamjabulisa kakhulu Themba kwaba yimfene. Kungako watsatsa bhanana wakhe wayiphosela,  ayipha.
P6	Btali ba Themba bachubeka bamtjengisa tinhlobonhlobo tetilwane. Silwane labamkhombisa sona kwaba nguleso lekasesaba kakhulu,  lokuyingwenya.
P7	Waphatseka kabuhlungu Themba nasekusikhatsi sekuhamba,  ngobe lilanga lakhe esikhungwini setilwane belimnandzi kakhulu.
