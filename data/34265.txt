page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, in a small village there live a  hunter.
P2	He went to the bush to check if his trap got an animal.
P3	On his way, he lost his way.
P4	But he found a giraffe caught in another hunters trap.
P5	He was happy and decided to kill the giraffe for himself.
P6	He flung his arrow towards the giraffe to kill it but he rather saved it.
P7	The giraffe escaped!
P8	He got back home without meat for his hungry family.
