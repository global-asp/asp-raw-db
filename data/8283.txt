desc:	Eshitabu shiolupala nende oluswairi

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping. Ndachama okhuruka omukoye.
P2	 Alone. Sienyene.
P3	I also enjoy skipping with my friend. Ndachama okhuruka nomwitsa wanje.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Ndachama khusoma tsingano tsindayi.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Ndachama okhuchenda mushikuri nende abetsa banje.
P6	I enjoy washing dishes for my mother. Ndachama okhwosiria mama efindu.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Ndachama khwandika tsinukuta tsikhongo tsienyala okhusoma mubwangu.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit. Ndachama khulia efiamo biaukhane.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Ndachama okhukona khushitanda eshikhongo.
P10	I enjoy listening to Mother's interesting stories. Ndachama okhurecheresia tsingano tsindayi mama niyanganira.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Aah! Ndachama okhukhola efindu fino fiosi!
