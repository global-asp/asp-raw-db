page_text:

P1	Beam has a fresh breath and a brilliant smile!@@What is her secret?
P2	Beam uses her red toothbrush to brush her teeth.@@She brushes briskly, twice a day with toothpaste.
P3	One bright morning after breakfast, Beam did not feel like brushing her teeth.@@"I will take a brief break from my toothbrush!" she thought.
P4	But, Beam's break from brushing was not brief. @@Beam did not brush for three weeks!
P5	"I do not need my red toothbrush anymore," said Beam.@@Her teeth began to turn brown.
P6	She lost her bright smile and fresh breath.@@"You need to put the brakes on your break!" said Beam's best friend.
P7	What will bring back Beam's fresh breath and brilliant smile?
P8	Do you know?
