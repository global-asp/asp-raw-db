desc:	Iyi ni inkuru ivugaku mwana muto wumukobwa urwaye

page_text:

P1	Mu cyumweru gishize nakiriye amabaruwa menshi.
P2	 Kuwa Mbere, muganga yandikiye ibaruwa mwarimu wange.
P3	Tabisile ararwaye. Agomba kuguma mu rugo.
P4	Kuwa Kabiri nakiriye ibaruwa y'abanyeshuri twigana.
P5	Tabisile we, turakwifuriza gukira vuba!
P6	Kuwa Gatatu nabonye ibaruwa ivuye ku ncuti yange magara.
P7	Reba, nagushushanyirije agashushanyo. Ni Dudu ugukunda.
P8	Kuwa Kane nakiriye ibaruwa yanditswe na mama wange.
