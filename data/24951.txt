desc:	Naani anasinga ku Waikovu ni Wakamyu mu misinde.

page_text:

P1	Lwali  lulala, Wakamyu  yaayagaana  Waikovu. Yaakoba Waikovu, "Tosobola kulumuka! Oidhi bwa kwavula ku itaka."
P2	Waikovu  yaakoba, "Idha  tubone ku saabbiiti."
P3	Waikovu  yaategeka obukutukutu obw'empapulo kikumi (100) yaabughandiikaku obubaka bulala obwo.
P4	Waikovu yaagha  baine obukutukutu obw'empapulo yaakoba, "Wakamyu   gha naidha ghano  nga ali kundeta, kwimwe kubeeku  akoba: Ninze Waikovu."
P5	Waikovu  yaakoba  baine ati  beekweke enhuma ey'eisubi Wakamyu ni  Waikovu ghe  balina okubta.
P6	Ku  sabbiiti, Wakamyu yaatuuka yaayagaana Waikovu. Wakamyu  yaakoba, "Twinhame emisinde. Aye ndi n'okusinga."
P7	Bawaikovu baaba einhuma ow'emiti nga buli Wakamyu wabita, Waikovu akeyanira ghaigulu ati, "Ndi Waikovu mu misinde! Wakamyu yaayeta, "Waikovu! Waikovu!"
P8	Mulala  ku  ba Waikovu yeekweka enhumaow'eisubi yairamu ati, "Ninze Waikovu! Ninze Waikovu!"
P9	Olumalirira, Wakamyu yaakoowa inho. Yaalambaala ghansi, nga akoba, "Waikovu, nkooye; Ompangwire."Waikovu mulala oyo yaakengesa nga  ali  inhuma  owe  eisubi.
P10	Agho Waikovu  yaaghangula emisinde kubanga  yali mugezi  inho.
