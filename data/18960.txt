title:	Amahoose Gamugaso Hi?  
author:	Mubbalya Charles
artist:	Magoro John
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Omugaso gwa mahoose.

page_text:

P1	Wafula galoma Nafula ati,  "Bugula olutu; onewihirire efula yiri huupi hutonya." 
P2	Efula yahena yatonya Nafula gewihirira. Olwohuba gaali agegire olutu gagenda bulaƞi mu fula. 
P3	Amatu n'amahoose gatambisiwa mu ngeri nyingi. 
P4	Abandi batambisa amatu n'amahoose ohwewihirira, ohusimba enyumba n'ebigangu, ohwalirira mu nimiro  n'abandi baholaamo amayenje.
P5	Era nindi babbotaamo ekufira, ebisero, ebyonjo n'ebidugi. 
P6	Amahoose agandi bagalya ng'eryani. 
P7	Amahoose agandi abaatu bagatambisa ng'obuleesi. 
P8	Amahoose agandi mere y'ebisolo. 
P9	Nenda hubalomera ti omulimo omubba ogwa mahoose nj'ogwo hubbota emere y'ebimera. 
P10	Amahoose gahola hino ni gatambisa amaani ge ryuba, embeƞo ko n'amaaji. 
P11	Ebimera birya emere yabyo n'ebitira mu misi n'amahoose.
