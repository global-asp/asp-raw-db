title:	Ensiri enhiiza

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Twenha twenha twidhi kereere ensiri yekuba nga bwe lugaana obwiire. Kawuka katono kati aye kinhununsi kiba!

page_text:

P1	koona! koona!
P2	"Anni aligho?" nbuuza mu ndoolo.
P3	"Ninze,"eiroboozi eridhwira likuba.
P4	"Ani 'nze'?"Mbuuza.
P5	"Nkokobye,ni,o-n-l m-e,"eiroboozi lyena elyo likoba.
P6	"Tidhi muntu yenayena ayetebwa,'o-n-l-y m-e',"Ndhiramu.Nhekenhenia okwetoloza obuliri bwange akatimba kensiri.
P7	"Kale,eriinha lyange nize Amos."Eroboozi lidhwiira inho     okusiingako mukusooka.Erikumpinho ni kutu yange.
P8	"Amos ani?"Mbuuza nga bwenigalinga ghansi gha katimba kange akensiri.
P9	"Nsiri,"eiroboozi mukukomenkereza likoba.Nmulira okulumibwa ku kutu yange.
P10	"Ooo!" Ntuuma ghaigulu era ntandiika okunhonia."Oidha kusasulira kinho!"Ndheekana.
P11	Mubusungughavu,Nva mubuliri era nazikiza ekitangaala.
P12	Ndigulagho olutabaalo ku nsiri eniiza!
