desc:	Murume asina kurongeka akatadza kuchengeta zvokudya zvakanaka

page_text:

P1	Paivapo neumwe murume ainzi Joel aigara mumusha weAya.
P2	Haana kufunga zvekuchengeta zvokudya kuitira remangwana,  aingopfachura.
P3	Mudura makanga muri gwenga.
P4	Chibage chakanga chiri mumunda, chakaoma nezuva.
P5	Kwakava nemhepo inesimba yakasimudza denga redura.
P6	Panguva yekudya vana vakaita mahwekwe pazvokudya zvishoma zvakanga zvasara muchikari.
P7	Nokuda kwekupererwa kuti vachapei vana mune ramangwana, amai vakachema.
P8	Baba vakagara muderere remba, vachifunga nezvemazuva aitevera.
