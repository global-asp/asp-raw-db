P1	Once upon a time the was a boy named Rocxie he loved eating cheese. One morning Rocxie was ready to go to school and his father made breakfast for him and called him for breakfast. Soon the school bus came.
P2	Later on Rocxie arrived at school and shouted wait for me and his friends ran to him to ask about the homework they had. Miss Kenny was in class. As miss Kenny said did "everyone write their homework" And everyone said "yes"
P3	Miss Kenny said "would someone let us know what did you have to do" Cosmo the bully raised his hand up and said we had to do our homework. Miss Kenny got angry and sent Cosmo to the principals office.
P4	But Cosmo did not go to the principals office he just played outside and ate his orange. Rocxie saw that Cosmo was playing outside and told miss Kenny. Miss Kenny went out angry and pulled Cosmo to the principals office.
P5	She told the principal Cosmo is a problem at school. So the principal Called Cosmo parents. They came to school and the principal told the story to them and they yelled at Cosmo and the principal suspended him for two weeks.
P6	Unfortunately Cosmo was happy it seemed like a holiday to him so he ran out of the school shouting "happy holidays Cosmo" and his parents went out of the school , he's mom celebrated with him. As his father was depressed.
P7	Soon it was luch time. Everyone went outside to play and eat. Rocxie and his friends had a great time without Cosmo interrupting them while eating or playing their favorite games.
P8	Soon it was science class and miss shimark was in the classroom. As miss shimark said "Everyone today were going to work as partners and discover amazing things so please find a partner and discover amazing things around, you can start now.
P9	Miss shimark showed the class a cool experiment on their hands.
P10	TWO WEEKS LATER @Cosmo was so tall because he has grown up. No one at school knew that Cosmo was tall. So he stayed at home and told his mom he won't go back until his fine. When his little brother saw him he was shocked.
P11	Cosmo gave up because he was not getting better so he went outside and rode his bike. All the school kids laughed at him and some were paying revenge for being such a bully.
