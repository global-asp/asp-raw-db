title:	Pulakena kolutu lwandje

artist:	Angie and  Upesh

desc:	Ohopulakene tuu ngele oluntu lyoye tali kulombwele?

page_text:

P1	Nena ita ndiyi kosikola oshoka efudho.
P2	Nena ita ndika tala Otivii, molwashoka kapena olusheno.
P3	Ote ka ninga shike mbela? 

Nena ote kapulakena kolutu lwandje!
P4	Tango ondina oku mwena, opo ndi vule okupulakena kolutu lwandje.
P5	Eheno paife onduu viite omufudho gwandje. 

Ote futhilemo no te futhamo, natango te futhilemo no ku futhamo.
P6	Ota ndi vulu oku futha mokule. Sssssssssssssssss! 

Noku futha pevi.  Mmmmmm.
P7	Nngaashi ngeyi onduu vite omutima gwandje tagu thenge! 

Doodom, doodoom, doo doom.
P8	Ote vulu oku ninga omutima gwandje gu futhe tagwendele ile ta gu futha mule? 

Eheno ngele ndanhuka pombanda noposhi lwiikando omilongo mbali.
P9	Paife tala nee, omutima gwandje otagu thenge tagwe ndelele.
P10	Ngele onda tula ominwe thandje poshikesho, ondu uvite omiinyengo gandje golutu.
P11	Ondii yu vite teyolo. 

Haha, haha, haah, haa!
P12	Ondii yu vite telili. 

Boohoooo hooo!
P13	Ondii yu vite tepandula. 

Pwaka, pwaka, pwaka.
P14	Ondii yu vite mepunda lyandje ta mulili. 

Guddu, guddu, guddu.
P15	Epunda lyandje ota liti, "Pendje ndilye!"
P16	Omayulu gandje ogu uvite ezimba lyuukuki tau kangwa pelugo kumeme.

Ngaashi ngeyi ondahala okwi yuva te taasina uukuki mboka.
