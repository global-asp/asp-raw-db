desc:	Lengulube enenkani ayifuni ukulalela umlimi.

page_text:

P1	Bekuyisikhathi sezilwane sokulala. Esinye sezilwane azange silalela.
P2	Umlimi wacela uPiggy  ukuthi aye egoqweni lakhe. Yena wala.
P3	Umlimi wacela u Trusty Whip ukuthi ashaye U Piggy. Yena wala.
P4	u Fiery Fire wala ukushisa u Trusty Whip ngokungalaleli.
P5	U Clear Water akalalelanga umlimi ngokungacimi u Fiery  Fire.
P6	Umlimi wacela uMilky Moo ukuthi aphuze u Clear Water.
P7	UMilky Moo wathi, "Ngizophuza lamanzi. Ngomile."
P8	uClear water wakhala, "Ungangiphuzi. Ngizocima Umlilo."
P9	U Fiery Fire wamemeza, "Cha angeke. Ngizomshisa u Trusty Whip."
P10	UTrusty Whip wakhala, "Ngicela ungangishisi. Ngizomshaya u Piggy."
P11	UPiggy wacasuka. "Ngizongena egoqweni."
P12	UPiggy wangena egoqweni lakhe futhi bekakulungele ukulala.
