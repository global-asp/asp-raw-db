title:	Metswalle
author:	Tseliso Masolane
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)

desc:	Tloho

page_text:

P1	Lebitso la ka  ke Sello.
P2	Ke na le metswalle e mene.
P3	Mabitso a bona ke Zizo, Lele, Sisa le Ayanda.
P4	Motswalle waka Zizo o rata ho bapala bolo ya maoto.
P5	Motswalle wa ka Lele o rata ho sesa.
P6	Motswalle wa ka Sisa o rata ho bapala boleke maipatile.
P7	Motswalle wa ka Ayanda o rata ho bala.
P8	Nna?

Ke rata ho etsa dintho tsohle tseo ba di etsang.
P9	Ke bapala bolo ya maoto le Zizo.
P10	Ke sesa le Lele.
P11	Ke bapala boleke maipatile le Sisa.
P12	Tloho, motswalle, wena o rata ho etsang?
P13	Tloho, o tlo bapala bolo ya maoto le rona.
P14	Tloho, o tlo sesa le rona.
P15	Tloho, o tlo bapala boleke maipatile le rona.
P16	Tloho, o tlo bala le rona!
