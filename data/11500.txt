page_text:

P1	Lona ngu Jaaka u Mdobi ovela eNaminya.
P2	UDoba emfuleni iNile.
P3	Unesikebhe,  isisefo Sokudoba kanye nodobo.
P4	Uvuka ekuseni njalo ukuze abambe uhlobo olukhethekile lwenhlanzi yaseNile.
P5	Ngehora leshumi ekuseni,  uthengisa izinhlanzi azibambile phakathi nedolobha iJinja.
P6	Jaaka kwanzenjani? Awuyenzanga inzuzo? Udumele kakhuku uJaaka.
P7	"Namhlanje isimo besijabhisa. Umoya womfula iNile awuvumanga ukuthi ngidobe. Enye inhlanzi imuke nodobo lwami."
P8	"Kodwa nginethemba lokuthi kusasa kuzobangcono,  ngoba ngithenge udobo olusha."
