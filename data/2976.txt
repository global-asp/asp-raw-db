desc:	Ndi kwirayo ku isomero. Ndi no okwegeza mu byambalo byange ebyeisomero. Nnhenda kubona oba bintuuka bukalamu.

page_text:

P1	Ekiteeteeyi kino kileeyi inho.
P2	Omudhoozi guno munene inho.
P3	Ensawo eno nnene inho.
P4	Omusipi guno mumpi inho.
P5	Enkoofiira eno ntono inho.
P6	Sookisi dhino nnhimpi inho.
P7	Engaito dhino mpyaka inho.
P8	 Dhintuuka nabukalamu inho.
