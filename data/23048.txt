desc:	Ekyigano ekyi nikyishoborera omwishikyi gubaakweta Polina arikukunda kuhurikyiza nokugana ebigano. Ekyi kyikamureetera kukunda omushoomesa we na nyina.

page_text:

P1	Ogu ni muhaara wangye Polina.
P2	No'mwishikyi murungyi.
P3	Nakunda ku tebya ebyigano.
P4	Nimutebeeza ebyigano.
P5	Polina nawe natebeeza bagyenzi beeye ebyigano.
P6	Orunaaku rumwe Polina akatebya ekyigano kirungyi.
P7	Ekyigano kika kyri aha kuteeka.
P8	Omushoomesa we akamuheereza omugati boona bashemererwa.
