page_text:

P1	Nkuku oa chakela. Otla le Thabo le mazambane le litlapole mili le mahapu. 

Thabo unea nkhunu sabo thlokoa.
P2	Boka elokile empa, nkhunu otseba hobala na? 

Haafihla libenkeleng utlafihlela sebokehang na?

"Aremo etsetse sikhoama," Thabo a tseha. "Sikhoama setiileng sesiholo ebile sesitala."
P3	Mme onka lilapi ale rekileng benkeleng. Uletseha kasetsoantso sasi khoama. 

Mathlakuri amaholo amabeli pele kea maholo abaphara.
P4	Ebile amanyenyane amabeli hoboga sikhoama. 

Thoba oa aphotha le amaholo amabeli. Oa lebelela Mme hanka sitsoantso hape.
P5	Mme otseha lephatla lathlase lenna amabeli. Mo hotla etsa sikhoama sitie haholo. 

Thabo oa aphotha hamme anka sitsoantso hape.
P6	Otseha thapo tse nne tse lileele tsephara. 

Tsepeli lirokoa mmoho ho etsa thapo elengoe. Sikhoama sithla tlea hoho phethiloeng habeli.
P7	Mme onka muchini oa horoka. 

Orokantsa maphato ohle atsehiloeng hoetsa sikhoama sankuku. 

Onea Thabo sikhoama Thabo uea thaba are, "Kea leboha Mme, skihoama sankuku sesitala!"
