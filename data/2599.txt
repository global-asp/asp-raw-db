desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	Ewa alu mu yi mvu.
P2	Ondere iri muu yi mvu.
P3	Odru na aria su be. Yi dria mu yi mvuria.
P4	Yere towi azini ezoo azia yi mu yi mvuria.
P5	Odrukudru aziri azini e'bi aro yi yi alia.
P6	Bure oromi yi mu yi mvuria.
P7	Kami alu emu yi mvuria.
P8	A`di ma asi mba muzu yi mvuza be drile ni?
