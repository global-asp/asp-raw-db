page_text:

P1	muzeeyi ghe koleo yakagya mukibuga.
P2	mukazi ghe yeenha yakagya mukibuga.
P3	mutabanhi gha mukazi gha koleo yenha yakagya mukibuga.
P4	mugandha gha mutabanhi gha mukazi gha muzeeyi koleo yakagya mukibuga.
P5	embwa ya mugandha gha mutabanhi gha muka muzeeyi koleo yakagya mukibuga.
