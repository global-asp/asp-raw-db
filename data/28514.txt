desc:	Wamusota ayega okwewuga aye katono adimire. Ekyomukisa Wankudu aidhi ekyokukola.

page_text:

P1	Eira einho, ghaaliyo abemikwano babiri, Wankudu ni Wamusota.
P2	Lulala Wankudu yakoba Wamusota, "tuje twewuge." "Kale, aye tiidhi kuwuga," yairamu. "Nda kukwegeresa," Wamnkudu yairamu.
P3	Baaja mw'iwugiro. "Naana anasooka okugwa mumaadhi?" Wankudu yabuuza. "Ighe, nkwetegereze," Omusota gwakoba.
P4	Wankudu yainjira mw'iwugiro. Wamusota yali ku madaala yetegereza.
P5	Oluvainhuma, Wamusota yagwa mu maadhi nga Wankudu bwamugha ebiragiro.
P6	Wankudu ni Wamusota Beewugira mu maadhi amatontono.
P7	Baalera ku maadhi.
P8	Baali bakalera, Wankudu yainhama okwewuga ng'aira einhuma. Wamusota bweyagezaaku okwegeresa Wankudu yaatandiika okudimira.
P9	Ono bweyabona nga Wamusota tali kuboneka, Wankudu yeyuna mu maadhi eifo yaawonia obulamu bwa Wamusota.
P10	Wankudu bweyafuluma mu maadhi yaagalamiriza Wamusota asobole okumunuunamu amaadhi geyali amize.
P11	Wankudu yata omunhwagwe ku gwa Wamusota yaamunuunamu amadhi.
