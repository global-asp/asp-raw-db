page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a boy named Jack. He lived with his grandmother.
P2	One afternoon Jack's grandmother sent him to go buy some oil and fish.
P3	On his way to the shop he walked past an empty house and he went inside.
P4	Inside he saw a big gold mirror. In the mirror a lady was watching him.
P5	The lady called him closer and he walked slowly towards her.
P6	She asked him what he wants. He told her he wanted fish and oil.
P7	The lady in the mirror gave him fish and oil to give to his grandmother.
P8	He went home and gave his grandmother the fish and oil.
P9	His grandmother ate the fish and got very sick.
P10	Jack went back to the lady and told her that his grandmother got sick.
P11	Jack told the lady that his grandmother is sick and that he is very mad.
P12	The lady laughed and Jack took the mirror and threw it on the ground.
P13	The mirror shattered into pieces and he ran back home.
P14	Jack told his grandmother everything and she hugged him.
P15	She asked him what he learned, he said " never take things from strangers."
