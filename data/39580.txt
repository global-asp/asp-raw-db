desc:	U faityô u eren ishima i hanmô  or ga. Kwagh-hir ka ior mba hen Itiopia ve samber a min ye.

page_text:

P1	Ter man wan na ve mough gôgô zende vea jaki ve. @@Mba dugh sha gbenda yô, ior mbagenev pine ve ityôkyaa i ve henda jaki ga yô.
P2	Tsô ter la kaa a wan na er a unde sha jaki. Un di lu zenden angahar. @@Ve yem nahan dôlôô je.
P3	Kwe ior ugen zua a ve gba zôhôn, "Wan ne cihi kwagh tsô! Nenge a tema sha jaki, ter na gema ngu zenden angahar!"
P4	Kwagh ne na wan la tsuwe gba shin nya man ter na kar henda. @@Ve zahemen a zende.
P5	Ior mbagenev mba nengen a ve yô, ve gba ahon, "Ter ne lan kwagh tsô! A henda jaki wan na ngu zenden angahar!"
P6	Ter man wan cii man ve henda jaki imôngu.
P7	Shien kpua yô, ior mbagenev gba kaan er, "Nenge abumeaior ne! Ve soo u wuan jaki ne?"
P8	Ken masejime yô, ter man wan ve mase kaven ye gbenda môm kpa ngu u palegh alam a ior ga. @@Ve mase eren er ve nenge doo ve yô.
