desc:	Olugelo lukwata ku kabaga akategekedwa omwami wanfudu obumyu no buwundo.

page_text:

P1	olugendo lwatandika sawa biri ezokumakya.kulugendo lwakabaga ko buwundo enfudu yagamba  akamyu  nti we batukila kukabaga,ajakuyitibwa"wena"ne akamyu ne kakiriza
P2	wezatukila sawa nya ezokumankya bali batuseda kumukolo,bayanilizibwa nesanyu obuwundo ne bawebwa obutebe mu kisenge kimu.
P3	obuwundo bwabaletela chai olwo enfudu nebuza ani alina okunywa chai?obuwundo nebudamu "wamwena."
P4	enfudu yeyanza obuwundo olwo natandika okunywa chai yeka ate oba bamuyita mwena  kukabaga.
P5	sawa zokulya wezatuka emere nejulwa.obuwundo nebugamba "eno emere yamwena "mubwangu enfudu yatandika okulya nga akamyu wekalindilila emere yako.
P6	ekyembi obuwundo tebwamanya nti enfudu yali eyitibwa mwena ate obuwundo bwakizula luvanyuma nti akamyu tekalide mere awonga bakimye esuwani.kino kyewunyiisa nyo obuwundo kubanga emere yali ewedewo.
P7	awo akamyu kafuluma nga kaduka nga enfudu no buwundo banyumirwa omukolo.
P8	okuva olwo enfudu etambula mpola kulwemere gyeyalya kumukolo,obuwundo bwewuba mubanga kubanga butya engyala yakamyu ate akamyu kaduka nyo kubanga kalina okufuna emere ewantu ewalala.
