title:	Ekichunchu ekyabiire nikyend'omunywaani

desc:	Hariho ekichunchu ekirikwenda kunywaana omuntu. Bakaguma hamwe nembabazi akaanya kakya

page_text:

P1	Eizooba rimwe, ekichunchu kikenda kuruga omuchishaarara kikanywaana omuntu.
P2	Ekichunchu no'muntu barugaho ba'ba abanywaani.
P3	Bakarya hamwe ba'baganisa enyama. Ekichunchu kikareeba ngu ogwo omushaija akaba nakunda enyama munonga. Kyatandika kutiina ngu naaza kurugaho akiite.
P4	Ekichunchu kikatiina ngu ogu mushaija arya'tunga eichumu rye aki'chumite. Nawe omushaija akatiinire ngu ekichunchu kirya'murya nyekiro yaaba agwejegeire.
P5	Ekiiro kimwe, omushaija akaroota ngu ekichunchu kikaba nikimubaza mpora mpora nikyenda kumwiita kimurye.
P6	Omushaija akakwaata eichumu'rye na'boroga ati, "Waba noyenda kundya, ninza kukuchimta." Ekichunchu kikaimuka kyahunga kiti, "Nkabagaambira ngu ogu mushaija aryanyita."
P7	Kuruga eizooba eryo, ekichunchu no'mushaija bakangana. Buri kyareeba omushaija, ekichunchu kirya mwaita. Kandi nabantu baakishanga, barya kyaita.
P8	Nikyo barikugyira ngu: omuntu kwarabe nayenda kukunywaana, obanze omwetegyereze. Omanye mwaba nimubaasa kutuura hamwe omu'mbabazi mutaine buzibu.
