P1	Bataata balayandisha ceele.
P2	Mebo ndaayanda musozya.
P3	Bataata balayandisha kofi.
P4	Ndaayanda mukupa.
P5	Bataata balayandisha ma apo.
P6	Ndaayanda mafuleenke.
P7	Bataata balayandisha cinkwa. Ndaayanda cinkwa ciyonkendwe.
P8	Boonse tulayandisha keke munono.
