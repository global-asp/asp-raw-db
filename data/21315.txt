id:	21315
title:	Dabbobi Sun Samu Kafafu
author:	Muhammad Tukur Bala
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Hausa (Nigeria)
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21315.png
desc:	Maguru ya yanke shawarar ba dabbobi kafafuwa don suji dadin tafiya kamar mutane.

page_text:

P1	Zamanin da dabbobi basu da kafafu
P2	Maguru mutane kadai yaba kafafu
P3	Maguru ya yanke shawara yaba kowace dabba kafafu
P4	Dabbobin na bukatar Kafafu don tafiya da kuma gudu
P5	Jan jiki yana yi masu wahala
P6	Da ranar tazo dabbobi daban daban suka ja jiki zuwa gidan maguru
P7	Maguru yaba dabbobi da tsuntsaye kafafu
P8	Wasu dabbbobin sun yi rawa wasu sun fadi kasa
P9	Sai suka ce ba za mu kara jan jiki ba
P10	Kadandoniya ta zo daga karshe
P11	Maguru ya ba kadandonya dukan kafafun da suka rage
P12	Sai Kadandoniya cikin farin ciki tace sai nafi kowa sauri
P13	Bayan zuwan kadandoniya sai ga maciji yazo yana rogon maguru ya bashi kafaf
P14	Maciji ya cewa maguru nayi nauyin barci
P15	Maguru bai samo wa maciji kafufu ba
P16	Tun daga nan maciji yake zaman jiran kafafu
