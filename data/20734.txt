title:	Shisirani Namwe Ihabi?
author:	Namee Lillian

desc:	Abe ifula ihabi namwe namwe shisirani? Babandu lulala bambasa ifula ihabi namwe shisirani khubulamu bwomundu.

page_text:

P1	Chimiesi Chimikali nga mugulu  mwomele. Lomongin umureri wefula akanikha ni Wele wefulanga abandu bebusire ango wewemulubusaano nga balindire kamawulire kanabesamu kamaani
P2	Nga arura anze, wabolela babandu baari umubumbi atsa khubawa ifula kane banyale khubyala biryo
P3	Babaana abe balihwinyayiira anzze nga baliindire ifula. Angolobe  khulunakhu lwene olwo. Babona lufumbi luwanga nategera busa ndi uyu Namufeeri
P4	Babaandu abe bashambasa shesi umureri wefula alomere. Mayi wahoha ari, "Nimubone! Namuferi wamalile nimwingire mukari."
P5	Ifuula yatsutsukha ingali.
P6	Abe khwalindire ifula! Khwasanyukha luchiboole ne ifula yekaana khuwesa.
P7	Kametsi katembelela buliwando. Bulalo bubwama musahalo uhwolesa isi luguudo luba lwafuniikha.
P8	Tsinzu tsosi tsonkha lwekametsi kefula. Ihabi yahwichelela shisilani.
P9	Abe bulimundu werekhekere khubyala ari abe shibanyalata. Babandu mushalo shese abe bayisunga ifula ne ari shibachikombata. Ari shihuli ni bulalo bwekhuchenderakho ta. Babandu baandi shibali ni tsingo tsekhumenya ta.
P10	Ari hwafuna shizibu shindi! Hwaboona gonya nga walmiire buliwandu. Shino shakhubatsakawo tawe nga mbawo ulikhushitegera tawe.
P11	Abe shkhunyala khutsa khukula ishindu tawe khuba abe kamadukha kosi kamekale. Babaana abe shibanyala khutsa khusomero khuba balinikhukhalisa ifuubu niyo boole khusomero.
P12	Ihabere ihabi yechela shisirani khu fe. Lomongin, umureri wefula naye watsinikha. Kapuus ni pusi bwewe munana bonyene abe bali mulisaanyu.
