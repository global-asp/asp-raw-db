desc:	Abaghala basatu baaja okuwerera enku mukasana. Baasalagho baaja okwewuga aye ebiseera byabajaaku me baayangugha okwira ghaka nga babugwitana.

page_text:

P1	Era  einho, abaghala  baajanga okugherera  enku   olweigulo.
P2	Abaghala  basatu  baaja  okugherera  enku  aye  nga  akasana  kangi  inho  baasalagho  baje  beewugeku.
P3	Baaja  opkola  bati  nga  obwire  bujiire  olwo  baayangugha  okwirayo  eka.
P4	Baali  bali  kumpi  kutuuka  omughala  owundi  yaaja  okugema  munkoto  ati  nga  yeerabiireyo  ekinere  kye. Yaakoba  baine  bamwizeeyo.
P5	Abaghala  abandi  baadheema  okumwizaayo  era   yeerayo  yenka  aye  obwire  bwamuzibiraku  eyo  enjira  yaamubulaku.
P6	Bweyalinga  ati  emberi  nga ekitangaala  kiri  mu kayumba,  Yaajayo  yaakonkona.
P7	Ekyamweghuunhisa  n'okwagaana  wambwa  nga  aigwiregho  olwigi  era  yaamubuuza  ati, "olikunoonhia  ki?  Omughala   yaamwiramu  ati  enjira,   "n'embuzeeku"
P8	Wambwa  yaamukoba  ati  nfumbira  aye  omughala  yamukoba  ati nze  tifumbirwangaku  wambwa.
P9	Ekyo bwekyagha  wambwa  yamukoba  ati, "ndhalira  obuliri"
P10	Buli  lunaku  yali  ali  n'okwalira  wambwa  obuliri  n'okumufumbira.
P11	Nga  wambwa yankavaawo ati,  omughala  yafuna  enviiri  isatu,  olundi  yaaluta  ghansiiaghobuliri, olundi kulwigi  olundi   mukyayiro,  meni    yaalumuka  paka ghaka.
P12	Wambwa   bweyaira  yaayeta  ati, "mughala  iwe. mughala iwe. Mughala  wooo?
P13	Wambwa  yaakizuula  nti omughala  amusaliire  amagezi.  rea  yaataaku   embiro okuja   kukyalo  ekyomughala  aye  yayagaana  nga  bamulindhiriire  n'emiigo.
