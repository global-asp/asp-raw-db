page_text:

P1	Tingi yali abeera ne jaaja we.
P2	Yali atera okulabilila ente jaaja we.
P3	Lwali lumu, abasilikale baaja.
P4	Nebatwala ente.
P5	Tingi ne jaaja we baduka.
P6	Bekweeka munsiko mpaka obude bwebwaziba.
P7	Abasilikale ne bakomawo.
P8	Jaaja yakweka Tingi wansi we bikoola.
P9	Omusilikale yamulinyako naye yasigala asilise.
P10	Embeera bweyatelela, Tingi ne jaja we ne bavayo.
P11	Nebasooba okuda ewaka.
