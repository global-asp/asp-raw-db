title:	Omusaadha Omuleeyi  Einho
author:	Cornelius Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/919.png
desc:	Olugero lwogera ku kutawaana kwo omusaadha eyali omuleeyi einho. Buli kintu ekyali kimwetooloire kyali kimpi inho. Kyamukakataku okwekolera ebintu ebituukaana no obughanvu bwe.

page_text:

P1	Embago ye yali nhimpi inho.
P2	Omulyango gwe gwaali mumpi inho.
P3	Ekitanda kye kyaali kimpi inho.
P4	Eigaali lye lyaali limpi inho.
P5	Omusaadha ono yali muleeyi inho!
P6	Yaabaadha omuyini omughanvu einho.
P7	Yaaghanga emyango emileeyi einho.
P8	Yaasita ekitanda ekighanvu einho.
P9	Yaagula eigaali eighanvu einho.
P10	Yaatyama ku ntebe empanvu einho. Yaaliisa eiwuuma eighanvu einho.
P11	Yaava mu nhumba ye enhimpi. Yaazimba mu kibira ekye miti emileeyi einho. Yaghangaala emyaka mingi inho.
