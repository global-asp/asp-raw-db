desc:	Amwana eyaja mulugendho ezanzibar okulambuula ebintu byobutonde.

page_text:

P1	Akata mu Zanzibar, kiki kyobona? amaduuka agatunda switi, mu bukuubo.
P2	Okwewuga mu buzanziba, kiki kyo bona? ebyangu, obutaasa, ne bindhi
P3	Akwevuga mu buzanziba, kiki kyo obona? ebisoro mu nsiko, nga obilengerera ghala.
P4	Mu makina ebu zanzibar, kiki kyo bona? oluswahiri,olungereza,oluwalabu,bona bona nga bakina tarabusi
P5	Okuja okunhwa mu e buzanzibar, kiki kyobana? miwa, madhafu,obutundha, kawa,ni amadhani ga buwowo.
P6	Olugendo  Ebuzanziba, oyinza okudhuuba okwira yo.
