page_text:

P1	One day, the animals in Kontagora were hungry.
P2	They searched for food but found no food.
P3	Mr Tortoise traveled to a far land to search for food. Then he saw Mr Monkey eating some bananas.
P4	Mr tortoise asked, "Can I have a banana?"
P5	Mr Monkey replied, "No I can't give you my banana!
P6	"Take this mango, I have no banana," said Mr Monkey.
P7	"I love bananas but I will take the mango," said Mr Tortoise.
P8	Mr tortoise ate the mango and said, "Yum, yum, yum!"
