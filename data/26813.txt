P1	Nviira  mukyalo e Uganda ekyetebwa  Kighangala.  Kyalo kirungi  inho.
P2	Abaana  mu kyalo benda  okuja  ku  isomero. Twenda  abasomesa  baife.
P3	Kwisomero   twenda okusoma. Twenda okubala namba. 1, 2, 3,  4, 5,!
P4	Twenda  okuzaanha ni  mikwano  gyaife.
P5	Twenda  okulya  amatooke  mu  kyalo kyaife.
P6	twenda  okubinga  e  mbiidhi.
P7	Twenda  okuzaanha  ni  mikwano  gyaife.
