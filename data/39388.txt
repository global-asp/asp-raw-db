page_text:

P1	Peolwane o itlhomile mo pitseng ya boithomelo go ithoma.
P2	Peolwane o tsaya seiphimolo go iphimola morago ga go ithoma
P3	Morago ga go iphimola o latlhela pampitshana mo lehuting la boitiketso, a bo a kgotha tshitswana ya kgopo metsi
P4	Fa a fetsa o iphepafatsa diatla ka metsi le several.
