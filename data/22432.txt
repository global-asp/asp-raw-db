desc:	Ibali elinomdla lamakhwenkwe athinjelwa imali yentlawulo.

page_text:

P1	Mna nomhlobo wam uKiki sihamba kunye ukuphuma kwesikolo.
P2	￼￼Ngenye imini, saqaphela indoda ende isilandela.
P3	Yayigcwele amaqhakuva, inenxeba elinzulu ubusweni.
P4	Sema sajongana nalendoda. Yakhwaza yathi, "Kwedini, undijonga ntoni?"
P5	￼￼Sajika sabaleka. Yandibamba le ndoda. UKiki waqhwesha.
P6	￼￼Yandijula ngemva kwiveni. Yahamba imoto.
P7	Ndavalelwa kwigumbi elimnyama. Izandla zam zazibotshiwe.
P8	"Naku ukutya, uzakuthabatha uhambo olude," kwatsho indoda.
P9	￼￼Njengoko ndisitya, le ndoda yayihleli phantsi itshaya.
P10	￼￼Kwangena amanye amadoda amabini erhuqa uKiki.
P11	￼Seva la madoda exambulisana ngaphandle kweli gumbi sikulo.
P12	Wathi uKiki, "Masiqhweshe. Abazali bethu abanayo imali yentlawulo."
P13	￼Satsala amaplanga efestileni. Yavuleka ifestile.
P14	￼￼UKiki waphuma ngaloo festile. Wabaleka ukuyokufuna uncedo.
P15	￼La madoda ambonzeleka angena. Aphinda awabethelela amaplanga efestileni.
P16	￼￼Aphuma amadoda. Ndaweva exambulisana kwakhona.
P17	Kwavakala ilizwi lisithi, "Ungoyiki. Amapolisa akhona."
P18	￼￼Kwabakho ingxolo enkulu, kukho nezithonga zemipu.
P19	￼￼Ipolisakazi lathi, "Ukhuselekile ngoku."
P20	￼￼Ukususela loo mini, mna noKiki saba nononophelo xa sihamba.
