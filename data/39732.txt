page_text:

P1	One day, Charisma was lying on the grass with her best friend Lizzy.
P2	They soon started looking at the colourful pictures of the rainbow.
P3	The people of the village loved the colourful rainbow a lot.
P4	Together they formed a rainbow.
P5	Before they left, they watched the rainbow in the sky.
P6	Charisma showed her young sister, the rainbow.
P7	Charisma is a smart and clever girl.
