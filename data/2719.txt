desc:	This is a counting story. Work out how many cupcakes Ben and Thabo left for Granny.

page_text:

P1	Abiria pi Leku be anzi iri abiri ni fule ra `diyi.
P2	Dede ni otilipatia mudri a`diria. Eri yi o`ba saani dria.
P3	Abiria `du otilipatia alu. Leku `du otilipatia iri.
P4	Abiria `du vini otilipatia azini yi su. Leku `du ivile ri si otilipatia iri.
P5	Abiria nya otilopatia dria si ya? Leku nya otilipatia dria si ya? Otilipatia ku yi dede ni si ya?
