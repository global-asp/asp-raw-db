P1	The Villagers and their animals are on the mountains.
P2	On their way home from the mountains they saw a tiger.
P3	The tiger also saw them, the tiger hidden himself from the Villagers
P4	The were running around because they think the tiger will eat them.
P5	Now what happened is that they want and meet the tiger and his people preparing for war
P6	They are tiny bit of the tiger. The the mountain king help them out of the danger
P7	When they get home, they call meeting to inform the Village what happened to them in the mountains
P8	The strong man in the village pick up his weapons off to the mountains. On his way going a zebra was tied up on the way
P9	So he decide to untie the zebra and the zebra start to fight him
P10	Tiger and his people become annoyed with the people in the village
P11	Tiger begin to train his people
P12	What happened then is lift to the strong man in the village
