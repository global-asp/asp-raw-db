page_text:

P1	Once apon a time a girl named Maya and her mom saw a rainbow they planed on drawing one.
P2	The looked in the sky to see what they can draw.
P3	They drew and drew until they finished.
P4	They were finished  and went to the park were they saw children looking at the rainbow.
P5	They also saw grown-ups looking at clouds but the clouds were colored,  some were blue,red,orange,yellow,green,pink.
P6	We loved the rainbow.The end.
