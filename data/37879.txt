P1	Once upon a time, there lived a cow with one horn. One day,the cow was moving around looking for grass and water in the nearby swamp.
P2	While he was walking he saw chameleon changing his colour but he was so slow. The cow wanted the chameleon to be his friend. But the chameleon refused.
P3	The cow was very sad. The cow wonder why would chameleon refused to be his friend. He did not know what to do.
P4	A dog was walking around looking for bones. He heard the conversation between the chameleon and the cow with one horn. The dog ask the chameleon,"why do you refuse to be cow's friend? Oooh! You have refused him because of his one horn?"
P5	The chameleon said yes. The dog turned to the Cow and said " don't worry,I will be your best friend, I will not refuse to be our friend because you have one horn. I will take care of you and be our faithfully friend. "
P6	The cow said "Thank you for your friendship.
