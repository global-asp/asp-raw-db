page_text:

P1	Billy ono. N' embuzi Jan gye yaghebwa  bbaaba ghenga ekirabo.Ghaire nga Billy wa kyedhu, jan amwenda inho.Azaala obwaana obulamu Jan ni Bbaaba ghe bw'ebatunda.
P2	Eido billy yajire kuluya no bwaana bwe  bubiri. kino Jan ta kyenda  kubanga obwaana buibibwa oba bw'alibwa embwa.
P3	Billy yagaaya ebitabo ebirimu ebyo kw'egezamu waka Ann bye yali akoze. Ann yali mu n'akughavu inho ati yali agya ku kangaivulwa omusomesa ghe.Ya samba Billy inho. kino kya nikaaza Jan.Ye galiza mu kisenge kye  azaane ni bidole bye.
P4	Jan  atya ati bbaaba ghe aidha kutunda Billy ku lye kyo kya koze.Ghabula Mwami Tom, bbaaba ghe  ta y'enda kunhiiza mutabane nga atunda Billy. Asuubiza okulinda ku okutuusa nga obwaana  bukuze asobole oku ghaku Jan kalala.
P5	Tubenga  Jan ni Ann. Tu labirirenga bukalamu ebisolo byaife. Ebisolo bya mugaso kubanga bitugha era bya tukuuma okuva eri abntu ababi.
