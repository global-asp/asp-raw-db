title:	Naange Suklii-nge
author:	Mohammed Also and Naseh  Yusuf
artist:	Joe  Werna
lang:	Fulfulde
level:	First sentences

desc:	Ngeccawol ngool ɗum dow naange ba no pukaraajo janngirde yiiri-nge gila nge fuɗa haa nge muta.

page_text:

P1	Naange e fuɗa fajiri, wakkere fuuna.@@Emi nana njakardi e kukkita bimbi.
P2	Naange ngen e tokka dow saare Terfa'en hiddee moylaari.@@Mi nanii uurngol njaram e nyaamdu tokkii naange nden faa yottii hinorde am.
P3	Naange ngen e tokka ɓaawo leggal to janngirde amin wakkati kasitaari.@@Nde nge waɗa taarde caka nokkuure fijirde.
P4	Naange ngen e hedda dow hoore am. Mbeelu am e daroo seraa am.@@E soobiraaɓe am min pija fijo mbeelu.
P5	Mbeelu ngun ɓeyda mawnugo nden ngu famɗita. Min ndogga min tokkita ngu.@@Mbeelu am mawna juuta, nden ngu rammiɗa. Min ndogga min tokkita ngu.
P6	Mi daroo, sooɓiraaɓe am maa ndaroo. Min ngi'a beeli amin e njownjowtiri.@@To min comii min koota hijibiiru. Ɓaawo janngirde, min koota saare.
P7	Naange ngaaɓoo.@@Mi yi'a naange ngen e wirnoo seese-seese wakkere hiirna. Mi yi'a mbeelu am dow mahol.@@Ɗum wakkati ɗaanaago.
P8	Naange ngen majjita gaɗa duule.@@Mi fukkoo dow leeso, nden mi hoyɗa naange e waɗa jahaangal daayngal.
