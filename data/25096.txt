desc:	Buti oidhi lwaki embuzi dhikola inho obutumbaavu! Now you know why goats are so stubborn!

page_text:

P1	Wambuzi n'eyali kyabazinga ow'ensolo n'enhonhi. 

Yaalalika olwekobaano.
P2	Wambuzi yaabakobera ati yaloota ekirooto.

Boonaboona baamuwulirisa.
P3	Ekirooto kyali ku ndhala mugudya.
Kapa yaabuuza ati: "Tukole ki?"
P4	Wankoko ni Kiyoyo baakoba bati: "Tubiike emere mu kyagi kya kyabazinga.
P5	Agho Kyabazinga Wambuzi yaalagira ati: "Musibe oyo yeenayeena adheema.
P6	Byali bisera bya kulonda kyabazinga omuyaaka.

Baalonda Kapa
P7	Wambuzi tiyayenda Kapa abe Kyabazinga. Nga akoba ati: "Ninze kyabazinga."
P8	Wambuzi mwene tiyatwala mmereye

Wante yaabuuza ati: "Tukole ki?"
P9	Ensolo n'enhonhi dhaakyogeraku.

Dhaansugughalira Wambuzi.
P10	Wambwa yaakoba ati: "Buliidho nabanga naye nga akaali kyabazinga."
P11	Wantaama yaakoba ati: "Nagha abaanabe obuviiri bwange obuboneka obulungi.
P12	Wambiidhi yaayana ati: "Ghe yaberanga kyabazinga nalabiriranga ennimiroye."
P13	Wantaama yabuuza ati: "Wambuzi yadhuuba kuba kyabazinga byanda na byanda?"
P14	Ensolo dhaisania dhiti Wambuzi ali n'okutwala emere ye Kyabazinga Kapa
P15	Ensolo dhaja ye Wambuzi. Yaakoba ati, ndidha kubanga kyabazinga buliidho.
P16	Tuja kukusiba tukutwale ye kyabazinga omuyaaka
P17	Wante yasika Wambuzi okutusa mu kooti ya kyabazinga. Abandi baamusekerera.
P18	Embuzi kyedhiva nidhiroba okutambula nga dhiri kusikibwa
