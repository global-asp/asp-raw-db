desc:	Ipoto kaNatabo ayithanda khulu iphukile

page_text:

P1	Lo nguNatabo. @@Unabafowabo abasithandathu.
P2	Ababelethi bakhe bamupha ipoto encani. @@Wabe ayithanda khulu.
P3	Ngelinye ilanga abafowabo baphula ipoto leyo.
P4	UNatabo akayitholi ipoto yakhe. @@Walila, walila, walila.
P5	UNatabo uyabaleka. @@Ukhwelela umuthi omude.
P6	Ababelethi bakhe bayamthola. @@"Sibawa wehlele phasi," batjho bambawa.
P7	Umfowabo wavuma ingoma, ethi, "Ngibawa wehlele phasi."
P8	UNatabo wamane wayihleka ingoma yabo.
P9	Umngani kaNatabo naye weza, wafika wavuma ingoma ethi, "Ngibawa wehlele phasi."
P10	Umngani kaNatabo wavuma bekwaba kulapha ehla khona emthini.
P11	Abangani bakhamba bobabili baya ekhaya.
P12	Woke umuntu wathabela bona uNatabo usekhaya. @@UNatabo wathola ipoto etja!
