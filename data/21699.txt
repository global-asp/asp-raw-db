title:	Luka Bineensoota
author:	Mulualem  Daba
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Afaan Oromo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21699.png
desc:	Waaqayoo bineensoota hundaaf akka nama akka deemanif luka keenefi.

page_text:

P1	Bara durii, bineensooni luka hinqaban ture.
P2	Waaqayo namootaf qofaa luka keenna.
P3	Waaqayo bineensoota hundaaf luka keennuuf murtese.
P4	Bineensooni deemufi fiiguf luka isaan barbaachisa.
P5	Lafaa irraa lo'uuf itti ulfaata.
P6	Bineensootni yeroo adda adda gara mana waaqayoo foqoqani deemu.
P7	Waaqayoos bineensotafi sinbirootaf luka kenne.
P8	Bineensooni gar tokko yommuu shubisaan, kan haafan immoo in kufan.
P9	"Lammaffa hinfoqooqnu!" jedhani.
P10	Dhuma irraati kan haafe mil-kumee ture.
P11	Waaqayoos lukaa hafee hundumaa kennef.
P12	Mil-kumeenis, "Hunda isaani caala saffisan nan deema," jedhe gammachuun.
P13	Mil-kumee booda bofni gara mana waaqayoo deeme, "Maalo lukaa naafkenni," jedhe kadhaate.
P14	Bofti, "Ani deemu hindanda'uu," jedhe.
P15	Waaqayoos luka keenufif jedhe barbaade dhabe.
P16	Bara sana kaase hangaa ammatti boofti lukti nafkeenama jedhe eega jira.
