desc:	Henry was a lost dog who had no home. Henna took Henry in as her pet he became her best friend.

page_text:

P1	It was a beautiful day. Henna and her cousin Jack were playing in the field
P2	As they were play they saw a black dog.Henry was his name!
P3	It was time to go but Henna didn't want to leave Henry. So Jack went home.
P4	Henna stayed  outside and played with Henry,"you are now my pet" she said.
P5	It became dark but they kept on playing outside in the field.
