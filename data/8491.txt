desc:	A description of the life of a hardworking Nile fisherman.

page_text:

P1	This is Jaaka the fisherman from Naminya.
P2	He fishes on the river Nile.
P3	He has a boat,  fishing net and a hook.
P4	He always wakes up very early in the morning to catch the Nile perch.
P5	At around 10am,  he's selling his catch in Jinja central market.
P6	Jaaka what happened? Was it a loss? He's so sad.
P7	"Today the situation is bad. The Nile breeze did not permit me to fish. And one perch swam away with my hook."
P8	"But I have hope that tomorrow will be good,  because I've bought a new hook."
