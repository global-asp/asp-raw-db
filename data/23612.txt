title:	Nalambaire ku Buliri

desc:	Muganda wange alya inho. Tayenda kuzaanha. Munda mutera okumuluma.

page_text:

P1	Ninze Namusobya. Nnhenda okukina no okuzaanha.
P2	Muganda wange ni Nsoobani. Tayenda kuzaanha.
P3	Bbaaba ni Maama batutwala mu isomero. Tweega okubala no okusiiga.
P4	Nze ni banange tusoma ebitabo.
P5	"Abaana batyaime ku ntebe. Bali kunhwa buugi."
P6	"Abaana batenduka mu kitanda kirala. Bbaaba ni Maama bali kughaya."
P7	Wuudha akoba, "Muganda wange ansobeire. Amaze okunhampa yanhaala ku buliri."
P8	"Olumaze, yebwika omutwe. Yaseka ate yeyongera okunhampa."
P9	Akayumba kaife ni kano. Tusulamu abantu bana.
