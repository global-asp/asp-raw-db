desc:	Roger and Rosine wake up in the morning and brush their teeth as their mother trained them.

page_text:

P1	Dawn in the morning
P2	Roger and Rosine hear their mother outside, coughing.
P3	They immediately wake up.
P4	Roger approaches his sister
P5	He reminds her what they mother
P6	Tells them every morning.
P7	She tells them to take a cup,
P8	fill it with water,
P9	Toothpaste,
P10	and then brush their teeth.
P11	Rosine takes a cup
P12	Toothbrush with toothpaste.
P13	She sees nothing in the bucket.
P14	Rosine advises her brother, Roger to go to fetch water at the river.
P15	He takes a ten-little jerry cane and leaves.
Rosine stays home while sweeping the compound.
