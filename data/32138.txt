desc:	ǂōǂōsiba xu ra huiǂuihe ǀgururon ra mûǂams ǃnaga sâuhe (kōǃgâhe) ǃkhaib xa ra ǃgû ǂhôas ge nēsa ǀôaisa khoen xa ra mûǂamhese.

page_text:

P1	Mûǂamaob tsî ǁîb mûǃgâs ǁgûo ǀgururon ǂama.
P2	Aoǁkhumu ǁkhâb ǃnân ge kōǃgâ-aob tsî hui-aon ona ǂoaǂamsa sîsenxase ra hâ.
P3	ǀGui kuris ge kaira taras Mkitesa ǂguro !nās ase ge sari.
P4	Nē hâǃkhaib di mûǂamaon ge Mkitesa kaise ge ǀkhom, ǂgawasa tsîs ge ǀnorosa i amaga.
P5	Xawes ge ǁîsa !nawetamase ge ǀgai. Tsîs ge ǀnaiǀgui nau ǀgururon ǀkha gere ǀguru.
P6	ǀGuitsēs ge ǂoakuirosa Aoǁkhumub ǁkhâb !nâ nē ǀgururon hâǃkhaib ai ge hā ǂnû.
P7	Danab tsî hui-aon hoan ge !khoeǂoa. Nē ǂoakuniros ǃnâb ge ǂkhoaroba hanagam ǂnams !nâ anaǁguihe hâ.
P8	ǁȊb ge Ndiwa, ti ge ǀōn hâ i.  ǁȊb ge ge koro tsēde ge !nae hâ i.  Mûǂamaob ge ǁnāxa ǁîroba ǂkhorob ǃnâ gere ǃhai.
P9	!Nonaǁîs ge ǀnabasa hîa ge nē hâǃkhaib !oa ǀkhī-ūhes ge Maleas ti ge ǀōn hâ i.  ǁȊs ge ǀawa !hūǃgao-ūtas !nâ ge !naohe hâ i.
P10	Nē ǃ!nabaros, Maleas ge ǃhaese ǂkham !garogomab, hîa Enkare ti ǀōnhâb ǀkha ge ǀhōsagu.
P11	ǀGui !oe i ge mâ khoe-i hoa-e Ambias di hās xa ge ǂkhaiǂkhai e.
P12	Ambias ge ge koro ǁkhâxa i.  ǁȊros ge ge ǂkhawusa i ne ǁnā kaixu daob di !gûs xa.
P13	ǁNā kaixu ǃaob ǁîs dib ǃaromab ge mûǂamaoba !hai ǂkhororoba ǀgapise gere ūkhâi.
P14	ǀGēra, Abei tsî Moiti hâra ge ǁaeguros Aoǁkhumub dis !nâ ge hā.  Nē ǃkhaupirirora ge ǁaupexa ǀnîkhamako irgu ǀguiga ge !nae hâ i.  Kaise ge ǀaekusa i.
P15	Wekheba !kharu tsîs ge Abeisa ǁō, on ge hoaraga khoena ge !oa hâ i.  Xaweb ge mûǂamaoba, hui-aon di mâxoǀkhas ǀkha Moitsa ge ûi kai.
P16	ǀUniga ge Aoǁkhumub !nâ hā-ūhen ge Kopis, Kepip tsî Kejib hâna.  Nē ǂkhari ǀgururon ge ǀgam wekhexas ǀguisa ge i i.  Kaisen ge ge !â hâ i.
P17	Mûǂamaob tsî hui-aon on ge hoa xamarirona ge ǀnam i.  ǁNān hîa kaise ge ǁorexa in tsîna.
P18	Hoana ge ǁgoeǂam hâ ib ge Loboliaba.  ǁȊb ge mûǂamaob di ǂkhorona ge sausau.
P19	Sîsenaon nē !khaib din ge ǂorisase nē ǂkham xamarirona gere kōǃgâ.  ǁȊn ra !âubasens ge nē xamariron nî ǀguia sī ǁî-aitsama mâbasensa.  !Norasa ûib !oan ni ǀguitsē oasa.
