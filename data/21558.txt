page_text:

P1	NgoSondo, uJimmy Zogba ufake igondelo lokuthumba.
P2	NgoMvulo i-Cool Cat Crew yakhambakhamba ngokuzithemba nokuqhoma okukhulu.
P3	"Hayi, Rafiki siphi isitayela sakho seenhluthu zeduba?

Iinhluthu zakho azikalungiswa kuhle, sengathi awuzithandi."
P4	"Hayi, Rafiki iphi isinga thathu yakho? 

Isikhumba sakho asithabisi nakancani."
P5	"Hayi, Rafiki buphi ubukhazikhazi bakho bemlonyeni?  

Amazinyo wakho akakahlali kuhle."
P6	Ngalelolanga uRafiki wakhamba kabuthaka asiya ekhaya.
P7	"Kghari, ngingaba nazo iinhluthu zeduba?"

"Angeze ngikghone ukukukerela iinhluthu zeduba. Eqinisweni angeze ngikghone."
P8	"Isikhumba sami asithabisi Dade. Ungakghona ukungenzela isinga thathu?"

"Awudingi bona kube khona omunye umuntu ofana nawe."
P9	"Babamkhulu, ngifisa sengathi ngingaba nento ebukhazikhazi."

"Ubukhazikhazi mntwanami abutjho litho."
P10	Ipelaveke elandelako uSanti Ramires wabetha igondelo lokuthumba.
P11	NgoMvulo i-Cool Cat Crew yakhambakhamba ngokuzithemba nokuqhoma okukhulu.

"Hayi, uRafiki! Ubonakala amuhle!"
P12	URafiki wasikinya amahlombe. "Ngifana nami, isitayela lesi ngesami."
