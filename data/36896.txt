desc:	My message is to all parents and youngsters driving dont answer your cellphone while driving and more importantly dont drink and drive.

page_text:

P1	There once was a man named Trevor, Trevor had a wife and her name was Anne.
P2	Trevor was out drinking with friends and having fun, his wife was at home with the kids.
P3	Anne was worried about Trevor, it was getting late and Trevor still wasn't home. Anne decides to call Trevor to find out where he is and when he'll be home.
P4	Trevor had just left from drinking with friends and is driving home, when he receives a call from his wife Anne, which he answers.
P5	Speeding home in his red bakkie,  Trevor skipped a red robot and drove straight into Mrs Wilson's.  Mrs Wilson drives a small green SUV.
P6	Her SUV flung off the road, rolled twice until it came to rest. Trevor and Mrs Wilson were both seriously injured.
P7	Cars stopped alongside the road to see if they could help the people who had just been involved in a car accident. The ambulance arrived at the scene.
P8	Both Trevor and Mrs Wilson was rushed to the emergency room. Doctors worked for hours in surgery on Trevor and Mrs Wilson.
P9	The family members of both Trevor and Mrs Wilson are pacing up and down the corridors of the hospital.
P10	Unfortunately Mrs Wilson dies. Trevor is left paralyzed.@The end.
