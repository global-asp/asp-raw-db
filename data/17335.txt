title:	Ibhasi Elikhulu Eliluhlaza
author:	Pumla Mdontswa and Zanele Zuma
artist:	Mango Tree
lang:	isiZulu

desc:	UZweli nomama wakhe balindele ibhasi enkulu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ezobayisa edolobheni. Kodwa ibhasi eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ifile. Bazofika kanjani edolobheni?

page_text:

P1	Lalilodwa ibhasi elihamba emphakathini wakubo kaZweli. Lalilikhulu futhi liluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Lalinomsindo omkhulu.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga umama kaZweli wathi, "Kusasa sizoya edolobheni siyothenga umfaniswano wakho."
P3	UZweli wajabula kakhulu. Babezohamba ngebhasi elikhulu eliluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Kwakungavumi ukuba alale ebusuku.
P4	UZweli wayesegqokile lapho umama wakhe engena ezomvusa.
P5	UZweli nomama wakhe baya esitobhini. Balindela ibhasi elikhulu eliluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Kodwa do ibhasi!
P6	Abanye abantu bafika esitobhini. Babekhononda ngoba ibhasi lalingasigcinanga isikhathi. "Ngabe ibhasi lisishiyile?" bebuzana bodwa.
P7	UZweli wakhathazeka. "Ngeke sikwazi ukuya edolobheni. Ngeke ngikwazi ukuthola umfaniswano wami," ecabanga.
P8	Abanye abantu baduba babuyela emakhaya. Kodwa uZweli wayekhala engafuni ukuya ekhaya. Umama wakhe wayemduduza ethi, "Sizolinda isikhashana."
P9	Masishane, bezwa umsindo. Babona izintuli emoyeni. Laliza ibhasi!
P10	Kodwa leli bhasi lalingeluhlaza. Leli bhasi lalibomvu futhi lilincane. Abantu ababelindile bavela balibuka leli bhasi. Abazange bagibele.
P11	"Ngenani! Ngenani! Sishiywe isikhathi," kumemeza umshayeli.
P12	UZweli nomama wakhe bangena kuqala. Ngokushesha bonke abantu bangena ebhasini.
P13	Lapho uZweli elunguza ngefasitela, wabona abantu abaningi esitobhini.
P14	Abanye babegijima bejahe ukugibela ibhasi. Kodwa ibhasi lase ligcwele. Naliya ibhasi elibomvu liya edolobheni.
P15	"Liphi ibhasi elikhulu eliluhlaza?" kubuza umama ka Zweli. "Lifile," kuphendula umshayeli. "Siyalilungisa,  lizoba khona kusasa," kusho umshayeli.
P16	UZweli wayengenandaba nombala webhasi. Wayengenandaba nobukhulu bebhasi. Wayejabuliswa ukuthi laliya edolobheni.
