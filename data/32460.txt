page_text:

P1	My friend gave me beans from her garden.
P2	I plant a bean in the soil.
P3	The bean germinates in the soil.
P4	The seedling grows up to the sun.@@The root grows down.
P5	The seedling sprouts above the soil.
P6	The seedling grows,
P7	and grows.
P8	I plant more beans.
P9	The plants grow,
P10	and grow.
P11	The plants produce bean pods.
P12	The plants grow up, up, over my wall!
P13	I pick the bean pods when they are dry.
P14	I collect the beans from the pods.
P15	I cook the beans with red onion and herbs.
P16	These beans are ready to eat.
