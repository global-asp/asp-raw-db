P4	At school,  she draw and paint. Here are some of her drawing. She said, its a story about a rabbit and a chicken. They were once  best friends. They loved each other very much. The teacher use to say it's important to have a friend.

P6	Visitors. His name is Mr Dogg. He was protecting them from criminals. Mr Dogg is very brave. They lived happily ever after.
