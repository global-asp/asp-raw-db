page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there were two kids named Rebecca And Andrew.
P2	As the school bus came the kids were extremely excited.
P3	So they had breakfast.
P4	Rebecca was mostly excited.
P5	So they went to school.
P6	Then  Andrew went to get more school uniform.
P7	Andrew picked his uniform.
P8	On the way home they crashed.
P9	The kids were OK but the dad went to the hospital.
P10	So the next day Andrew woke up thinking about his dad.
P11	Then they went to school.
P12	And  they went back home.
P13	And they lived happily ever after. The End.
