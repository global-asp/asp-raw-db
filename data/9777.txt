title:	Azizi Dolli Yakwa
author:	Kariha Nancy Wamaitha
artist:	Natalie Propa
lang:	Gikuyu

desc:	Nyathi mawinjo maber ka orito doli mare.   To kendo doli ne no osiko ka omor.

page_text:

P1	Ŭyŭ nȋ Azizi. Azizi nȋ dolli yakwa.
P2	Nȋnyedete Azizi tondŭ aikaraga akenete hindi ciothe.
P3	Ndokira kiroko ni ndi thambaga uthiu. Gacoka gamwetha Azizi.
P4	Ndamŭona Azizi nȋ njanŭraga juere yake.
P5	Ngȋria kȋroko nȋ ndi mŭheyaga Azizi.
P6	Ndaria thuȋtȋ nȋ ndi muheyaga Azizi.
P7	Nŭŭ Azizi ndariyaga thuiti yake. Nagamaka nȋkȋ!
P8	Nȋ thiyaga nja gituwaga mahŭa.
P9	Ni heyaga Azizi ihua. Nŭŭ ndacokagia,  "Ngatho."
P10	Nȋ ndimuthambagia agathera mbere ya mami athambie.
P11	Azizi ndȋmŭȋkagira ngŭo ndune tondŭ ninyendete rangi mutune.
P12	Ndathȋe toro,  Azizi ndȋmŭkomagia mwenaine haria ndȋ.
