title:	My Mother's Grocery
author:	Aminga Tata

desc:	A story of a grocery that belongs to Julians mother and how helpful it has been to her family.

page_text:

P1	I am Juliana. My mother's name is Maria. She owns this grocery. Mr Popo operates this grocery for my mother.
P2	I usually escort my mother to the grocery when she visits there. My mothers grocery is found at Kioge market.
P3	My mother mainly sells  oranges at the grocery. She some times adds mangoes, bananas and vegetables in her stock.
P4	This is my mother's farm. She picks some vegetables that she sells at Kioge market. She usually takes me to the farm to help her pick and record the quantity of the vegetables.
P5	My mother is training me on how to pick the produce.I enjoy the company of my mother at the farm.
P6	At last my mother manages to expand her grocery to an extremely large business. She has employed some three more people to work  her  behalf.
P7	This is the larger Kioge market. It is divided into sections depending on what it sells. My mother is searching for a section that sells domestic animals like goats, sheep and cows.
P8	This is the goat that my mother bought. It is a good one. My brother never liked goats but he has tried to like  them.
P9	The kid soon grew into a goat. We named the goat Aki. Aki the goat sired two other kids. We like feeding them together with my brother.
P10	My mother sold the two kids that Aki the goat sired and left her alone. She used the money to buy us school uniforms.
