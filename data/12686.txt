page_text:

P1	Sherry has got very little hair. But she looks smart.
P2	Nandi has plated hair. She looks smart too.
P3	Cathy has the longest hair.
P4	Tom covers his hair whenever it is raining.
P5	Uncle Jimmy has beards.
P6	Nebanda normally combs her hair nicely before she lives for any function.
P7	Tabo has cut his hair.
P8	Mubbalya has shaved his hair off.
