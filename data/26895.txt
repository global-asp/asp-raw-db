desc:	Buli ebinerengeta ti zaabbu. (Olufumo luno lwafumwa amasomesa mu kubangulibwa okwali mu Akula

page_text:

P1	Eira  einho ku kyalo  Kalaamu, yaliyo Kalaamu nnhingi.
P2	Waaligho  zaabbu, Biiki, Boolopoyinti, Geelo, Maaka, ni ebindi.
P3	Zaabbu yali  Kalaamu nnungi.

Kalaamu dhoonadhoona mu kyalo  Kalaamu deegombanga  okuba nga yeene.
P4	Bbiiki yakoba eti, "ndida kuba Kalaamu  esinga kuba  nsanhufu  bwenaaba oti zaabbu."
P5	Aye  zaabbu yabanga  munnaku  buli kaseera. Yasalagho  okuba  yenka  okuva  ku  mikwano  gye.
P6	Ghazira  muntu  kukyalo  zaabbu yali  munakughavu  buli kiseera  ate nga  mweinamivu. Balowooza nti  ali  nensonga  okuba omusanhufu.
P7	Aye,  Zaabbu yali  nekyama ekinene  kyeyali eidhi.

Zaabbu  yali nakyama ki?
P8	Waire Zaabbu  enherengeta era nga  eyendhebwa, yali eziramu  bwino!

Zaabbu  yalira bwekoba eti,  "Kalaamu ya Zaabbu eziramu bwino erimu muganulo ki?"
