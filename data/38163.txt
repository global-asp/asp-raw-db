P1	Little Billy woke up and jumped out of bed. "Woo-hoo I'm happy!". He went to his brother's room and saw that his brother Alex had his legs hanging off the bed. Oh No!
P2	Little Billy woke up his brother Alex and said, " Your legs!".
P3	His brother jumped out of bed and wanted to go for a bike ride. But the bike was too small.
P4	Little Billy found a bike that Alex could fit.
P5	While the boys were out, Little Billy had an idea. He said that they should get a longer bed for him too.
P6	The boys got the bed and headed back home. Night came, and Alex was comfy in his new bed.@@@@Goodnight!@The End
