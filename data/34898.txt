page_text:

P1	Ada tayi dariya da jin tatsuniya mai ban dariya.@  @@  Ta na murmushi.@  @@  Mai ada take ji?
P2	Gisa na fushi.@  @@  Ya turbune fuskan@  sa.@  @@  Mai ya bashi haushi?
P3	Yuadoo na tsoron duhu.@  @@  Kowa na jin tsoro wani lokaci.@  @@  Amma, Yuadoo na iya neman taimako.
P4	Chidubem ya damu da aikin makarantar sa.@  @@  Zai iya gayawa malamin sa.@  @@  Zai iya neman taimako.
P5	Lushan na wasa!@  @@  Ba laifi bane ka zama wawa@  wani lokaci.
P6	Eruro na bakin ciki.@  @@  Yanayi kan zo ya tafi
P7	Ayator na cike da farin ciki.@  @@  yana cikin@  murna.@  @@  Yanayi kan zo ya tafi.
P8	Hadiza na kewa.@  @@  Ya kamata ta nemi@  abun yi.
P9	Ayo na fushi.@  @@  Yana cikin takaici.@  @@  Fushi abu ne mara@  daxi.
P10	Labake na farin ciki.@  @@  Mai dalilin farin cikin ta?@  @@  Mama ta yi abincin da tafi sha’awa.
P11	Zege yaji barci.@  @@  Ya gaji sosai.@  @@  “Amma dakata, barci yanayi ne?” Efe ya tambaya.
P12	Efe ya rikice.@  @@  Ka na iya ba shi amsa?
P13	Tega yana jin mamaki da kaxuwa.@  @@  Ya ji wata gulma.@  @@  Gaskiya ne?
P14	Yebo ba shi da lafiya.@  @@  Tana jin takaici.@  @@  Tana bukatan magani@  da hutawa domin samun sauki.
P15	Sekyen na gamsuwa.@  @@  Ta gama aikace-aikacen gida.@  @@  Yanzu ta na iya wasa.
P16	Vandefan bai da aikace-aikacen@  gida yau.@  @@  Ya nuna wa Sekyen manyan yatsun sa biyu.@  @@  “Yawwa, yanzu muyi wasa!@  ya ce.
