page_text:

P1	Sanyu azainhe olunaku luno lwona lwona.
P2	Maama ghe  yamukobye okwoza engoye.
P3	Yatoola akasenia ke.
P4	Maama ghe yavudhumuliira amaadhi mu baafu.
P5	Yakaza ekyenhi kye.
P6	Yaja ye muganda ghe baalambala.
P7	Kunco yasenhiiza amainho ge.
P8	Maama ghe yamuyambaku okuvaala.
P9	Yasiba engaito dhe.
P10	Sanyu aja kwisomero.
P11	Sanyu ali mu kibiina.
