title:	Ibbasi Lyangu Lipati Lya Bbulu

desc:	Ebei abanyina bayanda kuya kudolopo abbaasi lyabbulu. Ibbaasi lyabbulu tiilyaboola nkaambo lilifwide. Inoono balaunka buti kudolopo?

page_text:

P1	Kwakali biyo bbaasi lyomwe mumunzi mwaka Ebei. Lyakali lipati nkabela kalili mumusyobo wa bbulu. Lyakali lyacoongo kapati.
P2	Bumwi buzuba banyina Ebei bakati,  "Cinfumo tuyakuunka kudolopo tukaakuule yunifoomu yako yakucikolo."
P3	Ebei wakakkomana kapati. Bakeelede kuti babelesye bbaasi lyamusyobo wa bbulu. Kwiina nakoona akoona busiku oobu.
P4	Ebei wakasamide kaindi ciindi banyina nibakaboola kuti bamubusye mamfumo-fumo.
P5	Ebei abanyina bakanyamuka akuunka kucitisyini camabbaasi. Bakaililindila bbaasi lipati ilisiya. Pele mukubula coolwe bbaasi taakwe nilyakaboola.
P6	Abamwi bantu bakasika acitisyini camabbaasi. Bakatongooka nkaambo kakuti bbaasi lyakamuka kusika. "Sena bbaasi lyatusiya?" bakabuzya.
P7	Ebei wakanyongana mukati mumizeezo. "Tatukoocikozya kuunka to kudolopo." "Tanditiicikozye kuula yunifoomu yangu sunu," wakayeeya.
P8	Bamwi bantu bakakatala kulindila mpoonya bakaunka mwabo mwabo. Pele Ebei wakalila eelyo wakakaka akuunka kung'anda. Banyina bakamuyumyayumya kabati,  "Tulalindila biyo kwa ciindi cisyoonto."
P9	Mpobatayeeyeli bakamvwa icoongo. Bakakabona kasuko mujulu. Lyaboola bbaasi!
P10	Pesi bbaasi eeli tiilyakali lyabbulu. Lyakalikusubila. Lyakalikusubila alimwi kalilisyoonto. Ibantu bakalikulindilila bakalilanga biyo bbaasi. Kwiina nibakatanta mubbaasi.
P11	"Amunjile! Amunjile! Amunjile! Amunjile!" Wakaponzomoka namutekenya. "Twamuka kapati sunu," wakaamba namutekenya.
P12	Ebei abanyina mbubakanjila kusaanguna. Kufwambaana bantu boonse bakanjila mubbaasi eeli lisyoonto lisubila.
P13	Ebei naakalanga anze kwiinda awindo,  wakabona bantu banji kapati acitisyini.
P14	Alimwi bantu banji bakalikulunduka kuti abalo bakkwele bbaasi pesi bakajanika bamuka. Ibbaasi lyakazwide kaindi. Eelyo aboobo ibbaasi eeli lisubila lyakanyamuka kutalika kuunka kudolopo.
P15	"Ino bbaasi lipati lya bbulu lilikuli?" bakabuzya banyina Ebei. "Lilifwide," wakamvwiila namutekenya. "Tujisi bubi kulibamba. Cifumo liyakuboola," wakayungizya namutekenya.
P16	Ebei wakanyinaandaba kumusyobo wabbaasi. Wakanyina abubi kubupati bwandilyo. Wakalikkomene nkaambo bbaasi eeli lyakalikuya kudolopo.
