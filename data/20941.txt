title:	Omugabe Kufunira Omuhara Omushaija

desc:	Ekitebyo nikikwata aha mugabe ayayangire ngu omuhara atakayetoranira omushaija kwonka ahamuheru yahender atweirwe enyamaishwa.

page_text:

P1	Bukaba buri obwo, hakaba hariho omugabe owubabeire nibeeta Opale nana omugabe kazi weye murungyi. Bakaba beine muhara waabo omwe murungi.
P2	Eizooba rimwe, omugabe  akareeba omuhara yagambira omukazi we ngu "Ningyira ngu muhara weitu akuzire, nituteekwa kumurondera omushaija murungi kuruga omukyaro ekyi."
P3	" Hati nooha arashwere muhara wangye omurungyi?" Omugabe akebuuza. Akatekateka aha kijezo ekyaraahe buri mushaija araije kutekateka kutaatsya muhara wangye. "Ninza kuteeka enyungu yamaizi geijwire kwenda ngu omushaija weena aranywe amaizi ago garikwotsya niwe aratatsye muhara wangye omurungyi." Omugabe nikwe yagyizire.
P4	Eizooba eryakuratsireho,omugabe akataho ekirango arikweta abashaija boona ababeire bataka taahize abomukyaaro ekyo. Ekirango! Ekirango! Nikyiza aha bashaija abomukyaaro ekyi abato nana boona abatakashweire, Omugabe nayenda kutoranamu omushaija murungyi oratatsye  muharaweye omurungyi. mwije, mwije mweena!
P5	Ahakuhurira ekirango ekyi, abashaija abato boona bakashemererwa  heza beri omwe yayeterereza eizooba eryo.
P6	Omwe aha bojo abato akagamira Ishe,ngu "nahurira omugabe yayeta  abashaija boona abato oweye omuka,nayenda kutoranira omuhara omushaija aramutatsye. Taata, "Ninteekwa kuzayo nyemerere omumaisho gomukama, nyowe nyine amatsiko ninza kwija nana muhara womukama."
P7	Nkaburiijo, eizooba rikaika, Omushorongo gwaba bashaija abataine bakazi kandi bajweire gye bakareebwa barikuza owomugabe.Buri omwe akaba ashemereirwe.
P8	"Nimbkyiira mweena" omugabe nikweyagizire. Aho Omugabe yatandika kugambira abashaija bato ababeire beijire, omuntu weena aranywe enyungu yamaizi garikwotsya goona omurundi gumwe, niwe arabaase kutwaara muhara wangye murungi.
P9	Bakaba bari baingyi okugira ngu ekyijezo kyikaba kitari kyeizooba rimwe. Abashaija beingyi bakagyezaho  nana amaani gaabo goona kwonka tibaratweire muhara womugabe.
P10	Ahizooba  eryakuratsireho, hakaija wakame owahuriire ekirango obweire bwagyenzire. Akachiirwa yaheebwa entebe.
P11	Omugabe akamugyira ngu kutwaara muhara wangye, omuntu natekwa kunywa enyungu yamaizi garikwotsya omurundi gumwe. Ako nakantu buntu owangye  mukama wangye, nikwe wakame yagizire.
P12	Wakame akaba ayine obwengye  kandi ari omushaija wobukodyo bwingyi, akatandikaho ekitebyo kirungyi munonga ekyatweire omugabe obwongo akahurikiriza obwe amaizi gariyo nigahora.
P13	Abayambi nana omugabe bakashemererwa barikugira ngu" ogu niwe omushaija owutwaaba nitwenda." Omukeire kakye, amaizi gakaba gahozire obwe nikwe wakame yaganywa
P14	Omugabe nana abayambi beye  bakatangara kureeba wakame arikunywa amaizi garikwotsya.
P15	Ahamuheru Wakame akatwara muhara womugabe omurungyi kandi akaba ashemereirwe kwonka omugabe akatsigara anyigire ngu muhara weye yagyenda nana akanyameishwa.
