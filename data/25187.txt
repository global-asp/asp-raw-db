desc:	Mu lugero luno dhaadha yaghonia obulamu bwa Dingidingi.

page_text:

P1	Dingidingi  yabanga ni  dhaadha  ghe.
P2	Yali  nga  naayaya  ente.
P3	Lunaku  lulala  abaisulukale  baidha.
P4	Baatwala  ente  dhoonadhoona.
P5	Dingidingi  ni dhaadha  ghe  baalumuka.
P6	Beekweka mu  nsiko  paka  obwire.
P7	Abaisulukale  baamala  baira.
P8	Dhaadha  gha  Dingidingi  yaamukweka  mu bisubi.
P9	Omwisulukale  mulala  yaanhiinha  ku  Dingidingi  ekigere, aye  Dingidingi  yaasirika.
P10	Ghe byaaja, Dingidingi   ni  dhaadha  ghe baavaayo.
P11	Baalumuka  mangu  inho okwirayo  ghaka.
