title:	Isabukuru itangaje ya Nirere

desc:	Nirere yakoze ibishoboka byose kugira ngo ibirori byisabukuru ye yamavuko bigende neza. Buriya se umutsima wa keke ye bashiririje ntuzangiza byinshi?

page_text:

P1	Nirere, Karake na Kalisa barimo gutegura ibirori by'umunsi w'isabukuru y'amavuko ya Nirere.
P2	Kalisa na Nirere bagiye guteka umutsima wa keke. Karake na we ari gusoma amabwiriza y'uko bakora umutsima wa keke.
P3	Nirere aravuga ati: "Reka tuvangavange ibi birungo mu isafuriya nini."
P4	Kalisa yaravangavanze maze akora igipondo kinini cyane, bimwe biranyanyagira.
P5	Karake aravuga ati: "Uyu mutsima wa Keke uraremereye cyane!"
P6	Karake aravuga ati: "Incuti zawe zahageze."
P7	Kalisa yatekereje ko impano ya mbere iza kuba iremereye kubera ko yari nini cyane.
P8	Wari umusego kandi wapimaga ibiro bike cyane.
P9	Kalisa yatekereje ko impano ikurikiraho iza kuba itaremereye kubera ko yari ntoya cyane.
P10	Ryari ibuye risize amabara kandi ripima ibiro byinshi.
P11	Abana bavuze ko ikiro kimwe cy'amabuye gipima kimwe n'ikiro kimwe cy'amababa.
P12	Keke ya Nirere barayishiririje.
P13	Nirere yarababaye cyane.
P14	Mariya, nyirasenge wa Nirere yabajije Kalisa ngo afore uburemere bw'impano ye.
P15	Kalisa yaramushubije ati: "Impano yawe iremereye nk'iriya keke yashiririye."
P16	Buri wese yahise avuga ngo: "Isabukuru nziza y'amavuko, Nirere."
