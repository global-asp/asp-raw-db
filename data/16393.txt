page_text:

P1	Hello Happy Hippo! What do you like to do? I like to read! Can I read with you?
P2	We can read a magazine. We can read a book. We can read everywhere,  all we have to do is look!
P3	Hello Little Lion! What do you like to do? I like to jump. Can I jump with you?
P4	We can jump at home. We can jump outside. Let's jump from a mountaintopÂ way up into the sky!
P5	Hello Bush Baby! What do you like to do? I like to draw! Can I draw with you?
P6	We can draw a frog. We can draw a sun. We can even draw a rainbow. Wouldn't that be fun?
P7	Hello Monkey! What do you like to do? I like to dance! Can I dance with you?
P8	We can dance to music. We can dance to drums. We can dance together with everyone!
P9	Hello Elephant! What do you like to do? I like to sing! Can I sing with you?
P10	We can sing in the morning or in the afternoon. We can sing for mother,  and for father too!
P11	Hello Akili! What do you like to do?
P12	I like to read with Happy Hippo.
P13	I like to jump with Little Lion.
P14	I like to draw with Bush Baby.
P15	I like to dance with Monkey.
P16	I like to sing with Elephant.
P17	And I like to learn with my friends!
P18	What about YOU? What do YOU like to do?
