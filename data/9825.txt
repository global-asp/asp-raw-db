desc:	This is a story about a group of young people who enjoy playing soccer. Their coach has a big surprise for the team!

page_text:

P1	We love soccer.
P2	Moloko is teaching us soccer.
P3	We are playing soccer.
P4	Moloko is the best teacher!
P5	After the game,  Moloko told us that she has a surprise for us.
P6	But she will only let us know tomorrow.
P7	The next day we all came early to the field. We all love surprises.
P8	Moloko has new soccer kits and balls for all of us! What a great surprise!
