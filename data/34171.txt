page_text:

P1	Wanthu winango akhambabwera na wana kwa Jezu kuti awakhuye.
P2	Wakupfundzira wace akhambawatsimula mwa ukali.
P3	Jezu adakalipa.
P4	Jezu adawauza kuti: "Alekeni wanawa abwere kwa Ine."
P5	"Omwe an'bvuma lini Umambo bwa Mulungu ninga mwana, umweyo an'dzabupita lini."
P6	Na tenepo Jezu adayamba kuwakumbatira wana wale, aciwasandzika boko na kuwadalisa.
