title:	Mothers love
author:	Lubogo James
artist:	Rob Owen, Benjamin Mitchley and Catherine Groenewald
lang:	English

thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/24573.png
desc:	There is no anyone on this planet earth can show you love apart from your mother.

page_text:

P1	She carries me for 9 months.
P2	Wakes me up to go for studies.
P3	Makes me to know how to dig.
P4	Counsels me when i have gone wrong.
P5	Takes me for evening walk.
P6	Allows me choose whatever i desire.
P7	Makes sure that i cover myself in the bed every night.
P8	I give respect to all mothers who shows that love.
