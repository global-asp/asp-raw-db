P1	It's such a beautiful day right!!@We would like to narrate you all a story about an adorable girl MEERA!
P2	Meera is a 7 year old girl like us. She  is a simple, generous and thoughtful girl who lives with her Amma Ms. Naina and her Achi Rajnikanth and her lovely friends@Jack, Jayashree and Jessica in a small village !
P3	Meera loves to draw, play football, swim in the backwaters, make jewellery with coconut leaves and many such fun things. @Apart from all these things, she enjoys celebrating everything around her!!
P4	It's her birthday tomorrow and she is super excited about it. She has been planning from a very long time to celebrate her birthday in the best way possible.
P5	It's her birthday today and all her friends :@Jack,Jaya shree and Jessica came to her early in the morning to celebrate her day.... They were very happy to celebrate Meera's birthday the way she wanted!!!
P6	Meera sold the jewellery which she made with the coconut leaves, @to celebrate her birthday. With that money she and her friends bought everything which is necessary for her celebration.They split up in to groups and got started.
P7	Ms. Naina, Jack and Jayashree teamed up to bake a chocolate cake and cook. Mr.Rajnikanth and Jessica teamed up to decorate the whole garden bright and colourful!
P8	Meera is so happy already seeing everyone on tasks, sharing, caring and enjoying what they are doing for her. Everyone had a great time baking the cake, getting the lights done and putting up the balloons!!
P9	They are all set and her guests have arrived right on time..@Her guests are the children of her age who have not so great things in their life. The poor and needy children of her place.@Jayashree started singing a song and everyone
P10	Danced to her song wishing and greeting Meera a very happy birthday, the needy kids feasted their tummies with yummy food Meera and her friends have prepared for them, sang happily, danced joyfully and enjoyed to fullest..
P11	Meera was super happy seeing them happy and bid them bye with a a planter, Saying..take care of this plant and it will take care of you!@She said, @What you give is what you get back. @Nurture the plant and the plant will nurture you!
P12	That's how a girl from a village dreamed her birthday to be..@She feeded the poor and needy,@Cared for the nature, enjoyed her day with her friends and family. @@MEERA'S BEST BIRTHDAY EVER!!
P13	The story didn't come to an end. That night two fairies Stella and flora visited Meera and gifted her a a piece of land.. Ever since than Meera believed even more strongly that,@"what we give is what we get"!
