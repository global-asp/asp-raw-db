title:	Leswi ndzi nga lahlekisa xiswona emakete
author:	Arnold Mushwana and Given J Hlongwani
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Xitsonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37124.png
desc:	Jaha ri tshembisiwa nyiko yo hlamarisa hi siku ra ku tlangela tsevu wa malembe ya ku velekiwa. Kuma leswi nga humelela.

page_text:

P1	Tiko ra ka hina ri na timbuti.@@Ri le kule na le madorobenikulu.
P2	Manana a ku, "Elly, hi na nyiko ya wena ya ku tlangela tsevu wa malembe ya ku velekiwa."
P3	Manana, hahani na mina hi yimele bazi.
P4	Ndzi tshamile ehenhla ka nenge wa manana.@@Ndzi tlele ndlela hinkwayo.
P5	Hi fikile emakete.@@Ndzi vonile xihahampfhukaphatsa xo saseka xa wasi.
P6	Ximawusa xin'wana a xi ri na mihandzu.@@Ndzi vutisa manana, "I ncini leswi?
P7	Manana a hlamula, "I maapula."@@Ndzi kombela, "Ndzi kombela ku xaveriwa rin'we."
P8	Ndzi rivala voko ra manana.@@Se mianakanyo ya mina a yi ri eka apula.
P9	Loko ndzi heta ku dya, se a ndzi ri ndzexe.@@Ndzi sungula ku rila.
P10	Waxinsati a ndzi teka a ndzi yisa laha a ku ri na vana van'wana.
P11	A ndzi anakanya leswaku kumbe laha makete ku xavisiwa vana.@@Ndzi rila swinene.
P12	Wanuna a vutisa, "U kwihi Elly?"@@Ndzi tumbela leswaku a nga ndzi voni.
P13	Ivi rito ra manana leri ndzi ri toloveleke ri vitana, "Elly?"@@A ndzi tsakile swinene.
P14	Hahani a humesa xihahampfhukaphatsa xo saseka.@@"I xa wena!" ku vula hahani.
