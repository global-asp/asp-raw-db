desc:	Joseph teaches us ball control and other soccer skills.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there lived a boy called Joseph who liked football. He could kick the ball using either of his two legs.
P2	Joseph plays the ball using his head.
P3	Sometimes he controls the ball using his chest.
P4	One day he dribbled through the defenders using the outside of his foot.
P5	If the ball comes from the air,  he controls it very well using his thigh.
P6	He also dribbles the ball very well using the inside of his foot.
P7	He traps the ball using his instep.
P8	One time he volleyed the ball using his instep.
P9	But he could not score because the keeper caught the ball.
P10	The keeper also punched the ball which was shot by Joseph.
