P1	Hello! My name is Bella. I woke up at 7:33 today and I am late for school.
P2	One day I wake up so late that my mom was mad at me for waking up late.
P3	After school I go wash my hands and visit my friend Tim.
P4	One day I got hurt from the rain so I got a little bit sick that night.
P5	I am good at football and I play it with my friends.
P6	I saw a rainy rainbow too.
P7	I always have to go to the hospital for a check up.
P8	My brother helps my dad get some food from the tree.
P9	I see the birds always in the bluey sky.
