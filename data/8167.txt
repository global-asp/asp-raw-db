title:	Okukina
author:	Cornelius Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8167.png
desc:	A poem about a girl who loves to dance. Her teacher finds this irritating

page_text:

P1	Nkina bwemba ndi isanhu,  Nkina bwemba ndi naku,  Nkina bwemba ndi gholongera,  Nkina bwemba ndi ilalu.
P2	Omusomesa wange ankoba,  "Tyama oteleere! Tokinhiriza buli kiseera."
P3	Aye Maama ankoba,  "Okukina kukulamya,  Okukina kukuwanvuya,  Lulala oidha kuba mukini,  Obeewunise bonaboona!"
P4	Nze naakoba,  "Lulala ndha kukina abantu basasulire Okubona nze nga ndi kubuuka bwe neetala. 'Akina lubabu, ' bwe baidha okukoba."
