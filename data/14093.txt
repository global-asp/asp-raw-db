page_text:

P1	Uno ni omwechesia Lucia. This is teacher Lucia.
P2	Uno ni omwechesia Rakeli. Ali nende abaana. This is teacher Rakeli. She is with pupils.
P3	Abaana baamuchama omwechesia Rakeli po. Children love teacher Rakeli very much.
P4	Uno ni omulima. This is a farmer.
P5	Arakaanga imwo. He is planting seeds.
P6	Uno ni omusirishi. This is a doctor.
P7	Asirisha abalwaale. He treats sick people.
P8	Uno naye ni omuyali. Ayaalilanga abaandu. This one is a preacher. Sometimes he preaches to people.
