title:	Omuzaano gwo kucumba
author:	Christine Karamagi
artist:	Reginaldo Vicente Manhice
lang:	Rutooro
level:	First sentences

desc:	Wakame yayeta munywani we nkende kuzaana beitu ebintu tibyamaliira kurungi.

page_text:

P1	Omubiro byaira muno, Wakame na'Nkende bakaba banywani. Kiro kimu, mu bwire bwe enjara, Wakame akeeta Nkende kuzaana nawe.
P2	Wakame yagamba, "leka tuzaane ebyo kwecumba!" Nkende yakaguza, "Ekyo ntukikora tuta?"
P3	Wakame yamugarukamu, "Twiina kubanza kuhemba omuuro."
P4	Wakame yayeyongerayo, "Ntwijuza ensoha na'maizi, ntigiteka ha muro."
P5	"Ninyija kubanza kutahamu. Obunda teera omuhiha gw'ensoha, ogwiheho. Nturukemu." Nkende yaikiriza.
P6	Wakame yataaha mu'nsoha
P7	Amaizi  obugatandikire kutagata, Wakame yakonkona, Nkende yaihaho omuhiha.
P8	Omurundi gwa Nkende obugwahikire, Wakame yateeka ekibaale ekikooto ha muhiha gw'ensoha kandi yagwikaaraho.
P9	Amaizi obugatandikire kucamuka, Nkende akakonkona beitu Wakame tiyayihaho omuhiha.
P10	Nkenda akafa, Wakame yalya munywani we nfenanfe!
