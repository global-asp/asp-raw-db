title:	Okuyandja ondunge ombukise

desc:	Two men twice accept the advice of a bad judge. It is only when they eventually consult the elders

page_text:

P1	Kombe na Juma vari ovararanganda ovasiona. Aveungura ovina avihe oveni.
P2	Avezeri kutja andaku vari no Kasino. Atja kevevatera okuturika ovina viao.
P3	Kombe watja indjo turande okasino pamwe.
P4	Ihe ja Kombe tja ta, Kombe warumata outumbe uaIhe. Azembi Juma.
P5	Kombe wavangere onyama jo kasino kozombwa ze.@Nu posia Juma avanga okasino.
P6	Omupangure watja, "auhe wenu ngakambure ohinga jo kasino."
P7	Kombe wazepa okasino.@Juma eritoorere ovina kuye omwini rukwao.
P8	Kombe wari amavanga okundjosa okaruuona ke nu matunge karue.
P9	Juma watja, "Yandje maipi wina." Omupangure waikumwa na Kombe.
P10	Okaruwona ka Juma kapi. Omupangure watja, "Kombe wanyosa okaruwona ke."
P11	Juma kaari no kasino, kaari no karuwona, arara kehi jo muti.
P12	Juma waungura oukukutu. Wari no vikokotwa no makunde.
P13	Ovazandona va Kombe varia omakunde omengi okuza motjikunino tja Juma.
P14	Ovazandona vatja ku Juma "hungira kuna tate masute omakunde woye."
P15	Posia Juma watja, "ami hino kuvanga ovimariva vienu mevanga omakunde wandje
P16	Omupangure watja ku Juma, "konda ovazandona upaturure unane omakunde woye."
P17	Juma wapanda okupuratena. Kombe wariri "atja ovanene ngaverire mbuvatje.
P18	Ovandu imbo ovanene varaere Kombe, ove wazepa okasino, nu wanyosa okaruwona
P19	Nu ovanene vatja "okuzepa ovazandona va Kombe onavi wina"
P20	Kombe wayandja ohinga wowini we ku Juma.@Nu owo avehe vahupa nawa mohange.
