page_text:

P1	Once upon a time a girl named Faula had newly shifted in a town.She was super excited this morning because she was going to visit the garden that was just few kilometer far from her new house.She lived with her mother.Her father had past.
P2	Because of a serious illness.Although,she was poor but she had a great talent. She is a brilliant nature artist.She had made paintings of flowers and butterflies. She was living in a town that had jolly people.They valued money.
P3	She wanted to do something that will benefit her sick mother and her.However she had lost her father but she didn't want to lost her mother either.She visited the garden and saw beautiful flowers.
P4	Suddenly a girl came there and saw the painting of Faula.Faula was busy in painting that she didn't noticed her.The girl seem to be jealous. She challenged Faula to compete with her.Faula never had participate in any competition.
P5	Faula said "ok".The girl was glad to hear that.She showed Faula her painting to make Faula lose hope that she will never able been able to compete her.But Faula said that "I will do my best".The girl said the competition will be tomorrow.
P6	Faula was very worried that which flower she should paint.She was thinking for about 3hrs.It was afternoon now.When she was going to home by crossing the garden she saw something very beautiful at the corner of the garden.
P7	It was beautiful flower she ever saw.Faula was so happy that she has got a best flower for painting.She ran to home and brought a canvas.She started painting.It was getting darker.And now it was time to go home.She took her canvas with her.
P8	It was finally tomorrow,Faula woke up and get ready for the competition. Faula and her mother left the house on time. When she reached the place with her canvas.Faula and her mother were waiting for their name to be called.
P9	After a few minutes,Faula saw the girl,who she met in the garden, was talking with her friends.When the girl saw Faula she said "you'll never win.HAHAHA".Faula believed in her self.
P10	While,Faula was waiting with her mother and canvas.She heard that"Faula please come on the stage to represent your painting".Faula was super nervous.Her feet got cold and she started sweating.
P11	She went to the stage and showed her painting.HER PAINTING WAS UPSTANDING!The judges gave her 10.She was shocked to see the expressions of everyone.A judge came over the stage and gave Faula a check of 50 dollars and a price of best painter
P12	And they asked Faula to give her painting for displaying in the famous gallery. Faula happily gave them her canvas.She started crying tears of happiness.
P13	She was happy because now she can take her mother in a better hospital for a better treatment. @Moral:@Money is not everything.@Jolly people will be once a joker for everyone else also.
