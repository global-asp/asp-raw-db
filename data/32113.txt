title:	Ntwari n' Umusaza
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First paragraphs

desc:	Aka gakuru karavuga ku muhungu wakoze ibintu byiza maze bigahesha ishema ababyeyi be ishema.

page_text:

P1	Ntwari yari atuye mu nzu nziza mu mujyi wa Musanze. Ababyeyi be bari abakire.@@Ntwari yari afite imbwa yitwa Makasi. Yafataga imbwaye neza.
P2	Buri munsi, Papa wa Ntwari yamujyana ku ishuri mu udoka ye. Nyuma yo kwiga, yajyaga kumucyura mu modoka. Umunsi umwe ari nyuma ya saa sita bataha, Papa wa Ntwari yahagaritse imodoka ajya kugura ibintu mu iduka rinini.
P3	Ntwari yasohotse mu modoka abona umusaza wari wikoreye umutwaro uremereye cyane.@@Yari ananiwe kandi agenda buhoro. Ntwari yakomeje kumureba.
P4	Umusaza yicaye munsi y'igiti cyari hafi y'inzira afungura uruhago rwe. @@Yari yambaye inkweto yakoze mu macupa.
P5	Ntwari yamaze igihekinini atekereza wa musaza. Byaramubabaje. Ageze mu rugo ntiyariye.@@Yatekereje icyo yakora.
P6	Yarabyutse afata amafaranga mu gahago ke, ahamagara Makasi maze yurira igare rye.
P7	Uwo muhungu yirukankiye mu iduka asohokana uruhago yerekeza aho wa musaza yari yicaye yegamye ku giti aruhuka.@@Ntwari yaramusuhuje ati: "Mwiriwe Nyakubahwa?". Umusaza arasubiza ati, "Amahoro abe kuri wowe mwana wange!"
P8	Ntwari aramubaza ati, "Nyakubahwa, uturutse he?" Umusaza aramusubiza ati, "Mwana wa, ubu naje mpunga inzara yateye umudugudu w'iwacu. Ubu naje hano gushaka amafaranga.
P9	Ntwari yamuhaye rwa ruhago rwarimo ibyo yaguze. Afunguye, yasanzemo inkweto maze amarira amuzenga mu maso. Yashimiye Ntwari umutima mwiza agira.
P10	Ntwari yabwiye umusaza ati: "Ngomba gutaha kare Mama ataratangira kunshaka." Umusaza yapepeye Ntwari n'imbwa ye, baragenda avuga amwenyura ati, "Nkwifurije umugisha mwana wa!"
P11	Ntwari akigera mu rugo, Mama we yari yahangayitse aramubaza ati: "Ntwari, wahoze he?"@@Ntwari yamubwiye byose ntacyo amuhishe, maze iyo  nkuru ibabaje imukora ku mutima.
P12	Hashize akanya, Papa wa Ntwari arahagera aramubwira ati: "Muhungu wacu, turakwishimiye." Ubu, tugukubiye incuro ebyiri z'amafaranga wakoresheje.
