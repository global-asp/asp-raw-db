P1	Kwakali@  chiwena wanzala kapati.
P2	Wakalikuyandaula@  cakulya asyoonto alimwi katasabili.
P3	Kumane.
P4	POH!@  Wajata ciwena!
P5	Kumane@  wayo wakakkuta ciwena, alimwi wakakkomana.
P6	Amana@  akafwe nzala alimwi.
