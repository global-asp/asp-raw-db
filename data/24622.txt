page_text:

P1	Ab’ekka ya Fana n’ebatura
  omu tawuni.
P2	Fana ayine kappa, enkoko ebiri, embuzzi emwe n’akanyonyi kamwe.
P3	Hati, Fana akareeba abaana
  abandi abakunaga amabaare omubunyonyi
P4	Fana aka yiruka ah’obwana bwa buli.
  Yayomba nti balekyelaho
  kunaga amabaare.
P5	Fana akatwala obunyonyi bubiri omuka weye deero
  y’abureberwa.
P6	Obunyonyi y’aburiisa kandi
  y’agambira ab’eeka yeeye kw’abuyambire.
P7	Fana n’abazeirebe bakatwala obunyonyi ow’omushawo.
P8	Fana y’agambira abanwayibe ngu “Ebinyamwishwa n’abanwanyi beitu. Twiine ku zireberwa gye.”
