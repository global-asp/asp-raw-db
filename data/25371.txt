page_text:

P1	Lulala, Wakaduuyu ni Wakembo baali bamukwano. Kwali kwndhala, Wakaduuyu yayaniriza Wankembo bazaanhe.
P2	"Tuzaanhe omuzaanho ogwo okwefumba." Wakaduuyu yakoba. "Ekyo tunaakikola tutya?" Wankembo yabuuza.
P3	"Tulina okusooka okukuma omuliro." Wakaduuyu yairamu.
P4	"Twidhuze amaadhi mukasugha, tukate ku muliro." Wakaduuyu yamukoba.
P5	"Ninze ndhida okusookamu. Bwenkoona ku kisandikira ekya akasugha, okisandukula. Meeni nfulumemu. Wankembo yaikiriza.
P6	Wakaduuyu yesoiga akasugha.
P7	Amaadhi bwegainhama okusuugha, Wakaduuyu yakoona ku, Wankembo yasandukulaku.
P8	Akagobo ka Wankembo bwekaatuuka,  Wakaduuyo yata eibaale einene kukisandikiza ekya akasugha era yeena yaatyamaku.
P9	Amaadhi bwegaatoolera okutogota, Wankembo yakoona ku kisandikiza aye Wakaduuyu ti yasandukula.
P10	Wankembo yafa, Wakaduuyu yaalya mukwanogwe.
