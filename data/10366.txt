title:	Bana Ba Entsoeng Ka Kerese

desc:	Once there was a family with three very obedient children. The problem was they were made of wax

page_text:

P1	Khale khale hone ho ena le lelapa leleng le lula le thabile.
P2	Bana ba lelapa leo,  bane basa rate ho loana ba lula ha 'moho. Ba thusa batsoali babona hae le masimong.
P3	Empa bane basa lumelloe ho atamela pela mollo.
P4	Ka hore batla qhilibiea,  bane ba etsa mosebetsi oa bona bosiu feela.
P5	Eaba e mong oa bashanyana bao,  o batla hotsoela kantle ele kannete hoea letsatsing.
P6	Ka letsatsi leleng,  o ne a sa khone ho ithiba hoohang. Eaba bo abuti oa hae baea monothisa.
P7	Empa nako e ne e se eile. Eaba o ea qhilibiea letsatsing.
P8	Eaba bo abuti oahae baea koata,  hobane abuti oa bona o qhilibilihile ke letsatsi.
P9	Empa baba le menahano e meng. Ba etsa nonyana ka kholu eno ea abuti oa bona ea qhilibisitsoeng ke letsatsi.
P10	Banka abuti oa bona eo eseng ele nonyana,  ho moisa holima thaba.
P11	Eare letsatsi hele hlaha,  ea fofela holimo entse e bina.
