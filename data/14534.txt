desc:	It is Amelias first day of school. What will she do on her first day?

page_text:

P1	Amelia wakes up and thinks,  "Today is my first day of school."  .
P2	"Carlos,  what do I have to do?" asks Amelia. "I will tell you," replies Carlos. .
P3	Carlos tells Amelia to wash her face. Amelia says,  "I can wash my face. And then?" .
P4	Carlos tells Amelia to brush her teeth. Amelia says,  "I can brush my teeth. And then?" .
P5	Carlos tells Amelia that she must eat. Amelia says,  "I know how to eat. And then?" .
P6	Carlos tells Amelia to put on her uniform. Amelia says,  "I can put on my uniform. And then?" .
P7	Carlos tells Amelia to pick up her bag. Amelia says,  "I can pick up my bag. And then?"     .
P8	Carlos says,  "You must go to school." Amelia says,  "I can go to school. And then?" .
P9	Carlos says,  "You must enter the classroom."Amelia replies,  "I can enter the classroom. And then?" .
