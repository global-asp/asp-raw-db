page_text:

P1	Wampologoma yagemwa mu lutuula.
P2	Waifumo yali aidye kumufumita.
P3	Wampologoma bambi ali mu bulumi!
P4	Tumuleke ekigere igu.
P5	Waifumo yaalumukagho.
P6	Wuuyo no olutuula aizeeyo ghaka.
P7	Abaana bali kwebuuza, "kiki ekimubaireku?"
