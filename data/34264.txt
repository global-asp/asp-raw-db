P1	The chicken was looking for the rabbit but it was afraid.
P2	The donkey was having a birthday, all the animals hid to surprise him!
P3	Snakes were hissing near by and all animals were running for their dear life.
P4	The animals were running to cross the finish line for a prize.
P5	Even the wind blew the animals about.
P6	The rabbit was shouting for help because their was something inside the house.
P7	The animals went to visit the horse.
P8	The cow was sad all animals tried to make him smile.
