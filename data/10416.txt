desc:	This short story celebrates the abilities of disabled people.

page_text:

P1	This is Agnes. She is my teacher.
P2	Agnes uses crutches to walk.
P3	This is Metobo. He is my favourite radio DJ.
P4	Metobo has problems with his eyes.
P5	This is Moraa,  my neighbour. Moraa is a farmer.
P6	Moraa cannot hear. She speaks using Sign Language.
P7	This is Osero.    He is the best soccer player at my school.
P8	Osero has no hands. But he scores many goals for our team!
