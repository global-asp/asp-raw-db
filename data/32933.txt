page_text:

P1	I live in a house.@@It shelters us from the rain, wind and sun.
P2	We have cups in our house.@@We use them to drink water, tea, juice and smoothies.
P3	I see flowers around me. @@Flowers are pretty and they smell nice too.
P4	I like to eat cake at parties. @@Cakes are sweet!
P5	I see trucks on the road.@@Trucks carry different things around.
P6	My brother and I ride our bicycles.@@ We have fun doing this.
P7	I am a super hero.@@I have rainbow powers! @@I love bright rainbow colours!
P8	I love my cat!
P9	If I had a horse, I would ride it to my castle.
P10	If I had a tortoise, I would feed it lettuce.
P11	Because I am little, I can move easily.@@I can enter a mouse's home to visit.
P12	I can enter a submarine.@@I can see water animals.
