title:	Umlindzi siciwi setilwane nekunakekela
author:	Fakazile Armooh

desc:	Lendzaba iphatselene nekuhlengwa kwebantfwana betilwane letitintsandzane letiletfwa kulesiciwi. Letilwane letincane tiyanakekelwa ngumgcinisiciwi kanye netisebenti takhe.

page_text:

P1	Lena yindzaba lemayelana nemgcini wesiciwi setilwane letitintsandzane.
P2	Inyanga lendlulile bekuyinyanga lephitsitelako lapha esiciwini. Nomusa unemnyaka munye budzala futsi nguye lowaba wekucala kufika kulesiciwi. Bekondzile futsi alusizi.
P3	Kodvwa ngekushesha, Nomusa wagcina waba nemandla. Wacala kudlala naletinye tilwane.
P4	Kwatsi ngalelinye lilanga enyangeni, kwefika ihelikhoptha yehlela esiciwini.
P5	Lapha kulehelikhoptha bekukhona umntfwana lomncane wendlovu labembatsiswe ingubo lenkhulu.
P6	Ndiwa bekanetinsuku letisihlanu budzala. Umgcini wesiciwi abencelisa Ndiwa lubisi ngelibhodlela.
P7	Kwatsi ngalobunye busuku, bonkhe bantfu bavuswa ngumsindvo nakufika Ambiya.
P8	Ambiya abenetinyanga letisihlanu budzala. Bekangenamandla kahle ngemva kweluhambo lwakhe loludze.
P9	Ngenca yentsamo yakhe lendze kakhulu, umgcini wesiciwi bekabamba libhodlela lakhe aliphakamisele etulu kute akwati kufunta Ambiya.
P10	Tintsandzane letafika ekugcineni ngaleyonyanga bekungemawundlu labelambile boKopi, Kepi naKeji.
P11	Umgcini wesiciwi nebasiti bakhe bayatitsandza tonkhe tilwane letikulesiciwi, ngisho naleti letingeva.
P12	Bonkhe bantfu balapha esiciwini basebenta ngekutikhandla. Banelitsemba lekutsi nato tintsandzane ngalelinye lilanga titawukhula tikwati kutinakekela. Nase tikhulile letilwane titawubuyela endle.
