page_text:

P1	Oha ndi djala alushe okamaska ngeenge andi dimo meumbo.@@Okamaska oha ka ame nenge. Osho yoo vakwetu.
P2	Oha ndikosho omake ange nofewa osho yoo nomeva omanga inandi ndjamo meumbo. @@Naaashi ha ndi alukile meumbo, oha ndilikosho.
P3	Eshi handi popifa ookaume kange, ohatu pumafana poungolo!
P4	Tatekulu wange okwa li tavele. Okwa ya koshipangelo. @@Tate oha denge ongodi keshe efiku akundane kombinga yaTatekulu.
P5	Tatekulu okwali ena oukolele ye okuli nawa kolutu omanga ina kwatwa kouvela. @@Okwa kala nawa.
P6	Ye, omu mwameme mati okwa tameka okukolola.@@"Omuninho wange owa dinda," osho apopya.
P7	"Kololela pokangolo koye!" mumwameme wameme osho ati.@@"Ndjala okamaska. Kaleka oshinanho! Kala mombete yoye!" osho emulombwela.
P8	osho, omu mwameme mati akala mombete nokulesha.
