id:	40410
title:	Nyerere ndi Nkhunda ya ulesi
author:	Peter Msaka
artist:	Joe  Werna
lang:	ChiChewa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40410.png
desc:	Amene amagwira ntchito molimbika atha kukhala mafumu. Amene amatha kuimba atha kunyengerera mafumu.

page_text:

P1	Nyerere anali mlimi wabwino.@@Amakhala m'nthaka ndi banja lake.
P2	Amagwira ntchito molimbika pa munda wawo.@@Banja la nyerere limakhala bwino.
P3	Nkhunda anali neba wa Nyerere.@@Banja la Nkhunda limakhala mumtengo.
P4	Nkhunda ndi banja lake samalima.@@Amapumula tsiku lonse.
P5	Nkhunda ndi banja lake amayimba bwino kwambiri.
P6	"Tigawireni chakudya?" Nkhunda inapempha.@@"Ayi! Mukalime zanu!"
P7	Nkhunda inawulukira pansi ndikuyamba kuyimba, "Nyerere ndi mfumu!"
P8	"Nyerere ndi mfumu! Nyerere ndi mfumu!"@@Nyerere inasangalala ndi nyimboyi.
P9	Atamaliza kudya, Nkhunda ndi banja lake anawulukiranso mumtengo wawo uja.
P10	Tsiku linalo, Nkhunda inaseka Nyerere.@@"Mafumu amakhala ndi mutu waukulu?"
P11	"Ndiwe osayamika komanso waulesi!" inadandaula motero Nyerere.
P12	"Pepa!" inatero Nkhunda, ndipo inayamba kuyimba, "Nyerere ndi mfumu!"
