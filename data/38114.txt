desc:	This is story is about a boy who loves football and his dream is to be a great footballer in the world.

page_text:

P1	Kizito is 13years old. He loves to play football. He tells his father that when he grow up he shall be a football player.
P2	One day his father took him to visit his uncle who lives in Nairobi. He was very excited to visit his cousins and play football with them.
P3	His cousins introduced him to their football coach. Kizito was very happy to join their football team. The Coach trained them how to play football and win goals. They agreed to be meeting everyday to play football.
P4	After practicing, the Coach organiser a small tornament for the team. Kizito was among the players. Later on, their team won the game. They all celebrated.
P5	When he returned home, he taught his friends how to play football. His teacher chose him as the Coach of their team at school.
