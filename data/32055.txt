title:	Kuleeru Iilu
author:	Kulsumi Iisa
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore

desc:	Iilu mari kuleeru wiʼeteendu Safi. Safi e woodi hakkiilo soosey.

page_text:

P1	Wuro amin, e woodi biʼeteeɗoo Iilu. O mari kulleru wiʼeteendu Safi.
P2	To Iilu dillay kuugal o maɓɓataa dammugal.
P3	Safi e jooɗii haade dammugal e ayna wuro.
P4	To ɓoosaaru kedduʼen warii, safi hulnayndu riiwandu.
P5	Ɓoosaaru sey dogga.
P6	To keddu Iilu silminake safi hulnamo waɗa ba ŋarsaymo.
P7	Keddu Iilu sey dogga.
P8	To Safi nanii weelo, sey maɓɓi dammugal.
P9	Sey Safi nyaama, kayru tan.
P10	Ɓaawo ɗon Safi ƴeenga korowal ɗaanoo, "gurr! urr! urr!"
P11	To Iilu wartii, Safi sey naaɗoo taɓɓoomo.
P12	Iilu e Safi e njooɗoo, e njannga habaruuji.
