desc:	Omughala ono omuto ayogera ku bantu baakolagana nabo.

page_text:

P1	Amaina gange ninze
  Anita. Ndi ne emyaka 8 egyobukulu.
P2	Nnhenda okuzaanha.
P3	Nnhenda okuzaanha ni mikwano gyange ku isomero.
P4	Yokaana muganda wange. Nansinga obukulu.
P5	Baliraanwa baife balungi ku ife.
P6	Yokaana, Peetero, ni Nze tugya ku isomero ilala.
P7	Maama wange ni Nze tugya mu katale buli lwa
  Saabbiiti.
P8	Muganda wange ni Nze tukuba ebifaanani
  ebisesa.
P9	Muganda wange Yokaana
  musomi wa bitabo mulungi. Asomera baine ebitabo mu kibiina kye.
P10	Bbaabba wange mukuumi
  wa bitabo ku isomero lyaife.
  Alondera abaana ebitabo bye engero.
P11	Eisomero olunaigala, tugya
  kukyalira ba dhaadda baife mu kyalo.
P12	Nnhenda inho abaaghaka,
  badhaadha, ni baliraanwa
  baife.
