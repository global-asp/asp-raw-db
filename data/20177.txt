title:	Tikya Akinyi

desc:	Solem gel n nwala giiwa ya tikya soŋ ɔ ne ompaan te sier.

page_text:

P1	Man tikya nwala. O wuor buona Akinyi. O nong te pam.
P2	Tikya Akinyi pa'an te laa antoa biila. O pa'a te la vuut biila. Man nan nyang yel vuut biila: a, e, i, o, u.
P3	Tikya Akinyi pa'an te laa muolug bɔker. Man mi bun ziog, bun duog, bun sableg, bun paahug, bun siog.
P4	Guom saha, Tikya Akinyi yeena nan-nan-do teet te ka te gbihet.
P5	Guom, guom, nan-nan-do: Ya n te neege, ya nan la. Ya gbihim guom ka bah kaaheg, ka m yeel nan-nan-do te ya.
P6	Tikya Akinyi mi solma pam. O pa'an te laa solm paala daar woo bakoe ne.
P7	Ateni daar woo buraah kpeemnam solma ka o yet te.
P8	Atalata daar woo bunpoeh kpeemnam solma ka o yet te.
P9	Alariba daar woo o pa'an te laa saal n doot bunsiel ya kateh.
P10	Laamiih daar woo o pa'an te laa kueh yela solma.
P11	Ka Azuma daar woo tam meng n solmet solma teet taaba.
P12	Tam Tikya n gaad Tikyanam waabe tengɔugɔne!

Man n zoona, man bɔɔt ne na'awoo Tikya Akinyi.
