page_text:

P1	takateateka owkwija kwa bagyeny.
"agandi, twija kubatayayira!" nibaraga.
"nimukurikyeyo!" nagarukamu.
P2	Omusheija ogu nayirukira owa muriranwa.
Nayeta, "Banwani bagye beija kutayaya kandi tinyiine emire. Nyamba ope emiganti eshatu."
P3	muriranwa nagaruama ati,"otateganisa. omuryago gusibire, kandi twena turimukitanda. "Tiihine ekidenda kukuha."
P4	Konka omusheija wakubanza akateza kundi aha gwingi gwa muriranwa.
akaguma nateza kondi. naguma nabunza.
bwanyiima ogwigyi gwayigura.
P5	kandi omusheija akabasa kugaruka owa banwani be no mugati
