P1	Amina and her friends love to play together. They love swimming, playing football, jumping skipping rope and playing hide and seek.
P2	During Christmas they decided to go swimming at Kiruji swimming pool.@"We will have fun " Said Amina.@"Yes! I we will have fun" replied Alex and Adan
P3	On Christmas day, the three friends carried their swimming costumes and headed to Kiruji swimming pool. There were many children at the swimming pool.
P4	Amina was scared she that will not be able to swim. Her friends told her there will be a teacher at the swimming pool to teach her how to swim.
P5	At the swimming pool,they met teacher  Nkirote who taught Amina how to swim. She was very happy  and she joined her friends in the swimming adventure.
P6	Amina and her friends spent the whole day enjoying at the Kiruji swimming pool. Later in the evening they returned home.
