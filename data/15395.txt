title:	Musar
author:	Dessailly A.B. Chirchir
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Kalenjin

desc:	Musar cho kicheb aboo.

page_text:

P1	Kikichopkei Zama kuwo sukul karon.
P2	Mi kichobe aboo musarek ab karon.
P3	Kimwa Zama kile, "Nget iyoo kichob musar cho koromen kisir aboo!"
P4	Torete musar Zama mu sukul simaam ruba.
P5	Ochu musarek kikururyo.
P6	Rong'u aboo cheko mu chomnyot nyo wou.
P7	Kimwa Zama kile, "Aboo, Bere chii tikimeche musarechu sukaru keny."
P8	Kipkaat Zama mi metinyi kile, "Anyinyen musarekab iyoo kisir chobo aboo."
P9	Kisom Zama sukaru kile "Aboo kono sukaruk atesyi musarek amu mayem."
P10	Mi kukochinii aboto Zama sukaruk kamining kitesyi musarekchi.
P11	"Woo!Aboo!Ketesyi chumbi musarekchu!"
P12	Kiteb Zama abonyii kile "chone ayu iyoo kaa keny."
