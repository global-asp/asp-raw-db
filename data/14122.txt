desc:	UPalesa yintombazana encinane ethetha nezityalo ezahlukeneyo.

page_text:

P1	Lo nguPalesa. Uneminyaka esixhenxe ubudala. Igama lakhe lithetha ukuthi 'intyatyambo' kulwimi lwakhe iSetswana.
P2	UPalesa uyavuka athethe nomthi weeorenji. "Nceda mthi weeorenji,  khula ube mkhulu usinike amaorenji amaninzi avuthiweyo."
P3	UPalesa uhamba ngeenyawo ukuya esikolweni. Endleleni uthetha nengca. "Nceda ngca,  yiba luhlaza kakhulu ungomi."
P4	UPalesa udlula kwiintyatyambo zasendle. "Ncedani zintyatyambo,  qhamani ukuze ndihombise ngani iinwele zam."
P5	Esikolweni uPalesa uthetha nomthi osembindini wesakhiwo. "Nceda mthi,  khupha amasebe amakhulu ukwenzela sifundele phantsi komthunzi."
P6	UPalesa uthetha nemithi yokubiyela ejikeleze isikolo sakhe. "Nceda ukhule womelele,  unqande abantu abakhohlakeleyo bangangeni."
P7	Xa uPalesa ebuyela ekhaya esuka esikolweni,  undwendwela umthi weeorenji. "Ingaba awakavuthwa amaorenji wakho?" uyabuza uPalesa.
P8	"Amaorenji aseluhlaza," wanesingqala uPalesa. "Ndiyakukubona ngomso mthi weeorenji," watsho uPalesa. "Mhlawumbi uyakube sele unalo iorenji elivuthiweyo lam."
