title:	Abangane
author:	Sifundo Nkomo
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	IsiNdebele (Zimbabwe)
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Indaba emayelana lomfana labangane bakhe kanye lemidlalo abayithandayo.

page_text:

P1	Ibizo lami nginguSimo.
P2	Ngilabangane abane.
P3	Amabizo abo nguZizo, loLele, loSisa kanye loAyanda.
P4	Umngane wami uZizo uthanda ukudlala ibhola lenyawo.
P5	Umngane wami uLele uthanda ukubhukutsha.
P6	Umngane wami uSisa uthanda ukudlala umacatshelana.
P7	Umngane wami uAyanda uthanda ukubala.
P8	Ngithanda izinto abathanda ukuzenza.
P9	Ngiyadlala ibhola lenyawo loZizo.
P10	Ngiyabhukutsha loLele.
P11	Ngiyadlala umacatshelana loSisa.
P12	Woza, mngane, wena uthandani?
P13	Woza, sizodlala sonke ibhola lenyawo.
P14	Woza, sizobhukutsha sonke.
P15	Woza, uzodlala lathi umacatshelana.
P16	Woza, bala lathi!
