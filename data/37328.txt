P1	Their was a girl named Joslyn who was not having a good day, Joslyn kept falling down on the floor. By the end of the day Joslyn had 11 bandaids!
P2	Joslyn was sad. Joslyn wanted to cry but didn't. Joslyn told her mom that she had 11 bandaids Joslyn's mom helped her. The next day Joslyn felt better so she told her mom that she felt better Joslyn's mom was happy that Joslyn felt better.
P3	Sense Joslyn was feeling better she went to her friend's house to do something. Joslyn knocked on her friend's door her friend wasn't there so Joslyn left her friend's house. Joslyn went back to her house to call her friend
