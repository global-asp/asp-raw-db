desc:	Omughala eyafuna engaito omukuzi gwe ogusooka mu bulamu bwe.

page_text:

P1	Nakanda yali azira ngaito. Abaana abandi bwebagayaga baalina engaito.
P2	"kikalangufu inho okuzaana nga ozira ngaito,"atyo Nakanda bwe yakoba inhinha.
P3	"Nakanda, ndowooza nti n'akaire ofune engaito,"inhinha bweyakoba.
P4	Inhinha yamuzukusa enkeera mu nco nco.
P5	Nakanda ni mama ghe baaja mu itanilo.
P6	Baaja mu iduuka lye eigaito.mama ghe yamugulira eigaito.
P7	Obwire, Nakanda yalowoza ku ngaito ye empyakirira.
P8	Nakanda yayambala akagaito ke ayakirira.
P9	Nakanda yalowoza engeri yayinza okwolesa akagaito ke kano.
P10	Nakanda yataamu akagaito ke omukuzi ogwokubiri. yakinira mu kisenge kye.
P11	"Nakanda, toyambalira ngaito mu buliri," atyo inhinha bweyakoba.
P12	Nakanda yali musanhufu okufunha eigaito omukuzi ogusooka mu bulamu bwe.
