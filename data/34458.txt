title:	Tilwane tiyabaleka

desc:	Tilwane letine letindzala tahlangana ndzawonye kute titikhulule kubanikati labebanelunya

page_text:

P1	Salukati besinetinkhukhu kanye nelichudze lelidzala.
P2	Savakashelwa ngumtukulu waso.@@Sancuma kuhlaba lelichudze lelidzala.
P3	Lelichudze lelidzala labalekela edolobheni lelikhulu.
P4	Lelichudze lahlangana nelikati lebelibalekela umnikati walo.
P5	Lelichudze lelidzala kanye nelikati bahlangana nenja lebeyibaleka.
P6	Tahlangana nelihhashi nalo lebelibaleka.
P7	Tilwane tefika endlini. @@Tabona emasela angekhatsi!
P8	Letilwane tahlabelela kakhulu kute ticoshe emasela.
P9	Tadla kudla letakutfola endlini.
P10	Ngemuva kwesikhatsi emasela abuya.@@Letilwane tawahlasela.
P11	Emasela abaleka angaphindze abuya.
P12	Letilwane tabe setihlala ndzawonye ngekujabula endlini.
