desc:	Abaana banhumirwa okuzaanha emizaanho egyendhawulo okusinziira ku myaka gyaibwe. Soma olufumo luno obonemu gyosinga kwenda ogizaanhe ni baino.

page_text:

P1	Abaana abato benda okuzaanha n'ebyokuzaanhisa.
P2	Nnenda okutuuma omuguwa, n'okwesuuba.
P3	Kooni ayenda kyekweko.
P4	Abalenzi abandi bazaanha omupiira ogw'ebigere.
P5	Samwiri ayenda okulumuka.
P6	Abaghala abandhi bazaanha omupiira ogw'okubaka.
P7	Eresi ayenda okutuuma ng'oti kikere.
P8	Abaghala bazaanhisa amaadhi.
P9	Neeri ayenda gogolo.
P10	Abaanha benda okwezaanhinkiriza eby'embaga.
P11	Naaki ni Maali benda okuzaana ni dole.
P12	Ate ighe?
