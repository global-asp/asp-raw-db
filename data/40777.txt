page_text:

P1	There ones a forest near our village with big trees but one of them was the biggest  of all
P2	The tree had many fruits, each year it produces fruits and people liked to sit under its shade and see the nature around it and when they see it they are very happy
P3	One day two girls were walking  in the forest to pick berries and when they saw the tree the other one wanted to climb to the top ,her friend said no and she insisted.
P4	And then her friend agreed and after the girl had reached the top, she looked around and saw wild berries flowers and fir trees.
P5	Trees are very important to use because we use it as firewood we use it as a material to built houses and the very important thing is that trees gives us oxygen.
P6	And we give it carbondioxide
