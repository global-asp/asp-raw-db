desc:	This story teaches children that it is not good to use bad language on other people. We should live in peace with one another.

page_text:

P1	In our village there lived a Rich man. His name was Karufafa.He was a farmer and kept animals like cows,goats and poultry.
P2	He would find mistakes in poor people's work and start using bad language to talk to them.
P3	One morning his cow wanted to give birth.The farmer made a place for the cow to calf.As the man waited he saw the cow producing a cat followed by a dog.
P4	Bad Rich man sreamed! and screamed!.He went to the village and apologized to everyone.@He later lived peacefully with the villagers.
