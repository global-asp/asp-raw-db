desc:	Grandmother saves Tingis life.

page_text:

P1	UTingi wayehlala nogogo wakhe.
P2	UTingi wayejwayele ukwalusa izinkomo nogogo wakhe.
P3	Ngelinye ilanga kwafika amasosha.
P4	Amasosha athatha izinkomo ahamba nazo.
P5	UTingi nogogo wakhe babaleka bayocasha.
P6	Bacasha ehlathini kwaze kwaba sebusuku.
P7	Abaye abuya amasosha.
P8	Ugogo wafihla uTingi ngaphansi kwamaqabunga.
P9	Elinye lamasosha lamnyathela kodwa wathula.
P10	UTingi nogogo baphuma sebebona ukuthi kuphephile.
P11	Banyonyoba bagoduka.
