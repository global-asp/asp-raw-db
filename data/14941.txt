page_text:

P1	Obusumi bwaira muno hakaba haroho embeba ibiri emu yaikaraga munju endi yaikaraga mukisaka.
P2	Embeba endi yakaraga munju.
P3	Kiro kimu embeba y' omunju ekagambira eyomukisaka,  "Iwe olya kubi,  obyamu kubi,  enjura ekuteera."
P4	"Baitu nyowe mbyama munju y'omunyoro. Ndya ebyokulya ebirungi kiro kinu oije ombungire," embeba y'omunju n' egambira eyomukasaka. "Ninyija kwija kiro kinu ekiro," embeba y'omukisaka yagamba. "Nyina ekihika nkakaseka kokunywa soda."
P5	Obu kunu mukama w'enju akaba atamirwe. "Embeba habwokumuliira ebyokulya yacwamu kuzitega!" Akwata omutego atamu sukaali.
P6	Embeba ekiro zaija kulya ebwokulya ebirungi zikaija niseka,  "Chuilchuilchuilchuil!"
P7	Obuzahikire munju embeba y'omunju egamba,  "Kandye hasukaali enu. Orole ebirungi tulya kunu."
P8	Baitu ekaba etakaliire sukaali omutego gwayebbasura,  PA! Gwagikwata omutwe amaiso gagitugunduka nkagenjagi.
P9	Embeba y'omukisaka yarora mukuhunirra yagamba,  "Eee! Munywani wange byokulyaki ebi ebimurukulya bikaba tugundura amaiso?"
P10	Baitu enu etagarukemu.
P11	Embeba y'o'mukisaka yayegendera n'egamba,  "Haa! Timbisobole ebyokulya orukuhaisa."
P12	Eka yawe embi n'ekira eyamutaahi waawe enungi.
