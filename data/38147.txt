title:	The poor family.
author:	Victoria Tanaka
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	This is story of poor family who had no food. They were going to starve to death but a man called Jabo helped them.

page_text:

P1	Once there was a man who had a wife and child this man,s name was Jabo.Their life was so hard because they were poor and had no food.
P2	So the man had a plan to get food. He decided to hunt a big giraffe so his family can survive.
P3	He took a rope and tied it around a tree. He kept on waiting for the giraffe to come
P4	He saw the  giraffe come and he came and untied the rope , he tried to hold onto the tail but it ran away.
P5	He let go of the giraffe because the giraffe hurt him . Jabo was not happy at all.
P6	He almost gave up but he thought of a plan. He took another rope and tied it around the tree and waited for the giraffe.
P7	Jabo finally saw the giraffe and he was happy because this was his chance to kill it.
P8	He threw the spear at the giraffe and  the hithe giraffe died immediately. He took the giraffe to his family and they finally had food.
