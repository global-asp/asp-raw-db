desc:	Aardvark learns the importance of teamwork.

page_text:

P1	Aardvark is hungry. She wants to eat ants.
P2	She sees a big anthill. It has lots of ants inside.
P3	Aardvark digs for ants.
P4	The anthill is very hard. It takes a long time to reach the ants.
P5	Aardvark is very hungry now.
P6	Some of her friends come. They are also hungry.
P7	They dig together.
P8	Soon they reach the ants.
P9	Everyone has enough to eat.
P10	Shona Proverb: One cannot dig an anthill alone. (Rumwe rimwe harikombe churu.)Moral: Teamwork is the best approach to solving a problem.
