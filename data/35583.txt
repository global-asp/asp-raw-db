desc:	Asmau and Rukayya belong to different religions. They are still the closest friends. This story was created at the IDGC Bauchi 2020 celebration.

page_text:

P1	Asmau lived in Jimeta Town. She was a Christian.
P2	She had a friend called Rukayya. She was a Moslem.
P3	Asmau got very ill and could not eat very well.
P4	Rukayya stayed by her bedside in the hospital.
P5	Rukayya cooked all kinds of food for Asmau to eat.
P6	Asmau finally got well. "Rukayya is my best friend," she said.
P7	Both of them got married after some years.
P8	Their children learned from them to be friendly to be friendly to all.
