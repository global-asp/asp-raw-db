P1	As an African child, I wake up early morning and brush my teeth, go to the stream and fetch water for mum's domestic work.@    I  join my friends at the football field to play full field football match.@I also go for hucking in the market.
P2	Mummy and daddy usually pray every evening thanking God for the day Gon by, and as well giving him thanks for protection over there children.@   I love my parents for being such a caring parents.@Daddy alway buys oranges from the market.
