title:	Bayi Ba Akinyi
author:	Edith Sikota Habwanda
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	ChiTonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Kaano kafwaafwi kabayi babotu ba Akinyi anzyobatwiiyisya.

page_text:

P1	Aaba mbayi bangu. Zyina lyabo mbaa Akinyi. Ba Akinyi balandiyanda kapati.
P2	Bayi ba Akinyi balatwiiyisya cipele atubala tusyoonto.    Inga ndaamba mizuzumina ya a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Bayi ba Akinyi,  balatwiiyisya mibala isalala,  yanyanzabili,  yamungunga waji,  ausiya.
P4	Ciindi cakupuna,  bayi balatwiimbila kutegwa toone. Balatwiimbila lwiimbo lwakutubutika.
P5	Balatwaanina twaano twabalombwana basyomwa,  mumuvwulo.
P6	Bwabili,  balatwaanina twaano twabamakaintu basyomwa.
P7	Bwatatu,  balatwaanina twaano twazyakweendela.
P8	Bwane,  inga balatwaanina alizyabulimi.
P9	Muli Bwasanu,  Bayi ba Akinyi batwaambila kwaana twaano twasu.
P10	Bayi besu mbaahabupampu bainda munyika. Ndiyanda kuyakuba mbuli bayi ba Akinyi ndaakukkomena.
