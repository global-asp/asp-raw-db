page_text:

P1	Kanyi kuna hali. Hali dzinobhikigwa kene tjengetegwa vula kene mahewu kene busukwa.
P2	Kanyi kobhikwa tjinkwa nemasimbe. Tjinkwa tjakhona tjoyi tjikundamoyo.
P3	Kanyi komela whatjulo. Whatjulo dzozipa senyama.
P4	Kanyi kolimiwa manongo. Manongo anokhwiwa kuti abe bukhwilo.
P5	Kanyi kokaminwa nkaka wen'ombe. Nkaka unalubomba unozipa nehadza.
P6	Kanyi kowulusiwa zwidlo zwipalalanyiwa nemayenge. Zwidlo zokhwiwa kuti zwibebgwisa.
P7	Kanyi kunamiti minjinji inozwala bana banotjelamisa. Miti inonga mitungulu.
P8	Kanyi kunazila dzemakumbo. Zila dzkanyi dzoyenda kumatshime, kudanga, kugwizi kene kubabakidzani.
P9	Kanyi kunan'umba dzakapfuligwa nebuhwa. Pfulila kobviwa hhalila denga kuti vula isinile.
