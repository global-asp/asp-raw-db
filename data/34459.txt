desc:	Kwaphihlika ludziwo lwaNatabo lalutsandzako

page_text:

P1	Lona nguNatabo.@@Unabomnakabo labasitfupha.
P2	Batali bakhe bamupha ludziwo loluncane.@@Uyalutsandza.
P3	Ngalelinye lilanga bomnakabo baliphihlita loludziwo.
P4	Natabo akalutfoli ludziwo lwakhe.@@Ukhala kakhulu.
P5	Natabo uyabaleka.@@Ugibela esihlahleni lesidze.
P6	Batali bakhe bayamtfola.@@"Sicela wehlele phasi," bayacela.
P7	Bomnakabo bayahlabela, "Sicela wehlele phasi."
P8	Natabo uyayihleka lengoma yabo.
P9	Ngemuva kwaloko kufika umngani waNatabo.@@"Ngicela wehlele phasi," uyahlabela.
P10	Umngani waNatabo uhlabela aze agcine ehlile esihlahleni.
P11	Labangani babuyela ndzawonye ekhaya.
P12	Wonkhe umuntfu uyajabula ngekutsi Natabo usekhaya.@@Natabo utfola ludziwo lolusha!
