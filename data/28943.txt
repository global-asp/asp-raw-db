desc:	Kibonherezo ki ekigheebwa omwana oghomululu alya ebiibala byonhabyonha natalekeragho bainhe?

page_text:

P1	Olunaku  lulala,  maama  yaleeta  ebibala,  enhanhansi,  amenvu,  emithungwa,  emiyembe,  n'amapeera.  Yaabighanika  ku  kabada  ghaigulu.
P2	Twabuuza  maama  tuti,  "Ife   tunaalyali  ebibala?".  Maama  yaatukoba  ati,  "twidha  kulya  olwaleero."
P3	Muganda ghange  Kairu  wa muluvu  inho. Aja  bw'aluma  buli  ku  kibala.  Ebisinga  n'eyabiriire.
P4	Bwe yamala  okubirya  me yaaja  okuzaanha.
P5	Muto  ghange  oghundi  yakoba  ati,  "Dhala  bonha  Kairu  kyakoze!"  Kairu  timuntu  mulungi  ate  alinha  omuluvu.
P6	Maama  yaanhigaa  inho  olw'ekikolwa kya Kairu.
P7	Era  tweena  twanhiigira  Kairu.  Aye  nga  Kairu  iye  tafaayo.
P8	Muto  ghange  yaabuza  maama  ati,  "Kairu  toja  kumukangaivula?"
P9	"Kairu,  ali no  okutwetondera  mangu  inho."
P10	Buti  Kairu  taghulira  bulungi.
P11	Kairu  ayogera  mukaama  ati,  "amabundha  gali  kunnhuma  inho."
P12	Maama  yali  akitegeire  ira  ati  endha  eja  kumuluma.  Buti  ebibala  biri  kubonereza  Kairu!
P13	Oluvainhuma,  Kairu  yatusaba  tumusonighe. Yaakoba  ati, "tikairayo  kuba  gha  muluvu  mulundi  gundi."  Niife  twamusonigha."
