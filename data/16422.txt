title:	Neŋamba

desc:	Neŋamba muŋayi ate mugesigesi

page_text:

P1	Hale hale ŋaliiŋo omuhana eyi balanganga bati Neŋamba. Neŋamba gali muhana owelomalomanianga. Nyina ya Neŋamba gamulabula aye Neŋamba natamuŋulira.
P2	Lulala,  masenge ya Neŋamba galwaye. Galiinga engereha w'erungu.
P3	Hu lunaku olwo nyiina ya Neŋamba gali n'ebyohuhola. Egulo,  enyanga ni jibitiriye,  nyina ya Neŋamba gamuŋaye emeere ohuŋirira masengewe.
P4	Hungira gageene ogu Nalaŋa ogwecuusa gwafuha muutu.
P5	Ogu Nalaŋa gwamubuusa ehi gaali nagegiire,  Neŋamba gagugoboyemo ati,  "Nyama,  amaagi n'amabere." Nyina ya Neŋamba gali amulabuye ohutaloma ehyagegire.
P6	Era Neŋamba galomeye ogu Nalaŋa ati,  "Masengewe mulwaye era ati nje eyi gali nali huŋirira emeere."
P7	Nalaŋa gaduluma ni geyuna geherere Neŋamba ohwola ewa masengewe.
P8	Nalaŋa gamira masenge ya Neŋamba nga gewiiha ebbulangiti yiiye.
P9	Neŋamba ni golire,  ehidaala hyali hiŋolongeye hyeene. Gattiina mu nyumba nga langa ati,  "Senge,  oli ŋeena?"
P10	Nigawene n'ataŋulira janjasi lya masengewe,  Neŋamba gatiina mu hisenge ehyo mugati eyi Masenge we gaŋenyuhahanga. Ehyamutusa efiiri hu mutwe nj'ohubona omuutu ni gewihire mu bbulangiti.
P11	Neŋamba gagubuusa ati,  "Senge,  lwahiina amatwi gago mabbala tyo?" Ogu Nalaŋa gwagoboyemo ni guli mu bbulangiti guti,  "Ko nahasobola ohuhuŋulira bulaŋi."
P12	Neŋamba nindi gagubuusa ati,  "Senge lwahiina emooni jijo mbala jityo?" Ogu Nalaŋa gwa goboyemo guuti,  "Ko nahahubona bulaŋi."
P13	Muhuheneresa Neŋamba gagubuusa ati,  "Senge,  lwahiina omunwa gugwo mubbala olwaleero?" Ogu Nalaŋa gwagoboyemo guuti,  "Ko nahasobola ohuhumira." Nalaŋa ga pwanguha mu buliiri nga amiira Neŋamba.
P14	Olw'ohuba Neŋamba gali muhaana owe lomalomya,  gegumya n'ohweromyalomya n'olugaali mu hiida hy'ogu Nalaŋa. Gabuusa ebibuuso ebyeene ebingi.
P15	Hu komerero,  ogu Nalaŋa gwasinya ebibuuso bya Neŋamba ebingi nga gusalaŋo ohumuhaasa.
P16	Abehitehere bayeeda Neŋamba ni masengewe. Ohuŋwa olwo ni n'olwo Neŋamba sigelomalomanianga n'abatamanyiire.
