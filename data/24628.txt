page_text:

P1	empisi ekenetu omirebe omuka yayo
P2	esoba rimye ekasakunya amizi yashanga ebare remirebe rikurebeka ahamisi.
P3	eri ebare rikani rirebekwa obwere bwo mushana ka tehene eshikoko ekyabiri kene oku higa ninga okwitana. burikikoko kekashene kutura omirembe,ebare ryarebeka
P4	omushana gukaka gwayaka burikimwe kikoma
P5	ekikikyunkyu ekenta orukiko kugamba aha mushana ogwa yomesa burikemwe
P6	muuha oyine omukoryo byenge nobasa kukora ejura
P7	muha ekakwata omuhanda aza kuronda ejura
P8	muha yahika oyomufumu kufuna ejura
 omufumu yajira mbu muha iyine kufuna omukazi eshwere okureta ejura
P9	muha ekafuna omukasi yashwera
P10	ejura yantandishiraho okugwa omu mushana 
kweha obwo ejura yagwa omumushana niba gera empisi washwerya
