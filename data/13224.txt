P1	It was a dry winter in the Sahara desert. Lots of African animals around such as Horses,  Lions and vulturous.
P2	Horse went to look for some water because he was very thirsty.
P3	And a monkey did not want him to drink water from that watering hole and through him into the fire and burned his beautiful soft fur.
