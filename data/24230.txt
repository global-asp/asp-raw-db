title:	MUUHA NA WALUSHAAKI
author:	ROZALINDA MUKAMA
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Runyankore

desc:	Walushaaki aguma nabiiha ebinyamishwa ebindi mpaaka muuha amugwilemu.

page_text:

P1	Aha  bwire obwo, ebinyamishwa bikaturaana nke eminywaani. Izooba riimwe byashaaramu ku giira orukiko ku toraana omukama wabwo.
P2	Warushaaki akabanayenda kuba omukama. yashaaramu kubiiha ebinyamiishwa ebindi. Yabajiira nti "Ekishunshura ekirikutukura a'ha mutwe gwaangye n'omuriiro!" Mutakakwata a'ha kishunshura a'ha mutwe gwangye!"
P3	Ebinyamishwa  haza byayikiriza warushaki. Byashaaramu ku terera warushaaki n'komukama waabo. Aha'bwokuba bakaaba bine amatisko omuriiro gwa warushaaki  kubayamba omubwire bw'embeho.
P4	Hija h'abaaho izooba enjura bwegwa. Ekaagwa. yaagwa. Hazho embeho yamaanyi. Burikiimwe kyatooba kaandi kyafuuka.
P5	"Nituuza kwihaahi omuriiro gwo kutaagasa emibiri yeitu?" wakame yabuuza ebinyamishwa ebindi.
P6	" nitubaasa kufuuna omuriiro aha kishunshura kya warushaaki," enkyende yagamba." warushaaki n'omukama weitu ahabwokugiira ayiine omuriiro a'ha mutwe gwe," enkyende yagamba.
P7	Ebinyamishwa byikiriiza kumwe n,enkyende. Batuuma muuha kwakiira omuriiro aha mukama wabo warushaaki.
P8	Muuha kwahiika ho ashanga warushaaki omutuuro twaamanyi. Asharaamu ku twara omuriiro atabandiize kwebuuza ahari warushaaki.
P9	Muuha akakuunganya ebinyantsi byomiire. Yabiteka heihi n'ekishunshura a'ha mutwe gwa warushaaki. Tihiine kyabileho. Ebinyantsi tibilakwastiire muriiro.
P10	" Yimuka Omukama warushaki!" muuha yayomba." Nitwenda omuriiro hati hati."
P11	Kwomka byoona ebyo biryaho  warushaki akaba atakubaasa kuha muuha omuriiro.
P12	Muuha yakwatwa ekiniiga. Yagaluka yo ow'ebinyamishwa ebiindi. Yabagambiira amaziima aha kishunshuura ky'ahamutwe gwa warushaki. 
Kuluuga eriizooba, tihiine wagarukiiremu kutiina warushaaki. Kandi kwihoobwe muuha nizihiga ziirya za warushaaki n'abantu baazo.
