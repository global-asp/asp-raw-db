page_text:

P1	Shie ka ikyagh imôm.
P2	Ken ikyagh i shie ne, u zuwa a akagh a  kiriki.
P3	Iyange imôm ka ikyagh. @@Sagh ikyagh ne vough vough.
P4	Ertom a shie.@@Hen akaa a he.
P5	Ya kwagh, numbe, mem, er mhen.
P6	Lôô ikyon, ikô i mende.@@Sôr mbamzeiyol kua ugbaiyol.
P7	Inyaregh ki a vese yô, vihi kpuôô, koso, kar vea mbagenev.
P8	Aluer wea gba yô, mough.@@Shie kange akagh agen ka ayange.
P9	Hiitom man Tomahar.@@Tomatar man Tomanyiin.@@Tomataan man Sati, kua Lahadi.
P10	Ayange ne ka ahuraior a dedoo.@@Biishi a ve kôôsôô.
P11	Alu u ungwa agogo gbidye imemegh yô, umbur kwaghôron wam.
P12	Shie ka iyua i kiriki ga,@@Er tom a min tsembele.
