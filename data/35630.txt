page_text:

P1	There once lived a mother and her daughters.
P2	She loved the second daughter more because she always told the truth.
P3	Her mother bought a golden tray for her.
P4	A long dress.
P5	And a bicycle for her school.
P6	Her mother goes around with her and spoils her more.
P7	They built a house for her and they parked in.
P8	Everything changed. The sibling's learnt from their sister.
