desc:	This is a story of Sam 

page_text:

P1	This is Sam. He is cooking popcorns. His father asked him,"Sam, what are you doing?" "Why are you cooking popcorns?"Sam answered, "I am sorry father, I know I have done wrong". Sam's popcorns had spilled all over the kitchen.
P2	"Father I want to know how to cook popcorns, my friend Ray knows how to cook, can you show me father?" Father answered, "Yes I will but first eat the ones you have cooked. Don't repeat that again, you could have burnt yourself".
P3	The following day, father showed Sam how to cook popcorns. They cooked a lot and Sam carried some to school to share with his friends. Sam friends were very happy. "Sam can we come to your house so that your father can show us how to cook?"
P4	Sam answered them, ,"yes I will ask my father". Sam's friends were very happy.
P5	When Sam got home in the evening, he asked his father if he could show his friends how to cook popcorns and he agreed. They agreed with Sam and his friends to cook popcorns on the following Saturday.
P6	Sam's friends were shown how to cook popcorns by Sam's father and they were all very happy.
