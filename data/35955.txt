P1	Jedidiah wanted a special toy for his 5th birthday.
P2	He kept dreaming about it. Everyday he will dream about it.
P3	Finally on his birthday, his daddy and mommy gave him a surprise gift, and when he opened it, he found the toy he has been dreaming about. He was very happy.
P4	He thanked his parents, his sister Zoe and thanked God for the beautiful toy car.
P5	And he named the car robocar Poli. Jedidiah loved the car and took everywhere he went to.
