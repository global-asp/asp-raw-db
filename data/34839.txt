page_text:

P1	Fati vi ozi oweyi@  enezuechi ankahi.@  @@  Oyara nyiyi, doyara@  ukoro amekhomen.
P2	Ekuyioyann, Fati oniriadan@  ati oyan enesi oku.
P3	Fati osi kunor gu oyan ati@  adan ama isa oname ati@  onere ora ruru.@  @@  Ayanyi inomi, oniri@  onchere, ati ebeba.
P4	Oyanika, Fati, suto@  pete! Shoku izeni, oniri izosor.
P5	Fati, ariehini, sheyi@  pete uzuwwanor Pataki.@  @@  Fati ka, ma ho oyami.
P6	Umeche, onyiani ati@  ada deya mukoro.
P7	Ere ochi obanyi.@  @@  Ere ochi oweyi.
P8	Fati onira mukoro.@  @@  Ori ochi oweyi.@  @  @  Ori ochi anaweyi@  Pataki.
P9	Fati okaneyi ebeba.@  @  @  Dori inomi ovivi@  ini inavi ogodo.
P10	Di Fati, do ve eyi chi.@  @@  Dori iyine ini ozeahu.
P11	Fati ore avi enezoza.@  Ana za yinyinyin.@  @@  Osi igege oyan kanyi avi ene@  zoza.
P12	Di Fati ka, osoni eya@  anki avi.@  @@  Imoyan, di avi onon@  do zizi.@  @@  Di Fati ka, oson eviavi.@  Ewu ovini.
P13	Di Fati nowu, “Eii!”@  @@  Ozuechi nesi oyan ati adan.
P14	Fati ore adan do nowu,@  “Ewu! “Ewu!”@  @@  Di adan ka, “Izoya? Izoya?”@  @@  Di Fati ka, “Izo! Izo!”
P15	Adan osi ochi do@  si du ewu.@  @@  Di ewu ononi do sihu.
P16	Adi Fati ka, “Eyin semewu.”@  @@  Vihenetu oyanwu di ya@  ka yo ireyi@  ewu ononi.
P17	Oyan oka, “Fati,@  ayami oyine ke ewu odawuri.”
P18	“Iyosa, kaneyi izuwano etere.”@  @@  Izo otatu@  ni.
