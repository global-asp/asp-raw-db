page_text:

P1	Besivame kuhlala nagogo kanye namake wami.
P2	Bomzala bami, Christina, Thandi, Junior kanye na anti bebahlala natsi.
P3	Gogo wami abesifundzisa imidlalo.@@Bekuba sikhatsi lesimnandzi.
P4	Ngemuva kwaloko, Junior waya eMalawi, nagogo waya eMalawi.
P5	Bo anti naChristina baya eMalawi.
P6	Wase naThandi uya eMalawi.
P7	Kwaba buhlungu mbamba.@@Bekungekho muntfu lotawuhlala natsi.
P8	Kepha bekungasikubi kakhulu, ngobe make wami bekasolo alapha kanye nami.
