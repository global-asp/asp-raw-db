desc:	The girl helps the mother on house chores

page_text:

P1	Girls do the work at home while boys are sited
P2	They fetch water,fetch firewood while boys are playing football
P3	They cook food for the whole family,wash utensils and serve food.
P4	They sweep the compound,wash clothes and make sure the home is clean.
P5	They help go to gardens to help the mothers on digging.
P6	Sometimes girls are denied going to school.
