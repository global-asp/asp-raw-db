page_text:

P1	Leka nkukobere olufumo lwange. Ntandiika  nga  okubbubbuka okutono.
P2	Nnumukira  munsiko  empegho bwentwala.
P3	Tuli  baamukwano  nze  ni iwe.
P4	Nkufumbira emere.
P5	Nkumuliikirira  obwire.
P6	Ndi  n'obukodyo  kamaala.
P7	Ndi  kitundu  ku kumakaago.
P8	Aye  ba mwegendereza  bwonsangaaza   mu makaago.   tyenda  kungayaalirira.
