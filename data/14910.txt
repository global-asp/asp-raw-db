title:	Iphalishi
author:	Zimbili Dlamini
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/14910.png
desc:	Mom is away and Dad is making the morning porridge.

page_text:

P1	UZama  ulungisela ukuya esikoleni.
P2	Ubaba upheka iphalishi abazolidla ekuseni.
P3	"Umama upheka iphalishi elimnandi!" kusho uZama.
P4	Iphalishi linikeza uZama umndlandla esikoleni.
P5	Selilungile iphalishi.
P6	Ubaba uthela ubisi ephalishini ngothando.
P7	"Baba,  ngicabanga ukuthi leliphalishi lidinga ushukela othe xaxa," kusho uZama.
P8	"Iphalishi likamama limnandi kunelikababa," kucabanga uZama.
P9	"Baba,  ngicela omunye ushukela,  kusho uZama''."
P10	Ubaba wathelela uZama omunye ushukela.
P11	"Wu! Baba! Ufake iswayi ephalishini lami."
P12	"Baba,  umama uzobuya nini?" kubuza uZama.
