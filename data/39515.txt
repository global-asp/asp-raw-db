P1	John and Bili are members of the Strikers Football team.
P2	Coach Joy likes to inspire her team before the game starts.
P3	Coach Joy bought jerseys for her players to encourage them. They were happy to have their new jersey's.
P4	Coach Joy is instructing her players to stay in their positions.
P5	She reminds them to work harder because she believes in them.
