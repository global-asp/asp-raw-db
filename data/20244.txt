desc:	Iyi nkuru iratubwira uko Bakame yabeshye inshuti ye Nzovu wamutsindaga mu mupira wamaguru. Yamubeshye ko guhagarara mu muriro byatuma agira amaguru mato

page_text:

P1	Bakame na Nzovu bari inshuti magara.
P2	Baragiraga amatungo ya bo ahari ubwatsi n'amazi.
P3	 Bakundaga gukina umupira w'amaguru.
P4	Nzovu yatsindaga ibitego byinshi. Bakame ntibimushimishe.
P5	Nuko Bakame abaza Nzovu ati: "Ese ni iki gituma uba umukinnyi w'umuhanga?" Nzovu arasubiza ati: "Ni aya maguru yanjye manini."
P6	Bakame na Nzovu bakinnye umukino wo gusiganwa ku maguru.  Bakame ariruka aramusiga.
P7	Nzovu byaramubabaje. Nuko abaza Bakame ati:"Ese ni iki gituma umenya kwiruka cyane?" Bakame aramusubiza ati: "Ni aya maguru yanjye ananutse."
P8	Nzovu yabajije Bakame ati: "Ni iki nakora ngo ngire amaguru ananutse?" Bakame aramusubiza ati: "Ndakwereka uko ubigenza."
P9	Bakame aramubwira ati: "Hagarara mu muriro." Nzovu arataka cyane agira ati: "Ndimo gushya weeee!"
P10	Bakame abwira Nzovu ati: "Ubu nibwo buryo bwonyine bwagufasha kugira ngo ugire amaguru ananutse."
P11	Nzovu ava mu muriro.  Atembagara hasi.
P12	Nzovu yamaze iminsi myinshi adashobora guhagarara.
P13	Nzovu amaze koroherwa, yasanze umuryango we.
P14	Bakame byamukoze ku mutima. Nuko asaba Nzovu imbabazi.
