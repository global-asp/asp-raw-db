title:	Tshwene Le Kwena
author:	Lorato Trok
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Setswana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/19922.png
desc:	Botsala jwa ga Kwena le Tshwene.

page_text:

P1	Tshwene o kopana le Kwena kwa nokeng.
P2	"O dula kae",  ga botsa Tshwene. "Mo nokeng," Kwena a araba.
P3	"Wena o dula kae?" Kwena a botsa. "Mo ditlhareng," Tshwene a araba.
P4	"A o kgona go thuma?" Kwena a botsa.
P5	"Ga ke kgone go thuma," ga bua Tshwene. "Nka go ruta."
P6	"Re ditsala. O seke wa tshoga," ga rialo Kwena.
P7	"Tshwene,  malome wa me o a lwala. O tlhoka go ja nama," ga rialo Kwena.
P8	Thswene o tshogile thata. A Kwena o tlile go mo ja?
P9	Tshwene o batla go tshaba.
P10	Tshwene o nale leano.
P11	"Ke tla go fa pelo ya me gore o mo fe yona," Tshwene a tshepisa.
P12	"Pelo ya me e setse kwa setlhareng," ga bua Tshwene.
P13	"A o tla ya go tsaya pelo ya gago?" Kwena a. "Ee," ga bua Tshwene.
P14	Kwena o ne a thumela morago kwa lefatsheng. Tshwene o ne a tabogela kwa ditlhareng.
P15	"Ga o tsala ya me. O buile maaka!" ga rialo Kwena.
P16	"Ga re ditsala. O batla go nja!" Tshwene a rialo.
