title:	Usozimfihlo

desc:	Emva kokuthunjwa kwabazali bakaKay uDr Devious

page_text:

P1	Kwakukhona intombazane igama layo kunguKay.@@Abazali bakhe babengososayensi abavelele. Babematasa nomsebenzi owawuyimfihlo enkulu esikhungweni sabo sezesayensi.
P2	Esinye isikhohlakali sikasosayensi esasibizwa ngoDr Devious sasifuna ukuthola imfihlo yalo msebenzi ababewenza.@@Ngobunye ubusuku uDr Devious wathumela uzakwabo uDan Diabolical ukuthi azothumba abazali bakaKay.
P3	UDan wababamba abazali. Wacishe wamthola uKay kodwa waphunyuka ngoba wayemncane futhi egijima kakhulu. @@UKay wagijima njengonyazi.
P4	Lapho ama khona wabona esesehlathini. Izihlahla zazizinde kakhulu futhi wayengakaze azibone ezinje.@@Wabheka phezulu ethi uzama ukubona lapho zigcina khona, wakhubeka wawa!
P5	Waginqika, waginqika, waginqika.@@Wathi lapho ephaphama wazibona esendlini eneTV enkulu.
P6	"Sawubona Kay," kusho indoda ekuTV. @@Ngisebenza ngezimfihlo zomhlaba! Manje nguwena osuyisisebenzi sezimfihlo zomhlaba esisha!"@@UKay ezwa lawo magama, wazizwa eba nesibindi esikhulu.
P7	Usozimfihlo uKay washiya isihlahla esephuma ngomnyango owawusesiqwini saso isihlahla.@@Wagijima waze wayofika lapho kwakuhlala khona uDr Devious wafike wamcela inselelo.@@Balwa izinsuku ezimbili, uKay wagcina eyinqobile lempi!
P8	Sebebuyele ekhaya abazali bakhe, uKay wabuyela esihlahleni leso ukuyofuna indoda eyayikuTV.@@Wasithola isihlahla, kodwa sasingenawo umnyango.@@Wahlala phansi wabheka phezulu ezihlahleni.
