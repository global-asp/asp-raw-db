title:	Batategulu bakabegwala

desc:	Tebela lungano gwensikana nebonha uwe begwisana nebhembolufu.

page_text:

P1	Ndombala masiki lubaka gose andibhuda kuhhe.@Masiki inondidzibilila. Kakale inodzibilila bamwe.
P2	Ndongula maboko angu nevula inasepa ndisanhu ndabhuda kanyi.@Ndongula kakale andihanduka kanyi.
P3	Andilezwa bakhwinya bangu, tokunhana zwikokola koga.
P4	Batategulu bakabegwala kwazo. @Bakayenda kubuwongelo.@Tate bakabefona mahhuba ose behuzwa nekwabatategulu.
P5	Batategulu bakabebakasima besanhu bekagwala.@Bakanaya.
P6	Nkulu wangu wakatangisa kohola.@Eti: "Whulo yangu inogwadza"
P7	Bamhadzi bakati kuwunli: "Ukoholele mukokola!  Umbale masiki. Usisedzelelane nebamwe! Ugale mubuyezelo gugo!"
P8	Ndizo, nkulu wangu wakagala mubuyezelo gugwe ebala mabhuku.
