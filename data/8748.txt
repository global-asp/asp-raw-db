desc:	Bala izilwane eziyophuza. Kwenzekani uma ibhubesi lifika ukuzophuza?

page_text:

P1	Indlovu eyodwa yahamba yayophuza amanzi.
P2	Izindlulamithi ezimbili zahamba zayophuza amanzi.
P3	Kunezinyathi ezine namalanda amane. Zonke ziyophuza amanzi.
P4	Izimpala ezinhlanu nezintibane eziyisithupha ziyophuza amanzi.
P5	Amaxoxo ayisikhombisa nezinhlanzi eziyisishiyagalombili kusemanzini.
P6	Amadube ayisishiyagalolunye ayophuza amanzi.
P7	Kwafika ibhubesi elilodwa lizophuza amanzi.
P8	Ubani onesibindi ngokwanele sokuqhubeka aphuze?
