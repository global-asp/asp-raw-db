title:	Ese Nyumirwa
author:	Bukheye Mulongo Christopher
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lunyole English
level:	First words

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8400.png
desc:	Olugero mu ndimi ebiri olusungu n’oluswayiri niruɳamba hubiitu byosi byosi ebi mwaana muhaana anyumirwa ohuhola.

page_text:

P1	Nyumirwa ohutuuma omugoye. 
P2	Nyumirwa ohutuuma omugoye seeka. 
P3	Nyumirwa ohugutuuma ni meeri yange.
P4	Nyumirwa ohusoma engero eji nyoonya. 
P5	Nyumirwa ohugenda mu hisaaŋe n'abameeri bange. I enjoy walking in the field with my friends.
P6	Nyumirwa ohwohesa maama ebyo'hunyiyiramo.
P7	Nyumirwa ohuŋandiiha enjuguta embala eji paanga nasoma muwangu.
P8	Nyumirwa ohulya ebibala ebyengeri n'engeri. 
P9	Nyumirwa ohugona huhitaliri ehibbala. 
P10	Nyumirwa ohuŋulirisa maama naagana engero eji nyoonya. 
P11	Ese ohuhola ebiitu bino byoosi hunyoonya.
