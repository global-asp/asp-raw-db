P1	Wingo lives with his parents and his little sister in the tiny village
P2	He has a very unusual friend that he likes talking to
P3	Siko the tree likes Wingo as well .He likes to listen to his stories
P4	Oscar is also wingo´s friend.He likes playing with Wingo
P5	Wingo refuses to share the ball ,Oscar is not happy so he leaves.
P6	Wingo is sad too. He was mean towards his best friend. So he tells Siko
P7	Wingo we should always be kind to our friends .You made your friend sad and you have to say sorry to him.
P8	I´m sorry Oscar ,i will be kind next time.The two friends are happy again
P9	Siko is happy too
