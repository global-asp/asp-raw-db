desc:	Xitori xa swifaniso xa ntila wunwe hi pheji xa vutomi bya nyawa: mabyalelo

page_text:

P1	Munghana wa mina u ndzi nyikile tinyawa ku suka exirhapeni xa yena.
P2	Ndzi byala nyawa emisaveni.
P3	Nyawa yi mila emisaveni.
P4	Ximilani xi kula xi ya edyambini.@@Timitsu ti kula ti ya ehansi.
P5	Ximilana xibaleka xi ya ehenhla ka misava.
P6	Ximilani xi kula,
P7	xi kula.
P8	Ndzi byala tinyawa to tala.
P9	Swimilani swi kula,
P10	swi kula.
P11	Swimila swi humesa swinyawani.
P12	Swimilana swi kula, swikula swi famba na khumbi ra mina!
P13	Ivi ndzi tshovela tinyawa loko ti omile.
P14	Ndzi humesa tinyawa endzeni ka swikhamba.
P15	Ndzi sweka tinyawa ndzi chela na nyala yo tshwuka na swipayisisi.
P16	Tinyawa se ti lulamerile ku dyiwa.
