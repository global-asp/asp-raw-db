title:	Be Mungo  Mwase Ni Samwene.
author:	Fabian Wakholi
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lumasaaba
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8296.png
desc:	Lukano luhamba khumwana umufwiti umwayaayi atwela ni mungo mukhali mwabusyele ta.

page_text:

P1	Imbwa yase ni samwene khulolelela binywiinywi bwase mungaki.
P2	Binywiinywi byase nisamwene khwakanana ni buyuusi mu sisaala.
P3	Buyuusi wase ni samwene khwinyaha nitsitika tsyase mu lukingi.
P4	Tsitika tsase ni samwene khubumba tsikhobe mwiloba.
P5	Ikhobe yase nisamwene khulya kamamwa ni wanasilikhe busyele.
P6	Wanasilikhe wase ni ikhobe barya Wanakhutu mu buli sambo yoosi.
P7	Wanakhutu nisamwene khuloota mbolambola nu mukhana Nandemu.
P8	Umukhana Nandemu nisamwene khukana khuwooleela ni khukhwimba kimyenya ambi ni nyatsa.
P9	Be mungo mwase boosi nisamwene khumenya atwela mumwikhoyo kumubiliilifu.
