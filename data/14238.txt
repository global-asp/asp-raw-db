page_text:

P1	Mary is my name.I am the only girl in my family.I like to go out with my family.
P2	I go to Kimura primary school and am in class four.I like to play with my friends so much.
P3	After school i always help my mother to do work in the house. Later on she helps me do my homework.
P4	During weekends i like to play with my pet together with my brother. Tom is the name of my pet.
P5	I really love going to the market with my mother.I usually help her choose the things to buy.
P6	We have chickens at home which i usually help my mother to feed.
P7	Drawing is my hobby. My motherÂ  supports me by buying me colors and drawing books.
P8	During my free time i usually go around our farm with my mother.
P9	During holidays I love to go to my grandparents home to visit them.I always get a present from them.
P10	After our stay at my grandparents home we always have a family reunion where we eat and say stories about our stay and how happy it has been.
P11	Leaving from my grandparents home is always boring but it is a must since schools are always re-opening.I always love opening of school since i get to meet my friends again. In my life my parents teach me and my brother good morals which helps us live a normal life.
