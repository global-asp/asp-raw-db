title:	Mihlovo ya nkwangulatilo
author:	Given J Hlongwani
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Xitsonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34334.png
desc:	Ntsheketo lowu wu longoloxa mihlovo ya nkwangulatilo na swilo leswi u nga swi vonaka eka vutomi bya masiku hinkwawo leswi nga namihlovo leyi fanaka.

page_text:

P1	Nkwangulatilo wu na mihlovo ya nkombo.
P2	Mihlovo wo sungula i wo tshwuka.
P3	Rhumani u ambarile hembhe yo tshwuka.
P4	Mihlovo wa vumbirhi i wa xitshopi.
P5	Leri i lamula. @@Xana i ncini lexi u xi vonaka lexi muhlovo wa xona wu nga wa xitshopi?
P6	Muhlovo wa vunharhu i wa xitshopana.
P7	Tsutsu wa tandza i wa xitshopana. @@Xana i ncini lexi u xi vonaka lexi muhlovo wa xona wu nga wa xitshopana?
P8	Muhlovo wa vumune i rihlaza.
P9	Xirhapa xa mabanana i xa rihlaza.
P10	Muhlovo wa vuntlhanu i wa wasi.
P11	Matilo i ma wasi.
P12	Muhlovo wa vutsevu i wa indigo.
P13	Tirubeyila leti i ta muhlovo wa indigo.
P14	Muhlovo wa nkombo i wa vhayolete.
P15	Rhoko ya mina i ya vhayolete. Vhayolete i muhlovo lowu ndzi wu rhandzaka! @@Xana hi wihi muhlovo lowu i wu rhandzaka?
P16	Xana wa yi tiva mihlovo ya nkombo ya nkwangulatilo?
