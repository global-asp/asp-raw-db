page_text:

P1	Lendlu iseCongo.@@Ngiyayitsandza lendlu.
P2	Besinelikamelo letfu lekugezela.@@Besinemthoyi wetfu.
P3	Besinengadze.@@Bengihlanyela engadzeni yami.
P4	Make watsi, "Make wami wangifundzisa kuhlanyela. Nyalo sengifundzisa wena."
P5	Bengihlanyela ematamatisi nesipinatji.@@Bengikwenta ngingedvwa.
P6	Bengitsatsa ematamatisi ngiwayise kumake.@@Make bekatsi, "Ngiyabonga."
P7	Sayishiya leyondlu leseCongo.
P8	Site ingadze lapha eNingizimu Afrika.
