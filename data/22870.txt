desc:	Kakyo is excited to find out that she has a lot in common with her new friend Nankya and yet they come from different parts of Uganda.

page_text:

P1	Kakyo is a girl from Bududa in Eastern Uganda.
P2	Today at school she made a new friend called Nankya.
P3	Nankya is from Masaka in Central Uganda.
P4	They realized they both like to play.
P5	Both girls also like swimming.
P6	Kakyo invited Nankya to her home to play and it became a daily routine.
P7	They played together everyday.
P8	They swam every weekend.
P9	And their friendship lasted until they were older.
