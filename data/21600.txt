title:	Inyaryenge Bakame
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21600.png
desc:	Bakame ikomeje ubucakura. Soma wumve uko ikomeje kubeshya izindi nyamaswa.

page_text:

P1	Bakame yahuye n'Inzovu yikoreye akabya k'ubuki.
P2	Inzovu yahetse Bakame ku mugongo wayo. Bakame yariye ubuki bwa Nzovu.
P3	Basanze Imbogo mu mugezi irimo kunywa amazi.
P4	Bakame yashatse kumenya ufite imbaraga nyinshi hagati y'Inzovu n'imbogo.
P5	Sungura iribwira iti, "Nzi uburyo ndamenya ukuri".
P6	Bakame yaziritse Inzovu umugozi mu nda ku ruhande rumwe.
P7	Irongera izirika Inzovu umugozi mu nda kurundi ruhande.
P8	Bakame ijya kwihisha mu giti iravuga iti, "Ngaho umwe nakurure undi"
P9	Imbogo n'Inzovu birakurura. Inzovu ukurura Imbogo.
P10	Inzovu ikurura Inzovu. Habura n'umwe utsinda.
P11	Bakame ikeba umugozi ahegereye Imbogo. Inzovu ihita igwa hasi.
P12	Bakame ikomera amashyi Imbogo iti,"Ni wowe utsinze!Ni wowe ufite imbaraga!"
