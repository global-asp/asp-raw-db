page_text:

P1	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng aicodokin. I can do this. I can hop.
P2	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng ailam. I can do this. I can jump.
P3	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng aikosi. I can do this. I can dance.
P4	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng aiduk. I can do this. I can build.
P5	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng adɨɨkɨn akongu. I can do this. I can wink.
P6	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng awoere. I can do this. I can sing.
P7	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng abolya. I can do this. I can play.
P8	Apedori ɛɔng aswam nʉ. Apedori ɛɔng aisyom. I can do this. I can read.
