page_text:

P1	Yaho khaale, Ingokho nende Likongolo bali abalina.
P2	Inyanga ndala, batsia okhubaya lisubo.
P3	Ingokho yekalilanga lisubo okhunywa.
P4	Likongolo lianywesia amapao kataru.
P5	Ingokho yasinyikha muno.
P6	Mana naamila Likongolo.
P7	Ingokho naakanila nyina Likongolo.
P8	"Khoonye mama! Emililwe," Likongolo liopa olungu khurula munda ye Ingokho.
P9	Likongolo liunyila Ingokho obubi nikachira Ingokho yahulila okhusala.
P10	Ingokho naachaka okhukholola.
P11	Niyasala Likongolo.
P12	Khulunya inyanga ilia, Ingokho nende Likongolo na abasuku.
