title:	Amazaribwa ga lynne

desc:	Lyn yankora bingi kufuna ebintu bwo okwetenkatera amazaribwa ge

page_text:

P1	Lynne,Oupa Karel na Anton batenkateka ebintu byona ebwa ebwokugenderaho ahaamazaribwa ga Lynn.
P2	Anton na Lynne banza kuteka omuganti.Oupa Karel natenkateka ebwokusikisa
P3	"leka tutabule ebwokusikisa omubakuri empago"kyangabwa Lynne.
P4	Anton yatabura ebwokusikisa yabisisa
P5	"Omuganti niguremera hati" kyagabwa Oupa Karel.
P6	"banwani bawe biza"kyagabwa Oupa Karel.
P7	Anton yatekyereza ngu ekirabo kyonkubaza nikyiremera ahakuba ni kihango
P8	Ekaba eripillo kandi neremera kakye.
P9	Anton akabanatekyereza ngu ekirabo tikyikuremera aha kuba nikikye.
P10	Rikaba rili ibale risigire kandi rikaba nirilemera munonga.
P11	Abanna bakagira ngu kilo emwe eya amabare neremera nka kiro emwe ya ebwoya.
P12	Omugati gwa Lynne bakarwosya.
P13	Lynne akanyinga munonga
P14	swenkazi Mavis akabuza Anton kutebereza ekyirabo kyeye okukyabire nikiremera.
P15	"kyikaba nikyiremera nka omuganti ogubayokyize"kyagabwa Anton.
P16	Burwomwe akagaba,"amazaribwa amarungi Lynne".
