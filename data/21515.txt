page_text:

P1	nifashishije izuru ryange
P2	ndumva udukweto twanduye
P3	ndumva uturabo twiza
P4	nifashishije amaso yanjye
P5	ndabona umupira wo gukina mu kirere, kure mu muyaga
P6	ndabona agahuku kishimye cyane
P7	nkoresheje umunwa wanjye
P8	ndanywa ku mazi yanduye yo mu nyanja
P9	ndanywa ku mutobe uryohereye w'amacunga
P10	nkoresheje ikiganza cyange
P11	ndafata ikiganza cyawe
