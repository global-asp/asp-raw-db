P1	One day Josh and his brother Nouh and his rooster Doodl were at the park until Josh said''i do not feel well'' so they whent home.
P2	When they got  home Josh went to his room why he fellt bad and then he saw that his legs were long but he fell asleep but then Nouh and Doodl came in and they were shoked!
P3	But they soon got used to it and always go to the park the end.
