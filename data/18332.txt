title:	Manu and the Lion

artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	English

desc:	How a man escapes from certain death.

page_text:

P1	One day,  Manu was walking.
P2	At that time,  a lion was resting in the grass.
P3	Manu was full of fear.
P4	The lion woke up.
P5	Manu climbed a tall tree.
P6	The lion waited for Manu under the tree.
P7	Manu fell asleep.
P8	He fell down from the tree.
P9	 The lion ran away.
P10	Manu started to run in the same direction as the lion.
P11	When he realised,  he changed and run away.
