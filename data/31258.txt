desc:	Omukulu Mawata ali nokapa omugezi ogwebeeta Anisha.

page_text:

P1	Okukyalo kyange, kuliku omukulu Mawata. Omukulu omawata ali nokapa ogwebeeta Anisha.
P2	Omukulu omawata obwayaba okukola, aleka olwigi nga lwigule.
P3	Anisha atyama omu mulyango ku lingirira nyumba.
P4	Abomuliraana abembwa olwiza okumpi, Anisha abagudyagho.
P5	Ombwa apyata naazwagho.
P6	Omuliraanwa obwakubba okoodi okulwigi, Anisha abba mwetegefu okukwadula.
P7	Owomuliraanwa acuuka era napyatagho.
P8	Anisha olulumwa enzala, aigalagho olwigi.
P9	Anisha naalya emere ye.
P10	Nga amalire, Anisha atuumira okumufaliso na gona, "aaaa! aaaa! aaaa!"
P11	Omukulu omawata olwira, Anisha yejumuula neisanyu era namwanyiriza eika.
P12	Omukulu omawata no anisha batyama aghansi, era buli omu nasoma amawulire.
