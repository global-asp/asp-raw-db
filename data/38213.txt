P1	Then the rabbit come with the hen and told the dog also that 'A man was going to kill the giraffe then he heard that news and the dog also started  running with them..
P2	Then suddenly the rabbit came with her friends and saw that bad man came to kill the girrafe.
P3	Then the man thinking how to kill the giraffe. Rabbit and her friends saw all things ..
P4	Then the man goes to kill the giraffe..
P5	He goes slowly because if he not goes slowly the giraffe will be understood that the danger is coming..
P6	The giraffe has sensed the a man is coming to kill .When the man had came to kill the giraffe.The giraffe was trying to running but the man had caught the  giraffe 's tail
P7	Then he was going while holding the giraffe 's tail .The giraffe is running with the man.
P8	At last the had passed by killing the giraffe. The rabbit and their friends saw all things..
P9	Then he informed the king of the jungle. The king of the jungle had send an elephant to kill the man.
P10	Then elephant goes to kill man but elephant had many power than a man .So elephant was successful to kill the man.. The End.....
