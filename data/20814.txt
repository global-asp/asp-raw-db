page_text:

P1	Nusha was a little girl who wasn't very good at anything.
P2	But when she was 12, she found she could fly.
P3	She flew to school when she was late.
P4	She flew to her grandma's house on weekends.
P5	She flew to the market because it was quicker.
P6	She flew across the sea to church.
P7	She flew along the beach and saw people kissing.
P8	She flew up to get coconuts from trees.
P9	People paid her to help fix their roofs.
P10	All her friends asked her how she did it.
P11	"You have to think very, very hard," she said, "and you have to really really want to do it."
P12	One day she met a boy who could fly too.
P13	They started to go out together.
P14	Maybe they got married and had children in the end.
