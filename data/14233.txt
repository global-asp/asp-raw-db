page_text:

P1	Wayeqala ukuya emarikeni uIzipho.
P2	Wayebonisa umama wakhe zonke izinto ezazithengiswa.
P3	Izipinatshi, namazambane.
P4	Wabona abantu abanizi emarikeni.
P5	Babejikeleza bethenga izinto yinto abazifunayo.
P6	Wamangaliswa kukubona izinto ezininzi ezithengiswa apho.
P7	Emva kokuba bejikeleze imini yonke bagoduka. Wayezele luvuyo uIzipho, ecinga yonke into ayibonileyo emarikeni.
P8	Umama wakhe wafika wenza isidlo esimnandi. UIzipho wayencokolela uKwanda ngemarike.
