P1	Hello I am Warona and this is my life
P2	My life is full of entertainment and friends
P3	I do lots of extra murals it is very fun I do swimming karate and also I Do Ballet and I also do piano they are very fun for me and I also do netball and my favourite extra marold is Netball because it's makes you stronger.
P4	I have a wonderful family and wonderful friends they are very kind to me and I appreciate that I have four people in my family my brother my mum and my dad my brother is 4 years old
P5	I go to a nice school it is Weltevreden Park Primary School it has a lot of people there and I have lots of friends there it is so much fun there no bullies are allowed there can I have a cool teacher she is very nice to us she doesn't yell
P6	My brother also goes two extra murals like he does soccer and swimming and he is very athletic he likes to run a lot and play with his friends and the most thing he likes to do is watch TV because he likes watching paw Patrol and also sweet
P7	My mum work at a bank she works very hard he works all day she also helps my dad with his work because he is also very busy every day he goes out of the house and goes to work but my mum works at home because of the Corona virus
P8	My dad has his own company that's why he is so busy he has to run it every day he's always working the only time he gets to relax is Sundays or Saturday like us going to school we go for the whole week and then get to rest for the weekend
P9	When I grow up I want to be a principal that's why I work very hard I am in grade 4 and I am 9 years old because I skipped a grade so I turned mine I know principal work is very hard but I still wanna be a principal because my grandpa was
P10	So that is my life and you can see it's full of excitement and entertainment and friends and see you soon bye bye
