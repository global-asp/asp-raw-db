page_text:

P1	Kwakahhaligwa zwikwele kukayi tiyende kanyi.
P2	Atitjazana nebamwe bana.
P3	Ndoyemula bakhwinya bangu.
P4	Ndohuba yenda kukwele.
P5	Mme  abazogadzikana nekwabasekulu. @Banogwala bakubuwongelo.
P6	Bazwadzi bedu abazogadzikana nentha yezwinhu zwinjinji.
P7	Banhu bose abazogadzikana nehla bhakhwa nebugwele gwebhembolufu.
P8	Mahhuba ose towha nekwe bhembolufu. Katshene lingapela!
