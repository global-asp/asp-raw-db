desc:	Listen to the many different sounds that animals make.

page_text:

P1	Crickets chirp.
P2	Mice squeak.
P3	Snakes hiss.
P4	Dogs bark.
P5	Goats bleat.
P6	Cows moo.
P7	Lions roar.
P8	 And snails make no sound at all.
P9	Babies babble.
P10	 And children talk.
