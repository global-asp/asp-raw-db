desc:	Wambwa yayagaana amagi kumbali kwomwiga

page_text:

P1	Wambwa yayagaana amagi kumpi nomwiga,"kisolo ki ekirese amagi ghano?"
P2	Yata amagi mu nsawo ye.
P3	Wambwa yatwala amagi ghaka.Yagata kumpi na muliro.
P4	Wangoina yabuuza wambwa,"obwaine ku amagi gange?"Wambwa yakoba,"busa."
P5	Wangoina yabuuza buli kisolo,"mubwoine ku amagi gange?"
P6	Amagi gayalulwa kalala ku kalala.
P7	Wambwa yagha wangoina omuto emere ntono.
P8	Wangoina yawulira ba wangoina abato balira.
P9	Wangoina yakuba Wambwa no mukira.Wambwa yalumukagho.
P10	Wangoina yabinga Wambwa.
P11	Wambwa yalira,"nsonhigha.Tyategera nga amagi gago
P12	Wangoina yasasira Wambwa. Yaaja okuwuga na baana.
