title:	Magezi azuula ekilooto kye

desc:	Omulenzi yeeyagaana ku lugudo na abaana abandi. Luno lufumo luleeta ebilowoozo

page_text:

P1	Mu kibuga kilala, yaliyo abalenzi abokunguudo.

Eyabasingamu obuto nga bamweeta Magezi.
P2	Magezi bwe yali agheza emyaka etaanu egy'obukulu, abazaire be baafa.

Yaja okuba ni dhaadha ghe Baamu.
P3	Dhaadha ghe Baamu yali musaadha mubi.

Magezi yalumukawo era yaaja ku ngudo.
P4	Obulamu ku ngudo bwali bukalangafu.

Abaana bano baasabirizanga busabirize.
P5	Lwali lulala, Magezi yaayagaana akatabo ake enfumo ku isolongezo lye bikaba.
P6	Akatabo kaalimu ebifaanani ebyo omuvuzi ghe enhonhi.

Magezi yaayenda okuba omuvuzi owe enhonhi asinga yo obunene.
P7	Lwali lulala Magezi yayagaana Tomasi.
P8	Tomasi yakwala Magezi ni mikwano gye okufuna emere.
P9	Magezi yaakoba,"Nze tiidhi kusoma."
P10	Magezi  yaakobera Tomasi," Nalumuka okuva mu maka ga dhaadha Baamu. Yali musaadha mubi.
P11	Tomasi yaabuuza Magezi, "Oyenda okwega okusoma?"

Yaatoola akatabo akayaaka ake enfumo yaakagha Magezi.
P12	Magezi yaalowooza ku ky'okuja ku isomero.

Yaidhukira dhaadha Baamu kye yakoba, "Oli musiru. Ezira kyodhi."
P13	Magezi yatya inho.

Tomasi yamukoba,"Totya. Oidha kuba mugezi mukalamu."
P14	Tomasi yaatwala Magezi era na abalenzi abandi mu maka ga abaana abaziraku bazaire.

Bali boonaboona basanhufu.
P15	Magezi yaaja ku isomero.

Yaayenda inho okuba mu isomero. Yakola bulungi inho.
P16	Lwali lulala, Magezi yaakoba Tomasi,"Nja kuba muvuzi gha nhonhi asinga obunene!"
