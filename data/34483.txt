page_text:

P1	Lona ngu Azizi.@@Azizi ngumdoli wami.
P2	Ngiyamtsandza Azizi ngobe uhlale amoyitela.
P3	Nangicedza kuvuka ngigeza buso bami. @@Bese ngifuna Azizi.
P4	Nangitfola Azizi ngimkama tinwele takhe.
P5	Nangidla sidlo sasekuseni ngifunta Azizi.
P6	Nangidla emaswidi nginika Azizi lelinye.
P7	Kepha Azizi akalokotsi adle liswidi lakhe. @@Ngiyatibuta kutsi kungani!
P8	Ngihambahamba egcekeni ngikhe timbali.
P9	Nginika Azizi imbali. @@Kepha akalokotsi atsi, "Ngiyabonga."
P10	Ngigeza Azizi ahlanteke ngaphambi kwekutsi make angigeze.
P11	Ngigcokisa Azizi ingubo lebovu ngobe ngiyawutsandza umbala lobovu.
P12	Nangiya kuyolala, ngibeka Azizi eceleni kwami.
