desc:	Wantye u Natabo hembe. Ishima vihi un je a yevese a tume toho. (U Sha utar kpa ngu).

page_text:

P1	Wankwase ne ka Natabo. Ngu a anigbianev mba nomso ateratar.
P2	Mbamaren nav na un wantye! A soo un kpishi.
P3	Yange igen yô, angbianev nav hembe wantye la.
P4	Natabo nenge a wantye na ga. A mindi mliam.
P5	Natabo yevese tume toho. A za unde sha gôgôkon.
P6	Mbamaren nav va gba zamber. "Hungwa shin nya."
P7	Anigbianev nav tôô icam, "Se zamber we, hungwa."
P8	Natabo naha ve a naha tar.
P9	Huror u Natabo kpa va a icam, "Mzamber we, hungwa!"
P10	Huror u Natabo wa icam gbem tsô a hungwa.
P11	Natabo vea huror ne yem ken ya nongu.
P12	Hanmô or ember er Natabo hide. Natabo zua a wantye uhe.
