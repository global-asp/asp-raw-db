page_text:

P1	Elupa yaakhole ni Ekonya yaapaka onthamwene aya. @@Yaawo yaathata vamoha. Yaalya vamoha. Ichu soothene yeerano vamoha.
P2	Nto nihiku nimoha, Ekonya yaatepa wiiceliwa enahaawela okhwa.@@Ekonya yoonnaaya wi yootepasha wiiceliwa yaaya wanamuku, yoowo aloca ariki waachunaka openuwa okhuure emece ya elupa.
P3	Ekonya yiiwalaaya yaatepa othanana.@@Hiyaarino makhalelo makina aweera voohikhala omukhuura enthamwene awe Elupa.
P4	Nto Elupa yaavinyaalaaya eyaaka omoona nthamwene aya. @@Ekonya, yaamuyara nthamwene aya oculi, emukeriha vachiyani nawiisha na muhice.
P5	Ekonya yaahi: "Namuku ookileela wi kaachunaka openuwa kookhuure, nthamwene."
P6	Elupa yaahaakhula eriki: "Hooye nthamwene, ohikikhuure miyo kootepa oyeva. Owehererye elukuluku yavakhaani kinamuwiichana amunna. Yaawo aatepa winnwa okipwaha miyo. Vaavaa onamuvona."
P7	Hiihaa Ekonya yaahi: "Ari woowi amunnaa?"@@Elupa yaahi: "Ari mwiiri ole ori weevilivili vale."@@Hiihaa Ekonya yaahi: "Waakelele wi miyaano kaakhuure."
P8	Elupa yeele yaacupha oculi weiwe wa Ekonya eya makupaaya. Echimaka ewela mmwirini, ekhala mwemmo. @@Ekonya yaawehererya. Ehinoona Elupa erwaaka.@@Ala yaari makuchuwelo a onthamwene.
