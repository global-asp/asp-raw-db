P1	Once upon a time there were wild animals living together. They were living happily, there were no hunger. Their king was a Lion. Everyone love a ruling of king Lion because he treated everyone equally. He was loved and respected by everyone
P2	One day a king Lion heald a meeting with his people to tell them about a competition he was gonna host in his palace he said to them" My people I'm a calling you today to tell you about the competition I will have in my palace"
P3	Every animals was happy. They were clapping their hands and said "yebo nkosi, sakukhetha njengenkosi yethu kuba sikuthembile. He continue to address them as said" the winner of this competition will get a chance to be a king's Advisor"
P4	Every animal was so happy about the price to won in the competition. He said to them" the competition will be a running competition, you will be running a distance of about 500km so everyone must go and prepare their selves" they were happy
P5	Every animal seem so happy about what they heard except a tortoise. He was so slow than eny animal but that did not make him to give up because he sat down and think about a plan of how he can win this competition
