page_text:

P1	Leti tindlulamitsi, tinetintsamo letindze, tidla emacembe etihlahla. @unjani umbala wendlulamitsi?
P2	Lena yiNdlovu, indlovu lendvuna inetimphondvo. tindlovu tinetindlebe letindze kanye nemphoko lomudze, tisebentisa lomphoko kukha emacembe ekudla.@unjani umbala weNdlovu?
P3	Lena yiNgwenya, ihlala emantini emifuleni nobe emadamini. inemlomo lomkhulu futsi lomudze kanye nematinyo lamanyenti futsi lakhaliphile. unjani umbala weNgwenya?
P4	Leli Libhubesi, linematinyo lamadze futsi lakhaliphile. libhubesi lidla inyama kuphela futsi lihlakaniphile uma litingela. unjani umbala weLibhubesi?
P5	Lona nguDvoye, usilwane lesitsatfwa njengeluphawu lwelive iUganda. Dvoye unetinyawo letindze kanye neboya lobudze enhloko.@unjani umbala waDvoye?
P6	Lawa ngemaBuffalo, atidlela tjani, atihlalela eceleni kwemifula naseceleni kwemadamu.@unjani umbala waDvoye?
P7	Lawa ngema Zebra, anembala lomhlophe nalomnyama longemalayini, atidlela inyama netjani.@unjani umbala weZebra?
P8	Nangu umhlobo lombili wetilwane. yiMphisi kanye nenyamatane. letilwane leti tinetimphondvo letihle. @tingaki timphisi kulesitfombe? unjani umbala wenyamatane?
P9	Ngutiphi tilwane lotibonako lapha? @Titfolakala kuphi?@Ngusiphi silwane lesidliwako kuleti?
