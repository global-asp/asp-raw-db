page_text:

P1	When the man with the long legs wanted to go to sleep, his bed was too short.
P2	So he had to buy a new, longer bed.
P3	When he rides his bicycle to buy breakfast, he bends over and it hurts.
P4	So he has to buy a bigger bicycle.
