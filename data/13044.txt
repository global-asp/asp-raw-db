page_text:

P1	Sahamba sonkhe saya endalini yasemakethe.
P2	Babe watsengela bhuti tibuko letinhle. Make wangitsengela ikepisi legcame ngembala loluhlata sasibhakabhaka. Umntfwana yena watfola liswidi.
P3	Endleleni leya ekhaya,  kwaba nemoya lomkhulu. Waphephula ikepisi yami.
P4	Ikepisi yami yabambeka egaleni lesihlahla lesikhulu lesidzala.
P5	Ngakhala kakhulu. Angitange ngidle nekudla kwakusihlwa.
P6	Ngalobo busuku,  kwaphuma inyanga yabuka ikepisi yami esihlahleni lesikhulu lesidzala.
P7	Inyanga yalinganisela ikepisi yami. Yamamatseka ijabulile. Nami kanjalo ngamamatseka.
P8	Ngelusuku lolulandzelako nakuphuma sikolo,  make wanginika ikepisi lensha lebovu. "Itfunyelwe yinyanga," kwasho make.
P9	Ngalobo busuku,  tsine nenyanga sagcoka emakepisi etfu samamatseka. Besijabulile.
P10	Ucabanga kutsi lilanga liyasidzinga sigcoko?
P11	Combela ngifake yiphi ikepisi lomuhla!
