desc:	Oscar the Shark has a problem with his eyes – he can’t see well enough to catch fish. His friend the seahorse encourages Oscar to eat his carrots

page_text:

P1	UMazinyo wayenenkinga yaamehlo. Wayengakwazi ukubona ezinye izinhlanzi.
P2	Kodwa wayengakwazi ukudla izaqathi zakhe.
P3	USivinini ihhashi lasolwandle wathi "Mazinyo dlana izaqathi zakho."
P4	Kodwa uMazinyo wayengakuthandi ukunambitheka kwezaqathi.
P5	"Izaqathi zizokusiza ukuthi ubonekahle" kusho uSivinini.
P6	UMazinyo wabe eseya kwadokotela. UMazinyo noSivinini balindela udokotela Shakes.
P7	Udokotela Mazibuko uthi,  "Izibuko zamehlo zizokusiza ubone kangcono."
P8	UMazinyo wayengathandi ukugqoka izibuko.
P9	Kodwa izibuko zamsiza ukuthi akwazi ukubona ezinye izinhlanzi. Manje useyazidla izaqathi zakhe nezinhlanzi.
