desc:	Enjura ekaba eri omugyisha oba omucheeno?

page_text:

P1	Omu meezi meingyi, eiguru rikaba ry'omire. Lomongin,omwamuzi w'enjura, akagamba nana bwaruhanga bweye. Abantu bakaba berundaana aheru y'enju ye,barinzire amakuru gamatsiko n'anomujinya mwingyi.
P2	Kuyaijire aheru, akahamiza abantu ngu omuhangi akaba naaza kubaha enjura omu bw'eire butari bwahare. Obundi bakabaasa kuhinga ebihingwa byaabo.
P3	Abaana bakaba bazaanira aheruobwe turiyo turinzire enjura. Nabw'anyima omw'izooba niryo rimwe Tukareeba ekyikyu kyihango kirikweera  omu buruga bw'ezooba. Nk'amanya ngu nebikyu byenjura.
P4	Abantu b'akaba bakyiriyo nibateekateeka aha ky'omwamuzi yabaire agambire. Mama akayomba namaani ngu," reeba ebikyu byeiragura. mweije omunda.
P5	Enjura ekashesheka ahansi.
P6	Tukaba turinzire enjura ngu obundi ekagwa! Omukubanza  tukashemererwa. Kwonka enjura terarekyeire aho.
P7	Amaizi gakaba gari burihamwe. Orutindo orw'abeire rurikugeita eky'aaro kyeitu nana eitaka erihango ruk'ogyebwaho enjura.
P8	Amaju g'akogyebwaho omutuunga. Ogw'abeire guri omugyisha gukahindukamu ekyisiraani ahariitwe.
P9	Bury'omwe akaba ayetekatekire kubyara. kwonka hati bakaba batarikubaasa kubyaara. Abantu b'omukyaara ky'eitu bakaba batahereire njura kwonkwa hati bakaba batakyigyenda. Tukaba tuteine rutindo rw'okutwambutsa. Abeingyi bakaba bateine maka oba owaabo.
P10	Kndi tukaba tweine ekyizibu ekyindi! Tukaba tweine goonya zirikweibira burihamwe. Ekyi kyikaba kyitakabahoga. Tukashoberwa.
P11	Tukaba tutakyibaasa kugura kyintu ky'oona ahabw'okuba amaduuka gakaba gari eseeri y'omugyera. Abaana bakaba batakyibaasa kuza kweega ahakuba amashomero gakaba gari eseeri y'omugyera.
P12	Eky'abeire kyeine kuba omugyisha  Kyikahindukamu ekyisirani aharitwe. Lomongin, omwamuzi w'enjura nawe akahwa amaani by'amutama. Ayabeire ashemereirwe wenka  akabaari Kapus oyine enjangu munaana.
