desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	Obu etome ediopet olot aimat akipi.
P2	Opote ikorin iyarei da aimat akipi.
P3	Ajaasi ibuwin iwuni k'akweny awongon, elosete aimat akipi.
P4	Akalia akany k'apegei akanyapei ajaa alosit aimat akipi.
P5	Ajaa aidodokai akanyarei k'agaria akanyauni toma akipi.
P6	Ojaasi itukoi ikanyangon aimat akipi.
P7	Obuni edope kosobwan aimat akipi.
P8	Ingai etiting akere,  aisal emasi akipi.
