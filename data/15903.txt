title:	Kokonyana Le Leeba
author:	Khothatso Ranoosi and Marion Drew
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15903.png
desc:	Leeba lepolosa kokonyana nokeng. Le kokonyane e polosa leeba.

page_text:

P1	Hone ho chesa. Hone ho omeletse. Kokonyana e ne e nyeriloe.
P2	"Ke batla metsi," ho rialo Kokonyana. Hone hosena metsi.
P3	"Ketla batla noka moo eleng teng," ho rialo Kokonyana. "E be ketla noa," ho rialo Kokonyana. Eaba,  "Mutlanyana ore,  o hlokomela hantle,  noka e kotsi haholo!"
P4	Kokonyana ea tsamaea holo batla noka. Ea tsamaea nako e telehele. Ea fumana noka. Ea noa metsi a noka.
P5	Aaiee! Ea oela ka nokeng. "Nthuseng,  nthuseng,  kea kopa,  oee!" ea hoelehelitse ka lerata.
P6	Leeba le lesoeu latla lenkile thupa. Leeba lena lare,  "Ts'oara thupa ena. Ketla o thusa."
P7	Kokonyana ea khona ho hloella thupeng. Leeba lafofa lenkile Kokonyana ena ka thupa. Eaba ke ho pholoha hooa Kokonyana.
P8	"Ke tlameha holeboha leeba," ke Kokonyana eoo.
P9	Bashanyana ba babeli baea nokeng. Bankile liqha. Bane ba batla hoja nama ea leeba.
P10	"Oh,  aee,  ke tlameha ho thiba bashanyana bana baseke ba bolaha Leeba," ho rialo Kokonyana. Kokonyana ena eaba le menahano e meng. Ea qhomela holima leoto la moshanyana e mong. Ea moloma.
P11	"Heleee!" a buoa ka bohale. A etsa lerata. Leeba le lesoeu la fofa. La pholoha.
P12	"Kea leboha Kokonyana,  kea leboha!" ho rialo Leeba. Ke qetello ea pale ea rona.
