desc:	Mama nakunda abanabe kandi nayenda bagume bashemereirwe. Aine omwami mulunji. Najenda nabaana be omukatale kandi abareberera batakabyamire.

page_text:

P1	Mama wangye nibamweta Edith kandi nanyegyesa emicwe mirunji.
P2	Nanyegyesa okuba omuyonjo nokuba omwisikyi atebekine.
P3	Mama nanshaba okushaba Ruhanga ntakabyamile ahabwokuba Ruhanga ni tata.
P4	Nangira ndalegye deelu anshweka omukitanda.
P5	Mama waitu nakunda muto waangye. Namutwala aishomero bulizooba.
P6	Twena nitukunda mama munonga kandi nimulunji.
