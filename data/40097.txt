page_text:

P1	Worm was surprised that Tortoise was carrying a huge house.
P2	Tortoise was surprised that Worm was walking without clothes.
P3	"Why are you carrying your house everywhere?" Asked Worm.@@"I am slow. I cannot run back home in times of danger," replied Tortoise.@@"It's the house that slows you down! Leave it behind," said Worm mockingly.
P4	"And where are your clothes, Worm?" asked Tortoise.@@"Clothes can slow me down when running away from danger," said Worm.@@"Clothes can protect you from danger. Wear something!" Tortoise commanded.
P5	Suddenly, Raven appeared from the sky.@@Tortoise quickly retreated into his house.@@Worm disappeared into the soil.
P6	Tortoise and Worm were both safe.
