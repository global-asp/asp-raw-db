desc:	It is a story about a shamba.

page_text:

P1	This is Papa's shamba. It is big and beautiful. Papa plants so many crops in it.
P2	This is a mango tree Papa has planted. The mangoes are ripe and appear juicy. They make me salivate.
P3	Mama and I usually visit the shamba to get some maize for roasting. At times, Papa goes to sell the maize at the market place.
P4	Papa's shamba has got big pumpkins and tomatoes. Mama is staring at one of the giant pumpkin.
P5	This are neighbor's cows feeding on papa's sweet potato vines. Papa is so frustrated as he chases them away with my brother.
P6	Papa has also planted sugarcane.I love hiding inside them while playing with my friends.
