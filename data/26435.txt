title:	Wakayima  asendasenda  wandhovu,  ate
author:	Emmanuel  Kunya  and Kyambogo  University

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Mulugero luno twega lwaki wakayima mulumusi inho nengeri gya sendasenda mu mukwano gwe wanovu nokusaga okubi.

page_text:

P1	Wandhovu  ni  wakayima  bali  bamukwano  inho.
P2	Bayagana  eisubi  na  madhi  age  bisolo  byaibwe.
P3	Bayenda  nga  okuzanha  omupira.
P4	Wandhovu  yateba  egolo  nhingi.  Wakayima  tiyali  musanhufu.
P5	Wakayima yabuza wandhovu,"Ki ekikufula omuzanhi omulungi?" Bigere byange.
P6	Wakayima  ni  wandhovu  balumuka  emisinde.Wakayima  yasinga.
P7	Wandhovu yabuza Wakayima, ki ekikufula  omulumusi  omulungi?Bugere bwange.
P8	Wandhuvu yabuza,Nfunha ntya obegere obutono? Ndakulagaa engeri.
P9	Wakayima yakoba,"yemerera mumuliro."Wandhovu yakikola.yalira,"ndikuya!"
P10	Wakayima yakoba Wandhovu Eno n'engeri yonka ebigere byo gye binatonighamu.
P11	Wandhovu yava mumuliro. Kati bite ghansi.
P12	Enaku  nhingi,Wandhovu yali tasobola kwemerera.
P13	Bweyaterera, yatambula yagya eka mubomumakage.
P14	Wakayima yenenia. yakoba wandovu amusonighe.
