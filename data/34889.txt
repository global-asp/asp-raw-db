page_text:

P1	Fati kwa raka kuma tsah hir huwhi apa.@@Tsah hir kum shi, kuma tsana hara ki thir nathi g3 pala.
P2	Ashine ghir ghel makaranta.@@Fati hir ghel ka ball kuma tsana ka hu hwi.
P3	Tsaka ka makaranta ka kuri, tsa livi ka pajir ni Hawa.@@Hawa pla, "Fati gana hwi sosai."
P4	Fati livi anthi wuthi myani kwata susum.@@Tha, "Thawa miya ni."@@Miya Fati, "Thawa ni tsuwa?"
P5	Fati pla, "Da hara vir ghel da akwa makaranta ashine. E hwi sosai.@@Ya wanka bor-bor, kuma ya kwanga mir."
P6	Miya ni kum shi ka pla, "Kwana isin gana hwi sosai!"@@ "Ya thalaga su kwa miji ka ghi kwa si kulini."
P7	Miyani kuthi mall mamza akwa tum bwallaga a atha kra uh'uh.@@Saka ti mall ni ka hara bor-bor tsa pu antum ka alik ka shina har ta hara. @@Tsapu chimba ka tumatur ka yimi. Tsakathi ta kuri tsa pu kumur thla.
P8	Miya, "Fati pal aka tsa darni ka tsa shina su kwani."@@Fati pla, "Toh, ma."
P9	Fati shina su kwani so dukku.@@Tsa pla, "Sukwani sirra."
P10	Fati shina su kwani so suda.@@Tsa pla, "Sukwani sirra sosai."
P11	Fati shina su kwani so mak3r.@@ Tsa pla, "Sukwani sirra sosai!"
P12	Fati wul hu miya ni.@@Miya ni de na wul ni wa. Tsakwa thli thlali sukwar.
P13	Fati k3ti kum dukku ka dir akwa thum.@@Tsa pla, "Mmm."
P14	Fati j3kti miya ni.@@Miya ni dena wulni wa. Tsakwa thli hi-hir sukwar.
P15	Fati k3ti kum damwa akwai tum n anthi ta dumya.@@Ka tsa pla, "Mmm. Mmm."
P16	Fati j3kti wul miya ni.@@Miya ni dena wulni wa. Tsakwa thli miji a sukwar.
P17	Fati Jak thi k3th kum na mak3r akwa tum ni ka dum y. @@Tsa pla, "Mmm. Mmm. Mmm!"
P18	Miya ni si z3 uh'uh' ni ka thlali, hi-hi ka miiji.@@  Fati ta k3zi chokali sukwar.
P19	Miya ni kum shi ka pla, "Igwaiti ka sukwani Fati."@@Fati ta di sun suna tu ta plawa.
P20	Miya ni putu thlahi, hi-hi kamji ni akwa sukani.@@Thladu chokali ni azhi Fati anthi ta bethi shina sukwani.
P21	Miya ni wul tum ni.Ta wut3 sukwani ka akwa hara ath3 k3ra uh'uh ni.@  @Miya ni bara kum kwa  akwa kuta tum ni. "Tam, suda, mak3r." Miya ni kum mak3r anbil dachi.@  @Miya ni tsadi pla komiwa. Fati tsadi pla komiwa.
P22	Tsakathi Miyani na lada sukwa, Fati tsadi wutu kum wa.@  @Fati pala, "Miya, ni ka tsa hara hakuri. Ya da jakthi harawa."@  @Tsadi jakthi harawa an apani anthi suni kuri.
