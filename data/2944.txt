page_text:

P1	Anikina iintloko ezama ukususa loo mabhabhathane esithi: "Akukho zingwenya apha,  masize neminatha ukuze sibambe la mabhabhathane." Atsho ehamba la madoda. Zadibana zonke iingwenya zabulela zathi: "Enkosi,  Nogolide." "Enkosi mabhabhathane."
P2	Ngemini elandelayo uJoja wabona amadoda aphethe iminatha. Wakhumbula awayekuxelelwe nguNogolide ngeminatha. "Nogolide,  iminatha! Khawuleza uze apha! Amadoda aneminatha ayeza." Zonke iingwenya zaya elunxwemeni lomlambo. Amabhabhathane athi ngcu emiqolweni nakwiintloko zeengwenya.
