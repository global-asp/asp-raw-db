title:	Granny's cellphone

desc:	The story is about a girl calked Mmaborse who lived with her granny. The granny grew tall maize fields that when she came back from school it was difficult to locate her until a cellphone came to the rescue

page_text:

P1	Granny lived with her granddaughter Mmabotse in the village called Ga-Nchabeleng.Mmabotse's mother worked far from home and only came back at the end of the month.Mmabotse loved her grandmother very much.
P2	Granny had a large maize field.She went  to the field everyday to check on the plants.Her plants grew very tall that you could not see granny.
P3	Every time Mmabotse came back from school she will call her granny loudly but she could not hear her.She would get worried when she could not find her granny.
P4	Mmabotse asked her mother to buy a cellphone for her granny.She told her mother she will teach her granny how to use the phone, so that she can finds her quicker in the fields.Her mother did so.
P5	Mmabotse taught her granny how to answer the phone.She said"Granny, when you hear the phone ringing, press the green sign and put it next to your ear and answer".
P6	She also said to het grandmother"After speaking, press the red sign".She practised with her granny many times.
P7	Mmabotse came back from school and called her granny on the cellphone.Her granny answered the phone and came back home.Now it was easy to find granny without shouting.
P8	Granny was very happy to be able to use the cellphone  and said to Mmabotse"I love you my granddaughter, you also have a good heart just like your name.Granny was now able to call Mmabotse 's mother and other people.
