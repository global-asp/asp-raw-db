page_text:

P1	My name is Buhle and my younger sister's name is Busisiwe we saw a rainbow in the sky she asked this rainbow is very very beautiful
P2	My young sister said you can draw let's go to the park she said then we went to the park
P3	When we got to the park we played hide-and-seek we got tired and then rested we started to talk about the rainbow that we saw In The sky
P4	then we got home we took the colouring book and then we started to colour after that we saw butterflies it wes so beautiful the end
