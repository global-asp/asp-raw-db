desc:	Mister Kiio bekanelikati lelihlakaniphile labelibitwa ngekutsi nguSafi.

page_text:

P1	Endzaweni yakitsi, kuhlala uMnumzane Kio.@@UMnumzane Kio unelikati lelibitwa ngekutsi nguSafi.
P2	Nakaya emsebentini, uMnumzane Kio ushiya umnyango uvulekile.
P3	Safi uhlala emnyango kuze agadze indlu.
P4	Nakusondzela inja yakamakhelwane, Safi uyayetfusa kute isuke.
P5	Inja iyabaleka.
P6	Nakanconcotsa emnyango makhelwane, Safi uyalunga kute amhwebhe.
P7	Makhelwane uyajika bese uyabaleka.
P8	Nasalambile Safi, uvala umnyango.
P9	Safi udla kudla kwakhe.
P10	Ngemuva kwaloko, Safi uyazuba ahlale kusofa bese uyalala, "Ngrr! Ngrr! Ngrr!"
P11	Nakabuya uMnumzane Kio, Safi uyatelula ajabulile futsi uyamemukela ekhaya.
P12	Umnumzane Kio kanye naSafi bahlala phasi, futsi nguloyo naloyo ufundza liphephandzaba.
