desc:	Washeog ne ka te kaan bokoabid n kyena na ne ba ŋu kuom. Gbiamed ya te kyena ne o ŋuh

page_text:

P1	Wɔbog kokɔ n ya ne ko ŋu ko'om.
P2	Kpenkpareh seyi n ya ne se ŋu ko'om.
P3	Loleh seta ne ni'ih senaahe me n ya ne ba ŋu ko'om.
P4	Impalas banu ne deeh sewoobe n ya ko'om la zi'i.
P5	Kpenkpareh sewopoe n zuut ya ko'om la ne.
P6	Kulɔya anii ne zimi awae n dugit ko'om la poone.
P7	Gbeamer dekɔ n bo'om. Ɔ me boat ne o ŋu me.
P8	Ɔn n zuut gbeamer e? Wɔbog kokɔ n ŋuut ko'om ne gbeame.
