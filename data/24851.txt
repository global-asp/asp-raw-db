desc:	Amaka agomusaadha atafaayo galumwa endhala.

page_text:

P1	Eira einho, waliiyo omasaadha ye baayetanga Yoweri mu kyalo Kabukye.
P2	Omusaadha ono ti yafangayo kweterekera mmere ya biseera bya mu maiso, yoona yoona yagyonoona.
P3	Mu kyagi ti mwasigala ghaire.
P4	Ebisimbe bya duuma byaniara era byakala.
P5	Kikunta ow'amaani yaagingula ku kyagi akasulya.
P6	Abaana baalwanira obumere obutonioli obwaali bulekeibwa mu buseepiki.
P7	Maama w'abaana yaagwa ku itaka nga abbologa tiyeebuuza, "kyennaaliisa abaana olunaku olwiraku kinavaagha."
P8	Bbaabba w'abaana atyama ku balaza nga alowooza ebyomumaiso.
