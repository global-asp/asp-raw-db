P1	Mimi and her friends swim every morning in the stream. @@They love swimming in running waters.
P2	Terfa and his siblings play by the riverside. @@They love playing in open spaces.
P3	The cock crows. We know when to wake up by cockcrows.@@It wakes up Ngutor and his father who go to the farm every morning.
P4	Dooga and his brother love watching the singing birds. @@The birds build nests in the trees in the compound.
P5	My mother sits in the shade of the tree. @@She loves the breeze that makes the tree sing and dance.
P6	My little sister loves watching the twinkling stars. @@They surround the moon and listen to the moon telling them stories.
P7	My grandfather tells us stories every night when the moon is bright. @@We play many games when the moon is full.
P8	How lovely and beautiful to experience nature! @@Everybody loves nature.
