title:	Mukukulume Na Didingwe
author:	Yvonne Mulovhedzi
artist:	Mango Tree
lang:	Tshivenda
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/14718.png
desc:	Mukukulume wo vha u khosi ya dziphukha u swikela didingwe li tshi wanulula vhukwila ha mukukulume.

page_text:

P1	Kale-Kale phukha dzothe dzo vha dzi tshi dzula dzothe ḓakani. Mukukulume wo vha u wone khosi ya hone.
P2	Mukukulume wa vhudza phukha dzothe uri nama ire ntha ha thoho ya mukukulume yo itwa nga mulilo. Nga zwenezwo phukha dzo vha dzi tshi ofha mukukulume.
P3	Maduvhani hayo mulilo wo vha u tshi itiwa nga dzithanda. Duvha linwe mvula ya na vhura ha nngwe ha nukala. Didingwe ya balelwa u itela muta wayo mulilo.
P4	Didingwe ya ruma vhana uri vhaye u humbela mulilo u bva kha thoho ya mukukulume.
P5	Mukukulume wo vha u songo vuwa zwavhudi,  wa humisa vhana vha didinngwe vha sina mulila. Didinngwe ya sinyuwa zwihulu u vhona vhana vho vhuya vhe sina mulilo.
P6	Vhusiku honovho mukukulume wo edela didinngwe na vhana vhada u tswa mulilo kha thoho ya mukukulume. Didinngwe ya dobedza mahatsi o omaho nnda ha nndu  ya mukukulume.
P7	Didingwe ya  vheya mahatsi o omaho tsini na thoho ya mukukulume. Mahatsi asi ṋange mulilo. Didinngwe ya fara thoho ya mukukulume. Thoho ya mukukulume yovha i tshi khou rothola!
P8	Vhana vha didinngwe vha fara thoho ya mukukulume vha pfa hu sina mulilo. Muṱa wa didingwe wa thoma u fhela nga maseo.
P9	Thiseo tsha didinngwe tsha vusa mukukulume. Mukukulume wa vusa thoho kha didingweu tshi khou vhidzelela,  wa ri "Ni fanela u shavha. A ni ofhi?"
P10	Didingwe ya fhindula ya ri,  "Ro fara kale thoho yau a u kuni u ita mulilo! Zwino ri khou ya u vhudza phukha dzothe dzi ḓo u sinyutshela."
P11	Zwi kukwana zwo mbo  shavha na mukukulume wa shavha. Zwikukwana zwa tuwa dakani zwa ya u dzula na vhathu.
P12	U bva zwezwo khuhu dza thoma u ofha didinngwe dza dzula na vhathu u itela tsireledzo.
