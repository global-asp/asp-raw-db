P1	Once upon a time@There live a Girl called Fatimah Abubakar Zanna.
P2	8
P3	She is a very brilliant Girl, that she teaches her friends at school.
P4	One day, she told her Mom, "Mommy, someday I will be a Doctor In shaa Allah.
P5	Fatimah thought her friends almost everything.
P6	One day, she came back from school and said to her Mom "Mommy, I think I want to become a pilot"
P7	Her Mother said "Fatimah, do as you wish. I am proud of you"
P8	Fatimah was very happy and danced all day. @@The End.
