desc:	Man ododo pa lurem aryo moo ma ono gi poko pye ngwen.

page_text:

P1	Cuburia gini ked agulu ono gitye lurem aryo ma tek adada. Gu bedo karacel ki yom cwiny pi mwaki madwong adada.
P2	Nino moo acel, cuburia owaco ki agulu ni kong gi ci lim larem gi cwan.
P3	Agulu owaco ite ni, "An pe atwero wot. ka teke awoto abi too woko pyen an agoro".
P4	Cuburia obito agulu paka naka oyee.
P5	Iyoo ka ceto, cuburia ocee opoto i wii agulu, agulu opoto piny oginye oo weng.
