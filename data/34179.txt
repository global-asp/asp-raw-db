page_text:

P1	Apwiya ti mukukhula aka. Hayiivo enii ekithowe.
P2	Annakiichuuwiha onikuwani na munyepu.
P3	Onnakikeriha omaahini awuuramela.
P4	Annalipiha okumi aka. Annakukhula mumphironi yooreera mu ncicimiho na nsina naya.
P5	Naari keecaka mpopani mwa muurushi wookhwa,
P6	nkoovaru yoonanara, ntakhara nyu muri ni miyo.
P7	Ekopo anyu ni ekumputu anyu, iha sinnakihakalaliha miyo.
P8	Munnakirehererya yoolya amwiicani aka ekoonaka.
P9	Munnakoociha murwaaka makhurwa, ekahi aka enayeehereya.
P10	Aayi mahala anyu ni osivela wanyu sinnakichara mahiku oothene ookumi aka.
P11	Kinarwa kiichaala mpa mwa Apwiya mahiku oothene.
