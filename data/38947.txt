title:	UMpovani noNdlovu
author:	Tshedza Tlhako

desc:	Izilwanyana zifuna inkosi

page_text:

P1	——Ngalanga lithile, izilwanyana zangogana zanomhlangano zifuna ozozilawula phakathi kwazo.Kweza zonke izilwanyana kunye nezinambuzelayo.Into efika yethusa, kukungabikho kweendlovu ekubeni iziziilwanyana ezinkulu kunezinye.
P2	Umhlangano waqhubeka naxasibeni kubonakala zingekho iindlovu.“Sizoqhuba nevoti noba zingekho iindlovu. Sizobeka inkosi namhlanje,” watsho uSikhova.
P3	Izilwanyana zavotela ukuba inkosi kuzoba nguMpovani.UMpovani wavumela phezulu nakubeni heyi zothwala omxinzi.“Luxanduva olufaka umsebenzi omninzi,” waacinga.
P4	Iindaba zoba uMpovani wenyulwe uba abeyinkosi zahamba ngokutshetsha.Zifikile ezindlebeni zenkosana yeendlovu.Yamcunukisa kakhulu inkosana.
P5	Inkosana yeendlovu yaya kufuna ukudla ehlathini.Ngokuziqqaja, ngamakhulu amatshamba yayizazi uba inamandla futhi ingenandaba kwanampovani.Enoba heyihamba kumgade wazo na kakade, yona heyidlisela.
P6	Ngamandla esiyinawo, inkosana isiphule isiphunzi kwaphuma iimpovani ezininzi!INkosi uMpovani uchola ithuba angene kuwo umboko.“Kumele uhloniphe wonke umntu,” watsho uMpovani eyiluma inkosana yeendlovu.
P7	Inkosana yeendlovu yagxwala yacela utshweli ngelithi izokwenza kwasani exelwa nguMpovani mayingayibulali.Indlovu yagoduka itshwencile ingaphathanga kudla, suka zee mekenxe ezinye iindlovu zixakiwe uba kwenzekeni.Yachaza konke okuyehleleyo kunye nezethembiso azenze kuMpovani.
P8	Ezinye iindlovu zagcobela ukuthola inkosana yazo iphila.Zangabi sabi nandaba nokulawula kwabo ke nguMpovani.Kanjalo ulawulo lucholwa zimbovani noba zincane.
