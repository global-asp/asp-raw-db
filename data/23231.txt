page_text:

P1	Kikere na Njoka banywani.
P2	Kikere yagamba, "Ija tugende hamu tubungire nyina nyowe."
P3	"Webale," Njoka yagamba. "Noha ara lorera amaka geitu?"
P4	"Ninyija kurolera amaka ganyu," Monkey yagamba.
P5	Kikere yatekera nyina enswa munsaho.
P6	Kikere na Njoka basimbura murukyakya.
P7	Baatangana obusoro. Njoka natiina.
P8	Kikere yalinda munywani we.
P9	Bahika omwa nyina nenjara.
P10	Banaaba, bayetekaniza kulya.
P11	"Njoka, chaali ikara kurungi," Kikere yagamba.
P12	Njoka yalengaho kwikaara kurungi beitu tali kikere.
P13	"Tinkusobora kwikaara nke'kikere," Njoka yagamba.
P14	Njoka takuhuura.
P15	"Tokunyetegereza. Leka ntahe."
P16	Njoka abihiirwe. "Nkeire ngizire Kikere munywani wange."
