page_text:

P1	Kolong kaangorot, alomito ng'itieng daang kaapei.@@Arai Kuruk ka Kwee ng'ipaalon luapolok.
P2	Abu akamu tomin. Tong'op ibore imujete ng'itieng.
P3	Toloto Kwee ka Kuruk akisak ng'imujen.@@Apot toriamut akiring. Kisiek Kuruk akinyam bon.
P4	King'olik Kwee Kuruk enyami akiring' bon. Abu ng'esi toruakin Kuruk.@@"Kaari kori kimiekini?" king'itakin Kwee.
P5	"Wuaae, nikiyar kare! Noinoi, nikichaka yayie ekipetot," kimaimai Kuruk.
P6	Tatacak Kwee, "Mam nyakachari ayong iyong yayie ekipetot."
P7	Atiakunit Kwee achakar Kuruk alokipetot.
P8	Abue Kwee tachaka Kuruk alokipetot.@@Sodi towuo ng'esi alokwas ekipetot esaki aking'olikin akwaan a Kuruk.
P9	Kaneni, abu Kuruk kibelukaar eroko nyenang'a kwap ka aporor.
P10	Abu Kuruk todo lokitoe.@@Tokienyikinos Kuruk ka Kwee.
P11	Tolimok Kuruk Kwee tama, "Kelanyuwari ayong iyong kotere aos ayong."
P12	Tatacak Kwee Kuruk tama, "Iyakari iyong kotere kemiekini ayong iyong tolot."
