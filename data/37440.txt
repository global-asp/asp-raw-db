page_text:

P1	Tingi@  wakalikukkala mumunzi @  abaneene bakwe
P2	Bumwi@  buzuba kwakasika basilumamba mumunzi.
P3	Tingi@  aba baneene bakwe bakatija kuzwa mumunzi.
P4	Bakayuba@  mushokwe limwi kwasiya.
P5	Basilumamba@  bakajoka alimwi kumunzi ooye.
P6	Baneene@  bakamusisa Tingi kunsi amatuvwu.
P7	Uumwi@  wabasilumamba wakalyata aakali Tingi, peele Tingi wakaumuna wiii.
P8	Nobakaunka@  basilumamba, Tingi abaneene bakayubuluka. @  @ @@@Mukaumuumu bakasobelela kuya kumaanda
