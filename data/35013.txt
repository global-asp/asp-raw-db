title:	Mbuyawu ndi Galu ajambuwa ndi kupenta
author:	Pilirani  Kamanga and Winfred  Mkochi
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Chitonga (Malawi)
level:	Read aloud
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/35013.png
desc:	Mbuyawu ndi Galu ŵe pa ubwezi. Mbakunozya ndipu ŵakondwa kuchitiya vinthu vevosi limoza.

page_text:

P1	Uyu ndi Mbuyawu.@@Uyu ndi Galu.
P2	Mbuyawu ndi Galu ŵapenja kujambuwa. @@Mbuyawu ndi Galu ŵapenja kupenta.@@Mbuyawu ndi Galu ŵapenja Kujambuwa chithuthuzi.@@Galu ndiyu wangwamba.@@Wanguto pensulu ndi pepala. Pepela laki ndakulingana mbali zosi.
P3	Chakwamba Galu wajambuwa chinthu chakuzungunya nge zila.@@Chakuzungunya nge zila ndi liŵavu laku mbuyawu.@@Bama! Ntchipusu!
P4	Sonu wangujambuwa matilayangu ngaŵi pachanya paku chakuzungunya nge zila.@@Ma tilayangu ngaŵi ndingu makutu ngaku mbuyawu ndipu wangujambuwaso tilayangu ifipa mukati mwachakuzungunya nge zila chiya.@@Tilayangu ifipa ndiyu mphunu yaku mbuyawu ndipu wangujambuwa kamuzeli pachanya pamphunu.
P5	Sonu wangujambuwa masu ndi vikopi vaku mbuyawu.@@Masu nditimadonthu tiŵi.@@Vikopi nditimizeli tiŵi.@@Bama! Ntchipusu.
P6	Sonu wangujambuwa mulomu waku mbuyawu.@@Kamuzeli ndiku mulomu.@@Kaoneka nge chilembu cha 'w' ndipu wangujambuwa weya waku mbuyawu.@@Weya utatu kumazye ndi weya so utatu kumalyi.
P7	Chakujambuwa sonu chaja kamanavi kumala.@@Wangujambuwa janja ndi malundi ngaku mbuyawu.@@Manja ndi malundi nthimizeli. Wangujambuwa vikumbu vakumalundi vaku mbuyawu.@@Vikumbu vakumalundi ndi vo vakuzungunya ndipu sonu wangujambuwa muchila waku mbuyawu.@@Muchila ngutali.
P8	Paumalilu wangujambuwa timizeli pa luŵavu laku mbuyawu ndipu wangulipenta mtundu wa olenji.@@Chakujambuwa chamala.@@Awa! Ntchakutowa.
P9	Sonu ndi nyengu yaku mbuyawu.@@Wanguto pensulu ndi pepala.@@Pepala nda lekitangu.
P10	Chakwamba mbuyawu wangujambuwa chakuzungunya nge zila.@@Chakuzungunya nge zila ndilu liŵavu laku galu.@@Bama! Ichi ntchamampha.
P11	Sonu wangujambuwa tinthu tifipa pachanya pa chakuzungunya nge zila.@@Tinthu tifipa nditu makutu ngaku Galu ndipu wangujambuwa chakuzungunya mukati mwaku chakuzungunya nge zila chiya.@@Chakuzungunya chifipa ndichu mphunu zaku Galu ndipu wangujambuwa timizeli pachanya pa mphunu.
P12	Sonu wangujambuwa masu ndi vikopi vaku galu.@@Masu ndingu timadonthu tiŵi.@@Vikopi ndivu timizeli tiŵi.
P13	Sonu wangujambuwa ko kaoneka nge chilembu cha 'w' ndipu wangujambuwa timadonthu kuzunguliya mulomu waku Galu.@@Titatu kumazye ndi titatu kumalyi.
P14	Chakujambuwa chaja kamanavi kumala.@@Wangujambuwa manja ndi malundi ngaku Galu. @@Manja ndi malundi ndi timizeli ndipu wangujambuwa vikumbu vakumalundi vaku Galu.@@Vikumbu vakumalundi ndi vo vakuzunguliya ndipu wangujambuwa muchila waku Galu.@@Muchila uwoneka nge maluŵa.
P15	Paumalilu wangujambuwa tinthu takuzungunya paliwavu laku Galu ndipu wangulipenta mtundu wantcheti liŵavu laku Galu. @@Chakujambuwa chamala.@@Awa, Ntchamampha!
P16	Ndaluta mbuyawu.@@Ndaluta chithuthuzi chaku mbuyawu.@@Ndaluta Galu.@@Ndaluta chithuthuzi chaku Galu.
