P1	I was my mother's only child.
P2	My mother made sure that I went to school.
P3	My mother likes to take me to the market.
P4	My mother and I enjoy shopping together.
P5	My mother loves God.@@She encourages me to pray every morning.
P6	My mother loves me very much.
P7	My mother loves my cousin, too. @@He comes to visit every weekend.
P8	At night, my mother always tucks me into bed before I sleep.
