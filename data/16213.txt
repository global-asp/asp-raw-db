title:	Mo Fẹ́ràn Kàwé
author:	Blessing Williamson and Victor Williamson
artist:	Wiehan de Jager and Vusi Malindi
lang:	Yoruba
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Tálo fẹ́ràn kàwé? Àwa ọmọdẹ́ fẹ́ràn kàwé gidi gidi gan ní èdè Yorùbá.

page_text:

P1	Mo fẹ́ràn kàwé.
P2	Mo máa n kàwé pẹ̀lú ọ̀rẹ́ mi.
P3	Olùkọ́ mi ń rán mi lọ́wọ́ láti kàwé.
P4	Tani mo lè kàwé sí i?
P5	Mo ń kàwé pẹ̀lú àwọn ọ̀rẹ́ mi.
P6	Tani mo lè kàwé sí i?
P7	Bàbá àti bàbá bàbá mi ń ṣiṣẹ́.
P8	Tani mo lè kàwé sí i? Mo lè kàwé fúnara mi!
