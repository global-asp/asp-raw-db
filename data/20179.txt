desc:	Nonyaŋ ne sanguine n gyaar taaba ba beehid ne. Daansier ka nonyaang suih daa nyii ka o daan tom jeriged.

page_text:

P1	Nɔbil ne sanguin aa zuat.
P2	Ba kab ken ŋmera bɔɔl.
P3	Nobil n gur gool!
P4	Sanguin diya! Nobil eng Sunguina.
P5	Nobil vol Sanguin.
P6	Nobil kyereg Sanguin ma.
P7	"Soŋ mem mmaa!" Sanguin kaahya.
P8	Ba'a gba'a Nobil.
P9	Sanguin pu le maha.
P10	Nobil ɔg Sanguin bah na.
P11	Sunguina zuya.
P12	Nobibeh ne Sunguina aa dataah.
