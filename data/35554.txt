page_text:

P1	My name is SimNom
P2	I have a friend called Gyeyok who comes from the village of Nok in Kaduna State Nigeria.
P3	Gyeyok and I love to read our ABC
P4	But our other friends prefer to play all-day.
P5	Even though playing is good for the body, reading and playing help us to improve our reading skills while playing keeps us strong and healthy.
