title:	Dumpootirol Mootaaji
author:	Abdulaahi Aadamu Bappa and Dayyibu  Abdullahi
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ɓiɗɗo gorko e yiʼa no mootaaji ɗiɗi ndumpootiri.

page_text:

P1	Nyalaande feere min bee minyiraawo am e min njaha haade laawol.
P2	Min ngiʼi mootawa mannga e nga dilla dow laawol.
P3	Moota pamara maa e waroya dow laawol.
P4	Laanyoowo mootawa mannga boo e wolwa bee telefon.
P5	Kaay-kaay!  Sey ɓe ndumpootiri.
P6	Dottaʼen bee moota law-law maɓɓe bee taadiiɓe ngaroyi ngam wallugo.
P7	Mootaaji mawɗi cemmbiɗɗi ɗiɗi ngaroyi. Ɗi pooɗi mootawa bee moota pamara.
P8	Min ngiʼiiɗum. Min kultorii.
