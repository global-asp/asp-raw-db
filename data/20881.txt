title:	Mautoto Asanu Ndi Awiri Autawaleza
author:	Lusaka Teachers
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Chinyanja
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20881.png
desc:	This story introduces numbers as well as colours. (Also available at Level 2.).

page_text:

P1	Mautoto asanu ndi wiri muutawaleza.
P2	Yoyamba ndi fira.
P3	Mnyamata abvala malaya yo fira.
P4	Yaciwiri ndi lalanje.
P5	Kodi ndi ciani comwe cili ndi utoto wa lalanje mcithunzi ici?
P6	Yacitatu ndi cikasu.
P7	Kodi ndi cicani comwe cili ndi utoto wa cikasu mcithunzi ici?
P8	Ya anai ndi msipu?
P9	Kodi, ndi ciani comwe cili ndi utoto wa msipu mcithunzi ici?
P10	Yacisani ndi biliwira.
P11	Kodi ndi ciani comwe cili ndi utoto wo biliwira mcithunzi ici?
P12	Cisanu ndi cimozi ndi indigo.
P13	Kodi ndi ciani comwe cili ndi utoto wa indigo mcithunzi ici?
P14	Cisanu ndi ciwiri ndi vaileti.
P15	Vaoleti ndiwo utoto wa bwino kwambiri. Kodi ukonda utoto wotani?
P16	Ja'mbulani mautoto asanu ndi awiri a utawaleza?
