title:	Eneikuna Enkalaoni Pee Eitajeu Enturkuluo
author:	Leonard Kotikash
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Maa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7864.png
desc:	Etoyíó enkôp néata enkálaóni enkuré. Nélo olkejú aing’orú enkáré náôk

page_text:

P1	Enkolong nairowua te atua entim natoyio.
P2	Neitu eok enkalaoni enkare too' nkolong'i kumok.
P3	"Kayieu olkitong'ati le enkare,  odou ata te embeneyeio."
P4	Kake etoyio sii ninye enkoileelio.
P5	"Tenamatum enkare,  naa kaye," neishir enkalaoni. "Kalo olkeju latoning'oyie."
P6	"Ekinap olkeju aya," neliki karbobo ng'en.
P7	Kake keata enkalaoni enkure naleng. "Kaye tenamaok enkare."
P8	Nelo enkalaoni aing'oru olkeju.
P9	Nedung atua inkujit naatoito,  nedooyo too' inkeek naatoito.
P10	 Ometoning'o ilopir le enkare naruko.
P11	Neng'obu enkalaoni enkare nairobi.
P12	Neshipa naleng. Neitu edol olopiro le enkare elotu.
P13	Nenyok enkalaoni ajo aibung inkujit,  nkeek kake ewa enkare.
P14	"Entaretuoki. Entaretuoki sii ake."
P15	"Tasioki,  takedo," nejoki enturkuluo naibor,  eibung'ita olng'osila te enkutuk enye.
P16	"Malo omatiaaki enturkuluo ashe. Katon aanyu omeeu aok enkare."
P17	Ore nabo olong egira aanyisho,  neponu inkayiok are naata inareta oo motonyi.
P18	"Etii enturkuluo naibor sapuk nalotu oshi ene aok enkare," nejo enkayioni nabo. "Ekinya taata teipa."
P19	"Maisho kuna ayiok ear enturkuluo naibor. Kake kara kiti,  kaa aas?"
P20	Ore ina kata ake nedou enturkuluo naibor tolchani alotu aok enkare.
P21	Neyiolou enkalaoni enaas.
P22	Neipidoki enkeju e enkayioni,  neony oleng.
P23	Neipid enkayioni!
P24	Neing'asia enturkuluo naibor. Neisik alo eserian.
P25	Neija eikuna enkalaoni teneisho enturkuluo naibor enashe te enkaraki naitajeuo.
