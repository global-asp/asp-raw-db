title:	Isigubhu sikaMagezi

desc:	UMagezi wayedlala isigubhu sakhe kuthuleke cwaka.

page_text:

P1	UMagezi wayeqala asishaye kancane isigubhu sakhe esinombala omnyama nomhlophe.
P2	Abantwana babeqoqana, bacule behambisana nesigubhu.
P3	Bacula, "Ikasi Masingatani i nkata mani? I nkata mfana kuloni."
P4	Omama bayadansa bazungeze ngenxa yesigubhu sikaMagezi.
P5	Obaba baginqike emhlabathini, baye phansi naphezulu, benanela isigubhu sikaMagezi.
P6	Omama banyakazise izinqe futhi balilizele. Wonke umuntu uyasithanda isigubhu sikaMagezi.
P7	UMdungwazi agingqe amehlo akhe enze nemidanso ehlekisayo.
P8	Obaba bashaya amakhwela futhi benqekuzise namakhanda behambisana nesigumbhu sikaMagezi.
