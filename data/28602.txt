page_text:

P1	Mwami seepiki ni Mwami nsuwa baali bakagwa balungi inho.Baali mwisanhu okumala emyeeka miingi.
P2	Lwaali lunaku lulala, Mwami Seepiki yaakoba Mwami Nsuwa bagye bakyalireku mukagwa waibwa Mwami Sowaanhi.
P3	Mwami Nsuwa yaakoba"Tisobola kutambula, ndhi munafu,bwantambula nfa.''
P4	Mwami Seepiki yalimbalimba Mwami Nsuwa era kunkomerero yaikiriza okuja.
P5	Mukuja, Mwami Seepiki yatyereera era yaagwa ku Mwami Nsuwa era yaameneka bukutukutu.
