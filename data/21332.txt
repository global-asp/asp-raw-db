desc:	Peace is something you feel in you so that you can get on something you want because it creates hope in you.

page_text:

P1	Peace is very important thing in our daily life.
P2	whoever does not have peace does not plan or do anything right.
P3	If you don't have peace, you lose the living hope and you feel that whatever you do yields nothing.
P4	Peace is the root of living hope, wealthy and sustainable development.
P5	A family with peace is so strong that nothing can demolish or divide its members.
P6	Friends with peace love each other a true love and they live without any disruption.
P7	A country with peace does not have wars and what the citizens will, they gather their force and earn it.
P8	May peace be with you all!
