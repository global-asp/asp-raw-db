desc:	Lyn aholele emirimo mingi nga yetekehera omuholo kwaye kwohwibulwa

page_text:

P1	Lynne, Cukka Karel nende Antony bali batekeha ebindhu byo omukholo kwa Lynne kwo'hwibulwa.
P2	Antony nende Lynne bakyodeha cake.  Cukka Karel abasomera ehola ya cake
P3	"Hahutabule ebiholwa bino mu bakuli." Lynne yabola
P4	Antony yatabula ebihola bya cake yakyuha bilisindu asi
P5	"Cake yisidoye," Cukka Karel yobola
P6	"Abekya bawo bolere" Karel yabola
P7	Antony yakala ati esirabo shamberi shali esisito hulwohuba shali esihongo
P8	Yale epilo handi yali enyangu muno
P9	Antony yakala ati esirabo shohubiri shali eshangu hulwohuba shali esidudu.
P10	hali ahakina halai handi hali ahasito
P11	Abaana babola bati ekilo ndala eya makina yaka ndede ekilo ndala eya moya.
P12	Cake ya Lynne yali yilalire
P13	Lynne yali nendalo
P14	Ssenge Mavis yabola Antony ateberesa obusito bwesirabo shaye.
P15	"Yi sidowa koti cake yilalile," Antony yabola
P16	Bulimundu yabola, "Amebulwa malai Lynne"
