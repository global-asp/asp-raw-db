page_text:

P1	Amasela amabili ayefuna ukuntshontsha umumbu esivandeni somlimisi ngobunye ubusuku. Ayengazani lamasela.
P2	Isela lokuqala lafika lavuna isaka lomumbu.
P3	Elesibili langena esivandeni somlimisi latshiya ibhayisilili lalo ngaphandle.
P4	Amasela la adibana ensimini. Bahlaba umkhosi," Ungubani wena?"
P5	Bethuka bonke babaleka bagcwala indawo yonke.
P6	Isela lakuqala latshiya emva isaka lomumbu.
P7	Isela lesibili latshiya emva ibhayisikili lalo.
P8	UMlimisi uthe efika wasebenzisa ibhayisikili ukuthwala isaka lomumbu.
