desc:	Bbaabba anyiya obusera obwe hyomumugamba.

page_text:

P1	Zama getegeha ohutiina hu somero.
P2	Bbabba anyiya obusera obwe hyomu mugamba.
P3	Zama pogaloma ati, ''Maama aŋosa obusera bulaŋi''.
P4	Obusera buŋa Zama amaani aga tiina hu somero.
P5	Obusera bunyiiye.
P6	Bbabba ajuha amabeere mu mu hihopo.
P7	Bbaabba peega ti obusera buno bwendamo sukaali obundi.
P8	Obusera bwa maama buŋooma ohuhiraho obwa bbabba.
P9	"Bbaabba peeho sukaali owundi?" Zama gasunga.
P10	Bbabba gaŋa, "Wo-o-o bbabba otaaye cumbi mu busera bwange!"
P11	''Wu bbaabba otaye cumbi mu busera wange!''.
P12	Zama gabusa semwaana ati, "Maama anahagole ŋaali?"
