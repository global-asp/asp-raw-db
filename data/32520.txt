title:	Big-head Ant and lazy Pigeon
author:	African Storybook
artist:	Joe  Werna
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Those who work hard can be kings. Those who sing well can persuade kings.

page_text:

P1	Big-head Ant was a good farmer.@@He lived in the ground with his family.
P2	They worked very hard on their farm.@@Ant's family lived well.
P3	Pigeon was Ant's neighbour. @@Pigeon lived in a tree with her family.
P4	Pigeon and her family did not farm.@@They rested all day.
P5	Pigeon and her family were very good singers.
P6	"Share your food?" Pigeon asked Ant.@@"No! Go and farm!" replied Ant.
P7	Pigeon flew down and began to sing, "Ant is king!"
P8	"Ant is king! Ant is king! Ant is king!"@@Ant loved this song.
P9	After they ate, Pigeon and her family flew up to their tree.
P10	The next day, Pigeon mocked Ant. @@"Do kings have big heads?" she laughed.
P11	"You are ungrateful and lazy!" complained Ant.
P12	"I am sorry!" said Pigeon, and began to sing, "Ant is king!"
