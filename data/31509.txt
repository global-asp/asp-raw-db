page_text:

P1	Gemo na Nelson oya li aamwayinamati.
P2	Gemo okwali hai kosikola esiku kehe, ihe Nelson ka kwali ha yi.
P3	Tate gwao okwali halongo mondoolopa. @@Meme gwawo okwali halongo moshikunino.
P4	Esiku limwe tate gwe okwali athigi iimaliwa poshitafula. @@Nelson okwe yi kuthapo.
P5	Tate gwe okwali ageya noonkondo.
P6	Na Nelson okwa fadhukapo. @@Okweende no kweende esiku awuhe.
P7	Okwa kuutumba kohi yomuti uusiku alihe.
P8	Omumwainamati Gemo okwe mwaadha kohi yomuti, no kwe mu kuthapo ee temufala kegumbo.
P9	"Omolwashike wayakapo po iimaliwa?" tate gwe tapula.
P10	"Omolwashike wafadhukapo?" meme gwe ta pula.
P11	"Ombili, ombili," osho Nelson ati, no kwa galula iimaliwa.
P12	Oye mu papatele no ya li yanyanyukwa natango.
