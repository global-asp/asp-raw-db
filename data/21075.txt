title:	Magezi Na Kasiira

desc:	Hadithi ya kuchekesha inayohusu urafiki na ujanja unaotokana na mtama.

page_text:

P1	Hapo zamani, Magezi na Kasiira waliishi kwa kushiriki chakula pamoja.
P2	Magezi alisema, “Hebu tulime pamoja namna tunavyokula pamoja.”
P3	Magezi alimwambia Kasiira, “Wewe lima mimi nitapanda.”
P4	Magezi pia alimwambia Kasiira, “Wewe panda mimi nitapalilia.”
P5	Wakati mtama ulipokomaa, Kasiira alimwuliza Magezi kuvuna.
P6	Magezi alijibu, “Wewe vuna. Mimi nitahifadhi kwenye ghala.”
P7	Magezi alikuwa mvivu.  Kwa hivyo, Kasiira alimpatia kifungu kimoja tu cha mtama.
P8	Magezi alichukuwa kile kifungu cha mtama.  

Alikutana na Kuku.
P9	Kuku alikula ule mtama.  

Alimpatia Magezi yai.
P10	Magezi aliwakuta watoto wekicheza. 

Walilivunja lile yai.
P11	Watoto wale walimpatia Magezi embe.
P12	Magezi alimkuta Kasuku. Kasuku alilikula lile embe kisha akampatia unyoya.
P13	Ziwa liliuchukuwa unyoya. Likampatia Magezi maji.
P14	Magezi aliyatumia maji kuzima moto uliotumiwa kuchoma makaa.
P15	Wachoma makaa walimpatia Magezi shoka.
P16	Magezi aliwakuta wachinjaji.

Waliivunja shoka.
P17	Magezi alipatiwa kichwa na mkia wa fahali.  

Tazama alichofanya Magezi!
P18	Alipiga mayowe, “Fahali wa mfalme amekwama matopeni!”
P19	Watu walikuja kumsaidia. 

Magezi aliwaambia, “Vuteni mkia na kichwa.”
P20	Magezi aliwaambia watu,  “Nipeni ng’ombe wenu nimpelekee mfalme.”
