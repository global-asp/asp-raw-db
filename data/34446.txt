page_text:

P1	Like most children Michelle and her friends loved rainbows
P2	They always like to go to the park just to admire their bright colors
P3	One day Michelle saw the rainbow next to her house,she was so happy and she called her mother to come and see
P4	She started naming the rainbow colors “orange,red,yellow,green,indigo,violet and blue”she said
P5	Rainbow has 7 beautiful colors
P6	Michelle like the rainbow so much and she started drawing clouds with rainbow colors
P7	She then said to the rainbow” rainbow i am really fond of you would you come and stay at my house”
P8	The rainbow smiled at Michelle,she became so happy
P9	She bought bought the rainbow some flowers
