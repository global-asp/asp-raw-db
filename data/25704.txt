desc:	Kantono mughala mulungi ate ayenda inho okuzuula byataidhi

page_text:

P1	Yaliyo omughala ng'eriinalye ni Kantono. Yayogeranga inho.
P2	Lwali olwo, songaawe yaalwala.
P3	Eigulo eryo, Kantono yatwalira songaawe emere.
P4	Kantono yayagaana Wampiti  eyali yeefwire omuntu.
P5	Kantono yaamukoba, "Ndi kutwala mmamba, magi n'amata."
P6	Wampiti yeekomba ensaya bwalowooza ku mmamba.
P7	Wampiti yalumuka yaasooka Kantono okutuuka ku nhumba ya songaawe.
P8	Bweyatuukayo, yamira songa wa Kantono. Yaamala yeebwika bbulangitiye.
P9	Kantono yatuuka yaayeta, "Songa, oli gha?"
P10	Kantono yaabona omuntu eyeebwise mu bbulangiti enene.
P11	Kantono yaabuza, "Songa, lwaki amatuugo manene inho olwaleero?"
P12	"Songa, lwaki amaisogo manene inho?"
P13	"Songa lwaki omunwagwo munene?"

Wampiti yaamumira!
P14	Ni bweyali mu nda, Kantono yeeyongera okubuuza Wampiti ebibuuzo.
P15	Wampiti yeetamwa ebibuuzo bya Kantono. Yaamusesema.
P16	Abataka baidha baayamba Kantono ni Songaawe.
