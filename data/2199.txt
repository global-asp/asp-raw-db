desc:	Olukano lwa abaana lwe ing’ombe ye shifwanani ye eshindu shiokhubaira mushiteere

page_text:

P1	Ino ne ing'ombe. Ne Ing'ombe imboywa!
P2	Mushiteere,  ne Ing'ombe busa.
P3	Ipurusia eshifwanani shieliyoni khulusia.
P4	Nomba ikhupa omupira kwefilenje.
P5	Ne mushiro,  neing'ombe yokhuuchisia! Khonye.
P6	Ionia obulamu.
P7	Nende okhwirana nobusuku.
P8	Ne,  busa notimba tsimoni tsio tatatata,  nosaba eshiawikomba.
P9	Ing'ombe eyo inyala okhwitsa okhukhucheniyakho wesi!
P10	Kawere.
