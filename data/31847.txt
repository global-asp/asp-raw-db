desc:	Nakehi spills the beans and gets whats coming to him.

page_text:

P1	Nihiku nimosa, Nakehi ni mwaar'awe yaahirowa omuxikurya Nipapariya.
P2	Atthu awo vaaphiyalyaya yaahaakheliwa saana.
P3	Ntoko yoolimalela y'ephattu, yaahieveliwa mulyakhu.
P4	Yaahilyasa vamosa ootheene aya.
P5	Esumana ekina, nave Nipapariya aahirowa ni mwaar'awe omuxikurya nthamwene awe Nakehi.
P6	Vaaphiyalyaya, Nakehi khiyaamphwannye.
P7	Owo aaronwe oreya miraakho sawe wiira ohophe ekhwali.
P8	Nakehi vaatthikenlyawe owaani, aahimoona nthamwene awe. Owo aahitteeliwa murima.
P9	Vano, owo aahirowa opuro waapeya amwaar'awe.
P10	Aaphwannye yaapeyaka epwiri wiira yaavahe aletto aya.
P11	Okhala wiira murakho awe aareiye nipuro naarenle ekhwali owo aahipittula mwaapu, epwiri eyeeheya.
P12	Evaanelaka siiso, ni mwappathani awe.
P13	Nakehi aahiiwa ekhwali yinlaka, yaathoonyerya wiira yohomphwa.
P14	Tthiri ekhwali yaahihomphwa vamuraakhoni.
P15	Naanaanoru aahikuxa mwaalo awe, ohererya muyuununi onarowa. Aahitthimakela, khophwanya ekhwali ehomphwale-ene, ntoko suupuwenlyawe.
P16	Owo aahiphattela ekhwali othukumelaka, aahitaphula vamurookhoni, ovarenle-ene mmatatan ohiivale.
P17	Ntata nikina aakunxe maace.
P18	Aahivenya orowaka owaani, otteeliwe-ene murima,
P19	Nto okhala wiira owo aawanre tooto, mwaalo wiira othikilaka musako wa tooto vakhaani-vakhaani.
P20	Nakehi aanitthapa mwaha wotteeliwa murima, orowaka owannyawe.
P21	Ophiyakaru mmukwipini woowannyawe, musako waahiikittheya. Nto tooto awe aahimorela vathi atthu emoonaka.
P22	Vano okathi waaphavelawe onvarela tooto, ekhwali yaahimusonyowa evava.
P23	Nave maace yaahimorela vathi ephweeya othene.
P24	Nakehi ni mwaar'awe yaahuuliwa muru vanci-ene.
P25	Weemaleloni, yaahikhupeliwa ephwiri yeele yaayehiwe, ererya vamosa n'aletto aya.
