page_text:

P1	My name is Bongiwe. I live in a town called Mpophomeni in KwaZulu-Natal.
P2	I have a small dog called Scruffy.
P3	After school on Fridays, Scruffy and I go to Funda Nenja classes with lots of other children and their dogs.
P4	Funda Nenja means learning with the dog in isiZulu.
P5	Funda Nenja teaches us how to care for our dogs in a kind and gentle way.
P6	Funda Nenja teaches the dogs how to jump over logs and run through tunnels.
P7	Scruffy and the other dogs enjoy their happy times together.
P8	I love Scruffy and Scruffy loves me.
