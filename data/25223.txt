page_text:

P1	Agho olwatuuka, nga ghaabaayo Wangoina eyabanga ku mwiga na abaana be.
P2	Wangoina yaayegesa abaana be obuteesiga bantu. Esonga yali eti abantu bali n'obwongo.
P3	Lwali olwo, ghaabaagho omusaadha eyasala omwiga ogwo.
P4	Omusaadha yaayagaana Wangoina ghagati mu mwiga era Wangoina yaamugema.
P5	Wangoina bwe yali ali kumpi kumwita, Wangoina yaidhukira ebigambo bya ise.
P6	Omusaadha yaasuubiiza Wangoina ati aidha kumuleetera obwongo bw'omuntu.
P7	Wangoina n'abaanabe bakaalindirira ku mmandi kwomwiga okufuna obwongo obwo.
P8	Okutuusa buti engoina dhikaali nnhiivu kuba tidhifunanga bwongo muntu bwe yadhisuubiza.
