desc:	Rre o tshela letswai mo motogong!

page_text:

P1	Lebo o ipaakanyetsa go ya sekolong.
P2	Rre o apaya motogo gore re fitlhole.
P3	"Mme o apaya motogo o o monate!" ga rialo Lebo.
P4	Motogo o fa Lebo maatla kwa sekolong.
P5	Motogo o butswile.
P6	Rre o tshela mašwi,  ka lerato.
P7	"Rra,  ke akanya gore motogo o o tlhoka sukiri e ntsi," ga rialo Lebo.
P8	"Motogo wa ga mme o monate go feta wa ga rre," Lebo a akanya.
P9	"Rre,  ke kopa o mphe  sukiri e nngwe gape" Lebo a kopa.
P10	Rre o fa Lebo sukiri gape.
P11	"Iyo! Rre! O tshetse letswai mo motogong wa me!"
P12	"Rre,  mme o tlile go boela leng gae?" Lebo a botsa.
