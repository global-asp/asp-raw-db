page_text:

P1	Epuri, Mwalapwa ni Emope yaapaka onthwamene aya. Nihiku nimoha, yaaheeca mukwaha aya mukhaaroni.
P2	Yaaphiyalaaya yaayaayawo, mulipa aweeciha aahaaveka ikhorowa wi elive mukwaha aya.@@Emope yooliva.
P3	Mwalapwa aaliva moopwapwiha, ntakhara haarino saakhweleya.
P4	Mulipa aweeciha arehereryaka ikhorowa, wi amucikiherye Mwalapwa, Epuri yaachawa ehilinve echu.
P5	Mulipa aweeciha aananariwa, apwaha ahimunnya Mwalapwa ikhorowa sawe aahanleiyeno.
P6	Vanthowa nenla ophiyerya olelo Mwalapwa oona ekhaaro onniikara awehaka mpuwa wi amoone mulipa aweeciha aahanleno ikhorowa sawe.
P7	Epuri ennachawa yiiwaru oruma wa ekhaaro enoova wi enaphariwa vawi hiyaalinve.
P8	Emope henuukhuwaru yoona ekhaaro erwaaka, eneeca okhwela waya muramphani, ntakhara yoosuwela wi yooliva mwawaakwana.
