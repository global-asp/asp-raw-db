desc:	A story about the roles of the different musical instruments in the Jopadhola culture.

page_text:

P1	Bul and Fumbo.@Teke and Tongoli.@Milege, Sos and Gwara.
P2	Tongoli has many strings. @@Tongoli sings high and sings low.
P3	Fumbo sings loud and clear.@@Fumbo makes people dance at all times.
P4	Dancers tie Milege around their ankles.@@Her melody is loud and sharp.
P5	Bul is made of skin stretched over hollow cylinders. @@He announces events.
P6	Gwara is loud and attracts attention.
P7	Sos always sings with Bul, Fumbo, Teke, Tongoli, Milege and Gwara.
P8	They all chose Bul as their King.@@Bul is in every Jopadhola home.
P9	Questions:@1. Name the instruments mentioned in the story.
P10	2.  How do you call them in your home language?
P11	3. Where have you seen these instruments being used?
P12	4. How are they called in English?@5. Do you play any musical instrument?
