title:	Akhapao Khanje Khali Yena?

desc:	Omusiani akhaba buli habundu akhapao khe. Alakhanyoola?

page_text:

P1	Engosiye akhapao khanje. Ne ekhairwe okhukhanyola.
P2	Ekhabire inyuma wo muliango. Shikhabereyo tawe.
P3	Ekhabire khumusereswa. Shekhanyolirekho ta.
P4	Ekhabire mulwalo weshitali. Kata hosi,  khabulao.
P5	Ekhabire mukari welisanduku. Eshiechesiakho mbu khalimo shibulileho.
P6	Ekhabire mushisuli mwosi.
P7	Ekhabire ano,  alia nende buli abundu,  shenyolirekho abundu wosi tawe.
