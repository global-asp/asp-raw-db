desc:	Nkoko o sireleditse botshelo ba ga Tingi.

page_text:

P1	Tingi o be a dula le nkoko wa gagwe
P2	O ne a tlwaetse go dusa le nkoko wa gagwe
P3	Tsatsi lengwe ga tla lesole
P4	Ba tsaya dikgomo
P5	Tingi le nkoko wa gagwe basia mo lefoleng leo
P6	Ba iphitla mo sekgweng go fitlha bosigo
P7	Jaanong masole a bowa gape
P8	A fitlha Tingi mo tlase ga matlhare
P9	O mongwe wa masole a gata ka leeto l a moja mo godimo gagwe a didimala a re tuu
P10	Fa go sena go sireletsega Tingi le nkoko ba tswa mo sekgweng
P11	Ba siela kwa gae ka bonako
