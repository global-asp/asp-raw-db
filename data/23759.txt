page_text:

P1	Sipho arajya mu mugi hamwe na mushiki we.
P2	Mushiki we arimo arareba imyenda mu iduka. Sipho byamurambiye.
P3	Sipho abona ikintu aragikunda.
P4	Inkweto ziru kugurishwra.
P5	Zose arazigerageza.
P6	Mushiki we amugurira izo gukimisha umupira.
P7	Kubi bari kwihutira gutahaa.
P8	Ku bwo'amahirwe, yahageze ku kibuga cy'umupira kare! Yambaye imkweto.
