P1	Umalume uhleli nabashana bakhe bayadla.@Umalume,"Sipho  no Sbahle uma niqeda ngifuna ukunithuma kwa gogo Dlomo niyomuhambisela isipho "."yebo malume "kuphendula u Sipho ngokujabula.
P2	USipho no Sbahle bahamba endleleni bezixoxela behleka.Phela bayazi ukuthi ugogo angahle abaphe imali yoswidi uma sebebuya.
P3	Kusenjalo bezwa ngento ehoshozayo.uma bebheka abona imfene.USipho no Sbahle bethuka .
P4	Imfene yathi'"lethani leyonto eniyiphethe lapho".Izingane zayinika ngokwesaba.Imfene yajabula ithembe ukuba izothola oluya esiswini.
P5	Nangebhadi ithe isavula yalunywa inja.Abafana bayihleka lemfene
P6	Inja yabaleka ,imfene yathola ukudla yadla .Abafana bavele bakhala Bengazi ukuba bazakuthini ku malume wabo
P7	Abafana baphindela ekhaya batshela umalume ukuba kwenzakaleni .Wayecasukile umalume Kodwa wabaxolela
