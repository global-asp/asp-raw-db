title:	Fantu

desc:	Fantu and her plan is a story of a grade one student. She wants to be a good girl and tells the children about her daily activities.

page_text:

P1	Faantuu Baddiluu naan jedhu. Barattuu kutaa lammaffaati. Umriin koo waggaa torba. Mucaa gaarii fi barattuu cimtuu ta'uun barbaada.
P2	Ganama yammuun irribarraa ka'u mana qulqullinaan deema.
P3	Harkakoo fi fuulakoo nin dhiqadha. Ilkaankoo nin rigadha. Afaankoos nin lulluuqadha.
P4	Cireekoo nyaadhee dabtaraa fi kitaabakiyya qabadhee gara manabarumsaa nin deema.
P5	Karaarratli hintaphadhu. Daandii konkolaataas ofeeggadheen ce'a.
P6	Mana barumsaatti yeroonin argama.
P7	Faaruu fi kabaja sirna alaabaarratti ciminaanin hirmaadha.
P8	Daree seenee yeroo maqaankoo waamamu nin howwaadha.
P9	Waan barsiiftuun Keenya jettus sirriittin dhaggeeffadha. Waan naaf hin galles nan gafadha.
P10	Waan barreeffames dabtarakoorratti garagalchee nin qo'adha.
P11	Hiriyootakoo waliinis gareen nin qo'adha.
P12	Hojii manaafi shaakala addaddaa hojjadhee akka naaf soroorfamu dursee nin dhiyeeffadha.
P13	Yeroo boqonnaa fi laaqanaa hiriyootakoo waliin nin taphadha.
P14	Hiriyootakoo waliin gonkumaa wal hin lolu. Yoon balleessellee dhiifama nin gaafadha.
P15	Barsiiftuu fi warrikoo waayee barumsa kiyyaa yeroo,  yeroon ni mari'atu.
P16	Waan barsiiftuu fi maatiinkoo na gorsan kabajaanin fudhadha.
P17	Akkan barumsaatii galeen booda,  takka taphadhee,  hanga yeroo irbaataatti waanin gugyaa barreesse nin dubbisa. Hojii manaakoos nan hojjadha.
P18	Irbaata booda harkakoo dhiqadhee,  afaankoos lulluqqadheen yeroon rafa.
