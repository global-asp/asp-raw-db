desc:	Once there was a family with three very obedient children. The problem was they were made of wax

page_text:

P1	Abwo kare efamili yarengoo yamenyete nomogoko sana.
P2	Tibarenge korwana nonde bwensi. Nigo barenge gokonya abaibori babo nka sobo amo nase emegondo.
P3	Rakini mbaganeirie kogenda ang'e nomorero.
P4	Nigo baganeirie gokora chigasi chiabo botuko rioka.
P5	Rakini oyomo bwa'bamura nigo aganetie sana kogenda ase omogaso.
P6	Rituko erimo okagania kwaye gokaba okonene. Bamura bamwabo bagachi komoonya.
P7	Rakini tibanyareti,  bagaika gochererwa! Akabera akaera ase omogaso omororo.
P8	Abana baria ba amana bakaigwa bobe korora oyomwabo gakobera nakoera igo.
P9	Nonya nigo begekong'ia bakarosia omoreberio. Bakarenta amana aria aberete bayarosia akaba enyoni.
P10	Bakaira enyoni eri yarenge momura omwabo baka ase egetunwa ekenene.
P11	Nomogaso buna okiamogete,  ekairuruka gekonyagotera na gosirera ime yoborabu boria bwomogaso omambia.
