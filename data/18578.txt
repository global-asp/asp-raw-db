title:	Umusunga
author:	Edith Sikota Habwanda and Eunice Mukonde-Mulenga
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	IciBemba
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Baamaayo tabalipo nomba baataata ebaleipika umusunga uwalucelo.

page_text:

P1	Zama aleeipekanya ukuya kusukulu.
P2	Baataata baleeipika umusunga uwaluceelo.
P3	"Baamaayo baalishiba ukwipika umusunga uusuma!" efyalandile Zama.
P4	Umusunga ulaapeela Zama amaka ayakusukulu.
P5	Umusunga wena naupya.
P6	Baataata baleebika umukaka baleemwentula.
P7	"Baataata,  umusunga uyu ucili uleefwayikwa shuga," efyasosele Zama.
P8	"Umusunga uwo beepika baamaayo waliwama ukucila uwabaataata," Zama efyo atontonkenye.
P9	"Baataata,  ndelombako shuga," efyalombele Zama.
P10	Bashi Zama baamupeele shuga naimbi.
P11	"Mmu! Baataata! Mwacibika umucele uwingi mumusunga!"
P12	"Taata,  nga baamaayo baalaisa nshita nshi?" Zama aipwishe.
