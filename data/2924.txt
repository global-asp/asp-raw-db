title:	Makwalo
author:	Lorato Trok
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Setswana
level:	First words

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2924.png
desc:	Kagang e ke ka ga mosetsana o o lwalalng mme a se kgone go ya sekolong. O kwala makwalo go bua le batho.

page_text:

P1	Beke e e fetileng ke amogetse makwalo a mantsi.
P2	Ka Mosupologo ngaka ya me e kwaletse morutabana wa me lekwalo.
P3	Thabisile o a lwala. O tshwanetse go nna kwa gae.
P4	Ka Labobedi ke amogetse lekwalo go tswa phaposing ya me.
P5	Fola ka bonako,  Thabisile!
P6	Ka Labobedi ke amogetse lekwalo go tswa phaposing ya me.
P7	Ke go taketse setshwantso se. Ka lerato go tswa go Dudu.
P8	Ka Labone ke amogetse lekwalo go tswa go mme.
