page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a small village called Kabubbu. This village was found in the forest of Kasolokamponye.
P2	In Kabubbu village, there were very few people who had never gone to school as there was not even a single school in that area, not even in the neighborhood.
P3	A few of the people in this village were farmers.
P4	Most of them were hunters. They liked bush meat so much.
P5	And others used to cut down trees to make charcoal to earn a living.
P6	The people in this village had no good houses but built grass thatched houses.
P7	One day,  an elder in the village thought of how to bring development to this village. He called some few members and told them about it.
P8	He then asked for the village elders' meeting to share the matter. One member said,  "can we get a good Samaritan to help build for us a school?" But how?
P9	At last they found a rich man called Don Luso, he agreed to start developing this village.
P10	The villagers were very happy to receive the good news about the good Samaritan who had accepted to help in the community.
P11	They organized for a simple thanks function. There was a lot of dancing.
P12	Mr Don promised to set up two structures first for children to use as classrooms.
P13	The problem of unsafe water was also to be solved by constructing boreholes.
P14	He not only looked at education but also thought of health, people needed to have a health center around.
