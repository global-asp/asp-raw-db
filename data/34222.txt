P1	In the faraway veld in Incwadi, there was frog who lived there in the grass with her children.
P2	But the littlest frog wanted to know where her mother went to find food.@@One day, while Mummy Frog was away, the Littlest Frog went out to see the world.
P3	Little Frog jumped high up into the air.@@Then she jumped as fast she could, down the road, back home.@@Little Frog did not look back.
