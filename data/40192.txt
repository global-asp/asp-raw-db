page_text:

P1	Nderingo is a farmer in Moshi .He has a lovely wife called Eva .
P2	They have a large farm and a dog called Tutu.Nderingo and his wife are very hardworking.
P3	Nderingo loves his wife.Both of them laugh and joke alot.
P4	Most of the time,farm work can be tiring .But Nderingo is a hardworking farmer
P5	They are almost at harvest season.Nderingo and his wife support each other through good and bad times
P6	They are a happy family and happy farmers.Despite all the difficulties,they face them together.
