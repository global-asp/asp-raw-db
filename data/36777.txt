page_text:

P1	Once apon a time a boy named Sam and a boy named zane where at a fanfare when they saw aman selling a donkey.
P2	Sam was the first one to ride the donkey.
P3	It was Zack's turn.They went for a pee break.But when they returned the donkey was not there.They thought it ran away.Zack only rode it for two seconds.
P4	Zack shouted its my turn and you don't care of me! me! me! They beat each other and blessed but they found it on the road and said sorry the end.
