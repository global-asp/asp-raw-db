desc:	It is about a tricky turtle and a rabbit. The rabbit was cheated by the turtle through the family of the turtle.

page_text:

P1	Ricky the rabbit and Tera the turtle met by the edge of the river. "No one is capable of beating  me in a race! " Ricky said. He was confident - his smile conveyed that.
P2	"I can beat you, " Tera said. Ricky laughed. Tera said that they would race tomorrow and the destination would be the hill.
P3	Tera concentrated on winning the race. She was not faster than Ricky. She told her family about the race she was going to cheat. She dictated her instructions to them. They all looked the same. Her family members hid in shadows on the path.
P4	The race began. Tera was soon far behind. However, Tera's brother behind a bush in the valley below. When Ricky got close, Tera's brother began to run. He looked just like Tera. Ricky ran as fast as he could along the path.
P5	He reached the top, but Tera's sister was already there. "Well you win, " Ricky said. Ricky never suspected. He had been tricked by a family of slow turtles.
