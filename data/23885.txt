title:	Zama omwa nawa

desc:	Okahokolo hano otaka tengeneke nkene tuna okulonga uunona omikalo

page_text:

P1	Oka mwa meme mati oha ka kotha kwa toka nookondo.

Ohependuka kuyela, oshoka ngaye ondili nawa!
P2	Ongaye handi ethelemo etango.
P3	"Ongoye onyothi yongula," Ma tati.
P4	Ohandii yogo esiku kehe, inepumbwa ekwatho lasha.
P5	Kandinako nasha nomeya omatala, ile nofewa yafa egulu ndjo hai yogo iikutu.
P6	Ma ta thimbulukitha, "Ino thimbwa omayego goye." 

Onda yamukula, "Ahawe, hangaye!"
P7	Konima yokuyoga, onda kundu tatekulu nomumwaina gwameme. 

Ondeya halele esiku ewa nawa.
P8	Ondii zaleke gaye mwene. 

"Ngaye ondakoka paife, Ma,'' tati.
P9	Otandi vulu oku pata oombadhi dhandje noku manga omiya thoongaku dhandje.
P10	Ohesi oshipwiyu kutya oka mwameme mati okeshi oonkundana adhihe dhosikola.
P11	Mongulu yosikola oheningi nawa mukehe shoka.
P12	Iinima mbyoka iiwana ohandiyi ningi esiku kehe.

Ihe shoka ndihole unene osho oku dhana no ku dhana uka.
