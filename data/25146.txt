desc:	Wamaaya yeesiga mukwanogwe Wampologoma omukazi.

page_text:

P1	Wamaaya ni Wampologoma omukazi baali ba mukwano inho.
P2	Wamaaya yali aliisa abaana be bulungi.
P3	Abaana ba Wampologoma omukazi baali bazira byakulya bimala.
P4	Wampologoma omukazi yalingirira obwana bwa Wamaaya yaaloghooza, "Nga bufaanana bulungi! Singa bwaali bwange."
P5	Wamaaya bwe yali aziragho, Wampologoma omukazi yaatwala obaana be ewuwe.
P6	Wamaaya yaja okwira yaayagaana abaana ba Wampologoma omukazi ewuwe. Yaalira, "Abaana bange bali gha?"
P7	Wamaaya yaalumuka yaaja mu kasiisiira ka Wampologoma omukazi yaabona abaana be nga bali ni Wampologoma omukazi.
P8	Wampologoma omukazi yaaloba okugha Wamaaya abaana be. Yaamukoba, "Buti bano bange. Iwe osobola okutwaala abo bawampologoma abato."
P9	Kino kyanhiiza inho Wamaaya. "Nnaakola ntya okutoolayo abaana bange? Yeewuunia.
P10	Aghoni yaalowooza, "Ndidhi eky'okukola. Nd'ha kweta ba wansolo boonaboona mu lwekobaano."
P11	Ba wansolo boonaboona baidha mu lwekobaano.
P12	Wandhovu yaakoba Wamaaya ni Wampologoma omukazi boogere.
P13	Ba wansolo abaasinga bungi baali batya okunenhya Wampologoma omukazi.
P14	Mu nkomerero, Wakadaada yaayemerera era yaakoba, "Abaana abafaanana ne ebinhonhi, ba Wamaaya. Abo abali n'emikira ba Wampologoma omukazi."
P15	Wakadaada yeedimya mu nkompe ye. Ba wansolo boonaboona baamukubira mu ngaalo.
P16	Wampologoma omukazi yaanhiiga inho. Yairayo ewuwe naabaana be, ba Wampologoma abato.
