desc:	This story asks the question: should children always follow the advice of adults?

page_text:

P1	There was a man called Zoba.

Zoba was a father.
P2	Zoba's son was Azibo.
P3	One day Zoba and Azibo went for a walk.
P4	They saw a suitcase.
P5	"Pick it up and we will take it home," said Zoba.
P6	Azibo picked up the suitcase and put it on his head.
P7	On the way home, a snake came out of the suitcase!
P8	Azibo dropped the suitcase.

Father and son ran away!
