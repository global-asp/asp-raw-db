desc:	Mwongo agumira omumwi

page_text:

P1	Mwogo biryo biraŋi.
P2	Mwogo bamuhoma mwiroba.
P3	Mwogo bamulya mu ngeri nyingi. Bamulya omunyiye,  olundi omwohye e oba omuwu/efuge.
P4	Aŋandi agehewamo obugehe, hwasa cwada. Oba hwahandamo kabalagala oba mweniole. Mwogo husowola ohubbebbena omubisi.
P5	Obugehe wa mwogo hutunda hwasunamo ebbesa.
P6	Mwogo aŋonia enjala singa hiisi lufanyuma lwemwesi edatu ohoma obundi Mwogo gende ohumwagirisa.
P7	Hunyikire ohuhoma mwogo.
