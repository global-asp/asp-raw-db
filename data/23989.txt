desc:	Ekikyere kurikyabonire nkeekinya kuriyahurire.

page_text:

P1	emyaka mingi eyuma ekinya nane ekire bikanibye nywana. lumwe basharaho okulonda abishiki babo omu kibuga
P2	ekikyere kikanyigagira ekinya abwokuba kikaniki tangara
P3	kikamubuza ngu okakola ki okushana kulungi batyo,nyowe kandi mubi munonga. omubiri gwanje ngukorere ki
P4	ekinya kyamugarukamu,hurira  teka amizi omukasisotekeho omuriro
P5	konka ekikyere kyagamba ngu, ninkimany,ninkimanya hunama, munywani wanje yajenda
P6	kuyahikire omuka yateka amizi omunkyeka
P7	amizi kugatandikire okutagata, yagenagamu
P8	omubiri yagwosya nomukira gwa rugaho
P9	omumyanya gwookuba murungi nokutetema nka ekinya akaeyojera okuba mubi munonga
