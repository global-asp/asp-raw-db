page_text:

P1	Abby and Layla are two dogs who live in Johannesburg.@@They are both two years old.
P2	They like to play in their garden.
P3	Abby and Layla love to go to the park. @@At the park they can run around very fast.
P4	They can jump over the little streams at the park.
P5	Abby and Layla also like to run around on the hills.
P6	On the hills, they play with other dogs.
P7	When they get home, after running around, they are very tired. @@Shush, Abby and Layla are sleeping!
