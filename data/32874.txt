page_text:

P1	Ilaya lyakwa nkashi yandi lilanseela.
P2	Sweeta yakwa ndume wandi nayo ikulu taininga.
P3	Icoola candi cikulu leelo tecikulu saana.
P4	Uyu mushipi wakale ulampata.
P5	Ici, ciisote cacilubi candi.
P6	Insokoshi shandi shilaninga lelo ilangi talilingile.
P7	Ishi ninsapato shandi ishipya.
P8	Shaninga bwino saana.
