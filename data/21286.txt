page_text:

P1	Wamaitha ni
  baidukulubbe batyama kuriya wenumba yaibwe. A baana bali basanyufu kubanga banyumirwa
  okuwuliliza sitore yadaada waibwe.wamaitha yatandika,``ira,ira’’.yachuka
  era yaringa mumaiso gabadukurubbe ngabakuze mu  okuringaringa.
P2	Wamaitha yeyongera
  okubakobera sitore eno.okuswika kunsozi ne emiga. Wariwo ekafo ekyayetebwa
  Mongu.nga emwaka jiyissewo,abantu okuva mumirinano eKitwe batwara
  Mongu.ebantu mu Mongu bakakibwa okukora esawa empavu kubusente obutono.bwona
  bwona bwebafuna bwatwaribwa e Kitwe.obutono obwasigarawo bwatundibwa eri
  abantu ba Mongu kumiwendo emiwanvu.
P3	Olunaku lurara,abantu
  be Mongu batyanya olukiko.Bari basazewo nti ekimara kimara.bayenda etaka
  rwaibwe ribairizibwe okuva e Kitwe.Batesa kumgeri jebanawanguramu.Bategera
  nti Kitwe erina abarwani abamani ne bwokurwanisa ebirungi.Wariwo amagezi
  manji,ayera ekisera ngakiyisewo tibarinansa.Eranga barikumpi
  okurekurira,Omusadda omuto yasituka yakobanti,``ndowoza ndidi enjiira.’’
P4	Bonabona bamagamaga
  era babuza nti,’’Tukobere!’’Omusada omutu yatandika,’’Daada wange omusada
  yatukobera kumusada erinarwe Ubuntu avira ngobisiza ekibira.Arima ekibara
  kyebeta Uhuru ekiwa abantu amani okukora ebiziramu.Reka nonye omussada
  ono.’’Bona bona baribasirikivu nga era tibaidi kyakukora. Engeri bwebari
  ezira eidhi kyakukora,bakoba nti,’’Eweja ononye Ubuntu.’’
P5	Omusada omuto
  yeteketeka okunonya Ubuntu.Ngawabisewo enaku etaanu edokuja mukibibira
  yafuruma okufuna enuumba.yari musanuufu inho.yayagana Ubuntu.eyabuza, oyenda
  ki?’Omusada omuto yairamu, Ndikwenda Uhuru.’’Bweyakoba atyo, amaiso gabasada
  abandi gageda nokwewunya.Kibaire kiwanvu nga ezira ajakunonya ekibara kya
  Uhuru.
P6	Your translated text
P7	Omusada omuto yairayo
  e Mongu. Nga Ubuntu bweyakoba, bali basobora okubinga abantu be Kitwe
  barizibwe eitaka rwabwe.Olukungano orwokusanyuka rwaliwo.omusada omuto
  yatenderezebwa orwamanige.yafuka omukurembaze eyasoka owa Mongu eyari
  yeyawire.Omusada ono yari mugezi era yafuga abantube burungi.yabawereza
  namani eranga abatamuri kyeyakoranga.
P8	Wamaitha
  yakoba,’’bayidhukuru bange abemikyano,ekibara ektyemirembe kidira burwomu
  kwife awantu wonawona.mube bagezi 
  mukikozese burungi.mwidukire ebikorwa bwamwe tibikosa emwemwenka
  ayera  nababetoirere.’’ Abaidhukuru
  abafunamu bajamunuumba.wamaihta yatyama enuma yababona ngabaja.yasuubira
  esitore deyabakobera dhinabazimba okuba abantu abalungi era abafuzi abentyo.
