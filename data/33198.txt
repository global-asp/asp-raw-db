title:	Mere parani ma akeng swa

desc:	Obedo nyithintho aryo ma mere swaa judongo kanyachiel ikyalo ma Iyolwa. Achielo towameno kisoma to maani medere kisoma sano keng mere pere swaa.

page_text:

P1	Kisi odikini kachiewi aredo swa chamo mudikini
P2	kacho chiemo aruko nanga parani manyabululu awoki iyot takatho rango mere paran Anyango.
P3	Kanuango Anyango, wiwani willi gikisi gimoro gyie.
P4	Anyango kodan wachako tuko tuko ma pondo, apondo yungetti olwa maduongi ka Anyango pondo yusimba.
P5	ido ani ganyango wamaro katho ijuomi, wadimo yeni kanyachieli kendo ido wamaro katho isomero kanyachieli.
P6	Wayeere ni wakitho kisoma gimeni swa nuangoni wajujie wachowo kisoma tirukowani digiri.
P7	too obedo ndiri achieli ma Anyango kubinno isomero, achungere idhioti na atemo ye kurugo to dhelo no Anyango kubinno.
P8	Munyo adoki pecho ndeleno awachoye ri mama wacho pa Anyango.
P9	Kada beni fodo a medere kisoma tosano chunyani fodi keng Anyango.
P10	Kisi kakatho isomero fodi aranga ranga Anyango kisi kamoro gyie.
P11	same nyo a thiki a dhako maduong tosano fodi chunyani yenyo aka keng Anyango.
