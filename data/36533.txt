P1	Our library is called kongoni community library.it is the best library in the world. Our library is located in kongoni. I love my library because it teaches me many things. Like playing baseball. Football and other games.
P2	We enjoy reading books using computers and playing piano it's a better place to be. There is a computer room in our library, there is twelve rooms in our library there are six computers in our library.
P3	We have cameras, CCTV And other things like chairs, tables, office and kitchen we enjoy eating lunch in our library. Our library is the best library in the world ever.
P4	There is a place where you can play baseball, football and basketball 🏀.we thank our government for bullding Us such a nice place like that.we thank our teachers too much because they help Us with our matters.
P5	I love 💕💕 my library so  very much.
