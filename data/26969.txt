desc:	Omwisuka bweyali mu bisaaghe yabona musoke omulundi ogusooka. Taidhi musoke niki.

page_text:

P1	Ninze Kobole. mba eyo kukaalo katono.
P2	Nali ndikunoga bibala nabona ekintu kyentabonangaku naire.
P3	Langi kamala dhaboneka mubireri.
P4	Nalekula agho ebibala byenali nnonze nalumuka okuja okukobeera abeka.
P5	Abantu  baali bemereire kuluya nga bewunhinkirire.
P6	Nakoba ne'isanhu, "Ibaati irungi eryeghanike mu bire." "mbe!Ndoghoza eriyo omuntu alikufukirira langi okuva mubire." Neno bweyakoba.
P7	Abandhi bairizigania ninze. Abandhi baloghoza nga Neno nomutufu. Twatandiika okughakana.
P8	Omusadha okuva kukyalo ekyeghala yakoba ati, "Mwenamwena mugwire."
P9	Twayasikana,"Tukobere lwaki tugwire."
P10	Omugeni yatukoba ati, "Eyo musoke.Timugibonangaku?"
P11	Twabuza omugeni, "Mu-so-ke niki?"
P12	Neno yabuza, Yaife? "Tusobola okugyikumira wano?" Titwafuna kwiribwamu kuba omusadha yali aviiregho.
