P1	Once upon a time, there was a village called Nkiru. We love each other in our village so much.
P2	There are many farms, many people and Domestic animals. We like playing all day.
P3	Our house is located at the far end of the village which led to the river. And we have a farm in our house
P4	We love each other so much and always care for each other.
