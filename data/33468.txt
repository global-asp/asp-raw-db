desc:	Ehandukilo li li ngahelipi mbela ta li dulu oku pewa okaana kafuka aka kalya oiimati ihapu unene?

page_text:

P1	Efiku limwe, Meme okwa landa oiimati yomaludi a yooloka.
P2	"Oiimati otai liwa efimbo peni?" osho twa pula Meme.@@"Otai liwa konguloshi," Meme tati.
P3	Omukulu wange Rahim okwa fuka.@@Oiimati aishe okwe i lola. Ihapu okwe i lya po.
P4	"Tala eshi Rahim a ninga!" okandenge kange osho ka ingida.@@Ame handi nyamukula, "Rahim iha lombwelwa yee okuna efukutu."
P5	Meme okwa li a handukila Rahim.
P6	Nafye otwa li twa handukila Rahim.@@Ashike Rahim ka li e wete e na epuko lasha.
P7	"Rahim ota pewa ngoo ehandukilo?" okandenge kange taka pula.
P8	"Rahim, oto lipe ombedi paife," Meme osho e mu kunghilila.
P9	Rahim okwa hovela e he udite nawa.
P10	"Oha ndi vele medimo," Rahim ta nongofola.
P11	Meme oku shii eshi tashi vele Rahim.@@"Oiimati oyo ta i feta Rahim ngaho," osho eli popila.
P12	Konima yefimbo Rahim okwe tu pa ombili. "Oufukedi onde u tula po," osho a udaneka.@@Otwa mona kutya okwe li pa ombedi shili.
