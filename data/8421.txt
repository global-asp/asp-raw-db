title:	Engeri Eyi Ege Lyaŋoniamo Eŋuusi
author:	Irene Nawibo
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ehyalo  hyomu  era  Ahage hali buluŋo bugali weene 
Hatiina hu lwaabi olw’ohunywa era nga gahahulubbya.
Eŋuusi liŋonia obulamu w’Ahage. Ahage heyaasa haatye eŋuusi?

page_text:

P1	Lwali ludaalo mundobooli mu edeŋo eŋalahali.
P2	Ahage haŋuma amaaji ohuhena endaalo.
P3	Nenda ekambi y'amaaji,  n'olugahaŋwa huŋwa huhihoose.
P4	Aye n'olume lwali lwomeeye.
P5	Ahage halira,  singa sinywa nja hufa. Ndi n'ohutiina hulwaabi olupuliiyeho.
P6	Wakereŋe omulabuhi gamulabula ati,  "Olwaabi luja huhu hulubbya."
P7	Aye Ahage haali buluŋo bugali. "Naafe singa sinywaho amaaji."
P8	Ahage hatiina ohwendula olwaabi.
P9	Ohubita hu enyaasi eryomu era n'ohumasaga amoomu.
P10	Ohwola olu gaŋulira ohuwoluuma hw'amaaji nigasambuha.
P11	Ahage hahwesa ekambi ej'amaaji amaŋololohi ejiŋera.
P12	Hali hasangafu ti sihawona himoto hy'amaaji ni hiija.
P13	Ege lyagesyaho ohwebbaha hu nyaasi eryomu erya mubitangaho niri rerebba. Aye amaaji gamuyoolaŋo.
P14	Yeeda, . Njeede wange.
P15	"Niina mangu." Lityo eŋuusi ehosa po lyaloma niriŋambiriye ahasaga muhanwa haayo.
P16	Sipanga huleha ohwosa olunosa oweyaase eri eŋuusi. Nalinde ohwosa aŋalinagaluhire ohunywa.
P17	Oludaalo lulala ni lyali nihirindire abaseere babiri baja hu lwaabi n'epanda.
P18	Omuseere mulala galoma ati, ' "Ŋaliŋo oguŋuusi oguhosa ogujaanga ŋano ohunywa. Hunalirye hu hyegulo hya ŋahani."
P19	Sipanga huleha abaseere ohwita Eŋuusi Ehosa. Aye ndi hadidiri,  panga huhola hiina?
P20	Aŋo naaŋo Eŋuusi Ehosa lyaguluha lyagwa ŋaasi ohuŋwa humusaala ohunywa.
P21	Ahage hafuna ehiŋeego.
P22	Hatumira hu hugulu hw'omuseere nga hamupiiha bugali nga ndolu haŋanga.
P23	Omuseere gatuuma,  galira ati,  "Ouch!"
P24	Eŋuusi Ehosa lyeŋuunya era lya pululuha lyatiina hubuŋonero.
P25	Hatyo Ahage po heyaasa Eŋuusi Ehosa olw'ohuhaŋonia.
