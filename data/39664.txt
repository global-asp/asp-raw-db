P1	In the village of mutiti lived a little girl called mia who loved reading the Bible and going to church along praying alot because she believed in God a lot
P2	One day she was with her Elder sister and saw a beautiful rainbow  with all its wonderful colours
P3	Mia was really happy to see the rainbow till her and her sister sat down and admired the wonderful things God had created on earch
P4	While sitting on the grass Mia and her sister thought of drawing the beautiful rainbow but Mia was sad because she thought she would never see the beautiful rainbow again
P5	Before sleeping Mia prayed to God that the rainbow should come  back tomorrow too. When she woke up she went on a walk with her sister  and on there way they saw a big beautiful rainbow  and Mia knew that God answered her prayers
P6	They went back homr to tell the rest of the family they all played thanked God and they lived happily ever after
P7	MOROL: god will always answer your prayers no matter @What it is just pray like little Mia
