desc:	Carlos and Maria are feeding their animals. What do they give the hungry animals to eat? Do you know what sounds the different animals make?

page_text:

P1	Ekyaalo kye sinaire
  kyetolorwa ebisozzi. Emisoota mminji jawangawalilanga mukibira  kubisoozi
  ebyo.omusoota omuba abaanakyalo bagweetanga unyalego.unyalego yakanga abantu kamala.yakankamira embuzi nne ntaama
  Nnamba.
P2	Lwaali lulaala,abaguuna baasatu,Apiyo,Ajoh
  nni Atieno baaja mukibiira okuwelela enku.Daada waabagunna baano yaabalabula  nnti,Nmwegendeleze ogusoota guwa unyalego
P3	Abaguuna baasiba amaata,amatooke,emboli
   nna amaadi.baajja nne emiguwa ejjo okusibisa enku nne ekiso ekyooji
  ekyo okutemesatemesa enku.awo nni baasimba mukibira  
P4	Nga batambula bwebaaja,abaguuna
  baawaaya era baaseeka.Ekyembi
  mukuluwayibwe Apio yaabakooba,musilike.tutuse omusoota
  unyango wegusisira
P5	Atieno yaayeta bayine,muboone,gaano mayinoga  unyalego ag omuwendo.tujje naago.apiyo yabaidukiza  temulabila kulaburwa kwa dada. Titutekwa kutwaara kintu kyonakyona okutoolaku enku
P6	Ajoh, yakooba nnti, nze tyesiga daada mukaazi. okutulobera okufuna kyetwenda. Ndikwenda amayino gaazabu. Baguuna bayine beelalikira. Ajoh tukulabwire,bwe bakooba. Aye ajoh ye tiyafayo.
P7	 mukasela ako unyaiego yaida nga anonya amayino
  ge agazaabu.yakambuwalabwaatagaboona.awo
  yawulila abaguuna nga bawaya bwebaseka.unyalengo
  yejisa wansi weibaale okumpi ne enjira abaguna webaalina obitira nga bagolora
