title:	Ng'ikiel Lu Ikote Ai?
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/12649.png
desc:	Arai ekoi lo etetemi ekikwane angikiel alu eyakatar ngitieng.

page_text:

P1	Eyakar akinyang ng'ikiel luitayek.
P2	Eyakar eng'atuny dang ng'ikiel luitayek.
P3	Eyakar ekolia lo apolon ng'ikiel luitayek noi! Kotere nyo eyakatar ng'itieng ng'iche ng'ikiel luitayek?
P4	Eyakar aite ng'ikiel lukaagong'ok lunyakaet.
P5	Emakar ikeny ng'ikiel. Imuji ikeny kwai?
P6	Eyakatar ng'irunyei ng'ikiel lu along'aren loapolok.
P7	Ayakar kolong ayong ng'ikile lu along'aren.
P8	Tolokoros ng'ikiel kang lu along'aren! Iyakar iyong ng'ikiel ng'iai.
