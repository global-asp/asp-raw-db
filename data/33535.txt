desc:	ULyn beseka yenze umsebenzi omningi ukulungisela izinto enzela usuku lakhe lokuzalwa

page_text:

P1	uLyn, Mkhulu Karel kanye noAnton beba lungisa izinto belungisela usuku luka Lynne lokuzalwa
P2	uAnthon noLynne bayo bhaka ikhekhe. uMkhulu Karel ufunda iphepha elinemniningwane yokubhaka.
P3	"Asihlanganise lezinto zokubhaka esitsheni esikhulu" kusho uLynne.
P4	uAnton wagocoza izinto zokubhaka wase uyenza umonakalo omkhulu.
P5	"Leli khekhe seliyasinda manje" kusho umkhulu Karel.
P6	"Abangani bakho sebe fikile" kuhso umkhulu Karel.
P7	uAnton ebecabanga ukuthi isipho sokucala sizoba esisindayo ngoba sasi sikhulu.
P8	Bekungu mcamelo futhi wawukala kancane nje.
P9	uAnton wacabanga ukuthi isipho esilandelayo sizoba lula ngoba sasi sincane.
P10	Kwaku yitshe elipendiwe lalikala isisindo isiningi.
P11	Izingane zasho ukuthi ikhilo elilodwa lamatshe likala okufanayo nekhilo elilodwa lezinsiba.
P12	Ikhekhe lika Lynne belishile.
P13	uLynne ebedumele kakhulu.
P14	Umamncane Mavis wabuza uAnton ukuthi makagcekele ukuthi isispho sakhe sikala kangakanani.
P15	"Likala okulinganayo nekhekhe leli elishile" kusho uAnton.
P16	Wonke umuntu wathi "sukoluhle lokuzalwa, Lynne"
