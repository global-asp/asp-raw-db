desc:	Luno olufumo lugema ku baana abato abanhumirwa Cesooko. Omutendesi waibwe ali nokwewunhia kungi ku kikunsu kyaibwe.

page_text:

P1	Ngonja inho cesooko, ate ighe?
P2	Maaliki alikusoma cesooko, gheena onhumirwa?
P3	Tuje mu kisaaghe.
P4	Maaliki ono omuzaanhi ghaife asinga.
P5	Olumaliira lw'omuzaanho, omutendesi atukoba ati, "Ndi n'ekiidha okubewuunhi
P6	Aye akoba ati, "Ndidha kukibakobera enco."
P7	Olunaku olwairirira, boonaboona baidha ku kisaaghe.
"Twenatwena tubigonza."
P8	"Maaliki ali n'eby'okukozesa ebiyaaka n'emipiira ebitumala twenatwena!"
