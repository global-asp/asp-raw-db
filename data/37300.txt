page_text:

P1	Ariana was a very intelligent young girl , she was at the top of her class . She had difficulty In finding her future career
P2	Her mom was a doctor and her father was a lawyer
P3	So she thought of a career that would make her legendary
P4	In her lesure she talked to her mom about what to be, her mom asked her, what do you like to do, she said she likes swimming
P5	She tried swimming but she was not that good
P6	Then she tried football , she had fun but it was not her career
P7	Then her mom told her and the team that , do not try so hard,when grow up you will decide
P8	And she played happily with her brother
P9	The end
