title:	Ino ninzi Nita ncalanzyide ansi

desc:	Nita ulalengenga kalanzyide ansi. Ino inga walipindamuna?

page_text:

P1	Nita ulanzyide mujulu ansi, masusu akwe malamfu kakwezya ansi. Zisamu, bwizu, azyoonse zintu zilanzyide ansi.
P2	Maulu akwe, asika kujulu, Nkinga Navi kalendeenda. kalaamba, "Ndakubona okuno alimwi. Ulanzyide ansi alimwi. Ino nkaambo nzi?"
P3	Maulu akwe alanyaya muluwo. Usola kuyuba kunze kwamasusu akwe. "Nciyumu yumu kwambaulisya, waamba kuli nguwe. "Nsecili ndime. Tandeeli kwaambaula."
P4	Navi wamujatilila akwaanza. Uyanda kugwasya kuti amvwe. Bakanta  atala awo Navi mpabona kabotu. Bakabona  zintu zinji. Cakaumuumu bakabona bana kabasobana awa awalya.
P5	.
P6	Bana aba tabakozyanyi pe.
P7	Chi camankwinya.
P8	Lala mulamfu kwindilila.
P9	Bambam takkalikene Alimwi uyandaula kwangululwa.
P10	Lulu ulabala cakaumuumu.
P11	Langa Fuleya masusu kunyongana.
P12	A Sid usama magilazi coonse ciindi.
P13	Andime ndili cikanda buyo. Anduwe ndiwe. Toloolikke. Aumwi muntu kuli mbwabede, wabona. Eci cipa nduwe kuba mbubwena mbuli nime. Eeyi nyika ncisobano comwe cipati.
P14	Nita walimwa kuti uliluleme, walomba mweenzinyina ngwajana. Kusulama tacili cintu cinonenzya.
