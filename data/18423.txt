desc:	Nzuhɔne ɛlɛku nane ne mɔɔ amuala. Tia bɛ kɛ bɛkɔ banlo nzule la.

page_text:

P1	Ɛzonle ko ɛlɛkɔ anlo nzule.
P2	Ɛtɔnga nwiɔ ɛlɛkɔ anlo nzule.
P3	Εbonu ɛnlankɛ nsa nee noma nna noko ɛlɛkɔ anlo nzule.
P4	Hwɛlɛ nnu nee kɔkɔte nsia ɛlɛtia ahɔ nzule ne anwo.
P5	Zibila nnsu ɛlɛnradi ahɔ nzule ne anwo.
P6	Ahelɛne mɔtwe nee fɛlɛ ngɔnla ɛlɛbia nzule ne anu.
P7	Anwenade ko ɛlɛsu. Ɔdaye noko ɔkpondɛ kɛ ɔno nzule. Nwane a sulo anwenade ne a?
P8	Ɛzonle ko nee anwenade ne ɛlɛno nzule.
