title:	MUHA NA AKANYANKOGOTE

desc:	Omubwile bwakale nakale

page_text:

P1	omukyaalo kyaheihi, hakaba nihatuulamu akanyankogote.  Muha na akanyankogote bakaba bali abanyani.
P2	Omukyalo kyaheihi hakaba nihatuulamu akanyankogote kakanyabwoba. Muha na akanyankogote bakaba bari abanyani
P3	Muha akahulira ngu okulenga omulindi gwamaani ogwaburyo hakaba nihatuulaho enjoki zilikukola obwooki bunuzilen munoonga. Ezo njoki zikaba niziza ahalugyendo hale na ebihumi byaazo kumala esande yoona. "nituteekwa kubona obwe bwooki enjoki zitaliho," Muha yagambila akanyankogote.
P4	Akanyankogote kakaba keine obwooba bwokuzayo. Akasheeshe akakulatsileho enyameishwa zoona eibiri zaatandika olugyendo lwa okuza owa ebihumi. Muha yataambula juba juba koonka akanyankogote kataambula mpola omubwooba.
P5	bakaza aheigulu yomushozi, ahaasi oluhaanga kulaba. leero bayambuka omuliindi omuhaango gwaburyo, bayeyongye kutambula.
P6	Ahamuhelu baleba ebihumi omumiti aha mushozi. muha yayilukanga yaza omumiti namani meingi. Akanyankogote kakaba kaliheihi kugwa kihumula ahabwokuba kakaba kaluhile munonga kandi keine enjala.
P7	Muha kuyahikile aha kihumi ekilikukilayo obuhango, talashiishile obwile. yaayeta akanyankogote yagila ati, "Gila juba, temba omuti onagte ekihumi ahaasi"
P8	muha ekagyezaho kutemba omuti. Ekaba nemanya ngu nebaasa kutelela egwe. Eki kikanyiiza muha owatandikile kuyombelela akanyankogote.
P9	Akanyankogote kukakwatsile aha kihumi, yahulira okuhuuma kwenjoki. Zikaba zikyali omubihumi byaazo! Akanyankogote kayekanga kangwa ahasi. Nekihumi kwagwa.
P10	Ekihumi kyagwa aha kanyankogote. obwoki bwakasweeka weena kasigalakeshelekile. Muha yatangika kuligasa obwoki. Enjoki tizashemelerwa.
P11	Enjoki zikateela Muha envuuli omubili gwa yo gwena. Muha yashalamu kwiluka kukiza amagala konka enjoki zamukulatila.
P12	Muha yaashuma omushozi, yahingula omulindi, emilunga, neihanga. enjoki zamwetoolola omuhanda gweena zilikumuluma.
P13	akanyankogote, kakyali ahasi yekihumi, ya huliraku bulikimwe kyabiile kihunami. ekihumi nobwoki bikoomela ahamugongo gwayo.
P14	akanyankogote kamanya kukaabile katakyayetenga kwesheleka omukishaka.
P15	Muha ekaba eluhire3 munonga eijweire ebihele etakahikile omuka. Ekeimuka, yashanga ngu eine emyanya aha ruhu rwayo.
P16	Bati nikwe akanyankogoteb katungile oruhuryayo kandi na muha emyanya aha luhuryayo. Muha eka lahila ngu telishuba kwiba konka muha na aqkanyankogote tibakyali banyani.
