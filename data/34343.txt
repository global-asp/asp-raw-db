page_text:

P1	Na Yeesu a lǝ abakǝŋja wàà : @@« Ɩ ta naa nyîŋǝ,@lǝ̂ ma wàà :@@"Atayà,
P2	pâla baa aŋa a yɔ̂ wàà, akɩŋyɩɖa kagʊsʊ̀rá gʊ mana.
P3	Akɩɩyarɩ gɩ pɩ̂.
P4	...
P5	Shêe atʊ ʊshilé baa ʊpaŋa na...
P6	... ʊjɩʊ nɖee gɩ na hɩyɛŋ ma.
P7	Tǝ̂ɖǝ atʊ atɩmbɔnɔ,
P8	ɖaa atʊ gboo gɩ na tǝɖǝ mbaɖee ba tǝrǝtǝrǝ nʼatʊ ma.
P9	Pàá pâla mbɔnɔ n tʊ̂ŋa atʊ aŋǝ na." »
P10	Lʊkaasɩ 11.2-4
