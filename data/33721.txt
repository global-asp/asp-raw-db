P1	My friend Dennis from next door called me. @@"Come and see my new dog," said Dennis.
P2	The dog had black spots and a big head. @@It frightened me! @@I felt worried that it would bite me.
P3	We took the new dog for a walk, Dennis and me.
P4	The dog wagged its tail.
P5	It barked!
P6	Then it peed on me! @@On my boot! @@Yuuuk!
P7	Dennis took a tissue and wiped off the pee.
P8	I felt that Dennis was a good friend to me.
