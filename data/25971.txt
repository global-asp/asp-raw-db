desc:	Amaka gaaja gagula ebintu ku lunaku lwe mbaga.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala amaka gaja mu katale okugula ebintu bya mbaga.
P2	Baali abantu bataanu. Maama, Bbaabba, Omwisuka omuguna n'omukozi.
P3	Omwisuka ghaibwe Yokaana n'omukozi, Maliza, beetwika ebiibo.
P4	Bbaabba yali aginze Joy mwanhinaze omuto ate maama yali agemye nsawoye.
P5	Bwebaatuuka mu kiya, baagula ebintu kamaala.
P6	Baagula ebintu nga eiva, ebibala, emamba, amatooke n'e4bindi kamaala.
P7	Joy omuto yatandiika okulirira buli kintu, switi, doli,buli kintu.
P8	Bbaabbaa yamugulira omupiira omunene era yaaba musanhufu.
P9	Yokaana yeena yayenda omupiira era baamugulira.
P10	Boona baagulira omukozi ekiteeteeyi ekirungi era yaaba musanhufu.
P11	Buli mutu yaja eka nga musanhufu.
