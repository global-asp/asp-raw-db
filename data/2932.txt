title:	Mamelang Sebopeho Sa Ka
author:	Moeketsi Buku
artist:	Angie & Upesh
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Kaofeela ha rona re na le mmele e shahlileng empa na re kile ra e mamela hore na e reng?

page_text:

P1	Ha ke e sekolong tsatsing lena. Ke letsatsi la phomolo.
P2	Ha ke ilo shebella TV tsatsing lena. Hape ho ntse ho se na motlakase.
P3	Ebe ke tlo etsa eng tsatsing lee? Ketlo mamela mmele oaka!
P4	Pele ke tšoanela hothola,  ke thole haholo. Ele hore ke tle ke tsebe ho mamela 'mmele ona oa ka hantle.
P5	Eea,  hona joale kea ikutloa ha ke hemela ka hare le ka ntle.
P6	Ebe nka etsa hore ha ke hema moea oa ka o etse leratanyana. Mamela,  Sssssssssssssssssssssssssss Hape hanyane Mamala,  Mmmmmmmmmmm.
P7	Hajoale kea utloa hore pelo ea ka entse e runya-runya e otla e re toluum,  toluuum,  toluuuuum!
P8	Na ebe nka etsa hore pelo ea ka e runya-runya kapele kapa hore e lume haholo? Eea,  ha nka qhomela holimo le tlase ka makhetlo a ka bang mashome a mabeli.
P9	Hona joale pelo ea ka e otla ka pele. Tolum,  tolum,  tolum!
P10	Hape haeba nka ka beha menoana ea ka seatleng sa ka,  nka utloa ho runya-runya.
P11	Hape nka ikutloa ha ke tšeha. Haha haha haaah haaa!
P12	Le ho lla hape Boohoooohooo!
P13	Leho opa liatla. Tap tappa tap tapaa.
P14	Hape hara lintho boholo Ke utloa mpa ea ka ha e E re Guddu guddu guddu.
P15	Ha ere mfepe! Mfepe!
P16	Linko tsa ka tsona li khona ho nkhella monkho o monate o tsoang ka kichining moo 'Me a ntseng a pheha masutsa teng.
P17	Eea ho oa qabola ho mamela mmele ona oa ka. Hona joale ke batla ho utloa mehlare eaka ha e hlafuna.
P18	Empa hona joale mpa eaka e thabetse makoenya ana a monate a chesang.
