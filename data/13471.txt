page_text:

P1	Zama is getting ready for school.
P2	Dad is cooking porridge for breakfast. He mixes it with milk.
P3	"Mummy has got the sweetest breast milk!" says Zama.
P4	Milk has got fats, vitamins, calcium which gives Zama alot of energy for school.
P5	The milk is ready.
P6	Dad pours the milk,  with love.
P7	"Dad,  I think this porridge needs more sugar and milk," says Zama.
P8	"Mummy's milk tastes much better than Dad's milk," says Zama.
P9	"Dad,  please in this milk asks Zama."
P10	Dad adds sugar to Zama's milk.
P11	"Wu! Dad! You added water in my milk!"
P12	"Dad,  when will Mom be home?" asks Zama.
