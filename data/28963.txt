page_text:

P1	Mpologoma, ekisolo ekisinga obulabe kyabbaire kyaba mu biseera byakyo eb'eidembe aye ekyembbiibbi kyagwire omukiina ekibba. Okunoonia obuyambi, mangu inho yakolere eiroboozi eghanvu. "maama nze, maama nze, maama nze."
P2	Ebisolo ebindi byabbaire bitambula omulunyiriri ni bighulira Owampologoma ng'ali kukubba ekeereere. Byasembeire okumpi ni byetoloola ekiina. Okwagirya ompologoma omukiina, byonabyona byasekere, "Aaaaa-aaaaa wooo! titusobola kukutoolamu omukiinha kubanga obbaire otutiisya."
P3	Ebisolo byazwiregho ni bireka ompologoma yenka omukiinha. Niiyo ensonga lwaki ompologoma yasuukire nsolo owobulabe eri ebisolo ebindi tete n'abantu. Okutakibwa buli muntu kisobola okukununula omubuzibu.
