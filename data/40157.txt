desc:	The story is about a grade R teacher who tells her learners a story about how animals got their names.

page_text:

P1	Kayla is a learner doing grade R at Zamani primary school, Kayla's mother had just bought her a book which had all kinds of animals and their different names on it, Kayla's mother bought the book as a birthday present for her daughter.
P2	Kayla was so excited about her present that she could not even wait to go to school the following day so that she will show all her friends and her classmates what her mother had just bought for her on her birthday.
P3	Kayla had always admired and loved animals that she even asked her mother to buy an elephant for her which she can keep as a pet in their house.
P4	When Kayla got to school the following day, the first thing she wanted to tell her friends about was the animal book her mother had just bought for her, which she also wanted to show it to her favourite class teacher Ma'am Getrude
P5	After Kayla had showed her favourite teacher(Ms Getrude) the animal book, her teacher asked the entire classroom " Do you know how animals got their names? If anyone does can you please tell us how animals got their names?"
P6	Kayla was afraid to answer the question because she did not know the answer to what was asked and one of her classmates raised her and and shouted 'God gave animals their names' then their teacher asked...
P7	Can I tell you all a story about how animals got their names? 'Yes, story time is always the best' they shouted.@Then Ms Getrude tells the story@'Once upon a time, so many many years ago, in the garden of eden before everyone was born...
P8	God saw how Adam was bored since Adam was the only person who was living in the world, God decided to create all kinds of animals, animals that can fly, animals that lived on the ground and also animals which lived in the sea(water).
P9	So after God had created all the animals in the world, the animals did not have names, then God brought all the animals to Adam so that Adam can give each an every animal a name which he preferred, and Adam would be like
P10	You as from today will be called a lion, king of the jungle, then he moves to the next animal you will be called elephant, Adam did these up until he named all the animals God had created, that is how animals got their names.....
