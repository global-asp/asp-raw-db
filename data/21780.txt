title:	Baesekele Ya Ga Bontle
author:	Lorato Trok
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Setswana

desc:	Tshwene e tshaba ka baesekele ya ga Bontle.

page_text:

P1	Bontle le monnawe ba ipaakanyetsa go ya sekolong.
P2	Bontle o palamisa monnawe baesekele ya gagwe.
P3	Ditsala tsa bona di a ba itumelela fa ba fitlha kwa sekolong.
P4	Fa ba le mo phaposing, tshwene ya fitlha kwa ntle. Ya fitlha ya palama baesekele ya ga Bontle mme ya tshaba ka yona!
P5	Bana ba lelekisa tshwene.
P6	Tshwene ya tlogela baesekele kwa tlase ga setlhare.
P7	Tshwene ya thumela kwa setlhaketlhakeng mo gare ga noka. Ya palama kwa godimo ga setlhare.
P8	Tshwene ya ba sadisa sentle! Bana ba tsholetsa matsogo gore e tsamaye sentle. Bontle o nale baesekele ya gagwe gape.
