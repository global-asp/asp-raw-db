desc:	Edewuro musinke no pa ogu mumati gomakurulya anamana kulya enyango lyenzi.

page_text:

P1	Ezuva limwe onane kwarende enyango lyokulisiga-siga.
P2	"Ruveze musinke natu lya eli enyango?" Tatupura.@@Natulilya masiku, "Yimo valimbwilire onane."
P3	Mukurwange Rahim age makurulya.@@Age kwa makerere enyango nalinye. Kumwe nokulya enyango lyenzi.
P4	"Tareni eyi arugana Rahim!'' Yimo gazigilire mumbyange gomunona gomumati.@@"Rahim utwini ntani udumbe," tani mutantere.
P5	Onane kwamuhandukilire Rahim.
P6	Nose hena kwamuhandukilire Rahim.@@Mara age Rahim kapi galizuvhire yoyidona.
P7	Kapi nomukamutengeka Rahim? Tapura mumbya gomunona gomumati.
P8	"Rahim, ntaantani okalivere," yimo vamurondwere Onane.
P9	Makura Rahim age yipo gaverekere kuvera.
P10	Rahim Tawowota asi, "Mezimo lyange kunakukora."
P11	Onane vayidivire asi morwasinke ana kuverera Rahim.@@"Enyango kuna kumudewura Rahim," yimo vagazarere onane.
P12	Konyima, Rahim tatupe mbili. "Kapi ngani kara hena makurulya," taligana.@@Twapulire nonkango dendi.
