page_text:

P1	"Live in Rome as Romans do" - Anonymous The same way live in nature as nature does.@Are you confused? Ah don't be there is a message in every failure and every problem. Today as we live in the era of COVID-19, we have to understand.
P2	what it is trying to teach us. When the " May I come in teacher " has now changed up to The Waiting room.. The Greenboards to whiteboards. Life is changed right. But there is something which you forgot. You are getting the results of your
P3	mistakes. We all love our homes right? Why do we write "Home sweet Home" at the front gate. Well it is written to state that there is only sweetness and love in our life. But the truth is always bitter. We love our home but we forget where
P4	our home is. Obviously our home is in the nature. In that place where we have left only dirt and constructions. Coronavirus came up for a short bit of time or maybe can stretch to a long period of time..
P5	But we should not be dejected. We should learn that whatever the reason for this is.. Either through nature or through man made process this was a unhealthy and problematic substance. People say that these type of diseases come once in
P6	Why should it come at all? We have to fight up with this virus. With the help of all hands together combining up with nature we shall always succeed. We should tell and advise people to take this disease seriously as this
P7	Is not a joke? @Fight against this virus this should be the motto. We should behold nature and respect it up. Stop cutting trees can be said by our mouth too, why to cut a tree to get a piece of paper to write that slogan?
