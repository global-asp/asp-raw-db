desc:	Nkwenkwana ithile iphiwa ikepisi eluhlaza ngumama wayo. Suke umoya uyivuthuze ikepisi iyokuwela emthini. Ebusuku kuthi gqi omnye umntu enxibe le kepisi

page_text:

P1	Sonke saya kwitheko lorhwebo eDasara.
P2	Utata wathengela uChintu iindondo ezinomtsalane. Umama wandithengela ikepusi eqaqambileyo eluhlaza. Ubhabha wafumana iilekese.
P3	Endleleni egodukayo,  umoya omkhulu weza. Wavuthuza,  ikepusi yam yaphaphatheka.
P4	Ikepusi yam yaxhaseka esebeni lomthi omdala wephayphali.
P5	Ndalila kakhulu. Kwaye andasitya isidlo sam sangokuhlwa.
P6	Emva kwexesha ngobo busuku,  inyanga yavela. Yajonga ikepusi yam emthini omdala wePhayphali.
P7	Yayilinganisa ikepusi yam. Inyanga yancuma ngolonwabo. Ndancuma nam ngokunjalo.
P8	Ukuphuma kwesikolo ngemini elandelayo,  umama wam wandipha ikepusi ekhazimlayo,  entsha,  ebomvu. "Inyanga iyithumele," watsho.
P9	Ngobo busuku,  sobabini inyanga nam sathwala iikepusi zethu,  sancuma. Sasonwabile.
P10	Ucinga ukuba ilanga lifuna umnqwazi?
P11	Qaqadisa yeyiphi ikepusi endiyithwalayo namhlanje?
