title:	Gweno Gi Ogongolo
author:	Athieno Gertrude and Owino Ogot
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Dhopadhola
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Kigana pa gweno gi dhok sewe (level4) mokwany wok i level 1.

page_text:

P1	Cango,  cango,  cango gweno gi Ogongolo jo bedo mere.
P2	Ndir acel jo kidho tuko mupira.
P3	Gweno okuro gole.
P4	Ogongolo ogoyo mupira to rwako gole!
P5	To gweno mako nge.
P6	Gi kenyo Gweno omwonyo Ogongolo.
P7	Gweno oromo gi min Ogongolo.
P8	To Ogongolo ywak ni,  "Mama konyan!"
P9	To cuny gweno cako lem.
P10	Ogongolo lemo cuny jo.
P11	Gweno owolo to ŋoko Ogongolo. To Ogongolo ŋweco.
P12	Wok ndir no gweno gi Ogongolo jo bedo jo kwor.
