P1	Once apon a time a boy named Mario always heard people talking about a kingdom he did not even know.
P2	One day in the summer he saw people running and di not know why.
P3	A strong wind moved the sands and a giant kingdom came out.@He pute a cloak on it and tried to tell his friends but they did not  Listen.
P4	When he went in he turned into a leopard.
P5	A dear went to him and raised his hand shouting here's your king.
P6	He told the others he's not the king and said help me get out.
P7	When they helped him he wentt as human and said now thats the kingdom of joy the end.
