page_text:

P1	This is my dog. His name is Bobby. @@He is a hunting dog.
P2	Bobby likes to hunt smaller animals like rabbits. @@He also likes to play in the long grass.
P3	One Saturday afternoon, Bobby left home alone to hunt. Along the way he saw a rabbit on a rock. @@Bobby started dripping with saliva.
P4	The rabbit saw Bobby looking at him with red eyes and became scared.@@He ran away very fast.
P5	Bobby ran after the rabbit.@@He does not see a big hole ahead.
P6	Bobby fell into the big hole.@@The rabbit kept running away.
P7	Two boys heard Bobby barking, "Woof! Woof! woof!" @@They ran toward the big hole to help him.
P8	They use a rope to pull Bobby out. They count, "One, two, three!" @@"He is so heavy," they said.@@They finally pulled Bobby out. He was very happy.
