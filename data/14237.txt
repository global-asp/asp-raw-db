page_text:

P1	My name is NJERI, I am in standard six.
P2	My school is called UZALENDO primary school.
P3	My favorite subject is Drawing.
P4	Madam KARIMI teaches us English and drawing.
P5	DAVID is my best friend. He is older than me.
P6	I  love playing football when i am not in class.
P7	After taking  lunch, we go back to home to assist our parents.
P8	I love my mother,  she is called TERESA.
P9	Everyday, we go to the market with her to buy food.
P10	We buy Unga from Kariuki's posho mill.
P11	I like eating potatoes, Teacher Rehema said they are important for our health.
P12	Waiyaki is my elder brother, He loves playing alone.
P13	On holidays we visit the Game park to see the Elephants, cheetahs and Giraffes.
P14	We plant Maize in our Shamba. My mother loves eating Maize.
P15	Every morning i wake up early to go to school.
