desc:	This story is about a boy who read a book and dreamt about it and it felt real.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there were four children Sam,Lucas,Pearl and Lydia.They were best friends they cared for each other they liked reading comic books but they loved reading fictional stories the most.
P2	The children lived in a small town in Italy they went to a school called The Bees they were in seventh grade.One day after school the children went to the library in the afternoon just after school.They came across a book called Mega Land.
P3	The children sat down and started reading right after going through the second page time was up.The children began to fight they wanted to go home with the book but there was only one book for one person, so Lucas went home with it.
P4	Lucas got home and changed his uniform,did his homework,ate dinner and washed the dishes.He had to miss his favourite show so that he could read his book Mega Land.
P5	He read a lot about Mega Land evil robots that had took over the City  and destroyed everything and a super hero who was betrayed and trapped in a cave by his best friend and could not stop the disaster.The book was a bit scary.
P6	Mom said it is bed time and Lucas went to sleep,he closed his eyes and found himself at the park with Sam, Lydia and Pearl.They saw a tree house and opened the door and went in.Instead of going in the tree house they went to another place.
P7	They were suddenly surrounded by robots robotic cities they were in Mega Land.A robot came to them and looked at them in suprise "human children" he said,I guess you read about the book Mega Land the robot offered to help save the city.
P8	The children encountered against the gigantic robotic octopus which sprayed them with slime and mud and they fought with the Robot Pirate the most evil pirate.The children swam through the seven seas and battled with the sea creatures.
P9	They reached the fort where the superhero was hidden they rescued the superhero and the superhero defeated all the evil robots. The superhero thanked the children and especially Lucas.Lucas woke up and got ready for school.
P10	He shared his dream with his friends and they laughed at such ridiculousness, but he really dreamt about that.
