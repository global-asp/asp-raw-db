title:	Back to school
author:	Thenjiwe Lebesa
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Vusi Malindi, Abraham Muzee and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	None
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	My message here is that we should have fun and enjoy life

page_text:

P1	I woke up in the morning and I got ready for school.
P2	I saw my mother making food for me in the kitchen I was very hungry.
P3	I ate and ate it was so delicious.
P4	I was soon ful and I ran out of the house because I was running late for school.
P5	When I was at school the learners were outside to do the activities.
P6	It was then break I went to go have fun with my friends.
P7	The teacher  taught us alphabets.
P8	After school we went out for sports and our teacher chose for us the clothes that were going to wear.
P9	We got ready.
P10	We were separated for teams and we played football.
P11	Our teacher was always guiding us.
P12	After that we went to our homes and met at the river to swim
P13	After that I went home to sleep and my little brother came in my room to say goodnight.
