page_text:

P1	Tụ́lị̃ ꞌbã drị̃ꞌbị́ ãlị́ŋá.
P2	Ánã ꞌbã drị̃ꞌbị́ ãzo.
P3	Kátị̃ ꞌbã drị̃ꞌbị́ zo ãndánĩ.
P4	Zámã cã drị̃ꞌbị́ ĩꞌdidrị̂ cãcã.
P5	Bãbá tị́ꞌbị̂ trũ.
P6	Zãnélẽ nze drị̃ꞌbị́ ĩꞌdidrị̂ nzẽ-nzẽ.
P7	Tábõ lị drị̃ꞌbị́ ĩꞌdidrị̂ rá.
P8	Témbã ufá drị̃ꞌbị́ ĩꞌdidrị̂ rá.
