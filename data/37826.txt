desc:	Check out di five senses

page_text:

P1	I de use my leg run, jump, and to kick.
P2	I de use my hand eat, work and to hug.
P3	But head dey more important pass my leg and hand.
P4	Head get eyes. @@I de use my eyes see.
P5	Head get nose. @@I de use my nose smell.
P6	Head get ears. @@I de use my ears hear.
P7	Head get tongue. @@I de use am taste tins wey get salt or wey sweet.
P8	Head get mouth. @@I de use my mouth talk.
P9	Head de control di whole body!
