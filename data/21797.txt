desc:	Who will be king of the animals after King Leopard dies?

page_text:

P1	King Leopard was getting old. 

How will he choose the next king?
P2	King Leopard called animals to the party. 

He would announce his plan.
P3	After the party, King Leopard spoke. 

The animals listened.
P4	He said, “A contest will decided the next king.”
P5	One who counts to ten before the spear falls, will be king.
P6	Elephant pushed everyone aside, “I’m the biggest. I should be king.”
P7	Elephant only counted up to four. 

The spear fell.
P8	Boar pushed everyone saying, “I’m the strongest. I should be king.”
P9	Boar counted up to six and the spear fell. 

He left.
P10	Everyone said, “Big Elephant and strong Boar failed. Who can win?”
P11	Ape counted, “One! Two! Three! Four! Five! Six! Seven!" 

He cried.
P12	King Leopard said, “Ape, you only get one chance." 

Ape left.
P13	Some animals started leaving. 

Then, Little Antelope said, “Let me try.”
P14	King Leopard shouted, “Give Antelope a chance. Let her try.”
P15	Antelope said, “Five and five is ten.” 

The spear fell down.
P16	The contest showed the smartest animal. 

Antelope was queen after leopard.
