desc:	The book is about a tall man called Otieno from Gatimbi who had a friend called Fatuma 
Otieno had short bicycle and a bed but latter bought a tall bicycle and long bed .

page_text:

P1	In the village of Gatimbi there lived a tall man . @The tall man was called Otieno . Otieno had a long bicycle . Otieno could not ride his bicycle ,he had to place his leg on the front set .
P2	Otieno bought a tall bicycle . Otieno rides his bicycle carrying his friend and his pet from the market . Otieno's friend was called Fatuma @Otieno and Fatuma liked each other . Otieno's pet was a hen and it's name was ngoho.
P3	Otieno had a short bed , whenever he slept his legs could not fit in the bed.@Otieno had tall legs . He had a short blanket . @Fatuma had come to visit his friend . He finds Otieno still asleep with his pet besides him.
P4	Otieno bought a  tall bed . Otieno brought a long blanket, he can now sleep comfortably , his leg covered . Fatuma has visited his friend again @Otieno is sleeping .@Fatuma is talking to Otieno , Fatuma is standing besides otieno's bed .
