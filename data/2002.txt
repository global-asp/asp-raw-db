desc:	This story is about planting a bean seed and what happens when you water and look after it.

page_text:

P1	Impande ikhula kuqala.
P2	Ikhula iye phansi emhlabathini.
P3	Bese kukhula isiqu. Sona sikhula siye phezulu silandele ukukhanya.
P4	Amahlamvu okuqala ayavela.
P5	Isithombo sikabhontshisi sikhule sibe side.
P6	Kube sekuvela izimbali. Izimbali ziphenduka zibe ubhontshisi.
P7	Kunembewu eningi kuwona ubhontshisi.
