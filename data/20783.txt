page_text:

P1	"Nginekinga ekulu," kulila iKosi eFitjhani begodu eBanzi.
P2	"Ngilahlekelwe liphondo lami," kutjho iKosi eFitjhani neBanzi.
P3	"Ngizolithola iphondo lakho," kutjho uJojo. "Ngiyolifuna ngendlini kababa uRekhthengele."
P4	UJojo nobaba uRekhthengele bafuna yoke indawo kodwana azange balithole iphondo.
P5	"Khamba uyokuqala bonyana umma uNcantathu akanalo iphondo," kutjho ubaba uRekhthengele.
P6	UJojo wadlula endlini yakhe ukuyokuthela amanzi amakhaza.
P7	UJojo wavakatjhela uDudu Dayimani wambawa bonyana akhambe naye.
P8	"Akusikho kude kodwana ngizokupaka ukudla esizokukhamba nakho," kutjho uDudu.
P9	UDudu noJojo bajama ngaphasi kwentaba uncantathu bonyana badle bebaphumule.
P10	Bathe lokha nebaphakathi nendawo basiya phezulu kwentaba, babona ubulwembu obumangazako besiswebu.
P11	UDudu noJojo bafika endlini kamma uNcantathu.
P12	"IKosi azange itjhiye iphondo layo lapha," kutjho umma uNcantathu.
P13	Umma uNcantathu bekasazi bonyana bangalithola kuphi iphondo.
P14	Besele kusentambama lokha nabafika endini yeKosi eFitjhani neBanzi.
P15	Umma uNcantathu wakhomba ikosi. "Nanto iphondo lakho ehloko."
P16	UKosi uMfitjhani neBanzi wanikela uJojo iphondo bonyana alivuthele. Bebathabe khulu.
