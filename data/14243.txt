title:	Our Classroom

desc:	This story is about pupils class room and how they make use of it.It meant for lower primary pupils.

page_text:

P1	Welcome to our classroom. Our classroom has one door. Also it has two windows. There is a big tree outside our class. Our classroom is very beautiful.
P2	This is Mrs. Joshua. She is our teacher. She teaches us well. We love our teacher.
P3	This is john. He is my friend. We sit together in class. He is a very polite friend.I love my friend.
P4	These are our charts. They are painted on our class-wall. Our teacher uses them to teach us. We enjoy using them in class.
P5	This is a blackboard. It is in front of the class. Our teacher writes on the blackboard. She also draws pictures on it.
P6	These are our books. We use them in class when writing. Our teacher marks them.
P7	Our class has desks. We use them for sitting. We also put our books on them when writing.
P8	These are my classmates. They very noise in class. They do not listen to our new  teacher.
P9	This is a table. It is found in our class. Our teacher puts chalks and books on it.
P10	We sit in groups sometimes in class. Our teacher helps us to read in groups. We like our classroom.
