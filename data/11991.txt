desc:	A story about a much loved family cow.

page_text:

P1	This is Ndalo.
P2	Ndalo likes to read books.
P3	This is Pendo.
P4	Pendo likes to eat carrots.
P5	Ndalo grazes Pendo after school.
P6	He gives fresh water to Pendo.
P7	Father gives grain to Pendo.
P8	He milks Pendo every day.
P9	Pendo gives a lot of milk.
P10	Father sells Pendo's milk.
P11	Ndalo drinks fresh milk every day.
P12	He says,  "Thank you Pendo!"
