P1	Bona ebisolo.
P2	Eŋombe yiroma yiiti, “Mo-o-o.”
P3	Embusi
  yiroma yiiti,
  "me-e-e.”
P4	Embalaasi
  yiroma yiiti,
  "Ur-r-r."
P5	Embiiji yiroma yiiti, "U-u-u-u."
P6	Engoho
  yiroma yiiti,
  ”Ko-ko-ko.”
P7	Embwa
  yiroma yiiti,”Wo-wo-wo.”
P8	Omulimi aloma ati,
  "Ssshhhh!"
