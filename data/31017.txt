id:	31017
title:	Lwaaki yayeri Wakitalo?
author:	Juliet Namugaya
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31017.png
desc:	Mulufumo luno mulimu omuwana adhubibwa okuba owekitalo. Omusomi ayambibwa okudhuuba kumbala nebikolwa ebifuula omuntu okuba owaamaanhi. Yayeri wa kitalo inho?

page_text:

P1	Yayeri owekitalo asituuka munco bulildho.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P2	Musagaalufu lwa obwiire obwabise.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P3	Ateekateeka obuliri bwe bukalamu.Era tweduuza lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P4	Alamusa bazaire be ni ba dhaadha. Twebuuza lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P5	Ayondha mumaiso ge ni saabbuunhi wa bbalulu.Tweghuunhia,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P6	Asenhia amainho ge nolughaanhi.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P7	Anaaba amaadhi amainogovu.Era twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P8	Ayambala indhifomu ye eya kathungwa.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P9	Avaala enbaito endhirugavu.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P10	Yeyunha kwisomero e'Nakibungulya.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P11	Yasoma sayansa,ebyeidhiinhi nokubala.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
P12	Ayemba,yakinha,nokuzaanha omupiira.Twebuuza,''lwaaki Yayeri wakitalo?
