desc:	The story entitledWorry encapsulates the contestation linked to forced marriages in northern Nigeria.

page_text:

P1	Wata rana baban Aisha ya ce, "Zan miki aure."
P2	Ta ce ba ta so, tana da wanda take so. Sai uban ya ce, "Za ki gani."
P3	Daga nan, Aisha ta shiga damuwa. Ta faɗawa uwar, sai ta ce uban bai isa ba.
P4	Daga nan, uban ya ce, "Ai nine babanta."
P5	Aisha kuwa ta ce, "In Allah ya so ya yarda sai ta auri Adamu."
P6	Sai baban ya ce, "Sai kin auri Auwal."
P7	Daga nan, uwar Aisha ta ce, "In Allah ya yarda, kuma tana da rai, Adamu ne ɗiyar zata aura."
P8	Sai Aisha, da murna, ta ce, "Allah ya bar mu tare. Ina son ki mama."
