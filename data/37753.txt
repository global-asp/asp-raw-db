P1	Chicken saw Rabbit. @@"Aha!" Chicken said.
P2	They went to Dog's house. @@"Aha!" said Chicken and Rabbit.
P3	They ran around saying, "Aha! Aha!"
P4	They met Hyena. @@"Look over there," said Chicken, Rabbit and Dog.
P5	They all ran away saying, "Aha! Aha! Aha!"
P6	Then, they met Donkey. @@"Aha," they all said.
P7	They all ran saying, "Aha! Aha! Aha! Aha!"
P8	"Aha," they all said. @@"What are you all scared about?" asked a boy. @@They said, "It was just you!"
