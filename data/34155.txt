page_text:

P1	Likhaya lakitsi lihle futsi likhulu.
P2	Sineligumbi lebantfwana lekulala.
P3	Sineligumbi luhlantela timphahla tetfu.
P4	Siphindze sibe neligumbi lekuphumula singumndeni.
P5	Make utsandza ligumbi lakhe lekuphekela, likhishi.
P6	Sivame kugezela egumbini lekugeza.
P7	Made aphekile make sidlela egumbini lekudlela.
P8	Uma lishisa lilanga siphumula phasi kwesihlahla ngaphandle.
