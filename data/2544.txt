desc:	Hlohletša ka melodi ye e fapanego yeo e dirwago ke diphooflo tša polaseng.Na molemi o dira molodi o fe?

page_text:

P1	Theeletša diphoofolo tša ka gae ge di lla.
P2	Kgomo e re,  "M-m-u-u. M-m-u-u." Kgomo e nyaka meetse.
P3	Pudi e re,  "Me-e-e. Me-e-e." Pudi e nyaka meetse.
P4	Pere e re,  "Yi-hi-hi-hi. Yi-hi-hi-hi." Pere e nyaka meetse.
P5	Kolobe e re,  "Kgo-kgo-kgo." Kolobe e nyaka meetse.
P6	Kgogo e re,  "Ke-kekee-kekee." Kgogo e nyaka meetse.
P7	Mpša e re,  "Hou-hou-hou." Mpša e nyaka meetse.
P8	Molemi o re,  "Ššš! Meetse a gago šia."
