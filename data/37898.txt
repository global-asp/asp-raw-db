P1	Ella loves reading! She tells everyone at school about what she reads about and there so interested and inspired! Ella feels amazing each time she reads! She loves reading so much she reads at home and at school! Ella feels overjoyed!
P2	When Ella goes to school today, the library is closed and the school books have been stolen. Ella felt so sad, she had never spent a day without reading at school. She sat at her desk sad and crying.
P3	A few weeks past and still no books. Everyone missed Ella's fun and amazing story's! So the he school decided that they would get new books! The next day Ella came to school, she saw the library was open and the bookshelf were FULL!
P4	Ella was so happy! A couple days past and the school was having a talent show! When Ella saw the poster, she JUMPED UP and participated right away! She was going to tell one of her amazing story's! She was so excited!
P5	On the day of the talent show, Ella was nicely dressed and she was up first! She was so happy she got to share with the whole school! At the end of the talent show, kids were surrounding Ella and asking her for more stories! Ella felt happy
P6	Ella continued to tell her stories to the whole school in the study area! Ella was so happy and amazed that everyone loved her! She felt loved, happy and included! Ella loved reading and she always will! Her teachers were very proud of Ella
