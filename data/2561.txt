title:	Things I Know

artist:	Michael Nakuwa
lang:	English

desc:	Different things that children are familiar with at home and in school.

page_text:

P1	This is a manyatta. It is my home. My home is in Turkana County.
P2	This is a camel. In Turkana County people keep many different animals. But the camel is very important.
P3	These are my teachers. I like going to school. My school is called Lolupe Primary School. I like my Art teacher very much.
P4	This is a ship. Ships are big. They are used to transport people and goods on water. If I go to Mombasa,  I can see a ship.
P5	This is a bed. I like the bed that my father made for me. I share it with my younger brother. It is comfortable.
P6	This is a cock. At home,  we have two cocks. We also have many chickens. The chickens lay many eggs.
P7	This is a mouse. Mice are dangerous animals. They eat food. They also destroy clothes. They can cause disease.
P8	This is a dog. Dogs are domestic animals. Our dog is called Daa. He barks a lot.
P9	This is an umbrella. My parents have a big umbrella. It has red,  blue,  orange and green colours. When it rains,  we use an umbrella so we do not get wet.
P10	I am in class two and I know many things. I want to know many more. What I do not know,  I ask my parents or my teacher.
