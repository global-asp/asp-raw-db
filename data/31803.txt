title:	Zwimange zwa Tshilima
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Marleen Visser and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tshivenḓa

desc:	Tshilima u na zwimange zwinzhi. Ndi zwimange zwinga zwine Tshilima a vha nazwo?

page_text:

P1	Tshilima zwimange u a zwi funa. Muḓini wa hawe hu na zwimange zwinzhi.
P2	Tshilima u na tshimange tshitswu tshithihi. Tshimange itshi a tshi ḽi zwone ṋama.
P3	Tshilima u na tshimange tshithihi tsha u nona. Itshi tshimange tshi ḽa tshiṅwe na tshiṅwe.
P4	Tshimange tsha vhuraru, tsha mbo gonya muri. A tshi tsha kona u tsa!
P5	Tshilima a mbo gonya uyu muri. Murini a hanganea. Mme a Tshilima vha tea u tsitsa avha vhavhili murini.
P6	Tshilima u na zwiṅwe zwimange zwivhili zwa vhubva. Zwi ṱwa zwo lala ḓuvhani ḓuvha ḽoṱhe.
P7	Tshilima u dovha a vha na zwimange zwiraru zwi sa dzuli fhasi. Zwi bata mbevha khishini vhusiku.
P8	Tshilima u na mmbwa nthihi. I pfi Tshamaṱo. Tshamaṱo a i na khonani. Zwi a i ṱungufhadza.
P9	Zwimange zwa Tshilima a zwi pfani na Tshamaṱo. Zwi a i pandela nḓuni. Tshamaṱo ya mbo paṱa muḓini.
P10	Tshilima a wana he Tshamaṱo ya shavhela hone a vhuya nayo hayani. Zwimange zwi si takale na luthihi. A zwi tsha ṱoḓou  vhona Tshamaṱo.
P11	Tshilima u na zwimange zwingana? U na zwimange zwa malo.
P12	Tshilima u na mmbwa nngana? U na mmbwa nthihi. Tshamaṱo i ngafhi zwino?
P13	Ni na zwimange zwingana hayani? Ni na mmbwa nngana hayani?
