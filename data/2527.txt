title:	Udyakalashe Neediliya Ezimuncu
author:	Kholeka Mabeta
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Eli bali limalunga nodyakalashe olambe kakhulu ozifumana edibana nesitiya seediliya esijinga emthini. 

page_text:

P1	Kudaladala uDyakalashe wavuka elambe kakhulu. Waphakama wangena ehlathini ekhangela indawo enokutya.
P2	Wadibana nesitiya seediliya. Ezi diliya zazintle zijinga zibonakala ukuba zivuthiwe kwaye zivuzisa izinkcwe. Ingxaki yayikukuba zazijinga phezulu kakhulu emthini.
P3	"Kwowu zikhangeleka zimnandi kanobom ke ezi diliya," wacinga watsho umfo wakwaDyaki. Wahlehla umva elungiselela ukuthatha owona mtsi uphezulu ukuze afikelele kwezo diliya.
P4	Nka phantsi! Kwavakala isithonga sokubhuleka kukaDyalakashe esiwa ngomqolo phantsi. Ezi diliya zaziphezulu kakhulu kunokuba ebecinga.
P5	Waphakama umfo omkhulu ezixelele ukuba akasoze atyhafe. Wahlehla umva ngakumbi elungiselela ukuthatha isantya ukuze atsibele phezulu kakhulu. Nka kwakhona! Wabetheka ngamandla kwesi sihlandlo.
P6	Wazama wazama uDyakalashe eqhutywa liphango kodwa nkqi wancama. Wayetsiba abetheke phantsi wade wabuhlungu umqolo wakhe.
P7	Ekugqibeleni wancama wanikezela. Warhotyoza egoduka eqaqanjelwa esabanjwe linxele likakhetsekile.
P8	Wayekhathazeke kakhulu uDyakalashe. Wema wajonga ngemva kwezi diliya azincamayo. "Bezimuncu kakade nezi diliya," watsho uDyaki. Wangena endleleni encamile elambe elufele.
