title:	Amasafu
author:	Catherine Were
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Oluwanga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2922.png
desc:	Olukano luno nilwo omukhana omutiti omulwale na shanyalirwa okhutsia mwisomero tawe. Amasafu kaba injira ya obukhonyi khu mukhana nga akarumikhira okhumanyisia abeetsa nalwala.

page_text:

P1	Lichuma liawere ndanyola amasafu amanji.
P2	Mubarasa yawere,  omusirishi wanje yahandichira omusomesia wanje lisafu.
P3	Wamachina nomulwale. Akhoyere okhwikhala nango.
P4	Olwakhabiri,  ndanyoola lisafu okhurula khu abomushisomero shianje.
P5	Owone lwangu Wamachina!
P6	Muchitaru,  ndanyola lisafu okhurula khu mwitsa wanje wokhumwoyo.
P7	Ekhuchorere eshifwanani shino. Owakhuchama,  Wandanje.
P8	Muchine,  ndanyoola lisafu okhurula khu mama wanje.
