page_text:

P1	Oscar alina ekizibu namaiso ge. Tasobola kubona ekyenandha kyonakyona.
P2	Aye talya carroti de.
P3	Johnnie embalasi ye nanda akoba, ’Oscar lya carroti doh!’
P4	Aye Oscar tayenda ewomelela ye carroti.
P5	'Carroti dida kuyamba okubona okusinga awo, ’Johnnie akoba.
P6	Oscar aja eli omusawo.Oscar ne Johnnie balinda Dr Shakes.
P7	Dr Shakes akoba, ’galubindi dida kuyamba okubona okusinga awo.
P8	Oscar tayenda kwambala galubindi.
P9	Aye galubindi diyamba Oscar okubona engege. Buti alya kaloti na ngege.
