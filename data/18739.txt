title:	Mushimani Yanasalatwi Ki Mutu
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	SiLozi
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mushimani yamaswe aya mwamushitu kuyobata mubisi wanasepisizwe kikacembele kamwendanjangula kuli abe yomunde.

page_text:

P1	Kale kwa nako yasimuluho,  nekuna nimushimani yanali yamaswe yanasalatwi. Neli bashemi bahae nebasamulati.    Kwandu,  nainanga anosi lubasi lwahae inge lubuwa mazimangala.
P2	Kwasikolo,  nainanga anosi banana babang'wi inge babapala.
P3	Nazamayanga ni kwamulaho wabalikani bahae anobayanga kwakunga likomu. Nebasalati kuzamaya niyena.
P4	Lizazi leling'wi ahupula kuzwa fandu. Azamaya mane akena mwamushitu. Akala kusaba,  kono aikutwa tabo bakeng'isa kuli nekusina wakubulela niyena. Kakuli nekusina yanamulata.
P5	Nasakena mwamushitu kacembele kamasila,  akataha kuyena. Kamulumelisa nikumubuza kuli king'i aukena mwamushitu osabisa. Mushimani akomoka kuhutwa mutu ya ambola niyena. Abulelela kacembele kuli akuna kwanaya,  Kakuli akuna yanamulata.
P6	Kacembele kamubuza kuli kappa nabata tuso. Ankufa kualaba,  "Eni." Kacembele kabulela kuli kasikamutusa kale,  nakabata kuli akalwaze kwa meto kufitela kakena nikubonaha bunde.
P7	Mushimani nasina kuketa kakuli nabata tuso. Alwaza kacembele kwameto,  alwaza mamina mwango yakona,  alwaza masila amwazebe akacembele. Alwaza kwameto akacembele nikufitela kabonahala hande kacembele.
P8	Kacembele kamulumba mushimani. Kabulela,  "Ukafumana lika zende mwamushitu,  kono usike kuunga nesilising'wi falika zeo. Utise feela mubisi okafumana mwamushitu."
P9	Mushimani amata,  kubata feela kubona lika zefumaneha mwamushitu. Asikakena kale mwamushiti kacembele kamubiza. Kabulela,  "Mushimani! Taha kwanu!" Amatela kukona kacembele. Kakutela,  "Usike kunga kwalika zende neli silising'wi mwamushitu." Mushimani alumela nikumatela mwamushitu.
P10	Asika kakena kale mwamushitu,  ahutwa linzwi lelibulela,  "Mushimani! Taha kwanu!" Akala kulyanganeya zanakabata kacembele. Akutela kukona hape. Kacembele kabulela,  "Ukafumana lika zende zeng'ata mwamushitu. Kono utise feela mubisi okafumana." Mushimani alumela nikumatela mwamushitu.
P11	Asakeni mwamushitu,  ahutwa linzwi lelibulela,  "Mushimani! Kuta kwanu!" Akutela hape kuka kacembele. Kacembele kabulela,  "Niteleze. Utise feela mubisi okafumana mwamushitu. Usike kunga sesing'wi hape mwamushitu." Mushimani anyema kwakutela manzwi aswana kuzwelela kwa kacembele. Amatela mwamushitu.
P12	Asafitile mwamushitu,  abona mukeke otezi masheleng'i. Anga masheleng'i nikwalonga mwapokoto. Kanako yenyanyani,  ahupula zana kabulezi kacembele. Alongola masheleng'i mwapokoto nikwakutisa mwamukeke.
P13	Aikeza kuzamaya. Abona libyana zende ahulu. Atubula libyana zahae nikutina zenca. Kono ahupula zenekabulezi kacembele. Atubula libyana zenca nikutina zakale.
P14	Kwatuko abona mubisi. Nese uhomisizwe ki lizazi. Aipuza kuli kacembele nakanana kuli mubisi ukausebelisa ng'i. Kwatuko nimubisi nekuna ni mukeke walico. Lico neli nunkela hande hape mushimani nashwile natala. Nasakwanisi kuitima zakuca.
P15	Asafelize kuca,  ahupula zenekabulezi kacembele. Kacembele nekabulezi kuli kikunga mubisi unosi. Atalimela mubisi fonowinzi,  kono nasakoni kuubona hape.
P16	Mushimani asiya mushitu inge anyemile maswe. Akutela kuyobulelela kacembele zeneli ezalile. Kono kacembele nekasabonahali.
