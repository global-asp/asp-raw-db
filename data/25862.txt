desc:	Enpologoma enkazi yateesa okutwala abaana ba maaya.

page_text:

P1	Empologoma  ni  maaya  mukusooka  baali  baamukwano.
P2	Obutafaanana  nga  mpologoma, maaya  yaliisanga obwana bwayo  bukalamu.
P3	Obwana obwe mpologoma tibwali  nammere  ebumala  kulya.
P4	Empologoma yeegwanhiza  obwana  bwa  maaya.
P5	Maaya  bweyali  nga  eziragho  ghaka, Empologoma  yatwala obwana  bwayo.
P6	Maaya  bwe  yaira, yaabuuza, "Abaana  bange  baligha?"
P7	Maaya  yayagaana  obwana  bwayo  n'empologoma  mu  kayumba  kaayo.
P8	Empologoma  yaakoba, "
Bumaaya  bwange buti. Twala  obupologoma."
P9	Maaya  yeebuuza,  "Nnaafuna  ntya  abaana  bange?"
P10	Yayeta  ebisolo  byonabyona  mulukiiko.
P11	Ebisolo  byeida  mulukiiko.
P12	Endhovu yakoba  maaya  n'empologoma boogere.
P13	Ebisolo  ebisinga  byatya okusalira  empologoma  omusango.
P14	Akadhaadha kaakoba ati, "Abaana  abafaanana ni  maaya biba  bya maaya."
P15	Akadhaadha kaalumukira  mu kiina.
P16	Empologpma  yali  nsungughavu  inho. Yairayo  ghaka n'obupologoma bwayo.
