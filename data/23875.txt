page_text:

P1	ondyek kodi ochulu nwango jo mere to joyere nike weyi wa furi rabwo kanyacheli.
P2	to jo tiyo gi meni indelo.
P3	kisi kajo chowo tich ochulu gi ondyek ido jo tuko kanyacheli.
P4	to ido ruman ondye omaro ryemo ochulu. ochulu nwago tera ngwecho.
P5	to di cheli ochulu penjo, "kineni imere paran irengo imaro ryeman?"
P6	to ochulu penjo nike, kineni ikimere para irengo wa tiyo to watuko kanyacheli.
P7	mere meno thano ma donga donga mere nin chil gi chil.
