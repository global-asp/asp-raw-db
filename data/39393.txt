page_text:

P1	kudugu medix meme kennix. @@appa meme, riye meme, ayya meme, naginye meme kerra, riye meme, a a.
P2	kudugu medi meme kennix ngo nna ka 'bus' ngo thux kadidi u'da ma darra. @@ka 'bus' ka'do, ayya meme, nye ma ayya, riye meme, naginye meme kerra, a a.
P3	ngo 'dix u'da ma darra anna ka 'bus'.
P4	ngo 'dix lixvox.
P5	ngo 'dix tigirixshix.
P6	ngo 'dix ndagamu.
P7	ngo 'dix mixzox. ngo shshene ngo shshene ka mixzox, 'da…
P8	'da mizo agudu ka fa!
