page_text:

P1	Leli ligciwane lelinyakatise umhlaba wonkhana.Alibonakali ngemehlo enyama.Bochwepheshe  labaceceshelwe loko bodvwa ngemishini lehlolisisa emagciwane  labakhonako kulibonako.
P2	Leligciwane lingaphila emahora lasiphohlongo kuya kulalishumi esigodvweni, emabhodleleni, kutindvwangu, emaphepheni nobe kukuphi lapho lifike lahlala khona.
P3	Asitivikeleni, sivikele imindeni yetfu, sivikele ummango wangakitsi, njalo siyobe sivikele iNingizimu Afrika yonkhana kunangu mashayabhuce weligciwane.
P4	Mine:@Angigeze tandla njalonjalo ngemanti nensipho nobe ngifutse ngesibulala magciwane. Angigweme kutibamba umlomo, emehlo netimphumulo ngetandla letingcolile.
P5	Singumndeni:@Asitimbonye ngengcoza lapho sikhwehlela nobe sitsimula.Asigweme kuba sendzaweni lephitsitelako nasigula.Asifakeni sivikela-mlomo nemphumulo nasigula nobe sinakekela sigulane.
P6	Ummango:@Uma singativa kahle asiye emtfolamphilo.Uma sinetimphawu temkhuhlane asitivalele endlini sifune lusito kubetemphilo.Singatfumeli imilayeto longanasiciniseko ngayo ngalelinye ngetindlela tekuchumana emmangweni.
P7	Nawudzinga lwati lolutse gcagca hhayi emahemuhemu ngaleligciwane, chumana nelitiko letemphilo kunati tinombolo : @+91-11239 7846 nobe ncov2019@gmail.com
P8	Likhambi lisengakatfolakali kepha liyagwemeka.Sisonkhe singayincoba lemphk ye Covid19
