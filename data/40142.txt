desc:	Duma nɨn ghɨ bèŋsɨ̀ 10. Ntè àŋena nɨn ghɨ ma ikoI zɨ̀ a ghɨ n-toŋtɨ na kòlonà

page_text:

P1	Iyeynì nɨ ndû sɨ woyn ghɨ̀ jɨ̀m, a tɨla' tɨ̀ jɨ̀m a Aflikà.
P2	Izɨyn ì yemi nɨn ghɨ Duma. Mɨ n-kelɨ bèŋsɨ̀ ivɨm.@@Mɨ̀ n-chi ghès ɨ̀ jêmtɨ̀ yem ɨ̀ Zinle. Wù n-ghɨ bèngsɨ̀ njvàsɨ̀ tal.@@Ghès nɨn chî ghèsɨ̀  nà wom, nɨ̀ bò wom, nɨ̀ wayna wès ɨ̀ twelâ Wandile , nɨ̀ bɨ ghes Bubesi.
P3	Sɨ ɨtu' a kòlonà gvì, ichi ɨ̀ ghesi ti meyn kfɨ̀nɨ.@@A n-ghɨ kɨ fɨ fôlɨ̀ teyn mɨ̀tɨ̀i ma fɨ kà' a fɨ nî na ghèsɨ̀nà na ko'i nô sɨ a ŋaŋ. No mɨ̀ ndà kà' a wù fe iko'i bòm kòlonà.
P4	Mɨ n-kumî wuyn bòm ta mà kà' a mà bu na dɨm ghèsɨ̀ sùyn semsɨ.@@Wandile nɨn nsàŋlɨ wì bòm ta wù kà' wù bu ba'nɨ sùynsɨ ŋweynsɨ.@@Zinle nɨn kumi kɨmɨ wuyn bòm ta wù kà' wù bù fì gwòtɨ̀ àmbàŋsɨ̀ ɨ̀chfɨ̀ nɨ̀ ŋweyn ŋêyn suynsɨ  ŋweynsɨ.
P5	Nà wàyn nɨn sû ɨ̀lwe' a ndô àfɨ̀ a ghèsa ntè'. Bàba bêy'lɨ mɨ̀tam nɨ̀ mbàysɨ̀ a ntè'.@@No mɨ nɨ̀ bɨ̀sì-bɨ̀sì achi à kà, àŋena nɨn fvɨ se' alè' a felɨna, a Zinle nà kɨ̀nî ghès.
P6	Wul ntè' nɨ̀ ghès ɨ̀lvɨ̀, izɨyn i ŋweyni ghɨ Gogo, wù n-ghɨ ma wu lema meyn fi kelɨ iko'I atem.@@Ghɨ n-visi wì na nô wul ndù bà'sɨ̀ a ŋweyna ngùŋ lvɨyn. Kòlonà yèyn nɨn bem chwòsɨ̀ sɨ̂ ghɨ̀ lema.@@Ghès nɨn kôŋ wì na wù na ko'i-a.
P7	Ɨlvɨ ɨ̀kfanɨ, nàwayn ɨ̀ ndù sɨ yeyn nâ Gogo yìsi ti-a, wù fu mɨ̀tam ɨ̀n yvɨ̀ sɨ̂ ŋweyn.@@Nàwom nɨn ndû lêm mitam màteyn kɨ ɨchfɨ ndo bòm ta wù kà' wù bu zɨ a ndo.
P8	No mɨ nɨ̀ bɨ̀sì-bɨ̀sì achi àkà, Zinle n-ndù tu mu a bôm sìsɨ̀ ta sɨ n-ba'sɨ.@@Ghelɨ ghɨli nɨn chɨ̂ytɨ achfɨ̀ àŋena nɨ̀ ɨ̀byàsɨ̀ ndzɨsɨ, ghɨli chɨ̂ytɨ wì. Zinle ti meyn fàytɨ ànkul ɨchfɨ̀ nɨ̀ ŋweyn nɨ̀ ndzɨsɨ ŋweynsɨ.@@Wù n-sû ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ ŋweyn a ndo, nàwom wêtɨ àfo a ŋweyn awu sɨ laynsɨ.
P9	Nɨ̀ ntu' no mɨ achi àkà, nàwom ɨ̀ bètɨ̀ sɨ̀ ghès na:@@"Wà tuŋ ànkul ɨchfɨ̀ nɨ̀ và afi-a mɨchi ɨ̀n jɨ̀m. Wà sù ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ và nɨ̀ àsù-à sa mɨntɨ̀mlɨ 20 ɨlvɨ ta wa ndu no mɨ awo. Wa chɨ̂ytɨ ɨ̀chfɨ̀ ɨ̀ va ɨlvɨ ta wa n-kesi kèsa ichem."@@Ghèsɨ bê na, "Ghèsɨ̀ yvɨ meyn a nà."
P10	1. Wa sù ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ va nɨ̀ mû.@2. Wa sù ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ va nɨ̀ asù-a.@3. Wa sù silæ silæ sa mɨntɨ̀mlɨ 20, kèsa wà na taŋtɨ̀-à sɨ @     zɨ̀tɨ̀ a 1 sɨ̀ chem a 20.@4. Wa chù'ti iwu ɨ̀ va nɨ̀ mû.@5. Wa yes ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ va nɨ̀ àbas a ndzɨsɨ a yumina.@6. Lvɨ̂yn, ɨwu ɨ̀ va nɨ̀n ghɨ nɨ̀ ìlayni!
