page_text:

P1	Amaka is a beautiful girl she lives with her parents she does not have any brother or sister she is an only child
P2	She has a friend named Joy, she and joy attend the same school
P3	Amaka dreams of being a pilot and her mother always tells her that if she study hard she can be a pilot
P4	So she studied hard so that she can be a pilot when she grows up
P5	Amaka is happy that she has read hard and she will be a successful pilot in future
