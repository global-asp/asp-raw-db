page_text:

P1	Safiya went to Mafa village to visit her grandmother. On arrival, she mistakenly hit Shafa'atu while running
P2	Safiya apologised to Shafa'atu and they became friends. She later accompanied Safiya to her grandmother's house.
P3	On their way, Shafa'atu asked Safiya if she goes to school and know the essence of education.
P4	Safiya said she knows and thats why she came visiting her grandmother, so she could talk to her father to send her to School.
P5	Eventually, Safiya's father sent her to School. Years past, she graduated and became a successful Engineer. She takes very good care of her family and community.
