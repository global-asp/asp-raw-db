page_text:

P1	Yantwise amezi ikenda.
P2	Yakoze ibishoboka byose nkura mfite ubuzima bwiza n'imbaraga.
P3	Buri joro, yarandyamishaga.
P4	Buri gitondo, yazaga kureba ko nabyutse.
P5	Twajyanaga mu isoko guhaha.
P6	Yaranyemereraga ngahitamo inkweto nshaka.
P7	Igihe nabaga nakosheje, yangiraga inama.
P8	Mu buzima bwange, sinzibagirwa urukundo mama yankundaga.
