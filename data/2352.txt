page_text:

P1	Lipurupru liobwengu khumuyeshe.
P2	Shipumbu eshiengu munzu.
P3	Isishiri ifwana obwengu khumwaanda.
P4	Eshimo tsa lukongo mwitokho.
P5	Eshikaye shiobwengu khwiloba.
P6	Efimuli efiengu munyungu.
P7	Eshichinganga efindu khunjira.
P8	Omuswerurukho kwobwengu mwikunda.
P9	Inzu inyengu khu masanganjira.
P10	Afrika inyengu khu murwe kwanje.
P11	Ne efwe fwesi khwamenya heena? Mushialo shiabamali shia Afrika.
