page_text:

P1	Have you ever heared about the Amampondo? This group of people lived in Nkagala in the Province.
P2	There lived a man and a woman.Their names were Mrs Nompumelelo and Mr Donald Dubula.
P3	They have five children two boys and three girls. The boys names were Sobuka and Njalo. The girls names were Nthando, Chika and Sabu.
P4	There was an old woman. Her name was Gogo Mampinga. She had no children and stays alone.
P5	The family always visited an old woman to help her. They all loved her so much.
P6	While visiting Gogo Mampinga. She will cook them a delicious soup, made of beans and vegetables.
P7	When they arrived at Gogo's place. The two boys will go to fetch some water for granny.
P8	The girls will make tea for all and after that, they will go and sit under the tree and enjoy granny Mampingas' soup.
