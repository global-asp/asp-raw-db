desc:	Twanza was a bad girl who disrespected her parents. But she learned her lesson.

page_text:

P1	It was very early in the morning.
Everyone was waking up to go and dig.
P2	Mother woke Twanza up to go and help with digging the garden.
P3	But Twanza refused to listen to her mother.
P4	Mother left her alone and called Twanza's siblings to help her dig.
P5	They went with Father to dig the garden.
P6	Then they went to fetch firewood for mother.
P7	Twanza's mother cooked food using the firewood the siblings had fetched.
P8	Mother gave the siblings food but did not give Twanza.
P9	Twanza became upset and started crying.
P10	Twanza begged for mother's forgiveness. She promised to be obedient.
P11	Mother was finally happy with Twanza and gave her food. She ate the food.
P12	Twanza started helping out with house work at home.
She became a good girl.
