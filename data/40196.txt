title:	Ikpyu-ikpyu Ikyegh i kpiligh-iyol
author:	Terkule Aorabee
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	Tiv
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40196.png
desc:	Wan-ikegh u gen ken wan-sule u ken wan-gar u kiriki lu awashima u purugh. Mbaikyegh cii se un. Kpa tavershima gbem tsô a purugh.

page_text:

P1	Ngise ansule u kpeghee lu hen wan-gar u kiriki.
P2	Wan ikyegh lu her i yilan un er Ikpyu-ikpyu.
P3	Ikpyu-ikpyu kaa er, "Zum u me vese yô, msoo u purugh yemen sha abeen ca fiiniin."
P4	Mbaikyegh cica cii nande ahan gba sen un.
P5	Ve kaa er, "U ngu vough ga. Se kera numbe vea we ga cii!"
P6	Ngô na kaa er, "Ikpyu-ikpyu, se cii se faityô u gbidyen akper kpa taver u se mbaikyegh se purugh."
P7	Ikpyu-ikpyu gba wer ga. Hanmô iyange a kar iyol u gbidyen akper. @@A gbidye akper kpur kpur kpa a faityô u moughun hen inya ga.
P8	Zum u lu karen-iyol yô, a gba nengen a iyol na ken ishima na er un lu purugh geman kenger ikyegh igen zenden hen inya nahan. @@A nenge a iyol na ken ishima na lu purugh karen ugbargbar man mbaatsator kpaa. "Gbeke!" Inyon igen kpaa i a wuese. "Ikyegh ne ngi purugh!"
P9	Kpur-kpur  kpur-kpur, kpur-kpur, Ikpyu-ipkyu a shi gbidyen akper hanmô iyange.
P10	A mough hen inya kpa a hidye shi a gba.
P11	Ikpyu-ikpyu vaa iyol a ngô na er, "Me faityô u purugh ga. Kwaghôron u mbaikyegh ôr la ka  mimi."
P12	Ngô na kaa er, "Ikpyu-ikpyu we u ngu kposo a mbaikyegh cii. Ve yô ve soo u purugh ga kpa we u soo. U faityô!"
P13	Kpernan yô Ikpyu-ikpyu unde sha ityôô-Ikyegh shi gbidye akper kpur-kpur. @@A purugh sha ahumbe a gbidye akper kimbir-ki-kimbir man…
P14	KPUGH!
P15	Mbaikyegh mbagenev nande ahan gba sen. @@"He-he hieee! Se vande kaan we! Mbaikyegh mba purugh ga!"
P16	Kper nan yô Ikpyu-ikpyu unde yem sha tor-youghu sha atô ica. @@Ikpyu-ikpyu gbidye akper kpur-kpur, kpur-kpur, kpur-kpur.
P17	A purugh sha ahumbe man agbidye akper, agbidye akper kimbir-ki-kimbir man…
P18	A gba purugh yemen hemen! Ahumbe a shin akper na gba taver seer a seer. A purugh ayem ca  sha abeen. @@@Mbaatsator kua ugbargbar gba kpiligh iyol er, "Gbeke! Kwaghkpilighyol! Ikyegh ngi purugh!"
P19	Mbaikyegh cii soo u lun vough er un nahan.
P20	Ve cii ve ember er, "Aye, Ikpyu-ikpyu, kwagh wou gande!"
