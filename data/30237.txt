desc:	Olufumo olwa abasaadha ababiri ne entwiiga.

page_text:

P1	Ghaligho abasaadha babiri abalina amaka gaibwe eyo kukyaalo.
P2	Obusiisira bwali nga bweswiirana ku kiketezo ekibba.
P3	Obulunzi no ogwaali omulimo gwaibwe.Balundha ebisolo kamaala.
P4	Mukiseera ekyo,ghaligho ekyeeya.Ebirime byakala.
P5	Endhala kakutiya yali yaamaanhi ino.Abantu badhuuba kungeri ye banabaamu bukalamu ne ebisolo byaibwe.
P6	Lulala abasaadha babiri baaja okuyiiga,baabona entwiiga nga eri kulya amakoola ne ebibala byonabyoona ku muti.
P7	Bakubagana obwaama kungeri ye banagiitamu era bakavuule emmamba.
P8	Oghundhi yakoba ati,''ndha kugifumita eifumo!''Bwe yagolola omukono ne eifumo agiite,omuyiizi oghundhi yamugema omukono.
P9	Omuyiizi oyo ya nhiiga yagolola.Eyamulobera yaghalampa omuti entwiiga gwe yali eri kulyaku amakoola.Yatuumira munkoto yaayo yagikalangusa.
P10	Entwiiga yatya inho.Yaghalabanhira mu kibira nga yekuutira ku gasubi.
P11	Omuyiizi yakoogha yafunha na agabwa.Yagwa okuva ku ntwiiga.Omubiri gwe gwavulubana omusaayi.
P12	Yadhunhibwa omuntu eyali abita era yamutwaala eghughe yamwidhandhaba.
P13	Ngali ghaka yaghaya kungeri okuyiiga entwiiga bwe kwamutuusa kumitaghaana.Okuva kwolwo yalayira obutairayo kuyiiga bisolo byamunsiko.
