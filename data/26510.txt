desc:	Wampube owomunsiko acaalira Wampube womunhumba okubona obulamu obwewunhisa munda omwo. Aye obuluvu bwa Wampubi

page_text:

P1	Agho  olwatuuka, yaliyo  ba Wampube babiri.
P2	Wampube  oghundi  yali  wa  munsiko  ate  oghundi  yali  wa  mu kyalo.
P3	Lwali  lulala, Wampube ow'omukibuga  yaakoba  ow'omukyalo, "Olya  bubi  ate  waasula  bubi inho."
P4	Ife  tusula  mu ndhu ya mugaiga era  tulya  ebika  eby'emere  eby'endhawulo buli lunaku. Idha oncaalireku obone, "Wampube w'omukibuga  neyakoba."
P5	"Ndha  kwidha obwire  bwaleero," Wampube  w'omukyalo  neyakoba.
P6	Mukaseera  ako  omukungu  yali  n'obusungu era yaalaama  empube edhimuliira  emereye, dhiidha kubona!" akaseera ako,  yaatega  akatego nga kaliku sukaali.
P7	Ba  Wampube  baingira  okuja  okulya  ebirungi  ebiri  mu nhumba. Baidha  nga  beeseka, "chuchuchichicheche!"
P8	Bwe  baatuuka  ku  katego, Wampube  w'omukibuga  yaakoba, "Leka ndozeeku ku sukaali ono.  Olikubona  ebirungi byetulya  eno.
P9	Aye nga  Wampube  akaali kuloza kusukaali, akatego kaamugema, pa! Akatego kaagema omutwe gwa  Wampube, amaiso  ge  gaamutunguka.
P10	Wampube w'omukyalo  yaamulingirira  bulingirire, yeewuunhia. Yaakoba, "E. Heee! mukwano  gwange, birungi ki  byoliire  ebikanwire  amaisogo ekyagaghano?"
P11	Aye nga  mwine tasobola  nakumwiramu.
P12	Agho  Wampube  yaafuluma  nga  akoba, "Haa! Tiidha  kusobola  birungi  byompaniire."
P13	Amaka go  amabi  gasingaku amalungi  agatali gago.
