desc:	Indatjana imayelana nabentwana beenlwana abahlengiweko abafika eentandanini. Iinlwanyana lezi zitlhogonyelwa mumuntu okhethekileko nethimba lakhe.

page_text:

P1	Indatjana le imayelana noMgcini nendawo yakhe yokuhlalisa iinlwanyana eziziintandani.
P2	Inyanga edlulileko bekuyinyanga ematasatasa khulu.

UNomsa onomnyaka owodwa kwaba nguye owafika kokuthoma. Bekondile begodu angakathabi.
P3	Msinyazana nje, uNomsa waba namandla. Wathoma ukudlala nezinye iinlwana.
P4	Ngelinye ilanga ngenyanga leyo, unophehlwana wahlala endaweni yeenlwana.
P5	Ngaphakathi kwakanophehlwana bekunendlovu encani yembeswe ngengubo.
P6	UNdiwa bekanamalanga amahlanu ubudala. 

UMgcini wadlisa uNdiwa ngebhodlelo.
P7	Ngobunye ubusuku, woke umuntu wavuswa kufika kuka-Ambiya
P8	U-Ambiya bekaneenyanga ezihlanu ubudala. Bekadiniwe ngemva kwakhambo elide.
P9	Ngebanga lentamo yakhe ede, uMgcini waphakamisela ibhodlelo phezulu ukuze amuphe ukudla.
P10	Iintandani ezafika kokugcina ngenyanga leyo, bekumakonyana alambileko uKopi, uKepi noKeji.
P11	UMgcini nabasizi bakhe bebathanda zoke iinlwana ngitjho nalezo ezilelesako.
P12	Abantu abasebenza endaweni yeentandani bebasebenza budisi. 

Bebathemba bona ngelinye ilanga iintandani zizokwazi ukuzitlhogomela. Bese iinlwana lezi zizakubuyela emmangweni.
