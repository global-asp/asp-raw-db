title:	Aḽilali ha funi zwone vhaeni
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	African Storybook, Rasheedat Sadiq and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs

desc:	Aḽilali ndi musidzana a no funa vhaeni nahone u a kona u vha takadza.

page_text:

P1	Khokhokho!@@Hu pfala u khokhonya vothini ḽa phanḓa.
P2	Aḽilali o takala nga maanḓa.@ @"Ndi nnyi?" u a vhudzisa.@  @"Mma, ndi na mueni!"
P3	"Ndi zwone," hu fhindula mme awe. "Ndi Vho Hassan. Vha vuleleni."
P4	Aḽilali a vula muṋango a tshi khou ṅwethuwa. @@"Aa, Vho Hassan, kha vha dzhene. Mma vha khou ḓa."@  @Vho Hassan vha mwemwela-vho na vhone musi vha tshi vhona Aḽilali.
P5	"Ndi vha ḓisele mini tsha u nwa?" hu vhudzisa Aḽilali.@ @"A songo ḓidina, Aḽilali, ndo livhuha," hu fhindula Vho Hassan.
P6	"Ri a vusa, Vho Hassan," hu amba mme a Aḽilali. "Vha mpfarele ndo lenga."@ @"A hu na dinani khonani yanga, Aḽilali o ntswielela."
P7	Mme a Aḽilali vha ṅwethuwa vhukuma.@ @"Na zwino Aḽilali ndi musidzana wa mafunda nahone ndi pfa ndi tshi ḓihudza ngae," vha vhudza Vho Hassan.
