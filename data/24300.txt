page_text:

P1	Aniy'amanya was a brave young girl who was born into a family of four.
P2	She was the only girl among three boys.
P3	She was kind to everyone in the village and was popular for it.
P4	One day, the village was attacked by cattle raiders.
P5	The fifteen year old Aniy'amanya was awake.
P6	She screamed very loudly when she saw the raiders.
P7	The whole village woke up in shock at her very loud scream.
P8	She was brave enough to beat up one of the raiders who was stealing a cow.
P9	She stopped him from running away.
P10	Aniy'amanya was praised by everyone for her bravery and kindness.
