desc:	This are two boys from single parent families who have passion for education especially in music and whose parents have overcome gender barriers in their carrier choices.

page_text:

P1	Charo's friend is called Pascal Yaa.@They are in the same class at Mbaraka Chembe Primary
P2	Charo and Yaa like singing.@They like singing in the school and on their way home.@One day they will be singers on radio and television.
P3	Charo and Yaa like reading story books.@They have cards to read stories on the computer.@Their teacher likes them to read to the class.@Other pupils like the way Charo and Yaa read in class
P4	Charo's mother is a fisherwoman.@Yaa's father is a cook in a hotel in Malindi.@Charo's mother sells fish to Yaa's father.@They use the money to buy books for their children.
P5	Charo's mother and Yaa's father have lots of money.@They have good plans for their children.@They tell them to sing at school, in church and at home.@Singing at night in disco matanga is a bad habit.@It makes children drop out of school.
