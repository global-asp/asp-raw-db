desc:	Kibonerezo ki ekyomulenzi omuluvu eyalya ebibala ebingi?

page_text:

P1	Dadha  yaleta  ebibala  okuva  mukatale.
P2	Byabonheka  nga  bighoma.
P3	Mugandaghange  Ivaibi  yali  gha  muluvu.
P4	Nanhi  akubye  ebibala?
P5	Dadha  yanhiga  yaalekana.
P6	Tusubiire  ivaibi.
P7	Twakobera  daadha.
P8	Daadha  yamubuza  era  yaikiriza.
P9	Ekyairirira,  Ivaibi  tiyaghulira  bulungi.
P10	Yalumuka  yagya  mumugwana.
P11	Ivaibi  yagema    kunda  ye.
P12	Dadhd  yabuza,"Ki  kyoyeze  munebiya  ya  Ivaibi?"
