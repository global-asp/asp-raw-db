desc:	Emboozi egema ku musaadha eyazimba amayumba amalungi eri abe kyalo ate nga yali asula mu kayumba kabi.

page_text:

P1	Yaliyo omuzimbi owa maani ku kyalo kabuku.
P2	Yazimba amayumba mangi ku kyalo.
P3	Aye yali asula mu kayumba kabi.
P4	Abantu babuuza endhu ye buli lunaku."mutafaayo,nina entekateka,"omuzimbi yairamu.
P5	Yasonda sente era yagula amatafaali
P6	Yagula omukyanga,sementi,amabaati,ne misumaali.
P7	Mpolampola, yezimbira endhu enene,enungi.
P8	Mikwano gye jasanhuka okubona endhu ye empyaka.
P9	Yatekateka omukolo okwanhiriza buli muntu.Balya banwa
P10	Olwo bakina.
P11	Omuzimbi yafuna ebirabo bingi olwe ndhu ye empyaka.
P12	Buti abazimbi ku kyalo kabuka basula mu mayumba malungi.
