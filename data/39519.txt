page_text:

P1	Chioma and her mother are outside admiring the rainbow.
P2	Mother took her to the garden to see some colourful butterflies and flowers.
P3	They read a book about nature.
P4	They went to the pack to relax and watch people and the rainbow.
P5	Mother told her the story of the festival of colours...
P6	Where people celebrated the rainbow by wearing colourful costumes.
