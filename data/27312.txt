title:	Magezi ayagaanna amaka

desc:	Omulenzi yeeyagaana nga ali ku luguudo nabaana abandi. Emboozi eno ya kuloghooza era ya buzibu ku bulamu bwomulenzi nengeri yainhamamu okucuuka mu mbeera enhungi.

page_text:

P1	Ghaligho ekibuga ekyali nga kidwire baana ba ku nguudo. Omwana omuto mwabo baali bamweta Magezi.
P2	Magezi we yagheza emyaka etaanu, abazairebe baafa. Yaaja yaabanga ni koojaaghe Bunu.
P3	Kooja Bunu ono yali musaadha mubi. Kino kyaleetera Magezi okulumuka mu makaage yaaja yasenga ku nguudo.
P4	Obulamu obw'okuluguudo tibwamubeera bulungi kuba abaana baali bawoda  mu bantu.
P5	Lwali lulala, Magezi yayagaana akatabo ak'emboozi ku kiina eky'ebikaba.
P6	Akatabo ako kaalimu ebifaananie eby'omuvuzi ow'enhoni. Magezi yayenda kuba omuvuzi ow'enhoni asinga buli kiseera.
P7	Lwali lunaku lulala Magezi ya yagaana Tomasi.
P8	Tomasi yatwala Magezi ni mikwanogye okufuna emere.
P9	Tomasi yakoba Magezi asome. Magezi yeena yairamu ati "tiidhi kusoma."
P10	Magezi yeena yakoba Tomasi," Nalumuka mu maka ga kooja wange Bunu kuba yali musaadha mubi."
P11	Tomasi yabuuza Magezi, "Oyenda okwega n'okusoma?" Yamala yaagha Magezi akatabo ak'emboozi akayaaka.
P12	Magezi yaloghooza okwirayo kwisomero. Yaidhukira koojaaghe Bunu ebibono byeyamukobanga ati, "Oli musiru ate ozira ky'oidhi."
P13	Kino kyagemya Magezi enaku. Tomasi yakoba," Otaba munakughavu. oidha kuba bukalamu."
P14	Tomasi yatwala magezi n'abaana abandi mu maka agaalimu abaana. Bano boonaboona baali basanhufu inho.
P15	Magezi yairayo kwisomero. Yayenda inho okuba kwisomero era ya soma inho.
P16	Lulala Magezi yakobera Tomasi," Nda kuba omuvuzi ow'enhoni asinga buli kiseera."
