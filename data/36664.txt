P1	Once upon a time in a jungle there lives a giraffe. His name was Gerry. Gerry was very kind and helpful to other animals. He loves to play with his friends elephant,zebra etc.
P2	One day there came a hunter to hunt anomals. He laid the trap and waited for animals to come and be caught on that trap.
P3	As soon as the giraffe came he was stuck in the trap and the hunter was thinking how to kill the giraffe.
P4	Giraffee thought how to teach the hunter a lesson and then he had a brilliant idea in his mind.
P5	The giraffee called his friend elephant to help him. The elephant losen the knot and ran away.
P6	As soon as the hunter came the giraffee was runnig away.
P7	So he holded the tail of giraffe to stop him butthe giraffe was running away.
P8	Then the giraffee threw the hunter and went home running.
P9	Now the giraffee was safe and with his friends happily playing
