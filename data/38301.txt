title:	The TWO BROTHERS AND THE MONKEY

desc:	The story consist of a family of three people. A father and his two sons

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there lived a family. The family consisted of a father and his two sons. The sons were Oganga and Kurumi.They loved each so much that where Oganga went Kurumi followed him. On this evening  they were taking their supper.
P2	As their were taking their evening meal, their father told them that the following morning they were going to the shamba to plant some seeds. The following morning they woke earlier to go the shamba. They carried the seeds and their jembes.
P3	They hurried to the shamba and they left their father at home with their dog. As they were on their way to the shamba they heard some sound from a nearby bush.They were scared but continued to the shamba. Suddenly, a monkey jumped infront.
P4	They were scared more and left the basket carrying their seeds of mangoes fall down. The monkey hurriedly went to where the basket was and started eating the seeds of the mangoes. They stared at the monkey eat all mangoes.
P5	Oganga and Kurumi cried all their way until they reached home. They found their father drinking from his calabash. When their father saw them ,he asked them why they were crying. They told him that the monkey had eaten all the seeds.
P6	The father decided to teach the monkey a lesson. He told them that the following morning they were to carry another basket with their dog inside. The following day they went to the shamba . The monkey was on the way waiting for them.
P7	The monkey was very happy as he took the basket from Oganga and Kurumi. He was sure he will be satisfied because the basket was so heavy.The brothers were very sure to teach the monkey a lesson through their dog.
P8	Oganga and his brother Kurumi were very happy when the dog jumped from the basket. The dog attacked the monkey. The monkey cried loud but the dog continued attacking him. The monkey was wounded so badly.
