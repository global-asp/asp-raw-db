desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school. Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Last week I received a lot of letters.
P2	On Monday my doctor wrote a letter to my teacher.
P3	Thabisile is sick. She must stay at home.
P4	On Tuesday I got a letter from my class.
P5	Get well soon Thabisile!
P6	On Wednesday I got a letter from my best friend.
P7	I drew this picture for you. Love from Dudu.
P8	On Thursday I got a letter from my mother.
