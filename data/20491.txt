desc:	A story about the creation of the world and people.

page_text:

P1	God wanted someone to look after his creation.
P2	Lion roared, "I am the king, I can do that."
P3	Elephant shouted, "I am big. I am right for the job."
P4	Whale said, "I swim and care for fish. I am the right one."
P5	Shark thought his sharp teeth made him the right one.
P6	Eagle laughed, "I fly and see everything. I am fit."
P7	God said, "I will create man. He will do a better job."
P8	He created man and woman with shiny black skins.
P9	Others had white skin, blue eyes and golden hair.
P10	Others had yellow skin, black hair and slanted eyes.
P11	Some had brown skin, black hair and black eyes.
P12	Lion roared, "I can kill them all. I am strong."
P13	Other animals thought humans looked strange and different.
P14	Shark and Fish laughed at humans, "How will they swim?"
P15	God smiled, "Humans must care for what I have created."
