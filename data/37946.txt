desc:	Nkhani iyi ikutsutsana ndi zikhulupiliro zakuti ntchito zina ndi zoyenera amuna kapena akazi okha.

page_text:

P1	Njingalimoto ndi galimoto ya matayala atatu.@@Imagwiritsidwa ntchito mtawuni kuti anthu ayende mwachangu kupita malo osiyanasiyana.
P2	Mdera lathu, kuyendetsa galimoto ndi ntchito ya amuna.@@Akazi sagwira ntchito imeneyi.
P3	Tsiku lina, Abebech anapempha makolo ake kuti amupatse ndalama zokaphunzirira kuyendetsa galimoto.@@Makolo ake anati, "Ntchito imeneyi siyabwino kwa atsikana. Anthu aziti chiyani?"
P4	Komabe, Abebech anati, "Ndili ndi kuthekera kochita chilichonse chimene anthu ena angathe." Anakwanitsa kuwanyengerera makolo ake.@@Makolo ake anavomera kuti akayambe kuphunzira kuyendetsa galimoto.
P5	Abebech anamaliza ndi kukhonza maphunziro aja.@@Makolo ake anakambirana zoti achite kutsatira maphunziro ake.
P6	Pamapeto pake, anagwirizana zomugulira njingalimoto.@@Kotero, Abebech anayamba kuyendetsa njingalimoto m'misewu ya mumzinda wa Debre Birhan.
P7	Tsiku lina Abebech anaganiza mwakuya.@@Anamata uthenga ndi nambala yake ya foni kumbuyo kwa njingalimoto yake.
P8	Uthengawo unati, "Ndimanyamula ulele amayi oyembekezera, amayi amene abereka kumene, komanso ana ang'ono."@@Amayi onse amene ana awo adwala amamuimbira Abebech.
P9	Abebech anapanga ndalama zambiri ponyamula anthu.@@Anapitiliza kunyamula ulele anthu amene analibe ndalama.
P10	Abebech anali okondwa kwambiri ndi ntchito yake. Anthu okalamba anamudalitsa. Aliyense amakamba za iye nthawi zonse.@@Abebech anawauza kuti, "Kuchita zabwino kumadalitsa."
