P1	A king named bedhar always showed of his riches and made others feel very sad. People always mutter that the king they have is not doing anything for us
P2	Sometimes the king hurts some children  . Which makes most of the kids sad. This way a man said he had to stop this reign of show off and had a nice little plan
P3	The man told his plant to their family and they understood exactly what to do.
P4	So the man now disguised as a hunter slowly walked by until he saw the king
P5	And just on time the man saw the king walk by.
P6	As usual he was shouting about his riches and showing off
P7	The disguised man said to the king."don't you hear girls shouting near that tree . Yes said the king confused. "Oh sir they have a magical tree were they can get as much money as they want . You should shout mounter and u will get the money
P8	There the disguised man's daughter was climbing up and shouting which was the girl voice. She hid her snake mounter behind the tree and climbed up as fast as she could.
P9	When the man took the king near the tree. The king was so excited he shouted MOUNTER and wishes the amount of money he wants. But as soon as he said mounter . The snake behind the tree who's name was mounter appeared in the feet of the king
P10	But he doesn't bite at all. But the king hate snakes. The man said . Majesty you said you can do anything with money so please stop the snake with your money.the king regretted.no never money doesn't buy everything and he ran away .
