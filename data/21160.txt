title:	Segwagwa Le Noga

artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Setswana

desc:	Tlhamane e e re ruta gore botsala bongwe bo nale kwa bo felelang.

page_text:

P1	Segwagwa le Noga ke ditsala.
P2	Segwagwa a re, “Tsamaya le nna go ya go etela mme.”
P3	“Ke a leboga." Noga a rialo. “Ke mang yo o tla lebelelang matlo a rona?”
P4	“Ke tla lebelela matlo a lona.” Ga rialo Tshwene.
P5	Segwagwa o ne a tseela mmagwe ditshoswane tse di tshweu.
P6	Segwagwa le Noga ba ne ba tsamaya ka masa. Ba tsena mo sekgweng.
P7	Ba ne ba kopana le setlhopa sa bo moshe. Noga o a tshaba.
P8	Segwagwa o sireletsa tsala ya gagwe.
P9	Ba tshwerwe ke tlala fa ba fitlha kwa ntlong ya ga Mme.
P10	Ba ile ba tlhapa matsogo mme ba ipaakanyetsa go ja.
P11	“Noga, ke kopa a dule sentle,“ ga rialo Segwagwa.
P12	Noga o ne a leka go dula sentle, mme fela ga ise segwagwa.
P13	“Ga ke kgone go dula sentle jaaka segwagwa, Noga a rialo.
P14	Segwagwa o ne a sa batle go utlwa.
P15	“Ga o ntlhaloganye. Nna ke ya gae.”
P16	Noga o tenegile. “Ke ne ke gopola gore Segwagwa ke tsala ya me.”
