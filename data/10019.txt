title:	Abeebi B'emiyembe

desc:	Abegi bane baali bebbi bemiyembe. Omwene miyembe gabanghambire gabanghira hu somero babahangavula.

page_text:

P1	Ebiseera ebyahale,  yaaliyo otuseere tune.
P2	Twasomeranga hu Kaguja primary school.
P3	Otuseere twatinanga hu somero ni tutaliyeho maŋolu goosi.
P4	Tweŋongereranga mu hibiina olw'enjala.
P5	Lulala tweyibire mu somero ohuja ohuheja eby'ohulya.
P6	Ni twasomohire hula twagwirihirira oguyembe oguleremayeho emiyembe emirabire.
P7	Otuseere twadulumire twola hu musaala twanoga emiyembe,  twamwenyera era twatagiha ohujirya.
P8	Omanyire ehi tuseere tuno twebirira?
P9	Tuba ko tulya omweene miyembe nga aŋwayo naŋaala,  huŋaala.
P10	Otuseere twali situmubona. Ni gahabuha,  twafulumuha twatiina twehweeha.
P11	Hiisi halala sihehirisanga hahye huholola,  huloma oba huhayaana.
P12	Omweene miyembe gawene atyo nga ahubba endulu ejaleeta abaapi n'abaleeŋi.
P13	Otuseere tuno batuŋambire era baatuŋira hu somero balanga abasaaye b'otwibi,  abasomesa ko n'abeegi bahyawe ohuhumbana. Ehyali ŋala hyaali hihulu.
