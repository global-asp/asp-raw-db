desc:	Byantaka ekaba nayenda ensoha enkooto

page_text:

P1	Byantaka akaba  aina ente n’ensoha entaito yamaizi.
P2	Yayetiza enkooto owomutahi.
P3	Yakwata ensoha entaito yagita omunkooto.
P4	Yazitwara owomutahi.
P5	Yamugamba  ngu ensoha yawe ezaire endi.
P6	Yagarukayo owomutaihi kwetiiza endi.
P7	Atagigareyo, omutahi yagenda kugitabarra.
P8	Byantaka yamugamba ensoha ekafa, mbaire
  ninyija kukugambira
P9	Omutahi yatoka nagamba ati tinkahurraga ensoha
  efire.
P10	Byantanka yagamba, munyani wange buli
  ekyazairwe kifa.
P11	Omutaahi yagenda  omukooti kumunyegerra.
P12	Omuramuzi yagamba ngu ekintu kyona ekizarwa
  kifa.
P13	Byantaka yatunga ensoha enkooto,omutahi
  yafeerwa..
