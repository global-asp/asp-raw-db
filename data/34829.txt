desc:	Akụkọ a bụ maka otu nwata akwụkwọ nwoke si hu anyanwụ site na- mmalite na odida ya.

page_text:

P1	Anwụ ụtụtụ malitere@  site na east.@  @@  Anuru m ka ọkukọ kwara akwa n’ụtụtụ
P2	Anwụ gafere ngwuru@  Terfa tupu nri ụtụtụ.@  @@  Anuru m isi bier na@  akpupa nke soro anwu ekubata@  na ṅpio.
P3	Anwụ gara na-azu@  ụlọ akwukwo n’oge nri ehihe.@  @@  Owee nọrọ na-etiti@  ama egwuregwu
P4	Anwụ kwụrụ n’elu@  isi m. Onyinyo m kwụrụ na-akuku m.@  @@  Mụ na ndi enyi m gwuru egwu onyinyo.
P5	Onyinyo m biara buwanye,@  biakwa pee mpe. Anyi gbasobe ya.@  @@  Onyinyo m towara ogologo,@  biakwa di nkenke. Anyi gbasobe ya.
P6	M kwụụrụ, ndi enyi m kwurụ.@  Anyi hụru onyinyo anyi ka ọ na-efeghari.@  @@  Ike gwụrụ anyi, anyi wee laghachi n’ime klasi. Ka akwụkwọ@  gbasara, anyi laghachi n’ụlọ.
P7	Anwụ yere uyere.@  @@  Ahuru m anyanwụ ka@  o ji nwaayo nwaayo agbada n’odida. Ahuru m onyinyo m n’ahu aja.@  @@  Ǫ bụ oge eji ehi@  ụra.
P8	Anyanwụ wee daa.@  @@  E dinara m n’akwa m, wee rọọ nrọ ebe anyanwụ@  gara njem di aṅya.
