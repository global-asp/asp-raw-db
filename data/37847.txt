desc:	Sithini isohlwayo senkwenkwe enyolukileyo eyatya iziqhamo ezininzi?

page_text:

P1	Ngenye imini, umama wathenga iintlobo-ntlobo zeziqhamo.
P2	"Singazitya nini iziqhamo?" sabuza.@@"Siza kuzitya namhlanje ebusuku," watsho umama.
P3	Umntakwethu uRahim unyolukile.@@Wazingcamla zonke iziqhamo. Watya ezininzi.
P4	"Jonga wenze ntoni uRhahim!" wakhwaza watsho umntakwethu omncinci. @@"URahim uyageza yaye ucingela yena yedwa," ndatsho.
P5	Umama wayenomsindo ngakuRahim.
P6	Nathi sasinomsindo ngakuRahim.@@Kodwa uRhahim wayengazisoli.
P7	"Uza kumohlwaya uRahim?" wabuza umntakwethu omncinci.
P8	"URahim uza kuzisola kungekudala," walumkisa watsho umama.
P9	URahim waqalisa ukuziva engaphilanga.
P10	"Isisu sam sibuhlungu," wasebeza watsho uRahim.
P11	Umama wayesazi ukuba uRahim uguliswa yintoni.@@"Iziqhamo ziyamohlwaya uRahim," wacinga njalo.
P12	Ethubeni, uRahim waxolisa kuthi. "Andisoze ndiphinde ndinyoluke," wathembisa.@@Samkholelwa.
