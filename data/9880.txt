title:	Abahlobo
author:	Nolitha Bikitsha
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First words

desc:	Ibali ngekwenkwe nabahlobo bayo

page_text:

P1	Igama lam nguSimilo.
P2	Ndinabahlobo abane.
P3	Amagama abo nguZuko,  uLizo,  uViwe noSiyanda.
P4	Umhlobo wam uZuko uthanda ibhola.
P5	Umhlobo wam uLizo uthanda ukudada.
P6	Umhlobo wam uViwe uthanda undize.
P7	Umhlobo wam uSiyanda uthanda ukufunda.
P8	Mna,  ndithanda konke abakuthandayo.
P9	Ndidlala ibhola noZuko.
P10	Ndidada edamini noLizo.
P11	Ndidlala undize noViwe.
P12	Yiza mhlobo. Wena,  uthandani?
P13	Yiza,  uzodlala ibhola nathi.
P14	Yiza,  uzodada nathi.
P15	Yiza,  uzodlala undize nathi.
P16	Yiza,  uzofunda nathi!
