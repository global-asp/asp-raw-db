desc:	Ubusambo bwatuma ubura byose. Umuhigi yize iri somo bigoranye.

page_text:

P1	Habayeho umuhigiwari utunzwe no gutega inyamaswa zo mu ishyamba. Haza guca iminsi nta nyamaswa afashe. Umuryango we utangira gusonza. Ubuhigi nibwo bw'ibanze bakuragamo ifunguro.
P2	Igitondo kimwe yagiye kureba imitego ye mu ishyamba. yagize amahirwe uwo munsi, umwe mu mitego ye wari wafashe agasumbashyamba.
P3	Yahise yishima akibona ako gasumbshyamba. Yishimiye ko umuryango we wari bubone ifunguro. Nuko aravuga "Reka nitonde mfate agasumbashyamba nta n'iraso ryako ntakaje."
P4	"Reka nshe umutsi wo ku kabombampori nibwo hava amaraso make."
P5	Agendesha inda, icumu yaritunze ku mutsi w'akabombampori. Ikibabaje ni uko yahushije. Icumu rica umutego.
P6	Agasumbashyamba karasimbutse katararenga, umuhigi agafata umurizo. Karamukuruta.
P7	Kamukurura ishyamba ryose. Mu nyuma arekura umurizo. Asigara mu gashyamba k'amahwa yakomeretse.
P8	Arwana no kwivana muri ayo mahwa, ajya imuhira yakomerekejwe kandi anababaye.

Ubusambo n'ubuswa bwe bwamuteye kubura ifunguro.
