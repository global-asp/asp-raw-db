page_text:

P1	There was a man named Mike. He had been searching for a wife  since he was 22 years. He finally met a girl called Aisha from a good family and they fell in love. Thier parents agreed for marriage and they were very happy. Aisha had beauty
P2	Aisha and Mike got married and stayed together happily with Mike for two years without children. Mike requested to Aisha to do a test to check whether she is potent enough to give birth.
P3	Aisha did some tests and she got her result paper.She went home and gave the result to Mike. He opened it and found out that she was infertile . Aisha cried  and Mike told her that he is getting a new wife. She cried even more.
P4	The next day, Mike came back with a new wife. Her name was Zamu. She wasn't that attractive but she was fertile. Mike introduced her to me and they went to their room. Zamu came out and told me that she was going to win the heart of Mike.
P5	Aisha asked Mike the reason for all these and he told her that he still loves her but he wants a child. Aisha suggested that they would have adopted a child and he thought of it and agreed. He said he will kick Zamu out of the house
P6	The next day, Mike kicked Aisha out of the house and went with Aisha to the orphanage. They got a boy and a girl and they were very happy
