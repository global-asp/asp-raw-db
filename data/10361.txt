desc:	A story for toddlers about a cow with a daytime identity as a soft toy

page_text:

P1	Ke Supercow.   Super entle!
P2	Ka letsatsi le letsatsi e lula e ts'oana le likhomo tse ling.
P3	Supercow u fofisa qhaete.
P4	Supercow o raha bolo.
P5	Bosiung bo bong le bo bong entse ele Supercow!
P6	O boloka maphelo a batho ha monate.
P7	O loants'a masholu.
P8	Empa ha o ka koala mahlo a hao ka thata. Ebe o etsa litakatso -.
P9	Supercow e kanna eatla ho o chakela le oena!
P10	Pale ea rona e felile!
