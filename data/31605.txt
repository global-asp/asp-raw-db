desc:	Sara is helping people and animals across the river in her canoe

page_text:

P1	Saaraookhalana ekalawa yuulupale.@@Eyo eri okhopela wa muro.
P2	Athiyana akina yaahirwa omuro. Muthiyana mmosa aakunxe ekamela yaari mihokorokho. Mukina aamukunxe mulyakhu. Mukina aakunxe epuri. Mukina-tho aakunxe ipuri piili. Awo yaahimukoha Saara wiira: "Hiyo nilapuwele sayi ekhopela ekina ya muro?"@@Saara aahiwaakhula wiira: "Mukele mukalawani mwaka!"
P3	Vahipinse, yaahirwa alopwana. Mmosa aakunxe ihopa, mukina aari ni paaxikoro, mukina aamukunxe nakhuwo musaakhoni.
P4	Mukina aamukunxe nakhuwo isaakho piili. Awo yaahimukoha Saara wiira: "Hiyo nilapuwele sayi ekhopela ekina ya muro?" @@Saara aahiwaakhula wiira: "Mukele mukalawani mwaka!"
P5	Axinama akina yaahirwa omuro weiwo. Axinam'awo yaahimukoha Saara wiira: "Hiyo nilapuwele sayi ekhopela ekina ya muro?"@@Saara aahiwaakhula wiira: "Mukele mukalawani mwaka!"
P6	Axinama yaakenle mukalawani taala: mwalapwa, kwaatu, khole, namarokolo, khapa ni naahe.
P7	Vano, etthepo yaahirwa omuro weiwo. Eyo yaahikoha wiira: "Miyo kilapuwele sayi ekhopela ekina ya muro?"@@Saara aahaakhula wiira: "Mukele mukalawani mwaka!"@@Etthepo yaahikelamo mukalawani, nto mukalawani mwaahisareyamo maasi.
P8	Ekalawa yooyeva, khokhanlemo opuro.
P9	Saara aahihimya wiira: "Mukilipelele vakhaani! Kimpacerya waakuxa athiyana ala."
P10	"Vahoolo waya, kirweeke kaakuxeke alopwana."
P11	Saara aahihimya-tho wiira: "Ekwaha yokiserya kinrwa okuxa inama."
P12	Nto etthepo yaahiira: "Eeyii, miyo kinrowa mweentto."
