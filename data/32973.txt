page_text:

P1	UJabu bekamfundi ohlakaniphileko,bekunguye yedwa umntwana ngakwabo.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga,uJabu wakhamba wayokudlala nabangani bakhe.
P3	Wabona injana ibambekile."Ngizokusiza",kwatjho uJabu.
P4	Inja yakhonkotha,yagidagida yakhotha uJabu ngethabo.
P5	Abangani bakaJabu baqotha inja. "Jamani,jamani!"kurhwelela uJabu.
P6	Inja yabaleka,yabhacela abesana abangakalungi.
P7	Abangani bathola uJabu alila bathi,"Asikhambe siyokufuna inja yakho."
P8	UJabu bekanganathemba.Abangani bakhe bathi,"Sizoyifumana."
P9	Bafuna yoke indawo.UJabu wathi,"Kufanele ibe semlanjeni."
P10	Inja beyisaba abesana.UJabu wabiza,"Ungeqi!"
P11	Inja yagijimela kuJabu.Bathabelana.
P12	Abangani bathi,"Siyaqolisa.Naye uzakuba mnganethu."
