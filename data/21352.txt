desc:	Whatever you want to do say by Gods will.

page_text:

P1	One day Joha was going to the market. He came across a friend.
P2	The friend asked: Where are you going Joha? I'm going to the market.
P3	To buy a donkey. The friend told him to say by God will.
P4	No! I have money and there are donkeys in the market
P5	In the market the money was stolen. Oh! I can no more buy the donkey.
P6	Joha regretted for not saying by God's will.
