title:	Acɩrɩ na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa
author:	Salimatou  Bababody
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Anii
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/24784.png
desc:	Mbʊpá njala nɖe n tɩ lǝ atʊ bʊkɔ́ɩ bʊ paɖa acɩrɩ na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa ma.

page_text:

P1	Pǝ̂rǝ ma ! Arà !
Amʊmpǝrǝ n ce atɩ ri acɩrɩ na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa. Ba ɖa halɩ ajɩ gʊsoro.
P2	Ʊshilé ɖǝn na ba ce atɩ na kǝ bɔl.
P3	Acɩrɩ a lee bɔl karija.
P4	Na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa n jɩ nɖi.
P5	N'acɩrɩ a cam ʊnyana.
P6	Ŋkǝŋ na a mǝn ncaŋgɩlɩpapa.
P7	Acɩrɩ a na shɛʊ ma, n'a wa na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa kagʊna.
P8	Ba wa ma, ncaŋgɩlɩpapa nɖe n tɩ kuro acɩrɩ ancǝna nɩ wàà : « Amaŋana, fʊ̂ra amʊ ! Acɩrɩ a mǝn amʊ ! »
P9	Ncaŋgɩlɩpapa kɩ n san na, naa acɩrɩ a mǝn njɩ ma, n'ɩ lee nɩ ʊkʊrʊ̀.
P10	N'a ce atɩ kʊra njɩ abǝɖa.
P11	Kpaarɩ na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa n cu.
P12	Bʊkɔ́ɩ bʊ pala acɩrɩ na ncaŋgɩlɩpapa kǝ ba kɔɔ na rǝŋǝ na bawor ma na gǝŋ.

Ampǝrǝ kaayala jɩ gǝŋ.
