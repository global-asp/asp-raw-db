desc:	Lukano  lwo’mukhana umufwiti ni bibindu bibimikhoyesa khukhola

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping rope. Nikhoya nga ndikhusuna kumukosi.
P2	… alone. Senyene.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too. Nikhoya ngandikhusuna kumukosi atwela nu musaale wase.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Nikhoya khusoma tsingano tsitsikhoyesa.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Nikhoya khukenda mukyewa ni basaale basye.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother. Nikhoya khusingila Maayi bibindu byekhulilakho.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Nikhoya khurona tsinyukuta tsimboofutsyesi inyalisa khusoma khangu.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit. Nikhoya khulya bibyano byabulisambo.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Nikhoya khukona khubutunde bubalayi.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories. Nikhoya khu rkeresa maayi nga anganikhila tsingano tsitsikhoyesa.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Nikhoya khukhola bibindu ibi byoosi.
