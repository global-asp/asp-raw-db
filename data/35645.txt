desc:	Adding letters to words forms plurals and changes things. Lets find out more about what Letters does to words.

page_text:

P1	Letter 's' builds words.@@Letter 's' makes more.
P2	You can add an 's' to a word to have more.@@ Adding 's'  makes more things.
P3	Adding 's' to a word forms a plural. @@Removing 's' from a word forms a singular.
P4	Even though letter 's' builds words there are some words that change the rules.
P5	If a word ends in ch, sh, s, ss, x or z then use 'es.'
P6	If a word ends in 'y,' swap the 'y' with an 'i' before adding 'es.'
P7	But if there is a vowel before the 'y' add 's.'@@ Letter 's' changes things.
P8	Adding 's' means more than one. @@For everything add 's' unless the rules change.
