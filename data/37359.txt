P3	She went home and cried and stayed in bed the following day and missed school. When she came back to school the next day there was a new girl, she also had acne. When Felicia walked into class, the new girl was sitting at the back all alone
P4	Felicia approached her and they instantly hit it off as friends. When it came time for group projects they worked together and got the best marks. After that all the girls wanted to be friends with Felicia.
