title:	Udukarito dutatu tw'ubukire
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Adonay  Gebru
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Umubyeyi yasigiye abana be umurage ubakwiriye bitewe n impano zabo.

page_text:

P1	Habayeho umusaz umwe akitwa Higiro. Yabana n'abahungu be batatu. 

Higiro yifuzaga ko abana be bazaba abakire nyuma y'urupfu rwe. Yateguye udukarito dutatu.
P2	Higiro yagiye ku muturanyi we Habimana aramubwira ati, "Ndashaka ko abahungu bange bazaba abakire. Nateguye udukarito dutatu. Ndasha ko uzabampera utwo dukarito nimara gupfa."
P3	Higiro amaze gupfa, Habimana ahamagara abahungu ba Higiro arababwira ati, "So yampaye utu dukarito mbere y'uko apfa. Buri wese arebe ahanditse izina rye, agatware."
P4	Abo bahungu bafashe udukarito maze baradufungura. 

Agakarito ka mbere karimo izahabu. Agakarito ka kabiri karimo igitaka. Aka gatatu ko karimo amase y' inyamaswa.
P5	Uwa mbere babonye izahabu yarishiye cyane naho abandi babiri barababara. Batangiye gutongana na we.
P6	Habimana yarababwiye ati, "Ntimutongane, muri abavandimwe. Reka mbajyanire umusaza w'umunyabwenge abagire inama."
P7	Habimana abajyana ku musaza w' inararibonye. 

Habimana arasuhuza ati, "Mwaramutse, tuje hano gusaba inama kubijyanye n'umurage."
P8	Habimana yamubwiye iby'udukarito dutatu Higiro yasigiye abahungu be. 

Habimana amusobanurira ko barimo gutongana kuberako umwe yasanzemo izahabu.
P9	Umusaza w'Umunyabwenge yemeza avuga ati, "So yari afite impamvu zo guha buri wese agakarito."
P10	Yarakomeje ati, "Uwabonye izahabu, so yashakaga ko uzaba umucuruzi. Uwabonye igitaka, so yashakaga ko uzaba umuhinzimworozi."
P11	"Uwasanzemo amase, so yashatse ko uzaba umushumba." Umusaza yasoje ati, "So yashakaga ko buri wese agira umwuga we."
P12	Nyuma yo gutega amatwi inama z'uwo musaza, abahungu batatu bose baremera. Bakoze imirimo yabo kandi babaho bishimye.
