title:	Ehibbwe Ehiyala N’Amapeera Agajujuma
author:	Gershom Hirome
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8399.png
desc:	Ehibbwe ehiyala higwirihirira omupeera oguliho ehisaganda hy’ amapeera amalabire nigalereja hulusaga.

page_text:

P1	Lulala mu hiseera ehyahale ŋaaliŋo ehibbwe ehiyala.  Hyali hirerenga mwisugu ohwendula ebiryo.
P2	Hyagwirihirira esamba yebibala eby'amapeera ameene amalabire. Hyalengera ehisaganda             hy'amapeera agalereja ŋa mugulu hu lusaga.
P3	Ehibbwe hyaŋeega mula hiiti, "Ago gaboneha bulaŋi!" Hyesendaho awagulu gatyane egongo ko hyahaŋanga ohutuuma ŋa mugulu obugali.
P4	Amapeera gaali ŋa mugulu bugali. Ehibbwe hyagwa magalamiriiye ni hitolire hu mapeera.
P5	Wahibbwe genyoha,  nga leero atiinira erala egongo ŋale naŋa gatandihira. Humulundi guno gaali ategeha hutuuma ŋamugulu bugali. Huupi goole ŋamugulu ehihena aye gagwa magalama ohuhira lula.
P6	Gagesyaho nindi olundi nolundi aye nataŋanga hwola hu mapeera. Geyongera ohugwa ohwosa omugongo gwahyo olugwaluma bugali.
P7	Kadi hale hyabiŋwaho era hyagenda hyaŋwaŋo.
P8	Ehibbwe sihyali hisangafu nayire. Hyemerera hyaheja egongo hu mapeera era hyaŋeega hiiti, "Lwosi amapeera ago gaboneha oti gajujuma." Hyeyongera ohugenda ate niriri hiyala nohuhiraho oludaayi.
