title:	Simbi Gets A New Mother
author:	African Storybook and Dorcas Wepukhulu

desc:	After Simbis mother dies

page_text:

P1	Simbi’s mother died. Simbi and her father helped each other.
P2	One day, Simbi’s father came home with a woman called Anita.
P3	Simbi’s father wanted her to accept Anita as her mother.
P4	Simbi was unhappy. 

She was comforted by her mother’s colourful blanket.
P5	Simbi’s father went away to work. 

Anita and Simbi were unhappy.
P6	Sometimes Anita hit Simbi.

She did not give Simbi any food.
P7	Anita pulled Simbi out of bed. 

Her mother’s blanket tore.
P8	Simbi took the blanket and some food. 

She ran away.
P9	She stayed up on a tree and sang to her mother.
P10	One woman listened carefully to Simbi’s song.
P11	The woman knew that Simbi as her brother’s daughter.
P12	Simbi went with her aunt. 

She ate and slept well.
P13	Simbi’s father found Simbi missing when he came home.
P14	Simbi could choose to stay with her aunt whenever she wanted.
P15	Anita and Simbi began to love each other.
P16	Anita cooked enough food. 

Simbi knew she would return home soon.
