desc:	Akuve kumnandi ukuphuza isiphuzo esibandayo esimnandi uma ilanga lishisa. (Indaba nemifanekiso kwaqanjwa engqungqutheleni yokufundisana eyayigqugquzelwe abasebenzi bezengqondo baseSophiatown eGoli ngamaholide kaJuni 2019).

page_text:

P1	Kukhona isitolo esincane eduze kwasekhaya.
P2	Umnikazi waso yindoda ezacile enezwi elincane.
P3	Ngiqonda efilijini.@@Ngithatha isiphuzo esibandayo iKingsley yestrobheli.
P4	Ngiyasikhokhela nginikeza le ndoda ezacile imali.
P5	Ngivula iKingsley yami yestrobheli, ngiphuze.
P6	IKingsley yestrobheli ebandayo imnandi!
