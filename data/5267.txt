desc:	Luno lugero lwa mulenzi ali kwetaaya mu maka agage niba wansolo!

page_text:

P1	Ekimenke kyange ninze tuseka no bunhonhi bwange waigulu mu bire.
P2	Ebinhonhi byange ninze twesuuba Wansude mu muti.
P3	Wansude wange ninze tuzaanha ni Wantwiga wange mu nsozi.
P4	Wantwiga wange ni Nze tuwumba obupiira bwa Wankembo okuva mu itoosi.
P5	Wankembo wange ni Nze tulya amenvu ni Wairike buliidho.
P6	Wairike wange ni Wankembo batya Mwami Wankudu mu ngeri yonayoona.
P7	Mwami Wankudu ni Nze twirayo eka kasoobo ni Mukyala Namusota.
P8	Mukyala Namusota ni Nze twenda okusiisa n'okwemba enhembo ku mwaalo.
P9	Amaka gange gonagoona ninze tuba ghalala mulaala!
