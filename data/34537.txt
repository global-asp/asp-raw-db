page_text:

P1	Bola is a good boy and he has good friends too, they do everything together.@His friends names are, wole, abike, Vera and shola.
P2	Bola and his friends attend the same school so whenever they have assignment to do they always do it together, they read together during exams also.
P3	Sometimes when they are less busy they go to the river to swim, and play some interesting games
P4	During their interhouse sports they all work together as a team with their couch so that they can win.
P5	At last they won and they are all happy, and their couch is very happy and she is proud of them.
