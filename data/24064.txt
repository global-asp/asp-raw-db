page_text:

P1	Khalekho nikhalekho wabayo uumusani umufubuha  ulangibwa Kumbana.
P2	Mumyakha kyewe abili mutaano,akanaa umukhana ulangibwa Zangane.
P3	Babaa mubukhwale musisela tsye kimyakha kitaru,ne tsabaa ni lingooti limalaa khumukhwa taa.
P4	Lunakhu lutwelaa enyolaa ali ni ndaa,khulwekhuba tsibarambisaa itsilindisa taa.
P5	Umwaana umukhaana asalibwa,bamutyukha Kheri.
P6	Kumbana abaa asisoma,Zangane aba nikhalimo mukhaalo khe akhuleeyi,ari baakhalawo khutahumenya atweela taa.
P7	Mumbukaa iindayiho,Zangane afuna umukanwaa ukundi.Umukanwaa wewew umuya etsa amenya atwelaa ninaye numukhaana wewe umufiiti.
P8	Kumbana atsiinibwa humwooyo.Aalila nabii khulwekhumwawula nuumukhaana wewe umufiiti.
P9	Iimbuka yabirawoo,Kumbana naye waafuna uumukanwa uukundi.baamenya atweela,ne sibasala abaana taa.
P10	Kheri tsaakana nabii paapa wewe umuya taa.saba umusangafu taa,atee abee amupa.
P11	itsi abaa nikimyakha likhumi na kibili,maawe akhalawo khumusindiha eeleyo atsye amenye nii rarawe.
P12	Arii Kumbana nii Keri beela mubutwela ni busangafu lundii.
