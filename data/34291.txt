desc:	Ariana Wangari was born in America. Her mother tells her many stories about Kenya. Now Ariana wants to visit Kenya.

page_text:

P1	Ariana is in America.@@She wants to visit Kenya.
P2	On the road, she will see sheep.@@She will also see boda boda bikes and tuktuks.
P3	She will see her Kenyan relatives.
P4	Her relatives will prepare a big celebration.@@Ariana will celebrate and be happy with them.
P5	Ariana will play with other children.
P6	If she goes to the market, she will see many different things.@@She will also see people dressed differently.
P7	She will eat many fruit.@@She will eat oranges, bananas and pineapples.
P8	Ariana will try to climb tall trees.@@She has never done that in America.
P9	She will see farms with different crops.@@Many Kenyans are farmers.
P10	She will visit parks and see wild animals.@@She will see a lion, an elephant, buffaloes, a giraffe, warthogs, antelopes and zebras.
P11	She will see domestic animals like chicken, cat, goat and dove.@@In her home in America, she has two cats.
P12	She will witness a village wedding.@@She will see people singing and dancing in a traditional way.
P13	She will visit the Kenyan coast.@@There, she will swim in the Indian Ocean.
P14	On Sundays, Ariana will join her relatives to go to church.
P15	Ariana will be so happy with her visit to Kenya.
