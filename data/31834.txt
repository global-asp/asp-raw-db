title:	Omotugi n'enasari yaye
author:	Gladys Nyachieo
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Ekegusii

desc:	Omogano igora ya ogokonya ebirerwa biechingiti chikoretwa ase enasari ye chitangana. Ebirerwa ebio nigo bikorerwa nomonto obene na abakonyi bande.

page_text:

P1	Oyo nomogano bwomotugi nenasari yaye ye ching'iti chitakana.
P2	Omotienyi bweekoromoni nigo orenge bwegasi enyinge ase omotugi na abakonyi baye ase enasari.
P3	Mkite bwoomwaka oyomo nere otangete goika. Nigo aigete korwa egari engotu ya machani.
P4	Omotugi na abakonyi baye nigo barocherete Mkite amabeera. Nigo are omoreu nainde abeete.
P5	Mkite nigo anyorete chinguru ase obwango. Agachaka kogosoria n'eching'iti chinde ase enasari.
P6	Rituko rinde ria ekoromoni, erikobuta ekagwa ase enasari.
P7	Omotugi na abakonyi baye bakaminyoka isiko. Erikobuta ime nkiarenge ekererwa ki'enchogu kebisirie oborangeti.
P8	Ndiwa orenge. Nigo arenge bwaamatuko atano. Omotugi akarageria ndiwa nechuba.
P9	Entakana ya gatato omotienyi oyio Malea orenge. Nigo aigete enasari korwa erori embariri ime. Nigo abwate emetienyi etano n'emo.
P10	Ekiobieria Malea akaba obosani na Enkare, eng'era ekerwa. Bakagenda kerase bare amo.
P11	Obotuko obomo kera oyomo agaturwa nogoika gwa Ambia.
P12	Ambia nigo abwate nemetienyi etano. Nigo arenge omorosu ase eng'encho yesabari etambe.
P13	Ase eng'encho yebigoti biaye ebitambe, omotugi  akaimokereria echuba igoro korageria Ambia.
P14	Chisare chindeu Abei na Moit chigaika gatigati ya omotienyi bwekoromoni. Chingabi echio nigo konyora chiaiboirwe chinsa chinke igo konyora chietire. Nigo chiarenge chindwaire mono.
P15	Kera omonto nigo abeete ekero Abei akuete ewiki eyemo nyuma yogoika ase enasari. Korende  omotugi na abakonyi baye bagakonya Moit.
P16	Chitankana chiomoerio goika ase enasari omotienyi bweokoromoni nigo chiarenge Kebi, Kepi na Keji.  Chimanwa echio chiarenge chiaemekubio ebere korwa okoiborwa. Nigo chire nchara mono ekero chiaigete.
P17	Omotugi na abakonyi baye nigo baachetu ching'ti, nonya  nchiri chiburaburu.
P18	Ekererwa ekeburaburu kobua bionsi nigo kiare korokwa Lobolia. Lobolia nigo anchete  kobisa ebirore bia omotugi.
P19	Abakonyi benasari nigo babwate omokia ase okorenda ebirerwa. Nigo basemeretie buna rituko erimo chitankana nchinyare kwerenda chinyene.  Chinyare koirana gochia orosana.
P20	Ekegori na irenga? Soma omogano oiraneria amaborio 1. Ching'iti irenga chiaigete ase enasari ase omotienyi bweokoromoni? 2. Ning'o otang'anete goika omotienyi oyio? 3. Nkegori ki eng'iti eyio yarenge? 4. Ning'o orenge bwoomoerio goika? Nkegori ki ching'iti chiare? 5. Ning'o orenge omoke  goika ase omotienyi oyio? Nkegori ki? Ning'o orengo omonene?
