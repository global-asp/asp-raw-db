title:	Mbasi Yaka Yamubala Wa Mbilu
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa
artist:	Mango Tree
lang:	SiLozi
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ebei nibomae balibelela mbasi yamubala wambilu kubayisa kwatolopo. Kono mbasi yambilu ishwile. Bakaya cwani kwatolopo?

page_text:

P1	Nekuna ni mbasi iling'wi feela mwahae. Neili yetuna yamubala wa mbilu. Neili yalilata ahulu.
P2	Lizazi leling'wi bomaye Ebei bali,  "Kamuso lakuya kwa tolopo luyoleka yunifomu yahao."
P3	Ebei ataba ahulu. Nabaswanela kusebelisa mbasi yamubala wa mbilu.    Apalelelwa kufumana buloko busihu bo.
P4	Ebei natinile kale libyana abatomuzusa bomaye.
P5	Ebei ni bomaye bazaya amoho kuyokela mbasi yetuna. Bailitela mbasi yetuna kono kakutokwa litonolo neisi kapunya mbasi.
P6	Babang'wi batu bafita falikwelelo la mbasi. Bataha inge baomana kakuli mbasi neisikafita. "Kono kuli mbasi ilusiile?" Babuza.
P7	Ebei nafilikana mwamihupulo. "Aluna kuyokwanisa kuya kwatolopo alimwi. Anisana kubona yunifomu yaka hape,"  Ebei anahana.
P8	Babang'wi batu bakatala kulibelela,  bakutela kwa mandu abona. Kono Ebei alila mane nihana kukutela kwa hae. Bomaye bamushembashemba nikuli,  "Lukalibelela feela ainyani."
P9	Mwanako yeinyani bautwa lilata. Babona liluli mwahalimu. Yafita mbasi!
P10	Kono mbasi ye nesi mubala wa mbilu. Mbasi yeitile neili yafubelu hape neili yenyinyani. Batu banabalibelela baitalima mbasi. Akuna yanaikwezi mbasi ye.
P11	"Amukene! Amukene! "Amukene!" Apunda muzamaisi wamota."
P12	Ebei ni bamaye kicona mwane bakwelezi kuba bamakalelo mi bomaye baba bamakalelo.    Kakusasinya nako batu kaufeela bakwela mwa mbasi yenyinyani yefubelu.
P13	Ebei anangela fande yaw indo abona batu babang'ata fasiteshini.
P14	Batu babang'ata nebamatela kuli bakwele mwa mbasi kono nebaliyeile. Mbasi neiteze kale. Onafo mbasi yefubelu yakala kuya kwatolopo.
P15	"Cwale mbasi yetuna ya mbilu ikai?" babuza bomaye Ebei. "Ishwile," abulela muzamaisi wa mbasi basi. "Lusweli mutiyo wakwilukisa. Ikataha kamuso," abulela hape muzamaisi wa mbasi.
P16	Ebei nasina taba nimubala wa mbasi. Nasina taba hape nibutuna bwa mbasi. Natabile ahulu kakuli mbasi ye neiliba kwa tolopo.
