desc:	Abu edia king’olik ipudat ka napoleon ebukorosi

page_text:

P1	Abu ayong eng'olik aloor ekokori kwap emoru.
P2	Topeu ipudat edoki emoru.
P3	Eruoro ekamian aloori kang'ina anasim keng.
P4	Pe amisikinit ngesi erukudi ang'amotokae.
P5	Apotu ng'amotokae ng'una tanang'as retete.
P6	Abu awuiwui na esibitar to na arek todolut aking'arakin lu awanan.
P7	Todolut ng'amotokae ng'aarei kiriasi aloori ka ipudat ng'ini.
P8	Abu ayong atoriang' akin lokojokon.
