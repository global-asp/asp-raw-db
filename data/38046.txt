desc:	A young boy who was raised by the old grand mother who once gave her 50c coin to buy with it at the shop. Only to find out that the shopkeeper was not accepting coins in his shop but he boy did not give up he kept on collecting as many coin

page_text:

P1	Leano e be e le moshimane yo monnyane yo a bego a godishwa ke koko wa gagwe. Ka letjatji le lengwe o Ile a kgopela tjhelete go koko wa gagwe ya go reka malekere ka lebenkeleng la Morena Tjhali
P2	Tseleng ya gagwe a kopana le bagwera ba gagwe ba babedi e le go Thabo le Tebatjo. Bona ba be ba swere tjhelete ya pampiri ba kgona go reka seo ba bego ba se nyaka ka lebenkeleng. Leano ga a kgona go reka gobane o be a swere masheleng a mahl
P3	Ka madimabe Morena Tjhali a mmotja gore yena ga a tjee tjhelete ye tsoto lebenkeleng la gagwe. Leano o Ile a kwa bohloko  a fihla ka gae a lla kudu. Bagwera ba gagwe ba Ile ba mo arolela seo ba bego ba se rekile.
P4	Ge a fihla ka gae o Ile a hlaloshetja koko wa gagwe, gomme yena a mo gokara le go mo phaphata a mmotja gore a se ke a hwa matwa mo bophelong gomme a kgoboketje tjhelete ye tsoto ka gare ga lebotlelo la dilitara tje pedi.
P5	Aowa seo se Ile sa thabisha Leano le bagwera ba gagwe ba thoma go kgoboketja masheleng ao go fihlela ba tlatja lebotlelo. Ge lebotlelo le tletje koko o ile a ba laela go isha tjhelete yeo bokgobatjhelete toropong.
P6	Bona ba ile ba dira ka mokgwa wo, ba ba tjhentjhetja tjhelete yeo go pampiri le tjhipi a kgona go reka lebenkeleng la Morena Tjhali bjalo ka bagwera ba gagwe
P7	Yena o Ile a leboga koko le bagwera ba gagwe ka go mo hlohleletja go kgoboketja tjhelete ye tsoto. O Ile a ya lebenkeleng la Morena Tjhali go mo leboga gore ge nkabe e se ka yena nkabe a sa kgona go dira seo a se dirilego.
P8	Leano a ipotja gore o tla tjwela pele ka go kgoboketja sekolong le mo nageng ya gabo
