title:	Ngonza - I Enjoy

desc:	Orubazo rwomwisiki nebyagonza kukora.

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping rope. Ngonza kuguruka omuguha.
P2	Alone. Nyenka.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too. Ngonza kuguguruka namunywani wange.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Ngonza kusoma embazo ezirukusemeza.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Ngonza kurubata nabanywani bange.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother. Ngonza kwogezaaho mau ebintu by'okuliraho.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Ngonza kuhandiika enyuguta enkooto ezikunyanguhira kusoma.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit. Ngonza kulya ebijuma ebitalibimu.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Ngonza kubyama hakitabu ekikooto.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories. Ngonza kuhuliriza mau natebya embazo ezirikusemeza.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Oo! Ngonza kukora ebi byona.
