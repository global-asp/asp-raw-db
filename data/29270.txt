P1	Lwali lulala,ghaligho omusaadha ayetebwa Hector, Hector yali no mukazi ayetebwa,Malayika,malayika yali mumbendagha bungereza era Hector yali ava munsi ya Belgium, Hector yaidha mu Bungereza kunonhia omusaadha omusaadha ayinza okumuyamba okufuga obya kyabazinga, yamala yabona omukazi,Hector ya mwegomba,bakomekereza nga bafumbiraganhwa,omukazi yazaala omwana omulenzi,bamutuuma eriina lya Daudi.Lwali lulala  kyabazinga yayeta Daudi, Kyabazinga yakoba Daudi agye aleete ebibala.
