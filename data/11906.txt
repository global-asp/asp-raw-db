title:	Eŋombe Ey’Olwiga Lulala
author:	Gershom Hirome
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/11906.png
desc:	Wali oweneho eŋombe eyo lwiga lulala ate neŋumaho muhira.

page_text:

P1	Wange ew-e-e-e! Eriyo eŋombe enyene endaŋi engo eyo.
P2	Eŋumaaho muhira era ya lwiga lulala lwoŋene. Eboneha bulaŋi bugali.
P3	Ngeji,  era eri n'ehida hibba.
P4	Nj' eŋombe ehirira erala amaani.
P5	Omwayi w'eŋombe yino ayenda bugali. Geegesa eŋombe yino huŋulirisa ejanjaasi lirye iyonyene.
P6	Ehidooli ni hitiina mwisugu ohwaya,  eŋombe yino esiiba ŋayonyene.
P7	Eŋombe yino sooli yambaha,  kale eŋombe ejindi jiyireha yonyene.
P8	Abaatu,  eŋombe yino abaali bayimanyire. Bayitya,  lwohuba yiŋanga ohusoola netambisa olwiga olulala olwo. Singa oba wageene eŋombe hy'eyo,  ohola hiina?
