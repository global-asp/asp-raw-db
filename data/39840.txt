P2	She wakes up from the bed, whispering '' l wish my dad was alive".She went to the kitchen to make breakfast '' Ouch'' my finger is cut.‚Äù Then the lady walks in the kitchen she saying'' you always do things wrongly" as she slaps her.
P3	She starts removing the clothes outside while tears dropping down from her eyes. "I have to run away from this house.' After the lady goes out of the house, I will carry all my belongings."
P4	The evil lady went out of the house to work. This is my only chance.Mariumu went into her room and packed all her belongings and left immediately. She went to nearest police station to report the evil lady.
P5	After reporting the lady,  the police went and arrested the evil lady. Mariamu was now free and was taken to an orphanage where she gots new friends,safe without fear of being beaten or threaten anymore.
