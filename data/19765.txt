desc:	The reason why spiders have long thin legs. (Also available at Level 4.)

page_text:

P1	He loves food,  but he is too lazy to cook.
P2	"Yes. Help me with stirring," says Rabbit. But Anansi is lazy.
P3	Anansi ties some web around his leg and to Rabbit's pot.
P4	"Monkeys,  can I share your beans?" "Yes. Help us with chopping."
P5	"Warthog,  can I share your potatoes?" "Yes. Help me with preparing."
P6	Soon,  each of Anansi's 8 legs is tied to a pot.
P7	All 8 legs are being pulled! "Stop pulling!" cries Anansi.
P8	Anansi's legs stretched! And so all spiders have long thin legs.
