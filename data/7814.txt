id:	7814
title:	Tshoswane O Thusa Leeba
author:	Lorato Trok
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Setswana
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7814.png
desc:	Tshoswane o ne a nyorilwe thata mme a ya kwa nokeng go ya go batla metsi. O ne a tsewa ke metsi. Leeba o ne a thusa go pholosa botshelo jwa gagwe. Tshoswane ena o tla mo thusa ka eng?

page_text:

P1	E ne e le letsatsi le le mogote mo sekgweng se se omeletseng.
P2	Tshoswane o ne a nyorilwe thata. O ne a na le malatsi a sa nwe metsi.
P3	"Ke tlhoka thoti fela ya metsi, le fa e ka wa mo letlhareng."
P4	Le lona letlhatsana tota le ne le omeletse.
P5	"Fa ke sa nwe metsi, ke tlile go swa," Tshoswane a lela. "Ke tshwanetse go ya nokeng e ke e utlwaletseng."
P6	"Noka e tla tsamaya ka wena," Mosha yo o botlhale a mo thiba.
P7	Fela Tshoswane o ne a nyorilwe thata. "Ke tlile go swa fa ke sa nwe metsi."
P8	Tshoswane o ne a ya go batla noka.
P9	Go kgabaganya majang a a omileng le dikala tse di omeletsetseng.
P10	Go fitlhela a utlwa mosumo wa makhubu a metsi.
P11	Tshoswane o ne a nwa metsi a a monate a a tsiditsana.
P12	O ne a itumetse thata mme a se bone lekhubu le legolo la metsi le atamela.
P13	Tshoswane o ne a leka go itshwarelela ka bojang. Go le jalo metsi a feta ka ena.
P14	"Thusang! Thusang!"
P15	"Ka bonako, palama," ga bua Leeba le lesweu, a tshwere kala mo molomomg wa gagwe.
P16	"Ga ke na go tsamaya ke ise ke leboge Leeba. Ke tla leta go fitlhela a tla go nwa metsi."
P17	Ka letsatsi lengwe fa a letile, basimane ba babedi ba ne ba tla kwa nokeng ba tshwere diragamaje.
P18	"Go na le leeba le le tlang fano ka metlha go tla go nwa metsi," ga bua mongwe wa basimane. "Re tla le dira nama ya rona ya go lalela gompieno."
P19	"Ga ke na go letla basimane ba go bolaya Leeba le lesweu. Fela ke monnye, nka dira eng?"
P20	Ka yone nako eo Leeba le lesweu la fologa setlhare go ya go nwa metsi.
P21	Tshoswane o ne a tlelwa ke kakanyo.
P22	O ne a tlolela mo godimo ga leoto la mongwe wa basimane mme a mo loma botlhoko.
P23	Mosimane o ne a tlola. "Ijoo!" a lela.
P24	Leeba le lesweu le ne la tshoga mme la fofela kwa godimo.
P25	Se e ne e le thuso ya ga Tshoswane go leboga Leeba le lesweu ka a mo pholositse mo metsing.
