title:	Bunyaalifu
author:	Cornelius Wekunya
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lumasaaba
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/916.png
desc:	Umusooleli abona imotokha ifwiiti nga ikunyana ni loole mu bunyaalifu

page_text:

P1	Naboone iloole imboofu itima khu lukuuto mwiikha.
P2	Imotokha benzi yaniinile khu lukiingi musiila.
P3	Umufuki we loole abeele ali khuukanikhila khu siimu.
P4	Abeele siwaloleelele khu lukuuto ta.
P5	Yakwawo bunyaalifu lubaluba biitima tsana bya kuunyana.
P6	Ambyulensi ni ba polisi beetsa khuuyeeta.
P7	Tsimotokha tsitsikhweesa tseetsa tsaakhweesa iloole ni motokha ifiiti.
P8	Neelulukha!
