page_text:

P1	My name is Iember.@@I am the boss of my body! @@Covered or seen, my body parts are all mine.
P2	If someone touches me and it's not okay, I shout:@@"NO! DON'T TOUCH ME!" @@I run away and tell a grown-up I can trust.
P3	Bad touching makes me feel scared, embarrassed, and uncomfortable.  @@I tell on anyone who makes me feel that way.
P4	My body belongs to me. @@Every part of my body is mine.@@Good touching makes me feel safe, happy, and comfortable.
P5	No one can touch my body unless I say it is okay.@@No one can touch my private parts.
P6	No one can take photos of my private parts.@@And I do not want to see photos of anyone else's private parts!
P7	I tell my family about anyone who asks me to keep a secret.@@I tell someone I trust about anyone who makes me feel uncomfortable.
P8	My name is Iember. @@I am the boss of my body and you are the boss of your body!
