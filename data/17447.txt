title:	Marqaa
author:	Mulualem Daba
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Afaan Oromo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/17447.png
desc:	Abbaa Tolaa Tolaaf Marqaa qopheesa haati Tolaa waan hin jirref.

page_text:

P1	Tolaan mana barumsaa deemmuf qophahaa jira.
P2	Abbaan tolaa ciree marqaa tolaaf qopheessa jira.
P3	Tolaan haati koo marqaa mi'aawa qophessitti jeedhe.
P4	Marqaan Tolaan humna ni kenna.
P5	Marqaan qopha'eera.
P6	Tolaaf abbaan isaa anaan buuseef.
P7	Tolaan abbaa isaatin,  "Marqaan kun ashaboo hanqatera" jeedhen.
P8	Tolaan marqaan Haati isaa qopheesitu mi'aadha jeedhe yaada.
P9	Tolaan abbaa isaa ashaboo gafate.
P10	Abbaan Tolaan marqaati ashaaboo dabaleefi.
P11	"Uhhh! Abbaa marqaa kootii ashaboo osoo hin taane sukkara naaf dabalte!" Jedhee Tolaan.
P12	Tolaan abbaa isaa haatii isaa yoom akka dhuuftu gafate.
