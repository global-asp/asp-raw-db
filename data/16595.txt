desc:	Mokoko o tshameka motlae o o maswe mo go Phakalane.

page_text:

P1	Bogologotala Koko le Phakalane e ne e le ditsala. Ka letsatsi le lengwe Phakalane o ile a fitlhela Koko a ikhutsitse mo tlase ga setlhare a tswa mabenkeleng. Koko o ne a eme ka leoto le le lengwe.
P2	"Leoto la gago le lengwe le kae?"  Phakalane a botsa Mokoko.
P3	"Ralebenkele o kgaotse leoto la ka mme a mpha dikgetsi tse di tletseng dijo tse mo boemong," Mokoko a bolelela Phakalane.
P4	Phakalane a botsa Mokoko gore a le ene a ka dira jalo. Le ene o ne a batla kgetse ya dijo. Mokoko a dumela.
P5	Phakalane o ile a ya go ralebenkele mme a kopa gore a mo kgaole leoto mme a mo fe kgetse ya dijo mo boemong. Ralebenkele o ile a dumela.
P6	Phakalane o ile a tlola-tlola ka leoto le le lengwe go ya gae. Mme fela o ne a itumeletse dilo tse ralebenkele o neng a mo file tsone.
P7	Fa Phakalane a fitlha kwa gae,  o ile a fithela Mokoko a nale maoto a mabedi.
P8	Phakalane o ne a tenegile mme a lelekisa Mokoko. Mme fela a se mo tshware. Ka jalo,  Phakalane o dula a batlana le bana ba dikoko. Fa a tshwara mongwe wa bone,  o a ba ja.
