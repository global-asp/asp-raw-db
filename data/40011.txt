Thanks for your reading and your support

page_text:

P1	Munna hoyu hahawe vhana vha we na musadzi wawe avhana zwiliwa.Lenwe duvha munna ahumbula ngau zwima dakani.
P2	Atshi khou thismbila a vhona thwurwa
P3	Thwurwa heyo yovha yo bofhiwa zwino munna a humbula ui vhulaya ya vha tshisevho
P4	atshikhouri ukhoi vhula ya akhakha ambo di  posa pfumo fhethu husihone.
P5	Ya takuwa nga tshipidi ya muraha a wela mipfani
P6	Ambodiya hayani a songo fara tshithu.
