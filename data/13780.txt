desc:	Inyoka ifunda ukududa bese pheze yaqwila.

page_text:

P1	Ekadeni bekunabangani ababili,  iKghuru kanye neNyoka.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga ikghuru yathi ku Nyoka,  "Asikhambe siyokududa."
P3	Bakhamba baya edamini lokududa.
P4	IKghuru yangena ngedamini lokududa. Inyoka yaqala amagadango weKhguru.
P5	Ngemva kwesikhathi eside,  iNyoka yangena ngedamini iKghuru imlayela.
P6	IKghuru neNyoka badudela edamini ekugcineni lapha kungatihingiko.
P7	Bathoma bathaya ngemanzimi.
P8	Bathi basathaya njalo,  IKghuru yathoma yaduda ngomhlana. Kwathi iNyoka isalingisela iKghuru ngendlela iduda ngayo,  INyoka khenge ikghone ukujika kuhle yathoma yaqwila.
P9	Kwathi ukulemuka bonyana iNyoka inyamalele,  iKghuru ngokurhaba yangena ngaphasi kwamanzi yasindisa ipilo yeNyoka.
P10	Ngemva ngokuhti iKghuru iphuma ngemanzini,  yaqalisa iNyoka phasi yenzelela ukuth inyoka ikhiphe amanzi ewagwinyileko.
P11	Emaphethelweni,  iKghuru yasebenzisa indlela yomlomo kumlomo ukukhipha amanzi amanengi.
