title:	Olufumo Meere ayogera ku bulamu bw'omughala
author:	Abdul Maged Mbago

desc:	Meere ayogera ku mugala no bulamu bwe obwabuliidho.

page_text:

P1	Ninze Meereomwana omughala yenka mu maka nhenda inho okufuluma ku luya n'abantube.
P2	Nja kwisomero Kiruma erandi mu kya kuna.Nnhenda inho okuzaanha ni banange.
P3	Bwenva kwisomero nhamba maama n'emirimo ja ghaka era Toomu ni mwanhinaze.
P4	Ku nkomerero kwa saasira nzaanha ni mwanhinaze Toomu.
P5	Nnhenda inho okuja mu katale ni Maama era muyambaku okulonda ebyendebwa okugulwa.
P6	Tuli n'enkoko  ghaka era nhamba Maama okudhaaya.
P7	Nnhumirwa inho okukuba ebufaananie eraMaama angulira ebimyula n'ebitabo ebikubwamu ebifaananie.
P8	Ebiseera byange ebyeidembe nambuula faamu yaife ni Maama.
P9	Mu kiwuumulo mpuumulirw budhaada bwange era mu kwirayo bansibirira obulabo.
P10	Oluvainhuma olwokulambuula ba dhaadha ghetukungaanira nga tumaze okulya tufuma enfumo era twaghayaku nga ebintu bwebitambwire.
P11	OLuvainhuma nga nviire eudhaadha mbona nga tiri ghalala mu bidhuubo. Aye olwokuba ebiseera ebyokwinama okusoma biba bituuse, mba musanhufu inho kuba mba nja kwagaanana ni banange kwisomero. Mu bulamu bwange abazire bange ni mwanhinaze bansomesa empisa enkalamu.
