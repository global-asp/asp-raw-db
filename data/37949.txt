title:	Chibasi chabuluu

desc:	Limbani ndi mayi ake akudikira basi yayikulu ya mtundu wabuluu kuti iwatenge kupita ku tawuni.

page_text:

P1	M'mudzi wa Limbani mumadutsa basi imodzi yokha. Inali yayikulu ya mtundu wa buluu. @@Inali ya phokoso kwambiri.
P2	"Mawa tipita ku tawuni," anatero amayi a Limbani. @@"Tikagula yunifolomu yako."
P3	Limbani anasangalala kwambiri. Adzakwera basi yayikulu yabuluu. @@Usiku umenewo Limbani sanagone.
P4	Amayi ake atabwera kudzamudzutsa, Limbani anali atavala kale.
P5	Limbani ndi amayi ake anayenda kupita kokwerera basi. Anayidikira basi yayikulu yabuluu ija. @@Koma basi siinafike.
P6	Anthu ena anafika pokwerera basi. Anadandaula chifukwa basi inachedwa. @@"Basi ifika nthawi yanji?" anafunsa motero.
P7	Limbani anadandaula. "Sitipita ku tawuni," anaganiza choncho. @@"Sindikhala ndi yunifolomu yatsopano."
P8	Anthu ena anatopa kudikira ndikubwerera kunyumba kwawo. @@Limbani analira. Samafuna kubwerera kunyumba. "Tidikirabe," anatero mayi ake.
P9	Mwadzidzidzi, anamva phokoso. Anawona fumbi m'mwamba. @@Basi ija imabwera!
P10	Koma siinali basi yabuluu. Siinalinso basi yayikulu. Inali basi yofiira komanso yaying'ono.@@Anthu sanafune kukwera basi imeneyi.
P11	"Kwerani! Kwerani! Tachedwa kwambiri lero," anakuwa woyendetsa basi.
P12	Limbani ndi mayi ake anali oyamba kukwera. @@Kenako ena onse anakwera basi yofiira yaying'ono ija.
P13	Limbani anawona kunja kudzera pa zenera. @@Anawona anthu ambiri pokwerera basi paja.
P14	Anthu ena ambirinso anathamangira basi ija. Koma anachedwa kwambiri. @@Basi yofiira ija inali itadzadza. Inanyamuka kupita ku tawuni.
P15	"Basi yabuluu yaikulu ija ili kuti?" anafunsa amayi a Limbani. @@"Yawonongeka. Tikuikonza. Ibwera mawa," anayankha woyendetsa basi uja.
P16	Limbani sanasamale za mtundu wa basi. Sanasamale za kukula kwa basi. @@Basiyi imapita ku tawuni!
