id:	17359
title:	Utsheketshe Usindisa Ujuba
author:	Zanele Zuma and Pumla mdontswa
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/17359.png
desc:	Kwakomise kakhulu ezweni uTsheketshe omncane wayenxanwe kakhulu. Wehlela emfuleni ukuyophuza kodwa amanzi amshanelela le kude. Wasizwa uJuba. Kodwa naye wakuqinisekisa ukuthi uyabonga.

page_text:

P1	Kusehlobo,  ilanga liphumile. Kusehlathini elomile.
P2	Sekudlule izinsuku eziningi uTsheketshe engenamanzi.
P3	Ngidinga iconsi lamanzi,  noma ngabe liphuma eqabungeni nje.
P4	Kodwa akusekho namazolo lawa.
P5	"Uma ngingatholi amanzi manje,  ngizofa. Kuzomele ngiye kulo mfula engake ngezwa ngawo," kusho uTsheketshe.
P6	"Hhayi bo! Uzomuka nomfula,  umfula ugcwele," kuxwayisa uNgwejeje ohlakaniphile.
P7	Kodwa uTsheketshe wayome kakhulu. "Ngizofa uma ngingatholi manzi," kusho yena.
P8	UTsheketsheke wahamba wayofuna umfula.
P9	Wahamba enqamula otshanini obomile nasemagatsheni omile.
P10	Wezwa ukuhaza kwamanzi.
P11	UTsheketshe wathi,  "Ngibonwa yini,  amanzi! Waphuza ephindelela."
P12	Uthe engazelele,  wangenisa ngamandla umfula.  Shwi! Zaya kuTsheketshe.
P13	Wazama ukubambelela otshanini obomile eduze kwakhe. Amanzi amphonsa le kude.
P14	"Ngisizeni bo! Ngisizeni bo!"
P15	"Shesha,  gibela lapha," kusho uJuba omhlophe ebambe ukhuni ngomlomo.
P16	"Ngizohlala lapha ukuze ngikwazi ukumbonga uJuba," kusho uTsheketshe.
P17	Ngesikhathi esalindile uTsheketshe,  kwaqhamuka abafana ababili bephethe izihlilingi.
P18	"Kunejuba elikhulu elimhlophe eliphuza kulo mfula. Namhlanje,  lizoba ukudla kwethu," kusho omunye wabafana.
P19	"Angeke ngibavumele laba bafana babulale uJuba. Yize ngimncane,  konje yini nje engingayenza?"
P20	Ngawo lowo mzuzu uJuba wandiza wehlela phansi ukuzophuza.
P21	UTsheketshe wayesenalo isu.
P22	Wagxumela onyaweni lomfana wamncinza kakhulu.
P23	"Eshuuu!" kukhala umfana egxuma.
P24	UJuba wethuka wandizela kude. Waphepha kanjalo-ke uJuba.
P25	Wahleka yedwa uTsheketsheke. Wazibongela kanjalo kuJuba.
