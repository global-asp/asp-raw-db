desc:	Le ndoda inde ngokwenene. Iyisombulula kanjani le ngxaki yokuba mde kangaka?

page_text:

P1	Igaba lakhe lalilifutshane ngokwenene.
P2	Umnyango wakhe wawusezantsi ngokwenene.
P3	Ibhedi yakhe yayimfutshane ngokwenene.
P4	Ibhayisekile yakhe yayimfutshane ngokwenene.
P5	Lendoda yayinde ngokwenene!
P6	Yenza intonga yegaba ende kakhulu.
P7	Yenza imigubasi emide kakhulu.
P8	Yenza ibhedi ende kakhulu.
P9	Yathenga ibhayisekile ephakame kakhulu.
P10	Yahlala esitulweni esiphakame kakhulu. Yatya ngefolokhwe ende kakhulu.
P11	Yayishiya indlu yayo yayohlala kwihlathi elikhulu. Yaphila iminyaka emininzi.
