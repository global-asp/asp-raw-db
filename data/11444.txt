title:	Dembe Omusuubuzi

desc:	Olugero luno lukwata ku musuubuzi omututumufu akolera awamu n`abenyumba ye mu kabuga ke Gayaza.

page_text:

P1	Ku kyaalo kyewaffe waliyo omusajja omusuubuzi erinnya lye Dembe.
P2	Dembe alina edduuka ddene nyo mu kabuga ke Gayaza.
P3	Mu duuka lya Dembe mulimu obuwunga,  sukali,  ebizigo,  omuceere,  ebijanjaalo,  nebirala bingi.
P4	Dembe alina mukazi we nabaana basatu. Bona bakola nyo namanyi mu duuka.
P5	Mukiseera ekyoluwummula,  abaana bayambako bazadde baabwe okukola mu dduuka nga baweereza abaguzi.
P6	Dembe alina ebitabo mwawandiika ebya sente buli lunaku.
P7	Dembe nemukyaala we balina enyumba ennungi ne mmotoka.
P8	Balunda embuzi,  ente,  enkoko nobumyu. Obulamu bwaabwe bulungi.
P9	Dembe ne mukazi we bafuna sente ezimala okulabirira abaana baabwe nokubatwaala mu masomero amalungi.
