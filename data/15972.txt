desc:	Neo loses her pig in an accident. What happens after that?

page_text:

P1	Neo takes her pig for a ride on her bicycle.
P2	She hits a stone in the road. The pig falls down. It cries. It says,  "Hooeeeee,  hooeeeee!"
P3	Tsotang finds it. He says,  "Haaiee,  who does this pig belong to?"
P4	Tsotang chases it,  until he catches it.
P5	He takes the pig to the chief. The chief tells him to look after it.
P6	Neo passes by on the path. She hears the pig grunting,  "Hmmm,  hmmm,  hmmm!"
P7	She goes in. She is happy to see her pig.
P8	Neo says to her pig,  "Come,  let's go home. We will not ride the bicycle again."
