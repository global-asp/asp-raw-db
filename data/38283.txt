title:	Baɓatti
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Baɓatti nasti gese gure feere-feere

page_text:

P1	Nyalnde feere, min ngi'i ruldere ɓaleere e wanngoya daga daayiiɗum. @@Ɗum ruldere baɓatti, e ɗi paɗoyi gure amin.
P2	Baɓatti nasti gese, ɗi nyaami aaweeji fuu.
P3	Min kaɓdi riiwugoɗi, ammaa ɗi cali dillugo.
P4	Min ngaɗi hooloore, min ngulli, "Wuuu! Wuuu!" @@Ammaa baɓatti ndillaayi.
P5	Min piyi goŋgoŋji bee payanɗe jamɗe, ammaa baɓatti ndillaayi.
P6	Min kuɓɓi hiite ngam riiwugo baɓatti, ammaa ɗi ndillaayi.
P7	Ɗi koɗi dow leɗɗe. Lise ngewi ngam tedduɗum baɓatti.
P8	Sey min numti, asee, baɓatti juɗaaɗi e mbeli nyaamugo! @@Koo moye fuɗɗi mooɓugo baɓatti nder buhuuje bee raagaaji.
P9	Ɓiɓɓe ngaddi paali kebbiniɗi bee baɓatti.
P10	Min njuɗi baɓatti, min ca'iɗi, haa min njorni goɗɗi, kewtayɗi ndunngu, ngam sigaago. @@Ammaa baɓatti timmaayi, e ɗi lutti.
P11	Sey fini fajiri, baɓatti piiri, ndilli. @@Ruldere nden ali wuro amin.
P12	Nde remooɓe ngi'i gese maɓɓe, ɓe mboyi semmbee, "Gese amin mbonnidaama!" @@Himɓe wuro mboyi, "Ɗume nyaameten hikka? Ɗume baɓatti alani'en?"
