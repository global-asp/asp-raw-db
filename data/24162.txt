title:	Abaana abato banyumirwa kuzana emizano
author:	Byakutaga Samantha and Uganda Christian University

desc:	Abaana abato banyumirwa kuzaana emizaano eyemilingo nyingi kusigikira hamyaka yaabu. Soma orugero runu orolemu emizaano eyokukirayo kugonza muno oyoleke abanywani baawe.

page_text:

P1	Aboojo bato bagonza kuzana omupiira.
P2	Nyowe nkira muno kugonza kuguruka omuguha.
P3	Anna na Samu bagonza muno kuzaana nenkoko kandi nibaziliisa no kuziliisa.
P4	Haroho aboojo abanyumirwa kuzaana nkweserekiire.
P5	Salimu agonza kwiruka.
P6	Abaisiki bagonza kuziha.
P7	Elisa agonza kuguruka nkeekikere.
P8	Abaisiki bamu bend kuzaana naamaizi.
P9	Neria agonza kuzaana ha myesuuso eyekuserengeta.
P10	Abaana abandi bagonza kuzaana emizaano eyokweteraniza ha mbaga.
P11	Keziah na Dona bagonza kuzaana ne'bidole ebyebyaahi.
P12	Kandi iwe? Onyumirwa kuzaana emizaano oba kwaha?
