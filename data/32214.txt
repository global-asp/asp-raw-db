desc:	Ngô-Iwa

page_text:

P1	One day, Ngô-Iwa, the dog found eggs at the river.
P2	Ngô-Iwa stole the ten eggs and put them in her bag.
P3	Ngô-Iwa kept the eggs warm. She wanted them to hatch.
P4	Crocodile asked Ngô-Iwa, "Have you seen my eggs?"
P5	When Ngô-Iwa denied, Crocodile asked all animals about her eggs.
P6	Meanwhile,  the eggs hatched one by one when Ngô-Iwa went hunting.
P7	Ngô-Iwa could not give the young crocodiles enough food.
P8	One day, Crocodile heard the young crocodiles crying for food!
P9	Crocodile entered the hut and hit Ngô-Iwa with her tail.
P10	Ngô-Iwa ran out through the window. Crocodile chased her.
P11	Ngô-Iwa stopped and begged, "I am sorry!"
P12	Crocodile forgave her and took her children to the river.
