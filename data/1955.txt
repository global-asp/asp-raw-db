title:	Tubala Bazira

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Ku nsulo eyo mu kibira tubala ensolo edhija okunhwa amaadhi. Ologhooza kiki ekibaawo nga empologoma eidhye?

page_text:

P1	Ndovu ndala yaja okunhwa amaadhi.
P2	Ntwiga ibiri dhaaja okunhwa amaadhi.
P3	Mbogo isatu na binhonhi bina byaaja okunhwa amaadhi.
P4	Mpongo itaanu na mbiidhi enkambwe mukaaga dhaaja okunhwa amaadhi.
P5	Bikere musanvu na byanhanda munaana byaali bili mu maadhi.
P6	Ntulege mwenda dhoona dhaaja okunhwa amaadhi.
P7	Mpologoma ndala yaidha yoona okunhwa amaadhi.
P8	Bazira bameka abaasigala nga bali kunhwa amaadhi?
