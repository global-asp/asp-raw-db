page_text:

P1	Dhikusooka aja mu kibuga ni mwanina.
P2	Mwanina alikulinga kungoye  empyaka mu idhuuka nga Dhikusooka yewumbye.
P3	Dhikusooka abona ekintu kyasinga kwenda.
P4	Engaito edhiri ku tundhibwa.
P5	Dhoona dhoona yadhegezaamu.
P6	Mwanina ya mugulira engaito da mupiira.
P7	Lwaki baali inho mu bwangu okwirayo eka?
P8	Ekyo omukisa yatuuka mu kisaghe mu kiseera! Ayambala engaito empyaka.
