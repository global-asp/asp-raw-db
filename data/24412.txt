title:	Lule and Kitty the Cat

desc:	It is a story about a boy who loves his pet so much that he steals milk for it. His mother chases the cat away.

page_text:

P1	Lule has a cat. It is called Kitty.
P2	He loves the cat so much. He is always stroking it. Peter his friend joins them too.
P3	Kitty loves drinking milk.
P4	Lule steals milk for Kitty. His mother is unhappy about it.
P5	She chases Kitty away. Lule starts crying.
