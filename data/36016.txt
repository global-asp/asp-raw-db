P2	Tsikulina aphunzitsi akulu wa pa sukulu iyo ana uza ana ao APA sukulu iyo kuti apite kumanyumba@Yao akakonzeko ulendo womwe  azakhala nao,wama phunziro.@Tsiku yaulendo ina kwana,ndipo ana ana ana konzeka mokwana bwino-bwino cifukwa anaweren
P3	Anapita pamalo ochedwa kamvi.Malo away yanali molo yokoma kwambiri,ngkhale ana asukulu aja anawa kinda kwambiri.@Ana asukulu anafika bwino pa kamvi.Ana aja sanafune kuti akhale pansi kuti aphumule,anafuna kuti@ayanganile malo ya kamvi cifuk
P4	Pamwe anali kuzungulila malo ya kamvi ana asukulu,panali ana asanu omwe anatuluka pagula la anzao napita kwaokha.@Ana aja anapita paka kuchile komwe anayamba kusewele mpaka kunawadela.pa ana asanu awa panali Matha,Petelu,Maliya,Monde@ndi Di
P5	Aphunzitsi awo,anayamba kusakila mpaka kunala,ndipo aphunzitsi anadankhawa."Anagniza kuti ana awa anga pits kuti kuno popeza malo ano ndi kuyamba kubwelako"@Cinali cinthu covuta kwambiri kuti apeze ana aja omwe ana sowa.Mpakani analepela ku
P6	Ana asanu aja omwe anathawa kupanda ulayila,anasowela kotelo kuti anakondwa kwambiri muchile muja anali kusewelera.pomwe anatha usewela,anafuna kuti spite komwe achokela kuliabwenzi awo,koma codabwisa nicakuti sana kumbukile komwa anacokela
P7	Matha anali yemwe analumidwa ndi njoka,koma abwenzi aske sana ziwe cocita mpaka Maths iya ana mwalila,muchilemo ndimomwe anashika thupi lake.
P8	Tsiku lina pomwe anapitiliza kusakila njira kuti apeze komwe kunali ana asukulu anzao analili,ana kumana ndi Bambi yaswa.
P9	Pomwe anafika pamalo ya kamvi,sana wapeze ana asukulu anzao kapena aphunzitsi. Anthu aja ana bwerelamo Ku manyumba ao.
P10	Panali cinthu cimodzi come China dabwisa kolo LA Matha.kholo ija inafunsa," kodi Matha alikuti,popeza kuti aphunzitsi anakamba kuti alipomodzi ndi inu.
P11	Thupi la Matha linatengedwa ndi kupelekedwa kumudzi kwamakolo,komwe anashikila.@Kucokela tsiku iya kunalibenso mwana wasukulu omwe anacita zimeneizi cifukwa cakuti ana ambiri anatengelapo phunziro yaikulu kwambiri.
