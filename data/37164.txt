page_text:

P1	Ke rata dimpša  tsa ka. Di botse kudu.@@I love my dogs.They are very beautiful.
P2	Gae re nale di pudi. Di Ja bojawang ga golo.@@At home we have goats.They eat a lot of grass..
P3	Nonyane ya ka e fofa godimo.@@My bird is flying up high.
P4	Mpša  ya ka e kitima ka kudu.@@My dog is running a lot.
P5	Dikatse tša gae di rata batho.@@Our cats at home love people.
P6	Lebitšo la katse ya ka ke Snowy.@@The name of my cat is Snowy.
P7	Mmutla wa ka o rata go tshega.@@My rabbits love to laugh.
