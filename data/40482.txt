title:	Gerogal ƴoyri dutal

desc:	Gerogal ƴoyri dutal bee ƴoyre hallunde ɓurnde semmbe.

page_text:

P1	Naane, gerogal bee dutal ɗi soobiraaji. Nyalnde feere dutal sey tawi gerogal e siwtoo ley lekki.@@E ngal darori koyngal gootal, boorooje e keewi kuuje haade maggal.
P2	Dutal sey ƴami gerogal, "Toye koyngal maaɗa gootal?"
P3	Gerogal sey wi'i dutal, "Cippoowo kuuje taƴaniiyam koyngal, nden hokkiyam kuuje ɗe'e."
P4	Dutal sey ƴami gerogal, hanum maa foti waɗa non na?@@E ngal yiɗi booroore kuuje. Sey gerogal jaɓi.
P5	Dutal sey tawi cippoowo wi'i, "Taƴu koyngal am gootal, hokkam booroore kuuje."@@Sey cippoowo jaɓi.
P6	Dutal diwiri koyngal gootal haa hooti.@@E ngal seyori kuuje ɗe cippoowo hokkingal.
P7	Nde dutal yotti wuro, sey tawi gerogal bee koyɗe maggal fuu ɗiɗi.
P8	Dutal ɓerni, sey wurni gerogal, ammaa nanngaayingal. Diga ɗon dutal fuɗɗi ɗaɓɓutugo ɓikkoy gerogal.@@To dutal nanngii gootel fuu, sey nyaamangel.
