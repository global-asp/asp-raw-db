page_text:

P1	onu omwisiki nibamweta Norah. Dolle ye nibajyeta Selah
P2	Norah nagonza munno dolle ye. Norah nazanna na Selah buli kiiro. Naraara na dolle ye buli kiiro.
P3	Dimu, Norah akabba atakuroora dolle ye. Akaseera buli haamu,beitu atakuroora Selah. Norah akeralikira munno.
P4	Norah akaaba atakimanyiire ngu nyinna akatwaala Selah kumwojya. Akanikka dolle aheeru kwooma
P5	"Tinkurrora dolleh yange,"Norah akaara. "Oterarikiira,"nyinna yagamba.
P6	Rwebajyo orru,nnyinna Norah dolleh akajiiha Nora. Dolleh ekabba neyerra munno. Selah akabba nasana dolleh enshyaka.
P7	Norah akasemererwa munno. Akagurukka heiguru na hansi. Akazina. Akasekeera heiguru.
P8	Akasiima nyinna.
