title:	Colours of a Rainbow

desc:	This story lists the colours of the rainbow and things you might see in everyday life that share these colours.

page_text:

P1	A rainbow has seven colours.
P2	The first colour is red.
P3	Kewyu is wearing a red shirt.
P4	The second colour is orange.
P5	This is an orange. What else can you see that is the colour orange?
P6	The third colour is yellow.
P7	The yolk of an egg is yellow. What else can you see that is the colour yellow?
P8	The fourth colour is green.
P9	Our banana garden is green.
P10	The fifth colour is blue.
P11	The sky is blue.
P12	The sixth colour is indigo.
P13	These berries are the colour indigo.
P14	The seventh colour is violet.
P15	My dress is violet. Violet is my favourite colour! What is your favourite colour?
P16	Do you know the seven colours of the rainbow?
