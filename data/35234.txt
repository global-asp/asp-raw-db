title:	Abantwana bamafutha
author:	Sponono  Mahlangu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiNdebele

desc:	Kade kwakunomndeni onabantwana abathathu abanehlonipho. Umraro kukobana bebenziwe ngamafutha.

page_text:

P1	Kade, lapha kwakuhlala umndeni ohlala uthabile.
P2	Abantwana bakhona bebathandana bangalwi. @@Bebasiza ababelethi babo ekhaya nemasimini.
P3	Kodwana bebangakavunyelwa ukobana baye eduze nomlilo.
P4	Bekufanele benze yoke imisebenzi yabo ebusuku.
P5	Ngesimanga sokobana bebenziwe ngamafutha!
P6	Kodwana omunye wabesana warhalela ukuphuma aye elangeni.
P7	Ngelinye ilanga wazizwa arhalela khulu ukuya ngaphandle. @@Umnakwabo banodadwabo bamkhalima.
P8	Kodwana besele baladelwe! @@Wancibilika elangeni elabe litjhisa bhe!
P9	Abentwana abenziwe ngamafutha bakhihla isililo nababona umnakwabo ancibilika aphela.
P10	Kodwana benza iqhinga. @@Benza  ijamo  lenyoni ngeensalela zamafutha ancibilikileko.
P11	Bathatha inyoni esele ingumnakwabo bakhamba nayo entabeni ekulu.
P12	Lokha ilanga naliphumako, yaphapha yabaleka, yavuma emkhanyweni wekuseni.
