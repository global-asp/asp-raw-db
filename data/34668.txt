title:	Where is Nina ?

desc:	Nina was sick. She decided to go to the Sunflower Field Farm but things didnt go very well as planned.

page_text:

P1	Nina is sick . Her parents look after her often so that she can get better.@Nina is lying on her bed. Her parents are talking to her . Nina's dad asked "How are you feeling today my child ?" @Nina replied "I'm very much better dad , Thanks"
P2	Nina's mother surprised Nina with a sunflower. Nina was very happy . She told her parents that she will be going to Sunflower field farm at Living Land City. Her parents agreed but they warned her that there will be a storm.
P3	Nina arrived at Sunflower field farm. She enjoyed watching the flowers and also she enjoy watching the pollinators. She even forgot that there is a storm coming.
P4	Nina looked and looked to the flowers. She now sees the sky that it is turning dark. She was scared. She said "The storm is coming they warned me. They warned me the storm is coming ". @Then it started raining heavily with hail.
P5	Nina was now even more scared because there was a lightning. She crawled on the green grass of the field until at the gate of the farm. Sadly the gate is locked. Nina must find the way to be out of the Sunflower field.
P6	Nina screamed for help at the field gate but no one responded. Nina was scared but she told her self not to worry but must be strong.
P7	At home, Nina's parents are worried that Nina is not back at home and there are floods outside their house. Nina's parents waited until the storm stopped and the floods were clear so that it can be safe to look for her.
P8	The storm and the floods cleared. Now Nina's parents must look for Nina .
P9	At the field ,Nina thought that maybe if she runs and pushes the gate , the gate will open. Nina tried her idea. Sadly she fell and she got injured on her forehead. She cried.
P10	Back at home , her parents and her brother looked for her everywhere but she was not found. Her parents asked people if they saw her and they didn't.
P11	Nina's brother told her father that Nina must be at the Sunflower Field. Nina's dad said "Yes, my boy . She must be there . Let us go there "
P12	They rushed there. They found her injured. Nina's father asked the security to open the Fields gate. The security said "The gate opener is at the School Uniform Shop , he will be back." Nina's father and her brother rushed there.
P13	They found the gate opener at the shop. @They arrived at the Sunflower Field and the gate was unlocked. They were very happy to see her but@sadly they found Nina injured on her forehead and her father rushed her to a local clinic.
P14	They went to a local clinic . Nina was checked and was given medicine by her doctor to take at home.
P15	They returned home . Nina told her parents and siblings of what happened. They forgave her.@Nina healed on her forehead.
P16	Nina told herself that what she did is wrong. She told everyone in her community of what happened at the Sunflower field.
