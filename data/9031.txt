desc:	Mother and father are planting gardens. The family works together to plant and harvest.

page_text:

P1	Maama wange yalima. Yalima akalimiro. Akalimiro keyalima kaali ka mpindi.
P2	Baaba yalima. Yalima akalimiro. Akalimiro keyalima kaali ka bimuli.
P3	Maama wange yadhuba mu bulimiro bwombi.
P4	Baaba wange yafukirira mu bulimiro bwombi.
P5	Abazaire bange ni Nze twakola mu bulimiro obutono. Mwaninhaze ni Nze twayeza ebimuli ne mpande.
P6	Akalimiro akatono ake mpande kavaamu agasawo ge mpindi. Akalimiro akatono ake bimuli kavaamu agasawo ge bimuli.
P7	Maama wange yalima. Yalima ogulimiro. Ogulimiro gwe yalima gwaali gwa mpindi.
P8	Baaba yalima. Yalima ogulimiro. Ogulimiro gwe yalima gwaali gwa bimuli.
