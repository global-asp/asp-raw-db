desc:	Qwallon kafa rai ne ma yara a qungiyar wasan nan.

page_text:

P1	Bling ya so qwallon kafa so sai.@  @In ya na magana aka'n wasa, sai ya ce, "Qwallon kafa rai ne."
P2	Bling ya na xaya mafi yar wasa'n koci Tsepo.@  @Koci'n murna aka'n qungiyar 'yar wasa. Mutane da yawa su'n so su shiga qungiyar.'
P3	Wani wasa me muhimanci na zuwa.@ @ Qungiyar su'n na ta shiri ko wace rana.@  @Kowa na murna game da bavvan@  wasa'r.
P4	Koci daga xayan qungiyar mutumin me kishi ne.@  @Ya yi kishi'n nasara'n koci Tsepo so sai.
P5	Koci me kishi ya tafi filin da xare kafin ranar wasa'n.@  @Ya shuka abin haxari.@  @ Ya shuka umuthi!
P6	Bling ya fara wasa'n da zafi.@  @Ya na guduwa ya ci qwallon.
P7	Ya yar da harbi qwallo, sai ya faxi.@  @"Bling"? taron mutanen sun yi ihu.@  @ koci Tsedo ya roke. "Bling?"
P8	"Ba a bin da zai ajiye ni a kasa!" Bling ya faxi@  @"Ma kan sa."@  @Ya tashi sama, sai ya yi wasa har qarshe.
