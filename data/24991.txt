title:	Omwana w'emirembe

desc:	Omukazi nomwana ghe mukibira ki ekyabatukaku?

page_text:

P1	Omukazi yaja mukibira n'omwana ghe okunhoga ku kibala.
P2	Mukibira omukazi yayaganayo omuti oguliku ekibala ekyenvu.
P3	Yatoola omwana ghe alambaire yamuta ghansi, male yaniina omuti.
P4	Omulaguzi okuva ku kyalo ekindi, yali atambula yabona omwana. Yewuunia inho era yebuuza,"Maama w'omwana ono aligha?"
P5	Yakutama abone omwana. Okughaala okw'ebinhere bye eby'omunkoto byasitula omwana alambaire.
P6	Omwana ya yatandiika ozaanha n'ebinhere by'omulaguzi munkoto. Kino canhoona inho omwana era yeyongera okuzanisa ebinhere.
P7	Omukazi yaghalirizibwa okulinga ghansi okuzuula ekyaali kiri kusesa omwana alambaire. Yabona omuntu omugwira gwataidhi.
P8	Omukazi yafuna entisa yamaani eyamuletera n'okulekula ekisawo ky'ebibala okugwa ghansi.
P9	Omulaguzi yalinga ghangulu yabona omukazi. Yamukoba,"Totya. Ndibwakuzaanaku n'omwana w'ono omulungi.
P10	Kale omukazi yeika ghansi okuva ku muti.
P11	Omulaguzi yatoolaku ekinhere kwebyo byeyalinabyo, yaakiwa omwana. Yakoba omwana,"Kino kirabo kyo."
P12	"Ja waka n'omwana wo. Kobera musaadha wo aje mu byalo eby'emirembe. Omwana wo ampaire emiremebe," omuswezi yakoba.
