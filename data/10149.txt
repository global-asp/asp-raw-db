desc:	The rhyme talks about sound  Aa.

page_text:

P1	I am Ana Maria Namata.
P2	My dad is Mr Agaba Ali.
P3	My mum is Ananga Asha. She cares and loves so much.
P4	Dad wants apples and ants.
P5	Apples and ants are sweet.
P6	Angela and Agatha play with toy cats and rabbits.
P7	Male is a tall man.
P8	Apples are packed in a basket.
P9	Namata,  Cathy,  Sara, Ali and Aba sat together.
P10	Mummy taught me words with sound Aa.
P11	Namagala is a prayerful girl.
P12	Asaba and Akello learnt about a train.
P13	Sound Aaa i will never forget you.
