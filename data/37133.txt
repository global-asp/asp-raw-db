P1	It is Zayb's birthday, and she is super excited to spend the day with all her friends.@@Zayb was going to be 8 years old, and she couldn't wait to celebrate her new age. She was also so excited to get new.presents from her Mom and Dad.
P2	Suddenly the door bell rang and Zayb ran to the door to see who it was. Much to her surprise, it was all her fiends at once. They all shouted 'surprise!' Zayb invited them in and a few minutes later they went to the park to run about.
