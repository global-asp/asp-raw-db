title:	Listen to my Body
author:	Teachers of Bubaali Primary School
artist:	Angie & Upesh, Karlien de Villiers, Marike le Roux, Catherine Groenewald, Jesse Pietersen and Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Each of us has a wonderful body. Have you ever listened to what your body tells you?

page_text:

P1	Today I am going to school with my brother.
P2	I feel like eating food.
P3	What am I going to do? Today I am going to listen to my body!
P4	I want to read story book with my mum.
P5	Yes,  now I can hear my breath. In. Out. In. Out.
P6	We can sing after the lesson to refresh our minds,  can we?
P7	And now I can hear my heart beating! Doodom,  doodoom,  dooodooom.
P8	I can run.
P9	And if I put my fingers on my wrist then I can hear my pulse!
P10	And I can hear myself laughing Haha haha haaah haaa!
P11	I hate crying.
P12	And clapping tap tappa tap tapaa.
P13	And saying feed me,  feed me.
P14	My body enjoys riding a lot. What about you?
P15	It is fun listening to my body. An now I want to hear my jaws chewing.
