title:	Kweyock Cee Leelac

desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Kaweetokey sipho ceekwaam muŋeet.
P2	His sister is looking at the clothes in shops. Sipho is bored.
P3	Kakay Sipho kito ake nyee caamet.
P4	Colyoi Kwoyok.
P5	Kasyeem neeto kwoyok tokol.
P6	Kakwoolyi ceekwaam kwooyookap nopiret.
P7	Amune sakusartakey cektoka wunyi?
P8	Kupeyok kule,  kuponta neeto wule lworone mpiret lakwart. Miite neeto kulole kwoyokyi coo leelac.
