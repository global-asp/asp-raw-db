page_text:

P1	Amaka lives in the village of Ozuma.
P2	Amaka went to the forest with her friend, Joann.
P3	Amaka climbed a tree to pluck some leaves for herbs. Her parents needed the herbs.
P4	She climbed so high and couldn't climb down.
P5	Joann hurried to get help for Amaka. Her parents came. They called out to her to trust them and jump.
P6	Amaka cried when she got down. Jumping down was so scary. Everyone was comforting her.
P7	Amaka was happy to be back home.  Everyone celebrated Amaka's safe return.
