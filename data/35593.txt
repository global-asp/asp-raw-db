desc:	2020 International Day of the Girl Child. The story is about a 13 years old girl called Amina
 Her Principal attempted raping her but so was rescued by people.

page_text:

P1	Amina is a very beautiful girl. She is 13 years old.
P2	Amina goes to Government Girls' Secondary School, Rafi.
P3	One day, Amina was playing with her mates in the school compound.
P4	The Principal came and called Amina to his office.
P5	When Amina entered,  the Principal sent her to clean his toilet.
P6	The Principal entered and started undressing himself.
P7	"Please don't rape me", she shouted. People rushed and saved her.
P8	The Principal was sacked. They no longer gave them male Principal.
