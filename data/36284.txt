desc:	In African languages we talk out of the nouns. Mo - Ba Mosadi-Basadi. The whole language is built and formed out of the nouns. A noun can be a subject

page_text:

P1	Lelapa le kopana bosigo go bua. Basadi ba tla apaya dijo.
P2	Dinaledi tsa Gauteng bosigo di dintle. Ke rata dinaledi.
P3	Abuti o robale sentle. Kgaitsedi, le wena o robale sentle. Bana ba rata go bala beibele pele ba robala.
P4	Ntate Modise o rapela bosigo pele a robala. O rapelela bana ba gagwe.  Kgogo e lebelela ntate.  Lebone le a tuka.
P5	Mosimane o ja dijo tsa phakela le ntate mogolo. Dijo di fa maatla.
P6	lelapa = family;  basadi = women;  dinaledi = stars;  rata = love or like;  bala = read;  rapela = pray;  lebone = light;  tuka = shine;  fa = give;  lebelela = watch
