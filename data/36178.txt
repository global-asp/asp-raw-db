P1	Once upon a time there lived two friends
P2	They lived in the same house,one day they visited their neighbour dog,then bunny screamed run it is the mighty horse
P3	So they started running,they run and run,but they got tired
P4	I just wanted to play with you said horse,sorry we thought you might want to eat us.Me I eat plants only, I am a herbivores
P5	This is my stable you can come in if you want to
P6	Lets go to cow
P7	So as they went they met more friends,hi donkey
P8	They kept going, donkey also joined.
