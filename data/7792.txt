title:	A Dog
author:	Ritah Katetemera and Cissy Namugerwa
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Jean Fullalove, Sandy Campbell, Ursula Nafula and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7792.png
desc:	The story is about a man who had his own dog which was very faithful and obedient to him.

page_text:

P1	There was a man with his one dog.
P2	He loved his dog so much.
P3	It was hard working and it used to go to the market to buy meat.
P4	Fetching water from the well.
P5	Guarding every thing at home.
P6	One day the man thanked his dog in a special way for being faithful.
P7	The dog became so happy and it continued working hard.
