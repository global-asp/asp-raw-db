title:	Apotis Ka Aooku
author:	Alfred Esinyen and Simon Ipoo
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2858.png
desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	Inyo epot? Ekukut kori akopiro?
P2	Inyo eook? Ng'atur kori epeipe?
P3	Ani-koku epot?
P4	Ng'ae epot,  etom kori ekile?
P5	Nyani ni epot,  akilo ang'amoru.
P6	Kori agram na esimit?
P7	Ani epot? Akilo na esukari a?
P8	Kori akilo ang'akopir?
