title:	Maria akawala ke somero

desc:	Akagero kano kali kumuwala Maria

page_text:

P1	Maria  akeera nasenya amanyo  kumakya nayambala yinifomu nalya nekyenkya  nagenda kusomero
P2	Ku somero Maria ayela  ekibiina nemikwano gye nalongosa ne ntebe
P3	Kino kye kibina  kya Maria  omusomesa we  ye mukyala Marion  mukyala mulungi  era Maria amwagala nyo
P4	Maria yakulira  ekibiina aleeta ebitabo okuva mu yafiisi nabiwa bayizi banne okusoma
P5	budde bwakuwumula Maria nemikwano gye bali mukisawe bazanya mupiira kuba kilungi  okuzannya
P6	Budde bwa malya gakyamisana Maria nemikwano gye badayo waka  ewaka Maria ayamba ku bazaddebe ku mirimu gya waka
P7	Ewaka Maria nabewabwe batula  kumeza nebalya  emmere  oluvanyuma lwe ssala ya jjaja weomukazi
P8	Maria ayagala nnyo okulongosa ennyumba  nokwoza ebintu oluvanyuma lwe ekyemisana Maria afumba nnyo okulaba nge kiyungu ekinyirila  okugogoba  obuwuka
P9	Maria ne muto we bawa ensolo zabwe  emmere eba esigadde wo era bakikola  mbudde bwabwe obwedembe
P10	Kati buzibye  buli omu akoye era otulo tumuluma Maria ne muto we bali mukisenge kyabwe bagenda kwebaka
