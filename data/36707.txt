title:	The king of the jungle

desc:	You should never tell other people what to do and appreciate other people

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a cheetah. He was the king of the jungle all the animals used to follow his orders
P2	Everyone followed his orders. He would screamed and all the animals would rush and leave everything they were busy with.
P3	Everyone got tired of him because he was always bossing them around telling them to do this and that
P4	Elephant answers him so rudely everyone was shocked.Everyone was looking at elephant
P5	Elephant took kings speer and he wanted to throw it away
P6	And then bull came and took the speer by elephant.King got so scared cause they played with his speer telling him to stop telling them what to do
P7	Baboon threw the speer that side where king was standing
P8	Then king told them how he was so sorry telling them what to do. He said "from now on you can do what you want to"
P9	Kudu came front wards and said they accept kings order unless he throw the speer away.King agreed and said to kudu that he should throw it away
P10	Kudu agreed and king said "yes throw it away now it's time " kudu didn't throw it so far. He threw it just here in front of everyone.
P11	King represented kudu as new king everyone was angry but king never worried about them he lifted kudus hand up and screamed TO THE NEW KING!!!!.Although the animals was furious
