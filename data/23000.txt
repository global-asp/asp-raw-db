desc:	Jadit moku ma jaswa obedo gwoku ngo cham maber

page_text:

P1	Ikare moku, obedo jadit moku manyinge obedo Joel Ipacu mi Aya.
P2	Ebed eparu ngo pilembe mi kanu cham mi anyim, re ebed enyotha anyotha.
P3	Piny moko upe zo mudong iodu cham.
P4	Anywagi mi podhu zo uthwo.
P5	Yamu matek uleku wii odu cham chen.
P6	Awiya uchaku ka laro nyithi cham mudong i supiriya.
P7	Mer uvutu tatro nang eywagu cham mukende mi miko ni awiya.
P8	Wego obedo itheot nang eparu pi anyim.
