P1	Once upon a time,  there lived a man called Joel in Aya village.
P2	He did not mind of storing food for future use,  but waste them.
P3	Nothing was left in the granary.
P4	Maize plants wilted and dried up.
P5	A strong wind blew off the granary roof.
P6	Children scramble for little food left in the saucepan.
P7	Mother laid flat down crying about what to give to the children next time.
P8	Father sat under the veranda thinking about the future.
