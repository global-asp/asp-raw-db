desc:	Which meat do you like? Ever wondered where it comes from. Lets see which animal gives us what.

page_text:

P1	Hello, I am Chicken.@What can we get from Chicken?@We can get eggs and meat.
P2	Hello I am goat.@What can we get from Goat?@We can get meat, milk, cheese and wool.
P3	Hello, I am cow.@What can we get from Cow?@We can get steak, cheese, milk, beef and leather.
P4	Hello, I am sheep.@What can we get from Sheep.@We can get cheese, wool and lamb chops.
P5	Hello, I am pig.@What can we get from Pig?@We can get bacon, pork sausages, ham and pork chops.
P6	Thank you animals!
