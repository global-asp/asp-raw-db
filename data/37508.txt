P1	Kuinhwa Mlume nraisi@ ela kukala Mlume weee@Pana vumba... nkazi....
P2	Anzaka tamtaki kulola ni uchoyo tu na mwisho mndalola vivyo. Nya mtakao lola chanhi akachesa kupheka pkwa alume ayawenu na kupigizwa masama anaambwa alaphize mpaka makaka zao "kaka mjinga kaka nimjinga, kaka hafai kaka hafai kaka alioza....
P3	Wedze kula Dubai upige ndowa uuye  Katari, yuno mwanangwa unamrichira ani  ebu  utaka auyire nyo arinho vumba nanho. Ngawa sikumtuluza  hamu chimwaka phanhi ndipho ukaphia hijaa hooooofwi kurichira atu gongo ra mboto mnaatari himwi.
P4	Mwatwenye niendzerani kazi ya kauntieeeee nami ninwe chai ya saane....saasita niyadzifitsa msikitini nchiswali situluka nalala mpka saanane....🤣🤣🤣
P5	Aphu hipha ni udine sana mana lipkwale ra hiyu umlolaye tariwezwa nimlume  mmwenga...🤣🤣🤣
P6	Mnaamba mpkwaza fulani achere warabuni, uphieni nkakuonyeseni ariko mniambire napho Magwangwani...anavwala vininja kataki kumanyiwa!🤣🤣🤣
P7	Uchitaka kuona vitsimbakazi, nchiliku, mkuse, tsanza ooosi inyo usienze kayambani hiko ni adisi tuphu ela phiya harusini ukaone laivulaivu...... atu akadzipodoa ela avi mmasanamu gakuondosa nguluwe akalani...lazima yaniii phanhii.🤣🤣🤣
P8	"Alume achili unanyinji maarifa nkidogo, Achetu taana achili ela maarifa anamanji"
