page_text:

P1	This is Matende. Huyu ni Matende.
P2	Matende cried all the time. Matende alilia kila wakati.
P3	When his mother fed him,  Matende cried. Mamake alipomnywesha maziwa,  Matende alilia.
P4	When Matende was put down to rest,  he still cried. Matende alipowekwa kupumzika,  bado alilia.
P5	When his mother changed his nappies,  Matende continued to cry. Mamake alipobadilisha pampa,  Matende aliendelea kulia.
P6	When his mother carried him on her shoulder,  Matende cried more. Mamake alipombeba begani,  Matende alilia zaidi.
P7	When he fell asleep,  Matende looked like he would start to cry again. Alipopatwa na usingizi,  Matende alionekana kuwa angeenza kulia tena.
P8	Matende's mother looked at her son. She did not know what was wrong with him. Mamake Matende alimwangalia mwanawe. Hakujua kilichomsumbua.
