desc:	Woro omiyo ŋok gi dyeo ocando Otal.

page_text:

P1	Adha wan okelo olemo kweth kweth wok gine i kisiya.
P2	Oyido olemo neno maŋon tektek.
P3	Omin wan Otal obedo gi woro to kayo kis olemo.
P4	Ŋa ma okayo kayo olemo me ama?
P5	Adha wan obedo gi keme ma thoth to koko.
P6	Wa ŋico ni Otal ama okayo kayo olemo.
P7	Ameno apaka wawaco ri adha wan.
P8	Adha wan openjo go to yeyo.
P9	Otal to cako winjo tuwo abwap.
P10	To ŋweco i coron piyo piyo.
P11	Ŋok gi dyewo obino ri go abwap.
P12	Adha wan openjo gi ma wa fonjero kwoŋ tim p'Otal.
