page_text:

P1	Omukazi nomwana we bakaza omwihamba. Akaza kutorotora ebijuuma.
P2	Omwihamba akashangamu omuti gwine ebijuuma bihire.
P3	Akakwasa ahansi omwana weye owabire ahungiire yatemba omuti.
P4	Omushaija araruga omukyaro ekindi akaijaho. Yareeba omwana. Yahoberwa. Yayebuza,"Nyina arinkahe?"
P5	Akainama ahansi. Okuyomba kwomukufu gwomumaraka ge kwasisimura omwana.
P6	Akareeka omwana yazanisa omukufu gweye. Omwana akasheka obwe arazaana.
P7	Omukazi akaranzya ahansi kureeba ekirashesya omwana. Yareeba omuntu owu ataramanya.
P8	Akatiina munonga yanaga ahansi enshaho yebijuuma.
P9	Omushaija akareeba ahaiguru. Yagira ati, "Reeka kutiina. Ndimukuzaana nomwana wawe murungi."
P10	Omukazi akatemburuka omumuti.
P11	Omushaija akaihamu kamwe ahamikufu yeye. Yakhereza omwana. "Eki nekihembo kyawe," nikwe yagambire.
P12	"Rooko otahe omuka nomwana wawe. Ogambire baro agyende omukyaro ekyobusingye. Omwana wawe yampa obusingye,"omushaija nikwo yagira.
