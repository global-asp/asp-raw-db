desc:	Ntano yamapikica yomwe inenelela umoyo wa nyemba: kushanga

page_text:

P1	Munzanga ananipasa nyemba zomwe anakolola mumunda wake.
P2	Nishanga nyemba mudothi.
P3	Nyemba yayamba kuphukila mu dothi.
P4	M'mela pokula upita kumwamba kuzuba. Mizu zimela kungena mudothi.
P5	M'mela utuluka pamwamba pa nthaka.
P6	M'mela uyamba kukula,
P7	Na kukula.
P8	Nishanganso zina nyemba.
P9	Nyemba zikula,
P10	Na kukula.
P11	Nyemba zamela makoko.
P12	Nyemba zakula kupita kumwamba, na kumwamba kukwela cipupa.
P13	Nikolola makoko akayuma.
P14	Nicosa nyemba mu makoko.
P15	Niphika nyemba, kuphikila pamodzi na anyezi ni ma spaici ena.
P16	Izi nyemba zapya kuti tidye.
