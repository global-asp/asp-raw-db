title:	Nḓila ye nda xela ngayo makete
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37298.png
desc:	Mutukana o fulufhedziswa mpho yavhuḓi nga ḓuvha ḽawe ḽa mabebo musi a tshi fara miṅwaha ya rathi. Wanani uri ho itea mini.

page_text:

P1	Muḓanani wahashu hu na dzimbudzi.@@Ndi kule na dziḓorobo khulwane.
P2	Mme anga vho ri, "Elly, ri na mpho yaṋu ya ḓuvha ḽa mabebo musi ni tshi fara miṅwaha ya rathi."
P3	Mma, mme-muhulu na nṋe, ro vha ro lindela bisi.
P4	Ndo dzula nṱha ha tshirumbi tsha mma.@@Ndo eḓela lwendo lwoṱhe.
P5	Ro swika makete.@@Nda vhona helikhoptha yo nakaho ya muvhala wa lutombo.
P6	Huṅwe fhethu ho vha hu na mitshelo.@@Ndo vhudzisa mma u ri, "Ndi mitshelo-ḓe yeneyi?"
P7	Mma vha ri, "Haya ndi maapuḽa."@@Nda vha luvhelela nda ri, "Ndi khou humbela u rengelwa ḽithihi."
P8	Ndo mbo ḓi hangwa u fara tshanḓa tsha mme anga.@@Ndo vha ndo sedza apuḽa fhedzi.
P9	Musi ndi tshi fhedza u ḽa, ndo vha ndo no sala ndi ndoṱhe.@@Nda thoma u lila.
P10	Vhaṅwe mufumakadzi vha ṱuwa na nṋe ra ya hu re na vhaṅwe vhana.
P11	Ndo humbula uri vhana vha rengiswa makete.@@Nda lila nga maanḓa.
P12	Vhaṅwe munna vho vhudzisa vha ri, "Elly u ngafhi?"@@Nda dzumbama uri vha si mmbone.
P13	Nda pfa ipfi ḽa mme anga ḽine nda ḽi ḓivha ḽi tshi ri, "Elly!"@@Ndo vha ndo takala nga maanḓa.
P14	Mme-muhulu wanga vha bvisa helikhoptha ya u naka.@@Vha ri, "Ndi yaṋu!"
