title:	Gerogal bee Kaatoota
author:	Ibraahim Baabayo and Muhammed Muktaar
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	E woodi soobaaku hakkunde gerogal bee kaatoota

page_text:

P1	Gerogal bee kaatoota soobiriino.
P2	Gerogal bee kaatoota njahii fijugo bol.
P3	Gerogal reenata.
P4	Sey kaatoota nastini tati!
P5	Ɓernde gerogal metti.
P6	Gerogal moɗii kaatoota.
P7	Sey gerogal fotti e inna kaatoota.
P8	Kaatoota woyi nder reedu gerogal wiʼi, "Inna, wallitam! Gerogal moɗiiyam!"
P9	Kaatoota welaa, nga walaa ndakam, sey gerogal nyawi.
P10	Gerogal fuɗɗi ɗojjugo.
P11	Gerogal ɗojji haa tuuti kaatoota.
P12	Jooni, gerogal bee kaatoota wanyindirii.
