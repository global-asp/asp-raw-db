page_text:

P1	This is father hare, he is tired ,  father hare is hardworking. Since mother hare died father hare looks for food for his little hare's father hare is good Enter text here.
P2	Father hare stays with the greedy hyena and his family, the  greedy hyena always steals father hare's food, when father hyena goes to look for food father hyena sleeps, the greedy hyena is lazy, he is bad.
P3	One day the greedy hyena ate one little hare when father hare was not around, father hare felt bad, he was very sad. He tricked the hyena to go and steal lions food, the lion caught the greedy hyena and he ate him up with is cabs.
P4	Mother hyena ran to the forest, she went to stay with the other animals since she could not stay with father hare anymore. Enter text here.
P5	This is father hare's  homestead, they are inside the house. Father hare protects his children from dangerous animals  during free time father hare teaches his children how to hunt for food,  they are hardworking too. Father hare's family  is happy.
