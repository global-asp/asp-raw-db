title:	Tsingaato Tsiimbya
author:	Cornelius Wekunya
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lumasaaba
level:	First words

desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Wasinya atsya mu shiriimba n'umukooko weewe.
P2	Umukooko weewe ali khukhwikheenga tsingubo mwi kulaanilo. Wasinya waasinyaane.
P3	Wasinya waabona sisindu syesi naye akana.
P4	Bali khuukulisa bilaalo khu layisi.
P5	Watsiikhakamwo.
P6	Umukooko weewe waamukulila tsingaato tse khuusaambilamwo kumupiila.
P7	Shiina kila bali khuuhakara khuukhwiila ingo?
P8	Sye khabi indayi bola khu shisaawe mu mbuka. Nga wakwaarire tsingaato tseewe tsiimbya.
