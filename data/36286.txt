desc:	Subject concords we need to use the prefix of the noun to determine the balance or agreement between your subject noun and your verb. E.g. Banna ba nwa bojwala.

page_text:

P1	Morutisi o ruta barutwana  sekolong.
P2	Lelapa  le  bua kgang e botlhokwa.
P3	Sechaba se itumetse  gompieno.
P4	Koloi e lebile toropong.
P5	Dipitsi di tshameka mmogo.
P6	Seatla se amogela dijo.
P7	Lonao lo raga bolo.
P8	Noun Class @1. Mo - Ba/Bo@2. Mo - Me@3. Le - Ma @4. Se - Di @5. No prefix - Di @6. Lo - Di @7. Bo - Ma
