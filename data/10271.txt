desc:	Samba is excited about his new shirt. But unfortunately he doesnt look after his shirt very well.

page_text:

P1	Samba's dad bought him a new school shirt last market day.
P2	Samba never misses school. He walks to school with his little sister.
P3	Samba likes playing football after school. He wears his new shirt.
P4	The shirt gets dirty. But Samba does not want to wash it!
P5	On the field,  Samba wants everyone to see his new shirt.
P6	In the morning Samba goes to school with the dirty shirt. He sees the teacher waiting at the gate.
P7	Samba dodges school that day and goes across the road to play football.
P8	Unfortunately the new shirt gets torn. Now Samba is in trouble for dodging school and for tearing his new school shirt.
