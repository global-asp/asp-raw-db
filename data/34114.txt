Lʊkaasɩ 15.11-32

Mbʊpá njala nnɖe n na lǝ ʊpilika agakɛɛshɩlei. Yeesu a kɛɛshɩɩ galei gaɖe acere bɛrɛ.

page_text:

P1	Na Yeesu a lǝ wàà : « Ʊsoro ɖǝn a wʊɖa bayifala banyɩʊ.
P2	Na kajala kpataa a lǝ ayar wàà : "Amawɛɛ, jɩ̂ suuru, naa ashee amʊ amaja tiibi." Ŋkǝŋ na, ʼbaya a yʊga amaana ɖe.
P3	Ɩ lee ɩshilé ɩtʊrka ma, na kajala ɖe a yala atiibi aŋunii naa atɔɔ ɩtanɩ ɖe afʊba mpá gaɖulǝŋka.
P4	A kpa ŋkǝŋ ma, a lee fɔɩfɔɩ naa ajɩ ɩtanɩ ɩŋunii.
P5	A jɩ ɩtanɩ ɖe ɩŋunii ma, gakɔŋja ga kpa mpá nɖe nɩ, nʼʊpi ɖe a sǝba na jɩ wahala.
P6	Nʼa ce atɩ ɖa nʼarɛ̀ ɖǝn mpá nɖe nɩ. Nʼarɛ̀ ɖe a tɔɔ nɩ ace na ŋŋɔ nɩ, a naa na kîɖe ɩkuruci. A tɩ yɛɛ a jɩ̂ ɩkuruci bʊjɩʊ, ama kǝ ba shee nɩ na.
P7	Ŋkǝŋ na a kɔɔ na gʊfaŋa naa alǝ agʊlɔ wàà : "Amawɛɛ agaɖu ʊjɩʊ ʊ rŋǝ rŋǝ ashee batǝnleeja halɩ afǝl sǝriya. Nʼamʊ, n ɖa atɩya na ci akɔ­mɔ.
P8	N tǝ ma kɔɔ kɔ̂ɔ amawɛɛ sul, naa ma lǝ̂ nɩ wàà : Amawɛɛ, n tǝrǝtǝrǝ na Gaja naa atǝrǝtǝrǝ nʼakɩ. Galaŋ, kǝ ma kɔɔ sim á yɩ̂ɖa amʊ wàà, akapi na. Sâra nʼamʊ ɖaa akǝbatǝnleeja ma."
P9	Nʼa ta akɔɔ kɔɔ ayar agaɖu.
P10	Ayar a ŋǝ nɩ gaɖulǝŋka ma, nʼa ɖa nʼɩshɛɩ nʼa cu ace atɩ wa na nɩ naa alee nɩ atuu. @@	Ŋkǝŋ na, ʊpi a lǝ ayar wàà : "Amawɛɛ, n tǝrǝtǝrǝ na Gaja naa atǝrǝtǝrǝ nʼakɩ. Galaŋ, kǝ ma kɔɔ sim á yɩ̂ɖa amʊ wàà, akapi na."
P11	Ama ayar a lǝ abatǝnleeja wàà : "Were ! Pɩ̂ ma na jɩŋgaarɩ katoko asoŋsono naa akara ampi. Kâra ma nɩ gɩsǝkɔɩ agɩsǝwa nɩ naa akara nɩ akɔɖɔkɔɖɔ ʼɩkei nɩ. @@Pɩ̂ ma nʼana nɖee a sǝŋkǝla gɩshɛbʊ ma, naa afʊ nɩ gɩ jɩ̂ na jɩŋgaarɩ ajaŋ nʼampi ! Ɩɩŋ, ampi nɖee a cee cii ma, a kɔɔ pɩ ŋŋuro nɩ. A cee lii, nʼa kɔɔ pɩ !" @@Na ba sǝba na jɩ jɩŋgaarɩ.
P12	Kaŋkǝlǝ́ nɖe, ʊpur ʊŋono aɖe ɖa awar. @@A ta atʊ na pɩ asǝla na gafala ma, nʼa rǝŋǝ gʊcǝma gʊ na yu ma. Nʼa yɩɖa atǝnleeja ɖǝn naa ataba nɩ wàà : "Maakɔ na lee ŋkǝŋ ?" @@Nʼatǝnleeja a tǝlǝ ashee nɩ wàà : "Akapal a kpa ka, nʼakawɛɛ a fʊ ana nɖee a sǝŋkǝla gɩshɛbʊ ma, aɖʊwaa a ŋǝ ʊpur nʼalaafɩya."
P13	Ŋkǝŋ na ʊpur ʊŋono a cam ʊnyana naa abǝla wàà, kʼa kpâ afal na. @@Nʼayar a ɖoo naa apǝlapǝla nɩ wàà, a kpâ afal.@@Ama ʊpur ɖe a lǝ ashee ayar wàà : "Ɖʊ̂ gʊtɔ ! Ɖoo bʊja tuu­tuuma n na lee ntǝma na shee akɩ ɖaa aɖɔmpǝla ma, kʼá ɖʊ amʊ nɖǝn, n bǝla ayɔ na.
P14	Ama kʼá shee amʊ gacipì gaɖǝn ma jɩ̂ jɩŋgaarɩ nʼambasoro ayɔ na. @@Akapi nɖee a kpa ma, a jɩ akɩtanɩ ɩŋunii na bapigii bayeeyeeka, ama a kpa ma, nʼʊ fʊ ana nɖee a sǝŋkǝla gɩshɛbʊ ma, ashee nɩ."
P15	Nʼayar a lǝ ashee nɩ wàà : "Ampi, akɩ ʊ ɖa nʼamʊ baa sáà apaŋa na, bʊkɔ mbʊɖee n wʊɖa ma, bʊŋunii akɩ jee. @@Ɩ sim gɩ jɩ̂ jɩŋgaarɩ nʼʊciŋkǝrka, aɖʊwaa akapal a kpa ma, a cee cii, nʼa kɔɔ pɩ ŋŋuro nɩ. A cee lii, nʼa kɔɔ pɩ !" »
