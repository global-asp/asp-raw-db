title:	Baaba, ɓiɗɗo e wamnde

desc:	Baaba e ɓiɗɗo ngaɗii yaadu bee wamnde maɓɓe. To ɓe njahi fuu

page_text:

P1	Baaba e ɓiɗɗo maako bee wamnde maɓɓe ɓe ngaɗii yaadu juutundu.@@Dow laawol, himɓe feere-feere ƴamiɓe, ko waɗi ɓe mbaɗɗaaki wamnde nden.
P2	Baaba sey wi'i ɓiɗɗo mum waɗɗoo wamnde nden, hanko mo yahay bee koyɗe.@@Sey ɓe njahiri non.
P3	Sey ɓe tawi himɓe feere. Himɓe ɓen mbi'i, "Ndaaree ɓiɗɗo o'o, mo walaa hakkiilo, e mo waɗɗii wamnde, baaba maako e yaha e koyɗe!"
P4	Nanugo non, sey ɓiɗɗo jippii wamnde nden, baaba maako waɗɗiinde. @@Sey ɓe ndilliri non.
P5	Yeeso seɗɗa, himɓe feere mbi'i, "Ndaaree ndottiijo o'o, mo walaa enɗam, e mo waɗɗii wamnde, ɓiɗɗo maako e yaha e koyɗe!"
P6	Sey Baaba bee ɓiɗɗo numi, ndikka ɓe ɗiɗo fuu ɓe mbaɗɗodoo wamnde.
P7	Ammaa neeɓaayi, himɓe feere mbi'i, "Ndaaree himɓe ɓe'e, ɓe ngalaa barka, ɓe mbaray wamnde nde'e."
P8	Sey baaba e ɓiɗɗo killitii, no ɓe ngaɗi fuu ɓe kisataa wolweego.@@Sey ɓe ngi'i ndikka huutinirgo dabare maɓɓe ngam waɗugo ko haani.
