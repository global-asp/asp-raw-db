desc:	This story was created on World Book Day 2021 by Emmauella and Folashade Adekunle during a story-making workshop with Mimi Werna. This story celebrates their beautiful friendship as sisters.

page_text:

P1	This is Ella and Shade.@@They enjoy skipping, ropes.
P2	Ella loves learning and so does Shade.
P3	Ella loves fruits and so does Shade.
P4	They enjoy playing football with their friends.
P5	They love swimming in the pool.
P6	They enjoy reading with their friends.
P7	Ella and Shade are lucky. @@They have nice friends and each other.
P8	Ella and Shade may be sisters, but they also enjoy being friends too.
