page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there were two kingdoms.@@ One kingdom in the South, and one in the North.
P2	The rulers of these kingdoms used ravens to send messages to each other.
P3	One day, the king of the Northern kingdom sent a message to the Southern kingdom.
P4	The raven took the message and went home to her family. @@They read the message together.
P5	The Northern king was declaring war on the South! @@The ravens discussed this and decided to change the message.
P6	The raven delivered the new message to the Southern king.
P7	The message was: Let peace prevail among us. @@The king smiled and sent the same message back to the Northern king.
P8	To this day, the kingdoms are at peace.@@And no one knows that ravens can read.
