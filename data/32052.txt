desc:	Dabbaaji fuu e nana ɗonka. Limuɗi no ɗi njahirta yaroygo ndiyam.

page_text:

P1	Nyiiwa goʼo yahi yarugo ndiyam.
P2	Tireeji ɗiɗi njahi yarugo ndiyam.
P3	Bani tati e colli nayi njahi yarugo ndiyam.
P4	Baɗɗi jowi e gaduuji ladde joweegoʼo njahi yarugo ndiyam.
P5	Bamɗi ladde ɗi ciifi kanji joweeɗiɗi e ndoggana yaroygo ndiyam.
P6	Paaɓi joweetati e liƴƴi joweenayi e ɗi nginoo nder mbeela.
P7	Mawndu e woya. Kayru maa ndu yiɗi yarugo ndiyam. Ɗume hulataa mawndu?
P8	Nyiiwa goʼo lutti e yara ndiyam bee mawndu.
