page_text:

P1	The frog and the lizard were once good friends.
P2	They used to live at the beach which was the frog's compound.
P3	The frog lived in water but could always come out to the beach.
P4	The lizard lived on one of the tall dry trees near the beach.
P5	One day,  the lizard requested the frog to pay a visit to him at his home on the tree.
P6	''I cannot climb a tree'' a frog explained to his friend," I will help you to climb" answered the lizard.
P7	They then agreed to a certain date,  the one frog could pay the visit.
P8	The day came and the lizard tied a rope onto the frog's tail.
P9	He started pulling the frog upwards the tree. The frog cried out "am feeling a lot of pain,  the lizard however,  continued pulling the rope upwards the frog's tail got removed and the Frog fell down."
P10	The frog lost his tail to date the frog does not have its tail.
