title:	Omukazi omugezi
author:	Christopher Lutta
artist:	Adonay  Gebru

desc:	Omukyala omugezi ayamba baze okuddamu ebibuuzo ebyamakulu.

page_text:

P1	Olwatuuka olwatuuka, Ngawabaayo ab'emikwano babiri. Omu yali mugezi, ng'omulala musiru.

omukyala w'omusajja omusiru yali mugezi. Omukyala w'omusajja omugezi yali musiru.
P2	Webali banyumya, omusajja omugezi kwekugamba nti, "mukyala wo mugezi, naye atte gwe oli musiru. Mukyala wange naye musiru, era muyinza okwatagana.
P3	Omusajja omusiru kwekubuuza, "Kunze nawe, ani akyasinze obusiru?" era nagamba, "Ndi mugezi nga gwe."
P4	Mu maaso g'omulamuzi, Omusajja omugezi yabuuza, "Munyenye mekka eziri mubwengula? Amakkati g'ensi yonna go gegaliwa? ddamu ebibuuzo byombi, oba tuwanyisiganye abakyala." Omulamuzi yawa omusajja omusiru obudde akomewo n'ebidibwamu.
P5	Ekiro kimu, Omusajja omusiru yali abala emunyenye, ng'atudde kubaati ly'enyumba ye. "Okolaki?" mukyala we kwe kumubuuza.
P6	N'addamu nti, "Mukwano gwange yabuuziza emunyenye mekka eziri mubwengula, na makkati ge ensi yonna wegali. Kati mbala munyenye."
P7	"Tewelarikirira, ngya kugamba ebidibwamu," Mukyala we kwe kumugamba. Yawa baze ekutiya y'obuweke. Ng'amugamba, "Webakubuuza emunyenye eziri mubwengula, bagambe z'ezimu ng'obuweke mu kutiya.
P8	Namuwa omutti ngabwagamba nti, "Webakubuuza amakkati g'ensi yonna wegasangibwa, simba omutti mu ttaka olyoke obagambe ago ng'emakkati. Webawakana, bagambe  bagyepimire.
P9	Omusajja omusiru yatuuka n'amagezi ga mukyala we. Yasooka naddamu nti, "omuwendo gw'emunyenye gwe gumu n'omuwendo gw'empeke mweno ekutiya." Nalyoka nasimba omutti mu ttaka ng'amba nti, "gegano amakkati g'ensi yonna."
P10	Omulamuzi yewunya namala nabuuza, "Ani yakugambye bino?" Omusajja omusiru kwekuddamu nti, "Mukyala wange." Omulamuzi kwekweyongerayo okugamba nti, "Mukyala wo mugezi nnyo, n'olwekyo ayina okuba n'omusajja omugezi. Mujja kuwanyisiganya abakyala." Olwo, omulamuzi natekawo eteggeka yokuwanyisa abakyala babwe.
P11	Omusajja omusiru yagamba mukyala we, "Nasinze, naye omulamuzi yagambye nti oli mugezi nnyo, tosanidde kubeera nange." Mukyala we kwekumuddamu nti, "Tewelarikirira n'ekyo nyina ekyokikolera.
P12	Ekiseera kyatuuka ekyo omulamuzi okuwanyisa abakyala. Omukyala omugezi yatonera omulamuzi entamu y'emmere g'ekirabo. Omulamuzi bweyasanukula entamu, yalaba enkonko enkalirire ng'eriko nebyooya byayo byona! Mukikangabwa, nagamba, "Eeh nkilaba, asanidde abeere ne bawe omusiru!"
