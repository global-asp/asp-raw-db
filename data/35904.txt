desc:	This story is all about the journey of a young football aspirant together with his best friend and supportive father as they venture his dreams and own a spot in the Cameroonian Football Team. It shows that a support system is essential.

page_text:

P1	Anthony and Lleambert are good friends. They both attend the same school. Enjoy the same video game and love the same sport which is football. They are both residents from a remote area in Africa called Cameroon
P2	The town's spoke person announced that there will be an open try out for the official provincial team that will represent Cameroon in the upcoming African Football League that will take place in summer.
P3	After hearing the exciting news, Anthony then forced his dad to hurry back home so that he can prepare for tomorrow's try out. He needs to exercise and put his body in the right condition before attempting to get a spot in the team.
P4	As the sun appears in the wide sky, Anthony got up from his bed and wore his sports attire immediately. He was so excited that he almost forgot to tie his shoelace.
P5	While he was preparing his things and essentials, his supportive father cooked his breakfast. His father wants to guide him towards his dreams. He is very proud of his young man and he confessed that he is growing up so fast!
P6	After everything, he then rushed his way to the table and greeted his father a good morning. His father then prepared his breakfast and told him to eat his grains so that he well have enough energy as he avail to grab a spot in the  team.
P7	As he arrived in the try out venue, the coach then discussed the rules and debrief them on the coveted prize that they will own if they pass the try out level and become official members of the Cameroon Provincial Football Team.
P8	After the debriefing, they started the try outs. Anthony showed his full potentials and excelled among his fellow Cameroonian football aspirants. He was energized as he gazed over and saw his supportive father cheering and rooting for him.
P9	The head coach was delighted because he saw so much potential in this bunch of young football aspirants. He was amazed and had all hopes that they will win this year's African Football League and give honor to their homeland, Cameroon.
P10	Anthony and Lleambert were both accepted in the team. Anthony garnered an extra responsibility because he was tasked to be the team captain. He went directly to his father and gave him a tight hug. A sign of gratitude for being supportive.
