desc:	The story is about Khosi who teaches her friends library rules. She tells them what to expect in the library.

page_text:

P1	A library is a place where books are kept. Libraries have existed for centuries, dating from ancient Egypt. Books were in a different format than what we  have today.@@Regardless of the type of book, all books need to kept the same way in a library.@@If you love reading books, then you should learn how to take care of books in the library.
P2	Library Rules@@Like the classroom, the library has a set of rules. Rules are meant to help us keep the books safe so that our young brothers and sisters can also use them.@@Rules also help us keep our library clean  and it teaches us to be responsible learners.
P3	As we experience the world through books, we should know that:@@1. A book must not be held with wet hands. If you open and read a book with wet hands, you will tear the book.@@2. Books must be kept away from food. If you eat your food when reading you will spill your food on your book.@@3.  Books must not be tossed when done reading.  Throwing around books in the library may damage the spine of the book.@@4.  Make sure to return the book when it is due.
P4	Reading will open up your world. @@You can learn about different countries and their culture through books.@@You can also use books to learn a new language. You can learn a new skill by reading books explaining.@@So if you want to learn a new language, check in the library. You can also learn new words in reading books.@@Make sure to get a book that is of your age. If you can read at least three to five words in a sentence then you can take that book home to finish it off.
P5	Every library should have a librarian. A librarian is a professional man or woman who is skilled at managing the library.@@The librarian can conduct a library tour to new library members. Next time you visit the library, ask the librarian some questions about the library.@@The librarian is very helpful in the library, they can conduct library activities like story-telling sessions.@@Librarians can show you were to find books in the library.
