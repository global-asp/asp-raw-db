desc:	Umukobwa muto baramutumye kwa segukuru munzira agyenda abonana nibikoko mukibira.

page_text:

P1	Umukobwa muto yyari atumye naba byeyi be kwa segukuru we yaturaga inyuma ikibira aragyenda anezerewe nibyishimo.
P2	Agyeze hafi yikibira abonana nihene isaze iramubwira  ngo ugyende neeza mukibira ko hari ibinyamaswa byinshi  mukibira.
P3	Agyeje mohagati yikibira, asanga ibinyamaswa byinshi hapfi yumuhanda ariruka  atera induru.
P4	Agyeje hanze yikibira arasanga ibinyonyi byinshi bisakuza byose. Umukobwa muto ariruka nawe atera induru asakuze, Segukuru aramwunva.
P5	Segukuru aravuga nomugore we ngo uwo nimuzukuru wangye. Aragyenda mangu kumureba asanga arushe.
P6	Bagyera murugobarataha munzu, arasuhuza abandi baana ararya. Barangjije kurya aravuga kubyamubayeho aza murugyendo rwe.
P7	Arangije kuba bwira nabo bamuha ijambo ngo atazongyera kukya  mukibira bamumwira ko atazongyera gukya mukibira wenyine  arakina nabandi baana  barishima.
P8	Nago yongyeye  gukya mukibira wenyine.
