title:	Tshimange na Mmbwa: Tshimange u ṱaḓa
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37290.png
desc:	Mmbwa u vhona muthu wa muvhala wa ṱaḓa! Mmbwa u a vhudzisa uri

page_text:

P1	Hoyu ndi Mmbwa.
P2	Mmbwa u nḓuni yawe. Mmbwa o dzula nḓuni yawe.@@Muṅwe muthu a khokhonya munangoni.
P3	Mmbwa a vula munango. @@Mmbwa a vhona muthu wa muvhala wa ṱaḓa!
P4	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "U nnyi?"@@Ndi Tshimange. Tshimange wa muvhala wa ṱaḓa!
P5	Ṱhoho ya Tshimange i na muvhala wa ṱaḓa. @@Mavhudzi a Tshimange ndi a ṱaḓa.
P6	Zwanḓa zwa Tshimange ndi zwa ṱaḓa.
P7	Mulomo wa Tshimange ndi wa ṱaḓa. Ningo ya Tshimange ndi ya ṱaḓa@@Nḓevhe dza Tshimange ndi dza ṱaḓa. Maṱo a Tshimange ndi a ṱaḓa.
P8	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Ndi ngani u ṱaḓa?"@@Tshimange a ri, "A thi ḓivhi."
P9	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "O vha u ngafhi?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Ndo vha ndi tshikoloni."
P10	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Tshikoloni tshifhio?"@@Tshimange tsha ri, "Ndo vha ndi tshikoloni tsha muḓanani."
P11	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "O vha o ya lini tshikoloni?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Ndo vha ndi tshikoloni nga matsheloni."
P12	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "O vha a tshi khou ita mini tshikoloni?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Ndo vha ndo eḓela."
P13	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "O vha o eḓela tshikoloni?! O vha o eḓela ngafhi?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Ndo vha ndo eḓela nga murahu ha tshikolo."
P14	Mmbwa a ḽa marambo a ṱhoho, Mmbwa a ṅwala fhasi. A ṅwala zwiṅwe zwithu kha bammbiri.@@Nnyi: Tshimange@Ngafhi: murahu ha tshikolo@Tshikolo tshifhio: tshikolo tshi re muḓanani @Lini: nga matsheloni @Mini: o eḓela @Ndi ngani: ?
P15	Tshimange a ri, "Ndo vha ndi si ndoṱhe tshikoloni. Ho vha hu na muṅwe muthu.
P16	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Ndi nnyi we a vha e tshikoloni?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Nḓou o vha e tshikoloni."
P17	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou ita mini?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou pennda."
P18	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou pennda mini?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou pennda tshikolo."
P19	Mmbwa a vhudzisa, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou pennda tshikolo nga mini?"@@Tshimange a ri, "Nḓou o vha a tshi khou pennda tshikolo nga buratsho khulwane..."
P20	Tshimange a fhumula a si ambe tshithu. Tshimange a humbula.@@A ri, "Zwino ndi a zwi ḓivha uri ndi ngani ndi ṱaḓa!"
P21	Mmbwa na Tshimange vha ya tshikoloni.@@Nḓou u khou pennda tshikolo. @@Nḓou u khou pennda tshikolonga muvhala wa ṱaḓa.
P22	Nḓou o pennda zwithu zwoṱhe. @@Nḓou ha  ngo pennda tshikolo fhedzi, fhedzi o pennda na Tshimange!
P23	Ṱa-ṱa Tshimange tsha ṱaḓa.@@Ṱa-ṱa Mmbwa.@@Ṱa-ṱa Nḓou.
