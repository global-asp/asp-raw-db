title:	Ombande Daisy
author:	Auhe  Tjaveondja
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	Otjiherero
level:	First paragraphs

desc:	Daisy akara koresovate hkuna okahunguriva

page_text:

P1	Ozombura ozengi ndakapita po kahambo okatiti mene orezovate.
P2	Pari noka hunguriva kena Daisy.
P3	Daisy atja, "Tjimbarire omune mevanga okutuka mejuru."
P4	Ozohunguriva azehe aze mujoro.
P5	"Ove kori nawa," azetja ku Daisy. "Katuna kundja naove rukuao."
P6	Ina Daisy emuraere kutja, "Ozohunguriva zetuka imba na mbene uri kaze tuka mejuru."
P7	Daisy kaumbu ohama. Ejuva arihe ekerirora. Pozija kazako pehi. Aroro ovivava kutja vimujere tjinene.
P8	Tjatuka erimunu mouripura ue kutja, urimejuru matara ozohunguriva indjo zarue pehi. Nokuripura kutja ovipuka imbijo vijarue mavitja, "Ohunguriva maituka tjiri."
P9	Daisy ka kara nejuva ndehena kuka tuka. Aroro ejuva arihe tjinene.
P10	Aje, ajeruruka potjija akotoka pehi rukuao.
P11	Daisy atja, "Ami hene katuka ozohunguriva indo zarue zesora."
P12	Ina Daisy emuraere kutja, "Ove kori tjimuna ozohunguriva zarue. Ozo kazevanga okutuka posija ove uvanga."
P13	Ejuva indo ekongorerua ohunguriva ja tuka rukuao. Opojakonjisa ovivava tjine-ne-ne. Ajere ovivava ajarura pehi nao, nao, nao.
P14	BOOOOO!
P15	Ozohunguriva indo zarue aze joro. "Ha ha ha kana tuekuraere po? Ozohunguriva kazetuka."
P16	Ejuva indo ekongorerua Daisy uarombo komba otjivongo tjozohunguriva. Atuka rukao.
P17	Otjikando itji kana uaua  ajere ovivava vije pehi nokombanda.
P18	Daisy uari mejuru. Ombepo tjijari kehi je aimujere. Atuka kombanda nokombanda tjinene. @@Ozohunguriva indo zarue aze tara nokutja, "Tara Daisy matuka nu  kaze kambura."
P19	Ozohunguriva indo zarue azevanga okukara tjimuna Daisy.
P20	Avetja, "Daisy ove omuhimise ove ombande!"
