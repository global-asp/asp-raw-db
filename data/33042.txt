desc:	A lovely story about a Sipho and his flower garden.

page_text:

P1	Sipho woke up in the morning and went to the garden to plant the flowers
P2	After he planted two flowers it started to rain.
P3	The water was all over the garden
P4	When the sun rose in the morning two flowers grew.
P5	They blossomed with happiness and beauty.
