page_text:

P1	   Sɑrɑ yɛɛ kúninɔi mɑ.@   @     Kúninɔi kpɛɛ@  kumɑ́ɑlɛ.@  Kun nkói ritimɛ we.   
P2	Pinɔ́si@  ɑpi kɛyɑ́ɑ kɛnyinɛ nkói sĩ. Ucɔ un kucɑ́ri nɛ ɑ́minɑŋ topiri. Ucɔ un icɑ̃́@  isɛ múílɛ̃. Ucɔ ipoi isɛ. Ucɔ ipoi itɛ́.@  Pinɔ́si@  pɛ̃ ɑpi Sɑrɑ pisɛ rɛ: Íye kɑri yɛ́ wɑ tɔkɛ́ nɛ nkói ritɛ́ŋ́?@  Sɑrɑ@  uu rɛ: Ani kunɛ́ninɔi kɛ́mɛɛ lommɛ!
P3	Pitisi@  pɔ́ɔ́ weri. Ucɔ un íkpíntomɛ́ topiri. Ucɔ nɛ iwenisɑ̃. Ucɔ pɔ̃́ un@  kupúusirɛ́ sɑɑki topiri.
P4	Utisi@  ucɔ un ɑsɑɑki ɑtɛ́ topiri. Pɛ̃ pɔ́ɔ́ kɔ Sɑrɑ pisɛ rɛ: Íye kɑri yɛ́ wɑ tɔkɛ́@  nɛ nkói ritɛ́ŋ́?@  Sɑrɑ@  uu kɔ rɛ: Ani kunɛ́ninɔi kɛ́mɛɛ lommɛ!
P5	Isɛɛ@  pɔ́ɔ́ kɔ nkói ritimɛ weri. Ii Sɑrɑ pisɛ rɛ: Íye kɑri yɛ́ wɑ tɔkɛ́ nɛ nkói@  ritɛ́ŋ́?@  Sɑrɑ@  uu kɔ rɛ: Ani kunɛ́ninɔi kɛ́mɛɛ lommɛ!
P6	              Isɛɛ ii kúninɔi@  kɛ́mɛɛ lompɔ.@  Kɛpɔpi ɑkɛ lompɔ.@   @    Kɛmɑmpi ɑkɛ lompɔ.@   @  Kɛwɑmɛ ɑkɛ lompɔ.@     @   Kɛŋmúmpi ɑkɛ lompɔ.@   Tikulɛ ɑri lompɔ.    .@  Inɑ́mɛ ii lompɔ.     .
P7	Lɛ̃@  mɛmɑ́ɑ́ kɛ ísu pɔ́ɔ́ kɔ nkói-i kɑ. Ii pisɛ rɛ: Íye kɑm yɛ́ wɑ kɛ́ nɛ nkói@  ritɛ́ŋ́?@  Sɑrɑ@  uu rɛ: A kunɛ́ninɔi kɛ́mɛɛ lompɔ!@  Ísu@  ii kúninɔi kɛ́mɛɛ lompɔ, kúninɔi ɑku mí.
P8	 
P9	Kúninɔi ɑ́ku piyɛ. Kɛtonɛ ɑ́kɛ we.
P10	Sɑrɑ@  uu rɛ: Ani nyɛrɛ kɛ́ nɛ pinɔ́si mɛfoí ritɛ́ŋ́!
P11	Nɛn@  m mɑsí, ɑm nɛ pitisi tɛ́ŋ́.
P12	Sɑrɑ@  uu rɛ: Am pɛɛ mɛtɔ́rɔɔ nɛ isɛɛ tɛ́ŋ́.
P13	Ísu ii rɛ: Nɛ̃́ nɛ́ ɑ́nɑ sɔ́nɛ!
