page_text:

P1	A hi tshama na Kokwana na mhani.
P2	Vazala wa mina, Christina, Thandi, Junior na hahani a hi tshama swin'we.
P3	Kokwana a ta hi dyondzisa mitlangu.@@A ku ri nkarhi wa khale.
P4	Kutani, Junior u yile eMalawi, na Kokwana u yile eMalawi.
P5	Na hahani na Christina va yile eMalawi.
P6	Kutani na Thandi u yile eMalawi.
P7	A swi vava swinene.@@A ku ri hava loyi a tshama na hina.
P8	Kambe a swi bihangi hi ndlela yaleyo, hikuva manana wa mina wa ha ri na mina.
