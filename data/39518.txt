page_text:

P1	Rabbit asks chicken, "Can we have a race? "
P2	Chicken asks dog, "will you join the race? "
P3	The race begins.
P4	Dog asks the fox, "Will you join the race?"
P5	The race continues...
P6	Fox asks the donkey, "Will you join the race?"
P7	The race continues...
P8	All the animals asked the cow, "Will you join the race?" The cow said, "No!" And the race ended.
