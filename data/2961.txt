page_text:

P1	Sipho goes to town with his sister.
P2	Mosubati omwabo akaba gakorigereria chianga ase chituka. Osipho akarosa sana.
P3	Osipho akarora egento gete ekemo keria anchete.
P4	Ebikoroto mbire biarenge kooniwa.
P5	Akabibeka kobitema bionsi pi.
P6	Mosubati omwabo akamogorera ebikoroto bi'ogaka omopira.
P7	Inaki bare nobwango iga kogenda nka?
P8	Ase ebahati engiya agaika egetii ase chinsa chingiya! Nigo abegete ebikoroto biria ebiyia agoreretwe.
