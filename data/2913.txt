title:	Chibarwa
author:	Joseph Saka
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lubukusu
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2913.png
desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school.  Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Lichonekha nanyolile chibarwa chingali.
P2	Olwabarasa omusilihi wase kaandikile omwalimu wase ebarwa.
P3	Wamakina alikho aluala. Harama engo.
P4	Mu mulimo kibili nanyolile ebarwa khukhwama mukilasi yase.
P5	Sosa bwangu Wamakina!
P6	Lwakhataru nanyolile ebarwa khukhwama wo musale wange wo kumwoyo.
P7	Nakhuchorera ewe sifwanani sino. Busime khukhwama khu Wandanje.
P8	Lwokhune nanyolile ebwarwa khukhwama khu mayi wase.
