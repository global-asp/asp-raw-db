title:	Alake nakuunda abagyenyi
author:	MBABAZI ELIZABETH
artist:	Rasheedat Sadiq
lang:	Runyankore

desc:	Alake nomwishikyi ayanguhi kaandi nakuunda abagyenyi kaandi namaanya kubashemeza.

page_text:

P1	kodi,kodi,hariyo oruyombo aha rwigi
P2	Alake ashemererwa. ''noha ariyo?''ayomba.''maama....twine omugyenyi!''
P3	'kale,''maama weye agarukamu.''ogwe nabaasa kuba ari muka Hassan nakana ke. Bigurire orwiji.''
P4	Alake ayigura orwiji nana akamwenyo.
''nakwakyira madame,bambi akateebe.mama wangye nakuuba akwine omukakye.''
Muka Hassan areeba Alake arikuzana nakana akato
P5	Bambe toora amaizi. Nimbaasa kukutungira ekiintu ekiindi? habuza Alake.
"Ndigye neekyi Alake, Yebare." yagarukamu muka Hassan.
P6	''eizooba rirungi muka Hassan,''mama Alake yagamba arikwija omu muryango.''nsasira kakurinza''
''Tikiine buzibu munwaniwangye,Alake yatushemeza,''
P7	mama wa Alake  yamumwenyera.
''yego,''yagarukamu mpora.''Alake nimurungi aha kureberera kaandi nimwesimisa.''
