desc:	Indzaba ngengadze.

page_text:

P1	Ingadze yakitsi inetitselo letihle.
P2	Titselo tasengadzeni yamake tinhle.
P3	Mine nabhutiwami sicosha tinkhomo,  ngoba tidla engadzeni.
P4	Bokati bayatsandza kulala celeni kwetimbali letibuya engadzeni.
P5	Bomake batfole litsanga lelikhulu engadze.
P6	Sisi wami udlala ngemdoli wakhe celeni kwengadze.
P7	Make utsengisa titselo tasengadzeni emaketse.
