title:	Neymar's U Dalela Africa

desc:	Neymar u dalela mashango o fhambananaho a Africa a tshi sumbedza zwikili zwo fhambananaho zwa bola.

page_text:

P1	Musi Neymar atshi swika Africa,  o swikela capital city ya Nigeria. Vhathu vha Nigeria vha mu humbela uri avha sumbedze zwitaila zwa u dirivhula bola.
P2	U bva Nigeria ombo di fhirela Ghana u vha sumbedza u raha bola a tshi kho shumisa mulendzhe wa nga ngomu. Ezwi zwa takadza nga maanda vhathu vha Ghana.
P3	Musi a tshe Ghana,  Neyma ombo di rabiwa Africa Tshipembe uri a vha sumbedze u imisa bola a tshi khou shumisa phanda ha muledzhe.
P4	U bva Africa Tshipembe a mbo di fhirela Chad. Musi e Chad ombo di vha sumbedza u imisa bola a tshi khou shumisa matungo a mulendzhe.
P5	Musi Neymar a tshi swika DR Congo,  o mangadza shango nga tshikili tshau imisa bola a tshi khou shumisa tshirumbi.
P6	Vhathu vha Uganda vho vhonaho Neymar musi e Congo vho mbo di muramba uri avha sumbedze tshikili tsha u thivha bola mudavhini.
P7	Mafhungo aya ombo di swika hothe East-Africa. Vhathu vha Kenya vha tanganedza Neyma uri a vha sumbedze u thivhela bola mapalani.
P8	Kha vhege yeneyo nthihi,  vhathu vha Tanzania vha vhidza Neymar uri a vha sumbedze tshikili tsha u fhirisa bola nga thoho.
P9	Nga murahu a fhirela Rwanda u vha sumbedza tshikili tsha u phasa bola a tshi khou shumisa nga nnda ha muledzhe.
P10	U bva Rwanda Neymar o mbo di fhedzisa lwendo lwawe lwa Africa South-Sudan,  he a sumbedzisa tshikili tsha u thivhela bola uri isi kore mapalani.
