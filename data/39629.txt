page_text:

P1	Josefa abelikhwa futsi ayintsandvokati kuyise Jakobe.
P2	Bekangutfunjana wemadvodzana lali-12 atondzwa bomnakabo
P3	Uyise wamtfungela libhantji lemibalabala kubonisa lutsandvo lwakhe.
P4	Njalo abephupha emaphupho abacocele, base bamtondza kakhulu bomnakabo
P5	Benta lisu babheca libhantji lakhe ngengati yesilwane ,yena bamtsengisa kubantfu betive batsi ufile kuyise
P6	Kwefika sikhatsi sendlala eveni lakubo, babe wabo Jakobe wabatfuma kuyotsenga kudla eGibhithe.
P7	Befika eGibhithe Josefa abeyindvuna wababona kodvwa bomnakabo bebangamboni.
P8	Josefa wababonisa kutsi Nguye, wabacolela ngako konkhe lokubi labamente kona.
