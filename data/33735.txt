page_text:

P1	We used to stay with my Grandma and mom.
P2	My cousins, Christina, Thandi, Junior and Aunty lived with us.
P3	My Grandma would teach us games. @@It was a good time.
P4	Then, Junior went to Malawi, and Grandma went to Malawi.
P5	And Aunty and Christina went to Malawi.
P6	And then Thandi went to Malawi.
P7	It was really sad.@@No one would stay with us.
P8	But it is not so bad, because my mother is still here with me.
