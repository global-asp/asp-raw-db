desc:	Lendzaba ikhuluma nge Libhubesi ne Ligundvwane.Libhubesi belingafuni kubangumngani we Ligundvwane kodvwa ekupheleni kwendzaba Libhubesi labona kubaluleka kwekuba ngumngani we Libhubesi.

page_text:

P1	Libhubesi belitihlalela lodvwa ehlatsini. Kwatsi ngalelinye lilanga libhubesi belitilalele ehlatsini,  leva kutsi dzimu ligundvwane etikwalo.
P2	Ligundvwane lahlala etikwe libhubesi langanyakati ngobe belisaba libhubesi.
P3	Libhubesi latsi nalibona le ligundvwane lajabula kutsi selitawutfola sidlo sakusihlwa.
P4	Ligundvwane latincengela kutsi lingadliwa,  latjela Libhubesi kutsi nalo ngalelinye lilanga litawulisita.
P5	Libhubesi lamangala latsi "hawu wena umncane nakangaka ungasita mina?"  .
P6	Libhubesi labese litjela ligundvwane kutsi alihambe ngoba sigcino lona litawulidla liphila.
P7	Kutsite ngalelinye lilanga libhubesi liyotingela ehlatsini ladzibana nendvodza lehamba itingela tilwane,   iphetse sikhali lesikhulu.
P8	Lendvodza yalijaka ngematubane lelibhubesi yalifaka enethini lenkhulu yekutingela tilwane.
P9	Libhubesi lahlala sikhatsi lesidze enetheni lingasitwa ngumuntfu.  ladzimate lalamba nekulamba. Lakhumbula lamemeta ligundvwane kutsi litolisita.
P10	Lafika Ligundvwane ladzabula lenethi ngematinyo kwadzimate kwavela sikhala lesikhulu. Libhubesi lakhona kuphuma.
P11	Libhubesi lajabula kakhulu labese litjela ligundvwane kutsi licela kuba ngumngani walo ngoba limsite kakhulu.
