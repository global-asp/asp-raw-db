desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	One day an elephant was going to drink water.
P2	There were two giraffes who were also going to drink water.
P3	There were three Buffaloes and four birds. They were all going to drink water.
P4	Five impalas and six warthogs were also going to drink water.
P5	Seven frogs and eight fishes were in the water.
P6	Nine Zebras were going to drink water.
P7	One lion was going to drink water.
P8	Off all the animals, who is the toughest of them all and will be the first one to drink water?
