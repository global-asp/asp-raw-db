author:	Ingrid Schechter
artist:	Magriet Brink

desc:	Chicken and Millipede have a very competitive friendship. One day Chicken becomes so angry for losing that she does something very silly.

page_text:

P1	Chicken and Millipede were friends.
P2	They went to play football.
P3	Chicken was the goal keeper.
P4	Millipede scored three goals!
P5	Chicken was annoyed.
P6	Chicken swallowed Millipede.
P7	Then, Chicken met with Millipede's Mother.
P8	"Help Mother! Chicken swallowed me!" cried Millipede from inside Chicken.
P9	Millipede tasted bad. @@Chicken felt sick.
P10	Chicken started coughing.
P11	Chicken coughed out Millipede.
P12	Chicken and Millipede are now enemies.
