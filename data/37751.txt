desc:	This story introduces the colours of the rainbow in French and things you might see in everyday life that share these colours. Try and be a word detective and work out the French words in the story.

page_text:

P1	Un arc-en-ciel has seven colours.
P2	The first colour is rouge.
P3	Kewyu is wearing une chemise rouge.
P4	The second couleur is orange.
P5	This is une orange. What else can you see that is le couleur orange?
P6	The third colour is jaune.
P7	Le jaune of an egg is jaune. What else can you see that is jaune?
P8	The fourth colour is vert.
P9	Our jardin de bananes is vert.
P10	The fifth colour is bleu.
P11	The ciel is bleu.
P12	The sixième couleur is indigo.
P13	These berries are le couleur indigo.
P14	The seventh couleur est violet.
P15	Violet is my couleur préférée! What is your couleur préférée?
P16	Do you know the seven colours of the rainbow in French?
