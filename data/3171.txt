title:	Ngi Na Mũtwe Mũnene
author:	Anna Kula
artist:	Joshua Waswa
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/3171.png
desc:	Mr. Fly and Mr. Big head were two great friends. Then one day they argued about who could swim across the river.  They decided to go out and swim to prove who was right.  Mr. Big Head failed to swim. Mr. Fly laughed so much that his mouth became wider and wider…

page_text:

P1	Ũũ ni Ngi.
P2	Ũũ nĩ mũtwe mũnene.
P3	Ngi and Mũtwe mũnene maĩ ndũũ. Mũthenya ũmwe nĩmaendie kũtembea.
P4	Nĩmavikie ngũkanĩ ya ũsĩ na manzĩĩa kũkananĩsy.
P5	"Ndwamba kũkĩla ũsĩ ũũ!" "Nonĩkĩle. Niwe ũtakĩla!"
P6	Ngi nĩwaisye,  "we twĩla!" "Aiee,  we twĩla mbee," Mũtwe mũnene nĩwaisye. Kwou Ngi nĩwolũkile akĩla ũsĩ ũsu.
P7	Mũtwe mũnene nĩwatwĩlie ũsĩnĩ. Ĩndĩ nũndũ mũtwe wake waĩ mũnene ndaalela; nĩwanzĩĩe kw'indanw'a nĩ kĩw'ũ.
P8	Ngi nĩwathekie,  "he, hee!" Ĩndĩ athekie mũno mũvaka kanyw'a wake waatũka miondo ĩlĩ.
