desc:	Lendaba imyelana nabangani ababili ababethembene kodwa ngelinye ilanga omunye wathi abavakashele abanye abangani. Wazama ukuchaza ngesimo sempilo yakhe omunye kodwa bagcina bevelelwe ingozi.

page_text:

P1	UMnumzane Sosipani noMnumzane Bhodwe babengabangani abakhulu. Babehlezi ngokujabula iminyaka eminingi.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga uMnumzane Sosipani watshela uMnumzane Bhodwe ukuthi ahambe avakashele umngane wabo uMnumzane Puleti.
P3	Mnumzane Bhodwe angikwazi ukuhamba,  anginamandla,  uma ngingahamba ngingafa.
P4	UMnumzane Sosipani wambonisa uMnumzane Bhodwe,  ekugcineni wavuma ukuhamba.
P5	Endleleni uMnumzane Sosipani washibilika wawela phezu kwaMnumzane Bhodwe. UMnumzane Bhodwe wephuka waba izingcezu.
