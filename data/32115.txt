desc:	Aka gakuru karatubwira ubwenge mu micungire yigihe.

page_text:

P1	Igihe ni umurima.
P2	Muri uyu murima w'igihe, ubonamo utundi turima twinshi.
P3	Umurima ni Umunsi.@@Umunsi ni amasegonda 86.400.
P4	Ugomba gutangira igihe ukagikoresha neza. Iga ibindi bintu bishya.
P5	Rya.@Kina.@Ruhuka.@Rota.@Tekereza.
P6	Tera igiti, ishyamba rizakura. Kemura ikibazo.
P7	Niba ubonye amafaranga, zigama amwe, ukoreshe andi, asigaye uyafashishe abatishoboye.
P8	Niba bikunaniye, ntucike intege, ongera utangire. Uzabishobora uko iminsi ihita indi ikaza.
P9	Kuwa Mbere no Kuwa Kabiri. Kuwa Gatatu no Kuwa Kane. Kuwa Gatanu no Kuwa Gatandatu, ndetse no Kucyumweru.
P10	Hari inshuti zidasanzwe. Ishimire ubucuti.
P11	Niba wumvise urushinge rw'isaha, uge w'ibuka ibyo nakubwiye.
P12	Igihe ni Umurima mwiza, ni  Impano idasanzwe. Gikoreshe neza.
