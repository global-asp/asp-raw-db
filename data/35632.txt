P1	Nauman has a sister and a brother.@@ She loves them very much.
P2	She attends school and reads her books.
P3	"This is my friend, her name is Raudat," Nauman says.
P4	Nauman says to her friend, ''this is my village."
P5	"This is my home, you are welcome," she adds.
P6	She is always happy to see the people from her village.
P7	She wants to be a medical pilot so that she can visit many countries, travel around the world and treat people.
