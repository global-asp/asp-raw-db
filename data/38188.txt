desc:	Imprisoned by reason and conscience. How do we save one another? This is the riddle.

page_text:

P1	People running in speed often need...
P2	Need what? Do you know what that means? .
P3	It means peace of mind, body and soul.
P4	Some time soon, I will be home. God knows when.
P5	My hero is in prison. That snake has held him prisoner.
P6	He needs help. Let's go and offer some help. The snake must not win.
