page_text:

P1	Leli liBhubezi. Ikosi yehlathi ebhodla ithi grrr!
P2	Le yiNdlovu. Inomzimba omkhulu begodu ithanda ukudla inthelo.
P3	Le yiNdlulamithi. Inentamo ede khulu ukuze idle amakari emthini.
P4	Le yIngwe. Ukatsu wehlathini,  odla inyama.
P5	Le yiNgwenya. Inamazinyo aqinileko ukuze idle inyama. Ilila ithi RRRRR. RRRRR!
P6	Le yona Ifene. Ithanda ukuhlala emthini.
P7	Lesi sona Mkhombe. Silwane esikhulu esine phondo ehloko.
