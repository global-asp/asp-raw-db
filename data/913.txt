desc:	Bunty likes to play but she does not like soap and refuses to clean up. One night she has a dream about germs. Will her dream change her mind about getting clean?

page_text:

P1	Bunty loves to play with butterflies.
P2	 And with birds.
P3	She loves to play with paper boats.
P4	She also likes to make sand castles.
P5	When Bunty goes back home,  her mother asks her to clean up. She refuses.
P6	"I hate soap!" she screams.
P7	One night,  she has a dream. The germs are all around her castle,  and are attacking her.
P8	Bunty is chased by the germs. She runs for her life and screams,  "Help. Help!"
P9	Suddenly,  Soap King Bubbly appears. "Bunty,  don't be afraid," he says. "Go attack the germs!" the Soap King orders his army of bubbles.
P10	The army of bubbles chases the germs away.
P11	These days,  Bunty likes to use soap,  and she brushes and scrubs herself clean.
