desc:	Emboozi eno egemagana ku mughaala eyaiba esente asobole okugula switi.

page_text:

P1	Ghaliyo omughala nga bamweta Nabirye.
P2	Nabirye yali ayenda inho switi aye nga azira
  waire esente edhigula.
P3	Yaiba sente yagula switi. Y’ekweka asobole okumunuuna.
P4	Nabirye yaawulira muganda we ng’alikubuuza.
  “Naani eyatoire esente dhe twabaire tutereike?”
P5	Nabirye yaatya mugandawe omuniivu. Yalumuka
  wo.
P6	Yayagana ogusolo ogubi. Gw’akoba, “nja
  kukulya.”
P7	Nabirye yatya inho. Yaatumira mu lyato alumuke
  ku gusolo ogubi.
P8	Maama yamubuuza “Lwaki waibye?” Nabirye
  yaakoba, “Nsonigha.”
P9	Nabirye ya soniyibwa. Awooni yaagya yaazaana
  ni mikwano gye.
