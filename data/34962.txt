title:	Kumvetsera thupi langa

desc:	Kodi munayamba mwamvetsera zomwe thupi lanu limakuuzani?

page_text:

P1	Lero sindipita ku sukulu.@@Ndi tsiku la tchuthi!
P2	Lero sindiwonera kanema.@@Kulibe magetsi.
P3	Ndiye ndizitani?@@Lero ndi mvetsera thupi langa!
P4	Choyamba, ndikhale chete.@@Kuti ndimvetsere thupi langa.
P5	Eyaa, tsopano ndikutha kumvera kupuma kwanga.@@Ndikupumira mkati ndi kunja, mkati ndi kunja.
P6	Ndingathe kupuma mwaphokoso. Fweeee!@@Komanso motsitsa. Shiiiiii!
P7	Tsopano ndikumva kugunda kwa mtima wanga!@@Gugu! Gugu! Gugu!
P8	Kodi ndingauchititse mtima wanga kugunda mofulumira kapena mokweza?@@Eya, pojowa kwa maulendo makumi awiri.
P9	Tsopano onani, mtima wanga ukugunda mofulumira koposa.
P10	Ndikayika zala zanga pothera mkono wanga, ndikumva kugunda kwa mtima.
P11	Ndikudzimva ndikamaseka. @@Hahahaa hahahaa, hahahaa!
P12	Ndikudzimva ndikamalira.@@Mayooo, Mayooo!
P13	Ndikudzimva ndikamaomba m'manja.@@Phwaaa, phwaaa, phwaaa.
P14	Ndikumva mmimba mwanga mukamalira.@@Gugululu, gugululu, gugululu.
P15	Mimba yanga ikunena kuti, "Ndidyetse!"
P16	Mphuno yanga ikununkhiza kuphikidwa kwa makeke m'khitchini la amayi anga.@@Tsopano ndikufuna ndimve zibwano zanga zikutafuna makekewo.
