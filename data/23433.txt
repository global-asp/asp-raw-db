desc:	Agupi azini adikoru tu ri te nyaka ta mba kilili kuye

page_text:

P1	Saa  azini alu agupi azini rube Joel a'aa Aya village.
P2	Agu nde e'gani eyo nyakata mbaza madria ewu dria  si ku,e'ri nyaka ezaza.
P3	Afazi iku pi eroa ni yo
P4	Kaka pati amvuria nyuki woro azini kweeki te.
P5	Akuku  du  ero  e'dini nyakata mbazuri madri te.
P6	Anziki ei ari nyaka were ikupi sufuria di madria
P7	Ayia laa i  vaa kpampkala owuria nyaka anzini  dimu nya  saa vuleri si ri madria.
P8	Ata ri jo  ma barazaa  eyo ega ewu  dria ri madria.
