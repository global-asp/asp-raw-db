desc:	Learn to know a set of primary colors consists of colorants or coloured lights that can be mixed in varying amounts to produce a gamut of colors.

page_text:

P1	Apple and tomatoes are red.
P2	Red is beautiful.
P3	Bananas and the sun are yellow.
P4	Yellow is bright and cheerful.
P5	The sky and the ocean are blue.
P6	Blue is cool and calm.
P7	Mix red and yellow, you get orange.
P8	Mix blue and yellow, you get green.
P9	Mix red and blue, you get purple.
P10	Red, yellow and blue are primary colours. Now you try mixing the primary colours, and see what colours you can make.
