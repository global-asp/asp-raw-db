title:	Nayega eisomo

desc:	Omughala omuto ono eyayenda inho okutegera dhaadha we bwayengeka amenvu.

page_text:

P1	Dhaadha yanhenda inho. Yankobera ebyama bingi by'atakobera waire wundi.
P2	Dhaadha yakoba," ebyeirogo."
P3	Naayenda okwetegereza.  Aye dhaadha yantuma okukola ebintu ebindhi.
P4	"Baligha bano?" "Bali bwamu kifo ky'eirogo lyange, "dhaadha neeyakoba.
P5	Dhaadha yantuma mu kisenge kye. Na wuuniza amenvu gebari kwengeka.
P6	Naazuula ekifo kya dhaadha eky'eirogo.
P7	Naalyaku irala. Lyali ne lyenvu erikasinze yo kuwooma ku gendyangaku.
P8	Naatwala eminwe ena. Nagyikweka ghansi ghe kiteeteeyi kyange.
P9	Ku lunaku lwa akatale, dhaadha yaatwala eby'okulya okutana.
P10	bwe yandheta, naategera lwaki yandheta. 
Tyairamu kwiba.
