title:	Imbali engaboni
author:	Thulisile Dhlamini
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi
lang:	isiZulu

desc:	Indaba yentombazanyana eyimpumputhe ethola injabulo ngokuzihambela engadini.

page_text:

P1	UChat wazalwa eyimpumputhe.@@Ubengekaze ababone abazali bakhe, abafowabo kanye nodadewabo ngamehlo. Ubesazi ubuso babo uma ebathinta.
P2	Ngolunye usuku, uChat wayezihambela engadini.@@Wayelithanda iphunga lezimbali. Wayethanda nokuthinta amagcembe ezimbali.
P3	UChat wayefisa ukuzibona izimbali noma kukanye nje. "Zinhle kakhulu," ezisholo nje.
P4	UChat wayezihambela engadini nsuku zonke. Wayeyazi indlela yengadi. @@Namuhla uphakamisela ikhanda  lakhe esibhakabhakeni.
P5	Esithubeni nje, wezwa ukuduma kwezulu kanye nombani.@@"Angisheshe ngibuyele ekhaya," washo ezicabangela.
P6	Yaqala imvula yana.@@UChat washelela, walahla ukuzimelela wawa.@@Washaya ngekhanda etsheni.
P7	Wathi evuka kwakugcwele abantu phambi kwakhe. "Kwenzakaleni?" ebuza.
P8	"Uwile washaya ngekhanda phansi," kusho ubaba wakhe.@@"Ngiyabonga Nkosi uphilile," kusho umama wakhe.
P9	"Mama, baba?" kubuza uChat. @@Wabiza amagama odadewabo kanye nabafowabo. "Ngicela ningitholele imbali."@@Bonke babemangele.
P10	Udadewabo wabuya nayo imbali. UChat wayibamba ngothando. "Yaze yayinhle," esisho.
P11	Umndeni wakhe wabukana wodwa.@@"Chat, uyayibona lembali?" kubuza umama wakhe.
P12	UChat wadlala ngayo imbali esandleni sakhe. @@"Nibahle nonke, nifana nalembali," washo emamatheka.
