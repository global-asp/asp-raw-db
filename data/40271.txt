desc:	Mwewa gets stuck in an orange tree trying to steal oranges

page_text:

P1	This is Mwewa @She is a girl @She is in grade three and lives with her grandmother
P2	Mwewa wants to be a pilot when she grows up.@Mwewa studies hard every day at school @Mwewa is a good girl
P3	This is Mwewa and her friend Chongo.@They are walking home from school.@Look, says Chongo. There is an orange tree, let us get some oranges and eat. Mr.Musenga will not know.@Mwewa knows it is bad to steal but she agrees.
P4	Mwewa is climbing up the tree
P5	Chongo calls Mr.Musenge and his wife to help her.@They ask her to slowly climb down the tree.@Do not be afraid, they say.
P6	Mwewa cannot get down the tree she is stuck.@And Chongo cannot help her.
P7	Mwewa finally climbs down the tree.@She is sad@She is sorry for trying to steal oranges @Do not steal, Mrs Musenga says, you should always ask when you need something.
P8	Mwewa is happy again.
