page_text:

P1	Obabirye yatakire okapa.
P2	Amaka ga babirye gayabire okucaalira ekapa.
P3	Obabirye yatakire eno epuusi. Yabbaire munene inho kuye.
P4	Opaul yatakire okapa ono omutomuto. Yaali mutono inho kwiye.
P5	Omaama yatakire kaano akatokato. Kabbaire kangu inho kwiye.
P6	Buli muntu yatakire kapa ono omutomuto.
P7	Batwaire okapa omutomuto.
P8	Buli muntu musanyuki n'ekapa empyaka.
