title:	Usuku Luka Lynne Lokuzalwa

desc:	ULynne usebenze kanzima elungiselela iphathi yakhe yokugubha usuku lwakhe lokuzalwa

page_text:

P1	ULynne, umkhulu Kheswa no Bheki bamatasa balungisela iphathi yosuku lokuzalwa kwakhe uLynne.
P2	UBheki no Lynne bazobhaka ikhekhe. Umkhulu Kheswa ubafundela izinto abazozidinga ukwenza leli khekhe.
P3	ULynne athi, "Masihlanganise zonke lezi zinto endishini enkulu."
P4	Nangu uBheki ehlanganisa konke, kwenzeke inyakanyaka.
P5	Kuzwakale umkhulu ethi, "Inhlama yekhekhe iyasinda manje."
P6	Aqhubeke athi, "Nabangani bakho sebefikile."
P7	UBheki wayecabanga ukuthi ngoba isipho sokuqala sisinda nje, yingoba sasisikhulu.
P8	Kwakungumcamelo kodwa ulula, ungasindi.
P9	UBheki wayecabanga ukuthi isipho esilandelayo sizoba lula ngoba sasisincane.
P10	Kwakuyitshe futhi lalisinda.
P11	Izingane zathi ikhiligremu yetshe ilingana nekhiligremu yezimpaphe.
P12	Ikhekhe likaLynne lalishe langqongqa.
P13	Wakhathazeka kakhulu uLynne.
P14	UAnti Naledi wathi kuBheki makaqagele esakhe isipho sisinda kangakanani.
P15	UBheki wathi, "Isipho sakho sisinda okulinganayo nekhekhe elishileyo."
P16	Wonke umuntu endlini wahalalisela uLynne, "usuku olumnandi kuwe."
