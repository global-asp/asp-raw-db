desc:	Lugero lutukobera omusaadha bweyaghona okufa.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala omusaadha yali alikutambula munsiko mangu ago yaayagaana emologoma
P2	Empologoma yali nga eri kugholaku mu busubi obumpi
P3	Okwagaanana kuno kwali kwa mangu mu ngeri nti omusaadha tiyamanha kyakukola era yatya inho
P4	Mu kusooka wampologoma yeena yeewuunhia aye yatyamuuku katono aye mundaaye endhoka dhaali dhiri kumweniola ekyalaga kiti endhala yali eri kumuluma
P5	Aye omusaadha yeeyagaana nga ali ghaigulu ku muti omuleeyiriri yatandiika okubwoigolera Wampologoma
P6	Wampologoma yalambaala ghansi gh'omuti nga eri kulindirira eky'okulya kyayo Ebiseera bwebyaja Wampologoma yatenduka oba yeefuula atendwike
P7	Omusaadha yeena yatenduka kabiriyaasituka bweyamala yaghongerera irala era yaatendukira irala
P8	Oluvainhuma omusaadha yagwa ghansi ku mpologoma yoona eyali etendwike
P9	Bombi baazuukanga tibeetegeera nga ate batiire Wampologoma yalumuka eky'okulya kyayo yaalumuka olwokutaasa obulamu bwe
P10	Omusaadha  yeena yalumukira Wampologoma yeyalumukira oluvainhuma yamanha nga alikubinga Wampologoma
P11	Omusaadha yamala yaacuusa engira yaaghona era empologoma tiyamulya
