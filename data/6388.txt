page_text:

P1	One day three boys from Mugulu village went to hunt. They used dogs to help them.
P2	When they were moving they come across two guinea fowls. They chased until they killed them.
P3	When it came to time for going back home,  they wanted to share the birds. One boy ran away with the two birds.
P4	The other two boys chased him and removed the birds from him.
