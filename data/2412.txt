desc:	Raka linakanda olole shikholekha notsushiririra amatsi nende khulilinda obulayi.

page_text:

P1	Omusi kuranjirira okhumera.
P2	Kukhula nikwikha mwiloba hasi.
P3	Nashio eshisina shiosi shikhula. Omumera kwe linakanda lino kukhula nikurambiya hekulu.
P4	Amasafu kambeli kasebukha.
P5	Omumera kwe linakanda kukhula.
P6	Efimuli filondakho. Efimuli tsana fiikhola amanakanda.
P7	Obufwa buli mumisoka chielinakanda obunji po.
