desc:	Ana camuko ca mcanda juŵalile yisogosi yejinji mnope ciciciŵa cici?

page_text:

P1	Lyuŵa line mama ŵasumile yisogosi.
P2	"Ana yisogosi citulye katema kaci?" uwe twawusisye. @@"Citulye cilo cino," ŵatite m'yo mama.
P3	Mcimwene jwangu jwakulosoka. @@Jwapasisye yisogosi yosope. Jwalile yejinji.
P4	"Mlole yatesile a Rahim!" ŵaagumisile mcimwene jwangu jwamnandi. @@"A Rahim ŵakusawusya soni ŵakulinonyela," une natite m'yo.
P5	Mama ŵatumbilile a Rahim.
P6	Uwe soni twatumbilile a Rahim. @@Nambo a Rahim nganalosya kuligamba.
P7	"Ana cimwape camuko a Rahim?" ŵawusiyse mcimwene jwangu jwamnandi.
P8	"A Rahim caligambe pangakaŵapa," ŵatetele mamawo.
P9	A Rahim ŵatandite kulwala.
P10	"M'citumbo mwangu mukupweteka," ŵaŵecete a Rahim mwakusongona.
P11	Mama ŵacimanyilile cicatendekasisye kuti a Rahim alwale.@@"Yisogosi yikwapa camuko a Rahim," ŵaganisisye m'yo mamawo.
P12	Panyuma pakwe a Rahim ŵatupepesisye. "Ngasimba jwakulosoka soni," ŵalakwewo ŵapelece cilanga. @@Uwe twakulupilile yiŵaŵeceteyo.
