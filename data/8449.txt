page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping. Alakarikina ayong akilam.
P2	 Alone. Anapei.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too. Alakarikina ayong akilam auno ka napaekang.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Alakarikina ayong akisomare ng'ikoio luketalarak.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Alakarikinang akipasare anaro ka ng'apalon kang.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother. Alakarikina ayong alasi ng'iboro a itiokang.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Alakarikina ayong egiri ng'agiran naapolok emam ng'imasimasia.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit. Alakarikina ayong akinyam ageor negelagela.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Alakarikina ayong akiper loperiwoi loapolon.
P10	I enjoy listening to interesting stories told by my mother. Alakarikina ayong anitiapupi itiokang isarae ng'ikoio luajokak.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Oitiakoi! Alakarikina ayong akisub ng'iticisio lu daang!
