desc:	Sokwe anasaidia Mamba ingawa Mamba ana mpango tofauti.

page_text:

P1	Sokwe alimkuta Mamba kando ya mto mkubwa.
P2	Mamba alimwambia, "Nina maumivu. Tafadhali nisaidie."
P3	Sokwe alimuuliza, "Unahitaji nini?"
P4	Mamba akamjibu, "Usiponipatia moyo wako, sitapona."
P5	Sokwe akasema, "Kwanza nibebe hadi upande ule wa mto."
P6	Mamba alimbeba Sokwe mgongoni wakavuka mto.
P7	Sokwe alisema, "Ninahitaji kisu ili nikate moyo wangu."
P8	Wakati huo huo, upepo mkali ulianza kuvuma.
P9	Mamba aliinuliwa juu karibu amuangushe Sokwe.
P10	Sokwe alipoiona nchi kavu, alimwambia Mamba aharakishe.
P11	Ghafla, Sokwe aliurukia mti.
P12	Mamba alikasirika akasema, "Ulinidanganya! Utajuta!"
