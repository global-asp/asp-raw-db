page_text:

P1	We love soccer. Hwenda nyo omupiira.
P2	Moloko is teaching us soccer. Moloko ali hutwegeresa omupiira.
P3	We are playing soccer. Huli hugudula omupiira.
P4	Moloko is the best teacher! Maloko njo musomesa asingayo obulaŋi.
P5	After the game,  Moloko told us that she has a surprise for us. Olufanyuma lwo mugudulo, Maloko gatusubisa ahalabo eyihuli.
P6	But she will only let us know tomorrow. Naye anahatutegese olweejo.
P7	The next day we all came early to the field. We all love surprises. Oludaalo olwalondaho,  hwatina hu hisaŋe mangu daala. Hwesi hwesi hwenda ebirabo.
P8	Moloko has new soccer kits and balls for all of us! What a great surprise! Maloko ali n'ebyo mupiira ebinyaha ate byeefe hwesihwesi!
