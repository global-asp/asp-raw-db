title:	Tintfo lengititsandzako
author:	Gugu  Makhubela and Michelle Longwe
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Siswati
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Lena indzaba lefanele bantfwana labancane lemayelana nentfombatane kanye netintfo letitsandzako.

page_text:

P1	Mine ngitsandza ingcatfu.
P2	Ngitsandza kudlala ingcatfu.
P3	Ngitsandza kudlala ingcatfu nemngani wami.
P4	Ngitsandza kufundza incwadzi yami.
P5	Ngitsandza kudlala nebangani bami engadzini yetimbali.
P6	Ngiyatsandza kubona emagwebu nangigeza titja.
P7	Ngitsandza kubhala ebhodini.
P8	Ngitsandza kudla titselo ngobe tinemsoco.
P9	Ngitsandza kulala embhedzeni wami lomkhulu.
P10	Ngiyatsandza kulalela make wami nakacoca indzaba ngetilwane.
P11	Leti tintfo lengititsandzako!
