page_text:

P1	Yo ke Mara.
P2	Lo ke losika lwa ga Mara.
P3	Yo ke mogoloaagwe Mara.@@Mogolwaagwe o tshameka kgwele ya dinao.
P4	Yo ke kgaitsadiaagwe Mara.@@Kgaitsadie o robetse.
P5	This is Mara's mother.@@Her mother is cooking food.
P6	Yo ke rraagwe Mara.@@O aga ntlo.
P7	Yo ke nkokwaage Mara.@@O ntse ka fa tlase ga setlhare.
