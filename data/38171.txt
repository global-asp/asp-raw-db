page_text:

P1	Once upon a time in a small village of Ukhanta it lived Melitta and her parents and also the villagers of Ukhanta.Melitta loved playing with her friend Emelda.They would play hide and seek or even walk around.
P2	One day as they were walking around talking they saw a huge tall tree but Melitta was excited to climb on top of the tree    Melitta rushed and quickly climb up on the tree.
P3	She reached up high and when her friend tryed talking to her but Melitta could not hear then Emelda shout out loud to get Melitta to hear her.
P4	Melitta tryed to get down but couldn't so Emelda told Melitta that she's going to get help but Melitta was quite scared.
P5	When Emelda was going to find help, Melitta was not doing quite well she bumbed into a snake and screamed helllllllllp! Nobody could hear.Sssssss the snake.
P6	Finally Emelda got help from Melitta's father he told Melitta to jump then they'll catch her she jumped and they were happy that Melitta was not bitten by a snake.
P7	The villagers were happy that Melitta was back and safe.They gave Melitta a small party They wanted to celebrate that Melitta is back and rejoiced ohh Melitta said the villagers.
