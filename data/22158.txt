desc:	Inkuru yinka ikunzwe cyane mu muryango.

page_text:

P1	Nitwa Ndalo.
P2	Data yoroye inka. Iyo nka yitwa Rukundo.
P3	Rukundo  ikunda kurya ubwatsi
  umunsi wose..
P4	Haba iyo data ayihaye
  ibigorigori.
P5	Rukundo inywa amazi
  menshi
P6	Rukundo ndayikunda
P7	Data arayikama. Rukundo turayigaburira nayo ikadufasha
P8	Rukundo iduha amata
  menshi
P9	Njyewe nywa amata menshi
P10	Data agurisha amata. Amafaranga avuyemo akayangurira ibitabo by’imigani.
P11	Njyewe nkunda gusoma
  imigani
P12	Wumvise impamvu nkunda
  inka yacu Rukundo?
