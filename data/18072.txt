title:	Akine Nakedong'an
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	José Nhambo
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First sentences

desc:	Akine na adikino epeot enyami anamoni.

page_text:

P1	Apei kolong'it alosi akine namoni,  sodi kirim amoni elakara. Nakaneni abu ng'esi towoliyo!
P2	Napei kona kiriama ka epeot! Dio epeot taapu nikeng tama,  "Kanyamit ayong akoro."
P3	Tatachu akine tama,  "Epeot,  ayen atamar isaki iyong akinyam ayong. Nakaneni asaki ayong atotoana alakara!"
P4	King'it nabo epeot akine tama,  "Inyo eger ang'akiro kang'una? Atonere ilakara? Kwai?"
P5	Tatachu akine tama, "Kerai akitu,  kajoikinit ayong akitu edong'ae!"
P6	Ekisidi tolemu epeot agita. Kiboikin kisiak akisidong'are.
P7	Kisiak akine akidong'are,  nakaneni aring'a cha ng'esi ebulit wadio.
P8	Ayakasi ng'ing'wokwo diye. Apotu taaput aking'olikin ibore niasuba kaneni.
P9	Tobul epeot na eng'olikinia ng'ing'wokwo. Abu ng'esi torei wadio dio kipung'ar!
P10	Torubak akine idong'ae kotere abu epeot lotete asaki akiar ng'esi toeka.
