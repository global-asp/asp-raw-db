P1	One Morning Chicken came screaming...... It has begin it has begin.... Runing straight to Rabbit sitting in the sun.
P2	Rabbit got such a big fright that he also started Screaming and he and chicken ran over to the barn.
P3	Near the barn Lucky the dog was sleeping in the sun.  He awakes with screaming animals around him shouting it has begun, it has begun ... He also started to act crazy.
P4	Chicken, Rabbit and Lucky the dog all started running screaming, it has begun it has begun.
P5	They ran to the stable and Miss Horse asked:  Why are you making such a noise?  They all answered at the same time it has begun, it has begun .....
P6	Miss Horse was so terrified about the noise that she too started yelling and joined the other crazy animals.
P7	At the bottom off the road they ran into Donkey.  What is the matter with you?  Are you all crazy?  They only shouted it has begun, it has begun...... So poor Donkey decided to join the crazy animals.
P8	They ran across the field, over the hills and across the river screaming and shouting.
P9	Down the medow they met Miss Cow,  she looked at them and moo.... Why are you all running like crazy animals she asked?Donkey replied :  I really don't know, it looked like fun running around with them.
P10	Everybody stopped. Looking at each other? Donkey asks horse: Why are we running?  I don't know.
P11	Everybody stopped and look at chicken.@You.... What is coming?  Chicken laughed, well I was playing a game with myself, I was running away from my shadow, pretending it was a monster chasing me.@Why were you also running and screaming?
