id:	38054
title:	Nnanfofoɔ nkumaa mmienu
author:	Ofosu  Frimpong
artist:	Jesse Pietersen
lang:	Asante Twi
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38054.png
desc:	Ethiopian tete adomankomasɛm a ɛfa nnanfonom nkumaa mmienu a wɔhyiaeɛ nanso wa agoro ankyɛ efiri sɛ na wɔn bɔ abira.

page_text:

P1	Na apɔnkye ba ne ɔsebɔ ba yɛ nanfofoɔ.
P2	Wɔn kɔ asasetam so kɔ di agorɔ.
P3	Na wɔn kyɛɛ bere a na ɛsɛsɛ ɔsane ba fie no.
P4	Apɔnkye ba no bɛka kyerɛɛ ne maame sɛ, "Nani agye paa efirisɛ w'akɔ nya adanfoɔ a neho yɛ fɛ enti wɔn bɛ san ahyia bio ɔkyena."
P5	Ne maame bua no sɛ, "Oo meba ɛyɛ medɛ sɛ wasan aba asomdwie mu. Ɔnyɛ yɛn adanfo. Ma ɛnkɔ nkɔhyia no ɔkyena."
P6	Saa bere koro noa, ɔsebɔ ba no nso ka kyerɛɛ ne maame sɛ, "Nani agye sɛ ɔkɔ hyia apɔnkye ba a na neho yɛ fɛ a wɔn ahyehyɛ ho sɛ wɔn bɛ hyia ɔkyena.''
P7	Ɔsebɔ maame no bua se no sɛ, "Oo me ba, kafra, adɛn na wo gyae no maano kɔe na wawe no. Sɛ wo hyia no ɔkyena a, kum no na weno."
P8	Adeɛ kyee no, ɔsebɔ ba no kɔɔ apɔnkye ba no akurase kɔ hwehwɛɛ no.
P9	Apɔnkye ba no anpue anba abɔnten. Na ɔne ne maame tetɛɛ wɔn fie hɔ.
P10	Ɔsebɔ ba no tea mu, "Yɛn kɔ ɛkɔdi agorɔ wɔ asasetam no so."
P11	Apɔnkye ba no kaa sɛ, "Yɛ nyɛ nnanfofoɔ biem. Ma tiee me maame. Mmofra papa biara tie wɔn maame asɛm."
P12	Ɔsebɔ ba no werɛho. Na ɔsan kɔ ne maame nkyɛn.
