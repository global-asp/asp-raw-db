title:	Ekyalekire ekikere ikyathabya  kibuya

desc:	Ekikere kyanganabere kibuya ngekihangalha kukyahulikirira!

page_text:

P1	Emyaka minene enyuma,
  iya kihangalha na wa kikere
  babya banywani. Kiro kighuma, mubatwamo erisondya abambesa omwa muyi.
P2	Ekikere kyabya kikayighomba
  engobi eye’kihangalha kusangwa yikangabasanirye kandi yikamelemeta.
P3	Mwabugha," Niki
kwehi ekyalekire iwabya mubuya?" Nyithungerere, ngalimubi kutsibu. Nangakolera engobi yayi yaki?"
P4	Kihangalha mwamusubirya ati,”
  Hulikirira! Uhire amaghetse omwa liregha ghu tsumbegho.
  Neryo nghu”
P5	Nikwa ekikere mukyabugha
  kiti,”Nyinasi, nganasi, yihunire, mwira waghe. “
  neryo mwaghenda.
P6	Mwahika ewiwe amahira
  amaghetse omwaliregha.
P7	Amaghetse mwabya akatsuka
  eribera, mwayighusamo.
P8	Engobi yiwe muyathoghotha
  nomukira wuwe mwakuthukalha.
P9	Omwamwanya owerikangabasania neribya
  mubuya nga kihangalha, mwathasabya mubi kutsibu.
