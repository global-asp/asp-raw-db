page_text:

P1	A-lways look both ways at streets before crossing.
P2	B-e quiet in the classroom while the teacher is talking.
P3	C-lean my room before going out to play.
P4	D-o all of my homework every day.
P5	E-at all of the vegetables on my plate.
P6	F-loss and brush my teeth to keep them looking great.
P7	G-et help from my Teacher if I don't understand.
P8	H-elp my classmates whenever I can.
P9	I-nclude my Parents and invite them along.
P10	J-ump run and exercise to keep me strong.
P11	K-eep my promises and never ever lie.
P12	L-isten to my parents and my teachers I will try.
P13	M-ake good friends with everyone that I meet.
P14	N-ever tease others or anyone that I greet.
P15	O-ffer a helping hand to my Mom and my Dad.
P16	P-ut my arms around them whenever they are sad.
P17	Q-uietly raise my hand before speaking out in class.
P18	R-emember to ask Mom if she needs help with the trash.
P19	S-peak clearly and softly and try not to shout.
P20	T-alk to my Parents if ever in doubt.
P21	U-se my time wisely in school and in play.
P22	V-ow to be kind to others each and every day.
P23	W-ake with a smile and a good attitude.
P24	eX-press myself always without being rude.
P25	Y-es I will try to do all of these things, and
P26	Z-oon through each day with the joy that it brings.
