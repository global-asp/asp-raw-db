page_text:

P1	Lino e isoero lyange, lili ne ebibiina munaana, ekizimbe abasomesa bwebaba kikira ekyayawuulibwa mu emirundi ebiri oruuyi gha basomesa era oluuyi ghamukulu gha iseomero.
P2	Tuli na basomesa ikumi na bataanu. Omusemsa yensinga kwendha ni Mukyalo Idhuma, mulungi inho. Afaayo era atwenda inho. Nnendha inho mukyala Idhuma.
P3	Ekiseera  kyo kuwola ku, tuzanhira mu mukisaghe. Kinhenhe inho. Tuli ne migugha ne mipiira gyetukozesa okuzaanha. Ekiseera kyo kuwola kyesiga kwenda.
P4	Lino neiterekero lye bitabo lye eisomero lyeife. Limu ebitabo kamaala. Mu biseera bwaife ebyeidhembe, tuja mu iterekero twasoma. Mulimu amateka. Kinhonha inho okusoma.
P5	Eisomero lyeife linhirira, buli kunco tulonda ebuli kikaba. Mukulu Makooma na vunanhizibwa okulabirira oluya. Mukambwe inho. Abaana boona boona bamutya.
P6	Tuli baana bikumi bitaanu mu isomero lyange. Ekibiina ekyo omukaaga ne kisinga baana kamaala, eisomero lyange likola bukalamu liiza mu amaani. Omwaka ogughaire eisomero lyange lyali lyakubiri mu disitulikiti. Nnhenda inho eisomero lyange.
P7	Eisomero lyange irungi. Nerisinga mu kibuga, ndyenhumirizaamu inho.
