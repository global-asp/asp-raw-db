desc:	Greedy Anansi tries to trick his friends into giving him more than his share of figs but his friends find out.

page_text:

P1	During famine, only crows picked figs. Anansi wished for some.
P2	Anansi planned and took a fig from the crows.
P3	Another time, the crows told Anansi, “You just want our food!”
P4	Together, they went to pick figs. Anansi took all of them.
P5	Anansi jumped. He fell into the river full of crocodiles.
P6	Anansi said, “I am your lost relative.” He was not eaten.
P7	Crocodiles tested Anansi. Anansi pretended to eat brown water like crocodiles.
P8	Crocodiles believed Anansi. They allowed him to sleep in their hole.
P9	Anansi said, “Take me across the river to fetch my family.”
P10	The crocodile carried Anansi to the bank of the river.
P11	Anansi ran away. The crocodile is still waiting for him.
