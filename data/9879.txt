desc:	A story about a boy and his friends

page_text:

P1	My name is Simo.
P2	I have four friends.
P3	Their names are Zizo,  Lele,  Sisa and Ayanda.
P4	My friend Zizo likes to play soccer.
P5	My friend Lele likes to swim.
P6	My friend Sisa likes to play hide-and-seek.
P7	My friend Ayanda likes to read.
P8	Me? I like to the things they like to do.
P9	I play soccer with Zizo.
P10	I swim with Lele.
P11	I play hide-and-seek with Sisa.
P12	Come,  friend,  what do you like?
P13	Come,  play soccer with us.
P14	Come,  swim with us.
P15	Come,  play hide-and-seek with us.
P16	Come,  read with us!
