page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a naughty monkey. It lived in the forest.
P2	Closed to the forest. There was an old farm house. The farmer lived there.
P3	Next to the farm house there was a big banana tree. The monkey steals the bananas from the tree.
P4	The next day the farmer went to his shed. Looking for the magic banana seeds. He planted the seeds.
P5	Then, the monkey hear the sound coming out from the stomach. It says, "why did you ate me?" The monkey was scared and ran to the forest. It never returned.
P6	The farmer lived happily ever. Since the Monkey ran away.
P7	The monkey steals the bananas from the tree.
