title:	Kalabushe Mukanazi
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	SiLozi

desc:	Kalabushe ubulela ahulu nikulata kuzibisisa lika. Kubulela ahulu kumukeng’isa mwa butata

page_text:

P1	Kale kwasimuluho nekupila musizani yana bizwa Kalabushe. Kalabushe neli lyambombola. Bomaye Kalabushe bamweleza kuli asike kwikambolelanga ahulu.
P2	Lizazi leleling'wi,  banyani babomaye Kalabushe bakula. Neba pila mwamunzi onoli fabuse yanuka.
P3	Bomaye Kalabushe nebapatehile ahulu lizazi leo. Neli manzibwana maswe nako yanabaluma kuli alwale lico zabakuli bona banyani babomaye.
P4	Mwanzila Kalabushe akopana ni Sinson,  sitongwani senesi ipetuzi kuba mutu.
P5	Sinson abuza Kalabushe mutiyo wanashimbile. Kalabushe ahalaba,  "Nama,  mayi ni mabisi." Bomaye Kalabushe bamweleza kuli asike kubulelela mutu kaza nalwezi.
P6	Kalabushe abulelela Sinson nizamakulano abanyani abomaye nikuli hape lico zana shimbile naiseza bona bakuli. Sinson akala kumiza mati nikushukela nama yanalwezi Kalabushe.
P7	Sinson amatela kwapata Kalabushe kuya kwandu yamukuli.
P8	Sitongwani se singa Sinson samiza banyani babamaye Kalabushe nikuikapesa mwa kubo yabona.
P9	Kalabushe atopunya,  mwandu nekukuzize ahulu. Akena mwandu nikubiza,  "Ima,  mukayi?"
P10	Kalabushe asaboni kuli akuna ya alaba,  akena mwamusiyo wabanyani wa bomaye monabalobalanga. Akomoka abona kuli kuna nimutu usili yana ikapesize kubo yetuna.
P11	"Ima,  King'i mazebe amina ali amatuna kaceku?" abuza Kalabushe. Sinson ahala mwa kubo yetuna,  "Kono nikakuhutwa hande."
P12	Kalabushe abuza hape,  "Ima,  king'i meto amina ali amatuna kaceku?" Sinson ahalaba,  "Kuli nikubone hande."
P13	Kalabushe abuza lwamafelelezo,  "Ima,  king'i mulomo wamina ahuli omutuna?" Sinson ahalaba,  "Kuli nikumize kappa kukuca." Sinson atula kuzwa fa mumbeta nikumuca Kalabushe.
P14	Bakeng'isa kuli Kalabushe neli lyambombola,  aikeza kwaambola ni mwamba ya sitongwani singa Sinson. Kalabushe abuza lipuzo zeng'ata.
P15	Kwamafelelezo Sitongwani akataliswa kili puzo za Kalabushe mane ahupula feela kumukwa.
P16	Nimaye yomuinyanyani bapiliswa kibatu ba mwahae. Kuzwa lizazi leo,  Kalabushe nasabuleli ahulu kwabatu basazibi.
