page_text:

P1	Kwakukhona umfana igama lakhe kunguRodney. URodney wayehlala nomndeni wakhe. @@Umama wakhe kwakunguJamima kanti ubaba wakhe kwakunguMike. Wayenodadewabo ababili uSuzy noLola. @@URodney wayenguthunjana.
P2	Lo mfana wayethanda kakhulu ukudlala ichess. @@Wayeyithanda kangangokuba wayethula tu! angakhulumi uma eseyidlala.
P3	Kuthe ngelinye ilanga ezidlalela lo mdlalo  nabangane bakhe esikoleni, wathola ukuthi ungacishe ufane ncimishi nokwenzeka emndenini wakhe.
P4	Ubaba wakhe uMike yiNkosi kanti umama wakhe uJamima yiNdlovukazi enamandla. @@Bese kuthi uSuzy abe yiKnight, uLola yena abe yiBishop.@@URodney-ke yena uyi pawn ngoba nguyena omncane.
P5	Wathi angathola lokho waqaqa ngejubane eqonde ekhaya eyobaxoxela ngalokhu.
P6	Baqala ngokumhleka kodwa ekugcineni bayiqondisisa lento ayishoyo. @@URodney waqala wachaza ngeqhaza elibanjwa yilowo nalowo nangokuthi kungani yena eyipawn.
P7	Watshela umama wakhe ukuthi uyindlovukazi nokuthi ubaba wakhe uyinkosi. @@Udadewabo omdala uyiknight kanti omncane uyibishop.
P8	URodney wayezibona eyipawn ngoba uyayithanda impilo yangaphandle! @@Angaba noma yini ayifunayo noma kungathiwa akadele okuthile.
