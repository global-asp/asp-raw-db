P1	Halima loves school but the COVID-19 Pandemic came.
P2	At first, she was excited when schools closed because she wasn't properly prepared for her exams.
P3	She spent months preparing for her exams but @the COVID-19 pandemic wouldn't go away. @@She missed school so much.
P4	Her parents didn't know when schools would resume so Halima started teaching her neighbours all she was learning.
P5	She is now ready to take the exams but schools are still closed.
P6	Finally, she has heard the goodnews over the radio today!@@Schools are resuming!
P7	She is so excited to return to the classroom that she doubts if she can wait for Monday!
P8	She can't wait to see her friend's again and learn more in school.
