desc:	Akanyamasyo kihutiye kwinjira mu mirwano kagira ngo gatabare inkima. Kahakuye izuru ribwataraye.

page_text:

P1	Akanyamasyo n'inkima bikeneye amafaranga.
P2	Birakora amasahani yo gucuruza ku isoko.
P3	Akanyamasyo kabona intambara mu isoko.
P4	Inkima n'ifuku birimo kurwana.
P5	Akanyamasyo karasakuza kati "Ndekera inshuti!"
P6	Ifuku iruma akanyamasyo ku zuru.
P7	Izuru ryako ryakomeretse rirakababaza.
P8	Izuru ryako riba rirabwase kubera ko kihutiye kurwana.
