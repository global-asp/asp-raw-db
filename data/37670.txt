title:	Di moon and di cap

desc:	One small boy get blue cap from him mama. Wind com blow di cap land am on top big tree

page_text:

P1	All of us go di fun fair.
P2	Papa buy my broda fine eyeglass. Mama buy me blue cap wey dey shine.
P3	As we de go house, strong wind come blow. E blow my cap comot.
P4	My cap com hang on top branch of one big tree.
P5	I cry well, well. Na so I no com eat my night food.
P6	Di moon com comot later for night. E begin look my cap wey hang for di big
P7	Di moon wear my cap. E sweet am sotay e com de smile. Me sef com smile.
P8	You tink say sun need to de wear cap?
P9	You fit guess di cap wey I wear today?
