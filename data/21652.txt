title:	Illeetif Warabesa Midhaan Facasan

desc:	Illeetif Warabesi hirriya haatahan male

page_text:

P1	Bara durii, Illeetif Warabessi hirriya turan.
P2	Illeetin, "Waliin maalif miidhan hinfacasnu," jette Warabesan.
P3	Illeetinis walgargarani boqqoolo akka qootan yaada dhiyeesite.
P4	Illeetin Warabesatiin, "Hojiin sinbiroo adamsu rakkisadha," jette.
P5	Warabessi qophaa isaa lafaa qotuuf waligale.
P6	Warabessi yommuu qophaa isaa qotuu, Illeetin tessee sirbiti.
P7	Yommuu boqqoolon gahuu, Illeeetin sinbiroo irraa eegdi.
P8	Illeetin, "Ani midhaan lafaa irraa sassaba. 

Ati lafaa gadi sassabi," jette.
P9	Illeetin boqqoolo hundumaa sassabde gumbi isheeti naqte.
P10	Warabesi hidda boqqoolo qofaa argate.
P11	Warabesi gowwomfamu isaa bare baay'e aare.
P12	Ammas lama tahani dinnicha facasan.

Warabesi, "Ani isaa lafaa irraa jiru sassaba," jedhe.
P13	Waliin sirbaa qonna dinicha waliin hojjatan.
P14	Dinnichi yommuu gahuus, Warabesi balaa dinnicha qofaa argate.
P15	Illeetin dinnicha gurguda lafa jala sassabde kuufatte.
P16	Warabesis xiiqi qabate. 

Hirriyuman isaanis addan cite.
