desc:	Lynne otimo gikipinyi kweth ri mbaaga ma nywolok pere

page_text:

P1	Lynne, Oupa Karel and Anton ido gyo panga ri mbaga ma nywole pa Lynne.
P2	Anton gi Lynne ido gyo kitho tedo cake. Oupa Karel soma ndiko ma tedo mere.
P3	"Wa nwowi gikipinyi aora me i'bakuli madwongi", Lynne wacho.
P4	Anton onwowo gikipiny aora ma cake to konjo kiri pinyi.
P5	"Cake winjere pa pek pama," Oupa Karel to wacho.
P6	"Merin peri gyo tundo," OUpa Karel owacho.
P7	Anton oparo ni michii marapena ido peki ru'mbo mere.
P8	Ido gi theno wiyijo ikanindo aka ido kipeki kada.
P9	Anton oparo ni miichi mararyo d'obedo masewi rupiri ido thini.
P10	Ido obedo tele mowiri rangi aka ido peki.
P11	Nyithintho gyo wacho ni kilo 1 ma tele rom gi yer winyo ma kilo achieli.
P12	Cake pa Lynne ido owangi.
P13	Lynne owingyo remi.
P14	Waya Mavis opengyo Anton kitewa peko pa gira miichi pere.
P15	"Peko mere rom gi cake mo wangi" Anton to dwoko go.
P16	Kisidhano to wacho, "mbaga ma nywole ma syemi, Lynne".
