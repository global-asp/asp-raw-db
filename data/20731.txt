desc:	Imbozi ifashe kumugabo wali ufite imbwa ye yamwubahirizaga chane.

page_text:

P1	hari hariho umugabo wari ufite imbwa
P2	yakundaga iyi imbwa chane chane
P3	iyi imbwa yakoraga chane kandi yajaga nomugatale kugura inyama
P4	yajaga nokumugezi kuvoma amazi
P5	yalindaga bulikimwe mulugo
P6	umusi gumwe uyumugabo yashimye imbwaye mungeri idasanzwe kumbwo kuba imbwa irunvira
P7	imbwa yalishimye chane nuko ikomeza gukora chane
