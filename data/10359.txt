page_text:

P1	The night has come again and it is dark.
P2	After meals I go to bed with my young sister Anna.
P3	I am always scared because every night I wet the bed.
P4	Whenever I dream,  I suddenly wake up wet.
P5	I quickly change position and sleep in Anna's which is dry and warm.
P6	In the morning,  I wake up first and leave Anna in bed.
P7	And when mother asks who wet the bed,  I loudly say it is Anna.
P8	So the next day mother decides to sleep with Anna in her bed.
P9	Unfortunately,  the same thing happens and now there is no one to else to blame.
P10	Finally mother finds out that Anna does not wet the bed but I do.
