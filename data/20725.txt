title:	Unkawana Nesomiso
author:	Pumla Mdontswa
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences

desc:	Sonke singahlalisana ngokuthula uma nje singabelana ngesinakho.

page_text:

P1	Kwakunesomiso esikhulu ezweni.
P2	Inkawu yasuka ekhaya iyofuna amanzi nokudla.
P3	Yahamba idlula izintaba nezigodi.
P4	Yafika endaweni eyayaziwa ngokuthi kuseThembeni.
P5	uNkawana wayithanda le ndawo yaseThembeni.
P6	Wanethezeka, wadla izithelo wakhuluphala. Noma kwakumnandi lapha, wayekhumbula abangane bakhe.
P7	Ngakho-ke uNkawana wathatha uhambo olude ephindela ekhaya.
P8	Zajabula ezinye izinkawu ekhaya uma zimbona uNkawana.
P9	"Sitshele, ikuphi le ndawo enezithelo okhuluma ngayo?" kubuza ezinye izinkawu.
P10	"Ngizonisa khona," kusho uMkawana.
P11	Nazo lezi ezinye izinkawu zayithanda le ndawo yaseThembeni. Zanquma ukuhlala lapho unomphela.
P12	Izinkawu zaseThembeni zakhathazeka ngalesi sinqumo soNkawana.
P13	"Lezi zinkawu zizodla zonke izithelo zethu," zisho ngokukhathazeka.
P14	Izinkawu zaseThembeni zahlasela izinkawu zokufika, oNkawana.
P15	Omunye omdala eqenjini loNkawana wathi, "Kungani nilwa? Izithelo ziningi zizosanele sonke.
P16	Kwakuyiqiniso, zaziziningi izithelo. Ngakho-ke zonke izinkawu zanquma ukuhlala ndawonye ngokuthula.
