P1	There is a small shop near my house.
P2	The shop owner is a thin man with a little voice.
P3	It is hot outside.@@I go to the fridge.@@I get a strawberry Kingsley.
P4	I give the thin man my money.
P5	Then, I open my strawberry Kingsley and drink.
P6	That cold Kingsley tastes so sweet!
