title:	P'aka a Charo
author:	Titus  Kazungu
artist:	Marleen Visser and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	KiGiryama

desc:	Charo ana paka anji. Vidze

page_text:

P1	Charo anahendza p'aka. @@Ko nyumbani kwakwe kuna p'aka anji.
P2	Charo ana p'aka mwiru. @@P'aka yuyu anarya nyama nyinji zhomu.
P3	Charo ana p'aka wa kitumbotumbo. @@P'aka yuyu anarya kila kit'u.
P4	P'aka wa hahu, anakwera dzulu za muhi. @@P'aka yuyu anashindwa kuthima.
P5	Charo anakwera dzulu za muhi. Anakwama kuko na p'aka wa hahu. @@Mameye Charo ana aviza kuthima kula ko dzulu za muhi.
P6	Charo ana p'aka airi akaha. @@P'aka aa manaoha dzuwa siku yosi.
P7	Charo ana p'aka ahahu mario mana chadi. @@P'aka aa managwira panya jikoni kila usiku.
P8	Charo ana k'uro mumwenga. K'uro yuyu anaifwa Ndokolani. @@Ndokolani k'ana asena. K'ana raha bule.
P9	P'aka a Charo k'amamwendzi Ndokolani. @@Manamuinga kula ko nyumbani. @@Ndokolani anavura malo.
P10	Charo anamupata Ndokolani na kumudzya mudzini. @@P'aka osi kamana raha. @@K'amendzi kumona Ndokolani kaheri.
P11	Yuno Charo ana p'aka angahi? @@Ana p'aka anane.
P12	Yuno Charo ana k'uro angahi? @@Ana k'uro mumwenga. @@Yuno Ndokolani a hiko vino?
P13	We una p'aka angahi ko mudzini kwenu? @@Una k'uro angahi ko mudzini kwenu?
