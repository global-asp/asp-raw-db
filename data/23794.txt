page_text:

P1	Nsamba asamba busambe.
P2	Ono yepanka bwepanke.
P3	Nze ntambula butambule.
P4	Maama akooma bukoome.
P5	Embuzi etyaime butyaime.
P6	Tulye bulye.
P7	Tunhwe bunhwe.
P8	Mbu, "Tukine bukine!"
P9	Twepakire bwepakire.
P10	Tughaye bughaye.
P11	Eno keeke bukeeke?
P12	Ife tweghumbe bweghumbe.
P13	Nze mpulira bughulire.
P14	Abo badhaguza budhaguze.
