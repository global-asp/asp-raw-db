desc:	Bakame n impyisi bitwijyisha akamaro kubushuti.

page_text:

P1	Bakame ni'mpyisi bari inshuti. igihe cyimwe bahitamo guhinga ibijumba bombi
P2	Bakora ubudahwema mumurima wabo.
P3	Nyuma yakazi,bakame ni'mpyisi bakinaga bombi
P4	Ariko igihe kimwe mpyisi yirukansa bakame.Bakame nawe arirukanga
P5	Umunsi umwe,bakame abaza mpyisi,"niba urinshuti, kuki unyirukansa?"
P6	Hanyuma bakame arabaza ati"niba utarinshuti yangye,kuki dukora tukanakina twembi?"
P7	Inshuti niwa muntu uguma arinshti
