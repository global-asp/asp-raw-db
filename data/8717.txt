desc:	All children should learn to love and care their animals kept at home.

page_text:

P1	Jojo is a young boy.
P2	Jojo living with his mother and a milk cow.
P3	Jojo loves the cow.
P4	Every morning Jojo's mother milks the cow.
P5	Jojo takes the milk.
P6	Jojo takes the cow to graze.
P7	Jojo gives the cow the water.
P8	Jojo's mother cooks food.
P9	Jojo enjoys food.
P10	Jojo plays the ball with his friend.
P11	Jojo reads his book.
P12	Jojo says bye to the cow and go to bed to sleep.
