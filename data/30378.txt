desc:	Mulufumo luno Dhaadha wa Tingi omukazi aghonia obulamubwe.

page_text:

P1	Tingi yalinga ni dhaadhawe omukazi.
P2	Tingi yalabiriranga ente dhd dgaadhawe.
P3	Lulala abasirikale baidha.
P4	Batwala ente dhonadhona.
P5	Tingi ni dhaadhawe balumuka.
P6	Bekweka mukibira paka bwire.
P7	Abasirikal kabiri.
P8	dhaadhawa Tingi yali amukwese ghansi mumakaba.
P9	Omusirikale owundi yamuninaku, aye Tingi yasirika.
P10	Embeera bweyatereera Tingi ni dhaadhawe bavaayo.
P11	Bekulula mpolampola kisirise okutuka bwe batuuka eka.
