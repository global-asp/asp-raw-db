P1	My name is Juma  Shomari .I am nine years old.My father takes me to the bazaar every Saturday.
P2	My father takes me to the bazaar every weekend.I like and enjoy shopping very much.
P3	Shopping with my father is very fun.I am usually very happy.I meet many people here and learn many things
P4	I love going on trips to the bazaar.Today I am shopping for my school uniforms.
P5	I had so much fun shopping.I had a nice time today.See you all next time when we come back shopping again yaah!
