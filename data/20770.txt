page_text:

P1	Ooyu ngu Kkiti.
Ooyu Mubwa.
P2	Kkiti a Mubwa zileenda. Zileenda mumunzi wazyo.
P3	Mpamunya babona iJi.

 IJi lyaali mubwizu. 

Eli Ji ilayaali iyolike mubwizu.
 
IJi lilibuyo iyolike.
P4	Kkiti a mubwa bakeenda kuya kukayuni. 

Bakabuzya kayuni, "Hena ji eli ndilyaako?"
P5	Aboobo kayuni kakati, "Peepe talili ji lyangu. Amabuzye tumba ndiza ngulaayo ji."
P6	Kkiti a Mubwa bakeenda kuya kuli tumba.

Bamubuzya tumba, "Hena ji eli ndilyaako?"
P7	Aboobo tumba wakati, "Peepe talili ji lyangu. Amabuzye nacisekwe ndiza ngulaayo ji."
P8	Kkiti a mubwa bakeenda kuya kuli nacisekwe.

Bamubuzya nacisekwe, "Hena ji eli ndilaako?"
P9	Aboobo nacisekwe wakati, "Peepe talili ji layangu. Amabuzye madada obile ndiza kuli ulaayo ji."
P10	Kkiti a Mubwa bakeenda kuya kumadada obile bakabuzya, "Hena ji eli ndilyanu?"
P11	Aboobo madada obile akati, "Peepe talili ji lyeso."

Mpoonya ji lyakapwaika.
P12	Mu ji mwakali kana kantombela.

Kana kantombela.
P13	Kana kantombela kakati, "Ino baama balikuli? Ino bataata balikuli? Ino bamaama abataata balikuli?"

Kkiti a Mubwa bakaleta kana kantombela kuli banyina a bawishi.
P14	Caala Kkiti.

Caala oMubwa.

Caala mwana ntombela.
