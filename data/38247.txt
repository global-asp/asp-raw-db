title:	Juleyka bee laasi hoore tati
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38247.png
desc:	Ɓiɓɓe rewɓe tato teenoyi sey nyalnde nden naange nyaaɗi. Sey ɓe nasti maayo ngam yinaago

page_text:

P1	Ko faɓɓuno ɓiɓɓe rewɓe tato njahiino teenol.
P2	Gulɗum waɗi, sey ɓe njahi maayo ngam yinaago.@@Ɓe nginii, ɓe piji, ɓe mbiccindiri ndiyam, ɓe nani belɗum.
P3	Sey ɓe killitii, asee hiiri! Sey ɓe njawɗani hootugo.
P4	Nde ɓe ɓaddi wuro, Juleyka sey meemi daande muuɗum: @@Mo nani mo yejjitii koɗol maako! @@Mo tori sooɓiraaɓe maako, "Useni ɗofteeyam, co''ooɗen maayo!" Kamɓe sey ɓe mbi'imo ɗum jenngii waanee.
P5	Juleyka sey so''ii maayo feere maako. @@Mo heɓti koɗol maako, sey mo yawɗani wuro, ammaa mo majji ngam nyiɓre waɗii.
P6	Juleyka sey teyni njayri to suudu.  @@Mo yaawi yottaagondu, sey mo silminii.
P7	Mo hayɗini nde ɓoosaaru maɓɓiti dammugal.@@Ndu ƴamimo, "Ko ngiɗɗa?" @@Sey Juleyka wi'i, "Mi majju, e mi ɗaɓɓita to mi waalata."@@Sey ɓoosaaru wiʼimo, "Nastu, sinaanon boo mi ŋatuma!" Sey Juleyka nasti.
P8	Ɓoosaaru wi'i, "Defanam!"@@Sey Juleyka jaabi, "Mi meeɗaayi defango ɓoosaaru." @@Sey ndu wiʼimo, "Defu, sinaanon boo mi ŋatuma!" @@Sey Juleyka defanindu nyamndu seɗɗa.
P9	Ɓoosaaru wi'i, "We''itanam leeso!"@@Sey Juleyka jaabi, "Mi meeɗaayi we''itango ɓoosaaru."@@Sey ndu wi'imo, "We''itanam sinaanon boo mi ŋatuma!" sey Juleyka we''itanindu leeso.
P10	Nde weeti fuu Juleyka defanandu, wuwanandu, layɓanandu. @@Sey nyalnde feere ɓoosaaru wi'i, "Juleyka, hannde mi yahay sumpoyaago lenyol am. Wuuwu suudu, defu nyamndu nden lonnanam ko mi warta."
P11	No ɓoosaaru dilliri Juleyka itti laasi tati e hoore muuɗum.@@Mo resi laasol gootol ley leeso, mo resi gootol gaɗa maɓɓoode dammugal bee gootol yaasi suudu. @@Nden mo itti pargal, mo doggi semmbee, mo hooti.
P12	Nde ɓoosaaru ndun warti, ndu ɗaɓɓiti Juleyka.@@Ndu noddi, "Juleyka, toye ma?" @@Laasol artungol wi''i, "Rayyam ley leeso." @@Laasol ɗiɗaɓol wi'i, "Rayyam gaɗa maɓɓoode dammugal."@@Laasol tataɓol wi'i, "Rayyam yaasi suudu."
P13	Ɓoosaaru heɓtii Juleyka ƴoyriindu.@@Ndu doggi, ndu doggi haa ndu yottii seele. @@Ammaa sakiraaɓe Juleyka worɓe e ndeenunoondu bee cabbi.@@Ɓoosaaru ndun sey wayliti doggi, ndu meetaayi yi'eego fahin.
