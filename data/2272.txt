P1	Tshivenda I a vhuya mulobilo,  Kolongonya! Vho-mmane mbebeni,  Kolongonya! Nndu khulu dzi na biko,  Kolongonya! English Here the rain comes,  pouring down,  Kolongonya! Carry me on your back,  aunty,  Kolongonya! Let me shelter in the verandah,  Kolongonya! Big houses are warm,  Kolongonya!
