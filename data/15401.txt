desc:	Enkerai nayawua eseriani.

page_text:

P1	Netii ena tomononi nanapita enkerai enye entim alo aitau irganayio.
P2	  Ore te ntim netum olchani ooata irganayio ooto.
P3	Neirajie ninye enkerai enye nairura abori olchani,  neked ninye olchani.
P4	Nelotu olmurrani lo likae osho neingasia ninye oleng'neikilikwan kweon nejo,  "Koree ngotonye ena kerai?"
P5	Nejo ninye airrugo neinyoyie enkerai oltuala loo maasaa naishopito ninye temurt enye.
P6	Nisho ninye enkerai edalare imasaa enyanak neshipakino ninye enkerai oleng adalare nena masaa ilo murrani.
P7	Neingorr ninye ngotonye abori ainguraa ajo kanyio nagira aitakweni enkerai,  nedol ninye oltungani lemeyiolo oogira ataaniki enkerai enye.
P8	Ore pee edol ngotonye enkerai egira omurrani adalare enkerai enye neirut ninye oleng omeitiai orbene obore irganayio.
P9	Ore pee edol omurrani ajo eiruto ngotonye nejoki mintoki auresho amu kagira ake adalare enkerai ena kerai ino sidai.
P10	Inaa taa pee edou ninye entomononi to ichani.
P11	Neitayu ninye omurrani emaasai enye nabo neisho enkerai nejoki,  "Nena enkishoroto ino."
P12	Nejoki ninye ina tomononi,  "Tudumu enkerai ino orbene lino shomo ang nijoki orpayian lino pee eidur alo emurrua natii eseriani amu aishoo enkerai ino eseriani."
