page_text:

P1	Bès ɨ̀ yem bà?
P2	Yì tì n-ghɨ ìtsɨ̀yn i kuyn ma?
P3	Yì tì n-ghɨ atu àntayntayn a bomsɨ̀ ma?
P4	Yì tì n-ghɨ ibam i achi a yê'tɨna ma?
P5	Yì tì n-ghɨ a gùŋ alè' ìlɨŋ ma?
P6	Yì tì n-ghɨ ànteynɨ̀ nkwò ma?
P7	Yì tì n-ghɨ abe ma?
P8	Yì yèyn afêyn!
