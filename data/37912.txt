page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a farmer.He was greedy.He thought to get more profit so he used unorganic things.He went to the market to sell the unorganic things.People bougth so many things from him.
P2	There were many more people working in the farm.when the people saw the crops and mud they got to know that the unorganic material was used.
P3	They told everyone working at the field.
P4	The rest of people worked hard.But the farmer continued to use the unorganic material.He was so into thinking of money that he fell asleep
P5	When he was sleeping, his wife came to his field and collected the crops which.her husband grew and she not knowing about the unorganic material
P6	She served everyone the fruits and vegetables which she bought from the field.
P7	When the family ate the unorganic material so they were like from where did you get this. After some time they were ill.They were in coma
P8	And he learnt a lesson that never to be greedy.So kids remember that you should not be greedy.THE END
