title:	Eghaimwe eriyo ki?

artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini, Danica Strydom, Catherine Groenewald, Wiehan de Jager, Carol Liddiment and Salim Kasamba
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Olufumo luno lwegeresa ku bintu ebyagaanibwa mu maka ate lulaga nti obenganda ba muganhulo inho eri banaibwe.

page_text:

P1	Eghaife eriyo endhu yetusulamu. Bbaabba n'eyagizimba.
P2	Ghaliyo ente edhitugha amata.
P3	Eghaife eriyo emiti edhireeta amaadhi agagwa. Dhitugha ebinsense.
P4	Eghaife ghaliyo ebibala. Maama ni eyabirima mu nnimiro.
P5	Eghaife eriyo ebimuli ebisanhusa amaiso.
P6	Eghaife eriyo ensawo maama gy'agema.
P7	Nze ndi n'ekiteeteeyi baaba ni eyakigula.
P8	Suuna ali ni esaati. Dhaadha ni eyagigula.
P9	Muto ghaife ali ni engaito. Songa ni eyadhugula.
P10	Byonabyona birungi inho.
