title:	Taliika ni mukagwa we
author:	Cornelius Mugulusi
artist:	Isaac Okwir
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/25475.png
desc:	Omwaayi omuto aghonezebwa okugwa mu bulwa ni mukagwa we. Ikaanhya kino bwe kyaali.

page_text:

P1	Taliika n'abomumaka ge baaviiranga ghala okuva mu kibuga.
P2	Taliika yali ne embwa ye nga ni Bukya.
Taliika ni Bukya nga ba mukago inho.
P3	Lwali lwa mukaga,Taliika ni Baidhu batwaala ebisolo okulya ku nsiko.
P4	Baidu nga ayenda kuzanha ayenga Taliika ayenda kusoma.
P5	Kunkomerero, Baidhu ni Taliika baira mu kwezaanira.
P6	Nga bali mukuzaanha,entaama dhaibwe dhabulagho.
Bombi bainhama okudhinonia.
P7	Omusege gwali gwise entaama dha Taliika.
Taliika yaatya ekubonerezebwa ise.
P8	Taliika n'okusalagho okulambaala ku muti.
Baidhu yena yaalambaala kumpimpi.
P9	Nga buzibye, Empiti dhayasiikanira ghaigulu.
Taliika yaatya inho.
P10	Taliika yaghanuka ku muti yagwa ekigwo.
Yaghulirira kerere eno kumpi inho.
P11	Eigulu bwe lyamyansa, Taliika yaalengera Baidhu nga ali kumughonhya empiti.
P12	Baidhu yoghonia Taliika.
Taliika ni Baidhu basigala mu muti okutuka munco.
