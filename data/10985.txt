title:	Nitumie Nini Kusafiri?
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Sue Kramer, Catherine Groenewald, Marion Drew, Louwrisa Blaauw, Rob Owen, Vusi Malindi, Jesse Breytenbach, Natalie Propa, Wiehan de Jager and Mango Tree
lang:	Kiswahili
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/10985.png
desc:	Mtoto anawaza juu ya kusafiri na kujiuliza ni chombo kipi mwafaka na salama cha kusafiria.

page_text:

P1	Kuna njia nyingi za kusafiri. Nichague gani?
P2	Je,  nitumie ndege?
P3	Ndege inaruka juu sana. Nami naogopa kuruka!
P4	Au nitumie helikopta?
P5	Lakini,  helikopta na ndege ni sawa!
P6	Au,  labda,  nitatumia gari la moshi.
P7	Lakini,  stesheni iko mbali sana. Nitachoka kutembea.
P8	Je,  nitumie basi?
P9	Kijiji chetu kina basi moja pekee!
P10	Labda nitatumia lori kusafiri.
P11	Lakini,  lori,  hasa,  ni la kubebea mizigo mizito.
P12	Je,  nitumie baiskeli kusafiri?
P13	Sijui ikiwa baba atanipatia baiskeli yake.
P14	Au nijaribu kusafiri kwa mashua?
P15	Lakini mimi siamini mashua sana!
P16	Aah! Najua nitakachotumia. Nitatumia trekta kusafiri!
