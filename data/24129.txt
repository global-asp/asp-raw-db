desc:	Okahokololo aka otake tu lombwele kutya mokutekula ounona onatu va tange tuu noku va pandula ngee tava longo oilonga iwa.

page_text:

P1	Okandenge kange oha ka kofa fiyo etango ta li piti.

Ame ohandi penduka alushe ongula shaashi ame omunandjungu.
P2	Ohandi piti netango.
P3	"Oove mawila wange," Meme osho ha ti.
P4	Ohandi li kosho ame mwene keshe fiku.
P5	Kutya nee omeva naakale matalala, ile opena ashike ekandanga, ohandi li kosho ashike.
P6	"Ino dimbwa oku li kusha komayoo!" Meme ta dimbulukifa nge.

"Aaye itandi dimbwa Meme," osho nda nyamukula.
P7	Eshi nda mana oku likosha, ohandi ka popifa Meekulu naTatekulu.

Handi va halele efiku liwa.
P8	Okudja opo ohandi i ndi ka djale oikutu yange yofikola.

"Ame onda kula paife Meme," handi lombwele Meme.
P9	Ondi shii oku pata eembadi doshikutu shange neemhato deenghaku dange.
P10	Okandenge kange ohandi ka lombwele keshe eshi sha ningwa kofikola.
P11	Kofikola ohandi li hongo noudiinini.
P12	Keshe efiku ohandi wanifa po oinakuwanifwa yange.

Ashike oshinima ndi hole unene, oku danauka.
