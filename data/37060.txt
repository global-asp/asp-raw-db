desc:	Owuuditeko ngahelipi omapukululo taandi ku Tate wa Abulas ? (Okahokololo aka oka totwa okundja moshinyangandalwa sheyambulepo ko Ras Abebe mongulumambo yili mu Debre Birham mu Mai 2018.

page_text:

P1	Momukunda, Okwa li muna okamati ke dina Abula.@@Ka kwa li ehole okulesha ile oku penuna membo. Okwa li okanona okananyalo. No he liumbata nai.
P2	Abula okwa li ha vakemo oimaliwa ya meme waye opo akalande ouleke. Nonande meme waye oimaliwa okwa li heiholeke, okuna ashike okuimona ndee tei kufapo.@@Konima eshi amana okulya ouleke. Abula okwa li handanauka nookaume kaye.
P3	Nokonima yefimbo, Abula okwa efapo ofikola. Oha kala ta ndanauka efiku alishe. Keumbo oha iko alushe komatango.@@Meme wa Abula's okwe shidindilika no kwa hovela okukala alimbililwa.
P4	"Okamati ketu ita keli umbata nawa nande. Kandishishi nokuli ngeenge nokofikola oko hai. Ye noho oha vakemo oimaliwa yange," Meme wa Abula's osho apopya.
P5	okwa tya komukulukadi waye, "Moshikando ta shilandula, holeka oimaliwa mokati komapandja embo. Osheshi kena efiku apenhune embo, ongheene oimaliwa ota ikala ili meameno."
P6	Efiku lalandula, Abula okwa li takongo oimaliwa ya meme waye. Okwa konga apeshe ashike inamonasha.@@Okwa tokola opo aye pomatala eli popepi opo elimonene oimaliwa.
P7	Tate waye okwa mona apa Abula kwa li tai.@@"Mumati wange, Ondishishi kutya omolwaashike toile komatala. Paife shuna meumbo ndee totale oimaliwa momambo," Tate waye osho ati.
P8	Abula okwa li asholola, Ashike okwaya ndee tatale momambo.@@Okwa hangamo oimaliwa oyo meme waye kwa li aholeka.
P9	Efiku lalandula, Okwa tala momambo natango. Ashike ka kwali munasha shaholekwamo.@@Okwaya kuTate waye ekemupule kutya omolwashike momambo kamuna oimaliwa.
P10	Tate waye okwe limemesha ndee temunyamukula, "Mumati wange, owahala okumona oimaliwa ihapu ulilandele ouleke vahapu?"@@Abula okwa nyamukula, "Osho lela, Tate."
P11	Tate waye okwa tya kuye, "Pwilikinange nawa. Lesha omambo ndee tokale hoi keetundi. Oto kamona oindjemo iwa ta i kudiilile momambo. Inosholo nande."@@"Ashi Tate, okulesha oshinima shindjuu. Omambo ota ekuningi ukale waleela. Abula osho anyamukula."
P12	konima yokafimbo, Tate wa Abula's okwa li amwena. Okwa diladila nghene ena oku twa okamona mati kaye omukumo.@@Ndele ye okwa tya ku Abula, "Na tu leshele pamwe. Ohandi ku kwafele opo umone ouyamba momambo."
