title:	Mme Anga
author:	Acherlager Lesedi Milanzi

lang:	Tshivenda

desc:	Description of a good mother

page_text:

P1	Hayani ha ha shu ndi ha vhudi. Nndu ya hashu ndi tshitanga tsho fulelwaho nga mahatsi. Mahatsi aya a ita uri mvula itshina ri si pfe nga maanda phosho nahone hu a dudela musi hutshi rothola.
P2	Ha hashu ri dzula ri vhararu. Nne,  mme anga na khaladzi anga mutuku. Khotsi anga avha ho. Mme anga vha dzulela uri vhudza uri vho tuwa u ya u dzula ha Mudzimu.
P3	Mme anga ndi a vha funa ngauri vha dzulela u mbudza zwitori zwa u takadza. Tshifhinga tshothe ndi tshi vhuya tshikoloni vha anzela u mbudzisa uri ho vha hu hani tshikoloni.
P4	Mme anga vha ri gudisa u vha na vhudele tshifhinga tshothe. Vha dzulela uri vhudza uri vhudele vhu tsini na Mudzimu.
P5	Mme anga vha ri funza u funa Mudzimu,  u di funa,  na u funa vhanwe vhathu. Nga mugivhela mme anga vha tuwa na nne makhetheni. Musi ri tshi swika vha anzela u mbudza uri ndi tea u lumelisa vhathu.
P6	Musi ndi tshi aluwa ndi toda u fana na mme anga. Ndi funze vhana vhanga zwothe zwe mme anga vha npfudza zwone.
