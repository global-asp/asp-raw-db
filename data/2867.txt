page_text:

P1	Kapere aba mu hasisira.
P2	Kawere aba mu nyumba eye sonda ene.
P3	Enyumba yino yiri n'esonda ebiribiri ejagagana.
P4	Ahasolya he nyumba yino he sonda edatu.
P5	Omusinde ono ali hutongola enyumba yiye.
P6	Enyumba yino pyaha.
P7	Enyumba yino kayire.
P8	Kale mbanga nindi mu nyumba hya yino!
