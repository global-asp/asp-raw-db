page_text:

P1	Fana ne eka ye nibatula Debre birhan, ekibuga kitungukire omuli Ethiopia. Fana ali omu kibina kya kashatu. Aine omutima mulungi hamwe na magezi.
P2	Fana nakunda ebisholo. Aine kappa, enkoko ibili, embuzi hamwe na ekombe. namala obwile nabyo, akubilisa hanwe nokuzana nabyo
P3	Olunaku lumwe, Fana aka nazana ahishomero na minwanyi gyeye yaleba abana bakulekyela amabale aha nkombe.
"ahabwenki nibatela enkombe?'' yayebuza.
P4	Yalekyelaho kuzana yayiluka ahi abaana abakulekyela amabale bali. Okubanza banwani beye tibakyengili ekyili kubaho, kubahelize bamukulatila. ''mulekyelaho okutela amabale,''  Fana yayomba
P5	Fana yakwata enkombe ibili ezili kushasha. Yaleba ebisebe aha mapapa. yashalaho kuzitwala omuka kuzilebelela
P6	Yazilisa. Omumwazo yagambila oba eka ye ekiyalebile ne kiyakozile omumushana nokuyaziyambile.
P7	Omukasheshe Fana nabazile be baza omwilariro batunga omubazi. Ahanyima yenaku nkye, ebisebe byakila. Fana akahulila yashemererwa.
P8	Fananatula nagambila banwani beye, ''ninkunda ebisholo. Ebisholo nibitugasa kandi nibinwani bitu. twine kubilebelela hamwe nokubikuma.''
