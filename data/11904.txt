title:	Enffa Y’Ensekere

desc:	Abavubuka babiri abaali banonnya ente zaabwe

page_text:

P1	Edda ennyo,  waaliwo abavubuka babiri abaabulwako ente zaabwe. Baanoonya ente,  obudde ne bubazibako.
P2	Obudde nga buzibye,  baalengera ekitangaala mu nnyumba. Bakkiriziganya okugendayo basuzibwe.
P3	Baakonkona ku nju, oluggi nelweggula,  ne bayingira. Abavubuka tebaasangamu muntu yenna,  wabula baawulira eddoboozi eribalamusa.
P4	Eddoboozi lyagamba,  "Ndi nsekere. Musaanukule ensuwa muly'emmere. Musaanukule n'akasuumbi munywe omubisi."
P5	Oluvannyuma ensekere n'eyambala eddiba lyayo,  n'efuluma.
P6	Abavubuka baalya,  ne banywa era ne beeyanza. Nebaafuluma ennyumba.
P7	Nga bakakufuluma,  baafuna ekirowoozo ekibi ne bagamba,  "Tekisoboka ensekere okutuwa emmere." Era amangu ago bakkiriziganya okuddayo batte ensekere.
P8	Baddayo mu nnyumba ne batta ensekere. Amangu ddala ensekere yafa,  n'ennymba n'esaanawo! Beesanga bali mu kyangaala.
