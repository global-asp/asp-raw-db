title:	Magezi ni Kunya

desc:	Olufumo olunhuvu nga lugema ku mwikwamo ne emigaso egiva muntole ya akalo.

page_text:

P1	Eira einho,Magezi ni kunya nga bagabana ebyokulya.
P2	Magezi yakoba,"Tulimire ghalala nga bwetukikola mu kulya."
P3	Magezi yaakoba Kunya,"Ighe lima nze ndhakusimba."
P4	Magezi yakooba Kunya,"Ighe simba nze ndha kudhuba."
P5	Nga obulo bwenze, Kunya yasaba Magezi okubukesa.
P6	Magezi yaakoba,"Ighe kesa, nze ndhakubuta mu kyagi."
P7	Magezi yali mugayavu.
kulwe ekyo Kunya yaamugha entole ya akalo ndala yonka
P8	Magezi yatwala entole yakalo eyo.
Yayagana enkoko.
P9	Yaagha Magezi eigi.
P10	Magezi yayagana abaana.
Bamenha eigi.
P11	Abaana baamugha omuyembe.
P12	Magezi yayagana enkusu.
Enkusu yalya omuyembe era yamugha eiviiri.
P13	Enhandha yatwala eiviiri,era yamughaku amaadhi.
P14	Magezi yakozesa amaadhi okuziikirisa abamanda omuliiro.
P15	Abatemi bamanda bamugha empasa.
P16	Magezi yayagana abakindhagi.
Baamenhamu empasa.
P17	Magezi yafuna omutwe gwe ente enhume no omukira.
Bona Magezi kyeyakola!
P18	Yayasikana,"Ente yakyabazinga eraliire mubityanka!"
P19	Abantu mwidhe tuyambe.
"Musikule omukiira no omutwe,"Magezi bweyakoba.
P20	Magezi yakoba abantu,"Mumpe ente dhaimwe ndhighe kyabazinga."
