page_text:

P1	Neymar obuyahikire bufirika,  akahikira murubuga rwa Nigeria. Aba Nigeria. Aba Nigeria bamugamba aboleke akakoolya kokuzanisa omupiira nokozesa haiguru yekigere kokulereza.
P2	Obuyarugire Nigeria,  akagenda Ghana kwoleka akakoolya kokuhereza omupiira nakozesa munda yekigere. Feki kikasemeza muno abantu baahana.
P3	Obwakaba akyali Ghana,  Neymar yatunga kuralizibwa kuruga mu South Afirika kwoleka kutega omupiira nokozesa ekigere kyomumaiso.
P4	Kuruga mu South Afirika,  yagenda Chad obwakaba ali Chad akoleka kutega omupiira nakozesa orubaju rwekigere.
P5	Naymar obuyahikire DR Congo,  akatukumiza ihanga nakakoolya kokulega omupiira nakozesa ekibero.
P6	Abanya Uganda abasungire Neymar mukango bakacwamu kumuraliza kwoleka akakoolya kokwihaho omupiira.
P7	Amakuru gararanga mubuturuka musana byo Afirika yoona.
P8	Sabiiti egi Abatanzaniya bayeta Neymar kwijka kwoleka akakoolyo kokuhereza omupiira nokozesa omutwe.
P9	Hanyima yagenda mu Rwanda kwoleka akakoolyo kokuhereza omupiira nakozesa ekigere kyaheru.
P10	Hanyima ya Rwanda,  Neymar yamalirirra orubungorwe mu South Sudan,  mbere yayolekere akakoolyo kokukwata omupiira mugolo akaguleera namaani gukagenda.
