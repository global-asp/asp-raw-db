page_text:

P1	My first day to school ,I was not willing to be taken to school@I could not leave my mother ..I cried but nothing happened@That day I felt bad.
P2	We played with my friends,and really enjoyed.@My first friend whom I made was called Keegen@I loved him so much how caring and responsible he was.
P3	After finishing with school my mother came to pick me@I was so happy.
P4	From that day I started going to school alone .because had already created my friends who could help me by giving me directions in school.
