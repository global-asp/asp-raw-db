title:	UMgcini nomzi wakhe wezilwanyana
author:	Bukeka  Mahlangabeza and Neliswa Pretty Magangxa

desc:	Kweli baliizilwanyana ezincinci ezingenabazali ezithe zasindiswa zifika kumzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci. Ezi zilwanyana zincinci ziyongiwa zize zikhathalelwe ngumntu oyincutshe ekwenzeni oko kunye neqela lakhe labancedisi.

page_text:

P1	Eli libali likaMgcini kunye nomzi wakhe wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci ezingenabazali.
P2	EyeThupha ibiyinyanga exakekileyo kuMgcini kwakunye nabancedisi bakhe kumzi wakhe wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci.
P3	UMkite ononyaka omnye wafika kuqala.@ @Wafika ngesigadla esidala esiluhlaza.
P4	UMgcini nabancedisi bakhe bamsizela kakhulu uMkite. @@Wayebhitye kakhulu yaye ekhathazekile.
P5	Kodwa ngokukhawuleza uMkite waba namandla. @@Waqalisa ukudlala nezinye izilwanyana kumzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci.
P6	Ngenye imini ngeyeThupha, kwafika inqwelo-ntaka kulo mzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci.
P7	UMgcini nabancedisi bakhe baya phandle ngokukhawuleza. @@Kule nqwelo-ntaka kwakukho ithole lendlovu elalambathiswe ngengubo.
P8	YayinguNdiwa. @@Wayeneentsuku ezintlanu ezelwe. @@UMgcini wamncancisa ibhotile uNdiwa.
P9	Inkedama yesithathu kuloo nyanga yayinguMaliya. @@Wafika kulo mzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci ngetrektara ebomvu. @@Wayeneenyanga ezintandathu ezelwe.
P10	UMaliya, umkhombe, waba nobuhlobo no-Enkare, ithole lenyathi. @@Babesiya kuyo yonke indawo kunye.
P11	Ngabusuku buthile, wonke umntu wavuswa kukufika kuka-Ambiya.
P12	U-Ambiya wayeneenyanga ezintlanu ezelwe. @@Wayebuthathaka emva kohambo lwakhe olude.
P13	Ngenxa yentamo yakhe ende, uMgcini wayeyibambela phezulu ibhotile ka-Ambiya xa emncancisa.
P14	Amawele abhityilelyo amabini, u-Abheyi noMoyiti bafika phakathi kweyeThupha. @@La maqhude ayeneeyure ezimbalwa ezelwe. @@Ayegula kakhulu.
P15	Wonke umntu wayekhathazekile kukusweleka kuka-Abheyi emva kweveki enye efikile kumzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci.@@Kodwa uMgcini nabancedisi bakhe bamsindisa uMoyiti.
P16	Iinkedama zokugqibela ukufika yayinguKopi, uKepi kwakunye noKeji.@@La mathole engonyama ayeneeveki ezimbini ezelwe.  @@Ayelambile kakhulu ngelixa efika.
P17	UMgcini nabancedisi bakhe babezithanda zonke izilwanyana, nezo zazigeza.
P18	Esona sasigeza yayinguLoboliya.@@ULoboliya wayethanda ukufihla izipekisi zikaMgcini.
P19	Abantu kulo mzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci basebenza nzima ukukhathalela ezi zilwanyana. @@Banethemba lokuba ngenye imini ezi nkedama zakukwazi ukuzikhathalela ngokwazo. Zize ke zibuyele endle.
P20	Zazingakanani yaye zazingaphi? Funda ibali kwakhona, uze uphendule le mibuzo.@@1. Zingaphi izilwanyana ezafika kumzi wokugcina izilwanyana ezincinci ngenyanga yeThupha? 2. Ngubani owafika kuqala ngala nyanga? 3. Sasinangaphi esi silwanyana? 4. Ngoobani abafika mva? Zazinangaphiezi zilwanyana? 5. Zeziphi ezona zilwanyana zincinci ezafikayo ngeyeThupha? 6. Zazingakanani? 7. Sesiphi esona silwanyana sidala ukufika ngeyeThupha?
