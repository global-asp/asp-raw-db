page_text:

P1	Time is a parcel.
P2	In this parcel of time, you find more parcels.
P3	A parcel is a day.@@A day is 86,400 seconds.
P4	You must unwrap time and use it well.@@Learn new things.
P5	Eat.@Play.@Rest.@Dream.@Think.
P6	Plant a tree, a forest grows. @@Solve a problem.
P7	When your money grows,@save some, @spend some, @share some.
P8	If you fail, start again.@@Time wraps gifts as days.
P9	Monday and Tuesday.@Wednesday and Thursday.@Friday and Saturday, even Sunday.
P10	These are special friends.@@Enjoy their friendship.
P11	When the clock says tick tock remember my words.
P12	Time is a peculiar parcel, a unique gift.@@Use it well.
