P1	Once upon a time there was a girl named Anita and her younger sister Nishi. Nishi was very kind and a good girl but Anita was very Cruel to Nishi.One day Nishi Found a magical pencil and when she draws something it becomes real!
P2	When Anita saw that a magical pencil is of Nisha she thought of a wicked plan and snaches the pencil from Nisha.Then Anita drawed an apple and it become real and she ate it.Then a voice came out from Anita room.
P3	He said - Anita you were always jealous of Nisha now you will be punished for it. Then all things from Anita room disappeared! and Anita said zorry to her and then all things were appeared again and they lived happily ever after
