title:	Wroth marach
author:	Catherine  Amia
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Alur
level:	First words

desc:	Dong ingyeyo pirango madyel bekwero jutele!

page_text:

P1	Dyel ubedu wroth pa leyi kudu wingu.
P2	Dyel uwachigi nia eleku lek moku. Gizo giwinju.
P3	Lek ne umaku kum kech.@@"Wa bitimunyanedi?" Kajango upenju.
P4	Gwenu ku mbata giwakyu, "Wa kan cham i udi pa wroth."
P5	Wroth madyel uwachu, "Wu twi ngati mu kwero lubu chik."
P6	Kare milondu wroth utuch. Gilondu kajango
P7	Dyel nang umitu ngo kajango ubed woth. "An re ma wroth."
P8	Dyel uteru ngo cham. Dhyang upenju, "Wabi timunyanedi?"
P9	Leyi kud uwinyu uwechu karachelu.@@Ngey cwiny umakugi ikum dyel.
P10	Gwok uwachu, "Abedu kude ikare mebedu wroth."
P11	Urombu uwachu, "Amiku ni awiya pare pamba mabechu."
P12	Upegu uwak, "Kare mebedu wroth, anenu bang podhu pare."
P13	"Dyel ogenu ebibedu wroth kare zo?" Urombu upenju.
P14	Leyi uyiyo nia dyel nang mak uter cham ni wrothi kajango.
P15	Leyi uromu ku dyel. Dyel uwachu, "Naka abi bedu wroth."
P16	Wabi twiyi wagam wa teri iba wroth manyen."
P17	Diyang utellu dyel kanitere ipachu pa wroth. Leyi mukende ukok kud anyonga.
P18	Eno uketho dyel bekwero kawotho ka jubetele.
