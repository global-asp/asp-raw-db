title:	Ibhasi Enkulu Eluhlaza Okwe Sibhakabhaka
author:	Nokuthula Zondi
artist:	
lang:	isiZulu

desc:	U-Ebei nomama wakhe balindele ibhasi enkulu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ezobayisa edolobheni. Kodwa ibhasi eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ifile. Bazofika kanjani edolobheni?

page_text:

P1	Yayiyodwa ibhasi edolobheni lika-Ebei. Yayinkulu futhi iluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Yayinomsindo omkhulu.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga umama ka-Ebei wathi,  "Kusasa sizokuya edolobheni siyothenga umfaniswano wakho."
P3	U-Ebei wajabula kakhulu. Babezohamba ngebhasi enkulu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Kwakungavumi ukuba alale ebusuku.
P4	U-Ebei wayesevele esegqokile lapho umama wakhe engena ezomvusa.
P5	U-Ebei nomama wakhe baya esitobhini. Balindela ibhasi enkulu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Kodwa ibhasi ayizange ifike.
P6	Abanye abantu bafika esitobhini. Babekhononda ngoba ibhasi yayingasigcinanga isikhathi. "Ngabe ibhasi isishiyile?" Babebuza.
P7	U-Ebei wakhathazeka. "Ngeke sikwazi ukuya edolobheni. Ngeke ngikwazi ukuthola umfaniswano wami," ecabanga.
P8	Abanye abantu baduba babuyela emakhaya. Kodwa u-Ebei wayekhala engafuni ukuya ekhaya. Kodwa umama wakhe wayemduduza ethi,  "Sizolinda isikhashana eside."
P9	Masishane,  bezwa umsindo. Babona izintuli emoyeni. Yayiza ibhasi!
P10	Kodwa le bhasi yayingeluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Yiyingenkulu. Le bhasi yayibomvu futhi incane. Abantu ababelindile bayibuka ibhasi. Abazange bagibele.
P11	"Ngenani! "Ngenani!" kumemeza umshayeli ethi,  "Sishiywe isikhathi namuhla.
P12	U-Ebei nomama wakhe bangena kuqala. Ngesikhashana wonke umuntu wangena ebhasini elibomvu elincane.
P13	U-Ebei wabuka phandle ngefasitela. Wabona abanye abantu abaningi esitobhini.
P14	Abanye abantu abaningi babegijima bejahe ukugibela ibhasi. Kodwa basebeshiywe isikhathi. Ibhasi yase igcwele. Ibhasi ebomvu yahamba yaya edolobheni.
P15	"Iphi ibhasi enkulu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka?"  Kubuza umama ka Ebei. "Ifile," kuphendula umshayeli. "Siyayilungisa,  izobakhona kusasa," kusho umshayeli.
P16	U-Ebei wayengenandaba nombala webhasi. Wayengenandaba nobukhulu bebhasi. Wayejabuliswa ukuthi yayiya edolobheni.
