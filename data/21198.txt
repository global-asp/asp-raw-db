title:	Buugi
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21198.png
desc:	Maama azirawo era Bbaabba afumba buugi wa kyankyo.

page_text:

P1	Kazaana ali kwetegekera isomero.
P2	Bbaabba ali kufumba buugi
  wa kyankyo.
P3	“Maama naasinga
  kufumba buugi!” Kazaanha bwa akoba.
P4	Buugi agha Kazaana amaani okuzaanha ku isomero.
P5	Obuugi buyiire.
P6	Bbaabba ayuwamu amata, no okwenda.
P7	Kazaana akoba, “Bbaabba, ndoghooza mu buugi muno timunhoze sukaali.”
P8	Kazaana aloghooza, “Buugi wa Maama aghoma
  okusingaku owa Bbaabba.”
P9	Kazaana yakoba, “Bbaaba, nnhongera ku sukaali.”
P10	Bbaabba ayongera Kazaana sukaali.
P11	Maama Nze! Bbaabba,
  otaire omuunhu mu buugi wange!”
P12	Kazaana yaabuuza, “Bbaabba, iye Mmaama ali
  ira li?”
