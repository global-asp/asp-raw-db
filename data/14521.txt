title:	Muhlayisi Na Vuhlayiselo Byo Hlawuleka
author:	Arnold Mushwana
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Xitsonga

desc:	Xitori xa vana va swihari lava pfuniweke no hlayisiwa loko va fika evuhlayiselweni bya swihari swo pfumala vatswari. Swihari leswi swi hlayisiwa hi munhu wo hlawuleka na ntlawa wa yena.

page_text:

P1	Lexi i xitori xa muhlayisi na vuhlayiselo bya swihari swo pfumala vatswari.
P2	Nhweti ya ku hela mphesaphesa evuhlayiselweni. Nomsa wa lembe rin'we hi yena wo sungula ku fika. A larile na ku hlundzuka.
P3	Endzhaku ka masiku Nomsa a tiyela. A sungula ku tlanga na swihari swin'wana.
P4	Siku rin'wana enhwetini ku fikile xihahampfhuka evuhlayiselweni.
P5	Endzeni ka xihahampfhuka a ku ri na xindlopfana xi funengetiwile nkumba lowukulu. Vito ra xona i Ndzima.
P6	Ndzima a funengele nkumba. A ri na ntlhanu wa masiku. Muhlayisi a mamisa Ndzima hi bodhlela.
P7	Hi vusiku byin'wana un'wana na un'wana u pfuxiwile hi ku fika ka Ambiya.
P8	Ambiya a ri na ntlhanu wa tinhweti. A heleriwile hi matimba endzhaku ko famba nkarhi wo leha.
P9	Hikwalaho ka nkolo wa yena wo leha,  muhlayisi u khomile bodhlela a ri yisa ehenhla ku n'wi mamisa.
P10	Swihari swin'wana leswi fikeke nhweti yaleyo ku vile swinghalana. Mavito ya swona i Kopi,  Kepi na Kipi.
P11	Muhlayisi u rhandza swihari hinkwaswo. Na leswi phungelaka.
P12	Vanhu va e vuhlayiselweni va tirha hi matimba. Va tshemba leswaku siku rin'wana swihari leswo pfumala vatswari swi ta tihlayisa. Swi ta tlhelela enhoveni.
