title:	Kungani Sicoco Sibe Sibi
author:	Busile Ndhlovu
artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	siSwati
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/12610.png
desc:	Kube sicoco salalela ngabe sihle njengemgolololo!

page_text:

P1	Eminyekeni leminyenti leyengcile,  umgololo nesicoco bewungebangani. Ngalelinye lilanga tancuma kuyobuka tintfombi edolobheni.
P2	Sicoco besinemona nesikhumba semgololo lesicwebetelako nalesihle.
P3	Satsi: Wente ngani kutsi kute ube muhle kangaka? Buka mine,  Ngimubi kakhulu. Ngingenta njani ngesikhumba sami?
P4	Umgololo watsi: Lalela lapha! Faka emanti ebhodweni bese uwabeka emmlilweni. Ngemva kwaloko ubese u.
P5	Kepha sicoco satsi: Sengiyati,  ngiyati. Ngicela usale ubindza mngani wami. Sicoco sase siyahamba.
P6	Sitsite nasifika ekhaya safaka emanti ebhodweni. Sase siwafaka emlilweni.
P7	Atsite emanti nasacala kubila,  satiphosela ekhatsi ebhodweni.
P8	Sikhumba saso sasha,  umsila waso wabuye wajubeka.
P9	Kun ekutsi sibe sihle njengemgololo,  saba sibi kakhulu kundlula kucala!
