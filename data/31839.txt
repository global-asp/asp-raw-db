title:	Ekebusi amo ne'Esese amo nogoaka Erangi
author:	Gladys Nyachieo
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Ekegusii
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ekebusi neEsese nigo bire abasani. Nigo bibwate echeche nainde bikogoka gokora ebinto bionsi amo.

page_text:

P1	Eke nekebusi.@@Eye nesese.
P2	Ekebusi ne'Esese nigo bitagete gochora.@@Ekebusi ne'Esese nigo bitagete goaka erangi.@@Ekebusi ne'Esese nigo bitagete koroisia ebirondoria.@@Esese yang'ea.@@Ekoira ekaramu nerisakara. Risakara nigo rire esikweya.
P3	Ritang'ani Esese yachora mochoro.@@Mochoro noomobere bweekebusi.@@ Aya, nigo ere raisi!
P4	Rende yachora eturayango igoro ya mochoro.@@Chiturayango ibere namato yekebusi nainde yachora eturayango emwamu ime ya mochoro.@@Eturayango emwamu nemioro yekebusi nainde yachora omositari igoro yemioro.
P5	Bono yachora amaiso amo nebioge biekebusi.@@Amaiso nebitone.@@Ebioge nemesitari eyio ebere.@@Aya, raisi igo!
P6	Ekero ekio yachora omonwa bweekebusi.@@Omonwa noomositari.@@Nigo okororekana buna enuguta 'w' nainde yachora etuki yeekebusi.@@Chituki isato ase okobee amo naisato ase okorio.
P7	Omochoro oisirie koera.@@Yachora amaboka na amagoro ye'ekebusi.@@Ebiara netanania nainde yachora obosuguni bweekebusia.@@@Obosugunia noomositaria omotambe.
P8	Omoerio yachora emecherere omobere bwekebusi nainde yagiaka erangi yeritunda.@@Omochoro bwerire.@@Aya, eyio ekogokia!
P9	Bono neribaga ria Ekebusi. @@Yaira ekaramu nerisangara.@@Risangara nigo rireturayango.
P10	Ritang'ani Ekebusi giachora omochoro.@@Mochoro nomobere bwesese.@@Aya, igo nari buya!
P11	Nainde giachora emebwekano ebere ememwamu igoro ya mochoro.@@Emebwekano eyio ememwamu na amato yesese nainde giachora omwetananio ime yamochoro.@@Omwetananio oyio omomwamu nemioro yesese nainde giachoro omositari igoro yemioro.
P12	Bono giachora amaiso nebioge biesese.@@Amaiso nebitonebibere.@@Ebioge nemesitari ebere.@@Aya, eyio neng'ikeranio.
P13	Nainde yachora omonwa bwesese.@@Omonwa nomositari.@@Omobwekanio bweenuguta 'w' nainde gioachora ebitone goetanana omonwa bwesese.@@Bitato ase okobee na bitato ase okorio.
P14	Omochoro oisire koera.@@Giachora amaboko naamagoro yesese.@@Amagoro naamaboko nemesitari nainde giachora ebiara biesese.@@Ebiara nomoetananio nainde giachora ekemicha giesese.@@Ekemicha nigo kebuekaine rioga.
P15	Omoerio giachora emietananio ememwamu omobere bwesese igoro nainde giaka omobere erangi yamayaye. @@Rende omochoro bwerire.@@Aya! Eke nekiogokumia!
P16	Kwaeri kwaeri Ekebusi.@@Kwaeri kwaeri ogochora gwekebusi.@@Kwaeri kwaeri Esese.@@Kwaeri kwaeri ogochora gwesese.
