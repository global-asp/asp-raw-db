desc:	This story asks us to count how many insects

page_text:

P1	Osama ili syi mauu manata?
P2	Nyunyi inya syi nzwau syiana?
P3	Ngii Itatu syimauu mana ata?
P4	Nzou itano syi mbya syianata?
P5	Ngiti monza syi na matu manaata?
P6	Makuli atatu me na mithe yanaata?
P7	Mbaka thanzatu syi na metho manata?
P8	Ngongoo imwe ii mayu manaata?
