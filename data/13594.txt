title:	Omutwe Gwange

page_text:

P1	Ese tambisa amagulu ohugenda natiina hiisi aŋanenda,  ohuduluma embiro,  ŋalala n'ohusamba omupiira. I use my feet to move to wherever I want,  run and kick the ball.
P2	Aye bbe. Huuse omutwe njogusinga amagulu. However,  to me the head is more useful than the feet.
P3	Ese tambisa engalo ohulya ebiryo,  ohuhola emirimo ŋalala n'ohusaagula. I use the arm to eat food,  do work and dig,  wash and sweep.
P4	More still,  to me the head is better than the arm due to five reasons: Aye bbe. Huuse omutwe njogusinga engalo. Gusinga olw'ebiitu bino.
P5	Omutwe gusinga kubanga guliho emoni. Kozesa emoni ohubona. The head is better because it has the eyes on it. I use the eyes to see.
P6	Omutwe guliho esuulu. Kozesa esuulu ohuŋunyira ahaboobo oba ehibulu. The head has the nose on it. I use the nose to smell any scent.
P7	Omutwe guliho amatwi. Amatwi nj'agakozesa ohuŋulira. The head has ears on it. I use the ears to hear.
P8	.
P9	Omutwe njo gufuga omubiiri mubyosibyosi ebiguhola. The head is the controller of all actions in the whole body!
