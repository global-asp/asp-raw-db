title:	Onger Kede Nyang
author:	Agnes Kabonesa
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Kumam
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Epone kame Onger oko konyo kede kwo mere kur Nyang chame.

page_text:

P1	Onger oriamo kede Nyang ooko me nam.
P2	Onger oko penye be, in ibedo tuai?
Nyang oko dwoko be ii nam.
P3	Nyang oko penyo Onger be in ibedo tuai? Onger oko dwoko be i wii yen.
P4	Nyang oko penye be itwero kwang?
P5	Likatwero, di Onger dwoko, apwonyi
P6	Wan obedo adyereson. Kur ibed kede lworo, Nyang oko yamo ne Onger.
P7	Onger, nerunango tuo. Emito ringo, Nyang okoyamo
P8	Onger tiye kede lworo, pien Nyang twero came.
P9	Onger mito lwii ki kakame Nyang tiye iye.
P10	Onger tiye kede ngec
P11	Ayaro mini cunya, Onger oko cikere.
P12	Di Onger oko yamo be cunya nono kom tiye iyat iiye itela.
P13	Itwero ot kwanyo na cunyi nono? Nyang oko penyere, eba Onger oko dwoko
P14	Nyang oko ikwang di edok iye itela. Onger di ringo i wii yene.
P15	Likame itye nyawota, ingala! Nyang oko yamo.
P16	Wan liko tiye awotino. Imito cama! di Onger dwoko.
