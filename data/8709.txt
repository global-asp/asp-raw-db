desc:	Bikwaro bya Lebe bili  bibombi naabi

page_text:

P1	Lebe alanga ari" Maayi yiitsa ubone. Bikwaro byase bili bibombi naabi khwise."Maayi ari, lekha imbone.
P2	Lebe ari " Sitweya syase sili sibombi naabi." Maayi ari, "Ngali nga nuloma. "Namasa anyala wayila sitweya syowo.''.
P3	Lebe ari, "Boona ilonyi yase iya jiini ili imombi naabi."Maayi ari, 'inga nuuloma, ili imombi naabi. Namasa anyala wakiyila.
P4	Lebe ari, khatisyati khase khabombi naabi,  Maayi ari, "Inga nulooma" "Namasa nyala waakhayila."
P5	Lebe ari, "Kumusweta kwase kumubombi naabi. Maayi ari," ngali" kuli kumubombi. "Namasa anyala wakuyila.
P6	Lebe ari, "Ikoti yase iyefula yabomba naabi." Maayi ari, "Ngali yabomba nabi. Namasa anyala khukiyila."
P7	Lebe ari, "Boona tsisokisi tsatse, tsili tsimombi naabi. "Maayi ari, "Ngali tsili tsimombi,  Namasa anyala khutsiyila."
P8	Lebe ari, ''bona bilalo byase bili bibombi naabi." Maayi ari, "Ngali bili bibombi. Namasa anyala khubiyila.
P9	Lebe ari, "Namasa ulini bikwaro biikali. Namasa ari, "Tawe biikwaro ibyo byosi sibitsola ta. Ingangabilamo busa.
