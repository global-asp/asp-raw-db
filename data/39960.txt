title:	The evil stepmother

page_text:

P1	Sandra and Victor are twins. They are very nice children. Unfortunately, their mother died while giving birth to them. @@Their father had to marry again. The name of their stepmother is Milka.
P2	Their stepmother loved Victor more than Sandra. @@Sandra was always cleaning the utensils and doing all the house work.@@Victor was always playing.
P3	Milka also treated Sandra badly. She beat her and spoke badly about her. @@One day Victor saw his sister being beaten. He went to the nearest police station to report his stepmother to the police. @@The police went immediately to their home.
P4	When they arrived they saw the stepmother beating Sandra with a belt. The chief warn her and also told Sandra to forgive her. The cheif ensure that Sandra was given right  to education. She became doctor. One day their stepmother was sick.
P5	The mother was taken to Sandra's clinic to be treated until she was well. Sandra went to London for further studies to become a better doctor.
P6	When she came back from London, she was very beautiful and had a partner. They got married in a big beautiful wedding.
