page_text:

P1	Hii ni kamba ya kuruka. This is a skipping rope.
P2	Mumbi anaruka kamba pekee yake. Mumbi is skipping the rope alone.
P3	Mumbi ameshikiwa kamba na rafiki yake. Mumbiâ€™ s friend is holding the rope for her.
P4	Sasa Mumbi anasoma kitabu. Mumbi is now reading a book.
P5	Mumbi anawaaga rafiki zake. Mumbi is bidding her friends goodbye.
P6	Mumbi is washing utensils in the kitchen. Mumbi anaosha vyomba jikoni.
P7	Mumbi anaandika ubaoni. Mumbi is writing on the board.
P8	Sasa Mumbi anakula matunda. Mumbi is now eating friut.
P9	Sasa ni usiku. Mumbi analala. Now,  it is night time. Mumbi is sleeping.
P10	Mama anamzungumzia Mumbi. Mother is talking to Mumbi.
P11	Mumbi anafikiria. Mumbi is thinking.
