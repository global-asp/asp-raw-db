desc:	U-Alake mntazanyana othandekako othanda ukuba neemvakatjhi nokwazi ukwenza iimvakatjhi zizithole zamukelekile.

page_text:

P1	Koko, koko!@@Kunetjhada elikhulu emnyango.
P2	U-Alake uthabe kwamanikelela.@ @"Ungubani?" utjho abuza.@@"Mma sinesivakatjhi!"
P3	"Awa kulungile," kuphendula unina. @@"Loyo kufanele kube nguMma uHassan. Ngibawa umvulele umnyango."
P4	U-Alake uvula umnyango anokumomotheka. "Siyakwamukela mma, ngena ngaphakathi. Umma uyeza uzokuba nawe msinyazana."@@UMma uHassan uqala u-Alake naye uyamomotheka.
P5	Ngingakutholela okuselwako?" kubuza u-Alake.@@"Awa ingasi okwanje, ngiyathokoza Alake," kuphendula uMma uHassan.
P6	Lotjha Mma uHassan," kutjho unina ka-Alake alotjhisa. "Ngiyancancabeza ngokukulindisa."@@"Awa umraro awukho mnganami, u-Alake bekangitjhejile."
P7	Unina ka-Alake wamomotheka.@@Iye, u-Alake  mntazanyana omncani otjheja abantu kuhle, yeke ngiyazikhakhazisa ngaye," utjho kuMma uHassan.
