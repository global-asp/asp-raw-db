title:	Joy Agenda Ku Somero
author:	Annet Ssebaggala
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/9056.png
desc:	Olugero luno lukwata ku Joy nga yetetekateeka okugenda ku somero nga ayambibwako maama we.

page_text:

P1	Joy yazannya olunaku lwonna.
P2	Maama we yamugamba ayoze.
P3	Yakwata akasenya ke.
P4	Maama we yamuteera amazzi mu baafu.
P5	Yesimuula mu maaso.
P6	Yagenda ne yebaka wamu ne muganda we.
P7	Ku makya yakeera nasenya amannyo.
P8	Maama we yamuyambako okwambala.
P9	Yasiba obuguwa bwengatto ze.
P10	Joy agenda ku somero.
P11	Joy ali mu Kibiina.
