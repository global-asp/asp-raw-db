page_text:

P1	Imibala eyisikhombisa yothingo lwenkosazana.
P2	Owokuqala ubomvu.
P3	USenzo ugqoke ihembe eilibovu.
P4	Owesibili usawolintshi.
P5	Yini esawolintshi kulesithombe.
P6	Owesithathu uphuzi.
P7	Yini ephuzi kulesithombe.
P8	Owesine uluhlaza njengotshani.
P9	Yini eluhlaza njengotshani kulesithombe.
P10	Owesihlanu uluhlaza njengesibhakabhaka.
P11	Yini eluhlaza njengesibhakabhaka kulesithombe.
P12	Owesithupha uluhlaza njengesibhakabhaka kakhulu.
P13	Yini eluhlaza njengesibhakabhaka kakhulu kulesithombe.
P14	Owesikhombisa unsomu.
P15	Yini ensomu kulesithombe.
P16	Yimiphi imibala eyisikhombisa yothingo lwenkosazana.
