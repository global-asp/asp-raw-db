desc:	A poor girl who started  hawking to earn some money to enable her go to school is almost raped. This story was created at the IDGC Bauchi 2020 Celebration.

page_text:

P1	Opeyemi was a good and intelligent girl from a good home.
P2	Her parents wanted her to go to school but were poor.
P3	Opeyemi told her parents, "I would like to start school."
P4	But we don't have money, " they told her.
P5	Opeyemi borrowed some money from her friends to start selling bananas.
P6	A man bought bananas and tricked her to follow him.
P7	Once they reached an unknown place, he tried to rape her.
P8	She shouted and some people rescued her. They took her home.
