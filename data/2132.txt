desc:	In this story we look at the different kinds of teeth animals have and what happens to our teeth when we start to grow up.

page_text:

P1	King'angi ki mayo manene.
P2	Munyambu wi mayo manene onaw'o.
P3	Ng'ombe yi maeo mevinya.
P4	Masuni mai mayo.
P5	Mbiya syi mayo manene mambee.
P6	Makuyu me mayo moii.
P7	Nethiyawa na mayo maingi.
P8	Nayuyu ndina mayo maingi.
