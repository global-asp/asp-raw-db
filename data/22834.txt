desc:	Omusatsa nende omukhasiwe balinda

page_text:

P1	Omundu, Italanyi, Imbwa, Inzukha, Likhokho nende Isishiri batsia okhulwana.
P2	Omusatsa nende omukhasi bafuchirira okhuberera ing'ombe yabu.
P3	Buli mundu yenya okhulia eshiayachama.
P4	Omusatsa nende omukhasi bahelesia buli omundu eshiayenyanga okhulia.
P5	Lwabamala okhulia, bakona obulayi.

Nibushia, nibabwao.
P6	Inyuma wamawichi amanji Italanyi yabarerera olwika olukhongo olweinzofu.
P7	Imbwa yalinda olwika olwo lulebwa tawe.
P8	Omusiani wabu omusatsa yabola, "Ndaleba olwika olwo."
P9	Olwa yatsia okhwiba, Inzukha niyimuluma nafwa.
P10	Omusatsa nende omukhasi nibabukula tsing'ombe tsia owali nafwile.
P11	Likhokho lialola abayinda nibachinjire amamondo nende efindu efiobukusi.
P12	Likhokho liabukula fiosi.

Niliyirira omusatsa nende omukhasi waye.
P13	Isishiri yachinga likunia lisiro liamamondo. Shiyahera omuyinda waye tawe.
P14	Yayirira omusatsa nende omukhasiwe likunia liamamondo ako.
P15	Omusatsa nabola, "Tsisolo  tsikhurerire efihanwa halali omundu ashiri."
P16	Balinda, balinda halali omundu shiyacherera tawe.
