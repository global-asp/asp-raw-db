id:	20943
title:	Daizy Wigitangaza
author:	Gaudah Gahima
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	Rufumbira
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20943.png
desc:	Kumilima mutoya habagaho agakoko gatoya kitwa Daizy kifuzaga kuguluka. Izindi nkoko zose zilayisekaga buli musi ubwo igelagezaga kwiga kuguluka. Aliko umusi gumwe igitangaza chalabaye.

page_text:

P1	Kela habayeho umulima mutoya uluhande dyichalo
P2	Habagaho agakoko gatoya kitwa Daizy.
P3	"Ninzakula chakakuguluka hejulu, hejulu mukilele" niko Daisy yavugaga.
P4	Aliko izindi nkoko zose zalayisekaga
P5	Walasaze ntabwo tuzongela gukina nawe niko za mubwilaga
P6	Nyina yalamubwilaga ngo, "Daizy twese twa papaza amababa aliko ntabwo twaguluka"
P7	Daisy ntabwo yigeze ashila ibyilingilo. Buli musi yalagelagezaga aligupapaza aliko ntabwo yigeze ava  kutaka.
P8	Ubwoyagelagezaga yatekelezaga alikuguluka yejulu chane mukilele alikuleba izindi nkoko hasiye. Yatekelezaga alikuguluka alikunyula kubindi binyoni byinshi byose bilikumwibazaho.
P9	Noneho buli musi Dizy yalapapazaga amababage
P10	Yalagulukaga akava kutaka aliko akongela akagwa nabwo.
P11	Umusi gumwe yabwiye nyina ngo ntabwo nzaguliku. Bagenzi banje balashitsaga
P12	Nyina yalamubwiye ngo Daizy utandukanye nnizindi nkoko zose kubelako zo ntabwo zishakakuguluka aliko weho ulabishaka nokubwiyo mpanvu waguluka.
P13	Umusi wakulikiyeho Daizy yatalamye yejulu yinzu yinkoko nuko apapaza amababage. Yagulutse hejulu mukilele nuko apapaza amababa chane
P14	BAMU!
P15	Izindi nkoko zalasetse chane. Twalakubwiye ngo inkoko ntabwo zigulukaga
P16	Aliko kuwundi musi Daizy yatalamye nohejulu chane hejulu yinzu. Nubundi alapapaza amababa ge.
P17	Yasimbutse hejulu  mumuyaga nuko apapaza amababa nuko
P18	Yalagulutse! Umuyagamusiye wabaye mwinshi nuko aguluka yejulu chane chane. Ibinyoni byose mukilele byalavuze biti ikinigitangaza chi nkoko kuguluka.
P19	Nuko nizindi nkoko zalizishaka kuba nkayo
P20	Zalavuze ziti Daizy we uligitangaza.
