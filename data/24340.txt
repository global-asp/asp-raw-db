desc:	It is Marias fifth birthday. Her mom surprises her with a chocolate cake. She is excited by the cake.

page_text:

P1	Maria's mom is in the kitchen getting ready to bake.
P2	She mixes the eggs in the flour, adds milk and salt. 
She adds chocolate too.
P3	Maria's mom pours butter onto the cake pan.
P4	After 30 minutes, the cake is ready.
P5	Maria is outside playing with her friends.
P6	Her mom and dad come out of the house singing  happy birthday.
P7	Maria is very excited and starts dancing.
P8	Her friends join the celebrations.
P9	She finally cuts the cake.
