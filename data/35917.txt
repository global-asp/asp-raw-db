page_text:

P1	Kodi mukuona anthu angati ali pamthuthuthu.
P2	Nanga mnyamata ndi mtsikana Ali kucita ciani?
P3	Nanga apolisi ali apa abvala ciani. Kodi ndi mpolosi wamkazi kapena wamuna.
P4	Taonani mzibambo ali kumenya mnyamata. Kodi I imeneyi sinkhanza kodi?
P5	Amai ali kusambika mwana.Mwana ali mu dishi.
P6	Maria ndi yohane akwera bulu.
P7	Mtsikana uyu ndi wokongola kwambiri ndipo alupesa tsitsi pogwiritsa nchito kalilole kudziyang'anira.
P8	Amaonje abvala khoti kuopa kuti angabvumbwe ndi mvula.
P9	Mnyamata ali kusamba kumanja pofuna kuteteza matenda osiya-siyana.
P10	Mateyo ali kupalasa njinga atanyamula katundu.
P11	Matulina ali kutsuka ndiwo zamasamba.
P12	Abambo Wakale ali kugela tsitsi.
P13	Garu alikufuna kulima mwana wa bambo sitolo, bambo sitolo ali kuteteza mwana.
P14	Azibambo awiri alikuyang'ana galimoto lomwe yaonongeka.
P15	Mnyamata wocepa wanyamula katundu wolemera
