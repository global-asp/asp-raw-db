title:	Sauro Mai Ban Haushi
author:	Mohammed Also and Sanusi Hussain Kano
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Hausa (Nigeria)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40300.png
desc:	Duk mun san ƙarar sauro a lokacin da yake shawagi a cikin dare. Wannan ɗan ƙaramin ƙwaron amma babban mai ɓarna!

page_text:

P1	Кwan! Кwan!
P2	"Waye a nan?" na yi tambaya cikin magagi.
P3	"Ni ne," in ji wata murya mai rikitarwa.
P4	"Kamar ya ni ne?" na tambaya.
P5	"Na gaya maka dai ni ne," muryar ta sake faɗa.
P6	"Ni ban san wani 'ni ne' ba," na ba shi amsa. @@Na duba sai na ga sauro a maƙale a jikin gadona.
P7	"Toh, sunana Sau," muryar ta amsa da ƙarfi fiye da da, har cikin kunnena.
P8	"Wane Sau ɗin?" na tambaya lokacin da nake duba ƙarƙashin gidan saurona.
P9	"Sau…ro." A ƙarshe ya faɗa. Kawai sai na ji cizo a kunnena.
P10	"Ah! Na yi tsalle na fara dubawa. "Za ka yi da na sani." Na faɗa da ƙarfi.
P11	Cikin fushi na tashi daga kan gado, na kunna fitila.
P12	Tun daga nan na ƙulla yaƙi da sauro mai abin haushi!
