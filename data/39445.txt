title:	La keny gendit
author:	Philip Lual Bol
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Dinka

desc:	Ee thɔk yen eci la keny bi wunlɛɛn la neem gendit kec yen kaŋ la thin!

page_text:

P1	Go turum kɔɔc ke ci thiaŋ raan geeu.
P2	Go raan e kɔc ciaal turumic jal kiiu, "Ala geeu! Ajal yemen."
P3	Go raan eben rot jɔt ku, lek turumic.
P4	Diaar ku mith aci jal nyuc piny, ku ke rin bi la keny yuul looi.
P5	Guɔ jal nyuc aluiit nyin, mony tuy lɔɔm.
P6	Guɔ jal tak, "lɔn le ɣɛn gendit! Ku baai aca ben yok."
P7	Thai kɔk cit gup cuɛr a ɣɔɔc kaken wei.
P8	Kɔc kɔk wen ciath ok aci ɣɔɔc kaŋ. @@Ɣen aŋɔŋ weu ɣɔɔc ɣɛn kaŋ.
P9	Ke kaam thin wen, ke raan ger turum koth kaaŋic. @@E kaam ken ke kɔc ajal.
P10	Ku cuɛr wen arot jal pik ka ke wic bɛn bei turumic.
P11	"Ba dhuk baai nɛn?" ɣɛn arot jal thiec arot.
P12	Ku turum ke ciyic jal tuoc. @@Gou ɣɛn anyin niɛn.
P13	Ku tak ma baai kek wamaadh.
P14	Ke kaam thin wen, ke nin go adɔm nyin.
P15	Na ca jal yuul, ka ɣɛ paac. @@Tɛwen ka yok kɔc juaac ka ke ci jal.
P16	Ke kaam ken yen aci ɣɛn jal la ba pan walɛn jal la yɔɔp.
