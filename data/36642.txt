P1	Once upon a Time there was my grandfather and my grandmother my grandfather found a big garden so he made it his farm he brought seed to plant so he planted sugar canes and mealies he was very happy so he called my grandmother to come
P2	So my grandmother came she brought some food for my grandfather to eat and her and the dog so they started to plant
P3	My grandmother went in the house so my grandfather got tired and then rested he was getting hot
P4	So he rested and then my grandmother came they started to talk so my grandmother gave my grandfather water to drink
P5	So he threw his stick away and then the dogs chases it
P6	So the dog caught it and then the dog broke it so my grandmother and my grandfather were very sad the end
