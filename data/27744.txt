desc:	Enkola ya Shafiki yiye yenka

page_text:

P1	Ku  lunaku  lwa  sabbiiti, Jamusi  yateeba  egoolo.
P2	Ku  lwe  bbalaza, ghaabaagho  eikukubira  ery'abantu.
P3	"Shafiki, nviiri  dho  edifaanana  n'entwiga  dhirigha? Enviiri  dho  ndakanirivu  inho,  olyoti tidhifaaku."
P4	Shafiki, Tatyu  yo  erigha? olususu  lwo  tiruli  kunhuma."
P5	Shafiki, nga  olusaya lwo lwise? Amaino  go  gali kubitira  bwawo."
P6	Olunaku  olwo, Shafiki  yairayo  eka  mpolampola.
P7	Jowani,  nsobola  okufunaku  enviiri  edhiri  oti  dha  ntwiga?" "Salaku  ku  ntwiga  yo  ofune. Ekyo  tikisoboka."
P8	"Olususu  lwange  tiruli  kuboneka  bulungi, Jukiet. Osobola  okulukubaku  tatyu?"
"Tigheetaaga  kufaanana  muntu  wundi."
P9	"Nabirye, Nsubira  nga  zeena  nsobola  okubaaku  n'amatama, kuba  nderegebwa  inho.
P10	Wiiki  ey'okubiri, Yozefu yaakuba  goolo.
P11	Olwe bbalaza, eikukuubira  lyabita. Shafiki! Nga  oli  kuboneka  bulungi!"
P12	Shafiki  yaasanhuka. "Buti  nefaanana; eno  enkola  yange.
