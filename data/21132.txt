page_text:

P1	Ayei kolong eron. Amam akimuj ayei nawi a Mulongo.
P2	Abu Mulongo toriko awi keng namoni asakar akimuj.
P3	Torukaut amana ang’idison ekonit tamam ilope.
P4	Napei kona topeleu ebuu! Tachamu kechi toloma neni.
P5	Ataparachu apei tama, "Taarakinae lokookon atanyam."
P6	Atabong ng’ina, tama nabo ebuu, "Taarakinae nakookon atanyam moi."
P7	Apotu ng’ikauriak ng’ulu kiwaa nakookech alotooma ekitoe.
P8	Tama ebuu ng’olo elimokini aberu, "Ani moi kidorokis ekile atanyam."
P9	Atabong ng’ina, tama ebuu kidorokin bon katanyam!
P10	Arumoret akimuj, kinapakin ebuu kwap ekitoe king’oorak.
P11	Abu ebuu ng’olo king’olik kechi daang eyarete. Tong’oo ng’esi.
P12	Ikwa kolong kimokorate ng’itung’a ng’ulu ebuu.
