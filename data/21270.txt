title:	Engeri Enswa Yeyaghonia  Eighemba
author:	Bishop Willis CPTC Student Teachers
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences

desc:	Eitaka ikalu ate Akaswa kayonta. Kagya ku mwiga okunhwa kayeyebwa.

page_text:

P1	Olunaku lwali lwokya
  mu kibira ekikalu.
P2	Akaswa kaali kazira
  maadhi okumala enaku nhingi.
P3	”nhendaku eitondo lya’maadhi nhi gherinaava kw’ikoola,” enswa ento yaboologa.
P4	Aye n’omusulo gukaze.
P5	“ghemba
  nzira maadhi ngya kufa,” enswa
  ento yabolooga. “ nhinha okugya ku mwiga gwenaghuliraku”
P6	“Omwiga mwidhuvu ate gw’amaanhi. Gwidha ‘kweya,” akadaada akadhagudhagu kaalabula.
P7	Aye akaswa kali n’enonta yamaani. “ndhafa bwentanhwa
  ku maadhi”. Era,kaagya okunhoonia
  omwiga.
P8	Your translated text
P9	Kavunvwiga mu nsiko ni
  munsaga enkalu.
P10	Katambula paka bwekaghulira
  nga amaadhi gayuyika. Gwali mwiga! Kaali  kasobola
  okughulira amayengo.
P11	Akaswa kalega ku olulega lwa’maadhi.
P12	Kaali kasanhufu tikawuliraku
  iyengo nga liidha.
P13	Akaswa kageza okugema
  eisubi eryali liri kulera. Aye kayeyebwa amaadhi.
P14	“Omuntu anambe. Munambe bambi!” yayeta.
P15	“Nhiina mangu,” Eighemba eryeru lyakoba nga ligemye olusaga
  mu munwa gwalyo.
P16	Your translated text
P17	“nda
  kuba ghano paka lwenhebaza eighemba”, akaswa kasalawo. “ ndaateega
  paka gha nairira okunhwa.”
P18	Olunaku lulala nga ateega, abalenzi babiri baidha ku mwiga. Baali
  n’ensumuulo.
P19	“Eriyo eighemba eryeru eriidha okunhwa ghano amaadhi. “Tulina olirya ekyeigulo.”
P20	Your translated text
P21	“Tisobola kwikiriza balenzi kwita eighemba eryeru,” Akaswa kaloghooza. “Aye ndhimudini, kiki kyensobola
  okolagho?”
P22	Aghoni Eighemba eryeru lyaguluka okuva ku muti okwika linhwe.
P23	Your translated text
P24	Akaswa kaali n’endoghooza
P25	Kabuukira ku kimu ku kigere ky’omuvubuka.
  Kaluma omulenzi inho nga bwekaali
  kasobola.
P26	Omulenzi  yabuuka. “oooh” yeleekaana.
P27	Eighemba eryeru lyekanga
  lyaguluka okughona ekibambulira.
P28	Eyo n’engeri Akaswa
  yekebazaamu Eighemba eryeru okaghonia.
