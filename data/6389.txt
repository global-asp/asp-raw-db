page_text:

P1	A man called Kato lived in Bunghumu village. He used to steal peoples' animals.
P2	One day he went and stole a goat from the neighbouring village. He slaughtered and kept the meat in his house.
P3	The following morning the people found out that Kato had stolen the goat. They started looking for him.
P4	When they reached Kato's home,  they found him roasting the meat.
