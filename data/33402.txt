desc:	Siyini sijeziso lesatfolwa ngumfana losimiti lowadla kakhulu titselo?

page_text:

P1	Ngalelinye lilanga, make watsenga titselo letehlukehlukene.
P2	"Sitotidla nini titselo?" sabuta.@@Make watsi, "Sitotidla namuhla ebusuku."
P3	Umnaketfu Rahim usimiti.@@Wanambitsa tonkhe titselo. Watidla kakhulu.
P4	"Buka kutsi Rahim wenteni!" kumemeta umnaketfu lomncane.@@"Rahim akeva futsi uligovu," kusho mine.
P5	Make wamtfukutselela kakhulu Rahim.
P6	Natsi futsi samtfukutselela Rahim.@@Kodwa Rahim akazange atisole.
P7	"Utomjezisa yini Rahim?" kubuta umnaketfu lomncane.
P8	"Rahim, utawutisola masinyane," kucwayisa make.
P9	Rahim wacala kungativa kahle.
P10	"Sisu sami sibuhlungu," kuhleba Rahim.
P11	Make bekati kutsi yini lena leyenta Rahim agule.@@"Letitselo tiyamjezisa Rahim," wacabanga.
P12	Ngekuhamba kwesikhatsi, Rahim wacolisa kitsi. Wetsembisa watsi, "Ngeke ngiphindze ngibe simiti."@@Samkholwa.
