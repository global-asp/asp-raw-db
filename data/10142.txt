P1	My baby boy is John Francis.
P2	He likes yellow bananas and apples.
P3	After taking his breakfast, he goes to play with a friend.
P4	He likes swimming during afternoon hours.
P5	He does his homework and draws different pictures.
P6	John Francis has a big cock.
P7	He plays hide and seek with friends.
P8	His favorite color is yellow.
P9	He likes pet animals very much.
P10	John Francis encourages his friends to read hard,  to have a bright future.
