title:	Lanji da Musoke
author:	Kyambogo  University and peter kyawera
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words

desc:	Kano akogera ku lanji dha musoke ni byo bosola okubona mubyabulidho ebiba ni lanji dhino.

page_text:

P1	Musoke alina kala musanvu.
P2	Kala esooka nga mmyufu.
P3	Kalisuba ayambaile sati mmyufu.
P4	Lanji ey'okubiri n'eyakathungwa.
P5	Guno muthungwa. Kiki ekindi ky'olikubona nga kya kathungwa?
P6	Lanji eyo kusatu neya kyenvu.
P7	Endhuba ey'eiji. Yakyenvu.Ekindhi ki ekya kyenvu ky'olikubona?
P8	Lanji ey'o kunha, nhi Kiragala.
P9	Olusuku lwaife olw'a matooke gaife lwa kiragala.
P10	Lanji ey'o kutaanu neya bululu.
P11	Eigulu lya bululu.
P12	Lanji eyindhi yanakasooma dedeme.
P13	Enziru dhino dha nakasooma.
P14	Lanji ey'omusamvu neya kakobe
P15	Olugoye lwange lwa kakobe.Kakobe ni lanji jensinga kwenda!Ighe ki kunhumira
P16	Oidhi lnji omusanvu edha musoke?
