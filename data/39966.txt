page_text:

P1	Mariam is a grade 6 learner. Last year, she was given the smartest girl award during the prize giving day. @@Everyday before she goes to school, she takes a bath, brushes her teeth, combs her hair and wears a clean uniform.
P2	In the evening when she comes from school, she washes her clothes and prepares what she will wear the next day. @@Mariam also takes a bath after school and changes into clean clothes.
P3	She washes her long hair every week to removed dirt and avoids lice infestation. @@She also applies oil to her skin to moisturise it.
