title:	Hunter can't hunt giraffe.

page_text:

P1	One fine morning , there was a family who was poor but they hunt animals and eat  flesh and bone of animals. On this day, Sinhalese says " I want to have plant protein also because red meat can  give pain to heart".
P2	The hunter said that he tried to find plant protein but other herbivores are eating plants. But child said " Oh today my cousin gave me 10 million maize" in a very excited way. But his father said " only eat carnivore protein".
P3	His family was hungry for giraffe. And the hunter was going to hunt the giraffe. This hunter was clever and smart in hunting animals.
P4	He says song " I am the hunter ! I am the hunter who hunts animals for bones and flesh for his family and he is clever and smart in hunting and eating animals".
P5	He saw a giraffe sitting down and his leg tightly tied through a rope. This hunter was happy and think that he would attack on this giraffe and eat all his body parts.
P6	He attacked on giraffe's leg . And giraffe was feeling pain .
P7	Then Giraffe was angry , he stand up and tried to escape and hunter fall down and giraffe had escaped.
P8	The hunter's plan was failed .
P9	His family was sitting and were very happy about maize that they have eaten in their lunch. Hunter said that he was hungry and can't hunt giraffe. Hunter eaten the roasted maize.
P10	Killing animals is not good for us because some animals give us milk and many things to us. We should protect animals and according to this story the giraffe was wiser then hunter and escaped from the obstacle.
