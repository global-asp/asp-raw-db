page_text:

P1	Mushamba rya Miwa, habaga ikyende yahoraga irakaye. Ibi byaterwaga nogunfa kwabana burik' ibyaye.
P2	Agahinda kogunfa kwabanabe kamwirizaga arigusimbagurika mubiti. Yavaga kuhango rimwe akajakurindi, nuko akarira, "Kwi! kwi! Kwi!
P3	Inkyende yahoraga irakaye. Yarushagaho kurakara ubwoyabonaga izindi nkyende zikina nabana bazo
P4	Yahoraga yichaye kwihango, gusa Imisi ko yanyuragaho, yarushagaho kurira.
P5	Agezeho asubira kubyara. Yahisemo kujana umwana wayo kunzira, ngo wenda abantu bamubona bakamwifuriza ineza.
P6	Inkyende yamanutse mugiti ishira umwana wayo kunzira. mukanya, umuhigi yararigusubira murugo abona akana kinkyende karyamye uruhande rwakayira.
P7	Umuhigi yajanye akana kinkyende murugo. Ageze murugo, abanabe bose bari bashaka gufata kukana kinkyende.
P8	Babana batatu ko bakyinaga baribari kuririmba, "terera hejuru! terera hasi! nterera! muterere!" akana kikyende babari kugaterera kuva kurume mpaka ku wundi.
P9	Ukobabana batatu bakyinaga nakana kinkyende, nyiwako yariyishe mugiti. irikubareba. Yarari gutinya ngo umwana nawe abasha gunfa, nkabandi.
P10	umugore wumuhingi yaraje obona abana be barigukina nakana kinkyende. arabwira ati, "mwigengesereze! Mukazane hano, mutagakubita hasi!"
P11	Yashize akana kinkyende mumaboko, nuko agaha imigisha.
P12	Umugore wumuhigyi yashize amwana winkyende hasi. Nyina aramushikira. Nuko aburira mwishamba. kuvubwo, abana bose bagiye bararamuka.
