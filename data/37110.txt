title:	Nsuna yo dzindela
author:	Arnold Mushwana and Given J Hlongwani
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Xitsonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37110.png
desc:	Hinkwerhu ha wu tiva mpfumawulo wa nsuna loko wu karhi ku nininini nivusiku. Xitsotswana xitsongo kambe ku dzindela i kukulu.

page_text:

P1	Gogogoo! Gogogoo!
P2	"I mani?" hi mina ndzi vutisaka ndzi ri na vurhongo.
P3	"Hi mina," mpfumawulo wo tontswela wu hlamula.
P4	"I mani 'hi mina'?" ndzi vutisa.
P5	"Ndzi ku byerile, hi m-i-n-a n-t-s-e-n-a," mpfumawulo wu vuya nakambe.
P6	"A ndzi tivi munhu loyi a vitaniwaka, 'm-i-n-a n-t-s-e-n-a'," ndzi hlamula. @@Ndzi xiya ku vona loko nete wa mina wa tinsuna wu rhendzerile mubedwa wa mina.
P7	"Hi swona, hi mina Amos." @@Mpfumawulo wu twakala wu tontswile swinene ku tlula emasungulweni.
P8	"Amos wihi?" ndzi vutisa ndzi ri karhi ndzi languta ehansi ka nete.
P9	"Amos Nsuna," mpfumawulo wu vula. @@Ndzi twa ku lumiwa endleveni.
P10	"Yoo!" Ndzi tlula ndzi sungula ku lavalava. @@"U ta swi hakela leswi!" hi mina ndzi cemaka.
P11	Ndzi hlundzukile, ndzi pfuka ndzi ya layita rivoni.
P12	Ndzi lava ku susa nyimpi eka Amos Nsuna wo dzindela!
