desc:	This is an adventure of a elephant who helped everyone.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time in a far great jungle, there lived a wise elephant. The animals lived in peace but one day there was a mean lion who wanted to be king. All the animals were afraid of him.
P2	Everytime when they went to drink water he would chase them . Elephant had enough so she thought of a plan.
P3	So she had an idea and thought it might work so she went to the well were lion was. She told lion that there is another lion that wanted water for himself. Lion was angry and went to look for the lion.
P4	When he was still looking for the lion he was thirsty and found some water in a pond. He so another lion in the water and tried to kill it . Then he jumped in the water and drowned. Now all the animals lived in peace again.
