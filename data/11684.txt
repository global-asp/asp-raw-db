desc:	The cat wants to catch the rat. But someone else wants to catch the cat! Who will catch whom?

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there lived a cat and a rat. The cat ran after the rat. The rat hid under the stone.
P2	The cat looked for the rat and the cat saw a dog! The dog ran after the cat. The cat hid in the bush.
P3	The dog looked for the cat and the dog saw a big,  big lion. The lion chased the dog. The dog ran in the bush.
P4	The lion looked for the dog. Then the lion saw a big tough elephant. The elephant chased the lion. The lion ran into the bush.
