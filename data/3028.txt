P2	I smell dirty socks.

P5	I see a balloon blowing away in the wind.
P6	I see a happy cat.
P7	With my mouth.
P8	I taste the salty sea.
P9	I taste a juicy sweet orange.
P10	With my hand.
P11	I take your hand.
