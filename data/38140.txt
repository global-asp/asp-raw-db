page_text:

P1	There live two girls called falma and salma they are best friends there lived a ije village falma is 15 while Salma is 14 they also lived together what only separate them was sleeping falma was hard working while Salma is lazy
P2	One good morning falma's mother ask falma and her friend to go and get some firewood for them to use and cook food for them falma and her friend went out on there way to the forest
P3	Salma saw a tall tree and she said falma should wait for her falma asked her not to climb the tree but she refused then Salma  @go ahead and start climbing the tree
P4	Salma countine climbing the tree after she climbed the tree she cut a leave an gave falma ,falma asked her to come down because it is getting late after trying to get down Salma get stocked on the tree and she can't come down and
P5	And have no idea on how to come down and the tree is tall and risking falma asked Salma to jump down and  if she jump down she will fall an break herself
P6	Luckily there was a man in there village he saved Salma and he asked her not climb any tree again as a girl she apologized and thanks and took her to her mother
P7	Her mother called the other boys playing the ball and told them what happened and she learnt her lesson from that very day
P8	Falma had a very big party because that day was her brothers birthday and they celebrate the party happily
