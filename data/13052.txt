desc:	IInengi leenlwana zinamazinyo abukhali wokuluma. Ezinye iinlwana zinamazinyo apataleleko wokuhlafuna namkha ukugaya. Wakho anjani?

page_text:

P1	Ingwenya inamazinyo akhaliphako.
P2	Ibhubezi nalo linamazinyo akhaliphako futhi.
P3	Ushaka unamazinyo akhalipha kwamanikelela. Kubayini ezinye iinlwana zibe namazinyo akhaliphako?
P4	Ikomo inamazinyo aqinile wokusotlha namkha ukuhlafuna.
P5	Inyoni ayinawo amazinyo. Idla bunjani inyoni?
P6	Amakhondlo anamazinyo amakhulu wangaphambili.
P7	Khengaba nawo amazinyo wangaphambili.
P8	Izinyo lami langaphambili laphuma! Unamazinyo amangakhi?
