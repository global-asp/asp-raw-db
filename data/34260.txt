title:	Mivhala ya musengavhadzimu
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34260.png
desc:	Hetshi tshiṱori tshi na mutevhe wa mivhala ya musengavhadzimu na zwithu zwine na nga zwi vhona ḓuvha na ḓuvha zwine zwa vha na yeneyi mivhala.

page_text:

P1	Musengavhadzimu u na mivhala ya sumbe.
P2	Muvhala wa u thoma ndi mutswuku.
P3	Kewyu o ambara hemmbe tswuku.
P4	Muvhala wa vhuvhili ndi wa swiri.
P5	Heḽi ndi swiri. Ndi tshifhio tshiṅwe tshithu tshi re na muvhala wa swiri?
P6	Muvhala wa vhuraru ndi wa ṱaḓa.
P7	Kumba ḽi na tsha ṱaḓa. Ndi tshifhio tshiṅwe tshithu tshi re na muvhala wa ṱaḓa?
P8	Muvhala wa vhuṋa ndi dala.
P9	Ngade yashu ya muomva ndi dala.
P10	Muvhala wa vhuṱanu ndi lutombo.
P11	Ḽiṱaḓulu ḽi na muvhala wa lutombo.
P12	Muvhala wa vhurathi ndi wa 'indigo'.
P13	Hedzi beri dzi na muvhala wa 'indigo'.
P14	Muvhala wa vhusumbe ndi wa 'violet'.
P15	Rokho yanga  i na muvhala wa 'violet'.@@'Violet' ndi muvhala une nda u funesa! Ndi ufhio muvhala une inwi na u funesa?
P16	Naa ni a i ḓivha mivhala ya sumbe ya musengavhadzimu?
