title:	Thishela Nkosi
author:	Fakazile Armooh
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Siswati

desc:	Indzaba lemfishane lelula lemayelana nathishela lolungile nekutsi usifundzisani.

page_text:

P1	Lona nguthishela wami. 

Sibongo sakhe wakaNkosi. Uyasitsandza kakhulu.
P2	Thishela Nkosi usifundzisa imisindvo nabonkhamisa.
Sengiyabati bonkhamisa a, e, i, o, u.
P3	Thishela Nkosi usifundzise nayi imibala. 

Lobovu, loluhlata sasibhakabhaka, loluhlata satjani, lomtfubi nalomnyama.
P4	Nakufike sikhatsi sekulala eklasini, Thishela Nkosi usiculela kamnandzi bese siyalala. 

Usiculela tingoma tekulala.
P5	Lena ngulenye yetingoma laticulako. Umlolotelo utsi:

Nika emehlo akho butfongo lobumnandzi. Ulindvwe kumamatseka nawuvuka. Lalani titsandvwa tami, ningakhali futsi.
P6	Thishela Nkosi wati tindzaba letinyenti kakhulu.

Uhlala njalo asicocela tindzaba letinsha emalanga onkhe evikini.
P7	NgeMsombuluko, usicocela tindzaba ngalabadvuna labadvumile.
P8	NgaLesibili, usicocela tindzaba ngalabasikati labadvumile.
P9	NgaLesitsatfu, usicocela tindzaba ngetindlela letehlukene tekutfutsa.
P10	NgaLesine, usicocela tindzaba ngetekulima.
P11	NgaLesihlanu, Thishela Nkosi usinika litfuba lekutsi natsi sicoce tetfu tindzaba eklasini.
P12	Thishela wetfu nguthishela lobengca bonkhe emhlabeni jikelele! 

Nami nasengikhulile, ngifuna kufana naThishela Nkosi.
