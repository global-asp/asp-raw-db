desc:	Hares are popular creatures in African folktales. They are famous for behaving badly and always getting away with it. In this Nyungwe folktale

page_text:

P1	Ngalelinye lilanga Logwaja beka hamba ngale eceleni kwemfula.
P2	Nemvubu beyikhona nayo,  beyitihambela nje itiyalukela,  idlana netjani lobuluhlata lobumnandzi.
P3	Imvubu ayizange ibone kwekutsi logwaja ukhona naye, ngeliphutsa imvubu yanyatsela linyawo lalogwaja. Logwaja wamemeta watsi,  "Wena Mvubu! Awuboni kwekutsi uyangenyatsela?"
P4	Yaticolisela iMvubu. "Ngiyacolisa mngani wami. Angizange ngikubone. Ngicela ungicolele!" Logwaja akalalelanga watsetsisa iMvubu, "Wente ngemabomu! Ngaleliye lilanga utowubona! Utakubhadalela lokwentile."
P5	Wasuka Logwaja wafuna umlilo wawutfola watsi,  "Hamba uyoshisa Mvubu uma aphuma kuyodla tjani. Unginyatselile!" Umlilo waphendvula watsi,  "Akunakinga Logwaja mgani wami. Ngitokwenta njalo."
P6	Kwatsi ngelikadze,  Mvubu bekadla tjani kudze lena emfuleni "bhu" umlilo wavutsa waba lilangabi,  lilangabi lashisa tinwele temvubu.
P7	Imvubu yacala yakhala yagijimela emantini. IMvubu yachubeka yakhala,  "Tinwele tami tishiswe ngumlilo! Tonkhe,  tinwele tami tonkhe tihambile! Tinwele tami letinhle!"
P8	Yingako imvubu ingahambeli kudze nemanti,  kungenca yekwesabela kushiswa ngumlilo. Logwaja wajabula makabona imvubu isha,  atsi, "Ngimtfolile."
