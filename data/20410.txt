title:	Eger Lo Aanyunia Ikidodok Elowu Long'arab
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	Rob Owen

desc:	Eger lo aanyunia akidodok eleu longarab nabo topedorea akiboi ani emam ngakipi.

page_text:

P1	Aburuni kolong itaok akidodok akiboi anakipi.
P2	"Tochoite elowu kon eoni," tolimok abokok ng'esi.
P3	Nakaneni apegana ikidodok ng'ini. Dio arotokin.
P4	Sodi tokienyia lokwaasin daang, torotok.
P5	Kiriamun ka ageya lokwaas abwel ang'akipi.
P6	Ani isiakini ageya anyang'aaniyar! Torotok ikidodok.
P7	King'olik nabo ebuu inokakini akim.
P8	Kiriam akim ni ayei ikidodok ng'ini. Kikalou ng'esi!
P9	Kiwarak akim elowu keng, dio toporok tooma nakipi.
P10	Nakaneni abu kilebeleb elowu keng lokojokon.
P11	Taanyu akai alokwap ataaba. Towon elowu tang'arabab!
P12	Eger erakar akidodok naapolon bon esulut elowu ng'olo!
