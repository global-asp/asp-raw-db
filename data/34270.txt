page_text:

P1	Obinna is a happy boy.  He has people who love him in his life.
P2	He wakes up and gets ready for school.  He can't wait to eat the meal father is making.
P3	The meal looks yummy. Father is an amazing chef.
P4	Father adds milk to the meal.  Obinna  is excited,  he loves milk.
P5	After school,  Obinna plays football with his friends.
P6	Obinna and his friends decide to read their books.
P7	Then they smile for the camera.
P8	After their photo,  they went swimming  in the pool.  Obinna is lucky to have nice friends.
