title:	Azizi mo Pupejo

desc:	Ɓinngel ngel yardi sukulanaago pupe maako. Nden pupe maako boo laati soobaajo maako gonnga.

page_text:

P1	Nda Azizi.@@Nda pupe.
P2	Mi yiɗi Azizi jur ngam o ɗon jala wakati fuu.
P3	Ɓaawo to mi fini, mi loota gite.@@Nden mi ɗon tefa Azizi.
P4	To mi tawi Azizi, mi ɗon loota nyi'e maako.
P5	To mi ɗon nyaama dezene am, mi ɗon hokka Azizi haa o nyaama.
P6	To mi ɗon nyaama bonbon, mi ɗon hokka gootel haa Azizi.
P7	Amma Azizi nyaamata bonbon maako sam.
P8	To min ɗon wancca nder jarne ngam haa mi itta fəler.
P9	Min ɗon hokka Azizi gootel.@@Amma o meeɗaay wi'igo useyko.
P10	Hiddeko daada am yiwa mi, mi ɗon yiwa Azizi tawon.
P11	Min ɗon bornanamo limce bodeejum, ngam mi yardi ɗum jur.
P12	To mi ɗon ɗaana, mi ɗon wallina Azizi les am.
