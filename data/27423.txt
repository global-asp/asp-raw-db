desc:	Luno olufumo lugema ku bantu abato abanhumirwa okuzaanha sooka. Omutendesi waibwe ali nokweghuunhya kungi ku kikunsu kyaibwe.

page_text:

P1	Nnhenda inho sooka, ate ighe?
P2	Maliki ali kutusomesa sooka, onhumirwa weena?
P3	Tuje mu kisaaghe.
P4	Maliki no omuzaanhi waife asinga.
P5	Oluvainhuma lw'omuzaanho, omutendesi atukoba ati, "Ndi n'ekinaabeghunhisa."
P6	Aye abakoba ati, "Ndidha kukibakobera enco."
P7	Olunaku olwairirira, boonaboona baidha ku kisaawe. "Twenatwena tubyenda."
P8	"Maliki ali neebyokukozesa ebiyaaka ebitumala tweenatweena n'emipiira!"
