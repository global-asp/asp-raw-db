desc:	Ologhooza kiki ekibaagho singa endhuba ebulagho.

page_text:

P1	Maama Ndhuba yasalagho okuja okukyaliraku muganda waayo mwezi. Muganda waayo mwezi aviira ku mbali kwa kire. "Nda kwira nga bukaali," Ndhuba akobera ebire.
P2	Endhuba bwe yavaagho, ensozi dha taaku eiwembo.
P3	Empewo yali ni okughakana ni emiti. Emiti gyalumwa endhala. Gysalagho okusuula amakoola gaagyo buli ghantu.
P4	Ekire kyatandiika okwemuluguunia kyafuuka kya kivuuvu.
P5	Ebireri byali binakughavu okubona kino. Byatandiika okulira. Ghaaligho amaziga mangi inho.
P6	Ensi yoonayoona yatandiika okudimira ghansi mu maadhi.
P7	Waabita akaseera katono, ku lule olwe ekire, endhubayali neetegefu okuleka mugandaghe mwezi. Banuunagana eminwa beesiibula era ndhuba yaagolola waka.
P8	Ekire kyali kisanhufu inho okubona endhuba kyacuuka mu langi eya bbululu. Ensozi dhataaku ebiteeteeyi ebya kiragala.
P9	Empewo yaja okutenduka. Emiti gya golola ensaga dhaadho gya taaku akamwenumwenu.
P10	Ebireri byona byali bisanhufu okubona maama Ndhuba. byegiira okuzaanha.
P11	Ebirime bingi ebito byavaayo mu nsi byakoba,"muli mutya." Ensi yoonayoona yamansula.
P12	Maama Ndhuba yalaga ekitangaali buli ghantu, "Nabakoba nti nda kwira," yayaka.
