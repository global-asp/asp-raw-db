P1	Once upon a time in a family that lived next to a forest, there lived 2boys
P2	One was called John and the other was called Huruma. They were naughty boys
P3	They lived in a village called Samara. One day they went to the forest.
P4	to search for fruits suddenly, a lion came out of nowhere and attacked them
P5	They burst into tears and shouted for help. Fortunately they were not hurt.
