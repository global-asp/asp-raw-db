title:	Ilanga Lamabeletho Likalynne

desc:	ULynne wenze umsenenzi omnengi ukulungisa izinto zomnyanya welanga lakhe lamabeletho

page_text:

P1	ULynne, ubamkhulu uKareli no-Antoni bebabuthelela izinto balungiselela ilanga likaLynne lamabeletho.
P2	U-Antoni noLynne bazokubhaga ikhekhe. Ubamkhulu uKareli uzokufunda iresebhe.
P3	"Asihlanganiseleni umvango lo ngesitjeni esikhulu," kutjho uLynne.
P4	U-Antoni ubonda umvango wenza ubutjhapha obukhulu.
P5	"Ikhekhe leli silibudisi kwanjesi," kutjho ubamkhulu uKareli.
P6	"Abangani benu sebafikile," kutjho ubamkhulu uKareli.
P7	U-Antoni bekacabanga bonyana isipho sokuthoma sizokuba bodisi ngombana besibonakala sisikhulu.
P8	Bekusisipho somsamela begodu besinobudisi obuncani.
P9	U-Antoni bekacabanga bonyana isipho esilandelako sizokuba lula ngombana besibonakala sisincani.
P10	Bekulilitje elipendiweko begodu besinobudisi obukhulu.
P11	Abentwana bathi khilogramu eli-1 lelitje linobudisi obulinganako nekhilogramu eli-1 leensiba.
P12	Ikhekhe likaLynne latjha.
P13	ULynne bekangakathabi.
P14	Ukgari uMavis wabawa u-Antoni kobana afunisele kobana isipho sakhe besinobudisi obungangani.
P15	U-Antoni wathi, "Sinobudisi obulingana nekhekhe elitjhileko."
P16	Abantu boke bathi, "langelihle lamabeletho Lynne."
