page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was girl called Leo. She lived with her mother and her brother. She had a wonderful life with her family. They like to play together.
P2	One day Leo and her family went to the mall. Leo wanted to go to toy shop but her family chose to go the shoe shop. She did not like the shoe shop so she cried.
P3	Then Leo`s mother decided to go to the toy shop. Leo had lot of toys she wanted to buy. Her mother had no ides of what to do so she brought her what she wanted.
P4	When they finished shopping,  they went to the park,  to have a picnic. They sat down and ate their food. They had so much fun even play at the swing.
P5	When it was time to go home,  Leo met her friend Neo. Neo was the one and the only friend that she ever had. Leo had to say good bye to her friend as they were leaving. It was not easy she started crying. Her mother did not know what to do,  so she decided to go with Leo`s friend.
P6	When they arrived home Neo went in and it was her first time to be at Leo`s house. After dinner time Leo`s mother asked Neo to call her parents asking to sleep over.
P7	Neo`s parents agreed so Leo show her friend her room to sleep. They stayed in their room playing.
P8	When it was bed time Leo`s mother called them saying "Bed time' and Leo and her friend went to sleep. That is how Leo had a wonderful day."
