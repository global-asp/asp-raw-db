P1	They were really good at football but they were practicing to play with a better football team.
P2	The coach wasn't fair to the other team every time she would give out red cards and declare hand balls for no reason.
P3	Till one of the players got hurt 🤕 and she said I don't care 😂.
P4	She was taken to hospital 🏥 and wile the rest were putting on their new 🆕 jersey.
P5	The friends took a picture 🖼️ together.
