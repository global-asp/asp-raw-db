title:	Makhulu, Muvhuḓa na Nḓou
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences

desc:	Ṅwana u ṱalutshedza nḓila ye makhulu wawe vha vha vha tshi vha anetshela ngayo dzingano musi vha sa athu eḓela.

page_text:

P1	Makhulu vho thoma nga uri, "Muvhuḓa na Nḓou vho vha vhe vhahura vha dovha vha vha dzikhonani."
P2	Muvhuḓa o vha a tshi funa ngowa, fhedzi o vha a tshi bvafha u ṱavha.
P3	Ho vha hu si na ngowa.@@Muvhuḓa a mbo ḓi thoma u tswa miomva ya Nḓou.
P4	Nḓou a imba a ri, "Mbava ndi ḓo i wana!"@@Muvhuḓa a tshuwa.
P5	Ṱhoho o vha a tshi linda bulasi, fhedzi muvhuḓa ha ngo tswa.
P6	Nḓou a ya u sedza.@@Muvhuḓa o vha o dzumbama vhukati ha mafhuri na muroho wa thanga.
P7	Nḓou a sedza hoṱhe-hoṱhe, fhedzi mbava a si i wane.
P8	Ḽiṅwe ḓuvha muvhuḓa o dzumbama ngomu ha fhuri ḽihulwane a kona u ponyoka.
P9	Nḓou a vhona fhuri ḽihulwane a mbo ḓi ḽi mila.
P10	Fhuri ḽo vha ḽi tshi khou kunguluwa ngomu thumbuni ya Nḓou.
P11	Nḓou a ṱanza ḽia fhuri.@@Muvhuḓa a shavha.
P12	Makhulu vha ri, "Ndi u fa ha lungano."
