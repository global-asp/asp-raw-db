page_text:

P1	Nhongana i xitsotswana.
P2	Nhangana yi tshama endhawini ya thyaka.
P3	Etithoyilete,  swichelathyaka na le swakudyeni.
P4	Swi haha ku suka eka tindhawu ta thyaka swi ta emakaya ya hina.
P5	Swi wela eswakudyeni swa hina swi vanga thyaka.
P6	Loko munhu a dya swakudya swa thyaka,  u ta lumalumiwa ekhwirini.
P7	Nhongana yi na khombo swinene.
