page_text:

P1	Once upon a time a little rooster was wandering around
P2	Little rooster said cock-a-doodle-doo!! until… meow! he heard a cat now there’s a rooster and a cat what is they doing?
P3	The rooster said cock-a-doodle-doo! and the cat said meow-meow-meow! Utill… woof! then they Heard a dog and spot one now there’s a rooster and a cat and a dog what is they doing?
P4	The rooster said cock-a-doodle-doo! and the cat said meow-meow-meow and the dog said woof-woof-woof and then heard a Naa! The horse was welling because there’s no food to find!
P5	So this is what they were doing! The rooster said cock-a-doodle-doo there was a nearby college so they decided to check what’s inside?
P6	So this is what they were doing trying to look for food! They said cock-a-doodle-doo! Meow woof and naa! so happily
P7	So all of them ate delicious food and had a full stomach
