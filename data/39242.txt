desc:	A girl who became blind due to playing with dangerous chemicals.At the end she finally gets help from a stranger to help her receive her sight.Help can come from anyone no matter the situation.

page_text:

P1	Ka letsatsi lengwe go ne go na le ba lelapa la ga Phiri kwa Winterveldt .Rre,mme le morwadi wa bona Thato.
P2	Ba ne ba tshela ka go rekisa maungo le merogo kwa mebileng .
P3	Ka letsatsi lengwe Thato o ne a ya go tshameka le ditsala tsa gagwe.
P4	Moragonyana o ne a boela kwa gae a lela matlho a gagwe a le mahibidu gape a le botlhoko.
P5	Batsadi ba gagwe ba ne ba mo isa kwa bookelong .Ngaka e ile ya mo tlhatlhoba,mme ya ba bolelela gore a ka sa bona gape .
P6	Ka letsatsi lengwe mo mosong ba ile batswa go rekisa jaaka ba tlhole ba dira. Fa ba ntse ba rekisa go ile gwa tlhagelela mohumagatsana Mmathuso,a ema go reka.
P7	Mohumagatsana Mmathuso o ile a bona Thato mme a botsa ka ena.Ka kutlobotlhoko le dikeledi mmaagwe Thato a tlhalosa.
P8	Mohumagatsana Mmathuso a ineelela go duelela ngaka ya matlho go thusa Thato.@Thato a tla bona gape.
