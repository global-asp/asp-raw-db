page_text:

P1	Ngozi loves eating fruits.
P2	She loves playing with her friends.
P3	Ngozi is always learning.
P4	She loves reading  her books,  be like Ngozi.
P5	Terna is ill.  His bed is very uncomfortable  because  he is very tall.
P6	So he made a new bed that is very long.
P7	Terna is uncomfortable on his bicycle.
P8	So he made a new bicycle, now he is very happy.  Be like Terna,  find solutions  to your problems.
