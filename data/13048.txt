page_text:

P1	Kubayini isibhakabhaka sihlaza kwesibhakabhaka?
P2	Asihlali sihlaza kwesibhakabha. Sinjalo na?
P3	Kubayini amakari ahlaza kotjani?
P4	Akahlali ahlaza kotjani. Anjalo na?
P5	Kubayini amabhanana asarulani?
P6	Amabhanana avuthiwe asarulani. Amabhanana angakavuthwa ahlaza kotjani.
P7	Kubayini ama-ambrela anzima?
P8	Amanye ama-ambrela akasinzima.
P9	Kubayini igazi libovu?
P10	Kune ayoni egazini. Eyenza igazi libe bovu.
P11	Kubayini itswayi limhlophe?
P12	Angazi!
