page_text:

P1	Edi and his friends like to keep LalaLand clean and tidy.
P2	Every morning Monkey makes his bed.
P3	Edi folds his clothes and puts them away, ready to wear another day.
P4	Rabbit sweeps away the water after showering. She knows this will help the bathroom floor to dry.
P5	After playing, Rabbit and Tiger put away all the toys and books.
P6	If Monkey spills food on the floor, he cleans it up right away.
P7	Tiger never draws or writes on the walls. She draws on paper so the walls stay clean.
P8	Rabbit takes off her shoes when she goes inside. She doesn't want to bring dirt into the house.
P9	Edi and his friends put litter in the dustbin.
P10	They like to keep the streets clean.
P11	Thank you, Monkey.@Thank you, Rabbit.@Thank you, Tiger.@Thank you, Edi.@You all help to keep Lala Land clean!
P12	What can you do to keep your home clean and tidy?
