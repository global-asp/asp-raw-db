page_text:

P1	Nsanhwike okukubona!
P2	Nhiinze ekifaananhie.
P3	Nnhingirira bukalamu.
P4	Ghandiika era osome erina lyange.
P5	Nja ghaka.
P6	Nkooye!
P7	Amaka agalimu ebifananhie gaanhumira inho.
P8	Ghano nighaka.
P9	Nhinze Kagoya Irene.
P10	Ono niimuganda wange.
P11	Tusomera ku Isomero lyaife.
P12	Nnhendha inho langi ya bbululu.
P13	Endhuba ensanhusa okulingirira eyakyenvu.
P14	Tuja kulya dhuuma.
P15	Amaadhi gali kutoonha.
