title:	Hulwasi ehere liiboneha buubi muno
author:	Nasirumbi Lynn
artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	Lusamia

desc:	Ehele lyandi bele liboneha bulayi liba lyawulila.

page_text:

P1	Emyaka mingi ekibitilewo, ahahohome nende ehele bali abobwicha. oludalo lulala bahengawo okonya abadya musibuga.
P2	Ehele lyali liwulila bubi hulwomubili kwa hahohome kwaduha.
P3	Yabola ati, "Waholasi ohuboneha bulayi otyo? Bonaho ese, ndikubi muno. Nyala nda holasi omubiri kwange kwa boneha bulayi?"
P4	Ahahohome habola hati, "Wulila! Bukula amachi ote mushongo omale ote humuliro. ofunyeho o."
P5	Naye ehere labola, "Manyire ebyo hare, manyire. Wulere mwichange." Yamala yawawo.
P6	Nanu yola engo yata amachi mushongo yakata humuliro.
P7	Amachi nikanja otokota, yaduma yakakwamu.
P8	Amachi kasamaba omubiri kwaye nomuhira kwaye kwa tulaho!
P9	Musifo shoboneha bulayi koti ahahohome, yemeda obubi.
