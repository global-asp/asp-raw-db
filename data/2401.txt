desc:	Lukano luno luwaamba khu khubala. Balilila umanye tsikeeki tseenga tseesi Wanda ni Wantsala baalya

page_text:

P1	Wantsala ni Wanda bali basooleli babili be ntsala.
P2	Kuukhu ali khuukhola tsikeeki likhumi watsireere khu sowaani.
P3	Wantsala waabukulakho ikeeki indwela ne Wanda waabukulakho tsibili.
P4	Wantsala waabukula tsitsiindi tsine. Wanda naye waabukula tsibili.
P5	Wantsala alya tsikeeki tsenga? Ne Wanda alya tsenga? Balekhela kuukhu tsenga?
