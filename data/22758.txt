desc:	Iki nigisigo chumukobwa wakundaga kubyina. Ibi ariko bikarakaza mwarimu we

page_text:

P1	Mbyina iyo nishimye, mbyina iyo mbabaye, mbyina ndi njyenyine, mbyina iyo ndakaye.
P2	Mwarimu wanjye ati, "Ichara utuze ntubyine umunsi wose!"
P3	Ariko mama wanjye akavuga ati, "Kubyina nibyiza kubuzima. Kubyina byongera uburebure, umunsi umwe uzaba umubyinnyi!"
P4	Nanjye nti, "Umunsi umwe nzabyina abantu bishyurire kureba uko nsimbuka n'uko nikaraga, maze bagire bati, abyina neza chane."
