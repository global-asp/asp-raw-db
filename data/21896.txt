desc:	A story about rescuing young orphaned animals and caring for them.

page_text:

P1	This is Karoli.

Karoli looks after orphaned animals.
P2	Karoli found a sick baby elephant. 

They named her Nusa.
P3	Nusa got stronger. 

She played with the other animals.
P4	One day, a helicopter came.
P5	Another baby elephant had been found. 

He was given the name Miwa.
P6	Miwa was five days' old. 

Karoli fed Miwa with a bottle.
P7	A baby giraffe was brought one night.

He was named Ambia.
P8	Ambia was five months' old. 

He was very tired.
P9	Karoli climbed a tree to feed Ambia.
P10	Kopi, Kepi and Keji were three hungry cubs.

They arrived on the same day.
P11	Karoli loves all of the animals.


But there are some naughty ones!
P12	When the animals are strong, Karoli returns them to the wild.
