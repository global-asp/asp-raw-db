page_text:

P1	Ese tambisa amagulu ohugenda natiina hiisi aŋanenda,  ohuduluma embiro,  ŋalala n'ohusamba omupiira. 
P2	Aye bbe ese,  ndoma ti,  "Omutwe nj'ogusinga amagulu." 
P3	Ese tambisa engalo ohulya ebiryo ohuhola emirimo,  ohulima,  ohwosa ŋalala n'ohusaagula. 
P4	Aye mbe ese,  ndoma ti,  "Omutwe nj'ogusinga engalo." Gusinga olw'ebiitu bino. However,  the head is more important than the hands and arms. For five reasons.
P5	Omutwe gusinga kubanga guliho emoni. Tambisa emoni ohubona. The head has the eyes on it. I use my eyes to see.
P6	Omutwe guliho esuulu. Tambisa esuulu ohuŋunyira ahawoowo oba ehibulu. 
P7	Omutwe guliho amatwi. Amatwi nj'agakozesa ohuŋulira. 
P8	Omutwe guliho omunwa omuli olulimi. Tambisa olulimi ohuhomba hu lyani nafania ni linogiremo cumbi oba caayi nanogiremo sukaali. 
P9	Omutwe guliho omunwa. Omunwa gunjeeda ohuloma. 
P10	Omutwe nj'ogufuga omubiiri mu byosibyosi ebi guhola!
