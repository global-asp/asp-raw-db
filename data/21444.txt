desc:	Alyana akoze emilimu mingi okwetegekera amazalibwa ge

page_text:

P1	Alyana, Okuba Kalooli ni Antoni na abaali bali kutegeka ebintu ebya amazaalibwa ga Alyana.
P2	Antoni ni Alyana baaja okukola keeke. Okuba Kalooli yasoma kyebeetaaga okukola.
P3	“Tugaite ebilugo bino mu bbakuli enene,” Alyana yakoba.
P4	Antoni yaatabula ebilungo byonabyona yaayonoona ekifo kyali kukolelamu.
P5	“Keeke ezitowa kati,” Okuba Kalooli yaakoba.
P6	“Mikwano gyo batuuse,” Okuba
  Kalooli yaakoba.
P7	Kalooli yaalowoza ati, "lyi ekilabo ekisooka kija kuba kizito kubanga kinene."
P8	Kyali kya mwito nga tikizitowa.
P9	Antoni yaalowooza ekilabo ekiiraku kiida kuba ti kizito
  kubanga kyali kitini.
P10	Kyali eibaale elisiige ku nga lizitowa inho.
P11	Abaana baakoba bati kilo ndhala eya amabaale eyagayaga mu buziito ni kilo endhala eya amawawa.
P12	Kakee ya Alyana yaasiriira.
P13	Alyana yaanhiiga.
P14	Songawe Malyamu yaabuuza Antoni okuteebereza obuzito obwe ekilabo kye.
P15	“Kizitowa otise keeke eva okusiriira,” Antoni yaakoba.
P16	Buli muntu yaakoba, ”amazaalibwa amalungi, Alyana.”
