title:	No Soobaaku wartirta Ngayngu
author:	Abdullaahi Aadamu Bappa
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	No Fowru heɓtiri no soobaajo maaru Mboju wai.

page_text:

P1	E woodi soobaaɓe ɗiɗo, Fowru e Mboju. Fowru no remooru, somataa kuugal sam. @@Nde weeti fuu ndu daway, ndu dilla ngesa.
P2	Mboju ummataako law, sey to naange fuɗi, njayri waɗi. @@Ɗum ummoo ɗum ŋaaɓa honnduko, ɗum forta ɓaawo maajum nden ɗum dilla ngam ɗaɓɓitoygo nyamndu.
P3	Ɓurna fuu, to fini Mboju e wujja awdi seɗɗa-seɗɗa diga nder beembal Fowru. @@Nden Mboju yaha ngesa muuɗum, ɗum holla bana ɗum huway ɗum rema. Sey Mboju huɓɓa hiite ɗum sa'a awdi ɗum nyaama. @@Walaa ko Mboju huwataa sam.
P4	Nde wakkati girbol waɗi, sey Mboju wujjani soobaajo muuɗum butaali peetaaɗi. @@Ɗum hooriɗi beembal maajum.
P5	Fowru wulli ngam butaali muuɗum ngujjaama. @@Sey Mboju wi'i, "To hakkilaayi, keddu'en maaɗa ngujjantama butaali ngam kamɓe njarata bone soynde nyamndu ngam ɓe dakkiiɓe."
P6	Sey Fowru hiimi naa' seɗɗa, sey ndu heɓti dabare. @@Ndu itti ɗacce takkooje diga leɗɗe, nden ndu tarni ngesa maaru takkillum-takkillum fuu, ɗum ɗagginay gujjo.
P7	Jemmaare nde'e Mboju yahi ngesa Fowru ngam wujjugo butaali. @@Heddeeko nastugo ngesa kan, Mboju raari koo toye ngam ɗum hisa yi'eego.
P8	Mboju yi'aayi takkillum ɗum Fowru tarni ngesa kan. @@Sey takkillum nanngi Mboju. Mboju wulli-wulli ɗi mballaayiɗum.
P9	Fowru nani gullaali, ndu wurtii ndu doggi ngam nanngugo gujjo. @@Nde Fowru yi'i gujjo on, ndu hayɗini, ndu wi'i, "Too, wooɗi. Asee ɗum soobaajo am Mboju? Kanyum takkillum nanngi?"
P10	Daga jemmaare nden Fowru e Mboju senndiri, soobaaku timmi.
