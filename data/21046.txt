title:	Telefoni Ya Mama
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana and Paul
artist:	Ingrid Schechter, Sandy Lightly and Venolin Keanan Govender
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21046.png
desc:	Iyi ni inkuru ivuga ku mwana w umukobwa na telefoni ya mama we.

page_text:

P1	Nabyukaga mu gitondo kare nitegura kujya ku ishuri.
P2	Mama byaramubabaje kuko nitegura buhoro ngo nge ku ishuri.
P3	Nafashe ifunguro rya mu gitondo vuba vuba.
P4	Kwishuri nahahuriye n'incuti zange.
P5	Nafunguye agafuka ntwaramo ibitabo n'amakaye. Yooo! Nasanze mfitemo telefoni ya mama mpita nyisubiza mu gafuka.
P6	Igihe cyo kutaha kirageze. Narebye mu gafuka kange. Yooo! Nta telefoni iri mu gafuka kange!
P7	Natangiye kurira. Mwarmu arambaza ati "Wanjiku, ni ikihe kibazo ufite?" Sinashoboraga kuvuga. Nahise nihanagura mu maso n'ikiganza.
P8	Hanyuma naravuze nti "Telefoni ya mama yabuze." Mwarimu yaratangaye, dutangira gushaka telefoni.
P9	Nabyinnye nzeguruka ishuri mvuga nti "Urakoze mwarimu, urakoze mwarimu."
P10	Nashyize telefoni ya mama mu gafuka nuko ndavuga nti "Sinzongera gukinisha telefoni ya mama ukundi!"
