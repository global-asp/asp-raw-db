page_text:

P1	There lived a girl called Fatima who loved school,but she was not allow because her father does not believe in the education of a female child. One day, her mother was fed up with her father's attitude toward education.
P2	'Baban Fatima what you are doing is not is right .that you did not go to school does not mean Fatima should not ',said Fatima's mother .
P3	' keep quite,I am not going  to enrol my daughter in school.'Fatima's mother was not  happy. After some time Fatima's mother  requested  the father to allow Fatima to live with her sister,Zainab, in the city.
P4	Zainab enrolled Fatima in school. Fatima later became a nurse and worked in a hospital.
P5	One day, Fatima's  father, malam Tanko, took ill and was taken to the hospital  where Fatima works suddenly, he saw that it was Fatima who was to him he was  sorry  and apologised to his daughter after realising he has made a great mistake.
