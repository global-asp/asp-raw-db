page_text:

P1	Kaale hakaba hariyo omushaija muuto owu bakweta Kumbana
P2	Kuyabere aine emyaka abiri n'e tano, akakundwana n'omweishiki owu babeire ni beta Zangane.
P3	Bakaguma hamwe emyaka eshatu konka akaba ateine sente zo'ku mushwerwa.
P4	Eizoba rumwe omwesheiki ya yeshanga aine omwana omu kibondo ahabwokuba bakaba batakukozesa akapila.
P5	Bakazala omwana w'omwesheiki bamweta Kheri.
P6	Konka Kumbana akaba akiri omu mushomo kandi Zangane akaba aine omurimo omu ka towni hare, kyabalemesa okutura hamwe.
P7	Obweire kubwa genzire, Zangane ake funira omushaija mussya. Akeija kutula nawe nana omweisheiki we.
P8	Kumbana akashasha. Akarira yarira ahabwokuba baka mweima omuhara we.
P9	Obwere bukagyenda na Kumbana ya ffuna omukundanwa musya. Bakatula hamwe konka bakabula ruzaro.
P10	Hati Keri akaba atakukunda ishe omusya. Akaba atiine mashemererwa ahabwokuba ishe omussya akaba amutera
P11	Kuyashikere emyaka ikumi nibiri, nyoko yashara mu ngu amusindike owe ishe weiye.
P12	Thero Kumbana na Kheri bagaluka mu amashemererwa.
