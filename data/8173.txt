title:	Iliibwa
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Cathy Feek, Mlungisi Dlamini, Vusi Malindi and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8173.png
desc:	Ekigete kyogera ku nkula na migaso gya iliibwa.

page_text:

P1	Eiliibwa! Eiliibwa! Eiliibwa liwooma!
P2	Kale liwooma inho!Liwoomera ilala!
P3	Ate bwolilya,  lileeta amagezi!
P4	Nali tiidhi, nti eiliibwa nhunhu! Aye bwolilyaku,  linhuunhuula!
P5	Eliibwa likuza abaana! Lileeta amaani!
P6	Ku nvuluga! Weekomba wakamala!
P7	Nze maama owange ampa bwa iliibwa!
P8	Buti ndi mugimu! Ndya bwa bulamu!
P9	Ndi na amagezi! Ndya ne empambo!
P10	Bwoba ne ekilyo! Olyanga ne eisuusa!
P11	Alini gha asimba! Simba bwa mpambo!
P12	Eiliibwa bwe likula! Olyaku ne eitundwe!
