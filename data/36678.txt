P1	Once apon a time a man named layering saw a snake coming out of the sea.
P2	He was so afraid.  But the snake said don't  be afraid i mean no harm. All I want is for you to put me on your  head. And I will make you wise,rich,smart, strong and clever. So he put the snake on his head.
P3	He felt different he saw a fox shouting put the snake down!
P4	He did not want but the fox said the snake is going to bite out your lungs the snakes  name is beaterhem and so the snake jumped of his heard and lies.
P5	The snake tried to bite the fox but layering heat it and burned the  snake him and the fox lived happily ever after the end.
