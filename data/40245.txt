P1	I am  Lilian Kikoti  and my young brothers name is Kelvin  Kikoti . Kelvin and I  love going to school. We learn so many things at school.
P2	Teacher Eva is my favourite teacher. She is kind and loving and i enjoy English more.Our teachers are the best in the world.
P3	During the holidays , we usually visit our grandmother .She lives in Kagera  in a small hut
P4	Our grandmother is a kind woman. She loves us and tells us nice bedtime stories whenever we visit. She works very hard at the farm , cooks for us and is a village chairwoman
P5	Today we went fishing with our grandmother.It was a really fun experience together
P6	While fishing , i fell into the river .Everyone was so kind to help out. Our grandmother encourages being kind to all people .She says that kindness is a beautiful thing
