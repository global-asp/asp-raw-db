title:	Mbweha Asiye Na Shukurani

artist:	Jacob Kono

desc:	Sungura anamwokoa Mbweha kutoka shidani. Lakini

page_text:

P1	Mbweha mwenye njaa alikuwa akiwinda.
P2	Alimfukuza panya hadi akaingia chini ya jiwe.
P3	Mbweha alipochimba, jiwe liliangukia mguu wake.
P4	Sungura alimsikia Mbweha akilia. Alikimbia kuona kilichotokea.
P5	Sungura alikubali kumsaidia Mbweha. Mbweha aliahidi kumpatia chakula.
P6	Sungura alilisukuma jiwe hadi likaondokea mguu wa Mbweha.
P7	Sungura alimwuliza zawadi yake.  Mbweha alitisha kumla Sungura!
P8	Mbweha na Sungura walimtafuta mzee awaamulie kesi yao.
P9	Mbweha alitisha kumla yule mzee pia!
P10	Mzee alisema, “Hebu nione lile jiwe kabla kuamua.”
P11	Mbweha na Sungura walimwonyesha mzee lile jiwe.
P12	Mbweha alieleza jinsi jiwe liliangukia mguu wake.
P13	Mzee alimwuliza Sungura kuonyesha namna alivyolisukuma jiwe.
P14	Sungura alilisukuma jiwe hadi likaangukia mguu wa Mbweha.
P15	Mzee alisema, “Sasa ni sawa. Hebu twende nyumbani.”
P16	Mzee na Sungura walienda zao. Mbweha aliachwa akiwa amenaswa.
