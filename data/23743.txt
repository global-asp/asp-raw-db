page_text:

P1	erizooba no ryamukaaga. rurikwosa munonga.
P2	Alice, Arthur na Anabel nibenda kwoga omurindi
P3	"leka twihemu ebijwaru ryeitu tuze kwoga" Anabel na gamba
P4	"twena tugurukye twoge", Arthur nagamba
P5	eeee nimwe! murekye twoge, okwoga na kazaano" Alice nagamba
P6	omushaana gurikuza hasi, nituhurira ebeho. ebijwaru bweitu birihe?
P7	Murekye tubironde, Hasi ye miti. Mukibira. Ninga burihamwe
P8	Aha! ente! nenkyi ekiri mukanwa? "esati yangye" Arthur narira.
P9	mukama wangye! endijo,"enkyo ne kiteteyi kagye!" Alice nayomba.
P10	baana banngye,"neki ekya babaho?"
