page_text:

P1	Ibizo lami ngingu Simphiwe.
P2	Lona mndeni wami. Ngimi,  ngumma,  ngubaba kanye nomnakwethu u Sipho.
P3	Sinomndeni wami sithanda ukudla soke.
P4	Ngelanga lami lamabeletho mina nomndeni wami siya eZoo.
P5	EZoo sibona iinlwane zemangweni. Iinlwane ezifana nendlulamithi.
P6	Sinomndeni wami sithanda nokubukela abantu abagidako.
P7	Sinomndeni wami sibuyela ekhaya.Â umma ungithengele indlhulamithi yokudlala.
P8	Bekumnandi ukuba nomndeni wami ngelanga lami lamabeletho.
P9	Sinomndeni wami sithenge ukudla sadla soke.
P10	Ngiyawuthanda umndeni wami.
