desc:	Nyathi me omaro kuro dole pere. Aka dole pere obedo mere pere ma sangala kisi hongo je.

page_text:

P1	Me Azizi. Azizi obedo dole paran.
P2	Amaro Azizi rupir go bwonjo i kisi sawa.
P3	Ka acew alwoko waŋan. To arango Azizi.
P4	Ka anwaŋo Azizi agwaro rigo wiye.
P5	Apidho Azizi bende ka acamo cemo m'odikin.
P6	Ka acamo swit amiyo Azizi acel.
P7	To Azizi ki cam swit pere. Akuya ni iraŋo!
P8	Awotho thoko dyedipo ka adimo thiw yen.
P9	Amiyo Azizi thiw yen. Go kiwac kada ni,  "Walwa!"
P10	A lwoko Azizi m'olony ka fodi mama ku lwokan.
P11	A ruko ri Aziz nanga m'obokere rupir amaro cal m'obokere.
P12	Aketho Azizi dhu butan ka akidho nindo.
