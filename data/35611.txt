P1	A house is where we sleep, eat and shelter from the cold.
P2	Sadly, many  girls have lost their homes during the insurgency in Garkida village, Adamawa State.
P3	The girls and  their parents  are  homeless.
P4	They now live in open  fields and IDP camps. They are not happy.
