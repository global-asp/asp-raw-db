P1	One day, lion called a meeting for all animals.He roared very loud . Then all animals came.Then the asked.Why did you called us.And the lion said I called because I want to tell you that we have another king.
P2	Then the king said his name is called king lion.Then the animals said this small lion can not be hower king.Then the lion said he will be the king became he his my son.
P3	Then the elephant said you should have put me to be the king.Then the lion laughed ha ha ha you can not be the king when my son is still a life.And then all animals laughed at the lion.The lion was very sad.
P4	Then the lion said "let us celebrate our new king". The lion was very happy because his son was going to be the king.  The animals   wanted  a king.
