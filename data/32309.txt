page_text:

P1	Once upon a time on a little _____________ out in the country.
P2	There was a little ______________  named Daisy. "When I grow up,  I want to fly high,  high into the sky," Daisy said.
P3	But all the other chickens _____________ at her.
P4	Daisy thought I have ___________, and ________________, like other birds so I can learn to ____________.
P5	So Flap,  flap,  flap,  every day Daisy would flap her ______________.
P6	"Daisy,  we can all ______________ our wings but it's very difficult for chickens to fly," Mama told her.
P7	She looked into the ____________ and saw other _______________ flying by and dreamed that she could _________________.
P8	She would lift off the ground but _________ down again.
P9	The following day Daisy ____________ to the top of the chicken coop and flap,  flap,  flap,  she flapped her ____________. She flew into the air and flapped her wings and flapped her wings and flapped her wings and...
P10	BAM! She ________ to the ground.
P11	"I'm never going to ___________!" Daisy cried to Mama.  But mama said  that's OK Daisy you are different than other birds.  You are big and you are strong but your wings are too ________ for you to _________.
