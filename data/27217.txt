title:	Olunaaku lwaife ku ikumiro lye ebisolo
author:	Cornelius Mugulusi and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Melany Pietersen
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Amaka ganogano goonagoona gaba no olunaku olwendawulo ku ikumiro lye bisoro.

page_text:

P1	Leero tuli kuja ku ikumiro lya bisolo.
P2	Tuli kujiira mu motoka ya balambuuzi.
P3	Tuli kulengera ebisolo mu ikumiro lyabyo.
P4	Bona ekinene! Ekiyovu!
Bona ekikambwe! Ekighologoma!
P5	Bona ekinhoni kya pinki.
P6	Bona! bbalafu.
Bona! swiiti.
P7	Bona! ekifangata kya bbaluni.
P8	Maama onaatugulira bbaluuni?.
P9	Twetegekera okwira yo ghaka.
P10	Tuboine ebisolo ne ebyamasanhu bingi olwaleero.
P11	Baana imwe tusagambe. Tuli basanhufu waire bakooghu!
P12	Singa buli lunaku lubanga luno! Twandikuze mangungu!
