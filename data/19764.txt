desc:	If a basin can give birth

page_text:

P1	Hare owned a donkey and a small red basin.
P2	When Hare needed a big basin,  he borrowed Hyena's blue basin.
P3	Hyena wanted to wash his clothes in his basin.
P4	"Your basin had a baby," said Hare. Hyena took both basins.
P5	Another day,  Hare borrowed Hyena's basin again.
P6	When Hyena went to get his basin,  Hare had bad news.
P7	Hyena called the big animals to decide the truth.
P8	"Things that live and give birth,  also die," explained Hare.
