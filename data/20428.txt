page_text:

P1	Anansin shararitii dadhaaba dha.
P2	Nyaata nijallata garu billchesuudhan dadhaba dha.
P3	Anansinis Illeti bira dhaqe akka fudurashe kenituuf gafate.
P4	Illetinis, "Tole. Nyaata sochosu nagargari," jette. Ananis garuu dadhaba dha.
P5	Anansin, "Deebi’en dhufaa." Jedhe deeme
P6	Anansinis dirii isaa miila tokkoti fiixa isaa distii Illettiti maree.
P7	"Yommuu nyaanni bilchaatu dirii koo qabi naharkisii," Jedhe deeme.
P8	Anansin, "Baqeela kee naaf latta Jaldeesso?" Jedhe. Jaldeesis, "Eyyee. Muraa isaa nagargaari," jedheen.
P9	"Deebi’en dhufaa," jedhee ammas dirii isaa Baqeelichatti maree deeme.
P10	"Dinnicha kee naflaata karkaroo?" jedhee gaafate. Karkaroonis, "Eyyee qopheesu isaa nagargarii," jedheen.
P11	"Deebi’en dhufaa," jedhee ammas dirii isaa itti maree deeme.
P12	Haala kanan Anansin miila isaa 8 distii adda adda irraatti maree.
P13	Anansin diriin milaa isaa tokkoffa, lamaffa, sadaffa yommu harkifamu itti dhagahame.
P14	Diriin miila isaa 8 diistitti qabsiise harkifama dhufee. Anansinis, "Na hinharkisina!" jedhee booye.
P15	Anansinis kan dhagaye garuu hinturee. Diriichis baay’ee isaa harkisa cicita dhufee.
P16	Miili Anansi baay'ee harkifame waan tureef isaa booda shararitin miila qal'aa dheera godhaatan.
