title:	Bantfwana bemtfwebeba

desc:	Kwake kwaba nemndeni labewunebantfwana labatsatfu labalalela kakhulu. Inkinga beyikutsi bebentiwe ngemtfwebeba.

page_text:

P1	Kwesukasukela, bekunemndeni lophila ngekujabula.
P2	Bantfwana bakhona bebangakaze balwe nalomunye.@@Bebasita batali babo ekhaya kanye nasemasimini.
P3	Kepha bebangavunyelwa kutsi basondzele edvute nemlilo.
P4	Bekufanele bente wonkhe umsebenti wabo ebusuku.
P5	Ngobe bebentiwe ngemtfwebeba!
P6	Kepha lomunye webafana wafisa kuya ngaphandle elangeni lelishisako.
P7	Ngalelinye lilanga lesifiso saba nemandla kakhulu.@@Umnakabo nadzadzewabo bamecwayisa.
P8	Kepha besekwephute kakhulu!@@Wancibilika elangeni lelishisako.
P9	Labantfwana labentiwe ngemtfwebeba bakhala nababona umnakabo ancibilika aphela.
P10	Kepha benta lisu.@@Babumba lomtfwebeba loncibilikile bawenta waba yinyoni.
P11	Batsatsa lomnakabo loyinyoni bambeka entsabeni lephakeme.
P12	Njengobe bekuphuma lilanga, wandiza, ahlabelela ekuseni.
