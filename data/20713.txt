desc:	Labarin Bunu a kan damisa

page_text:

P1	A wani gari mai suna Magarya. An yi wani mutum mai suna Bunu.
P2	Da aka zo kan Bunu sai ya ce nasa labarin a kan damisa ne.
P3	Yana fadar haka sai kowa ya yi tsit.
P4	Bunu ya gyara murya, ya fara cewa “Damisa kamar kyanwa ta ke”.
P5	Sai kowa ya ce “Lallai, haka ne”.
P6	Ya ce kuma “Tana da tsawon bindi”.
P7	Sai aka ce “Ummm, gaskiyar ka!
P8	Lokacin da Bunu ya fahimci ya fara birge jama’a.
P9	Sai ya yi farat ya ce “Ai lokacin da muke yara, malamin mu ya taba nuna mana damisa a bakin rafi.”
P10	Damisa, tana da kahon ta biyu irin na barewa.”
P11	Sai kowa ya kyalkyace da dariya yana yin masa ba’a.
P12	“Bunu makaryaci, Bunu, baka san damisa ba!”
P13	Bunu ya gane kuskuren sa, sai ya ce “Daga yau ba zan
sake ba da labarin abinda ban sani ba.
P14	A garin Magarya, a kan zauna a yi ta hira da bada labarai masu kayatarwa
