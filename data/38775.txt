title:	Lebanta la  Monnamoholo Moorosi
author:	Masechaba Woza
artist:	Offei Tettey Eugene, Leo Daly, Magriet Brink and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38775.png
desc:	The story is about a young girl who saw a colourful belt in the sky. She thought it belonged to an old man who liked telling stories about colours.

page_text:

P1	Monnamoholo Moorosi o rata ho ema hara motse a phete dipale. O rata ho pheta dipale ka mebala.
P2	Maoba o ne a pheta pale ya noha e mebalabala.
P3	Maobane o ne a pheta pale ya diphoofolo tse mebalabala.
P4	Kajeno o pheta pale ya maru a mebalabala.
P5	Pula e qeta ho na mme Mooki o bona lebanta le mebalabala marung. O maketse!
P6	Baahi le bona ba maketse! Bohle ba nahana hore ke lebanta la Monnamoholo Moorosi.
P7	Mme wa Mooki o mmalla buka e buang ka diphoofolo. Sehloho sa buka ena ke Mookodi marung.
P8	Jwale Mooki o se a tseba hore lebanta la Monnamoholo Moorosi ke MOOKODI!
