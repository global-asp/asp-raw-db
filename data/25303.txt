desc:	Mwami kiio ali ni puusi engezi eyetebwa safi.

page_text:

P1	Mukyalo kyange,ghaligho mwami kiio. mwami kiio ali ni puusi gyebeta safi.
P2	Mwami kiio bwaja okukola, aleka olwigi nga lwigule.
P3	Safi atyama mu mulyango okukuuma enhumba.
P4	Embwa yamulilwana ghe bwaidha okumpi,safi agikanga.
P5	Embwa elumuka gho.
P6	mulirwana bw'akonkona kulwigi, safi mw'etefutefu
okukwadhula.
P7	mulilwana acuuka era yalumuka
P8	Safi bwaba endhala, aigalagho olwigi.
P9	Safi alya emere  ye.
P10	Oluvanhuma,Safi abukiira mu  ntebe, "tulya!
tulya!  tulya!"
P11	Mwami kiio bwaira, safi  y'edhumuula mu sanhu era ya musangaza  waka.
P12	Mwami kio ni Safi  batyaama  wansi,era bombi basoma amawulire.
