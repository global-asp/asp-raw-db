desc:	Omusajja yatandika okumeera ebyoya nga amazze okulya empanga za mwanyina. (Kano akageero gageelebwa worku debele. Mulungereza kawandikibwa Elizabeth Laird.).

page_text:

P1	Olwa tuka, wabelangawo omussajja omuggaga, yalina abana abalenzi babbiri. Ono omugagga yawandika mu dame lye mbu omwana we asooka ayina okutwala ente ze zona. Ne mutabaniwe asembayo yeeka ayina otwala ezimu ku mpanga ze.
P2	Nolwekyo, olwamala omusajja nafaa, omwana we asooka yatwala ente zona. Nomwana we owokubiri natwala empanga ezamuwebwa.
P3	Oluvanyuma lwe saawa, omutabani omuggaga yalwalaa nyoo, na genda omwo musaawo owekinansi okubuza kii kyalina okola okuwona. omusawo yamudamu ntii, "Oyiina okulaa empanga eya langi eyenjawulo."
P4	Omutabani omugagga yali tamanyi wagenda okuffuna empanga eyenjawulo. Awo yajjukila enkoko ya mutabani wee. Yatumizza abantu ewaa mugandawe.
P5	Mugandawe yabadamu, "Kyona kyenina yeeno empanga, naye wekiba nga kyekinawonya muganda wange, wakili nfiirwa eno enkoko." Olwo mutiima omugabi nabawa empanga.
P6	Abantu baa jyisaala neba jjiwa omulwade. Olwamala mugandawe yawona okuva kubulwade bweyalina.
P7	Oluvanyuma lwe saawa, ekyali kyewunyisa kyatukawo. Omubiri gwo mugagga gwatandika okumela ebyoya.
P8	Amaaso ge galitegakyikiliza.
P9	Weyagenda ewaabakulu, bamugamba nti, "Kyekino ekiivamu wooba toli mwenkanya ewa mugandawo. Otutte ente zoona, ne mpanger  ya kitamwe jyeyamulekela." Abakulu bo mukyalo ngabagamba mutabani omuggaga, "Okuwoona, mugandawo ayiina okusonyiwa."
P10	Abakulu bo mukyalo bagenda mumaso ngabagamba nti, "Tujja twala eno nekilesi nga akaboneero ka kusonyiwagana no kujyiwa mwanyinawe. Wajyiwandulako amalusu, ojja kuba owonye."
P11	Abaakulu batwala akakomo ewa mugandawe nebamugamba  nti, "Kawanduleko then osonyiwe mugandawo." Olwamala wandulako.
P12	Oluvanyuma abakulu basalawo okuwa mugandawe omwavu kitundu ya kye okya ente zeyali yatwala.
