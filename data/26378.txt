desc:	Luno lufumo lwangu olwinhonhola olunaku olwomusomi olusooka mu isomero.

page_text:

P1	Bwali ku nco inho.
P2	Nasituka netegeka okuja ku isomero.
P3	Bwali nvaala, nalowooza," Nja ku boneka ntya mu ngoye dhange edh'eisomero.
P4	Bbaabba ghange ya mperekeraku ku isomero.
P5	Neigaita ku baana banange mu kibiina. No lwali olunaku lwange olusooka mu isomero. Nafuna  okusoma kwange okusooka.
P6	Omusomesa eyairaku okwidha mu kibiina yali musomesa Maliya. Yali muleeyiriri.
P7	Bwendikula, nnhendha kuba nga omusomesa ghange.
P8	Nayenda inho okujanga ku isomero.
