page_text:

P1	Tivuliwe tikole Ngiyakujabulela kuya esikolweni
P2	Sihle sikole Setfu Kufundza bafana nemantfombatane.
P3	Sifundza ngemacembu nangebangani.Sifundza bubindze nangekuphimisa.
P4	Thishela usifundzela incwadzi lenkhulu netindzaba.
P5	Bangani bayapenda, basebentisa imibala lehlukene.
P6	Mine ngitsandza kudvweba titfombe letinkhulu naletincane.
P7	Labanye bajuba bobunjwa netitfombe
P8	Linyenti letfu sitsandza kutivocavoca nemidlalo yangaphandle.
