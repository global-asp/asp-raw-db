title:	Lunyunyu lu sinyusaho
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37289.png
desc:	Roṱhe ri a ḓivha nḓila ine lunyunyu lwa sinyusa ngayo musi lu tshi fhufha-fhufha vhusiku. Ndi tshikhokhonono tshiṱuku-ṱuku fhedzi tshine tsha nga sinyusa nga nḓila khulwane!

page_text:

P1	Koko! Koko!
P2	"Ndi nnyi?" ndi a vhudzisa ndi tshe ndo farwaho nga khofhe.
P3	"Ndi nṋe," hu amba ipfi nga u tou ṱavha mukosi.
P4	"Ndi nnyi ane a pfi nṋe?" ndi a vhudzisa.
P5	"Ndo ni vhudza uri ndi n-ṋ-e-f-h-e-d-z-i," hu amba ipfi ḽi fanaho.
P6	Ndo fhindula nda ri, "A thi ḓivhi muthu ane a pfi n-ṋ-e-f-h-e-d-z-i."@@Nda sedza arali nethe yanga ya u ḓitsireledza kha vhunyunyu yo thivha zwavhuḓi mmbete wanga.
P7	"Ndi pfi Lunyu." @@Ipfi ḽi vho tou ṱavhesa mukosi u fhira u thomani. Ḽi tou vha tsini-tsini na nḓevhe yanga.
P8	"Lunyu ufhio?" ndi a vhudzisa musi ndi tshi khou sedza nga fhasi ha nethe.
P9	Ipfi ḽa amba uri, "Lunyu-Nyu." @@Nda pfa uri ndo lumiwa kha nḓevhe."
P10	"Yowee!" nda mbo ḓi pindumuku nda thoma u lu ṱoḓa.@@Ndi tshi khou ambela nṱha nda ri, "U ḓo zwi badela."
P11	Nge nda vha ndo sinyuwa vhukuma, nda tsa mmbeteni, nda funga luvhone.
P12	I ḓo vha nndwakhulu vhukati hanga na Lunyu-Nyu lu sinyusaho!
