page_text:

P1	Let me tell you my story. I start as a small spark.
P2	I run wild when the wind carries me.
P3	We are good friends,  you and I.
P4	I cook your food.
P5	I light your night.
P6	I have many tricks.
P7	I am part of your family.
P8	But be careful when you invite me to your home. I don't like to be left alone.
