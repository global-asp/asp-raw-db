page_text:

P1	Hiina ehihira ohusitoŋa? Ngoho naŋo lwoya?
P2	Hiina ehihira owangu? Himuli naŋo ehiduuma?
P3	Mwana hi ahiraho owahye ohusitoŋa?
P4	Nj'ani ahiraho ohusitoŋa,  wananjofu oba bbaabba?
P5	Hiina ehihira ohusitoŋa,  ekiro y'amabaale.
P6	Naŋo ahagulaamu ha simiti?
P7	Hiina ehihira ohusitoŋa? Ekiro ya sukaali?
P8	Naŋo ekiro y'amooya?
