desc:	Things are fine for good friends Crocodile and Baboon

page_text:

P1	Echom ka Akinyang arai ng'ipalon luaminin. Abolianasi apei. Emujenet apei. Asubenetet ibore daang apei.
P2	Akwar apei abu Akinyang todekakin tonyou akitu. Nanyunia atamar epot ekedeke,  abu tolot nia Nyabezi loa abala ng'esia,  "Kisaki iyong ajokerea,  echamakina iyong inyami akiring ae chom."
P3	Na eiraria ng'una abu Akinyang tong'o. Nakaneni amam nga'che naepedorete akitojok ngesi pe arai akinyam lopaekeng echom.
P4	Na alosio Echom anyun lopaekeng,  Abu Akinyang tanapa ng'esi nichul ang'olol.
P5	Abu Akinyang tama,  "Kalimoki ng'esi Nyabezi atamar,  kisaki ajoker tonyam lopaekon."
P6	Abu Echom tama,  Emam ekone. Nyikinyam ayong. Adit ayong. Kidarou wadio katanyarak apaakang. Epolot ngesi akidwang ayong. Tonyam ng'esi kimwon.
P7	Nakaneni abu Akinyang tama,  "Nyali tokona apaakon?" Abu Echom tolim,  Eyei ngesi lokitoi loeyei lokwas ang'olol. "Tolimok Akinyang ng'esi tama, Kape yau ngesi atanyam."
P8	Topor Echom alokaku akinyang tamatar todoka kidiama lokitoe. Abu Akinyang kidar na tong'opia ng'esi jik. Nakaneni tosal epalonu kech kanen.
