desc:	Buli kyoyenda okukola koba oti Katonda bwa yenda.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala Yona yagya mukatale. Yayaganana ni mwine bwebaghaya.
P2	Mwine yabuuza: Ogyagha Yona? Nja mukatale.
P3	Ngya kugula  Ndogoyi. Mwine yamukoba ati, kuba oti olwokugonza kwa Katonda.
P4	Busa! indine empiya Endogoyi dhiriyo mukatale.
P5	Mu katale empiya badhibye. Haaa! Tikagula Ndogoyi mukatale.
P6	Yona yeidhusa obutakoba ati olwokugonza kwa Katonda.
