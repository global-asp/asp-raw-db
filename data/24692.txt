desc:	Once upon a time there lived a young boy Nyonga. He was born in a family of four with one elder brother. They all loved him so much.

page_text:

P1	Nyonga always helped with work at home. Even though young, he worked hard.
P2	Nyonga always joined his parents working in the field.
P3	When he got tired he rested a little under the shade of a tree.
P4	Nyonga watched as his father cut down a tree to learn how to do it.
P5	He also helped to plant a new tree to replace the one cut down.
P6	In the afternoon they headed home after a long day of work.
P7	When they got home he enjoyed playing with his dog.
