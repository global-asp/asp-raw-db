P2	He had an amma and appa. They lived him very much.
P3	Jordan loved to eat chocolates and cake.
P4	He also loved his ipad very much.Every time while eating food he wanted to see his ipad
P5	One day jordan did not eat rice and so amma did bot give him ipad. Then jordan started crying
P6	Then amma said stop crying. Then jordan stopped crying and finished eating rice.then amma gave him ipad.
