title:	Strange Baby
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Meghan Judge
lang:	English
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15033.png
desc:	A thought-provoking story about losing

page_text:

P1	I looked again more carefully.
P2	I saw a pregnant woman holding her belly.
P3	The people in my village let the woman stay with us.
P4	Later that night,  the pregnant woman cried,  "The baby! The baby is coming."
P5	The woman gave birth to a baby donkey.
P6	  "What shall we do?" the people wondered.
P7	The people ran away. The woman thought,  "What shall I do with a baby donkey?"
P8	In the end,  she decided to love her strange baby.
P9	The baby donkey grew bigger and his mother found it difficult to carry him.
P10	Mother was sad. Baby donkey was angry. Baby donkey kicked his mother.
P11	When he saw his mother on the ground,  baby donkey ran away.
P12	Baby donkey was hungry,  tired and alone. He fell asleep.
P13	When baby donkey woke up,  he saw a man standing next to him.
P14	The strange man carried baby donkey to his home.
P15	When baby donkey was old enough,  he carried the man on his back.
P16	One day,  they went to the top of a hill and lay down to rest.
P17	When baby donkey woke up,  he was alone,  again.
P18	Baby donkey was worried. He went looking for his mother.
P19	"Could that be my mother?" baby donkey wondered.
P20	Baby donkey and his mother live together again. He carries his mother around their village.
