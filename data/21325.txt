page_text:

P1	An nyinga kilwongo ni Otim Justin Junior. An awobi me mwaka apar dok abedo Kitgum. An amito bedo lagedo madit ka ceng ma adongo. Ma ono atye i kilac angwen, ono akwano i gang kwan 'Mother Majeri Primary School' ento  i mwaka 2016, ki koba i 'St. Jude Primary School' matye i Nakasongola i kabedo ma ki lwongo ni Kakooge.
P2	Deka me amara tye gweno ki layata munu ma ki waqngo i moo.
P3	Filim ma me amara ki lwongo ni 'Born to Race'(kinwyalo ka pyem ngwech). Amaro Brian ki i filim eno ni pyen en twero dwoyo motoka ma dwir adada.
P4	Dul tuku odilo na me amara tye Chelsea. Amito rwate ki latugu Diego Costa ma pwud pe atoo. Amito rwate kede pyen en pango dano ma tye idye bar tugu oko weng.
P5	Amito bene rwate ki latela lobo me Kenya, Preciden Uhuru Kenyata.
P6	An alworo tibu ento pe alworo dano. Man dong agiki me lok koma.
