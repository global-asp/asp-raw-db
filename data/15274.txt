desc:	What do you enjoy doing? Je

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping with my friend.

Ninafurahi kuruka kamba na rafiki yangu.
P2	I enjoy writing letters on the board. 

Ninafurahi kuandika herufi ubaoni.
P3	I enjoy reading books with pictures. 

Ninafurahi kusoma hadithi za picha.
P4	I enjoy eating different fruit. 

Ninafurahi kula matunda tofauti.
P5	I enjoy helping mother in the kitchen. 

Ninafurahi kumsaidia mama jikoni.
P6	I enjoy listening to my mother's stories. 

Ninafurahi kusikiliza hadithi za mama.
P7	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. 

Ninafurahi kulala kwenye kitanda kikubwa.
P8	I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?

Ninafurahi kufanya vitu hivi. Je, wewe unafurahi kufanya nini?
