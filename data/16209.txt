title:	Happy Akai

artist:	Rob Owen

desc:	A girl gets shoes for the first time.

page_text:

P1	Akai had no shoes. Other children her age     had shoes.
P2	"It is difficult to play without shoes," said Akai  to her mother.
P3	"Akai,  I think it is time for you to have shoes," said mother. .
P4	Mother woke up Akai   early the next morning.   .
P5	Akai and her mother went to the market. .
P6	They went into a shoe shop. Mother bought shoes for Akai.
P7	At night,  Akai thought about her new shoes. .
P8	Akai put on her new shoes. .
P9	Akai thought about how she would show off her shoes. .
P10	Akai wore her new shoes again. She danced around the room.
P11	"Akai,  you must not wear shoes in bed," said mother. .
P12	Akai was so happy to have shoes for the first time.
