title:	Heavy and Light
author:	Kholeka Mabeta
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle
lang:	English

desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	What is heavier? The chicken or the feather?
P2	What is lighter? The flower or the corn?
P3	Which baby is heavier?
P4	Who is heavier,  elephant or dad?
P5	Which is heavier? A kilo of stones?
P6	Or a gram of cement?
P7	Which is heavier? A kilo of sugar?
P8	Or a kilo of feathers?
