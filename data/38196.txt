page_text:

P1	Ibrahim and Amina were best friends. Ibrahim and his dad liked to walk to Amina's house.But Amina only liked to talk about herself and the money her parents had.
P2	They were going to be promoted to class 5 so the school made sure that everyone had paid for the school uniform. Ibrahim's dad couldn't pay for it. Ibrahim was sad but that didn't stop him from learning.
P3	His dad didn't have money but that didn't stop him. Everyday after school he helped other people to get money and went to his mother's store at the market to help her then went home to learn.
P4	Meanwhile, Amina went home and was playing with her neighbourhood friends.
P5	After a month Ibrahim's dad got the money to buy him a school uniform. Ibrahim was so happy.
P6	Now they were in class 5 and there was a quiz going on Amina and Ibrahim were in different groups because of that Amina became mean and told Ibrahim that they were not friends again. Watch out for part two
