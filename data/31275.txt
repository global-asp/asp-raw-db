title:	Embalugha ya mughange
author:	Mpala Paul
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lulamoogi

desc:	Omaama ali kwaba mwirwaliro ku byala era omughala we amughandiikira ebbaluwa.

page_text:

P1	Omaama yabbaire  ali kumaaliirya okupakira engoye gye omukasawo akatono.
P2	Olwabbaire afuluma omulyango, yangwire omukifubba era nakoba, "ale buti."
P3	"Oyaba ghaina, maama?" namulingiriire omumaiso nga mmubuulya.
P4	Natandikire okukunga nga ndikutaka kwaba naye aye obbaabba yansitwire okumabega ge.
P5	Obbaabba yakobere, "omaawo ali kwaba mu irwaliro kuzaala."
P6	Olwawuliire "omwana"nalekeire okukunga era nimulingirira. "Kulwaki ali kwaba okusuna omwana omwirwaliro?" niimubuulya.
P7	Obbaabba yantyamisirye okuntebe era nakoba, "ngiza kukukobera lwaki ali kwaba okusuna omwana omwirwaliro."
P8	Namuwulisisirye mpola nga ali kwinyonyola waire tinategeire kulwaki tiyabbaire na mwana eika. Obbaabba olwamalire okwinyonyola, nnbwirye, "yasigala nga antaka olaira no omwana ogondi?"
P9	Obbaabba yansembezerye okumpi era nankoba, "twiza kukutaka buli lunaku."
P10	"Nsobola okumughandiikira ebbalugha ghaire nga alyeyo?" nabwirye obbaabba.
P11	Eigulo eryo, natandiikire okughandiika ebbalugha egyo omaama.
P12	Nakwire olupapula omukitabo kyange era nintandiika okughandiika.
P13	"Maama, maama, obbaabba ankobere oyabire kuleeta mwana gondi."
P14	Kale oyanguwe era oire eika.
P15	Ndi kutaka oleete mwana mughala, nga nsobola okuzaanya naye nga akulire.
P16	Osobola okuleeta babiri nga ogondi wange.
P17	Ndi kukulindira ghaano aghanza wenyumba yaisu.
P18	Nazingiremu ebbaluwa yange okusobola okugyigha obbaabba ghange agyitwalire omaama. Olwasitukire amakeezi, ebbaluwa yaali eyabire.
