title:	Maneno ya Heshima

artist:	Ubongo
lang:	Kiswahili

desc:	Jifunze jinsi ya kusema tafadhali na asante! Karibu Lala Land! Dunia inayoshangaza ambapo watoto wenye miaka mitatu mpaka sita wanaimba na kucheza pamoja na Atu. Atu ni mtoto mwenye miaka minne ambaye ni mdadisi sana. Atu ana siri!  Kila usiku ukiingia

page_text:

P1	Ng'ombe, ng'ombe, nipe maziwa!@@Moo moo, omba vizuri!
P2	Ng'ombe, Ng'ombe, tafadhali naomba maziwa.
P3	Haya hapa tayari kwa kunywa.@@Asante rafiki!
P4	Kuku, Kuku, nipe mayai!@@Kokoo, kokoo, omba vizuri!
P5	Kuku, Kuku, tafadhali naomba mayai.@@Sawa Atu, chukua mawili!@@Asante rafiki!
P6	Chura, Chura, nifundishe kuruka!@@Hapana Atu, omba vizuri.
P7	Chura, Chura, tafadhali naomba unifundishe kuruka?@@Sawa Atu, turuke pamoja!@@Asante rafiki!
P8	Kipepeo, Kipepeo, nisukume kwa nyuma! @@Hapana Atu, omba vizuri!
P9	Kipepeo, Kipepeo, tafadhali naomba unisukume kwa nyuma. @@Sawa Atu, furahia bembea! Asante rafiki!
P10	Baiskeli, Baiskeli  nipe lifti.@@Ngrii, ngrii, omba vizuri!
P11	Baiskeli, Baiskeli  tafadhali naomba lifti.@@Sawa Atu, panda juu.@@Asante rafiki.
P12	Paka, Paka, fukuza panya! @@Nyau, nyau, omba vizuri!
P13	Paka, Paka, tafadhali naomba umfukuze panya?
P14	Panya, ondoka, Atu anakuogopa! @@Asante rafiki!
P15	Mbuzi, Mbuzi, nipeleke nyumbani!@@Meh, meh, omba vizuri.
P16	Mbuzi, Mbuzi, tafadhali unipeleke nyumbani? @@Sawa Atu, panda mgongoni.@@Asante rafiki!
P17	Atu, Atu, tupeleke Lala Land. @@Hapana, rafiki, omba vizuri!
P18	Atu, Atu, tafadhali tunaomba utupeleke Lala Land.
P19	Sawa nitawapeleka! Tucheze siku nzima na tusubiri usiku. @@Tukifumba macho upinde utakuja kutupeleka Lala Land.
P20	Angalieni, unatokea! Twende Lala Land!
