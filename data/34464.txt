page_text:

P1	Neo watsatsa ingulube yakhe kute bagibele libhayisikili lakhe.
P2	Washayisa litje endleleni. Ingulube yawa.@@Yakhala. Yatsi, "Hhontjiiii, hhontjiiii!"
P3	Tsotang wayitfola. Watsi, "Hhayi bo, yabani lengulube?"
P4	Tsotang wayigijimisa, waze wayibamba.
P5	Watsatsa ingulube wayiyisa esikhulwini sendzawo.@@Sikhulu samtjela kutsi ayibeke.
P6	Neo wendlula ngendlela.@@Weva umsindvo wengulube, itsi "Hmmm, hmmm, hmmm!"
P7	Wangena. Wajabula nakabona ingulube yakhe.
P8	Neo watsi engulubeni yakhe, "Asambe, siye ekhaya. Ngeke siphindze siligibele libhayisikili."
