title:	U tshina
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37291.png
desc:	Tshirendo tshi nga ha musidzana we a vha a tshi funa u tshina. Mudededzi wawe ha zwi funi

page_text:

P1	Musi ndo takala ndi a tshina, @Musi ndo ṱungufhala ndi a tshina, @Musi ndi na vhuludu ndi a tshina, @Na musi ndo sinyuwa ndi a tshina.
P2	Mudededzi wanga vha ri, "Dzulani fhasi! Ni songo sokou tshina ḓuvha ḽoṱhe."
P3	Fhedzi mme anga vha ri: @"U tshina zwi ita uri ni vhe na mutakalo wavhuḓi, @U tshina zwi ita uri ni vhe mulapfu, Ḽiṅwe ḓuvha ni ḓo vha mutshini-makone, @Na vha mangadza vhoṱhe!"
P4	Nṋe ndi ri, "Ḽiṅwe ḓuvha ndi ḓo tshina, nahone vhathu vha ḓo badela uri vha mmbone musi ndi tshi thamuwa na u mona-mona." @@Vha ḓo ri, "U a kona u tshina NGA maanḓa."
