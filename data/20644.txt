desc:	Embozi eno eli hebwa no musaija akaba ine ebwa eyamhulilizaga muno ne muha ekitinnisa.

page_text:

P1	Hakabahaliyo omusaija ne ebwa' e
P2	Ya gyendaga muno.
P3	Embwa yakoraga muno kandi ya genda mukatare kugura eyama
P4	Yatabaraga emaizi kuruga haiziba
P5	Neloreraga buli kintu omuka
P6	Ekirokimu, omusaija akasima embwa ye mugenri eyamanyi muno habyokuba owesiga
P7	Embwa yasemereya mno kandi yeyongeza kukoora muno.
