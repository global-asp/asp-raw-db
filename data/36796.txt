desc:	Ngun ka icam sha kwagh u wankwse u gen u soo amar kpishi yô. Tica na soo kwagh la ga

page_text:

P1	Ka a saan mo iyol, @M vine amar, ka mea zungwen, @M vine amar, ka a huan mo, @M vine amar, shima ngi a vihim, @M vine amar.
P2	Tica wam kaa er, "Tema girgir! Nyian cii de vinen amar ga."
P3	Ngôm di kaam er, "Amar a vinen ka a nau mkpeiyol.@Amar a vinen ka a nau u tav, @Iyange igen me lu kwase u vinen amar, @A Kpiligh hanmô or iyol!"
P4	Shi M kaa mer, "Iyange igen me vine amar ior a kimbi nyaregh u nengen mo zômun, tiligh ishor. Vea kaa er, "Wankwase ne fa amar DEDOO."
