page_text:

P1	One day@ there are couples who dream of having their own house. they are farmers who work hard for their children to provide a good place to live. even if they are struggling and it's hard you can see on their faces the smile
P2	the man often says that the house he wants is a big house and has a beautiful pink color. and the woman wants the color to be blue even if they don't agree with the color they go through it with laughter.
P3	you can see the two of them being happy to be married. when they talked about the rooms, the woman said that she wanted three rooms to keep her child alone.
P4	the man agreed as he wished. I am one of those who support their dream. they will fulfill my story as far as it goes.@-L.P.S.
