title:	Akipi
author:	John Emongot
artist:	Wiehan de Jager, Benjamin Mitchley, Rijuta Ghate, Zablon Alex Nguku, Rob Owen, Catherine Groenewald, Jonathan Field, Katrien Coetzer, Melany Pietersen and Natalie Propa

desc:	Ikamunit akipi elimonori epone lo itwasamayere kesi.

page_text:

P1	Ajok engo noi otoma akwap ana. Engarakit engo eesi otoma iboro lu'ipuu.
P2	Ikerete eesi na'jengo aimat emwana akolong.
P3	Itosomayete engo Iduwe aiduu.
P4	Arai engo erot lo ataker.
P5	Arai engo aijar na me'jaasi iitoo.
P6	Arai engo ere lo agarya.
P7	Itwasamayete eesu engo aicuu ikus guwen.
P8	Emwanu akwap,  atepuni engo kwape edowu.
P9	Enoku ekus togo, erai engo abuni aitudeng.
P10	Iwomunete eesi engo angolol aitwasam orerya kus.
P11	Ipote eesi na'jai engo aikam agarya nu inyamete ore ka'gwelanar.
