page_text:

P1	Attino kaŋ bisa no Nakitto sintin ga koy lokkol.
P2	A furo kalaasu taji ra,  a du coro taji yaŋ.
P3	Tirey kaŋ gonda foto yaŋ ga kaanu Nakitto se.
P4	Cawkey fatta taray ga caw taji yaŋ te.
P5	I waani hantumiizey tuuyaŋey.
P6	Cawo banda,  i goro ga fulanzam. Nakitto jirbi cawandika jare ga.
P7	Cawo waate,  cawkey ga fatta mo ga koy bare-bare.
P8	Nakitto nda nga ɲa ga caw care bande da I go fu.
