desc:	A boy sees a car and a truck involved in an accident.

page_text:

P1	Etei eongo elore ekeri oguudo koleleuni kwap.
P2	Aanyuni do bobo eongo imotoka yen didi edoki amukurat.
P3	Outa do ederepa lo elore eineri kosimu ke kitei ekesa je kinga.
P4	Mam ketei oguudo.
P5	Otakanuni do akangas naka apolon.
P6	Opote do imotokan iyarei. Adiope na ipolisin keda na edakenenei adekak (abiolos) kanu aingarakin lu emusunguna.
P7	Opote do bobo amotokan acie aarei airiar alore keda abiolos.
P8	Abu eongo ariengu.
