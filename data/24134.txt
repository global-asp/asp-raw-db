desc:	Ouna ngoo fiku wa pwilikinine olutu loye eshi tali kulombwele?

page_text:

P1	Nena itandi i kofikola. Ofikola oya fuda!
P2	Nena itandi tale oTV. Ka kuna nolusheno nokuli.
P3	Ohandi ningi shike mbela?

Ohandi pwilikine olutu lange eshi tali ti.
P4	Ondina oku kala nda mwena filu. Opo ndi ude eshi tali ti.
P5	Paife ondi udite eshi handi fuda.

Omufudo tau i mo, tau aluka mo.
P6	Ohandi dulu oku fuda mokule. Sssssssssssss!

Ohandi dulu oku fuda pedu. Mmmmmmm.
P7	Pafe ondi udite eshi omutima wange tau denge.

Doodom, doodoom, dooo dooom.
P8	Oshike mbela ndi na oku ninga opo omutima wange udenge mokule ile udenge tau endelele?

Ah! Ondi na oku nuka mombada lwomilongo mbali.
P9	Paife omutima wange otau denge nee tau endelele.
P10	Shama nda tula ominwe dange poshikesho shange, ohandi dulu oku uda eshi omutima wange tau denge.
P11	Ondi li udite eshi handi yolo.

Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Paife ondi li udite eshi handi kwena. 

Waaaa haaa!
P13	Ondi udite eshi handi pandula omake.

Pwaka, pwaka, pwaka.
P14	Ondi udite medimo lange tamu kukuma.

Grrrr, grrrr, grrrr.
P15	Edimo lange ota li ti "Ondi udite ondjala!"
P16	Eyulu lange oli udite edimba loukuki tali di kokombifa yaMeme.

Paife onda hala oku uda eehamama dange tadi taafina oukuki ovo!
