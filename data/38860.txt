P1	Freeda, the brown, soft, Burmese cat, lives next door to the school.
P2	Freeda wants to visit and help the children with their work.
P3	During the maths lesson. Leeto screams “eeeeeeee”! David jumps up. “ What’s the matter, Leeto?”
P4	David runs to the office to fetch Miss Smith.
P5	Miss Smith picks Freeda up. She scratches Miss Smith. “ Oh, no, Freeda has scratched me” says Miss Smith. “Naughty, Freeda!” says Leeto.
P6	Freeda does not want to go out of the classroom.
P7	Miss Smith tickles Freeda’s soft brown head. Freeda says “purrrpurrpurrr”.
