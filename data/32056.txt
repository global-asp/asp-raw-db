title:	Taalol: Maaguru Hokkii Koyɗe
author:	Jibrilla Eggi
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Maaguru wiʼi hokkay dabbaaji koyɗe ɗi nana belɗum yahugo ba yimɓe.

page_text:

P1	Ndenno dabbaaji ngaʼlaa koyɗe. Ɗi fuu e ɗi ndasoo.
P2	Yimɓe tan ngoodi koyɗe ɗe Maaguru hokkiɓe.
P3	Nyalaande feere Maaguru. Wiʼi hokkay dabbaji fuu koyɗe. O yiɗi ɗi njaha. @  @Nden o anndiniɗi.
P4	Dabbaaji mbiʼi, "Boɗɗum, min keɓa koyɗe." @@Ɗi ngaɗi ka yimugo e wamugo.
P5	Dabbaaji mbiʼi, "Dasoraago reedu ɗum bone."@ @E ɗi nana nawɗum.
P6	Nde nyalaande nden wari dabaaji fuu njahi wuro@Maaguru.@  @Dabbaaji ngaɗi corol, tireeji, nyiibi, boji, ngilanngeeji, colli, baaɗi bee mawndu fuu.
P7	Kala dabba fuu heɓii koyɗe nayi, colli boo koyɗe ɗiɗi.
P8	Koo ɗume sanjii nde ɗi keɓi koyɗe.@  @Goɗɗi ngami bee seyo, goɗɗi boo njani.
P9	Ɗi njahi ɗi kolli yimɓe. Dabbaaji mbiʼi, "Min meetataa dasaago e leydi."
P10	Kaatoota sakitii warugo. Maaguru ƴaminga, "Walaa ko lutti gaɗa na?"@  @Kaatoota jaabii, "Eh, min tan sakitii."
P11	Maaguru numi, "Hannga tan lutti. Ko mi waɗata bee koyɗe luttuɗe?"@  @Nden Maaguru hokki kaatoota koyɗe luttuɗe fuu.
P12	Kaatoota nani belɗum wiʼi, "Mi ɓuray dabbaaji luttuɗi fuu yaawugo."
P13	Baawo kaatoota dillii, mboodi maa yottii wuro Maaguru. Ndi wiʼi Maaguru, "Hokkam koyɗe."
P14	Maaguru wiʼi, "Mi hokkitirii koyɗe fuu. Toye ngonnoɗa?"
P15	Maaguru raari koo e woodi koyɗe lutti.
P16	Maaguru wiʼi mboodi, "Koyɗe luttaayi." Mboodi dasii hooti, heɓaayi koyɗe.@  @Diga nyalaande nden, mboodi acci ɗaanaago. E ndi reena nyalaande, Maaguru fuɗɗitata hokkugo koyɗe.
