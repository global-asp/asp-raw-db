desc:	Rabbit invites his friend Monkey over to play

page_text:

P1	A siku rin'wi a Mpfundla ni Havu avali va nganu. Hi nkama wa dlala Mpfundla wu rambi a mu nganu a kuva va tlanga.
P2	A Mpfundla wuku: A hi tlangi hiku svekana. A Havu yi hlamula yiku: Hinga svi yentsisa aku yini svolesvo?
P3	A Mpfundla u hlamula wuku: A hi tsiveli ndzilu.
P4	Hi cela mati ka bota,  hi gama hi thlathleka a ndzilweni.
P5	Mina ni ta ngena a ku sungula. A loko ni gongondza a xi mbonyo xa bota ni pfuleli ni ta huma.
P6	A Mpfundla yi ngena a ku sungula. A nkama a mati manga sungula a ku kufumela.
P7	A Mpfundla wu gongondza,  a Havu yi pfula.
P8	A nkama ku nga ngena a Havu,  a Mpfundla wu teka a ribzi dza dzikulu u thlanteka a henhla ka xi mbonyo xa bota.
P9	A nkama a mati manga sungula aku bila,  a Havu yi gongondza a Mpfundla a wu pfulanga a xi mbonyo xa bota.
P10	A Havu yi gama yifa,  a Mpfundla yi gama yida a munganu wakwe!
