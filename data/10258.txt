page_text:

P1	Look at our animals we keep at home.
P2	The cow gives us milk.
P3	The rabbits and cats are very friendly to human beings.
P4	The horse says,  "Neigh."
P5	The cows and goats are paid as bride price in Uganda.
P6	The chicken says,  "Cluck."
P7	The dog is a source of security at our homes.
P8	The farmer looks after them so much.
