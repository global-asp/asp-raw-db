title:	Abakozi

desc:	Abakozi nono ya Bana Uganda. Enono eno ekungaania abantu baayambagana mu kukola emirimu. Olufumo lugema ku kuyambaku Nangobi Mutesi okudhuba mu nnimiroye.

page_text:

P1	Ta! Ta! Ta! Twawulira akade ak'ekyalo nga kakuba munco inho. "Abaisuka, musituke mutegeke embago dhaimwe," dhaadha yayeta ng'ali mu kisenge.
P2	Twayayuuya twegolola twava mu buliri. Olunaku olwo kwali kukola e Butende, Buwaanume mw'isamba lya  Nangobi Mutesi. twaja mw'iterekero twatolayo embago dhoona dhoona.
P3	Twaja n'embago mw'isamba. Abebutende boona boona baidha okulimiraku  Nangobi Mutesi.
P4	Buli biseera bya Mwaka, abataka baalimiranga abakaire. Buli muntu bamugha eikatala. Eikatala lino balyeta kola.
P5	Mukasana ghagati, abataka baawuumuliranga mu muvule omunene. Baawuumuriranga ku malwa.
P6	Oluvainhuma, abataka baaduba baamaliriza era  Nangobi Mutesi yaasagamba. yeebaza abataka boona boona olw'obuyambi.
P7	Mu Uganda, abantu bakola okuyambaku abandi basobole okukola emirimu gyaibwe. Oyambaku abantu ku kyalo kyaimwe?
