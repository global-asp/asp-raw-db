page_text:

P1	Musa utsandza kudlala ibhola. Musa utsandza kutidlalela yedvwana.
P2	Sifiso naye utsandza kudlala ibhola. Sifiso utidlalela yedvwana naye futsi.
P3	Musa udlala ibhola nebangani bakhe bo Sipho,  Sifiso na Mandla. Musa nebangani bakhe batsandza kudlala libhola letinyawo.
P4	Esikolweni Musa udlala libhola letinyawo nebangani bakhe bo Sipho,  Mandla kanye naSifiso. Bonkhe badlala umdlalo labawutsandzako ndzawonye.
P5	Musa ubuya esikolweni akasayiboni ibhola yakhe,  uyakhala akasakhoni kujabulela Mpilo longumnakabo.
P6	Ndzadze wakhe lomncane longu Dudu wenta imitamo yekumtfulisa,  Dudu utsembise umnakabo Musa kumfunisa ibhola yakhe.
P7	Musa ujabulile nemndeni wakhe,  phela batali bakhe bamtsengele ibhola lensha kanye netembatfo ekudlala ibhola.
