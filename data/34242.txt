page_text:

P1	Macario lived with his son, Yoyo in a small village. @@His wife had left him for a rich magician.
P2	When Macario and Yoyo woke up the next morning, she was gone. To this day, they have neither seen, heard nor known where she is.
P3	After waiting for a year, Macario and Yoyo left the sad life behind. @@They took their donkey and traveled far.
