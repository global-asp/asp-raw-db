P1	Once in the night when it was thundering,there lived a girl called Maddie that lived in a mansion.Maddie's mum said "Let's go to the zoo." Maddie replied "ok" so they went.On there way,Maddie found something furry looking at her.
P2	So she got closer it was white furry.Then she picked it up and it was...........a kitten!@Maddie thought it was so cute that she hid it in her bag away from her mum.
