P1	One day, two boys and their dad were sitting and eating. They were poor and did not have that much money.
P2	So their dad told them to go give their friend nopil food because he also doesn't have that money for food.
P3	Until, they were stopped by a big, bad monkey.
P4	He took the food on a side and started eating it so fast. The two little boys were scared.
P5	They went home and told their dad about everything that happened but, he did not believe them. He gave them another basket of food they went again but, they got attacked by the monkey again
P6	The 2 boys started to pull it. The monkey started to pull.
P7	Until finally their friend nopil's dog Peter came to the rescue
P8	The End
