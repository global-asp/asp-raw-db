desc:	Singuli abaabira okumukulu babwirye entegeka gyembiibbi

page_text:

P1	Omunywi yaali nsude munene. Yaali mutulugunyi.
P2	Lyabbaire ikulu lya malike.
P3	Abamwabiraku bamughaire olugoye olwenjawulo.
P4	''Ngiza kwambala luno okumutwe gwange,''Omunywi nakoba
P5	''Buli muntu ayabire okunkola gyange. Timwambuulya!'' Omunywi na kaabuka.
P6	Buti, abagoberezi be bamwabiireku. Omunywi obwatuuma, ni gyituuma.
P7	Aye enkoofiira okumutwe gwa Munywi yaali enyweire.
P8	Yekwaite okumutwe aghaigulu. Ensude ni gyekwata okumitwe.
P9	''Kino kiruma,'' Omunywi yakobere,'' gyaibbiirye.
P10	''Kino kinyweire inho!'' kino kinyweire inho,'' gyaibbiirye.
P11	Omunywi yagwire aghansi, ghaigulu ghekimante. Gyona gyona gyamwibbiire.
P12	Omukulu gh'ensude yafiire. Abagoberezi be kyabalumire, aye babbairegho.
