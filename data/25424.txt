desc:	Olufumo luno lugema ku Wakamyu eyali tabangaku na kabaga koonakoona okuva bwe yazaalibwa kubanga amaka gaibwe gaali maavu inho aye bwe baafunaku empiiya

page_text:

P1	Ninze Wakamyu aviira ku kaalo akatono ketweeta Bukaye
P2	Amaka gaife gaali maavu nga titusobola nakuzimba  nhumba nkalamu.
P3	Nga tulya ekibu kiralala kyonka olunaku.
P4	Nga buli nco, nsindikibwa mu nhimiro okuja  okulabirira omuwemba.
P5	Okukungula bwe kwatuuka, n'olwali oluingayo mu  nkungula dhoonadhoona.
P6	Bbaabba wange yasanhuka inho era yansubuza  okunkolera akabaga.
P7	Lwali luvainhuma lwa myaka 25 ghe nakuliza amazaalibwa gange.
P8	Mwami Wankoko yali musanhufu aye yalowoozaamu okuja n'obwana bwe obuto.
P9	Mu nco  inho, bbaabba yatusitula okutandiika  okutegeka emere.
P10	Amangu ago twatandiika okugabula abageni baife.
P11	Nga okulya kuweire, abageni baawoomerwa omugaati.
P12	Oluvainhuma  Wakamyu yaafuna ebirabo kamaala  okuva mu mikwano gye.
P13	Ku nkomerero, mwami  Wambuzi yaigulawo amakina. Yali mulungi mu kukina.
