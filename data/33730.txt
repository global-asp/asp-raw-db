P1	Bling was passionate about soccer. @@When talking about the game, he often said, "Soccer is life."
P2	Bling was one of Coach Tsepo's best players.@@The coach was proud of his team. Many wanted to join them.
P3	An important match was coming. @@The team practiced every day.@@Everyone was excited about the big game!
P4	The coach from the opposing team was a jealous man.@@He was very envious of Coach Tsepo's success.
P5	The jealous coach went to the field the night before the match. @@He planted something dangerous. @@He planted umuthi!
P6	Bling got off to a hot start in the match. @@He was running to score.
P7	He missed the shot, and fell down!@@"Bling?" shouted the crowd@@"Bling?" pleaded Coach Tsepo.
P8	"Nothing will keep me down!" said Bling to himself.@@"Soccer is life."@@He got up, and played until the end.
