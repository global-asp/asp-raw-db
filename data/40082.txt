page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there lived a man called Chalo.He loved traveling and touring new places.Chalo had a long time friend called Chausiku.One day decided to visit near by park.
P2	On his way he meet Chausiku going to the market who got interested in joining him.After learning that the entrance fee is fifty shilling he decided to accompany his friend to the park.They both walked happily together.
P3	In the park Chausiku's mother saw us playing together.She called him "let's go home, I will punish you!" Chausiku apologized for not asking for permission to visit the park.She forgave him and cautioned him not to repeat again.
P4	In the park we saw various animals and had a good time.After planning on our next visit  to MBOTELA PARK we waved wishing each other a nice time at 4.00 o'clock.I went home happily looking forward to the next visit.
