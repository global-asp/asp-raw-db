desc:	Papa afuluka obusera.

page_text:

P1	Juma yekasinjia okhutsia mwisomero.
P2	Papa nae afulukanga obusera khulwe shokhulia eshie itsuli.
P3	"Mama yamanya okhufuluka obusera obunulu!" Juma nabola.
P4	Obusera buwetsanga Juma amani akokhutsia mwisomero.
P5	Obusera buyire.
P6	Papa atsukha amabere mubusera.
P7	"Papa,  embara mbuometakho isukari musera khobube obunulu muno," Juma nabola.
P8	"Mama afulukanga busera bunulu khushira papa," Juma yapara.
P9	"Papa,  onyala okhuchama emetekho isukari mubusera?" Juma areba.
P10	Papa aheresia Juma isukari.
P11	"Ma! Papa! Orere omunyu mubusera bwanje?"
P12	"Papa,  mama nakalushe nango lina?" Juma areba.
