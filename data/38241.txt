desc:	The story about attractive and energetic teacher

page_text:

P1	One day, in Islam's class the new sport trainer. The students are gathered by teacher. And they are greeting with each other. The teacher introduce herself. "Hi guys, my name is Khuni. I'am your new Pi teacher...."
P2	In evening, she gather all students and warn them about new competition between schools. " All pupils may participate. From today we should prepare for this."
P3	Amani and Husein are the best players in the preparation.
P4	In the competition the group is winner. All pupils are happy. After it, all students say together "we love you teacher" to their lovely teacher.
