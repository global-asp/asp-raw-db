desc:	Sibuza ukuthi kungani izinto zinemibala eyahluka hlukene.

page_text:

P1	Kungani isibhakabhaka siluhlaza?
P2	Asihlali siluhlaza. Kunjalo?
P3	Kungani amaqabunga eluhlaza satshani?
P4	Awahlali eluhlaza satshani. Akunjalo?
P5	Kungani ubhanana uphuzi?
P6	Ubhanana ovuthiwe uphuzi. Ongakavuthwa uluhlaza satshani.
P7	Kungani isambulela simnyama?
P8	Akusizona zonke izambulela ezimnyama.
P9	Kungani igazi libomvu?
P10	Kukhona izinikamandla egazini okuyizona ezenza igazi libe bomvu.
P11	Kungani usawoti umhlophe?
P12	Angazi.
