desc:	Kodi chilango cha mnyamata wadyera amene anadya zipatso zambiri ndi chiyani?

page_text:

P1	Tsiku lina, amayi anagula zipatso zosiyanasiyana.
P2	"Kodi zipatsozi tidya nthawi yanji?" Tonse tinafunsa.@@"Tidya zipatso zimenezi usiku uno," anatero amayi.
P3	Mchimwene wanga Rahim ndi wadyera.@@Analawa zipatso zonse. Ndipo anadya zambiri.
P4	Taonani zimene wachita Rahim!" anakuwa mchimwene wanga wamng'ono.@@"Rahim ali ndi khalidwe loipa komanso lodzikonda," ndinanena.
P5	Amayi anakwiya ndi Rahim.
P6	Tonse tinakwiya ndi Rahim.@@Koma Rahim sanafune kupepesa.
P7	"Kodi mumulanga Rahim?" anafunsa mchimwene wanga wamng'ono.
P8	"Rahim, upepesa posachedwapa," anamuchenjeza amayi.
P9	Rahim anayamba kusamva bwino.
P10	"Mmimba mwanga mukuwawa," Rahim ananong'ona.
P11	Amayi anadziwa chifukwa chiyani Rahim anayamba kudwala.@@"Zipatso wadya zija zikumulanga ameneyo." Amayi anaganiza choncho.
P12	Pamapeto pake, Rahim anatipepesa tonse. @@"Sindidzachitanso dyera," analonjeza motero.@@Tonse tinakhulupilira zimene ananenazo.
