desc:	Kalabushe okuna omwenyo omwanawa ye okuhole okupopya

page_text:

P1	Nale nale okwa li kuna okakadhona edhina lako OKalabushe. 

Kalabushe okakadhona oka popyagani noonkondo. Ashike meme gwe okwe muganda opo ahakale ta popyagana unene. Kalabushe I na pwaakena kumeme gwe.
P2	Meme omu mwaina gwa meme gwakalabushe. Okwali hazi kooha dhodudu, Kombinga ndjiya yomukunda gwawo. 

Esiku limwe omu mwaina gwaina okwa kwatwa kuuvu. No kapali omuntu gumwe ngoka ta vulu okumu yambidhidha.
P3	Meme gwakalabushe okwali iipyakidhila noonkondo. Okwali kwa toka sho ape kalabushe iikulya ei faalele omu mwaina gwaina ngoka ali taalukwa kuuvu.
P4	Mondjila yokuya Kalabushe okwa tsakaneke Sinson. 

Sinson okwali embungu ndoo la lundulukile molupe lomuntu.
P5	Sinson okwa pula kalabushe, "Oshike shoka wuumbata?" Kalabushe okwa ya mukula, "Onduumbata onyama, omai noshoyo omahini." 

Ashike Kalabushe okwali akumagidhwa kumeme gwee opo aapopye shoka uumbata.
P6	Kalabushe okwa ti ishewe, "Otefala iikulya komumwaina gwameme ngoka kuuvite nawa." Sinson okwii lafa komilungu molokuuva onyama ndjoka kwali yuumbatwa kukalabushe.
P7	Sinson okwa matuka nokuya komeho gakalabushe sigo okegumbo lyo mumwaina gwaina.
P8	Okwa ponapo meme omumwaina gwaKalabushe ndee tisiikile nekumbatha ndjoka omumwaina gwaina ali hiisikile nalo.
P9	Sho Kalabushe athiki, megumbo omwali mwa mwena noonkondo. Okwa yi megumbo no kwiithana, "Meme oulipeni?"
P10	Kalabushe inuuva nande ondaka ya sha yamumwaina gwaina. Okwa yi meni lyondunda moka hamulala omumwaina gwaina. No kwa li ahaluka noonkondo sho amono omuntu iisikila nekumbafa enene.
P11	Kalabushe okwa pula, "Meme, Omolwashike omakutsi goye omanene nena?" 

Sinson okwa yamukula eli kohi yekumbafa, "Opo tevulu okukuva nawa."
P12	Kalabushe no kwa pula ishewe, "Meme omolwashike ano omeho goye omanene nena?" 

Sinson okwa yamukula, "Opo te vulu okukumona nawa."
P13	Nalwa shugunina Kalabushe okwa pula, "Meme, omolwashike okana koye okanene nena?" 

Sinson okwa yamukula, "Opo ndivule okukulyapo!" Sinson okwa nhuka kombete nokuponapo Kalabushe.
P14	Kalabushe okwa tsikile nokupopyagana sho eli mela lyaSinson. Okwa pula omapulo ogendji.
P15	Peshulilo, Sinson okwali aloloka mokupulwapulwa olundji kuKalabushe nokwe musiyako.
P16	Kalabushe namu mwaina gwaina okwali ya kwa thelwa kaanamukunda yawo. 

Okuza tuu esiku ndjoka, Kalabushe ina popya we naantu keeyashi.
