desc:	This story shares the caring insights of a girl about how young and old people can bring happiness to one another. (This story was created at a holiday programme run by Sophiatown Community Psychological Services in Johannesburg

page_text:

P1	Ngizonixoxela ngami neKhaya labadala.
P2	Lesi isakhiwo esikhulu esiseBertrams, eGoli. @@Izindonga zakhona zimbalabala, zihlanzekile futhi uma ungena ngaphakathi kuthule cwaka. Abantu abahlala lapha bedala kakhulu.@@Ngelinye ilanga savakashela kule ndawo nomama.
P3	Leli Khaya labadala ngilithanda kakhulu!@@Njalo uma ngibona abantu abadala ngiyajabula futhi ngiyakwazi ukubasiza.
P4	Ngelinye ilanga ngafika khona ngabageza izinwele abanye.
P5	Ngaphinde ngabapha ukudla.@@Nakuba abanye babo babengethusa, kodwa kwakungijabulisa ukuba nabo.
P6	Kwakukhona ugogo owayenolaka futhi engafuni nakancane ukusizwa. Wayevele athethe nje.
P7	Kwakukhona omunye engambamba izandla wangabe esafuna ukungidedela. Izandla zakhe zazithambile futhi zifudumele.@@Wayenegama elifana nelikadadewethu, u-Aisha.
P8	Ngibathanda kakhulu abantu abadala.  Basixoxela izindaba zakudala futhi engingeke ngizazi.
