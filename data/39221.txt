desc:	Read how the animals fall into a trap and never get a chance to eat the royal feast in 40 years.

page_text:

P1	How are you lion said father elephant isn't it a great day to drink water in the summer? hmm said lion it is,why don't we catch some fish for our royal feast yes my king said father elephant they both caught lots of fish and made a feast.
P2	Zebras we're you going to screamed mother elephant I don't know said father zebra this stampede just started okay in that case I'll call the king and queen oh king Lionel, queen Lisa we have a stampede all the Zebras have gone astray.
P3	Mother elephant started looking for the Zebras she went everywhere but still no sign of the Zebras only baby zebra returned and said "they have gone to the village they might get killed there hurry up king and queen baby lion also came.
P4	The king sent more animals to the village to find the stampede of Zebras but unfortunately they were caught and had no escape the king heard this news and was very furious but still the king sent George the giraffe to go to the village.
P5	Giraffe eventually got distracted by other animals and he met a cat a chicken two birds and a man and a woman he said goodbye and continued his quest his next stop was the village. He got lost in the city and reached the village at noon.
P6	Bad luck for giraffe there was a hunter who set up booby-traps giraffe got stuck and was helpless he cried out names like king! queen!! why did you send me on this quest why!! screamed giraffe.
P7	The hunter saw giraffe and wanted to kill him he threw a spear and by mistake hit the rope giraffe came out.
P8	Giraffe ran but still didn't manage to escape.
P9	The hunter grabbed on to his tail and while giraffe was running the hunter thought he will find delicious animals he and giraffe reached the kingdom the hunter was not scared of lions or any wild animal he killed all the animals.
