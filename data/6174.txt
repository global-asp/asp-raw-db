title:	Ewammwe Waliyo Ki?

desc:	Olugero luno luyigiriza ku bintu ebimu ebisangibwa mumaka ate lulaga  abenganda nti bamugaso eri bannaabwe.

page_text:

P1	Ewaffe waliyo enyumba gyetusulamu. Taata yeyagizimba.
P2	Waliyo ente zituwa amata.
P3	Waliyo emiti gireeta enkuba. Gituwa ebisiikirize.
P4	Ewaffe waliyo ebibala. Maama yeyabirima mu nnimiro.
P5	Ewaliyo ebimuli ebisanyusa amaaso.
P6	Ewaffe waliyo ensawo maama gyakwata.
P7	Nze nina ekiteeteyi baaba yeyakigula.
P8	Suuna alina essaati. Kkojja yeyegigula.
P9	Muto waffe alina engatto. Senga yeyazigula.
P10	Byonna birungi nnyo.
