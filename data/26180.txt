desc:	Maako akyala ku mu zuu

page_text:

P1	Maako ava eka okwolera zuu ni mukenkedhe ghe Mariya.
P2	Mu lugendho lwaibwe, Mariya ni maako baagula ebyokulya.
P3	Mariya yalumwa omutwe era yatandiika okulira.
P4	Maako yanonia akalwariro okumpi afune ku bulezi
P5	Tiyayagaana yo musawo oba omwidhandhabi
P6	Baali bolekeire okwogera ku naabantu ku byobulamu.
P7	Maako ya woigana afune ku buyambi era abantu bayeta omusawo.
P8	Maako yagha Mariya eidhagala okuva yo'musawo era yamwiza yo eka.
P9	Era tiyaja kubona bisolo, kuba mukenkedhe ghe yali alwaire.
