P1	Once upon a time, there lived a boy named Alex. He is 27 years old and lives far far away. He takes care of an elephant. One Day, the boy brought the elephant to the lake for it to drink water.
P2	After a few minutes, a lion came to drink. The boy shouted and ran away with his elephant and came back to his cave. The Next Day, when the boy came out of his cave he saw the elephant and the lion fighting!!!
P3	The boy ran and called all of the Zebra's. They ran to the cave a to save his elephant. Alex just hid to his cave cause he was so scared. Alex thinked of how he could help, and he thought of getting the stick that he brought to his cave.
P4	He shouted "GET OUT OF HERE LION!!!!!" The lion got scared and ran away. The elphant ran with the lion. The Next Day, the elephant came back with the lion and became friends. The Elphant and the Lion lived happy with Alex.
