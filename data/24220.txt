title:	My Beautiful Mother
author:	Nyangoma Peninah
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/24220.png
desc:	This is a story about a mother who loves her children and wants to keep them happy. She goes with her children to the market and also watches over them before sleeping.

page_text:

P1	My mother is called Edith and she teaches me how to behave well.
P2	She also teaches me how to keep clean and organized because am a girl.
P3	Mother asks me to pray to God before i sleep because God is my father.
P4	She then wishes me a good night and tucks me in my bed.
P5	Our mother also loves my brother. She takes him to school everyday.
P6	She also takes me to the market. We love her so much because she is good.
