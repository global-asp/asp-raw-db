page_text:

P1	Eriamun kolong Echom ka Akinyang lokwaas a anam.
P2	"Ilomit ai?" king'is Echom, "nakipi." tama Akinyang.
P3	"Ai ilomit yong?" tama Akinyang. "Lokito."
P4	"Ipedori yong akilulumare a?" King'is Akinyang.
P5	"Nyapedori ayong akilulumare," tatachu Echom.
P6	"Ibwa ketatam. Nyiyem," tolimu Akinyang.
P7	Ani edoli kiding anam tama Akinyang asak akiring.
P8	Tobul Echom noi. Adikino Akinyang enyami ng'esi!
P9	Abu Echom tasak eger lo iidakinia Akinyang.
P10	Abu Echom toriamu atamet.
P11	"Kainakini  yong etau kang, nak ng'esi," tama Echom.
P12	"Nakaneni eyei etau kidiama ekitoe yeke," tama Echom.
P13	"Ipedori yau a?" King'is Akinyang.  "Ee," tama Echom.
P14	Dio akinyang kilulumaea lokwaas anam. Kipung'ar Echom.
P15	"Mere yong lopaekang. Iliokono yong!" Tolimu Akinyang.
P16	"Mere ng'on ng'ipaalon. isaki yong Akinyam ayong!"
