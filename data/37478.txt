page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there is a Young beautiful girl called Alisha, Alisha came from a family of five and she is the last born of the family. Alisha live with her family in a small town called Dakata. Alisha is very popular in their small town
P2	Ontill one-day she was going to school and Alisha was hit by a car, the neighbors rush down to meet Alisha where she was lying unconscious and immediately Alisha was rush to a nearby Hospital where her family came to visit her.
P3	When Alisha parents saw her they rush quickly to her and alisha mother screamed alisha alisha!  Oh my dear how did this happen?  Are you felling alright my dear?  Alisha mother ask. Yes mama it was just a minor incident.
P4	The Doctor just came in and he has given me some medications and he said I will be  fine Alisha respond back to her father. Thank God you will be, i was so worried about you Alisha said with a bit relief. Oh oh father you need not to worry.
P5	Just a few hours later the doctor came in and give them the good news that Alisha has been discharge from the hospital and she can go home. Alisha how are you feeling now the doctor ask?  I'm feeling much better doctor with a smile.
P6	Well am glad you are feeling much better the doctor said, so can I go home now alisha include yes u can go home now. The doctor reply with a beautiful smile on her face both Alisha and her family were so happy to hear the news.
