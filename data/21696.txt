desc:	Kana bhavha richigona kuzvara mwana

page_text:

P1	Bere naTsuro vaive
  neushamwari.
P2	Tsuro aive nedhongi nekabhavha
  katsvuku.
P3	Tsuro paaida bhavha
  hombe aitokumbira raBere rine ruvara
  rwedenga.
P4	Bere aida kuwacha
  mbatya mubhavha rake.
P5	Bere akaenda kuna
  Tsuro. “Ndiri kuda bhavha rangu,”
  akadaro.
P6	“Bhavha rakazvara,” akadaro Tsuro. Bere ndokutora
  mabhavha.
P7	Nerimwe zuva Tsuro akakumbirazve bhavha raBere.
P8	Bere paakaenda kunotora
  bhavha, nhau yaTsuro yaisafadza.
P9	“Bhavha rako rakafa,” akadaro Tsuro. Bere haana kuzvitenda.
P10	Bere akashevedza mhuka
  huru kuti dzione chakwadi.
P11	“Zvinhu zvipenyu zvinobereka zvinofawo,” Tsuro akatsanangura.
P12	Mhuka dzakabvumirana naTsuro.
  Bere akarasikirwa nebhavha rake.
