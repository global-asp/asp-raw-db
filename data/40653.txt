title:	Nne na thama yanga

artist:	Inez McClenton, Wiehan de Jager, Rob Owen, Vusi Malindi, Brian Wambi, Offei Tettey Eugene and Sunaina  Coelho

desc:	Mulalo and Mukundi are friends

page_text:

P1	Dzina langa ndi pfi Mulalo. ndi khou tshimbila na thama yanga Mukundi ri khou bva tshikoloni.
P2	Vhutama hanga na Mukundi vhuva kule. Ro aluwa rothe na nga minwaha ri a edana.
P3	Afha ndi musi rikhou uya u awela phakhani ro ambara na zwiambaro zwau fana ro fara na zwi munamune zwa u di homolosa.
P4	Phakha iyi a si unaka, vhonani mahohovho na muratho wa phuka hafhala. "namusi ridodi phina wee" uyo ndi Mukovhe a noralo. "Ndoni vhudza uri ndi havhudisa hafha fhethu", uyo ndi Mulalo ano fhindula.
P5	Musi vha khadivha vho dzula hanefho hombo di bvelela  Mbila. Mukundi na u sa ofha, ombodi thoma u tamba nayo atshi ita na umba nayo otakala vhukuma.
P6	Ro do vhona vho na dzinwe phukha dza daka sa Thudwa, ndou na zwinwe nzwinzhi.
P7	Duvha litshi vho fhisesa, Aiwa! nne na Mukundi rombodi thoma u bambela na vhanwe kha mulambo ure hanefho phakhani.
P8	Musi duvha litshi vho tota tombo nne na Mukundi rombodi humela hayani.
P9	Namusi ndi Musumbuluwo , hafha ndi kilasirumu yashu ri dzula tsini na tsini ra thetshelesa musi mudedekadzi washu vhatshi khou funza..
P10	Tshikolo tshi tshiva nne na khonani yanga ri tutuwedzana ngau ita mushumo wa tshikolo na u vhala. Nne na Mukundi ri toda uvha vhatshaeli vha vhu fhufhane ha muyani.
