desc:	Obwa Kyabazinga obwebisolo bwidwire bisolo bya ndhawulo.

page_text:

P1	Mu limu endhovu esinga mu bunene mu dhonadhoona.
P2	Mulimu Mwami Wakayiima omugezi mu dhonadhoona.
P3	Ni Wankembo atwalibwa nga omugayaavu, ataayendhanga kugya mu nimiro.
P4	Mwabaamu Mwami Ghakikere ayabanga mu bwina nga mwe yeekukumye.
P5	Ni Ghampologoma eyali Kyabazinga mu nsiko.
P6	Boonaboona baakolera ghalala ghaka ni mu nsiko.
P7	Baali bendagana oti kirevu na mpaata.
