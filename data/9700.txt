title:	Teacher Akinyi

desc:	A short and simple story about a wonderful teacher and what she teaches us.

page_text:

P1	This is my teacher. Her name is Teacher Akinyi. She loves us a lot.
P2	Teacher Akinyi teaches us the alphabet. She teaches us the vowels. I can say the letters a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Teacher Akinyi teaches us colours. I know red,  blue,  green,  yellow,  and black.
P4	During nap time,  Teacher Akinyi sings us to sleep. She sings a lullaby to us.
P5	The lullaby goes like this: Golden slumber give your eyes Smiles await you when you rise Sleep pretty darlings,  do not cry And I'll sing you a sweet lullaby.
P6	Teacher Akinyi knows many stories. She tells us a different story each day of the week.
P7	On Monday,  she tells us stories about great men.
P8	On Tuesday,  she tells us stories about great women.
P9	On Wednesday,  she tells us stories about means of transport.
P10	On Thursday,  she tells us stories about farming.
P11	And on Friday,  we tell our own stories to the class.
P12	Our teacher is the best teacher in the world! When I grow up,  I want to be like Teacher Akinyi.
