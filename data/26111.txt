desc:	Perina mughala muto ayenda inho ebimera.

page_text:

P1	Ono ni Palesa. Eriina lye litegeeza kimuli mu lulimi lwe olu Seswana.
P2	Munco inho, Palesa ayogera no muthungwa. "Muthungwa iwe, tuwe emithungwa kamaala."
P3	Palesa aja ku isomero. Mu lugendo lwe ku isomero ayogera ne isubi, "Isubi iwe, kula obe gha kilagala inho era ti mukala."
P4	Palesa abita ku bimuli. "Bimuli imwe, mwebale kulwa langi edhimesunkana.
P5	Ku isomero, Palesa ayogera no muti omuto. "Muti iwe, taku ensaga enene tusobole okutyama nga ku mu kiwolo.
P6	Palesa ayogera no  lukomera lwe isomero. "Kula obe mugumu inho era abantu ababi balobe okwingira muno."
P7	Palesa bwaira eka, ekyalira omuti gwo mu muthungwa. "Oliku ne mithungwa emyenvu?" Palesa yabuuza.
P8	"Emithungwa gikaali gya kiragala," Palesa ya kemuka. "Ndhaku bona olwe enco mu muthungwa iwe," yakoba. "Oba olyawo onaaba onfunireyo omuthungwa omwenvu!"
