desc:	A description of the life of a hardworking Nile fisherman. Ibiranga umurobyi wa Nil wakoraga cyane.

page_text:

P1	Dore Jaka umurobyi wa Naminya.
P2	Aroba ku ruzi rwa Nili.
P3	Afite ubwato, urushundura rwo kuroba n`indobani.
P4	Abyuka buri gihe mu museke kugira ngo afate umuhigo muri Nili.
P5	Nka saa yine, ajya kugurisha ibyo yafashe ku isoko rya Jinja.
P6	Jaaka, bigenze gute? Ni iki kibaye? Arababara.
P7	Uyu munsi, ibintu bimeze nabi. Umuyaga wa Nili wambujije kuroba.
P8	Ariko mfite ikizere ko ejo bizambere byiza kuko naguze indi ndobani nshya.
