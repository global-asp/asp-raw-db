P1	Ekimenke kyange ninze tulinga ku bunhonhi bwange waigulu mu bire. My Dinosaur and I look at my birds in the sky.
P2	Ebinhonhi byange ninze tuyagaana Wansude mu miti. My birds and I meet Chimpanzee in the tree.
P3	Wansude wange ninze tuzaanha ni Wantwiga wange mu nsozi. My Chimpanzee and I play with my giraffes in the hill.
P4	Wantwiga wange ninze tukola emipiira ni Wankembo okuva mu itoosi. My Giraffes and I make Monkey balls from clay.
P5	Wankembo wange ninze tulya ni Wairike buliidho. My Monkey and I eat banana with Gorilla every day.
P6	Wairike wange ni Wankembo batya Mwami Wankudu mu ngeri yonayoona. My Gorilla and Monkey fear Mr Tortoise in every way.
P7	Mwami Wankudu ninze twirayo eka kasobo ni Mukyala Namusota. Mr Tortoise and I go home very slowly with Miss Snake.
P8	Mukyala Namusota ninze twenda okusiisa n'okwemba enhembo ku mwaalo. Miss Snake and I love to hiss and sing songs by the lake.
P9	Amaka gange gonagoona ninze tuba ghalala mu isanhu eibitirivu!My entire family and I live together in great happiness!
