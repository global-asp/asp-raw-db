title:	Joe is late !
author:	Ntokozo Tshabalala
artist:	Catherine Groenewald and Abraham Muzee
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34670.png
desc:	Joe slept very late. He thought that he was going to school. Only to find out that Joe was dreaming of him being late to school.

page_text:

P1	Joe slept very late yesterday . He supposed to sleep at 8pm, but instead he slept at 11pm because he was watching his favourite movie.
P2	Joe supposed to wake up at 6am but instead he woke up at 7:30am. Joe said to himself "I am late , I have to bath , I have to eat my breakfast , I have to make my bed  Ohhh no ! I am very late."
P3	Joe bathed , he checked his books and he didn't make his bed because he was very late . It was 7:45am. Joe rushed to the kitchen to eat his breakfast.
P4	Joe found his father in the kitchen. His father said to Joe , "Joe you are late ". Joe ate his porridge very fast.
P5	Joe ate his porridge . He looked at the clock. It was now 8am. Joe rushed to school .
P6	Joe's little brother went to Joe's bedroom to wake him up.@Joe said "Haaaah, I'm late"@Joe's brother said "You are not late , it is very early". @Joe laughed to himself because he was dreaming of him being late.
P7	Joe told his father of what he dreamt. His father laughed but he told Joe not to sleep very late again because it is wrong.
P8	THE END.
