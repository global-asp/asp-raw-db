desc:	Ubani onamandla phakathi kwelanga nemvula?

page_text:

P1	Ngolunye usuku Ilanga neMvula zaphikisana. "Yimi onamandla," kusho Ilanga "Yimi onamandla, "Kusho Imvula.
P2	Ngosuku olulandelayo,  Ilanga lavuka ekuseni kakhulu. Abantu bageza zonke izingubo zabo. Izingubo zashesha ukoma. "Uyabona,  yimi onamandla," kusho Ilanga.
P3	  Ngosuku olulandelayo,  Imvula yavuka ekuseni kakhulu. Yayithela usuku lonke. Abantu bagcwalisa amabhakede namathangi.  "Uyabona,  yimina onamandla," kusho Imvula.
P4	NgoMasingana noNhlolanja,  Ilanga lavuka ekuseni kakhulu,  zonke izinsuku lalibalele. Zonke izitshalo zoma.  Abantu bomile. Izilwane zingenamanzi.
P5	Kwakushisa yonke indawo.  Abantu bafuna imifula ukubhukuda.
P6	NgoMbasa imvula yaqala ukuna.  Yana ekuseni. Yana ntambama. Yana kusihlwa nasebusuku.
P7	 Yonke into yayimanzi. Bese,  kwaqala ukubanda. Abantu bagqoka amajazi nezingubo. Kwakunodaka yonke indawo.
P8	Abalimi bajabula. Balima kakhulu. Babethembe ukuthi isilimo siyomila uma imvula seyimile.
P9	Emva kwesikhathi,  abantu basebekhathele yiMvula. Izilwane zafuna izindawo ezomile eziphephile.
P10	 Ekugcineni,  Ilanga laphuma kodwa lingashisi ngokwanele. Izulu labanda.
P11	Kwakungekho muntu othokozela lesimo sezulu.
P12	Abantu bazibuza ngeLanga neMvula.  Babefuna ukwazi ukuthi ngubani onamandla. Wena? Ucabangani? Ucabanga ukuthi ngubani onamandla? YiLanga noma yiMvula.
