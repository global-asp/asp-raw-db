title:	Serious Accident

lang:	English

desc:	Two children see a terrible accident between a truck and a car.

page_text:

P1	One day my sister and I were walking near the road.
P2	A big truck was driving down the hill.
P3	A car was speeding up the hill.
P4	The truck driver was talking on his mobile phone. He was not looking at the road.
P5	The truck smashed into the car!
P6	An ambulance and the police came to help.
P7	Two tow trucks came. One towed the truck. The other towed the car.
P8	We saw everything. We were shocked.
