P1	Nivekelo na Apwiya.
P2	Atiithihu muri wirimu,  nsina nanyu neelihiwe.
P3	Omwene anyu orwe.
P4	Okhwela wanyu weereye,  ntoko wirimu hiihaa tho vathi vaava.
P5	Munivahe olelo yoolya ahu ya olelo. Muhinihiye neereriwaka,  nto munivulushe wa oonanara owo.
P6	Munlevelele soocheka sahu.
P7	Ntoko hiyo tho nnaalevelelaahu ananchekela.
P8	Muhinihiye neereriwaka.
P9	Nto munivulushe wa oonanara owo.
P10	Ntakhara wa winyu ori omwene,  ikuru ni ncicimiho mahiku oothene. Aamee.
