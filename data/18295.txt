title:	Pendo, Wɔtsina Yoo Lɛ
author:	Divine Apedo (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Catherine Groenewald and Rob Owen
lang:	Ga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18295.png
desc:	Tsina yoo ko ni eweku sumɔɔ esane jogbaŋŋ he sane.

page_text:

P1	Moi nɛ.
P2	Moi sumɔ ni ekane woji.
P3	Pendo nɛ.
P4	Pendo sumɔ ni eye karɔti.
P5	Moi haa Pendo yakpeɔ jwɛi kɛji ekpa skul.
P6	Ehaa Pendo nu ni mli tse.
P7	Tsɛ ataa haa Pendo ŋmaa.
P8	Eŋaa Pendo fufɔ mli nu daa gbi.
P9	Pendo haa fufɔ mli nu pii.
P10	Tsɛ ataa hɔɔ Pendo fufɔ mli nu lɛ.
P11	Moi nuɔ fufɔ mli nu daa gbi.
P12	Ekɛɛ,  "Pendo Oyiwaladɔŋŋ!"
