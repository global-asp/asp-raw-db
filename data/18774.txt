page_text:

P1	Jukwo aryo gimito ni kwalu anywagi kud ipodhu pa wone idyewor acel. Gilar gingeyo ngo pigi gigi ikindgi.
P2	Jakwo mir acel ubino uturu anywagi gunia acel.
P3	Kan jakwo mucelo umondu ipodhu,  ma eweku kanamola pare idhu podhu.
P4	Kinde ma ecaku wothu ipodhu anywagi,  gi rombu ikindgi kan gicaku kok ikindgi,  "In inga?"
P5	Lworu umaku gi ikindgi man gipyei ku ngwec iyoo matung tung.
P6	Jakwo mir acel uweko anywagi gunia acel.
P7	Mir aryo uweko kanamola pare ingeye.
P8	Ma won podhu ubino etiyo ku kanamola eca ni dwoku anywagi ipacu.
