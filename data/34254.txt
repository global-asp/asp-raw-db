title:	Thekhisi ya Funi
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs

desc:	Funi na mme awe vho lindela thekhisi uri i vha dzhie i vha ise hayani. Funi u vhona vhathu na zwithu zwinzhi zwi takadzaho musi vho lindela. Fhedzi naa thekhisi yavho i ḓo swika?

page_text:

P1	Ri a vusa! Dzina ḽanga ndi Funi. @@Nṋe na mme anga ro lindela thekhisi. Ri khou ya hayani.
P2	Hafha ndo ima kha ḽaini. Mme anga vho nnyimbela luimbo. @@"Kha vha ḓe Vho Thekhisi kani a vha zwi vhoni? Nṋe na Funi, ro vha lindela!"
P3	Mufumakadzi a re tsini na nṋe u khou isa khuhu dzawe hayani. Dzi ḓo kudzela makumba uri vha ḽe.
P4	Havha vhanna vha khou bva mushumoni. Vha ṅwethuwa na nṋe. @@Ndi pfa vha tshi imba, "Funi muṱuku a songo vhilaela. Thekhisi i khou ḓa nga u ṱavhanya."
P5	Ha fhira muṅwe mufumakadzi. Ndi mufumakadzi o nakelelaho. Naa zwienda zwawe a zwo ngo naka? @@Musi a tshi tshimbila zwi ri "goo, goo, goo goo, goo, goo."
P6	Havha munna vha khou rengisa zwinwiwa zwi rotholelaho. Ndi funa zwinwiwa zwi rotholelaho. @@Zwi a rotholela nahone zwi na swigiri. Ni a zwi funa?
P7	Sedzani! Huna vhafumakadzi vho hwalaho zwiḽiwa zwa nga madekwana kha ṱhoho dzavho. Vho ṱalifha lungafhani?
P8	Havhaḽa mufumakadzi vha khou itwa mavhudzi. @@Vha khou ḓibvisa na khonani dzavho nga madekwana.
P9	Ndo neta zwino. Mme anga vha mmbeba muṱanani wavho uri ndi awele. @@Vha imba, "Vho Thekhisi ndi khou humbela uri vha songo lenga, Makhulu vho ri lindela getheni yashu."
P10	Khonani yanga Vhutshilo khoyu. Na ene o awela. Ri ḓo swika hayani hu si kale.
P11	Nṱha nṱha zwiṋoni zwi khou fhufhela hayani. Zwi fanela u swika hayani ḽi sa athu kovhela.
P12	Hoyu mufumakadzi u na zwiṅina zwo nakaho. Ndi humbula uri o zwi renga ṋamusi ḓoroboni. @@Musi ndi tshi aluwa, na nṋe ndi ḓo rengela mme anga  zwiṅina zwinzhi.
P13	Mme anga vha khou neta zwino. @@Ndi vha imbela luimbo, "Vho Thekhisi ri khou humbela uri vha ḓe, nṋe na mme anga ro vha lindela."
P14	Imani! Ndi khou ipfa! Kheyi i khou ḓa!
P15	Heyi ndi thekhisi yashu tswuku khulwane. Yo ḓa u dzhia nṋe na mme anga u ri isa hayani. Ndo takala vhukuma!
P16	Ṱa-ṱa!
