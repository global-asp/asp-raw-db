title:	U vhala phukha
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31843.png
desc:	Zwipuka zwoṱhe zwo farwa nga ḓora. Zwi vhaleni musi zwi tshi ya u nwa maḓi.

page_text:

P1	Nḓou nthihi i khou ya u nwa maḓi.
P2	Ṱhuḓa mbili dzi khou ya u nwa maḓi.
P3	Ṋari tharu na zwiṋoni zwiṋa zwi khou ya u nwa maḓi.
P4	Tsepe ṱhanu na nguluvheḓaka dza rathi dzi khou ya tivhani.
P5	Mbiḓi dza sumbe dzi khou gidimela tivhani.
P6	Zwiḓula zwa malo na khovhe dza ṱahe zwi khou tala maḓini.
P7	Ndau nthihi ya mbo vhomba. I khou ṱoḓou nwa maḓi. Ndi nnyi a no ofha ndau?
P8	Nḓou nthihi i khou nwa maḓi na ndau.
