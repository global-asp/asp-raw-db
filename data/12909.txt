page_text:

P1	Esoŋera hibuuka.
P2	Esoŋera emenya mu bifo ebicaafu.
P3	Eba mu migwanya, mu bibbomboyizi ko nohu biryo.
P4	Esoŋera egululuha yaŋwa mu bifo ebyo ebicaafu yaaja mu mago geefe.
P5	Niyoola yigwa hu biryo byeefe yabicaafuŋasa.
P6	Omuutu naalya ebiryo ebyo ebicafuŋaaye alumiziwa mu hida.
P7	Esoŋera yabulabe bugali weene.
