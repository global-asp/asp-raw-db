page_text:

P1	Ila hakaba haliho omushaija muto bali kumweta Kumbana.
P2	Obuyabile aine emyaka abilinetano, akagwa omu rukundo nomwishikyi wubakweta Zangane.
P3	Bakaguma hamwe kumala emyaka eshantu, konka akaba ataine esente zikumala.
P4	Ndelu eizooba limwe akashanga ayiine enda, ahakuba tibalakozise okwerinda.
P5	Omwishikyi mukye akazalwa, bamweta kheri.
P6	Konka Kumbana akaba nakyishoma, kandi Zangane akayiine omulimo omutauni haleho, bwenu baka biine kutana.
P7	Kuhahwileho obwile, Zangane yatunga omukundwa omusya. Yaija kutula nawe n'omwana we.
P8	Kumbana akahendaguluka omutima. Akalila yalila ahakuba bakamutanisa n'omwana we w'omwishikyi.
P9	Obwile kubwahwileho, Kumbana nawe yatuga omukundwa omusya, batula hamwe, konka tibalazile baana.
P10	Hati kheri akatakukunda ishe we omusya n'akakye. Akatashemelilwe, kandi akakyila kumutela.
P11	Bwenu kiyahikyize emyaka ikuminebili, nyina yashalamu kumwoheleza ngu atule nishe we weyini.
P12	Ndelu Kumbana na kheri bagaluka bashemelelwa ogundi omulundi.
