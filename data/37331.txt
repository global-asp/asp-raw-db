title:	Feelings

desc:	This is aimed towards creating sentences that will help express feelings in the language.

page_text:

P1	Thabang o itumetsi gompieno.@@O tlo ya gae mafelo a beke go bona bana.
P2	Thabo o swabile ka taba e botlhoko. @@O lebetse dijo tsa  mosegare gae.
P3	Ke gakantshile ka mmele wa me gompieno.@@Ke thoma go utlwa botlhoko mo mmeleng wa me.
P4	Rre o taboga ka potlako ga golo. @@O tshaba ditau tsa nageng.
P5	Thabang o maketse gore setlhare se tseba go bua Setswana.
P6	Ke kutlobotlhoko gore Rre o a lwala. @@O ne a nagana ka bana letsatsi ka moka.
P7	Morutisi o bogale thata ka seaparo sa bana sekolong. @@Ba tshwanetse go apara sentle.
P8	Barutwana ba maketse gore morutwana wa lekgowa o tseba go bua Setswana. @@O bua Setswana  sentle.
