page_text:

P1	Ngithanda imoto ebomvu (red).
P2	Ngithanda imoto eluhlaza kotshani ( green ).
P3	Umama uthanda imoto ephuzi ( yellow ).
P4	Ubaba uthanda imoto eluhlaza ( blue ) kwesibhakabhaka.
P5	Udadewathu uthanda imoto ensundu (black ).
P6	Umfowethu uthanda imoto emhlope (white ) njenjeqwa.
P7	Sonke sithanda imibala eqhakazayo,  efana nedoni yamanzi.
