page_text:

P1	Once apon a time a boy named Gift was a farmer.
P2	One day he was taking heating a tree he tried so hard.
P3	And  he broke the stick.
P4	Everyone asked whu did it .The king did not find his stick so he  thought that the stick that broke was his.
P5	The king  was so angry and said  who ever broke a stick should go into a fire .
P6	He ran when the  king's  guards  were  searching for the person who broke the stick a person helped him to run with his sheep.
P7	He spent the day's hunting and looking at cities.
P8	One day he was chasing a giraffe when a guard was about to see him.
P9	The king got a son and he was so happy.
P10	The king also got married.
P11	Gift started missing his city.
P12	One day Gift saw two boys and a monkey.
P13	He sent a  dog to bite  it.
P14	And he was brave to to to the king .
P15	He told the king that the stick was his and he broke  it and the king said sorry to everyone the end.
