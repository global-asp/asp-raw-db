page_text:

P1	Abazeire b'omwishiki muto bakamutuma owa ishenkuru owabeire natura enyuma y'ekibira kandi akegyerayo ashemereirwe.
P2	Kuyabire arihihi kwihika aha kibira akabugana embuzi etarikweferayo yamugambira ngu agyende nokwegyendesereza ahabwokuba hakaba hariho orukiko rwenyamishwa omukibira.
P3	Kuyahikire ahagati yekibira, akashanga ebinyamishwa byingi heihi n'omuhanda yeiruka munonga arikutera enduru.
P4	Kuyashohweire omukibira akabugana ebinyonyi byingi birikutera orwari. Omwishiki muto ogu akiruka arikutera orwari orukukiraho. Ishenkuru akamuhurira.
P5	Ishenkuru akagamba n'omukyala we ngu ogwo n'omwijukuru wabo. Akagyenda kumureeba arikurahuka n'embwa ye yamushanga aruhire.
P6	Kuyahikire omuka, akataahamu yaramusya abandi abaana reeru yaarya ebyokurya. Kuyaherize kurya yagamba ahabyabeireho omu muhanda.
P7	Kuyaherize kubagambira ebyabeireho bakamuhabura ngu atagaruka kuraba omukibira ateine ondeijo muntu akagyenda ashemereirwe kuzana nabandi abaana.
P8	Taragarukire kuraba omukibira ateine ondeijo muntu.
