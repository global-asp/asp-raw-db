title:	Mwamene Kanyelele Kanapulumusila Nkunda
author:	Bether Mwale Moyo
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	CiNyanja
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ntanka niyoyuma ndipo Kanyelele kanali na njota. Kanayenda kumusinje mukumwa manzi

page_text:

P1	Zuba inali inapya ndipo kunali koyuma musanga.
P2	Kanyelele sikanamwepo manzi masiku yapitapo.
P3	"Nifuna tumanzi tung'ono cabe olo kuti nitokugwa kumatepo," kanati Kanyelele.
P4	Koma kunayumila kumozi na mame.
P5	"Ngati sinizamwa manzi,  nizafa," kanalila Kanyelelele.    "Nifunikila niyende kumusinje wamene ninamvelapo."
P6	"Musinje niwozula ndipo uli na mpamvu,  uzakudonsa," anati Kamundi wanzelu.
P7	Koma Kanayelele kanali nanjota kwambili. "Nizafa ngati sinikumwa manzi." Ndipo Kanyelele kanayenda mukusakila manzi.
P8	Kanapita pa vimauzu na pavinkuni voyuma.
P9	Kanayenda mpaka kanamvela congo ca manzi. Unali ni musinje! Kanamvela mafunde ya manzi.
P10	Kanyelele kanamwa manzi yozizila.
P11	Kanakondwela kwambili ndipo sikanamvele mafunde pamene yanali kubwela.
P12	Kanyelele kanayesa kugwila mauzu yamene yanali kuyangalala pafupi pake. Koma manzi yanakakanka ndipo kanadonsewa na manzi.
P13	''Nitandizeni! Nitandizeni!" kanaitana."
P14	''Mwamusanga-musanga,  kwela apa," inati Nkunda yotuba,  yagwililila kamutengo mukamwa mwake."
P15	"Nizankala pano kuti nionge zikomo kwa Nkunda," Kanyelele kanaganiza.  "Nizayembekeza mpaka abwele kumwa manzi Nkunda." Koma Nkunda sanaonekele.
P16	Siku lina pamene Kanyelele kanali kuyembekeza Nkunda,  anyamata awili anabwela ku musinje. Anyamata awa anali na malegeni.
P17	''Kuli Nkunda yotuba yamene imabwela kuno mukumwa manzi," anati munnyamata umozi.    "Lelo usiku tizayidya.
P18	''Sinizalekelela anyamata awa kuti apaye Nkunda yotuba," kanaganiza Kanyelele."
P19	Pamenepo,  Nkunda yotuba inambululuka kubwela pansi kuti imwe manzi.
P20	Kanyelele kanaganizila vocita.
P21	Kanajumpila pa kwendo kwa munyamata umozi. Kanyelele kanaluma munyamata uja nampamvu zake zonse.
P22	Munyamata anajumpa. Ndipo anakuwa nati,  "Maiwe! Maiwe!"
P23	Nkunda yotuba ndiye mwamene ina tilimukila ndipo inambululuka. Ndiye mwamene inapulumukila.
P24	Ndiponso ndiye mwamene Kanyelele kana ongela Nkunda yotuba cifukwa co pulumusa umoyo wake.
