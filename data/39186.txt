page_text:

P1	Ka thanya lole tsebeng:@Onne tsala le motho o motshepa,ditla morago magareng ga gago leena enne bosula.@Basimane ba e neng ele ditsala tsa mma tota morago ba kgaogane ka bosula.
P2	Tsatsi lengwe kitso le Boikhutso baya madisong gaufi le motse setoropo wa (Vryburg) ga bane ba tshepisana goya go disetsa gaufi le toropo, gore batle ba kgone go bontshana se base kwadileng mo dibuka tsatsi tsa bona tsa ngwaga ee fitileng.
P3	Kitso yoo o neng a boleletse Boikhutso fa atla neela lokwalo lea tswanetseng kego le boisa pele gaaka buisa bukatsatsi yagagwe.@Boikhutso one a gana gonne bane ba dumalatsane gore batlile go buisa di buaka tsa bone ntle lego rerisana.
