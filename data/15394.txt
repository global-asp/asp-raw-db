title:	The Lazy Cheptoo
author:	Sigei Gilbert

desc:	The story is about a young girl called Cheptoo. Her mother beats her for laziness.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,  there live a very lazy girl called Cheptoo in Kimongo village. She was so lazy that she could not do anything.
P2	Whenever her mother sends her to the river to fetch water, she always walk slowly and slowly and return home after a long long time.
P3	One day her mother called her "Cheptoo where are you?" she emerges from the smoky filled house and reply "yes mum" her mother told her "make sure you swept the house and wash the utensils, am going to the Shamba."
P4	As soon as her mother left she began to sweep the house, she swept and swept that she took a very long long time doing so. Her mother arrive from the shamba and began calling her "Cheptoo?" she replied in a low tone "ii.i. Yoo! " after a long hesitation, tears started rolling down her eyes because she had not washed the utensils as she was told to do so.
P5	Her mother get so hungry and she began to beat Cheptoo. She began crying " wuuii! Wuuii! Wuuii,  forgi. Iive m. Ee" she cried and cried all day long while blinking like an owl, she was punished to do a lot of work that day.
P6	From that day Cheptoo did not become lazy again,  she automatically change after being beaten thoroughly by her mother.
