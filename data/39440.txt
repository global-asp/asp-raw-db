title:	Acol ku man dɛt yam
author:	Philip Lual Bol
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Dinka
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Tawen ci man Acol thou

page_text:

P1	Acol aci man thou. @@Acol kek wun aci ŋiec cieŋ kamken.
P2	Na ɣɔn akal tok, ke wun Acol bɔ baai kek tik. @@Riɛnke acɔl Adut.
P3	Go wun Acol lueel, "Adut ee moor yemen."
P4	Acol a kec puou miɛt. @@Aci tɔc matania man ci gɔt kɔu.
P5	Go wun Acol jal bi la luɔi yic. @@Adut kua Acol aci jal reer ka ke ci puoth mit.
P6	Akol dɛt ka Adut yup Acol. @@Tadɛt ka ci Acol muɔc ciɛm.
P7	Adut atheel Acol wei akɔtic. @@Matania man aci yic rɛɛt.
P8	Acol aci matania loom ku jɔl abaŋ ciɛm. @@Aci kat.
P9	Acol aci la reer e tim nhom. @@Aci wɛɛk lɛɛŋ kɛt man.
P10	Go tiɛŋ tok waak jal piŋ apath.
P11	Aci tik guɔ ŋic lɔn ye yen Acol nyiɛn mɛnh.
P12	Acol aci wɛc nyiai kek yen. @@Aci ciam ku niin apɛth.
P13	Wun Acol aci lɔk bɛn baai. @@Acol aliu.
P14	Acol aci wun yok. @@Ku lueel, "Rɛɛr ka wek wac ta cit ta ye wiec yeen."
P15	Adut kua Acol aci rot jal bɛn nhiar kedhie.
P16	Adut aci thal tɛn kek. @@Aci Acol jal ŋic lɔn ci yen man dɛt yam bɛn yok.
