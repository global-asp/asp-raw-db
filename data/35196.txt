page_text:

P1	Mumuganga medu munogara muisa unozwi Ngungunyana. @@Ngungunyana une chimangowe chinozwi Matsapindi.
P2	Ngungunyana unosiya musuwo wakavhurika paanoenda kumushando.
P3	Matsapindi unogarira pamusuwo eirinda mphatso.
P4	Matsapindi unothyithyidzira imbwa yepachigarisano chake kana ikaguma padhuze.
P5	Imbwa iya inotiza yeihwirira sure kwayabva.
P6	Chigarisano chikada kuguma pamusuwo, Matsapindi unobudisa mazino nendwara eitoda kuvhanga nekuruma.
P7	Chigarisano chinopetuka cheirumba.
P8	Kana oozwa nzara, Matsapindi unombonya musuwo.
P9	Matsapindi unorhya chikhafu chake.
P10	Apedza unothomukira pachihlayo otofodora hope eiridza n'ono.
P11	Panopetuka Ngungunyana kanyi, Matsapindi unodamura omuashira eidakara.
P12	Ngungunyana naMatsapindi vanogara pashi veifunda phepha renthau.
