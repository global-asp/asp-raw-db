page_text:

P1	Wampube ali kunoonia nhumba mpyaka.
P2	Eno nhumba eboneka bulungi.
P3	“Osobola okwidha okutenduka ni nze,” Akabwa bwe kaakoba. 
“Webale,” Wampube yairamu.
P4	Obwire obwo, ebilooto ebya Wapube byali bibukabuka era nga bilimu ne ebinhaga
P5	“Osobola okwidha watenduka ni nze,” Mukusu bwe yakoba. 
“webale,” Wampube   yairamu.
P6	Obwire obwo, ebilooto ebya Wapube byali bileenkaana nga tibiwulikana.
P7	“Osobola okwida watenduka ni nze,” Kyenhanda yaakoba. 
“Weebale,” Wampube yaakoba.
P8	Obwire obwo, ebilooto ebya Wampube byali biinhogovu era bitobu.
P9	Wampube yali yeetaaga ekifo ekilimu eibugumu era nga kikalu.
P10	Your translated text
P11	Wampube yaayagaana kabada eye ebitabo okumpi agho.
P12	Obwire obwo, Wampube yali ne ebilooto ebibugumu nga bilungi. 
Sula bulungi Wampube.
