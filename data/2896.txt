title:	Lazy Little Brother

desc:	Big brother gets very grumpy with his little brother who spends his whole time playing while the elder boy does all the household chores.

page_text:

P1	I wake up and make fire.
P2	I boil some water.
P3	I chop the firewood.
P4	I stir the pot.
P5	I sweep the floor.
P6	I wash the dishes.
P7	Why do I work so hard?
P8	When my brother is busy playing!
