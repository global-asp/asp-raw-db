title:	Nyuka
author:	Owino Ogot and Athieno Gertrude
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Dhopadhola
level:	First words

desc:	Mama ongoye aka Baba ama guro nyuka ma odikini.

page_text:

P1	Opiyo yikere makidho ka fonjirok.
P2	Baba guro nyuka m'odikini.
P3	Opiyo owaco ni,  "Mama tedo nyuka ma ber maloyo!"
P4	Nyuka miyo Opiyo meni ka fonjirok.
P5	Nyuka ocek.
P6	Baba konjo cak i nyuka gi mar.
P7	Opiyo owaco ni,  "Baba,  aparo ni nyuka me mito medo iye sukari."
P8	Opiyo paro ni,  "Nyuka ma mama tedo miti loyo ma baba tedo!"
P9	Opiyo okwayo ni,  "Baba,  i nyalo medan sukari?"
P10	Ba Opiyo medo go sukari.
P11	"Wu! Baba! I ketho ran cumbi ayin!"
P12	Opiyo openjo ni,  "Baba,  mama le dwoko karaŋo?"
