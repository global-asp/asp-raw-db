page_text:

P1	Mpša ya nageng e nyaka dijo. E swere ke tlala.@@The wild dog is looking for food. It is hungry.
P2	Kubu e ja dijo tša mesong.@@The hippo is eating breakfast.
P3	Leobu le Mmutla ba sega mmogo.@@The lizard and Rabbit are laughing together.
P4	Noga le Mmutla ba swere di taba.@@The snake and lizard are having a conversation.
P5	Tau ke Kgosi ya nageng.@@The Lion is the king of the jungle.
