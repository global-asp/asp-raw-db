title:	Inzu Y'imbeba
author:	Niyifasha Epimaque
artist:	Amy Uzzell and Jennifer Jacobs
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20854.png
desc:	Imbeba yagerageje ahantu henshi kugeza ubwo ibonye aho kuryama hayinyuze.

page_text:

P1	Imbeba yarimo gushaka inzu nshya.
P2	Iyi irasa n'inzu nziza.
P3	Ikibwana giti "Waje ukararana nange!" Imbeba iti "Urakoze!"
P4	Iryo joro, imbeba irota isimbagurika inafatwa mu byondo.
P5	Gasuku iti"waje tukararana!" Imbeba iti "urakoze"
P6	Iryo joro imbeba igira inzozi mbi.
P7	Ifi iti "waje ukararana nange!" Imbeba iti "urakoze!"
P8	Iryo joro, inzozi z' imbeba zari zikonje zinatose
P9	Imbeba yashatse ahantu hashyushye kandi humutse.
P10	Imbeba ibona akabati k'ibitabo hafi aho.
P11	Iryo joro imbeba igira inzozi zishyushye kandi zishimishije. Ijoro ryiza Mbeba.
