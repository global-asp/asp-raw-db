P1	Fati yarinya ce karama wanda ke gudu kamar@  iska.@  @@  Tana son yin dariya kuma tana qoqari@  Kaman yadda aka umarce ta.
P2	Yau ranar wassanin makaranta ne.@  @@  Fati na wasan kwallon qafa@  kuma tayi wasannin gudu kashi uku.
P3	Bayan makaranta, ta tafi gida tare da baban qawan@  ta Hawa.@  @@  Hawa ta ce, “Fati, ki na gudu da sauri!”
P4	Fati ta iso gida ta tarar da mamanta tana@  shirye-shiryen abinci.@  @@  Ta ce, “Mama, ina ini.”@  @@  Maman Fati ta amsa, “Ina ini. Yaya kike?”
P5	Fati ta ce, “Yau mun yi wasannin makaranta. Na@  yi gudu sosoi.@  @@  Ina jin zafi, na gaji kuma ina jin yunwa.”
P6	Mama ta yi dariya, “Ta ce yar lele Fati na, an@  haifeki da saurin qafa!”@  @@  “zan yi miyan kuvewa@  kuma abinci zai nuna bada daxewa ba.”
P7	Mama ta sa dan man ja a cikin baban tukunya ta@  aza bisa wuta.@  @@  Lokacin da manjan ta yi zafi, sai ta yanka@  albasa da tafarnuwa ta kuma juya kayan miyan har ya yi laushi.@  @@  Sai ta sa barkono, tumatiri da dan ruwa. Sai@  kuma ta kara qwayan naman SA guda@  shida.
P8	Mama ta ce, “Fati, ki kula ki dinga juya@  miyan.”@  Fati ta ce, “ Toh, mama.”
P9	Fati ta juya miyan sau xaya.@  @@  Sai tace, “Miyan na kamshin daxi.”
P10	Fati ta juya miyan sau biyu.@  @@  Sai tace, “Wannan miyan da xanxanon@  daxi.”
P11	Fati ta juya miyan sau uku.@  @@  Sai tace, “Wannan miyan gaskiya da daxi!”
P12	Fati ta kalli maman ta.@  @@  Mamanta ba ta kallon ta. Tana yankan alaiho da@  za ta sa a miya.
P13	Fati ta dauki kwayan nama xaya@  daga tukunyan miyan ta ci.@  @@  “Mmm,” ta ce.
P14	Sai Fati kuma ta dubi mamanta.@  @@  Mamanta ba ta kallon ta. Tana yanka kwai da za@  ta haxa cikin miyan.
P15	Fati da wuri ta dauki nama na biyu daga cikin@  tukunyan miyan ta ci.@  @@  “Mmm. Mmm,” ta ce.
P16	Fati ta kuma duban mamanta.@  @@  Mamanta ba ta kallonta. Tana yankan kuvewa@  da za ta haxa a miyan.
P17	Fati da wurwuri ta xauki@  nama na uku daga cikin tukunyan miya ta ci.@  @@  “Mmm. Mmm. Mmm!” Fati ta ce.
P18	Mama ta zo bakin murhu da koren ganye, da kwai@  da kuvewa.@  @@  Fati na nan rike da baban chokalin katako.
P19	Mama ta yi murmushi, “Na gode da juya wannan@  miyan Fati.”@  @@  Fati ba ta san abin da za ta ce ba.
P20	Mama ta sa koren ganye, kwai da kuvewa@  a cikin miya.@  @@  Ta karbi chokali daga hannun fati ta fara juya@  miyan.
P21	Mama ta kalli cikin tukunyan miyan. Tana ganin@  miyan na tafasa a kan wuta.@  @@  Mama tana neman kayan nama shida da ke cikin@  tukunyan miyan.@  “Xaya, biyu, uku.” Mama@  ta ga kwayan nama uku kawai a cikin tukunyan miyan.@  @@  Mama ba ta ce komai ba. Fati ba ta ce komai@  ba.
P22	Da mama ta raba abinci, Fati bata samu nama@  ba.@  @@  Fati ta ce, “Mama, yi haquri.@  Ba zan qara yi ba.”@  @@  Ba ta qara yi ba. Wannan shi@  ne qarshe.
