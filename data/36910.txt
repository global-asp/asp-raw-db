desc:	The story is about a good soccer player called Thando who was bullied and he lost his confidence

page_text:

P1	There was a boy named Thando who had passion and was a good soccer player.He was chosen as the best player when he won on a small competition.Thando lives in the township of Alexandra and he is known of his kindness and good behaviour.
P2	Not everyone likes Thando there are a group of boys led by Allan who bully Thando.They always lower Thando's confidence and try to make Thando lose because he is a good player.
P3	Lindi is Thando's team's coach and she does her best that they train and win but it's not always about winning but having fun.The team meets up and the coach tells the children about a tournament happening in two days.
P4	The next day Thando practices with his friends and they get ready for the competition after practicing Thando sees the bullies and they tell Thando that they are going to lose the match because they are also in the tournament.
P5	Today is the day of the tournament Thando is very ready to play.The team meets up and at the locker room Allan mocks Thando until he has no confidence anymore.Thando fails to play and win because he now has low self confidence.
P6	After the break Lindi talks to Thando and she tells him that bullies seek attention and the best thing to do is ignore so they think they are talking to themselves.Thando feels more confident and he plays really well and his team wins.
P7	The bullies feel bad about what they did and they come and apologize and Thando forgives them and tells them not to bully people because they hurt people's feelings and low their self confidence.
