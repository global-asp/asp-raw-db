page_text:

P1	Lona nguBongi. Unguthishela wami.
P2	Bongi usebentisa tindvuku nakahamba.
P3	Lona nguMancoba. Ungumsakati lengimtsandza kunabo bonkhe.
P4	Mancoba unenkinga yemehlo.
P5	Lona nguBandile, makhelwane wami. Bandile ngumlimi.
P6	Bandile akeva etindlebeni. Ukhuluma ngekusebentisa lulwimi lwetimphawu.
P7	Lona nguBhiza. Ungumdlali webhola lodvumile esikolweni setfu.
P8	Bhiza akanato tandla. Kodvwa sewufake emagoli lamanyenti ecenjini letfu.
