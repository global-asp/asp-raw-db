title:	Zama Ngbagbe!
author:	Isaac Monday Elisama
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Zande
level:	First sentences

desc:	This story suggests that as much as we discipline children

page_text:

P1	Tooni tamere na ra gbe kusa. Mina guari wiso gbe,  mbiko minga wene boro gbe.
P2	Midu na sungo rogo uru.
P3	"Mo nga gimi wene wiso kerekuru," mina peke.
P4	Mina zunda tire,  mia ida nga undu te.
P5	Mire ngbe na mbira wene zezere ime na wene fuo sabunu.
P6	Mina tingidaro,  "Ka bangiro gonga ti rindi roya." Mina karaga pai,  "Wasate,  mite!"
P7	Fuo zundatire,  mi dakuti pai ka iriso atiare na deriba,  na kini fu wene uru fuyo.
P8	Mina voda tire,  "Mina kio awere," hee mina pehe.
P9	Mine ngbe na kiso ngbadure kini mbakadi kimi rakata.
P10	Midu wakami inihe ya tooni tamere ini voro apai du tipa madarasa.
P11	Rogo gimi fasiri midu wa kami mangi wene ngai.
P12	Mina manga agi wene apaire na uru du ono gu hee mia kpinyemu he gbe,  nga kangi mbaro kini ngi mbaro!
