title:	(Elangi omu) wakame eyobukoryo
author:	Uganda Christian University and nahwera jonah
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Runyankore

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Elangi-omu katabo kembozi. Wakame erye nezana obukoryo.

page_text:

P1	EIzooba rimwe, wakame ekaba ari aheru nelonda ebwokurya. yabugana enjojo,eyabile eyine anyungu yobwokyi. wakame ekabuza enjojo "bambi ba owe embabazi ompeke. tinyimi mani
P2	enjojo ekikyiliza okuheka wakame. wakame ekaza aha mugongo gwa enjojo deelu wagikwata wagihanya. wakama watandika okurya obwokti bwe enjojo ahonaho.
P3	obwokyi bukatandika kutonyera enjojo omugongo.enjojo kuwabuliza wakame ekamugila ngu ekyilondakye nikyokyitutonya.
P4	Bakika aha mujela bashanga embogo mpango elikunywa amizi.
P5	wakame ekabamenda kumanya owabile aine amani, embogo ninga enjojo.
P6	bakatandika kuhakana. enjojo ekagila gu eina amani nana embogo wagila ngu eina amani.
P7	wakame ekagila ngu enyine amagezi kumanya aine amani. akababuza kumulinda aha mujela.
P8	wakame ekagaluka nomuguha mulingwa. ekakona ahamuhelu omunda ye enjojo delu wagitwala hale nomujela.
P9	wakame ekajezaho aha muheru womuguha aha muguha omunda we enjojo delu yamuleka hihi ne omujela.
P10	wakame ekiruka yayesheleka omumuti. yayomba, "yeteleze! emwe, ebiri, eshatu! nyula"
P11	Embogo ekanyula enjojo. enjojo wanyula embogo.
P12	Embogo ekanyulila enjojo. kuleba eyabile ekusinga.
P13	ohuyabile eyeshelekyile, wakame ekagumizamu elikurya obwokyi bwe enjojo.
P14	kuyamazile obwokyi, ya shala omuguha hihi ne embogo.
P15	enjojo ekagwa ahansi munonga kandi yatela omuti.
P16	wakame ekajenda owa embogo yajilamya, harukugila hati oyine amani.
