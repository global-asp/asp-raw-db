P1	Zwo vha zwi  zwa masana o tavhaho a ha mululumaa.Ovha tanzhe  a Wana vhutali vhu bvaho  mudzimuni . Ho ngo dii hima a ri uakona , fhedzi zwo dura tshilidzi na mbavhalelo zwa ?was tadulu . Nyimele  na ndado o anganana, sa mulilo wo fungiwaho
P2	Hi ngo tends u vha wamurahunaho vha tshi to tanzhe all ngo thanya fhedzivhutali ho kalulaho ones.Ovha mututuwedzi , muneina mulambedzi was vha shayho .Kha zwothe fhulufhelo lo vha home , zwanga zwa Nehemiah a tshi visa mitsheto ya Jerusalem
