title:	Iḽani mivhala yoṱhe!
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Pixabay  Website
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences

desc:	Mitshelo na miroho zwo lugela mutakalo washu

page_text:

P1	Naa ni a zwi ḓivha uri musi ni tshi ḽa mitshelo ni vha ni tshi khou ḽa mivhala?
P2	Apuḽa ḽi na muvhala ufhio?@@I ḽani mifuda yoṱhe ya maapuḽa.@@Iḽani maapuḽa madala, matswuku, a ṱaḓa, na a pinki!
P3	Khokhonathi i na muvhala ufhio?@@I ḽani mifuda yoṱhe ya khokhonathi.@@Iḽani khokhonathi i songo omiswaho na yo omiswaho.
P4	Ndi ifhio mitshelo i re na muvhala wa swiri?@@Ndi lini hune swiri ḽa vha na muvhala mudala?
P5	Habu ḽi na muvhala wa pinki.@@Ndi funesa mutshelo wa muvhala wa pinki!@@Musi ni tshi ḽa mitshelo, ni vha ni tshi khou ḽa mivhala.
P6	Gadaphele ndi mutshelo u re na thambo nthihi khulwane.@@Musi ni tshi ḽa gadaphele, ni vha ni tshi khou ḽa muvhala ufhio?
P7	Phepha idzi dzi na muvhala ufhio?@@Mufuda muiṅwe na muṅwe wa phepha ndi mutshelo.@@Mitshelo yo fhambana na miroho.
P8	Mitshelo i aluwa kha maluvha a re kha tshimela. @@Mitshelo i na dzimbeu.@@Ndi ifhio mitshelo ine na funa u i ḽa?
