desc:	I am going back to school and have to try on all my school clothes to see if they fit.

page_text:

P1	Bongo di ezuru tu.
P2	Mavile bongo angu ambiza ni 'di ambu ru.
P3	Mavile jurua buku 'bazu 'diri amburu.
P4	Ma bongo ba 'di caku.
P5	Sapau 'diri gaa ru.
P6	Bongo su pa 'diyi alia ru.
P7	Te jijima 'diyi o'diru.
P8	Caki ma ni kilili.
