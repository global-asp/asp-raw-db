P1	The Jungle has different animals.@The Elephant and the Lion are great friends.@They have gone to take water together from the swamp.
P2	The Jungle has Zebras.@Zebras like walking in groups.@They are done with grazing and now they are headed to the riverbank to drink water.@They drink water every evening.
P3	Lion is the King of the Jungle.@He has called all the other animals for a meeting.@They are discussing matters of the Jungle.
P4	Elephant is the biggest  Animal in the Jungle.@All the other animals fear him except his friend Lion.@He likes grazing alone.
P5	All the animals stay in the Jungle.@They live in unity@They help each other in times of problems.
