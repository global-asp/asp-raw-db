title:	Alokakinet Akiru

desc:	Ngimunyenin lukan-kaarei lu alokakinet.

page_text:

P1	Erai ng'imunyenin lu alokakinet ng'ikan-kaarei.
P2	1.Loareng'an
P3	Enapit ng'esi eworu loareng'an.
P4	2.lokipurat.
P5	Inyo erai ekupurat kane?
P6	3.Lonyang.
P7	Inyo enyang'aana kane?
P8	4.Loaliban
P9	Inyo elibit kane?
P10	5.Lopus
P11	Inyo epuseana kane?
P12	6.Lopus-kirion.
P13	Inyo erai ipus-kirion kane?
P14	7.Lopus-kipurat.
P15	Ejok emunyen lopus-kipurat! ali munyen imina yong?
P16	Alu ng'imunyenin lukan-kaarei alokakinet.
