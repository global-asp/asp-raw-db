desc:	Ebintu omuwala omuto by’anyumirwa okukola.

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping rope. Nyumirwa okubuuka omuguwa.
P2	… alone. Nebwembera nzekka.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too. Nyumirwa okugubuuka omuguwa ngandi ne mikwano gyange.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Nyumirwa okusoma obugero obuunyuma.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Nyumirwa okutambula mu kisaawe ne mikwano gyange.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother. Nyumirwa okworezako Maama wange ebintu.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Nyumirwa okuwandiika ennukuta ennene z'ensobola okuzisoma obulungi.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit. Nyumirwa okulya ebibala ebyenjawulo.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Nyumirwa okweebaka kubuliri obunene.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories. Nyumirwa okuwuliriza Maama nga agera engero ezinyuma.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Eeeee! Nyumirwa nnyo okukola ebintu bino byonna.
