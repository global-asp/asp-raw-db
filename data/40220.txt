desc:	This story is about Thandeka

page_text:

P1	Thandeka wakes up early in the morning every day. She takes a bath, brushes her teeth, gets ready, and rushes down to the kitchen to eat with her family. She sits down for breakfast to eat her favourite fruits which help her to be strong.
P2	Thandeka likes to write . She can spell difficult words and write them down. Even though she is six years old, she is very smart and intelligent.  She likes to write at school on the chalkboard.  She also likes to help her friends to read.
P3	During break time at school. Thandeka plays with her friend. She likes to play the skipping rope. She says playing games after reading is fun. She becomes very energetic when jumping the skipping rope. After playing she then starts to read.
P4	Thandeka likes to read because she becomes smarter.  When she grows up she wants to be a teacher and also a writer. She wants to teach every child how to read and write. So that they can become more smarter and  intelligent,  just like her.
