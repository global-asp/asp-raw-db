desc:	Maicibi the brave girl kills Crocodile.
The whole village is now free from the killer Crocodile. This story was created at IDGC in Bauchi 2020 celebration.

page_text:

P1	In the village of Farin-shinge, there lived a girl called Mai cibi.
P2	Maicibi lives with her parents.
P3	She went to the river to catch fish.
P4	She met the wicked crocodile who kills women.
P5	The Crocodile asked Mai cibi, "what you want in the river?
P6	Maicibi answered, "I came to catch fish.
P7	Maicibi killed the Crocodile, the story was everywhere in the village.
P8	The king gave her scholarship. Women went to the river happily.
