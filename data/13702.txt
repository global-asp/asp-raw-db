desc:	Tom omutundi wamalafu.

page_text:

P1	Tom agega ahasuniya h'amalafu.
P2	Tom atiina mu hatale ohutunda amalafu.
P3	Abaatu mu hatale bali hugula ebibala.
P4	Naye eŋuma ali hugula amalafu ga Tom. Benda hugula bibala hu bahaasi.
P5	''Mu hitehere hyefe,  abahasi nja batunda ebibala. Musinde hi ono?" bawuusa."
P6	Aye Tomu sigesonia. Alangirira,  "Mugule amalafu gange! Mugule amalafu gange!"
P7	Omuhasi nabugula amalafu hu sununiya,  getegeresa bugali bwene.
P8	Omuhasi agula amalafu.
P9	Abaatu abandi balya. Bagula amalafu ga Tom balya.
P10	Amalafu ni gaŋwa. Tom nga abala epiiya ejasunire.
P11	Tom gagula sabbuni,  sukaali n'omugati gabiita hu suniya.
P12	Tom gagega esuniya hu mutwe gatiina engo.
