page_text:

P1	This is Selsy.She likes talking about her style and how rich they are.She is popular because of her parents wealth.She is not as polite as other girls.
P2	This is Kelsy,Selsy's twin sister.She is popular because of her polite altitude.
P3	This is precious.She is poor but popular because of her beauty, everyone respects her because she respects herself.
