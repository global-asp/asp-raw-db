title:	Delu Qwararriyar 'yar  wasar Qwallon qafa
author:	Habibu Ahmed Jibrin
artist:	Eden Daniels
lang:	Hausa (Nigeria)
level:	First sentences

desc:	Haqiqa

page_text:

P1	Delu ta kalli yara maza suna
  wasar qwallon qafa.Ta yi shaawar
  shiga tayi wasar da su.
  Ta tambayi Maihorar da 'yan wasar,
  "Ko zata  iya
  shiga ayi wasar da ita?"
P2	Maihorar da 'yan wasar ya riqe
  qugunsa da hannuwansa. "A wannan makarantar, Maza ne kawai aka yarda suyi wasan qwallon
  qafa." Ya ce.
P3	'Yan mazan suka ce mata,
  "ta je tayi wasan qwallon raga."  Suka ce, "Qwallon raga na mata ne, qwallon
  qafa kuwa na maza ne."  Wannan ya vata wa
  Delu rai.
P4	Washe gari, makarantar
  tana da babban wasa. Maihorar da 'yan wasan ya
  damu matuqa saboda haziqin xanwasan sa bashi
  da lafiya, bazai sami dammar yin wasa ba.
P5	Delu ta roqi Maihorar da 'yan wasa cewa,
  "Don Allah ka bani dama
  in buga wasan." Maihorarwar, ya rasa abin da zaiyi.
  Sai ya yanke shawarar sa Delu cikin
  'yan wasan.
P6	Wasa  ya yi zafi. Duk qungiyoyin basu sa qwallo
  a raga ba har zuwa hutun rabin
  lokaci.
P7	Bayan dawowa daga
  hutun rabin lokaci, Delu ta amshi qwallo.
  Sai ta ja qwallon zuwa
  ragar abokan karawar su. Sai
  Delu ta daki qwllon da
  karfi sai ta shiga raga.
P8	Taron 'yan kallo sunyi ihu don murna. Tun daga
  wannan lokaci, aka baiwa 'Yan mata damar yin wasan qwallon qafa a makarantar.
