title:	Doggu mboju, doggu!
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Dukuure yanii diga dow lekki

page_text:

P1	Dukuure yanii diga dow lekki, mboju e ɗaanino ley lekki kin.
P2	Mboju sey nani bolle, "Doggu mboju, doggu!"
P3	Mboju wi'ika gerogal, "Goɗɗum yanii nden mi nani, 'Doggu mboju, doggu!'"
P4	Nde gerogal nani ko mboju wi'i, ngal fuɗɗi doggugo.
P5	Ɗi mbi'ika ɓoosaaru, "Mboju nani goɗɗum yani, sey ɗum doggi."
P6	Ɓoosaaru maa fuɗɗi doggugo bee mboju e gerogal.
P7	Ɓoosaaru wi'ika puccu, "Mboju nani goɗɗum yani, sey ɗum doggi."
P8	Puccu sey fuɗɗi doggugo bee maaji.
P9	Wamnde ƴami, "Ɗume doggoton?" Puccu jaabi, "Mi annda."
P10	Wamnde maa sey fuɗɗi doggugo bee maaji.
P11	Wamnde wi'ika nagge, "Mboju nani goɗɗum yani, sey ɗum doggi."
P12	Nagge hultori, sey nge fuɗɗi doggugo.
P13	Nagge wi'ika muusuuru, "Mboju nani goɗɗum yani, sey ɗum doggi."
P14	Muusuuru sey fuɗɗi doggugo bee dabbaaji luttuɗi ɗin.
P15	Sey dabbaaji ɗin potti bee ɓiɗɗo gorko.@@Mo ƴami, "On anndaa ko waɗi na?"
P16	"Ɗum min wi'uno, 'Doggu mboju, doggu!'" Sey mo waɗi ka jalugo.
