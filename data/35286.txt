page_text:

P1	Ingaba kuzobanjani esikolweni namhlanje?@@Andikwazi nokulinda ukubona abahlobo bam!@@Sizokwenza njani ngezifundo ezisiphosileyo?
P2	Ukuqala kokuvaleleka endlini ndandisoyika.@@Umama wayengakwazi nokuya emsebenzini.@@Kwakungekho nemali eyoneleyo yokuthenga ukutya.
P3	Umama notata babexabana rhoqo!@@Kwaye nathi sobabini sasisilwa rhoqo.
P4	Umnumzana Khumalo wayegula kakhulu. Ndandisoyika ukuba utata uza kugula naye.@@Sabathandazela, uThixo wabanceda. Waze uThixo wasibonelela ngokutya!
P5	Yesu Nkosi,@Nceda usapho lwethu.@@Yiba nezihlobo zethu uzincede nazo.@@Enkosi ngokusithanda nokuva imithandazo yethu.@Amen.
P6	"UThixo ulihlathi, uligwiba kuthi; Ezimbandezelweni ufumaneke eluncedo kunene."@@IiNdumiso 46:1@@(Bible in isiXhosa 1859/1975 © BSSA 1975)
