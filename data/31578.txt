title:	The fat donkey

desc:	This story tells a tale of a donkey that fell into a deep hole and couldnt get out until it had an idea.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time the was a fat donkey who loved walking and eating.
P2	As he was walking along dancing and singing he fell into a big hole. He tried to get out but could not.
P3	He then cried and shouted for help and then the owner heard him and came running to help the donkey.
P4	He tried to take the donkey out but could not as the donkey was fat and could not get out. Then he decided to bury the donkey inside the hole.
P5	The poor donkey thought he was going to die but then an idea came to his mind. He thought that as his owner added dirt into the hole he will climb out.
P6	As the owner added the dirt the donkey started climbing out using the dirt until it come out and was free from the hole.
P7	The donkey was happy to come out of the hole and walked with the owner home.
P8	The End
