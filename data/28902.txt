P1	Maliya mugayaavu.
P2	Ava mu maka magaiga.
P3	Tasoobola kufumba mmere.
P4	Aja kikereze ki isomero.
P5	Azira mikwano era tafa ku bandi.
P6	Bbaabba we amwenda inho.
P7	Atendhuka mu kibina.
P8	Tagha basomesa be kitibwa.
P9	Atambula mpola okuva mu kibina kuba yali mugayaavu.
