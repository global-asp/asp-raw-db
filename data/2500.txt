title:	Abantwana Bekhandlela
author:	Ntombizodwa Gxowa-Dlayedwa
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiXhosa

desc:	Kwakukho ikhaya elalinabantwana abaziphethe kakuhle kakhulu. Ingxaki yayikukuba babenziwe ngekhandlela

page_text:

P1	Kudala,  kwakuhlala usapho olonwabileyo.
P2	Abazange balwe omnye nomnye. Babebanceda abazali babo ekhaya nasemasimini.
P3	Kodwa babengavume-lekanga ukuba basondele emlilweni.
P4	Kwakufuneka benze wonke umsebenzi wabo ebusuku.
P5	Kuba babenziwe ngekhandlela!
P6	Kodwa omnye wamakhwenkwe wayelangazelela ukuphuma phandle elangeni.
P7	Ngenye imini ulangazelelo lwakhula ngamandla. Abantakwabo bamlumkisa.
P8	Kodwa kwaku semva kwexesha! Wanyibilika elangeni elishushu.
P9	Abantwana bekhandlela baba lusizi kakhulu ukubona untakwabo enyibilike waphela.
P10	Kodwa,  benza icebo. Babumba igaqa lekhandlela laba yintaka.
P11	Bathatha umntakwabo oyintaka,  bamsa phezu kwentaba ephakamileyo.
P12	Njengoko ilanga liphuma,  wabhabhela kude evuma kukukhanya kwakusasa.
