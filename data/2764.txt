desc:	Inkwenkwana ifuna iphini layo lokubetha ibhola. Ingaba izakulifumana?

page_text:

P1	Ndililahlile iphini lam lokubetha ibhola,  kwaye andilifumani.
P2	Ndikhangele emva kocango. Belingekho phaya.
P3	Ndilikhangele ethaleni. Akhange ndilifumane phaya.
P4	Ndilikhangele phantsi kwebhedi. Alikho nalapha.
P5	Ndiphengulule phakathi etyesini/ebhokisini. Akukho nophawu lwalo.
P6	Ndiphengulule indawo ephantsi kophahla. Andikhange ndilifumane naphi na.
P7	Ndikhangele apha,  phaya,  nakwiindawo zonke,  Andilifumananga naphina.
