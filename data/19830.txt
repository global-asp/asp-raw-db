P1	My name is Kafui and I have a little sister called Elinam.
P2	Elinam is always happy,  whenever it is time for school.
P3	Their teacher Madam Esi,  tells and reads interesting stories to them everyday.
P4	She also gives them,  storybooks to read at home.
P5	My little sister is always happy to go to school.
