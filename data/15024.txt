desc:	A man goes against community norms and brings his bananas to sell at the market.

page_text:

P1	UTom uphethe ingobozi yeebhanana ezivuthiweyo.
P2	UTom uya emarikeni uyothengisa iibhanana.
P3	Abantu abasemarikeni bathenga iziqhamo.
P4	Kodwa akukho mntu ozithengayo iibhanana zikaTom. Bakhetha ukuthenga iziqhamo ebafazini.
P5	"Ekuhlaleni,  ngabafazi bodwa abathengisa iziqhamo," batsho abantu. "Yindoda enjani na le?" bayabuza abantu.
P6	Kodwa uTom akancami. Uyakhwaza,  "Thengani iibhanana zam! Thengani iibhanana zam ezinencasa ezivuthiweyo!"
P7	Omnye umfazi uthatha ipakethi yeebhanana engobozini. Ujonga iibhanana ngononophelo.
P8	Lo mfazi uthenga iibhanana.
P9	Abantu abaninzi beza esitendini. Bathenga iibhanana zikaTom bazitye.
P10	Msinyane, Â  ingobozi ayinanto. UTom ubala imali ayenzileyo.
P11	Aze uTom athenge isepha,  iswekile,  nesonka. Ufaka izinto engobozini yakhe.
P12	UTom ungcekelela ingobozi entlokweni yakhe agoduke.
