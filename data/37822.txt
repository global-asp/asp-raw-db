page_text:

P1	Wia my cat dey?@@I no fit find my cat.@@I no fit find my cat for anywia.
P2	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey under di bed?@@No, him de run comot from room.
P3	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey on top di cupboard?@@No, him dey on top di bottom of di shelf.
P4	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey for di back of di chiar?@@No, him dey for yard.
P5	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey next to di dustbin?@@No, him de jump comot from di window.
P6	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey for inside basket?@@No, him dey for inside di box.
P7	Wia my cat dey?@@Him dey outside for yard?@@No, him dey for di back of di curtain.
P8	Wia my cat dey?@@See am here!@@him de hide for inside cupboard!
