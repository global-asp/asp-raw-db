title:	Wenda Langi Hi?
author:	Rose Alungho
artist:	Sue Kramer, Rijuta Ghate, Sandy Lightly, Catherine Groenewald, Wiehan de Jager and Amir Bachir António Necas
lang:	Lunyole

desc:	Enghambagana hu ma langi.

page_text:

P1	Dinah likes a purple dress. Dinah genda ehiteteyi ehya papo.
P2	My aunt likes an orange dress. Dad bought it for her. Senge genda ehiteteyi ehye kapapaali. Bbabba nja ga muguliye.
P3	Jjajja likes a blue dress. Nguhwa genda ehiteteyi ehya bbululu.
P4	Dan likes a red van. Dani genda emotoka enando.
P5	The baby likes a yellow chair. Omwana genda etebe eya namagi.
P6	Customers like red tomatoes. Abagusi benda enyaanya enyululuhi.
P7	School children like to wear blue tops for games. Abaana basomi benda ebbulawuzi eja bbululu ohuguduliramo.
P8	My sister likes a yellow blouse. Muganda yange genda a ebbulawuzi eya namagi.
P9	Jane likes a pink dress. Jane genda ehiteteyi ehya pinki.
P10	My neighbour likes a yellow cottage. Mulirwana yange genda amago aga namagi.
P11	Insects like green places. Ebibuka byenda ebifo ebya kilagala.
P12	My friend and I like green apples. Ese ni meri yange hwenda bugali weene emiyembe emibisi.
