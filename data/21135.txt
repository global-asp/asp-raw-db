desc:	Aperi Apoo alokwap ekitoe

page_text:

P1	Achakun kolong echokit ekitoe kwap ni aperio Apoo.
P2	Abu Apoo kiira etoil lo abala, "Kipung’ar, kipung’ar!"
P3	Tama Apoo Ekukut, "Abe achakun bo ibore, tama etoil kipungar!"
P4	Kipung’ar Ekukut na eirarea Apoo ebala neni.
P5	Tolimokis Ing’ok tamasi, "Abu Apoo kipung’ar na eirarea ibore echakun."
P6	Kipung’ar Ing’ok dae kiwapak Apoo ka Ekukut.
P7	Tolimok Ing’ok Aparas, "Eirari Apoo ibore echakun dio kipungar."
P8	Kipung’ar Aparas dae kiwapak kechi.
P9	King’itakin Esikiria, "Inyo kireng’it eesi?" Tama Aparas, "Nyayeni ayong."
P10	Kiwapaki Esikiria dae ajore kipung’ar.
P11	Tolimok Esikiria Aite, "Be eirari Apoo ibore echakun dio kipung’ar."
P12	Tobul Aite noi dio ng’esi dae kipung’ar.
P13	Kisisak Aite Ipaka tama, "Eirari Apoo ibore echakun dio kipung’ar."
P14	Dio Ipaka dae kipung’ar kiwapak akerit.
P15	Kiriamunos ka Edia. 

"Nyiyeni yong na asubakinosi a?" king’is ng’esi.
P16	"Abe ayong bo abala Apoo kipung’ar!" tokieni edia tong’edar.
