desc:	This story talks about how a fly is dangerous.

page_text:

P1	Flies are insects.
P2	Flies like living in dirty places.
P3	In toilets, rubbish pits and on food.
P4	Flies move from dirty places to our homes.
P5	They fall on our food and make it dirty.
P6	When one eats dirty food,  he gets stomach ache.
P7	A flies are very dangerous.
