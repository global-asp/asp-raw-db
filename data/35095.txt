title:	Daisy odabwiha

desc:	Va emmatani engonongono wakhalla Daisy

page_text:

P1	Khallekhalle va emmtani eng'onong'ono watamella mudzi mmoddene mung'onong'ono.
P2	Vakhalla mwanakhu mung'onong'ono mddina naye Daisy.
P3	"Kunnuwa  kimpalla kivave otthullu, otthullu orekamesesa," allotta Daisy.
P4	Ana mwanakhu akhwaye yamuttheya.
P5	"Murwawo khomphara," akhwaaye yallota. "Khintthellattho na weyano."
P6	"Daisy, atthene tthukoza otthapulla  mumphellotthihu nave tthiwovutta wi mwanakhu avave," amoowe yamullella.
P7	Daisy khamallenlle vavo. Navirive nllapo aneserera yekhaye, otthapulla mumphellaye. @@Phuru! Phuru! Phuru! Ohulla mumphellaye wi avave koma tthamallephereya.
P8	Mvo eseraye anaphwanaphwaniha ari maddullu awehaka anakhu akhwaye vatthi. @@Anaphwanaphwaniha avava mokwaranya a nnekhettha nakhwiwa. "Haa!" Epallame ddanllotta.  "Mwanakhu ovava!"
P9	Nave Phuru! Phuru! Phuru! Nillive nllapo aneserera otthapulla mimphelloye.
P10	Aheserera wi avave, koma amorattho.
P11	"Ngiwa kivavattho!" Daisy ahella wa moowe. "Alle yallotta tthowona."
P12	"Daisy, ori ohiyana na anakhu amodda. Khepalla ovava nave weyano onapalla," Amoowe yamullella.
P13	Nllapo nimodda, Daisy awella vaddulu vanikokhoro na anakhu, nave phuru, phuru, phuru, mumphellaye. @@Avava mutthullu nuhulla mumphellaye, nawukutthiha mumphello nawukutthihattho...
P14	BAM!
P15	Anakhu amodda yamuttheyesesa. "Ha ha ha ha! Nahullella! Mwanakhu khani avave!"
P16	Koma nllapo nimodda Daisy awella vaddullu oposella vowambella valle. @@Phuru! Phuru! Phuru! Ahukuttiha mumphellaye.
P17	Avava muddulu nawukuttiha mumphellaye wukutiha na wukuttiha…
P18	Avavave! Niiphevo vatthi va muuphello nahimuwa nave avava mudduluu! Nekettha nakhwaye yaraya, "Ddodabwiha! Mwanakhu ovava!"
P19	Anakhu akhwaye yahambera wi ephwanaphwaneke nulliyeni.
P20	Yahera, "Ha Daisy, ori odabweha!"
