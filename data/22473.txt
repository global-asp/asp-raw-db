title:	Omuvubuka eyaduka n'agenda
author:	Samson  Nabbimba
artist:	Megan  Barry
lang:	Luganda

desc:	Emboozi eno ekwatagana ku ndowooza ebeera mumaka singa omuntu aba asobezza.

page_text:

P1	Gemo ne Nelisoni baali baluganda.
P2	Gemo y'agendanga ku somero buli lunaku, naye ate Nelisoni ye teyagendanga kusoma.
P3	Taata we y'agendanga okukola mu kibuga. Maama we ye yalinga akola mu nimiro.
P4	Olunaku olumu Taata we yaleka sente ku meeza. Nelisoni sente n'azibba nazitwala!
P5	Taata kino kyamunyiiza nyo nayagala okumukuba.
P6	Naye Nelisoni naduka nagenda. Era yasiiba atambula yeka mu'luguudo olunaku lwona.
P7	Y'agenda natuula wansi w'omuti ekiro kyona.
P8	Gemo mugandawe  y'amusanga wansi womuti namutwala ewaka.
P9	"Lwaki wabbye sente?" Taata we bweyamubuuza nga bwamukuba.
P10	Lwaki waduse nogenda?"Maama we bweyamubuuza.
P11	"Nasobezza, nasobezza, nasobezza," Nelisoni bweyategeeza era sente nazizaayo.
P12	Era bona nebamusanyukira era nebamugwa nemukifuba.
