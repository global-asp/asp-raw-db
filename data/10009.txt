title:	Kayannga E Jollooru Muuɗum
author:	Plan Niger - Projet NECS - Usaid
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Fulfulde

desc:	Saarooɓe Kayannga maayii

page_text:

P1	Kayannga no wuuridi e sakiraaɓe muuɗum nder seho faa yottii to ɓe maayi wakkati muuɗum imo woodi duuɓi sappo.
P2	Roja no jeydaaɗo Kayannga gooɗom kam jogii-mo. Roja no moƴƴi e Kayannga,  ammaa o nayeejo mo walaa semmbe,  woolaa risku.
P3	Wakkati yahde wakkati fuu Kayannga no juurowo saabeere sakiraaɓe muuɗum faa o fillanoo ɓe tampiri makko.
P4	Nyannde gooɗom ko juurowii ɓe,  o heɓi dokkal gooɗom. Jollooru hayndeeru gooɗom kam wanngi dow saabeere sakiraaɓe makko.
P5	Jollooru ndun yimi jimol belngol gooɗom,  pukkinoowol hakkillo. Kayannga faamitii nder maggol sawta daande inniiɗum maayɗo. Sawtooji ɗin no mbi'a nii:.
P6	To Kayannga yahta fuu no jogii jollooru muuɗum hayndeeru. Nde Kaynnga no jogii jollooru muuɗum fuu,  imo maata reenaare sakiraaɓe makko.
P7	Nyannde gooɗom,  say gacce hewtii jollooru hayndeeru ndun. Ndun fusi wakkati Kayannga foondinoo weendu faa filowo ndiyam. Ɓernde Kayannga taƴi kan doo.
P8	Kayannga naati no yima,  imo jogii laalaaɗe jollooru ɗen nder juukoy makko pamaroy:.
P9	Kayannga nani sawto daande inniiɗum wi'I mo: "Ɓiyam day,  adu laalaaɗe koddiiɗe ɗen. Adu ɗe pilo-ɗaa ndiyam nden lootaa koyɗe maaɗa. Nde a lootii faa gasii fuu,  mummu gite maaɗa." Kayannga waɗi no yamiraa,  say tan,  jollooru ndun warti no wontirnoo.
P10	Kayannga fuɗɗiti no yahda e jollooru muuɗum to o yahta fuu. To o wittorii fuu,  himɓe no nyo'a caka muuɗum en. "Nduye woni jollooru nduu?" Jollooru Kayannga hayndeeru nduu wadii o heɓi ko o haajaa fuu.
P11	Jollooru makko hayndeeru nduu faaminii Kayannga ko sakiraaɓe muuɗum no kakkilii e muudum. Gacce fuu waawaa heɓude-mo.
