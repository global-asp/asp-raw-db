title:	Waraga Yi
author:	Doris Maandebo
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lugbarati
level:	First words

desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school.  Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Sabiti aje risi ma esu waraga tre.
P2	O`du Alu si dakitari ma vile ri si waraga ma vile imbapi ni.
P3	Tabucia ni azoru. Le ma li ava akua.
P4	O`du Iri si ma esu waraga engazu ma agii kilasia 'diyi vu.
P5	Mi ati mbele,  Tabucia!
P6	O`du Na si ma esu waraga engazu ma agiyi mani lele turi vu.
P7	Agba e`da `di mini. Leta be engazu Dudu vu.
P8	O`du su si ma esu waraga engazu ma andri vu.
