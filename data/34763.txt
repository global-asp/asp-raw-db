page_text:

P1	Mo motseng,ne gona le mosimane o monnye a bidiwa Abula.@  O ne a sa rate go bala le eseng go bula buka.E ne ele moithuti o botswa. O ne a sa itshwara sentle.
P2	Abula one a utswa madi a ga mmagwe@  a reka dimonamone.lefa mmagwe a fitlhile madi, o ne a bona mme a a tseye.@  Fa a fetsa go ja@  dimonamone,Abula o  tshameka le ditsala tsa gagwe.
P3	Morago ga sebaka, Abula o ne a emisa go  ya sekolong.@  O ne a tsamaya letsatsi lotlhe a ile@  go tshameka, a boela gae fela mantsiboa.@  Mmagwe Abula o ne a lemoga@  se mme o ne a tshwenyegile.
P4	Morwa wa rona ga a itshwara@  sentle. Ga ke nagane gore o ya sekolong. O nkutswetsa madi,”ga bua mmagwe Abula.@  Rragwe o ne a nagana gore a ka dira jang go fetola maitshwaro a ga Abula.
P5	A raya mosadi wa gagwe@  are, “ka nako e latelang@  o fitlhe madi mo teng ga ditsebe@  tsa buka. Ganke a bula buka,ka jalo@  madi a tla bolokega moo.
P6	Letsatsi le le latelang,@  Abula a batla madi
