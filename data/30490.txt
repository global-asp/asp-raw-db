desc:	Mulufumo luno dhadha wa Tingi omukazi ataasa obulamu bw.

page_text:

P1	tingi yali nibadhadhaabe.
P2	yalinga albirira e'nte ni ye.
P3	lunaku lulala abadhaasi beidha.
P4	batwaala e,nte.
P5	Tingi ni dhaadha we balumuka.
P6	Beekweka munsiko okutuusa obwiire.
P7	abadhaasi bamala beira.
P8	dahdaha we omukazi yekweeka mumakoola.
P9	omudhaasi owundhi yamutaaku ekigere, aye yasirikirira.
P10	bweyawona, Tingi ni dhadhaawe omusaadha bafuluma.
P11	beira ghaka mubwaangu.
