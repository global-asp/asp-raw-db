desc:	This is a story about a wedding. The narrator describes what a child sees when people are getting ready for a wedding and the excitement which follows.

page_text:

P1	Zuva remuchato waTobhiasi ndiro zuva ratakaiona ino Bhenzi nhema yaida kuzosungirirwa mabharuma ichizotakura mwenga. Motikari yakange yakanaka zvayo iyoyo. Ko zvaigodii, Tobhiasi anga afunga kuchata.
P2	Tobhiasi akange akura, ndokufunga kuroora musikana wake Chido. Kwapera mazuva shoko rakafamba rekuti vaviri vakange vafunga kuchata. Isu patakazvinzwa takafara vakomana.
P3	Iye Tobhi waizombomuziva here achena chena arimo musutu chena. Waitoti ingirosi zve yadzika zvayo kuzotandara nesu kuno.
P4	Chezuva iri hapaidanwa anonzwa kani. Takazopedzisira zvedu todeedzera "Makorokoto Tobhi achata, nhasi tirikufaraaa!!!"
