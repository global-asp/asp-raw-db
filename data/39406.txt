P1	Once upon a time there live a boy who was called John he was a very Greedy boy.Everybody in the village knew him for his greediness,Then one day a boy came up to him for some food while he was eating then he said get out you boy you smell
P2	like a garbage after saying all those words to The boy,The boy felt like crying afterwards the boy went at home then he was sitting down very hungry one day John himself was looking for some food while his parents were not at home he walk
P3	All around The village but nobody could give him food because everybody knew him for his greediness then why he was working he met that boy that he abused in front of his house he begged the boy ba several times but the boy did listen to hi
P4	Him then the boy abused him the same as he did in front of his house then the boy told him when I was begging you you abused me and now it's my turn to abuse you now get out of my house said boy John went home so hungry and then he realized
P5	What he had done then on that day John learned a lesson that's being greedy it's not good since that day John starts giving food stuffs to all the little children in their village then everybody was surprised at John's attitude then John
P6	Became so happy then John got so many friends because he stopped what he was doing which was greediness then all the villagers that's giving John some reward fruits toys then John became happy again they were so proud of him
P7	And to all of you who is reading my story please stop the greediness towards your friends and people and you'll be successful thank you for reading
