page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping.Káshípákíno oloidí lé olgosói.
P2	 Alone.ópeny.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too.Káshípákíno siî tenáid tenébo ó ilchoréta láainéí.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories.Káshípákíno enkísúmátá oó inkaátini.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends.Káshípákíno elótóto é óng'átá tenébo ilchoréta láainéí.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother.Káshípákíno tenáret yieyíó aisujakí intokitín.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily.Káshípákíno enkígérótó oó isirát sapúki láídim aisúma.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit.Káshípákíno enkínósátá oó ilng'anayíó oópaasha.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.Káshípákíno enkirurá te errúat sapúk.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories.Káshípákíno tenáíníning yieyíó éínosû inkaátini sidaîn.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Oo! Káshípákíno eásátá e kúna tokitín pookí.
