title:	Amabara y'umukororombya
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/23174.png
desc:	Iyi nkuru irimo urutonde rw amabara agize umukororombya hamwe nibintu ushobora kubona aho uri hose bifite ayo mabara.

page_text:

P1	Umukororombya ufite amabara arindwi.
P2	Ibara rya mbere ni umutuku.
P3	Kagabo yambaye ishati y'umutuku.
P4	Ibara rya kabiri ni oranje (risa n'icunga rihishije).
P5	Iri ni ibara rya oranje. Ni iki kindi ubona gisa n'icunga rihishije?
P6	Ibara rya gatatu ni umuhondo.
P7	Umuhondo w'igi usa n'umuhondo. Ni iki kindi ubona gifite ibara ry'umuhondo?
P8	Ibara rya kane ni icyatsi.
P9	Urutoki rwacu rufite ibara ry'icyatsi.
P10	Ibara rya gatanu ni ubururu bukeye.
P11	Ikirere gifite ibara ry'ubururu bukeye.
P12	Ibara rya gatandatu ni ubururu bwijimye (indigo).
P13	Izi nkeri zifite ibara ry'ubururu bwijimye cyangwa indigo.
P14	Ibara rya karindwi ni idoma (violet).
P15	Ikanzu yange ifite ibara ry'idoma. Nkunda idoma. Wowe ukunda irihe bara?
P16	None se waba uzi amabara arindwi agize umukororombya?
