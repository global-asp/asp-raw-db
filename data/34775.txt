P1	When l in was in class two in Lions primary school.It was my duty to look after my little sisterJane,she was in nursery school.Our parents were both farmers.They would leave early in the morning and come late in the evening.One day as I was
P2	reading a story book l heard people shouting.I decided to go and see what was happening.I was shivering and sweating.When I opened the door I saw people running heading to a nearby shop.I called my sister Jane and we joined the people who
P3	were going to the shop.We saw an old lady laying on the ground near the shop.Her hands were tied with a rope.Next to her there was a loaf of bread and a packet of milk.She had been caught stealing from Mr Kangethe's shop.I told my sister my
P4	reason she stole the loaf of bread and the packet of milk.I also felt pity for the lady so I ran back home and took all my savings and gave to the old lady so that she could buy food, the lady was so grateful.My parents were proud of me and
P5	they bought me a bike because of my generosity.She was told never to steal people's property.
