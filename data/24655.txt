page_text:

P1	ǀGui ǂkhoab ge ǁgama garu.
P2	ǀGam ǃgaroǃnai kha ge ǁgama garu.
P3	ǃNona ǃgarogomagu tsî haka anin on ge ǁgama garu.
P4	Koro ǃkhaupirin tsî ǃnani dirigu on ge ǁgama garu.
P5	Hû ǃgoren ge ǁgama garu.
P6	ǁKhaisa ǁgôadi tsî khoese ǁaun on ra tsâ.
P7	ǁGâ ra xammi on ra ā ǂgao.  Hame nē xamma ra ǃao.
P8	ǀGui ǂkhoab ge xammi ǀkha ra ǀhûbe ā.
