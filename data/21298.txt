desc:	Enkhudhu elikunoonia enhumba yaayo. Oidhiku welikujojagana?

page_text:

P1	Olundhi enkhudhu yali
  elikutambula mukisawe. Yali elikunoonia ekintu. Yayaya mu buwanvhu ate yekweka mwiisuubi. Yamala yatambula yabiita kwikovu.
P2	“ olina kyoli kunhonia?”
  eikovhu lyabuuza. Ieee, ikovhu, ndhikunhonia numbha yanghe. Togibweinhe, nhomukisa ghwona ghwona?”
P3	Eikovhu lyakooba, “ mbhe nkali, aye ndhakuyamba onhonya!” yanhinha kukibuuti kyenkudhu. Enkudhu yatambula neikovhu kumugongo gwayo. Banhonia banhonia, aye tiwaliwo kaboonhelo kanumba. Omusana gwali waigulu mubileli
P4	Katonoinho babita ku kanooni, “ mulinakyemulikunhonia?”
  akanoni kabuuza, “ieee, kanoni, ndhikunhonia numba yange. Ogibweinhe, omukisagwonagwona?"
P5	Yakuba amawawa yabuluka
  ate ya kolootelayavawo. Aye
  olikujja manghu kunzhe,” yemulugunya enkudhu. Kanoni ila. “kale, kati
  nja kubukka.
P6	Enkudhu yaikiliza, neikovhu
  nakanhoni bayagayaga ku kabuuti. Banhonia
  banhonia, ayenga waziila kabonelo ka nhumba. Omusana nga gukendheile wansi mubileli.
P7	Katonoinho babita ku kanoni akakazi. “ olina kyolimukunhonia?”
  kabuuza kanoni akakazi, ndhikunhonia nhumba yange. Ogibweinhe, nomukisa gwonagwona?”
P8	Akanoni akakazi kakoba,
  mbhe nkali, ayenjakuyamba!” kabukiila ku mugongo wenkudhu
  yajja, neikovhu nekinhoni na kaanoni akakazi ku mugongo gwe.
  Banhonia banhonia, aye nga wazila kaboneloo
  kanhumba. Empewo yatandika okufhuwa.
P9	Katonhoino babita ku musonso.yali alikola omugandha gwebimuli. “mulina kyemuli
  kunhonia?” omusonso gwabuza. Iee, musonso, ndhikunhonia nhumba yange. Ogibweineku nomukisa gwhonagwhona?”
P10	Omusonso gwakooba. “ mbe, nkali,aye jakuyambaku okunhonia!” gwabukilaku mugongo gwenkudhu. “lindha,
  tisoboola kuleka bimulibyange inhuma.”enkudhu yalindaku. Omusonso gwe dhumula ate yaiba ebimuli
P11	Enkudhu yatambula, neikovhu
  ne kinoni ne akanoni akakazi ne omusonso kumugongo gwayo. Banhonia banhonia, aye tiwali kabonelo ka nhumba. Empewo yayeyawo  ebikoola wansi yabikunganikiza webigele byenkudhu. Yakoowa. “imwe
  mwenamwena muli bazito, “yakooba.
P12	Empowo yeyongela eyamani.
  Ebileli byakuka byalugala. “oh, enhumba yange eliwa?” enkudhu yabuza. Keleele yabunha olusozi. Akakuba kakuba mubuwanvhu. Obutoneze bwatandika mu madi
P13	Empewo yaffuwa ate yayeya.
  Yaffuwa eikovhu ne kinoni ne kanoni akakazi ne omusonso gwava ku mugongo
  gwe enkudhu.
P14	Yamala yaidha amadi
  amagi. Enkudhu yaffuna okutya yekweka mu kabutiyayo. Kyali kisuuwilila ate nga kilungi mundha.
  “Oh enhumba yange yiinho!” enkudhu yamwena.
P15	“oh, enhum,ba yenkudhu yiinho!” yakooba eikovhu ne kinoni ne omusonso.
