title:	Embaluwa
author:	Isaac Kagoda
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2920.png
desc:	Olugero luli ku mughala mulwaire era ali nookusigala nga tagiire ku isomero. Embaluwa dha mugaso inho okumukuuma nga mwiikaikamu.

page_text:

P1	Saabiti eyo nafuna embaluwa kamaala.
P2	Kulwabbalaza omusawo wange yampandiikira embaluwa eyomusomesa wange.
P3	Naisanga mulwaire. Alina kusigala ghaka.
P4	Kulwokubili nafuna embaluwa okuva mu bana kibiina kyange.
P5	Naisanga,  tukwendereza okuwona kwa mangu!
P6	Kulwokusatu nafuna embaluwa okuva ye mukwano gwange nfiirabulago.
P7	Nakukubiire akafaanani kano. Bukwenda buva ye Dudu.
P8	Kulwokuna nafuna embaluwa okuva ye maama wange.
