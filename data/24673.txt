title:	The beauty of a mother

desc:	A mother is natures biggest gift to a child.

page_text:

P1	Mum is always there when we need her,she takes us to school every day.
P2	She takes us  to the market with her to buy vegetables,potatoes and yams.
P3	Mother is so good,she buys me and my brothers shoes and boots.
P4	When i fall sick,mother is always there to take good care of me.
P5	That is why i love my mama very much.
