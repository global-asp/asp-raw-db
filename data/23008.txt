page_text:

P1	Wandiga yali abeera mu kibuga.
Yali akooye obulamu bw'ekibuga n'asalaawo agende mu kibiira.

ku lugendo lwe yasisinkana Wakayima eyali amazze ennaku nga talya.
P2	Yabuuza Wakayima emirundi gyʻomuddiriŋŋana nti, "Ow'oluganda wakayima oli otya? Ow'oluganda Wakayima oli otya?"

Wakayima y'amuddamu nti, "Tombuuza mirundi  gya muddiriŋŋana.Bako kyokola okuwonya enjala yange."
P3	Wandiga bwe yawulira bino, n'atandikiirawo emisinde.

Mbagirawo, yatuuka mu kisibo kya Bawampologoma.Wakayima yali amugoba.

Kunkomerero, ne wakayima yatuuka ku kisibo kya bawampologoma nasiba, naffumuliza Wampologoma enfuufu mu maaso.
P4	Wampologoma yabuuza Wakayima nti, "Tetusobola kufuna ddembe ne mubisibo byaffe? Nʻonfumuliza enfuufu mu maaso!"

Wakayima nʻamuddamu nti, "Kabaka, sigenderedde kuyisamu maaso. Ngoba mmere yange."
P5	Wampologoma nʻamuddamu nti, "Oli mmere gyendi nawe Wakayima. Nkugobyeko? Gwe okugya gyendi.Toonenya gyebuggya."
P6	Oluvanyuma Wampologoma nʻagamba Wandiga nti, "Lwaki wazze mu kibiira?"
Wandiga nʻamuddamu nti, "Nazze kubanga ndi musawo wakinansi."
P7	Wampologoma nʻamugamba nti, "Ntegekera eddagala lyʻekinansi nkakase nti oli musawo." Wandiga nʻamuddamu nti, "Eddagala lyange lyabbeeyi."

Wampologoma nʻamugamba nti, "Ekyo sikizibu, kyonna kye kyetagiisa. Teri kitasoboka gyendi."
P8	Wandiga nʻamugamba nti, "Eddagala mattu ga wakayima."
Wampologoma nasalako okuttu kwa Wakayima nakuwa wandiga.
Wandiga nakuteeka mu jjaga y`omubiisi nagiwa Wampologoma.
P9	Wampologoma nakulya era nawomerwa nnyo.
Nʻabuuza Wandiga nti, "Kisoboka nze okufuna nokwokubbiri?"
P10	Wandiga nʻamuddamu nti, "Ye, kisoboka kabaka wange."N'olwekyo Wampologoma nasalaako okuttu kwa Wakayima okwʻokubiri nakuleetera Wandiga.

Wandiga nʻakuteeka mu jjaga yʻomubiisi nakuwa Wampologoma
P11	Wampologoma nʻagamba nti, "Kisoboka okufuna eddiba?"
Wandiga nʻamuddamu nti, "Ndowooza kisoboka kabaka wange."

Wakayima bwe yawulira bino natya okufa nadduka.
Wampologoma namugoba.
P12	"Bwe buba ngʻobulamu bwʻomukibiira bwe butyo,kisingako nzire ekka."Wandiga nʻagamba.
