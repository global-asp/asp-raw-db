title:	Akidodok Ka Emun
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20392.png
desc:	Itatami poyo naga kidiama epaalonu loechamit eyakar arimatoi.

page_text:

P1	Arai kolong akidodok ka emun ng'ipaalon.
P2	Tama akidodok, "Ibwa apana apeyo nawi aitokang."
P3	"Ejok noi," tatachu Emun. "Kiyok ng'ae ng'auyei yok?"
P4	"Edong'i ayong eyok ng'auyei kus," tatachu ekadokot.
P5	Abu Akidodok kileleb ng'ikong  lochwee ainaar itokeng.
P6	Kiswokakis Akidodok ka Emun arotokin. Todeng'o namoni.
P7	Apotu kiriamun ka asung'sung ang'ikunyuko. Tobul Emun.
P8	Abu Akidododok kigagaa Emun ani ang'ikunyuko.
P9	Todolo kechi nawi aitokeng Akidodok enyamit akoro noi.
P10	Dio kilataros ering'a idarito akimuj nanyamat.
P11	" Ekone, tonyou kiboikin," tolimoki akidodok emun.
P12	Tama emun eboikin, nakaneni pe arai ng'esi akidodok.
P13	"Nyapedori ayong eboikin kwa Akidodok," tama Emun.
P14	Tong'er akidodok akiirar ng'akiro ng'una.
P15	"Nyinupit iyong ng'akiro kang. Alosi ayong nawi."
P16	Tong'o Emun. "Abala kolong kori erai Akidodok epae!"
