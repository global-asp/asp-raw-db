page_text:

P1	Lolo na amukaaga. emwana noi.
P2	Ekotosi Alice, Arthur keda Anabel aiduu ocilet.
P3	Ebala Anabel, "alacasi iwok igween alototo aiduu".
P4	"oni kere asomakinos eduwoto", Ebala Arthur.
P5	"Ooo! opotu eduwoto, erai aiduu alakanar!" ebala Alice.
P6	Ejesar akolong kwap, ipupi oni aiterot. aiyobo eja iwok igween?
P7	Amowata kes. kwap na ikitoo. Toma okutuwoo. Arai bonata ediwaii.
P8	Otukwii! inyobo ngin aitukuke? "esati ka", Emonyii Arthur.
P9	Okwe Edekeka! Ecee bobo,"Eka sikat ngon!" Ilulukin Alice.
P10	Ikaiduwe, "inyobo atakaniki yesi?"
