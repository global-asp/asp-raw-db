desc:	Ɓinngel Ɓowngel heɓii janngirde mawde juuɗe ɓiɓɓe-Aadamaen. (Ngeel deftel winndaama gila Apilikeeson ɗum African Storybook Maker

page_text:

P1	Nder jamaanu neeɓuɗum, kala dabba fuu waawi wolwugo.@@Daada Ɓowngel e Ɓiyiingel e wondi nder daare.
P2	Nde Daada Ɓowngel dillata ɗaɓɓugo nyaamndu fuu, o accay Ɓinngel maako to saare.
P3	Nyalnde wonnde, nde o acci ɓinngel maako to saare, ɓinngel ngel yiɗi wurtoo yaasi.
P4	Ɓaawo nde Daada wartoyi saare, o tawi ɓinngel maako dillii yaasi.
P5	Daada Ɓowngel e wannii dow maaka. @@Kadi Ɓowngel wurtake feere mum.
P6	Ɓinngel Ɓowngel e seyii masin.@@"Inna, yimɓe e seyi nde ɓe ngi'i-yam. Nde mi saalotoo haade maɓɓe fuu maa ɓe kellii."
P7	Daada Ɓowngel ɓeydi wannaago.@@"A wi'i yimɓe e seyii kella juuɗe maɓɓe ngam maa?" o ƴami ɓiɗɗo maako.
P8	"Ɓe seyaaki sam yi'ugo ma. E ɓe kaɓana halkugo-ma!" Daada faamtini-ngel.@@"Ɓiɓɓe-Aadama'en ngiɗaa yi'ugo ɓowɗi," o waggini.
