page_text:

P1	Ku kyalo kyaife yaliyo omusaadha omusuubuzi liina lye ni Dembe.
P2	Dembe yali ne eidhuuka einene einho mu kibuga kya Gayaaza.
P3	mwiidhuka lya Dembe mulimu obughunga,sukaali,ebizigo,omutyere,ebidhandhali,n'ebindhi kamaala.
P4	Dembe alinha omukazi ghe n'abaana basatu.
P5	mukisera ky'olughumula,abaana bayambaku abazaile baibwe okukola mwiidhuka nga baghereza abaguzi.
P6	Dembe alina ebitabo mwaghandhiika eby'esente buli lunhaku.
P7	Dembe nhi mukazighe balinha enhumba enhungi n'emotoka.
P8	Baaya embiidhi,ente,enkoko,n'obumyu.obulamu bwaaibwe bulungi.
P9	Dembe nhi mukazi ghe baafunha seente edhimala okulabilira abaana baibwe n'okubataala mumasomero amalungi.
