page_text:

P1	Ba Wansolo bali kulumuka olwo kuwulira amaloboozi ga Wampologoma azira kivunhika mu mwoyo era buli nsolo yoona yoona egitya.mu kitundu ebamu yonka kuba nsolo nkambwe.
P2	Wampologoma atabwike ali kunoonia bawansolo abalye kuba emere ye mmamba yabaine era buli gwagemyaku amulya bulye.
P3	Bawansolo abandi olwa wulirira ebiri mu kitundu nti mwene mu atyaime kwikonero bafa emighabo era baaja mu bitundu eby'endhawulo okunoonia obubudamo.
P4	Bawansolo abaamani batandiika okusekerera banabwe ababafu wampologoma tatutisa ife era tumusinga amagezi no bukodyo oyo tatusobola.
P5	Aye owa maani aba wa maani ife titusobola kubita ghali wampologoma kuba oyo mutuju akwita ngobona bu kofu kuba wakabi inho.
