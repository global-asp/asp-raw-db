title:	Indlela Iindlovu Ezafumana Ngayo Imiboko Emide
author:	Xolisa Guzula
artist:	Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Inkonyane lendlovu lalilangazelela ukwazi ngempilo yezinye izilwanyana elaliphila nazo.

page_text:

P1	Sonke siyayazi into yokuba indlovu inomboko omde.
P2	Kodwa kudala umboko wendlovu wawumfutshane kakhulu futhi utyebile okwesihlangu esibekwe ebusweni bayo. Wawuyimpumlo nje eqhelekileyo.
P3	Ngenye imini kwazalwa ithole lendlovu. Lalinomdla ngayo yonke into. Lalisoloko linombuzo kwisilwanyana ngasinye.
P4	Lalilangazelela ukwazi ngendlulamthi. ''Kutheni unomqala omde?'' labuza kwindlulamthi.
P5	Lalilangazelela ukwazi ngomkhombe. ''Kutheni uneempondo ezitsolo?'' Labuza kumkhombe.
P6	Lalilangazelela ukwazi ngemvubu. ''Kutheni unamehlo abomvu nje?'' Labuza kwimvubu.
P7	Kanti lalilangazelela KAKHULU ukwazi ngengwenya. ''Ingwenya itya ntoni ngesidlo sangokuhlwa?''.
P8	''Ungakulinge uphinde ubuze umbuzo onjalo!'' watsho umama walo. Waze wahamba engatyhilekanga.
P9	Kodwa ngokukhawuleza kwafika uNomyayi owayekrelekrele wathi,  ''Ndilandele siye emlanjeni. Apho uza kutsho ubone ukuba ingwenya itya ntoni ngesopholo.''.
P10	Ngenene ithole lendlovu lalandela unomyayi ukuya emlanjeni.
P11	Lazinyanzela ezingcongolweni laze lema enyeleni yomlambo. Lajonga emanzini. Yayiphi ingwenya?
P12	''Molo, '' kwatsho ilitye elalingakwinyele yomlambo. ''Ewe, '' laphendula ithole lendlovu. ''Ungandichazela ukuba ingwenya itya ntoni ngesopholo?''.
P13	''Goba uze ezantsi ndizokukuxelela, '' laphendula ilitye. ''Yehla,  yehla,  kwakhona.'' Ngenene ithole lendlovu lagoba,  lehla,  laphinda lehla laze.
P14	''Xhakamfu!'' Impumlo yethole lendlovu yabe sele isezindlathini zengwenya. ''Ingwenya iza kutya wena ngesopholo!'' Wakhala watsho unomyayi.
P15	Ithole lendlovu laxhathisa ngemilenze yalo yangemva eqinileyo,  latsala,  latsala,  latsala. Kodwa ingwenya ayatsho ukuliyeka.
P16	Impumlo yethole lendlovu yanwebeka,  yanwebeka,  yanwebeka laze. ''Dudlu!'' lawa labetheka ngomqolo.
P17	Ngokudikwa kukuphuncukwa sisidlo,  ingwenya yatshona phantsi kwamazi.
P18	Ithole lendlovu lajonga impumlo yalo. Kodwa lalingakwazi ukubona apho yayiphela khona!
P19	Impumlo yalo yayinde kangangokuba yayinokukwazi ukukha iziqhamo emasebeni aphezulu emithi.
P20	Impumlo yalo yayinde kangangokuba yayinokukwazi nokuhlamba umqolo walo ngamanzi Ukusukela ngaloo mini,  zonke iindlovu zaba nemiboko emide eluncedo.
