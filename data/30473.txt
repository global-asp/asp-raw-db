page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala akanhonhi kaalya obulo obw'omusaadha yakagaana yakagema. Yakakoba ati olwaleerero nja kulya bucanga olya bulo bwange.
P2	Akanhonhi kamwegayirira nti ghaire oja kundya,ghaliwo byenhenda okukukoba atenga bikulu inho.
P3	Omusaadha yakakoba ati,"Nkobera mangu nga nkali kukulya."Tisobola kwogerera mungalodho,nta ghale kukiti ndhogere bu
kalaamu."Bwekamwiramu.
P4	Omusaadha yaasirughala yakata kukiti, akanhoni kamukoba nti,"bwofunanga ekintu kyonakyona okinhwezanga, tighesumbanga kyokaali kufuna n'ekindi, ndi ni zaabbu munda.Akonhoni kaguluka kavaagho.
