title:	Vara Garem
author:	Joseph Kandwe (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Kasem
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18208.png
desc:	Nanyɔm jege vara maama mo. Gare ba ba na vei se ba nyɔ na.

page_text:

P1	Tuu dedoa mo maa vei se ko nyɔ na.
P2	Sigi selei mo maa vei se se nyɔ na.
P3	Ga-naane detɔ de zuna bana de mo maa vei se ba nyɔ na.
P4	Tɔrro tenu de ga-teira yaredo mo maa vei na bam ne.
P5	Nambworro terepɛ mo duri te vei na bam ne.
P6	Mantwaaro nana de ywona nogo mo wora ba pugi na bam wone.
P7	Nywoŋo dedoa ŋuura. Ko de lage na mo se ko nyɔ. Wɔ mo kware nywoŋo?
P8	Tuu dedoa mo wora ko nyɔ na de nywoŋo kom.
