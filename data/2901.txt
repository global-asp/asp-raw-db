desc:	In this story the big brother gets very grumpy with his little brother who spends his whole time playing while he is doing all the chores.

page_text:

P1	Ma enga o'dua ri si dika ma aci odu.
P2	Ma yi dri.
P3	Ma eja asi.
P4	Ma afa emvu alia ri osa.
P5	Ma joale we.
P6	Ma afajo yi oji.
P7	 De ma adri gaa ri ni lu ini avi 'i.
