title:	Dorooji bee Weelo
author:	Abdulaahi Aadamu Bappa and Dayyibu  Abdullahi
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	En mbaawi jooɗodaago jam bee saahaago ko ngooduɗen.

page_text:

P1	Ndunngu jippaaki taw, leydi e yoori.
P2	Dorooru ndun alii to wonno ngam ɗaɓɓitoygo ndiyam e nyamndu.
P3	Ndu taarake kooseeje bee lugge.
P4	Sey ndu yottii babal biʼeteengal Tirkol.
P5	Dorooru ndun nanii belɗum babal ngaʼal.
P6	Ndu nyaamii ɓikkoy leɗɗe ndu fayii. Ammaa ndu yennii banndiraaji maaru.
P7	Sey ndu nanngi laawol juutungol ndu hooti.
P8	Dorooji banndiraaji nanii belɗum yiʼugondu.
P9	Ɗi ƴamindu, "Toye keɓuɗa iri ɓikkoy leɗɗe koʼoy belkoy?"
P10	Dorooru wiʼi, "Mi yaarayʼon ton."
P11	Banndiraaji dorooru ngiɗi Tirkol. Ɗi mbiʼi ɗi njooɗoto ton haa abada.
P12	Ammaa dorooji gonɗi Tirkol turti.
P13	Ɗi mbiʼi, "Dorooji garɗi ɗin ɗi timminanayʼen ɓikkoy leɗɗe."
P14	Sey dorooji Tirkol ummanii garɗi ɗin haɓre.
P15	Ammaa arɗo wiʼi, "Ngam ɗume kaɓoton? Ɓikkoy leɗɗe keƴanayʼen en fuu."
P16	Ɗi fuu ɗi njaɓi ɗi mbiʼi, "Goonga." Ɗorooji narrii, ɗi njooɗodo jam.
