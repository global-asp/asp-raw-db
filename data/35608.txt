page_text:

P1	There is a girl called Shukuri. She lives in yankari.
P2	She has three sisters.
P3	Her sisters love going to school.
P4	Shukuri doesn't want likes school but loves dancing.@@Her sisters went to school and she stayed home.
P5	She decided to go to the bush to pixk some fruits. On her to the bush, she saw some ojujus.
P6	The queen of the ojujus said, "I now have a daughter!"
P7	Her mother was home worrying about her daughter.
P8	It is for your own good to let people know where you are going.
