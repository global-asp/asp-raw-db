page_text:

P1	Long ago, Thunder and Lightning lived on earth among people.
P2	Thunder was the mother of Lightning.
P3	Lightning had a bad temper.
P4	When Lightning was angry, he destroyed people, houses and trees.
P5	When Lightning caused such damage, Thunder made a loud noise.
P6	Lightning did not pay attention to his mother.
P7	At last,  people complained to the king.
P8	The king sent Thunder and Lightning to live far away.
P9	Lightning still burnt forests and crops when he was angry.
P10	So people complained to the king again.
P11	The king sent Thunder and Lightning to live in the sky.
P12	But Lightning still burns and destroys when he is angry.
