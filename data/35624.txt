P1	On my way to the airport, I bought many sweet things.
P2	When we got to the airport, I saw many people there.
P3	It was time to enter the plane, I was so happy.
P4	When I entered the plane, it was so amazing. I loved it!
P5	I did not expect to see so many chairs in the plane.
P6	The food I ate, was very delicious.
P7	When we arrived Dubai, the hotel was beautiful.
P8	I took lot's of ice cream. @Dubai is beautiful.
