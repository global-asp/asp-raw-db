title:	Mbiwa Yeew'aa Nzaa Na Mba
author:	Fatuma Nzive
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/9682.png
desc:	A hungry jackal comes across an orchard where a ripe bunch of grapes is hanging from a branch. The jackal jumps up to grab the grapes but he falls hard on his back. He keeps jumping even higher and each time he falls even harder on his back. Will he ever get the grapes?

page_text:

P1	Tene muno vai Mbiwa yeew'aa nzaa. Yaenda kithekani kumantha liu.
P2	Oona muti munene wina mba mbiu. Asisya kitungo kya mba kiniinite uvongeni iulu muno vyu.
P3	"Iia syi muyo muno kwakusisya," asuania we mwene. Asyoka na itina vanini nikana atulile iulu muno.
P4	I mba syai iulu muno vyu. Mbiwa yavalukile nthi na muongo itavikiite i mba.
P5	Mbiwa yookila na yasyokanga itina wa vala yambiie. Yeeyumbany'a kutuulila iulu muno vyu. Yu aivakuvi kuvikila mba indi avalukile na muongo wake munangango.
P6	Atata ingi na ingi,  indi ndaavikia i mba. Aendeeie na kuvalukanga muvaka muongo wake wathuka vyu.
P7	Muminukilyoni niweeyitukie na aekana nasyo na athi.
P8	Mbiwa ndaaiw'a muyo ona vanini. Aungama,  asisya i mba na asuania,  "Ona mba isu ti mbiu." Aendeea na kyalo kyake. Ai na nzaa mbingi muno vyu woondu weew'aa mbee.
