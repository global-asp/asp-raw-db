title:	Umgcini Nekhaya Leenkedama
author:	Kholeka Mabeta
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/14123.png
desc:	Ibali ngezilwanyane ezisindisiweyo zaze zagcinwa kwikhaya leenkedama. Ezi zilwanyana zigcinwa ngumntu okhethekileyo neqela lakhe.

page_text:

P1	Eli bali lithetha ngoMgcini nekhaya lakhe lezilwanyana eziziinkedama.
P2	Le nyanga iphelileyo ibixakeke kakhulu kweli khaya. UNomsa ononyaka omnye ezelwe waba ngokowokuqala ukufika. Wayebhityile ekhathazekile.
P3	Kodwa ngokukhawuleza,  uNomsa womelela. Waqalisa ukudlala nezinye izilwanyana.
P4	Ngenye imini kulonyanga,  kwafika inqwelo-ntaka kwelo khaya leenkedama.
P5	Kulo nqwelo-ntaka kwakukho intshontsho lendlovu lombathiswe ngengubo enkulu.
P6	UNdiwa wayephantsi kwengubo. Wayeneentsuku ezintlanu ubudala. UMgcini wancancisa uNdiwa ibhotile.
P7	Ngobunye ubusuku,  wonke umntu wavuswa kukufika kuka Ambia.
P8	UAmbia wayeneenyanga ezintlanu ubudala. Wayebuthathaka emva kohambo lwakhe olude.
P9	Ngenxa yentamo yakhe ende,  uMgcini wayibambela phezulu ibhotile encancisa uAmbia.
P10	Iinkedama zokugqibela ukufika kulo nyanga zilambile yayingamantshontsho uKopi,  uKepi kunye noKeji.
P11	UMgcini nabancedi bakhe bazithanda zonke izilwanyana,  nditsho nezihluphayo.
P12	Abantu abakwikhaya lenkedama basebenza ngamandla. Bathemba ukuba ngenye imini iinkedama zingakwazi ukuziphilela. Ezi zilwanyana zibuyele endle.
