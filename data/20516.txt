desc:	Dukkanin dabbobin suna jin kishirwa. Kidaya su a yayin da suke tafiya wajen shan ruwa.

page_text:

P1	Wata giwa daya na tafiya wajen shan ruwa
P2	Rakumin daji biyu na tafiya wajen shan ruwa
P3	Karkandai ukku da tsuntsaye hudu na tafiya shan ruwa
P4	Barewa biyar da wasu mugun dawa na tafiya shan ruwa
P5	Jakin daji bakwai na gudu wajen shan ruwa
P6	Kwadi takwas da kifaye tara na iyo cikin ruwa
P7	Zaki yayi ruri. Yana son shan ruwa. Wa ke tsoron zaki?
P8	Giwa daya na shan ruwa tare da zaki
