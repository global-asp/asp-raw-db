desc:	Mark goes to the zoo

page_text:

P1	Mark leaves home to go the zoo with his friend Mary.
P2	On their way, Mary and Mark buy eats.
P3	Mary develops a bad headache  and she starts crying.
P4	Mark looks for a near by clinic to get medicine.
P5	He finds there is no doctor or nurse.
P6	They had gone to talk to the people about living a healthy life.
P7	Mark shouted for help and people called the doctor.
P8	Mark gave Mary the doctor's medicine and took her back home.
P9	So he did not get to see the animals, because his friend was sick.
