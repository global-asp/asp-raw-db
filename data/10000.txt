title:	Haayndeeji Ɗi Nagge

desc:	Taariki mo sukaaɓe faro ko daarta nagge

page_text:

P1	Nanii nagge. Nagge no wonndee.
P2	Nder nyallooma,  nge nagge niinon.
P3	Inge fija fijo piirneteengel.
P4	Inge fiya baaluure.
P5	Ammaa jemma kay nge nagge haayndeeye.
P6	Inge faaboo yonkiiji.
P7	Inge haɓida e kirnyuɓe.
P8	Se a maɓbii gite no wooɗiri,  nden ko ngaɗu-ɗaa nufayeere.
P9	Nagge ngen no waawi duukay juuraade ma.
P10	Timmoode.
