page_text:

P1	Iminsi minshi, mukilele hari humye. Lomongin, umupfumu winvura, aravugana nubumana bwe. Abanu barikunganya kunzu ye, chaane bategyereje ijambo ryiza.
P2	Yaraje ava munzu,arabwira abaanu ko imana we azabaha invura. Aho baza rimaa ibiryo.
P3	Abaana bakinaga hanze abakuru bategyereje invura. Hashizeho umwanya, baboona hejuru hifashe ko invura igiye kugwa. Bamenya kwinura igiye kugwa.
P4	Abaanu barakyatekyereza icho uyu muhanzi winvura yavuze. "Ibicu birofata byirabura. Mwingire munzu."
P5	Invura yara guye.
P6	Twari tutegyereje invura! Ubwambere twarishimye. Ariko invura yarakomeje igwa.
P7	Amazi yari yuzuye ahanu hose. Urutindo rulachica umkyalo gagyera hansi kumataka  abanu babaga.
P8	Amazu yose nayo aragyenda amazi yinvura arayajana. Ichari mugisha ki ba ikyago kuri tewbwe.
P9	Burimunu wese yashakaga kurima. Ariko noneho ibicu sibyabemera. Abbanu mukyaro abashakaka invura noneho sibayishaka ugundi murundi.Narutindo tufite noneho wukwambukiraho. Benshi namazu bafite.
P10	Kandi, tigife ikibazo ikindi! Turabona ingona zuga mumazi hose. Iki nago kyari kyabaho. Turayoberwa.
P11	Sitwagyendaga mwisoko kubera amaduka invura yarayajanye. Abaana sibagyendaga kwishuri kubera amashuri wachaga kurutindo rwaramanutse.
P12	Ichari kuba umugisha kiba ikyaha changa umukyeno.Lomongin, umuhanzi winvura, byaramuyobeye nawe. Umunu umwe yari anaezerewe yari kaapa, yari afite izindi umunani  kaapa!
