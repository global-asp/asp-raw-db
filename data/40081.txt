P1	In a village called Maparomoko, there lived a boy called Mwenza.@He had loving and caring parents.One day they decided to surprise him."We are going to visit to museum",the father announced.
P2	Mwenza was as happy as a king because it was his first time to visit a museum.@He begun thanking the parents for the chance.He could not imagine seeing the things he had read in books about a museum.
P3	The time to visit was finally ripe.In the museum Mwenza was excited to see monkeys,birds,scareling snakes,artifacts and historical tools.He had a memorable time with his parents.Mwenza realised museum is a good place and left very excited.
