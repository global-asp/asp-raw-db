page_text:

P1	There is a woman called Mrs Rubandama. She lives in Kakuuto village.
P2	She has a sewing machine. She uses it to make clothes of different fashions.
P3	She makes different costumes.
P4	She makes school uniforms of grey and white for boys and girls.
P5	She makes blue uniforms and many other colours.
P6	She makes aprons for chefs.
P7	Her husband helps to measure and cut patterns.
P8	She makes casual clothes for outings.
P9	She makes tops,  skirts and shorts.
P10	She also makes beautiful bridal clothes.
