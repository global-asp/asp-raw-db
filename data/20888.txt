page_text:

P1	An apple fell from a tree under which Rabbit was sleeping.
P2	Rabbit heard a voice say, “Run Rabbit run!”
P3	Rabbit told Chicken, “Something fell and I heard, ‘Run Rabbit run!'”
P4	Chicken started running when she heard what Rabbit said.
P5	They told Dog, “Rabbit heard something falling and she ran.”
P6	Dog started running with Rabbit and Chicken.
P7	Dog told Horse, “Rabbit heard something falling and she ran.”
P8	Horse started to run with the others.
P9	Donkey asked, “Why are you running?” Horse replied, “I don’t know.”
P10	Donkey started running with them.
P11	Donkey told Cow, “Rabbit heard something falling and she ran.”
P12	Cow was worried and started to run as well.
P13	Cow told Cat, “Rabbit heard something falling and she ran.”
P14	Cat started running with the other animals.
P15	They met a boy. 

“Don’t you know what happened?” he asked.
P16	“It was me who said, ‘Run Rabbit run!’” laughed the boy.
