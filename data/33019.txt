desc:	Kube abalandeli basola imiyalo yekosi engakalungi

page_text:

P1	uMadumo khabe ayifene ekulu etlhorisa ezinye iinlwane.
P2	Gade ayikosi yeemfene.
P3	Abalandeli bakhe bamupha itjhila elikhethekileko.
P4	"Ngizolembatha ehloko," kutjho uMadomu.
P5	"Noke landelani imiyalo yami. Ningangisoli!" kurhuwelela uMadomu.
P6	Abalandeli bebalandela ikosi yabo. Nakeqako nabo bebeqa.
P7	Kodwana itjhila egade alibophe ehloko lalimminyile.
P8	Wabamba ihloko yakhe ebuhlungu. Iimfene nazo zamba zazo.
P9	"Lokhu kuyalimaza," kutjho uMadomu. "Lokhu kuyalimaza," bamlingisela.
P10	"Ingiminya khulu!" "Ingiminya khulu," bamlingisela.
P11	UMadomu wawela ngale kweliwa. Boke bamlingisela.
P12	Ikosi yafa. Abalandeli bayo balimala, kodwana baphila.
