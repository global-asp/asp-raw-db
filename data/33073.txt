title:	Umudlalo weBhola lezinyawo

desc:	UQhude noBhubhu babengakaze baba abangani.

page_text:

P1	oBhubhu ababili babezidlaleli emthini, noQhude ecofoza
P2	uQhude wathuka kwasuka impaphe KEKEEE! KEKEKE!".  Imina uBhubhu weQhude.
P3	Bhubhu ungithuselani@eshawa luvalo zi! zi!@ Cha! angikuthusi bengifisa sidlale noma ngabe imuphi umdlalo
P4	Wathuula! embukela phasi esathukile, ecabanga umdlalo abazowudlala mmh!
P5	Asambe siyodlala nali ibhola lezinyawo, esho elibambe ngesandla sokudla
P6	Awubheke la! weBhubhu alikho nelilodwa inqaku elizongena kulonozinti, esho evula izandla.
P7	Lasuka ibhola enkudleni, badlala kamnandi bekhahlela bephindelela.
P8	uQhude washibilika walala phansi ngomhlane, uBhubhu wathola ithuba lokushaya imbumbulu yenqaku ebhekise emapalini kaQhude @"Naliya ibhola liyokhalakathela enethini"
P9	Wajabula kakhulu uBhubhu, wafa insini waze walalaphansi ngomhlane.@ emfoma nawu mjuluko aaahhh!
P10	Akakholwanga uQhude ukuthi wehlulwa uBhubhu, @Wacasuka kakhulu ebuka kuthi uhlulwe into enomzimba omncane kanje @uBhubhu wayemamatheka ejabule.
P11	uQhude wathula wakhala ezibona kuthi awukho umsebenzi wakhe, uhlulwa isilwanyane esingeke samubinda noma esigwinya.
P12	Kodwa bahambisana sebeya ekhaya bebukeka bejabule, bexoxa ngomdlalo wabo.
