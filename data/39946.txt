desc:	A fascinating story about a maasai boy who is brave and saves his village and people

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time at the Maasailand, a brave boy was born .
P2	He was  named Oluyet  and  he was  very strong and brave.
P3	Oluyet's parents loved him very much .They wanted their son to be happy .
P4	One day while he was with his parents , the baby got lost into the forest.
P5	Oluyet's parents were so worried .They had searched everywhere for him .
P6	Lucky enough , he was found and he was now feeding on his mother's milk
P7	Years went by, and Oluyet grew older.His sister Kirio took good care of him
P8	When Oluyet grew up , he became a brave warrior and helped all people there
P9	All the people lived happily . The brave Oluyet would always help them out.
P10	The people were so proud of The brave Oluyet They lived happily ever after.
