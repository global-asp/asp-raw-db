desc:	Omulima yali nende Isurusi ingofu nende Isishiri.

page_text:

P1	Omulima yali nende Isurusi ingofu.
P2	Khandi yali nende Isishiri.

Isurusi nende Isishiri bali abetsa.
P3	Nibwakhera, Isurusi yabolera Isishiri, "Nyangayino
enchonyire muno."
P4	Inyanga yalondakho, Isurusi yeerotsakana khandi, "Lero sheuluushirekho ta."
P5	Isishiri yakalusia, "Emilimo chianje chibeere emisiro okhushira echichio."
P6	Nebusherenge, khandi Isurusi yabola, "Lero ibere inyanga imbi khwisie."
P7	Isishiri yakalusia, "Omulima nakalukha, omulilire, "Moo! Moo!"
P8	Isurusi yalilira omuruchi, "Moo! Moo!"
Omuruchi yachilekha auluushe.
P9	Omulima yaboha Isishiri khumbako.

Isishiri yalima khulwa Isurusi.
P10	Isishiri yakhola emirimo inyanga yosi.

Nebwirirenge yali ichonyere muno.
P11	Isishiri yabola, "Omulima alakhusinza. Ochonyanga lwangu khandi olwala."
P12	Isurusi yakalusia Isishiri, "Mutsuli ndalakhola 
emilimo chianje."
