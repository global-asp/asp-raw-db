page_text:

P1	Akasuba kalibalika sana mu mpanga mushinta yalusuba.
P2	Akanyelele kali necilaka panshita iitali.
P3	Kalekabila notumenshi utwingatona kwibila.
P4	Nomune nao takwali.
P5	Akanyelele katile, "Nga nshasange amenshi, nalafwa." "Ka ndeyafwaya umumana."
P6	Kote aebele akanyelele ati, "Umumana nawisula na mabimbi yaleima kuti yakusenda."
P7	Ine nga nshawe amenshi nalafwa ndi necilaka sana. Nkafwayefye umumana.
P8	Kapitile mufisakasaka namu minsanse iyauma.
P9	Mukwenda kaumfwile uko umumana. Ukalamba ulepoma! Wali mumana na mabimbi.
P10	Akanyelele kanwene amenshi mukwangufyanga panshita ii tali.
P11	Kunsansa ukufulisha takamwene uko amabimbi yaimine ilyo kalenwa amenshi.
P12	Akanyelele kalyeseshe ukwikatinula kucani lelo. Kalisendelwe na menshi.
P13	"Mutule!" "Mutule!"
P14	Iilyo inkundo yaishile no musambo kukanwa yaebele akanyelele aiti, "Angufyanya nina kukamusambo."
P15	Panuma yakukapususha, akanyelele katile "Nalakala panopene mpaka ikunda ikabwele inkese tasha."
P16	Bushikubumo kwaishile abalumendo babili na malegeni.
P17	Umo pabalumendo atile, "Pamumana uyu palesa inkunda. Twalalunga tobela pa cakulya ca cungulo."
P18	Akanyelele katontonkenye ifyo, "kenga pususha inkunda nelyo kaba nomubili uunono.
P19	Mukashita ananono Inkunda yalishile mukunwa a menshi.
P20	Akanyelele kali namapange yakupususha Inkunda.
P21	Icho katolokele pamulumendo nokumusuma palukasa icabipisha.
P22	Umulumendo apundile ati, "Mayo! Mayo!" Nafwa ine.
P23	Inkunda yaufwile icongo nokupupuka.
P24	Efyo akanyelele katashishe Inkunda pakukapususha.
