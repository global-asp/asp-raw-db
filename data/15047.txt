title:	Hare and Lion
author:	Christine Akello
artist:	Meghan Judge
lang:	English
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15047.png
desc:	The story talks about how Lion tricked other animals to go to his so that he would eat them.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,  there lived Hare and Lion. They were great friends. One day Lion fell sick and Hare decided to visit him.
P2	When Hare reached Lion's house,  he Lion did not open the door. He knocked again but Lion did not open.
P3	Hare decided to kick the door and the door broke open. Hare found Lion Laying on bed.
P4	"My friend Lion are you okay?" asked Hare. "I am not well" replied Lion.
P5	Hare noticed a pair of horns on the floor and on looking at Lion again Hare noticed some blood.
P6	"Why do you have blood around your mouth and whose horns are this? Asked Hare. Lion was quiet for sometime."
P7	"Lion,  have you eaten the Antelope? Asked the Hare. "Get out of my house or I will do the same to you" roared the Lion."
P8	Hare jumped out of the house and ran away.
