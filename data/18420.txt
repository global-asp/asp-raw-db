title:	Two Little Friends
author:	Belainesh Woubishet
artist:	Jesse Pietersen
lang:	English

desc:	A traditional Ethiopian tale about two young friends who meet and afterwards lose their friendship because of their differences.

page_text:

P1	There was Little Goat and Little Leopard. They became friends.
P2	They went to a field together to play.
P3	They returned home very late.
P4	Little Goat said to his mama,  "I met a beautiful friend. I am so happy. We will meet again tomorrow."
P5	Mama Goat answered,  "Oh my child. I am glad you are back safely. He is not our friend. Do not meet him tomorrow."
P6	At the same time,  Little Leopard told his mother,  "I met a beautiful friend. I am happy. We will meet tomorrow."
P7	Mama Leopard answered,  "Oh my son,  I am sorry you did not eat Little Goat. When you meet him tomorrow,  kill him and eat him."
P8	The next day,  Little Leopard went to the Little Goat's village.
P9	Little Goat was not outside. He was hiding inside with his mother.
P10	Little Leopard shouted,  "Let us go to the field to play."
P11	Little Goat said,  "We are not friends anymore. I listen to my mama. Good children listen to their mamas."
P12	Little Leopard was sad. He went back to his mama.
