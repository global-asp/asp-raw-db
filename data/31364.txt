title:	Inshuti
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31364.png
desc:	Iyi ni inkuru iyumuhungu ninshuti ze ndetse nimirimo bakunda gukora.

page_text:

P1	Nitwa Simoni.
P2	Mfite inshuti enye.
P3	Bitwa Kiza, Keza, Kaliza na Kazuba.
P4	Inshuti yange Kiza akunda gukina umupira.
P5	Inshuti yange Keza akunda koga.
P6	Inshuti yange Kaliza akunda gukina umukino wo kwihishana no guhishurana.
P7	Inshuti yange Kazuba akunda gusoma.
P8	Muzi ngewe? Nkunda gukora ibintu byose bakunda.
P9	Nkina umupira na Kiza.
P10	Nkunda kogana na Keza.
P11	Nkunda gukina umukino wo kwihishana no guhishurana na Kaliza.
P12	Umva nshuti, ukunda iki?
P13	Ngwino dukinane umupira.
P14	Ngwino twogane.
P15	Ngwino twihishane,tunahishurane.
P16	Ngwino dusomere hamwe!
