P1	"Grandma, Grandma! There's a Fairy!"I said.
P2	"I'm the Location Fairy." It said.
P3	"Stay in that position." The Location Fairy said.
P4	"Ah!"Grandma shouted.
P5	"What is this about!?" Grandma said.
P6	"Please stay in that position." The Fairy said.
P7	"Stop it!" The Location Fairy said.
P8	"I'm showing my anger!" The Fairy said.
P9	"Everything goes smoothly, but..."Said the Fairy,
P10	"Thank god." Grandma said.
