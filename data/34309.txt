title:	Ṅwananyana  a no funeswa
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Tshivenḓa

desc:	Kukali kune Natabo a ku funesa kwo kwashea

page_text:

P1	Natabo u na khaladzi dza rathi.
P2	Vhabebi vhawe vho mu fha kukali. U ku funa nga maanḓa.
P3	Ḽiṅwe ḓuvha khaladzi dzawe dza kwasha kukali kwawe.
P4	Natabo a ṱoḓa kukali kwawe hoṱhehoṱhe a si ku wane. @@A lila a sa fhumuli.
P5	Natabo a shavha afha hayani. @@A gonya muri mulapfulapfu.
P6	Vhabebi vhawe vha mu wana. "Itsani wee Natabo," vha mu humbela.
P7	Khaladzi dzawe dza thoma u imba, "Itsani wee Natabo, itsani."
P8	Natabo a nyefula luimbo lwavho.
P9	Hambo ḓi swika khonani ya Natabo. @@"Itsani wee Natabo, itsani," ndi ene a tshi imba.
P10	Khonani ya Natabo ya imba u swika Natabo a tshi tsa murini.
P11	Idzi khonani mbili dza tshimbila dzoṱhe dzi tshi humela hayani.
P12	Vhathu vhoṱhe vha pembelela u vhuya hayani ha Natabo. @@Vha mu fha kukali kuswa!
