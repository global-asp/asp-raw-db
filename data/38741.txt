P3	My name is titi, and I come from a very long line of Taiwo's my brother's name is Bayo while my sister's name is Pamela. She was the only child in our family that was not given a tribal name. To go further I am 13 years old and I am the youngest
P4	Even though I am the youngest I Also don't get that special treatment that younger children get, my parents love me but are very strict they try to protect me.My family is not poor neither are we rich we are just a middle class family.
P5	I attend Asumbo government secondary school at Onitsha, my friends names are Tayo, Bolu and Kehinde. Kehinde was a twin but her brother, had traveled for a better school at Abuja. My school is the best some might say it is not fit because it
P6	is a government school, because they think private schools are better than government school but I object because private school is still the same thing with public school it's just the standard that's all. My dad even wanted to put me in a
P7	private but I insisted on staying in the government school because it's free of charge.And also give good education. Thats by the way,One day while coming back from an errand a group of girls came up to me and started beating me for reasons
P8	which I did not know, I instantly recognized on of them she was the group leader her name was Toyin she was a very notorious girl at school, rumour has it that she was expelled from her former school for causing a disaster amongst staffs.
