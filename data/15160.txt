page_text:

P1	Ball.
P2	My ball.
P3	My red ball.
P4	My big red ball.
P5	I kick.
P6	I kick my ball.
P7	I kick my red ball.
P8	I kick my red ball hard!
P9	Where?
P10	Where is my ball?
P11	Where is my ball now?
P12	Where is my red ball now?
P13	It is up.
P14	It is high up.
P15	It is high up in the sky.
P16	It is high up in the sky. It is over the moon. It is gone!
