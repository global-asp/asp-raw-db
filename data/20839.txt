page_text:

P1	Kubonabona nichokimwe  kubintu byose bibaho kuliyisi. Imbwa hamwe nimiselebaba nibimwe kubintu bibaga musi. Imbwa hamwe nimiselebaba byalakundanaga. Ubwo byalibili ku ganila kubibazo, umuselebaba gwalavuze ngo gubonabonaga gusumba imbwa. Nuko imbwa ilasubiza ngo mushuti wanje ntabwo uzi nkuko mbonabonaga ndigushaka ibyokudya. Nuko umuselebaba gulasubiza ngo buli aho nchaka kudya isazi, zilasimbukakaga. Ubuzima bwanje bulakomey
P2	Ubwo balibali kudya umuselebaba gwagumye hejuru kugisika. Abantu kobatangiye kulya imbwa yalegeye. Balayikubise bayilukaho igende ilikwabila. Imbwa ntabwo yakirutse kule kubela ko yali ishonje ahubwo yalagarutse gusha ibidyo. Bayiteleye duke hasi kutaka. Ibi byose byabaye umuselebaba guli kule. Nuko mazima gulemela ngo imbwa ilababalaga.
P3	Niyo mpanvu umuselebaba gubonye imbwa  guzunguzaga umutwe nuko umuselebaba gulavuga guti koko ntawakunda byose. Utazigela ukuza ibibazo byawe kubelako abulikubwila balabashika kuba bafite ibisumba ibyawe bakaguseka.
