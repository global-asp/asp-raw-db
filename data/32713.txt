title:	Umukororombya iwacu
author:	Jean-François Rubanda

desc:	Umuhungu yatemberaga mu mirima arabukwa umukororombya bwa mbere kuva yabaho. Ntiyari azi umukorombya icyo ari cyo ariko yari ashimishijwe no kuwureba.

page_text:

P1	Karisa yiberaga iwabo mu cyaro.
P2	Umunsi umwe ku gicamunsi, arimo gusoroma amatunda abona ikintu kidasanzwe.
P3	Abona mu kirere imirongo yihese y'amabara anyuranye.
P4	Ajugunya imbuto yasoromye, ajya kubwira abaturanyi ibyo yabonye.
P5	Akigera mu rugo, asanga buri wese ahagaze imbere y'inzu ye, amaso yayahanze mu kirere.
P6	Karisa ati: "Mbega umwenda mwiza umanitse mu kirere! Gahigi ahita amusubiza ati: "Ntabwo ari umwenda! Ndibwira ko ahubwo hari umuntu wasize irangi mu kirere!"
P7	Bamwe bemeranyaga na Karisa ko ari umwenda. Abandi bagakeka ko Gahigi ari we ufite ukuri. Amatsinda yombi ntiyabyumvikanaho.
P8	Ni bwo hazaga umugabo uturutse kure arababwira ati: "Mwese muribeshya."
P9	Basakuriza icyarimwe bati: "None ni ibiki?"
P10	Uwo mugabo arabasubiza ati: "Ni umukororombya! Ese ni ubwa mbere muwubonye?"
P11	Bose bahanga amaso uwo munyamahanga bati: "Ni iki u-mu-ko-ro-ro-mbya?"
P12	Gahigi arabaza ati: "Ese ni uwacu? Dushobora kuwugumana?" Ariko uwo mugabo yari amaze kwigendera.
