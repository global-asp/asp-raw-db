page_text:

P1	Mr and Mrs Smart have been married for 3 years but have no child.
P2	Friends and relatives advised Mr Smart to remarry and send his wife parking.@Mr Smart refused.He believes his wife will bear him a child  some day.
P3	Patiently, Mr Smart waited and Alas! After some years, his wife became pregnant.
P4	Mr and Mrs Smart were so happy that they hosted their family and friends to a feast.@They wished Mrs Smart safe delivery.
