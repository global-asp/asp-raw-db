desc:	Tshwene e baleha ka baesekele ya Seipati.

page_text:

P1	Seipati le moena wa hae ba itokisedise ho ya sekolong.
P2	Seipati o palamisa moena wa hae baesekeleng ya hae.
P3	Metswalle ya bona e a ba thabela ha ba kena sekolong.
P4	Ha ba ntse ba le ka phaposing, tshwene ya fihla ka ntle. Tshwene ya palama baesekele ya Seipati ya baleha ka yona.
P5	Bana ba lelekisa tshwene.
P6	Tshwene ya siya baesekele ka tlasa sefate.
P7	Tshwene ya sesetsa sehlekehlekeng, mahareng a letsha. Ya palama hodimo sefateng.
P8	Tshwene ya ba sadisa hantle! Bana ba phahamisa matsoho hore e tsamaye hantle. Seipati o na le baesekele ya hae hape.
