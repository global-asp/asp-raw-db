desc:	The animal kingdom is full of different animals that stay together.

page_text:

P1	It has elephant the biggest of them all.
P2	It also has Mr hare the cunning of them all.
P3	And monkey who was considered  lazy,he did not like going to the garden
P4	It had Mr frog who lived in the stream and croaked all the time.
P5	and the the Lion who was the king of the jungle.
P6	they all worked together domestic and wild.
P7	they were a family.
