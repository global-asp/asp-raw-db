page_text:

P1	Uyu ndiKiti.@@Uyu ndi Imbwa.
P2	Kiti na Imbwa vanotarisa nepahwindo.@@Vanotarisa nepahwindo.
P3	Kiti na Imbwa vanobva vaona shavishavi!
P4	Kiti na Imbwa vanoda kubata shavishavi.@@Kiti na Imbwa vanotevera shavishavi.@@Vanotevera shavishavi vachimhanya.
P5	Kiti na Imbwa vanomhanya vachitevera shavishavi. Vanofamba vachitevera shavishavi asi shavishavi rinobhururuka richimhanyisa. Shavishavi rinomhanyisa zvekuti Kiti na Imbwa vanotadza kuribata nekuti havafambisi.
P6	Kiti na Imbwa vanotora bhasikoro votevera. Vanotevera shavishavi.@@Asi shavishavi rinomhanyisa. Shavishavi rinobhururuka richimhanyisa. Kiti na Imbwa vanotadza kuribata nekuti havamhanyisi.
P7	Kiti na Imbwa vanotora motokari. Vanotevera shavishavi nemotokari.@@Asi shavishavi rinomhanyisa. Shavishavi rinobhururuka richimhanyisa. Kiti naImbwa vanotadza kuribata nekuti havamhanyisi.
P8	Kiti na Imbwa vanotevera shavishavi nechikepe. Vanoyerera pamusoro pemvura vachitevera shavishavi.@@Asi shavishavi rinomhanyisa. Shavishavi rinobhururuka richimhanyisisa. Kiti na Imbwa vanotadza kuribata nekuti havamhanyisi.
P9	Kiti na Imbwa vanotevera shavishavi ne ndege. Vanobhururuka vachitevera shavishavi as vanotadza kuribata. Shavishavi ririkumhanyisa. Ivo havamhanyisi.
P10	Kiti na Imbwa vanomira. Vanomira vogara pasi. Vanogara pasi pemuti.@@Vanogara pasi pezimuti.@@Asi...
P11	Vanoona shavishavi rimwe mumuti.@@Vanoona mashavishavi maviri mumuti.@@Vanoona mashavishavi matatu mumuti.
P12	Vanoona zana remashavishavi!
P13	Chisarai Kiti.@@Chisarai Imbwa.@@Chisarai mashavishavi.
