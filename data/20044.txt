title:	Motoho
author:	Tseliso Masolane
artist:	Abraham Muzee and Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)

desc:	Mme ha a yo mme Ntate o pheha motoho.

page_text:

P1	Zama o itokisetsa ho ya sekolong.
P2	Ntate o pheha motoho bakeng sa dijo tsa hoseng.
P3	"Mme o pheha motoho o monate haholo!" ho rialo Zama.
P4	Motoho o fa Zama matla sekolong.
P5	Motoho o se o lokile.
P6	Ntate o tshela lebese, ka lerato.
P7	"Ntate, motoho o hloka tswekere hape," ha rialo Zama.
P8	"Motoho wa Mme o latsweha ho feta," Zama a nahana.
P9	"Ntate, o ka ntshella tswekere hape?" ho botsa Zama.
P10	Ntate o fa Zama tswekere hape.
P11	"Jo! Ntate! O tshetse letswai motohong wa ka!"
P12	"Ntate, Mme o tlo fihla neng hae?" ho botsa Zama.
