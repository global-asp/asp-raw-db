page_text:

P1	This is my dog. His name is Bobby. @@He is a hunting dog.
P2	Bobby likes to hunt small animals. @@He also likes to play in the long grass.
P3	One Saturday, Bobby went out alone to hunt. @@He saw a rabbit on a rock. @@Bobby's mouth started watering.
P4	The rabbit saw Bobby looking at him.@@The rabbit ran away very fast.
P5	Bobby ran after the rabbit.@@He could only think about eating rabbit.@@He did not see a big hole ahead.
P6	Bobby fell into the deep hole.@@The rabbit ran around the hole and kept running.
P7	Luckily, two boys heard Bobby barking, "Woof! Woof! woof!" @@They ran towards the big hole to help him.
P8	"He is too heavy!" cried the boys!@@But they managed to pull Bobby from the hole. @@He was a very happy dog!
