P2	Ndi wa myaha etanu ejobuhulu era somera hu Kaguja Education Center.
P3	Baasaaye mu mwesi gwahudatu.
P4	Bano jna bameeri bange.
P5	Ebiryo byange ebinyumirwa Marafu, amagii ko'nomugati.
P6	Nyumirwa nyoo ohulela omwana.
P7	Sobola ohulabirira ebisolo.
P8	Enamba y'esimu yange 0752-342658.
