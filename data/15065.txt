title:	Nàʼ Wo Wù Kér Loŋ E Mòʼón
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15065.png
desc:	Wò-oò ghàn ne yèn nàʼ wo wù wáa ker loŋ e mòʼón

page_text:

P1	Heeee! Nàʼ wùmòʼ dzə̀ fó laʼ,  a wù bòŋ feyì.
P2	Wù yòʼ kér kún,  yii bèy,  a wù kér loŋ,  e mòʼón cáʼ. Wù kiʼ fo si bèn e juŋ.
P3	Wù fàŋ e kfə̀n a ker bvəm ye faŋèné.
P4	Dzə nàʼ,  a wù kér vitaáví é ŋaŋ.
P5	Gày və koŋ nàʼ və̀n e ŋaŋ. Wù lànér nàʼ və̀n,  wù kú a yu a dzə̀ jí rə cáʼ cáʼ.
P6	When the herd go to the fields to graze,  this cow eats alone.
P7	This cow is a great fighter,  so the other cows leave it alone. Nàʼ vən funèn e ŋaŋ,  biʼ bey,  kibér ki lo,  ki nèn a bàʼàr wù wàa ghan wùn mòʼon.
P8	Wíri kì nàʼ və̀n kijuŋ. Á lo,  á wàa fàn wun,  biʼ fo a dzə̀ wù kù fùnèn e loŋ mòʼón bèy. A kí kòóy ji vèn kfəə́ nàʼ woo bey taŕnén,  a dzə à yèele?
