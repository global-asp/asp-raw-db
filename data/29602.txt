desc:	Ndhuuyu yayanhiriza mukagwa ghe Nkembo okuzaanha aye ebintu tibyatambula bulungi.

page_text:

P1	Agho olwatuuka, Ndhuuyu ni Nkembo baali bakagwa. Lunaku lulala, mu kiseera ekye'ndhala, Ndhuuyu yayanhiriza Nkembo okuzaanha.
P2	Ndhuuyu yaakoba, ''Leka tuzaanhe okwefumba beenenabeene!'' Nkembo yaabuuza, ''Ekyo tusobola tutya okukikola?''
P3	Ndhuuyu yaabuuza, ''Okusooka tuli n'okukola omuliro.''
P4	Ndhuuyu yeyongera, ''Twidhuze esuwa amaadhi,nnhi era tugite kumuliro.
P5	''Nze ndha kusookamu.Bwenkuba kukisembo ekye'nsuwa, oidha ku giigula. Mala nfulumemu.''Ndhuuyu ainhongola. Nkembo aikirizagania.
P6	Era, Ndhuuyu yasooka mu nsuwa.
P7	Amaadhi bwegatandhiika okubuguumirira, Ndhuuyu yakonkonha nnhi Nkembo yaigula ekisembo.
P8	Bwekyaali nga Nkembo nairaku, Ndhuuyu yaata eibaale einhenhe kukisembo ekye'nsuwa era nga no'kutyaamaku ngo'kwo kwo'taire
P9	Amaadhi bwegataandhiika okutogota, Nkembo yakonkonha, aye Ndhuuyu tiyaigulawo kisembo.
P10	Nkembo yafa, nnhi Ndhuuyu yaalya mukagwa ghe asinga.
