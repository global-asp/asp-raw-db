desc:	How do you cope with feeling worried and alone during the coronavirus pandemic?

page_text:

P1	They closed the schools, and sent us home.
P2	We cannot play with other children.
P3	I miss being with my friends.
P4	I miss going to school.
P5	Mother is worried about Uncle.@@He is sick in hospital.
P6	Our parents are worried about everything.
P7	Everyone is worried about getting sick from this virus.
P8	All we hear about is the coronavirus!@@I wish it would go away!
