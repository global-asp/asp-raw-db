desc:	Antakeda ngu sha cii mbayev sha u wasen ve henen a baa caa. Kpa a doo u mbamalen a wase ve sha u ôron akaa a lu keregh la tsembelee tsô vea lu masen henen u ôron ayol a ve. M such ne cii.

page_text:

P1	A.@Alôm.@Ter wase yam alôm kende ken gyar.@Alôm nyoho inyam kpenkpen.
P2	C.@Cootom.@Nyian iyange i Cootom.@Se de Sesooter ngu ôôn iyol na.
P3	B@Bua.@Bua ye toho.@Bua wase ya toho kehe doo kenger kpishi.
P4	D@Dwem.@Dwem va hen ya wase tugh.@Dwem la va kôr se ikyegh yem a mi.
P5	E@Engem.@Iyange due sha tembe nyian.@Iyange la ta engem dedoo.
P6	F@Fele.@Shater za yam abum sha tembe.@Shater yevese fele za yam abum.
P7	G.@Gafa.@Gafa ka inyon i zegenan je.@Gafa va kôr se ivyo yem a mi.
P8	I.@Ihyambe.@Ihyambe ka kwagh u numben a mi ga.@Ihyambe ka i tâ i nyoon tsembelee je.
P9	H.@Hum.@Hum ngee aa akondo kpishi.@Hum ka a gema ikyondo shie u a soo cii.
P10	J.@Jijingi.@Iyange tâ yô jijingi due.@Sesughter tile yô a nenge a jijingi na.
P11	L.@Lyaku.@Lyaku ka anyon u cuku.@Terungwa kôr lyaku na Sengohol.
P12	M.@Muemue.@Muemue ka ankwagh u purugh tugh.@Muemue ka a tsaren usu shi a pirin.
P13	K.@Kokombo.@Kokombo ngu purugh ken tembe.@Vershima kôr kokombo na Shater.
P14	N.@Normnger.@Normnger ka zege inyam.@Normnger ka inyam i shin zegemger.
P15	S.@Sôôrogh.@Sôôrogh ka ku sôô i nyoon dedoo.@Ka ankwagh u numben a mi ga cii.
P16	P.@Pandegh.@Nyinya tume Torkuma pandegh.@De numben a pendegh ku nyinya ga.
P17	R.@Rumun.@Sedoo ngu numben vea Msuurshima.@Msuurshima rumun u nan Sedoo wan er a kôr.
P18	O.@Ortyom.@Ortyom ve a loho hen avese.@Ortyom va kaa Maria loho u Aôndo.
P19	T.@Tsoghor.@Tsoghor ka inyon i yan ikyegh.@Tsoghor va kôr se wankyegh yem a mi.
P20	U.@Uagh.@Uagh kar se shin kungur.@Msughter man Sewuese mba peren uagh.
P21	V.@Vandegh.@Ka vandegh agh je ne.@Oryiman ver wegh sha vandegh nagh.
P22	W.@Waarawa.@Aôndokume ngu numben shin waarawa.@Waarawa ne ngu shin kpe ifi.
P23	Y.@Yevese.@Iyese ka i tâ i nyoon tsembelee je.@Nahan, we a nenge a iyese yô, yevese.
P24	Z.@Zamber.@Toryila ngu zamber Mngohol.@Ngu zamber er a de un a kwaghbo na.
