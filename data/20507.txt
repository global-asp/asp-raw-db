desc:	Labarin wata saniyar da ake kauna a wani gida.

page_text:

P1	Wannan shi ne Ndalo
P2	Ndalo na son karatun litattafai
P3	Wannan saniya ita ce Pendo
P4	Pendo tana son cin karas
P5	Ndalo na kai Pendo kiwo da ya taso makaranta
P6	Ya kan shayar da Pendo ruwa a cikin tsabtattaccen kwami
P7	Baba kan zuba wa Pendo hatsi a daro ta ci
P8	Ya kan tatsi madaran Pendo kullum
P9	Pendo na samar da madara bokitai da yawa
P10	Baba kan kai madaran Pendo a shagon Maziwa Hapa a sayar
P11	Ndalo na jin dadin shan madara sabon tatsa kullum
P12	Cikin murmushi, ya kan dáfa ta ya ce “Na gode, Pendo!”
