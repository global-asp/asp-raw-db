desc:	Wali owughulirizaaku omubiri gwo kye gukukoba.

page_text:

P1	Olwa leero luno tili kuja kwisomero. Lwa kughumuula!
P2	Leero tiliija kubona telefaina. Kuzira masanyalaze aye era.
P3	Aye ndikuja kolaki? Olwaleero ndi kuja kuwulirisa mubiri gwange!
P4	Mukusooka, ndina okusirika. Awo nsobole owulirisa omubiri gwange.
P5	Nekyo,,buti nsobola okuwulirisa omuka gwange.
Mpeera bwendisa bwenfulumya, munda ni kuluya.
P6	Nsobola okugherera ghangulu. Ssssssssssss! Ni ghansi. Mmmmmmm.
P7	Buti nsobola kughurira omutima gwange bwegukuba! Puuu, puuuu, puuuuu.
P8	Nnhongezemuuku omutima gwange okukuba amangu oba einho? Nekyo, nga mbuka ghaigulu bwendhika emirundi amakumi abiri.
P9	Buti bona, omutima gwange guli kuba inho.
P10	Bwenta engalo dhange ku nhingo yange eyo mukono, agho nsobola oghulira nga gukuba.
P11	Nsobola oghulira okuseka kwange. Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Nsobola okweghulira nga ndhi kulira. Boohoooo hooo!
P13	Ndhi kughulira nga nkuba mu ngalo. nkuba nkuba nkuba!
P14	Ndhighulira amabundha gange gabutuka butukuka! Guddu, guddu, guddu.
P15	Amabundha gange gali kwogera, "mpe emere.
P16	Enhindho yange eli kughunhiza emigaati maama gyali kufumba mu jokooni. Era buti ndhi kwendha mpulire emba dhange nga dhigaaya emigaati egyo.
