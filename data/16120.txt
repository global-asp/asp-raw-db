desc:	Norah loves her doll and is worried one day when she thinks the doll is lost.

page_text:

P1	This is Norah. Norah's doll is called Sellah.
P2	Norah loves Sellah very much.  She plays and sleeps with Sellah every night.
P3	One day,  Norah was very worried because she could not find Sellah.
P4	Norah's mother had washed Sellah.
P5	Norah went to her mother crying,  "I can't find Sellah."
P6	Before Norah slept,  her mother brought Sellah to her.
P7	Norah jumped out of bed and danced with Sellah.
P8	Norah hugged her mother and thanked her for cleaning Sellah.
