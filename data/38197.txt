P1	Ibrahim won the quiz and got a certificate to study abroad. Amina was jealous and sad she remembered how she treated Ibrahim.
P2	Amina started to learn and help people and now everyone liked her. She missed her friend
P3	One day she got a surprise visit from her friend. She was so happy and apologized to him he was also happy.
P4	They all had a party at Amina's house to celebrate their friendship and they all lived happily ever after.
