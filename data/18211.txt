title:	Pendo, De Nao Kom
author:	Joseph Kandwe (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Kasem
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18211.png
desc:	Na-swono kolo na wo sensɔla kanto wone to.

page_text:

P1	Ndalo mo tento.
P2	Ndalo lage se o taa gare twaano mo.
P3	Pendo mo tento.
P4	Pendo lage se o taa done kaarota mo.
P5	Ndalo pa se Pendo di gao mo o na nwoŋi sikuuli.
P6	O pae Pendo na-ŋona se o nyɔ.
P7	O kwo pae mina mo Pendo.
P8	O kane Pendo yela mo dɛmaama.
P9	Pendo jege nayela dedɛ.
P10	O kwo yage Pendo nanyela bam.
P11	Ndalo nyɔ nanyel-kapwoŋa mo dɛmaama.
P12	O we,  "De n lei Pendo!"
