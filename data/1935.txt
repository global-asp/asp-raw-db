title:	Ndixelele ... Ngoku! Imibala
author:	Ntombizodwa Gxowa-Dlayedwa
artist:	Rijuta Ghate
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/1935.png
desc:	Sibuza ukuba kutheni izinto zinemibala eyahlukeneyo.

page_text:

P1	Kutheni na le nto isibhakabhaka siluhlaza?
P2	Asisoloko siluhlaza. Kunjalo?
P3	Kutheni amagqabi eluhlaza nje?
P4	Akasoloko eluhlaza. Kunjalo?
P5	Kutheni na le nto iibhanana zimthubi?
P6	Libhanana ezivuthiweyo zimthubi. Iibhanana ezingavuthwana ziluhlaza.
P7	Kutheni na le nto izambreli zimnyama?
P8	Zonke izambreli azimnyamanga.
P9	Kutheni na le nto igazi libomvu?
P10	Kukho i-ayon egazini. Loo nto ilenza libe bomvu.
P11	Kutheni na le nto ityuwa imhlophe?
P12	Andazi!
