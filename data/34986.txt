title:	Upusi ni Nimbwa bojambula nu chekena
author:	Jane  Mulenga and Mary  Ng'ambi
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Chinkhonde
level:	Read aloud
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34986.png
desc:	Upusi ni Nimbwa bali pabumanyani. Bosekesha fiyo kangi bohoboka linga bobombela pampene ifindu fyopambana pambana.

page_text:

P1	Uyu yo Pusi.@@Iyi yo Nimbwa.
P2	Upusi ni Nimba bolonda pojambula.@@Upusi ni Nimbwa bolonda pocekena.@@Upusi ni Nimbwa bolonda kupanga fyojambula.@@Nimbwa yikwanda.@@Nimbwa yikwega mphesulo nu kwega pepala. Pepala lyofwana mbafu shosa mubutali.
P3	Pabwandilo Nimbwa yadindile Ovo.@@Ovo yombili wa pusi.@@Ehele, ichi chita chikafu.
P4	Kangi odinda matilayango mabili pamwanya pa Ovo.@@Amatilayango mabili sho mbulukutu sha Pusi.@@Kangi odinda itilayango ithitu mukati mwa Ovo.@@Itilayango inthitu yo mphuno ya Pusi kangi odinda umuzele pamwanya pa mphuno.
P5	Nosi ojambula maso ni fikumbi fya Pusi.@@Amaso to tumadoba tubili.@@Ifikumbi yo mizele mibili.@@Ehele, fitotamya.
P6	Nosi odinda mulomo wa Pusi.@@Akamuzele go mulomo.@@Ko fwana ni lemba lya "W" kangi odinda cheya cha Pusi.@@Ifyeya fitatu kulubafu lwa chimama ni fyeya fitatu kulubafu lwa chindyelo.
P7	Ichithuzi chilipapipi umalika.@@Odinda amaboko nu tulundi twa pusi.@@Amaboko nu tulundi yo mizele kangi ojambula inyobe sha malundi sha pusi.@@Ojambula sona inyobe shamalundi sha Pusi.@@ Ojambula sona inyobe sha malundi sha Pusi nu jambula uphyephye.@@Uphyephye kokamuzele katali.
P8	Pabumalilo odinda utumizele pa mbili wa Pusi kangi nuchekena fya orengi.@@Ichithuzi chimalike.@@Ehele, ichi cho chimogile!
P9	Apa kokabalilo ka Pusi.@@Pusi akwega phensulo nukwega pepala.@@Pepala lya lekitango.
P10	Pabwandilo Pusi odinda ovo.@@Ovo wo mbili wa Nimbwa.@@Ehele, cili kanunu!
P11	Kangi odinda fifwani fibili pa mwanya pa ovo.@@Efifwani fititu fibili sho mbulukutu sha Nimbwa kangi odinda aka seko katitu mukati mwa ovo.@@Aka seko akatitu yo mphuno yanimbwa kangi odinda umuzele pamwanya pa mphuno.
P12	Nosi odinda a maso ni fikumbi fya Nimbwa.@@Amaso to tumadoba tubili.@@Ifikumbi yo mizele mibili.@@Ehele, fili kanunu fiyo!
P13	Nosi odinda mulomo wa Nimbwa.@@Akamuzele go mulomo.@@Kangi ofwana ni lemba "W" sona odinda tumadoba tosyungulila mulomo wa Nimbwa.@@Tutatu kuchiboko chimama kangi tutatu kuchiboko chindyelo.
P14	Ichithuzi cili kubumalilo.@@Odinda amaboko nu tulundi twa Nimbwa.@@Amaboko nu tulundi yo mizele  kangi odinda inyobe sha m'malundi sha Nimbwa.@@Nyobe shamalundi to tumaseko nosi odinda umphyephye wa Nimbwa.@@Umphyephye wufwana ni luba.
P15	Pabumalilo odinda utuma seko ututitu pa mbili wa Nimbwa kangi ochekena.@@Ichinthuzi chimalike.@@Ehele, filikanunu!
P16	Tukwagana Pusi.@@Tukwagana cithuzi ca Pusi.@@Tukwagana Nimbwa.@@Tukwagana cithuzi ca Nimbwa.
