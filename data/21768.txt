page_text:

P1	Oludaalo olusooka  musomero lwalilugosi eri Ana. Ana gatiina ohutandiiha ohusoma mu pulayimale endaayi n’atasomangaho  wayire mu nasale. Olw’ehyo hiisi hiitu musomero hyamubeera  hinyaaha.
P2	Ehyali ehiraŋi ti gatiina ni mboojo  owapulayimale  ey’ohubiri. Aye era gasiiba mu hweŋunya ebyali   nibiholewa.
Gabona  abaana nibasagula abandi nibangala ebisasiro. Aba
P3	Aye  ehyamweŋunyisa nj’ehide  ohuhubba  abaana   bosibosi baduluma embiro  nibehumbania mu hifo hirala. Yeesi mboojo gamuŋira   ohuja   ohweyanja.
Ni bahena  ohweyanja   ngabasaba   ng’aŋo   omusomesa  aloma  eyibali. Nigahena ng’abaloma ohuja ohwingira mu bibiina.
P4	Mboojo gamuŋira  mu hisenge  omua  bapulayimale  endaayi  basomera ng’amuleha  omwo. Omusomesa gasigalaŋo  n’alinj’amuŋoyaŋoya ohwosa  oluganala abahye.
Ana nindi gabonaho  mugandawe muhuŋumula  husaawa ene. Gaalininaye  ohwosa olubengira. Nindi gamuŋira  buŋire mu hisenge nago  omuabapulayimale  endaayi basomera.
