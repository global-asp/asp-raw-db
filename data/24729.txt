page_text:

P1	Once upon time, there was Hare and Tortoise. They were good friends.
P2	They met to play every day. Hare always boasted that she could run.
P3	They decided to have a race.
P4	They choose a starting and finishing point.the hare run really fast
P5	soon left tortose far behind it thought it is too slow and left rest.
P6	for while so he stoped under tree and went to sleep mean while the tortose
P7	kept on moving on walking the while time and reached the the winning point.
P8	when the hare wakeup,he saw that tortose had already won the race.
P9	The hare said that this tortose was slow but sure.
