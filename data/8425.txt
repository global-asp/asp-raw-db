desc:	This is a story about a group of young people who enjoy playing soccer. Their coach has a big surprise for the team!

page_text:

P1	Kato alina akapiira. Nalule alina akapiira.
P2	Abaana bali mu kisaawe. Abaan balina omutendesi.
P3	Omutendesi abatendeka okuzannya akapiira.
P4	Omutendesi abwaebiragiro.
P5	Kato asiibula omutendesi. Nalule asiibula omutendesi.
P6	Bonna badda eka.
P7	Omukulu w'essomero abaleetera ebyambalo.
P8	Omutendesi abgabira ebyambalo. Abaana bonna basanyuka.
