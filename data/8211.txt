title:	Indlovu Eyayithanda Izinto
author:	Zimbili Dlamini
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8211.png
desc:	Inkonyane lendlovu elalilangazelele ukwazi ngempilo yezinye izilwane elaliphila nazo. Kodwa kwathi ngenye imini igwababa elihlakaniphile lathi liyolikhombisa ukuthi ingwenya idlani ngokudla kwakusihlwa. Kulapho yathola izimpendulo ezahlukene.

page_text:

P1	Siyazi ukuthi indlovu inomboko omude.
P2	Emandulo umboko wendlovu wawumfishane.  Wawubukeka kabi.
P3	Kwazalwa inkonyane eyayithanda izinto. Yayihlala inombuzo ngezinye izilwane.
P4	Yahlangana nendlulamithi. ''Kungani  unentamo ende?''.
P5	Ngakusasa yahlangana nobhejane. ''Kungani unophondo olucijile?''.
P6	Yaphinde yahlangana  nemvubu. ''Kungani unamehlo abomvu?''.
P7	Ngoba yayithanda izinto,  yafuna ukwazi ngengwenya. Ngabe ingwenya idlani ngesidlo sakusihlwa?
P8	''Ungalokothi ubuze umbuzo onjalo!'' kusho umama wayo. Yahamba inyukubele.
P9	Ngokushesha igwababa landizela phansi,  lahlala eduze kwendlovu. Lathi,  "Ngilandele siye emfuleni,  uzobona-ke ukuthi ingwenya idlani kusihlwa."
P10	Nangempela indlovu yalandela igwababa.
P11	Indlovu yazimpintsha  yangena emhlangeni yama osebeni lomfula. Iphi ingwenya pho?
P12	''Sawubona," kusho itshe ngasosebeni lomfula. "Yebo," kuphendula indlovu. ''Ungangitshela ukuthi ingwenya idlani ngesidlo sakusihlwa?''."
P13	''Lalela-ke,  goba ngizokutshela," kuphendula itshe. ''Goba futhi,  futhi.'' kuqhubeka itshe. Nangempela indlovu yehlela phansi yaze."
P14	''Ngqi!'' Ikhala layo lase lise mhlathini wengwenya. ''Helele! Izodla wena ingwenya ngesidlo sakusihlwa!'' kutswininiza igwababa.
P15	Indlovu yazabalaza ngemilenze yayo yangemuva eqinile,  yadonsa,  yadonsa,  yadonsa. Ingwenya ayizange iyidedele.
P16	Ikhala lendlovu ladonseka,  ladonseka,  ladonseka. Dalakaxa! Yawa yashayeka ngomhlane odakeni.
P17	Tshobe ingwenya emanzini. "Ngaze ngaphuthelwa ukudla okumnandi!"
P18	Indlovu yabheka umboko wayo.  "Maye! Ikhala lami leli,  elide kangaka?"
P19	Lalilide kangangoba lalikwazi ukufinyelela emagatsheni aphezulu esihlahleni.
P20	Indlovu kwayisiza lokhu. Yayisikwazi ukuzigeza umhlane wayo ngamanzi uma ilanga lishisa.
P21	Kusukela ngalelo langa,  zonke izindlovu zinemiboko emide elusizo kuzona.
