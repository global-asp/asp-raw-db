desc:	Inja kaDennis entsha yashushuzela ebhuzini lomngane wakhe! (Indaba nemifanekiso kwaqanjwa engqungqutheleni yokufundisana eyayigqugquzelwe abasebenzi bezengqondo baseSophiatown eGoli ngamaholide kaJuni 2019).

page_text:

P1	Ngibizwe umngane wami wakwamakhelwane.@@"Woza uzobona inja yami," kusho uDennis.
P2	Inja yayinamabala amnyama nekhanda elikhulu.@@Yangisabisa!@@Ngakhathazeka ngicabanga ukuthi izongiluma.
P3	SinoDennis sayithatha inja sazulazula nayo.
P4	Yayihamba itshikizisa umsila.
P5	Yakhonkotha!
P6	Yathi isuka yayingishushuzela!@@Phakathi ebhuzini lami!@@Hawu!
P7	UDennis wathatha ithishu wasula umshushuzo.
P8	Ngabona ukuthi uDennis ungumngane wami weqiniso.
