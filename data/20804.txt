title:	Eihuri Aha Bwenjugano

desc:	Ekitebyo ekyi nikyikwata aha mushaija muto nana eihuri ryomugasho

page_text:

P1	Rikaba riri rimwe, hakaba hariho omukaikuru owabeire nakunda omwijukuru wobwojo munonga. Akamuhereza eihuri eri nka ekirabo. Yagyira,"Okuruga omwihuri eri nooza kufuna enjugano yobushwere bwawe emyaka kwerahike." nabwanyima omwojo  yaaza omumihanda ye.
P2	Okubanza akabugana ababeire nibatoratoora emizabibu abaamuburize aheihuri. Bakarikozesa okurekyera aha mizabibu ryayatika.
P3	Omwojo akarira, EiEiEiEi! Mwandetera obuzibu. Eihuri ryaba ritari ryangye. ryaba riri erya nyakwenkuru. Kandi nyakwenkuru tara rimperire busha. Akarimpa ngu nfune enjugano.
P4	Aho naho ababeire nibatoratoora emizabibu bakamuza ekiti ekyibabeire nibakozesa kucwa emizabibu.  Omwojo yatamuriraho.
P5	Nikwe omwojo yabugana abombekyi bamushaba ekiti. Baakyikozesa kwombeka enju nikwe kyahendekamu ebicweka.
P6	Omwojo akarira, HeeHeeHee! Mwandetera ebizibu! Ekiti ekyo kyaaba kyitari kyangye, Kyaaba kiri ekyaba toratozi bemizabibu.Kandi tiba kyimpeera busha. Bakyimpa omumwanya gweihuri kandi neihuri ryaaba ritari ryangye. Ryaaba rir erya nyakwenkuru. Kandi nyakwenkuru tararimperire busha, akarimpa ngu nfune enjugano.
P7	Nabwanyima abombekyi baamuha ekyizingo kyebinyatsi. Nikwe omwojo yayeyonjerayo.
P8	Nikwe omwojo ashanga omuriisa ayamushabire ebinyatsi.  Omuriisa akabiha ente nikwe etyo ebirya byoona.
P9	Omwojo akarira, mwandetera obuzibu Ebinyatsi byaba bitari byangye. byaba biri ebyabombekyi. abombekyi tibabimpeera busha. bahenda akati kangye. akati kaaba kari akaba toratoozi bemizabibu. Nabo bakampa omumwanya gweihuri. ryaba riri erya nyakwenkuru. Akarimpa ngu nfune enjugano
P10	Atyo omuriisa ahereza omwojo ente. Omwojo yaye yongyerayo.
P11	Kuyabeire ari omumuhanda naaza omuka, omwojo akabugana embaga yobushwere. Abeeka yomugore bakamushaba ente atyo agyibaha. Baagibaaga batyo bagyirya yoona.
P12	Omwojo akarira. Mwandetera obuzibu. Ente yaaba etari yangye. yaaba eri eyomuriisa. yagyimpa omumwanya gwebinyatsi. Ebinyatsi byaba biri ebyabombekyi. babimpa bwanyima yokuhenda akati kangye Akati kaaba kari akaba toratoozi bemizabibu.Baakampa omumwanya  gweihuri. Ryaba riri erya nyakwenkuru. Akarimpa ngu nfune enjugano.
P13	Aho eka yomugore egyira ngu tiwasigaza kyintu kyoona ekyokukushashura. Kwonka  reka tukuhe omugore." Aho ekyagambirwe kyahikirira, Ekyikwetorora nikiheza kigaruka!"
