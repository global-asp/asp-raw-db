title:	Ngala Liche Liaonia Liusiu
author:	Margaret Amateshe
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Oluwanga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8274.png
desc:	Eshialo shumire na liche lititi lili nobulo. Litsia mumwalo okhunywa amatsi ne likholomokha na amatsi. Liusiu liwonia liche lititi. Liche Lititi likhupire lirie Liusiu orio?

page_text:

P1	Yali nyanga yeshimiu mushitsuru eshimiu.
P2	Liche liali nelibulile amatsi khutsinyanga tsinyinji.
P3	"Ndenya imburo ya matsi kata khurula khwisafu."
P4	Kata lime liali niliumire.
P5	"Nindalanywa tawe,  ndalafwa," Liche nelirira. "Ndalatsia mumwalo kwembuliranga."
P6	"Omwalo kulakhwishia," Eshituyu eshichesi neshibolera Liche.
P7	Liche liali nende obulo muno. "Ndalafwa nindalanywa amatsi tawe."
P8	Liche liatsia okhukhaba omwalo.
P9	Nelibura obunyasi obumu nende amasaka amomu.
P10	Khula lelianza okhuulira omwalo nekuwola.
P11	Liche lianywa imburo ikhongo ya matsi.
P12	Liasangala muno ne silialola lirunde likhongo nilitsa tawe.
P13	Liche liatema okhutira obunyasi obumu bwali nobubenganga khumatsi ne omwalo nokulikhomolosia.
P14	"Khonya,  bane khonya."
P15	"Ninakho lwangu," Liusiu neliboolera Liche nelilumire lisaka khumunwa.
P16	Liche liebashe liri,  "Sindalatsia neshiri okhupira Liusiu orio tawe. Ndalalinda okhula ngala liletsa okhunywa matsi."
P17	Inyanga ndala nilishilindanga,  abasiani babiri betsa mumwalo nende tsifandili.
P18	"Liliwo liusiu likhongo lia rachari litsanga okhunywa matsi ano," omusiani mulala naboola. "Khulalira angolobe."
P19	"Sindalafuchirira abasiani beere Liusiu lirachari tawe. Alali endi omutiti muno. Ndalakhola endie?" Liche liebara.
P20	Mufise efio,  Liusiu lirachari liayera okhurula khumusala khunywa matsi.
P21	Liche liali nende inganakani.
P22	Liaruchira khu shirenje shia musiani mulala neliluma efitinyu muno.
P23	Omusiani yaruka. "Uui!" Nakhupa oluungu.
P24	Liusiu liesundukha nelipurukha okhwionia.
P25	Endio no olwa Liche liakhupira Liusiu orio khulwa okhulionia.
