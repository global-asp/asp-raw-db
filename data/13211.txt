page_text:

P1	Kolong ang'orot,  ayei Ekokoroit ka Apoo. Arai kechi ng'ipaalon luminan.
P2	Atabong apei kilak Apoo Ekokoroit anawi keng. Kimuja kechi akimuj nakeebon aapei.
P3	Todol apak nalosiet akiper. Abu Apoo king'olik atamar ewoito ekokoroit ka akewi ng'akies eperete.
P4	Taanyung'a Apoo atamar eperete Ekokoroit ka akewi niajokon. Nyejotooro ng'esi kiiya kuwala.
P5	Ataparachu,  asaki Apoo akiyen ng'amunonoe Ekokoroit. "Aliwae iwanakinete eesi ng'akies nakwaare?" King'is ng'esi.
P6	Abu Ekokoroit tolimo aboliar ng'esi. Tolimok tama,  "Ekone,  kiyakar sua ng'ikieny ng'amunonoe elosio akiper. Eperio etuboritae ng'akies. Ejotooroi ejok emam ng'akies."
P7	"Ani nataparachu tobuakinae ng'akies nabo," kitatamia Ekokoroit.
P8	Tolot Apoo nawi kisisa ng'aumonoe ang'ikukui. Totiaku awi Apoo akiroo ng'una! Esibit iyong inyo abu tosubakin?
