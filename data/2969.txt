desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Sipho atsia khushiro nende omukhana wabu.
P2	Omukhana wabu atsiririra okhulinga tsingumbo mumaluka. Sipho achonyere muno.
P3	Sipho alutsi eshindu shiayachama.
P4	Efilaro fikusibwa.
P5	Yechera fiosi mufilenje fie.
P6	Omukhana wabu amukulira efilaro fiokhubaya omupira kwe filenje.
P7	Eshichira nibayuuka okhutsia nango nishi?
P8	Khulwe ikhabi,  yola mushikuri shiomupira kwefilenje mufise efilayi. Afwalire efilaro efiyiakha efio,  fiokhubaya omupira.
