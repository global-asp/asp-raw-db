desc:	Pakafaamu kanoono

page_text:

P1	Kale saana, pakafaamu akanoono mupeepi na kamushi akanoono.
P2	Paikele akakoko akanoono akeeshina lya Daisy.
P3	Daisy atiile, "Nganakula, ndefwaya nkapupuke mpaka nkafike muulu.
P4	Ukumfweefyo, inkoko shibiye shaalikasekele.
P5	Ashiti, "Waliibeela iwe. Tatwakulaangala noobe."
P6	Banyina nabo abati, "Iwe, Daisy, bonse kuti twapupusha amapindo, lelo teti tupupuke. Caalyafya."
P7	Daisy ena, taaleekele nakalya. Aaleesha ukupupuka inshiku shonse. Aaleepupusha amapindo, pupu, pupu, pupu, leelo ukutila  eeme afume pamushili, aalifililwe.
P8	Aaleelenganya ifyo engamoneka mulwelele pakupupuka uku aaleelolesha inkoko shibiye panshi neefyo engalapita utuuni utwapusana-pusana mumuulu. Ifyuuni kuti fileeti "Iyee! Inkoko iipupuka!
P9	Pupu, pupu, pupu, cila bushiku Daisy aleepupusha amapindo.
P10	Aaleti nga aaima ukufuma panshi, kabili, luntuuu! Apoona.
P11	"Nshakatale mpupukapo. Ifyo  inkoko shinandi shilanda fiishinka," Daisy aliliile kuli banyina.
P12	"Daisy, walipusana neenkoko shimbi. Inkonko shimbi ishi tashifwaya kupupuka, leelo iwe ulafwaya! Kuti wapupuka," e banyina.
P13	Ubushiku bwakonkelepo, Daisy aaninine pamuulu wa citele nookwesha ukupupuka. Aapupwishe amapindo, pupu!  Aapupukile mulwelele, aya aaleepupusha amapindo,  mpaka…
P14	Luntuuu!
P15	Inkoko shibiye shalisekele saana. "Ha ha ha! Twalikwebele! Inkoko teeti shipupuke!"
P16	Ubushiku ubwakokelepo, Daisy teewakuleka iyoo. Aaninine pamuulu saana ukucila, pamuulu wakayanda kakonko.  Pupu, pupu, Daisy aapupwishe amapindo.
P17	Aapupukiile mumwela nookupupusha amapindo, elyo aapupusha amapindo, nookupupusha amapindo elyo…
P18	Aakonkenyepo ukupupuka!  Umwela munshi yamapindo yakwe waalikuliileko, icalengele ukutila apupukishe. Ifyuuni fimbi fyatile, "Icisungusho! Inkoko ileepupuka!"
P19	Ukumoneefi, inkoko shimbi nasho shaleefwaya ukuba ngayena.
P20	Shatile, "Iyee, kwena Daisy uli wafisungusho!"
