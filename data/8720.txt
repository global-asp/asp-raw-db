title:	Omusajja Omuwanvu Ennyo
author:	Mwesigwa Joshua Waswa
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/8720.png
desc:	The man in this story is so tall that everything around him is too short or too small. He solves this problem by creating things to match his size.

page_text:

P1	Enkumbi yali nyiimpi nnyo.
P2	Omulyango gw'ali wansi nnyo.
P3	Ekitanda ky'ali kimpi nnyo.
P4	Egaali y'ali ntono nnyo.
P5	Omusajja y'ali muwanvu nnyo!
P6	Yakola omuyini gw'ekumbi omuwanvu.
P7	Yakola omulyango omuwanvu.
P8	Yakola ekitanda ekiwanvu ennyo.
P9	Yagula egaali empanvu ennyo.
P10	Yatuula nga ku ntebbe empanvu ennyo. Yalya nga n'ewuuma empanvu ennyo.
P11	Yaleka enyumba ye,  n'aberanga mu kibira ekinene. Yawangaala emyaka mingi.
