page_text:

P1	Maria miru iro iwi yat. Naomi pe miru iro iwi yat.  "rac tutwal!" uwago
P2	Maria upenyo, "wa it yat?" Naomi ugamo, "alworo. twol romo bedo kenyo"
P3	Maria unyero,  "Twol mo peh iwi yat. wa it yat."  anyira ariyo ni u iro iwi yat.
P4	Anyira ariyo ni tika tugu iwi yat.  Gu winyo, sssss
P5	Maria dong lwo.   "Twol! wa ring," Ukok.  Maria ki Naomi, no lworo ukero kom gi gu poro piny ki iwi yat.
P6	Anyira uriyo. Twoli romo kayo dano,  "Mama, key nek twoli"
P7	Mama ugamo  "Twol kayo dano no gi tiya lworo dano" Twol tye ma rac tutwal ki twol ma peh rac"
P8	Mama miru ngeyo kede twol nen chalo. Maria ki Naomi gu goyo chal kiyaya gu titu boreh.  Mama gi uwago bot anyira ni twol chamo uyo. Konyo lufu.
P9	Naomi uwago ni peh lworo twol.  Ucero inget yat muyembe kiyaga uwago ni  "shuuu!" Naomi peh miro buru twol.
