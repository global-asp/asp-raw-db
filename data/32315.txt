desc:	Abula awa zooriya aybidan beideti? Ha qaniishshe Ras Abebe Aregay maxaafa keettan

page_text:

P1	Issi heeran, issi Abula geetettiya na'ay de''es. Keehippe ozalla tamaare. Nabbabiyoganne xaafiyooga aynne dossenna.
P2	Abulli laqilaqiyabata shammanayoo miishshaa ayee dancuulappe wuuqees. Amishihsaa qottiyode wuuqi demmiddi ekkiichchees. Abulli laqilaqiyaba miidogappe guuyiyan ba laggetuura kaa''es.
P3	Amarattida wodiyappe guyyiyan Abulli luxetta keettaa biyoga essiis. Gallassa kumetta karen kaa''iiddi deriyan pe'idili amarssan soo gelees. Abulla ayyiya hagga erada yiilottaasu.
P4	''Nu naia yeeshshay lo'o giddenna. Luxettakka kaalliyaba tawu malatenna gaasu. Taappe miishshaa wuuqees" yaagasu Abulla ayyiya. Aaway Abula kanduwa waatti laammanna danddayyiyakko qofiis.
P5	Ba keettaayessi "Sinttanassi mishshaa maxxaafagidon qotta yaagis. Gaasoynne Abuli maxxaafa dooyyi erenna. Heqa gashshalu mishishay saruwan uttes" yaagis.
P6	Kaalliya gallasan Abulli baayye miishshaa koyyiyoqaa doomiis. Ubbassa koyyidikka demmanawu danddayibenna. Matan dei'ya giyeppe gutta mishshaa koyyanawu halchchiis.
P7	Aaaway Iawubiyaako be'iis. Ha'i soosimma gelada ne maxaafa giddoppe miishshaa koyya yeagiis.
P8	Abulli naniyoba xayiis, Gidikkokka qassi gelidi maxaafa giddo koyyis. Aaayyiya qottido miishshakka demmiiis.
P9	Kaalliya gallassan maxaafatu giddon zaaridikoyyis. Gidoppe attin, aybbakka qosettidaba demmibenna. Ba aawakko biidi maxaafatu giddon mishshayay de''ibeenako oychchiis.
P10	Aaaway ufaytiide "tana'awu dara laqilaqiyaba shamissiya miishshaa koyyadii?" giidi zaaris. @Abullikka "tamakka ta aawawu" yaagiis.
P11	Aaawaykka, ''loyttada ezgga. Ne maxaafata nabbaba. Ne luxettakka kaalla. Ne maxxaafatu giddon ne daafura gatiya demmasa. Hidoota qanxxoopa.'' Yaagisshin ta aawawunabbabboy wolqqaamaoosotannee yaagis. Maxaafatti hashshu oottokkonna! Giidi zaari Abulli.
P12	Issi kutti Abulla aawayico'u giis. Ba na''aa waatti minttettanaakko gofiis. Abullasikka "Issipe nabbabbos. Maxaafatu giddon de'iya duuretetaa koyyogan oosuwaa maaddana" yaagiis.
