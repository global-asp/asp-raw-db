P2	She lived in a lovely place called Heaven.

P8	So the adventure begins when she comes to Earth to find a friend.
P9	People have already planed to kill her!
P10	When she walked to a resturant  she sat at a table.
P11	People attacked  her trying to kill her.
P12	Just then a girl her age stood up for her and killed all the bad people.
P13	The girl became her best friend. THE END!
