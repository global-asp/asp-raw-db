desc:	Twese twajya tubana mu mahoro turamutse twemeye gusangirira hamwe.

page_text:

P1	Imvura yarabuze, maze ubutaka buruma.
P2	Iyi nkende yavuye mu rugo ijya gushaka amazi n'ibyo kurya.
P3	Yazamutse imisozi, imanuka ibibaya.
P4	Yaje kugera mu gace kitwa Bwiza.
P5	Inkende yishimiye cyane aho hantu.
P6	Yariye imbuto maze irabyibuha. Ariko ikumbura incuti zayo.
P7	Inkende yafashe urugendo isubira imuhira.
P8	Izindi nkende zishimiye cyane kongera kubona mugenzi wazo agarutse.
P9	Zarayibajije ziti:  "Aho hantu hari imbuto ni hehe?"
P10	Inkende irazibwira iti: "Nzahabajyana."
P11	Izindi nkende nazo zakunze mu Bwiza. Zafashe umwanzuro wo kuhaguma burundu.
P12	Gusa inkende kavukire zo mu Bwiza zagize impungenge.
P13	Zaravuze ziti: "Izi nkende zindi zaje zizatumarira imbuto."
P14	Inkende kavukire zo mu Bwiza zateye inkende nshyashya.
P15	Inkende nkuru irazibaza iti: "Kuki turwana? Ibiryo dufite byaduhaza twese!"
P16	Ibyo byari ukuri. Nuko inkende zose ziyemeza kubana mu mahoro.
