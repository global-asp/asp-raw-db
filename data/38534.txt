P1	Once Upon a Time there lived a man and a woman named Mr and Mrs Adebayo , they have been looking for a child for over 15 years but still no cry of a child in their home.
P2	they where always laughed at that they have no children so one day they decided to visit a pastor near their house. When they got there the pastor to them to adopt a child and also be prayerful , but they did not want to adopt a child .
P3	One day  his wife forced him to adopt the child. The next day they went to an orphanage home and adopt a 6 month old child and named her Lucy. Lucy was a beautiful and hard working girl she loves our parents so dearly.
P4	But she knew she was adopted, she was a respectful and gentle girl so all her friends, teachers and parents love her so much. When Lucy was 10 years old her mother found out that she was pregnant and Lucy was very happy.
P5	Lucy was very happy to have a younger sibling. When I'm on that gave birth she was very happy that it was a baby girl and her father told her to name her little sister since little sister since light to their family, Lucy named her Tessy.
P6	Tessy was a carbon copy of Lucy beautiful, intelligent and hard-working. When tessy was 5years old she went to the park with Lucy and she saw a little boy crying and went to him and pet him so he stopped crying and started playing with him.
P7	When the little boy's parents saw he was not crying they were shocked to see him laughing and playing and they gave her#1000 but she accept it and went back home with her sister. When tessy went to school the next day she helps the teacher.
P8	To carry her bag to the class. When she was in primary five she got first position and was happy when it was time for their primary 5 exam Lucy followed her to the place and waited outside. When she was through Lucy bought her ice cream.
P9	As they were going home Lucy bought her another ice cream. A month later tessy found out she was the highest in the class and was happy, unpromised to always get first position. After six years of hard work she was in SS1.
P10	And was still the best but tessy was sad because Lucy was leaving them to go too her husband house. So Lucy Tessy promice to never get distracted in her studies and Tessy aggried.in ss2 a new student came to their school and he was a boy .
P11	Named Eric which made all the girls in the class were all in love including Tessy but she didn't understand why sobshe told her friends and they told her to tell him so she did and they started dating.on Tessy birthday he took her outing.
P12	To a bar and her friends drugged it so they got drink and that was how Tessy lost her virgenity when she realized it she cried and remembered what Lucy told her and test regretted what she had turn into and left the school
P13	When she went to University she bumt into Eric and she slapped him and walk away.the next day Eric came to her and apologized and told her why they broke up and she was schoked after university they got married after all that happened.
