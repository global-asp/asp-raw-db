title:	The little playful children
author:	Martha Asiimwe
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Venolin Keanan Govender and Wiehan de Jager

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	These little children have grown up together in the same homestead and love playing.

page_text:

P1	They play hide and seek under the same mango tree.
P2	They go swimming in a nearby river.
P3	On a bright sunny morning, they wake up to play football in an orchard.
P4	When they go back home, they also show their parents what they are playing.
P5	After a while, they start going to school and always pay attention in class
P6	Even when at school, they always play together.
