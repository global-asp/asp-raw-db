desc:	A short story about a black sheep named Eep and her family activities.

page_text:

P1	Hello!@@My name is Eep.@@I am a black sheep.
P2	I have a family.@@I love my family.@I live with my mommy and brother.
P3	My favourite hobby is painting.@@I paint flowers.@I can draw too.@@Art is so much fun.
P4	My mommy and i love cooking.@We go to the kitchen and cook for the whole family.@We cook curry,roast and all kinds of tasty meals.
P5	My brother and i love fishing together.@@It is our favourite thing to do with each other.@We sit on the river dock all day trying to catch fish.
P6	I love playing outside.@@I like to look at the sky,sun,clouds and birds.
