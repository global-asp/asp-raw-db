page_text:

P1	"Nginenkinga enkulu," kukhala Induna uSgujana.
P2	"Ngilahlekelwe uphondo lwami lwasentwasahlobo," eqhubeka ekhalaza.
P3	UJojo omncane athembise, "Ungakhathazeki, ngizolubheka uphondo lwakho. Ngizoyobheka laphaya endlini kaBaba uNxande."
P4	UJojo noBaba Nxande babheka yonke indawo, kodwa lutho abangaluthola uphondo.
P5	UBaba Nxande waze wathi, "Ake uyobheka laphaya kuMama Nxantathu."
P6	Nanguya uJojo eyodlula endlini yakhe eyophuza amanzi abandayo, kade ehamba, wonyiwe.
P7	Wasuka lapho waya kuDudu Dayimane wamcela ukuba ahambe naye.
P8	"Akukude, kodwa-ke ngizopakisha ukudlana esingahamba nako," kuchaza uDudu.
P9	UDudu no Jojo sebelambile, bahlala phansi emazansi nentatshana ewunxathathu, badla, bathi ukuphumula kancane.
P10	Kwathi sebekhuphuke ibanga eliyingxenye yentaba, babona ulwembu lwesicabucabu oluyisimanga nje.
P11	UJojo no Dudu baze bafika endlini ka Mama Nxantathu.
P12	UMama Nxantathu wabaphendula, "Cha, induna ayishiyanga uphondo lwasentwasahlobo lapha."
P13	Kodwa-ke, uMama Nxathathu wayazi lapho bengaluthola khona uphondo lwasentwasahlobo.
P14	Kwase kuhlwile ngesikhathi befika emzini wenduna uSgujana.
P15	Umama Nxantathu wakhomba induna, "Ingani nalu uphondo ekhanda lakho."
P16	Induna uSgujana yanika uJojo uphondo ukuba alushaye. Bonke bajabula.
