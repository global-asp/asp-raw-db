title:	Murathu wa mubva
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0

desc:	Mukomana u a sinyuwa musi murathu wawe a tshi fhedza tshifhinga a tshi khou tamba ngeno mutukana muhulwane a tshi khou ita mishumo yoṱhe ya hayani.

page_text:

P1	Ndi vuwa nda vhasa mulilo.
P2	Nda vhilisa maḓi.
P3	Nda rema khuni.
P4	Nda nyonga khali.
P5	Nda swiela fhasi.
P6	Nda ṱanzwa ndilo.
P7	Ndi ngani ndi tshi shumesa nga u rali?
P8	Ngeno murathu wanga a tshi khou tamba!
