page_text:

P1	I love forests!
P2	Last week I went to Kibale forest.
P3	I saw many trees. I saw Fig,  Muvule,  Palm,  and Ebony trees.
P4	I saw chimpanzees,  baboons,  and monkeys.
P5	I saw different birds. I saw crested cranes,  eagles,  and owls.
P6	I laughed when I saw a big elephant with a little tail.
P7	I saw so many different animals,  birds,  and plants.
