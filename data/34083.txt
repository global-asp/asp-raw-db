desc:	All about buttons. Different colours and button qualities. (This storybook was originally created and published independently for African Storybook Community Publishing).

page_text:

P1	Vifungo vyangu vyote ni duara.@@All my buttons are circular.
P2	Baadhi ya vifungo vyangu ni kijani kibichi.@@Some of my buttons are green.
P3	Vifungo vyangu vyote ni vidogo.@@All my buttons are small.
P4	Baadhi ya vifungo vyangu ni manjano.@@Some of my buttons are yellow.
P5	Vifungo vyangu vyote in maridadi.@@All my buttons are beautiful/stylish.
P6	Baadhi ya vifungo vyangu ni vya rangi nyekundu.@@Some of my buttons are red in colour.
P7	Vifungo vyangu vyote vinameremeta.@@All my buttons are glowing.
P8	Baadhi ya vifungo vyangu ni bluu.@@Some of my buttons are blue.
P9	Vifungo vyangu vyote ni vizuri.@@All my buttons are good.
P10	Ninavipenda vifungo vyangu vyote.@@I love all my buttons.
