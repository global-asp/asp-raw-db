title:	Germinating Seed

desc:	The stages through which a bean seed germinates and how it is prepared in different ways.

page_text:

P1	Omulandira gwe gusoka okumera. Germination is the development of a seed into a seedling.
P2	Gumerera mu ttaka. The cotyledons come out and the roots remain in the soil.
P3	Ekikolo kye kiddako okumera. Kitutunuka kyolekera enjuba. The stem and small leaves aim at the sun.
P4	Ebikoola ebisooka bifuluma. The first two leaves come out.
P5	Ekimera ky'ekijanjaalo kikula ne kiwanvuyira ddala. The bean seedling grows tall.
P6	Oluvanyuma kimulisa (kireeta ebimuli). Ebimuli bifuukamu ebijanjaalo. Later, the seedling bears flowers and pods.
P7	Mu kijanjaalo mubeeramu ensigo nnyingi. The pods have many seeds.
P8	Oluvannyuma lw'essabitii munaana ebijanjjalo biba bituuse okulya. After eight weeks, beans are ready to eat and sale.
P9	Oluusi tulya by'ampogola, oba magera oba katogo. Beans can be cooked in different ways. Ebijjanjalo mmere ezimba emibiri gyaffe. Beans are body building food.
