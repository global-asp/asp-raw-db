title:	Eagle and the Hen

desc:	The story explains why hens lives with humans and why the eagles feed on chickens.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, the Eagle and the Hen were great friends. They helped one another in times of need.
P2	They played together. They played with the other animals like the tortoise.
P3	They had a great herd of cows,  sheep, goats and camels. They used to graze together in the plains while playing.
P4	One day hen went to borrow a razor blade from the eagle to shave her children. Eagle gave hen his razor without questioning.
P5	Hen shaved her children neatly. However she forgot to keep the razor safely.
P6	When the eagle came for his razor, hen could not find it. She scratched all over in vain. Eagle became angry and began eating hens chicks.
P7	Hen ran for dear life and went to stay with the humans. Till today hens live with humans and eagles feed on the chickens.
