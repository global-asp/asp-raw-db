desc:	Wanfuddu ne kamujje baali bagala sente kwekusalawo okukola amasowaani agokutunda mukatale gyebasanga egeege eryalumako enfuddu ennyido nefuuka yakikugu. Kuno kulabula nti topapanga kwenyigira mubuvuyo ojja kufuna obuzibu.

page_text:

P1	Wanfuddu ne kamujje baali bagala sente
P2	Nebakola esowaani ez'okutunda mu katale
P3	Bwebaali mukatale wanfuddu nalaba abalwana
P4	Kamujje n'egeege bebaali balwana
P5	Wanfuddu kwekulekaana, "vva kumukwano gwange!"
P6	Egeege kwekuluma wanfuddu ennyindo
P7	Ennyindo gyebalumako kati emuluma
P8	Yensonga lwaki wanfuddu alina ennyido engongobavu
