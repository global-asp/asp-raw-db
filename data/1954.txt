desc:	Ni hujiiye ohwemera hubala ebyayo ebiba ni bijiiye ohunywa amaaji aŋa hujinywehesa. Oŋeega oti hiina ehiŋanga ohubaŋo singa epologoma yiba yoosi yijire ohunywa amaaji?

page_text:

P1	Enjofu ndala yatiina ohunywa amaaji.
P2	Ekooli ebiri jatiina ohunywa amaaji.
P3	Jiri embogo edatu n'enyuni ene. Joosi jitiina ohunywa amaaji.
P4	Namuli etaanu n'embiiji ej'omwisugu mukaaga jitiina ohunywa amaaji.
P5	Amahere musanvu n'enyeeni munaana mu maaji.
P6	Gwapeto mwenda jitiina ohunywa amaaji.
P7	Epologoma ndala yajire ohunywa amaaji.
P8	Nj'ani omusira ehihena ohweyongera ohunywa?
