page_text:

P1	Hari umugabo nimbwaye imwe.
P2	Yaragikundaga chane.
P3	yakoraga chane kandi yagendaga mwisoko kugura inyama
P4	yavomaga amazi kukiziba
P5	ikumaga burikinu kyose murogo.
P6	Umunsi umwe umugabo yarashimye imbwa ye neza chane  kubera yizerwaga
P7	Imbwa yarishimwe kandi ikomeza kukora nimbaraga nyishi.
