desc:	Lendzaba imayelana nendvodza lebeyingafuni kuhlala edvute nalabanye bantfu. Lendvodza nemkayo basuka bayohlala endlini lekhashane nalapho kuhlala nalabanye. Lokutibandlulula kwabo kwaba nemphumela lomubi.

page_text:

P1	Bekunendvodza lebeyingafuni kuhlala dvute nabo makhelwane bayo. Kantsi futsi beyingafuni kutsi nkhosikati wayo avakashele bomakhelwane.
P2	Ngalelinye lilanga lendvodza yahamba yeyotakhela umuti wayo ehlatsini lelibucalu nemiti yabomakhelwane bayo.
P3	Lendvodza beyiphuma kulomuti wayo njalo ekuseni iphindze ebusuku kakhulu.
P4	Ngalobunye busuku lendvodza yafika ekhaya ebusuku kakhulu. Kantsi Imphisi iyayibona kutsi beyentani.
P5	Lemphisi yabese iyahamba iyonconcotsa emnyango walomuti walendvodza.
P6	Lomfati acabanga kutsi indvodza yakhe wavula umnyango. Imphisi yamzubela yamudla.
P7	Lendvodza yatsi nayifika ekhaya layo yatfola umkayo adliwe yimphisi. Bekungekho lebeyingakusho nayo.
P8	Konkhe loku kwenteka ngobe angafuni kwakhelana nabo makhelwane bakhe.
