desc:	Singa benseni ezaala

page_text:

P1	Wampiti ni Wakayima
  baali ba mukwano.
P2	Wakayina yali ne ndogoyi
  ne benseni entono emyufu.
P3	Wakayima bwe yali ayenda benseni enene, yeyazikwanga benseni enene eya bululu eya
  Wampiti.
P4	Wampiti yayenda okuyoza
  engoye dhe mu benseni ye.
P5	Wampiti yaja ye Wakayima.
  “Nnhenda benseni yange enene,” bwe yakoba.
P6	“Benseni yo yazaire,” Wakayima bwe yakoba. Wampiti yatwaala benseni dhombilili.
P7	Olunaku olundi, Wakayima
  kabili yeyazikwa benseni ya Wampiti.
P8	Wampiti bwe yaja okunona benseni ye, Wakaima yamukuba amasu.
P9	“Benseni yo yafa,” Wakayima
  bwe yakoba. Wampiti yali tasobola kukiikiriza kino.
P10	Wampiti yayeta ba wansolo abanene okusalawo amazima.
P11	“Wakayima yainhonhola ati, “Ebintu ebilama era ni bizaala, era bifa.”
P12	Ba wansolo baikiizagania ni Wakayima. Wampiti yabulwa ekyokukola. Wakayima yatwala benseni.
