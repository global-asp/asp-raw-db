page_text:

P1	Asake aine emyaka mwenda.
P2	Hakabaho ekiro ekimu, nyinento wa Asake, Dr. Laraba,  obuyaizire okubunga hamu na baana be Audu na Binta.@@"Kaija, Kaija, Kaija" buli omu akagamba.
P3	"No soma kyakaingaha, Asake?" Audu yamukaguza.@@" Tihariho sente zokusindika Asake haisomero," Ise wa Asake yagarukamu.@@"Baitu oine sente zo kujuga omukyara ondi," Nyinento wa Asake Dr Laraba yamugamba.
P4	Nyenkya, nyinento Laraba yatwara Asake omwa mukuru waisemero ryomukyaro okumuhandiisa.
P5	Ekiro eki, Asake yaburwo oturo. Nagenda haisomero nyenkya. Yayehindura mukitabu kunu nakuli.
P6	Asake yaijukira ebitebezo byagenzi be ebibamugambire hali isomero.@@Abasomesa nibaikara nebiti ebyokuteera abaana. Asake yayekaguza, "Abasome baranteeraga? Nanyowe ndatuunga abanywani?"
P7	Nyenkya kara, enkoko bwiyakokoire Asake yaimu, yanaaba, yajwara ekiteteyi kya uniform na sokis ne nkaito ze. Asake akaba aine kuhiimba ensaho ye eyebitabo enkooto.
P8	Obwiire bwo kugenda, nyina wa Asake yamukwata hamukono.@@Obubahikire haisomero, Asake yayekaguza habwaki obaana abamu bato muno. Baitu Asake yatiina kukikaguza nyina.
P9	Obubahikire haisomero, Asake yahuura ekide.
P10	Asake yarora abaana abakwiruka bakaba nibagenda munda mwisomero.@@Asake a kabahondera, Asake yasiga nyina.
P11	Mu kibiina, Asake akakaira mu maiso.@@Asake akaba nakira abaana boona okunyeeta, okuraina kandi nobukuru.
P12	Omwegesa akagonza Asake habwokuba Asake akaba mwana mugezi kandi omukozi.@@Abaana bakasekeera Asake habwo kunyeeta no kuraiha no bukuru.@@Baitu, Asake atafeeyo habwokuba ekyaba nayenda nukwo kuba omufumu wo muirwaro.
