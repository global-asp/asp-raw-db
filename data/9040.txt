desc:	What did my mother plant?

page_text:

P1	My mother planted. She planted a little garden. The small garden she planted was of peas. 
P2	My father planted. He planted a little garden. The little garden he plated was of flowers. 
P3	My mother dug in both gardens. 
P4	My father watered in both gardens. 
P5	My parents and I worked in both small gardens. My sister and I harvested peas and flowers. 
P6	The little garden of peas yielded large sacks of peas. The little garden of flowers yielded large sacks of flowers. 
P7	My mother planted. She planted a big garden. The garden she planted was of peas. 
P8	My father planted. He planted a big garden. The garden he planted was of flowers.
