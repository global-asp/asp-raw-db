page_text:

P1	Dzina langa ndine nkhuku.
P2	Ana anga achedwa anapiyo.
P3	Ndili ndi zinchito zambiri.
P4	Ndimakhala ndi ma zira.
P5	Anthu amadya kapena kugulitsa ma zira anga.
P6	Anthu ambiri amandipanga ndiwo cifukwa ndili ndi nyama yabwino kwambiri.
P7	Komanso ndili ndi nchito youtsa anthu cifukwa za nthawi  ndimazisata bwino kwambiri.
P8	Pazinthu zonse za padziko lapansi pano, mdani wanga ndi mpeni.
P9	KEY WORDS TRANSLATION@@1. Nchito............. work@2. Nkhuku........... Chicken@3. Anapiyo.......... Chicks@4. Mazira.......... Eggs@5. Nyama............ Meat@6. Mdani............. Enemy@7. Mpeni............. Knife
