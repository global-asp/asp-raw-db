title:	Kataito

desc:	Lebo akuzire muno yakira engoyeze zoona saaha zinu zitaito muno naija kuzikorarraki?

page_text:

P1	"Mau," Lebo yageyeta. "Ija orole. Zinu engoye zoone ntaito hali nyawe!" Mau yagamba,  "Leka ndole."
P2	"Rora ebijini yange taito muno," Lebo yagambe. Mau yagamba,  "Ngo nukwo. Nomsa aragitwara."
P3	"Rora ebijini byange bitaito muno," Lebo yagamba. Mau yagamba,  "Ego." "Nomsa abitwale."
P4	Lebo yagamba,  "Rora Tisaati yange ntaito muno." Mau yagamba,  "Ego." "Nomsa agitwale."
P5	Lebo yagamba,  Rora engoye zange. Zemizaano ntaito muno." Mau yagamba,  "Ego." "Nomsa arazitwara.
P6	Lebo yagamba,  "Rora ekijwwaro kyange kyenjure,  kitaito muno." Mau yagemba,  "Ego." "Nomsa agitwale."
P7	Lebo yagamba,  "Rora sokisi zange zitaito muno." Mau yagamba,  "Ego ntaito." "Nomsa azitwale."
P8	Lebo yagamba,  "Rora obutengo bweange bwona butaito." Mua yagamba,  "Ego." "Nomsa azitwale."
P9	Lebo yagamba,  Hati oine engoye nyingi." Nomsa yagamba,  "Aaah nangwa tinyine." "Engoye zinu zoona nkoto hali nyowe!
