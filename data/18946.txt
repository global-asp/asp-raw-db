title:	Musamariya a Ikharari

desc:	Jesus Parable of the Good Samaritan told as if it was in contemporary Africa.

page_text:

P1	Muhusiha a malamulo aamukoha Yeesu: "Kinakhwela okumi owo woohimala Muluku onavaheiye. Keere esheeni?" Yeesu aamwaakhula: "Malamulo a Muluku aniihayi?"Muhusiha yoole aahi: "Omukhweleke Muluku ni murima woothene. Onamukhwela mukhwaa ntoko yoowe mwaneene."
P2	Mulopwana mmoha aakhuma oYerusalemu ayaaka oYeeriko. Ahaaphwanya anawiiye mphironi emphara mulopwana yoole. Emmana,  emwaakha itchu sawe soothene,  emphoreiha. Uuwela mukhice wa mphiro aakhala ntoko ookweene. Anawiiye yaale,  echawa.
P3	Aavira vaavale mulipa a mukucho. Amoona yoole nto heemenleru.
P4	Aavira muLeevi ola tho heemenle.
P5	Aavira tho mulopwana a oSamariya. Musamariya yoole haavekela. Amphwanya mulopwana yoole aphoren'yesha.
P6	Amoriwano ikharari eemela. Akumiha murece mmurupani mwawe. Arapiha makhwaca awe. Amuteesherya vapuuruni vawe. Musamariya yoole aaphiyano onipuroni na aleco. Aveka omuhayiha weiwe.
P7	Amuliva mwaneene a empa yeele. Musamariya aahi: "Muneeraanyu mumvahe voopwaha ikhorowa iha,  kaakookaka,  kinamoolivaani."
P8	Yeesu aamukoha: "Taani mwa yaale araru aamweerenle yaphaama oophoreihiwa ole?" Muhusiha aahaakhula: "Yoole aamoriwe ikhakari eemela arapiha makhwaca awe. Yoole aakokhonre amuhela murece." Yeesu aahi: "Weyo ohaakhula phaama. Hawe waweerele hiihaa achu akina. Weereno ntoko muSamariya."
