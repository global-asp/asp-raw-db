title:	Zama ungumcemane!

artist:	Vusi Malindi

desc:	Lendzaba ibonisa kutsi ngesikhatsi sifundzisa bantfwana kulalela

page_text:

P1	Umnaketfu lomncane uyephuta kulala.@  @Ngisheshe ngivuke, ngoba ngingumcemane!
P2	Ngimi lovulela lilanga kutsi lingene.
P3	Make utsi, "Wena uyintfombatane yami lengitigcabha ngayo."
P4	Ngigeza onkhe malanga, angidzingi kutsi kube nalongisitako.
P5	Anginendzaba nekutsi emanti ayacandza, noma lugongolo lwekuwasha timphahla.
P6	Make uyangikhumbuta, "Ungawakhohlwa ematinyo akho."@@Ngiyaphendvula, "Ngeke, ngiwakhohlwe!"
P7	Ngemva kwekugeza, ngivusela mkhulu kanye na-anti.@@Ngibafisele kutsi babe nelilanga lelihle.
P8	Bese ngiyagcoka.@  @Ngitsi, "Sengimkhulu nyalo, make."
P9	Ngiyakwati kufasa emakinobho kanye nekubopha ticatfulo tami.
P10	Ngiyaciniseka kutsi umnaketfu wati tonkhe tindzaba tasesikoleni.
P11	Eklasini ngetama kwenta kahle ngato tonkhe tindlela.
P12	Ngenta tonkhe letintfo letinhle malanga onkhe.@  @Kepha lengikutsandza kakhulu, kudlala!
