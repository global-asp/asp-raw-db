page_text:

P1	Amanya gange nze Bongiwe. Mbera mu kibugga kyebayita Mpophomeni mu KwaZulu-Natal.
P2	Nina akabwa bakayita Scruffy.
P3	Wenva ku somero buli lwakutaano, Scruffy nange tugenda mu bibiina bya Funda Nenja na baana abalala ne mbwa zabwe.
P4	Funda Nenja kitegeza ku soma ne embwa mu luzulu
P5	Funda Nenja etusomesa engeli yo kulabililamu embwa zaffe mungeli enungi ela eyobukakamu
P6	Funda Nenja esomesa embwa engeli yo kubuka ebiti ne okudukila mumiwatwa
P7	Scruffy ne mbwa endala zinyumirwa wamu ebisela byazo ebyesanyu
P8	njagala nnyo Scruffy ela naye anjagala
