title:	Kaiko Engapi Enga Ri
author:	Doris Maandebo
artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini
lang:	Lugbarati
level:	First words

desc:	Plant a bean seed and see what happens when you water and look after it.

page_text:

P1	Impande ikhula kuqala.
P2	Eri zo nyaku alia.
P3	Pati ni zo. Eri zo oru etu vu 'dale ru.
P4	Bini oko 'di yi e'do efu.
P5	Pati ni ri zo drile drile.
P6	'Da ma vutia fu niri ni e'do efu. Fu nde 'i oja kaiko efi ru.
P7	There are lots of seeds in the bean.
