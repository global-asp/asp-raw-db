desc:	Ekadeni

page_text:

P1	Ekadeni, kade khulu eplasini elalihlanu komzana…
P2	bekukhona ikukhwana igama layo kunguDaisy.
P3	"Mzukwana ngikhulako ngifuna ukuphapha, ngiphaphele phezulu, l e emafini," kutjho uDaisy.
P4	Iinkukhu ezinye zamhleka.
P5	"Hayi awujayeleki wena," kwatjho ezinye iinkukhu. "Angekhe sadlala nawe nangelanga lomhlolo."
P6	Unina wathi, "Daisy, soke siyakwazi ukubhabhazisa amaphiko kodwana kubudisi bona ikukhu iphaphe."
P7	UDaisy azange aphele amandla. Qobe ngamalanga ubezama ukuzijayeza ngokuphapha, abhabhazise iimpiko zakhe.

Bhabhabha, bhabhabha, bhabhabha, abhabhazise iimpiko zakhe kodwana zibhale ukusuka emhlabathini.
P8	Nakasazijayezako nje, ucabanga sele aphaphela phezulu emkayini aqalela ezinye iinkukhu phasi. Uzicabanga sele aphapha adlula abotunwana adlula neenkonjani.

Ezinye iinkukhu zathi, "Wu! Ikukhu ekwazi ukuphapha!"
P9	Maye ngamalanga uDaisy bhabhabha, bhabhabha, bhabhabha amaphiko.
P10	Ubekwazi ukuphakamisa amaphiko abuye awele phasi.
P11	UDaisy walila khulu, "Ngekhe ngisakwazi ukuphapha! Abanye balungile."
P12	"Daisy uhlukile kwezinye iinkukhu. Azifuni ukuphapha kodwana wena uyakufuna! Kodwana ungakukghona," kwatjho unina kaDaisy.
P13	Ilanga elilandelako uDaisy wakhwela phezu kwendlwana yeenkukhu wathoma bhabhabha, bhabhabha, bhabhabha waphaphisa iimpiko
zakhe.

Waphaphela emmoyeni wabhabhazisa iimpiko zakhe…wazibhabhazisa godu…
P14	BAM!
P15	Ezinye iinkukhu zahleka, zahlekela phezulu. "Ha ha ha! Sikutjelile bona iinkukhu azikwazi ukuphapha!"
P16	Kodwana elangeni elilandelako uDaisy wakhwelela phezulu khulu, phezu kukarantabula.

Bhabhabha, bhabhabha, bhabhabha uDaisy wabhabhazisa iimpiko zakhe.
P17	Waphaphela emmoyeni wabhabhazisa iimpiko zakhe… wazibhabhazisa iimpiko…bhabhabha, bhabhabha, bhabhabha uDaisy wabhabhazisa iimpiko zakhe.
P18	Waragela phambili ngokuphapha! Ummoya ngaphasi kweempiko zakhe wamfuqula waphaphela
phezulu!

Abotunwana neenkonjana zathi ngokumangala, "Kuyamangaza! Ikukhu ephaphako!"
P19	Ezinye zifuna ukufana noDaisy.
P20	Zathi "Wu! Daisy uyamangaza."
