page_text:

P1	The president has said, we must go back to school on January 10. @@Did you hear that?
P2	No, I didn't. When was that? @@Did he say all children like us?
P3	How could you? @@This forest walk week is awesome. Nothing like it in our school.
P4	My parents have already booked the tickets for the journey.
P5	I am afraid. @@Going to boarding school is not good for me.
P6	Two years at home has changed me. @@I wonder how school will be like?
