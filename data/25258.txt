title:	Okujja mu kibuga

desc:	Omuvubuka akyalira dhaadha we mu kibuga ekinene omulundi gwe ogusooka!

page_text:

P1	Bbaasi ghe dheemerera mu kibuga ekye wange, ghaali omwidhuzo ogwa abantu.
P2	Kondakita wa bbaasi yeyasa, "Abaja mu kibuga! kusimbukiragho!"
P3	Abantu baasindikagana okutuka ku bbaasi.
P4	Abakazi aba abaana baatyama ghansi. Olugendo lwali luja luleeyi.
P5	Naatyamira ku idinisa, kumpi no musaadha.
P6	Naaloghoza, "Ndi kuja mu kibuga kinene! Eka nnaba nga nzira yo."
P7	Abatambuza ebintu nga batunda bali bangi.
P8	Abatambuze bali mu bbaasi bagula ebintu. Nze tyali na sente kugulaku naire.
P9	Mangu ago dereeva yafugha woloni.  Kyali kiseera kya kusimbuka.
P10	Abatunzi baasindikagana okufuluma mu bbaasi.
P11	"Bwe naira?" Nnebuuza mwene.
P12	Mu bbaasi mwalimu eibugumu. Naazibiriza amaiso.
P13	Natandika okuloghoza ku maama nhi muganda wange bendese eka.
P14	Mpolampola endoolo dhantoolagho.
P15	Oluvanhuma lw'ekiseera kinene, naazuuka. Aye kumpi buli muntu nga aviiremu.
P16	Kyali kiseera kya kunonia nhumba ya dhaadha mwaninha maama.
