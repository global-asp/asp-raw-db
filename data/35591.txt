page_text:

P1	Iman has a Cat called Lubna. @@She goes everywhere with her cat.
P2	She trained the cat to catch rats.
P3	One day, Iman was sent to the market to buy some things.@@ Instead of following her to the market, Lubna asked to stay home.
P4	After Iman left, Lubna went to the farm to hunt for rats and fell into a hole.
P5	Iman came back from the market and could not find Lubna.
P6	She knew where to find her.@@Iman imagined that Lubna must have gone hunting for rats in the farm.
P7	Iman found her at the farm.@@She helped her out of the hole and carried her home.
P8	Iman loves Lubna and Lubna loves her too.@@Do you have a cat?
