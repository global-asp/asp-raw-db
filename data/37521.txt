P1	Once upon a time, two girls were enemies, Lisa and Liya. One evening night, Lisa invited classmates for a party at her house.
P2	Liya pitched in too, Lisa played the fiddle, Liya happily clapped her hands. Lisa was shocked to see Liya there.
P3	One of Lisa's classmate threw food across thinking his throwing a bag of snacks to one of his classmate's
P4	The food hits Lisa while she was eating fruits, she threw it back but mistakenly hits someone else, then there was food fight
P5	Something magical happened during the food fight, Lisa hit Liya, Liya smiled and they both laughed about it.
P6	After that evening they became the best friends, they would go to shop together, they would share everything together.
P7	Moral of the story:@Enemies could also become good friends
