desc:	Omughala omuzaanhi einho yazaanha buli mu kikunsu kya mikwano gyeyayagaananga mu ngira. Yabuza embaluwa ya dhaadhaaghe.

page_text:

P1	Maama Bela yagha embaluwa Naisanga,"Twala embaluwa eno ewa dhaadha koloneeri" maama bela yakoba.
P2	Naisanga yataamu engaitode. Yata embaluwa mu kasaghoke akatono yaalumuka.
P3	Yayagaana mikwanogye abaali nga bali kuzaanha omupiira ogw'abaghala."Ndi kutwala mbaluwa eno wa dhaadha koloneeri. Nnenda okuzaanha omupiira ogw'abaghala  edakiika ntono. Ka mbeeyungeku saawa eno"Naisanga akobye. Yazaanha omupiira ni mikwanogye.
P4	Yayagaana omusaadha omukaire ni mukazighe nga bali kuliisa nkoko. "Ndi ktwala mbaluwa eno ewa dhaadha koloneeri, aye kinsesiiisa okubona nga baliisa enkoko, Kambeeyugeku esaawa eno" Naisanga yatandiika okuliisa enkoko.
P5	Yayagaana amikwano abaali benda okuniina olusozi."Ndi kutwala mbaluwa eno ewa dhaadha wange koloneeri, aye tiniinangaku lusozi lwonalwona. Kmbeeyugeku saawa eno."Yeeyunga ku kikunsu.
P6	Yayagaana emikwano abaali balonda emiyembe."Ndi kutwala mbaluwa eno ewa dhaadha wange koloneeri aye nnenda okulonda emiyembe. Ndi n'okubeeyungaku."Yatandiika okulonda emiyembeni mikwanogye.
P7	Yayagaanaemikwano egyali giri kwewuga."Ndi kutwala mbaluwa eno wa dhaadha koloneeri aye nnenda inho okwewuga. Nteekwa kubeeyungaku." Era yeeyunga ku kikunsu ekyali kiri kwewuga.
P8	Naisanga yamala yeerabira eby'embaluwa yebaali bamukobye okutwalira dhaadhaaghe koloneeri. Omuntu owundi yazaanhisa ensagho embaluwa yaagwa mwisubi. Agho gheen'agho ente wedhaatuukira okunwa amaadhi mu kisulo.
P9	Ente yanhaga embaluwa yatandiika okuginhaanha. Naisanga tiyagibona.
P10	"Dhinaatera okuwera esaawa omukaaga. Nteekwa kutwala embaluwa ya dhaadha koloneeri" Naisanga bweyakoba. Naisanga yasitula ensaghoye yaayaza munda mwayo nga muzira kintu.
P11	Yatoolera okusinda yaasaba obuyambi."Mikwano gyange munhambe tirikubona mbaluwa ya dhaadha wange koloneeri. N'ani agitwaire?".
P12	Mikwanogye baagezaaku okuginoonia ayenga busa.
P13	"Buti ndi mukatyabaga. Kandireyo eka aye maama Bela aja kumpa ekibonerezo." Yairayo eka n'omulala ku mikwanogye.
P14	"Maama Bela nsonhigha! Mmbuziiza embaluwa ya dhaadha koloneeri." Maama Bela yamubuuza ebibuuzo ebigheraku. Yakimanha ati Naisanga yazaanha yaabuza embaluwa eyo. Yamugha akabonerezo katono yaamala yaamulabula obutakiiramu kabiri.
