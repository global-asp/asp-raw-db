page_text:

P1	Omurundi gumwe, hakaba hariho omushaija arikumanywa nka Joel omu kyaaro  kirikumanywa nka Aya.
P2	Tarafeireyo ahakyitireine nano'okuhumbika oba okubiika  eby'okurya by'okukozesa omubweire bw'omumaisho, kwonka okubishisha.
P3	Tiheine ky'atsigeireomukitara nakimwe.
P4	Ebihingwa by'ebikyoori bikafa kandi byayoma.
P5	Omuyaga gw'amani gukacheenyaho eibaati ry'ekitara.
P6	Abaana nibarwanira eby'okurya ebikye ebitsigeire omu safuriya.
P7	Mama akaby'ama ahansi yarira ahabw'okwebuuza kandi akashoberwa ekyarareitse abaana be omurundi ogurakurateho.
P8	Taata akashitama ahansi omweirembo  yatekateka aha by'omumaisho.
