page_text:

P1	Today we decided to take a trip to Mbale to visit our friends.
P2	We went to the taxi park and found the taxi. We were the first ones to sit down.
P3	We waited and waited until the taxi was "full."
P4	We started driving down the road. Along the way we picked up more people.
P5	And more people.
P6	And more people.
P7	And MORE PEOPLE!
P8	We drove by fields of rice and maize.
P9	We saw cows,  goats and chickens.
P10	We saw bicycles,  boda bodas and trucks.
P11	We also saw a big mountain called Mount Elgon.
P12	It was very hot and crowded in the taxi.
P13	But we finally arrived to Mbale and saw our friends!
P14	It was a great day.
