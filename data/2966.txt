title:	Jijima O`di Ri
author:	Doris Maandebo
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lugbarati

desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Asite ni mu tawunia ima amvi be.
P2	Eri ma amvi ni bongo dukania si 'di one. Asite ni vi koko.
P3	Asite ne afa azi ini lera ni.
P4	Jijima `diyi oziza ru.
P5	O`bi yi alu alu dria.
P6	Amvi je erini jijima mupira tuzu ri `i.
P7	Afa yini lezu cazu akua mbele ri a`dini?
P8	Drileba ru,  ca mupira paria drio ru! Su jijima 'ini mupira tuzu o'di ri.
