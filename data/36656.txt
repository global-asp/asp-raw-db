P1	Once apon a time a lion king was telling rules.
P2	Every animal was angry because they could not do it.
P3	One of them even tried to attack the king@.
P4	Some tried to run away at night.
P5	Som animals tried to drown the king.
P6	A lion even shouted  everyone  attack the king.
P7	The king's guards tried to attack  everyone.
P8	One day a boy was playing when he saw a chicken.
P9	He called his friends and they helped the animals.
P10	The king turned into a man the end.
