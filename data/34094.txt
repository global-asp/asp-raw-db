page_text:

P1	How many children are standing by the window?
P2	They are going to school. Their schools are in opposite directions.
P3	The village chief has called for a meeting.
P4	They are fetching water for drinking and cooking.
P5	People coming. People going. Everyone is busy.
P6	Mother and father are talking, the children are listening.
P7	This is a supermarket. It is fancier than a regular market.
P8	Farmers are important, without them there will be no food.
