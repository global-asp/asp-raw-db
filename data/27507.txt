desc:	Maama ni bbaabba bali kusimba nnimiro. Abaaghaka boonaboona beenhigira mu kusimba nokukungula.

page_text:

P1	Maama ghange yasimba. Yasimba enimiro dhoonadhoona. Yasimba akalimiro ake empindi.
P2	Bbaabba ghange yasimba. Yasimba akalimiro. Yasimba akalimiro ake mbooli.
P3	Maama ghange yadhuba. Yadhuba mu bulimiro obwe beeyi.
P4	Bbaabba ghange yafukirira. Yafukirira enimiro dhombiriri.
P5	Nzeena nakola. Nakola naabazaire bange mu bulimiro obwe beeyi.
P6	Akalimiro ake empande kaavamu ekiibo kye mpande. 
Akalimiro ake bimuli kavaamu ekiibo kye bimuli.
P7	Maama ghange yasimba. Yaasimba enimiro edhindi. Yaasimba enimiro enene eye empande ne eye mbooli.
P8	Bbaabba ghange yaasimba. Yaasimba enimiro eyindi eye bimuli enene.
