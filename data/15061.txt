title:	Ŋgvəv Wùn Ngom – Ŋkèʼ Mbìy
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15061.png
desc:	Kimfèr ke ŋgvəv wùn ngom keé lav ŋẁaʼ yee kweè mo á bìnèrìn

page_text:

P1	Ŋgvəv wùn ngom dzə̀ ŋkarsi.
P2	Áwune nèn dù,  a te bor.
P3	Yì-iì koó ŋgvəv bor woo shooy.
P4	Ŋgom nèn wày bor.
P5	Kí nyár ŋgvəv.
P6	Ŋgvəv nèn mìy ngom.
P7	E nèn dù,  e yèn yìy à ŋgom.
P8	Ŋgom nèn tòŋ jii,  "Yèwor,  ku sə̀ mo."
P9	Ŋgvəv nèn yù,  a wúné koó wun ntéŋ.
P10	Ŋgom-eè koó e shuù e bí féyì.
P11	Ŋgvəv nèn wòʼtín,  e cù ŋgom rə̀y. Ngom léʼ e kitú kfə́ kí.
P12	Ŋgvəv si yòʼ yii dzə́ kiwó run wùn à.
