page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there lived a boy who had long legs one day the legs put him in trouble he broke his hand when he was walking on the road he was rushed to the hospital
P2	He works as a  bicycle transporter all his costumers did not like his leg so they stopped using his bike he only drives his brother to school
P3	He did a surgery and it was successful he had normal legs like his brother
P4	Every body paid attention to him now and he lived happily ever after
