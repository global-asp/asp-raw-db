desc:	This book is about taking care of ourselves during the Coronavirus time. It is about understanding how dangerous the virus is. It kills

page_text:

P1	Coronavirus virus is so dangerous. It is harmful to children and adults. The virus kills. As children we must avoid going to crowded areas. We must always wear our masks. It is not safe to remove your mask in the public.
P2	When we inside the taxi we must keep the masks on and open the windows for some fresh air. We must practice social distancing everywhere be at school or church or wedding. We must never share masks as that will make us sick.
P3	We must wash our face masks everyday after school. They must be washed everyday and never wear them twice before washing. When you forget a mask at home make sure you ask from your teacher or security at the gate.
P4	Sharing of food with other children can be dangerous during this time. Coronavirus spread so easily. Wash your hands and fruits before you eat them. Avoid traveling too much and stay at home. You can be safe by staying home reading books.
P5	One day Coronavirus will go. It is only for now we have to be careful with how we live our lives. Coronavirus Kills, Stay at home and be safe. Sanitise your hands, I will protect my mother and family by wearing a mask.
