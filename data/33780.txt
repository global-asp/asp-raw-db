P1	Ubaba uhlala eYeoville.
P2	Mina ngihlala eBertrams nomama nosisi bami ababili.
P3	Ubaba ngimbona kuphela uma ezovakasha ngosuku lwami lokuzalwa.
P4	Ngiyafisa azohlala nathi.@@Angazi ukuthi kungani singahlali sonke ndawonye.
