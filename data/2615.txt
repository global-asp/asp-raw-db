title:	Where is my Bat?
author:	Meera Tendolkar
artist:	Rijuta Ghate
lang:	English
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2615.png
desc:	A boy is looking everywhere for his cricket bat. Will he find it?

page_text:

P1	I have lost my bat,  and I cannot find it.
P2	I looked behind the door. It was not there.
P3	I looked for it on the shelf. I could not find it there.
P4	I looked for it under the bed. Not there either.
P5	I searched inside the box. There was no sign of it.
P6	I searched the attic. It was not there.
P7	I looked here,  there and everywhere. I could not find it anywhere.
