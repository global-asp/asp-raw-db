title:	Raraihe Na Guhe Araa Nga Ya
author:	Isaac Monday Elisama
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle
lang:	Zande
level:	First words

desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	Ginihe duni raraihe? Kondo wara ba gudee?
P2	Ginihe na kiaki gbe? Mambudo wara gidigidi mara?
P3	Gini gude du ni rarai ru?
P4	Dadu rai gbe,  mbara wara bamu?
P5	Gini he rai gbe,  kiro wara mbia.
P6	Wara kisi a simindi?
P7	Ginihe duni raraihe? Kiro sukara.
P8	Wara gungo degi fina?
