title:	Noone Timbotimbola
author:	Ibraahim Baabayo and Muhammed Muktaar
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32258.png
desc:	Habaru ɗuʼum e anndina inɗe noone timbotimbola. Fahin e ɗum wolwa dow kuuje marɗe noone ɗeʼe.

page_text:

P1	Timbotimbola woodi noone joweeɗiɗi.
P2	Noonde arandeere nde woɗeere.
P3	Kariimu e ɓornii limsere woɗeere.
P4	Noonde ɗiɗaɓre nde leemu-leemuure.
P5	Ndaa leemuure. Ɗume ngiʼata nannduɗum bee noonde leemuure?
P6	Noonde tataɓre boo, nde nareeriire.
P7	Njuulaandi yeeraande ndi nareeriiri. Ɗume nanndi bee nareeri?
P8	Noonde nayaɓre boo, nde haako-haakoore.
P9	Ngesa kondoŋ amin no haako-haakoha.
P10	Noonde jowaɓre nde ciwɗiire.
P11	Asamanji ɗin, ɗi ciwɗiiji.
P12	Noonde joweegoʼoore nde indigo.
P13	Ɓikkon leɗɗe koʼon noonde indigo.
P14	Noonde joweeɗiɗaɓre nde woɗeere hukkunde.
P15	Limsere am nde woɗeere hukkunde.
P16	A anndi noone joweeɗiɗi ɗe timbotimbola na?
