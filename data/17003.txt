page_text:

P1	Erinnya ly'embwa yange ye Stopie.
P2	Stopie eyagala nnyo okubogola ekiro.
P3	Stopie erina obubwa obuto bubiri.
P4	Akabwa ka Stopie akasinga obuto kagala nnyo okubba emmere mufumbiro.
P5	Ate akabwa akalala kalina amagulu asatu.
P6	Embwa zino esatu zibogola obudde bwonna emisana n'ekiro.
