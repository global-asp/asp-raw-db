title:	Amer eke no bu Tawney pas

page_text:

P1	Boye amereke otawnu, obu aijar no tawny pas etemuni alosit okitwi.
oroto ke obuodumu Hinayana lo-mam anyamit aparassha nu ipu
P2	yoga nesi Hinayana a bongo bong, onak Hinayana, byaibo Jo? Onak Hinayana, byaibo Jo?
Ebala Hinayana elimuni be amereke, ''mam ibongbongi e yoga en go. oswam e bore adawun ekatenge.''
P3	Na Pupuna Amer-eke nu ogewuni akerit.
Nepatupet, odoli ere etyang. orukye nesi Hinayana esipidee.
odoli do Hinayana ekayi etyang do apalal, okyakani asinge akonye eta-yang
P4	Olimokini etyang Hinayana be ''mam do ber en go apedori adumum eyeng okayika? ekyakaninkini Jo asinge akonye wok!''
P5	olimuni etyang be ''erai joda ekayamat. en go erusit Jo?
ejo ebunit Na jay i en go. mam elimuni be arono en go Nat
P6	olimikini etyang amereke be inyobo ebunia Jo ekitwi 
obongokini maereke be abunit en go narayi arai en go amuron
P7	olimoyini nesi etyang be ekonak BO Jo en go ekiya arai erai Jo chut amuron''. obongokini nesi amereke be Polo eke kiya ebeyi
olimuni etyang be mam akye ikonak ka jo. emame ebore etyonikit engo''
P8	olimuni amererke be erai ekiya akit Hinayana.''
otubi etyang akit Hinayana do ejayikini amereke 
egwayikini amereke a kit toma aw do ejayikini etyang
P9	onyamar etyang akit Hinayana adumun be ejijim
engisi amereke erai epedor adumun akit nayaret
P10	obongokini nesi amererke be epedor otubi etyang akit nayaret do ayayikin amereke.
egwayikin amereke akit toma awu do ejayikin etyang
P11	olimokini etyang amereke be epedor adumun emukule?
olimuni amererke be epedor lapolon.
napupuna Hinayana nu okuryanar atwanere okeri, otupayini nesi etyang
P12	''ariayi eray Na aijar no okitwi en go lope a bongo ore.'' elimuni amererke.
