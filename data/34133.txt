page_text:

P1	Tsine sibafundzi baseZamokuhle sikolo semabanga laphasi.Siyakujabulela Kufundza kulesikole.
P2	Sifundza libanga lesibili.Thishela wetfu nguNkhosatana Malaza ,unelutsandvo futsi ukhutsele
P3	Njalo ngensimbi yemfica sibanelikhefu.Bomake labasitako basiphakele kudla.
P4	Nemhlengikati uye asivakashele, atosicilonga aphindze asifundzise ngetifo letitsatselanako.
P5	Siyaba nalo litfuba lekudlala, sicoce nebangani ngembi kwekubuyela etindlini tekufundzela.
P6	ngeMgcibelo asifundzise. Sitsi singacedza umsebenti wasekhaya siyodlala epaki
P7	Ngelisontfo sihlangana sonkhe singumndeni.Gogo usicocela tinganekwane.
P8	Ngiyakujabulela kuba ngumfundzi waseZamokuhle, sikole lesingumgwabula-ngcondvo.
