desc:	We ask why things are different colours.

page_text:

P1	Why is the sky blue?
P2	It is not always blue. Is it?
P3	Why are leaves green?
P4	They are not always green. Are they?
P5	Why are bananas yellow?
P6	Ripe bananas are yellow. Unripe bananas are green.
P7	Why are umbrellas black?
P8	Not all umbrellas are black.
P9	Why is blood red?
P10	There is iron in the blood. That makes it red.
P11	Why is salt white?
P12	I don't know!
