desc:	Azuma nyɛla ŋɔn wuri zori ni kpre

page_text:

P1	Bi punsoa n-daa bɛani ka u nyuuri booni Azuma.  Azuma daa nyala bi punsoa ŋɔntoa asiri pam.  Amaa  u daa bu wumna.
P2	Dahensheli ka Azuma priba daa gbai dooro.  U daa bɛla Azuma tinŋima ni.
P3	Azuma ma daa ka saha lala dab sirima. Di daa ti niŋla yun ŋu ka u daa ti Azuma bun dirigu ni u man kyaŋ n tisi u piriba.
P4	Azuma daa paai Talaata, Kundunŋ ŋɔn lagbi nin baala.
P5	Talaata boosi Azuma boa ka u ziiri. Ka Azuma labsi, numni, jala ni bisim, "Azuma ma daa pun kpam mu ni u miira. Ka u yali ŋɔn ziiri bun sheeli."
P6	Azuma daa yali Talaata u piri ba ŋɔn beeri n seem ka u mar, diibu nu tuu ceem. Talaata daa piili n tee sirs ŋɔn ziiri num ni shela.
P7	Ka daa dang n-zɔ kyanŋ Azuma Piriba yiri.
P8	U daa rali Azuma Piriba maa ka zang kuntu n-pili u ma nŋa.
P9	Azuma ŋɔn daa paana yiri maa daa foni ka u kpea ka booli m-piribi i lee?
P10	Ŋun bi yum u Piriba maa ku kpli, Azuma kyanŋ n-kpea u Piriba maadu toomi. Lala Zibsi daa gbaai u pam soa ŋɔn daa pili u maŋa ni kuntu.
P11	"M-piriba umla ka I tubagalsi lala zuna?" Azuma boosi Talaata labsi kuntu maa puuni.
P12	Azuma labi n-boosi, "M-Piriba, nula ka I nini bara lala zuna?" 

Talaata labsi, "Naawuni ni taasi alaafɛa."
P13	Azuma boosi, "M-Piriba, mula ka I noori bari lala Zuma?" 

Talaata labsi, "N-nin tooi n-valii." Talaata signa gado zugu n-vali Azuma.
P14	Dama Azuma daa nyala ŋɔn toa asira, ka u voli da ka kpalim n-toa sirini daa puri nii.
P15	Di naari, Talaata daa jani ni Azuma boosa maa ka u daa tusi u m-basi.
P16	Azuma ni u piriba daa nya faako zanŋ kyang tin kpandi maa ni. Diridaari halli ni zuna. Azuma daa bu laa mari ya toa gbalingu Zan kpa Saami ni.
