desc:	Omusaadha akolera bwito munhumbaye. Empube dhendha bwito ono. Dhiteekateeka okwumwiba. Kinaasoboka?

page_text:

P1	Ghaaliyo omusaadha eyantundanga bwito gweyakoleranga mu nhumba.
P2	Mu nhumba mwaliu empube. Empube enhingi!
P3	Empube dhaalingiriranga bwito dhaakoba, "Bito oyo tumufunie tutya?"
P4	Empube dhaagezaaku okusonseka eminhwa mu nthupa aye obutuli obwenthupa bwali butono.
P5	Dhaagezaaku okugunaghuna ku nthupa aye bwito ga tayuyika.
P6	Empube dhaakola olwekobaano okwogeraku kizibu kino. Oluvainhuma empube ndhala yaakoba, "Tute emikira gyaife mu nthupa tumale tugikombe."
P7	Era ekyo kyedhaakola. Dhaata emikira mu nthupa, era gyakombagana. Empube dhaakikola okutuusa bwedhaamalamu bwito.
P8	Mwene nhumba yayagaana nga bwitowe yena yeena aweirewo!
P9	Eyo neeyali enkomerero.
