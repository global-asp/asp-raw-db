title:	Kpatema vea Iwa: Kpatema ngu agbedaang
author:	Terkule Aorabee
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Tiv

desc:	Kwaghngeren sha ciu mbayev u wasen ve u fan anza a anyamev shi henen ati a kaa kpaa.

page_text:

P1	Ka Iwa je ne.
P2	Iwa ngi ken ya u i. Iwa tema ken ya na.@@Tsô orgen va mande wegh sha hunda.
P3	Iwa bugh hunda.@@Iwa nenge a or nan lu iyol agbedaang.
P4	Iwa pine er, "Ka we ana ne?"@@Ka Kpatema. Kpatema ngu iyol agbedaang.
P5	Ityough ki Kpatema ki agbedaang. @@Iche i Kpatema ngi agbedaang.
P6	Akase-aveegh a Kpatema nga  agbedaang.@@ Akev-aveegh man ajime-aveegh a Kpatema nga agbedaang.
P7	Zwa u Kpatema ngu  agbedaang. Ihinga i Kpatema ngi agbedaang.@@Ato a Kpatema nga agbedaang. Ashe a Kpatema nga agbedaang.
P8	Iwa pine er, "Er nan iyol you cii i hingir agbedaang nahana?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "M fa ga."
P9	Iwa pine er, "Ka hana ashen u lu?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "M lu sha makeranta."
P10	Iwa pine er, "Hanmô makeranta?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "Makeranta u shin wan-gar u kiriki la."
P11	Iwa pine er, "Ka hanmô shie shen u lu sha makeranta?"@@@Kpatema kaa er, "M lu sha makeranta pepe."
P12	Iwa pine er, "Ka nyi ashen u lu eren sha makeranta?"@@@Kpatema kaa er, "Ashen m lu yaven."
P13	Iwa pine er, "Ashen u lu yaven sha makeranta?! Ka han ashen u lu yavena?"@@@Kpatema kaa er, "Ashen m lu yaven ken kungur u makeranta."
P14	Iwa er mhen. Iwa nger kwagh. A nger kwagh sha kwa ruamabera.@Ana: Kpatema@Hana: ken kungur u makeranta@Hanmô makeranta: makeranta u shin wan-gar u kiriki la.@Hanmô shie: pepe ne.@Nyi: yaven.@Ityôkyaa:?
P15	Tsô Kpatema kaa er, "Ashen m lu sha makeranta tswen ga. Orgen kpaa ashen nan lu."
P16	Iwa pine er, "Ka an ugen kpaa ashen nan lu sha makeranta?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "Nor-tar ashen a lu sha makeranta."
P17	Iwa pine er, "Nor-tar ashen a lu eren nyi?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "Nor-tar ashen a lu ishin mar."
P18	Iwa pine er, "Nor-tar a lu ishin mar sha nyi?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "Nor-tar a lu ishin mar sha kpekpe u makeranta."
P19	Iwa pine er, "Ashen Nor-tar a lu ishin mar sha kpekpe u makeranta nena?"@@Kpatema kaa er, "Nor-tar a lu ishin mar sha kpekpe u makeranta a zege chagh..."
P20	Kpatema huan viing. Kpatema hen a shima.@@Tsô a kaa er, "Hegen yô m fa er i er ve m lu iyol agbedaang yô!"
P21	Kpatema man Iwa zende za sha makeranta. @@Nor-tar ngu ishin mar sha kpekpe u makeranta.@@Nor-tar ngu ishin mar u agbedaang sha kpekpe u makeranta.
P22	Nor-tar ishi mar sha hanmô kwagh.@@Lu makeranta tsegher ga, Nor-tar ishi Kpatema kpaa!
P23	Lu dedoo Kpatema u agbedaang.@@Lu dedoo Iwa.@@Lu dedoo Nor-tar.
