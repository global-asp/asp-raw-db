P1	Once,in a city called Accra lived two friends namely "Cindy and Jason. These friends always went out for flowers.
P2	One day, Jason told Cindy "why don't we tell aunt about the pretty flowers we saw". "Yes it is a good idea", answered Cindy so the kids went to tell their aunt (beauty).
P3	So , after telling beauty about the pretty flowers, beauty herself felt curious so she decided to go and see the flowers üåºüåπ.
P4	So the kids told beauty about the flowers and it's location. On the way to the flowers, beauty noticed a strike of thunder but she didn't mind and kept moving towards the location of the flowers.
P5	After a few seconds, it started raining heavily so beauty started running. On her way home she slipped and fell down.
P6	Beauty got hurt but she didn't mind. She took a stick and continued to look for the  flowers the kids told her about. At last , she saw the flowers ,she plucked it and started to go back home.
P7	Beauty sneaked into her bedroom with the Flowers and slept with it without any one noticing it.
P8	At dawn, all the family members went to beauty 's room but to their surprise, she was hurt and was sleeping with a flower. They waked up beauty and forced her to say the truth. Beauty cried and told the truth and they lived happily ever aft
