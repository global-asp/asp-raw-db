title:	Uthisha Uzanele
author:	Margaret Nokuthula Zondi
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	isiZulu

desc:	Indaba emfushane elula emayelana nothisha oncomekayo nokuthi usifundisani.

page_text:

P1	Uthisha wami lo. Igama lakhe uthisha uZanele. Ungithanda kakhulu.
P2	Uthisha uZanele usifundisa uhlu losonhlamvu nonkamisa. Ngiyakwazi ukuphimisa izinhlamvu a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Uthisha uZanele sewasifundisa imibala: bomvu,  luhlaza,  okwesibhakabhaka,  luhlaza,  phuzi,  nomnyama.
P4	Ngesikhathi sokucambalala uthisha uZanele uyasilolozela. Usiculela umlolozelo.
P5	NgoMsombuluko usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana namadoda aqavile.
P6	NgoLwesibili usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana nabesifazane abaqavile.
P7	NgoLwesithathu usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana nezinto zokuhamba.
P8	NgoLwesine usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana nezokulima.
P9	NgoLwesihlanu uthisha uZanele usitshela ukuthi sixoxele iklasi lonke ezethu izindaba.
P10	Uthisha wethu ungcono kunabo bonke othisha umhlaba wonke! Ngifuna ukufana nothisha uZanele uma sengikhulile.
