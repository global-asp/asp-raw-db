desc:	A story about the Magic Pumpkin that had mysterious power that amazed everyone.

page_text:

P1	There once lived a family near a forest.The family was very poor and they don't have anything to eat.The family lived together in peace and harmony.
P2	All they had was a pumpkin growing in their farm.@@One Day the man decided to visit his farm and look for food.
P3	When he got to the farm,he was surprised to see abig Pumpkin.The Pumpkin looked at him and smiled. At first he got scared to see the Pumpkin smile.
P4	He moved closer to it,he picked it up and held it in his room.Days went on months went on and the Pumpkin grew bigger and larger.The wife had no Information about the Pumpkin.
P5	The man went into his room and sat down on the floor.He removed the Pumpkin and said that he wishes he had a big house, beautiful clothes and plenty of food.
P6	Within a twinkle of an eye, their house had changed into a big house.their clothes changed and there was plenty of food to eat.His wife was very surprised at the turn of events until she lost consciousness.
P7	When she gained consciousness, her husband explained to her that he had found a magic Pumpkin on their farm and hence the reason their lives had transformed. she was equally excited and they lived happily ever after.
