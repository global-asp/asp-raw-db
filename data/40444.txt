desc:	Habaru ɗuum e ƴama: e haani koo ndeye ɓinngel tokka wagginoore mawɗo mum na?

page_text:

P1	E wooduno gorko feere bi'eteeɗo Jaɓɓo.@Jaɓɓo no Baaba.
P2	Ɓiɗɗo Jaɓɓo e wi'e Gayya.
P3	Nyalnde feere Jaɓɓo bee Gayya ndilli wancoygo ladde.
P4	Sey ɓe tawi booroore dow laawol.
P5	Jaɓɓo wi'i, "Hoosunde kootiden bee maare."
P6	Gayya sey hoosi booroore nden, ronndiinde.
P7	E ɓe dow laawol hootugo, sey mboodi wurtii nder booroore nden!
P8	Gayya wicci booroore nden.@Sey Jaɓɓo bee ɓiɗɗo maako ndoggi!
