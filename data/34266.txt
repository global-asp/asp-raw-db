desc:	If you climb up a tree

page_text:

P1	Lola and her family had a party.
P2	Lola chatted with everyone.
P3	Lola and Shola were chatting.
P4	"Look, I am climbing up," said Lola.
P5	Lola climbed up the tree.@@She could not climb down!
P6	Lola began to cry.@@Shola went to get help.
P7	Everyone came to help. @@They took Lola home. @@She cried and cried!
P8	Lola felt better. @@Everyone went back to the party.
