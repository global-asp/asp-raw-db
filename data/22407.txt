title:	Ɖaa akʊnákʊná a fʊra alikuku afɔnɔ ma
author:	Malokia INOUSSA IBRAHIM
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Anii
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22407.png
desc:	La campagne est sèche

page_text:

P1	Ʊshilé ɖǝn na ʊshilè ʊ tǝʊ páá gʊlǝma kasáà, bʊto kǝ bʊ ɖa gʊɖo nɩ na.
P2	Akʊnákʊná ajala k'a ŋǝ bʊto a nyem na, ɩshilé tuutuuma.
P3	N'a lǝ wàà: "N hɩyɛŋ baa ɖaa ʊtusɔr ʊ na kʊn na gʊbɔ ma na."
P4	Ama ŋyaʊ gboo n kʊ.
P5	N'a lǝ wàà: "Na kǝ ma ŋǝ bʊto anyem na, n tǝ ma cîi." Akʊnákʊná ajala a kuro ka gǝŋ anɖǝnɖǝn. Na wàà: "N tǝ ma cê ʊtucine nɩ, nɖee n rǝŋǝ ba lǝ kaalei ma."
P6	Na laakaarɩ kaakɔmbɩlɩ ga tʊr nɩ wàà: "Ʊtucine ʊ tʊʊ shɛ̂ʊ n'akɩ."
P7	Ama ʊtunyem ʊɖe kpa akʊnákʊná ajala páá. Na wàà: "Na kǝ ma nyem bʊto na, n tǝ ma cîi."
P8	N'akʊnákʊná ajala a ce a yɛ̂ɛ ʊtucine.
P9	Ŋŋɔ ŋkɔnɔ nɩ, ɩpɛ ɩkɔnɔ laŋ.
P10	Halɩ a tɩ na rǝŋǝ ʊtucine kabʊto kantogi.
P11	Akʊnákʊná ajala a nyem bʊtuɖooɖooka tuutuuma.
P12	Ɩ san na nɩ ma, k'a ŋǝ wàà, bʊto kantǝŋtǝŋǝ n ta na pɩ na.
P13	Akʊnákʊná ajala a yɛɛ a rî gʊŋɔ gʊkɔnɔ gʊ sǝr bʊto laŋ aŋyɩɩ ma, ama bʊto bʊ shɛʊ na nɩ.
P14	Na wàà: "Fʊ̂ra ma amʊ! Kâŋa ma n'amʊ, jɩ̂ ma suuru!"
P15	"Bôɖo were", alikuku afɔnɔ a lǝ ka gǝŋ aɖe toŋo gɩpɛ gɩkɔnɔ n'agɩnɩɩ.
P16	...
P17	Akʊnákʊná ajala a lǝ wàà: "Kǝ ma sǝ̂ra ashɛʊ naa aye sala alikuku afɔnɔ na. N tǝ ma sâa halɩ a kɔɔ pɩ̂ bʊtunyemka."
P18	A yiɖe ŋkǝŋ na saa. N'ʊshilé ɖǝn bʊpisopi bʊnyɩʊ bʊ pɩ ʊtucine ɖe sul na bɩtaɩ.
P19	"Alikuku afɔnɔ ɖǝn a tɩ pɩ atɩya na nyem bʊto", gapisopi gaɖǝn ga lǝ ka gǝŋ. "Gɩ tɩ gɩ ŋǝ̂ nɩ ajɩ na gɩteŋshile kʊjɩʊ."
P20	...
P21	N'akʊnákʊná a lǝ wàà: "Kǝ ma sǝ̂ra apala bʊpisopi mbʊɖe bʊ kǝ̂ alikuku afɔnɔ nɖe na. Ama n jǝba, baɩ na ma lêe?"
P22	Katam alikuku afɔnɔ aɖe rgǝ gʊyo laŋ atʊ a tɩ nyêm bʊto.
P23	...
P24	Akʊnákʊná ajala a ŋǝ gʊfaŋa gʊɖǝn.
P25	N'a ta ajim gapisopi gaɖǝn kʊkei laŋ naa a tǝʊ jɩ gboo ɖaa a sǝra ma.
P26	Na gapisopi ga ta jim naa kuro "Waaɩ!"
P27	N'alikuku afɔnɔ a rǝŋǝlaŋ naa ata atǝŋǝ na gaɖuɩ gaɖǝn k'aa kɔɔ lee nɩ na ma.
P28	Ɖaa akʊnákʊná ajala a sala alikuku afɔnɔ aɖʊwaa a bʊŋa cam aŋŋuro alam atǝn.
