title:	Basi yaikulu yobiriwira
author:	Bether Mwale-Moyo and Moffat Moyo
artist:	Mango Tree
lang:	CiNyanja
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mangani na amai ake ayembekeza basi ikulu yobilibila kuti iwapeleke ku tauni. Koma basi ikulu yobilibila ya onongeka. Kodi Mangani na amai ake azayenda bwanji ku tauni?

page_text:

P1	Munali cabe basi imodzi cabe m'mudzi wa Mangani. Basiyo inali yaikulu ndi yobirwira. @@Ianali basi yaphokoso kwambiri.
P2	"Mawa tidzapita kutauni," anatero amai ake Mangani. "Tidzagula yunifomu yako mawa."
P3	Mangani anasangalala kwambiri. Iwo akanapita paulendo mu basi yaikulu yobiriwira. @@Usiku umenewo, Mangani sanagone.
P4	Pamene amai ake anabwera kudzamudzutsa, Mangani anali atadzuka kale ndipo anali atavala zovala zake.
P5	Mangani ndi amai ake anayenda ndi miyendo kukafika ku citesheni ca basi. @@Iwo anayembekezera basi yaikulu yobiriwira. Koma basi ija siinabwere.
P6	Anthu ena anafika pa citesheni ca basi. Iwo anadandaula cifukwa cakuti basi inacedwa kufika. @@"Kodi basi ilikuti?" iwo anafunsa.
P7	Mangani anadandaula. "Sitidzapita kutauni," iye anaganiza. @@"Sindidzakwanitsa kugula yunifomu yanga."
P8	Anthu ena analema kuyembekezera ndipo anapita kunyumba. Mangani anadandaula. Sanafune kuti abwerere kunyumba. @@Amai ake anati, "Tidzayembkezako panng'ono."
P9	Mwadzidzidzi, iwo anamva phokoso. Iwo anaona fumbi m'mwamba. @@Basi inali kubwera!
P10	Koma basi siinali yobiriwira. Siinalinso yaikulu. Basiyo inali yofiira ndipo yaing'ono. @@Anthu sanafune kukwera m'basi imeneyi.
P11	"Lowani! Lowani!" anatero dalaivala. "Tacedwa kwambiri lero," anatero iye.
P12	Mangani ndi amai ake anambirira kulowa m'basimo. @@Pambuyo pake wina aliyense analowa mu basi yofiira imeneyi.
P13	Mangani anapenya pawindo. @@Iye anaona anthu ambiri pa citesheni.
P14	Anthu ena ambiri anathamanga kuti akwere basi ija. Koma iwo anacedwa kwambiri. @@Basi yofiira inali itadzala ndi anthu. Iyo  inanyamuka ulendo wopita kutauni.
P15	"Kodi basi yaikulu?" anafunsa amai ake Managi. @@"Basi ija yafa," anayankha tero adalaivala. "Tikuikonza lero. Idzabwera mawa."
P16	Mangani sanavutike na kaonekedwa ka basi. Sanavutike na kucepa kapena kukula kwa basi. @@Anakondwela cifukwa iyi basi inali kuyenda ku tauni!
