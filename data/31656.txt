page_text:

P1	Kona le nale, Etango, Omwedhi, Omvula osho wo Ombepo okwa li hayi kodjithathana.
P2	Uupyakadhi etango olya li lya hala okutosha eshiku alihe. Omwedhi okwa ligwahala oku minikila woo esiku alihe. Omvula oya li ya hala okuloka esiku alihe. No mbepo oyali yahal oku pepa esiku alihe. Kaya li nando ya hala oku paathana oompito.
P3	Oya tokola okuninga omutumba opo yeshi kundathane. Etango olyapula Omwedhi, "Oho ningi shike shoka hashi kwathele evi?" Omwedhi ogwa yamukula, "Ohe minikile uusiku opo iinamwenyo yivule oku nyanga."
P4	Omwedhi ogwapula omvula, "Oho ningi shike shoka hashi kwatha evi?" Omvula oya yamukula, "Ohandipe evi omeya opo omiti niimeno yivule oku koka nawa. Oha ndi gandja woo omeya kiinamwenyo yinwe."
P5	Omvula oya pula ombepo, "Oho ningi shike shoka hashi kwathele evi?" Ombepo oya yamukula, "Ohe kombo kombanda yevi kukale kwa yela nawa. Ohandi hala kanithile omvula kevi alihe."
P6	Ombepo oya pula etango, "Oshike shoka honingi hashi kwa thele evi?" Etango olya yamukula, "Ohe gandja uuyele no mweenyo kombanda yevi, opo iinamwenyo niimeno yivule oku ka lako yina omwenyo."
P7	Etango, Omwedhi, Omvula nOmbepo oyii pula kene nawa.
P8	Etango olya ti, "Osho shili kehe gumwe gomutse oha kwathele evi momukalo gwe omwanawa." Eeno, omwedhi osho gwati, kape na ngoka evule mukwawo." "Ota tuvulu okulongela kumwe," Omvula osho yati. Ombepo oya ti, "Na tu topolelathaneni omasiku." Ayehe oya tsu kumwe.
P9	Sho osho ne ethimbo limwe homonene omwedhi una etango tali minikile. Osho wo ethimbo limwe ho uvu ombepo ngele ta ku lokwa. Etango, Omwedhi, Omvula osho wo Ombepo oha ya longele kumwe nawa. No yi uvite.
P10	Ouhole etango, Omwedhi, omvula nenge o mbepo? Thaneka shoka wu hole unene pokati kiikwawo.
P11	Otwa pumbwa evi opo tuhupe. Evi oha li tupe iikulya osho woo omizile, iinamwenyo niimeno. Ayihe oha i tukwatha momi kalo dhilwe. Natse woo otwa pumbwa oku ninga po sha opo tu kwathele evi. Ngwee oto ningipo shike opo wu kwathele evi? Shi shanga.
