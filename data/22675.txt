page_text:

P1	This is our process for helping people turn a story into a picture story book.
P2	We offer storybook development and other workshops with our partners in multiple African countries.
P3	We work with illustrators from concept to final colour.
P4	We encourage cultural rather than literal translation of storybooks.
P5	We help writers improve their work so that their stories can be approved.
P6	We support people to read our storybooks offline or online or from print copies.
P7	We can print and deliver large numbers of storybooks very cost effectively, with the help of our publishing network.
P8	And we also create material for educators, for example, these Guides, which you can download, print and adapt.
P9	There is no point in having storybooks if you don't use them with children.
