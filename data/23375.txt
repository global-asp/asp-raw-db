title:	igikinisho cya mushiki wa Abeli
author:	ukunzwe  tito
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Kinyarwanda

desc:	A gentle tale about a brother and sister

page_text:

P1	har' umuhunhungu witwanga Abel. yikoreye igitogotogo.
Abel ntiyarafite umufasha gutwara igitogotogo cye. nuko abwira mushiki we Meri "ntago mfite umfasha gutara igitogotogo ntiza igipupe cyawe ab' ari cyo ntwara"
P2	meri aramusubiza ati "oya, nkeneye igipupe cyanjye" Meri yari amwinye igipupe
P3	Meri ararira asanga nyoko, aramubwira "mama reba Abel akuruye amaboko y'igipupe cyanjye avaho. yashakaga kugitwara ku gitogotogo cye"
mama aramusubiza ati "Abel ntiyitwaye neza"
P4	nuko mama agira igitekerezo cy' uburyo yakwigisha Abel kudakubaganya ibikoresho bya mushiki we. 
ajya ku imwe mu inshuti ze yari umuganga aramubwira  ati "ndashaka k' umfasha" 
umuganga aramusubiza ati "nagufasha gute nshuti yanjye"
P5	mama aramusibiza ati "umuhungi wanjye Abel ari kwitwara nabi muri iyi minsi. yashikuje amaboko y'igipupe cya mushiki we. ntiyagomabanga kubikora. ejo nzamusaba kukizana hano kugirango uyasubize ho"
P6	"nurangiza kugikora, uzamusbe akwishyure, ntamafaranga afite, rero uzamubwire koza iriya modoka yawe ihorana ivumbi"
inshuti ye iraseka, iravuga iti "Eee-ee, ibyo ni byiza"
P7	Nyina wa Abel asubira imuhira. abaza Abel ati "iyo wakomeretse cya undwaye ujya he?" Abel aramusubiza ati "iyo ndwaye cg nakomeretse, njya kwa muganga"
Nyina wa Abel aramubwira ati "wakomerekeje cya gipupe, ugomaba kukinjyana kwa muganga"
P8	nuko Abel ajyana igipupe kwa muganaga aramubwira ati "iki gipupe cyakomeretse cyane. mama yambwiye kukikuzanira. hari ubwo wakiza ukuboko kwacyo" 
muganga arabyemera akiza ukuboko kw' igipupe
P9	nuko muganga abwira Abel "igipupe cyakize, ugombe kunyishyura" 
Abel aramusubiza ati "nt'amafaranga mfite, ntabwo nshobora kukwishyura"
P10	muganga aramusubiza ati "nt'amafaranga ufite? imodoka yanjye irsa nabi! genda uyoze neza ni uko uri bunyishyure" 
Abel afata indobo yuzuye amazi n' igifuka. byamufashe akanya kanino koza imodoka
P11	nuko Abel atahana igipupe agiha Meri. arishima cyane abwira Abel ati  "ur'umuvandimwe mwiza, wakoze gukora igipupe cyanjye" 
Abel aramusubiza ati "ndagusaba imbabazi zuko narakaye maze nkakubabaza"
P12	kuva uwo munsi. Abel ntiyongeye gufata  ibikoresho bya mushiki we, kandi agerageza kutongera kumurakarira
Abel yakomeje kwibuka umwnaya byamufashe koza imodoka y'umuganga. yemeza ko bidakwiye kongera kurakara nu kwica ibikoresho
