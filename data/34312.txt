title:	Nṋe na muṱa wa hashu
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34312.png
desc:	Ndi tshiṱori tsha mutukana na mashaka awe a songo ḓoweleaho na nyendo dzavho dzi no takadza!

page_text:

P1	Nṋe na Dainaso yanga ri ṱalela zwiṋoni zwi re makoleni.
P2	Nṋe na zwiṋoni zwanga ri ṱangana na Tshimpanzi tshi re murini.
P3	Nṋe na tshimpanzi tshanga ri tamba na ṱhuḓwa tshikwarani.
P4	Nṋe na ṱhuḓwa dzanga ri vhumba bola nga vumba sa ṱhoho.
P5	Nṋe na ṱhoho yanga ri ḽa miomva na Goriḽa maḓuvha oṱhe.
P6	Goriḽa ḽanga na ṱhoho yanga vha ofha Khamba tshoṱhe.
P7	Nṋe na khamba ri ya haya ri tshi ongolowa na Ṋowa.
P8	Nṋe na ṋowa ri funesa u imba nyimbo tsini na dzivha.
P9	Nṋe na muṱa wanga woṱhe ri tshila roṱhe ro takala!
