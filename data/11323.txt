page_text:

P1	Lona nguAgnes,  ungutitjhere.
P2	UAgnes ukgholophele,  ukhamba aberegisa iingodo zokukhamba.
P3	Lona nguMetobo,  uberega ukudlala iingoma emrhatjhweni.
P4	UMetobo likawu,  usogoliswa mamehlo.
P5	Lona nguMora,  ungumlimi.
P6	UMora akezwa eendlebeni,  ukhuluma aberegisa izandla.
P7	Lona nguOsero,  ungumdlali omkhulu waka makhakhulararhwe esikolweni.
P8	UOsero wathola ingozi,  yamrholophalisa izandla zakhe,  kodwa uyaphumelela emdlalweni ngokuberegisa iinyawo zakhe.
