page_text:

P1	One Day, Baby Shark, William, Chucks, Vola, Goldie and Hank Are about to Take a Ride on A Roller Coaster.
P2	Baby Shark said: "This is Gonna be So Scary!" William Agrees: "I Know about This, Baby. Every-Fishy On!" and Shadow Started the Ride.
P3	But, The Swingie Box Car was Riding Smoothly at First, and Then It Rode Out of Control! Every-Fishy On It Was Yelling!
P4	William Shouted: "Oh-No! A Massive Drop! Hold on Tight!" Every-Fishy Screamed in Terror! The Drop Kept Going on For 20 Miles, They're Gonna Pass out at any Moment!
P5	Goldie Wasn't Feeling so Good. She is Gonna Sneeze!
P6	Finally, The Ride Came to An End. Baby Shark and His Friends We're So Dizzy. "Oh-No. That was So Scary." said Baby Shark Brooklyn.
