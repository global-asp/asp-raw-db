page_text:

P1	In one village lived brothers Henry and Barry. Every day they would take the food their mother cooked to the neighboring village and sell it. They seem to be helping their parents in this way
P2	One day a obscene monkey appeared near the village. Henry and Barry set out to sellthe cookies. They encountered a rude monkey on the way. Horror! The obscene monkey took their cake and ate it.
P3	They returned home crying. When the father heard this,he rode in a rage. Dad pust Henry  and Barry in the basket the next day. On the way they encountered a monkey again.
P4	The obscene monkey run to wards the basket. Henry and Barry put the basket down and he behind the bushes. When the monkey opened the basket, a stray dog came out and threw it at him.
P5	The obscene monkey unable to bear the pain, fled in to the woods. After this incident, Henry and Barry's family lived a happy life.
