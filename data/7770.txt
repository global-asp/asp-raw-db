title:	Wamphephisa Kanjani Utsheketshe Ujuba
author:	Zimbili Dlamini
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7770.png
desc:	Kwakomise kakhulu ezweni uTsheketshe omncane wayenxanwe kakhulu. Wehlela emfuleni ukuyophuza kodwa amanzi amshanelela le kude. Wasizwa uJuba. Kodwa naye wakuqinisekisa ukuthi uyabonga.

page_text:

P1	Lalishisa ngelinye ilanga ehlobo,  ehlathini elomile.
P2	Kwase kuyizinsuku eziningi uTsheketshe omncane engenamanzi.
P3	Ngidinga iconsi lamanzi,  noma ngabe kungathiwa liconsa eqabungeni nje.
P4	Kodwa akusekho ngisho amazolo lawa.
P5	''Uma ngingaphuzi ngizofa,  kuzomele ngiye kulomfula engake ngezwa ngawo, '' kusho uTsheketshe omncane.
P6	''Uzomuka nomfula, '' kuxwayisa uNgwejeje ohlakaniphile.
P7	Kodwa uTsheketshe omncane wayenxanwe kakhulu. "Ngizofa uma ngingaphuzi," kusho yena.
P8	UTsheketsheke omncane wahamba wayofuna umfula.
P9	 Wayenqamula otshanini obomile,  nasemagatsheni omile.
P10	 Kwaze kwaba uzwa umsindo wamagagasi echaphaka lapha nalaphaya.
P11	UTsheketshe omncane wahabula amanzi engaphezi.
P12	Wayejabule kangangoba akalibonanga igagasi elikhulu elabe liza lizomemboza.
P13	UTsheketshe wazama ukubambelela otshanini obomile eduze kwakhe. Kodwa wathathwa amanzi amphonsa kude.
P14	''Ngisizeni bo! Ngisizeni bo!''.
P15	''Shesha gibela lana, '' kusho uJuba omhlophe ebambe ukhuni ngomlomo wakhe.
P16	''Angizukuhamba ngize ngibonge kujuba. Ngizolinda aze abuye azophuza futhi, '' kusho Utsheketshe.
P17	Ngelinye ilanga esaloku alinda njalo uTsheketshe,  kwaqhamuka abafana ababili bephethe izihlilingi.
P18	''Kunejuba elikhulu elimhlophe eliphuza kulomfula,  lizoba ukudla kwethu kwasebusuku, '' kusho omunye umfana.
P19	''Angeke ngavumela labafana babulale uJuba omhlophe. Kodwa ngimncane kakhulu,  yini nje engingayenza?''.
P20	Ngawo lowo mzuzu uJuba omhlophe wandiza wehlela phansi ukuzophuza.
P21	UTsheketshe omncane waba nesu.
P22	Wagxumela onyaweni lomfana wamluma ngamandla akhe wonke.
P23	Umfana wagxuma. ''Ishuuu!'' wakhala.
P24	UJuba omhlophe wethuka wandizela kude. Kwaba ukuphepha kwakhe.
P25	Kwaba ikona kubonga kwakhe uTsheketsheke kuJuba omhlophe.
