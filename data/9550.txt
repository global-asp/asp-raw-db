title:	Nakitto at School

desc:	This lovely story attracts and encourages parents and children who wish to start school.

page_text:

P1	Last Monday, Nakitto started school.
P2	She entered a new class and met new friends.
P3	Nakitto likes picture books.
P4	They went out for more lessons.
P5	They learn letter sounds.
P6	They rest after lessons. Nakitto sleeps near the teacher.
P7	They go for study tours.
P8	Nakitto reads with her mother at home.
