desc:	Ensuwa ya Babirye yasinga okwendha emeneike

page_text:

P1	  Onho ni Babirye.
  Ali ni banina mukaaga.
P2	Bazaire be bamuwa akasuwa
  akatono. Akendha inho.
P3	  Olunaku lulala banina bamenha akasuwa
P4	Babirye tabona kasuwa ke. Alira
  kabiri yalira
P5	Babirye
  yalumukawo. Yaninha omuti omuleeyi
P6	Bazaire
  be bamunonya bamwegayiilira. “okwiika
  wansi".
P7	 Ni banina boona bayemba nti, “Tuyambe Oike wansi
P8	 Babirye yasekerere olwambo lwaibwe.
P9	 Mukwano gwe yaidha.
  Yayemba nti, “yamba oike wansi”.
P10	Mukwano gwe yayemba
  okutusa Babirye lwayaninuka ku muti.
P11	 Bakagwa be batambula kitole bagolola eka.
P12	 Abantu bonabona basagamba olwa Babirye Okwiira awaka. Babirye bamuwa akasuwa a kandhi.
