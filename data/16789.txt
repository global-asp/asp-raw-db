page_text:

P1	Emnyakeni sineenkhathi zomnyaka ezine. Esinye isikhathi sibizwa ngokuthi 'Busika' kungombana kumakhaza,  imithi yome kere! Sibabantu kufuze sembathe izambatho ezifudumalako,  singangenwa makhaza ngombana abangelana umgomani nokukhohlela.
P2	Emnyakeni siba nesikhathi esibizwa 'Ngehlobo' lapho izulu liyana,  kuyatjhisa,  sidla iinthelo nemirorho ngombana sezivuthiwe. Ehlobo sivuka sithole ilanga lihlabile!
P3	Isiruthwana sikhathi sonyaka lapha imithi iyathintheka,  amakari wemithi atjhuguluka imibala yayo. Imithi ayisatheli asirhoni ukudla iinthelo nemirorho,  kuvunguza nomoya omakhaza.
P4	Isilimela sikhathi sonyaka lapha kuthoma ihlobo khona,  kuthoma ukutjhisa begodu nemithi ithoma ukuhluma nokuvuthwa,  utjani sebuthoma ukubahlaza,  abantwana sebakgona nokudlala ngamanzi bebadude.
P5	Kubalulekile bona sazi iinkhathi zonyaka ngendlela ezilandelana nyayo,  nangendlela yokumbatha bona ngesikhathi lesi sonyaka sembatha njani.
