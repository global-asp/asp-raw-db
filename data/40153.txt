P1	Kings of the tribe were soposed to be kind and help full. Kílǒ was not even close. He was mean grumpy and selfish to his people.
P2	He was sent to find a snake that would bite him. They never told him though. He went on the journey to find a big snake. It bit his heart making him evil and bitter like its venom. He was no longer king as any more.
P3	Animal's danced in happiness for no more cruel rules of there mean king
