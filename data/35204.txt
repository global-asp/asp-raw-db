desc:	Luwo sneaks out of church to go home. But finding his house isnt as easy as he thought.

page_text:

P1	Luwo vẹ oni rọye kpẹ ishọshi. Ọ nyọrobọ rọye whidjerhe.
P2	Iwho ve ishọshi na, aye suine.
P3	Luwo vrẹ n'ishọshi.@@  Jẹ oni rọye mrẹrẹ ẹ.
P4	Luwo mudia idjerhe.Ode@  nidjerhe na yaran, ko roro@  ne, uwevwi mẹ rho?
P5	Koneni idjerhe na nyara. Ọda mrẹ@  uwevwi ode ọvo. Ẹjo, ọnana dẹ@  uwevwi ravware ee.Uwevwi ravware vwa hanhavwe.
P6	Luwo kọ nyayo obaro. Ọda mrẹ@  uwevwi  ọvo ro kporori. @@"Ẹjo, ọnana dẹ uwevwi ravware rẹn. Uwevwi ravware@  kporori rẹn."
P7	Ọya phihọ obaro, ọda mrẹ uwevwi ọfa ra vwe ebọ bọ. @"Ẹjo, ọnana dẹ uwevwi ravware rẹn. Uwevwi ravware,igbeyin rekpẹn."
P8	Ọya sheri mamọ. Ọda mrẹ uwevwi rurhe ọvo baro rọye.@  @"Ẹjo, ọnana dẹ uwevwi ravware rẹn. Uwevwi ravware vwe irhe ivẹ obaro rọye."
P9	Luwo mrẹ oni rọye rọ guọnọ. Luwo da djẹ bro ra. @@Oni rọye da takẹ, "nẹ ọyarhe re kpo."
P10	Uwevwi vọ ọye uwevwi rẹ Luwo?
