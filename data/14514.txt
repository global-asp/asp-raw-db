title:	I Colour In

desc:	I like to colour in with different coloured pencils.

page_text:

P1	I colour in with my pencil crayons. .
P2	I colour in tomatoes with a red pencil. .
P3	I colour in oranges with an orange pencil. .
P4	I colour in the sun with a yellow pencil. .
P5	I colour in a frog with a green pencil. .
P6	I colour in the night sky with a blue pencil. .
P7	I colour in flowers with a pink pencil. .
P8	I colour in a cat with a black pencil. .
P9	I colour in the flag of Mozambique with many colours. .
P10	.
