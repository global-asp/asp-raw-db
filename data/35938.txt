P1	Quote of the week and book:@@Why are girrafes tall?@@Because it's on top of it's class
P2	In a jingale jangale jungle, east coast of africa in Kenya, lived three Giraffes in a very small cottage. One day they had to leave the cottage as it was too small.
P3	The next day, they slept in a hotel while the father and mother girrafe built a new cubby house as tall as the baby giraffes. The father and mother giraffe decided to make a cubby play house.
P4	While the mother and father girrafe were building the house, the baby giraffes were happily playing outside. The most mysterious and baddest thing quietly and slowly approached the house...
P5	What do you think the mysterious bad thing was? Find out in part two. Stay tuned..
