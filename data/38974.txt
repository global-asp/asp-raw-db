P1	Once upon a time, there was a beautiful small village. In the village a big tree in which crows lived. @@All the crows woke up early to go and find food. But one crow always woke up late. @@After some time, this crow would sit on a branch.
P2	That time crow feel very hungry. It fly and serching for food. A long time a good smell come. The crow follow the smell. A old women was cooking cookies. The crow think how to take the cookies. That time the old women want sugar.
P3	So she go to home for take sugar. That time crow take one cookie. It go to its big tree. That time one fox come. The fox saw the cookie in crow 's mouth it think how to get the cookie from crow. It say to crow hi crow you look so beautiful.
P4	Shal you sing some song asked fox. The crow open its mouth that time the cookie was fell down. The fox catch it. So the crow cannot eat.@  MORAL: We should not cheat others
