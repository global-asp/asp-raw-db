desc:	Mr. Fly and Mr. Big head were two great friends. Then one day they argued about who could swim across the river.  They decided to go out and swim to prove who was right.  Mr. Big Head failed to swim. Mr. Fly laughed so much that his mouth became wider and wider…

page_text:

P1	Erai logo ekile lobe Echut.
P2	Erai logo ekile lobe Akulului.
P3	Aria Akulului ka Echut ng'ipaalo. Apei kwaar apotu kechi kilaasi kaapei.
P4	Todolo kechi lokwaas ang'olol,  dio kisiakis apega make.
P5	"Ee,  nyipedori iyong akilam ang'olol naga!" "Ee,  nyapedori ayong! Iyong ngesi nyipedori!"
P6	"Topor tanite iyong!'' Tolim Echut. "Mam,  topor iyong ekingaren," Tatachak Akulului. Nakaneni dio Eechut topor kilam ang'olol."
P7	Sodi Akulului dae toporok tooma ang'olol. Apolot akou Akulului noi,  dio kisiak ng'esi asipor kwap ng'akipi.
P8	Tokieni Echut,  "He heee!" Tokieni ng'esi lokojokon tani niapakar akituk ng'aarei anyoun alokwaas eepei tani loche!
