page_text:

P1	There once was a flower that grew as tall as a tree.
P2	The flower's petals were so big. 
They were used to make boats.
P3	... and dresses.
P4	And houses...
Until one day, there were no pedals left. 
Everyone was sad.
P5	Someone came up with a solution, so that there could be more & more flowers
P6	Plant seeds!
Then, there was born a field of flowers. 
Everyone rejoiced!
