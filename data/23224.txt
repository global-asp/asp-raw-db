title:	Kabaka afunira omumbejja wiwo omwami
author:	nangereka lydia
artist:	Catherine Groenewald, Wiehan de Jager, Caroline Lentupuru, Laura Libertore and Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Luganda

desc:	Olugero lwogera ku kabaka eyama muwala we omukisa okwolondera omwami naye yakomerezembwa atwalibwa ensolo.

page_text:

P1	Awo o'lwatuka ngawabawo kabaka ayitibwa Opela ne Nabbagereka we omulungi. Balina omumbejja wabwe omulungi omu
P2	Lwali lumu, kabaka natunulira omubejja n'agamba Nabbagereka nti,"Ndowooza muwala waffe akuze, tulina omufunira omubeezi kuno kukyalo."
P3	" Ani annawasa omumbejja wange omubalagavu?"kabaka neyebuza. yalowooza ku kyokugezesa buli mu musajja aloowoza okuwasa omumbejja we omubalagavu."ngenda kufumba ensuwa yamazi omusajja wena ananywa amazzi ag'okya yajja okuwasa muwala wange omubalagavu."kabaka nagamba.
P4	olunaku olwadako Kabaka yakola ekilango ngayita abawoolo bona kukyalo. Ekirango! Ekirango! Eri abavubuka abatto na'bawoolu kukyalo, kabaka ayalagala kufunira mubeezi muwalawe omubalagavu. mujje mujje mujjemwena.
P5	Webawulira ekirango, abavubuka bona bali basanyufu buli olyomu neyetegekerara olunaku.
P6	Omu kubavubuka yagamba kitaawe nti,mpulide kabaka ayita abavubuka mu Lubiri, ayagala kulondera muwalawe mubeezi. Taata,"nina okugende nyimilire mu maaso ga kabaka.
P7	Nga buligyo,olunaku lwali lutesedwako,ekibinja kyabavubuka abali bambambade obulungi balabibwa nga bagenda mu lubiri.Buli olyomu yali musanyufu.
P8	"Mbaniriza mwena" Kabaka nagamba.Kabaka natandiika okwogerako eri abavubuka,Yena anywa ensuwa yamazzi ag'okya, ajakusobora okutwala omumbejja wange omwagalwa.
P9	Bali banji nnyo era ekigezo tekyasobuka kumalibwa kulunaku olwolo. Abasajja abasinga bagezako naye tebasobola kugenda na mumbejja.
P10	Olunaku olwadako, newajawo mwami Wakayima eyali awulide ekirango ekikerezi.Bamwaniriza nawebwa akatebe.
P11	Kabaka yamugamba,okutwala muwala wange,omuntu alina okunywa ensuwa y'amazzi ag'okya omulundi gumu. ekyo kitono kunze, mukama wange,Mwami Wakayima nagamba.
P12	Mwami Wakayima yali mugezigezi nnyo,natandiika emboozi enyuma eyatwala Kabaka ebirowoozo nga'mazzi bwegawola.
P13	Kabaka n'ebaami bee bali basanyufu nnyo,"Yono omusajja gwe twebadde twagala."mukasera katono,amazzi ag'okya gali gawooze.
P14	Kabaka ne Baami bbe bewunya nnyo okulaba Mwami Wakayima nga'kunywa amazzi ag'okya.
P15	kunkomerero, Mwami Wakayima yatwala omumbejja omubalagavu nasanyuka nnyo naye Kabaka yasigala simusanyufu olwa muwalawe okutwalibwa ensolo.
