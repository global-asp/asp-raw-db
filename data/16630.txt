title:	Omutwe Gwange

page_text:

P1	Ese tambisa amagulu ohugenda natiina hiisi aŋanenda,  ohuduluma embiro,  ŋalala n'ohusamba omupiira. I use my feet to move wherever I want on the field. I run and kick the ball.
P2	Aye bbe ese,  ndoma ti,  "Omutwe nj'ogusinga amagulu." However,  to me the head is more useful than the feet.
P3	Ese tambisa engalo ohulya ebiryo ohuhola emirimo,  ohulima,  ohwosa ŋalala n'ohusaagula. I use my hands and arms to eat,  work,  dig,  and wash.
P4	Aye bbe ese,  ndoma ti,  "Omutwe nj'ogusinga engalo." Gusinga olw'ebiitu bino. However,  the head is more important than the hands and arms. For five reasons.
P5	Omutwe gusinga kubanga guliho emoni. Kozesa emoni ohubona. The head has the eyes on it. I use my eyes to see.
P6	Omutwe guliho esuulu. Kozesa esuulu ohuŋunyira ahawoowo oba ehibulu. The head has the nose on it. I use my nose to smell.
P7	Omutwe guliho amatwi. Amatwi nj'agakozesa ohuŋulira. The head has the ears on it. I use my ears to hear.
P8	Omutwe guliho omunwa omuli olulimi. Tambisa olulimi ohuhomba hu lyani n'afania ni linogiremo cumbi oba caayi n'anogiremo sukaali. The head has the mouth and tongue. I use my tongue to taste salty and sweet.
P9	Omutwe guliho omunwa. Omunwa gunjeeda ohuloma. I use my mouth to speak.
P10	Omutwe nj'ogufuga omubiiri mu byosibyosi ebi guhola!  The head controls all the  actions of the whole body!
