desc:	Nakanda mughala agonza okukina. Aliba mukini mukalamu?

page_text:

P1	Eira einho ghaliwo omughala gwebeeta Nakanda.Nga agonza inho okukina.Yakinanga buli lunaku.
P2	Buli lunaku yajanga kwisomero.Nga aghuuna pwipwi,yasunsula enviiri dhe,yasenhia amainho me yaaja kwisomero.
P3	Yakinanga kwisomero.Nakanda yakina inho.Yateeranga gho abaana  obwoleso obutono.
P4	Lulala omusomesa yamubona nga ali kukina era yakoberaku mukagwa ghe akulira mwebakinira.
P5	Omukazi akulira mwebakinira yasangala inho okubona Nakanda era yamugha ekifo mwebakinira,mukibuga!
P6	Nakanda yaikiriza,kyali kirootokye! olwamala yaaba mubulamu obukalamu!
