title:	Chicken and Millipede
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	English

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18325.png
desc:	Chicken and Millipede have a very competitive friendship. One day Chicken gets so cross about losing that she does something very silly.

page_text:

P1	Chicken and Millipede were friends.
P2	They went to play football.
P3	Chicken was the goal keeper.
P4	Millipede scored a goal.
P5	Chicken was very angry.
P6	Chicken swallowed Millipede.
P7	Chicken met with Millipede's Mother.
P8	  Millipede cried from Chicken's stomach,  "Help me Mother. I have been swallowed."
P9	Millipede tasted bad. Chicken felt sick.
P10	Chicken coughed Millipede out.
P11	Millipede ran away.
P12	Chicken and Millipede became enemies.
