page_text:

P1	Okuzina Omukushemelerwa hamwe nomunaku
P2	Omushomesya akagira gu" ninzina izoba dyona kandi tikilamushemeza".
P3	Mama akangambila gu" okuzina kolaletela omubili kuba gye, kandi ndaba molingwa."
P4	Izoba Limwe dya zina kandi abantu badylija kushashula kundeba ndikuzina, ndi nokuguruka.
