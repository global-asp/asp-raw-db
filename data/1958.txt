desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	Ka letsatsi le leng tlou engoe ea honoa metsi.
P2	Eaba ho ba le lithuhlo tse peli li lo noa metsi le tsona.
P3	Ho ne ho ena le tse tharo tse tona le linonyana tse 'ne. Kaofela hoa tsona line linoa metsi.
P4	Likhants'i tse hlano le likolobe tse ts'eletseng line li lo noa metsi.
P5	Linqankane tse supileng le lithlapi tse robeli, line lile ka metsing.
P6	Li Qoakha tse robong line lilonoa metsi.
P7	Tau e le 'ngoe ea lonoa metsi.
P8	Ke mang ea senatla se seholo,  aka tsoelang pele ka ho noa metsi?
