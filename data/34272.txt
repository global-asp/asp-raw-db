page_text:

P1	Emaobong loved to play football  as a little girl.  Now she is a coach.
P2	When her team is discouraged she pumps them up with pep talks.
P3	She even gives them new sport uniforms as often as possible.
P4	She loves to encourage  them to be all they can ever be.
P5	Manya and his father are in the market.  He plays football  with Coach Emabong and her team.
P6	But right now he needs uniforms for school.
P7	While at the market,  Manya see his coach and is very excited.  Father reminds him to find the tailor.
P8	Now they have found the tailor.  Manya will have new school uniforms  as well as sport wears.
