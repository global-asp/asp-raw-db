title:	Kutotii!

artist:	Marion Drew

desc:	Nguruwe mtukutu hataki kumsikiliza mkulima!

page_text:

P1	Ulikuwa wakati wa wanyama kwenda kulala. 

Mmoja wao hakutii.
P2	Mkulima alimwambia Piggy aende kulala. 

Piggy Alikataa.
P3	Mkulima alimuuliza Trusty Whip kumcharaza Piggy. 

Trusty Whip alikataa.
P4	Moto Kali alikataa kumteketeza Trusty Whip kwa kutotii.
P5	Maji Safi hakumtii mkulima. 

Alikataa kumzima Moto Kali.
P6	Mkulima alimuuliza Maziwa Moo kunywa Maji Safi.
P7	Maziwa Moo alisema, “Nitayanywa maji haya. Nina kiu.”
P8	Maji Safi alilia, “Usininywe. Nitamzima Moto Kali.”
P9	Moto Kali alipiga kelele, “La, hutanizima. Nitamteketeza Trusty Whip.”
P10	Trusty Whip alilia, “Tafadhali usiniteketeze. Nitamcharaza Piggy.”
P11	Piggy alisema kwa hasira, “Nitaingia chumbani.”
P12	Piggy aliingia chumbani tayari kulala.
