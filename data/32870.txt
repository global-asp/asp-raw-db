desc:	Nita nasulama. Bushe kuti aiminina bwino?

page_text:

P1	Nita nasulama.Umushishi wakwe uutali uleekumya panshi. Ifimuti neecaani nafyonse filoseshe panshi.
P2	Amakasa yakwe yaleetunkula mumuulu. Umwaice Navi aleepita. Elyo ati, "Nalikumonapo pano. Nausulama nakabili. Ninshi ucitileefyo?"
P3	Navi apoosa amoolu mumuulu. Nita aesha ukufisama kunuma yamushishi wakwe elyo aeba Navi ati, "Ci-ci-cilafya ukulanda. Filya wanjishiibe tefyo naaba nomba. Naalipusanako.
P4	Navi abuulookuboko kwakwa Nita. Aleefwaya Nita eshibe. Amusenda apo atemwa ukwikala ukumona ifileecitika. Baniina pamuulu pakumona bwino ifyo abaana baleeangala.
P5	Balya baana tabaaba cimo-ciine. Abe aalibulungana.
P6	Chi alikwata amatoni kumenso.
P7	Lala muutali saana ukucila abanankwe.
P8	Bamubamu aalitemwa ukwangala elyo kabili tasakamana icili conse.
P9	Lulu ena atemwa ukubelenga mumutalalila.
P10	Monoomushishi wakwa Frezy ifyo ufulungene.
P11	Elyo Sid ena alaafwala amakalashi konse uko aleeya.
P12	Naine, nabafye inkanda naamafupa. Iwe niwe. Tawaba weka. Umuntu onse aaliibeela. Tuli bamo beene. Icaalo ecibansa umo bonse twangalila. Pakwangala teeti tube cimocine.
P13	Awe Nita aumfwa bwino pamulandu wafyo umunankwe asangile amweba. Ukusulama kwena takumusekesha nomba alangala na bambi.
