page_text:

P1	Ther was a man who was so tall that his feets would always get out of the bed.
P2	And he could not ride his bike anymore and he was upset.
P3	Then he thought what if he could get a new bed and he found a bed tall enough for him.
P4	Then he thought what if he could get a new bike and from than he was happy ever after!
