title:	Langi  edhiri  ku musoke
author:	Julius Bamwagala and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/30604.png
desc:	Olufumo luno lulaga langi dhoonadhoona edhiri ku musoke nebintu ebindi byosobola okubona buli lunaku.

page_text:

P1	Musoke  alina  langi  dha  mirundi musanvu.
P2	Langi  esooka  ya  kasaayi
P3	Yowaana  alikwambala  saati  ya  kasaayi.
P4	Langi  ey'okubiri  yaakathungwa.
P5	Guno  muthungwa. Kiki  kyosobola  okubona  ekyefaananirizaaku  n'omuthungwa
P6	Langi  ey'okusatu  ya kyenvu.
P7	Emonde  eyeigi  ya  langi  y  kyenvu. Kiki  ekindi  ekifaananku n'ekyo?
P8	Langi  y'okuna  ya  kiragala.
P9	Olusuku  lwaife  lwa  kiragala.
P10	Langi  ey'okutaanu  ya  bbululu.
P11	Eigulu  lya  bbululu.
P12	Langi  ey'omukaaga  ya  indigo.
P13	Dhino ensigo  dha  ndigo.
P14	Langi  ey'omusanvu  ya  nakasooma.
P15	Ekiteeteeyi  kyange  kya  nakasooma. Nakasooma  ni  langi  yange ye nendha.
P16	Oidha  langi omusanvu  edhiri  ku  musoke?
