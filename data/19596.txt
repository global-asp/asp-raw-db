desc:	Prisca abeyire okobwa.

page_text:

P1	Mundalo tsiabura khale,  abakhana bataru batsia okhurenya tsikhwi.
P2	Nibatsia okhwilama mumwalo.
P3	Betsulila mbu efise fiwere. Nibaalalasia okhutsia ingo.
P4	Prisca yali niyebirire eshiuma shie!
P5	Prisca nakalukha khumwalo yenyene.
P6	Yakora injira khulwe eshilima. Yalola obulafu okhurula mukhatili.
P7	Okubwa nokwekula omuliango nikumureba,  "Wenya shi?"
P8	Okubwa nikumuboolela,  "Yalikhao ufuchile!"
P9	Khandi nikumuboolela kuri,  "Nzalila eshitali!"
P10	Inyanga yindi okubwa nikumuboolela Prisca kuri,  "Nyanga ino enzia okhuchendera abetsa banje."
P11	Prisca yakhunyula obushinga butaru. Yara lulala mulwalo weshitali,  olundi inyuma wo muliango,  nolundi mulukaka. Mana niyelukha.
P12	Okubwa kwakalukha,  kwakhaaba,  "Prisca,  oli yena?"  "Endi ano,  mulwalo weshitali. Endi ano inyuma womuliango. Endi ano,  mulukaka."
P13	Bulano okubwa nikumanya mbu Prisca yali nakubayire.
