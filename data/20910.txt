desc:	A group of cranes sees a crying baby and they decide to take the baby away with them.

page_text:

P1	Mother and father left their baby with their older children.
P2	The older children played and forgot about the baby.
P3	One day, cranes came across the crying baby.
P4	They lifted the baby in its blanket and flew away.
P5	The mother saw four cranes flying away with her baby.
P6	The cranes looked after the baby.
P7	While the cranes were away, a frog swallowed the baby!
P8	For three flies, the frog returned the baby to its parents.
