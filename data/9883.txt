desc:	How we must not upset the balance of nature.

page_text:

P1	A kali ka masiku a hosi ya khwati akuhi n'wa nghonyamu.
P2	Yena a rumili a zvihari kasi za kota akuya miyeta a guwa gi nga kari gi zvala le tiveni lomu khwatini. Kanilezvi vona avazikotangi!
P3	A ndlovu yiti hlawulili akuya miyeta a guwa. A ndlovu yi rumiwili akuya wonelela.
P4	Laha ci kari ka wusiku a guwa ginu sangula,  hikumaha loku n'wa ndlovu nayenawu anu sangula aku dayela amakheli. Yena anuya a mahleni hiku dayela amandziku wa kona.
P5	Hiku fambeni ka maviki yu kari,  n'wa hosi n'wa nghonyamu anu tsaka hikusa n'wa ndlovu azi kotili aku mbeta a guwa.
P6	Kanilezvi a ndzaku ka hweti,  vona va wonili amabandzala hiku tsalangana ka wona lomu masin'wini ka vona.
P7	N'wa hosi n'wa nghonyamu a rumili n'wa ndlovu kasi a vitana amakhele ma taga amabandzala.
P8	N'wa ndlovu anu tala a hloku hikusa yena awa deli amakheli wothle. Aku ngalu: Rivalelu,  rivalelu,  nzi de-de-de-deli wothle!
P9	A ndlovu yi celilwe a ci kanikisuweni a malembi yo tala.
P10	Hikumaha loku,  a hosi yi beleli a nayu ka zvihari zonthle kasi ku zvi maha a hosi yi wulaku ka wothle a kama.
