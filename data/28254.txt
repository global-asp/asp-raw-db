desc:	Olugero lunho lugemaganha ku maama eyategekera abomunhumba ye eiva elya maidho. Baalya baghomerwa ihno.

page_text:

P1	amidho,amaidho,amaidho.
P2	maama ghange afumba eiva elyamaidho.
P3	eiva elya maidho lighoma inho.nze mpomerwa inho eiva lyamaidho.
P4	baabaa ghange aghomerwa inho eiva lya maidho.
P5	maama ghange adhighomerwa inho.
P6	eiva lya maidho lighomera inho ku matooke.anti bwodhilira obuundhi ghekomba olw'obughoomi.
P7	maama ataire eiva lya maidho kuziiko.atailemu akatungulu,cuumbi,ebinzaali n'emaamba.
P8	ataiile omuliro mutonhonho.eiva litokota mpoolampola.lileesa omuzigo era liyiire buluungi inho.
P9	maama,baabaa,ninze tweesunga kulya eitooke lyaife n'envuluga yaife ebobeire obulungi.
P10	maama aighuula emeere,buli muntu amugha eiva lingi.tukooza eitooke lyaife tw'aghomerwa inho.
P11	tweenha tweenha twaghomerwa inho,tweebaza maama nti ghebale kufumba inho.
