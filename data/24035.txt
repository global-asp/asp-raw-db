desc:	Egi empunu yomweshungo teri kuhurikiriza omuhingi.

page_text:

P1	Bukaba buli obwire bwe binyamishwa kubyama. 
Emwe aharibyo tera hurikirize
P2	Omuhingi akashaba empunu ngu etahe omu kiju kyayo. Konka akanga kutaha mu
P3	Omuhingi akashaba ekihuzo kye kiyabire nayesiga ngu kitere empunu.
P4	Omuriro nya buriro nabwo bukanga kwosya ekihuzo ahabwo butahurira
P5	Amaizi garikwera nago gakanga ku hurikiriza omuhingi kuraza omuriro
P6	Omuhingi akashaba ente yamate kunywa amaizi garikwera.
P7	Ente yamate ekajira, "Ninza kunywa amaizi. Nyine eliho."
P8	Amaizi galikwera gakara, "Otanywa ninza kulaza omuriro."
P9	Omuriro nagwa gukara guti, "Ngaha otakikora. Leka nyosye ekihuzo."
P10	Ekihuzo nakyo kyarira kiti, "Nakweshengereza ota nyosya. Leka ntere Empunu
P11	Empunu ekanyiga eti," Leka ntahe omu kiju kyangye."
P12	Empunu ekaza omu kiju yaye tebekanisa kubyama
