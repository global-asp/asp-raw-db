title:	Yiġjibni - I Enjoy
author:	Ramzi Hachani
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tunisian
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15382.png
desc:	Ħkèye bil-Inglìz w bit-Tùnsi

page_text:

P1	I enjoy skipping rope. It-tengìz b'il-ħbel yiġjibni.
P2	Alone. Waħdi.
P3	I enjoy skipping it with my friend too. It-tengìz b'il-ħbel mġa ṡàħibti yiġjibni kìf kìf.
P4	I enjoy reading interesting stories. Qràyet il-ħkèyèt il-mumtġa tiġjibni.
P5	I enjoy walking in the field with my friends. Il-miħwàṡ fi'l-hincìr mġa aṡħàbi yiġjibni.
P6	I enjoy washing utensils for my mother.Mġàwnet ummi fi ġsìl il-mèġùn tiġjibni.
P7	I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Ktìbet ħrùf kbàr illi nnejjim naqràhum b'shùle tiġjibni.
P8	I enjoy eating different fruit.Mèkelt il-ɍallèt il-mitnewġìn tiġjibni.
P9	I enjoy sleeping on a big bed. Ir-rqàd fi farc kbir yiġjibni.
P10	I enjoy listening to mother telling interesting stories. Smèġ ummi kìf taħki ħkèyèt muċìra yiġjibni.
P11	Ooh! I enjoy doing all these things!Aah! Il-hàjèt hèđùme il-kull yiġjbùni.
