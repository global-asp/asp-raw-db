title:	Dzhesi dza makhulu
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Sikhumbuzo  Sibanda and Simangaliso  Sibiya
lang:	Tshivenḓa

desc:	Makhulu wanga vho nthungela dzhesi yo nakaho. (U thomani heyi bugu ya nganea yo vha yo itwa kha Maker App

page_text:

P1	Ndi dzula na makhulu wanga.@@Ri dzula mutumbani muṱuku.
P2	Vhathu vha humbula uri ri vhashai.@@Nṋe ndi humbula uri u takala, ndi lwone lupfumo.@@Riṋe ro takala.
P3	Makhulu wanga vho nthungela dzhesi yo nakaho.@@Vhathu vhoṱhe muḓanani vho vha tshi amba nga hayo.
P4	Vhadededzi vhanga vho humbela makhulu wanga uri vha vha rungele-vho dzidzhesi.
P5	Nga u ṱavhanya, vhathu vhoṱhe muḓanani vho vha vha tshi ṱoḓa u rungelwa dzhesi.
P6	Makhulu vho rungela vhathu vhoṱhe dzidzhesi.@@Vha dzi rengisa.
P7	Zwino ro no vha na tshelede nnzhi.@@Ro no takalesa.@@A hu tsheena muthu ane a humbula uri ri vhashai.
P8	A thi ṱoḓi u bvula dzhesi ye makhulu wanga vha nthungela yone. @@I ita uri ndi ḓirwe khana ngavho.
