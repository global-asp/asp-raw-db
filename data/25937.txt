title:	Mbiizi oyab'eika

desc:	Ombiizi ataka kutwala mukisa gwobugabirizi bwomwinaye aye tetuuka ghala!

page_text:

P1	Ombiizi yafulumiremuuku okutambulamu. Yayagiirye ombuli, omukwano gwe asinga.
P2	Ombuli yakobere, ''Eee, mbiizi! Oize nanze omunyumba yange. Tulinyooku embaga.''
P3	Bwebabbaire balya, ombiizi naloghoozya, ''Emere eno eghooma inho. Ndoghoozya  ngiza kubba ghano paka.''
P4	Kaali kaseera ka buli muntu kukina. Aye ombiizi yakinire yenka okumbali.
P5	Okunkomerero kw'amakina,byabire. Nga ghankabita akaseera katono, ombiizi yakobere, ''mpulira obulwaire.''
P6	Ebindi byasaliregho okwiryayo ombiizi eika ghaayo.
P7	Aye ombiizi yakungire nakoba, ''Ghakiri mungiryeyo omunyumba yembuli. Eika ghange ghala inho.''
P8	Bya mutaire aghansi ni bikoba, ''oli muzito. Oghobba otaka otambule oireyo.''
P9	Ombiizi yayetere ku emikwano gy'ebiri, aye bya pyaitegho nga ghabula kumubuulyaku.
P10	Ombiizi yairireyo omunyumba yembuli. Yaloghoozerye,''ntaka kulya bwa mere eyo ombuli gyasumba.''
P11	Ombuli yeghuunyirye okubona ombiizi amang'ago. Yabwirye, ''olinayo ekyewerabiire?'' ombiizi yairiremu, ''mukwano gwange, ndi mulwaire.''
P12	Akaseera ako, ebindi byatuukire ni bitandiika okuzuma ombiizi. ''Oli kulimba.singuli oli mulwaire, tighandi tambwire wenka noira ghano.''
P13	Ombuli namanya nti ombiizi ali kwebuzyabuzya. Ombuli yanyigire nakoba, ''oyab'eika mbiizi.''
P14	Ombiizi yacuukire natuuma okuzwagho. ''ngezeryekuuku,'' yakobere.
P15	Ebindi byakobere, ''titukaali tweta mbiizi mu nyumba gyaisu tete!
