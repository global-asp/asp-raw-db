page_text:

P1	Khole ni Ekonya yaahipakana opatthani.@@Awo yaanithweelasa vamosa. Yaanilyasa vamosa. Itthu soothene yiira vamosa.
P2	Nihiku nimoha, Ekonya yaahitepa owereiwa enahala vakhaani okhwa.@@Ekonya vaawehaaya otepa owereiwa yaahirowa wakhulukana, khuleeliwa wiira: "Mwaatthunaka ovona mukhuure enama ya khole."
P3	Ekonya vaiwalaaya yaahitepa oriipiwa murima. @@Ehinoona-tho makhalelo makina oowiira vahikhanle omukhuura Khole, mpatthani aya.
P4	Khole vaavinyal'awe orowaka omuxikurya mpatthani awe, Ekonya yaahimukuxa Khole ottuli, emurowanaka mmuro vatthiyani voowiixa.
P5	Ekonya yaahiira: "Mukulukhana ookileela wiira kaatthunaka ovona, kihaana wookhuurani, mpatthani."
P6	Khole aahaakhula iiraka: "Nnaari mpatthani, muhikikhuure, miyo kootepa oyeva. Mukilipelele vakhaani kinrowa owiitthana apiipi. Awo khareere wunnuwa okivikana miyo."
P7	Vano Ekonya yaahiira: "Ari woowi apip'inyu awo?"@@Khole aahiira: "Awo ari ota, mwiiri ole vale."@@Nto Ekonya yaahiira: "Mwaarowele wiira miyo kaakhuure."
P8	Khole owo aahitupha ottuli iwo wa Ekonya orowaka ota, aahitthimakela owela mmwirini, okhala memmo. Ekonya yaahimulipelela. Ehinamoona Khole orwaaka.@@Nno waari omala w'opatthani.
