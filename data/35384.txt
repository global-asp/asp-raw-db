page_text:

P1	UNandi noGugu bathokozela ukudlala ingqathu ebaleni. Bayajabula ngoba sekuqala ukufudumala.@"Lithwese Ihlobo, lethwese Ihlobo, sizogxumagxuma"!
P2	Izingane zidlala zikhululekile otshanini obuluhlaza. Izintuli nokoma kwaseBusika sekundlulile.@"Lethwese Ihlobo, lethwese ihlobo, thokozani zingane"!
P3	"Ngiyayithanda Intwasahlobo ngoba kunezithelo eziningi", kusho uGugu ethokozile. Izithelo zikaGugu zinamagama, imibala nezimo ezahlukene. "Bangane ningayisho"? Kubuza uGugu.
P4	Bonke abantu bamatasata ngemisebenzi yasemasimini. Batshala ukudla okwahlukene, njenge......
P5	Intwasahlobo yisikhathi esithokozelwa yimindeni. Imindeni ithola uthuba lokubungaza imicimbi eyahlukene. Ungasho umcimbi owuthokozelayo emndenini wakho?@"Lethwese Ihlobo, lethwese Ihlobo, sizosina sijabule"!
