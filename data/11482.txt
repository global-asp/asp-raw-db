desc:	Kgang ka Khudu le Noga. Khudu ya raya Noga ya re a re ye go thuma. Ga simolola ga nna boima go Noga e nnwela mo metsing. Khudu ya falosa botshelo jwa Noga ka go jera molomo go molomo go ntsha metsi mo go Noga.

page_text:

P1	Ka nako e ngwe gone gona le ditsala,  Kundu le Noga.
P2	Ka letsatsi le lengwe Khudu ya re go Noga,  "a re ye go thuma." "Ga re itse go thuma" ga araba Noga. "Ke tla go ruta" ga fetola Khudu.
P3	Ba ya molapong. "Ke mang yo o tla tsenang pele?" ga botsa Khudu. "Tsena,  ke tla go lebelela go" araba Noga.
P4	Khudu ya tsena pele,  mo molapong. Noga ya gata mo bonamelelong.
P5	Morago ga nako,  Noga ya tsena mo molapong Khudu ya e bontsha.
P6	Khudu le Noga tsa thuma moboteng jwa molapong.
P7	Tsa simolola go thuma mo godimo gametsi.
P8	Ga dintse di thuma Khudu ya simolola go thuma ka sa morago. Ya simolola go etsa Khudu,  Noga ka go thoka matsogo ya simolola go nwela.
P9	Ka mogo Noga ya timelela,  Khudu ka bonako ya batla Noga mo metsing go e felosa.
P10	Ka bonako Khudu ya tswa mo metsing go felosa Noga,  Khudu ya robatsa Noga ka dimpa go e felosa.
P11	Ko bofelong,  Khudu ya dirisa molomo wa yona go ntsha metsi mo go Noga.
