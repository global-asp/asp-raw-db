desc:	A story written by a caring and thoughtful boy to celebrate his mother. (This story was created at a holiday programme run by Sophiatown Community Psychological Services in Johannesburg

page_text:

P1	This is me.
P2	I live with my mom, brother and sister.
P3	My mom likes to cook.
P4	Sometimes my mom visits her friends.
P5	When she is away, I clean the house and wash the dishes.
P6	When it is night time, my mom tells us to pray.
P7	When it is my birthday, my mom brings me something.
P8	I love my mom.
