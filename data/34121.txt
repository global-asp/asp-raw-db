page_text:

P1	Mfene naNgwenya bebabangani labakhulu.Bebacitsa lusuku baticocela ,batihlekela.
P2	Mfene abehlala etulu esihlahleni, Ngwenya ahlala phasi emfuleni.
P3	Langa limbe wagula umntfwana Ngwenya, Gogo Ngwenya watsi abatfole sibindzi semfene kutomelapha.
P4	Ngwenya wacela Mfene kutsi amvakashele kakhe, ngobe angakwati kuhlamba wamema emhlane waNgwenya.
P5	Endleleni Ngwenya wacocela Mfene kutsi ugulelwa ngumntfwanakhe, manje udzinga sibindzi semfene kutomelapha.
P6	Nembala bajike bayolandza sibindzi saMfene. Mfene abetfuke kakhulu.
P7	Mfene wazubela esihlahleni, wabatse uyamncenga Ngwenya, wahleka Mfene watsi bengitsi ungumngani kantsi ungumbulali?!
