page_text:

P1	Joyi yazaanha olunaku lwonalwona.
P2	maama ghe yamukoba okwoza.
P3	Y'agema omuswaki ogwamainho.
P4	Maama ghe yaamuteera amaadhi mu bbaafu.
P5	Yeesiimula mu maiso.
P6	Yaaja yemuganda ghe okulambala.
P7	Munco yeesenhia.
P8	Maama ghe yamwambaku okwambala.
P9	Yamusiba engaito.
P10	Joyi aja ku isomero.
P11	Joyi ali mu kibiina.
