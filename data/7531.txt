page_text:

P1	Má ádrị́pị la bụ́kụ̃ sụ̃kụ́lụ̃ drị̂ lã.
P2	Má ãndrẽ la Bĩbĩlíyã lã.
P3	Má ámvọ́pị la ị́jọ́ sĩlé ãkónã aꞌdíŋá drị̃ gá rĩ kí lã.
P4	Má átịdrị́ la ị́jọ́ sĩlé gãzẹ́tị̃ gá rĩ lã.
P5	Dẹ̃dẹ́ la mágázínĩ lã.
P6	Má ámvọ́pị were rĩ la bụ́kụ̃ íꞌdá drị̂ undré.
P7	Ma bụ́kụ̃ ꞌdĩ lã.
P8	Ace were má bụ́kụ̃ pírí kí lã rá.
