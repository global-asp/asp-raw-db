title:	Sisyutse Sye’Tsala Ni Kamatsangwali Kamalulu
author:	Aryada Naomi Nora
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	Lumasaaba

desc:	Sisyutse sye ‘tsala sinyoola kumukunda kwebibyano nga mulimu kamatsangwali kamarobe katsungatsunga khu lusaca. Sisyutse syesuta nio sibute kamatsangwali ne syemuula haasi makalama.Syakhakakho khu khwisutiila ilaala mu ngaaki kimirundi kimikali

page_text:

P1	Abaaho khaleekho sitsutsye,  ne'nga itsala ili khusilya. Yaba sili khukendakendakho mukyewa nga siheetsa byakhulya.
P2	Syatundubikha kumukunda kwe kamatsangwali kamarobe pe! Wabona kumuyooyo kwekamatsangwali katsungatsunga khu lusakya lwe murwe.
P3	Wilomela yenyene ari,  "ako kafaana kanukutsile bulayi." Wisyendakho inyuma khafwiti ari, ameeyo isutile ilaala mungaki.
P4	Kamatsangwali yaba kayiwanikha mungaki naabi. Wanatsutse ehena ne wikhupa haasi makalama nga mbakhwoola khu matsangwali ta.
P5	Wanatsutse winyukha, wisyendela ilaala inyuma khukhwama isi anyowa khuuba. Khuluno wingonangona khusunila ilaala mungaki. Afu, afu oleele ilaala angaki khumatsangwali, wimuula asi kwe'ngoolo.
P6	Witsemamu lundi na lundi ne'nga sanyala khukhwola khumatsangwali ta. Wilanganamu khukwa ni'nyukha khukhwolesa kumukongo kwitsula kamakotso.
P7	Imamalilisi wabirurakho wikendela waatsya.
P8	Wanatsutse aaba mukhalafu naabi. Wimikha wikhebukha walooleela kamatsangwali lundi wambasa ari," Nekamatsangwali keene kafwana malululu ndi!" Wisindikhilisa watsya. Itsala yaba yamuliliile ilaala khufura matayi.
