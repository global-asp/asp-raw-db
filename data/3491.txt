desc:	Indoda ende yayiyinde kakhulu

page_text:

P1	Igeja lalilifishane.
P2	Umnyango wakhe wawumncane ehluleka ukungena endlini.
P3	Umbhede wakhe wawumfishane.
P4	Ibhayisikili nalo lilifshane.
P5	Lendoda yayiyinde bo!
P6	Wenza igeja elide.
P7	Wenza umnyango omude.
P8	Wenza umbhede omude.
P9	Wathenga ibhayisikili elide.
P10	Wahlala esihlalweni esiphezulu. Wadla ngemfologo ende.
P11	Washiya umuzi wakhe,  wayohlala endle iminyaka.
