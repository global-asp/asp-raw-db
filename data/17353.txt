title:	Uthisha Wami Uzanele

desc:	Indaba emfushane elula emayelana nothisha oncomekayo nokuthi usifundisani.

page_text:

P1	Lona nguthisha wami. Igama lakhe  nguZanele. Ungithanda kakhulu.
P2	Uthisha uZanele usifundisa ongwaqa nonkamisa. Ngiyakwazi ukuphimisa onkamisa a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Uthisha usifundise imibala: -bomvu,  -luhlaza,  okwesibhakabhaka,  -luhlaza okotshani,  futhi -mnyama.
P4	Uthisha uZanele wazi izindaba eziningi. Usixoxela indaba ehlukile usuku nosuku.
P5	NgoMsombuluko,   usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana namadoda adumile.
P6	NgoLwesibili,  usixoxela izindaba ezimayelana nabesifazane abadumile.
P7	NgoLwesithathu,   usixoxela ngezithuthi.
P8	NgoLwesine,  usixoxela ngezolimo.
P9	NgoLwesihlanu,  yithuba lethu lokuxoxela abanye izindaba.
P10	Uthisha wethu uvelele. Ngifuna ukufana nothisha uZanele uma sengikhulile.
