page_text:

P1	Mɨ n-kayn nà wul ɨ̀lvɨ̀ fi ghɨ tèyn ta ma alè' alya a mbzɨ̀-a.
P2	Wu ke' kɨ̀ tèyn ta ma.
P3	Fì taŋi kɨ̀ tì.
P4	Fì kelɨ ɨ̀nkì inyuŋsɨ atu nɨ̀ mà.
P5	Ɨ wul wu kelɨ àsi àkema nɨ̀ ɨtuŋlɨ ɨ̀ wom.
P6	Fì chye' kɨ tèyn ta ma.
P7	Ma wù kà' a wù ni nô mɨ ghà vzɨ̀ a mɨ n- nî.
P8	Mɨ̀tɨ̀ ma wù kà' wù bu nì iwo zɨ̀ a mà kà' ma bu nì.
P9	Ma ki meyn fi kɨ̀ŋ nô mɨ̀ awo sɨ yeyn na mà kà' a mà yeyn wul ta ma-a. @@Ma ti meyn kàlì nô mɨ akù fì ki ibam i ɨ̀nkà'.
P10	Ma tî meyn ki jɨ̀ŋ kal, ki kò'sɨ, fì ki su'sɨ̀.
P11	No mɨ̀ ikè' ìkà ta mà yeyn.
P12	Mɨ̀ bu læ yeyn ɨ wul ɨ̀lvɨ̀ ta ma nô alè' alya.
P13	A n-ghɨ kɨ mà nìnyɨ̀ŋ a mbzɨ̀ afêyn! @@Mɨ n-ghɨ̀ kɨ nìnyɨ̀ŋ, o, yi n-ghɨ kɨno samo'.
P14	Mɨ̀tì, a n-ghɨ wi kɨ ma, yì yeyn ti-a? @@No mɨ zɨ̀ nɨ ghɨ kɨ nìnyɨ̀ŋ!
