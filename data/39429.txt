title:	Ee Muorŋak nhom ku rɛɛc
author:	Philip Lual Bol
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Dinka
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/39429.png
desc:	Abak ja ŋic ye rin ŋo ye thɔk kuec ta thel kek!

page_text:

P1	Thɔk eye mourŋak nhom e lai ku diɛt. @@Ku jɔl amat nhom cɔɔl.
P2	Ee ci Thɔk lɛk lai lɔn ci yen nyuoth. @@Go ke nyuc bik wɛtde piŋ.
P3	Ee ci nyouth lɔn bi cɔk bɛn. @@Go Aŋɔu thiec, "Yeŋo buk loi?"
P4	Go Ajith kek Tuot lueel, "Ok abi ciam tɔu atoŋ Muorŋak nhom ic."
P5	Go Thɔk Muorŋak nhom lueel, "lɔn na le raan dhal e wɛt kenic aka dhil  der."
P6	Na le bɛn akol bi Muorŋak nhom dɛt bɛn kuany. @@Go kɛ Aŋɔu kuany.
P7	Ku Thɔk ecii wic lɔn bi Aŋɔu ya Muorŋak nhom. @@"Ɣɛn e muorŋak nhom," go Thɔk lueel ke ci puou riek.
P8	Thɔk e kec ciam cak kaŋ nyiai.@@Go Weŋ thiec, "Yeŋo buk loi?"
P9	Go laai ku diɛt jal jam eben kam ken. @@Ku riek ke puoth tene Thɔk.
P10	Go Joŋ lueel, "Ɣɛn eye raande ta waar ye yen Muorŋak nhom."
P11	Go Amaal lueel, "Nhim cien dheeŋ ake ya gam ten miethke waar e yen Mourŋak nhom."
P12	Go Dier dhiau, "Ku lueel waar ye yen Muorŋak nhom, ka ɣɛn eye duomde tit."
P13	Ku lueel Amaal, "Ci Thɔk tak lɔn bi yen a Muorŋak nhom akolriec eben?"
P14	Go laai mɛt ku lueel ka lɔn dhil Thɔk ciam dhɛɛth pan Muorŋak nhom cɔl Aŋɔu.
P15	Laai aci bɛn Ku yok kɛ Thɔk ke rɛɛr. @@Thɔk aci lueel, "lɔn bi yen a Muorŋak nhom akol riec eben."
P16	"Yin abuk der ku yieth ku yin tene Muorŋak nhom yam."
P17	Go Weŋ Thɔk thɛl e luŋ Muorŋak nhom yic. @@Go abɛk puoth miɛt.
P18	Ke ye thok kuec ciath kan tɛ thel kek.
