desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Sipho aendete tauni na mwiitu yinya.
P2	Mwiitu yinya wake asyaisye ngua ndukani. Sipho niuboikie.
P3	Sipho asyaise kindu wivata nakyo.
P4	Siatu ni kuteewa.
P5	Aelile syonthe.
P6	Mwiitu inyia wake amuyiye siatu sya muvila.
P7	Kwa vaati nzeo alikie kiwanzani kya mauu isaani! Ekite siatu syake sya muvila wa mauu.
P8	Luckily he got to the soccer field on time! He is wearing his new soccer shoes.
