page_text:

P1	I'm Sheba lona . I have a little brother named Ian barter.we play together,read together,fight together and do much more.i like oranges and he also does
P2	I have a cat named monte.he is orange in colour and loves fish.we play catch catch together.and he loves the outdoors alot.we cuddle together at times.and we always feel safe
P3	These are my friends. My best friends are catrie,elen,Mortimer and Jessy. They always keep me in cheerful mood and we have never fought. We love to play football especially mortimer.i like them
P4	I have a nice blue sky above my home. I always see planes fly with grace. In the bright sky.blue birds always fly as group and never get separated,like friends. I like my world
P5	We friends love swimming . Especially elen. He falls in love with swimming.we play pool ball . We talk and chat about school. And after swimming we have some tea at my mom's home. These are the best days o can say
P6	And last of all........... I ♥️ my life
