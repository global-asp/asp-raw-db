title:	Akanhana  ka  Parna

artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Omulenzi omuto ali mu kwaya nte dhaghaka olwo akanhana akalungi kaazaalwa.

page_text:

P1	Parma  yali  mulenzi  mughanvu,  mutono  ng'ali  mw'idaala  lya  kusatu. Yayendanga  okuvaala  Kabuuti  endeeyi  endhirugavu Dhaadhaa  we  ye yamugha.
P2	Mu  kiseera  eky'eigandaalo,  Parna  yalabiriranga  ente  dh'abazaire  be.
P3	Lwali  lulala, nga  Parna    bwe  yali  alikulabirira  ente, amaadhi   g'atandiika  okugwa.
P4	Mu  kiseera  ekyo, ente  ya   Ise  y'asinga  okwenda, Kairugavu, yazaala. Akanhana  kaali  nga  n'akasinga bulungi  Parma  kaatabonangaku.
P5	Parna  yali    musanhufu  okulabirira  Kairugavu n'akanhana,  yerabira  ente  edhisigairegho.
P6	Y'aloghooza,"Baaba  y'ansuubiza  okumpa  akanhana     bwe  kaba  akakazi."
P7	Bwe  yali  ali kuloota  musana,  omukono  ogw'amaani  gwa  musitula. Parna  yabona  Dhaadhaawe   Teteyo me     yaidhukira,"Dhaadha, ente dhiri  gha?"
P8	Dhaadha  Teteyo  yairamu,"Mama wo ayagaine  ente  dhonadhona  mu  Kigo, okutoolaku  Kairugavu,  niiwe.  Atiire  era  n'okuntuma  ndidhe   nkunone."
P9	Parna  ng'asamalirire,  yakobera   Dhaadhawe nga Kairugavu  bwezaire  akanhana  akalungi. Dhaadha Teteyo yakoba,"Ndidha kutwala  akanhana  Iwe  ni Kairugavu munonereze."
P10	Buli  muntu yali musanhufu  okubona  Parna, Kairugavu  n'akanhana  nga  bali miraala.
P11	Parna  yabuuza  Ise,"Onandikiriza  n'afuna  akate  nga  bwe  wasuubiza?"
P12	"Ii, Mutabane  wange, Koonakoona  kako. Ekisuubizo kisuubizo."
