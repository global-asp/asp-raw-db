page_text:

P1	Nkunda amashyamba!
P2	Icyumweru gishize, nagiye mu ishyamba rya Kibale.
P3	Nabonye imizabibu, Muvule, imikindo n'ibiti by'amabara meza.
P4	Nabonye inguge, ibitera n'inkende.
P5	Nabonye inyoni zitanduknye. Nabonye imisambi, za kagoma n'ibihunyira.
P6	Narasetse cyane igihe nabonaga inzovu nini ifite umurizo mugufi.
P7	Nabonye ubwoko butandukanye bw'inyamaswa, inyoni n'ibimera.
P8	Ishyamba rya Kibale ni ahantu heza ho gusura. Nkunda amashyamba! Nawe se?
