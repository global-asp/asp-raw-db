P1	Nze Ana Maria.
P2	Taata wange ye Agaba.
P3	Maama anjagala.
P4	Taata ayagala amata.
P5	      Amata amaka.
P6	Talaba agaba mata maka.
P7	Male talaba wala.
P8	Abaana babala amagi.
P9	Namata asala emboga n'ennyama.
P10	Ani ayagala amasavu?
P11	Ate Namagala asaba.
P12	Asaba asamba akapiira.
