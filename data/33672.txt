page_text:

P1	Mahiku akhalayi nikhuuru nimoha naashinama a manyaka yahuupuwela opaka efesta etokweene, osulu wamwaako. @@Yaawoopa ashinamaru yaawo@a manyaka.
P2	Ekupa yiiwalaaya elociwaka efesta. Yeeyo yaahuupuwela, "Miyo nkaano nnyaka nimoha mano. Keere esheeni?"
P3	Mmeelo mwaya yaahupuwela wi ekushe manyaka ehele vamuru epateyevo ni epothi.
P4	Yaaheerano yeela yonnaaya wi yaaheemereryiwa ovolowa ofestani yaasiveliwa yoonaka wi yootepa ovyaka.@@Yookacamiha tiila: efesta ene yeele yeereya wansuwani. Efesta yaapacenryaaya, nsuwa naapacerya oviha, epothi tho epacerya otothowa manyaka emora.
P5	Ashinama yaale amanyaka yaananariwa, eloca eriki: "Taani yoole ohirino manyaka? Ntakhara sheeni orwa vaava?"
P6	Mwene aashinama yaale aahaaruma wi ephare ekupa yeele erihele vachayani. Ashinama oothene yiiwalaaya masu a mwene yaaheereno yeeyo ene.
P7	Yaaphara ekupa yeele erihela vachayani.@@Ekupa muhaawa waya! Yaamorenlaaya vachayani yaamaninhe mpichi aya vachayani, ohala otuturuwelanne.
P8	Yenreno yeela, ashinama oothene yaakookela ofestani. Yaawo yaalya yiina ohiyu woothene. Mwene aahakalala.
P9	Ohuukhuwe woona mpichi wa ekupa ori wootuturuwanelana, waavinyenrye vaavale ophiyerya olelo.
