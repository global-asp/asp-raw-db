page_text:

P1	Kabali and Kateera are friends.
P2	Kabali has a cat. Kateera has a rabbit.
P3	Kabali plays with his cat every day.
P4	Kateera feeds his rabbit twice a day.
P5	Kateera's rabbit produced six babies. Two rabbits were black. Two rabbits were brown. Two rabbits were white.
P6	Kabali's cat produced three babies. One kitten was male. Two kittens were female.
P7	Kateera sold four of the baby rabbits. He used the money to buy books and pencils for school.
P8	Kabali did not sell any kittens. The people in the village did not want cats.
