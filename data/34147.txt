desc:	Umongikati uvakashele esikolweni utofundzisa ngeligciwane le-Khorona.

page_text:

P1	Thishela wasatisa kutsi silindzele tivakashi letichamuka etikweni letemphilo esikoleni.Sonkhe salangatelela kutsi basiphatseleni.
P2	Wefika umongikati wasemtfolamphilo wangakitsi.Wasikhulula kutsi lomuhla aketelanga kutosigoma kodvwa kutosifundzisa.
P3	Ube sewuyachaza kutsi kuneligciwane lelibitwa ngekutsi yiKhorona, livumbuke eveni lase China, libhebhetseka ngesivinini lesikhulu.
P4	Wachaza kutsi litfolakala nome etukwani, futsi alinakhambi kwanyalo.Setfuka kabi kutsi ngabe umhlaba sewuyabhubha.
P5	Wasicinisekisa kutsi kwetfu nje kugeza tandla ngemanti nensipho nobe ufutse ngesibulala-gciwane.
P6	Umongikati usifundzise kutsi uma sinetimphawu temkhuhlane asivakashele emtfolamphilo losedvute.Uma ngabe siyakhwehlela nobe siyatsimula sitimbonye.
P7	Sifundze nekutsi Uma sinetinkhomba temkhuhlane nobe sinakekela sigulane lesinjalo asifake sivikela-mlomo nemphumulo singetuhabula.
P8	Nanome likhambi kusete laleligciwane kepha umongikati utsi alikhoni kumelana nesimo selitulu lapho lishisa likhipha umkhovu etjeni.
