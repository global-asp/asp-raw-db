desc:	The story is about exploring with children what they would do if they were ...

page_text:

P1	If I were a bank manager,  I would be giving money to everybody.
P2	If I were a vehicle,  I would transport people free of charge.
P3	If I were a hunter,  I would hunt for animals which have no hair.
P4	If I were a rat,  I would grow fat and chase the cat.
P5	If I were a baby,  I would cry for only expensive things.
P6	If I were a cat,  I would be eating rats to grow fatter.
P7	If I were a hen,  I would be laying golden eggs for people.
P8	If I were a beggar,  I would not accept coins.
P9	If I were a doctor,  I would treat people with slaps!
P10	If I were a priest,  I would be taking all people to heaven.
P11	If I were a mosquito,  I would be sleeping under a mosquito net.
