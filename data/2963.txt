title:	Engatto Empya

artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Luganda

desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Kigozi agenda mu kibuga ne mwannyina.
P2	Mwannyina agenda atunuulira engoye mu maduuka. Kigozi ebyo tabyagala.
P3	Kigozi alengera ekintu ky'ayagala.
P4	Engatto ze basaze ebbeeyi.
P5	Kigozi zonna azegezaamu.
P6	Mwannyina,  n'amugulira engatto ezisambirwamu omupiira.
P7	Lwaki banguyirizza okuddayo eka?
P8	Eky'omukisa Kigozi yatuuka ku kisaawe ky'ompiira mu budde,  ng'ayambadde engatto ze empya.
