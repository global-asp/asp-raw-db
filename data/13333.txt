desc:	A poem about a girl who loves to dance. Her teacher finds this irritating

page_text:

P1	Kwakunentombazana eyayikwazi ukudansa.
P2	Noma uthisda efundisa wayedansa.
P3	Noma eskhaya wayezidansela ephula ejabulile endansela nogogo wakhe.
P4	U Sindiswa wayedansela nabantu ehlolo. Ngemphela wayekutha futhi ekwazi ukudansa.
P5	Wagcina esedume kakhulu izithombe zakhe zazibekwe ema bhodini amakhulu edolobheni.
