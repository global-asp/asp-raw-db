desc:	This story introduces numbers as well as colours. (Also available at Level 2.).

page_text:

P1	7 colours in a rainbow.
P2	1 red.
P3	He wears a red shirt.
P4	2 orange.
P5	What is orange in this picture?
P6	3 yellow.
P7	What is yellow in this picture?
P8	4 green.
P9	What is green in this picture?
P10	5 blue.
P11	What is blue in this picture?
P12	6 indigo.
P13	What is indigo in this picture?
P14	7 violet.
P15	Violet is the best colour! What colour do you like?
P16	What are the 7 colours of a rainbow?
