page_text:

P1	Miŋguo pugu Ŋìe ni mbɔ pa taannu
P2	Pugu ni ŋgə̄ɨ lɨʼɨ tuŋ bo.
P3	Miŋguo ni mbɔ ŋgaŋ wɛ̄iŋ pu.
P4	Ŋìe ni niʼiŋ trɛi ŋkhwì.
P5	Miŋguo ni mbɨgɨ túa.
P6	Miŋguo ni mmī Ŋìe.
P7	Nnɛ, Miŋguo pāoŋ pugu mǔuŋ Ŋìe.
P8	Ŋìe kə̄ɨ puŋ Miŋguo, "Ghɛ̄rɛ a ma Mǔuŋ a! Miŋguo mmī a!"
P9	Lǔŋ Ŋìe ni mbɨgɨ.@@Miŋguo gū ghɨ̌nɔ.
P10	Miŋguo jɛ̄ kugwɛi nu.
P11	Miŋguo kugwɛi Ŋìe mfuʼu.
P12	Miŋguo pugu pa Ŋìe pɔ ni kìpɨna ndwɛ.
