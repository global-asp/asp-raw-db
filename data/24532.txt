title:	The boy who liked to laugh

desc:	A boy who likes to laugh gets stolen by a joyless wizard.

page_text:

P1	There was once a boy.
P2	He liked to laugh.
P3	He used to laugh so much even the birds in the sky would stop and stare.
P4	One day he walked alone in the forest.
P5	He was taken by a wizard.
P6	This wizard had no joy.
P7	He had been hearing the boy laughing and wanted some of his happiness.
P8	The boy was sad for a while.
P9	After sometime he remembered a joke his mom had told him.
P10	He started to laugh.
P11	The wizard was angry but when he heard the joke,
P12	he started to laugh too.
P13	Then he took the boy home.
P14	The boy who liked to laugh brought the sad wizard joy.
