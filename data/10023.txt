page_text:

P1	UNogwaja wayengumngani omkhulu wenkawu.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga unogwaja wathi: Masihambe siyofuna into esizoyidla. Inkawu yavuma,  kodwa wayesezigwaze ngowakhe.
P3	Bahamba baya emasimini ombila baqala badla umbila.
P4	Kodwa unogwaja ohlakaniphile wayesekhulume nabanikazi bepulazi,  ukuthi abambe inkawu bese eyikhombisa ukuthi iyisilima.
P5	Unogwaja ohlakaniphile waqala wamemeza: Amasela! Amasela! Umbila wakho usuhambe wonke! Wase egxuma ebaleka.
P6	Ngokuphazima kweso,  abalimi base befikile bathola inkawu izama ukubaleka. Kodwa inomsila omude kanti wawumbambezela.
P7	Abalimi bayilandela bememeza: Ima! Ima! Sikubonile,  uwena inkawu.
P8	Kodwa bamthola base beqala bemshaya.
P9	Akakwazanga ukubekezelela ubuhungu wase ethi: Benginomngani wami unogwaja. Uyena ongimemile.
P10	Kodwa abalimi bayinquma umsila base beyitshela ukuthi: Hamba uyotshela umngan wakho ukuthi sikunqume umsila!
P11	Base bahamba baya endlini kanogwaja apheka umsila wenkawu kuze bazowudla.
P12	Emva kwesidlo unogwaja wabuza inkawu ukuthi iyazi ukuthi beyidlani. Isilima senkawu saphendula:.
P13	Unogwaja wamtshela ukuthi ubedla umsila wakhe wase ebaleka. Kusukela lapho baphenduka izitha.
