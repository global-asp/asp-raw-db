title:	Goɗɗo mo yiiɗi jooɗugo feere maako
author:	Albert  Blangar
artist:	Little Zebra Books and Trevor Kellog
lang:	Fulfulde Adamawa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40409.png
desc:	Ɗum taaria goorko ferre mo yiiɗa jooɗugo les goɗɗo. Nden goorko e debbo maako dilli jooɗaago nder saare daaynde bee yimɓe fuu.

page_text:

P1	Woodi no taaria goɗɗo feere mo yiiɗa jooɗugo bee hedidiraaɓe maako.@@Nden o yiiɗa bo debbo maako yiila saanugo hedidiraaɓe.
P2	Nden yannde feere o hooci anniya yahugo ladde daayɗum bee saare maako, daayɗum bee hedidiraaɓe maako.
P3	O ɗon vurta fajjira fuu nden o ɗon warta bee kikiɗe.
P4	Nden yannde go, gorko o wartay saare bee law.@@Fowru laari ko goorko o waɗata.
P5	Fowru yalti faɗɗi haa dammugal saare.
P6	Debbo numi ɗum goorko maako, o maɓɓiti damugal.@@Fowru diwi dow maako ƴakki mo.
P7	Nde goorko o warti saare, o laari fowru ƴakki debbo maako, nden o tulla ko o waɗata.
P8	Ɗo fuu ngam o yiiɗa jooɗugo bee hedidiraaɓe maako.
