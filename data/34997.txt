title:	Pilikisha ifyo umbili wangu guyoba

artist:	Angie and  Upesh

desc:	Upilikishemo lumo ifyo umbili wako gukubula?

page_text:

P1	Mwishugu nthapobukako ku sukulu.@@Kuli holide!
P2	Mwishugu nthapoketelelapo vidiyo.@@Amagesi gope gazimile.
P3	Nosi nkhupobombangaci?@@Mwishugu nkhupogupilikisha umbili wangu!
P4	Ichakwanda, nkhulonda nje myee.@@Ukuti mphilikishe umbili wangu.
P5	Ena nosi khupilika utuya kwangu.@@Nkhutuyila mukati na panja, mukati na panja.
P6	Kangi ninkhapangisha ukutuya kwangu icongo. Ssssssssssss! @@Kangi nu tontha. Mmmmmmm.
P7	Nosi nkhupilika indumbula yangu yukung'untha!@@Kuukuumu, kuukuumu, kuuu kuuumu.
P8	Ka ninkhapangisha ukuti ndumbula yangu yituye fiyo pamo yituye ni congo?@@Ena, linga ninkhanyela mwanya na pasi kofyana makumi mabili.
P9	Keta nosi, indumbula yangu yutuya fiyo fiyo.
P10	Linga nabika inyobe shangu pa ciboko ningapilika ukuntha.
P11	Nkhwipilika nemwene useka.@@Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Nkhwipilika nemwene ulila.@@Hiihiiii hiii!
P13	Nkhwipilika nemwene ukuba maboko.@@Pwa, pwa, pwa.
P14	Nkhwipilika nemwene mulwanda lwangu ukuluma!@@Gulu, gulu, gulu.
P15	Ulwanda lwangu lukumbula ukuti, "Ndyesha!"
P16	Mphuno shangu shu nusha ikeke lilyo umama wangu obeka mukhicini.@@Kangi nosi nkhulonda mphilikishe figamu fyangu fyutafunya keke.
