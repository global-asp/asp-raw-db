P1	Once apon a man paul saw a woman sitting out when they asked what was the problems he said lots of people died by a virus.
P2	He checked the animals and some were dead.
P3	He made a vaccine and made a team the son of a farmer,a doctor,his friend and the son of a builder.
P4	He went up a tower and connected the vaccine with the laptop that gives data and first animals got cured.
P5	Everyone also got cured the end.
