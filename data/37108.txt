page_text:

P1	Lowu i ndyangu wa mina.@@Malume, khazi, manana, vasesi vambirhi (lava tshamelaka ro ambala ku fana), boti na mina!
P2	Lowu i ndyangu wa mina hi ri bazini ku ya vona swiharhi.@@Endzeni ka bazi ku na hahani, manana, boti, vasesi vambirhi (lava tshamelaka ro ambala ku fana), na mina!
P3	Hi vonile swiharhi hi ri bazini.
P4	Hi vonile nhutlwa.
P5	Hi vonile na mhala.
P6	Hi vonile nghala.
P7	Hi vonile ndlopfu.@@Hi hlekile, hi tlhela hi hleka ndlopfu, hikuva…
P8	…ndlopfu a yi humele emurhini!
