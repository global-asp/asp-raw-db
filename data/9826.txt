page_text:

P1	Ifikile imini ekade ixelwa. Kuza kudlalwa! I-Chiefs ne-Aces ebaleni! Yiza kubukela! Yiza nevuvuzela yakho!
P2	Makhosi amahle,  indebe mayilale ekhaya! Ndifuna sisebenzisane,  ukuze siyohlule i-Aces. Kufuneka sihlasele ngobuchule,  siqaphele sabelane ngebhola.
P3	Gqithisa,  gqithisa Portia,  Modise! Nqanda,  nqanda Jomo! Nantso ke,  nantso ke,  mayiye ezipalini! Po-o-o-o! Po-o-o-o! Po-o-o-o! Yavakala ivuvuzela!
P4	Nidlale kakuhle Makhosi amahle. Nihlasele ngobuchule. Niqaphele,  kwaye nabelene ngebhola. Nisebenzisene.
P5	Ezandleni! 'Ezandleni Makhosi,  ezandleni!' watsho encoma umqeqeshi u-Coach.
P6	Owu! Sidiniwe. Kodwa,  bekumnandi ukuziqhelisa. Siza kudlala nini kanene Coach?
P7	Ifikile imini ebikad'ixelwa! U-Coach usiphathele isinxibo. Halala Makhosi,  halala! Kuza kudlalwa! Ngeyethu indebe,  ngeyethu! Po-o-o-o-o! Po-o-o-o! Po-o-o-o-o! Yavakala ivuvuzela.
P8	Asisesihle isinxibo sethu! Siyabulela Coach. Ungumqeqeshi wokwenyani!
