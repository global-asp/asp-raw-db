desc:	Explore how we feel about different things and people.

page_text:

P1	Áma ásị́ la ị́jọ́ ũꞌbí kí ĩtrĩkã.
P2	Ma adru ãyĩkõ sĩ mâ ị̃dẹ́pị drĩ ãmaní áꞌdígó zã ĩndró sĩ.
P3	Ma ĩtrĩkã la ma aza-azâ ádrĩ avá mâ wọ̃rị̂ be.
P4	Ma uꞌá cãndí sĩ má átépi drĩ jọ lá séndẽ ĩꞌdidrị́ ꞌdáyụ.
P5	Ma lẽtáŋá ĩtrĩká má ãndrẽ drĩ áma amvu yãmbị̂ sĩ.
