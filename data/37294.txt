title:	U ya ḓoroboni khulwane
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mutukana u khou ya u dalela malume wawe ḓoroboni khulwane lwa u tou thoma!

page_text:

P1	Hu na vhathu vhanzhi vhuimabisi ḓoroboni ine nda dzula khayo.
P2	Mutshimbidzi wa bisi o huwelela a ri, "I khou ya ḓoroboni khulwane! I ḓo takuwa zwino!"
P3	Vhathu vho sukumedzana uri vha ṋamele bisi.
P4	Vhafumakadzi vha re na vhana vho vha vho dzula fhasi.@@Lwendo lwo vha lu tshi ḓo vha lulapfu.
P5	Nṋe ndo dzula tsini na fasiṱere tsini na vhaṅwe munna.
P6	Ndo humbula uri, "Ndi khou ya ḓoroboni khulwane! Ndi ḓo ṱuvha hayani."
P7	Ho vha hu na vharengisi vhanzhi vhane vha khou rengisa zwithu zwavho.
P8	Vhaṅwe vhaṋameli vha bisi vho renga zwithu.@@Ndo vha ndi si na tshelede ya u renga na tshithu na tshithihi.
P9	Ndo vha ndi si na tshelede ya u renga na tshithu na tshithihi.
P10	Vharengisi vho sukumedzana u itela uri vha tse bisini.
P11	Ndo ḓivhudzisa uri, "Ndi ḓo vhuya lini?"
P12	Ho vha hu tshi khou fhisa bisini.@@Ndo mbo ḓi bonya maṱo.
P13	Ndo vha ndi tshi khou humbula mme anga na murathu wanga vho salaho hayani.
P14	Nga zwiṱuku nga zwiṱuku, khofhe dza mbo ḓi fara.
P15	Nga murahu ha awara nnzhi, ndo mbo ḓi karuwa.@@Vhathu vhanzhi vho vha vho no tsa.
P16	Ho vha hu tshifhinga tshau wana nnḓu ya malume anga.
