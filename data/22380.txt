title:	Kungabya……
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere and Jenipher  Kabugho
artist:	Wiehan de Jager, Cathy Feek, Rebecca Riley, Marleen Visser, Catherine Groenewald, Jano Strydom, Alice Toich, Karen Lilje, Natalie Propa and Rob Owen

desc:	Olhughano lhuhambangene okwa ekyabanabalere bangakolire kubabya…….

page_text:

P1	Kungabya mukulhu omwakithongole
ekikakolha neribika esyambulho, nangabere iningahereraya bulimundu yesyambulho.
P2	Kungabya ngumbaghali, nangabere
ingatwalira abandu okwabusa.
P3	Kungabya muhighi, nangabere
ingahigha ebisoro ebithawithe obweya.
P4	Kungabya mbeba, nanganenehire
ingabinga epusu.
P5	Kungabya lhumekeke, nangabere
ingalirira ebindu obyobughuli bunene.
P6	Kungabya pusu, nangabere
ingalya esyambeba erineneha.
P7	Kungabya ngoko, nangabere
ingalekerera abandu amaya awomubongo.
P8	Kungabya musabiriri, sinangaberengaligha
ebinusu.
P9	Kungabya muthahwa, nangabere
ingathambira abandu namakofu.
P10	Kungabya muhereri, nangabere
ingatwalha abandubosi elhubulha.
P11	Kungabya, nangabere.
