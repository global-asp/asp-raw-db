page_text:

P1	Ga Giwa! Ga Giwa!
P2	Wannan Giwar ita kadai ce a cikin dajin nan.
P3	Wannan dajin sunan shi Kibale.
P4	Giwar ta je shan ruwa a rafi.
P5	Yara na wanka a rafin.
P6	Yara sun ruga suna cewa, "Ga giwa! Ga giwa."
