page_text:

P1	Amara yali wamyaka
  mwendha. Yajja kwitendekero lya kerema. Amasomo geyali asingha kwendha gali, Lungereza,Science nee’Enkolagana yoo’buntu. Yayendha kubamuwoleleza bwalikula. Amara yali ayendha yino ebisolo.
P2	  Amara yali aba
  nimamawe, Margy. Yali mukozi ino
  owebyobulimi.Baba wa Amara yaffa nga
  Amara alina emyaka etano.
P3	Amara bweyali ngatali
  kwiisomero,yamalanga ebisela bye nii Mbisa embuzi.Yagilongosa yagilisa.
P4	Amara yalina nekyokuzanisa,Simba.Mama wee yali tayendha yino mbwa,aye yaleka Amara akume Simba.Buli
  Simba bweyamusumbuwanga,yali alekana,
  “Jja nga nkali kusamba wabweru.”Amara yawulira bubii.
P5	 Kulwomukaga olulala, balina abageni okuva mukhikunsu kyabakazii.Amara yali musanuffu ntii myikwano gyamamawee gyali gyidye nabanabiyiibwe.Balibasobola okuzana
  omupiira ogwee’bigere.
P6	 Mamawee yali affumbye emele ekyikunsu kyabakyala gyebayendha.Amara yayidhukizibwa
  mamawee okukuma Simba nga mwigalize.Yabwoyigokira abantu beyali teyidhi.
P7	  Amara tiyayigariza Simba bulungii. Abagenyi bewunyya okubona embwa mukisenge.Mama Oto asked, “lwakhii oyiikiliza embwa okwiingiira mundha munumba?”Mama wa Amara yayeta Amara okwiingira afulumye Simba wabweru.
P8	Amara tiyawulila mamawee
  ngha yeeta kubangha bali balikuzana.
P9	Amara bweyajja munumba,
  yayeeta Simba wabweeru.Simba
  yali muwulilize eli Amara.Oluvayiinuma lwekiwulo, abagenyi bakoba, “Ffena twidha kubisa bulungii embwa dhaiffe nga Amara.”Tudhi bonyabonya atenga dhamugaso eeno
P10	Oluvayinuma mama wa Amara
  yetondhera Amara okubangha omunomyi noo’mukambwe eli Simba. Amara yalimusanuffu ntii mamawee yayikiiliza embwa yee oluvayiinuma.Amara waba ali kwiisomero mamawee alias samba.Myikwano gyamama wee gyeze okuba abalungii eli embwa dhaiibwe.
