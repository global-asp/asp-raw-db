title:	Apwoyo Gin Ki Laluu  Gu Pitu Cam
author:	Maurice Lamony
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Acholi

desc:	Kede bed ni gi tye lurem

page_text:

P1	Yang con adada, apwoyo gin ki laluu ono gi tye lurem ma pire tek.
P2	Apwoyo owacu ki laluu ni, “Wa cak pwur karacelo.”
P3	Apwoyo owaci ni gi pit anyogi, gi pok tice apoka.
P4	Apwoyo owaco ki laluu ni, “Tica me ryemo winyo tek adada.”
P5	Laluu oyee me pwuru poto kene.
P6	Ma Laluu tye ka pwutu doo, Apwoyo obedo mot ka wer.
P7	Ma dong anyogi oteki, Apwoyo oryemo winyo ki yom cwiny.
P8	Apwoyo owaci, “An mega cam ma I wi ngom, megi
  ma I te ngom”
P9	Apwoyo okayo anyogi weng ci ogwoko.
P10	Laluu onoo lwit keken ite ngom.
P11	Laluu onyango ni ki bwure. Okecho ma pe laare.
P12	Gu pito layata. Laluu owaci, “An abi kayo ma I wi ngom.”
P13	Gu tiyo i poto karacel, ki wer.
P14	Ikare me kaju, Laluu okayo pot layata keken.
P15	Apwoyo okayo layata ma dongo ma dwong adada okero idero ne.
P16	Laluu oryemo Apwoyo me wang kwo. Te lurem oket.
