title:	Olunaku endhuba lwe yaja
author:	Rebecca Ndhibogeza

desc:	Ologhooza ki ekibaagho singa endhuba yabulagho.

page_text:

P1	Maama musana yasalagho okukyalira mugandaghe. Mugandaghe mwezi aviira ku mbali kwa bire." Ndha kwira nga bukaali," musana yakobera ebire.
P2	We yavaagho, ensozi dhataagho eiwembo lyadho.
P3	Empewo yali ni okukeeyana ni emiti. Emiti gyalumwa endhala. Gyasuula amakoola gaadho buli wantu mu kifo ekyo.
P4	Ekire kyatandiika okwemuluguunia. Yafuuka ya kivuuvu.
P5	Ebire byali binakughavu okubona bino byonabyona. Byatandiika okulira. Ghaaligho amaziga mangi inho.
P6	Ensi yoonayoona yatandiika okudimira ghansi wa amaadhi.
P7	Waabitagho esaawa ntono, ku luuyi lule, endhuba yali neetegefu okuleka muganda ghaayo mwezi. Yaginuuna omunwa yaagisiibula yaaja waka.
P8	Ekire kyali kisanhufu okubona nga endhuba yeyali afuusiisa eya bbululu. Ensozi dha taaku engoye enungi.
P9	Empewo yaja okutenduka. Eamiti gya golola ensaga dhaadho gyataaku akamwenumwenu.
P10	Ebireri byali bisanhufu inho okubona maama musana kabiri. Byaja okwezaanhira.
P11	Ebirime ebito bingi byavaayo mu nsi byakoba,"muli mutya." Ensi yoonayoona yamyansula.
P12	Maama Ndhuba yalaga ekitangaala buli ghantu. "Nabakoba nti nda kwira," yayaka.
