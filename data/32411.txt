title:	Zama o etsa hantle
author:	Maria Vaz
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32411.png
desc:	Pale ena e bontsha hore jwaloka ha re kgalemela bana

page_text:

P1	Moena waka o robala bosiu haholo.@@Ke tsoha ka matjeke hobane ke etsa hantle.
P2	Ke nna ya bulang difensetere hore mahlasedi a letsatsi a kene.
P3	"O mphatlalatsane ya ka," ho rialo mme.
P4	Kamehla ke a itlhapisa, ha ke hloke thuso.
P5	Ke hlapa ka metsi a batang, le sesepa sa ho hlatswa diaparo.
P6	Mme o re, "O se lebale ho borosola meno."@@"Nkeke ka lebala mme," ke nna eo.
P7	Ha ke qeta ho hlapa, ke ya ho dumedisa ntatemoholo le rakgadi.
P8	Ebe ke a ikapesa.@@"Ke se ke hodile jwale mme," ke nna eo.
P9	Ke se ke kgona ho konopela jeresi le ho bofa dieta tsaka.
P10	Ke etsa bonnete ba hore moenaka o tseba ditaba tsohle tsa sekolo.
P11	Ka phaposing ke sebetsa ka thata.
P12	Ke etsa tsohle tse ntle kamehla, empa seo ke ratang ka ho fetisisa ke ho bapala.
