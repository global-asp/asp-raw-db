title:	Ani Afuka Kubuliri?
author:	Stellah Nakasango
artist:	Rob Owen, Felicity Bell, Jesse Breytenbach and Melany Pietersen
lang:	Luganda
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21077.png
desc:	Omuwaala afuka kubuliri naye aba awayiriza mutoowe Anna. Lumu mama we yasula na Anna namuleka yeka kinti kyekimu nekibaawo.

page_text:

P1	Buzibwe neera era bukutte.
P2	wemaala okulya geenga mu buliri ne mutoo wange Anna.
P3	Ntya nyoo lwakuba bulikiro nfuuka kubuliri.
P4	Buli wendoota, nzukuka nfuuse kubuliri.
P5	Mumangu ago nva mukifo kyange nenebaka ewa Anna.
P6	Kumakya, nzukuka nazukuka mangu ne ndeka Anna mubuliri.
P7	Maama wabuuza ani eya fuuse kubuliri, ngamba nti Anna.
P8	Olwadako maama yasalawo okusula ne Anna mu buliiri bwe.
P9	Ekyembi, kinti kye kimu ekyabaawo.
P10	Maama nakizuula nti Anna ta fuka kubuliiri.
