page_text:

P1	UKhotso ukhwele idonki. Idonki yakhe iluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. UKhotso nedonki yakhe batya kunye.
P2	UMakhulu umaThabo uyapheka. Upheka ngembiza emnyama. UMakhulu umaThabo nekati yakhe batya kunye.
P3	Inkomo kaNeo iluhlaza okwengca. Inkomo inyathele uNeo elunyaweni. Uwisa ibhakethe lakhe lobisi.
P4	Utata uPhakane unehashe elimhlophe. Ihashe lakhe lomelele kakhulu. Ihashe lakhe litsala ikhuba lokulima lilodwa.
P5	UThabang uqhuba imoto yakhe. Uhamba nenja yakhe. Inja kaThabang ingcolise imoto ngelindle!
P6	Idonki eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka,  inkomo eluhlaza okwengca,  ihashe elimnyama,  kunye nekati kamakhulu zingena kwimoto kaTata uThabang.
