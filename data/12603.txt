page_text:

P1	This is Lekishon. Lekishon is calling his dog.
P2	Lekishon and his dog are going to graze the animals. The sun is hot.
P3	Lekishon and his dog are thirsty and hungry.
P4	They go to look for water.
P5	Lekishon loses track of his cows.
P6	Lekishon starts to cry.
P7	He decides to go home with his dog.
P8	The cows found the way home! Lekishon and his dog are very happy.
