title:	Zwiambaro zwanga zwa tshikolo
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila

lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Tshiṱorini itshi ṅwana u edzisa zwiambaro zwi re zwihulusa khae

page_text:

P1	Rokho ya mukomana wanga ndi khulwanesa kha nṋe!
P2	Dzhesi ya khaladzi anga ndi khulwanesa kha nṋe.
P3	Bege ndi khulwane. @@Fhedzi a si khulwanesa!
P4	Iyi bannda ya kale ndi ṱhukhusa kha nṋe.
P5	Hoyu ndi muṅadzi wa mupopi wanga.
P6	Saizi ya sogisi dzanga ndi yone. @@Fhedzi muvhala a si wone!
P7	Zwienda zwanga zwiswa khezwi.
P8	Zwi tou nndingana kwakwakwa!
