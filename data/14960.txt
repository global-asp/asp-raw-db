page_text:

P1	Namhlanje andiyi esikolweni. Yiholide!
P2	Namhlanje andizokubukela mabonakude. Kuhleli kungekho mbane!
P3	Ndizakwenza ntoni kanene? Namhlanje ndizakumamela umzimba wam!
P4	Kuqala, kufuneka ndithule. Ndithule cwaka. Ukuze ndikwazi ukumamela umzimba wam.
P5	Ewe, ngoku ndiyawuva umphefumlo wam. Phakathi, phandle, phakathi, phandle.
P6	Kwaye ndiyakwazi ukuphefumlela phezulu. Haaaaaaaaa! Naphantsi. Mmmmmmm.
P7	Kwaye ndiyakuva nokubetha kwentliziyo yam! 
Doodom, doodoom, dooo dooom.
P8	Ingaba ndiyakwazi ukwenza intliziyo yam ibethe ngokukhawuleza okanye ibethele phezulu? Ewe,  ngokuthi ndixhumaxhume amatyeli angamashumi amabini.
P9	Jonga ngoku, intliziyo yam ibetha ngokukhawuleza.
P10	Kwaye xa ndibeka iminwe yam esihlahleni sam, ndiyakuva ukubetha kwentliziyo yam!
P11	Kwaye ndiyaziva ndihleka. Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Ndilila. Aaaaaaaaaaa!
P13	Ndiqhwaba. Qhwa, qhwa, qhwa.
P14	Ngapha koko, ndiyasiva isisu sam sigquma. Grrr, grrr, grrr.
P15	Sisithi, "Ndityise, ndityise."
P16	Nempumlo yam iyakwazi ukuva ivumba lamaqebengwane abhakwayo egumbini lopheka likamama.
P17	Kuyonwabisa ukumamela umzimba wam. Kwaye ngoku ndifuna ukuva imihlathi yam ihlafuna.
P18	Nesisu sam sibimbiliza amaqebengwane kamama ashushu kamnandi!
