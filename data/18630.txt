page_text:

P1	Ndakkomana kusobana alutambo amweenzuma.
P2	Ndakkomana kulemba aboodi.
P3	Ndakkomana kubala mabbuku ajisi zifwanikiso.
P4	Ndakkomana kulya micelo yaandeene-andeene.
P5	Ndakkomana kugwasya kung'anda.
P6	Ndakkomana kuswiilizya kutwaano twabaama.
P7	Ndakkomana koona abulo bupati.
P8	Ndakkomana kucita zintu eezi.    Ino yebo ukkomanina kucita nzi?
