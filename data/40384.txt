title:	Ɓinngel geeɗinooyel jam
author:	Hussain Muhammad Also and Mohammed Also
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Fulfulde

desc:	Nder laddude Turkana

page_text:

P1	Inna e ɓiyum nasti nder laynde.@@O yahii ngam ittugo ɓiɓɓe-leɗɗe.
P2	Nder laynde nden o tawi lekki marki ɓenndallo.
P3	O wallini ɓiɗɗo maako ɗaaniiɗo nden o ƴenti lekki.
P4	Goɗɗo gila labbaare wonnde wari. O tawi ɓiɗɗo on. O haayɗini.@@O ƴami hoore maako, "Kadi toye inna ɓiɗɗo o'o?"
P5	O turnii.@@Hooloore koɗol daande maako huylini ɓiɗɗo on.
P6	O acci-mo o fijira e koɗol ngol.@@Ɓiɗɗo e jala fija.
P7	Debbo gonɗo dow lekki laari ley ngam faama ko waɗi ɓiɗɗo on jalata.@@O yi'i koɗo on.
P8	O hultori masin haa o yoofti booro ɓiɓɓe-leɗɗe.
P9	Nden koɗo on laari dow.@@O wi'i, "Taa hultor. No mi fijay tan e ɓiɗɗo maa booɗɗo."
P10	Nden kadi debbo on jippii gila dow lekki.
P11	Koɗo on itti gootol nder koɗi maako.@@O hokki-ngol ɓiɗɗo on. "Ndaa dokkal ngam maa," o wi'i.
P12	"Hootu saare e ɓiɗɗo maa. Wi'u gorko maa egga hoota labbaare ɓurdunde jam. Ɓiɗɗo maa hokkii-yam jam," koɗo on wi'i.
