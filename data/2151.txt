title:	Ayoko Nyaza Ti Okpo Be Ri Ni Ri
author:	Saidi Omar Dramani
artist:	Danielle Bruckert
lang:	Lugbarati
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2151.png
desc:	A story for toddlers about a cow with daytime identity as a soft toy

page_text:

P1	'Di ti ni. Ti 'i bongo si ndriza.
P2	Etu si,  eri ti ti ni.
P3	Eri avi afa 'ba ni 'i baka si anga pi oli alia si ri si.
P4	Kani ku eri avi mupira si.
P5	Ka cha ini si,  eri ti okpo be ni!
P6	Agu eyi ma edri pa e'yo onzi aalia ri si.
P7	A'di 'di 'ba onzi 'dii be.
P8	Te ika mima mile obi ci.
P9	Ti echo emu mi nee ra! Mima a'i afa mini lele ri.
P10	Asiza.
