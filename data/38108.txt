P1	Snaily was going to King Snaily's castle. Meanwhile the king was making potions to infect the guards and the guests. Suddenly something hit the table and knocked a potion over and it touched King Snaily.
P2	King Snaily had a shock and fainted. Snaily came into the castle he didnt see the king on his chair, not in the kitchen but he was on the floor in the science lab. Snaily was shocked by what he saw. He called the rescue service to help.
P3	The rescue service came and took him to the hospital room. A few hours later King snaily woke up and he look horrifying. Snaily was scared of what he saw, he ran with fear back to his village. King Snaily started attacking the doctor's.
