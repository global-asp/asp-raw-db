desc:	Wakaima awonia Jaaka okuva mu buzibu. Aye mu kifo ekya Jaaka okusasula wakaima

page_text:

P1	Jaaka omuyala yali alikuyiiga.
P2	Yaabinga empube.
Empube yeegisa ghansi gheibaale.
P3	Jaaka yasima.
Eibaale lyamugwa ku mikono.
P4	Wakaima yaawulira nga Jaaka alikweta.
Yaalumuka okubona ekibairewo.
P5	Wakaima yaikiriza okuyamba Jaaka.
Jaaka yaasuubiza okugha wakaima emere.
P6	Wakaima yasindika eibaale.
Lyaava ku mikono gya Jaaka.
P7	Waikaima yaasaba empeeraye.
Jaaka yaatiisatiisa okulya wakaima.
P8	Jaaka ni wakaima baafuna omusaadha omuzeeyi asale omusango gwaibwe.
P9	Jaaka yaatiisatiisa okulya n'omusaadha omukaire yeena.
P10	Omusaadha yaakoba, "Kambone eibaale eryo meeni nsale omusango."
P11	Jaaka ni wakaima baatwala omusaadha eri eibaale.
P12	Jaaka yainhonhola eibaale bwerimugwireku.
P13	Omusaadha yaakoba wakaima alage engeri eibaale bwalisindiike.
P14	Eibaale lyaira ku mikono gya Jaaka.
P15	Omusaadha yaakoba,"Awo kale. Ife tije."
P16	Omusaadha ni wakaima baaja.
Jaaka baamuleka nga eibaale limugemye.
