title:	Ka Bwutu It Oyoo
author:	Maurice Lamony
artist:	Amy Uzzell and Jennifer Jacobs
lang:	Acholi
level:	First paragraphs

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20667.png
desc:	Oyoo otemo kama dwong adada paka onongo ka moo ma ber me nino.

page_text:

P1	Oyoo no tye ka yenyo ot butu manyen.
P2	Eni nen calo ot ma ber.
P3	okuru owace ni, "bin wa but kany"
ci oyoo ogamo ni "Apwoyo".
P4	Idye woo eno ni, lek pa oyoo weng obedo lenge ki daba daba.
P5	Akuri owace ni, "bin wa but kedi"
oyo ogamo ni "Apwoyo Matek"
P6	Idyewoo eno ni, lek pa oyoo weng obedo woo ki bwunga.
P7	rech owace ni"Bin i but bota",
ci oyoo ogamo ni, "apwoyo"
P8	Idyewoo eno ni, lek pa oyoo weng obedo ma ngic dak dyak.
P9	Oyoo ono mito ka moo ma lyet dak otwoo.
P10	Oyoo onongo kacano buk moo cok kenyo.
P11	Idye woo enoni, oyoo oleko lek ma mit keken.
