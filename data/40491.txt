author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu and Yusufa  Hammadu

desc:	Ɓinngel waɗi ko wooɗaa

page_text:

P1	Nuuru bee Yaaya no sakiraaɓe.
P2	Nuuru e yaha janngirde nde fini fuu.@@Ammaa Yaaya yahataa.
P3	Baaba maɓɓe e huwa nder gariiri, daada maɓɓe boo no demoowo.
P4	Nyalnde feere, sey baaba maɓɓe resi ceede dow danki.@@Sey Yaaya  hoosiɗe.
P5	Baaba maako ɓernii waanee.
P6	Sey Yaaya doggi.@@Mo yiili, mo yiili nyalaande nden fuu.
P7	Mo jooɗi ley lekki jemmaare nden fuu.
P8	Nde weeti, sakiraawo maako Nuuru tawimo ley lekki, sey hootidi bee maako.
P9	Baaba maako ƴami, "Ko waɗi koosuɗaa ceede ɗen?"
P10	Daada maako ƴami, "Ko waɗi ndogguɗaa wuro?"
P11	Yaaya wi'i, "Mi waɗi aybe, mi meetataa, njaafeeyam."@@Sey mo hokkiti ceede.
P12	Ɓe karliimo, ɓe njooɗodii jam.
