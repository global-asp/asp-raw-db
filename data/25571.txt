page_text:

P1	Eira  eino, ghaaligho  epuusi  engezi  n'embwa  ensiru.

Epuusi  engezi  yaviiranga mu maka  amagaiga  einho. Embwa  ensiru yalumwanga  endala kuluguudo.
P2	"Kiidha  kitya  amaka  amagaiga okwikiriza  okuba  munhumba yaibwe?" embwa yabuuza  epuusi n'obusungu.
P3	Epuusi engezi  yairamu  nti, "mba naabantu  kuba  ndhi muyondho era nziika ebikyafu  byange. nsaba  emere  nga  nko  nti, meow."
P4	"Bwenkola n'ekilala, balindikiriza okuba munhumba yaibwe? embwa yabuuza.

Epuusi  yairamu eti, "iyi, kola  kyenkukobye  baidha  kukwikiriza. Baidha kukugha  emere."
P5	Embwa  ensiru yairamu eti,  "leero tiidha  kukuluma." 

"Andagaana kudino  saawa. tyenda  andagaana mulundhi  gundi," Epuusi n'eyakoba, bwetuumatuuma.
P6	Embwa  ensiru  yaboigolera inho ku mulyango  gw'enhumba  nga esinziira  ku  magezi  ga  puusi.
P7	Beene  nhumba baagikubba n'omwigi.

Embwa ensiru  yaakoba eti,  "Epuusi yansughaba, ndhidha  kugiruma  bwenagigemyaku.
P8	Epuusi  bweyali  eri zaanhira  ghanhumba, embwa yaagigema.

Embwa  yaakoba, "kulule  ghansughaba. ghankoba  mwaigokere kumulyango ogw'e  nhumba. Beene yo  bankubba. buti ngyakulya."
P9	Epuusi  engezi yairamu eti, "wulira  iwe  embwa, osobola  okundya,  aye leka  nzaanheku  katono. Nga bwendhikiriza  empube okuzaanhaku  me ni  ndhilya".
P10	Embwa  yaabuuzaeti, "empube ezaanha etya?"

Epuusi yeiramu eti, "ngireka yaagololaku katono, era naagikoba ebale paka  ku  isatu. Empube  nga  eri  kubala, ngiswankura."
P11	Embwa  ensiru yaleka  epuusi  yaaja era yaajikoba  ebale paka ku  isatu.

Epuusi  engezi  yeibagho yaanhiinha  omuti.

Embwa  yaakoba eti, "ika  ghansi  kumuti obale  paka  kwisatu."
P12	Epuusi  engezi  yairamu eti, "wulira  embwa,tokairayo  kungema. Tiri  musiru.

osobola  okubala  paka  ku  kikumi, ku  lukumi,oba kukakaire, aye  tosobola  kwiramu  kungema."
