desc:	Mulimo shina okwoluyali okhushira? Emilimo chiosi nichiobukhonyi.

page_text:

P1	Mushikwenyi shilala, abandu baukhana khumulimo kuli noluyali.

Buli mulala yasubira mbu omulimo kukwe nokwoluyali okhushira chiosi.
P2	Omusomesia yabola mbu omulimo kwe nikwoluyali lweikulu.

"Nibalaliho abasomesia abulao yesiyesi ounyala okhutsia mwisomero okhweka."
P3	Omumbashi nabola mbu emilimo chichie nicholuyali okhushira chiosi.

"Nibalaliho abombashi, amasomero nende tsinzu tsiokhukona shitsiakhabereho tawe."
P4	Omubatsi naye nabola mbu niye ouli nende omulimo kwoluyali okhushirakho chiosi.

"Nibalali ababatsi, efisala fiokhwikhonyera hango nende mumasomero shifiakhabereho tawe."
P5	Omusirishi naye yelaya mbu, omulimo kwe nokwoluyali okhushira echindi chiosi.

"Nibalaliho abasirishi nende abalwasia babo, abandu bakhalwalirenje ne bafwa."
P6	Omulimi nabola mbu emilimo chie nicholuyali okhushira chiosi.

"Nibalaliho abalimi, efilibwa shifiakhabereho tawe."
P7	Omwechi naye nabola mbu, abechi nabali nende omulimo kwoluyali okhushira chiosi.

"Nibalaliho abechi, abasomesia, abombashi, abasirishi, abalimi, kata ababatsi shibakhabereho tawe."
P8	Lunyuma buli mulala yafuchirira mbu buli emilimo chiosi nichiobukhonyi. Khwenya abasomesia, abombashi, abasirishi, abalimi nende ababatsi.

Habula buli mulala akwana abe omwechi mberio.
