page_text:

P1	One day Nik and Neyo decided to go on a picnic. They grabbed thier basket and set of happily.
P2	Suddenly a monkey jumped down from a tree, Nik and Neyo didn't know what to do.
P3	They stood there, staring as the monkey took thier basket and began to eat their lunch.
P4	They went home and told father about their day. Father decided to set up a plan and help them have a good picnic.
P5	He gave them a bigger basket and told them to go picnicking again. On their way the monkey stopped them again.
P6	They began tugging at the basket and the monkey took it away, proud of himself.
P7	But when he opened it a dog jumped out! Nik and Neo laughed at the monkey and went home. They thanked their father for his clever idea.
