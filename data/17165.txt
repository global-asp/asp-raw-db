desc:	The story is about my pet.

page_text:

P1	My pet is a cat. She is called Koko. She is friendly and beautiful.
P2	My brother Tom and I like playing with Koko. My mother likes watching us while we play with Koko.
P3	Koko is so playful. Koko likes playing with her little sisters while her mother is asleep. I like watching Koko playing with her sisters.
P4	We all like watching Koko playing with butterflies. Koko keeps us busy during our free time.
P5	Everyone in our family loves Koko.  My brother bought a present for her. This made me very happy.
P6	Koko is always clean and smart.  I feel good when I see Koko.  She likes me too.
