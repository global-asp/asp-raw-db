title:	Petros finds a friend

desc:	A sweet story about a new friendship between a boy and a dog.

page_text:

P1	Petros was a clever student. He was an only child.
P2	One day, Petros went off to play with his friends.
P3	A little dog was stuck. "I will help you," said Petros.
P4	The dog barked, danced and licked Petros with joy.
P5	Petros' friends chased the dog. "Stop! Stop!" Petros shouted.
P6	The dog ran away. He hid from the bad boys.
P7	The friends found Petros crying. They said, "Let's find your dog."
P8	Petros had no hope. His friends said, "We will find him."
P9	They looked everywhere. Petros said, "He must be at the river."
P10	The dog was afraid of the boys. Petros called, "Don't jump!"
P11	The dog ran to Petros. They were happy to be together.
P12	The friends said, "We are sorry. He will be our friend too."
