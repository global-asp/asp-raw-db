title:	Tselane
author:	Victoria Gous
artist:	Brian Wambi, Magriet Brink, Leo Daly and Abraham Muzee
lang:	Setswana

desc:	This story is about a beautiful young lady that was staying with her grandmother

page_text:

P1	Ka le tsatsi le lengwe gone go nale ngwanyana o montle a bidiwa Tselane ,Tselana a nna le nkgono was gagwe, jaanong go nale monna eo bidiwang Dimo, Dimo a batla go utswa Tselane ka gonne e be e le ngwanyana o montle
P2	Dimo a le ka maano a go re a utswe Tselane ,le tsatsi le letastsi Dimo a be sa manje go re lentswe la gagwe le utlwalege jaaka la ga nkoko wa gagwe, letastsi lantlha Dimo a ya ,a opela Tselane ngwanaka tlo o nke bogobe o je
P3	Tselane a Mo raya a re tsamaya ga se wena nkoko ,le tsati le le latelang a tla ga a meditse lenjwe,Tselane ngwanaka tlo o nke bogobe o je,Tselane a Mo raya a re tsamaya ga se wena nkoko ke wena Dimo
P4	Ka letastsi la boraro atla Dimo a mmiditse manjwe a le matlhano ,a bitsa Tselane ngwanaka tlo o nke bogobe o je
P5	Tselane a bula kgoro Dimo a phamola tselane a Mo tsenya moteng ga kgetsi a ya go Mo tswalela ko go e ne ,a tsamaya a ya nageng, Tselane a sa la a kgona o tswa a siela gae
P6	Dimo ha a bowa ko nageng a bula kgetsi e a neng a tsentse Tselane Mo go yone gotswa dintshi tsa Di notsi a siela nokeng a ya go I tatlgela moteng a tlhokafala , Tselane a sa la a I tshelela Monate le nkoko wa gagwe,le balosika..
