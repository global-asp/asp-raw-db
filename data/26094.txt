desc:	A story is about a young man and an important egg

page_text:

P1	Omukazi omukaire yagha mwidhukulu we omulenzi eigi.
P2	Omulenzi yagha abanozi be mitonto eigi.Balimenha.
P3	Omulenzi yalira,"Eigi elyo libaire mwandhu.
P4	Abanhozi be mitonto bamugha akati
P5	Omulenzi yagha abazimbi akati.B'akamenha.
P6	Omulenzi yalira,"Akati ako kabaire kabazimbi!"
P7	Abazimbi bamugha ekinnhuwa kyeisubi.
P8	Omulenzi yayagaana omwaayi ow'ente.
P9	Omulenzi yalira, "eisubi elyo libaire lya bazimbi!"
P10	Omwaayi yagha omulenzi ente.
P11	Omulenzi yayagaana embaga eyobugole.Baasala ente ye.
P12	Omulenzi yalira,"Ente eyo ebaire ya mwaayi!"
P13	Enndhu y'omugole ya mugha omugole.
