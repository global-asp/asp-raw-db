title:	Wamnde bee ngaari
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/38246.png
desc:	Ɗum habaru no ngaari huuwiri bee tiiɗal bee no wamnde ƴoyririndi.

page_text:

P1	Demoowo e mari ngaari nayeeri.
P2	Nden e mo mari wamnde.@@Ngaari bee wamnde e coobiri.
P3	Alaasara sey ngaari wi'i wamnde, "Mi somii hannde naa' seɗɗa."
P4	Ngaari wi'i wamnde, "Mi siwtaaki hannde sam."
P5	Wamnde jaabi, "Mi ɓuriima huwugo."
P6	Nde fini fahin ngaari wi'i, "Nyalaade hannde maa welaa sam."
P7	Wamnde wi'i, "To demoowo warii, waɗu 'muuu!' Mo hokkete siwtaare."
P8	Fini fajiri sey ngaari waɗi 'muuu!'@@Demoowo hokkindi siwtaare.
P9	Demoowo sey remidoyi bee wamnde.
P10	Wamnde huwi soosey nyalaade nden fuu.@@Nde somi waanee nden nde warti.
P11	Wamnde sey wi'i ngaari, "Demoowo wi'i, o hirsete ngam kusel."
P12	Ngaari sey wi'i, "Jaango mi remoyay. Mi nanii daama soosey jooni."
