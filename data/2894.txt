page_text:

P1	Limtocini mukulotyonyimwani tokuuk ceecaaŋ.
P2	Aŋaraci ani yee kamwowee koko ceeptyaŋkocok kaseleny.
P3	Akasekey nyee kaaparpariti yee kayoren akoo corweet nyimwani.
P4	Akase kwapi yee kamwoy papa nyimwani kule maponte karik.
P5	Akase Kule /ceecaamoo yee kaaŋatuta yeyo nyimwani.
