title:	Umama Upheka Kamnandi
author:	Pumla mdontswa and Zanele Zuma
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Ngenkathi umama engekho

page_text:

P1	UZama uvukile ulungiselela ukuya esikoleni.
P2	Uyacabanga ukuthi ngubaba ozomphekela iphalishi.
P3	Kazi uzobuya nini umama? Limnandi iphalishi likamama.
P4	"Futhi linginikeza amandla engiwadingayo esikoleni."
P5	Iphalishi likababa selilungile.
P6	Ubaba uthela ubisi ngothando.
P7	"Alimnandi leli phalishi! Lidinga ushukela."
P8	Uyacabanga futhi uZama, "Ukuba beliphekwe ngumama, ngabe limnandi."
P9	"Baba, ngicela ushukela."
P10	Ubaba uthela ushukela.
P11	"Wu! Baba! Uthele usawoti!"
P12	"Kodwa umama uzobuya nini?"
