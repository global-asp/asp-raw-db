page_text:

P1	Amina is a 15 year old girl.
P2	She wants to become an Engineer.
P3	But her father doesn't want her to become and engineer.
P4	Her father insists on her getting married.
P5	Amina begged her father to allow her go to school.
P6	Amina is very happy because she will be an engineer.
P7	She can't wait to meet other girls who are studying engineering.
