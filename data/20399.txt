title:	Wankudu Ne Ennhindo Engudule
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Marlene Visser 
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words

desc:	Toghalabanira kulwana! (Kili ni ku Luse 3.).

page_text:

P1	Wankudu ni Wakadaada
  beetaaga sente.
P2	Bawumba ebibya okutunda
  mu katale.
P3	Wankudu abona okulwana
  mu katale.
P4	Wakadaada ni Wanfuko bali kulwana.
P5	Wankudu aleekaana, “Leka
  mukwano gwange!”
P6	Wanfuko aluma Wankudu
  ku nnhindo.
P7	Ennhindo ye engudule eluma.
P8	Wankudu wa nnhindo ngudule kuba yaghalabanira okulwana.
