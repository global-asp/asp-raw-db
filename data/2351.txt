page_text:

P1	Abeere nanyang anasinyen.
P2	Ajikrikan nanyang alotooma akai.
P3	Ajikrikan nanyang alotooma akai.
P4	Amotoka nanyang alotaun aloapolon.
P5	Atubwa nanyang eyei kwap.
P6	Ng'amauwae nanyang'ayek eya nabakit.
P7	Ikiwoket ng'iboro ninyang eyei lorot.
P8	Achuruset ang'ide nanyang anagule anakiyeng'et.
P9	Akai nanyang eyei nagule.
P10	Akwap ang'ikirionok enyang'aana anakou kang.
P11	Ani aliwae kilomit ng'oni daang? Nakwap ang'ikirionok a.
