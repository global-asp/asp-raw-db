page_text:

P1	Lokwa emandvulo, boSikhukhukati naLukhoti bebangebangani. 

Ngalelinye lilanga Lukhoti watfola Sikhukhukati atiphumulele ngaphasi kwesihlahla netikhwama takhe. Sikhukhukati abeme ngelunyawo lunye.
P2	Lukhoti wabuta Sikhukhukati, "Ukuphi lomunye umlente wakho?"
P3	Sikhukhukati waphendvula watsi, "Umphatsisitolo uwuncamulile umlente wami wase unginika imphahla esikhundleni sawo."
P4	Lukhoti wabuta Sikhukhukati, "Ngingakwenta nami yini loko?" 

Ingani mankunzi naye bekafuna timphahla. Sikhukhukati wavuma.
P5	Angakacabangi lutfo umphatsisitolo, kwafika Lukhoti. "Ngicela ungincamule umlente nami ngifuna timphahla." Wavuma umphatsisitolo.
P6	Lukhoti wazuba ngelunyawo lunye sekacondze ekhaya. 

Kodvwa bekajabulile ngetimphahla latinikwe ngumphatsisitolo.
P7	Watsi nakefika ekhaya Lukhoti, watfola Sikhukhukati ahamba ngayo yomibili imilente yakhe. 

"Hawu! Kantsi kwentekani?"
P8	Watfukutsela Lukhoti wagcumela Sikhukhukati, wamluma.

Kungako Lukhoti ahlala acoshisana nemantjwele aSikhukhukati. Natfuke wawabamba, uyawadla.
