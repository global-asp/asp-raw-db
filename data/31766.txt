title:	Buruxa aniros, Daisys xa
author:	Mattheus  Goraseb
artist:	Siya Masuku

desc:	ǂKhawusa ǁkhana-aos

page_text:

P1	ǀGuia, ǂkhari !āros ǁkhara ǁanǁguib ǀgūsas,
P2	Ge aniros, Daisys, timî ge ǂgaihesa ge ǁan hâ i.
P3	Kai ta ga o, ta ge ǀgapise, ǀgapise !āǂuisab !nâ ǁkhanasa ra ǁkhore.
P4	Ȋaǀguis ge nau aniron gere âiǂui sisa.
P5	"Ax, sa ǁgaixūse," sida ge !aruǀî sas ǀkha ǀhuru tide," ti ra mîse.
P6	"Okhatse, Daisis, sida ge khoranǁgawo ǁkha, xawe sa ǂkhamros !aroma i ge kaise !gom," tis ǁgûsa ra nana.
P7	!Nubai ǀguis Daisisa a ǀûsen ǁoa.  Tsēkorobes ra !âisen, khoranǁgawo, xawes !hūba xu guisensa a ǀkhai.
P8	!Ȃisen ga dītsâs ra, !nubai hanagam mûnanaib ǀguib.  Komas ra ǀgapisebaxu kōǁnâ !naga hân ǁga, timi.  Naunas ra ǁkhana !kharu!nâ ti ra ǂâise.
P9	!Nākorobe hâ dītsâs di ǁnāgaǁgapis ǀkhas khoranǁgapo.
P10	!Hūba xus a guisen ǁkha, xawe ǁkhawa ra ǂawoxusen.
P11	Ax hukas tsîna ta ǁkhana ǁkha tide," tis ge ǁgûsa ra mîba.  "Ama-e ra mîn ge nauna," tis ge ra tsûaǂgao.
P12	"Daisis, nauna xus ge a !goti ī.  ǁȊn ge ǁkhana ǂgao tama, xawe sas ge ra tura," tis ǁgûsa ra ǀgaiǀgai si.
P13	Sao ra tsēsas aisisa hoan ǂamai ǀgapise ra ǂharo tsî khoranǁgawo, ǂoab !nâ ra dūse.
P14	Xawe, "ǂGIPARA!"
P15	Nau anin xas ra âiǂuihese, "ohoos!" mîba si da go ǀnai, aniron ǁkhana ǁoa, ti.
P16	Xawe nau ǁgoas ge ǁîsa xū-i hoa-i ǂamai ǂharo sī oms ǂam!nâb kōse ǁnā-ka-ǁgapi, ǁnā-ka-ǁgapi…
P17	ǂOab !nâs ge ǁkhanaǂgâ ǁgaworogu xa ra huihese...
P18	ǁȊs ge ge ǁkhana !ari ǁîs !nakab ge ǂoaba ob ra ǀgai, o ge re ǀgai.  ǁȊs ge ai!â ǀgui gere ǁkhana.
P19	Tsîn ge nauna gere ǁkhore ǁîs khami ǁkhanasa.
P20	"O, Daisy, burupuruxas ge a!" tin ge ge ǂan!gâ.
