page_text:

P1	Torup ateni eking'aren.
P2	Erupi tooma nalup.
P3	Ani erumor torup ateni. Erupi elosi kidiama nakolong.
P4	Tojuput ng'akui nakeng'arenok.
P5	Torup ekitoe emarage edirisi kidiama.
P6	Sodi topudut ng'atur. Toliwor ng'atur emarage.
P7	Eya ng'ichok emarage lukaalak tooma napochot keng.
