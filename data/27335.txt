title:	Tukuume  obutonde  bwaife

desc:	Olufumo olugema ku ngeri ki abantu nebisolo ye bighanga okuba aghalala nga biri miraala.

page_text:

P1	Lwali  lulala, kyabazinga  "mpologoma" yaayeta ensolo  dhonadhona  mulwekobaano  olunene.  Yayenda  kuteesa  kungeri  ki  gye baghanga  okukuuma  obutonde bwaibwe.
P2	Bawansolo bwe baatuuka mu bungi, Kyabazinga Wampologoma yaabakoba bisirikirire.
P3	Boonaboona  bwe baaghaayo, Wapologoma yaabakoba  ati,  "Abantu bange mughulirise bukalamu."
P4	"Abazigu baife bali kutema emiti gyaife era baayokya  ensiko yaife. Tukokole ki?" Wampologoma yaabuuza.
P5	"Abaisuka, abaguna, abasaadha n'abakazi, bali kumalagho obutonde bwaife. Tubakomya tutya okwonoona amaka gaife." Wampologoma bwe yabuuza.
P6	Bawansolo boonaboona baanhiiga era baafuuka  beeghalikirivu.
P7	Baalonda Wandhovu, Wambogo, Wankula  ni Waikoli  babone engeri ey'okukomya ekizibu  kino.
P8	Bawansolo bano  baasalagho okugema buli muntu eyali atema emiti.
P9	Baasiba abayokya amanda na abalimira mu bibali. Baagema abookya empiira na abalima abandi.
P10	Baabatwala mu mbuga okubawozesa.
P11	Aghooni bawansolo baakola endagaano eye milala na abantu.
P12	Eitaka lingi lyaghebwa bawansolo, na bantu boona baafunaku. Okuva niku olwo, bawansolo baateeka. Naabandi basula na abantu mu maka giabwe.
