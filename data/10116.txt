page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,  there was a weak man.
P2	This man was called Wamaŋe.
P3	Wamaŋe did not want to work at all.
P4	He was an idler.
P5	Wamaŋe had no good clothes but rugs.
P6	This man had no hut to live in.
P7	He slept outside at night and watched the moon and stars as they gave out light.
P8	One day,  it rained heavily.
P9	The night was very cold.
P10	Wamaŋe felt very cold. He started thinking of making a hut for himself.
P11	He sung, "Tomorrow I will build myself a hut" ×4 In the morning when the sun started shinning clearly.
P12	Wamaŋe became warm. He changed the song. "It is hot why should I build a hut?" ×4.
