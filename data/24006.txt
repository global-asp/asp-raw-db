title:	uko izuru ry'akanyamasyo ryavunitse

desc:	Iyi nkuru ifite inyisho nziza cyane yanatubera inama nziza.

page_text:

P1	kera habayeho, akanyamasyo n'agasimba. byari inshuti magara. umunsi umwe, gasimba abwira kanyamasyo ati "nshuti yanjye, urabona ko nta mafaranga dufite, jandi umunsi w'isoko uregereje. n'iki twakora tukabona amafaranga"
P2	akanyamasyo karasubiza "nzi kubumba. ndumva wasaza ukamfasha maze tuzagurisha ibyo twakoze mu isoko." 
" ntakibazo nzaza ejo mu ma saa sita tubikore"
P3	ku munsi w'isoko, akanyamasyo kagiye ku isoko. kabona abantu babiri bari kundwana. atura ibyo yabubye ajya kureba.
P4	ahageza asanga ni ya nsuti ye iri kundwana n'ifuku
P5	uwo mwanya atabajije icyabiteye afata inkoni atangira gukubita fuku "reka inshuti yanjye" kanyamasyo arasakuza
P6	nuko ifuku irahindukira ikubita akanyamasyo ku izuru
P7	izuru rye ritangira kuvamo amaraso
P8	akanyamasyo katangiye kundwana katabajije impamvu. kuva uwo munsi izuru ry'akanyamasyo rikibutsa kudahutira ho
