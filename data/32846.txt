title:	Zama Niwopambana
author:	Bether Mwale-Moyo and Moffat Moyo
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	CiNyanja
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ntano iyi isonyeza kuti nicofunikira kutamanda ana omwe acita zabwino.

page_text:

P1	M'bale wanga amacedwa kuuka. Ine nimauka kuseni seni cifukwa ndine opambana.
P2	Nimasegula mazenera kuti zuwa lingene.
P3	"Ndiwe ntanda yanga," anena amai.
P4	Nimazisambika masiku onse kopanda tandizo lililonse.
P5	Sinivutika kusamba manzi ozizila kapena kusambila sopo yobiliwila  yocapila zovala.
P6	"osaiwala kusuka mano," akumbusa amai. @Niyanka kuti, "Sicingacitike kuli ine."
P7	Pambuyo posamba, nipeleka moni kwa ambuya na amayi aang'ono.
P8	Ndipo nimazivalika zovala. Nimanena kuti "Ndine wamukulu sopano amai."
P9	Nimakwanisa kumanga ma batani ndiponso kumanga nthambo za nsapato zanga.
P10	Nimasimikiza kuti m'bale wanga aziwa nkhani zonse zakusukulu.
P11	Kusukulu nimayikako nzelu mu zonse.
P12	Nimacita zonse izi zabwino masiku onse. Koma camene nikondesesa kwambili nikusowela, kuswowela ndi kusowela!
