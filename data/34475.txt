title:	Ithekisi yaNanu
author:	Mduduzi Benjie Shongwe
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Siswati

desc:	Nanu namake wakhe bebalindzele ithekisi letobatsatsa ibayise ekhaya. Nanu wabona tonkhe tinhlobo tebantfu netintfo letinhle ngesikhatsi balindzile. Kepha ingabe ithekisi itofika?

page_text:

P1	Sawubona! Libito lami nginguNanu.@@Sinamake wami silindze ithekisi. Siya ekhaya.
P2	Lapha ngime elayinini. Make ungihlabelelela ingoma.@@"Wota Babe weThekisi awuboni yini? SinaNanu silindzile!"
P3	Make loseceleni kwami uphetse tinkhukhu takhe utiyisa ekhaya.@@Titomtalela emacandza latowadla.
P4	Lawa ngemadvodza labuya emsebentini. Ayangibuka ayamoyitela.@@Ngiweva ahlabelela atsi, "Hheyi Nanu lomncane, ungakhatsateki. Ithekisi iyeta masinyane."
P5	Kwendlula lomunye make. Ugcoke uyantfonsa. Ticatfulo takhe ative titinhle?@@Titsi, "chwa, chwa, chwa, chwa, chwa, chwa" nakahamba.
P6	Lendvodza itsengisa tinatfo letibandzako.@@Ngiyatitsandza tinatfo letibandzako. Tiyabandza kakhulu futsi timnandzi.@@Wena uyatitsandza yini?
P7	Buka! Kunabomake labetfwele kudla kwabo kwakusihlwa etinhloko tabo.@@Bahlakaniphile sibili.
P8	Losisi ulungisa tinwele takhe.@@Utotikhipha nebangani bakhe namuhla ebusuku.
P9	Sengikhatsele nyalo. Make uyangimema kute ngiphumule.@@Uyahlabelela, "Siyacela Babe weThekisi, ungephuti, gogo usilindzile egedeni."
P10	Nangu umngani wami Tumi. Naye futsi uyaphumula.@@Sitawufika ekhaya masinyane.
P11	Esibhakabhakeni kunetinyoni letindiza tiya ekhaya.@@Kufanele tifike ekhaya kungakahwalali.
P12	Lomake unemacici lamahle. Ngicabanga kutsi uwatsenge lamuhla edolobheni.@@Nasengikhulile ngitotsenga emacici lamanyenti ami newamake wami.
P13	Make sewudziniwe nyalo.@@Ngiyamhlabelelela ingoma, "Siyacela Babe weThekisi, wota phela, sinamake silindzile."
P14	Mani kancane! Ngiva umsindvo wethekisi!@@Nayi seyita!
P15	Lena ithekisi yetfu lenkhulu lebovu. Ite kutotsatsa mine kanye namake wami siye ekhaya.@@Sengijabule kakhulu!
P16	Usale kahle!
