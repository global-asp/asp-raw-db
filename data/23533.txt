title:	Aŋɔrɔ na kɛɛfʊ
author:	Mounifatou Idrissou
artist:	Angie & Upesh
lang:	Anii
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/23533.png
desc:	Mbʊpá njala nɖe n tɩ lǝ atʊ ɖaa aŋɔrɔ a ŋǝ kɛɛfʊ ma.

page_text:

P1	Ʊshilé ɖǝn, ambʊŋʊma bʊŋunii bʊ ce ʊciŋkǝrka kagʊyá.
P2	Atayà a shɩ bʊŋǝ bʊsoŋsono ashee ampal, atana a shɩ kɛɛfʊ buruu ashee amʊ, na kokoli a ŋǝ bɔmbɔm.
P3	Ʊfalja laŋ, gʊsǝrǝ gʊ wu kǝŋkǝŋkǝŋ naa ataa amakɛɛfʊ.
P4	N ce atɩ raga gʊyo gʊbʊmbɔnɔ kalaŋlaŋ.
P5	Na n yu páá, halɩ kǝ ma tɩ́ jɩ gɩteŋshile kʊjɩʊ na.
P6	Kagɩjibɔŋɔ gɩɖe aŋɔrɔ a ɖoo ma, n'a ri amakɛɛfʊ gʊyo gʊbʊmbɔnɔ kalaŋlaŋ.
P7	Aŋɔrɔ a sǝu amakɛɛfʊ aŋǝ naa aɖa na lɛʊ mʊrʊmʊrʊ, n'amʊ gboo n lɛʊ mʊrʊmʊrʊ.
P8	Gaja ga kʊɖa, gɩ ɖoo na sukuru akpa ma, n'amaŋana a shee amʊ kɛɛfʊ afɔlɩ kɔla aránà. Naa alǝ amʊ wáá: «Aŋɔrɔ a shee ka akɩ.»
P9	Gɩjibɔŋɔ gɩɖe jɩ amʊ n'aŋɔrɔ gɩ sǝu atɩkɛɛfʊ na lɛʊ na ma mʊrʊmʊrʊ. Ɩɖe san n'atʊ.
P10	Ɩ yɔ wàà, ʊshilé gboo ʊ ɖa na yɛɛ kɛɛfʊ ɖǝn aa?
P11	Kʊ̂n ma aŋgɩsǝwa afaŋa kɛɛfʊ nɖee n sǝu ʊrɔŋ ma.
