page_text:

P1	Omuruchi  yali ne Isurusi ingofu  khandi indofu.
P2	Isurusi omwitsa we Isishiri yali yachonya muno.
P3	Isishiri yeberera khulwa obusiro bwe ichinganga.
P4	Isurusi yakona hasi khulwa amachonye amanji muno.
P5	Isishiri yauka lwayakhola emirimo okhuyomba Isurusi.
P6	Isishiri yachonya nobubeyi bwe Isurusi buli obushiere.
P7	"Bulilwosi, enjonyere! Lelo kona  wikhole mulware."
P8	"Nelime ndie oukhonyanga alwala?" Omulima yareba.
P9	Isishiri yaboywa lichoki okhulima neinalwa obululu.
P10	Isishiri yachonya ne Isurusi neiulukha hamsala.
P11	"Washanje, nola lima ta mutsuli bakhusinza."
P12	Isurusi neibola, "Eonere mutsuli esamula khu mirimo."
