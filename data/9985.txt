page_text:

P1	There was a great builder in Kabubbu village.
P2	He built many houses in the village.
P3	But he was sleeping in a bad hut.
P4	People asked about this hut every day. "Don't worry,  I have a plan," the builder always replied.
P5	He saved his money and bought bricks.
P6	Then he bought sand,  cement,  iron sheets,  and nails.
P7	Gradually,  he built himself a large,  beautiful house.
P8	His friends rejoiced to see this new house.
P9	He prepared for a party and invited everyone. They ate and drank.
P10	Then they danced.
P11	The builder received many gifts for his new home.
P12	Now the builder in Kabubbu village sleeps in a good house.
