title:	Ntja, Katse le manyowa
author:	Maria Vaz
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ntja le katse ba ne ba tsamatsamaya mme ba bona letshwao sefateng. Ha re utlweng hore ho ile ha etsahalang ka mora moo.

page_text:

P1	Enwa ke Katse.@@Enwa ke Ntja.
P2	Katse le Ntja ba bula monyako wa ntlo ya bona.@@Katse le Ntja ba tswa ka tlung.
P3	Katse le Ntja ba ya tsamaya.@@Ba tsamaya jwang? Ba tsamaya dipakeng tsa difate. Ba tsamaya thoteng.@@Jwale ba ya ema.
P4	Katse le Ntja ba bona letshwao sefateng.@@Katse le Ntja ba sheba letshwao. Ba sheba mantswe a ngotsweng letshwaong.
P5	Katse le Ntja ba a bala.@@Ba bala se ngotsweng letshwaong, mme ho ngotswe, "ela hlooko baki."
P6	Katse a botsa a re, "Baki ke eng?"@@Ntja a re, "Ke seaparo se tshwanang le jase."
P7	Katse le Ntja  ba se bone baki.
P8	Yaba ba a tsheha.@@Ba tsheha letshwao lena.@@Ba tsheha hobane ba nahana hore letshwao lena le beilwe ho etsa motlae ka bona.
P9	Yaba mantswe a ya fetoha.
P10	Katse le Ntja ba bala se ngotsweng letshwaong.
P11	'Ela hlooko morara.'
P12	Katse a botsa, "Morara ke eng?"@@Ntja a re, "Morara ke tholwana."
P13	Katse le Ntja  ba se bone morara.
P14	Yaba ba a tsheha.@@Ba tsheha letshwao lena.@@Ba tsheha hobane ba nahana hore letshwao lena le beilwe ho etsa motlae ka bona.
P15	Katse le Ntja ba sheba letshwao. Ba bona manyowa. Manyowa ana ke ona a ntseng a fetola mantswe.@@Katse le Ntja ba nka manyowa mme ba a lahla.
P16	Katse le Ntja ba bala.@@Ba bala se ngotsweng letshwaong, 'ela hlooko api'
P17	Katse a botsa, "Api ke eng?"@@Ntja a re, "Api ke tshwene."
P18	Katse le Ntja ba bona api, mme ba e supa.@@Api e kgolo haholo.@@Katse a re, "Baleha!" le Ntja ya re, "Baleha!"
P19	Katse le Ntja ba baleha.@@Ba balehela ntlong ya bona.@@Ba kwala monyako.
P20	Ba kwala monyako wa ntlo ya bona.
P21	Sala hantle Katse.@@Sala hantle Ntja.@@Sala hantle Api.@@Salang hantle manyowa.
