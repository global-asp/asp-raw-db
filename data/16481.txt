page_text:

P1	Cango cango Dyel,  Gwok gi Dhyaŋ jo bedo mere matek swa. Ndir acel jo kidho woth i motoka.
P2	Jo tundo kama joloro iye. Jagwey motoka openjo jo cul ma woth. Dhyaŋ oculo apere.
P3	Gwok oculo pesa ma thoth,  rupir go oyido ongoye gi pesa ma thin.
P4	Jagwey motoka oyido chegin dwoko ri Gwok pesa pere m'odoŋ. Dyel oŋweco woko abwap ma ku culo apere.
P5	Jagwey motoka omako nge swa. Go otugila motoka to kadho ma ku dwoko ri Gwok pesa pere m'odong.
P6	Meno,  ama omiyo kiri konon,  fodi Gwok ŋweco ryemo motoka ni woleŋo iye waŋe. Go rango jagwey motoka ma ku dwoko ri go pesa pere m'odoŋ.
P7	Dyel ŋweco aŋweca ka owinjo mor ma motoka. Go lworo ni ile mako go rupir ndir no ku culo jagwey motoka pesa ma woth.
P8	Dhyaŋ ki dew ri go kwoŋ motokin. Dhyaŋ gale a gala ka ŋado ngudo. Oŋeyo ni oculo pesa pere mawoth je.
