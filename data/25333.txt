title:	Ebisolo  bisima  ekidhiba
author:	Isaac Mapiti and Kyambogo  University
artist:	João Carlos Brito
lang:	Lusoga
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Amaadhi gamaze mwaka tigagwa. Ezira ghaakwagaana ku kaadhi. Ebisolo binaakola ki okughona ekizibu kino ekyamaadhi.

page_text:

P1	Kyalighooku  omwaka  mulala  amaadhi  mwegataagwa. Emiti gyakala  gyafa. Amakoola  gaawaaku  amaadhi  gafuumukamu.  Abantu  n'ebisolo  byatandiika  okufa  endila  n'enhonta.
P2	Empologoma  n'endhivu  ebikulu  byebisolobyeira  ghalala okwogeramu  kumbeera. Empologoma yaakoba, "twidha maliriza  nga  tufiire  nnhonta. twete  buli  muntu twogere  kukyo  okusima  ekidhiba." Empologoma  yeikiriza era  yailayo  okutuusa  amawulire  eri  ensolo  edhindi.
P3	Ensolo dhoonadhoona  daikiriza  okutoolaku  wakaima. Epologoma yaakoba,"mubone  baganda  bange  ni baanhinhaze, omwaka  guno amaadhi  tigagwire.Buli  muntu aja kufa  ndala na nhonta.
mukole tusale amagezi ag'osima ekidhiba tusobole okufuna amaadhi agookunhwa."
P4	Ebisolo byonabyona byaikiriza era  byagemeragho  embbago,byatandiika  okusima. byasima  byasima. okuma liriza  nga  bituuse ku maadhi. Empologoma  yaakoba eti, "lwaki wakaima taidhie  kusima kidhiba? taidha  kunhwa  kumaadhi gano!"
P5	Oluvainhuma  wakaima  yaidha  okubonaku ekidhiba. yaakoba, "mwidhe,mwidhe kukidhiba!" wazira kyeyawulira. yagwamu yaanhwa  amaadhi era  yalaazaaku akaseera kumbali agho.
P6	Olunaku olwairirira, ebisolo bwe byaidha  byabona nga ekidhiba kyonooneike. amaadhi gaali gafuuse bityanka. Byabuuza,  "naanhi aghanga okola kino?"  ebindi byakoba,  "ayinza  okuba wakaima. bwetwali tusima ekidhiba tiyabaagho."
P7	Kale ebisolo  byasalagho okulekagho omukuumi  kukidhiba.
Baalonda  wairike okukuuma  ekidhiba bukalamu. Wairike  yali ali kukidhiba wakaima  weyaidira obwire  nga akoba  ati, "mwide! mwide! tusime  ekidhiba!" Wairike  yairamu, kale! Agho wakaima yaidha yaalamusa Wairike, "ndhidye, mukwano  gwange, era ndhaaleta ekyo omuntu  kyasinga kwenda kulya!" Wairike yairamu ati, "idha, leeta tukiboneku."
P8	Wakaima n'omuzinga yaja ye wairike, otyo mukwano gwange, grzesaaku bino! Wakaima yaamugha omuzinga. Agho yasena amaadhi mukidhiba yaaganhwa. Nga wakaima yaakavaagho, wa mpiti yatuuka era yaamubona.
P9	Olunaku olweiraku wampiti  yaja  yaakobera ebisolo  ebindhi byonabyina  nti  wakaima yanhwaire amaadhi mu kidhiba. Era  baalonda  Wankereghe  okukuuma ekidhiba. Wakaima  yaatuuka  obwire,nga akoba  nti,  "Mwidhe tusime  ekidhiba!" bwataaghulira amwiramu  yaakoba ati: "Ninze  wakaima!  ndhidye  kunhwa ku maadhi"
P10	Wankereghe  bweyali atuuse mangu  kukidhiba,yeekweka mubibaale. Wakaima bweyagwa mukidhiba  ati,Wankrreghe  yaamugema  omugulu. Wankereghe tiyayogera, yalindhirira okutuuka  endhuba. bweyavaayo
P11	Ebisolo  bwebyatuuka, byabona nga wankereghe agemye  wakaima. byayeta ebisolo  ebindhi okusalagho  kiki  ekyokukolera  wakaima. Ebisolo byonabyona bwebyatuuka, Empologoma yaakoba eti,"kiki  kyetube  tukolere  wakaima?"
P12	Wankereghe  yakoba, "Wandhovu amugeme  n'omukonda gwe amusuule  ku gabaale." Ebisolo byonabyona  byeikiriza. Aye wakaima yaakoba, "Ee mwami wampologoma, toidhi nga ba wakaima tibafa bwobasuula  kumabaale? Wakaima eidhakufa nga  omuswire  mwivu mwonka." Era  wampologoma yaakoba  wandhovu ati asuule  wakaima mwivu.
P13	Nga  wakaima bamaze  okumubwikaku  eivu, wampologoma yamugema yaamusuula. wakaima bweyatuuka kwitaka yafuumuula enkungu embitirivu. Enkungu yaabuna buli wantu.
P14	Enkungu  bweyaghaagho, ebisolo  byalingirira. Wakaima yali agiire! baakitegeera nti  wakaima yali abakozeisa bwa kakodyo.
