page_text:

P1	Ekadeni kwakunomntazana, ibizo lakhe kwakunguKalabutjhe.

UKalabutjhe kwakumntazana othanda ukukhuluma. Unina kaKalabutjhe wamyelelisa bona angakhulumeli futhi, kodwana uKalabutjhe azange alalele.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga, ukghariyakhe wagula. Bekahlala ngaphetjheya komrhoba ongehla kwendawo ehlala uKalabutjhe.
P3	Unina kaKalabutjhe bakamatasatasa mhlokho. Kwasele kusebusuku lokha nakanikela uKalabutjhe ukudla bona akusele ukghariyakhe.
P4	Endleleni wahlangana noSinson, ipisi ekhabe iziphendule umuntu.
P5	USinson wabuza bona uphethe ini. 

UKalabutjhe waphendula, "Inyama, amaqanda nebisi." Unina bekamyelelisile bona angatjho bonyana uphethe ini.
P6	UKalabutjhe bewatjela uSinson bona ukghariyakhe uyagula nokuthi kungebanga lelo amsela ukudla. 

USinson wathoma waginya amathe lokha nakacabanga ngenyama ebeyiphethwe nguKalabutjhe.
P7	Wagijimela ngaphambili kukaKalabutjhe waya kwakghariyakhe.
P8	Waginya ukghariyakhe wabese uzigubuzesa ngengubo yakhe.
P9	Lokha uKalabutjhe nakafikako bekuthulile ngendlini. 

Wangena ngendlini warhuwelela, "Kghari,ukuphi?"
P10	Lokha nakangezwa ilizwi laka kghariyakhe, uKalabutjhe wangena ngekamerweni lapho kulala khona ukghariyakhe. 

Wamangala lokha nakathola kunomuntu ozigubuzese ngengubo.
P11	"Kghari kubayini namhlanje iindlebe zakho zizikulu kangaka?" kubuza uKalabutjhe.

USinson waphendula agubuzele ngengubo, "Kobana ngizokwazi ukukuzwa kuhle."
P12	UKalabutjhe wabuza godu, "Kghari, kubayini amehlo wakho amakhulu kangaka namhlanje?"

USinson uyaphendula, "Kobana ngikubone kuhle."
P13	UKalabutjhe ubuza kokugcina, "Kghari kuba yini umlomo wakho umkhulu kangaka namhlanje?"

USinson waphendula, "Kobana ngikwazi ukukuginya." USinson weqa embhedeni waginya uKalabutjhe.
P14	Ngombana uKalabutjhe bekakhulumela futhi waragela phambili nokukhuluma nanyana asele angaphakathi ngemathunjini kaSinson. Wabuza imibuzo eminengi.
P15	Ekugcineni, uSinson wadinwa mibuzo eminengi kaKalabutjhe wathatha isiqunto sokumkhafula.
P16	UKalabutjhe nokghari yakhe basindiswa babantu bemzini. 

Ukusukela mhlokho, uKalabutjhe azange aphinde akhulumele futhi ebantwini angabaziko.
