desc:	Muha akagwa omukishuba kye eshaki ekiyabire nebeha buri kicoco.

page_text:

P1	Hakahariho obwire ebikoko byona be ka nibitura omirembe anka abawani mpaka obu shitamisa okushiko okufuna omukama
P2	eshaki ekeneyenda okuba omukama. yasharamu okubiha buri ekicoco mbu eyine omuriro ahamutwe
P3	ebikoko byona bekaikiriza ebye eshaki yabire negaba. abyekyo ebikoko be kafura eshaki omukama
P4	hakabaho ezoba ryeshura, ejura ekagwa agwa biri omwe a katobere kand bene nebeho
P5	nitubasa kweha nkahi omuliro gu ratutagasy enibire etu'' wakame yabuza ebekoko
P6	ekyende yagarukamu  nitubasa okufuna omuliro ha mutwe gwe shanki. eshaki ni omukama wetu abwokugira iyene omuliro.
P7	ebikoko beki kiriza nebwe nkende yagambire nabekyo
muha zakwakira omuliro kuriga owarushaki
P8	Muha ekashanga eshaki eri omu tururo egira eyibe omuliro
P9	muha yafuna omunyasi yabutenka ahamutwe gwe shaki koka tihine ekabireho
P10	yemuka arushaki nitwenda omuliro
P11	koko eshaki ekatakubasa kukora omuliro
P12	Muha ecanyga munoga yagaruka yo kuganbira ebikoko ebindi. kyeha obyo tihene a rukutina eshaki. muha ekeneyra enkonko nabana bayo
