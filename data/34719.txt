title:	Alôm vea Dwem kaha iyiav
author:	Terkule Aorabee
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Tiv
level:	First words

desc:	Atseregh doo ga. Dwem vea Alôm pav Ijende sha ciu atseregh.

page_text:

P1	Ngise Alôm man Dwem lu ahuraior a dedoo.
P2	Alôm kaa a Dwem er, "Se er tom sule sha ijoghave."
P3	Alôm va a mhen er vea kaha ikyuleke shi vea kar ityom.
P4	Alôm kaa er, "Tom wam u huren inyon ne hemba taver."
P5	Dwem lumun u kahan kwagh tswen.
P6	Zum u Dwem yar u huran toho yô, Alôm yar amo a gberen.
P7	Zum u ikyuleke gba vian yô, Alôm lu huren inyon ken mkpeiyol.
P8	Alôm kaa er, "Mo me sunda hanmô kwagh u sha tar. We sunda u shin inya."
P9	Alôm sunda ikyuleke la cica za koso.
P10	Dwem time shin inya kpa mande sha amishe tsegher.
P11	Dwem kaa er Alôm bumeun yô. @@Ishima vihi in kpishi.
P12	Ve kaha atsaka.@@Dwem kaa er, "Mo me sunda hanmô kwagh u sha tar."
P13	We er tom imôngu a atsam zwa.
P14	Shien u sundan yô, Dwem sunda ahu a atsaka tsegher.
P15	Alôm sunda atam a atsaka a gbogbonan za koso.
P16	Dwem zenda Alôm. Ijende ve bee.
