desc:	Omughala omuto eyali ayenda einho okumanha nga era yekalirisa dhaadha ghe nga ayengeka amenvu.

page_text:

P1	Dhaadha ghange yangonza inho. Yakoberanga ebyama bing aye yaleka yo kirala.
P2	"ekibo n'ebikaba  byaki?  
Dhaadha yandiramu ati,"Byakufuusa."
P3	Nali nhenda kubona.
aye  dhaadha yatuma nje  nkole ekintu ekindi.
P4	"Birigha?"
"Bire bye ghafusiza,"Dhaadha bw'eyankoba.
P5	Dhaadha yantuma mu kisenge kye.
Naghuniriza amenvu agenze
P6	Nali nzuire ekifo dhaadha ghafusiza
Nali mboine amenvu agenze.
P7	Nalyaku irala.nelyenvu eridudula ly'enali ndoghozizaku bukyangambagho.
P8	Natwala amenvu agandi ana.
nagakweka munda mu kiteteeyi.
P9	ku lunaku olwa katale, dhaadha yatwala ebirime mu katale okubitana.
naswala
P10	Bweyandeta eiraku,namana lwaki andese.
Tiragamu kuiba omukuzi gundi.
