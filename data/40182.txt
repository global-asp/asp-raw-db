page_text:

P1	Beni amakhala ku tawuni.@Wabwera kumudzi kudzacheza ndi agogo.
P2	Agogo a Beni ali ndi nkhalango ya zipatso za mtchire.@“Lero ndikuonetsa zipatso za mtchire.” Agogo anamuuza Beni.
P3	Uwu ndi mtengo wa nthudza.@Nthudza zaziwisi zimachita khambi, zakupsa zimazuna. Mtengo wake ndi wa minga.
P4	Awa ndi masuku.@Amakoma akapsa, awisi amachita khambi.
P5	Awa ndi matowo. Timatafuna ngati chingamu.@Amazuna ngati muli shuga.
P6	Mtengo uwu ndi wa chimanga cha chizungu.@Mkati mwa chipatso mumakhala chimanga chofiira.
P7	Uwu ndi mtengo wa mpoza.@Samala osakwera, mpoza suchedwa kuthyoka.
P8	Uyu ndi katope.@Mulibe zipatso, nthawi yake siinakwane.
P9	Uyu ndi bwemba.@Bwemba umawawasa.
P10	Beni, ndatopa kuyenda. Tipitiliza mawa.@Ndakuonetsa zipatso zingati?
