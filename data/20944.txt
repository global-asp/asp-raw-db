title:	Myeli

lang:	Dhopadhola

desc:	Kigana mee nitye kwong nyako mumaro myeli. Jafwonji pere nwango nigime myigo nge to yoki go medo go meni italanta pere.

page_text:

P1	Amyelo kaanigi seimi, amyelo ka  ajulo, amyelo kaani kendani, amyelo ka amako nge.
P2	Jafwonji parani wacho ni, bedi lingi! ikiri myeli ndelo gipi.
P3	To yokani wacho ni myyeli niyini ibedo ma ichwe, myeli miyini ibedo mabori, ndelo achieli ilabedo jamyeli  aka ila miyo jo dhier.
P4	To awacho ni ndelo achieli alamyelo to jii ju chulani nyaka ma nenani ka awiyo  pyerani. Myeli pere nga ber apaka julwacho.
