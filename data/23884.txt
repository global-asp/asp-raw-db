desc:	Okahokololo Okaxupi kombinga yomurongi omwaana naashoka hetulongo.

page_text:

P1	Nguka omurongi gwandje. Edhina lye oye Akinyi. Okutuhole unene.
P2	Omurongi gwetu Akinyi ohetulongo Oalufabeta. Nohetu longo uuyitumbuli, no hevulu okutya a, e, i, o, u.
P3	Omurongi gwetu Akinyi ohetulongo omalwaala. Ondishi Olwaala olutiligane, olimbulau, oluzizi, lishunga no luludhe.
P4	Pethimbo lyo kafudhepo, Omurongi gwetu Akinyi Ohiimbi okaimbilo opo tukothe. Ohe twiimbile ko okahimbilo kokukotheka.
P5	Okahimbilo ohakati nee: Oshingolondo oshaa nawa gandja omeho goye neimemesho wa tegelelwa ngele topenduka moomposi omwanawa ngoye unene. Ino lila no tandi kwiimbile okahimbilo oka wanawa.
P6	Omurongi Akinyi okushi uuhokololo oundji, ohetu lombwele uuhokololo wa yoo lokathana esiku kehe lyoshiwike.
P7	Momaandaha, ohetu lombwele uuhokololo kombinga yomusamane omwaanawa.
P8	Metiyali, ohetu lombwele uuhokololo kombinga yomukulukadhi omwaanawa.
P9	Metitatu, ohetu lombwele uuhokololo kombinga yopumbwe yiiyeditho.
P10	Metinhe, ohetu lombwele uuhokololo kombinga yuunamapya.
P11	Metitanho, ohatu hokolola uuhokololo wetu yene mongulu yosikola.
P12	Omurongi gwetu oye Omurongi dhingi muuyuni auhe! Nongele ondakoko ondahala okufa omurongi gwetu Akinyi.
