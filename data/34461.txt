page_text:

P1	Mine nesibankhwakati sami sibuka tinyoni tami esibhakabhakeni.
P2	Mine netinyoni tami sihlangana nenkawu esihlahleni.
P3	Mine nenkawu yami sidlala netindlulamitsi tami egcumeni.
P4	Mine netindlulamitsi tami senta emabhola etingobiyane ngeludzaka.
P5	Mine netingobiyane tami sidla bobhanana kanye ne Gorilla onkhe malanga.
P6	Igorila yami nengobiyane besaba kakhulu lufudvu.
P7	Mine nelufundvu siya ekhaya sihamba kancane kakhulu kanye nenyoka.
P8	Mine nenyoka sitsandza kwenta umsindvo nekuhlabelela tingoma ngasechibini.
P9	Mine nemndeni wami wonkhe sihlala ndzawonye ngekujabula lokukhulu!
