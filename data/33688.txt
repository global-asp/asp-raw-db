page_text:

P1	One day one car was driving faster.
P2	A lady was also driving faster on the same day.
P3	They crashed each other.
P4	The father of the child called the ambulance and the ambulance came with the police.
P5	The tow truck came to take the two cars that were broken.
P6	People were not died. The end.
