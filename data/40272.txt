desc:	Mwansa climbs up a very tall tree. She is afraid to climb down. Who will help her?

page_text:

P1	This is Mwansa.@Mwansa is a girl. She is in grade three.
P2	This is Mr and Mrs Musonda. @They are Mwansa's parents.
P3	Mwansa wants to be a scientist when she grows up.@Mwansa studies hard at school.
P4	This is Mwansa and her friend Chongo. They are walking.@Look, says Chongo. @There is a tree, Can you  Climb a tree, Mwansa?
P5	Yes I can climb a tree. Says Mwansa. I will show you.
P6	Help. Says Mwansa. I cannot climb down the tree. @I am afraid, help me
P7	Chongo goes back to the farm and calls, Mwansa’s parents.@Climb down the tree, they say. Don’t be afraid. We are here and we will help you.
P8	Mwansa climbs down the tree. She is sad. I am sorry mommy, she says I will not climb any tall tree again.@Do not cry, her mommy says.
P9	Everyone is happy. @Mwansa is a brave girl and her parents are proud of her.
