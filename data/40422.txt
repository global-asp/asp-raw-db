title:	Gilangeeru e goyru

desc:	Goyru tawi geraaɗe les maayo e o waddi haa saare. Ko Gilangeeru wia taa tow o faami?

page_text:

P1	Yande feere, Goyru tawi geraaɗe les maayo.
P2	O rufi geraaɗe sappo ɗe nder sakru.
P3	Goyru ɗon sukulani geeraaɗe, o ɗon ayna ɗe nder nguli.
P4	Gilangeeru ƴami Goyru: "A laari geeraaɗe am naa?"
P5	Gilangeeru ƴami dabbaji fuu.
P6	Wakati maajum, geeraaɗe susɓini gootel be gootel.
P7	Goyru hokkay ɓikkoy Gilangeeru nyamdu bana ko haani.
P8	Yande feere, Gilangeeru nani hoolo nder reedu ɓikkoy Gillangeeru.
P9	Gilangeeru nasti nden o fuɗɗi faɗɗugo Goyru be wucco maako.
P10	Gilangeeru farti Goyru dow laawol.
P11	Goyru dari o wi'i gilangeeru: "Useyni yaafam!"
P12	Gilangeeru yaafani mo nden ɓaawo ɗon o diɓɓi nder ndiyam bee ɓikkoy maako.
