page_text:

P1	Ibizo lami ngingu Bathabile wakwaMahlangu isibongo. Ngineminyaka elitjhumi nanye begodu ngenza ibanga lesihlanu eSizakele Primary School.
P2	Mina ngilala ama iri abunane,  ukuze umzimba wami uphumule ngokwaneleko.
P3	Mina ngihlamba kabili ngelanga,  ukuze umzimba uhlale uhlanzekile.
P4	Amazinyo wami ngiwahlamba kabili ngelanga.
P5	Ngamalanga ngidla isidlo sekuseni ngombana siqakathekile emizimbeni yethu.
P6	Njalo nasiqeda ukudla kwethu,  ngihlanza inkotlelo.
P7	Mina ngiyathanda ukuzilolonga,  ngomdlalo wetenesi ukuze umzimba wami uhlale uphilile.
P8	Mina ngisela amarhalasi abunane wamanzi ngelanga ukuze umzimba wami uhlale uqinile.
P9	Lana ngiphethe injana yami engiyithanda khulu,  begodu ihlala ihlanzekile ngaso soke isikhathi. Bangani asitlhogomeleni imizimba yethu ngaso soke isikhathi sizokwazi ukuphila isikhathi eside.
