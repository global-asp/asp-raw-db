title:	Nema Buys Thread
author:	Little Zebra Books
artist:	Mirjam  Gyger
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/31612.png
desc:	Follow Nema through the market as she tries to buy some thread.

page_text:

P1	This is Nema. @Nema is sewing. @Nema is sewing a skirt.
P2	Nema is sewing with thread. @The thread runs out. @Nema needs to buy more thread. @She needs money for shopping. @Where will she get some money?
P3	Nema sees a tree. @The tree is nearby. @The tree has fruit. @The fruits are papayas.@Papayas are delicious.
P4	Nema has a stick. @Nema gets the papayas.
P5	Nema arrives at the market. @She arrives at the market with the papayas. @She wants to sell the papayas.
P6	Look at the man. @The man buys a papaya. @He gives money to Nema. @Nema sells lots of papayas. @She is going to buy thread with the money.
P7	In the market she sees bananas. @She doesn't buy bananas.
P8	In the market she sees tomatoes. @She doesn't buy tomatoes.
P9	In the market she sees a sieve. @She doesn't buy the sieve.
P10	In the market she sees bread. @She doesn't buy bread.
P11	Then Nema sees a man. @The man is selling thread. @Nema buys thread.
P12	Nema has thread! @Nema is so happy. @She will finish sewing her skirt with the thread.
