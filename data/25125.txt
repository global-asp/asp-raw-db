title:	Omukulu gha amalike
author:	Kyambogo  University and Victor  Muwanguzi
artist:	Isaac Okwir
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Wobanga abagoberezi bomukulu babuuza ebibuuso ebyekisiru

page_text:

P1	Kyagaba kyali kirike kibede.Yali nga atisatisa.
P2	Yali mukulu gha malike.
P3	Abagoberezi be bamuwa olugoye nga lwandawulo.
P4	"Ngyakwambala luno ku mutwe,"Kyagaba ne yakoba.
P5	"Buli muntu goberera amateeka gange.Ti mumbuuza nze!"Yawoingana.
P6	Kale, abagoberezi bamugondera. Kyagaba bweyabuka mwibanga, bulyomu yabuka.
P7	Aye akagoye kyagaba keyesiba kumutwe, kaali kamutipye.
P8	Yagema ku mabwa ag'okumutwe. Amalike gagema ku mitwe gyabyo.
P9	"Kino kiruma,"Kyagaba ne yakoba."Kino kiruma," bona bairamu kyene ekyo.
P10	"kino kinhwevu!" kino kinhwevu," era baibira.
P11	Kyagaba yagwa ghansi,okuva ghangulu. Boonera bamwibira bakikora.
P12	Omukulu gha amalike y'afa. Abagoberezi be banolwa inho, aye basigarawo.
