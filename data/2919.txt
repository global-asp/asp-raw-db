title:	Ebbaluŋa
author:	Gershom Hirome
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2919.png
desc:	Oluŋayo luno luŋamba hu hahaana ahatasobola hutiina husoma olwohuba ahalwaye. Ebbaluŋa jahena jaahola hibbala mu ngeri eyoŋwanyisagana obuhwenda mu hiseera ehi habeereeyemo halwaye.

page_text:

P1	Nasuna ebbaluŋa nyingi mu sabbiiti eyabita.
P2	Omusaŋu wange gaŋandiihira omusomesa wange ebbaluŋa olwabbalasa.
P3	Ebbaluŋa yali yisoma yiiti Tabbisa mulwaye. Ali n'ohusigala engo.
P4	Olwohubiri nasuna ebbaluŋa ohuŋwa eyiri ehibiina hyange.
P5	Abahyange ni banyendesa ohuŋona amangu.
P6	Olwohudatu nasuna ebbaluŋa ohuŋwa ewa meeri yange oyu singa ohwenda.
P7	"Nahubba ehifaanani hino hululwo." Ohwenda ohuŋwa ewa Dooso.
P8	Olwohune nasuna ebbaluŋa ohuŋwa eyiri maama.
