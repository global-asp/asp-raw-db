title:	Wankyundan u nan Bem

desc:	Ken tar u ihyom ngee la

page_text:

P1	Kwase wan ugen za ken ikyô. @@A za u za keren atamaikyon.
P2	Nyer ken ikyô yô a nenge a kon u atam na nyian kuma u yan yô.
P3	Tsô a gbir wankyundan na hen inya. @@Wan la yaven yô kwase la unde sha kon.
P4	Or gbertyo u ken ipaven igen va due her. A nenge a wankyundan ne. @@Kpiligh u iyol. A pine ishima na er, "Ngô u wan ne ye?"
P5	A shiligh azôngo inya. @@Mshirim ma sha mon na la uva man wankyundan la nder.
P6	A de wankyundan la gba numben a mshirim ma shonum. @@Wan la lu numben a ahan zwa.
P7	Kwase  la shir ashe er un a nenge ityôkyaa i wan na lu sen yô. @@A nenge a or u a vande kaven ga yô.
P8	Cier un iyol je gande. @@Ikpa i atamaikyon na kpa i osugh i gba.
P9	Or gbertyo la kenger sha. @@A kaa er, "De cie ga. M ngu numben a numbe vea wan wou u doon ishima ne."
P10	Tsô kwase la kpaa hunwga sha kon.
P11	Or gbertyo la man er shirigh ku genegh. A na wankyundan la. @@A kaa er, "Ngun ka iyua sha ci wou."
P12	"Yem a wan wou ken ya. Kaa nomu a mou twev a yem hen gar ugen pe bem a lu yô. Wankyundan ne nam bemshima," Or gbertyo pase un nahan.
