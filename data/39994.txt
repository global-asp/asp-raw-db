desc:	Sekani unsichana unoda bezi akale unota kuti bezi bebenolushanto kana bashanyila unzi wabo.

page_text:

P1	Nko! Nkoo! Nkoo!@@Pana untimbo wokunkonkoja ponkoba wengumba wokumbeli.
P2	Sekani wakashantilwa kasa.@@"Ndiani?" wakabhuza.
P3	Mai, tinanomwezi, "Pabuya," bakashandula mai babe. @@"Ndobona soti ndamaBuyani. Bazhulile unkoba bengwine."
P4	Sekani unozhula unkoba emwemwetela. "Tomutambula mai, Ngwinani zwenu. Mai bachabeli namwi ngweno ngweno."@@AmaBuyani bakamwemwetela nebo.
P5	"Ndingamuwanila zwimwe zwokunwa na?" wakubhuza Sekani.@@"Aa! Mane Sekani, Taboka kasa," bakashandula amaBuyani.
P6	"Chini, AmaBuyani," bakadalo mai baSekani. "Mundilekelele kumugajika kwonshaji undefu."@@"Apana chibi nkwaina, Sekani wabendikwejisisa yopa ekandilinda."
P7	Bakamwemwetela mai baSekani.@@"Sekani mwana una bulemu akale ndiloshantilwa kasa nezwito zwizwe," bakadalo kuna amaBuyani.
