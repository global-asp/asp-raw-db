desc:	A story about a little boy’s adventures with a very unusual family!

page_text:

P1	My dinosaur and I look at my birds in the sky.
P2	My birds and I meet Chimpanzee in the tree.
P3	My chimpanzee and I play with my giraffes on the hill.
P4	My giraffes and I make monkey balls from clay.
P5	My monkey and I eat bananas with Gorilla every day.
P6	My gorilla and monkey fear Tortoise in every way.
P7	Tortoise and I go home very slowly with Snake.
P8	Snake and I love to hiss and sing songs by the lake.
P9	My entire family and I live together in great happiness!
