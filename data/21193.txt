title:	Nguli Na King'ang'í
author:	Dorcas Wepukhulu
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21193.png
desc:	Undu Nguli yeeyovoisye kuuma kanywani ka Kingángi.

page_text:

P1	Nguli yakomana na Kingáng'i usini.
P2	"Uekala vaa?" Nguli akolia, "Usini," King'ang'i asungia.
P3	"Uekala vaa?" King'ang'i akolia. "Nikalaa mitini," Nguli asungia.
P4	"Niutuvie?" King'ang'i akolia.
P5	"Nditonyaa kutuvia," Nguli asungia. "Ninguuvundisya."
P6	"Twi anyanya. Ndukakie," King'ang'i amutavya.
P7	"Nguli, ango wakwa ni muuwau. Eenda nyama," King'ang'i aasya.
P8	Nguli aakya vyu. King'ang'i nukumuya?
P9	Nguli avanga kutoloka kuuma kwe King'ang'i.
P10	Nguli ena muvango.
P11	"Nekunenga ngoo yakwa," Nguli amuiya.
P12	"Ngoo yakwa yeemutini," Nguli aasya.
P13	"Nuumanthe ngoo yaku?" King'ang'i akolia. "Ii," Nguli asungia.
P14	King'ang'i atuvia amutunga Nguli mutini.
P15	"Wendwi munyanya waa. Nungengie," King'ang'i amutavia.
P16	"Tui anyanya, wenda kundya!" Nguli amutavia.
