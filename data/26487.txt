page_text:

P1	Ono ni mwami Nsoghera.
P2	Ono ni mwami Kyamutwe.
P3	Mwami Kyamutwe ni mwami Nsoghera bali bamukwano.Lwali lulala,baaja okukyakalamu bombi.
P4	Batuuka kumbali kwo omwiga, agho bainhama okughakana benenabeene. Mwami Nsoghera yaakoba,"Tosobola kutuuma mwiga guno!""Yii, nze nsobola! niiwe otasobola!"bweyamwiramu.
P5	"Ighe tuuma!"mwami Nsoghera bweyakoba."Mbe,ighe soka okutuuma,"mwami Kyamutwe bweyakoba yeena. Aghoni mwami Nsghera yaguluka yasala omwiga.
P6	Aye mwami Kyamutwe bweyatuuma yagwa bwamumwiga.Aye olwo omutwegwe omubba yaatandiika okudiimira mu mwiga.
P7	Mwami Nsoghera yaseka,"Hee hee!"Aye yaseka inho nomunwa gwayuzikamu ebitundu bibiri okuva mumbali ni mumbali.
