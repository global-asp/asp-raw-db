P1	One afternoon when Themba and Thabo were sitting with their grandfather having their favorite . Suddenly their grandfather stood up and brought a basket with a pink blanket on top. Grandpa said " I got you a dog boys".
P2	Themba and Thabo could not believe that their grandpa got them a dog. Their grandpa said "eat quick boys before it gets late". "Thank you grandpa" said Themba and Thabo. On their way home they heard a scary sound  then they walked so fast.
P3	The sound repeated again and Thabo looked at Themba shaking. " I'm so scared" said Thabo. As they were walking Themba said " Thabo watch out there's a monkey". They both nearly dropped the basked. The monkey said " hahaha!!!! I got you ".
P4	Themba looked at Thabo and they couldn't believe their eyes. " Bring the basket here i want some food" said Monkey. They boys were so scared they did not want to. The monkey shouted " bring it here!! ". The boys handed the basket to monkey.
P5	The monkey thought that it's food in the basket. The dog jumped out of the basket to bite the monkey. " Help help help !!!!! " shouted the monkey. Themba and Thabo were giggling at the back. Then the monkey run away crying.
