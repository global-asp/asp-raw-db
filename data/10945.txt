desc:	Chicken and Millipede have a very competitive friendship. One day Chicken gets so cross about losing that she does something very silly.

page_text:

P1	Chicken and Millipede were friends.
P2	They decided to play football.
P3	They made turns to defend the goal.
P4	Millipede won the game.
P5	Chicken was very mad.
P6	Chicken swallowed Millipede.
P7	Mother Millipede searched for Millipede.
P8	Mother Millipede heard her son in Chicken.
P9	Chicken began to feel ill.
P10	Chicken started to cough.
P11	Millipede fell out.
P12	Chicken and Millipede weren't friends anymore.
