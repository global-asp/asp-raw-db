desc:	We ask Mom and Dad why things are different colours.

page_text:

P1	Khusina elikulu niliba buluu?
P2	Silibechanga buluu buli lwosi dawe. Libechanga lirio?
P3	Khusina amsafu kabechanga majani?
P4	Sikabechanga buli lwosi majani dawe. Kabechanga kario?
P5	Khulwa sina amaremwa kabechanga namuki?
P6	Amaremwa amengu ni namuki. Amaremwa amabisi ni majani.
P7	Khu sina amnyinya nikaba rateng'i?
P8	Amanyinya kosi sikaraten'I dawe.
P9	Khu sina amabanga nikaba ranjayi?
P10	Khuli nende ebikeke. Ebikiranga amabanga nikaba ranjayi.
P11	Khu sina chumbi niba indafu?
P12	Engorwa.
