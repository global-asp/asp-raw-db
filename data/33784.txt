desc:	Ibhola liyimpilo yazo zonke izingane ezidlalela leli qembu. (Indaba nemifanekiso kuqanjwe engqungqutheleni yokufundisana eyayigqugquzelwe abasebenzi bezengqondo baseSophiatown eGoli ngamaholide kaJuni 2019).

page_text:

P1	UBling wayelithanda kakhulu ibhola.@@Uma ekhuluma ngalo, wayethi, "Ibhola impilo yami."
P2	Wayengomunye wabadlali abavelele ababeqeqeshwa uTshepo.@@UTshepo wayelikhonzile iqembu lakhe.@@Abadlali abaningi bafisa ukulidlalela.
P3	Babezodlala umdlalo obalulekile.@@Iqembu lalizilolonga zonke izinsuku.@@Bonke babezimisele ngalo mdlalo omkhulu!
P4	Umqeqeshi weqembu ababezodlala nalo wayeyindoda enomona.@@Wayenomona ngempumelelo kaTshepo.
P5	Lo mqeqeshi waya enkundleni ngosuku olwandulela umdlalo.@@Wafike wambela khona into enobungozi.@@Wambela khona umuthi!
P6	UBling wadlala kahle kakhulu kuqala umdlalo.@@Wathi eseyolifaka ezintini.@@Wakhubeka!
P7	Wakhahlela umoya, maqede wawa!@@"Bling?" kumemeza abalandeli.@@"Bling?" kuncenga uTshepo.
P8	"Akukho okuyongiwisa!" kusho uBling ngaphakathi.@@"Ibhola impilo yami."@@Wavuka, waqhubeka wadlala kwaze kwaphela umdlalo.
