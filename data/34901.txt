title:	Fati da Koren Maciji

artist:	Therson  Boadu

desc:	Ko wace rana sabuwa ce ga Fati

page_text:

P1	Fati karamar yarinya@  ce mai gudu kamar iska.@  @@  Ta kan so dariya, kuma ta kan yi haka@  Kamar yadda aka so.
P2	Wata rana Fati@  da maman ta da baban ta, su ka je neman@  itace.
P3	Fati ta kan so taimakon@  mamman ta da babban ta, amma akwai abubuwa@  da yawa na gani da koyo.@  @@  Akwai tsuntsaye, burai@  da sararin sama.
P4	Mama ta ce, “Fati saurara! Za ki iya@  neman itace anan da can.”
P5	Amma Fati, Kalli@  inda za ki kuma ki@  yi hankali.”@  @@  Sai ta ce, “Na ji@  mama.”
P6	Can sai maman Fati da Baban suna cikin@  aiki.
P7	Sun samu babban itatuwa.@  @@  Saun samu kananan@  itatuwa.
P8	Fati ma tana cikin@  aiki.@  @@  Ita ma ta samo yan@  itatuwa.@  @@  Ta samu kananun itatuwa sosai.
P9	Sai Fati ta kalli@  sama.@  @@  Sai ta ga jan tsuntu akan doguwar@  ciyawa.
P10	Sai Fati ta kalli@  kasa.@  @@  Sai ta ga tururuwa@  a cikin rairayi.
P11	Fati ta ga ganye@  mai kyau. Ganyen na shaiki@  kuma kore shar.@  @@  Sai ta sa yatsa@  daya akan ganyen,
P12	Fati tace, “Wannan@  ba yi kama@  da ganye ba.”@  @@  Nan take, sai ta ga ganyen mai@  kyau yayi motsi.@  @@  Fati tace, “Wannan@  ba ganye ne mai kyau ba.@  Wannan koren maciji ne!”
P13	Fati ta yi ihu,@  “Wayoo!”@  @@  Sai ta ruga da gudu@  zuwa neman babbanta da mammanta.
P14	Fati ta ga babbanta@  sai ta yi ihu, “Maciji! Maciji!”@  @@  Babbanta yace, “Ina yake?@  Ina yake?”@  @@  Fati ta ce, “Ga shi can! Ga shi@  can!”
P15	Sai babanta ya kori koren macijin@  da sanda.@  @@  Koren macijin ya subule.
P16	Baba yace, “Fati, Allah ya tsare ki.@  @@  Muje gurin mama, mu sanar@  da ita game da koren maciji.
P17	Mama ta ce, “Fati, ina miki@  murna da koren macijin bai cije@  ki ba.”
P18	Amma nan gaba, ki@  kula da inda kike zuwa, kuma ki@  yi hankali.”@  @@  Kuma karshen kenan.
