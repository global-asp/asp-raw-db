P1	Pakistan is our country. It got it's independence from British empire on 14th August 1947 after a long and hard struggles of Muslims of South Asia, under the leadership of Quaid-e-Azam Mohammad Ali Jinnah. Our country consists of five provinces, the Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Gilgit Baltistan, and Balochistan. Islamabad the most beautiful and modern city is capital of our country. Karachi is the biggest city, while Lahore, Quetta, Peshawar, Faisalabad, Multan, Sukkur and Hyderabad are other big cities. The population of our country is about 14 crores.The majority but non-muslims minorities enjoy equal rights.
