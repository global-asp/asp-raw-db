page_text:

P1	Epuri, Mwalapwa ni Enyompe yaahipakana onthamwene. Nihiku nimosa, yaaheetta mukwaha awela mukaaroni.
P2	Vaaphiyal'aya waarowaaya, mulipa-oweettiha aahaavekela musurukhu woolivela mukwahaaya. @@Enyompe yaahiliva.
P3	Mwalapwa aahiliva ovikaniha esisapo, okhala wiira khaarina musurukhu waaphaveliwa.
P4	Mulipa-ooweettiha ophavelaka omuttikiherya Mwalapwa musurukhu awe waahanle, okathi yoowo epuri yaahitthyawa ehilinve.
P5	Mulipa-ooweettiha aahinanariwa, onarowa ohimuttikihenrye Mwalapwa musurukhu awe waahanle.
P6	Tivo, mpakha olelo va, Mwalapwa oniiraawe oona ekaaro onoomola othurumelaka muhina wiira oone akhala wiira onoomphwanya mulipa-ooweettiha, yoole ahaamuttikihenrye musurukhu awe waahanle.
P7	Epuri yiiwaka-ru oruma w'ekaaro ennitthyawa. Enoova otthukiwa mwaha woohiliva musurukhu w'ekwah'aya.
P8	Enyompe yoona ekaaro erwaaka khenuukhuwa, eneetta sintthun'aya muramphani, okhala wiira yoosuwela wiira yooliva musurukhu woomalela wa mukwaha.
