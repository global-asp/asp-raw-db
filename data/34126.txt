page_text:

P1	If you would like to be strong,@A healthy lifestyle you must live.
P2	With a balanced diet you can't go wrong!
P3	Run, skip, swim, hike and ride,@To keep your bones and muscles fit.
P4	With exercise you can't lose your stride!
P5	Remember to wash after fun and play.@Cleanliness is a key part of health.
P6	With soap and water the germs can't stay!
P7	Your sparkling teeth show a beautiful smile .@Work to avoid cavities, plaque and decay.
P8	With brushing and flossing bad breath can't rile!
P9	In times of trouble your heart may feel sick.@Maybe you're sad, angry, lonely or afraid,
P10	With proper guidance bad moods can't stick!
P11	We need to keep our Earth alive.@Avoid deforestation and destruction.
P12	With eco-solutions pollutants can't thrive.
P13	New energy comes from rest.@Relax your body and mind.
P14	With a good night's sleep you can't help but be your best.
P15	Staying healthy is quite easy to do.@Take good care of your mind, soul and body.
P16	With a healthy you, you CAN have a Happy You!
