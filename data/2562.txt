P1	Wafumba akatooke Watwala kungira Omukaire yakalya Yasesa mukenda Omukyala wefune Akagele kano Akalimu obudondo Obweikere.
P2	Instructions 1. Sit in a circle with legs stretched out into the centre. 2. Start singing the song. 3. When the song stops,  fold the leg on which the song stops. 4. The play goes on until the last person to fold his/her leg has been found. 5. That one chooses the rest until he/she gets at least three of them. 6. The game is repeated.
