P1	My sister's dress is too big for me!
P2	My brother's jersey is also too big for me.
P3	My bag is big. @@But not too big!
P4	This old belt is too small for me.
P5	This is my doll's hat.
P6	My socks are the right size.@@But the wrong colour!
P7	These are my new shoes.
P8	They fit me very well.
