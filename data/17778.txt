title:	Te Naabo Pendo
author:	Raphael Lieber (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Dagaare
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/17778.png
desc:	A senseloŋ ŋa kyaarɛɛ naabo kaŋa a yirdeme naŋ nɔŋ o yɛlɛ yaga.

page_text:

P1	Ndalo la ŋa.
P2	Ndalo nɔŋ la gama kannoo.
P3	Pendo la ŋa.
P4	Pendo nɔŋ la karɔɔto ɔɔbo.
P5	Ka Ndalo wa yi sakuuri wa,  o maŋ de la Pendo yi ka te ɔɔ mõɔ.
P6	O maŋ ko la Pendo kɔŋvelaa ka o nyu.
P7	N saa ɔŋ la kamabie ko Pendo ka o ɔɔ.
P8	O maŋ pɛŋ la Pendo berre bebiri zaa.
P9	Pendo taa la beroŋ yaga.
P10	N saa maŋ koɔre la a Pendo beroŋ.
P11	Ndalo maŋ nyu la berkõɔ bebiri zaa.
P12	O maŋ yeli la,  "Pendo,  bareka!"
