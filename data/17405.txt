page_text:

P1	Tingi lived with his grandmother.
P2	He used to look after the cows with her.
P3	One day the soldiers came.
P4	They took the cows away.
P5	Tingi and his grandmother ran away.
P6	They hid in the bush until night.
P7	Then the soldiers came back.
P8	Grandmother hid Tingi under the leaves.
P9	One of the soldiers put his foot right on him,  but he kept quiet.
P10	When it was safe,  Tingi and his grandmother came out.
P11	They crept home very quietly.
