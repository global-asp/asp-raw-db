page_text:

P1	Munabwire muthukatwamo erighenda
  Embale eriyabungira banywani bethu.
P2	Mutwaghenda ahasyamotoka sikapakirira
  twamabana eyerighenderamo.
  mutwabya aberimbere erikalhamuyo.
P3	Mutwalinda ndambi nene
  kwihika emotoka yisulira.
P4	Mutwatsuka olhughendo. Omwanzira
  mutwaheka abandu abandi.
P5	Nabandi banene.
P6	Nabandi banene
P7	Kandi nabandi banene
P8	Mutwavuga omwa malima
  awomukyeri ne bikusa.
P9	Mutwalhangira esyonde, esyombene
  nesyongoko.
P10	Mutwalhangira esyogali, esyopikipiki
  ne bimotoka ebinene nene.
P11	Mutwalhangira ekitwa kinene
  ekikahulhawamo Eligoni.
P12	Yabya ekitunguthano kandi
  omwamotoka mwabya bandu banene.
P13	Nikwa mutwahika Embale
  kandi mutwabana banyani bethu.
P14	Kyabyakiro bibuyanga.
