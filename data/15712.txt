title:	Ng'ombe Mwenye Pembe Moja
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Kiswahili
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15712.png
desc:	Umewahi kumwona ngombe mwenye pembe moja na bila mkia?

page_text:

P1	Haaaa! Nyumbani kwetu,  kuna ng'ombe mzuri.
P2	Hana mkia na ana pembe moja tu. Ni ng'ombe mzuri sana.
P3	Ni ng'ombe mnono mwenye tumbo kubwa.
P4	Ni ng'ombe mwenye nguvu.
P5	Mchungaji anampenda ng'ombe huyu sana. Amemfunza ng'ombe huyu kuitambua sauti yake pekee.
P6	Malishoni,  ng'ombe huyu hula pekee yake.
P7	Ng'ombe huyu ni hodari kwa vita na ng'ombe wengine hawamsumbui.
P8	Watu wengi wanamjua ng'ombe huyu. Wanamwogopa kwa sababu yeye hupigana kwa pembe yake moja. Ukikutana na ng'ombe wa aina hii,  utafanya nini?
