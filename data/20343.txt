title:	Libungwe Nende Ikwena
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	
lang:	Oluwanga

desc:	Yechesia shinga Libungwe lieonokosia okhulibwa nende Ikwena.

page_text:

P1	Libungwe liakanana Ikwena khu nyanza.
P2	"Wamenya hena?" Ikwena yakalusia, "Munyanza."
P3	"Wamenya hena?" Libungwe liakalusia, "Mumisala."
P4	"Wamanya okhusoka?" Ikwena yareba Libungwe.
P5	Libungwe liekana. Ikwena neibola, "Enyala okhwechesia."
P6	Ikwena yabola, "Olahenda ta. Khuli abetsa."
P7	Ikwena yabola, "Khotsa wanje nomulwale. Yenya inyama."
P8	Libungwe neliba nende oburi. Ikwena naimulie?
P9	Libungwe lienya okhwibatsa.
P10	Libungwe lili nende liparo.
P11	"Ndalainia omwoyo kwanje omuheresie," Libungwe lialaka.
P12	"Omwoyo kwanje kuli khumusala," Libungwe liabola.
P13	Ikwena yareba, "Olenda omwoyo kwo?" Libungwe lieyama.
P14	Ikwena yakalukha mulukongo. Libungwe lianina khumusala.
P15	Ikwena yaboola, "Obeere! Sholi omwitsa wanje ta."
P16	"Shekhuli abetsa ta. Wenya okhundia!" Libungwe liabola.
