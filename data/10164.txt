page_text:

P1	A walk in kampala,  what do you see? Shops selling clothes,  in very congested streets.
P2	A shower in Nakivuubo,  what do you see? Owino market,  taxi park,  play ground,  petrol station,  mukwano arcade.
P3	A drive in kampala,  what do you see? Very tall buildings,  very many cars of different types.
P4	Languages in kampala,  what do you hear? Luganda, Swahili,  English and Lunyankole,  all are being spoken.
P5	A breakfast in Kampala,  what do you see? Menvu, black tea, milk tea and Bushera.
P6	A trip to kampala,  do you wish to go back?
