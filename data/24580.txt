desc:	When my mother died

page_text:

P1	She carried me for nine months.
P2	She taught me how to dig.
P3	She counselled me whenever i went wrong.
P4	We always had evening walks together.
P5	She made sure that I covered myself every night.
P6	She allowed me to choose whatever I wanted.
P7	I will never forget her love in my life.
