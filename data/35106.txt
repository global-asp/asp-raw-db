title:	Echibalo

desc:	Ddumullanga mwawitti msepuka amtiri oyo acha ezipasoni ddi ddinchichi?

page_text:

P1	Nllapo nimodda amma yakulla ezipaso dda mitundu yohiyanahiyana.
P2	"Ezipaso ddimodda nnakhallena llini?" nahakoha.@@"Nnache ddimoddha mahiwu," amma yanillella.
P3	Munnaka Rahim tta ntiri.@@Allawiha zipasoni ddittheddene. Acha ddinchichi.
P4	"Ngonani ddenre Rahim opanga!" Avuwiha maawu munnaka mung'onong'ono.@@"Rahim ddohiwa na oddikonda," kallota.
P5	Amma yasha murima na Rahim.
P6	Nahenenattho namushera murima Rahim.@@Nave Rahim khiddamukhudza.
P7	"Munoromulanga Rahim?" akoha munnaka mung'onong'ono.
P8	"Rahim, ddinorowukhuza nanuwene," amma yachenjeza.
P9	Rahim ahambella ohiwa phaama.
P10	"Mmirimani mwaka munnavoreya," Rahim allota maddu avatthivatthi.
P11	Amma yadduwella echifukwene Rahim avoriwaye.@@"Zipasoni ddomulanga Rahim," yaganiza.
P12	Vomallihella, Rahim anipepesa. "Ngiwattho ni ntiri willi," anilonjeza.@@Namukhullupillella.
