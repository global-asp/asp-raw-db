P1	Kwakukhona intombazane igama layo kwakunguLinda. Wayehlala nodadewabo ababili, umfowabo kanye nomama wakhe. @@Wayeyintombazane ejabule futhi ekhuthele kakhulu.
P2	Umngane wakhe omkhulu kwakunguStacy. @@Babethembana noStacy futhi benezimfihlo zabo bobabili.
P3	Njalo ngoLwesibili babeya ebhayisikobho ngoba babenikezwa uphophukhoni wamahhala.
P4	Emva kwebhayisikobho, babehlala epaki balalele umculo bese bezitika ngo-ayisikhilimu.
P5	ULwesibili lolu kwakungusuku lukaLinda olumnandi. @@Ukubuka ibhayisikobho emnandi nokudla uphophukhoni wamahhala.
P6	Emva kwalokho kube umculo ababewulalela noStacy epaki nokuzitika ngo-ayisikhilimu.
P7	Izikhathi ezimnandi azihlali zikhona. @@Kufanele ufunde ukuzithakasela futhi uzazise.
P8	Kanjalo nabantu ohlanganyela nabo kulobo bumnandi.
