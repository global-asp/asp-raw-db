desc:	A story about the creation of the world and people.

page_text:

P1	God wanted someone to take care of the world.
P2	Lion volunteered, "I am the king, I can do that."
P3	Elephant shouted, "I am big. I am right for the job."
P4	Shark thought his sharp teeth made him the right one.
P5	Eagle laughed, "I fly and see everything. I am fit."
P6	God said, "I will create humans for this job."
P7	God created men and women with black skin.
P8	Others had brown skin.
P9	Others had yellow skin.
P10	Others had pink skin.
P11	Shark laughed at the humans, "How will they swim?"
P12	God smiled, "They will learn."
