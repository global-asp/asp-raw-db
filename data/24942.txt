title:	Okukina
author:	Joseph  Mukwaya
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ekitontome ku mughala anhumirwa okukina. Omusomesa we yamwetamwa aye maama we amwizaamu amaani.

page_text:

P1	Nkina nga ndi musanhufu, nkina nga nnakughaire, nkina nga ndi zenka, nkina nga nnhiize.
P2	Omusomesa wange akoba, "Tyama nga tigheeneenhia! Tomala lunaku lwona lwona ng'okina bukine."
P3	Aye maama wange akoba, "Okukina kukukuuma ng'oli mulamu, kukuleeya, Luliba olwo ghaaba mukini, obeewuunise boonaboona!"
P4	Era nkoba, "Luliba olwo naakina era abantu balisasula okumbona nga needhaga." Baidha kukoba bati, "Akina bulungi inho."
P5	Gaagano amakina genkina.
