desc:	Kibonerezo ki ekisaanira omulenzi owomuluvu eyalya ebibala ebingi.

page_text:

P1	Dhaadha  yagula  ebibala  mu  katale.
P2	Nga  biboneka  nga  biwooma  inho.
P3	Mwanu.hina Vasiti   yali gha  muluvu
P4	Anaani aliire  ebibala?
P5	Dhaadha  yaaniiga  inho  era  yaalekaana.
P6	Tusuubiriza  mu  Vasiti.
P7	Twakobera  dhaadha.
P8	Dhaadha  yaamubuuza  era  yaasaba  ekisonhiwo.
P9	Eby'embi, Vasiti yatandiika  obutawulira  bukalamu.
P10	Yaalumukira  mu  tooyi.
P11	Vasiti yaagemerera  enda  ye.
P12	Dhaadha  yaamubuuza, "Oyega  ki  okuva  ku Vasiti?"
