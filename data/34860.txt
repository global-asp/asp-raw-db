desc:	Luwo sneaks out of church to go home. But finding his house isnt as easy as he thought.

page_text:

P1	Nna Luwo wun lasun gwa lo coci.
P2	Eza zhi dokun fi coci'o. Ace ko eni.
P3	Luwo ci, a tinya coci'o. Nna ma kpeka.
P4	Luwo cid a kpe gi eko'o.@@Ci jin tunani, "Ba emi yi dan'o."
P5	Unci lo egboro degi, unci ta eye le emi wanci ko ye. @@  "Kai, nana jhin emi yi nya. Emi yi kwaan ni'a."
P6	Unci ke lo yegboro degi be. Unci taye le evun na a'tu ta ebe'ona ye.@@Kai, nana jhin emi yi naya. Emi yi ta eba' bo'a.
P7	Unci cigaba be ezanda'e. Unci taye le emi zanna'e na ye.@  @"Kai, nana jhin emi yi nya. Emi yi de lankpa."
P8	Unce ke lo yegboro. Unfe keze le emi maka'gi ye be cigban'e gi egboro.@@"Kai, nana zhin emi yi nya. Cigban guba zhi egboro emi yi bo'o."
P9	Unda mini tunani'o, wun ce le nna'u ye. Wun ci bici@datsun'u. @@Nna cig an, "Bi'da emi."
P10	KiciyiemiLuwo zhi'o?
