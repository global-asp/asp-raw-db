title:	Nawudho mu biseera byo bulamu bwe
author:	Paul Wambi
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach, Vusi Malindi, Rob Owen and Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Olufumo lukukobera engeri Taape yaakozesaamu ebiseera eka ni ku isomero.

page_text:

P1	Nawudho Taape asituka ku saawa ndala ey'okunco. Anaaba mu maiso, yeesenhia, yaavaala yunifoomu yaaja ku isomero.
P2	Muganda ghe omukulu amugherekeraku. Bajanga bwe boogera okutuusa Nawudho bwa atuuka ku isomero.
P3	Ku saawa ibiri ne daakiika ikumi aba ali mu kibiina.
P4	Ku saawa ina, azaanha ni baine. Awuumuzaamu ku biroghoozo nga yeetegekera eisomo eriiraaku.
P5	Mu kibiina, akola bulungi inho. Nabirye omusomesa we ekyo kyayenda.
P6	Omusomesa abagha ekiseera eky'okuwuumula. Oluvainhuma, alambala kumpi n'omusomesa.
P7	Bwe basituka, bayambaganaku okusala enkomo edha gaalimuuka okutuuka eka.
P8	Nawudho bwatuuka eka, abaaku agho n'abantu abaghaka nga baghaya.
P9	Nga ekyeigulo kiwoire, anaaba, yaavala ekiteeteeyi ekituuka ku bigere. Aja ku bulili. Yaasaba yaamala yaatendhuka.
P10	Okozesa otya ebiseera eby'olunaku lwo?
