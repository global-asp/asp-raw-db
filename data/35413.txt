desc:	A boy is promised a wonderful present on his sixth birthday. Find out what happened.

page_text:

P1	My village has goats. @@It is far from big cities.
P2	Mother said, "Zainab, we have a present for your sixth birthday."
P3	Mother, aunt and I waited for the bus.
P4	I sat on mother’s lap. @@I slept the whole way.
P5	We reached the market. @@I saw a beautiful blue helicopter.
P6	One stall had fruits. @@I asked mother, “Which ones are these?”
P7	Mother said, “These are apples.” @@I begged, “Please, buy me one.”
P8	I forgot my mother’s hand. @@I thought only of my apple.
P9	When I finished eating, I was alone. @@I started to cry.
P10	A woman took me where there were other children.
P11	I thought children were sold at the market. @@I cried more.
P12	A man asked, "Where is Zainab?"@@I hid away from him.
P13	My mother's familiar voice called, "Zainab!" @@I was very happy.
P14	My aunt pulled out a beautiful helicopter. @@"It's yours!" she said.
