P1	Once upon a time there lived a man called Wisdom. He was a medicine man and his wife was called Blessing. They had a daughter called Proud. Wisdom exchanged medicines with money so that he can provide for his family.
P2	As usual, one day he went to the market so that he can market his medicine. He spread his mat and arranged his medicine for easy identification by the customers.One short rich man accidentally stepped on wisdom's medicine.
P3	The man walked away and he did not apologize.On his way his wife called and informed him about their daughter who was sick. The man remembered the mistake he done.He went back shouting"forgive me" "forgive me" and cure my daughter.
P4	Proud ,wisdom and Blessings and the Rich Man walked stealthily to where the daughter of the Rich Man.the daughter was so afraid to see the Medicine for him it was not usual thing."what happened father" the daughter asked.
P5	"what did you brought for  me" Daughter asked his Father.  I brought for you your favorite.which favourite?  Daughter asked again@"Chips kuku" and he asked the Rich why are this people here and I know what they have done to me.
P6	Questions lingered on the Rich Man,her Daughter feared the Medicine man could not heal so easily because of the envy which both had together. The Rich Man pleaded for the forgiveness as he saw the daughter her condition his worsening.
P7	The daughter's condition worsened he was unable to talk neither to eat because of what he uttered to the Medicine man.@The medicine walked out from the hospital so sad because of what the daughter said about him.
P8	Wisdom was filled with alot of hunger ,how she was shouting at him but thoughts could not be able to heal the daughter.he then thought again what if i forgive her what will happen.Wisdom thought that forgiving could be the healer.
P9	Wisdom forgave the Daughter and finally the daughter received the healing.the Rich Man was so happy that her daughter could be able resume her daily duties.
