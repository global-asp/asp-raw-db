page_text:

P1	Agho olwatuuka, ngaghabaayo ghakinoni gwebayentanga Mojo eyaviiranga mu bwakyabazinga bwa Baro. Ghankudhu neyali mukaagwa ghe. Mojo yali mubaka ela yajanga ghala okutuusa obubaka.
P2	Lulala kyabazinga Ghamplogoma yatumya Mojo. Kyabazinga yamwendha atuuse obubaka obwo'muganulwo mubwakyabazinga obundhi. Ogholugandha lwe Ghampala neyali kyabazinga eluuyi eyo.
P3	Ghankudhu bweyaghulira kwekyo, yaaghulira enuggu. Yaagha Mojo amagezi, "Otaja. Ako katego. Ghampologoma ayendha kukusindhikayo nga sadhaaka."
P4	Mojo yatyamuuku. Enkyo, yaajaku y'ogholugandha lwe Ghampaanga. Mojo yamukobera ku Ghankudhu byamukobye. Ghampaanga tiiyesiga Ghankudhu.
P5	"Njaakutegekerayooku akamere akaghoomu eigulo lino," ghankudhu yeyama atyo ngayaganaine Mojo olwailaku. Mojo yaja egha Ghankudhu kukyeigulo nga musagaalufu.
P6	Mojo ng'ali mwigandhaalo, Ghankudhu yamusuulamu akatimba! Aleeni yaaja endha okusolonga enku. Mojo ngatasobola kuvaagho. "Munambe, munambe!" yaalira.
P7	Ghampaanga yali kumpi nendhu ya Ghankudhu, yaghulira Mojo nga ghoigana. Yayeta "Mojo? Mojo?" Ghampaanga! idha onunule! Mojo yailamu. Ghampaanga yayangughaku okuja kukatego Mojo mweyali.
P8	Ghampaaga yatakuluula Mojo okuva mu katimba.
Mojo yebaza Ghampaanga ela ibo beteera yo.Mojo yakoberaku Ghampologoma kukino. Kyabazinga yaghera Ghankudhu mubwakabaka.
