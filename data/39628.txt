page_text:

P1	Mbali intfombatane letsandza timbali tamake wakhe.
P2	Uvuka atselele timbali njalo ekuseni
P3	Emini ujabulela kubuka timbali take tichakatile.
P4	Lesinye sikhatsi ukha timbali atihogele liphunga lato lelimnandzi.
P5	Uyatsandza kuba sesivandzeni make amfundzele tincwadzi.
P6	Mbali utsi Nome sekakhulile utoba nesivandze swtimbali lesikhulu.
P7	Kute lokhona kususa Mbali esivandzeni sakubo.
P8	Mbali sewufundzise bangani bakhe kunakekela timbali.
