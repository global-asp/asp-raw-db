desc:	Ndilipi jidzo lawungathama nelubaka gwebhembolufu? Bana haba banowotilakidza zila.

page_text:

P1	Layidza ludo gugo ulikulekule.@Usisedzele pedlo!
P2	Tsimuta kene ukoholele mukokola. Usiwaye zwipukanana!
P3	Ubekule nebamwe hawugwala.
P4	Ngula nesepa kobulaya zwipukanana zwebhembolufu.@Ngula maboko kanjinji.
P5	Ngula maboko kwemasekhondi agaswika makumi mabili (20 seconds).
P6	Lezwa uhingisa kokola. @Lezwa uhingisa gumbo. @Lezwa nemilidza luboko.
P7	Mbala masiki mbuyanana mahhuba ose. @Uhhalile zwimilo nenlomo.
P8	Usibvumile ileli bhembolufu lika pfumbila.
