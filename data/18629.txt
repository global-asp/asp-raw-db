title:	Beenzuma
author:	Vision Milimo
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	ChiTonga

desc:	Boola musa

page_text:

P1	Izina lyangu ndime Simo.
P2	Ndijisi benzuma bone.
P3	Mazina aabo ngaaya: Zizo,  Lele,  Sisa alimwi Ayanda.
P4	Mbuli mbomuzyi kale kuti muntu a muntu inga ulijisi cintu ncayandisisya kucita kapati mubuumi bwakwe. Zizo ulauyanda musobano wabbola citaambiki.
P5	Lele mwanamusankwa uyanda kudubbwida mukati mumeenda malamfu.
P6	Sisa walo ukkomanina kapati cisobano cakalaambe. Musobano ooyu linji-linji ucitika ciindi camasiku nokuli mwezi.
P7	Ayanda walo uyanda kubala.
P8	Ino muyeeya kuti mebo ncisobano nzi nceyanda. Mebo ndilayanda kucita kufumbwa ceeco beenzuma ncobayanda kucita. Mubbola,  ndajanika. Akube kukudubbwida,  nkweli. Kalaambe,  tacaambwi. Ikubala,  mumwesu.
P9	Eelyo aboobo kwiina nkwezulilwa,  nyina andaba. Kwaboola Zizo,  ndimubbola.
P10	Ndasika kuli Lele,  nkudubbwida.
P11	Mwaleta Sisa,  ndimukalaambe.
P12	Waitwa ayebo,  sunu,  sena ncisobano nzi ncoyandisisya kapati?
P13	Boola tuume bbola.
P14	Boola tudubbwide.
P15	Boola tusobane kalaambe.
P16	Langa waindwa,  boola tubale antoomwe!
