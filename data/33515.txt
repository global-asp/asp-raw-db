desc:	Neo o lahlehetswe ke fariki ya hae ka phoso. Ekaba ho etsahetseng kamora moo?

page_text:

P1	Neo o palame baesekele le fariki ya hae.
P2	A thula lejwe tseleng, fariki ya wela kwana, ya lla ya re, "Hoooi! hoooi!"
P3	Tsotang a bona fariki ya Neo e ntse e solla, yaba o a ipotsa o re, "Ebe fariki ee ke ya mang?"
P4	A e lelekisa a ba a e tshwara.
P5	A nka fariki a e isa moreneng. Morena a mmolella hore  a e boloke.
P6	Neo a utlwa sello, "Hmmm, hmmm, hmmm!"
P7	A potela a ya sheba kamora ntlo, a thaba haholo ha a bona fariki ya hae.
P8	Neo a bua le fariki ya hae a re, "Ha re ye, re ke ke ra hlola re palama baesekele hape."
