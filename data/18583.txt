title:	Ifyo Kanyelele Aapuswishe Inkunda
author:	Eunice Mukonde-Mulenga
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	IciBemba

desc:	Umushili nauma. Akanyelele kali neecilaka saana. Kaaya kumumana mukunwa amenshi nomba bakapyangila kumo naamenshi. Inkunda yapususha akanyelele. Nomba akanyelele katashishe shani inkunda?

page_text:

P1	Akasuba kalibalile saana mulusuba ninshi na mumpaanga na muuma.
P2	Kanyelele tanwinepo amenshi pa nshiku ishingi.
P3	"Ndefwaya itoni lya menshi nangu fye lya kutona kwibuula," efyasosele Kanyelele.
P4	Nomba nangu muume walyuma.
P5	"Nga nshinwinepo amenshi nalaafwa," efyalilile Kanyelele. "Ningile ukuya kumumana naumfwapo."
P6	"Umumana naawisula kabili uli na maka. Uleekusenda," efyo Akabundi kamano kasokele Kanyelele.
P7	Leelo Kanyelele aali neecilaka saana. "Nalaafwa nga nshinwine amenshi." Elyo abweleelemo mukufwaya umumana.
P8	Apitile pafikako fya fyaani na pamisambo yauma.
P9	Aendele mpaka aumfwile uko amenshi yaleesansa. Waali mumana! Aumfwile amabimbi ya menshi.
P10	Kanyelele aminine umusa uutali uwamenshi yatalala.
P11	Atemenwe cibi icakuti tamwene nooko ibimbi lyaleeisa.
P12	Kanyelele aeseshe ukwikatilila kucani caleelela ukupita apo ali. Leelo apyangiilwe kumo na menshi.
P13	''Mutuule! Mutuule!'' efyo aitile.
P14	''Endesha,  niina apa," efyo inkunda yabuuta yatiile,  naikata na kamusambo mukanwa kaiko."
P15	"Nalaaikala pano pakuti ntootele kunkunda," efyo Kanyelele atontonkenye.    "Nalaalolela mpaka abwele mukunwa amenshi."
P16	Ubushiku bumo ilyo aaleelolela inkunda,  abalumendo babili baishile ku mumana. Baliikwete imilegeni.
P17	''Kwaliba inkunda iikulu iyabuuta iisa mukunwa amenshi," efyasosele umulumendo umo. "Twalaaliila ubushiku.
P18	''Nshaleke abalumendo ukwipaya inkunda yabuuta," efyatontonkenye Kanyelele. "Nomba naalicepesha,  kuti nacita shani?
P19	Elyo inkunda yabuuta yaapupukile panshi ukufuma kucimuti mukunwa amenshi.
P20	Kanyelele atontonkenye ifyakucita.
P21	Aatolokele palukasa lwa mulumendo umo. Kanyelele asumine ulya mulumendo na maka yakwe yonse.
P22	Umulumendo aalitolweke. Elyo apundile," Mayo!"
P23	Inkunda ya buuta efyo yatulumwike nookupupuka. Efyo yapuswike.
P24	Efyo Kanyelele atooteele inkunda yabuuta pakumupususha bamupuswishe.
