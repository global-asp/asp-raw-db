page_text:

P1	Ninka ni kutantere esanseko lyange. Ame kwa varekere ngohanzegona.
P2	Ame kwatwere unene apa zina kupeperange mpepo.
P3	Ose vakaume wovanene, nove name.
P4	Ame kutereka nondya doge.
P5	Ame kukutemesera momasiku goge.
P6	Ame kwa kara noyineya yoyinzi.
P7	Ame gumwe gekoro lyoge.
P8	Nye nomena apa ono kuzigidange kembo lyoge. Kapi na hara kusigange nyamelike.
