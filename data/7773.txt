title:	My Daughter Polar
author:	Cissy Namugerwa
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/7773.png
desc:	This story describes a girl called Polar who liked listening and telling stories.This made her loved by both her teacher and mother.

page_text:

P1	This my daughter Polar.
P2	She is a good girl.
P3	She likes telling stories.
P4	I tell her stories.
P5	Polar tells her friends stories too.
P6	One day Polar told a very good story.
P7	The story was about cooking.
P8	Her teacher gave her a cake and all became happy.
