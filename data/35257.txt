page_text:

P1	Mbava mbili dzakadzihaka khwiba tjimanga muminda yenlimi gumwe busiku. Dzakabe dzisingazibane idzedzi mbava.
P2	Mbava yekutanga yakaswika ikavuna tjimanga ikatjilonga musaka.
P3	Mbava yebubili yakangina muminda yenlimi ikasiya bhasikili  layo kuhhe.
P4	Mbava idzedzi dzakahangana muminda. Bakakuwa bose beti, "Ndiwe yani?"
P5	Bakatotomoka, bekatihha. Umwe ekayenda ngeno umwe ekayenda yeno.
P6	Mbava yekutanga yakasiya saka letjimanga.
P7	Mbava yebubili ikasiya bhasikili layo.
P8	Nlimi wakati ehha ekahingisa bhasikili ekasenga saka letjimanga.
