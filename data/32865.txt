title:	Zama Mukali
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa and Maureen Musale
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	ChiTonga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Caano eeci caambilizya kuti mbotulaya bana

page_text:

P1	Mukwesu wangu musankwa ulamuka koona.  Ndilabuka kufwambana nkaambo ndimukali!
P2	Ndime ndileta zuba.
P3	"Uli nyenyenzi yangu.'' Baamba baama.
P4	Ndilalisanzya abuzuba,  kakunyina kugwasyigwa.
P5	Bamaama balandiyeezya,  ''Utalubi menyo yako.'' Ndilamvwila, '' Peepe, Kutali ndime.''
P6	Bamaama balandiyeezya,  ''Utalubi menyo yako.'' Ndilamvwila, '' Peepe, kutali ndime.''
P7	Ndamana kusamba ndilanzya bakaapa abamaama basyoonto. Ndilabakulwaizya buzuba bubotu.
P8	Ndamana ndalisamika akwaamba kuti "Ndimupati lino  baama."
P9	Ndilakwanisya kulikopela nkopela akulisamika mabbusu.
P10	Ndilabona kuti mukwesu wazyiba makani akucikolo.
P11	Mucikolo ndilabeleka canguzu munzila ili yoonse.
P12	Abuzuba dilacita zyoonse zinti ezyi zibotu. Pesi cintu nceyandisisya kapati nkusobana akusobana!.
