P1	Long ago when the Earth was still new there lived sun and the moon@@ ,the sun and the moon husband and wife. The sun had a friend called water the sun always wondered why the water never came to visit him
P2	The sun went to the water and said to him," why do you never visit me" @@and the water replied and said ,"because my family is too big so I cannot come to your house because the sea creatures don't have a place to live" said the water
P3	So the sun told the waters that he is gonna@@build a house little did the sun know that the water had a big family@@ when he was done building the house so he invited his friends over
P4	So the water and his friends came in one by one not knowingly @the house got full and so the sun and the moon had to go on the roof so that they will not drown
P5	When they had gone to the rooftop they that saw they had no place to live and they decided to live in the sky @they had to separate and live in the sky that is the Tale of why the sun and the moon live in the sky the end
