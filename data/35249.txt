desc:	Follow a little Mozambican girl and her family as they learn how to cope with the coronavirus

page_text:

P1	I heard that Granddad is sick. @@Now my brother is coughing, too.
P2	Grandmother shows him how to cough into his elbow.
P3	We call the hospital to talk to Granddad.
P4	I pray that he gets better soon.
P5	Mom buys masks so we can visit Granddad.
P6	Brother stays at home just in case.
P7	I wash my hands with soap before I leave.
P8	I keep my hands clean when I greet my friend.
P9	We take a taxi to the hospital.
P10	Granddad feels better already.
