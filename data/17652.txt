desc:	Ke eng se o ratang go se dira?

page_text:

P1	Ke rata go tlola kgathi le tsala ya me. I enjoy skipping with my friend.
P2	Ke rata go kwala ditlhaka mo botong. I enjoy writing letters on the board.
P3	Ke rata go buisa dibuka tse di na leng ditshwantsho. I enjoy reading books with pictures.
P4	Ke rata go ja maungo a a farologaneng. I enjoy eating different fruit.
P5	Ke rata go thusa mo gae. I enjoy helping at home.
P6	Ke rata go reetsa dipadi tsa ga mme. I enjoy listening to my mother's stories.
P7	Ke rata go robala mo bolaong jo bogolo. I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.
P8	Ke rata go dira dilo tse. Wena o rata go dira eng? I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?
