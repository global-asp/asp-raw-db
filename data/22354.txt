title:	Tomu omutunzi w'amenvu
author:	Samson  Nabbimba
artist:	Atilabachew Reda
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22354.png
desc:	A man goes against community norms and brings his bananas to sell at the market.

page_text:

P1	Tomu yetika olusaniya lw'amenvu.
P2	Tomu agenda mu katale natunda amenvu.
P3	Abantu mu katale bali mukugula bibala.
P4	Naye tewali muntu nomu agula menvu ga Tomu. Abantu basinga kwagala kugula bibala kuva ku bakazi.
P5	"Mukitundu kyaffe, abakazi bebatunda ebibala boka," abantu bwe'bagamba. "Ono omusajja wakika ki?"
P6	Naye Tomu teyagwaamu maanyi. Yayitanga bantu, "Mugule amenvu! Mugule amenvu gange agawooma ate agengedde."
P7	Omukazi omu kyeyava atoola ekiwagu kya'menvu okuva ku lusaniya. Neyekebejja amenvu gano nobwegendereza.
P8	Omukazi awo nasalawo okugula amenvu.
P9	Abantu nebeyongera okugenda kumudaala. Nebatandika okugula amenvu ga Tomu nebalya.
P10	Amangu olusaniya nelukalira. Awo Tomu yatandika okubala sente zeyali afunye.
P11	Era Tomu yagulamu Sabuuni, Sukaali n'omugaati. Nateeka ebintu bye ku lusaniya.
P12	Awo Tomu neyetika olusaniya lwe kumutwe nadayo ewaka.
