title:	Tikyɛa Talata
author:	R.Y. Tongo and J. Salifu  (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Mampruli
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Soalin gyinŋa kan btɔa tɔasira n wulsirila tikyâa sunŋu ni ŋɔn wulsiriti seem.

page_text:

P1	Nkarimma nŋɔ. U yuuri Tikyɛa Talata. U boori ti paam.
P2	Tikyɛa Talata wulisila bachi koaba. Uwulsiri ti la bachi koaba vuuri. N nintoi mbolli: a, e, i, o, u.
P3	Tikyɛa Talata wulisi ti la buna koom. Mmi bunzie, bunpeili, bundoazum ni bunsabli.
P4	Ti gbihibu saha yi ti paai,  U yi yuumini ni ti gbisi. Lala yuuma ma nyala goom yumma.
P5	Goom yuuni ma nyala nŋɔ: Salima goom timti i nini, laamusa musa gurii i ti nia. Gbihima nsuhuyura, dikum Ka nyuum goom yuunsuma nti.
P6	Tikyɛa Talata mi soalima paam. U tiritila soalim kokɔapa bɛo kam bakwai puuni.
P7	Atanidari u yi soalimla soalima zankyanŋi doa wa'ara.
P8	Atalatadari u yi soalimla soalima zan poa wa'ara.
P9	Alaribdari ka u titi looredim soalima.
P10	Alaamisi ka u titi pukpasigu soalima.
P11	Ka Arizuma, ka Tikyɛa Talata chaka ti soalim ti toori nti karimzongu ma.
P12	Ti karimba maa n-nya karimbaŋ sunŋu duniyawa ni. N-yi zoai, n boorila n-nya Kaman Tikyɛa Talata.
