desc:	Oduu durii ilmaa tokkofi hanqaaqu Akoon isaaf keenite. Mucaan kun bareedahinturee garuu erga hanqaaqun kennamefi booda carraa gaari argate.

page_text:

P1	Akkoon ilma mucaa isheetif hanqaaqu tokko kennite.
P2	Mucaanis hanqaaqicha namoota fudura funaananiif kenne. Isaanis hanqaaqicha
P3	Mucaanis, "Hanqaaqun qabeenya misiroodha," jedhe booye.
P4	Namootni fudura funaananis ulee kennaniif.
P5	Innis ulicha namoota mana ijaaranitti kenne. Isaanis ulicha cabsaani.
P6	Ammas mucaan sun, "Uliichi kan warra fudura funaanudha," jedhe booye.
P7	Warrii mana ijaaranis margaa kennanif.
P8	Mucaan sun tiksee arge. 

Sa'aan marga isaa nyaate.
P9	Mucaanis, "Margi kan warra mana ijaarudha," jedhe booye.
P10	Tikseen sunis sa'a kennefi.
P11	Mucaanis mana cidhaatti dhufe.

Warri cidhaas sa'aa isaa qalatani.
P12	Innis, "Sa'an kan tikseedha," jedhe booye.
P13	Waarri cidhaas misiroo kennanif.
