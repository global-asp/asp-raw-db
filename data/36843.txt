P1	My bestfriend and I @@My bestfriend name is Zara we attend the same school. At lunch time we normally shared our lunch with each other. My mother gave me nine apples to share equally with Zara. I gave zara three apples and ate three apples.
P2	After lunch we went to the library to borrow some reading books, upon reaching the library we observe that the book were almost finish. There was only two shelves of book left and on each shelves, there were only five books on them.
P3	I borrowed six books and zora borrowed the remaining amount.@3×3=9 & 2×5=10
