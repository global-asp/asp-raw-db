desc:	Kigana mako kwoŋ ji ma kuyere kula

page_text:

P1	Ndir acel,  simbwor omito wok i dudi ni wo nwaŋi dhano wobedi gine mere.
P2	Munyo simbwor owok onwaŋo jal moro to mako gine mere.
P3	Jo cemo kanyacel to jo pokere kir riŋo. Simbwor onyaŋ ni jalo omaro riŋo swa. Lworo ocako mako go ni jalo nyalo neko go.
P4	Simbwor oŋico ni jalo nyalo cwowo go gi toŋ pere. Jalo bende oŋico ni simbwor nyalo camo go wor ka nindo.
P5	Wor acel jalo oleko ni simbwor dhire bothe moth moth,  mito camo go.
P6	Jalo ogwaro toŋ pere,  to koko ni,  "Ale cwowin ka imito caman." Simbwor ocew to ringo woko ka waco ni,  "Aki waco ni jame le nekan ndir moro."
P7	Wok ndir no,  mere pa simbwor gi dhano to tho. Simbwor neko dhano ka oneno. Dhano bende neko simbwor ka onyalo.
