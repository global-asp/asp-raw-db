P1	This morning there was an boy named billy. He wanted to try one of his dad's oatmeal. He started to stare at the oatmeal like he wanted any. Can i have some? Said billy. Sure said dad.
P2	As he scooped a pile of oatmeal to the bowl. His dad poured water on to the oatmeal to make not that hard to eat. Then his dad stirred it. Its doesn't taste like anything said Billy. Want me to put a little sugar in it said dad. Yes pls. Ok
P3	As he poured the sugar. Dad i dont want that much sugar said billy. Ok I will slow down ok dad said. I'll stir now dad said billy. This looks gooey compared to me said billy. Same to me said billy's dad. Well go on and eat said dad. Ok dad!
P4	Billy's going for an bite. Mmmm this is great dad said billy. U should try it. No no said dad. I already ate u can eat. U sure i have alot in this bowl and i wanna share so are u sure? Said billy. Yes u dont have to share if u dont want to.
