page_text:

P1	Ko da yaushe, Damisa na qoqari ta kama Barewa.
P2	kuma Barewa  na tsere ma Damisa ko da yaushe.
P3	Wata rana, Damisa ta kira Barewa, "mu zama abokai. Abin da ki ke ci ba na ci. Babu wani abu da zai sa mu gaba."
P4	Barewa ta yarda. Don haka Damisa ta ce, "Bari mu ranste don mu zama abokai. Idan kowane xayan mu ya karya rantsuwar, yaro zai mutu."
P5	Su ka  rantsai@  don zama abokai. Da dare, Barewa tayi bacci a gindin bishiya. Damisa tayi bacci@  a kan rassan a sama.
P6	Ba da daxewa Barewa ta yi qiba. Damisa ta sirance.
P7	Damisa ta yi qwaxayin cin Barewa. Ta fada ma kanta, “ban damu da rantsuwar ba. Ba ni da yara!”
P8	Damisa ta yi tselle zuwa kasa  don kama Barewa. Amma ta maqale tsakanin@  rassan biyu.
P9	Barewa ta gigice. Ta yi tsalle ta fashe da kuka, “Beh! Beh!”
P10	Damisa ta yi roqo, “Aboki na, taimake@  ni. Mun yarda cewa duk@  wanda ya karya rantsuwar mu ta zama abokai, zai rasa yaro.”
P11	Barewa ta amsa, “ ya kamata mahaifin ki ce@  ta yi rantsuwar. Yanzu ta zama na ki!”
