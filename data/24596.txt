desc:	My father is my best friend at home

page_text:

P1	He takes me and my brother to school every morning.
P2	My father helps me with my home work when I come back from school.
P3	He settles conflicts between me and my siblings at home.
P4	He always participates during the community meetings.
P5	I love to go fishing with my father and brother as I watch others swim.
P6	Me and my brother feed the hens when our father goes to graze.
