desc:	Different things that children are familiar with at home and in school.

page_text:

P1	Lena iManyatta Likhaya lami Likhaya lami liseveni lase Turkana.
P2	Leli likamela. Eveni laseTurkana bantfu bagcina tilwane letahlukahlukene. Kepha likamela libaluleke kakhulu.
P3	Laba bothishela bami. Ngiyatsandza kuya eskolweni. Sikolo sami sibitwa ngekutsi ILolupe Primary School. Ngiyamtsandza thishela wami lofundzisa sifundvo sebuciko.
P4	Lona ngumkhumbi. Imikhumbi mikhulu. Isetjentiswa kutfutsa bantfu netimphahla emantini. Uma ngiya eMombasa,  Ngiyayibona imikhumbi.
P5	Lona ngumbhedze. Ngiyawutsandza umbhedze lengawakhelwa ngubabe wami. Siwusebentisa nemnaketfu lomncane. Lombhedze untofontofo.
P6	Leli lichudze. Ekhaya sinemachudze lamabili. Siphindze sibe netinkhukhu letinyenti. Tinkhukhu titalela emacandza lamaningi.
P7	Leli ligundvwane. Emagundvwane atilwane letiyingoti. Adla kudla. Aphindze futsi adla timphahla. Angabanga netifo.
P8	Lena inja. Tinja tilwane letifuywa emakhaya. Inja yakitsi ibitwa ngekutsi ngu Daa. Ikhonkhotsa kakhulu.
P9	Lesi sambulelo. Batali bami banesambulela lesikhulu. Sinemibala lebovu,  loluhlata sasibhakabhaka Kanye naloluhlata.
P10	Ngisegumbini lekufundza lesibili,  futsi ngati tintfo letinyenti. Ngisafuna kwati lokunyenti. Lengingakwati ngikubuta batali bami nobe thishela wami.
