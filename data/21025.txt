title:	Ahasuje  Ahatasiima

desc:	Wanduyu atusa wahasuje mu hizibu. Aye muhifo hy’ohwoheresa ehisubise eri wanduyu

page_text:

P1	Wahasuje omuyala gaali aŋiima.
P2	Gafulumusa embeba mu gubaale.
P3	Ni gaali n’ayaba, ogubaale gwamugwa hu hyaba.
P4	Wanduyu gamuŋuliiye ni g’eteera ng'amudulumirira.
P5	Wanduyu gehirisa ohumuyeeda gahamufunamo ehy'egulo.
P6	Wanduyu gaŋindiiha ogubaale ohuŋwa hu hyaba
 hya Wahasuje.
P7	Wahasuje ga tisa tisa ohumulya.
P8	Basuna omusaaja omuhulu ohu bagaya.
P9	Wahasuje gehanira ohulya omusaaja yeesi.
P10	Omusaaja galoma ati, "Mundehe mboneho ogubaale ko saleŋo."
P11	Wahasuje ni Wanduyu bamuŋira hu gubaale.
P12	Wahasuje ganyonyoye ng’olu gubaale gwamugwa hu
hyaba.
P13	Omusaaja galoma Wanduyu ohumulaga ng’olugasindiiha ogubaale.
P14	Wanduyu ga gobosa ogubaale hu hyaba hya wahasuje.
P15	Omusaaja galomire ati, "Paaŋo. Hwetiinire engo."
P16	Wahusuje bamupapasa.
