title:	Engaito Epyaha
author:	Gershom Hirome
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Lunyole

desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Siibo atiina mu hibuga ni mboojo.
P2	Mboojo ye ali huheja hu ngoye ejiri mwiduuka. Siibo ye adembire.
P3	Siibo abwene ehiitu ehi genda.
P4	Engaito bajitunda layisi.
P5	Geegesyamo mu joosi.
P6	Mboojo amugulirayo engaito ejibasambiramo omupiira.
P7	Lwahiina baŋiita baatyo ohwola engo?
P8	Ehyekabi goolire huhisaŋe hy'omupiira mu bwire obwene. Gambaaye engaito jije epyaha.
