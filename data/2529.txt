title:	Muvuko Unenaa
author:	Stephen Musyoka
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Kikamba
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Two little girls went out to collect firewood and met with a giant who pretended to need help with firewood. The giant was about to lie and take one of the little girls when the little girl identified herself.

page_text:

P1	Vau tene,  vai iimu ika. Muthenya umwe yiitembea vakuvi na iia niyonie twilitu tutatu tunini. Yatukulya "nuu kati wenyu wendetwe ni asyai onthe."
P2	Kelitu kamwe kasya "mwaitu ambendete muno" Kangi kasya "Tata ambendete muno" Kakatatu kasya,  "nendetwe ni asyai onthe."
P3	Iimu yeya kelitu kala kendetwe ni asyai onthe kayaikye ngu mwongo. Kwoou kelitu katiia iimu.
P4	Maendanga vanini,  kelitu kakulya iimu "ngu syaku syiva" Iimu yasungia,  "vakuvi na mii iyaa."
P5	Mavika mitini,  kelitu kakulya ingi " ngu syaku syiva?" Iimu yasungia kana mangie uvika.
P6	Maenda na makomana na mundu ula wakulilye iimu " kana kaku ketewa ata" Iimu yasya "Ketawa muvuko unenaa."
P7	Kelitu katheka muno na kaina  Tinye muvuko unenaa Nitawa Kaamungei Uui mwaitu numbendete Uui – tata numbendete Ui,  kikuu kyakwa Ninywiaa iia.
P8	Ila mundu wewei kelitu undu kaina,  akataanany'a na akatungia asyai mako. Ukewa wathelela vau!
