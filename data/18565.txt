title:	Kokwani Mone Ipiliselize Liba
author:	Limbali Muyendekwa
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	SiLozi
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18565.png
desc:	Mubu uomile. Kakokwani kashwile linyolwa ahulu. Kaya kwakunwa mezi kwa nuka mi bakakukulusa kwa mezi. Liba lapilisa kakokwani. Kucwani kuitumela kwa kaKokwani ku Liba?

page_text:

P1	Lizazi neli cisize haulu nako ya silimo mwa mushitu ohomile.
P2	Kakokwani nekasina mezi kwa mazazi amangáta.
P3	"Nibata feela lilotoli la mezi,  ni alikawa kwa litali," kalilauka kakokwani.
P4	Nipuka nei omile.
P5	"Kuli nitokwe mezi nikatimela," kalila kakokwani. "Niswanela kuya kwa nuka yeneni utwile."
P6	"Nuka itezi nikububa kamata. Ikakububisa," alaelwa ki kamale.
P7	Kono kakokwani nekana ni linyolwa ahulu. "Nikatimela kuli niseke kunwa kwa mezi." Cwale kaya kwa kubata nuka.
P8	Kayowana mwa bucwani bobutibani nimwa machechani aomile.
P9	Kazamaya mane kafitela fa kuhutwa mezi ayuwana ni kububa. Neili nuka! Nakautwa mandinda.
P10	Kakokwani kanwa mezi abata kamufumbo omutelele.
P11	Nakatabile ahulu nikusabona kuli nekutaha lindinda.
P12	Kakokwani kalika kulamatela fa bucwani bone bucimbauka kwatuko nikona. Kono kabubiswa kimezi.
P13	"Amunituse! Amunituse!" abiza.
P14	"Ankufa,  pahama," labulela liba leli sweu,  inge lishimbile katai mwa mulomo.
P15	"Nikaina onafa kufitela niitumela ku liba," kaikupulela kakokwani. "Nika libelela kufitela alikafita liba kwakunwa mezi."
P16	Lizazi lelingwi inge kalibelize,  kwataha batangana babeli kwa nuka. Nebalwezi malegeni.
P17	"Kuna ni liba leli taanga kutonwa mezi kwanu," abulela mutangana alimung'wi. "Lukakushuka lona liba busihu."
P18	"Anikoni kulumeleza batangana ba kubulaha liba," kanaana kakokwani. "Kono nikakainyani,  cwale nika eza ng'i?"
P19	Cwano onafo liba leli sweu lafufa kuzwa mwa kota kutonwa mezi.
P20	Kakokwani nekana nimuupulo.
P21	Katulela fa lihutu la mutangana. Kakokwani kaluma mutangana fa lihutu kakumu utwisa butuku kamaata akomokisa akona kaufeela.
P22	Mutangana atula. "Mawe!" apunda mutangana.
P23	Liba lasaba kakutilimuka niku fufela kwaule kuipilisa.
P24	Kicona cwalo mwane ka itumelezi kwa liba le lisweu kwaku kapilisa.
