page_text:

P1	My name is Clare Mukami.
P2	I am nine years old.
P3	I am in Grade Four.
P4	My school is called Stellamaris.
P5	This is my friend Juma.
P6	Juma and I like eating apples.
P7	This is my friend Zamu.
P8	Zamu likes watching the rainbow with her older sister.
P9	This is my friend Aisha.@@Aisha has a problem with her eyesight.
P10	One day, Aisha was in a flower garden.
P11	She heard a thunderstorm coming.
P12	Aisha started running and fell down.
P13	Aisha went home and lay down. @@Her parents took care of her.
P14	I brought Aisha a flower.@@She felt better.
