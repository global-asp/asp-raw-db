title:	Letsatsi La Likela
author:	Khothatso Ranoosi
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ka letsatsi le leng letsatsi la likela. Ha a ne a tsamaile

page_text:

P1	Maobane hoseng,  'M'e oa rona Letsatsi,  a be a chakela ausi oa hae,  Khoeli.   Ausi oa hae o ne a lula lehlakoreng le leng la leholimo.   Eaba Letsatsi lere,  "Seka ferekana ketla khutla."
P2	Eare ha antse a le siko,  Lithaba tsabe li roala lituku tse ts'oeu.
Moea oa be o se o ba lekhang le lifate.
P3	Lifate tsa koata. Tsa lahla makhasi a tsona a fefoloa ke moea.
Eaba leholimo le lona lea koata. Lafetoha laba maru a mats'o.
Maru a ne a koatile ha a bona tsena. Maru a kalella ho lla.
P4	A ne a le meokho e mengata.
Lefats'e ka bophara le le metsi hohle.
P5	Eare ka tinare Letsatsi la meta ausi oa lona,  lare,  "Sala hantle." Letsatsi la khutlela hae.
P6	Leholimo lona la fetoha laba leputsoa hape. La bulela Letsatsi lemati. Lithaba tsa rola lituku tsa tsona. Tsa tena mese e metle e metalana.
P7	Moea oa khutla oalo robala.
P8	Lifate tsa ikutlolla makala tsa bososela.
P9	Maru a thaba ha a bona 'Me' oa rona letsatsi hape. Maru a tsamaea holo bapala.
P10	Limela tse ngata tsohle tsenyane tsa qala ho hlaha,  hotlo lumelisa.
P11	Lefats'e lohle ka bophare la chaba.
P12	Letsatsi lare,  "O ea bona,  ke o bolelletse hore ketla khutla!"
