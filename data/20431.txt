desc:	Kibuyu anachokipenda Natabo kinavunjika naye anatorokea msituni kwa ajili ya uchungu alio nao.

page_text:

P1	Huyu ni Natabo. Ana ndugu sita wa kiume.
P2	Wazazi wake wanampatia chungu kidogo. Anakipenda sana.
P3	Siku moja, ndugu zake wanakivunja chungu hicho.
P4	Natabo hakioni chungu chake. Analia kwa muda mrefu.
P5	Natabo anatoroka nyumbani. Anapanda mti mrefu.
P6	Wazazi wake wanamsihi, “Tafadhali, shuka chini.”
P7	Ndugu zake wanaimba, “Tafadhali, shuka chini.”
P8	Natabo anaufanyia mzaha wimbo wao.
P9	Halafu, rafike ya Natabo anaimba, “Tafadhali, shuka chini.”
P10	Rafiki yake anaimba hadi Natabo anashuka chini.
P11	Marafiki hao wanaenda nyumbani pamoja.
P12	Wanasherehekea. Natabo anapata chungu kipya!
