page_text:

P1	Where are you going my little goat?
P2	I am going to market to buy a new coat.
P3	"A coat for a goat?" asked the chicken.
P4	"Can a goat have a coat?" asked the man.
P5	Everyone will laugh at a goat with a coat!
P6	But my little goat likes her new coat. Goodbye goat!
