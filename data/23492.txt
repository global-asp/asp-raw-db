page_text:

P1	Kodi kodi! haliho olikuteeza aharwiji lwomumeisho
P2	Aleke ashemeleirwe munonga. "Nooha Oliho?" yayomba. "Maama...twine omugyenyi!"
P3	"Kale," nyina yamugalukamu. "Ogwe ni muka Hassan na omwana we. Beigulile aharwigi"
P4	Alake yayigula aharwigi na akamwenyo.
"Kulikayo nyabo, akatebe nkako. Maama naza kukuhikaho omukaanya kakye.
Muka Hassan yashemelelerwa Alake obwe alikutandika kuzana
P5	" Toola ahamaizi nyaabo, heine ekindi ekinkakuletela?" Alake yaabuza.
"Tinyine shonga Alake, webale." Muka Hassan Yagalukamu.
P6	"Eizooba lirungi muka Hassan," Maama Alake yagamba alikutaaha omu diilo. "Nsasila okukulindisa obwile bulingwa." 
"Tikineshonga, Alake yatulebelela gye."
P7	Nyina wa Alake yashemelerwa. "Yeego," yagalukamu. "Alake nomutayayisa mulungi.
