page_text:

P1	Muzeeyi gha koleo yankaja mutaghuni.
P2	mukazi ghe yena yakaja mutaghuni
P3	mutabani gha mukazi gha koleo yena yankaja mutaghuni
P4	omwaana gha songa gha mutabani gha mukazi gha 
muzeeyi gha Koleo yankaja mutaghuni.
P5	Embwa yo mwaana gha songa gha mutabani gha mukazi gha muzeeyi gha koleo yankaja mutaghuni.
