desc:	Mouse tries out various places until he finds a comfortable spot to sleep.

page_text:

P1	Jilwa fato bǝlin majin.
P2	Adǝ alama fato ngalwobe.
P3	"Rangnǝm isǝm rokko bonyiyen," wono Kuturuye. "Askǝrngǝna," wono Jilwabe.
P4	Yimdǝga bunedǝro Jilwabe kǝnashinzǝ ye toloa ye.
P5	"Rangnǝm isǝm rokko bonyiyen," wono Akuye. "Askǝrngǝna," wono Jilwabe.
P6	Yimdǝga bunedǝro Jilwabe kǝnashinzǝdǝ Kalla ngǝwu-a tamtamba-a.
P7	"Rangnǝm isǝm rokko bonyiyen," wono Bǝnyiye. "Askǝrngǝna," wono Jilwabe.
P8	Yimdǝga bunedǝro, Jilwabe kǝnashinzǝdǝ amusu ye kǝli ye.
P9	Jilwa ye na laa zǝmzǝm-a hargata-a mǝradǝzo.
P10	Jilwaye dangarma kitawube shin karǝngǝn ciwando.
P11	Bune shimadǝ, Jilwabe kǝnashinzǝdǝ zǝmzǝm ye kǝji ye laa sǝwandi. Fajara Jilwa.
