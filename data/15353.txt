desc:	Abafana ababili nezilwane zabo zasekhaya.

page_text:

P1	UKabali no Kateera bangabangani.
P2	UKabali unekati. UKateera unonogwaja.
P3	Nsukuzonke uKabali udlala nekati lakhe.
P4	UKateera upha unogwaja wakhe ukudla kabili ngosuku.
P5	Unogwaja ka Kateera wazala abantwana abayisithupha. Ababili babemnyama. Ababili babensundu. Kanti ababili babemhlophe.
P6	Ikati lika Kabali lephuka abathinyana abathathu. Owodwa wabathinyana wawungowesilisa. Ababili babo kwakungabesifazane.
P7	Abane babantwana bakanogwaja wabadayisa uKateera. Imali wayisebenzisela ukuthenga izincwadi kanye namapeni esikole.
P8	UKabali akabadayisanga abathinyana bakhe. Abantu bendawo babengawafuni amakati.
