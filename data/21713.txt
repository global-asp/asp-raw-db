page_text:

P1	Long time ago, Tortoise and Rabbit ran a race.
P2	It was a long race.

Tortoise won, slow and steady.
P3	Tortoise and Rabbit remained friends.
P4	King Lion decided to send them to a neighbouring king.
P5	Tortoise and Rabbit set out together.
P6	King Lion watched them go.
P7	The road they used was full of stones and thorns.
P8	Rabbit carried Tortoise over thorns.
P9	When they reached the river, Tortoise carried Rabbit across.
P10	Tortoise swam very fast. 

Rabbit held on his back.
P11	Tortoise and Rabbit met with the neighbouring king.
P12	On the way home, they were even better friends!
