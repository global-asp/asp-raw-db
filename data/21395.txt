desc:	Who will solve the dispute between Monkey and Hyena?

page_text:

P1	Monkey and Hyena had an argument. 

They went to a judge.
P2	The judge thought, “Monkey will eat my maize.
Hyena my cattle!"
P3	The judge told Monkey and Hyena to ask the
village elders.
P4	The elders also feared to pass judgement on
Monkey or Hyena.
P5	The elders told Monkey and Hyena, “The case is
very difficult.”
P6	They told Monkey and Hyena to ask a poor woman.
P7	The poor woman agreed to judge their case.
P8	She said to Hyena, “You are respected, big and a hero.”
P9	Hyena said, “You are right. I will end this disagreement with Monkey."
P10	The woman said, “You are smart.” 

Monkey decided to stop quarrelling.
P11	She said, “You both agreed to stop quarrelling.
Forgive each other.”
P12	The poor woman solved the problem. 

People were surprised.
