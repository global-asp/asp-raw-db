page_text:

P1	Hakaba hariho omwaana Elijah otarikuhurira ebibabaire bamugambira.
P2	Ezoobarimwe akaba ayaine nyina nibagyenda bashsnga emiyembe eyezire atyo yatemba omukiti kyemiyembe yatandika yacwa
P3	Nyina kuyarebire yahika, mingi yamugira bagyende, kwonkawe yayanga
P4	Mama we yamutsinga rero kuyaherize yashitama yarya emiyembe ekicuncu kaija kyamushangaho kyamurya.
