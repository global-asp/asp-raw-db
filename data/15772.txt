page_text:

P1	  Wune ngupeta ni yakulembela yangu ya kupetela.
P2	  Wune ngupeta dipwete ni cakulembela ca ntundu wecejewu.
P3	  Wune ngupeta dilalanje ni cakulembela ca ntundu wadilalanje.
P4	  Wune ngupeta dyuva ni cakulembela ca ntundu wawunjano.
P5	  Wune ngupeta dyola ni cakulembela ca ntundu wawusamba.
P6	  Wune ngupeta kumawunde ni cakulembela ca ntundu wabuluwu.
P7	  Wune ngupeta maluva ni cakulembela ca ntundu wecejewu.
P8	  Wune ngupeta come cimo ni cakulembela ca ntundu wawupidiwu.
P9	  Wune ngupeta mbendela ja Mosambiki ni mitundu jejinji.
P10	.
