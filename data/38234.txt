P1	Now going to the story. We have to help the poor people . Now listen to the story about helping of poor people and one school girl.
P2	Once upon a time. The man going on the way suddenly fall down. There were no one are helping the man. @One of a girl going to school. She saw and take the water bottle and some water droplets throw to the man face.
P3	The men slowly opened the eyes.  The girl  asked the man. What happened uncle?,  .I have not enough money that's why I am not eating food . Wait uncle I will bring the some fruits.
P4	Shop uncle take this money me and give me e enough fruits the shopkeeper gives the enough fruits . the girl take the fruits and the girl  was running. The girl give the fruits for poor man.
P5	The girl leave the poor man and went to the school. The man was started walking. The poor man reached his house . Next morning the man going to a market. The man saw  12 inches Golden chain.
P6	The poor man went to give complaint this chain  who fall the  down  in market said to police. The man think I get the the name but there were no respect of the poor man the man was goingdull to her house.
P7	The man was sleeping after few hours at midnight the God came and asked the poor man hello Ram I am the God do you have any wish asking God for ram.
P8	The poor man told I am the bachelor .I have no family. yesterday someone forgot the gold chain set the old story for god.
P9	The godsaid I give mud you write anything that which will be e front of you .And the God gived  blessing and gone.
P10	After few months the man became rich the Man going on the way again. the men think  the same girl you are the helper of me. you have any wish ask me. the girl said.  I have no wish and  went again to school
