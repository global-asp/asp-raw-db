P1	E is for Elephant
P2	Once upon a time, there was a clever little elephant named Elly.@Elly the elephant woke up one morning and was very hungry, she called "mother! mother! I am hungry".
P3	Elly could not hear from her mother as her mother was busy working in the field.@@Elly then decided to go out to the bush to go and look for food.@She came across a tree with beautifull fruits and started to munch away.
P4	Elly's tummy was full and she was a happy little elephant.
P5	E is for End@E is for Ear@E is for Eat@E is for Elly@E is for Elephant@@@@@The End
