title:	A re ye phakeng

desc:	Re ithuta ka ga maitsholo a a siameng le go tlhokomela phaka tikologo ya rona. Kgang e

page_text:

P1	Ke moso wa Lamatlhatso.@@Bana ba kopane kwa phakeng. @@Mongwe le mongwe o a tshameka.
P2	Jaanong, Tshegofatso a goga Neo mo seikgadietsong! O lekgeriso! A o a bona?@@Ga go ope yo o batlang go tshameka le Tshegofatso gape.
P3	Mo karolong e nngwe ya phaka go tshamekiwa kgwele ya dinao. @@Setlhopha sa mmala wa botala jwa legodimo se nositse nno.@@Ba itumetse.
P4	Batshameki ba setlhopha se se pinki ba itumetse. Ba simolola go lwantsha ba setlhopha sa mmala wa botala jwa legodimo.@@A go siame gore o simolola go lwa ka gonne o a latlhegelwa?
P5	Mongwe le mongwe o itumelela phaka. Bangwe ba a tsamaya, bangwe ba a buisa. O bona ditirwana dife gape?@@Ke boitumelo jo bokana fa batho ba itapolositse.
P6	Lebelela setshwantsho ka kelotlhoko.@@A o bona gore go diragalang fa batho ba latlhela matlakala a bona fa fatshe?@@Go kgotlhela phaka.
P7	Fa re tshameka mmogo, go botlhokwa gore re tlotlane. @@Bangwe ba tlola kgati, bangwe ba tshameka ka dirolasekeiti. Ke eng se sengwe se o se bonang?
P8	Rotlhe re tshwanetse go tlhokomela phaka, mme re e tshole e le phepa.@@Ke pahaka ya rona.@@Bona fela gore phaka ya rona e lebega bontle jang!
