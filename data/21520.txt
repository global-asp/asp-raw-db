desc:	Buyani

page_text:

P1	Ligama lami nginguSimo.
P2	Nginebangani labane.
P3	Emagama abo boZizo, Lele, Sisa na-Ayanda.
P4	Umngani wami longuZizo utsandza kudlala ibhola.
P5	Umngami wami Lele utsandza kuhlamba.
P6	Umngani wami longuSisa utsandza kudlala mabhacelana.
P7	Umngani wami Ayanda utsandza kufundza.
P8	Mine-ke? Ngitsandza kwenta tonkhe tintfo lebatsandza kutenta.
P9	Ngidlala ibhola naZizo.
P10	Ngiyabhukusha naLele.
P11	Ngidlala mabhacelana naSisa.
P12	Buyani, bangani, nine-ke ngabe nitsandzani?
P13	Buyani, nidlale ibhola natsi.
P14	Buyani, nibhukushe natsi.
P15	Buyani, nidlale mabhacelana natsi.
P16	Buyani, nifundze natsi!
