page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,there lived Akila and Baliga. Akila is baligas brother who is 9 years old. And Bagila is Akila's sister who is 5 years old. One day they went to buy candy without telling there parents.
P2	They were having trouble crossing the road. But her sister started crying because she wanted candy. So her brother had no option but to cross.
P3	While crossing they were hit by a green car witch was driving very fast. People nearby called the ambulance and the police. First the police arrived. By then there were many injured people who needed to be rescued by police
P4	The ambulace came blazing fast and now we can here the sirens and the blue and red blinking lights just a mile away. Thdy rescued us but we were injured so badly.
P5	So kids the morel of the story is Never go somewhere without telling your mom or dad and always be patient and be carefull while crossing roads
