desc:	Kino kigema ku mbeere ya bwire. Kale mumpilize.

page_text:

P1	Ebireri kamanhiso ka maadhi agatoonha.
P2	Leero lwokya inho. lwaki?
P3	Ebireri bisobola okubwika endhuba. Nnhendha ebireri inho.
P4	Aye gali kutoonha kuluya?"omusomesa yabuuza namunene."
P5	Kwa mpewo?
P6	Nkobera ekisinga wo ku embeera.
