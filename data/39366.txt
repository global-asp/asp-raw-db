title:	Muntu uvu ukeba kusomba nkayende
author:	Gedeon  Kabuya
artist:	Little Zebra Books and Trevor Kellog
lang:	Tshiluba
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/39366.png
desc:	Eu muyuki wa muntu uvua kayi musue kusomba munkatshi mua bantu. Yeye ne mukajende bakaya kusombela mu nzubu kampanda pa nkayabu. Biobi kubakebela ntatu ya bunyi.

page_text:

P1	Musangu mukuabu muntu kampanda kavua musue kusomba pamue ne bena mutomba benda to. @@Kavua nansha musue bua mukajende aya kumona bena mutomba babu to.
P2	Dituku dikuabu, wakaya kasa nzubu mu tshisuku kule menemene ne bena mutumba bende.
P3	Uvua umbuka matuku onso mu dinda ne walukila anu dilolo.
P4	Dilolo kampanda, muntu au wakanenga bua kualukila kuenda. @@Nyama wa tshimungu wakamona bionso bivua muntu au wenza.
P5	Tshimungu tshiakaya kukokola ku tshibi tshia nzubu.
P6	Mukajende kunzululaye tshibi wela meji ne uvua bayende.@@Tshimungu tshiamukuata, tshiotshi kumudia.
P7	Pakalukila muntu au ku mbelu, wakamona ne tshimungu tshivua tshidia mukajende. @@Kavua ne tshikuabu tshia kuenza to.
P8	Ne bionso ebi bualu kavua musue kusombe ne bena mutumba bende to.
