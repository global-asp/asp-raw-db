desc:	Olugero mu ndimi ebiri olusungu n’oluswayiri niruɳamba hubiitu byosi byosi ebi mwaana muhaana anyumirwa ohuhola.

page_text:

P1	Nyumirwa ohutuuma omugoye. I enjoy skipping rope.
P2	Nyumirwa ohutuuma omugoye seeka. I enjoy skipping rope alone.
P3	Nyumirwa ohugutuuma ni meeri yange. I enjoy skipping rope with my friend.
P4	Nyumirwa ohusoma engero eji nyoonya. I enjoy reading interesting stories. Nyumirwa ohusoma engero ejinyonya.
P5	Nyumirwa ohugenda mu hisaaŋe n'abameeri bange. I enjoy walking in the field with my friends.
P6	Nyumirwa ohwohesa maama ebyo'hunyiyiramo. I enjoy washing dishes to help at home.
P7	Nyumirwa ohuŋandiiha enjuguta embala eji paanga nasoma muwangu. I enjoy writing big letters that I can read easily. Nyumirwa ohuŋandiiha enjuguta embala eji paanga nasoma muwangu.
P8	Nyumirwa ohulya ebibala ebyengeri n'engeri. I enjoy eating different fruit.
P9	Nyumirwa ohugona huhitaliri ehibbala. I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.
P10	Nyumirwa ohuŋulirisa maama naagana engero eji nyoonya. I enjoy listening to mother tell stories.
P11	Ese ohuhola ebiitu bino byoosi hunyoonya. I enjoy doing all these things!
