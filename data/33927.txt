page_text:

P1	There lived a very big Elephant in the Meru forest.@People loved to stop along the fence of the forest whenever the elephant was around.
P2	The giant Elephant was never scared of any animal in the whole forest.@All the animals ran way whenever they heard a roar of the lion.@But the giant Elephant was never afraid.
P3	The lion wondered why the elephant did not fear the king of the forest.@The lion decided to ask the other animals what they knew about the giant Elephant.
P4	The lion was so shocked when the animals told about the strength of the giant elephant.@"Giant Elephant is a great fighter" said all the animals.
P5	All the other animals could play together but not with the strong elephant.@They feared him because he was strong.
P6	Zebras were very afraid of his trumpet sound.@Whenever they heard it, they ran away into the forests.
P7	The lion decided to prove himself as a king of the jungle.@He started to think on how to be friend him.
P8	One morning, the Lion followed giant Elephant to the river.@As they were taking water, he talked to him and they became friends.
P9	King Lion was very happy to have a a giant as a friend.
