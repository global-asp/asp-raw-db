title:	Otirishe n' Intare y' ingore
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	Kinyarwanda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Otirishe yizeraga intare yingore ariko itazi imigambi ifitiye abana bayo.

page_text:

P1	Otirishe n' intare y'ingore byari inshuti magara. Byombi byari bifite abana batoya.
P2	Otirishe yagaburiraga imishwi yayo neza.
P3	Abana b'Intare bo bari bafite uturyo dukeya.
P4	Intare yitegereje iyo mishwi iratekereza iti: "Iyi mishwi ni myiza, icyampa ikaba iyange."
P5	Igihe Otirishe yari yagiye, Intare yatwaye imishwi yayo maze iyijyana iwayo.
P6	Otirishe yagarutse mu rugo ihasanga ibibwana by'intare gusa. Yararize, irasakuza ivuga iti: "Abana bange bari he?"
P7	Otirishe yirukiye mu nzu ya Ntare maze ihasanga imishwi yayo iri kumwe n'Intare.
P8	Intare yanze ko Otirishe itwara imishwi yayo. Iravuga iti: "Ubu yabaye iyange, nawe utware ibyana byange."
P9	Otirishe yarababaye ndetse iriheba cyane. Iribaza iti: "Ubu ndakora iki kugira ngo nsubirane abana bange?"
P10	Nuko irongera iratekereza iti: "Ubu mbonye icyo ndi bukore. Ngiye gutumiza inama ihuza inyamaswa zose."
P11	Inyamaswa zose zateraniye hamwe.
P12	Inzovu yasabye Otirishe n' Intare kwisobanura.
P13	Inyinshi mu nyamaswa zatinye kuvuga nabi no gucyaha Intare y'ingore.
P14	Kera kabaye, Inkima yarahagurutse iravuga iti: "Abana bafite amababa ni aba Otirishe. Abafite imirizo ni ab'Intare."
P15	Inkima yahise irengera mu mwobo. Inyamaswa zose zikoma amashyi.
P16	Intare yarababaye cyane. Nuko itaha imuhira n'ibyana byayo.
