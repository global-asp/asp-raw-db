page_text:

P1	Kade esikhathini esadlulako. Bekunabesana ababili abaloba iinkomo zabo. Bazuma iinkomo kwabe kwarhwaqela.
P2	Nasele kubanzima khulu,  babona umkhanyo ngendlini. Bavumelana bona bakhambe bayokubawa indawo yokulala.
P3	Bafika endlini bakokoda. Umnyango wavuleka bangena ngaphakathi. Abesanaba azange babone muntu. Kodwana bezwa izwi libalotjhisa.
P4	Izwi lathi,  "Ngiyintwala vulani ipoto nithathe ukudla. Vulani umjeke begodu nisele amarhewu."
P5	Intwala yambatha isikhumba sayo yaphuma.
P6	Abesana badla,  basela,  begodu bebathokoza. Bakhamba.
P7	Nabasendleleni bakhamba,  bafikelwa mcabango omumbi. Bathi, "Ngeze kwenzeka ukuthi sithole ukudla entwaleni." Bagqhina baphetha ngokuvumelana bona babuyele emuva bayoku putjuza intwala.
P8	Babuyele endlini baputjuza intwala. Ngokuphazima kwelihlo intwala beyingekho,  nendlu yanyamalala! Bazithola sebangaphandle ebalena lakuvuleke khona.
