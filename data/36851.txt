desc:	This king is so very bad that nobody can live in his kingdom.

page_text:

P1	Once apon a time a kingdom named gaffer was a new one.One day the king wanted to burn half of  the land for a big palace.
P2	The people shouted you can build a home fore your self but not us.The people went away and the kingdom was infamous and called the kingdom of dispose.
P3	Many years later a boy named Dave thoute a person will go to the king and demand the  throne.
P4	In the third day on the 9th month of the year Dave's baby brother was born.
P5	His name was Mosley and when he grew up he became a hunter.
P6	A little boy said go and hunt the king and he agreed.
P7	He went to the palace and threw a arrow in his heart and became a good king the end.
