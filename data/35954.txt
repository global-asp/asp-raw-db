page_text:

P1	Zoe is a very happy and smart girl that lives in Abuja. She loves to play with her toys and her younger brother .
P2	Zoe was very excited when her 6th birthday was approaching. She told all her friends and family and prepared for her birthday.
P3	On her 6th birthday. She got a beautiful cake with her picture on it, with a caption "Zoe is a Star".
P4	Zoe was very happy and she celebrated her birthday with her friends in her school, and everyone was happy. It was the most wonderful day for Zoe.
