desc:	Kasi mulikupulikizgapo ivyo thupi linu likumuphalirani?

page_text:

P1	Mwahuno nilutenge yayi kusukulu. @@Ni dazi lakupumula!
P2	Mwahuno nilaŵilirenge yayi sinema.@@Kulije magesi.
P3	Sono nicitinge vici?@@Mwahuno nipulikizgenge thupi lane!
P4	Cakwamba, nikhale dankha cete.@@Kuti nipulikizge thupi lane.
P5	Enya, sono nkhupulika mathutiro ghane.@@Nkhuthutira mkati na kuwalo, mkati na kuwalo.
P6	Ndipo ningapanga kuti mathutiro ghane ghawe ghaciwawa. Ssssssssssss!@@Na pasipasi. Mmmmmmmm.
P7	Sono nkhupulika kutchaya kwa mtima wane!@@Duu, duu, duu.
P8	Kasi ningawupanga mtima wane kuti utchayenge mwaluwiro panyakhe mwakukwezga?@@Enya, pakudukira mcanya makhumi ghawiri.
P9	Sono mtima wane ukutchaya mwaluwiro.
P10	Pala naŵika njowe zane pa woko lane, nkhupulika umo ndopa zane zikucimbilira.
P11	Nkhupulika kuseka kwane,@@Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Nkhupulika kulira kwane.@@Boohoooo hooo!
P13	Nkhupulika kukuŵa kwa mapi kwane.@@Khwa, khwa, khwa.
P14	Nkhupulika kulira kwa mnthumbo mwane!@@Guguluu, guguluu, guguluu.
P15	Nthumbo yane yikuyowoya kuti, "Niryeseni!"
P16	Mphuno yane yikununkhizga cikondamoyo ico cikuphikika mukhicini la ŵamama.@@Ndipo sono nkhukhumba nipulike cibwanyu cane cikusumba cikondamoyo!
