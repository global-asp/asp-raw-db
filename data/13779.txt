title:	Kubayini Uyayeri Alungile?
author:	Ntuli and MJ
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	isiNdebele
level:	First sentences

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/13779.png
desc:	Kulendatjana sitjelwa  ngomtwana  olunge ngazo zoke iindlela. Umfundi  ugqugquzeleke ngokuthi aqabange ngokuthi ngimiphi imikgwa  nezenzo ezenza umuntu olungileko. uYayeri ulungile?

page_text:

P1	UYayeri olungileko uvuka ekuseni woke malanga. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P2	Ulunge khulu emalangeni adlulileko. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini u Yayeri alungile?"
P3	Undlula umbhede wakhe kuhle kwamambala. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P4	Ulotjhisa ababelethi bakhe kanye nogogo nobamkhulu. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P5	Uhlamba ubuso bakhe ngesibha esihlaza kwesibhakabhaka. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P6	Uhlamba amazinyo wakhe nge tanabhosolo. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P7	Uhlamba ngamanzi amakhaza. Siyazibuza kobana, "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P8	Wembatha izambatho zakhe zesikolo zombala osalamule. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P9	Wembatha amanyathelo wakhe anzima. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P10	Urhabela esikolweni sakhe e-Mbala. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P11	Wenza iimfundo ze-Sayensi,  zokuPhilisana naBantu kanye neeMbalo. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
P12	Uyavuma,  uyagida,  begodu udlala nebholo yeenyawo. Siyazibuza kobana,  "Kubayini uYayeri alungile?"
