desc:	Eizoba rimwe wakame ekaba neroonda ebyokurya yaabugana warujojo eyabeire.

page_text:

P1	Eizoba rime, wake ekaba neroonda ebyokurya. Yaabugana warujojo, eyabeire eyine enyungu yobwokyi. Wakame akabuuza warujojo ati, "Bambe, nyamba onkyirize. Tinyine amaani."
P2	Warujojo ekaikiriza, yaakiriza wakame. Wakame yada aha mugongo gwa warujojo, yaahamya. Yatandiika kurya obwokyi bwa warujojo ahonaaho.
P3	Obwookyi bwaatandika kutonya aha mugongo gwenjojo. Warujojo kuyaaburize ekiriikumutonyera, wakame yaagira ngu nikiriga omukironda kye.
P4	Baahika aha mugyeera, baashanga enmbogo mpango nenywa amaizi.
P5	Wakame agaba narendra kumanya owaabeire naakyiza ondeijo amaani, Eembogo nari warujojo.
P6	Bakatandika kuhakana. Warujojo yaagyira ngu naakizaho embogo amaani, nembogo naayo yaagyira ngu neekyiza ho warujojo amaani.
P7	Wakame akagyira ekiteekateeko kyokwenda kumanya orikuchyiza ondeijo amaani. Yabagyira ngu bamutegyer'eze aho.
P8	Wakame yagarika nomuguha mureingwa. Yarenganisa omuguha warujojo yaagyikoma. Yayebembera enjojo arikugyiriza hare'hoo nomugyera.
P9	Wakame yagarika yakima ahamuheru gwa omuguha, nigwo gumwe, yarenganisa omuguha embogo.
P10	Wakame yeiruka yayeshereka omumuti. Yayetera ahaiguru "Naagira! emmwe, eibiri, eishatu, mukuu're!"
P11	Embogo na warujojo, baakur'a. Enjojo yaakur'ra Embogo.
P12	Embogo yaakur'ra warujojo. Kaabbe ogu ninga oriya, bombi, t'haine waasingyire.
P13	Ahi wakame yabeire ayesherekyire, akagumizamu narya obwokyi bwa warujojo.
P14	Kuyaherize obwokyi, yashara omoguha heihi ne'mbogo.
P15	Warujojo eketera ahansi munonga, yagwera omuti.
P16	Wakame yada owa embogo yamukarakarisya nagyira ngu nuwe yasinga amaani.
