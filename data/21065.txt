title:	Igipupe Azizi
author:	Daniel Tuyisenge
artist:	Natalie Propa
lang:	Kinyarwanda

desc:	Uyu mwana yakundaga cyane ipupe ke. Igipupe ke cyari inshuti ye magara.

page_text:

P1	Dore Azizi. Azizi ni igipupe cyange.
P2	Nkunda cyane Azizi kubera ko ahora aseka igihe cyose.
P3	Iyo maze gukanguka, nkaraba mu maso. Hanyuma nkashakisha Azizi.
P4	Iyo mbonye Azizi, musokoza imisatsi ye.
P5	Iyo maze gusamura, mpa Azizi na we  icyo kurya.
P6	Iyo ndi kurya bombo, mpa nanone Azizi.
P7	Ariko Azizi ntarya bombo ze. Nibaza impamvu!
P8	Ntembera mu busitani nkafata indabo.
P9	Mpereza rumwe Azizi. Ariko ntavuge nta mba "murakoze."
P10	Nkarabya Azizi mbere yuko mama ampereza ibase.
P11	Nambika Azizi imyambaro y'umutuku, kubera ko nunda umutuku.
P12	Iyo ngiye kuryama, ndyamisha Azizi iruhande rwange.
