desc:	A short and simple story about a wonderful teacher and what she teaches us.

page_text:

P1	This is my teacher. Her name is Teacher Mulongo. She loves me a lot.
P2	Teacher Mulongo teaches us the alphabet and the vowels. I can say the letters a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	She has taught us the colours red,  blue,  green,  yellow and orange.
P4	During nap time Teacher Mulongo sings us to sleep. She sings a lullaby to us.
P5	On Monday,  she tells us stories about great men.
P6	On Tuesday,  she tells us stories about great women.
P7	On Wednesday,  she tells us stories about special days.
P8	On Thursday,  she tells us stories about our environment.
P9	And on Friday,  Teacher Mulongo asks us to tell our own stories to the class.
P10	Our teacher is the best teacher in the world! When I grow up,  I want to be a teacher.
