desc:	Indaba imayelana umama nobaba onghlala tshalani amasimu munye yamapeas nezimbali.Ziyasishlazela I ukhula emasimini. Ingani kanye nosiza udadewabo ensimini.Futhi ekugcineni nensimu kuyeza esakeni enkulu yama peas nezimbali.

page_text:

P1	My mother planted. She planted a small garden. She planted a small garden of peas.
P2	My father planted. He planted a small garden. He planted a small garden of flowers.
P3	My mother weeded in both gardens.
P4	My father watered in both gardens.
P5	My parents and I worked in the small gardens. My sister and I harvested the flowers and peas.
P6	The small garden of peas yielded sacks of peas. The small garden of flowers yielded sacks of flowers.
P7	My mother planted. She planted a large garden. The large garden she planted was of peas.
P8	My father planted. He planted a large garden. The large garden he planted was of flowers.
