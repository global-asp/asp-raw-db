desc:	About the girls who played outside every evening.

page_text:

P1	Long time ago there were two girls who lived in  village called Tseka. Their names were Matshediso and Rethabile.
P2	These little girls liked to play outside. Every night they will go behind the house to play.
P3	There were no lights and depended on the moon. When the moon is full they become happy.
P4	When the moon is full they went outside and play there enjoying themselves until around ten o'clock.
P5	They would feel sad when the moon is no longer full.
P6	The little girls liked the light of the moon. They sing song clapping their hands. "All things bright and beautiful all creatures great and small."
P7	The following day they looked forward for They would sing until the mother called them in.
P8	The following day they looked forward to the moon to come out so that they can go and sing again.
