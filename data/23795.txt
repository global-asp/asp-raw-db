title:	Lee chaabate amu maakt
author:	Nathan Kiplangat Sikor
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Sabinyi
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/23795.png
desc:	Kujeekurtoji wertake kakotutyet nye taboon karomuun amu pushet aa yiitenyi nyepo looh. Kaas bane chee kutok.

page_text:

P1	Poondo saangyu wareek. Loo amu lo chang piko.
P2	Kungwoowo ayoonyu kule, "Elly, chee boonde kakotutyet nye chepore chekonu."
P3	Mama,saange ako anii lee kijekany baasit.
P4	Chaa buree karenget aa mama. Charuu amu araan tokol.
P5	Che buunde maakt. Chaa kas ndeget nye ku nyalil.
P6	Kuboondo tindiirit ange matundank. Ateepe ayoo kule,"Kunee chu?"
P7	Kuweek mama le,"Appoz chu." Asoom neto lee,"Yoolwo ange."
P8	Ayuutye yoowuta mama. Cha mitasooti appo.
P9	Yeechaa woonyi ayam lee, chaa yiku ngut. Achaaj ariir.
P10	Kukworo chepyoshet aake lekuumi leekok alaak.
P11	Chasooti le cholyoy leekok amuu maakt. Chariir karoomun.
P12	Kuteep chitaake lee,"Anduu Elly?" Chaa wuuny keey.
P13	Kukursoo ayoonyu kule,"Elly!" Chaa ngaraaj karoomun.
P14	Kinuum saanganyu ndegeet."Nyeengung yaat!" Kungwoy neto.
