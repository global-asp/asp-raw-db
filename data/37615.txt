title:	Bbaasi Lipati Lyabbuluu

desc:	Chimuka abanyina bayanda kuya kudolopo abbaasi lyabbulu. Ibbaasi lyabbulu tiilyaboola nkaambo lilifwide. Inoono balaunka buti kudolopo?

page_text:

P1	Mumunzi mwaakali kukkala Chimuka kwakali bbaasi lyomwe biyo. @@Eeli bbaasi lyakali lyamubala wabbuluu. Lyakali kusabila kapati.
P2	Bumwi buzuba banyina Chimuka bakati, "Cifwumo tuyakuunka kudolopo tukaakuule yunifoomu yako yakucikolo."
P3	Chimuka wakakkomana kapati. Akaambo kakuti bayakubelesya bbaasi lyabbuluu. @@Busiku oobu kunyina naakoona ŋonzi pe.
P4	Chimuka wakasamide kale ciindi banyina nibakaboola kuti bamubusye mafwumo-fwumo.
P5	Chimuka abanyina bakanyamuka akuunka kucitisyini camabbaasi. Bakalilindila bbaasi eeli lipati lyabbuluu. @@Pele mukubula coolwe, bbaasi taakwe nilyakaboola.
P6	Abambi bantu bakasika acitisyini camabbaasi. Bakatongooka akaambo kakuti bbaasi lyakamuka kusika. @@"Sena bbaasi lyatusiya na?" bakabuzya.
P7	Chimuka wakanyongana mumizeezo. "Tatutiiikonzye kuunka kudolopo. Tanditiicikonzye kuula yunifoomu yangu sunu," wakayeeya.
P8	Bamwi bantu nibakakatala kulindila, bakajokela mwabo-mwabo. Pele Chimuka wakalila akukaka kuunka kuŋanda. @@Banyina bakamuyumya-yumya kabati, "Tulalindila kwaciindi cisyoonto biyo."
P9	Mpobatayeeyeli, bakamvwa coongo. Bakakabona kasuko mujulu. @@Bbaasi lyaboola!
P10	Bbaasi lyakaboola tiilyakali lyabbuluu pe. Lyakalikusubila alimwi lyakali lisyoonto. @@Bantu bakali kulindilila bakalilanga biyo bbaasi. Kwiina nibakatanta mubbaasi.
P11	"Amunjile! Amunjile! Amunjile! Amunjile!" Wakaponzomoka kwiita bantu dulaiva.@@"Twamuka kapati sunu," wakaamba dulaiva.
P12	Chimuka abanyina mbubakasaanguna kunjila. @@Kufwambaana bantu boonse bakanjila mubbaasi eeli lisyoonto lisubila.
P13	Chimuka naakasondela awindo, wakabona bantu banji kapati acitisyini.
P14	Alimwi bantu banji bakali kulunduka kuti abalo bakkwele bbaasi pesi bakajanika bamuka. @@Bbaasi lyakazwide kale. Eelyo aboobo bbaasi eeli lyakanyamuka kutalika kuunka kudolopo.
P15	"Ino bbaasi lipati lyabbuluu lilikuli?" bakabuzya banyina Chimuka. @@"Lilifwide," wakamvwiila dulaiva. "Tujisi bubi kulibamba. Cifwumo liyakuboola," wakayungizya dulaiva.
P16	Chimuka wakanyina andaba kumusyobo wabbaasi. Wakanyina abubi kubupati bwandilyo. @@Wakalikkomene nkaambo bbaasi eeli lyakalikuya kudolopo.
