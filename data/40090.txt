page_text:

P1	Once upon a time there was a village called Amani.The village was  peaceful and everyone lived in peace.
P2	One day a boy called Kande went to graze his father's sheeps and goats.He went to graze the goats with his dog called Tom.As he was grazing the sheeps and goats a hungry leopard appeared from the bushes.Kande ran to get help.
P3	After sometimes Kande arrived at home very tired and crying. He told his father who was at home that the leopard has attacked the animals when he was grazing in the field.
P4	His father organized a group of warriors of the village and each one had warrior tools like a pangas, arrows and stones in order to attack the hungry leopard.Tom followed behind them at a distance.
P5	When they arrived in the field, they found the hungry leopard has attacked two animals; a goat and a sheep. They hide behind the bush silently and one of the warriors attacked the leopard on the kneck using an arrow and it died.
P6	Tom dragged the dead leopard from the field and they celebrated the delicious meat. Kande was so happy after his father's animals were saved from the hungry leopard.
P7	And from that day, no animal came to the village again and Kande grazed his father's animals without any fear of wild animals.
