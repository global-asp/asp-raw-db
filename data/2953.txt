desc:	This story is about the different sorts of books my family reads.

page_text:

P1	My brother is reading a school book.
P2	My mother is reading a bible.
P3	Mkhana weru asoma shitabu shukhu tekha.
P4	Khotsa asoma ligazeti.
P5	Kukhu asoma ligazeti.
P6	Mwana weru mti asoma shitabu shitsi picha.
P7	Soma shitabu hishi.
P8	Nasoma vuli shindu shosi matukhu kitsanga.
