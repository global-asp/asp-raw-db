page_text:

P1	There was a helpful girl named Emily who had helped everbody in her village
P2	She had done so by collecting recycable materials. She had loved helping.
P3	One day she had an amazing to make a toy out of the materials she collected
P4	She then made a lot of toys and made posters telling about the toys
P5	Everybody got very excited when they read the posters and wanted toys.
P6	So they had came to Emily to buy the toys. She had made lots of money.
P7	Her parents were very proud of her so from then on she had always helped.
