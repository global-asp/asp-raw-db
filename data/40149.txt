title:	Ɨ wâyn nto' wu kelɨ wi izɨyn
author:	Yoh Edelquin Nabi
artist:	Awwalu  Sakiwa
lang:	Kom
level:	Read aloud

desc:	Fɨtɨtɨ̂ fêynfɨ nɨn ghɨ kûm wâyn nto ta ghɨ læ faŋ tɨ chfɨ̀ izɨyn i ŋweyni (fɨtɨtɨ̂ fêynfɨ nɨ ghɨ ma ghɨ læ bàlɨ  a ndô mɨtɨtɨ̂ a Abuja a 2018

page_text:

P1	À n- læ meyn nà ghɨ ɨ̀tu' fɨ̀li, wâyn foyn ɨ̀lvɨ̂ nà ghɨ kelɨ wi izɨyn. Wù n- læ nà ghɨ kɨ̀ ŋweyn imò' sɨ̂ Fòyn Dunoma ta wù nà sɨ kɨŋ yɨ-àbe. Ghɨ læ bzɨ ŋweyn ibàm i bèŋsɨ̀ sî dvɨ ta nà ŋweyn nà chiytɨ ko', kelɨ wi wayn.@@A nà ghɨ ta ghɨ bzɨ ŋweyn, fòyn nâ wêyn nà sanglɨ chwòsɨ-à , ɨ kfà'tɨ̀ na wu lèy'tɨ̀ izɨyn i wayn nâ wêyn nô ta ka wù læ lèma. Tèyn, a no mɨ wâyn wi ɨ̀ kfà ta wù keli izɨyn i ŋweyni, a wù nà ghɨ wî wâyn nto'.
P2	A nà sɨ ghɨ tèyn ta wàyn nâ wêyn lèma kfèyn sɨ mala, fòyn ɨ tum ntum fòyn nà wù là'ì sɨ ghelɨ̀.@@"Iyeyni nɨn ghɨ ntum ì jùŋ sɨ woynda ngòynsɨ̀ a ntè' àfèyn! Fètɨ izɨyn i wâyn fòyn, nà ghɨ wî wâyn nto'."
P3	Woynda ngòynsɨ̀ a ntè' ɨ lêm mɨchi nâ mvì sɨ̀ nà ba'tɨ̀-à. Àŋena bò ɨtu, i seli ngèysɨ̀ ɨwu. Àŋena nywòtɨ̀ iwu nɨ̀ ɨvɨ nɨ̀ fɨ̀kàynfɨ̀, ɨ ndù sɨ tas ndɨ̀sɨ̀ fisɨ.@@Ghɨli ndù nô ɨlwè' mɨfɨ̀ nà ghɨ jèm sɨ àŋena nà atu àŋena jof. Yì, a ghɨ nà keli wi iweynsɨ, ɨ lù se' ifɨŋ i fɨ̀nya' nà ghɨ fetɨ iwo zɨ̀ a ghɨ n-gâyn sɨ̂ aŋena.
P4	Achi àteyna gvì kfèyn, dzɨsɨ jɨ̀msɨ̀ nà ndù kɨ a nto'. Wâyn fòyn nâ vzɨ̀ ŋêyn bò ŋweyn, nɨ̀ nchîsɨ nto'sɨ gvì alè' a nchîyntɨ nâ yè.@@Ngòynsɨ̀, ma sɨ faytɨ meyn laf, nà dèŋsɨ̀ jêl ndù a nto'.
P5	Ghɨ ba'tɨ no mɨ ghà, ntum fòyn ɨ fètɨ̀ na ɨlvɨ kfêyn meyn. No mɨ wayn wi ɨ̀ kfà ɨ fvɨ̀ gvî fètɨ àzɨyn a tal.@@À jof mɨ àtu a nda, a no mɨ ghà mæ kɨ̀ layn. Mɨ̀tì wù faŋ tɨ̀ keli, a wayn wi ɨ̀lvɨ̀ fvɨ gvi boŋ bè.
P6	Wâyn wi asɨ fvɨ̀ gvì ɨ bê na, "À n-ghɨ Modu, kesa Mommodu, kesa Mattaa?"@@Wâyn fòyn bê na, "Ngaŋ, à n-ghɨ wi tî a jèmtɨ̀. Wà bû keli."
P7	Wâyn wi ànkùmtɨ̀ fvɨ̀ gvì ɨ bê na, "À n-ghɨ Buka, kesa Uma, kesa Usman?"@@Wâyn fòyn bê na, "Ngaŋ, à n-ghɨ wi tî a jèmtɨ̀. Wà bû keli."
P8	Wâyn wi ɨ̀lvɨ̀ ɨ fvɨ̀ gvì na tal, ɨ bê na, "À n-ghɨ Ali, kesa Zubay, kesa Abba?"@@Wâyn fòyn bê na, "Ngaŋ, à n-ghɨ wi tî a jèmtɨ̀. Wà bû keli."@@Iwo nâ yèyni ɨ fi ndù kfêynsɨ sɨ-a mɨchi, mɨ̀tì nô wul ɨ yɨ wi. Ntum fòyn ɨ fi jàŋ na ghɨ kfeynsɨ meyn mɨchi.
P9	Wâyn wi ɨ̀lvɨ̀ læ na ghɨ ma à tùtɨ̀ kɨ̀ nà ŋweyn na wù boŋ ndù àfu. Wù se sɨ ndù na ŋweyn ɨ bê na wù ndù kɨ̀ nyà'à -nyà'à.@@Wù se sɨ ndù, ɨ yeyn fɨ̀nìni fɨ lifɨ wu du' a dzɨ kɨ ŋweyn nìnyɨŋ. Wayn wi na weynɨ̀ toŋtɨ ŋweyn ɨ bɨf na wù kelɨ nge' a?@@Nìni wêyn ɨ bèynsɨ̀ na, "Mɨ n-ghɨ afèyn, chiytɨ̀ na wùl bo atu akema. Woyn ghɨkɨ̀ yì a ghɨ chwò afèyn, le' meyn nô sɨ timi. Àŋena kelɨ wi ɨlvɨ sɨ̂ mà."
P10	Wâyn wi wêyn ɨ kfɨmtɨ nìni na vzɨ̀, ɨ bo atu a ŋweyna. Nìni ɨ bɨf sɨ ŋweyn na, "Wayn wom, wà n-ndù wo?"@@Wu bèynsɨ̀ na, "Nô wùl kya wi izɨyn i wâyn nto', à n- kya kɨ bò ŋweyn, mɨ̀tì ma à fètɨ̀ nô mi ndà izɨyn i ŋweyni a wù nà ghɨ wî ŋweyn."@@Nìni nâ vzɨ̀ ɨ bè na, "Izɨyn i wâyn nto' nɨn ghɨ Tatasko."
P11	Wâyn wi nâ wêyn ɨ̀ fu ayonɨ̀ sɨ̂ nìni nâ vzɨ̀, ɨ lù nà ndù ale' iwo nâ ghàyn. A nâ sɨ̀ ghɨ teyn ta wù gvì chem, wu chɨl gvɨ̀tɨ̀ ta wù yeyn inoyn i ghel i dvɨ kɨ teyn. Wu nà sɨ wumi- à, nà kɨŋ wi sɨ fvɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ ndù asɨ.@@Wu kasi kfà'tɨ̀ na, "A n-ghɨ kɨ ɨlvɨ wêyn imo sɨ̂ mà." Wu fvɨ ndù asɨ.@@Woyn ghɨki ghɨli nà sɨ chye' ŋweyn, mɨ̀tì wâyn nto' ɨ bè na ghɨ visi wù boŋ fvɨ̀ gvì be yi ŋweyni.
P12	Wu fvɨ̀ gvì ɨ zɨ̀tɨ̀ na, "À n-ghɨ Ibrahim, kesa Kassim, kesa Kagu?" Wâyn fòyn bê na, "Ngaŋ, à n-ghɨ wi tî a jèmtɨ̀. Wà bû keli." Wu bê na, "Mɨ n-kya mɨ̀tì fàyn gha' sɨ toŋtɨ."  @@Wâyn nto' ɨ tùtɨ̀ kɨ na wù be. Wu fi bè na, "À n-ghɨ Tatosko?" Wâyn nto' ɨ byal na wù keli meyn. @@Ghelɨ læ lù gvi ɨlwè' wu ba'sɨ-a, ɨlvɨ ɨ dyèblɨ̀-à kɨ̀sɨ yɨ achi a malà nɨ̀ àŋena. Tèyn ichi àŋena nà jôf kɨ tèyn.
