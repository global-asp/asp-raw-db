P1	Once upon a time there lived a family of zoo animals which were bossed about by a evil by the lepored King. His name was Liam.
P2	This went on for many years. It was atrocious. The family was were to sceard to stop it.
P3	One day Emily elephant and Edward the elephant had a baby boy called Ethan the elephant. He grew to be a strong really strong animal.
P4	Ethan stood up to Liam. He stompped up to him and said," I WILL NOT LET YOU BOSS US AROUND LIKE YOU ARE !!!!! OOWWW!!!" He said out of the blue. " What is it " said Emily , " Liam he shot an arrow at me" Come one let's go home "
P5	The next day everyone found out about it and were horrified. Liam felt guilty. He couldn't stand to look at himself.
P6	Two days later he went to animal prison for 6 months. As soon as he got out he was nice everyone because he realised that you will have a better life being kind than evil. The end.
