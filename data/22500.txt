title:	Wankembo mu Kyeya
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere and Uganda Christian University
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words

desc:	Tukaasobola okuba aghalala singa twikiriza okugabana. (kili ni ku luse olwokusatu).

page_text:

P1	Amaidhi ti gaidha. Kyeya nekyayeya enkungu.
P2	Wankembo ono yaaava gghaka okuja okunoonia amaadhi ne emere.
P3	Yaatambula obusozi no obuserengeto.
P4	Yaatuuka mu kifo ekyetebwa Ntoloko.
P5	Wankembo yaaba musagaalufu inho olwe ekifo kino.
P6	Yalya ebibala era yageedhuluka. Aye yali asubwa baine.
P7	Atyo Wankembo yaatoolera olugendo olughanvu okwirayo eka.
P8	Baine ba wankembo baali basangaavu okumubona.
P9	"Ekifo kino ekibala ebibala kili igha?" bwe baamubuuza.
P10	"Ndidha kubatwala yo," Wankembo bwe yakoba.
P11	Ba Wankembo baayenda inho Ntoloko. Baasalagho okusigalirayo irala.
P12	Aye ba Wankembo abomu Ntoloko baali beeraliikirivu inho.
P13	"Bu Mukembo buno obuyaka bwidha kutumalira ebibala byaife," bwe baakoba.
P14	Ba Wakembo abakaire baaja okudhodhobya ba Wankembo abayaaka.
P15	"Lwaki mulwana?" Omukulu bwe yabuuza. "Ebibala bimala buli aligho."
P16	Kyali kituufu. Era ba Wankembo baaleka amalala baaba ghalala mulalaala.
