title:	Tenngu Ɓiɓɓaangu
author:	Mohammed Also and Ruqayya Sa'id Also
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Fulfulde Adamawa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Woɓɓe derkeen ɗiɗo njaɓi walliinde tenngu

page_text:

P1	Nyalnde wonnde, woɓɓe derke'en ɗiɗi majjiraa na'i maɓɓe.@@Ɓe ɗaɓɓiti na'i maɓɓe haa jenngi.
P2	Nde nyiɓɓi, mi ngi'i leelewal nder saare wonnde.@@Ɓe ngerdootiri dow ɓe njaha ɓe tefa nokkuure waalugo.
P3	Ɓe njottii saare nden ɓe silminii. Dammugal maɓɓitii ɓe nasti.@@Sukaaɓe ɓen walaa mo ngi'i. Ammaa ɓe nani wolde hofnii-ɓe.
P4	Wolde nden wi'i, "Min woni tenngu. Maɓɓitee fayannde njamndi on taway nyaamndu. Maɓɓitee fayannde lesdi njaron kosam."
P5	Nden tenngu ɓili laral mum wurtii yaasi.
P6	Sukaaɓe ɓen nyaami, njari, ngetti.@@Nden ɓe ngurtii ɓe acci saare nden.
P7	Ɓaawo ɓe ndillii, numo wonngo mettungo warani-ɓe. Ɓe mbi'i, "Taa fa laatoo en nyaami nyaamndu gila saare tenngu."@@Ɓe narri dow ndikka ɓe co'oo ɓe ɓiɓɓa tenngu ngun.
P8	Ɓe co"ii saare ɓe ɓibbi ngu Kadi louse artii wurtake saare, nden saare nden majjiti.@@Ɓe tawi ko'e maɓɓe yaasi non.
