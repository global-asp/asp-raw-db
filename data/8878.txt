title:	Clever Cat
author:	Ursula Nafula
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	English

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Mister Kiio has a clever cat called Safi.

page_text:

P1	In my village,  lives Mister Kio. Mister Kio has a cat called Safi.
P2	When Mister Kio goes to work,  he leaves the door open.
P3	Safi sits in the doorway to watch over the house.
P4	When the neighbour's dog comes close,  Safi scares him away.
P5	The dog runs away.
P6	When a neighbour knocks at the door,  Safi is ready to scratch.
P7	The neighbour turns and runs away.
P8	When Safi is hungry,  she closes the door.
P9	Safi eats her food.
P10	Afterwards,  Safi jumps onto the sofa and sleeps,  "Purr! Purr! Purr!"
P11	When Mister Kio returns,  Safi stretches happily and welcomes him home.
P12	Mister Kio and Safi sit down,  and each of them reads the newspaper.
