desc:	Kate akaba tali kulungi.Kusemezebwa obwongo. Omusaho nikwo agambire. Nogire kiki ekyabeire ho?

page_text:

P1	Kate aine ekintu mu mutwe gwe.
Nibeija kukihamu baata?
P2	Niki mujwashya
Niki musasa.
Niki mulemeeza
P3	Takusobora kulya.Takusobora kunywa.
Tasemireirwe nkwoku aikeire asemerirwe.
P4	Caali  omukazi waitu nobusasi bwomutwe.
Omusaho amukebile omutwe gwe.
P5	Omusaho narora ekizimba mu mutwe gwe kandi nagamba,''Halimu ekiziba mu bwongo.''
P6	''Twina kukihamu mu bwongo saaha zino.Twina kukihamu hati.'' omusaho agambire.
P7	Balete akataabo.
P8	Batekaniiza obuwembe bwo kumusemeza.
P9	Niba mutela enkinzo.
P10	Kate bamusara omutwe.
P11	Obwongo babwihamu.
P12	Ekiziba ba ki salamu.
P13	Obwongo ba bugaramu mu kahanga.
Hati basoona omutwe hamu.
P14	Kate nahurra buli kintu ki ba kukola?
Nangwa!Ta kwetegereza.Mu saaha ebiri ne neina!
P15	Hati yagaruka mu bwomezi.
P16	Saahazinu ali kurungi.Nkwo'omwana eya ka zaalwa.
P17	Itwena tusemereirwe habwa munyani weitu Kate abaineho iguulu.
