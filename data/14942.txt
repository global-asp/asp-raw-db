title:	Kuziha
author:	Mugasa Nathanael Erisa
artist:	Alice Inzikuru
lang:	Rutooro
level:	First sentences

desc:	Enjoka neyega kuziha kandi ekaba erihaihi kudikira. Ekyomugisa kanyamankogoto akaba amanyira ekyokukora.

page_text:

P1	Busumi bwaira muno hakabaho abanywani babiri,  kanyamankogoto na Njojo.
P2	Kiro kimu Kanyamankogoto akagambira Njojo,  "Leka tugende kuziha." "Kihikire,  baintu nyowe timanyire kuziha," Njoka yagarukamu. "Ninyija kukwegesa," Kanyamankogoto yakarukamu.
P3	Bagenda ha kiyanja. "Noooha arabanza kugwamu?" Kanyamankogoto yakaguza. "Niiwe nukwo Nyowe ndakusunga," Njoka yagamba.
P4	Kanyamankogoto yataaha mumaizi ge kiyaja. Njoka yarora kandi yayetegereza rutambu ntambu ha rutambu.
P5	Hanyuma yakaire,  Njoka nawe yataaha mukiyanja obu nahondera kuhabura kwa kanyamankogoto.
P6	Kanyamankogoto na Njoka bakaziha paka kumalirra kwe kiyanja nambera hatahamire.
P7	Batandika kuserera hamaize.
P8	Obubali niba kyaserere,  kanyamankogoto yatandika kuziha nagaruka enyuma. Obuyali nasubirra Kanyamankogoto,  Njoka akaba atakusoboro kwesindika kandi yatandika kudikira.
P9	Kanyamankogoto obuyayetegerizi nkoku njoka abuzireho,  kanyamankogoto yayanguha yagenda hansi yamaizi kujuna obwomezi bwa Njoka.
P10	Aho naho kanyamakogoto obuyarugire mumaizi,  akahindura njoka hansi na haiguru nukwo aihemu amaizi agakaba amizire.
P11	Kumalirra,  kanyamankogoto akakozeza omunwagwe kusikamu amaizi agandi.
