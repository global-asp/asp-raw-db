desc:	This story talks about the different kinds of houses that we live in.

page_text:

P1	Wakabi aba mu nhumba nsereke ya isubi.
P2	Kirunda aba mu nhumba ya nsoda.
P3	Enhumba enho ya muserebende.
P4	Akasolya ke nhumba eno kansoda isatu.
P5	Omusadha onho ali kwezimbira ye mwene enhumba.
P6	Enhumba eno mpyaka.
P7	Enhumba eno nkaire.
P8	Nandyenze mbe owomunhumba eno.
