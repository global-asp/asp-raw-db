page_text:

P1	There was a man who didn't want to live close to his neighbours. And he also didn't want his wife to visit the homes of her neighbours.
P2	One day he went and built a home way out in the bush,  far from the homes of his neighbours.
P3	He would go out every day in the morning and come home late at night.
P4	One night the man was very late getting home. A hyena saw what that man was doing.
P5	The hyena went and knocked on the door of the house.
P6	The woman,  thinking it was her husband,  opened the door. The hyena jumped on her and devoured her.
P7	The man,  when he returned home saw that his wife had been eaten by a hyena. There was nothing he could say.
P8	All this happened because he didn't want to live near his neighbours.
