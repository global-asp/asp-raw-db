title:	Nyám sí léʼkirì
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh and Mbinkar Emmanuel Keelen
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22295.png
desc:	Nyám símóʼ sí sikweè see ŋgày sé wìr si suùsín e faàsi vitú vév fo atàr véví ve á nyavrì aáwune. Áwune nèn taʼ fó a dzə ji nyaáŋ moo ŋgày sé wìr.

page_text:

P1	Yaàyá-aà kér lúm kiyuu wùn à ŋgvəv si.
P2	Wan wàn wìy kingàn. Yaàyá nèn shù ji wun lo, e see a kiyuu kin.
P3	Lúm kiyuu vəyi nèn lèʼ e dù e mvə̀m laʼ.
P4	Wú nèn yèn shisi a shí léʼ sa fo tàr wov lìm.
P5	Lúm kiyuu və̀y wùn à shisi shi yén jwí a yì kù léʼ sa bèy.
P6	Nyam ŋkoʼ ye yì kì sí léʼ sa, tátí áwune, wùn áwune wáa leʼ.
P7	Nyám sìy sí léʼ bey, e tò e lav yìmòʼ, aàshóŋé dzə̀ e mvəm wó.
P8	Nyám si nèn a waamé luŋ waamén ji á jùm á ashóŋ viy.
P9	Á jùm, e nèn yì viyíkìr ve áwune yenen e lav.
P10	Ashóŋé yiì lò, e wìy e bàm. Nyám si fùnèn áwune.
P11	Ashóŋé sòsìn e nyéŋ, e nèn dù duùn ŋkuuŋkùŋ.
P12	Nyám sìy si nèn bàʼtí, a dzə e lav fèy, a yu vijuŋ vév.
