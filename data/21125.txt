title:	Ekeny Loaronon!
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	Abraham Muzee
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21125.png
desc:	Ng’isapa luesukul lu aponi tobunarai esakare ebachit ang’aropiyae alokauriak kech.

page_text:

P1	Kiloseene ayong ka lopaekang lobe Kiki losukul.
P2	Apei kolong’it, kiwap sua ekile alokaku.
P3	Ang’oreka ng’ikel keng. Eleleba ng’ikang’ok loreet.
P4	Nakikienyikinia sua ng’esi, tong'oo kisuburwak sua.
P5	Kerumut, kemasakis ayong tooma imotoka.
P6	Abu Kiki kipung’ar kilany kechi.
P7	Kewakis kai nati aribokinit chuch. Katoena ng’akan.
P8	Tama ekile, "Kimuj, elona ni elosio!"
P9	Ani emuji ayong, tamasi ng’esi asikara.
P10	Napei, todulut luche erikunito Kiki.
P11	Eirasi sua ng’ikiliok ng’ulu iburate anakai anache.
P12	Tama Kiki, "Bwa atosiwakisi. Nyepedorete ng’ikauriak yok akitach kesi."
P13	Kipotu eriutu ng'akito na aya lodirisa kileereu tani nikalapatan.
P14	Sodi Kiki kipung’ar king’a alimokin ng’itung’a.
P15	Todolut ng’ikiliok ng’ulu atipei. Ang’oito kechi.
P16	Nakaneni apotu tobong’osi nabo kai ekiyan.
P17	Abu ayong eira etoil ebala, "Nyiyem, adolu nang'olenyang."
P18	Eira ayong echale ka eruye ang’atomian.
P19	Tama apolisit naberu, "Arumor ng’akiro daang. Iyei tokona yong niajokon."
P20	Anyoun akwaar ng’ina, atarata ayong ka Kiki lukichoikinit.
