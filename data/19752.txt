P1	This is Natabo. She has six brothers.
P2	Her parents give her a small pot. She loves it.
P3	One day her brothers break the pot.
P4	Natabo cannot find her pot. She cries and cries.
P5	Natabo runs away. She climbs a tall tree.
P6	Her parents find her. "Please come down," they ask.
P7	Her brothers sing,  "Please come down."
P8	Natabo makes fun of their song.
P9	Then Natabo's friend comes. "Please come down," she sings.
P10	Natabo's friend sings until she comes down from the tree.
P11	The friends walk home together.
P12	Everyone celebrates that Natabo is home. Natabo gets a new pot!
