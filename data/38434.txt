desc:	This little book is a short story about a young boy who was so passionate about football. He became a superstar after too many struggles. The story is meant to inspire young children to dream big.

page_text:

P1	In a rural area was a child with a dream of becoming a footballer. He was so passionate about football. He could travel  to other places where he could watch football. He asked for a former coach of  National team to be his couch.
P2	He invited other kids to join him to build their dreams together. He was a tallented boy. He participated in a local sporting event that was hosted by the Football Association. He was selected to play in the under 20 of the country.
P3	He later went to the capital city to pursue his footballing career with one of his friends.  They both became superstars and became much popular in the whole world. Everyone was talking about their success and how they admired them.
P4	He was so into football that everyday and everywhere he went he could take the ball with him. This is how he enjoyed his day as a kid.
P5	He became a superstar and everyone looked up to him as he became the first person to go international as a footballer.  The little dream became reality.
P6	The dream that started under the tree bacame a reality. We have our dreams and potentials, let's not give up on our dreams...
