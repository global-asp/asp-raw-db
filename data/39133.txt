desc:	Ihashe lomdyarho uSoze ebesoloko eshiywa wade waphumelela.

page_text:

P1	Kudala kwakukho ihashe elalibaleka emdyarhweni.Igama lalo yayingu Soze.@Lalimhlophe ngebala.
P2	Lalinenkweli egama layo nguZola.Wayeyinkwenkwe emdaka ngombala engenamzimba.
P3	Babehlala kwilali yasePeelton.USoze wayesoloko eshiywa emdyarhweni.
P4	Lalilolongwa yonke imihla nguZola kulungiselelwa umdyarho.
P5	Ngemini yomdyarho kwakusasa wahamba uSoze noZola baya emdyarhweni ePeelton.
P6	Kwakugcwele ngababukeli kuzobukelwa uSoze nenkweli yakhe uZola.
P7	Wathi ukuba eve ingxolo yabantu nombhexeshi wakhe wabaleka waphuma phambili.
P8	Wabuya nodumo ngokuhlwa emdyarhweni kuba ephume phambili.Babenemincili nebhongo ngoSoze.
