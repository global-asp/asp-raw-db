page_text:

P1	I live with my grandmother. @@We live in a small hut.
P2	People think we are poor. @@I think happiness is wealth.@@We are happy.
P3	My grandmother made me a beautiful jersey. @@Everyone in the village was talking about it.
P4	My teachers asked my grandmother to make jerseys for them.
P5	Soon, everyone in the village wanted a jersey.
P6	Grandmother made a jersey for everyone. @@She sold the jerseys.
P7	Now, we have more money. @@We are happier. @@No one thinks we are poor anymore.
P8	I cannot take off the jersey my grandmother made for me. @@It makes me feel so proud of her.
