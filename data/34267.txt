This story was child created

page_text:

P1	Mama Arome lived alone in a hut near the forest.
P2	Ife the fairy visited and saw that she was sad.
P3	Ife the fairy asked her why she was so sad.
P4	She said she wanted a new house and Ife the fairy granted her wish.
P5	If the fairy visited Mama Arome again in her new home.
P6	Mama Arome complained that the new house was too big.
P7	Ife the fairy was unhappy with her many complaints.
P8	And that was how Mama Arome ended up back in her old house, alone.
