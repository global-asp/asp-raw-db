desc:	A story for toddlers about a cow with a daytime identity as a soft toy

page_text:

P1	This is cow. Cow is cool!
P2	By day,  it is an ordinary cow.
P3	Flying a kite.
P4	Kicking a ball.
P5	By night,  a supercow!
P6	Saving lives.
P7	 And fighting crime.
P8	But if you close your eyes really tight,  and make a wish.
P9	Cow might come to visit you too!
P10	The end.
