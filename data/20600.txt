page_text:

P1	mpoa kale ko ni kale,abawo umukana nga bamulanga bati Emily.Emily aba eyangala nabi
P2	buli lwesi maayi wewe amutebako ku kola chitu choesi aba asula
P3	lunaku lwo gune maayi wewe aba umu lwale.amuteba kuza wo kuku  ku muletela ko malesi,niye amile awone.kubanga aba Emily eyangala nabi  asalwo kuza kuwunga mu chisiba kulugendo lwewe wo kuku
P4	sawa zaza maayi wewe atadinka kwi yeyeberera
P5	maayi wewe alanga bantu be kuchalwo kumuyendako   kuyonda emily
P6	aba eyeberere kubanga aba eliwo guwonesiche  gu ndia baana bakana chilo
P7	nga bantu bali kuyonda Emily aba achiyumilwa muchisiba.aba achikumanyeko chilima nda
P8	Aba sawa zolele zechehagolove ante guwonesiche aba gwa tandikira kuyonda bidio.nga guli kugenda gwatandika kuwuyira kakana.bantu bekuchalo aba bali chipi ni chidiba
P9	Emily abona guwonesiche guli kweza antandika  kukuba liyombo.Bantu bekuchalo bawulilira lidoboi lye kakana badima esi kali.
P10	Bantu bei kuchalo baduya Emily nga achili mu chidiba  aba atile nabi Kwama lunaku lwo emily ayinga ku wulilizizanga maayi wewe ni kumwesamo chitwa.
