page_text:

P1	Intuthwane enekhanda elikhulu yayingumlimi omuhle.@@Yayihlala emhlabathini nomdeni wayo.
P2	Babesebenza kanzima kakhulu epulazini labo.@@Umndeni wenthuthwane wawuhleli kahle.
P3	Ijuba laliwumakhelwane wentuthwane. @@Ijuba lalihlala esihlahleni nomndeni walo.
P4	Ijuba nomndeni walo babengalimi.@@Babephumula usuku lonke.
P5	Ijuba nomndeni walo babengabaculi abahle kakhulu.
P6	"Sihlephulele ukudla kwakho," ijuba licela intuthwane.@@"Cha! Hamba uyolima!" kuphendula intuthwane.
P7	Ijuba landizela phansi laqala ukucula, "Intuthwane yinkosi!"
P8	"Intuthwane yinkosi! Intuthwane yinkosi!"@@Intuthwane yalithanda leliculo.
P9	Emuva kokudla, ijuba kanye nomndeni walo bandizela phezu kwesihlahla sabo.
P10	Ngosuku olulandelayo, ijuba lagcona intuthwane.@@"Ingabe amakhosi anamakhanda amakhulu?" ehleka.
P11	"Awubongi futhi uyivila!" kukhononda intuthwane.
P12	"Ngiyaxolisa," kusho ijuba, futhi laqala ukucula. "Inthuthwane iyinkosi!"
