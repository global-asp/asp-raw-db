desc:	Maama nekirabo ekyobutonde ekisinga bune eri omwana.

page_text:

P1	Maama abaagho buli kiseera. Atutwala kwisomero buli lunaku.
P2	Atutwala mu katale okugula eiva,embooli n'ebira.
P3	Maama mulungi, agulira nze ni baganda bange engaito ni butusi.
P4	Bwendwala, maama abaagho okudabirira.
P5	Kyova nhenda inho maama ghange.
