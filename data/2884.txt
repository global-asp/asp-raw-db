desc:	Explore how we feel about different things and people.

page_text:

P1	My heart feels a lot of things.
P2	I feel happy when my granny tells us stories in the evening.
P3	I feel silly when I play with my friend.
P4	I feel bad when my dad says he does not have money.
P5	I feel loved when my mom gives me a hug.
