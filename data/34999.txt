desc:	Cilango camulumyana umpafu uyo alya fiyo fipaso cingayacici?

page_text:

P1	Siku limo, mama agulile fipaso fya kukindana kindana.
P2	"Tupokulya ndili fipaso?" twalalusishe.@@"Tupakulya namayolo," mama ayobile.
P3	Mulumbu wangu Rahim mupafu fiyo. @@Agelile fipaso fyosa. Alile fingi fiyo.
P4	"Keta fifyo Rahim abombile!" akutile mulumbu wangu mukeke. @@"Rahim otamya kangi atakwinong'onela banyake," nayobile.
P5	Mama akalele fiyo nongwa ya Rahim.
P6	Uswe nuswe twamukalalile Rahim.@@Loli Rahim atakapasha.
P7	"Mupomupa cilango Rahim?" alalusishe mulumbu wangu mukeke.
P8	"Rahim, upolamba nosi lulu," mama wake amusokile.
P9	Rahim alyandile kubina.
P10	"Mulwanda mwangu mukumbaba," apephwile Rahim.
P11	Mama amenye nongwa yiyo yamupangishe Rahim kubina.@@"Fipaso fyumulanga Rahim," mama alinong'wine.
P12	Pabumalilo, Rahim alambile kwa uswe. "Nthasokuya sona mupafu," ayobile. @@Twamusubile.
