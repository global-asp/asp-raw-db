page_text:

P1	Ayanda always watched his father cook.
P2	Today he made rice and chicken.
P3	He went to his room to play.
P4	While playing, he thought of how he wanted to cook like this father.
P5	His father called him to eat.
P6	At the dinner table he said “Papa, I want to cook like you”
P7	The next day, his father called him to the kitchen. He said “Ayanda, today you will cook with me”
P8	Ayanda was happy. They had a lot of fun.
