desc:	This is a story about how a day was spent at the zoo.

page_text:

P1	Lehono ke be ke ile lešokeng la diphoofolo.
P2	Kebone diphoofolo ka mehutahutaKe bone tau,  morena wa diphoofolo.
P3	Ke bone tlou,  phoofolo ye boima.
P4	Ke bone kwena,  phoofolo eya meno a bogale.
P5	Ke bone le thutlwa,  phoofolo ye telele.
P6	Ke bone dipitsi tše dintsi,  tša mmala o mobotse.
P7	Gape ke bone le kgomo,  phoofolo ya go fepa ditjhaba kanama le maswi.
P8	Ke be ke thabile kudu. Bjale ke nako ya go boela gae.
