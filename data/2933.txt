page_text:

P1	Namhlanye andiyi esikolweni. Yiholideyi!
P2	Namhlanje andizubukela umabona-kude/ iTV. Akukho mbane!
P3	Ndiza kwenza ntoni? Namhlanje ndiza kumamela kumzimba wam!
P4	Okokuqala,  Ndiza kuthi cwaka. Cwaka kakhulu. Ukuze ndibe nokumamela kumzimba wam.
P5	Ewe,  ngoku Ndiyawuva umphefumlo wam. Ngaphakathi. Ngaphandle. Ngaphakathi. Ngaphandle.
P6	Kwaye ndingawenza umphefumlo wam ungxole kakhulwana. Ssssssssssssss! Ngokuthe cwaka Mmmmmm.
P7	Kwaye ngoku ndiyayivavs intliziyo yam,  ibetha! Doodom,  doodoom,  dooodooom.
P8	Ndingayenza intliziyo yam ihambe ngokukhawuleza okanye ngokungxolayo? Ewe,  ngokuxhumela phezulu naphantsi ngamaxesha amashumi amabini.
P9	Ngoku,  bona intliziyo yam ibetha ngokukhawuleza. Doodom,  doodoom,  dooodooom!
P10	Kwaye ukuba ndibeka iminwe yam esihlahleni sam,  ngoko ke ndingakwazi ukuva iPulse yam!
P11	Kwaye ndiyaziva ndihlekaleni sam Haha haha haaah haaa!
P12	Kwaye ndilila Boohoooohooo!
P13	Kwaye ndihleka Qhwa Qhwaba Qhwa Qhwaba.
P14	Ndingasiva isisu sam sixuxuzela Guddu guddu guddu.
P15	Kwaye sisithi ndityse' ndityse'.
P16	Kwaye impumlo yam iyaliva ivumba leejalebisi zigcadeka ekhitshini/egumbini lokuphekela likamama.
P17	Kuluyolo ukuphulaphula umzimba wam. Kwaye ngoku ndifuna ukuva imihlathi yam ihlafuna.
P18	Kwaye nesisu sam sigquma ziijalebisi ezimnandi nezishushu zikamama!
