page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there lived a woman call Asabe, she like to animals such as goat, dog, bird chicken and cat.
P2	One day, the the goat and dog ate up her food. The cat broke the eggs.
P3	Asabe became angry with all the animals and they cried and cried and cried  meaw wuwu.
