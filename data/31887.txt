page_text:

P1	Baba wathu muli kudzulu.
P2	Dzina yanu mbikhale na mbiri.
P3	Umambo wanu mbudze kuna ife.
P4	Pinafuna imwe mbapicitwe, pantsi pano ninga kudzulu.
P5	Tipaseni cakudya cathu ntsiku na ntsiku.
P6	Tilekerereni madawi athu
P7	ninga ifembo tinalekerera andzathu madawi awo.
P8	Lekani kutisiya mbatigwa n'kudawa,
P9	mbwenye tipulumuseni mwa kuipa.
P10	Thangwi umambo ndi wanu, na mphambvu, na mbiri yakukhaliratu. Ameni.
P11	Mateu 6:9-13
