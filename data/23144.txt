desc:	Le nkwenkwana iya kwamalume wayo ohlala esixekweni.

page_text:

P1	Indawo apho kusima khona iibhasi kwidolophu yam yayizele iqhuqhuma ngabantu.
P2	Omnye wabancedisi babaqhubi bebhasi wakhwaza esithi, "Kuyahanjwa ngoku, siya esixekweni!"
P3	Abantu batyhalana apho befuna ukungena ebhasini.
P4	Oomama ababenabantwana bahlala phantsi. Uhambo lwaluzaba lude.
P5	Ndahlala ngase festileni, ecaleni komnye utata.
P6	Ndandicinga ndisithi, "Ndiya esixekweni ngoku, ndizakulikhumbula ikhaya."
P7	Ibhasi yayizele ngabantu abathengisa izimuncu-muncu.
P8	Abanye babakhweli bathenga ezozimuncu-muncu. Mna ndandingenayo ne peni yokuthenga.
P9	Ngesaquphe, umqhubi wabetha ixilongo. 

Kwakulixehsa lokuba ibhasi induluke ukuya esixekweni.
P10	Abathengisi batyhala-tyhalana apho ukuphuma ebhasini.
P11	"Ngabe ndizakubuya nini na bethu?" Ndazibuza.
P12	Kwakutshisa kakhulu ngaphakathi ebhasini. Ndazivalela amehlo wam.
P13	Ndaye ndacinga ngomama nobhutana wam endandibashiye ekhaya.
P14	Emva kwethuba elide, ndazumeka apho ndalala.
P15	Emva kweyure eziliqela, ndaye ndaphaphama. 

Phantse wonke umntu owayesebhasini sele emkile.
P16	Kwakusokulixesha ngoku lokuba ndihambe ndiyokufuna umzi kamalume wam.
