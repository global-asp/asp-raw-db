title:	Kwena le Tshwene

desc:	Dilo dine dile dintle magareng ga ditsala tse disiameng ebong Kwena le Tshwene go fitlhela Kwena a lwala. Ga bonala gore Tshwene o tshwanetse gore enne ene motswasetlhabelo fa a batla gore tsala ya gage e fole.

page_text:

P1	Tshwene le Kwena ene ele ditsala tsa nnete.@Bane ba tshameka mogo. Bane baja mogo. Bane ba dira dilo tsotlhe mogo.
P2	MMe tsatsi le le  lengwe, Kwena a lwala. One a le gaufi le lesu.@Kwena a ya kwago Nyabenzi. "Fa o batla go nna botoka, o tshwanetse goja nama ya Tshwene, ga bua Nyabenzi.
P3	Fa a utlwa se, Kwena a lela.@Fa a batla go nna botoka, o ne a sena tlhopho ntle le gore a je tsala ya gagwe Tshwene.
P4	Fa Tshwene aya go etele tsala ya gagwe, one a namela fa godimo ga mokokotlo wa kwena.@Kwena o ile a isa Tshwene kwa botenng jwa noka.
P5	" O tshwentse keeng tsala ya me? ga botsa Tshwene?@@Kwena are, "Nyabenzi o mpoleletse gore fa ke batla gonna botoka ke tshwanetse go go ja, tsala yame.
P6	Tshwene a araba are, Nyaya, o seke waja nna. Ke monnye.@@Ema motsotswana mme ke tla bitsa ntatemogolo wame. Ene keo mo tonna thata go nale nna. Eja ene mme o tla kgora.
P7	Kwena are, o Kwa kae ene Ntatemogolo oo wa gago?"@@Tshwene a araba are,  O mo setlhareng kwa setlhakatlhakeng sele. "@Kwena are, "Tsamaya o tle le ene gore ke tle go moja."
P8	Tshwene a pagologa mo godimo ga Kwena a taboga a namela setlhare.@Kwena a ema, a ema. Mme Tshwene ga a ka a boa. @@Seo ya nna bofelo ba botsalano jwa bona.
