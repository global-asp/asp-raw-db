desc:	Mphika wa Natabo waphwanyika

page_text:

P1	Uyu ndi Natabo. @@Ali ndi achimwene ake sikisi.
P2	Makolo ake amupatsa kam'phika kakang'ono. @@Amaukonda m'phikawu.
P3	Tsiku lina mchimwene wake aswa m'phikawo.
P4	Natabo sakuupeza m'phika wake. @@Alira kosalekeza.
P5	Natabo athawa. @@Akwera mtengo wautali kwambiri.
P6	Makolo ake amupeza. @@"Chonde tsika," amupempha.
P7	Achimwene ake aja ayimba nyimbo, "Chonde tsika."
P8	Natabo aseka mayimbidwe a nyimboyo.
P9	Kenako, mzake wa Natabo afika.@@"Chonde tsika," ayimba motero.
P10	Mzake wa Natabo ayimba mpaka Natabo atsika mumtengo muja.
P11	Onse ayenda kupita kunyumba.
P12	Onse asangalala kuti Natabo wabwera kunyumba. @@Natabo amupatsa mphika wina.
