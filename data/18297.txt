page_text:

P1	Big houses and expensive cars are not good for the environment.
P2	Suɔ kake yaa nu nyu.
P3	Maybe other friends could help.
P4	Okpɔngɔ enyɔ yaa nu nyu.
P5	What about the poor vultures?
P6	Ngami na etɛ kɛ lohwebi ewie hu yaa nu nyu.
P7	Ogbetee enuɔ kɛ ngami kpotoo ekpa ngɛ nyie kɛ yaa nyuɔ he.
P8	To kpaango ngɛ fo mae keyaa nyu ɔ he.
P9	Kuɔwi kpaangɔ kɛ lo nɛɛ ngɛ nyu wue ngɛ nu ɔ mi.
P10	Jata kake wo gbi. Lɛ hu e ngɛ hlae nɛ enu. Mɛnɔ ngɛ jata gbejee?
P11	Suɔ kake ngɛ nyu nue kɛ jata a.
