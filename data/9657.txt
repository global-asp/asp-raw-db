title:	Lwaki Wakikere Mubi Nnyo?
author:	Ritah Katetemera
artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/9657.png
desc:	Olugero luno lukwata kungeri wamunya gye yalumbamu mukwano gwe wakikere olwo bubalagavu bwe.

page_text:

P1	Emyaka mingi emabega,  wamunya ne wakikere bali bamukwano. Olunaku lumu basalawo okugenda okunonya abagalwa mukibuga.
P2	Wakikere yalina obujja olw'okuba nti wamunya yali mubalagavu nnyo.
P3	Wakikere yagamba: Wakolaki okunyirira bwotyo? Ntunulira,  ndi mubi nnyo,  nkolentya olususu lwange?
P4	Wamunya nagamba: wuliriza! Teka amazzi munsuwa ogateke kumuliro. Ekidako.
P5	Naye wakikere nagamba: Ekyo mbadde nkimanyi. Sirika,  mukwano. Nagenda.
P6	Natuuka ewaka nateeka amazzi munsuwa.
P7	Amazzi bwegatandika okwesera,  neyebikamu.
P8	N'olususu lwe lwona lwajja nomukira negukutukako.
P9	Mukifo kyokubera omujagujagu era omubalagavu ng'omunya,  yafuuka kkangabaana.
