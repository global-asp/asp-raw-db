page_text:

P1	My heart feels a lot of things.
P2	I feel happy when my teacher tells us stories during story time.
P3	I feel great when I play with my friend.
P4	I feel bad when my father shouts at me because of the bad report.
P5	I feel loved when my mother gives me a sweet.
