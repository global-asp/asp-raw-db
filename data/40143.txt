title:	Bò bò wom ti nà ko'i-à
author:	Yoh Edelquin Nabi
artist:	Leo Daly and Magriet Brink
lang:	Kom
level:	First sentences

desc:	Ghèsɨ̀nà jùmtɨ ɨ̀wayn wi ɨ̀ twelâ ŋêyn isàs i ndo nɨ̀ àŋena ta àŋena nɨ nû ikoi kòlonà.

page_text:

P1	Mɨ lum kul ànkùl ɨ̀chfɨ̀ ɨ̀lvɨ̀ ta mɨ n-fvɨ̂. @@Ànkul ɨ̀chfɨ̀ nɨ̀ kɨ̀ŋtɨ̀ mà, fi kɨ̀ŋtɨ̀ kɨ̀mɨ̀ ghel ghɨ̀li.
P2	Mɨ n-sû ɨ̀wu ɨ̀ wom nɨ̀ àsù-à nɨ̀ mû jæ ta mà lu sɨ-a ndo. @@Kɨ̀mɨ teyn ta mà kasi kfa.
P3	Mɨ n-se sɨ toŋtɨ sûyn semsɨ, ghès àŋena ɨ kùm kɨ̀ ngèlsɨ̀ ɨkœ!
P4	Bò bò wom læ meyn nà ko'i  nô sɨ a ŋaŋ. Wu ndù a ndô ngàŋ. @@Bò wom læ na jàŋ kɨ mɨchi ɨ̀n jɨ̀m sɨ yvɨ sa' gha kùm bò bò wom.
P5	Bò bò wom ti nà to' kɨ̀ tèyn teŋ jæ ta wù fe iko'i.@@Wù ti meyn mboynɨ.
P6	Kɨ̀ tèyn, wayn nà wom ɨ zɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ̀ nà kesi-à.@@Wu bè na, "Àndoŋ ɨ̀toŋ nɨ̀ mà nɨn yafɨ-a."
P7	Waynà nà wom ɨ̀ bè na, "Kesi zɨ̀sɨ a wa ngèl-ɨ̀kœ̀!"@@"Tuŋ ànkul ɨchfɨ̀ nɨ̀ và. Lèm aghaf! Nyɨ̀ŋɨ̀ kɨ̀ a wa ɨ̀ kuyn!" wu bè sɨ̂ ŋweyn.
P8	Tèyn, wayn nàwom ɨ du'ì ka ŋweyn ɨkuyn ɨ nà jàŋà.
