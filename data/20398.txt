title:	Wakikere Ni Wamusota
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Mu lugero luno tuyega tuti emikwano giliku ekikomo.(Kili ni ku Luse 3.).

page_text:

P1	Wakikere ni Wamusota
  ba mukwano.
P2	Wakikere akoba, “Idha
  ninze tukyalireku maama wange.”
P3	“Weebale,” Aye Wamusota yabuuza. “Aani anaatukuumira amaka gaife?”
P4	“Ndidha kubakuumira amaka gaimwe,” Wankembo bwe yakoba.
P5	Wakikere yaata enswa
  mu kikapu kya twala ewa inhina.
P6	Wakikere ni Wamusota
  baasitula munkyonkyo. Baaja mu kibira.
P7	Baayagaana eigana lyaba
  Wakadaada. Wamusota yaatya.
P8	Wakikere yaakugirira mwine.
P9	Endhala yali ebaluma
  ghe baatukira mu maka ga Inhina.
P10	Obwo baanaaba era beetegeka
  okulya.
P11	Wakikere yakoba, “Wamusota,
  bambi tyama bukalamu.”
P12	Wamusota yagezaaku okutyama,
  aye iye ti Wakikere.
P13	“Tisobola kutyama nga ikere,”
  Wamusota bwe yakoba.
P14	Wakikere tiyawulirisa.
P15	“Tontegeera. Ndi kuja kwirayo
  eka.”
P16	Wamusota yaba munhiivu. “Nali
  ndoghooza nti Wakikere mukwano gwange.”
