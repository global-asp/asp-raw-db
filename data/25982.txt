page_text:

P1	Nhumirwa okwedalya ni bakagwa bange.
P2	Nhumirwa okughandiika nhuguta ku lubaawo.
P3	Nhumirwa okusoma ebitabo n'ebifaananie.
P4	Nhumirwa okulya ebibala eby'endhawulo.
P5	Nhumirwa okuyamba eka.
P6	Nhumirwa okuwulirisa enfumo dha maama.
P7	Nhumirwa okutenduka ku kitanda ekinene.
P8	Nhumirwa okukola ebintu bino. Ighe onhumirwa kukolaki?
