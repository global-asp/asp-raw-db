page_text:

P1	Eriina lyange ninze Yokaana.
P2	Nnina muganda wange gwebeeta kenesi. Kenesi annenda zeena mwenda.
P3	Tutambulira ghalala buli kunco nga tuja kwisomero.
P4	Akade ka bbuleeke bwekakuba., Kenesi anhamba okunhambaza yunifoomu y'okuzaana.
P5	Ntera okuzaana ni kenesi awalala ni mikwanogye mu kiseera kya bbuleeke. Akade bwekakuba akaigalawo olunaku. Twanguwa okuja ghaka.
P6	Bwetutuuka e ghaka, tuja twewuga mu mwiga.
P7	Nga tuja okutendhuka,Kenesi andidukiza okusenia amainho.
P8	Muunaku edhisemayo mu wiiki, tuja twasamba omupiira ogw'ebigere mu kisaawe.
P9	Nnenda inho muganda wange.
P10	Maama wange anhumirwa inho okubona abaanabe nga bazaana omupiira ogwebigere.
