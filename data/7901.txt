page_text:

P1	The name of my dog is Stopie.
P2	Stopie likes to bark at night.
P3	Stopie has two puppies.
P4	Stopie's young puppy likes to steal food in the kitchen.
P5	The other puppy has three legs.
P6	The three dogs barks during the day and at night.
