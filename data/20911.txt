desc:	Dog finds some eggs on the river shore

page_text:

P1	One day, Dog found eggs at the river
P2	Dog put the ten eggs in his bag.
P3	Dog looked after the eggs and kept them warm.
P4	Crocodile asked Dog, “Have you seen my eggs?”
P5	Crocodile asked all animals about her eggs.
P6	Meanwhile,  the eggs hatched one by one.
P7	Dog could not give the young crocodiles enough food.
P8	One day, Crocodile heard the rumbling bellies of young crocodiles!
P9	Crocodile went inside and hit Dog with her tail.
P10	Crocodile chased Dog down the road.
P11	Dog stopped and said to Crocodile, "I am sorry!"
P12	Crocodile forgave him and went to swim with her children.
