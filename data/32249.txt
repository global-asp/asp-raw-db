desc:	Have you ever seen a cow with only one horn and no tail?

page_text:

P1	Wëi! @@Sëcu dia rëni egeɽa rǝŋǝra.
P2	Dia rerṯo đǝl nenda đonto, na rero lǝper.
P3	Dia rađǝwo, na rama ara.
P4	Dia ronḏǝṯo kañ.
P5	Ummia igi gǝsia gëbwa diara kañ.
P6	Nda iria yesa ŋaña, dia irri ṯarǝso ronto.
P7	Dia rađowa lorba. @@Iria isi yiṯënu yaɽaŋa alǝsen ǝrtođ.
P8	Agiđi ndǝ agǝfiđu rarno irri, agiđi ŋiđiṯau?
