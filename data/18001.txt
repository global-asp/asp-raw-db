title:	Tšeo Ke Ratago Go Dira

desc:	Na o rata go dira eng?

page_text:

P1	Ke rata go fofa kgati le mogwera wa ka. I enjoy skipping with my friend.
P2	Ke rata go ngwala ditlhaka letlapeng. I enjoy writing letters on the board.
P3	Ke rata go bala dipuku tša go ba le diswantšho. I enjoy reading books with pictures.
P4	Ke rata go ja dienywa tša go fapafapana. I enjoy eating different fruit.
P5	Ke rata go thuša ka gae. I enjoy helping at home.
P6	Ke rata go theeletša dikanegelo tša mma. I enjoy listening to my mother's stories.
P7	Ke rata go robala bolaong bjo bogolo. I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.
P8	Ke rata go dira dilo tše. Na wena o rata go dira eng? I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?
