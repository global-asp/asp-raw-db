title:	Niong'or
author:	Alfred Esinyen and Simon Ipoo
artist:	Sue Kramer
lang:	Ng’aturkana

desc:	Eyakasi ng’agiran ta ng’itorubei lu idodiunito ng’iboro luong’ora lu ca a ng’irwa ka daang.

page_text:

P1	Ng'akito naang'ora tochii.
P2	Eng'atuny loepwasitae nieng'or.
P3	Amugati naang'or tononok.
P4	Ng'akekio naang'ora.
P5	Ng'abutio naang'ora tomojong'ito.
P6	Ekosowan loong'or –kamalamalan.
P7	Apukot naang'or tochek.
P8	Ing'ok nieng'or-sulut.
P9	Echoto loong'or kichapikinite.
P10	Eleu loong'or-kaapalan.
P11	Ani nyo nabo en? Ereet loasegan anakinwaket.
