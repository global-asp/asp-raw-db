page_text:

P1	Mkhulu wayehlala no gogo no mzukulu. Njalo ekuseni umkhulu waye vuka enze isidlo sasekuseni
P2	Mabeqeda ukudla baya emasimini beyilima
P3	Mfana wa khohlwa ukuvala ihhoko lezinkukhu, zaphuma izinkukhu zaya engadini. Gogo wamemeza mfana " Phuthuma uyo khipha izinkukhu engadini"
P4	Mkhulu wayehamba yena ayozingela ngesikhathi gogo esemasimini
P5	Ebusuku mfana wavuka wayo bheka gogo ngoba emuze ekhala
P6	Gogo wamxoxela ngephupho lakhe. Wathi uphuphe ikati ne nkukhu behlanganiso itulo lokuyomosha engadini.
