title:	Wankima alya omukira gwe
author:	Berkit Cizellah Desire and Murungi Hannington
artist:	Horácio José Cossa
lang:	Luganda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/23137.png
desc:	Tutunulidde olugero olulala wakaima wayolesa amagezige nga Wankima yali yefudde mugezi naye wakaima namusinza amagezi.

page_text:

P1	Aw'olwatuuka edda ennyo Wakaima n'eWankima baali bamukwano.
P2	Olunaku lumu  Wakaima yagamba Wankima nti, "Tutambulemu tunoonye okyokulya". Naye musajja watu Wankima teyamanya anti kano kaali atego.
P3	Baagenda mu nnimiro yakasooli nebatandiika okumulya
P4	Naye olwokubanga Wakaima yali mujagujagu yali ayogedde nebanannyini nnimiro nga abagambye okukwata Wankima okumulaga nti teyali mugezi
P5	Awo Wakaima yatandika okukuba omulanga nti, "Ababbi! Ababbi! Ababbi! Kasooli wamwe yena aweddewo.
Olwawogana yabuuka naduka nagende.
P6	Banannyini nnimiro bayanguwako okujja era nebasaanga nga Wankima agezaako okudduka. Naye olwokuba nti omukir gwe muwanvu yalemesebwa okudduka
P7	Bananyini nnimirro bamugoberera nga bawogana nti, "yimirira! "yimirira! Twakulabye dda Wankima.
P8	Bwebatyo bamukwaata n'ebatandika okumuka.
P9	Bweyalaba nga obulumi taabusobole kwekugamba nti, "Nabadde n'emukwano gwange Wakaima. Yeyampise wano."
P10	Naye bananyini nnimiro bamusalako omukira ne bamugamba nti, "Genda ogambe mukwano gwo nti tusazeeko omukira gwo."
P11	Oluvannyuma baagenda munyumba ya Wakaima era nebafumba omukira gwa Wankima okuliibya. Wakaima yasalawo okuyita Wankima ku kyemisana.
P12	Ekyemisana olwamala okugwa, Wakaima nabuuza Wankima oba kyalidde akimanyi. Wankima omusiru kwekuddamu nti, Nedda simanyi!"
P13	Wakaima yamugamba nti yali alidde omukira gwe era nadduka. Okuva olwo tebalima kambuggu.
