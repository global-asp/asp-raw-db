desc:	Nyirakuru wa Karisa amufasha mu buzima bwe.

page_text:

P1	Karisa yabanaga na nyirakuru.
P2	Yajyaga aragirana inka na nyirakuru.
P3	Umunsi umwe, abasirikari baraza.
P4	Babatwarira inka.
P5	Karisa na nyirakuru bariruka barabahunga.
P6	Bihisha mu gihuru  kugeza ijoro riguye.
P7	Nyuma abasirikari baragaruka.
P8	Nyirakuru amuhisha munsi y'amababi y'ibiti.
P9	Umwe muri abo basirikari akandagira kuri Karisa ariko ntiyakoma.
P10	Ba basirikare bamaze kugenda, Karisa na nyirakuru  bava mu bwihisho.
P11	Basubira iwabo bomboka.
