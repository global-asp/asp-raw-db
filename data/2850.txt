page_text:

P1	Indlovu eyodwa iyakophuza.
P2	Izindlulamithi ezimbili ziyakophuza.
P3	Izinyathi ezintathu namalanda amane. Zonke ziyakophuza.
P4	Izimpala ezinhlanu nezintibane eziyisithupha ziyakophuza.
P5	Amaxoxo ayisikhombisa nezinhlanzi eziyisishiyagalombili. Zihlezi emanzini.
P6	Amadube ayisishiyagalolunye ayakophuza.
P7	Kwafika ibhubesi elilodwa liyakophuza.
P8	Ubani onesibindi sokuqhubeka aphuze?
