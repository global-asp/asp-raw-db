desc:	This story is about a boy whose pet is a dog and how he brought it up.

page_text:

P1	Haran pet is called dog Peter. He feeds it every day.
P2	Peter plays with kitten lucy everyday and Haran enjoys seeing them playing.
P3	Haran loves Peter very much. He also plays with it after school. He feeds it with his food.
P4	During weekends he goes to hunt with his friends. Peter is very fierce. He barks to all strangers.
P5	Haran also feeds her mothers chicken. Peter hates chickens very much,  he chases them but Haran chase him away.
P6	Sometimes Peter goes to hunt alone. The lions in the forest chase him but Peter is so fast that he manages to escape.`.
P7	Haran makes sure that  Peters kennel is clean. He cleans it daily after school. He also washes Peter after two days.
P8	Haran has bought Peter a belt and tied it round his neck. Peter now looked  very nice in his belt.
P9	THE END.
