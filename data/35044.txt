page_text:

P1	Lekani nimuphalirani nkhani yane. @@Nkhwamba nakupantha waka pacoko.
P2	Nkhugolera na kwendelera uheni comene pala mphepo yaniyeya.
P3	Ndise ŵabwezi ŵawemi, imwe na ine.
P4	Nkhuphika cakurya cinu.
P5	Nkhungweluska usiku winu.
P6	Ninanjomba zinandi.
P7	Ndine yumoza wa banja linu.
P8	Kweni mukwenera kucenjela comene pala mwanicemera kunyumba kwinu. @@Nkhutemwa yayi kuti munilekenge ndekha.
