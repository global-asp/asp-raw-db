P1	Once upon a time there was a village called unemployment village. @This village had no technology, no food and no water to drink.@After Many many days, A boy called Yaw went to the city by foot, and discovered something called a cell phone
P2	This was a device that could allow him to communicate with his parent in the village. He enjoyed using this device so, he took a bag went to the cell phone shop and got some for his parents.  The next day Yaw was ready to leave the city to
P3	and it was time to leave. In few hours they arrived in the city. His parents had a big smile on their faces they got jobs and houses to live in. Yaw and his family were the first people from the village who went to the city .
P4	go and visit his parents in the village. He had made up his mind that he would send his parents to the city. After a few days Yaw got ready for the trip. His parents also got ready as well. In few minutes their bus arrived at the station
P5	Many years later, Yaw got married with 4 children two boys and two girls. One day his children went to the village to have a look at it
P6	and they introduced them to employment.  From then they lived happily every after
