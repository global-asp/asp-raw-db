page_text:

P1	Ghaligho  omusaadha  eyatunda  bwito  gweyakolera  ghaka  ghe.
P2	Munhumba  eyo  mwalimu  empube. Nhingi  inho!
P3	Buli  lunaku  empube  dhalinganga  ku  bwito  dhakoba, "Tusobola  tutya  okufunha  bwito  oyo?"
P4	Empube  dhagezaku  okuta  eminwa  dhadho  mu  nthupa. Aye  ebifo  mu  nthupa  byali  bitono  inho.
P5	Empube  dhagezaku  okunhinha  kungulu  ku  nthupa. Aye  nga  obwito  tibuyuyika.
P6	Empube  dhali  n'olukungano  okwogera  kunsonga  eyo.  Nkabisewo  ekiseraq,  "Tute  emikira  dhaife  mu  nthupa.  Agho  tusobole  okukomba  obwito  ku  mikiira  dhaife.
P7	Ekyo  nhikyene  empube  kye  dhakola. Dhata  emikiira  dhadho  mu  nthupa, era  dhakomba  bwito  kubuli  mukiira  gwayiinhayo.  Empube  dhakola  diiti  paka  bwedhakomba  mu  bwito  yenhayenha.
P8	Omusadha  o'womunhumba  yabonha  nga  bwito  we  yenhayenha  aweiremu!
P9	Era  eyo  nheyali  enkomerero  ya  kyo.
