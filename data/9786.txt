page_text:

P1	Kwasukasukela. Emandulo kwakukhona izinsizwa ezazilahlekelwe yizinkomo. Zahamba,  zahamba kwaze kwahlwa  zihamba zifuna izinkomo.
P2	Kwathi sekuhlwile,  babona umuzi okhanyayo. Bavumelana ngokuthi  kungcono bayocela indawo yokufihla ikhanda.
P3	Bafika bangqongqoza bavulelwa,  bangena. Kodwa uma sebengaphakathi bangabona muntu. Kodwa bezwa izwi libabingelela.
P4	Laqhubeka izwi lathi,  "Mina ngiyintwala. Vulani lapho ebhodweni niphake nidle. Nikhe amahewu lapho embizeni niphuze."
P5	Intwala yadonsa isikhumba sayo yaphumela ngaphandle.
P6	Badla,  baphuza,  babonga bahamba.
P7	Bathi  besasendleleni bafikelwa umcabango omubi,  bathi,  "Ngeke siloku sibusa entwaleni." Khona manjalo bavumelana bajika bathi sebeyoyichoba.
P8	Hhayi ke,  ngempela bafika kuyona bayichoba. Bathi beqeda nje,  awu! Bazithola sebehleli phandle umuzi usushabalele nayo intwala ingasekho. Cosi cosi yaphela!
