title:	The Sprouting Bean
author:	Clare Verbeek
artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2257.png
desc:	Plant a bean seed and see what happens when you water and look after it.

page_text:

P1	The root grows first.
P2	It grows down into the soil.
P3	Then the stem grows. It grows up towards the sun.
P4	The first leaves come out.
P5	The bean plant grows tall.
P6	Then the flowers come out. The flowers turn into beans.
P7	There are lots of seeds in the bean.
