page_text:

P1	Eira  einho,  kadhuuyu   ni musota  baali  ba  mukwano.
P2	lwali  lulala batandiika  okuwaya.  Aye  Kadhuuyu yali  mugezi  inho  okusinga  musota,  era  musota  yali  mukiidhi!
P3	Kale baali  bali kwe buuzaamu naanhi  asinga kulya nkaakale.
Musota yaakoba, "leka  tukigezeeku.  Ndha kukughangula na kukulingirira oti bwa ki."
P4	Omusota n'akadhuuyu byeinama okugalingagana. okulya enkaakale.  Beeyongera okikola  emikuzi  kamaala.
P5	Musota yaakoogha. Yaava  mu mubiri  luwu lwonka  nolwasigala  nga lulingira  kadhuuyu.
P6	Kadhuuyu yeyongera okulingirira oluwu lwa musota   okumala  entangama ey'obusaawa obuweraku.
P7	Kadhuuyu  yadhuubanga ati naakasinga bugezi   mu bisolo, aye  olunaku  olwo, obugezi  bwe  bwakoma.  Era eyo  n'ensonga  lwaki leero  kadhuuyu atenduka mamoga.
