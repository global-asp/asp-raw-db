title:	Yɛn Nantwibedeɛ Pendo
author:	Henrietta Afrani (OLE Ghana)

lang:	Asante Twi

desc:	Abasɛm a ɛfa nantwibedeɛ a abusua dɔ no paa.

page_text:

P1	Ndalo nie.
P2	Ndalo pɛ nwoma akenkan.
P3	Pendo nie.
P4	Pendo pɛ karrots.
P5	Ndalo pɔn sukuu a ɔde Pendo kɔ adidie.
P6	Ɔma Pendo nsu pa nom.
P7	Papa ma Pendo aburo.
P8	Ɔtwe Pendo nufusuo da biara.
P9	Pendo ma nufusuo bebree.
P10	Papa tɔn Pendo nufusuo no.
P11	Ndalo nom nufusuo dabiara.
P12	Ɔka sɛ,  "Pendo me da wase!"
