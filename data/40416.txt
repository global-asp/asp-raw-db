title:	Dzuwa lotanganika kwambiri
author:	Peter Msaka
artist:	Joe  Werna
lang:	ChiChewa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/40416.png
desc:	Nkhaniyi ndi yokhudza dzuwa monga mmene limaonedwera ndi mnyamata wamngono kuchoka mmawa mpaka madzulo.

page_text:

P1	Dzuwa limatuluka m'mawa, kuchokera ku m'mawa.@@Ndimamva tambala akulira mbandakucha.
P2	Dzuwa limadutsa pamwamba pa nyumba ya Tefa, chakudya cham'mawa tisanadye.@@Ndimamva kununkhira kwa tiyi ndi zitumbuwa kutsatira kuwala kwadzuwa pa windo langa.
P3	Dzuwa limapita kuseli kwa mitengo masana kusukulu.@@Kenako limafika pa madzi odikha pakati pa bwalo losewererapo.
P4	Dzuwa limayima pamwamba pa mutu wanga. Chithunzithunzi changa  chimakhala pambali panga.@@Timasewera masewero a chithunzithunzi ndi anzanga.
P5	Chithunzithunzi changa chimakula, kenako kuchepa. Timachithamangitsa.@@Chithunzithunzi changa chimatalika, kenako kufupika. Timachithamangitsa.
P6	Ndimaima, anzanganso amaima. Timaona zithunzithunzi zathu zikuvina.@@Timatopa kenako ndikubwereranso mkalasi. Tikaweruka timapita kunyumba.
P7	Dzuwa limayasamula.@@Ndimaliona dzuwa likuzimirira kumadzulo. Ndimaona chithunzithunzi changa pa khoma.@@Ndi nthawi yokagona.
P8	Dzuwa limatsika kuseli kwa mitambo.@@Ndimagona pabedi panga, ndipo ndimalota dzuwa likupita kutali kwambiri.
