title:	Hikuyini a Kheli Gi Svarili
author:	Rafael Ernesto Samuel
artist:	Hélder de Paz Alexandre
lang:	Xitswa
level:	First sentences

desc:	Frog might have been as beautiful as Lizard if he would only listen!

page_text:

P1	A kali ka masiku,  a nyakolongi ni kheli a vahi vangana. A siku gu kari,  vona va pimisili aku famba va laha a zigangu le doropeni.
P2	A kheli a gihi ni matsoho ni mungana wakwe nwa nyakolongi hikusa yena awa sasekili a miri wakwe wu phatima.
P3	Yena a mu wotisili aku ngalu: Zvini unga maha kasi wena wa kota aku saseka ngovu? Hinga nzi cuwuka,  mina nzi svarili ngovu. Nzi faneli aku maha a yini ni miri wa mina?
P4	A nyakolongi aku ngalu: Ingisa! Cela a mati lomu ka bota u gama u kungeta laha ka nzilu.
P5	Kanilezvi a kheli gi ti ngalo: A nzi zi tiva,  nza zi tiva. Miyela,  mungana! Yena a famba.
P6	Hiku maha loku,  yena a yile kaya aya cela a mati lomu ka bota.
P7	Loku a mati ma sangulili aku vila,  yena ati hoxelili lomu ka bota!
P8	A miri wakwe wu svili,  a mudoza wakwe wu tsemeka!
P9	Aku tsika aku phatima niku saseka ku kwatsi hi nwa nyakolongi,  yena a kumekili na a svarili ngovu!
