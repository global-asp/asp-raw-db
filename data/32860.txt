title:	Daisy Odabwisa
author:	Bether Mwale-Moyo and Moffat Moyo
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	CiNyanja
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32860.png
desc:	Pa munzi wina panali kunkala Daisy. Daisy anali kankuku kakangono kamene kanali kunfunisisa kuuluka. Nkuku zonse zinamuseka Daisy pamene anayesesa mobwezerabwezera kuyeselela kuuluka.

page_text:

P1	Kalekale pamunzi wina.
P2	Panali kankhuku kocedwa Daisy.
P3	"Ine nikakula, nifuna kuuluka mumwamba kufika kumlengalenga," anati Daisy.
P4	Koma nkhuku zonse zinamuseka Daisy.
P5	"Iwe ndiwe osokonezeka," zinati nkhuku zina. "Ise tizaleka kusowela na iwe."
P6	"Daisy, tingakwanise kupukusa mapiko koma nicovuta kwambili kuti nkhuku iuluke," anati amake Daisy.
P7	Koma Daisy sanaleke kuyesa. Masiku onse anayesesa mobwezelabwezela payeka, kupukusa mapiko ake. Puku, puku, puku, anapukusa mapiko ake koma sanakwanise kuuluka.
P8	Pamene anali kuyesesa mobwezelabwezela anayelekeza kuti alikuuluka mumwamba mumulengalenga apenyelela nkhuku pansi pake. Daisy anayelekeza kuti auluka kupitilila mbalame. "Taonani!" Mbalame zinati, "Onani nkhuku ikuuluka"
P9	Puku, puku, puku masiku onse Daisy anapukusa mapiko ake.
P10	Daisy akakwanisa kunyamuka anali kugwa ntawi zonse.
P11	"Siningakwanise kuuluka," Daisy analila nauza amai ake. "Anzanga akunena zoonadi."
P12	"Daisy iwe usiyana na nkhuku zina. Iwe ufuna kuuluka, nkhuku zina sizifuna kuuluka! Iwe ungakwanise," anati amake Daisy.
P13	Siku losatila Daisy anakwela pamwamba pa khola, puku,puku,puku anapukusa mapiko ake. Anauluka mumpepo ndipo anapukusa, napukusa, napukusa mapiko ake posatila pake……
P14	PHWE…
P15	Nkuku zina zinaseka kwambili. "Ha ha ha! Nkuku sizingauluke!"
P16	Koma siku losatila, Daisy anakwela pamwamba kwambili, pamwamba pa nyumba. Puku, puku, puku, Daisy anapukusa mapiko ake.
P17	Daisy anauluka napukusa, napukusa, napukusa mapiko ake posatila pake…..
P18	Anapitiliza kuuluka! Mpepo pansi pamapiko ake inapita na mphamvu ndipo Daisy anauuluka kupita mumwamba! Mbalame zinati, "Chodabwisa! Nkhuku ikuuluka!"
P19	Nkhuku zonse zinafuna kukhala ngati Daisy.
P20	Nkhuku zinati, "Taonani Daisy ndiwe odabwisa!"
