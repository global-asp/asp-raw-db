desc:	 The story tells you how Tembe spends her day both at school and at home.

page_text:

P1	Namuhungo wakes up at seven o'clock in the morning,  just after the first cockcrow. She washes her face, brushes her teeth, puts on her school uniform and leaves for school.
P2	Her elder brother escorts her,  they talk while moving until Namuhungo reaches school.
P3	At 8: 10 she is already in class,  ready for lessons.
P4	At break time she plays with her friends in order to refresh their minds and also in preparation for the next lesson.
P5	While in class, she participates fully and teacher Nabune likes that.
P6	The teacher gives them time to rest after a long lesson and Namuhungo lies next to the teacher.
P7	When they wake up,  the helps them cross the railway and get back home.
P8	When Namuhungo gets home, she spends some time with her family members.
P9	After supper,  she bathes,  and puts on her night dress. She goes to bed,  prays,  and finally sleeps till the next day.
P10	How do you spend your day?
