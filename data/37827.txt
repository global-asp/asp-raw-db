desc:	Goat borrow Hyena Knife

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, food been no dey, everiwia dry. Di animals com begin eat only grass. @@Notin been dey. Only di imbowa plant on top big trees dem been com survive.
P2	One day goat see wen Hyena pass wit knife. @@Goat ask for di knife so dat she go cut imbowa plant chop.
P3	Hyena giv goat di knife. She cut di plant, she eat am until  she belleful. @@Goat com forget about di knife wey she been even use.
P4	Wen Hyena com ask for di knife, Goat no com know wetin to do. @@She been no fit remember wia she drop di knife.
P5	Goat scatter everiwia de find di knife. @@Till today Goat still de find di knife.
P6	Hyena tell Goat, "SInce you don loss my kinfe, wey I been de take make money, I go chop you. Den I go com chop ya pikin too."
P7	From dat time till now, Hyena no de take eye see goat o, e must chop am.
P8	Meanwhile, goat still de waka de find Hyena Knife.
