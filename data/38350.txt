title:	Taariha Nyebbe: No nyebbe mawnirta
author:	Yunuusa  Yaakubu
artist:	Lisa Treffry-Goatley
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Ɗum habaru bee fotooji dow no nyebbe mawnirta. Diga aawugo

page_text:

P1	Soobaajo am hokkiyam nyebbe daga ngesa maako.
P2	Mi aawi nyebbere nder leydi.
P3	Nyebbere fuɗi nder leydi.
P4	Awdi e fuɗira dow, e tokka naange. Ɗaɗi e ndillira ley.
P5	Awdi ndin jalɓina, wannga dow leydi.
P6	Jalɓel mawna,
P7	ngel mawna.
P8	Mi aawi nyebbe ɗuɗɗe.
P9	E ɗe mawna,
P10	e ɗe mawna.
P11	Nyebbe ɗen ndimi.
P12	Nyebbe ɗen e ɗe mawnira dow, e ɗe tokkiti dakkawol am.
P13	Mi itti nyebbe nde ɗe njoori.
P14	Mi ɓoltiɗe.
P15	Mi defay nyebbe bee albasa boɗeejum bee haakooji feere.
P16	Nyebbe ɗen ɗe ɓenndi, ɗe kewti nyaameego.
