title:	Fijirde Bol!
author:	Salaamatu Yaakubu
artist:	Onesmus Kakungi
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32095.png
desc:	Maama lili taaniiko soodanamo liʼorɗum. Ammaa ɓinngel ngel nasti fijirde bol. Maama heɓay ko yiɗi na?

page_text:

P1	Mi yiɗi min pija, min e soobaajo am Cinndo.
P2	Min soobaaɓe soosey. To ɗum lili gooto amin fuu, min njaaday.
P3	Nyalaande feere, maama am liliyam mi soodanamo mannda e nebbam biriiji. Mi yaadi e Cinndo.
P4	Dow laawol min ngi'i soobaʼen amin e pija bol.
P5	"Kay! Raa fijirde bol!" Mi wiʼi, "Min maa, mi yiɗi fijugo."
P6	Mi wiʼi Cinndo, "En ƴamuɓe, to ɓe njaɓii sey pijiden bee maɓɓe. Mi yiɗi fijugo bol soosey."
P7	Cinndo wiʼi, "En artu yahugo luumo nden lorooɗen pijen."
P8	Ammaa mi wiʼimo, "En artu pijen taw!" @@"Ngam to en koorii lilal, maama haɗayʼen."
P9	Min nastii fijirde bol. Min, mi nanngoowo bol, mi resi kaaƴe ɗiɗi-ɗiɗi haade am, mi dari caka.
P10	Mi tiiɗi, mi heelanakemin boɗɗum, soobaaɓe am nyaamaayi koo bol goʼo.
P11	Min ngaɗi ka fijugo naaka babal fuu warti sollaare.
P12	Nden min njahi luumo. Min anndaa ceede maama njanii! Mi fuɗɗi woyugo. Cinndo hooli wiʼi, "Accu woyugo. An waɗika!"
P13	Min kooti, min soodaayi mannda e nebbam. Min kultori, nden limse amin fuu sollaare.
P14	Maama nanaayi belɗum, ɓerni o ƴamimin, "Toye ngonnooɗon haa jooni?"
P15	Doole, min mbiʼimo goonga. O yaafaniimin ammaa o wagginimin o wiʼi, "Taaʼ meetee yahugo fijirde sey to mi jaɓii."
P16	Min nyamnii agaagaaji maama am. Nden min ngiiwii.
