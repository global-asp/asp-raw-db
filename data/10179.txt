P1	I love the rainbow.
P2	I love all the colours of the rainbow.
P3	I wish I could have a rainbow house.
P4	It could have a red roof.
P5	It could have a blue windows.
P6	I could have it painted yellow outside.
P7	It could have violet walls inside.
P8	It could have orange flowers.
P9	It could have indigo bed.
P10	It could have a green garden.
