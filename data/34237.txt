desc:	Xitori xa mutsari lontsongo loyi a siyeke xirhapa xa yena eGiyani. (Xitori na swifaniso swi tshuriwile hi Khotavuxika 2019 eka ntirho lowu a wu fambisa hi va Sophiatown Community Psychological Services eJoni).

page_text:

P1	Yindlu leyi yi le Giyani.@@Ndza yi rhandza swinene.
P2	Ku na xiyindlwana xa hina xo hlambela kona.@@Ku na xihambukelo xa hina kona.
P3	Ku na xirhapa. @@Ndzi byala swibyariwa kona.
P4	Manana u ri, "Mana wa mina u ndzi dyondzise ku byala. Sweswi ndzi dyondzisa wena."
P5	Ndzi byarile matamatisi na xipinichi.@@Ndzi byale ndzi ri ndzexe.
P6	Ndzi ta kha matamatisi ndzi ma yisa eka manana.@@Manana u ta ku, "Ndza khensa."
P7	Hi sukule eGiyani.
P8	Ku hava xirhapa laha Joni.
