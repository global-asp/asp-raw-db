title:	Dhyaŋ Ma Ja Tunge Acel
author:	Owino Ogot and Athieno Gertrude
artist:	Marion Drew
lang:	Dhopadhola
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/19478.png
desc:	Kigana ma tito cal gi kula pa dhyaŋ.

page_text:

P1	Heeeee! Wan nitye gi dhyaŋ maŋon.
P2	Dhyaŋ no nitye gi tunge acel kende,  aka ongoye gi yiwe.
P3	Opoŋ,  aka iye dwoŋ.
P4	Obedo dhyaŋ ma ni gi men.
P5	Jakwath omaro dhyaŋ no m'oloyo.
P6	Dhyaŋ me ki cem gi wadi,  cemo acema ka kende.
P7	Dhyaŋ me ja gorok. Wadi kitundi bothe.
P8	Inyalo timo aŋo ka i romo gi dhyaŋ ngeri me?
