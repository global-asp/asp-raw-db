title:	Okucaalira dhaadha omukazi
author:	Abdul Maged Mbago and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	https://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/26225.png
desc:	Abaana babiri beesunga era bacamuka nga bateekateeka okucaalira dhaadha ghaibwe. Banhumirwa obulamu mukyaalo era tibaduuba kuvaayo.

page_text:

P1	Odango ni Apiyo baalindirira ekiwuumulo.
P2	Kyali kiseera kya kucaalira dhaadha omukazi.
P3	Baavuga baabita ku nsozi, ensolo enkambwe ni amasamba.
P4	Oluvainhuma, Odaongo ni Apiyo baalambaala.
P5	Baayagaana dhaadha ghaibwe ghansi gh'omuti.
P6	Dhaadha yakina era yaaemba. N'abaana baamuyambaku.
P7	Dhaadha yaabagha enkabi mu bughangwa.
P8	Odonga ni Apiyo baazaanhisa ebinhonhi n'ebiwoiwolo.
P9	Baaniina emiti.
Baazaanhira mu maadhi ag'enhandha.
P10	Baakoowa.
Baalambaala nga bakaali kulya kyaigulo.
P11	Baayambaku dhaadha.
Okusolooza amagi n'okwagha eiva.
P12	Dhaadha yayegeresa Odongo ni Apiyo okufumba emere ey'endhawulo.
P13	Odongo yaja okwaya ente.
Dhaalya enimiro ya muliraanwa ghaibwe.
P14	Baaja ki mudaala gwa dhaadha ghaibwe mu kiya.
P15	Eigulo, baayambaku dhaadha ghaibwe okubala empiiya.
P16	Amangu einho, ekiwuumulo kyaghaaku.
Dhaadha yaabasibira emere.
P17	Abaana tibayenda kuvaagho.
"Dhaadha, tuje tutyo."
P18	Odongo ni Apiyo baamugwa mu kifuba.
"Weeraba, dhaadha," Bwebaakoba.
P19	Kiki ekisinga, bulamu bwa mukyalo oba bwa mukibuga?
