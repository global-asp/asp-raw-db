desc:	Duk abin zaka yi ce in Allah yaso ya yarda.

page_text:

P1	Wata rana Joha zai je kasuwa. Sai ya hadu da abokinsa
P2	Sai abokin ya tambaya: Ina zaka Joha? Joha yace zani kasuwa.
P3	Dan in sayo jaki. Sai abokin yace masa ka ce in Allah yaso ya yarda.
P4	Aa! Ina da kudi kuma akwai jakai a kasuwa
P5	Sai aka sace kudin a kasuwar. Joha yace Oo! ba sauran sayen jaki a yau.
P6	Joha yayi nadama rashin fadin in Allah yaso ya yarda.
