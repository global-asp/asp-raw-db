desc:	Ebei na mme awe vha khou lindela bisi khulwane dala u vha endedza u ya ḓoroboni. Fhedzi bisi dala yo tshinyala vha ḓo isa hani ḓoroboni?

page_text:

P1	One day,  a goat went out in the bush,  and wandered around happily. But then she got lost!
P2	Ho vha hu na bisi nthihi fhedzi muvhunduni wa ha Ebei. Yo vha i khulwane nahone i dala. Yo vha i na phosho.
P3	Suddenly,  she met a wolf! The wolf came up to her,  and said,  "I am very hungry."
P4	Liṅwe ḓuvha mme a Ebei vha ri,  "Matshelo ri ḓo ya ḓoroboni u renga dzhunifomo yaṋu ya tshikolo."
P5	The goat said,  "Mr Wolf,  I know you want to eat me. But I would like to die happy!"
P6	Ebei a takala vhukuma. Vha ḓo ṱuwa nga bisi khulwane dala. Ho ngo eḓela hovho vhusiku.
P7	The wolf asked the goat,  "What do you mean? Die happy? How?"
P8	Ebei o vha o no ambara musi mme awe vha tshi ḓa u mu vusa.
P9	The goat answered,  "If I have to die,  I would like to die dancing!"
P10	Ebei na mme awe vha tshimbila vha ya vhuimabisi. Vha lindela bisi khulwane dala. Bisi i si bvelele.
P11	Then the wolf took his guitar. He sat down and started playing it.
P12	Vhaṅwe vhathu vha swika vhuimabisi. Vha vhilaela uri bisi yo lenga. "Bisi yo ri sia?" vha a vhudzisa.
P13	The goat started dancing,  though she was still a little bit worried.
P14	Ebei o vha a khou vhilaela.  "A ri nga koni u ya ḓoroboni. A thi nga koni u wana dzhunifomo yanga," a lelekanya zwenezwo.
P15	There were dogs nearby. They came to see what was happening.
P16	Vhaṅwe vhathu vha ṱovhowa vha humela hayani. Ebei ene a lila a sa ṱoḓi u ṱuwa. Mme awe vha mu khuthadza. "Ri ḓo lindela nyana," vho ralo vha tshi amba.
P17	The wolf was afraid of dogs. So,  he turned and ran away!
P18	Hu si kale vha pfa phosho. Vha vhona buse muyani.    Bisi yo vha i khou da!
P19	The goat kept on dancing,  because now she was free from the wolf!
P20	Fhedzi bisi ya hone a si dala nahone a si khulwane. Heyi yo vha i tswuku nahone i ṱhukhu. Vhathu vho lindelaho vha lavhelesa iyo bisi. Vha si ṋamele.
P21	"Kha vha dzhene! Kha vha dzhene." Mureili u a vhidzelela. "Ro no lenga nga maanḓa namusi," a tshi amba.
P22	Ebei na mme awe vho vha vhone vha u thoma u dzhena. Na avha vhaṅwe vha kona u tevhelavho.
P23	Ebei a sedza nnḓa nga fasiṱere. A vhona vhathu vhanzhi vhuima bisi.
P24	Nahone vhathu vhanzhi vho vha vha tshi khou gidimela u fara bisi. Fhedzi vho vha vho lenga. Bisi yo vha yo no ḓala. Bisi tswuku ya livha ḓoroboni.
P25	"Bisi khulwane dala i ngafhi?" Hu vhudzisa mme a Ebei. "Yo tshinyala," hu fhindula mureili. "Ri khou i lugisa,  i ḓo da matshelo." O ralo a tshi engedza.
P26	Ebei ho ngo londa zwa muvhala wa bisi. Ho ngo londa vhungafhani ha bisi. O takalela uri bisi i khou ya ḓoroboni.
