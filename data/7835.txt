title:	Ipeet Yen Acuwa
author:	John Emongot
artist:	Benjamin Mitchley and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Ateso

desc:	Icamunit ipeet yen obu omodomod ajele itoni ajawun akiring nadakit.

page_text:

P1	Ikolo sek ajai ipeet yen acoo noi.
P2	Eboye ipeet yen kotoma akidingot amagoro na alala noi.
P3	Adope paran ayarit ipeet yen etenge noi, olosi aimo ibore yen inyamat amagoro.
P4	Ajai ipeet yen alosit amagoro, obu owanyu ajele iboye atoromi na ekitoyi odakite akiring na'ti aituk.
P5	Obu ipeet yen osalak iteyi ajele na, obala otawu ke,  "epone bani ebeyitori engo adumun akiring ngin?"
P6	Owomowumoni ipeet yen acowa. Oh! Eyeyi ajele ejok,  inye alimok engo oyeyik ber ekoso.
P7	Otemari ipeet,  "Ongolya!Ilayi jo noiipedori jo ayeyikin engo?"
P8	Obu ajele na onakak akena ka'imungakin akowu.
P9	Anen obu ajele na opolor aiyalama noi. Konye nepengari aituk aiyee, ocakun akiring.
P10	Akawulo n'akiring acakun, obu ipeet iraru atipet ka'byolocar akerit.
P11	Ewanyu ajele na ebe ajai ipeet yen amodomod nges anu akiring, opotu olomakisi adap.
P12	Konye mam ajele obu opedo aikamun ipeet itoni na'lomara amagoro.
