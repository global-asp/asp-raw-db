desc:	Talks about the activities done during the school holidays.

page_text:

P1	It is now time for holidays.
P2	We do a lot of work and play.
P3	Mega and Kana wake up very early to feed the chicken, pick eggs and collect firewood.
P4	Mega and his father take the cow to graze.
P5	Mother,  Mega and Kana go to visit their grandmother in the village of Mulandu.
P6	They go to the bus stop and wait for the bus to take them.
P7	Grandmother was very happy to see her grandchildren!
P8	Grandfather was also very happy to see his grandchildren!
P9	Grandmother is preparing lunch for her grand children.
P10	Mega and Kana were served with fish and rice for lunch.
P11	The evening, Mega went to play football with his friends.
P12	Kana joins the reading club in the evening too.
P13	On the last day of the holiday,  Mega and Kana joined the children`s party. It was so colorful.
