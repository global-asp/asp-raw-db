desc:	Kuwamu kagawizuba nyumayisaha iridyi

page_text:

P1	hari hari kuwa mukaga hariho uruzuba dwichi chane chane. buli umwe yali afite ubute
P2	uwimana yaravuze ati tuje kungezi ariko irankunda aramusubiza ati mama yaratubujije. uwimana aramubwira ngo ntabwo turuga. ngo turakinira aho uruhande
P3	twamanjije twakuramo ingato.aroko dusigara tuchunva ikizuba. tugezaho dukuramo amasikati namabulawuzi. nabwo dusigara tuchunva uruzuba
P4	twashize amaguru mungezi. nuko twitelelaho amazi
P5	twaloze tuloga nuko twibagidwa amasaha
P6	izuba dyatangiye gukyendera nuko twunva akabehokeza. ariko si ibyambaro byachu biri hehe?
P7	twashakiye buli hamwe hose hose turabibula
P8	hari hariho inka aho uruhande dwingezi. Rukundo ko yalebye hejuru abona inka iri kudya ibyambaro byachu
P9	Inka yaliye ibyambaro byachu. aliko si mama yalabyemeye?. yadukubise inkoni zashuhishije ibibuno byachu
