page_text:

P1	Ngithanda ukudlala ibhola.@@Ngithanda ukudlala ibhola noThando, uNhlanhla noBen.
P2	Ngithanda umama wami. @@Ungenza ngihlale ngihleka.
P3	Ngithanda uNkulunkulu, ngoba nguyena owangidala.
P4	Ngithanda isikole, ngoba othisha bangifundisa konke okufanele ngikwazi.
P5	Ngithanda nokudlala nezinja, ngoba kuyangithokozisa.
P6	Ngithanda imigubho yezinsuku zokuzalwa, ngoba ngiyathanda ukujabula.
P7	Nami futhi ngiyazithanda ngoba…
P8	…ngoba ngiyazithanda!
