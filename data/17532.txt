page_text:

P1	Ngelinye lilanga,  tonkhe tilwane tasendle tahlangana kutokhetsa inkhosi. Tase tincuma kukhetsa Libhubesi kutsi libe yinkosi.
P2	Tashaya indumezulu yemcimbi leyatsatsa liviki,  ekujabuleleni kuba ne nkosi lesha. Tadla,  tanatsa,  tacula futsi tadansa.
P3	Kwatsi umawuphela lomcimbi wonkhe umuntfu waya ekhaya lapho ahlala khona.
P4	Nenkosi Bhubesi naye waya ekhaya lakhe naye. Kwatsi ngelilanga lelilandzelako,  Impisi nayecedza kufika kutovakashela inkosi,  yatfola Libhubesi ligula lise mbhedzeni. Latsi Libhubesi lisetinhlungiwini,  "Ngibanjwe kugula lokunemandla. Angati kutsi ngitsatseni ngihlanganise nani."
P5	Impisi yatsi,  "Nkosi yami lenkulu,  ungakhatsateki. Logwaja utolwati likhambi lalokugula."
P6	Libhubesi lajabula latfumela Impisi kutsi iyolandza Logwaja.
P7	Impisi yagijima kuyobita Logwaja,  "Asambe enkosini igula kakhulu,  ingitfume kutsi ngitoku bita." kwasho Impisi.Â  Logwaja akapholisanga maseko wavele waphutfumela indlini yeLibhubesi.
P8	Ngyajabula kuku bona Logwaja wami" kwasho libhubesi ngekukukhulu kujabula. "Ngikubite ngobe impilo yami iselidvwaleni. Nginesifo lesingalapheki. Impisi ingatise kutsi unelikhambi laloku kugula kwami.
P9	Laphendvula Libhubesi,  "Khululeka Logwaja ngebuntima bekutfolakala kwalelikhambi. Inyoni lephaphako ngulebanjwako. Chaza ngalo mutsi."
P10	Waphendvula Logwaja,  "Nkosi yami,  ngalesikhatsi ngitsi lelikhambi kumatima kulitfola bengingasho kutsi lisekudzeni. Kuze ulapheke kumele utsatse mafutsa inhlitiyo yeMpisi."
P11	When Hyena heard that,  he started trembling all over. He was the one who had told Lion that Hare had the medicine to cure his illness! It was useless now to try to contradict Hare about the medicine.
P12	Yavele yabaleka Impisi. Sukela ngalelo langa,  tiMpisi tadla Bologwaja,  kepha tibaleke emabhubesi.
