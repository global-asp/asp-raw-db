page_text:

P1	Gomwe naSindi banohaka mari.
P2	Banothama dzindilo dzonotengesa kumakete.
P3	Gomwe unobona ngwa inotikala kumakete.
P4	Sindi na Fuku ndibo banogwa.
P5	"Letja nkhwinya wangu!" Gomwe emimila epamha eloba Fuku nelukombo.
P6	Fuku newha zwogwadza, ebeluma Gomwe mutjimilo kwazo.
P7	Tjimilo tjitje tjapemuka tjegwadza kwazosa.
P8	Gomwe unatjimilo tjakakondama nentha yolabukila gwa esingazibe tjinotikala.
