P1	Edisak ng'akan ng'aarei neting'io.
P2	Edisak ng'akejen ng'aarei napetoro.
P3	Ng'akonyen nachik ng'aarei ing'olikinete.
P4	Ng'aki ng'aarei nachik epupete.
P5	And two loving arms to HUG!
P6	Ta anuakin ang'akan ang'arei alakara.
