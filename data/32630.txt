page_text:

P1	Kera habayeho umuryango wiberagaho mu byishimo.
P2	Abana ntibigeraga barwana hagati yabo. @Bafashaga ababyeyi mu rugo no mu mirima.
P3	Ariko ntibari bemerewe kwegera umuriro.
P4	Bagombaga gukora imirimo yabo yose nijoro.
P5	Bari bakozwe mu bishashara!
P6	Ariko umwe mu bahungu yifuzaga kota akazuba.
P7	Umunsi umwe kwihanganira kutajya ku zuba byaramunaniye. Abavandimwe be baramwihanangirije.
P8	Ariko byari byarangiye! Yayongeye mu bushyuhe bw'izuba.
P9	Abana b'ibishashara barijijwe no kubona umuvandimwe wabo ayongera ku zuba.
P10	Ariko bigiriye inama. Ibishashara byayonze babikozemo itara risa n'inyoni.
P11	Bajyanye umuvandimwe wabo bakoze mu nyoni ku musozi muremure.
P12	Uko izuba ryarasaga, inyoni yaragurukaga. Yaririmbiraga  mu rumuri rwa mu gitondo.
