title:	Ananse Pa Nase ne Akuraase Mmaa no
author:	Ofosu  Frimpong
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi
lang:	Asante Twi
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Ananse Pa Nase daadaafoɔ ɛne nsunsuansoɔ a ɛba ne so wɔ nakwan so.

page_text:

P1	Berɛ bi a atwam mu, ɛkɔm dee Ananse Pa Nase pa ara. Ɔyɛɛ n'adwene sɛ ɔbɛkɔ akɔpɛ aduane bi adi. @@Ɔkɔɔ n'adamfo a ɔtae yɛ ne mmoa fie nanso na n'adamfo no nni fie.
P2	Pa Nase a ɛkɔm de no no susuu kwan a ɔbɛfa so anya aduane adi. @@Ɔkae sotɔɔ a ɛwɔ akurase no ase a mmaa de wɔn aduane sie.
P3	Ɔbɔ wuraa sotɔɔ no mu kɔwiaa mpataa ne nkwadu bi.
P4	Berɛ a ɔrepue no, mmɔfra nketewa no hunuu sɛ deɛ ne nanteɛ asesa fa. @@Wɔfrɛɛ mmaa no mu baako. Mmaa no to hyɛɛ Pa Nase so.
P5	Ɛnam sɛ na ne bɔtom ayɛ ma nti, Pa Nase suroeɛ na ɔyɛɛ n'adwene sɛ ɔbɛdwane. @@Mmaa no kyee no na ɔyɛɛ sɛ wɔbɛtwe n'aso.
P6	"Yɛn twi no wɔ akuraa yi aboɔ no so," Yalol, Mmaa no mu baako na ɔkyerɛɛ n'adwene saa.
P7	Pa Nase seree na wɔsee wɔn sɛ, "Me nana barima te aboɔ yi mu. Na wɔtwi me wɔ so a ɛte sɛdeɛ wɔde me san rekɔ me akyi. Meda mo ase!"@@Mmaa no yɛɛ wɔn adwene sɛ wɔnnya ntwi no.
P8	"Yɛnto no ntwene Egya no mu," Yangbet na ɔkaeɛ. @@Bio, Pa Nase seree kaa sɛ, "Me nanabaa a ɔdɔ me pa ara na ɛgya nyinaa yɛ no dea. Enti sɛ moto me twene egya yi mu a ɛte sɛdeɛ mode merekɔ me nkyi. Meda mo ase!" @@Mmaa no aba mu buiɛ.
P9	"Yɛnhyehyɛ nnuaba pii wɔ kɛntɛn mu na yɛmfa no nhyɛ mu bi nto no ntwene nsuo mu," Yabuna na ɔsusuiɛ. @@Wei ho na Pa Nase suiiz kaa sɛ, "Mo aku me koraa!" @@Mmaa no ani gyee yie. Wɔhye hyɛɛ nuaba kɛntɛn ma na ɔde no hyɛɛ mu too no twene nsuo no mu.
P10	Na nsuo no nfinfin no Pa Nase gyee n'ani. @@Ɔyii nnuaba no bi diiɛ, na berɛ no ɔseree mmaa no.
P11	Mmaa no hunuu sɛ Pa Nase abu wɔn kwasia nti wɔyɛɛ wɔn adwene sɛ wɔbɛtoa no. @@Nanso nsuo no a na ɛrehuro fa so no amma wɔn kwan amma wɔantumi akye no.
P12	Yei nyinaa akyi, Pa Nase werɛ firi sɛ asaw wɔ nsuo no ase. Ankyɛ koraa na asaw no suum no afidie kyee no. @@Na obiara ankɔboa no anyi no ampue.
