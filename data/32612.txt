desc:	Tomasi agiye ku isoko kugurisha imineke. Kubera ko bidasanzwe muri ako gace ko abagabo bacuruza imbuto mu isoko

page_text:

P1	Tomasi yikoreye agataro k'imineke.
P2	Tomasi agiye ku isoko kugurisha imineke.
P3	Abantu  bari kugura imbuto mu isoko.
P4	Ariko  nta n'umwe uri kugura imineke ya Tomasi. Bakunda kwigurira  imputo z'abagore.
P5	Abantu bakavuga bati: "Mu gace k'iwacu, abagore bonyine ni bo bacuruza imbuto." Bakabazanya bati: "Uyu ni mugabo ki?"
P6	Ariko Tomasi ntava ku izima. Ahamagara agira ati: "Nimugure imineke yange! Ni myiza kandi irahiye!"
P7	Umugore umwe afata iseri ry'imineke ku gataro ka Tomasi. Arayitegereza yitonze.
P8	Nuko uwo mugore arayigura.
P9	Abandi bantu benshi baza aho Tomasi yari atanditse imineke. Bagura imineke ye baranayirya.
P10	Bidatinze agataro kaba karashize. Tomasi abara amafaranga yakoreye.
P11	Noneho Tomasi agura isabune, isukari n'umugati. Maze ashyira ibintu kugataro ke.
P12	Tomasi yikorera agataro ataha yishimye.
