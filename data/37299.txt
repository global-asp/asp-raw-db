title:	Ndo guda ngudo
author:	Doris  Manyamalala and Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37299.png
desc:	Musidzanyana u ṱoḓa u ḓivhesa musi a tshi vhona makhulu wawe vha tshi khou lugiselela u vhibvisa miomva.

page_text:

P1	Makhulu wanga vha a mpfuna.@@Vho mmbudza zwiphiri zwinzhi, nga nnḓa ha tshiphiri tshithihi fhedzi.
P2	Manngi na maṱari ndi zwa u ita mini?@@Makhulu vha ri, "Ndi zwa u ita vhuṱolo."
P3	Ndo vha ndi tshi ṱoḓa u vhona.@@Fhedzi makhulu vha nthuma uri ndi ite tshiṅwe tshithu.
P4	"Zwi ngafhi?"@@Makhulu vha ri, "Zwi fhethu hanga ha u ita vhuṱolo."
P5	Makhulu vho nthuma kamarani yavho.@@Ndo pfa hu tshi khou nukha miomva ine ya khou vhibva.
P6	Ndo hu wana fhethu ha makhulu ha u ita vhuṱolo.
P7	Ndo ḽa muthihi.@@Wo vha u muomva une wa ḓifha u fhira yoṱhe ye nda vhuya nda i ḽa.
P8	Nda dzhia miomva miṋa.@@Nda i dzumba nga fhasi ha rokho yanga.
P9	Nga ḓuvha ḽa u ya makete, makhulu vho dzhia zwiḽiwa u zwi rengisa.@@Ndo vha ndo shona zwihulu.
P10	Nga murahu, musi ndi tshi vhidzwa, ndo zwi ḓivha uri ndi ngani.@@A tho ngo tsha dovha nda tswa.
