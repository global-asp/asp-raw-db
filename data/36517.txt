page_text:

P1	Ŋǝ̂ ma Asim beebi. Asim a lee amabeebi na.
P2	N sɔlɔ nɩ páá, aɖʊwaa a tɩ lɛʊ baa tam apaŋa.
P3	N ta ŋǝ̂nǝ, amʊ n shire aŋgayɛ naa ayɛɛ Asim.
P4	N ta ŋǝ̂ nɩ, amʊ n taŋa 'aŋʊrʊ.
P5	N ta naa lâa sɔntɔ, amʊ n shee nɩ gboo.
P6	N ta naa kǝ̂m ɩbɔmbɔm, n tɩ shee Asim ʊɖɔŋkɔnɔ gboo.
P7	Ama Asim k'a na kǝm abɔmbɔm ayɔ na. N tɩ taba aŋgʊlɔ wàà, maakɔ laŋ na gǝŋ ?
P8	N tɩ yeeyee na gaka na piɖe ɩcaacaaca.
P9	N tɩ shee Asim ʊɖǝn, ama k'a na sala amʊ ayɔ na.
P10	N tɩ cɩ nɩ naa amaŋana a ye tɩ cɩ amʊ.
P11	N tɩ kara Asim 'ayɔkɔ ɩrana, aɖʊwaa n sɔlɔ bʊto bʊrana páá.
P12	N ta ɖâ atǝn, n tɩ ɖʊ nɩ amasul.
