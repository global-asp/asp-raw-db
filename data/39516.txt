title:	Yola and the rotten mango

page_text:

P1	Yola and Bisi were having a fight when they saw a mango tree with ripe fruits.
P2	Yola climbed the mango tree.
P3	He tried to pluck some mangoes but his hands couldn't reach them.
P4	Finally, he plucked one mango. Bisi asked Yola to come down before he hurts himself.
P5	Yola greedily ate the mango.
P6	He didn't know that the mango was rotten.  His stomach started to hurt.
