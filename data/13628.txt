title:	Bendera Yetu / Our Flag

artist:	Melany Pietersen

desc:	Hadithi hii katika lugha mbili inaelezea rangi ya bendera yetu.

page_text:

P1	Maziwa ni nyeupe. 


Milk is white.
P2	Tukisugua meno yetu, yanakuwa meupe zaidi. 


If we brush our teeth,  they become whiter.
P3	Nyasi ni za rangi ya kijani kibichi. 


The grass is green.
P4	Mti wa mwembe ni wa kijani kibichi. 


The mango tree is green.
P5	Damu yangu ni nyekundu. 


My blood is red.
P6	Damu yangu ni nyekundu kama nyanya. 


My blood is red like a tomato.
P7	Mama anakoka moto kutumia makaa meusi. 


Mother lights the stove with black charcoal.
P8	Nyeupe, kijani kibichi, nyekundu na nyeusi ni rangi za bendera yetu. 


White, green, red and black are colours of our flag.
