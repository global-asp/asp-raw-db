page_text:

P1	Umalume udla nabashana bakhe ababili uSipho no Sboniso badla isidlo sasemini.Umalume ubathuma kwa anti ukuba bahambise  ubhasikidi onesipho phakathi .Abashana badla baqeda bathatha ubhasikidi bahamba
P2	Usboniso no Sipho bahamba bexoxa endleleni bobabili.
P3	Bathe besahamba bezwa ngento ehoshozayo ,bathi uma bebheka babona imfene .uSboniso no sipho bamemeza becela ukusizwa
P4	Imfene yathi'lethani loyo bhasikidi noma ngizonidla .Abafana ababili banika imfene ubhasikidi bethukile.imfene yathatha ubhasikidi yawubamba i jabule ukuthi isayodla yona
P5	Kuthe kusenjalo kwagxuma inja yaluma imfene .Abafana bashaya izandla beyihleka lemfene .Inja yabaleka .Abafana base besaba ukuya ekhaya ngenxa youkuthi balahle inja
P6	Bahamba beya ekhaya bekhekile.uma befika batshrla umalume yonke into eyenzakalile.uMalume wacasuka wagana unwabu .kodwa ekugcineni wagcina exolile
