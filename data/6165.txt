title:	Inyang and the Bird

desc:	A story of a Turkana herds boy who spent many days hungry away from home.

page_text:

P1	Inyang was a herds boy.
P2	One day….
P3	… he was very hungry.
P4	He caught a bird..
P5	..and sent it home.
P6	The bird flew and flew.
P7	He landed on the hut.
P8	People heard the bird singing.
P9	Food was cooked.
P10	The food was served.
P11	Inyang thought about the food at home.
P12	He did not know that the cows had gone.
P13	Inyang imagined how he would be served the food.
P14	His father gathered the cows.
P15	Inyang reached home and ate enough food.
P16	Inyang was ready to lock up the cows for the night.
P17	The bird had saved Inyang!
