desc:	Kwana euriya atubwa

page_text:

P1	Arai kolong Ebuu ka Apoo ng'ipaalon luminan.
P2	Ayakar Apoo, esikiria keng ka itubwa niichi niareng'an.
P3	Arai nacha esakia Apoo atubwa naapolon, dio kilip Ebuu.
P4	Kitamu Ebuu ache kolong'it akilatar ng'iworui keng.
P5	Tama ng'esi ing'isi Apoo, "Yau atubwa nakang naapolon."
P6	"Auriu atubwa nakon itaok," tama niti ikobakini daang.
P7	Ache kwaar kilipaa Apoo atubwa nabo.
P8	Ani esakari Ebuu atubwa keng toriamu ekoi loarono.
P9	Tama Apoo, "Atona atubwa kon." Abu Ebuu tong'oo noi.
P10	Abu Ebuu tochunak ng'itiang luapolok daang.
P11	Tama Apoo, "Ng'iboro daang lueyarete, etuakete."
P12	Tonupa ng'itiang daang Apoo. Tolema Apoo ng'atubwae!
