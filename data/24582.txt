desc:	The story of Aminata

page_text:

P1	Aminata was born into a rich family. She was an only child and was loved.
P2	She never learned to do anything because she knew her parents were rich.
P3	For fifteen years, she got all the attention she wanted and everything else
P4	Then one day her parents had a car accident and died. Aminata wept.
P5	She went to live with her uncle who was not as rich as her father.
P6	She was very unhappy because she missed her parents.
P7	She refused to help with housework at home and slept all day, every day.
P8	She said she could not do housework because she was a rich man's daughter.
P9	Then one day her uncle ran out of patience and chased her from home.
P10	Aminata went to live on the streets and suffered.
P11	She wished she had not disrespected her uncle and regretted her pride.
