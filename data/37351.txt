page_text:

P1	Do you like nana? I do! Why? Oh well let me tell you why.
P2	Nana is yummy... I get nana from my mommy's breast
P3	Some get nana in bottles.
P4	I love nana because it has lots of flavors and it makes me big and strong!
P5	Do you like nana, because I do!
