desc:	Khaduyu makesi mangi. Khabachirisa Enjofu nende Emboko.

page_text:

P1	Khare khare, Akhaduyu khanyola Emboko nende Enjofu khumwalo.
P2	Mani nikhabateba wina ori naamani mangi.
P3	Mani Enjofu nende Emboko nichanja ofuuta nakho khachubia.
P4	Khamala nikhaboya Emboko nende Enjofu omukoye munda.
P5	Akhaduyu nakho nikhanina khumusala akulu.
P6	Emboko nende Enjofu chakhwesana otula echuri mpaka engolobe.
P7	Enjofu yari ijong'ere amatwi kademba sa.
P8	Emboko nende Enjofu chakhwesana paka omukoye kwakharikha. @@Emboko yajong'a mani niikona asi.
