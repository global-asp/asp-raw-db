page_text:

P1	dodo na mooni yo,kanino mo lee gu reepe ka tiimu myeel. ento myeli no petye pi lee weng.notye paa lee ma tumgi tyee kiken
P2	afwooyo no tyeka yupe me myeel. entoo uwo nyang ni myeel ennui pee per lee weng.no myeel pee lee manotye ki tumgi
P3	afwooyo ochero kamo kanya uwo knoo tum leemoo. ki uwo ruukwo yii wiyee yaakgaa uwo chero yi myeel
P4	yii myeek ennui afwooyo  uwo chamu uwo maaru ka mete who myeloo
P5	tim eni uwo weego afwooyo uwo poro ki uwo nino anina yo te cheing.ento en afwooyo ninoni tum lee mu rwuuwgo uwo pooro ki wiiye.
P6	chii lee weng gu nko ne afwooyo no pee tye lee ma niite. lee ni gu teelu eyiit afwooyo nakga owu kyagu meete ki diite
P7	kyii lee nyi gu reeyoo. ki enu uwo dugwo latam mu weegu afwoyo tye ki yire ma diiru
