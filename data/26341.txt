desc:	Kyabazinga ki anairaku nga kyabazinga Mpala aviireku?

page_text:

P1	Kyabazinga Mpala yali alikukairika.
Alironda atyakyabazinga anairaku?
P2	Kyabazinga mpala yayeta ebisolo ku mbaga.
Yayandha enteekateekadhe.
P3	Nga embaga egheire, Kyabazinga mpala yayogera.
Ebisolo byawulirisa.
P4	Yakoba, "Omwoleson gwidha kusalawo kyabazinga anairaku."
P5	Anaabala paka kwikumi nga eifumo likaali kugwa, naidha okuba kyabazinga.
P6	Endhovu yasindika ere bulu mulala, "Ndi omunene. nteekwa okuba kyabazinga."
P7	Empologoma yabala paka bwa kwina.
Eifumo lyagwa.
P8	Enkula yasindiika abandi, "Ndiw'awa'maanhi. Nteekwa okuba kyabazinga."
P9	Enkula yabala paka ku mukaaga eifumo lyagwa.
Yaaja.
P10	Buli mulala yakoba,"Empologoma n'Enkula ebinene bigwire. Ani ayinza okuba."
P11	Irike yabala, "Ndala! Ibiri! Isatu! Ina! Itaanu! Mukaaga! Musanvu!"
Yalira.
P12	Kyabazinga mpala yakoba, "Irike, osigaiza omukisa mulala." Irike yaaja.
P13	Ebisolo ebindi byatandiika okuja.
Awo, Empongo yaakoba, "kangezeeku."
P14	Kyabazinga mpala yaakoba, "Mughe Emongo omukisa. K'egezeeku."
P15	Empongo yaakoba, "Eitaanu n'eitaanu dhiba ikumi."
Eifumo lyagwa ghansi.
P16	Omwoleso gwalaga ekisolo ekisingamu obutono okuba Inh'ebisolo anairaku.
