desc:	Each of us has a wonderful body. But have you ever listened to what it tells you?

page_text:

P1	Leero sigenda kusomero Lunaku lw'a kuwumula.
P2	Leero sigenda kulaba TV. Tekuli masanyalaze.
P3	Ngenda kukola ki? Leelo ngenda kuwuliriza omubiri gwange.
P4	Okusookera ddala nina okusirika ennyo. Olwo ndyooke mpulirize omubiri gwange.
P5	Yee; kakati nsobola okuwulira bwe nzisa Mundawabwelu Munda. Wabwelu.
P6	Era nsobola okusa ennyo Ssssssssssssss! Era nsobola n'okusa empola Mmmmmm.
P7	Kakati nsobola okuwulira omutima gwange bwe gukuba. Doodom,  doodoom,  dooodooom.
P8	Nsobola okukubisa omutima gwange enyo? Yee,  bwe mbuuka buka emirundi amakumi abiiri.
P9	Kati laba omutima gwange gukuba nnyo. Doodom,  doodoom,  dooodooom!
P10	Era bwe nkwata ku mukono gwange mpulira entununsi.
P11	Era nsobola okwewulira nga nsekka Haha haha haaah haaa!
P12	Era n'okukaaba Boohoooohooo!
P13	N'okukuba mungalo Tapu tappa tapu tappa.
P14	N'okusingira ddala,  kati nsobola okuwulira okududuma mulubuto lwange Guddu guddu guddu.
P15	Nga lugamba nti; Ndiisa ndiisa.
P16	Era n'enyindo yange ewunyiriza akawoowo akava mufumbiro Maama gy'asikira.
P17	Kisanyusa okuwuliriza omubili gwange. Kakati njagala kuwulira emba zange nga bwezigaaya.
P18	Olubuto lwange lw'esunga obuggati obw'okya Maama bwakoze.
