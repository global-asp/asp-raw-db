page_text:

P1	Magezi nende Kasiira bamenyanga halala.

Khandi balitsanga halala.
P2	Magezi yasunga, "Khulimenje halala shinga khulitsanga halala."
P3	Magezi yasunjira Kasiira, "Ewe lima, nasi erache."
P4	Khandi Magezi yasunjira Kasiira, "Ewe raka, nasi ndalaka."
P5	Amabere kaangala.

Kasiira yasunjira Magezi okhuchesa.
P6	Magezi yakalusia, "Ewe chesa, nasi ndalabunga."
P7	Magezi yali omutofu.

Kasiira yamuwakho libere elalasa lionyene.
P8	Magezi yabukanana nende Ingokho.
P9	Ingokho yalia libere liatsana.

Yaheresia Magezi libuyu.
P10	Magezi yanyola abana nebabayanga.

Batisia libuyu elio.
P11	Abana baeresia Magezi liyembe.
P12	Magezi yanyola webotsa.

Webotsa yalia liyembe namuheresia libaa.
P13	Inyanza yabukula libaa.

Yamuheresia Magezi amatsi.
P14	Magezi yarumishira amatsi okhusumia omuliro okwa 
abasambi bamanda.
P15	Abasambi bamanda baheresia Magezi ihaywa.
P16	Magezi yanyola abasinzi.

Bafunaka ihaywa.
P17	Magezi yahebwa omurwe nende omushinga okwe Isurusi. 

Lola eshia yakhola!
P18	Yopa tsimbungu, "Isurusi yomwami ihandire mumatoyi."

Bwali obubeyi butswa.
P19	Abandu betsa okhumukhonya.

Magezi nabasunjira, "Khwese omurwe ne firenje."
P20	Magezi yabasunjira, "Mumbe ing'ombe yenyu embirire omwami."
