page_text:

P1	Tingi aleeikala naba@  mbuya wakwe.
P2	Aaleafwa bambuya wakwe@  ukusunga in’gombe.
P3	Bushiku bumo abashilika@  baishile.
P4	Baasendele In’ gombe no@  kuya nasho
P5	Tingi na bambuya wakwe baabutwike.
P6	Baabeleme mu mpanga mpaka bwaila.
P7	Elyo abashilika baishile nakabili.
P8	Bambuya baafishile Tingi@  panshi yamabula.
P9	Umo pa bashilika@  aabikile ulukasa palya pene pali Tingi, koma Tingi aali fye@  tondolo.
P10	Lintu abashilika baile,@  Tingi na bambuya wakwe bafisamwike.
P11	Baile ku n,ganda umwatalalila.
