page_text:

P1	Indlu le ise-Congo.@@Ngiyayithanda indlu le.
P2	Sinendlwana yethu yokuhlambela.@@Sinendlwana yethu yangasese.
P3	Sinetoni.@@Bengitjala etonini engeyami.
P4	Umma ungitjelile, "Umma ungifundise ukutjala. Njenganje mina sengifundisa wena."
P5	Ngitjale amatamati nesipinatjhi.@@Koke ngikwenze ngingedwa.
P6	Ngizakuthatha amatamati ngiyowapha umma.@@Uzakuthi, "Ngiyathokoza."
P7	Asinayo itoni lapha eSewula Afrika.
P8	Satjhiya indlu leyo e-Congo.
