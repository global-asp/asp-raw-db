page_text:

P1	Kwakas ani rorit nyee woo kumite kunolune tororyeet.
P2	Kumiite pensit kutoŋtoy.
P3	Kimiite cito nyo kisuute rorit kuŋolole.
P4	Kimakumiite neeto kukase aran.
P5	Kuryackey matakanik nyee minjat koromin.
P6	Kuco ampules akoo poolisek kyakukolyoyis.
P7	Kukwa matakanik ayeeny cee nyikisec kyakucut rorit akoo pensit.
P8	Akiite kot.
