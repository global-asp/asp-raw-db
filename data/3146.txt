desc:	On a very hot Saturday afternoon

page_text:

P1	Motseare oa mantsibua ka khoeli ea ts'itioe hone ho chesa haholoholo. Emong le emong o ne a sa thaba. 'Bontle mpho le Lerato ba khotha hotsoala kantle ho ea bapala, ' 'M'e oa bona are 'hobona,  hakebatle haleka bapalla tlasa maoto aka,  batsoela kantle hontlo.'.
P2	'Hare eng nokeng, ' Lerato a re ho pholile moo. 'Empa Mme a re reseke satole ka nokeng, ' ho rialo Botle. 'Harena hotola, ' ho araba Lerato,  're tlo bapala mono moreting pela metsi.'.
P3	Empa re bapala libeke. O chesang mosebetsi leha hole joalo. Pele noka moo retla rola lieta tsa rona pele. Empa hontse hochesa. Eaba re hlobola lihempe le mese ea rona,  Empa hontse hole joalo hoba hontse ho chesa.
P4	Eaba bakenya maotoa a bona ka nokeng hore a phoole. Ba hasana ka metsi. Ka mora nako e seng kae baiphumana base bale metsi hohle.
P5	'oh tloho retlo tola, ' ho rialo Mpho,  'ha ana hotseba Mme.' Batola,  baba batola ba qetelletse ba lebetse nako.
P6	Letsatsi laqala hoea malikelong,  be qala hohatsela. Liphahlo tsa rona li kae?
P7	Re li shebile tlasa lifate. Ra li sheba le limeleng tse ngata,  hohle re ile ra sheba.
P8	Pela noka moo,  honeng hoena le likhomo lintse li itjella joang bo bonolo. Bontle a sheba holimo,  'shebang khomo eane! Entse ejang?' 'Entse eja palesa e khubelu, ' horialo Lerato.
P9	A se palesa e khubelu, ' ho rialo Mpho ka bohale,  ke hempe ea hao!' Rasheba khomo engoe hape,  ba fumana entse eja ntho engoe e putsoa. 'Ke mose oaka, ' horialo Botle ka bohale.
P10	Baile baea ea hae,  ba te'ne mekoarela fela,  bahatsetse hoo ebileng ba thothomelang. Empa ene ese hobane ho pholile. 'Ke hobane likhomo tseno lijele liphahlo tsa bona, ' joale balla. Oile oa re kholoa Ntate? Kamora nako eseng kae eaba litono tsa rona lia oma,  empa line lisa ome hobane li omisoa ke letsatsi!
