P1	As the passing days fly by, Lexi asked for permission to go to the trip, "Dad, can I go to the trip! Please!!!!!" "Let me sign you up." Dad replied. "Yay I'm going! Yeah, oh yeah! Oh yeah!"
P2	"Hoopla Zoopla Poopla!" Screamed Lexi. She stood up and sneaked out of the house in the freezing Tuesday night, "Lexi? LEXI!! WHERE ARE YOU GOING!" "To the troublemaking terrible trip!" Jokes Lexi. "Ok, bye!" Appreciated Dad.
P3	They went to rob the dirty dusty and filthy uniform shop together until the police petrified their heart, "Hey, Get out of here!" Shouted the police team. "O-o-o-o-Ok?" Stuttered Lexi and Dad...
