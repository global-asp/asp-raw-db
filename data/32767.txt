title:	Alake o rata baeti
author:	Lucia Ntulanyane
artist:	Rasheedat Sadiq and Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Sesotho (South Africa)

desc:	Alake ke ngwanana ya ratehang ya ratang ho etelwa ebile o tseba ho etsa hore baeti ba ikutlwe ba amohelehile.

page_text:

P1	Kokoko, kokoko!@@Ho utlwahala modumo monyakong wa ka Pele.
P2	Alake o thabile haholo.@@"Ke mang ya kokotang?" o a botsa.@@"Mme, re na le moeti!"
P3	"Holokile," ho araba mmae. @@"E ka nna ya eba ke Mofumahadi Hassan. Ke kopa o mmulele."
P4	Alake o bula monyako ka pososelo. @@"Ke a o amohela mme, ke kopa o kene. Mme waka o sa le ka kamoreng, ke tla mmitsa."@@Mofumahadi Hassan o a bososela le yena.
P5	"Na nka o tlisetsa sa honwa?" ho botsa Alake.@@"Ee! ebile ke nyoruwe haholo," ho araba Mofumahadi Hassan.
P6	"Dumela, Mofumahadi Hassan," ho bua mme wa Alake. "Tshwarelo ka ho o emisa sebakanyana."@@"Tjhe! se tshwenyehe, motswalle, Alake o ntse a ontlhokometse."
P7	Mme wa Alake a bososela.@@"Ee! Alake ke kgosatsana e hlwahlwa e tsebang ho amohela baeti," MmaAlake o bolella Mofumahadi Hassan.
