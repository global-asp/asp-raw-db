title:	Tamara ayamba sukulu
author:	Ausward  Siwinda and Peter  Msaka
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Chichewa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34966.png
desc:	Kuyamba sukulu ndi chinthu chachikulu pa moyo wa mwana aliyense.

page_text:

P1	Tamara anali mtsikana wa zaka zisanu amene amakhala ndi banja la kwawo.@@Mudzi wawo unali pafupi ndi nkhalango yomwe munali nyama za kutchire.
P2	Tamara anali mwana womvera makolo ake.@@Amadyetsa nkhosa ndi mbuzi.
P3	Tamara amakonda kumvetsera kuyimba kwa mbalame pamene amadyetsa ziwetozo.@@Pamene mbalame zimayimba, Tamara amayika mawu ake m'mingoli ya mbalamezo.
P4	Tamara sanali wa msinkhu wopita ku sukulu. Makolo ake analola Nashipae ndi Tasieku kupita ku sukulu. @@Sukuluyi inali pa mtunda wa makilomita khumi.
P5	Nashipae ndi Tasieku amapita ndi ana anzawo a m'mudzi. Amapita m'mawa ndipo amabwerako madzulo.@@Amanyamula zakudya zawo m'makontena.
P6	Tamara anafunsa amayi ake, "Kusukulu ndidzapita liti?@@Amayi ake anayankha, "Upita posachedwa apa, mwana wanga."
P7	Tsiku lina, kunafika amfumu ndi amayi awiri pa galimoto.@@Amfumuwa amaonetsetsa kuti ana akupita ku sukulu pa msinkhu wabwino.
P8	Anthu amakhulupirira kuti ngati mwana watambalitsa dzanja lake pa mutu ndi kugwira khutu, anali woyenera kuyamba sukulu.
P9	Amfumu anafunsa, "Uli ndi zaka zingati?" Tamara anayankha, "Ndili ndi zaka zisanu." @@"Tiyeni tiwone ngati ungayambe kupita ku sukulu," amfumu anatero.
P10	Mzimayi m'modzi anagwira dzanja la Tamara la ku manja ndi kulitambalitsa pa mutu wake.@@Tamara anagwira khutu la ku manzere.
P11	Tamara azipita ku sukulu yosiyana ndi ya Nashipae ndi Tasieku.@@Inali sukulu yatsopano ndiponso ya pafupi ndi nyumba yawo.
P12	Tamara anasangalala kwambiri.@@"Ndiziwerenga ndi kulemba ngati Nashipae ndi Tasieku." Anaganiza motero.
