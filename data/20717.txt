title:	Wohe, Ngebhodwe Lami!
author:	Pumla Mdontswa
artist:	Brian Wambi
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20717.png
desc:	Ibhodwe lika Nontando lephukile

page_text:

P1	UNontando unabafowabo abayisithupha.
P2	Abazali bakhe sebemuphe ibhojwana. Ulikhonzile leli bhodwe.
P3	Nakhu ngelinye ilanga abafowabo sebeliphula leli bhodwe.
P4	Wakhathazeka. Akaliboni ibhodwe, uyacinga akalitholi. Wakhala, wakhala.
P5	Nanguya ephuma ekhaya. Nanguya ecaca isihlahla eside.
P6	Baphuma abazali bakhe beyomfuna, bamthola. "Sicela wehle lapho," kusho abazali bakhe.
P7	Nabafowabo bayamncenga, bayamculela, "Sicela wehle lapho."
P8	Kodwa uNontando wahlekisa ngeculo labafowabo, akangabanaka.
P9	Emva kwabafowabo, kwafika umngane kaNontando. Naye wacula lona lelo culo, "Ngicela wehle lapho."
P10	Umngane wakhe wacula waze wehla uNontando esihlahleni.
P11	Abangani bahamba bephindela ekhaya.
P12	Wonke umuntu ekhaya wajabula ukuthi uNontando ubuyile. UNontando wathola ibhodwe elisha!
