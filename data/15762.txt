title:	Jakur Gi Kakuro Nythindho Manyakare
author:	Owino Ogot and Athieno Gertrude
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Dhopadhola

desc:	Kigana ma mako kwoŋ nythindhi ogwange ma obothi jo to jotundo kama ikuro iye kic.

page_text:

P1	Me kigana ma mako kwoŋ Jakur gi kakuro nyithindhi ogwange ma kic.
P2	Dwe m'orumo tic odit ri Jakur kodi jokony pere i kakuro kic. Nyaithe ma jaoro acel am'otelo tundo. Okel go i lore m'oti ma nyabokependi.
P3	Jakur gi jokony pere jowinjo malith swa ri nyathi Nyaithe. Oyido mok aka ni gi rembo. To i hongo macek Nyaithe onwaŋo meni. Ocako tuko gi ogwange man.
P4	Ndelo man elikopita opie kam'ikuro iye nyithindhi ogwange ma kic.
P5	I elikopita no oyido nitie nyathi liec ma thin m'oum gi bilangit madwoŋ.
P6	Obute oyido oum gi bilangit. Oyido ja ndelo abic nyuka nywol. Jakur omiyo go cemo i cupa.
P7	Tundo pa Akor ocewo ji je wor moro acel.
P8	Akor oyido ja dwe abic. Oyido ool swa ru woth mabor.
P9	Jakur oidho gi cupa malo aka nyaka opidho Akor ru ŋute mabor swa.
P10	Kic ma jo tundo macowe i dwe m'orumo no obedo Akwile,  Acuta kodi Amwonya. Nythindhi simbwor me oyido jo tieko sabiti ario.
P11	Jakur gi jokony pere jomaro ogwange je,  kada ma kiriwidhi.
P12	Ji ma kakuro nythindhi ogwange jo tiyo gi men. Jo geno ni ndir moro kic no wegi jo le nyalo kurok. Aka nyaka jo nyali gik i dudi.
