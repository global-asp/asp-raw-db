page_text:

P1	Sipho yindvodza lebukene netinkinga emntini wayo. Libito lankkhosikati wakhe ngu Lomasontfo,  banebantfwana labili. Emabito abo nguMusa Kanye naNikiwe. Sipho ubonakala akhatsatekile, abalisa ngetinkinga temuti wakhe. Akati atsatseni ayihlanganise nani.
P2	Utsite asajulile ngemicabango. Wetfuka wafa nekeva livi likhuluma naye khona emuva kweSihlahla labehleti kuso. Nakatsi uyabaleka ngekwetfuka,  weve kukhuluma Sihlahla cobo "Hhawu Sipho wetfuka kangaka?ngingakusita kucazulula lenginga lonayo emtini wakho, ngiyati nekutsi indlu yakho ayinalo neluphahla lwemazenke. Timvula tidlala ngani.''."
P3	Ngitihlalele ngedvwa lapha bengingakalindzeli kutsi kungaba khona longakhuluma nami. Ngiyakutfokotela kungenelela kwakho mina ngingu Sipho ngitsandza kulahlekelwa ngumsebenti, simo sasekhayilami sibi kakhulu. Bantfwana bami abasakhoni nekuya esikolweni ngobe angiphumeleli kubakhokhela imaili yesigitjelwa lesibayisa esikolweni, indlu lengiyi yakile sengihluleka kuyifulela ngenca yekweswela kwemali imphilo imphela imatima.
P4	Ungakhatsateki ndvodza,  ngitakupha emagala ami kutsi uyofulela ngawo indlu yakho. Kodvwa kumele wente sitsembiso,  sekutsi njalo emini uyangivakashela ngobe sekute muntfu lotawungitsandza ngingana magala. Sipho wasitsembisa sihlahla kutsi njalo utawuhlala asivakashela atogcisa situnge naso.
P5	Emva kwemalanga lambalwa,  Sipho nemndeni wakhe babamba longentasi nabatfola kutsi sihlahla sewunemagala lamashe lamakhulu Kanye nemtfunti. Bantfwana baSipho bona bakutfokotela kugibela badlale kuSihlahla batali babo bona baticubekela bacoca naSihlahla.
