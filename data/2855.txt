title:	Nditu Na Mbũthũ
author:	Anna Kula
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2855.png
desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	Nĩ kyaũ kĩla kĩlitu,  Ngũkũ kana ĩvw'ĩa?
P2	Nĩ kyaũ kĩla kĩvũthũ,  Nĩ ĩlaa kana nĩ kĩlikyo?
P3	Nĩ kaana keva kalitu?
P4	Nũũ ũla mũlitu,  nzou kana nau?
P5	Ni kyaũ kĩla kĩlitu,  nĩ kilo kya maviaĩ.
P6	Kana ngulamu ya simiti?
P7	Ni kyaũ kĩla kĩlitu,  ni kilo kya sukalĩĩ.
P8	 Kana ni kilo kya mavw'ĩa?
