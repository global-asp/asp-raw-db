page_text:

P1	Libito lami nginguThabo ngivame kucitsa sikhatsi sami lesinyenti engadzeni yami.
P2	Thabo ucele batali bakhe kutsi bamutsengele emathulusi ekusebenta engadzeni. Thabo uyatitsandza timbali,  tihlahla Kanye netjani lobujutjwe kahle.
P3	Thabo uye avuke ekuseni ageze buso Kanye nematinyo,  ngembi kwekutsi ayonisela engadzeni.
P4	Ngalesinye sikhatsi uye acele dzadze wabo lomncane Busi kutsi amsite. Busi uyakutsandza kunisela titjalo Kanye nenja yakhe lengu Bhobi.
P5	Thabo ute nebangani bakhe boNomsa naDora engadzeni. Phela bekafuna kuhlola kutsi setiyamila yini letitjalo tenhlanyelo lekayitsengelwe batali bakhe. BoNomsa naDora bona bajabulela kudlala ngetimbali.
P6	Thabo ujabule kakhulu ngoba titjalo takhe setikhulile katsi netimbali setigcamile ngemibalabala,  leyehlukene.
P7	BoThabo nebangani bakhe bagijimela engadzeni ngalesikhatsi babona umushi wenkhosatane,  phela umushi wenkhosatane uye uvele nangabe imvula beyina. Â Bonkhe bantfwana bajabulile ngebuhle bengadze yaThabo nekubona umushi wenkhosatane.
