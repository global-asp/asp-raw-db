desc:	This story is about Sara who wants to go to school.

This story was child created  during the ABS story-making workshop hosted by UNICEF under the FCDO funded GEP3 programme;as part of the 2020 IDGC celebrations in Borno State.

page_text:

P1	My name is Sara, l live in Baga village with my mother. I am 10 years old. My  father died  when  I was 6 months old.
P2	One day after dinner, I asked  my mother why I was not going  to school like my friends.
P3	Hmm! "Sara you know we are poor and I can't afford to send you to  school but I promise to talk to your uncle to allow you stay with him and go to school."
P4	Hip, hip,hip, hurray! My uncle agreed. I now attend a primary school in the city of Maiduguri.
P5	After years of studies, Sara  became a medical doctor and  was welcomed home by her mother and the community.
