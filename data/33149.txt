desc:	A story about Jesus and some children

page_text:

P1	People were bringing little children to Jesus for him to touch.
P2	The disciples scolded those who brought them.
P3	Jesus was indignant.
P4	"Bring them here to me," Jesus said.
P5	"Whoever does not receive the kingdom of God like a child will never enter it."
P6	He took the children in his arms, placed his hands on them and blessed them.
