desc:	Enyungu eyi Natabo eyakukunda munonga yayatika

page_text:

P1	Obwakale nakale, haka hariho omshaija na omukazi we. Bakaba beine aboojo mukaaga na omwishiki omwe.
omwishiki bakaba nibamweta Natabo. Abazire bona bakaba nibakunda Natabo kukila aboojo.
P2	Abazire bakakora akayungu kakye ka muhara wabo.Natabo akaba nakunda akayungu ke.
Akaba nakunda kukazanisa.
P3	Izoba rimwe aboojo bakata enyungu ya Natabo. Ekatika omubukyweka.
Bakulube bakaba batakumanya akyo kukola. Bakakwata obushatuka bwe enyungu babunaga.
P4	Natabo akaronda enyungu ye konka taragifunire.Akanyiga, yarira kandi yayanga kurya ekitu kyona.
Akeshenjeleza taata we, maama na bakulube kuronda enyungu. Bakagiira ngu tibakumanya enyungu aheri.
P5	Natabo akayigira kimwe. Akiruka yaruga omuka yaza omukishaka. Haza yatemba omutii omulingwa munoga.
P6	Abazire bakaronda muhara wabo. Kuhahwereho ashaha, bamushanga omumuti omulingwa omu kishaka. 
Baka mugabira kushuma konka yayanga. Bamugabia kushuma konka yayangira kimwe.
P7	Bayanya ba Natabo bakerundana ahansi yomuti.Batandika kwesongora bakwenda Natobo asume. 
Ekyesongolo nikigenda kiti: "Macura,  shuma ahasi haza tugende omuka. Nitukufuna enyungu ensya."
P8	Natabo nawe akeshongora alikutolenza banyanya.
P9	Natabo akangakuruga omumuti. Haza mumwani we kuyizire, Natabo yashuma mpola.
yatandika kuweshongora ati,'Mumwani, shuma kandi tugende omuka, nitukufuna enyungu ansya."
P10	Natobo kuyrahuriire iraka ya munywaniiwe, yatadika kushuma ahasi. Munywaniiwe akagumizamu nayeshongora mpaka Natabo ahiiya hikile ahasi.
P11	Natabo na munywaniiwe bakashemererwa kandi bayegwa omukyifuba. Batambula mpola mpola bakugaruka omuka owa Natabo.
P12	Abaishiki kubahikiire omuuka, abanynya na Natabo na abazire be bakashemerwa. Bakatebekanisa obugyenyi, kandi bayeta abanwani na abayambuzare.
Buriomwe akashemelewa aha bugyenyi.okukilayo Natabo hamwe ne enyungu ye ensya.
