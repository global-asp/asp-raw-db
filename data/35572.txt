desc:	Aisha does not go to school. Her friend begs her uncle to allow her go to school.

page_text:

P1	I love my parents and my siblings.They help me to to school.
P2	My friend is Aisha. Her parents do not allow her go to school.
P3	I went to her uncle and begged him, "Please, allow Aisha to go to school with me."
P4	Her uncle said, "Because you're a good girl, I will allow her to follow you."
P5	Aisha and I are now in Primary Three. We can read and write well.
P6	We are the best students in my class. We also do things together.
P7	Yesterday Aisha wrote a letter to her uncle. She thanked her for allowing her go to school.
P8	The uncle was so happy. He promised to her her until she finishes university.
