page_text:

P1	My mother was talking on the phone.
P2	She heard that my grandmother was dead. @@She was crying so much.
P3	I was feeling so, so, so, sad. @@We all started crying. @@Grandmother was good to us, when no one else was good to us.
P4	All her children came to put her under the sand. @@We started to pray. @@Nothing must happen to the rest of our family.
