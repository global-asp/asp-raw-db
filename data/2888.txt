desc:	I describe my different feelings at different times and with different people.

page_text:

P1	Ma asi ni e`yo karakara etumu.
P2	Ma idi ka amani o`duko olu ondresi ayiko ni ma fu tu.
P3	Aka avi ma agiyi be ma e`da mani azaza ru.
P4	Ma ati ka `yo `i vu sende yo,  ma eri onzi.
P5	Ma andri ka ma o`de ma etumu kini `ba le ma ra.
