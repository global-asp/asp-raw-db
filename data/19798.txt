desc:	Abakhaana babiri ababukha batsia tsichendo okhukhaba fiokhulia. Bosi babiri baakana omushiere khunjira alali mulala khubo niye wiyama okhukhonya omushiere uyo.

page_text:

P1	Ababusi ba Akhabere nende Nechesa bali bafwa.
P2	Akhabere yali wo bukhonyi. Nechesa yali we isunga.
P3	Inyanga ndala,  Akhabere nende Nechesa batsia khukhaba fiokhulia.
P4	Nashikawa yali omushiere omuhani wo buyiinda. Omubiri kwe nikwetsula khwo amakakha.
P5	Nechesa yaakana no Nashikawa. Namulangisia,  "Omukhana wanje, orie? Orula ena? Otsia ena?"
P6	Nashikawa yaboola, "Itsa unjinge ne ndalakhwechesia shiolakhola." Nechesa yalolesia omukhasi obufuru.
P7	Nashikawa namuboolera abe noluchendo olulayi.
P8	Nechesa yali omuchonyu. Olwa yekhala hasi okhuulukha, efikoko fianza okhumuluma.
P9	Akhabere yesi yaakana Nashikawa. Nashikawa yamureeba amarebo malala kalia.
P10	Akhabere yafuchirira okhuchinga Nashikawa. Nashikawa namuboolera akalukhe nango, owalanyoola buli shindu shiayenya.
P11	Akhabere akhupira Nashikawa orio. Yakalukha mungo ne nanyoola buli shindu.
P12	Habula yekhuula okhukosia Nechesa.
