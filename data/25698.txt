desc:	John omulenzi omulugi yafa siliimu olwobweenzi.

page_text:

P1	jonh yali omulenzi asinga bulungi ate amaniikwa ku kyaalo.
P2	y'egomba buli mughala mu kyaalo ate bamweendha ihno
P3	ya gya kwiilwaaliro era bakamuteema nti alina akawuka ka sirimu.
P4	john y'elalikirira olw'obulamu bwe ate kibumba yamukola nga mulungi
P5	okwiirira kwa john kwamuletera okuloghoza ku nkomerero y'obolamu bwe.
P6	mukisera kitono obulungi bwa john byaatwaalibwa olw'obulwaiile
P7	munaaku ntono,john yafa era yazikiibwa na b'enhumba ye bonka
