title:	Ɨ̀ Suyn yem, mbàynɨ̀ yem
author:	Yoh Edelquin Nabi

lang:	Kom

desc:	Tabo ti ku suyn ŋêyn wayndà ɨ̀kùŋ ɨ̀ tàlɨnɨ. Awo ti kà a kɨ kfɨnɨ sɨ-a njùŋ à?

page_text:

P1	À n- læ meyn nà ghɨ ɨ̀tu' fɨ̀li, a ntè' Mfalele, wayndà ɨ̀kùŋ ɨ̀lvɨ̀ læ nà ghɨ, chi ŋêyn bò ŋweyn nɨ̀ nà ŋweyn.
P2	Wayndà ɨ̀kùŋ nâ wêyn læ nà gvɨmlɨ̀ bò ŋweyn ŋêyn nà ŋweyn, nɨ̀ ghelɨ ghɨ̀li a ntè' nâ yè. @@Izɨyn i ŋweyni læ nà ghɨ Tabo Maloba.
P3	Achi-a ka sɨ-a ɨ̀we, ta kfaŋ nɨn toŋtɨ na 'Mondèy', læ nà ghɨ̀ àchi a ntè' ta ghelɨ læ nà ghàblɨ̀-a, sɨ ba'tɨ i woynda na ghɨ kasɨ se'a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀, sɨ ibàmi mɨ̀chi ɨ̀n yvɨ̀tɨnɨ̀.@@Ɨtu layn achi alya, Tabo se sɨ se' a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀, ɨ kòynɨ̀ ŋêyn Sbàks. Àŋena nà bèsɨ̀ ndu nɨ̀ àwo a dzɨ, a ti ɨ ku suyn.
P4	Sbàks læ nà nù chwòsɨ̀-a, fu nge' sɨ woyn ghɨli fi le' ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀. Wù læ nà ghɨ wayn ɨ̀ bɨ, dɨm mbè' fì kôŋ sɨ nà dù'ì ŋêyn ghelɨ ghɨ bɨ. @@Kɨ̀ silæ silæ, Sbàks ɨ tutɨ Tabo. Tabo nà kòŋ wì sɨ̀ nà kè' ta ànkfês, ɨ zɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ nà chî nchînɨ̀ i bɨ̀ ta Sbàks.
P5	Tabo ɨ̀ zɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ̀ nà nyàblɨ̀ nɨ̀ woynda, bu fì gvɨmlɨ̀ wì bò ŋweyn ŋêyn nà ŋweyn nɨ̀ ghelɨ a dzɨ àfi a wù læ nà nì sɨ asɨ. @@Wu bêynlɨ̀ sa wul ɨ̀ mɨ̀lù'-ɨ̀-nyvɨnɨ̀, nà bu fì kelɨ̀ wi ɨ̀lvɨ̀ sɨ̂ ngêŋ ŋweyn.
P6	Tabo ɨ mà'ì ngêŋ ŋweyn nà bɨ̀m chî kɨ chi, nfamasɨ ku ŋweyn. Wu yum nô sɨ a ŋaŋ, Sbàks ɨ zɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ nà chye' ɨ ŋweyn, bu fì kòŋ wì sɨ̀ nà ghɨ̀ suyn ŋweyn. @@Bò Tabo ŋêyn nà ŋweyn ɨ̀ tò'tɨ̀ ŋweyn na wù ndu a ndô ngàŋ a ntè' na yè, ta ka wù keli afɨ. Wu zɨ̀tɨ̀ nà nyvɨ afɨ a ŋweyna, nà sɨ boynɨ-à.
P7	Tabo ɨ kasi kfà'tɨ̀ ichi i ŋweyni. Wu yeyn keli na wu læ ku suyn ŋêyn mbàynɨ̀ ŋweyn. @@Wu kasi zɨ̀tɨ̀ sɨ̀ nà se' a ndô ŋwà'lɨ̀, ɨ loyn ìlêsɨ̀ ifu sɨ bò ŋweyn ŋêyn nà ŋweyn, nɨ̀ ndyèynsɨ̀sɨ̀.
P8	Iwumi i ku Sbàks tèyn ta Tabo sɨ kelɨ̀ nchînɨ̀ ìjùŋ. @@Nô ŋweyn ɨ yeyn keli ìnkì I nchînɨ̀  zɨ̀ a wù ti na kelɨ̀ ɨ kfatɨ̀ sɨ kfɨnɨ sɨ nà ghɨ wul ɨ̀ jùŋ.
