desc:	Ityô yam Ker gbenda sha mbamzeiyol vev. Ijogh-zwa hemba.

page_text:

P1	Ior mba hen gar wase lu a mbamzeiyol wue-wue. Se gbidye gôgônongo u kesen mngerem shin ijôr.
P2	Ijen gba je se yar a yar ikyoo.
P3	Mbaiv ngee zan zan se gema cian imôngo i teman kpaa.
P4	Mbayev asev de zan makeranta sha ikyaa i kwata.
P5	Agum a ukase ase yem tom gbaga ken agar a gen.
P6	Agumaior a gen yem ijodo ken ishule i ior, mbagen ve gbidye dar gbem tugh mbu ile.
P7	Ajiir ase lu côv côv, ihyôghnov mbi purugh hanma ijiir.
P8	Mbayev ve lu vihin iyol sha akpe a hemben a a samber hanma ijiir yô.
P9	Iyange igen yô ijôr uma ta ikyembegh, imura kôr hanma or.
P10	Terem due zende sha uya uya lôhô ior cii mtem.
P11	Ikpilaior zua tema shin itine akinde, kegh ato aa na.
P12	Terem mough sha kaa er, "Hingir hange hange u se lu ken mzough sha u been mbamzeiyol asev."
P13	Terwase, wanye u anyom anieni, u tema sha kon kaa er, "Mo me wase u esen ihyôghnov."
P14	Kwase u gen yôô er, "Kasev mba ve soo yô ve kohol me, se kôr ihyumbe i yaren ken ityev."
P15	Nomsoor u gen kaa er, "Se nomso se time azôr."
P16	Se cii se ger imo imôm er, "Se sôr ajiir a ase man u uma asev kpaa."
