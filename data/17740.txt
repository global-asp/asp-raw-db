desc:	Fire tells its own story.

page_text:

P1	Ngivumele ngikudembele indaba yami. Mina ngithoma ngingelincani ilangabe.
P2	Mina ngigijima emangweni lokha ummoya ungithwala.
P3	Sibangani abahle, wena nami.
P4	Mina ngipheka ukudla kwakho.
P5	Mina ngikhanyisa ubusuku bakho.
P6	Mina nginamaqhinga nemilingo eminengi.
P7	Mina ngiyingcenye yomndeni wakho.
P8	Kodwa yelela ngesikhatheso ungimemela emzini wakho. Mina angithandi bona ungitjhiye ngedwa.
