desc:	People want to be professionals at football but all they need to succeed in there dream is practice 

page_text:

P1	Good day, if you want to become a footballer you must study and be taught these are the people that want to learn football they are listening to their teacher ,soon when they grow up they will become great footballers .
P2	Now they have listened to what there coach was saying they want to follow it .so they practice so one day there practice will come to past and not in vain .
P3	Now they are ready to play against teams and are ready for there uniforms and they are proud .
P4	They have tried now they are tired and they are listening again and they will try harder.
P5	They are happy and are full with excitement soon enough they became Professionals@The end
