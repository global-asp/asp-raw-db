P1	Billie is a 16 year old boy in high school. He loves dogs so much and he BEGGS his mom everyday for one, but his mom always says "No maybe another time." But he always says to himself "I'll get one soon!" "I hope."
P2	One day after school his mom told him "We have exiting news!" "What is it?" He excitingly  screemed. "We are going to get a dog!" His mom said. "OMG!" He said. So his mom had him get ready and they both got I the car and starting driving.
P3	Well they were in the car Billie asked "What kind of dog and what's it's name?" Mom said that she was not shure yet. They arrived at the pet shop and went in. The manager asked "What are you looking for?"
P4	"A DOG!" Billie said happily. The manager shook yes and told them "Follow me." When they got to the dog shelter Billie started surching for the perfect dog. There was so many colors! But then...
P5	Billie heard a cry coming from a small white dog. He looked at the dog and told his mom " This one it looks sad!" "Ok" Mom said they bought the dog and went to the store. They bought a color an food. Then they went home! Billie is so happy!
