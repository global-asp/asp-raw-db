desc:	A child tells of how his grandmother told them stories before they went to bed.

page_text:

P1	Grandmother began, “Hare and Elephant were neighbours and friends.”
P2	Hare liked mushrooms but he was too lazy to farm.
P3	There were no mushrooms. 

Hare began to steal Elephant’s bananas.
P4	Elephant sang, “I will catch the thief!” 

Hare was afraid.
P5	Monkey watched the farm but Hare did not steal.
P6	Elephant went to check. 

Hare hid in the pumpkin leaves.
P7	Elephant looked everywhere, but he did not find the thief.
P8	Another day, Hare escaped inside a big pumpkin.
P9	Elephant saw the big pumpkin and swallowed it.
P10	The pumpkin moved in Elephant’s stomach.
P11	Elephant spat out the pumpkin. 

Hare ran away.
P12	Grandmother said, "And that is the end of the story."
