desc:	A young shepherd is saved from danger by his best friend. Find out how it happened.

page_text:

P1	Tarik and his family lived far away from the capital city.
P2	Tarik’s dog was Buchie.

Tarik and Buchie were great friends.
P3	One Saturday, Tarik and Bedilu took the animals to graze.
P4	Bedilu wanted to play but Tarik wanted to read.
P5	In the end, Bedilu and Tarik played.
P6	As they played, their sheep disappeared.

The boys searched for them.
P7	A wolf killed Tarik’s sheep. 

Tarik feared his father’s punishment.
P8	Tarik decided to sleep in a tree. 

Buchie slept nearby.
P9	At night, hyenas made loud noises. 

Tarik was afraid.
P10	Tarik fell down from the tree. 

He heard noises near him.
P11	Lightning lit the sky. 

Tarik saw Buchie protecting him from hyenas!
P12	Buchie saved Tarik. 

Tarik and Buchie stayed in the tree until morning.
