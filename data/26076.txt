title:	Ekikere kyasunire kitya olususu lwakyo
author:	Kyambogo  University and Mpala Paul
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Lulamoogi

lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Omukugu ono ainyonyola lwaki ebikere birina emigongo emifuduki era bisobola okubba aghabula maizi.

page_text:

P1	Ogamufudu ekikere yabbaire akooghere okubba omumaizi.
P2	''Wegenderezye,'' ofudu nakoba.''omubiri gwo gwiza kukala.''
P3	Aye ogamufudu yazwire okumulamwa. Yaloghoozerye kuzwawo.
P4	Nikiringa linga.
P5	Kyayagiirye olukaaniavu olwa langi ya nakassoma okumbali kwa maizi.
P6	Okaaniavu yatandiikire okucuuka mu pinki! Ogamufudu yatuumire naazwagho.
P7	Tete yabweine ompiti ng'ali kukuma omusyo.
P8	Omusyo gwayakire paka gya Gamufudu. Yatiiremu.
P9	Omusyo gwa yokyerye omubiri gwe. Yatuumire kyeyuma, paka mu maizi.
P10	Aye omubiri gwe gwali gumalire okwizula ebibuubuuto.
P11	Ogamufudu yaagiirye amaka omwibbaale.Olususu lwe nga lukalu era nga lugumu.
P12	Buti obulenga bwonka niibwo obulina olususu olugondi era bubba mu maizi.
