page_text:

P1	On that fateful day, my father was performing ablution.@@It was while he was doing this that he heard the gun shots.
P2	Fear gripped us. @@My parents didn't know whether we should run away or hide in the house.
P3	The gun shots increased in number.@@ We shook in fear.
P4	My father, mother, brother and l were very scared.
P5	Then we saw the terrorists!
P6	They entered our house and grabbed my brother.@@We were all the more scared!
P7	We were shocked when one of them brought out a knife and slaughtered him before our eyes.
P8	Sunday January 7th 2018, still remains one of my most painful memories.@@It is always difficult to remember the day my brother died.
