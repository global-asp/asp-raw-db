desc:	Erai estore nna apese nebakai maria nne liling osomai nnoi.

page_text:

P1	Ekwekyunene maria tapruchu aiswag ikelai, onapakini euniform, omatari echayi keda alositte osomero.
P2	Kosomero, empyeyi maria akilasi keda ike papero. oputari aike bawo olayi.
P3	Akilasi ah maria nna. ake mwalimu ngesi Mrs Marion. elayette epokesho ngesi emina maria nnoi.
P4	Maria nges akilasi monitor. Nges eyagunnene etabon anyoun ko offisi nne jasi iduwe aishom.
P5	Erai esawa nna aimat echayi. Maria keda ike papero enjasi oisawe ebulyaitte emupira. Ejok abulya gin akuan ituet.
P6	Erai kwani esawa ni nyamat. Maria keda ike paperi opotu obongosi ore. Ore Maria egaranikini akewuryat eboro nwo togo.
P7	Ko ore, Maria keda ike itunga eboyette oh diro onyamette inyamat edautu ailip keda akes mojong
P8	Emina Maria aitila etogo keda ailot asanin nwa lunch. Emina Maria atte esomai keda amanin awanyun ebe elai efibiro keda ebe emamette inchuch.
P9	Maria keda onakye kke etinyamette akes aituk keda inyamat nwe peritos. Esomaitte kes nnu emamei imbore esomaite kesi
P10	Erai kwani kware, gin tuwan epasit osuti bala ekotosi kesi ajjo. Maria keda onaki ejasi agola kes oyapyeitte ajjo.
