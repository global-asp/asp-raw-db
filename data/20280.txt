page_text:

P1	Habayeho umugabo wari ufite imbwa imwe.
P2	Yakundaga imbwa ye cyane.
P3	Imbwa yarakoraga cyane kuko yanayitumaga ku isoko kugura inyama.
P4	Yavomaga amazi ku iriba.
P5	Yarindaga ibintu byose byo mu rugo.
P6	Umunsi umwe yashimiye imbwa ye mu buryo budasanzwe kuko yamubereye indahemuka.
P7	Imbwa yarishimye ndetse ikomeza gukora cyane.
