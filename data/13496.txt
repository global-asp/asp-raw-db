page_text:

P1	One day I met Agnes. Agnes is a teacher.
P2	Agnes uses crutches to walk.
P3	When I went to town,  I saw Metobo. He is a radio DJ.
P4	Metobo has albinism. He has problems with his eyes and with his skin.
P5	My neighbour is Moraa. She is a farmer.
P6	Moraa has a problem with hearing. She speaks using Sign language.
P7	Osero is the best soccer player in my school. I like to watch him play.
P8	Osero lost his hands in an accident. However,  he scores many goals for his team!
P9	My sister Jackie plays sports.  She is stronger than my brother!
P10	I know that people with disabilities can do anything.
P11	Every person has different abilities.
P12	We can all work,  play and have fun!
