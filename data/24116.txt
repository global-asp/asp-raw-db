title:	winyu ki gweno

desc:	Kyoch eni wajo wa pingo gweno bedo ki dano dak pingo winyu kyamo lutti gweno konenogi ka gitye katuku.

page_text:

P1	ninu moni. winyu ki gweno nogityee lurem maber
P2	nogituku kichelu. nogituku ki le mukene kyalo olik.
P3	nogityee ki dyangi madwong, dyeel, romo ki lee mukene.
nogimaro tuku ka' cheelu idibaa.
P4	nin achel gweno ogamo lyedi pa winyu ka lyello yeewi lutino ne. winyu pokwero kamino ne ire
P5	gweno olyero yee lutino ne maber. ento wiyeee owil kadwoko lyeedi ka winyu
P6	ma winyu obino kagamo lyedi ne, gweno norwenyo lyedi ni dong. otemo yenyo ne kaka weny ento nopitwere. winyu otany ki daa ki okyako kachamo lutino pa gweno.
P7	gweno ongwegyo pi kwo ne ki ochako kabedo ikin dano kikume. pakaa kyawani gweno bedo ki dano ki winyu kyamo lutino pa gweno.
