desc:	Ekyika ekiri kukora hamwe

page_text:

P1	Abaana nibimuka kale kuyabagana okwetereza kuza ahisomero kwenda batakyerererwa.
P2	Nibatabura hamwe kuza aha ishomero kweda bayabagane kuhakugira owafuna obuvune omumuhada rwabo bakutabura.
P3	Nibazana hamwe ahakire kokuhumura ho obwe bakukora emikago emirungi.
P4	Kumwagaruke omuka,muyabe abazire emirimo ya ahaka kweda bamare kale.
P5	Mubaze musabe rero mubone kurya ebokurya ika ekika kandi mugabire abazire ebwa ahisomero nebwa abasomesa babasomesa.
P6	Mutebwa kusaba ika ekyika,ahakuba ekyika ekikusaba hamwe, nikiguma hamwe.
