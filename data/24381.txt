desc:	The story of a woman who lost her husbands money in the market.

page_text:

P1	Wata mata ta je kasuwa, ta yarda kuɗin mijinta. Matar ta damu ƙwarai.
P2	Ta koma gida domin ta gayawa mijinta. Shi kuwa ya ce bai yarda ba
P3	Matar ta ce "Me zai sa ba zaka yarda ba ka haƙura saboda amanar auren mu?"
P4	Sai mijin ya ce "ba zan yarda ba, sai kin biyani kuɗi na"
P5	"Ki je ki nemo mani kuɗi na." Ya ce mata.
P6	Sai matar ta ce "to, kashe ni mana." Sai ya ce "zan sake ki saki uku"
P7	"Kuma ki biyani kuɗi na." Da ta fito ƙofar gida, sai ta haɗu da wani mutun.
P8	Ya tausaya mata, ya bata yawan kuɗin da suka ɓace. Ta yi farin ciki.
