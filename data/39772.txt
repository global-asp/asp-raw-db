page_text:

P1	Umama notata balime umbona emasimini.Utata uyaluhlakula  ukhula ukuze umbona ukhule kakuhle.Umama uphekela utata;ase ukutya emasimini ukuze angalambi.
P2	Akugqiba ukuhlakula utata;bahlala phantsi; batye;bancokole kube mnandi.USipoti uphiwa ukutya naye ukuze akhuthale abambe namasela.
P3	Nanko umvundlana othile ezokuba umbona emasimini.Utata wagibisela ngegaba lakhe;nanko no Sipoti eleqisa.Iwuuu!wagqotsa umvundla usoyikisela ubomi bakhe."Uyakuhlala usinda"watsho utata.
P4	Wakuvuthwa umbona siyawuvuna siwulayishe etrakhini uyokuthengiswa kubekho imali yokuthenga ezinye izinto.
