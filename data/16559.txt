page_text:

P1	Umnakwethu omncinci ulala ebusuku kakhulu. Ndivuka ekuseni kakhulu,  ngoba ndiyincutshe.
P2	Ilanga lingeniswa ndim endlini.
P3	"Uyinkwenkwezi yam yasekuseni," utsho uMa.
P4	Ndiyazihlamba,  andifuni kuncedwa.
P5	Ndiyamelana namanzi abandayo,  nesepha emzuba enukayo.
P6	UMa uyandikhumbuza,  "Ungawalibali amazinyo." Ndiyamphendula,  "Asoze,  hayi mna!"
P7	Ndakugqiba ukuhlamba,  ndibulisa utatomkhulu nomalumekazi,  ndibanqwenelele imini emnandi.
P8	Ze ndizinxibise,  "Ndikhulile ngoku mama," nditsho.
P9	Ndiyakwazi ukuvala amaqhosha kwaye ndiqhine nemitya yezihlangu zam.
P10	Kwaye ndiqinisekise ukuba umnakwethu omncinci uyazazi zonke iindaba zasesikolweni.
P11	Ndenza konke okusemandleni egumbini lokufundela.
P12	Yonke imihla ndenza zonke ezi zinto zilungileyo. Kodwa eyona nto ndiyithanda kakhulu,  kukudlala.
