P1	Long ago, there was a boy named Dawit.@@He lived with his mother and father.
P2	From his childhood, Dawit enjoyed drawing pictures. @@Dawit's mother enrolled him in an Art School at the age of seven.
P3	Dawit started carefully attending his art lessons.
P4	Dawit graduated from Art School. @@He drew many pictures and exhibited them to the public.
