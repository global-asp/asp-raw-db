page_text:

P1	Wankaiyua is a beautiful girl. She is nine months old. Her name means, a child is a gift from God.
P2	Wankaiyua is a Tiv girl. Her father is from Benue and her mother is Kaka from Taraba State.
P3	Wankaiyua was dedicated to God and named at the Church of Christ. The people prayed that God would bless her.
P4	Wankaiyua was born at Magami Maternity Clinic at 12 am on December 21, 2019.
