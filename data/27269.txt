title:	Ab'omukenkede ababiri  abatonyoli
author:	Bakali Kasolo and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Jesse Pietersen
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Olufumo olwebyobughangwa bwa Basopiya nga lufa ku bomukenkede abato ababiri abayagaanana ate oluvainhuma omukenkede gwabwe gwasansuka olwendhawukana dhaibwe. Dhaabwe.

page_text:

P1	Ghaaligho  Akabuzi  Akatono, n'Empala  Entono. Bafuuka ba  mukenkede.
P2	Baaja  mu  kizaanhiro   okuzaanha.
P3	B'airayo  eka  nga  buzimeire         inho.
P4	Akabuzi  Akatono  k'akoba mama  wako,"Ndhagaine mukagwa  omulungi. Ndi musangaavu  inho. Era idho  tuja  kwaganana."
P5	Mama  Wambuzi  y'airamu,"O  mwana  wange.Ndi  musangaavu  okangalwike  ng'oli  miraala. Timukagwa gwaife. Otaaganana  naye  idho."
P6	Mu kiseera n'ekirala,Wampala Omutono  y'akoba  mama we,"Ndhaganaine ni  mukagwa  wange omulungi.Ndi musanhufu. Tuja kwaganana  idho."
P7	Mama  Wampala  y'airamu,"O, mutabane  wange, nsaalilwa olw'obutalya  Akabuzi   Akatono. Ghe  mwaganana  olw'aidho, mwite  omulye."
P8	Ghe bwakya  ku nco,  Wampala  Omutono yaja ku kyalo ky'Akabuzi  Akatono.
P9	Akabuzi  Akatono  tikaali  ku  luya. Kaali  kekwese  ni mama  wako.
P10	Wampala  Omutono  y'aghoghoigana,"Tuje  mu kizaanhiro tuzaanhe."
P11	Akabuzi  Akatono  kaakoba,"Titukaali  ba mukenkede. Mpuliriza  mama  wange. Abaana  abalungi  b'awuliriza  ba  mama  baabwe."
P12	Wampala  Omutono  yanakughala. Y'airayo  ye mama we.
