page_text:

P1	Mbone and Akinyi were sisters. They lived with their parents at Utafiti town where they had a beautiful house.
P2	The schools were closing and the two girls were very happy. They would visit their grandmother during the holiday.
P3	Their father gave them a ride on his car. On their way, they enjoyed watching wild animals and beautiful flowers.
P4	They arrived at their grandparent's home in the afternoon. They ate sweet potatoes and rice.
P5	In the evening, their granny took them to her flower garden. Akinyi and her sister Mbone enjoyed plucking the flowers and tossing them around with their cousin.
P6	The following day,  they took their grandparent's cattle to the grazing field. The animals were peacefully as they played.
P7	They took the cattle back home. One cow chased their cousin but he was rescued by Mbone's father before he was knocked down.
P8	Life at their grandparent's home was enjoyable. Mbone and Akinyi played a lot. The End.
