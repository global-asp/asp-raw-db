title:	Tolimokinae Ayong… Tokona - Ng’Ajul
author:	Dennis Lokitare
artist:	Rijuta Ghate
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	King’ita atamar nyonte eyakatar ng’iboro ng’ajul nagelagelae.

page_text:

P1	Emam nyepusena ngirwa daang. Ikote neni a?
P2	Nyepusiana jiik. Kori ai?
P3	Nyonte erakatar ng'akui nakalibak?
P4	Emam nyelibak ng'irwa daang.
P5	Nyonte enyang'aketa kidison?
P6	Enyang'aka ng'idison lukakonok. Erai ng'idison lukajonak lukalibak.
P7	Nyonte eriokieta ng'akimuket a akiru?
P8	Emam ng'akimuket a akiru daang nyirioko.
P9	Nyonte ereng'ieta ng'akot?
P10	Eyei ibore iche nakot. Ng'esi itereng'it kesi.
P11	Nyonte ekwang'ia amakat?
P12	Nyayeni ayong.
