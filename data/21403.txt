title:	U-Daisy Omangalisayo!
author:	Zolile Kalashe
artist:	Siya Masuku
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/21403.png
desc:	Kwakukhona inkukhu encinane egama lingu Daisy. 

page_text:

P1	Kwathi ke kaloku ngantsomi, kwifama encinane kufuphi nelali encinane…
P2	kwakuhlala khona inkukhu encinane ebizwa ngokuba ngu Daisy.
P3	"Xa ndikhula, ndifuna ukubhabhela phezulu, phezulu esibhakabhakeni," watsho u-Daisy.
P4	Kodwa zonke ezinye iinkukhu zamhleka.
P5	"Awuqhelekanga," zatsho. "Asoze siphinde sidlale nawe."
P6	"Daisy, sonke singabhabhazela amaphiko ethu kodwa kunzima kakhulu kwiinkukhu ukuba zibhabhe," 
U-Mama wamxelela.
P7	U-Daisy zange anikezele. Ngazo zonke iintsuku waziqhelisa, eqhwaba amaphiko akhe.

Wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, ebhabhazela amaphiko akhe kodwa wayengenako ukuphakama asuke emhlabeni.
P8	Ngelixa eziqhelisa, umfanekiso-ngqondweni wokubhabhela phezulu esibhakabhakeni kwaye ejonge kwiinkukhu ngezantsi.

Ebebanomfanekiso-ngqondweni wokubhabha edlula oonondlwane kwaye edlula iinkonjane. "Huntshu!" iintaka zathi. "Inkukhu engabhabha!"
P9	Ngoko ke … wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, ngazo zonke iintsuku.
P10	U-Daisy ube ngabhabhazela amaphiko akhe. Ube ngaphakama ukusuka emhlabeni kodwa awele phantsi kwakhona.
P11	"Asoze ndikwazi ukubhabha!" U-Daisy walila kumama wakhe. "Abanye banyanisile."
P12	"Daisy, wohlukile kwezinye iinkukhu. Azifuni ukubhabha kodwa uyafuna! Ungakwenza oko," Watsho uMama.
P13	Kusuku olulandelayo u-Daisy wakhwela ngaphezulu kwendlu yeenkukhu kwaye wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe.

Wabhabhela emoyeni, kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe … kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe … kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe … kwaye …
P14	BAM!
P15	Ezinye iinkukhu zahlekela phezulu. "Ha ha ha! Sikuxelele! Iinkukhu azinako ukubhabha!"
P16	Kodwa kusuku olulandelayo u-Daisy wakhwela ngaphezulu, phezulu encotsheni karontabile.

Wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, wabhabhazela, u-Daisy wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe.
P17	Wabhabhela emoyeni kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe … kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe … kwaye wabhabhazela amaphiko akhe …
P18	Waqhubeka ebhabha! Umoya ngaphantsi kwamaphiko akhe wabamninzi kwaye wabhabha ukuya phezulu nangaphezulu!

Oonondlwane kunye neenkonjane zathi, "Ngumangaliso! Inkukhu ebhabhayo!"
P19	Kwaye ezinye iinkukhu zazifuna ukufana naye.
P20	Zathi, "Oh Daisy, uyamangalisa!"
