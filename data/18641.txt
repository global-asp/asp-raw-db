desc:	Palesa ulaambaula kuzisamu

page_text:

P1	Ooyu ngu Palesa. Izyina lyakwe lyaamba 'iduba' mumulaka wakwe waciSwana.
P2	Mafwumofwumo,  Palesa ulakanana kucisamu camafwuleenke.   "We ocisamu camafwuleenke,  Akaka kotupa mafwuleenke manji."
P3	Palesa uleenda kuya kucikolo. Munzila waambila bwizu wati,  "We obwizu,  koindila kusiya alimwi utayumi."
P4	Palesa wainda mumaluba akalimenena. "Nywe nomaluba,  ndamulumba nkaambo kamibala yanu imweka."
P5	A cikolo,  Palesa waamba kukasamu kacikkomena.    "Okasamu,  mitabi yako kiikkomena kutegwa katubalila mucimvule cako."
P6	Palesa wabandika kulukwakwa luzingulukide cikolo cakwe. "Akaka koyuma akukasya bantu babi kunjila mukati."
P7	Palesa najokela kung'anda kuzwa kucikolo,  waswaya cisamu camafwuleenke.    "Sena mafwuleenke ako abizwa kale?" Palesa wamubuzya.
P8	"Mafwuleenke taana bizwa," Palesa waamba. "Njakukubona cifwumo ocisamu camafwuleenke," Palesa waamba.  "Ndiza cifwumo uyakundibeda afwuleenke libizidwe."
