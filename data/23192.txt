page_text:

P1	Konkona, konkona! Ghaligho okughegana embeli kumulyango.
P2	Alake musanufu eno, "Ani alyagho?" yabuza. "Mama, tulina omugeni.
P3	"Kale" mama ghe yelamu. "Oyo ayinza okuba nga ni mukyala Hassan nomwanawe. Bambi begulile gho."
P4	Alake yegula olwiji naka mwenu, "Tusangele bambi tyamilaku agho mama ghange aidhakuba niwe mubwangu." Mukyala Hassan yeeza einuma alake bweyatandhika okuzana nomwana.
P5	Bambi gemaku amadhi mama oba nkufunileyo ekintu ekindhi. Yabuza Alake, "Ndhi bulungi nimukwano gwange alake, ghebale." yeelamu mukyala Hassan.
P6	Olunaku olulunji mukyala Hassan. "Kiikobwa alakes mama nga ali kwidha mwidhilo." Nsoniwa okulindhisa ghano  ghazila buzibu mukwanogwange. Alake ali tusannusangamuku.
P7	Mama Alake yamukuba akamwenu. "Yii," yeelamu mpolampola. "Alake nomwana atukilile okusangaza abageni ela nmwe numilizamu."
