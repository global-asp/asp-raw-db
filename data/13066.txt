title:	Imibala Yemushi Wenkhosatane
author:	Nkosingiphile Crusentia Nkosi and Sebenzile Thelma Mathabela
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Siswati
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/13066.png
desc:	Lendzaba isetfulela imibala yemushi wenkhosatane kanye netintfo longahle utibone emihleni yekuphila letingulemibala lena.

page_text:

P1	Umushi wenkhosatane unemibala lesikhombisa.
P2	Umbala wekucala ubovu.
P3	Kewyu ugcoke lihembe lelibovu.
P4	Umbala wesibili uluphuti.
P5	Leli li-olintji. Yini lokunye lokubonako lokunembala loluphuti?
P6	Umbala wesitsatfu umtfubi.
P7	Inhlitiyo yelicandza inembala lomtfubi.
P8	Umbala wesine uluhlata.
P9	Ingadze yetfu yabhanana iluhlata.
P10	Umbala wesihlanu uluhlata sasibhakabhaka.
P11	Sibhakabhaka siluhlata.
P12	Umbala wesitfupha uyi-indigo.
P13	Lawangema gumence lanembala lo-indigo.
P14	Umbala wesikhombisa uphephuli.
P15	Lilokwe lami liphephuli. Umbala lophephuli ngumbala lengiwutsandza kakhulu. Nguwuphi umbala lowutsandzako?
P16	Ngabe uyayati imibala lesikhombisa yemushi wenkhosatane.
