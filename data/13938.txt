P1	The old man of Koleo has just gone to town.
P2	The wife of the old man of Koleo has just gone to town.
P3	The son of the wife of the old man of Koleo has just gone to town.
P4	The cousin of the son of the wife of the old man of Koleo has just gone to town.
P5	The dog of the cousin of the son of the wife of the old man of Koleo has just gone to town.
