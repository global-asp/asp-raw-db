title:	Kũgiita ĩgamba
author:	Tarsilla  Thuranira
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Kῖmῖῖrũ 
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32827.png
desc:	Antũ ba ntũũra kũgiita igamba kũgarura motũũro jao.

page_text:

P1	Ntũũra yakwa yaarĩ na mathĩĩna jamaingĩ. Twekaga mũcitarĩ jũmũraja gũtaa rũũjĩ gacumene kamwe.
P2	Twetagĩĩra irio biria biejani ni bangĩ.
P3	Twaingaga nyomba cĩetũ thaa irio nĩuntũ bwa gũkĩra amba.
P4	Aana babaingĩ baratiga gwĩta cukuru.
P5	Twaarĩ twaritaga ngũgĩ cia nyomba ntũũrene ingĩ.
P6	Tũijĩ twathiurũkaga ntũũrene antũ bangĩ bakĩritaga ngũgĩ miundene ya antũ.
P7	Ruuo rwaurutana, marataci jarĩa jategi jagacuura mĩtĩne na ndwegone.
P8	Antũ batemangagwa nĩ cuuba inkũũ irĩa ciatei oouu.
P9	Ntũkũ ĩmwe, mĩberethi ĩrawa rũũjĩ na ikiiri biraa biitheri.
P10	Baaba areta nyomba ĩmwe na ĩngĩ akĩuragia antũ ntũũrene beeje mũcemanione.
P11	Antũ baroothũrana ruungu rwa gĩtina kĩa mũtĩ jũmũnene na barathikĩra.
P12	Baaba ararũngama na arauga, "Tũrienda tũrite ngũgĩ tũgwatanĩrĩte nĩkenda tuthiria thĩĩna cĩetũ."
P13	Koome wa ũkũrũ bwa miaka ĩnaana, akari nthĩ mũgogone aragandũka arauga, "Ũũni ng'atetheeria na kũtheria."
P14	Mwekũrũ ũmwe arauga, "Ekũrũ no bagwatane nani tũkũrie irio."
P15	Mũkũrũ ũngĩ ararungama arauga, "Arũme bakenja kĩthima."
P16	Twinthe tũragandũka na kajũ kamwe, "No mwanka tũgarure ũtũũro bwetũ." @@Kuuma ntũkũ ĩuu tũrarita ngũgĩ tũgwatanĩrĩte kenda tũthiria mathĩĩna jetũ.
