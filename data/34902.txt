P1	Fati yarinya ce karama mai gudu@  kamar iska.@  @@  Tana son yin dariya, kuma@  tana kokari Kaman yadda aka faxa mata.
P2	Wata rana, Fati@  da mahaifinta sun je gona@  bisa keke don yin ciyawa.
P3	Jim qadan, Fati ta gaji.@  Sai tace, “Baba, ina zufa kuma@  na gaji. Zan iya zama@  a karkashin bishiyan mangworo?”@  @@  Baba yace, “ee Fati, zaki@  iya zama. Amma, ki kula,@  kar ki hau kowace bishiya@  yau.”
P4	Fati ta zauna karkashin@  bishiyan mangwaro.@  Fati ta xaga kai sama. Sai ta hango daya, biyu, uku@  nunannun mangwaro!
P5	Fati ta waiga ta kalli@  babanta. Yana aiki bai lura da ita@  ba.@  @@  Sannu sannu, Fati@  ta fara hawan bishiyan mangoro. Sai ta tuno “Bari in zauna nan. Wannan wurin yana da kyan hutu don shan mangwaro.”@  @@  Sai ta zauna ta sha.
P6	Bayan ta gama shan mangwaron, Fati ta sauko kasa.@  @@  Sai ta hangi wata@  itaciyar.@  @@  Wannan itaciyar tana@  dauke da wani ruwa mai gangarowa@  daga bawon itacen.
P7	Ruwan zuma ne! Fati@  na son ruwan zuma! Amma ba@  ta son zuma.@  @@  Fati ta kalli sama@  sai ta ga yar zuma.
P8	Fati ta tsamanci wannan@  yar zuma ce. Ba za ta harbe ni ba@  in na hau bishiyan don neman ruwan zuma.
P9	Fati ta kalli babanta.@  Amma bai lura da ita ba.@  @@  Sai Fati ta hau@  saman bishiya ta xebo ruwan@  zuma daga cikin ramin.
P10	Fati ta ga ya@  dace, “Zan zauna nan don wajen na da kyan@  hutu kuma in sha zuma da yawa@  mai zaqi.”@  @@  Sai ta zauna ta sha.
P11	Bayan ta gama shan ruwan zuma,@  Fati ta sauko daga kan bishiyan.@  @@  Sai ta ga zuma.@  Ashe su na da yawa! BZZZ! BZZZ! BZZZ!@  @@  Fati ta gudu daga@  wurin zuman.
P12	Fati ta ruga wajen@  babanta tana numfashi da haki.@  @@  Babanta yace, “Fati,@  ina kika shiga?”@  @  @  Fati ba ta so ta fadi@  inda ta je ba.
P13	Sai tace, “Dubi@  baba, ina da mangoro.@  Kana son ka ci?”@  @@  Baba yace, “a’a nagode. Dole mu hanzarta zuwa gida yanzu. An@  kusan fara ruwa.”
P14	Baban ya dauki Fati a bayan keken shi a hanyan@  su ta komawa gida. Can sai aka fara ruwa.
P15	Baba ya garzaya da keke.@  @@  Sai keken ta kauce@  hanya.@  @@  HATSARI!
P16	Keken ya faxa kan kafar Fati.

P18	A gida, mama da kaka@  sun tambaya abin da ya gudana a gona.@  @@  “Fati ta hau bishiya,” Baba ya ce.@  @@  “Tsananin gudu da Baba yayi yasa muka fadi@  da keke har ya fada kan@  kafa na,” inji Fati.
P19	“Wayyo Fati,” Inji mama.@  @@  “Baiwar Allah Fati,” inji kaka. “Ya kamata mu kula da kafan ki.”@  @@  Haka ya faru. Karshen kenan.
