desc:	Dudunye ah keze kumanyako anti umusale wewe wokayima umudoto?

page_text:

P1	aba kaleko ni kale abwo basale babibili,dudunye ni wokayima.Dudunye aba umulimi uh kola nabi eyunkizanga mumamilimili kuza mugunda mwa wewe
P2	wokayima eyunka nga anze bechile nabi anza mweiyu  nga yesi egolola namala anza kuyonda beidiao
P3	mudikini gusinga wokayima aba abugula zimizo zo dudunye kwama mu chitala namale anze mugukunda mwe wewe evidise anti alikukola.aba ansa muliro amala ocha zimizo kawo chesi akola  nda  kutusako kudia zimizo zesi ochale
P4	nga sawa nze kukugula zolele wokiyima aba azizaga eba kasoli wo musale wewe.asunda mpaka muchitala chewe.
P5	dudunye elonza ku bilimi beiwe wokayima amwilamo anti "afanikana bawazula bowo niye bivele kasoli" anti bali kubonabona ni budabi kubanga badoto
P6	Dudunye alowoza ku chizibu chino lwa kasela Amala afuna magesi abusa gumu wiyabirila  kwama ku bisala amuta mugunda mwa wewe uhmwife nga ezele anyibirira ku gumu
P7	nga chilo cholele wokayima anza mugunda mwo Dudunye kwiba kasoli gundi.Nga achikwigira mugunda amagamaga kubona anti kawo umutu esi da
P8	wokayima kabona gumu wesi dudunye ata mugunda mwe wewe nda wokayima eima mu gumu asingala  muweine atndika kuba mulanga anti bamuyende
P9	Dudunye awulira liyombo muganda mwe wewe adima kuwaba umweifi kafujirisa chesi aba alikubona da  Aba umusale wewe wokayima niye wesi gumu ahabire.
P10	kwama lunaku lwo dudunue anza eizira ye wei wokayima naye anza eizira ye wei.owo yesi busale bwawe bwagama.

kagano kasei kagamire woo.
