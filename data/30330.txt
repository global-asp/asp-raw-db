page_text:

P1	Ab'emikwano  abasatu
P2	Ku  kyalo  matyama,  kwaliku  ab'emikwano   basatu  Yakobo,  Yokaana,ni  Petero.
P3	Lulala  bbaabba  wa  Petero  yaalwala  omutima.
P4	Baamutwala  mu  basawo  ab'ekinansi.
P5	Omusawo  ow'ekinansi  yaakoba  Petero  ati, "Mufumbe  omutima  ogw'omuntu bbaabba wo  agulye  mubisi.
P6	Agho bbaabba  gha  Petero yaamukoba afune mukwano  gwe amulimbe  amutoolemu  omutima.
P7	Era  Petero  yaakikola.
P8	Agho  oluvainhuma, Yokaana  bweyategeera  nga  Petero  naise  Yakobo, yaamucaagha  inho  era  omukwano  gwaibwe  gwafa.
