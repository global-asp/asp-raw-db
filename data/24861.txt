page_text:

P1	Eira einho, Kyabazinga Ow'ensiko yali Mwami Mpologoma.
P2	Yasindika ensolodhe okusirisa oluyookaano okuva mu kayandha ak'omunsiko. Aye tidhaghanga.
P3	Endhovu yeeghaayo okudhisirisa. Endhovu yaasindikibwayo nga omukuumi.
P4	Mu bwire ghagati, oluyookaano bwe lwainhama, endhovu yaatandiika okwita ebikere. Yakola kirala ekyo n'olunaku olwairirira.
P5	Olumaliira olwa saabbiiti edhaabitagho, Mwami Mpologoma yaaba musanhufu kuba Wandhovu yali asiriisa oluyookaano.
P6	Aye kumpi mwezi mulala mu maiso, baabona ebisiisa kamaala mu nnimiro yaibwe.
P7	Mwami Mpologoma yaakoba Wandhovu okweta ebikere okulya ebisiisa.
P8	Wandhovu yali mwinhamivu kuba yali nga yamala okwita ebikere byonabyona. Yaakoba, nso-ni-gha, nabiita byonabyona!"
P9	Mwami Wampologoma yaakoba,"Wandhovu, nakukoba kusiirisa luyookaano, tikwita bikere. Era Wandhovu yaateebwa mwituudha okumala emyaka kamaala.
P10	Era Kyabazinga yaakoba ensolo dhoonadhoona okukolanga ekyo kyene kyaba akobye.
