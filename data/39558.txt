desc:	A story about a new friendship at school. 
This storybook was developed at a storybook development workshop at Ras Abebe Aregay Library

page_text:

P1	It is Spring Season, when the rainy season in Ethiopia passes and school reopens. Students gathered in a school.
P2	Class time passed and now it is recess time. All of the students are playing with their friends.
P3	One student stood sad, weeping.
P4	Another student who saw this goes near her. Slowly she approached the girl who was weeping.
P5	With a little fearfulness, she asked, "What happened?" @The girl responded, "Nothing."@"How do you say nothing? You are crying!" she insisted.
P6	"I felt bad and cried because I missed my friends," she explained. @"Oh! Please don't cry. I will be a good friend to you," she said and hugged her.
P7	After she stopped crying her new friend asked her to tell her her name. @"I am Elsa, and you?"@"I am Hayat."
P8	After that incidence, they became good friends.
P9	They introduced their families.
P10	Then they became close friends who spend their times together at school and libraries.
