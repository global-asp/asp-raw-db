page_text:

P1	Thami wakes up early every day.
P2	Thami's mother helps him to get ready for school.
P3	Thami's school is far away from his home.@@He walks to school with his friends.
P4	One day, Thami's mother went to work early.@@He woke up late.@@His friends walked to school without him.
P5	Thami was walking alone through the forest.@@He heard strange sounds near the trees.
P6	Thami heard voices behind the bushes.@@He started to run. @@He ran all the way to school.
P7	Thami ran into his classroom!@@Later, his teacher talked to the class about the dangers of walking alone.
P8	Now Thami makes sure that he always walks with friends.
