desc:	What is the punishment for a greedy boy who ate too much fruit?

page_text:

P1	Grandmother brought us fruits from the market.
P2	They looked delicious.
P3	My brother Vusi got greedy and ate a piece from each fruit.
P4	Who bit the fruits?
P5	Grandmother became angry and shouted.
P6	We suspected Vusi.
P7	We told grandmother.
P8	Grandmother asked him and he confessed.
P9	Suddenly,  Vusi didn't feel well.
P10	He ran to the toilet.
P11	Vusi held his stomach.
P12	Grandmother asked what lesson we had learnt from Vusi's experience.
