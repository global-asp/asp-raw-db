page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there was a beautiful bird that loved to sing.
P2	It woke up every morning at sunrise and sang sweetly through the woods.
P3	All animals wondered where this sweet music came from.
P4	Because the bird was shy, it hid and no one knew who it was.
P5	One day, all the animals planned to wake up before sunrise.
P6	They wanted to find the source of this sweet music.
P7	When the sun begun to rise in the morning, the bird started to sing.
P8	Everyone came out from hiding and applauded the bird as soon as it had finished singing.
P9	The beautiful bird became the chief entertainer of the whole village from then on.
