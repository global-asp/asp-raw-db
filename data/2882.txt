page_text:

P1	Ngibone iloli elikhulu lehla ngomgwaqo.
P2	Imoto engu-Mercedes yayiza ikhuphuka ngomgwaqo.
P3	Umshayeli weloli wayekhuluma Kumakhalekhukhwini.
P4	Wayengawubhekile umgwaqo.
P5	Kwaba nengozi enkulu.
P6	I-ambulensi namaphoyisa beza ukuzosiza.
P7	Kwafika amaloli amabili adonsa izimoto. Adonsa kokubili imoto encane neloli.
P8	Ngethuka kakhulu!
