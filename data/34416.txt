title:	Sipho saGogo
author:	Mduduzi Benjie Shongwe
artist:	Sibongile Mnkandla
lang:	Siswati
level:	Longer paragraphs

desc:	Make usita Thabo kutsi ente sipho lesikhetseke kakhulu sagogo wakhe.

page_text:

P1	Kuta Gogo utewuvakasha. Kantsi njalo uphatsela Thabo emantongomane netitselo tesiganga nobe ummbila kanye nelijoti ngalesinye sikhatsi.@@Thabo naye ufuna kupha Gogo intfo letsite lekhetsekile.
P2	Incwadzi ingaba sipho lesihle, kepha Gogo akakwati kufundza?@@Nakaya etitolo, ingabe utayitfola yini intfo letsite lenhle?@@"Asimentele sikhwama," kusho Thabo amoyitela. "Sikhwama lesicinile lesikhulu ngagawini loluhlata njengesibhakabhaka."
P3	Make utsatsa indvwangu labebayitsenge esitolo. Wendlala iphethini bese ucala kuyisika.@@Kwekucala, usika tinhlangotsi letimbili letimcoka, letinkhulu futsi letibanti.
P4	Ngemuva kwaloko usika letimbili letincane kute ahlanganise ngato.@@Thabo utigoca ndzawonye kanye netinhlangotsi letimcoka letimbili. Uyabuka njengobe Make wakhe aphindza atsatsa iphethini.
P5	Usika lingentasi lesikhwama, ticephu letimbili letindze. Loku kutokwenta lesikhwama sicine mbamba.@@Thabo uyatigoca njengobe Make wakhe aphindze atsatsa iphethini.
P6	Usika imicu lemine lebanti nalemidze.@@Lemibili itawutfungelwa ndzawonye kute yente umucu munye. Loku kutawenta lesikhwama sicine ngalokuphindziwe.
P7	Ngemuva kwaloko make utsatsa umshini wekutfunga.@@Uhlanganisa tonkhe ticephu kute ente sikhwama saGogo.@@Usinika Thabo, Thabo umoyitela bese utsi, "Ngiyabonga Make, ngesikhwama saGogo lesiluhlata njengesibhakabhaka!"
