title:	Juma wa Maani!
author:	Ibrahim  Isabirye and Kyambogo  University
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Olugero luno lulombodha waire tukangaivula abana

page_text:

P1	Muganda wange omuto atendhuka kikeerezi. Nzuuka mangu, kubanga ndi wa maani.
P2	Ninze ndikiriza endhuba.
P3	"Nhiwe munhienhie yange eyo kunkyo," akoba maama.
P4	Ne naaza mwenhe buli lunaku, tyendha buyambi bwonana.
P5	Tifaayo oba maadhi mainhogovu, oba sabbuuni wa bbululu ayoza engoye.
P6	Maama andhidhusa, "Otelabira maino go."
Ndhira mu, "Mbe naire, tininze!"
P7	Oluvainhuma lwo kwoza, nnamusa badhaadha ni songa. Mbendeza olunaku olulungi.
P8	Oluvainhuma neyambaza mwenhe. "Ndimunene kati, maama," Naakoba.
P9	Nsobola okusiba amabbesa no okumweza engaito dhange.
P10	Ngezaaku okubona nti baganda bange abato bategeera amawulire ageisomero.
P11	Mukibiina neefaaku inho mungeri yange.
P12	Nkola ebintu bino byonabyona buli lunaku.
Aye ekintu kyensinga okwenda, no okuzaana no okuzaana.
