desc:	Nhimbe ingalilo yomuZimbabwe mugubungano inobhanchana kuta mishingo Lungano lunolingisisa chigaba chibhancha mbuya Sigauke beshakula muchilili chabo.

page_text:

P1	Go! Go! Go! @@Tawha undangulangu womuchigaba zungwanangwana. @@"Besana mukani mubambanyile mapaja," amegulu bakashomeja belimungumba yabo yolala.
P2	Takashama inyai tikazukolola tibhuda bpobulo. @@Yelyo zuba lyakabeli Nhimbe kuchilili chambuya Sigauke. @@Takenda muhozi likano kolela mapaja ose.
P3	Takanyamula mapaja kuchilili. @@Chigaba chose chokwa Chamutsa chakabezha kobhancha mbuya Sigauke.
P4	Panshaji wokulima wose, bomuchigaba banogala mudako montinkulwana woMutsamvi.
P5	Mbuya Sigauke wakapa bomuchigaba mahea kuti benwe.@@Bechigaba bakanyetutuka benwa maheu.
P6	Shule kokunyetutuka, bechigaba bakapejeleja kushakula muchilili. @@Mbuya Sigauke bakashanta.@@Wakaboka bose bomuchigaba nokumbhancha.
P7	MuZimbabwe banhu banota Nhimbe kubhancha bamwe kuta mishingo yabo.@@Mobhancha banhu na mugubungano lyenu?
