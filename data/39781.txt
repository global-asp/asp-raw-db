desc:	Mbʊpá njala nnɖe n tɩ lǝ atʊ ɖaa gɩjibɔŋɔ gɩɖǝn ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ kpa asna yɔgʊʊ ma.

page_text:

P1	Ŋǝ̂ ma ganyaʊ.@Ŋǝ̂ ma asna.
P2	Ʊshilè ʊ ci. Ŋkomo n shee.@Ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ ɖa. Ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ ɖa yɔgʊʊ.
P3	Asna a tɩ rǝŋǝ ʊnyǝŋkrǝ yɔgʊʊ.
P4	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna ɩtoko kpanlɛɛkɛ. Kpanlɛɛkɛ ɖǝn a kpa ajɩwa, kpanlɛɛkɛ ɖǝn a cǝm.
P5	Asna a cam kpanlɛɛkɛ nɖee a cǝm ma, akara. Ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ bǝla akpa nɩ.
P6	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna araʊ. Gʊraʊ gʊɖǝn gʊ sǝŋkǝla, gʊraʊ gʊɖǝn gʊ jǝba.
P7	Asna a laa gʊraʊ ŋgʊɖee gʊ sǝŋkǝla ma, akara. Ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ bǝla akpa nɩ.
P8	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna ɩcogoto balamaka. Ʊɖǝn a lama, ʊɖǝn a kǝla.
P9	Asna a laa cogoto nɖee a lama ma, ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ kɔɔ kpa nɩ.
P10	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna bʊjaakɛɛtɩ. Gajaakɛɛtɩ gaɖǝn ga ɖooɖoo, gajaakɛɛtɩ gaɖǝn ga kʊ. Asna a laa gajaakɛɛtɩ ŋgaɖee ga kʊ ma, akara. Ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ kɔɔ kpa nɩ.
P11	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna ɩtoko kootu. Kootu ɖǝn a cir, ʊɖǝn k'a cir na. Asna a laa kootu nɖee a cir ma, akara, ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ kɔɔ bǝla akpa nɩ.
P12	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna afu. Nfu nɖǝn n shemelaŋ, nfu nɖǝn n kǝla. Asna a laa nfu nɖee n shemelaŋ ma, asǝu. Ama ʊnyǝŋkrǝ ʊ kɔɔ bǝla akpa nɩ.
P13	Ganyaʊ ga shee asna akɔɖɔkɔɖɔ. Akɔɖɔkɔɖɔ anaŋa ɩnyɩʊ ɩ lee ɩfɔlɩ, akɔɖɔkɔɖɔ anaŋa ɩnyɩʊ ɩ lee ɩŋono.
P14	Asna a laa akɔɖɔkɔɖɔ anaŋa ɩnyɩʊ nɖee ɩ lee ɩfɔlɩ ma, akara. Nnyǝmǝ ʊnyǝŋkrǝ k'ʊ kɔɔ kpa nɩ na.
P15	Gɩjibɔŋɔ gɩ bon. Aŋɔrɔ a tɩ shɛʊ. Ʊshilè ʊ tɩ ɖoo.
P16	Gaja ga kʊɖa. Gaɖu ga tɩ kiɖe. Gaɖu ga sʊɖa. Gaɖu ga sʊɖa yɔgʊʊ.@Gaɖu ga sʊɖa páá ashee asna.
P17	Ɩ lee cei, ganyaʊ.@Ɩ lee cei, asna nɖee a na rǝŋǝ njisʊɖa ma.
