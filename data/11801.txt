title:	Kam Dadua
author:	Avec la contribution du Comité ASL
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Kanuri
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/11801.png
desc:	The man in this story is so tall that everything around him is too short or too small. He solves this problem by creating things to match his size.

page_text:

P1	Banonju jauro kori.
P2	Ci ngimnjuye jauro cidiyan.
P3	Dǝgǝlnju jauro kori.
P4	Belonju jauro gana.
P5	Kam adǝ dadua.
P6	Banonjuro ka kuruwu kikkaro.
P7	Ciya ngimnjuye kurajiyi.
P8	Dǝgǝl jauro kura cǝtandiǝ.
P9	Belo dadua ciwo.
P10	Kǝla kuris daduayen napce,  furshet kuran masǝna kido.
P11	Fanju kolje,  leje curo dǝrren saa kadaro napken.
