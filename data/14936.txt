desc:	Runu rubazo rwomusaija ataragonzaaga kwikara haihi nabantu abandi. Omusaija namukaziwe bakagenda.

page_text:

P1	Hakaba haroho omusaija owataragonzaaga kwikara haihi nabatahibe kandi tiyagonzaaga mukyarawe kubunga mumaka gabataahi.
P2	Kiro kimu akagenda yayombeka eka mwirungu hara namaka gabatahibe.
P3	Yagendaga bulikiro murukyakya agaruka muka mwitumbi.
P4	Itumbi limu omusaija akaba akereriirwe muno nagaruka omuka. Empisi ekarora omusaija ekyakaba nakora.
P5	Empisi ekagenda yakonkona harwigi rwenju.
P6	Omukazi yatekereza akaba ali iba yakingura orwigi. Empisi yamugurukira kandi yamulya wena.
P7	Omusaija obuyagarukire omuka yarora mukyarawe akaba aliirwe empisia. Akaba ataina ekyokubaza.
P8	Ebi byona bikabaho habwokuba tiyangonzaaga kwikara haihi nabataabi.
