desc:	Nakehi spills the beans and gets whats coming to him.

page_text:

P1	Nihiku nimoha, Annakehi yaavinya ni amwaaraya eyaaka oweeceecela Annipapariya.
P2	Yaaphiyalaaya yaahaakheliwa phaama,
P3	tiwi yiipheliwe mwanakhu
P4	elya vamoha.
P5	Evinrevo mahiku, Annipapariya yaavinya ni amwaaraya eya wa anthamwene aya.
P6	Yaaphiyalaaya, hiyaaphwannye Annakehi,
P7	yaakele woota miraakho saya.
P8	Annakehi erwaaka yaawoona anthamwene aya, ehakalala
P9	echimakela amwaaraya yaapeyaayavo.
P10	Yaaphwannye yaapenye epwiri wi yaacelekheleno aleco yaale.
P11	Vawi yahoona ekhwali yaarenle ereya muraakho, yaavokha mwaapo yoole epwiri eyeeheya.
P12	Avinya anachekula ni nthamwene awe.
P13	Echekulaka hiihaale Annakehi yaahiiwa ekhwale eheiwe eriki: "Kekelekekele."
P14	Yaavinya mwanvayi,
P15	ekusha mwaalo aya ehererya muyuununi, echimakelawo. Yaaphwanya chiri eheiwe.
P16	Murima owaalunwe yaachukula ekhwali epharela ehinyonke nari wuntaka.
P17	Ntata nikina yaapharelano mooce.
P18	Erukunuwa enachikela owaani ehakalalaka.
P19	Vawi achu akhalayi yaawara ichepe, mwaalo waanasokola vakhaani vakhaani ekhulula ya echepe awe.
P20	Erikimphiyaru vaate ekhulula yaapaseya,
P21	echepe yiicheya achu ewoonaka,
P22	eriki ekaphule echepaaya yaahiya ekhwali evava
P23	mooce ephweeya elamwa.
P24	.
P25	Ela ennanihusiha wi muchu onaphwanela opisha murima.
