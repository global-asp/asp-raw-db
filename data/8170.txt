title:	Ndhuuba Nhumba

desc:	Weetegereze ebika bya amayumba abantu mwe basula. Odhuuba kusula mu nhumba kika ki?

page_text:

P1	Muzaale asula mu nsiisira nsereke na isubi.
P2	Mbubani asula mu nhumba nsunkule ya nsonda.
P3	Eno nhumba muserebende.
P4	Asulya ka nhumba eno katangire era kasulike.
P5	Omusaadha ono ali kwezimbira nhumba.
P6	Nhumba eno mpyaka.
P7	Eno nhumba nkaire.
P8	Ndhuuba kusulaku mu nhumba eno.
