title:	Culeeys 'yo Fudeeyd
author:	Abdirizack yussuf abdalla
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle
lang:	Somali
level:	First words

desc:	In this story we are asked to work out what is heavier and what is lighter.

page_text:

P1	Ke culus? Digaagadda ama iyo dufda balka?
P2	Kee fudeeyd? Ubaxa ama sabuulka?
P3	Dhalaankee culus?
P4	Keebaa culus,  maroodi ama aabe?
P5	Keebaa culus,  hal kilo oo dagxaan ah.
P6	 Hal garaam oo shamiido ah?
P7	Keebaa culus? Hal kilo oo sokor ah.
P8	 Hal kilo oo duf ah?
