page_text:

P1	Kong kong! Kong kong!
P2	"Ke mang?" Ka boletsa mo borokong.
P3	"Ke nna," ga araba lentswe le le kwa tlase.
P4	"'Ke mang,  'nna'? Ka botsa."
P5	"Ke go boleletse,  ke n-n-a f-e-l-a-," lentswe le le la bua gape.
P6	"Ga ke itse ope yo o bidiwang 'n-n-a-f-e-l-a'," ka araba. Ka tlhola gore nete ya me ya montsane e ntse sentle mo bolaong.
P7	"Leina la me ke Kepi." Lentswe le utlwala le tshosa ebile le le gaufi le tsebe ya me.
P8	"Kepi ke mang?" Ka botsa ke ntse ke lebelela mo tlase ga nete.
P9	"Kepi Monang," lentswe la bua kwa bofelong. Ka utlwa ke longwa mo tsebeng.
P10	"Iyo!" Ka tlolela godimo mme ka tshimolola go phuruphutsha. Â "O tlile go duelela se!" Ka goa.
P11	Ka go tenega,  ka fologa mo bolaong mme ka tshuba lebone.
P12	Ka ipaakanyetsa ntwa e kgolo le Kepi Monang!
