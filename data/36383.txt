desc:	Possession in Siswati is expressed differently than it is in English.
In English we say Siphos room but in Siswati we say room of Sipho.

page_text:

P1	Kudla kwami kumnandzi
P2	Ngifundza Incwatsi yami
P3	Ngidlala ibhola ne Mngani wami
P4	Umbhede wa malume mncane kuye.
P5	Samu Utsandza tilwane takhe kakhulu
P6	Sibusiso ukhumula I-uniform yakhe
P7	Mine ne bangani bami sitsandza kudlala ibhola
P8	Make Utsandza timbali takhe. @Timbali takhe tiyamujabulisa.
P9	Siyabonga ne Bangani bakhe badlala emantini
P10	Nthabiseng ujabulile.@Ujabulele liroko lakhe lelisha.
