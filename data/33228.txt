page_text:

P1	Luka 18:1-8@@Nkwaha mmoca Yesu ahaattonkela eetthuci awe nlikanihero nimoca, aathoonyiheraka ifayita yoovekela woohitomwa ukati wothene, eeraka:@@Isittatti imoca ahaawo hukhumu mmoca ahaamoova Nluku, ni khaariwo tho ntthu aanlemelihaawe.
P2	Ciicammo yeeyo isittatti iyo ahaawo naankhwele mmoca aarweesa unkasarela hukhumu ule, eeraka: Nkuulumele wosariya nlattu aka, kontha kihookoseriya.
P3	Ule ahokhala mahuku menci akhottaka.
P4	Nanso wahoolo ahoopuwela veekhaawe eeraka: Mi akimmova Nluku, ni khaawo ntthu nonlemelihaaka. Nanso cinkeettelela aya ala athiyana ala, kinawoolumela nlattu aya uyo, wiira ahikinyoonyihe.
P5	Vano Apwiya yaheera: Mwiiwe yuulumacilyaawe hukhumu oohiloka uyo. Nluku khanhala waavaha isariya ale yaathanlilyaawe, anonlapela uhiyu ni uthana hata yoonaka upica waakhuliya?
P6	Kinoohimeryani wiira naahala woolumela wosariya nlattu aya uyo woohipica. Kaahiki wanhalaawe uwa Mwanawe Pinatamu, naahala waaphwanya atthu owaamini vathi va?
