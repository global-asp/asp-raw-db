P1	The ten commandments (Exodus 20).                            @1)You shall have no other gods before me
P2	2)You shall not make yourself an image in the form of anything in heaven above or on the earth beneath or in the waters below.You shall not bow down to them or worship them.
P3	3)You shall not misuse the name of the Lord your God
P4	4)Remember the Sabbath day by keeping it holy.Six days you shall labour and do all your work,but the seventh day is a Sabbath to the Lord your God.On it you shall not do any work,neither  your son or your daughter not your male or female s
P5	Servant,nor your animal,nor any Foreigner in your towns.For in six days the Lord made the heaven and the earth,the sea,and all that is in them,But he rest on the seventh day therefore the Lord blessed the Sabbath day and made it holy
P6	5)Honour your father and your mother,so that you may live long in the land the Lord your God is giving you
P7	6)You shall not murder
P8	7)You shall not commit adultery
P9	9)You shall not steal
P10	9)You shall not give false testimony against your neighbour.
P11	10)You shall not covet your neighbour's house.You shall not covet your neighbour's wife,or his male or female servant,his ox or donkey that belong to your neighbour.
