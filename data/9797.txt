page_text:

P1	This is a girl called Norah. Her doll's name is Selah.
P2	Norah loves her doll very much. Norah plays with Selah every day. She sleeps with her doll every night.
P3	One day,  Norah could not find her doll. She looked everywhere,  but could not find Selah. Norah got very worried.
P4	Norah did not know that her mother took Selah for washing. She hung the doll outside to dry.
P5	"I can't find my doll," cried Norah. "Don't worry," said her mother.
P6	That evening,  Norah's mother gave the doll to Norah. The doll was very clean. Selah looked like a new doll!
P7	Norah was very happy. She jumped up and down. She danced. She laughed loudly.
P8	And she thanked her mother.
