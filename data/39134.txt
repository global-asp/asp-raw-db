page_text:

P1	One morning Xolisile saw  beautiful pine trees.
P2	He asked his friends to cut down the big tree
P3	They took the tree to the furniture manufacturer.
P4	The remainder of the tree made papers.
P5	One day Xolisa went to borrow a book at the library.
P6	When she opened the book the book said "don't bend my spine.
P7	The chair also replied"i know that voice"it is from our tree.The table also laughed and said "Ha ha ha we are back together again.
