title:	Kiki Kyembenkozese Okutambula
author:	Muwanguzi Alex
artist:	Sue Kramer, Catherine Groenewald, Marion Drew, Louwrisa Blaauw, Rob Owen, Vusi Malindi, Jesse Breytenbach, Natalie Propa, Wiehan de Jager and Mango Tree

desc:	Abaana baloghoza okutambula era bewunya ntambulaki esinga mudonadona.

page_text:

P1	 Eriyo engeri
  ningi edikozesebwa mukutambula.eriwa kudho jembe nondeku?
P2	Nkozese noni?
P3	 Enoni elumuka
  inno.ntile ino olwenumuka yayo!
P4	 Obanandikozeisa
  elikopita?
P5	 Aye elikopita
  ne-enoni ne-ebilala!
P6	 Nkozesekuku
  turaini?
P7	 Eye ate
  turaini yona eri wala.
P8	 Oba,dida-akozesa
  basi?
P9	 Ekyalo kyaiffe
  kiliku basi ndala yonka!
P10	  Nda-akozesa elole okutambula.
P11	 Aye ate
  lole dikozesebwa kutwala bintu bizito.
P12	 Nkozese
  egali okutambula?
P13	 Aye baabawange
  ajakumpa egaliye?
P14	 Kale ndida-gezaku
  okukozesa-aku elyato?
P15	 Aye atenze
  tyesiga mato!
P16	 Haa! ndidi
  kyendijo-okozesa! Ndija-akozesa tulakita okutambula.
