P2	Adaku knew she would not find food at home,so she left looking for food.As she is leaving the house she starts to smell this delicious jollofrice aroma.
P3	The aroma is so good she decides to follow the smell leads her into the dark woods.Eventually its leads her deeper and deeper into the woods till she finds a back big pot.
P4	Finally, she has gotten to the home of that beautiful aroma.It filled her nose all she could think of is that jollof rice
