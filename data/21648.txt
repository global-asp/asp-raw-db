title:	Gangee
author:	Mulualem  Daba
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Afaan Oromo

desc:	Oduu durii sabaaba gangeen maseena tate haftee kan ibsu.

page_text:

P1	Bara durii, Gangee bareduu Wardit jedhamtuu jirti ture.
P2	Fardi bareda tokko, "Warditiin dirqaama fuudhun qaba," jedhe.
P3	Fardichis Warditin, "Koottu lagaa buuna," jedhe.
P4	Achuman warditiin haadha mana isaa akka tatuuf gaafate. Isheenis tole jetteni.
P5	Fardichi, "Haatiif abba kee eenyudha?" jedhe warditin gaafate.
P6	Warditis, "Haati koo mana mooti keessa jiraati."
P7	"Hinbeektuni obboleetin koo mana waaqeffana keessa jirati."
P8	"Adaadan koo immoo mana namicha naanno keenya bulchu keessa jiraati," jetten.
P9	Yommuu kana dubbatu, Harren dulomaa tokko darbaa ture. Harrichi abba Wardit ture.
P10	Warditis abbaa ishee hinlalle nagaas hingaafane.
P11	Abbaan ishee garuu, "Maali nagaadha intala koo?" jedhee.
P12	Fardichis, "Enyuudha harreen gadibadii kun?" jedhe Warditin gaafate.
P13	Warditis, "Anoo isaa hin beeku," jette.
P14	Fardichis harricha, abbaa Wardit, dhiite.
P15	Harrichi baay'ee gadde osoo boo'u duu'e.
P16	Warditis waan abbaa ishee hin kabajneef, masena tatee haftee.
