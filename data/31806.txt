page_text:

P1	Ingubo kadadewethu inkulu kakhulu kimi!
P2	Ijezi lomfowethu nalo likhulu kakhulu kimi.
P3	Isikhwama sami sikhulu.@@Kodwa asisikhulu kakhulu!
P4	Leli bhande lincane kakhulu kimi. Lidala futhi.
P5	Lesi yisigqoko sikanodoli wami.
P6	Amasokisi ami angilingana kahle.@@Kodwa akuwona umbala ofanele.
P7	Nazi izicathulo zami ezintsha.
P8	Zona-ke zingilingana kahle.
