page_text:

P1	Ka letsatsi le leng,Thabo le Mpho bane baya mabenkeleng,baile ba bona makganni wa lori antse a bua ka mohala wa thekwng.
P2	Ka pele hobona hone hotla koloi ene e kgannwa ke mofumahadi Mbele.
P3	mokganni wa lori oile a sitwa ho laola sepalangwang sahae,a thula Mofumahadi Mbele ka koloi yahae.@hwalla jwabakatla;jwabakatla;
P4	mokganni wa lori o ne a lemetse haholo,haele Mofumahadi Mbele o ne a tswile dikotsi tse nyenyane feels, koloi ya bakudi ile ya potlaka ho fihla ho potlakisetsa bakganni bana sepetlele,le maponesa a ne ale teng ho ngodisa setatemente.
P5	Yoh!ekaba bakganni bana batla fihla sepetle bantse ba phela?ho botsa Mpho.@eya Mpho,ba matsohong a bolokehilenge hona jwale,hobolela Thabo.empa haele Di palangwana tsena tsona disenyehile,lehaole jwang ditla lokiswa hape.
