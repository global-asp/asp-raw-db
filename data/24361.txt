page_text:

P1	Waliwo omwami gwebayitanga Lodipo. Yabeelanga ne bakyalabe ababili, Akai ne Akitela.
P2	Mukyala wa Lodipo asooka, Akai, yeyeka eyali ayambako maama we eyali omuzibe
P3	Olunaku lumu, mukyala wa Lodipo omuto,Akitela, yagamba, "tuvewo tugende mu kitundu kye dembe." Ekyalo kyebabelangako kyalumbibwanga abanyazi b'ente. Akitela yawulilanga atalina bukuumi.
P4	Lodipo yagamba Akitela nti maama we yali tayagala kusenguka. Akitela yalemelako nti basenguke, baleke ne maama wa Lodipo omuzibe. "Bwetutava wano nja kudila abazadde bange," Akitala yakaabila Lodipo.
P5	Lodipo yayagalanga nyo Akitela namwemaliza. Betegekela okusenguka. Omukyala asooka, Akai, yatunulila ebyali bigenda mumaaso noku buzabuzibwa.
P6	Maama wa Lodipo omuzibe yatuula ku diiba lye embuzi wansiwo omutti. Yali tamanyi ku kigenda mumaaso. "Tumuleka tutya kululwe?" Akai yewunya.
P7	Lodipo ne bakyalabe batiika ebintu byabwe ku ng'amya biili. Bagenda mubu silikilivu nga nomukyala omukadde tebamugambye nti welaba. Akai yali akaaba kubanga yali munaku eyo okuleka nazalawe kululwe.
P8	Omukyala omukadde yakaaba nayimba luno oluyimba lwe naku nga ayiita mutabaniwe.  Yewunya eyandi mulabilide. Yewunya eyandi mujje mukasana akwokya.
P9	Lodipo nebakyalabe webali bakasenguka, abanyazi baluumba ekitundu.
P10	Abanyazi babuuza omukyala omukade okubabulila mutabaniwe jyeyalaze. "Tugenda mugobelela tumutee," bamugamba. Yegayilila, "Temutaa omukyala asooka nabaanabe." Yabasaba bakomyewo endiga ye, Lodipo jyeyali atute naye.
P11	Abaajasi bagobelela Lodipo. Nga akyeteleza mu kitundu ekipya, mamugwako. Bamutaa ne mukyalawe omuto.
P12	Omukya asooka, Akai, yadayo n'endiga. Baabela bwombi mu sanyu.
