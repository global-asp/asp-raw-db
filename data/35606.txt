page_text:

P1	Mr Aliyu has a son who is disobedient.
P2	He does not listen  to his father.
P3	Salisu began to keep bad friends.
P4	Salisu started smoking and stealing from people.
P5	After some time, Salisu listened to his father's advice and become an obedient child.
