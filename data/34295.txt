title:	Katsi na Ṱukulu na zwivhungu
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Elke and René Leisink
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34295.png
desc:	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha khou tandula shango. Vha vhona luswayo kha muri. Zwino vha ḓo ita mini?

page_text:

P1	Hoyu ndi Katsi.@@Hoyu ndi Ṱukulu.
P2	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha vula vothi. Vha vula vothi ḽa nnḓu yavho.@@Katsi na Ṱukulu vha bvela nnḓa.
P3	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha a tshimbila.@@Vha tshimbila nṱha ha hatsi. Vha tshimbila vhukati ha miri. Vha tshimbila ḓakani.@@Vha swika huṅwe vha ima.
P4	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha vhona luswayo. Vha vhona luswayo kha muri.@@Katsi na Ṱukulu vha lavhelesa luswayo. Vha lavhelesa maipfi a re kha luswayo.
P5	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha mbo vhala.@@Vha mbo vhala luswayo, 'ni thanyele lupila.'
P6	Katsi a ri, "Lupila ndi mini?'@@Ṱukulu a ri, "Lupila ndi tshifuko."
P7	Katsi na Ṱukulu a vha vhoni lupila.
P8	Vha mbo fa nga zwiseo.@@Vha khou sea luswayo.@ @Vha khou sea mazwifhi a luswayo.
P9	Khathihi fhedzi aḽa maipfi ambo ḓi shanduka!
P10	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha mbo vhala maipfi maswa.@@Vha mbo vhala luswayo.
P11	'Ni thanyele tshiṱokola.'
P12	Katsi a ri, "Tshiṱokola ndi mini?"@@Ṱukulu a ri, "Tshiṱokola ndi tsha mitshelo."
P13	Katsi na Ṱukulu a vha vhoni tshiṱokola.
P14	Vha mbo fa nga zwiseo.@@Vha khou sea luswayo.@@Vha khou sea mazwifhi a luswayo.
P15	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha lavhelesa luswayo. Vha vhona zwivhungu kha luswayo. Zwivhungu zwa shandula luswayo!@@Katsi na Ṱukulu vha kwatula zwivhungu. Vha kwatula zwivhungu vha zwi laṱa kule.
P16	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha vhala luswayo. @@Vha mbo vhala luswayo, 'ni thanyele goriḽa.'
P17	Katsi a ri, "Goriḽa ndi mini"@@Ṱukulu a ri, "Goriḽa ndi ṱhoho khulukhulu."
P18	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha mbo vhona goriḽa. Katsi na Ṱukulu vha sumba goriḽa.@@Goriḽa ḽo hula. Goriḽa ndi ḽihuluhulu.@@"Katsi a ri, "Kha ri shavhe!" @Ṱukulu a ri, "Kha ri shavhe!"
P19	Katsi na Ṱukulu vha ri u shavha! Vha shavhesa.@@Katsi na Ṱukulu vha shavhela hayani.@@Katsi na Ṱukulu vha vala vothi.
P20	Vha vala vothi ḽa nnḓu yavho.
P21	Salani wee, Katsi.@@Salani wee, Ṱukulu.@@Salani wee, goriḽa. @@Salani wee, zwivhungu.
