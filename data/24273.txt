page_text:

P1	Abaibi babiri bayenda okwiiba duuma munimiro. Bali tibaidigani.
P2	Omwiibi eyasooka yaida yakuunguula esaawo yaduuma.
P3	Omwiibi owokubiri yayingila munimiro yomulimi, yaleka akaagali kuliya.
P4	Abaibi boombi bayaganana munimiro yaduuma.
P5	Boombi batya baluumuka mugira daandaawolo.
P6	Omwibi eyasooka yaaleka esaawo yaduuma.
P7	Omwibi owokubiri  yaaleka akaagali.
P8	Omulimi weyayida  yakoozesa akagaali okusituula esaawo yaduuma.
