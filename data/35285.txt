page_text:

P1	Ndidiniwe kukuhlala endlini imini yonke.@@Ndiyazikhumbula izihlobo zam.@@Ngumnqweno wam ukuba singaphuma phandle sidlale.
P2	Inye into endiyivayo yile ntsholongwane yeCorona!@@Ndinqwenela ukuba ihambe!
P3	Masidlale ngezinto zethu zokudlala.@@Umama uthi ukuba sihlala endlini, uThixo uzokusinceda asikhusele.
P4	Umnumzana Khumalo uyagula.@@Kuye kwafuneka ukuba aye esibhedlele.@@Masimthandazele ngoku.
P5	Nkosi Yesu, nceda uphilise uMnumzana Khumalo.@@Sikhusele kulentsholongwane yeCorona.@@Enkosi ngokubanathi xa siziva sisodwa sisoyika. Amen.
P6	"Nokuba ndihamba emfuleni wethunzi lokufa, Andiyi koyika bubi, ngokuba ùnam wena."@@IiNdumiso 23:4@@(Bible in isiXhosa 1859/1975 © BSSA 1975)
