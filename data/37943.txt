page_text:

P1	One summer afternoon, Paul and Paulina went for a picnic.
P2	They reached a nearby lake and prepared for lunch.
P3	The sky was cloudy and a light wind was blowing.
P4	"Paul, look! Look! Over there!"
P5	Small waves were seen sweeping ashore.
P6	As the sun came out of the cloud, a dark silhouette was seen swimming.
P7	"It must be a crocodile like those we've seen on TV"
P8	The creature swam nearer to two friends' spot and rose up.
P9	"Paulina, are you seeing it? It's a water lizard."
P10	
P11	The creature made a turn with its tail causing a splash and disappeared.
