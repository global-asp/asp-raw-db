title:	Shwér

lang:	Lámnsoʼ

desc:	Kimfer kee kum vitaáví vé shwer.

page_text:

P1	Shwéri dzəm wiyi. Wú wìy ne e shaà láʼ yér.
P2	Shwéri kér vitaáví. Wú tiìné kifá kém ké visəərí.
P3	M jum kifá kém ji m wàa koó, a shwéri tsə́y mo é bàm.
P4	Shwéri kfə̀n ne kiìkir. Wú lì ne kifá kém kù a koʼ sho koʼón é yuv.
P5	Kiìkir sùm ne mo súmen. M yòʼ yén kirə́ʼ. M yòʼ kum kifá.
P6	Kifá kém ké visəərí kí bèé lèn? A tàm bo kí tàám ne e kici.
P7	A tàm bo kifá kém kí dzəə́ féŋ e yuv.
P8	Shwéri kpùrín ne. M kù dzəə́ kár binkìrì.
P9	Shwéri kárir mo tiirì, m nèn a kiy virə́ʼ. M gháy ji shwéri sà dù a dzə̀ fee a?
P10	M yòʼ yén fo kifá kém kí dzəə̀n. M yòʼ dzəə́ yú fo shwéri kumè.
P11	A tàm kibvəə́shí m yèn kifá kém.
P12	Mo-óò wáa dù lèn fo laʼ fo shwer wúmóʼó sii kèʼ a wìy.
