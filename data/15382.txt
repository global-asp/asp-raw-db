title:	Yeɛjibni
author:	Ramzi Hachani
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Tunisian
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15382.png
desc:	Ħkēya ɛlel-ħājēt l-koll illi bneyya ṣɣīra tħibb taɛmelhom.

page_text:

P1	Yeɛjibni t-tengīz bi'l-ħbel.
P2	Waħdi.
P3	Yeɛjibni t-tengīz mɛa ṣħābi kīfkīf.
P4	Teɛjibni qrāyet l-ħkēyēt l-mumtɛa.
P5	Yeɛjibni l-meħwāṣ fi'l-hinċīr mɛa ṡħābi.
P6	Teɛjibni mɛāwnet ommi fi ɣsīl l-mēɛūn.
P7	Teɛjibni ktībet ħrūf kbār illi nnejjim naqrāhom b'shūla.
P8	Teɛjibni mēklet l-ɣlil l-mitnewwɛīn.
P9	Yeɛjibni r-rqād fi farċ kbīr.
P10	Yeɛjibni semɛān ommi kīf taħki ħkēyēt muŧīra.
P11	Ooh! L-ħājēt hēđūma l-koll yeɛjbūni!
