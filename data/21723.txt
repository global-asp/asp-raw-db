title:	Indlela iNtfutfwane lasindzisa ngayo liTuba
author:	Fakazile Armooh

desc:	Kunesomiso intfutfwana yome kakhulu. Iphuma iye emfuleni konatsa emanti

page_text:

P1	Kusehlobo lilanga lishisa kakhulu lihlatsi nalo lomile.
P2	Bese kungemalanga lamanyenti iNtfutfwane ingesawati emanti.
P3	"Ngidzinga nje kuphela litfonsi lemanti, noma ngabe liphuma ecembeni," kukhala iNtfutfwane lencane.
P4	Akusekho ngisho nematolo lawa.
P5	"Nangabe angiwatfoli emanti nyalo, ngitakufa," kukhala iNtfutfwane lencane.

"Kumele ngiye kulomfula lengike ngeva bakhuluma ngawo."
P6	"Umfula ugcwele futsi unemandla. Ubobona emanti angahambi nawe," kwecwayisa singwe lesihlakaniphile.
P7	INtfutfwane lencane  yome kakhulu. "Ngitakufa nakube angiwatfoli emanti."
P8	Yesuka yaya emfuleni iyobuka emanti.
P9	Yahamba yencamula etjanini lobomile nasemagaleni lowomile.
P10	Yahamba kwate kwevakala kuhaza kwemanti. 

Bekungemanti emfula! Kwevakala nekugobhota kwemanti.
P11	INtfutfwane yanele kubona emanti yatsatsa yatsi ngibonwa yini yawanatsa.
P12	Injabulo labenayo intfwane ayibonanga nekucamuka kwemagagasi.
P13	INtfutfwane yetama kubambelela eticintsini tetjani. Kodvwa loko akuzange kumsite ngobe emanti emuka nayo.
P14	"Ngisiteni bo! Maye! Ngicela ningisitebo!" Yamemeta.
P15	Ngasoleso sikhatsi kwefika liTuba libambe ligala ngemlomo.
P16	Latsi, "Sheshisa ugibele lapha."
P17	"Ngitawuhlala lapha kute ngibonge kahle kuTuba. Ngitawulindza ate abuye," kusho iNtfutfwane. 

"Ngitamlindza ate abuye futsi nakate atonatsa emanti."
P18	Kwatsi ngalelinye lilanga asalindzile emfuleni, kwachamuka bafana lababili baphetse tilingi tabo.
P19	"Kunelituba lelikhulu lelimhlophe lelinatsa kulomfula," kusholo munye webafana.
P20	"Namuhla litawuba kudla kwetfu kwakusihlwa," kusho umfana.
P21	"Angete nje ngivumele labafana babulale Tuba," kusho iNtfutfwane.

"Hheyi! Kodvwa nami ngimncane, yini nje lengingayenta?"
P22	Ngasoleso sikhatsi, Tuba wehlela emantini.
P23	Wasuka esihlahleni ngobe afuna kunatsa.
P24	INtfutfwane beseyi nelisu.
P25	Wazuba wayohlala enyaweni lalomunye umfana. Wacala kumncinta.
P26	Umfana wazuba. "Ekhu! Uyangilimata!" kukhala umfana.
P27	Tuba wetfuka wandizela etulu. Kwaba kuphepha kwayo iNtfutfwane.
P28	Lena ngiyo indlela iNtfutfwane labonga ngayo kuTuba ngekumsindzisa.
