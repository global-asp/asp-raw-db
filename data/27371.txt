page_text:

P1	Maama Wandhuba yakyaliraku muke we, Mwezi.
Mwezi  yaviiranga  ku luuyi  olundi  olweigulu. Wandhuba yaakoba  Kireri  ati, "Nd'ha kwira  mangu."
P2	Wandhuba bwe yavaawo,  Nsozi  yeesiba  ebikooyi ebyeru.
P3	Mpegho yali  nookwemuluguunhya ku Miti. Miti  yasungughala. Yaasuula amakoola  ghansi  goonagoona.
P4	Igulu yaatandiika okugumba. Yafuuka wa kivuuvu.
P5	Kireri yaanhiiga okubona kino. Yainhama okulira. Amaziga gaali kamaala.
P6	Ensi yoonayoona yatandiika okudhimira mu maziga yaadhabaadha.
P7	Ebyo nga biri bityo, ku luuyi lule, Ndhuba yaatuusa ekiseera ekyokusiibula muke we. Yaagwa Mwezi mu kafuba yaamusiibula yaira eka eghughe.
P8	Igulu yali musanhufu inho okubona nga Ndhuba afuuse gha bululu. Kileri yaavaala ekiteeteeyi kye ekya kiragala.
P9	Mpewo yaaja okulambala. Miti yagya okugolola Nsaga era yamwenheregeta.
P10	Kireri yaaba musanhufu okwiramu okubona inhina Ndhuba. Bwava agho bwaja mu kuzaanha.
P11	Busimbe kamaala bwakukunuka mu itaka bwakoba,"ne ekyo kyeene." 
Ensi yoonayoona yaasansaamuka.
P12	Maama Ndhuba yaayakira buli ghantu. Yaabakobera ati, "Nja kwira."
Yatumbukirairala.
