page_text:

P1	Enwere otu nwata nwanyi aha ya bụ Ayanda.@  @@  Ayanda bu so otu nwa nne na@  nna ya.@  @@  Nne na nna ya chọrọ ikewa.
P2	Ayanda kpebiri igbapu@  n’ulo.@  @@  Ọ gara n’oke ohia n’ihi@  na ọ maghi ebe ọzọ ọ nwere@  ike iga.
P3	Agu Owulu no n’ime@  ohia chụrụ Ayanda ọsọ, ọ wee rigoro n’elu osisi.@  @@  Agu owulu ahu chọrọ ita ya,@  ọ wee rigokwuo elu elu.
P4	N’elu ukwu osisi ahu, Ayanda hụrụ@  ọmalicha anyanwụ.@  @@  Onwee ihụnaṅya n’ebe@  anyanwụ nọ, wee kpebie inọ n’ime@  ohia
P5	Ayanda na-arigo ụkwụ@  osisi ahụ ụbọchi ọbụla ka ọ gwa anyanwụ@  ọkwụ.@  @@  Anyanwụ buruzia ezi@  na ụlọ ya.
P6	Otu ubochi, Ayanda@  hụrụ otu nwoke n’okpuru  osisi@  ahụ.@  @@  Nwoke ahụ bụ nna ya.@  @@  Ayanda rituru n’ukwu@  osisi ahụ, nna ya wee kowara@  ya ihe kpatara ọ ji noro ebe ahụ
P7	Ọ si, “Tupu amụọ gi,@  mu na nne gi na-ahurita onwe anyi ebea.@  Ọ bụ ebe pụrụ iche.”@  @@  Nna Ayanda wee kwuo,@  “Ọ bụ ukwu osisi a ka m na-arigo iji nweta udo ma mu na nne gi@  nwee esemokwu.”
P8	Oge adighi aṅya,@  nne Ayanda biakwuru n’ime ohia ahụ.@  @@  Ọ biara ka ya na@  nna Ayanda kpee udo.@  @@  Nke a bụ osisi udo ha.
