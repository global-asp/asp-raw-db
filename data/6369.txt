P1	Fruits,  fruits,  fruits.
P2	Fruit is sweet to eat.
P3	People eat different types of fruits.
P4	One may eat mangoes,  another may eat pineapples.
P5	We also enjoy oranges.
P6	Children enjoy apples.
P7	Birds enjoy corn.
P8	Oh fruits are good for health. People,  animals and birds like fruits so much.
