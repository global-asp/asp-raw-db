P2	She comes from a rich family.
P3	She cannot even cook food.
P4	She wakes up late to go to school.
P5	She has no friends and does not mind people.
P6	Her father loves her a lot.
P7	She sleeps in class.
P8	She does not respect her teachers.
P9	She walks out of her classroom slowly because she is lazy.
