desc:	Imbwa yamwirushira Petros. Basangala muno. Abetsa ba Petros bamubolera

page_text:

P1	Petros yali omwana yenyene. Yali omusomi omuchesi.
P2	Inyanga ndala Petros yahebwa obwiyangu okhutsia okhubaya nende abetsabe.
P3	Yalola imbwa niyihandire mwitikho. Petros nabola, "Ndalakhukhonya."
P4	Imbwa yasema, niibaya nende okhukhomba Petros mubusangafu.
P5	Abetsa ba Petros balonda imbwa eyo. Petros nabola, "Leshe! Leshe!"
P6	Imbwa yaria abasiani abo neyifisa.
P7	Abetsa bamunyola naliranga. Bamubolera, "Khulaikhaba imbwa yiyo."
P8	Petros shiyali nende obusubiri tawe. "Khulainyola." Abetsa baye babola.
P9	Baikhaba buli habundu. Petros nabola, "Inyala okhuba hambi nende khumwalo."
P10	Imbwa yenya okhukwa mumatsi. Petros nachibolera, "Olakwa mumatsi tawe."
P11	Imbwa yamwirushira Petros. Basangala muno.
P12	Abetsa ba Petros bamubolera, "Khubere alaba omwitsa wefu fwesi."
