title:	Kuponeswa Kwa Njiva naKaSvosve
author:	Precious  Gumbo
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	ChiShona
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/35452.png
desc:	The land is dry and Little Ant is very thirsty. He goes to the river for a drink and is swept away.

page_text:

P1	Chakanga chiri chirimo zuva richipisisa musango.
P2	KaSvosve akanga ave nemazuva asina mvura.
P3	"Ndinoda kadomwe kemvura, chero kachibva pashizha," KaSvosve akachema.
P4	Kana dova ranga raoma.
P5	"Kana ndisina mvura, ndingafa," akachema KaSvosve. "Ndinofanira kuenda kurwizi, rwandakanzwa nezvarwo."
P6	"Rwizi rwakazara uyezve rune simba. Runokuyeredza," akayambira tsindi.
P7	Asi KaSvosve akanga ane nyota zvakanyanyisisa. "Ndingafa ndikasanwa mvura." Nokudaro akaenda kunotsvaga rwizi.
P8	.
P9	Akafamba muhuswa nemiti yakaoma.
P10	Akafamba dzamara anzwa mvura ichifashuka. Rwaive rwizi! Akanzwa mafashamu.
P11	KaSvosve akanwa mvura inotonherera yakawanda.
P12	Akafara zvikuru zvekuti haana kuona mafashamu achiuya.
P13	KaSvosve akaedza kubatira pahuswa. Asi akayeredzwa nemvura.
P14	"Ndibatsirewo. Ndibatsireiwo kani!" akasheedzera.
P15	"Kasika kwira," akadaro Njiva, akatakura sanzu nomuromo.
P16	.
P17	"Ndichagara pano kusvikira ndatenda njiva," akafunga KaSvosve. "Ndichamu mirira dzamara adzoka kuzonwa mvura."
P18	Akamirira kudaro, pakasvika vakomana vaviri parwizi. Vaive nerekeni dzavo.
P19	"Pane njiva hombe chena inouya kuzonwa mvura pano," akadaro umwe wevakomana. "Tichaiita chisvusvuro nhasi manheru."
P20	.
P21	"Handinga regeri vakomana vachiuraya Njiva," akafunga KaSvosve. "Asi ndiri mudikidiki, ndingaita sei?"
P22	Ipapo Njiva akabhururuka kubva mumuti kuzonwa mvura.
P23	.
P24	KaSvosve akaronga zano.
P25	Akakwakukira pagumbo reumwe wevakomana. KaSvosve akaruma mukomana iyeye zvakasimba.
P26	Mukomana akakwakuka. "Maiwe!" akadaidzira.
P27	Njiva akavhunduka akabhururuka achitiza.
P28	Ndiwo matendero akaita KaSvosve kuna Njiva nekumuponesa kwaakaita.
