title:	Niareng’An
author:	Alfred Esinyen and Simon Ipoo
artist:	Sue Kramer
lang:	Ng’aturkana
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2752.png
desc:	Ng’agiran a ng’itorubei a ng’iboro alu ereng nacha paran daang.

page_text:

P1	Ilelebi ito adere nachukul nareng'an.
P2	Eboliasi Abongile ka amotoga nareng'an.
P3	Eting'it Deon ng'atur nareng'ak.
P4	Enapit eya eworu lo erarum loareng'an.
P5	Eyei akolong narengan kidiama apaakang.
P6	Eyei ekatuurut loareng'an itoweitae anapak.
P7	Eyei ayole nareng'an lopateit a Babalwa.
P8	Eyei ekitudung'et akim loarengan ni echunare.
P9	Sibhale lo enapit eworu lo erarum loareng'an.
P10	Itemonokin ng'esi ng'amuk nareng'ak.
P11	Nyitemokino kesi!
