desc:	Uboya bukaMvubu obuhle butshe bonke baphela.

page_text:

P1	UMvundlana wawuzihambela ngasemlanjeni.
P2	UMvubu wayezigramzela ingca emnandi eluhlaza.
P3	Ngempazamo, uMvubu wanyathela uMvundla.
P4	"Ndiyaxolisa kakhulu mhlobo wam, andikubonanga," watsho uMvubu.
P5	UMvundla wathi kuMlilo, "Hamba, uyotshisa uMvubu. Undinyathele."
P6	Kwevakala "u-wushu!" Watsho uMlilo ukubutshisa uboya bukaMvubu.
P7	Wabaleka uMvubu ukuya emanzini. Bonke uboya bakhe babutshe baphela.
P8	￼￼Yiyo loo nto imvubu ihambela kufuphi namanzi kuba yoyika ukutsha.
