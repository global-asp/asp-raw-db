page_text:

P1	Pǝ̂rǝ ma! Arà !@Ampǝrǝ n ce atɩ ri gafuŋfambɔrɩ gaɖǝn. Ʊshilé ɖǝn na gafuŋfambɔrɩ gaɖe ga lǝ wàà : « Ɩ cɔɔ kpa amʊ, n tɩ yɛɛ gʊyo kʊla ʊsoŋsono ʊɖǝn ʊ ɖa ɖaa gaɖo ma.
P2	Aɖʊwaa n yɔ gʊyuboɖoka n'ɩlajablaka kanɖi. Ama ŋgaɖee n cɔɔ sɔlɔ na yɛɛ awu ma na gǝŋ wàà, gɩfonyine.
P3	Ʊla nnɖe ʊ cɔɔ fana ! Ɩ sim ma ɖâ ʊlaŋ afol. Anyɩn ni ?  K'ɩ na faŋa wàà, gʊfaŋa gʊsoŋsono kʊ aa ?
P4	Kǝi, gafuŋfambɔrɩ ! Ŋkǝŋ na gʊrɛ́ɛ gʊ na jɩ. Gʊ tʊʊ tɔ̂ɔ akɩ alaa na sɔntɔ naa akur akaŋʊrʊ.
P5	Ʊla nnɖe ʊ cɔɔ fana ! Ɩ sim ma ɖâ ʊlaŋ afol. Anyɩn ni ?  K'ɩ na faŋa wàà, gʊfaŋa gʊsoŋsono kʊ aa ?
P6	Aaɩ ! Hmmm ! Gafuŋfambɔrɩ ! Ɩŋoŋo banɖaŋa n ɖa ŋkǝŋ ! Gaɖu gaɖe k'á sǝ̂ra aman n'ɩŋoŋo baɖe ba na kǝu na sir ma, afol na wooo !
P7	Ʊla nnɖe ʊ cɔɔ fana ! Ɩ sim ma ɖâ ʊlaŋ afol. Anyɩn ni ?  K'ɩ na faŋa wàà, gʊfaŋa gʊsoŋsono kʊ aa ?
P8	Aaɩ ! Gafuŋfambɔrɩ ! Ŋkǝŋ ʊbu na gǝŋ.  K'ɩ lee ʊla na ! Ʊ kara ʊbu wahalayar asampǝna ɩbʊmbɔnɔ.
P9	Ʊla nnɖe ʊ cɔɔ fana ! Ɩ sim ma ɖâ ʊlaŋ afol. Anyɩn ni ?  K'ɩ na faŋa wàà, gʊfaŋa gʊsoŋsono kʊ aa ?
P10	Hmmm ! Aaɩ, gafuŋfambɔrɩ ! Bʊsapí bʊ ɖa ŋkǝŋ na kuro kuro ! K'á sǝra afol gɩshɛbʊ ɖaa ŋkǝmprǝ nɖe n kaŋkǝr wáá ma na !
P11	Na baŋunii ba lǝ jɩ wàà : « Lêe koŋkaarɩ aboɖo ace alaŋlaŋ ʊla ʊfɔlɩ laŋ. » Ama nnɖe ʊ lee ka ʊla ʊsoŋsono nɖee ʊ pra gaɖo a ɖâ ʊlaŋ ma.
P12	Bʊsapí, ʊbu, gʊrɛ́ɛ n'ɩŋoŋo baŋunii bɩcine ɩ kǝr ɖaa gafuŋfambɔrɩ ga ɖa bagʊyo laŋ ma.@@Ampǝrǝ kɩɩja na kɩsǝi nɩ gǝŋ.
