page_text:

P1	"Ah, la!"
P2	"Lulu, wakati wa kwenda umefika," Mama anaita.
P3	Lulu yuko wapi?@@Je, yuko chini ya sofa?
P4	Je, yuko nyuma ya pazia?
P5	Lulu yuko wapi?@@Je, yuko karibu na jiko?
P6	Je, yuko katika kabati?
P7	"Lulu, wakati wa kwenda umefika! Uko wapi?" Mama anaita.
P8	"Wewe ndiye huyu sasa!" Mama anasema.@@"Sitaki kwendaaa!"
P9	"Ni lazima twende," Mama anasema.@@"Lakini, ninapenda hiki!"
P10	"Hebu twende sasa."
P11	"Lakini..."
P12	"Ninapenda hiki kweli!"
P13	"Na hiki pia!"
P14	"Na hiki vilevile!"@@"Hata na hiki!"
P15	"Tazama, unarejesha vitabu na kuomba vingine zaidi," Mama anasema.@@"YEE!"
P16	Lulu yuko wapi sasa?
