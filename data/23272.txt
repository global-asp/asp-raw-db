desc:	Fana loves animals. She spends time with them

page_text:

P1	Fana hamwe na eka ye nibatura omuri Debre Birhan, ekyibuga kyijjabirile omuri ethiopia. Fana aine omutima murungi kandi nomugyezi.
P2	Fana nakunda amatungo. Aine kappa, enkoko eibiri, embuzi hamwe nekyinyonyi. Namara obwire na matugo aga, nagalisya, kandi nazana nago.
P3	Eizooba rimwe, Fana akaba nazanira omwihema na banyani be,kuyarebire abaana nibarekyera amabare aha kyinyonyi, yababuza ati, "ahabwakyi nababonabonesa ekyinyonyi?" akebuza.
P4	Akareka okuzana rero yiruka ahabaana ababire ni barekyera amabare.

Ahakubaza banuani be tibaretegyerize ekyabire nikyigyenda omumisho, baheza bamukurata." murekyeraho okurekyera amaabare," Fana akayoba.
P5	Fana akakwata ebinyamishwa bibiri ebyahutara. yareba obuhuta ahamapaapa gabyo. Yasharamu kubitwara omuka kubileberera.
P6	Akabirisa. omwihagwe yagambire eka ye ekyiyarebire na nekyiyakozire eizooba edyo, nanokuyakyirize ebinyonyi ebyo.
P7	Omukasheshe Fana na neka ye bagyenda omwirwariro bafunayo omubazi gwe binyonyi.

Ahanyima yebiro bikye, obuhuta bwebinyonyi bwakyira. Fana akashemererwa munonga.
P8	Fana burijo nagambira banuani be," ninkunda anyamishwa. Eyamishwa nezo'omugasho aharitwe kandi nizinuani zaitu. Ntwine kuzirinda kandi tukazireberera.
