page_text:

P1	Sipho wavuka ekuseni waya engadzeni kuyohlanyela timbali.
P2	Utsite asahlanyele timbali letimbili kwana imvula.
P3	Kwagcwala emanti engadzeni yaSipho.
P4	Kutsite makuphuma lilanga ekuseni timbali letimbili tamila.
P5	Takhombisa injabulo ngekuchakata taba tinhle.
