page_text:

P1	Nhumirwa inho okutuuma omugugha ni bakagwa bange.
P2	Nhoonhebwa okughandiika ku lubaagho.
P3	Nhoonhebwa okusoma ebitabo ebirimu ebifaananie.
P4	Nhoonhebwa okulya ebibala eby'endhawulo.
P5	Nhoonhebwa okuyamba ab'eka ku mirimo eja 






















































Nhoonhebwa okubeeraku ab'eka ku mirimo eja ghaka.
P6	Nhoonhebwa okuwulirisa enfumo dha maama.
P7	Nhoonhebwa okutenduka mu buliri obugazi obukalamu.
P8	Nhoonhebwa okukola ebintu ebyo. lwe onhoonhebwa kukola ki?
