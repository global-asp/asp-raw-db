page_text:

P1	Lekha ekhubolere imbakha yanje.

Enjakanga shinga tsintsesere.
P2	Enzitsanga ewo omuyeka kumbirira.
P3	Efwe khuli abetsa.
P4	Endekhaanga eshiokhulia.
P5	Ekhuweresinjia obulafu mushiro.
P6	Endi nende obuchesi obunji.
P7	Endi mulala owoluyia lulwo.
P8	Obe meso niunanga ewuwo. Shindachama okhulekhwa sienyene tawe.
