title:	Liphohofolo Tsa Polasing
author:	Moeketsi Buku
artist:	Sandy Campbell
lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)
level:	First sentences

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2543.png
desc:	Liphohofolo kaofeela tse phelang polasing li etsa marata a tsona. Molemi ena na ebe o etsa lerata le fe?

page_text:

P1	Mamela liphohofolo tsa mahaeng.
P2	Khomo e re,  "M-m-u-u. M-m-u-u." Khomo e batla metsi.
P3	Poli e re,  "Me-e-e. Me-e-e." Poli e batla metsi.
P4	Pere e re,  "Yi-hi-hi-hi. Yi-hi-hi-hi." Pere e batla metsi.
P5	Fariki e re,  "Kgo-kgo-kgo." Fariki e batla metsi.
P6	Khoho e re,  "Ko-kolo-kolo." Khoho e batla metsi.
P7	Ntja e re,  "Hobu-hobu-hobu." Ntja e batla metsi.
P8	Molemi o re,  "Shhh! Metsi ke ana."
