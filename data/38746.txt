title:	My first time in school

desc:	The story is about how my grandmother bought me uniform and took me to school.Where I joined my agemates and even played with them.

page_text:

P1	My grandmother's name is Viola She often takes me out.I like staying with her all the time.She tells me so many stories about places and our past while I walk with her on the roads.
P2	My grandmother loves me so much.She takes me to the market and buys me beautiful clothes .
P3	She wanted to buy me school uniforms because every time I used to tell her that I wanted to join my agemates in school.She took me to the school uniform shop.
P4	I was so happy when she bought me the uniforms because I was going to see my friends there.She packaged the uniform and put them inside her bag.
P5	While at school I played football with my friends.Before we started playing our game captain made us sit so that we conduct ourselves.
P6	The game is so enjoyable.Though I could not hit the ball easily but I enjoyed running.
P7	When time for playing was over,the Captain called us again.She thanked us for taking part in the game.
P8	Now I met Tom a very strong footballer.He said that he was going to train me very well to be the best footballer in school .What a nice experience my first day in school!
