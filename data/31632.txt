title:	Nthowa Taani Achomoco Ahiraayano Mapururu?
author:	Little Zebra Books
artist:	Carol Liddiment
lang:	Elomwe

desc:	In this Nyungwe folktale

page_text:

P1	Nihiku nimoha, Namarokolo aamweeca musheresherye wa muhice.
P2	Chomoco tho aari weiwo eeceecaka alyaaka munyepu mukithi.
P3	Chomoco haamonne Namarokolo wi aari vaavaa, vanthowa nenla aamunyikala mweco ahisuwelaka.@@Namarokolo aahinla apacerya oruwana ariki: "We, Chomoco, honoona wi wookinyikala mweco?"
P4	Chomoco aamveka namarokolo wi amulevelele, ariki: "Ikharari sinnakimora, nthamwene aka, miyo nkaawoona, okilivelele!"@@Nari hiihaa Namarokolo hiiwale, aahi: "Ela weenreno mwaayiini! Nihiku nimoha onamoona! Onahaawela oliva!"
P5	Hiihaa Namarokolo aaya oloca ni Mooro ii: "Ovinye wamweeyele Chomoco ntakhara aakinyikala akhumaka mmaahini ayaaka olya munyepu!"@@Mooro waahaakhula, oriki: "Yeeyo tahi echu, nthamwene. Kinamweerano yeeyo okivenkaawe."
P6	Elukuluku ekina, Chomoco aanalya munyepu ociiwene wa muhice. Mooro wamphwanya wi: "Mwaahirweeliwa." Mooro opacerya weeyela mapururu oothene a Chomoco.
P7	Chomoco aapacerya wunla achimakela mmaahini. Mapururu awe oothene yaavya. @@Chomoco haamaala wunla, ariki: "Mapururu aka oothene Mooro oheeyela! Mooro, oheeyela mapururu aka oothene. Mapururu aka aacakanela! Mapururu aka ooreerasha!"
P8	Vanthowa nenla, Chomoco hanakhala ociiwene wa maahi, oovaka weeyeliwa ti Mooro. @@Namarokolo aatepa ohakalala aamoonneiye Chomoco eeyeliwe ni Mooro, aahi: "Eyaa koocikiha mukokoko."
