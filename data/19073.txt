title:	Mor Koth Kodi Lith Koth
author:	Owino Ogot
artist:	Jesse Breytenbach
lang:	Dhopadhola

desc:	Mor koth gi wode

page_text:

P1	Cango,  cango oyido Mor koth gi Lith koth jo bedo i piny ka gi ji.
P2	Mor koth obedo min Lith koth.
P3	Lith koth oyido obedo jange aka doko obedo ja kwinyo.
P4	Kisi ka Lith koth omako nge oyido kidho kisi kamoro waŋo udi ni,  "Pia-la-la-la,  pia-la-la-la!" gi redho yen. Go oyido nyeko kiri ndelin aka ruman neko ji.
P5	Kisi ka Lith koth otimo kite gikipiny me,  min go lwoŋo go gi koko ni,  "Bum-rambo-la-la-la,  la-bum," ka mito ni wogiki gi kenyo aka doko wokiri womedi nyeko gikipiny.
P6	To Lith koth ku dewo kwoŋ gi ma min waco. Go oyido onyo medo nyeko gikipiny tekin nge medi mako go.
P7	Hongo ma cowe ji jo dhiro ywak pajo ri kere.
P8	Kere owodho cik ni Mor koth gi wode,  Lith koth,  jo ripo weyo loka no jo wokadhi jo wobedi bor gi pecin pa ji.
P9	Meno ku konyo tektek. Ka Lith koth omako nge fodi waŋo awaŋa lule ni,  "Pia-la-la-la,  pia-la-la-la!" aka ruman mac lak to waŋo kir ndelin.
P10	Ji jo goyo koko ri kere kendo.
P11	Kere oryemo woko lith koth gi mor koth i piny. Omiyo jo bedo i polo,  kama jo kunyali timo iye teko.
P12	Woki ndirino ka Lith koth omako nge fodi go timo ni,  "Pia-la-la-la,  pia-la-la-la!"  Aka fodi wawinjo yok mere,  ka bwoko go ni,  "Bum-rambo-la-la-la,  la-bum!"
