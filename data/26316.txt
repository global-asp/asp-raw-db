desc:	Kibonerezo ki ekigheebwa omulenzi eyalya ebibala ebingi? SINIINI BATAAMYE OMUVUNUZI.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lulala, maama yaagula  ebibala  eby'endhawulo  kamaala.
P2	"Li lwetunaafuna kubibala?" Twabuuza.
"Twidha kubirya obwire bwaleero," maama  yakoba.
P3	Muganda wange Bulayimu muluvu.
Yaloza kubuli  kibala. Yalya ebisinga  bungi.
P4	"Bona  Bulayimu  kyeyakoze!" Muganda ghe yaakoba.
"Buylayimu tafaayo ate mwimi," Nakobye.
P5	Maama  yali n'obusungu  ku luuyi lwa Bulayimu.
P6	Twena twali baniivu  ku  luuyi  lwa  Bulayimu.
Aye  Bulayimu  yali  nga  tiyenenhia.
P7	"Olikuja  kubonereza  Bulayimu?" omutoyaabuuza.
P8	"Bulayimu, bwoneenenhia  amangu, oidha  kusonighibwa," Maama we amulabula.
P9	Bulayimu  yaatandiika  obutawulira  bukalamu.
P10	"Munda  muli  kunuma  inho,"Bulayimu yaakuba  akaama akaana  akato.
P11	Maama  yategeera  lwaki  Bulayimu  mulwaire.
"Ebibala  byali  biri kumubonereza," Yalowooza.
P12	Oluvanhuma, Bulayimu yaasaba ekisonhigho. "Tikairayo kuba  muluvu," Yasubiza.
