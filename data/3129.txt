title:	Uminti No Gwebu
author:	Kholeka Mabeta
artist:	Sorit Gupto
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First words

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/3129.png
desc:	UMinti uyathanda ukudlala kodwa uyayonqena isepha kwaye akafuni kuhlamba. Ngobunye ubusuku waphupha ngeentsholongwane. Ingaba eliphupha lizakuguqula/lingatshintsha iingcinga zakhe ngococeko?

page_text:

P1	UMinti uyakuthanda ukudlala namabhabhathane.
P2	Kwakunye neentaka.
P3	Uyakuthanda ukudlala ngezikhitshana zamaphepha.
P4	Uyakuthanda kanti nokwenza amapomakazi ngesanti.
P5	Xa uMinti ebuyela ekhaya,  umama wakhe umcela ukuba acoce ikhaya lakhe. Kodwa uyala.
P6	"Ndiyazicaphukela iisepha!" ukhwaza atsho.
P7	Kwathi ngabusuku buthile uMinti wafumana iphupha. Ipomakazi lakhe lesanti laligcwele iintsholongwane,  ezinye zazimhlasela.
P8	Nanko uMinti eleqwa ziintsholongwane. Akazange abalekele obu bomi! Wakhala ekhwaza,  "NcedaNceda!"
P9	Ngephanyazo,  kwavela uGwebu inkosi yeesepha. "Minti musa ukoyika," watsho uGwebu. "Hlaselani ezi ntsholongwane!" watsho uGwebu inkosi yeesepha eyalela umkhosi wakhe wogwebu.
P10	Yeka oko umkhosi wogwebu ukuleqa ezo ntsholongwane!
P11	Nanamhla uMinti uyakuthanda ukusebenzisa isepha. Uyazihlamba azigutyule ukuze acoceke.
