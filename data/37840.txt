page_text:

P1	Lo nguKati.@@Lo nguNja.
P2	UKati noNja bafuna ukuzoba. @@UKati noNja bafuna ukufaka umbala.@@UKati noNja bafuna ukwenza ezobugcisa.@@UNja uyaqalisa.@@Uthatha ipensile kunye nephepha.@Iphepha sisikwere.
P3	Kuqala uNja uzoba umbhoxo. @@Lo mbhoxo ngumzimba kaKati. @@Halala, kwaza kwalula!
P4	Waza wazoba oonxantathu ababini phezu kombhoxo. @@Oonxantathu ababini bamele iindlebe zikaKati. Emva koko wazoba unxantathu omnyama phakathi kombhoxo. @@Unxantathu omnyama umele impumlo kaKati. Waza emva koko wazoba umgca entla kwempumlo.
P5	Ngoku uzoba amehlo kwakunye namashiya kaKati.@@Amehlo ngamachaphaza amabini. @@Amashiya yimigca emibini. @@Halala, kwaza kwalula!
P6	Waza wazoba umlomo kaKati. @@Umlomo ngumgca.@@Ukhangeleka ngathi ngunobumba 'w.' Waza wazoba iinwele zikaKati. @@Iinwele ezintathu ngasekunxele kwakunye nezinye ezintathu ngasekunene.
P7	Umzobo sele uza kugqitywa. @@Uzoba iingalo nemilenze zikaKati. @@Iingalo nemilenze yimigca, aze azobe iinzwane zikaKati.@@Iinzwane zizangqa. Waza wazoba umsila kaKati. @@Umsila ngumgca omde.
P8	Ekugqibeleni uzoba imigca emzimbeni kakati aze awufake umbala o-orenji. @@Waza wagqibeka umzobo. @@Halala, kwaza kwakuhle!
P9	Ngoku lithuba likaKati. @@Uthatha ipensile kunye nephepha. @@Iphepha luxande.
P10	Kuqala uKati uzoba umbhoxo. @@Umbhoxo ngumzimba kaNja.@@Halala, kwaza kwakuhle!
P11	Waza wazoba izinto ezimbini ezimnyama phezu kombhoxo. @@Ezi zinto zimnyama zimbini ziindlebe zikaNja. Waza wazoba isangqa esimnyama phakathi kombhoxo. @@Esi sangqa simnyama yimpumlo kaNja. Waza wazoba umgca phezu kwempumlo.
P12	Ngoku uzoba amehlo kwakunye namashiya kanja. @@Amehlo ngamachapahza amabini.@@Amashiya yimigca emibini.@@Halala! Kwaza kwagqibelela!
P13	Waza wazoba umlomo kaNja.@@Umlomo ngumgca.@@Kwakhona umlomo umile oku kukanobumba 'w.' Waza wazoba amachaphaza ajikeleze umlomo kaNja. @@Amachaphaza amathathu ngasekunxele kwakunye namanye amathathu ngasekunene.
P14	Umzobo sele uza kugqitywa. @@Uzoba iingalo nemilenze kaNja. @@Iingalo nemilenze yimigca, aze azobe iinzwane zikaNja.@@Iinzwane zizangqa. Waza wazoba umsila kaNja. @@Umsila ukhangeleka ngokungathi yintyatyambo.
P15	Ekugqibeleni uzoba izangqa ezimnyama emzimbeni kaNja aze afake umzimba umbala omthubi. @@Umzobo ugqityiwe. @@Halala, kwaza kwamangalisa!
P16	Sala kakuhle Kati. @@Sala kakuhle mzobo kaKati.@@Sala kakuhle Nja.@@Sala kakuhle mzobo kaNja.
