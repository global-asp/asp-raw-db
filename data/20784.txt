desc:	Pamene ana badwa mwana wa njobvu

page_text:

P1	Tonse tidziwa kuti njobvu ili ndi citambi cacitali.
P2	Koma pci yambi citambi cinali cifupi ndiponso conenepa.
P3	Pamene ana bodwa mwana wa njobvu, anali ndi funso ku nyama iliyonse.
P4	Ana dabwa paku utali nyama likiti. Dabwana ana funsa, "Kodi ndi chani nyamalikiti ali ndi mukosi wau tali?"
P5	Dabwana ana dabwantso pomwe ana ona cipembele, ana funsa kuti, "Kodi ndicani nyanga yako ndiyo sompa ku sogolo?"
P6	Dabwani ana dabwanso pe ana wona mvuu. Ndipo ana funsa kuti, "Nicifukwa ndicani uli ndi matso wofila monga moto?"
P7	Ndipo ana dabwanso pe ana nkona. Dabwani ana funsa, "Kodi nkona umadya ciani mu madzulo?"
P8	"Maloza! Usaka bwezepo ku funsa funso iyo!" anamu cienjeza amai ake. Dabwiso ana cokapo wokumudwa.
P9	Mo fulu mila kwalala ocenjela ana uza wa njobvu, "ndi satile ku msinje nikaku wonesa cakudya cakumazulo ca nkona."
P10	Ndipo mwana wa njobvu ana konka kwalala kumu sinje kosa ziwa.
P11	Mwana wa njobvu ana salika mu mpepete mwa musinje. ANa yangana mu manzi no funsa, kodi cakundya camumazalo cako nicani?
P12	"Taone," mwala wamu mpepete wa musinje una peleka moni. Dabwana ana yanka nati, "Yewo." Dabwana ana funsa, "Ko di cakundya camumazulo cako nicani?"
P13	Belana ndiku uze, "Una yanka mwala", Pitiliza ku welama." Dabwana ana welamisa."
P14	"Kwacha!" mpumo yake ina guiliwa ndi mano ya nkona. "Waciona! Lelo uza kala cakudya caku mazulo cangwena," ana tero kwalala.
P15	Dabwani ana nkala pansino donsa mwa mphamvu, koma nkona ana gwilisa.
P16	Pomwepo citamba ca njobvu cina donsomoka koma cina talimpa ndiku tali mpilafu. "Bboo!" anagwa pansi.
P17	Ndipo nkona ina bwelela mumanga mwau kali cifukwa copanda kudya cakudya camumazulo.
P18	Mwana wa njobvu ana yangana citamba cake. Koma, sana one mapelo acitamba co!
P19	Citamba cake cina talimpa mwakuli ana kwanisa ku tyola zipaso mu mitengo ya itali.
P20	Ndipo ana kwanisa kuzitila manzi pa tupi lake lonse zuwa lita lenta. Ku fikila lelo njobvu zonse zili ndi zitamba zazitali.
