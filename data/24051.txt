page_text:

P1	Ono ye muwala ayitibwa Noorah ate selah lyelinya lya dole ye
P2	Noorah ayagala nyo dole ye. Noorah azzanya ne dole ye Bulirunaku. Noorah yebaka ne dole ye bulikiilo.
P3	Olunaku lumu, Noorah yali talaba dole ye. Yanonya buliwamu, naye nga talaba Selah. Noorah yali mwelalikirivu.
P4	Noorah teyakimanya nti mamawe yali atute selah kwozebwa.yayanikadole ebweru ekale.
P5	"Silaba dole yange,"Noorah yakaba."tewelalikirila,"maama nayogela.
P6	Akawungezi ako,maama  wa Noorah yawa Noorah dole ye.Dole yali etukula.Selah yali atunula nga dole empya
P7	Noorah yali musanyufu nyoo.Nabukabukaa.Nazinaa.Nasekela wagulu nyoo.
P8	Neyabaza maama we.
