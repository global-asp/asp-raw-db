title:	Umukoosi Wasame Ni Umukoosi Wakurwe
author:	Milton Eridad Shissa
artist:	Joshua Waswa
lang:	Lumasaaba
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/3174.png
desc:	Mr. Fly and Mr. Big head were two great friends. Then one day they argued about who could swim across the river.  They decided to go out and swim to prove who was right.  Mr. Big Head failed to swim. Mr. Fly laughed so much that his mouth became wider and wider…

page_text:

P1	Oyu niye Umukoosi Wasame.
P2	Oyu niye Umukoosi Wakurwe.
P3	Umukoosi Wakurwe ni Umukoosi Wasame baaba basaale. Shifukhu shitweela,  baatsya khukyeendakakho boombi.
P4	Bakyeenda boola khu ndulo khwe luluutsi,  baarakikha khuukhaabisana.
P5	"Iwe s'unyala khuruuka luluutsi luno ta!" "Iseese inyala! Iwe niye ukhanyala ta."
P6	Umukoosi Wasame waaloma ari,  "Iwe ruuka"! Umukoosi Wakurwe weelamo ari,  "Taawe,  iwe nyoowa uruuke." Aryo Umukoosi Wasame waapuruurukha waambukha luluutsi.
P7	Lwanyuma Umukoosi Wakurwe naye waaruuka waakwa mu meetsi. Waarakikha khukhwisiikha aasi lwekhuuba kumurwe kweewe kwaba kumuboofu naabi.
P8	Umukoosi Wasame waatsekha ari,  "Hee heeeeeee"! Ne khuutsekha khuno aatsekha naabi shaakila kumunwa kweewe kwaaranikhamo khabili khukhwaama khu lubeka lutweela khukhwoola khu luundi!
