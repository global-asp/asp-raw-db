title:	Omuruchi omubi
author:	Peter Mandu
artist:	Marleen Visser
lang:	Oluwanga
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Neshichiranga imbusi neiloba okhutsia neyikhweswa.

page_text:

P1	Imbusi yali omuuruchi wetsisolo nende amayoni.

Yalanga omukhung'ano.
P2	Imbusi yababirambu yaloora amalooro.

Bosi barecheresia.
P3	Amalooro kali khunzala.

"Khukhole khurie?" Ipusi yareba.
P4	Ingokho nende Liyoyo baboola, "Nobulayi khubishe efyakhulia ewomuruchi."
P5	Omuruchi Imbusi yasunga, "Mubohe yesiyesi oulalondata amalako."
P6	Efise efyokhwaula omuruchi omuyakha fyola.

Baula Ipusi.
P7	Imbusi shiyenya Ipusi ibe omuruchi tawe.

"Nesie omuruchi."
P8	Imbusi yaloba okhuyiira efilibwa.

Ing'ombe yareba, "Khukholekhurie?"
P9	Tsisolo nende amayoni vyasungana.

Vyamusinyishira Imbusi.
P10	Imbwa yasunga, "Olwa yali omuruchi ndali halala ninaye."
P11	Likondi liasunga, "Ndaheresia abanaye obwoya obutotro."
P12	Inguruwe yalila, "Olwayali omuruchi ndamulindiranga indaloyaye."
P13	Likondi liareba, "Imbusi yaparanga okhuruka okhula okhufwakhwaye?"
P14	Tsisolo tsiafuchirisania. Okhuula Imbusi yiire efyakhulia ewomwami Ipusi.
P15	Olwebanyola Imbusi, yababolelambu, "Nembe omuruchi ukhula okhufwa."
P16	Tsisolo tsiamukalusia, "Khulakhuboha omukoye
khukhuyire ewomwami."
P17	Ing'ombe yakhwesa Imbusi okhuchiira ewomuruchi.

Tsisolo tsiekhoya.
P18	Neshichiranga Imbusi neiloba okhutsia neyikhweswa.
