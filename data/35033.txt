page_text:

P1	I wonder if there is another me.
P2	That looks just like me.
P3	That talks just like me.
P4	That has a curly tail. Just like mine.
P5	Someone with eyes and ears like mine.
P6	That even laughs just like me.
P7	Who can do what I can do.....
P8	...and can't do what I can't do.
P9	I searched everywhere. @@I even went to the park to look behind the big tree.
P10	I looked up and down, left and right and all around.
P11	I looked at every animal I could find.
P12	But nowhere could I see, anybody that looked just like me.
P13	I am the only me. I am very special you see.
P14	But I am not the only special one. @@There is only one of you too!
