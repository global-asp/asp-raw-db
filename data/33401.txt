desc:	Wake wakulalela yini loko umtimba wakho lokutjela kona?

page_text:

P1	Lamuhla angiyi esikolweni. Liholidi!
P2	Lamuhla ngeke ngiyibukele i-TV, njengobe gesi ungekho.
P3	Yini lengitoyenta?@@Lamuhla ngitolalela umtimba wami!
P4	Kwekucala, kufanele ngithule dvu! Kute ngikwati kulalela umtimba wami.
P5	Nako-ke, nyalo sengingakwati kuva nangabe ngiphefumula.@@Ngiyaphefumula ngidvonsa umoya ngiphindze ngiwukhiphe, ngiyawudvonsa ngiphindze ngiwukhiphe.
P6	Futsi ngingaphefumulela etulu. Sssssssssssss!@@Bese ngiphefumulela phansi. Mmmmmmm.
P7	Nyalo sengingayiva inhlitiyo yami nayishaya!@@Doodom, doodoom. Dooo doom.
P8	Ngingakhona yini kwenta inhlitiyo yami kutsi ishaye ngekushesha nobe ibe nemsindvo?@@Yebo, ngekutsi ngizube ngiye etulu naphansi tikhatsi letingemashumi lamabili.
P9	Buka-ke nyalo, inhlitiyo yami seyishaya ngekushesha.
P10	Nangibeka iminwe yami esihlakaleni, ngitokwati kuva kushaya kwenhlitiyo yami!
P11	Ngingakwati kutiva nangabe ngihleka.@@Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Ngingakwati kutiva nangabe ngikhala.@@Hhiiiiii, hhiiiiii!
P13	Ngingakwati kutiva nangabe ngishaya tandla.@@Klap, klap, klap.
P14	Ngingasiva sisu sami nasenta umsindvo lomkhulu!@@Guddu, guddu, guddu.
P15	Sisu sami sitsi, "Ngiphe kudla!"
P16	Likhala lami liva liphunga lelimnandzi lemakhekhe labhakwa ngumake edladleni.@@Futsi nyalo sengifuna kuva imihlatsi yami nangabe seyihlafuna lawo makhekhe!
