desc:	This story is about the different sorts of books my family reads.

page_text:

P1	Waandayase Kutosi ali khuusoma shitabo sye lileekelo.
P2	Maayi ali khuusoma i Bayibuli.
P3	Umukooko wase Nelima ali khuusoma shitabo shishikanikha khu bye khuuteekha.
P4	Paapa waandaye wa paapa ali khuusoma luupapula lwe kamakhuuwa.
P5	Kuukhu ali khuusoma shiitabo.
P6	Umukooko wase umukyeekhe Nekesa naye ali khuusoma shiitabo she bifwaananyi.
P7	Indi khuusoma shiitabo shino.
P8	Imbuka inoola isome buli shiindu.
