desc:	About different types of shoes

page_text:

P1	There are shoes for all occasions.
P2	Shoes for when the sun shines go 'flip-flop'.
P3	Shoes for ladies at a party go 'clip-clop'.
P4	Shoes for girls and boys at school go 'tough-turf'.
P5	Shoes for daddies at work go 'shift-shaft'.
P6	My favourite shoes are quiet and clean.@@Not like my brother's noisy, muddy boots!
P7	My favourite shoes are prettier than my sister's smelly running shoes.@@My favourite shoes are for when I am relaxing.
P8	These are the shoes I wear at home. @@They are soft. @@They hug my feet and go 'fush-fush'.
