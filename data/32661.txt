title:	The search for Water

desc:	The animals search for water during a drought. This story was created at the Story-making Workshop Facilitated by Mimi Werna

page_text:

P1	The animals gathered at the town square to talk about the drought.
P2	Fox, hare and donkey decided to go to the well across the valley of Ajalango.
P3	Fox, Hare and donkey prepared for their journey picking out cloths that will serve them.
P4	Fox, Hare and donkey set out on their journey with a big bowl.
P5	As they walked on, they saw an ant gathering wood. They asked him if he knew where they could find water. He asked them to follow him.
P6	They followed him. Ant met an old man leaning on a stick. He asked him if he could help with water for his friends. The old man said, "yes."
P7	The old man leaning on a stick gave the ant and his colony water. Ant was worried about his new friends. He asked the old man for some more water.
P8	The ants beckoned on all the animals to assemble so they can all share from the generosity of the old man leaning on a stick.
