title:	Nna Le Ba Lelapa La Me

artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Setswana

desc:	E ke kgang-khutswe ka ga mosimane le ba lelapa la gagwe le le sa tlwaelegang!

page_text:

P1	Nna le kgogodumo ya me re lebelela dinonyane tsa me mo loaping.
P2	Nna le dinonyane tsa me re kopana le tshwene mo setlhareng.
P3	Nna le tshwene ya me re tshameka le ditlhutlwa tsa me mo thabaneng.
P4	Nna le dithutlwa tsa me re bopa dibolo tsa ditshwene ka taka.
P5	Nna le tshwene ya me re ja dipanana le Tshwene-kgolo letsatsi le letsatsi.
P6	Nna le Tshwene-kgolo ya me re tshaba Rre Khudu thata.
P7	Nna le Rre Khudu re ya gae ka iketlo le Mohumagatsana Noga.
P8	Nna le Mohumagatsana Noga re rata go opela dipina fa thoko ga noka.
P9	Nna le ba lelapa la me re dula mmogo ka boitumelo jo bogolo!
