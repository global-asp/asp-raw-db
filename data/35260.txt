P1	Ndanyala negala mun'umba hhuba lose.@Ndoyemula bakhwinya bangu.@Kadzene ntingabhuda kuhhe tikanozana.
P2	Ndowha kulebgwa nekwe bhembolufu.@Kadzene lingapela!
P3	Ayizanise bodoli zwedu.@Mme bati tikagala mun'umba, Ndzimu banowotidzibilila.
P4	Tate Nleya banogwala. Bayenda kubuwongelo.@Ayibatembezeleleni saku.
P5	He Yesu, Tokumbila mutjidze tate Nleya.@Mutidzibilile mubhembolufu.@Toboka kubanaswi, atilitoga tohla.@Akujalo.
P6	"Nenguba ndiyenda muntuzi wenkuku welufu, akuna bubi gwandinohla, nekuti munami." @Njimbo 23:4
