page_text:

P1	One day a boy named Russell was going for a trip with his sister her name was lisa they first went to the zoo they  saw a lion and a elephant.
P2	We also want to go to zebras said Russell and Lisa the parents let them go and they saw zebras.
P3	We also want to see every  animal the parents let  them go.
P4	We also went to a farm and we saw a cow and a chicken and a donkey a dog a bunny and a horse.
P5	We also took a photo shoot.
P6	We saw a rainbow  what a amazing trip we had the end.
