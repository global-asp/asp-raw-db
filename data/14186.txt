desc:	This family has a wonderful day together at the zoo.

page_text:

P1	Today we are going to the zoo.
P2	We are going in dad's car.
P3	We are going to see animals.
P4	Look! An elephant. Look! A lion.
P5	We walk through the whole zoo!
P6	Look! Ice cream. Look! Sweets.
P7	And there is a clown with balloons.
P8	Dad buys us nice things.
P9	Soon we must go home.
P10	We saw many animals today!
P11	We are happy and tired.
P12	I wish every day was like today!
