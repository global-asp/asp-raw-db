title:	Eŋombe Ey’Amaliholiho
author:	Ivan Kaibo
artist:	Danielle Bruckert
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2195.png
desc:	Olugano luno lwabaŋere. Luŋamba hu ŋombe eyiba mu hifaanani hyehidole mu muusi aye owiire yahola ebyamaliholiho.

page_text:

P1	Yino ŋombe. Ŋombe enyene endaŋi!
P2	Omuusi eba ŋombe ya bulijo.
P3	N'egululusa ekayiti.
P4	Oba n'esamba omupiira.
P5	Owiire yiba ŋombe y'amaliholiho!
P6	Eŋonia obulamu.
P7	Era n'ohusoosa ababbwaga amateeka.
P8	Ne singa olimbirira bugali waba n'ehi oŋemba.
P9	Eŋombe yino eŋanga ohuhuhyalira weesi!
P10	Nageeme ŋano.
