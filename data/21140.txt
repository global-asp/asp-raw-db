page_text:

P1	Tufaa lilianguka kutoka mti aliokuwa amelala Sungura.
P2	Sungura alisikia sauti ikisema, “Kimbia Sungura kimbia!”
P3	Sungura alimwambia Kuku, “Kitu kilianguka nami nikasikia, ‘Kimbia Sungura kimbia!’”
P4	Kuku alianza kukimbia aliposikia alivyosema Sungura.
P5	Walimwambia Mbwa, “Sungura alisikia kitu kikianguka naye akakimbia.”
P6	Mbwa alianza kukimbia pamoja na Sungura na Kuku.
P7	Mbwa alimwambia Farasi, “Sungura alisikia kitu kikianguka naye akakimbia.”
P8	Farasi alianza kukimbia nao.
P9	Punda alisema, “Mbona mnakimbia?” Farasi alijibu, “Sijui.”
P10	Punda pia alianza kukimbia nao.
P11	Punda alimwambia Ng’ombe, “Sungura alisikia kitu kikianguka akakimbia.”
P12	Ng’ombe alikuwa na wasiwasi. Alianza kukimbia pia.
P13	Ng’ombe alimwambia Paka, “Sungura alisikia kitu kikianguka naye akakimbia.”
P14	Paka alianza kukimbia na wanyama wengine.
P15	Walimkuta mvulana. 

Aliwauliza, “Kwani hamjui kilichotendeka?”
P16	“Ni mimi niliyesema, “Kimbia Sungura kimbia!” Mvulana alicheka.
