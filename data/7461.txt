page_text:

P1	Mina nedayinasoli lami sibuka izinyoni esibhakabhakeni.
P2	Mina nezinyoni zami sihlezi nemfene esihlahleni.
P3	Mina nemfene yami sidlale nezindlulamithi eqgumeni.
P4	Mina nezindlulamithi zami sakhele inkawu ibhola lobumba.
P5	Mina nenkawu yami noGolila sidla obhanana zonke izinsuku.
P6	Yonke indlela imfene yami nenkawu besaba uMnumzane Fudu.
P7	Mina noMnumzane Fudu sihamba kancane kancane,  siya ekhaya noNkosazana Nyoka.
P8	Mina noNkosazana Nyoka siyathanda ukusisiza sicule amaculo eceleni kwechibi.
P9	Mina nomndeni wami wonke sihlala ngokukhulu ukujabula!
