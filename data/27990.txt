desc:	Lyn akooze ebintu biingi okuteeka akabaga akamazalibwa ge

page_text:

P1	Lynne,Oupa Karel ni Anton baali bategeeka ebiintu eby'akabaga akamazaalibwa ga Lynne.
P2	Anton ni Lynne bagya okufuumba keeki.Oupa Karel asoma ebigobererwa.
P3	"katutabule ebiluungo binho mu kibya ekinhenhe".
P4	Anton yatabula ebirungo yakoola ensoobi enhenhe.
P5	"keeki buti eghulikika nga ezitoogha" Oupa karel yayogera.
P6	"mikwaanho gyo batuuse"Oupa kare yayogera.
P7	Anton yaloghoza nti ekirabo ekyaasose kyandhiba kizito kuba kyaali kinhenhe.
P8	gwaali mutto nga gogheghuka.
P9	Anton yaloghoza ekirabo ekiiraku kyandhiba ekigheghufu kuba kyali kitono.
P10	kyaali kighumbe ekisiige nga kizitogha inho.
P11	abaana bakoba nti latini nh'ekighumbe ekizitogha nh'ekimu nhi latini yebiviiri.
P12	keeki ya lynne ya silila.
P13	Lynne yali munhiivu inho.
P14	Auntie mavis yabuza anton okuteeba ekirabo kye byekizitogha.
P15	"kizitoogha nhekimu nga keeki esiliire,"Anton yayogera.
P16	buli muntu yayogera,"amazalibwa ageisanhu Lynne."
