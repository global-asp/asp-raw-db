page_text:

P1	My school is far away from my home.
P2	I walk alone along a busy part to school.
P3	One day, on my way to school, I saw a dog running towards me barking wo! wo!
P4	I ran quickly home to my mother to report.
P5	My mother held my hand and walked with me back to school.
P6	I never had to walk alone to school again.
