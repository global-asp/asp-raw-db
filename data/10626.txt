title:	Ntate Meharo O Meharo

lang:	Sesotho (Lesotho)

desc:	Ntate Meharo o leka ho fumana nama e ngata ka letsatsi le leleng.Empa ha atlehe. Hase hantle hoba meharo.

page_text:

P1	Khalekhale motseng o bitosang Thoteng,  hone ho lula monna ea bitsoang Meharo. O ne a rata hoja nama haholo.
P2	Kaleleng la matsatsi,  morena oa Thoteng a mema batho bohle ba motse hotla lechatong la mora oa hae.
P3	Ntate Meharo a be a utloa hore hona le mokete o mong hape Makhoseng. Mhhh! Mekete e 'meli ka letsatsi le ts'oanang? O ea ipotsa.
P4	Ntate Meharo a re,  "ketlameha hoea meketeng ena kaofela e le 'meli. Pele ketla qala hoea Makhoseng,  e be ke khutlela Thoteng."
P5	Ntate Meharo a tsoha hoseng ka malingoa-lingoane a ea Makhoseng. Ha a fihla Makhoseng a fumana mokete o so qaloe. A re,  "honajoale ke khutlela morao Thoteng,  ketla khutlela Makhoseng ha 'mamorao."
P6	Ntate Meharo a khutlela morao Thoteng. O ne a ts'epa hore otla fumana nama e se e butsuoe. Haajoale o ne a se a lapile haholo. Empa ha a fihla,  a be a bona bo 'M'e le bo Ntate bantse ba lukisetsa hotla ja lijo.
P7	Ntate Meharo a be a khetha ho khutlela morao Makhoseng. A be a re,  kesiile ba phehile mane,  li tlamehile li se li butsuoe joale.
P8	Ha Ntate Meharo a fihla Makhoseng,  a be a fumana batho base ba qetile hoja. Baeti bantse ba fana ka limpho. Empa Ntate Meharo o ne a sa rate ho fana ka limpho,  o ne a rata hoja fela.
P9	Ntate Meharo o ne a phoqehile haholo hobane o ne a sa fumana lijo. A be a ikhethela ho khutlela morao Thoteng. O ne a satsebe ho tsamaea ka pele,  hobane o ne a khathetse hape a se a lapile haholo.
P10	Ha a fihla Thoteng,  a bona batho ba kokopela e bile ba bina. Bane ba se ba jele lijo tsohle kaofela.
P11	Ha a utloa hore lijo li felile,  Ntate Meharo a oela fats'e. Haho mokete o lemong a fumaneng lijo ho ona. O ile a fumana motohonyana ka kopinyana fela.
P12	O ile a khutlela hae lapeng a koatile e bile a lapile haholo,  a tsamaea butle.
