desc:	UFikile umumuntu olungileko

page_text:

P1	Ekadeni kwakukhona umntazana ibizo lakhe kungu Fikile,  ahlala nonina. UFikile kwabe kumumuntu okhulumela futhi,  begodu bekangazitshwenyi ngokulalela unina lokha nakamkhuzako ngokukhulumela futhi kwakhe.
P2	UFikile waye anokghari obekahlala maqalanga nesikhisi lapha bekuhlala khona uFikile. Ngelinye ilanga ukghariyakhe waphathwa kugula.
P3	Unina kaFikile wabe amatasatasa ngalelo langa,  begodu kwabe sekuhlwile lokha nakamthuma bona asele ukghariyakhe ukudla.
P4	Endleleni wahlangana nepisi eyazitjhugulula yazenza umuntu,  uJabu.
P5	UJabu wabuza uFikile bonyana uphetheni,  wamphendula wathi,  "Ngiphethe inyama,  amaqanda Kanye nebisi." Kanti unina,  wabe amkhuzile bona angatjho litho bonyana uphetheni.
P6	UFikile waphinde watjela uJabu bona ukudla lokhu usela ukghariyakhe osele agula. Wathoma uJabu waginya amathe ngomqabango obesele anawo ngenyama ephethwe nguFikile.
P7	Wagijima uJabu waya emzini kakghari kaFikile ngaphambi kobana yena afike.
P8	Wafika waginya ukghari kaFikile,  wathi ngemvakwalokho waziphuthela ngengubo ukghari kaFikile waye aziphuthele ngayo.
P9	Wafika uFikile,  wathola indlu ithule ithe du! Wangena ngaphakathi warhuwelela ukghariyakhe,  "Kghari,  ukuphi?"
P10	Akhenge athole ipendulo,  wabona bonyana angene ngekumbeni engaphakathi lapha bekulala khona ukghariyakhe. Warareka lokha nakabona kunomuntu oziphuthele ngengubo ekulu.
P11	Wabuza uFikile,  "Kghari kubayini iindlebe zakho zizikulu kangaka namhlanje?" waphendula uJabu angaphakathi kwengubo,  "bonyana ngikghone ukukuzwa kuhle."
P12	Wabuza godu uFikile,  "Kghari,  kubayini amehlwakho amakhulu kangaka namhlanje?" Waphendula uJabu wathi,  "Wonyana ngikghone ukukubona kuhle."
P13	Wabuza kwamaphelelo uFikile "Kghari kubayini umlomakho umkhulu kangaka namhlanje?" Waphendula uJabu wathi,  "Bonyana ngikghone ukukuginya." Weqa embendeni uJabu,  waginya uFikile.
P14	Ngombana uFikile waye amumuntu okhulumela futhi,  bekakhuluma nalokha angaphakathi kwamathumbu kaJabu. Waye abuza imibuzo engapheliko.
P15	Waphetha ngokudinwa uJabu ngemibuzo kazi eminengi kaFikile,  wabona bonyana kuncono amkhafule.
P16	UFikile nokghariyakhe balanyulelwa babantu abahlala kuleyo ndawo,  kusukela ngalelo langa uFikile khenge asakhuluma khulu nabantu angabaziko.
