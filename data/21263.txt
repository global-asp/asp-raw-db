desc:	Omughala omuto ayamba atya abazaire be okuzuula mukulu we era no okutegeera eyali abasobiizaku?

page_text:

P1	Agho olwatuuka, yabaayo
  amaka agaali mu kyalo kyabayetanga Emalati. Mu maka gano mwalimu abaana abobughala babiri. Omughala omuto, ayetebwa Tito Kiti, ne eyali omudingu owa lata we.
P2	Lwali lulala Tito yaja
  okwogeraku ni ise. “Nona amata ago omwana, ali nhonta,”
  bwe yakoba inhaibwe.
P3	Tito Kiti yatandiika okukobera ise kiki mwaninha
  omukulu kye yali akoze. Yakoba,
  “Naipeyi alumwike ni moraani (OmuMasayi)
  mu mpuku edhili okumpi agho.”
P4	Agho ni mu mpuku,
  Naipeyi yabona omumorani nga ali kugeedhuluka buli kanaku. Yasalagho okunoonereza kiki kyali kulyaku.
P5	Omumorani tiyayenda kwiramu
  kibuuzo kye. Atyo Naipeyi yayemba olwembo yabuuza kiki kye abaire alya.Yayemba,
  “Lomurrani ele kainyooshi iyie inko. Pee itabainji, nibayu anaa osirua
  lenkop nalio?"
P6	Oyo neyali atwaire enume ya bbaabba!
P7	Okwirayo eka, Abomumaka
  ga Naipeyi baali beelalikirivu era basoberwa. Baali babuliirwaku ente yaibwe enume esinga bubba. Ara ni mughala waibwe
  omukulu yali alumwike apaire okuva ghaka.
P8	Tito Kiti yakoba bazaire be, “Ndidha kuyambaku Naipeyi asobole okwira ghaka. Ndidha kugheereza banange.” Abazaire be bamulingirira baamubuuza, “Mukwano gwo ki
  gwoli kwogeraku?”
P9	Tito Kiti yakuba mu ngalo dhe era akanhonhi akatono kaaguluka okubva mu muti. Yaatuma akanhonhi okuja kakobere Naipeyi kati inhina mulwaire.
P10	Akanhoinhi kagulukira ghala,
  ghaigulu aghe emiti mingi, era kaagwa ku mulwango
  gwe empuku embba. Naipeyi yava mu mpuku bwe yaghulira olwembo olwa akanhonhi. Naipeyi yalumuka okwirayo eka bwe yaghulira
  amaghuliro gano.
P11	Bwe yatuuka, yakoba
  bazaire be aani eyali atwaire ente yaibwe enume.
  Ise yamwebaza, era yasalagho okunoonia omumorani eyamwibaku ente.
