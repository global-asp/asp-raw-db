title:	Wiwellella mwillaka
author:	Elias  Mangulama and Syned  Mthatiwa
artist:	Angie and  Upesh
lang:	Ellomwe (Malawi)
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mwahambellavo wiwellella nddho mwillinyu ddinaullellani?

page_text:

P1	Ollello ngiya osukuluni.@@Tti holide.
P2	Ollello nkinamonella kanema.@@Magesi kheyiiwo.
P3	Kere ttini nave?@@Ollello kimpalla kiwellelle mwillaka.
P4	Yowambella kimpalla kifase.@@Wi kiwillelle mwillaka.
P5	Vano kiniwa mavumellaka.@@Kivumella muwari ni otte, muwari na otte.
P6	Kinera ovuma wa mwa niphokosoni. Sssssssssss!@@Na mwa vatthivatthi. Mmmmmm.
P7	Vano kinniwa murima waka wukuttha!@@Gu! Gu! Gu! Gu!
P8	Tthukhoza opange murima otthamakeke pena ovuweke?@@Eya! Vollupha muddullu na vatthi ttira makumi pilli.
P9	Vano ngonani murima waka onukuttha mwaliwironi.
P10	Kahiya eyalla tthaka vamurimani, kinona matthamakelloiwa!
P11	Kinniwa kittheyaka mekhamo.@@Haha, haha, haaah, haaa!
P12	Kinniwa mekhami kinllaka.@@Boohoooo hooo!
P13	Kinniwa mekhami kipaka matta.@@Phwa, phwa, phwa!
P14	Kinniwa mmirimani mwaka winlla.@@Gururu, gururu, gururu.
P15	Mmirimani munera, "Kichihe!"
P16	Ephunaka ttiniwa wukhella wa chigumu eyo ekhuruwiwiwa mukhitchini mwa amoowe.@@Vano kimpalla wiwa eyanaka emuchake chigumu!
