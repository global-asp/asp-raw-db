title:	The peace tree
author:	Mpumelelo  Mlangeni
artist:	Tshiamo  Msimanga, Tumi  Mofokane and Mpumelelo  Mlangeni

level:	First paragraphs

desc:	We all need a place where we can find peace. (Story created by children at a July 2019 holiday workshop

page_text:

P1	There was a girl named Ayanda. @@Ayanda was the only child of her parents.@@Her mother and father wanted to separate.
P2	Ayanda decided to run away from home.@@She went to the forest because she did not know where else to go.
P3	A wolf in the forest chased Ayanda, so she climbed a tree.@@The wolf tried to bite her, so she climbed higher.
P4	At the top of the tree, Ayanda saw the beautiful sun.@@She fell in love with the sun, and decided to stay in the forest.
P5	Ayanda climbed the tree every day to talk to the sun.@@The sun became her family.
P6	One day, Ayanda saw a man under the tree.@@The man was her father. @@Ayanda came down from the tree and her father explained why he was there.
P7	He said, "Long before you were born, your mother and I would meet here. It is a special place."@@"Now I climb this tree to get peace when your mother and I fight," said Ayanda's father.
P8	After a while, Ayanda's mother also came to the forest.@@She came to make peace with Ayanda's father. @@This was their peace tree.
