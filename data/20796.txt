page_text:

P1	Nkuku a Jongola bakali balongwe.
P2	Bakaunka kusobana bbola lya maulu.
P3	Nkuku njiyakali kujata bbola mu goolo.
P4	Jongola lya kanywisya bbola.
P5	Nkuku yakacimwa.
P6	Nkuku yakalimena Jongola.
P7	Nkuku yakaswangana abanyina Jongola.
P8	Ijongola lyakalila mwida lyankuku, Amundigwasye baama. Ndamenwa."
P9	Nkuku yakaciswa nkaombo. Jongola tilyakamvwika kabotu.
P10	Nkuku yakaliluka Jongola.
P11	Ijongola lyakatija.
P12	Nkuku aJongola bakaba basinkondonyina.
