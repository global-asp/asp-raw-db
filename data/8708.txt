title:	Ntono Nnyo

desc:	Engoye za Nakku ntono ku ye

page_text:

P1	Nakku yayita,  Maama we namugamba nti "Byangu olabe,  zino engoye zona ntono nnyo nkunze!" "Leka ndabe, "Maama nga bw'amugamba.
P2	Nakku n'amugamba nti "Tunulira sikaati yange,  ntono nnyo," ko Maama nti "Yeee,  ntono,  Namusisi agitwale."
P3	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunulira empale yange empanvu,  ntono nnyo." Ko Maama nti "Yeee,  ntono nnyo,  Namusisi agitwale."
P4	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunulira essaati yange,  ntono nnyo," ko Maama nti "Yeee,  ntono nnyo,  Namusisi agitwale."
P5	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunulira akasweeta kange,  katono nnyo." ko Maama nti,  "Yee,  katono,  Namusisi asobola okukatwala."
P6	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunulira ekkooti yange eyenkuba,  ntono nnyo," ko Maama nti "Yee ntono,  tugiwe Namusisi."
P7	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunuulira engatto zange,  ntono nnyo," ko Maama nti "Yeee,  ntono,  tuziwe Namusisi."
P8	Nakku n'amugamba "Tunuulira engatto zange,  ntono nnyo," ko Maama nti "Yeee,  ntono,  tuziwe Namusisi."
P9	Nakku n'agamba Namusisi nti "Kati olina engoye nnyingi, "; naye Namusisi n'amuddamu nti,  "Nedda,  sirina,  zonna engoye nnene nnyo kunze. Tezintuuka."
