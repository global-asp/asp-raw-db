page_text:

P1	Kupha kolo nkhuuma m'maso.
P2	Mbwaya ikakhuta imbaluma lini.
P3	Pamenya mbvuu mausva ndiyo yambabonera.
P4	Katiya kunyera m'thanga nkhuwona mamace.
P5	Leka kulewa kuti ndzou iyo ukali paphewa.
P6	Kubala kwa cule na kwa tsesi nkhubodzi.
P7	Ncala ibodzi imbapha lini ntswawa.
P8	Mphondolo ingasaya nyama imbakadya mauswa.
P9	Kambuzi kam'mambuyo ndiko kambabonera na michamu.
P10	Mun'gopa m'dima ulibe thika.
P11	Gondwa adafa na manyazi.
P12	Kuwona maso ya nkhono nkhuderekhera.
P13	Wanyoza kamba ndiye ana madzi.
P14	Mulendo ndiye adapha nyoka.
P15	Thika walira kumunda anidya lini mapira.
P16	Mphondolo yakudya munthu imbafamba lini usiku.
