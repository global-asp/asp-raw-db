desc:	Learn how to make yourself look clean and tidy with Atu.

page_text:

P1	Kila asubuhi huwa ninaosha uso wangu.
P2	Huwa ninapiga mswaki kwa kutumia dawa ya meno.
P3	Huwa ninachana nywele.
P4	Huwa ninaoga.
P5	Ninaosha mwili wangu na sabuni.
P6	Huwa ninavaa nguo safi.
P7	Nikitoka nje huwa ninavaa viatu.
P8	Huwa ninaosha mikono yangu kwa kutumia sabuni na maji safi...
P9	...baada ya kutumia choo...
P10	...kabla ya kula...
P11	...na baada ya kucheza nje.
P12	Huwa ninapiga mswaki tena kabla ya kulala.
P13	Ninapenda kunukia...
P14	...na kuwa msafi!
