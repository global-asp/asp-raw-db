desc:	We provide different uses of fire

page_text:

P1	I see fire again.
P2	The fire is burning brightly.
P3	The fire can cook meat and vegetables.
P4	The fire can keep me warm.
P5	The orange light of the fire gives me light when it is dark.
P6	The fire crackles and pops.
P7	Fire is powerful and can be dangerous.
P8	We must be careful when lighting fires or playing with fire.
