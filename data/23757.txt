desc:	Waba oyine enjara

page_text:

P1	Omubiro byakare, haka hariho omushija owobakweta kato. Akaba aratura mukibuga kya kabuusu. Akaba aratura nembwa yeye omukaju kakye.
P2	Ezoba rimwe akarwara munonga kandi atine omuntu wena owokumuyamba. obuyakizire yasharamu kutasya omukazi akutura hihi nawe.
P3	Yeta abanwani beye boona hamwe nabanyabuzele beye ahambaga yeye yokutasya. Kato akashemererwa munonga ahabwokuba akatekateka ngu yabona owokumuyambaho.
P4	Kato nomukazi weye bakaba sheba ebintu byinji.Hakabahariro obutebo bwoburo, emikyeka, eminekye, ebinyebwa hamwe nebikyonkyo byokushohoza.
P5	Embaga ekahwa abajenyi boona bagaruka omumaka gaabo. kato akaa ye tebenkine okutandika amagara amasya nomukazi weye hamwe nembwa.
P6	Ekiro ekyakuratireho, Kato yahomukazi weye eminekye, komka omukazi yayanga kugirya.
P7	Kato yaheza yazaheru okuhiiga, omukazi weye yabona kurya eminekye yona.
P8	Obu Kato yarugire ahaa kuhiiga akayine enjara nyinji. Yashaba omukazi weye eminekye. Omukazi yamugira ngu embwa yagirire.
P9	Ekiro ekindi, Kato akaaza omumusiri gweye. Obuyagaruka omuka, yashanga omukazi yarire enyama yoona kandi atahireho embwa.
P10	Orunaku orundi, Kato yaaza kutayayira munywani weye. Kuyagarukire omuuka yashanga omukazi yarire ebinyebwa byoona. Ekiibo kirimu busha. Kato yanyigira omukaazi weye.
P11	Kato yatekateka,''hariho ekizibu ekiri haryogu mukaazi.'' Yasharamu okukozesa amarogo. Yata amate omuka nyungu akamarogo, yakata hansi yekitanda, rero yaza kuhiiga.
P12	Omukazi akareba akanyungu, kijwiremu amate. Muryejo dakyika, yakata ahamunywa gweye. yamaramu amate goona embwa erikumureba.
P13	Ekyobusasi, akanyungu kakwatira hamunywa. Yajezaho kukihaho konka karemwa. Yatera enduru, araguruka ahiguru na ahansi. Konka kayanga kurugaho. Embwa yareba ebi byoona.
P14	Embwa yiruka yaza kuronda Kato. Obwo yamureba yatandika kumoka ya moka eraguruka ahiguru na hansi. Kato yamanya ngu hariho ekibi ekyabaho omuuka.
P15	Yiruka nembwa yeye. Kato akatangara okushanga omukazi weye nakanyungu kakwasire ahaa munywa gweye. Yamureba yashoberwa.
P16	Kato yakwata ahitama ryomukazi weye rero akanyungu karugaho kagwa ahansi. Omukazi yashwara munonga. Yasharamu okugarukayo owabazire beye.
