title:	Aziiza Doli

lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Ono omwana anhumirwa inho okulingilira doli. Doli we ni mukagwawe omusanhufu.

page_text:

P1	Ono ni Aziiza.Aziiza ni doli ghange.
P2	Nhenda inho Aziiza kuba mugagwa ghange musanhufu.
P3	Mpuna munco nanaaba mumaiso.Aghoni nanonia azizi.
P4	Ngamuboine musunsula enviri.
P5	Bwendya ekyenco ndisa ni aziiza
P6	Bwendya switi mpaku eyindi Aziiza.
P7	Aye aziiza talya switi we.Neghunia lwaki!
P8	Ntambula mu luya lwonalwona nga nnonda ebimuli.
P9	Mpa Aziiza ebimuli byange.Aye tankoba ati,"ghebale."
P10	Nnaaza namunhiriza nga maama ghange akaali ku nnaaza.
P11	Nnambaza aziiza engoye emyufu lwakuba ndenda inho.
P12	Bwenja okulambala,nambaza aziiza mumbali mwange.
