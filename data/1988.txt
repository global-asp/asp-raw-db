id:	1988
title:	Amarube
author:	Dennis Ogaro Ondieki
artist:	Cathy Feek
lang:	Ekegusii
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/1988.png
desc:	This story is about a little girl who is ill and has to stay away from school.  Letters are an important form of communication while she is off sick.

page_text:

P1	Ewiki eria yaeta inanyora amarube amange mono.
P2	Rituko ri'etichi rimo omonyagitari one nandikerete rirube gochia ase omwalimu one.
P3	Thabisile nomorwaire. Goika atigare nka.
P4	Etichi kabere inkanyora rirube korwa ase abana b'ekelasi kiane.
P5	Bwena bwango,  Thabisile!
P6	Etichi gatato inkanyora rirube korwa ase omosani one.
P7	Nigo nachorete ebicha eye okoba aye. Ninche omwanchwa Dudu.
P8	Etichi kane inkanyora rirube korwa ase mama ominto.
