desc:	Palesa nyɛla bipun bla ŋɔn wuri bunbula zaa.

page_text:

P1	Suhuyini n-ŋɔ. U yuuri maa gbini nya 'flower' ba yatɔani, pum.
P2	Suhuyini yi tɔasi n tila leemu tiya asusuba. “Leemu tiya, tim ti leemu paam.”
P3	Suhuyini kyɛnila nɔaba n wa sikuri. Sori maani ka uyali moori ma, “Fanåa, moori azoama n nsôabigi ka di kuui.”
P4	Suhuyini yipai pum anbεa mooni. “M pɛaya ni ya veilimwa.”
P5	Sikuri ni Suhuyini toasiri n ti tibisi. “Tiisi, fanŋa ya ninŋ wulkara ka ti zintiri maasim maa ni n karina."
P6	Suhuyini toasira ntri tita'ara ambɛa ba sikuri maani. “Fanŋa, ya zoam yoomyoma nta'ai ka nivuguberi bunyaŋi nkpɛana.”
P7	Suhuyini yi labi yini, u kyanŋi nkaila ba leemu tiya. “Ka boosi, ya leemu maa moaiya?” Suhuyini nboosi.
P8	“Leemu maa na nyɛala lee mu kasa,” Suhuyini gôahabu ni. “Nsaa labina nkai bio leemu tiya, dibuya’ai bio ka leemu seesi sa biya nti ma!”
