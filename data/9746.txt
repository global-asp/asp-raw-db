desc:	Omwana oyo nigo anchete korenda etoi yaye. Na etoi eye yaye nomosani ogogete rioka.

page_text:

P1	Oyo Azizi. Azizi netoi yane.
P2	Nigo mwanchete Azizi okoba agaseka botambe.
P3	Ekero nabokire,  ngwesibia obosia. Namanya komorenda Azizi.
P4	Akero namonyorire Azizi nkomogwarosa etuki.
P5	Ekero nkonyu echae ya mambi nkomorgeria Azizi.
P6	Ekero nkoria ebitamutamo nigo nkoa Azizi ekemo.
P7	Korende Azizi tari kori getamutamu kiaye.   Inche ngokumia naseki!
P8	Ngotara risiko rionsi gochora amaoga.
P9	Namoa Azizi rioga.   Korende tarigoteba,  "Mbuya mono."
P10	Nigo nkomosibi Azizi oraba se mama ataransibi.
P11	Nkoboi Azizi eyanga embariri ekiagera nyanchete erangi embariri.
P12	Ekero ngochikorara,  nkomobeka Azizi nsemo yane.
