title:	Nana Ne Lavi
author:	Andries du Plessis
artist:	Wiehan de Jager, Catherine Groenewald, Rijuta Ghate, Benjamin Mitchley, Kathy Arbuckle, Danielle Bruckert, Vusi Malindi and Marion Drew
lang:	Luganda

desc:	Buli muntu atekeddwa okubeera n`obuvunanyizibwa ku ngeri gyalabirira mu abaana abato. Olugero lino lwogera ku muwala Lavi eyalemererwa okulabirira omwana omuto kubanga naye yalina emyaka munaana gyoka.

page_text:

P1	Ono ye Nana alina emyaka ebiri.
P2	Ono ye Lavi alina emyaka munaana.
P3	Mu luwummula,  maama waabwe agenda mu kibuga okukola.
P4	Maama ategeka obuugi,  amata n`ebyokulya nga bya Nana.
P5	Maama ategeka akawunga,  omuceere ne bijanjaalo nga bya Lavi.
P6	Maama ajukiza Lavi okugabirira omwana mu budde,  nokumunaaza asobole okwebaka obulungi.
P7	Maama asiibula,  ''Nja kukomawo mangu.''.
P8	Kati omwana enjala emuluma. Naye Lavi tafaayo.
P9	Kyokka Lavi amukuba oluyi olunene mu kifo ky`okumuwa obuugi. Amutwaala mu buliri yebake.
P10	Lavi ayingira mu fumbiro nanywa era n`alya ebyokulya byomwana byonna!
P11	Maama akomawo okuva mu kibuga. Lavi amukulisaayo. ''Kulikayo maama.''.
P12	Omwana nga munafu nnyo! Alabika enjala emuluma ate ggwe olabika okkuse.
P13	Maama awa Nana ebyokulya ne byokunywa. Nana addamu amaanyi nasanyuka natandika okuzannya.
P14	Maama yayiga nti abaana tebasobola kulabirira baana bannaabwe. Teyaddamu kuleka Nana waka nga tewali muntu mukulu.
