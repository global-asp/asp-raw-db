id:	22271
title:	Ŋkar si
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh and Mbinkar Emmanuel Keelen
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/22271.png
desc:	Kimfèr ke kùm wán woo lumèn wùn à ŋkar sə́ sí

page_text:

P1	Yír yémé dzə̀ Simo.
P2	M kér ŋkàr sikweè.
P3	Yír sév sí dzə̀ Zizo, Lele, Sisa wùn à Ayanda.
P4	Ŋkàr yèm yee Zizo yii koŋ a te bòr.
P5	Ŋkàr yèm yee Lele yii koŋ a laʼ ndzə́v.
P6	Ŋkàr yèm yee Sisa yii koŋ kilaaylaáyín.
P7	Ŋkàr yèm yee Ayanda yii koŋ a yòŋ ŋwàʼ.
P8	Kém jika? M yii koŋ a yiì a dzə̀ vifá ve áwune yii yii.
P9	M lo, m wàa te bor vèr Zizo.
P10	M lo, m wàa laʼ ndzə́v vèr Lele.
P11	M lo, m wàa yii kilaaylaáyín ver Sisa.
P12	Ŋkar yèm wìyi, a yii koŋ ké a dzə̀ ka?
P13	Wìyì, é vèsən wáa te bor.
P14	Wìyì, é vèsən wáa laʼ ndzə́v.
P15	Wìyì, é vèsən yii kilaaylaáyín.
P16	Wìyì, é vèsən wáa yòŋ ŋwàʼ.
