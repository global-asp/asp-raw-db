id:	34308
title:	Zwipuka zwi shavha mahayani
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/34308.png
desc:	Phukha nṋa dzi re vhalala  dzi a thusana kha u ḓivhofholola kha vhaṋe vhadzo vhane vha sa dzi londe

page_text:

P1	Vhaṅwe mukegulu vho vha vhe na khuhu dza phambo na gukulume ḽa kale.
P2	Muḓuhulu wavho a vha dalela. Vha humbula u ṱhavha iḽi gukulume.
P3	Gukulume ḽa mbo ḓi shavhela ḓoroboni khulu.
P4	Gukulume ḽa ṱangana na tshimange tshe tsha shavha muṋe watsho.
P5	Gukulume na tshimange vha ṱangana na mmbwa i no khou shavha.
P6	Ha ḓa bere ye na yone ya vha yo shavha hayani.
P7	Zwipuka izwi zwa swika muṅwe muḓini. Zwa vhona ngomu nḓuni hu na mbava!
P8	Zwipuka zwa thoma u imba uri mbava dzi shavhe.
P9	Zwa dzhena nḓuni zwa ḽa zwiḽiwa zwe zwa zwi wana henefho.
P10	Mbava dza dovha dza vhuya. Zwipuka zwa lwa nadzo.
P11	Mbava dzo ri u shavha dzi si tsha vhuya.
P12	Zwipuka zwa tshila zwoṱhe afho nḓuni ḽi ḽa maladze.
