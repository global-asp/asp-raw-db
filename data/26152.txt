title:	Inshuti ebyiri ntoya
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Jesse Pietersen
lang:	Kinyarwanda

desc:	Iyi ni inkuru yakera y umwana wihene nuwingwe bahuye bakaba inshuti

page_text:

P1	Habayeo umwana w' Ihene n'uw' Ingwe bakaba bari inshuti.
P2	Bagiye ku gasozi gukina.
P3	Batashye mu rugo bakererewe cyane.
P4	Umwana w'ihene abwira nyina ati, "Mama nahuye n'inshuti nziza. Ndishimye cyane, tuzongera duhure ejo dukine."
P5	Nyina aramusubiza ati, "Yooo! Mwana wange. Nishimiye ko wagarutse amahoro. Umenye ko atari inshuti yacu, ntuzongere guhura nawe."
P6	Muri icyo gihe, Ingwe nayo yabwiye nyina iti, "Mama, nahuye n'inshuti nziza. Ndishimye cyane. Turongera duhure ejo dukine."
P7	Nyina aramusubiza ati, "Yooo muhungu wa, mbabajwe n'uko utariye uwo mwana w' ihene. Ejo nimuhura, umwice umurye."
P8	Umunsi wakurikiyeho, Umwana w' ingwe yagiye mu mudugudu aho Umwana w'ihene yaratuye.
P9	Umwana w' Ihene wari hanze. Wari urimo kwihisha hamwe na nyina.
P10	Umwana w'ingwe utera hejuri uti, "Tuge ku gasozi gukina."
P11	Umwana w'ihene uramubwira uti, "Ntabwo tukiri inshuti ukundi. Numviye mama. Abana beza bumvira ba mama babo."
P12	Umwana w' ingwe uraraka. Usubira kwa nyina ubabaye.
