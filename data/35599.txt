page_text:

P1	Doctor  Amina  is a hard working woman.
P2	She  wakes  up  very  early  to  dress up  her  children  for  school.
P3	She cleans  the  house  and  prepares  food  before  going  to  work.
P4	Doctor  Amina  is a widow but  she  takes care of  her  family  from  her  work.
P5	She  cares  for  her  patience very well.
P6	She  advises the  women in  the  community  to send  their  children  to school.
P7	Doctor Amina is good and  everyone  in the  community  loves her.
