desc:	Otuntun wa bu ke gbasiru enhebe nu anya nwo ruka oro okwukwor hite obochi oisisi no du nabali.

page_text:

P1	Nhebe gbalizoshi nu osisi, nu owo owunwa enhebe.@  @Nnurum ekwe orchichi nu ezi-oborchi.
P2	Nhebe galagu esezi bu Terfa nhe wey ri wiri osisi.@  @Nnumonu enkanra bier nu akpupa ntogu enhebe na ba onumgbom.
P3	Nhebe lahashii osun ishishizibudu nu oro-okwukwo nu ngeh wiri enhie.@  @Ngeyam oyahanshi nu mgbakor mini zi nu esilaburu ruhueli oro okwukwor.
P4	Nhebe gwuzogwu nu olu rishim. Ehninem gwuzogwu nu akwa ehim.@  @Mi nu ele enyim gwirishi mkpiri ehnine.
P5	Ehninem na ha ezi, yahanshi nmanti. Anutorushia.@  @Ehninem toru ogologo, yahanshi nkpunkpuh. Anutorushia.
P6	Ngwuzom, ele enyim gwuzo. A hunshi ehnine anyi.@  @Ike gwukweme ayi a yanha oro-okwukwor. Oro-okwukwor gbasa asah lanha oro.
P7	Nhebe ne berubere.@  @Nnumohun enhebe na laha wantinti. Nhum ehninem nu akwa ehi-oro.@  @Oduleh enge oshienyenrah.
P8	Nhebe segu nkpururu oligwe.@  @Ndeegum eli nu oro no ro mburo kpa enhebe lagu ekenya.
