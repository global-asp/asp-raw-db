desc:	Inkawu ibaleka nebhayisikili likaNamakuru.

page_text:

P1	UNamakuru nomfowabo sebelungele ukuya esikoleni.
P2	UNamakuru ugibelisa umfowabo ebhayisikilini lakhe.
P3	Abangani babo bayabahalalisela lapho bengena ngebhayisikili esikoleni.
P4	Bathi besesendlini yokufundela,  kwafika inkawu phandle. Inkawu igibela ibhayisikili likaNamakuru iyaphuma iyahamba.
P5	Izingane ziyayijaha inkawu.
P6	Inkawu ishiya ibhayisikili phansi kwesihlahla.
P7	Inkawu iyahlamba iya phakathi nechibi. Inombela kudekude phezulu esihlahleni.
P8	Inkawu iyabathathazela ngesandla sayo! Izingane nazo ziyathathaza. Selibuyile futhi ibhayisikili lika Namakuru.
