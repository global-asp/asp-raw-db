desc:	Lyi nkuru isobanura impamuu inkoko zibana nabantu ni mpamuu ibisiga birya inkoko.

page_text:

P1	Habayeho, igisiga ninkoko inshuti nyanshuti. Barafatanya ga muhihe bakeneranye.
P2	Barakinanye. Bakinye nizidi nyamanswa nk'akanyamasyo.
P3	Baribafite inka, intama, ihene n'ingamiya. Bororaga hamwe mu kibaya barimo gukina.
P4	Ununsii umwe inkoko yagite gutira urwembe igisiga kogosha abana be. Igisiga cyahaye inkoko urwembe kitabajije.
P5	inkoko yogoshe bana bayo neza. Gusa yibagiwe kubika neza urwembe
P6	Igihe igisiga cyaye gufuta urwembe rwanyo, inkoko yararubuze. inkoko yaharuye mubusa. Igisiga cyararakaye gitangira kurya imishwi.
P7	Inkoko yirukankiye ubuzima bwayo maze igya kubana nabantu. kugyeza nubu inkoko zibana nabantu kandi ibisiga birya imishwi.
