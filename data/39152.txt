P1	One day a teen named Michael he is 13. Michael was studying for a test then he heard glass shatter and gun shots! He ran to his door he peeked out then quickly locked it he was so surprised what he saw!
P2	Michael quickly started packing his stuff he quickly grabbed his phone out and texted his friends "meet me at there favorite park NOW",he texted to his friends. They were confused and scared if he was ok They said "Ok",they all texted back.
