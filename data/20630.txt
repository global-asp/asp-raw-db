desc:	Umusetsa uhafayo ta shabiha biryobulayi ari beshikuka bewe batamba shehulya.

page_text:

P1	Abekhale ni khale abawo umusetsa abe bamulanga  yeweri mushalo bari Aya
P2	Abeshafayo khubiha biryo byemumoni ate nga onakisabusa
P3	Mbawo ishasigala munungo tawe
P4	Kasooli yesi woma nabi
P5	Imbewo ingaali yawutsa yarusa libaati lyehunungo
P6	Babana balwanira biiryo husepichi
P7	Mayi akona as wefumaliha ngalira shina shesi atsa khulisa babana bwewe lulundi
P8	Papa wekhala khukutiyi nga alikhwambasa shekhukhola mumoni
