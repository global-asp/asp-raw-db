page_text:

P1	Olwohusooka nahuula olwoba lulala.  On Monday I uprooted one mushroom.
P2	 Olwohubiri nahuula obwoba bubiri.  On Tuesday I uprooted two mushrooms.
P3	Olwohudatu nahuula obwoba budatu.  On Wednesday I uprooted three mushrooms.
P4	 Olwohune nahuula obwoba bune. On Thursday I uprooted four mushrooms.
P5	 Olwohutaanu nahuula obwoba butaanu. On Friday I uprooted five mushrooms.
P6	Olwomukaaga nahuula obwoba mukaaga.  On Saturday I uprooted six mushrooms.
P7	Olwomusanvu nahuula obwoba musanvu. On Sunday I uprooted seven mushrooms.
P8	Mu naku musanvu nahuula obwoba obwene obungi. In seven days, I uprooted many mushrooms.
