id:	2750
title:	Umbala Obomvu
author:	Thandabantu Magengelele
artist:	Sue Kramer
lang:	isiXhosa
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution-Non Commercial 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2750.png
desc:	Izivakalisi kunye nemifanekiso yezinto ziqhelekileyo ezimbala ubomvu.

page_text:

P1	Umama ugcwalisa ipheyile elibomvu.
P2	U-Abongile udlala ngelori ebomvu.
P3	U-Deon uphethe iintyatyambo ezibomvu.
P4	U-Anti unxibe ijezi ebomvu.
P5	Kukho ilanga elibomvu entla kotat'omkhulu.
P6	Kukho isithuthuthu esibomvu esimise esitalatweni.
P7	Icikilishe elibomvu lihleli phezu kukaBabalwa.
P8	Injini ebomvu yokucima umlilo isesitishini.
P9	Nanku uSibahle enxibe umntla obomvu.
P10	Uzama ukulinganisa iibhutsi ezibomvu.
P11	Azimlingani!
