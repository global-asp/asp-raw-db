desc:	Othiri nyako ma thin pa kwari mere tunwango ogwange kwethi githoyo.

page_text:

P1	Othiri nyako ma thini pakwari mere muchowo bedo engeyi luli. gowoni okatho gikisangala.
P2	Minyo go otundo chegini gi luli, oromo gi makichari diel tuwari go ni kathi mino igwokere, piri mere ogwange jotiye giromo iluli.
P3	Minyo otundo idyeri luli, onwango ogwange kwethi kyegini githoyo tugo ngwecho teki teki minyo koko.
P4	Minyo gowoki woko ma luli oromo gi winyini mukyokere kanya cheli koko. Nyathi ma nyako ongwekyo kamedo koko. kwari mere owinjo go.
P5	kwari mere openjo dhako pere nyakwarani tyekenyo. oredo piyo piyo nenogogi gwoki pere tunwango o ole
P6	Minyo jutundo pecho odonjo tumotho nyithindo wade tutero kyemo. minyo ochowo kyemo owakyo gimutimere iloka ma thoyo.
P7	Minyo  go ochowo wacho  gimutimere ginwango ryeko miyogo weyo luwo luli mungoye gi ngatiman okatho minyo simyemere tuko gi soye mani
P8	kudoko luwo eluli mungoye ngati mani
