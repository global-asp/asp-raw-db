P1	Once upon a time, there lived two friends, Maryam and Serah.
P2	Serah went to school while Maryam stayed at home.
P3	Serah asked Maryam one day, "Why don't you go to school?"
P4	"I don't have money for uniforms and books, " Maryam said.
P5	One day, Serah said to Maryam, "Let's go to school."
P6	Serah showed Maryam the books and uniforms he has bought.
P7	Serah and Maryam now went to school together.
P8	They are very good friends.
