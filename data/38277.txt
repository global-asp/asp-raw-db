page_text:

P1	Miriame was an elephant everyone made fun of but she never gave up on her dream of living in a national park.
P2	She was an orphan whose parents died in a storm she lived with other animals who made fun of her they told her she would never get married and live in the national park.
P3	King Kavango was a fierce Leopard who killed enemies with one punch.
P4	Sooner or later she was known for her strength so the king told her she would be royal guard
P5	She was very happy while she was drinking water at the river she meet Musanshi the most dramatic lion, Musanshi said" "the king has chosen the smelling old elephant wow what a bad descion.
P6	There was a battle for the strongest in the land to be taken to musanazi national park and she signed up she fought strong.
P7	Punches flew in the air left right everyone was cheering drums beating maidens dancing.
P8	Antelope threw a spear it failed and the king said "Get out "he went in shame
P9	Shame shame people laughed
P10	At last she won and was taken to the national park safely
