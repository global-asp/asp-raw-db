title:	Shamwari
author:	Lilian Chaminuka
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	ChiShona
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Iyi inyaya yemukomana neshamwari dzake nezvavanofarira kuita.

page_text:

P1	Zita rangu ndinonzi Simo.
P2	Ndine Shamwari ina.
P3	Mazita adzo ndi Zizo, Lele, sisa naAyanda.
P4	Shamwari yangu Zizo anofarira kutamba nhabvu.
P5	Shamwari yangu Lele anofarira kutuhwina.
P6	Shamwari yangu Sisa anofarira kutamba chihwande hwande.
P7	Shamwari yangu Ayanda anofarira kuverenga.
P8	Ini? Ini ndinofarira zvese zvavanofarira kuita.
P9	Ndinotamba nhabvu naZizo.
P10	Ndinotuhwina na Lele.
P11	Ndinotamba chihwande-hwande naSisa.
P12	Huya, shamwari, unofarira kuitei?
P13	Huya titambe nhabvu.
P14	Huya tituhwine.
P15	Huya titambe chihwande-hwande.
P16	Huya tiverenge!
