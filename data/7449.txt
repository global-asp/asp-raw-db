page_text:

P1	Where is the rat?
P2	Find the rat in the bucket.
P3	Where is it?
P4	Look,  look! Find it under the bed.
P5	Where is it? A a a a! Are you behind the door? No,  you are not behind the door.
P6	Where are you? Are you in the bag? No,  you are not in the bag.
P7	Are you in the saucepan?No,  you are not in the saucepan.
P8	Where can you be? In the cupboard?
P9	Where,  where? I can not find you.
P10	Are you under the mat? No,  no.
P11	In the case,  oh yes. I can see the rat in the case.
