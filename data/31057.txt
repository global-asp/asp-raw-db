page_text:

P1	Eikere lyanonia aghali amaadhi nga bwekisoboka.
P2	Amaadhi geyasooka okufuna gaali masu inho inho!
P3	Okwokya kwabitirira inho era lyabuuka kunsgha.
P4	Lyanonia ensugha eyalimu amaadhi amainhogovu era lyangira omwo.
