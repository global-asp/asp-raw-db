title:	Hɛn Nantwi Pendo
author:	Gabriel Ampah Sam (OLE Ghana)
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Fante
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18496.png
desc:	Ebusua hɔn nantwi bi a wɔ dɔ no noho asɛm.

page_text:

P1	Iyi nye Ndalo.
P2	Ndalo pɛ buukuu akenkan.
P3	Iyi nye Pendo.
P4	Pendo pɛ kaarɔt.
P5	Wɔpon skuul a Ndalo dze Pendo dɔ sar do ma ɔkɔwe efuw.
P6	Ɔma Pendo nsupa ma ɔnom.
P7	Papa ma Pendo eburow.
P8	Dabiara okyikyi nomfonsu pii wɔ Pendo mu.
P9	Pendo ma enumfonsu pii.
P10	Papa tɔn Pendo n'enumfonsu no.
P11	Ndalo nom numfonsu amon dabiara.
P12	Dza ɔka nye dɛ,  "Pendo meda wo ase!"
