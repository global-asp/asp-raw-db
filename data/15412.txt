title:	Uchuru

artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Kimîîru

desc:	Dad prepares porridge for his son but ends up disappointing.

page_text:

P1	Kinoti okagira rukiri muno.
P2	Baba wawe nakuruga uchuru anyua mbere eta sukuru.
P3	"Mami niwe wije kuruga uchuru buuthongi mono," Kinoti arachiera.
P4	Uchuru bumuejaga inya ya guchetha.
P5	Uchuru bwi tayari.
P6	Baba wawe aramwikira iria na wendo.
P7	"Baba,  uchuru buu ibukwenda sukari," Kinoti aramwira.
P8	"Uchuru bwa mami ibu buthongi nkuruki ya baba," arachiera.
P9	"Baba,  itubu mpongera sukari," Kinoti arauga.
P10	Baba aramwongera sukari.
P11	"Woi baba wekira chumbi!"
P12	"Baba iri mami akeja?"
