desc:	Omulenzi alikukyalira Ddaadda mwanina maama we mu kibuga ekinene omukuzi gwe ogusooka!

page_text:

P1	Sitenseni ya bbaasi mu kibuga kyange waaliwo enkuyandha eya abantu
P2	Wololo wa bbaasi yaleekanha, "kuja mukibuga! tusimbula buti!"
P3	Abantu besindiika inho okutuuka ku bbaasi.
P4	Abakazi nabaana baatyama.
Olugendho lwali lughanvu.
P5	Natwamira gha dhinisa, okumpi no musaadha.
P6	Naadhuuba, ndhi kuja mu kibuga, aye ndese amaka.
P7	Ghaliiwo abatembeyi bangi inho nga batanha ebya maguzi
P8	Abasaabaaze baguula ebintu.
Nze twali na mpiiya kugula kintu kyona kyona.
P9	Ekyembi, omuvuuzi wa bbaasi yafuugha engombe.
P10	Abatembeyi basindikagana nga bafuluma e bbaasi.
P11	"Oba nnhaira yo li eka?" neebuuza nze mwene.
P12	Mundha mu bbaasi mwali mwooca.
P13	Nadhuuba muku maama ni muganda ghange bendese eka.
P14	Endoolo dhamaala dhandhiba wo mpola mpola.
P15	Ekiseera kighanvu bwekyabitawo, nnaira engule.
Abantu baali nga bagiire.
P16	Ekiseera kyali kituuse okunoonhia enumba ya ddaadda mwaniina maama.
