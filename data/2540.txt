desc:	Mamela imisindo eyenziwa yizilwane zasepulazini. Msindo muni owenziwa ngumlimi?

page_text:

P1	Nazi izilwane epulazini.
P2	Inkomo ikhala ithi,  "Mu-u-u. Mu-u-u." Inkomo ifuna amanzi.
P3	Imbuzi ikhala ithi,  "Me-e-e. Me-e-e." Imbuzi ifuna amanzi.
P4	Ihashi likhala lithi,  "Yi-hi-hi-hi. Yi-hi-hi-hi." Ihashi lifuna amanzi.
P5	Ingulube ikhala ithi,  "Ho-ho-ho i-i-i." Ingulube ifuna amanzi.
P6	Isikhukhukazi sikhala sithi,  "ke-ke-ke-ke-e-e,  ke-ke." Isikhukhukazi sifuna amanzi.
P7	Inja ikhala ithi,  "Hhawu-hhawu-hhawu." Inja ifuna amanzi.
P8	Umlimi uphendula athi,  "Shhh! Nawa amanzi."
