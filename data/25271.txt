page_text:

P1	Wakyaliraku badhaadha bo? bwoba nga okaali kikoleku kubanga kirungi.Nze naab'omumaka gange twasalagho okuja mu kyalo tukyalireku badhaadha bange,twasituka munco inho nga bwekisoboka twetegekera olugendo.Nga twamaze ira okuteekateeka engoye dhaife ku saawa edheigulo okuja okughera esaawa mukaaga edh'okunco nga tutuuse ku bbaasi paaka.Nze naabantu bange twatuuka ku kyalo kyaife kusaawa ina n'ekitundu.Aye bwetwatuuka,dhaadha omukazi yali musanhufu inho okutubona,nga obughangwa
P2	Bwetwamala okughuumulaku,badhaadha baayeta baidhukulu baibwe bonaboona.Omwana omulenzi ogha mwanhinaze, ekiseera eky'okulya ekyeigulo,kunco lyaku,abaana b'asenga baalamusa abakulu baagya amaadhi,ekyalo kyaife kyali kumpi n'enhandha alubbati.Kino kyansanhusa inho kubanga nali tijangaku kulubalama lwa nhandha yonayoona.Agho ni naategeera lwaki ebyalo ebitulinaine byayendha okutwala eitaka lyaife okuja ku nhandha nayega okuluka ebiibo kale,kintu kikulu inho kukyalo oyega amagezi
