title:	Ah! Unzano webhola!
author:	Milton  Nyathi
artist:	Onesmus Kakungi
lang:	ChiNambya
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/39995.png
desc:	Umbisana unotumwa namegulu babe kuzwintolo. Umbisana yoyu unonda nonkwaina uwe bakanopejeleja bezana ibhola lyamakumbo nabamwe bakwaina babo. Unobuza na zwinhu zwaatumwa namegulu babe?

page_text:

P1	Ndosoda kuzana nonkwaina wangu Jibani.
P2	Tibakwaina babuya.@@Umwe wedu akabatumwa kumwe, tosonda tose.
P3	Limwe izhuba nene bangu bakandituma kuzwintolo kotenga munyu namafuta okubhikisa.@@Ndakenda naJibani.
P4	Tili munzila takabona bamwe bakwaina bedu bezana ibhola.
P5	"Ah! Unzano webhola!" ndakadalo.@@Ndakabeda kuzana nami.
P6	Ndakati kunaJibani, "Ngatibhuze kuti bangada kuzana nasu na. Ndokuda kasa kuzana ibhola lyamakumbo."
P7	Jibani akati, "Ande kuzwintolo tangwa kojozha tizana ibhola."
P8	Asi imi ndikati, "Ngatizane tangwa! Nene abatootivumila kuti tibuye kozana."
P9	Takangwina tikazana. Imi ndakabeli wokubhata mugedi.@@Igedi lyakabekatiwa namabwe mabili makulwana.
P10	Ndakshingilila kubhata ibhola mugedi.@@Bakwaina bangu abajakatubula kugoja kana limwempela.
P11	Takazana ibhola kuswikila igwa lyazhala unseche.
P12	Takenda kuzwintolo. Imali yanene yakabalashika! Ndakatanga kulila.@@Jibani wakandikalalila eti, "Leka zwokulila. Wabeda kuzana tangwa."
P13	Takabwilila kusha tisina munyu namafuta okubhikisa.@@Takabekachenuluka, tili nolutyo.
P14	Nene bakanyema kasa patakabwilila kusha.@@Umbuzo wokutanga wabakatibhuza ndiwokuti, "Mwabeli kupi unshaji wose yoyu?"
P15	Takababuja ichokomende.@@Bakati, "Musubenda kozana musijavumijwa."
P16	Takenda tikanopa madada zwokulya.@@Tapeja yopo, tikenda kunoshamba.
