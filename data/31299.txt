page_text:

P1	Ekituli mukimante nga kiboneka bukalamu. Abantu baviira mukituli eky'o mukimante. Boonaboona bendha ekituli ekiri mukisenge. Kubanga kifo kinhuvu. Mulusozi muviiramu omusota omunene n'a Masese benda okweghugira mu nnhandha.
P2	Damali mughala mubalagavu. Ayendha oku mwenha buli lunaku. Ayenda okulya embooli kubanga endala eba eri kumuluma inho. Yenda okumwenha eri ayendha okuyamba okusomesa okubala. Bw'o kuba olukero ng'a tandiika okwogera n'o kulekaana. Aniiga. Damali alina enhindho ennene n'o bugere obutono. Alina endha nnene kubanga alya embooli buli lunaku.
P3	Damali atandiika okulira kubanga edala erikumuluma.  Ajja mu jokooni ye Miriyeri amukobereku obuzibu bwe. Miriyeri amukoba ati toola ennusu nkumi ibiri ajje gule embooli kumudaala gwa mmaama Miriya.
P4	Damali aloghozamu ku okwiiba sente ediri ghansi gh'e kitanda kya Miriyeri kubanga endala yali emuli bubi ate ng'a yenda okulya nnhinjiku.Yaloghoozamu ati oba aibe embooli kumudaala gwa mmaamma miriya.
P5	Damali tiyaiba mbooli. Yasasula sente ye mmaamma Miriya era buti musanhufu. Yailamu kabiri okumwena alina emundha nnhene era ng'a likughulira kabiri mirala.
P6	Damali musanhufu kubanga mwigufu era nga asaswire sente era yaira n'e mbooli edindhi okuva kumudaala.
P7	Damali elikulya mbooli era ali kuwulira bukalamu ate alina emunda nnene era musanhufu. Yeyanza ati innabo Miriya okumpa akasente ningula embooli kumudaali gwa mmaamma miriyeri
P8	Damali buti ali bukalamu. Kubangaali bulunji, ate alina emunda ennene, yatandiika okuzaana nga abaka omupiira.
