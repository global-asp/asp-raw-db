desc:	ǂōǂōsiba xu ra huiǂuihe ǀgururon ra mûǂams ǃnaga sâuhe (kōǃgâhe) ǃkhaib xa ra ǃgû ǂhôas ge nēsa ǀôaisa khoen xa ra mûǂamhese.

page_text:

P1	Mûǂamaob tsî ǁîb mûǃgâs ǁgûo ǀgururon ǂama.
P2	Aoǁkhumu ǁkhâb ǃnân ge kōǃgâ-aob tsî hui-aon ona ǂoaǂamsa sîsenxase ra hâ.
P3	ǀGui kuris ge kaira taras Mkitesa ǂguro ǃnās ase ge sari.
P4	Nē hâǃkhaib di mûǂamaon ge Mkitesa kaise ge ǀkhom, ǂgawasa tsîs ge ǀnorosa i amaga.
P5	Xawes ge ǁîsa ǃnawetamase ge ǀgai. Tsîs ge ǀnaiǀgui nau ǀgururon ǀkha gere ǀguru.
P6	ǀGuitsēs ge ǂoakuirosa Aoǁkhumub ǁkhâb ǃnâ nē ǀgururon hâǃkhaib ai ge hā ǂnû.
P7	Danab tsî hui-aon hoan ge ǃkhoeǂoa. Nē ǂoakuniros ǃnâb ge ǂkhoaroba hanagam ǂnams ǃnâ anaǁguihe hâ.
P8	ǁȊb ge Ndiwa, ti ge ǀōn hâ i.  ǁȊb ge ge koro tsēde ge ǃnae hâ i.  Mûǂamaob ge ǁnāxa ǁîroba ǂkhorob ǃnâ gere ǃhai.
P9	ǃNonaǁîs ge ǀnabasa hîa ge nē hâǃkhaib ǃoa ǀkhī-ūhes ge Maleas ti ge ǀōn hâ i.  ǁȊs ge ǀawa ǃhūǃgao-ūtas ǃnâ ge ǃnaohe hâ i.
P10	Nē ǃnabaros, Maleas ge ǃhaese ǂkham ǃgarogomab, hîa Enkare ti ǀōnhâb ǀkha ge ǀhōsagu.
P11	ǀGui ǃoe i ge mâ khoe-i hoa-e Ambias di hās xa ge ǂkhaiǂkhai e.
P12	Ambias ge ge koro ǁkhâxa i.  ǁȊros ge ge ǂkhawusa i ne ǁnā kaixu daob di ǃgûs xa.
P13	ǁNā kaixu ǃaob ǁîs dib ǃaromab ge mûǂamaoba ǃhai ǂkhororoba ǀgapise gere ūkhâi.
P14	ǀGēra, Abei tsî Moiti hâra ge ǁaeguros Aoǁkhumub dis ǃnâ ge hā.  Nē ǃkhaupirirora ge ǁaupexa ǀnîkhamako irgu ǀguiga ge ǃnae hâ i.  Kaise ge ǀaekusa i.
P15	Wekheba ǃkharu tsîs ge Abeisa ǁō, on ge hoaraga khoena ge ǃoa hâ i.  Xaweb ge mûǂamaoba, hui-aon di mâxoǀkhas ǀkha Moitsa ge ûi kai.
P16	ǀUniga ge Aoǁkhumub ǃnâ hā-ūhen ge Kopis, Kepip tsî Kejib hâna.  Nē ǂkhari ǀgururon ge ǀgam wekhexas ǀguisa ge i i.  Kaisen ge ge ǃâ hâ i.
P17	Mûǂamaob tsî hui-aon on ge hoa xamarirona ge ǀnam i.  ǁNān hîa kaise ge ǁorexa in tsîna.
P18	Hoana ge ǁgoeǂam hâ ib ge Loboliaba.  ǁȊb ge mûǂamaob di ǂkhorona ge sausau.
P19	Sîsenaon nē ǃkhaib din ge ǂorisase nē ǂkham xamarirona gere kōǃgâ.  ǁȊn ra ǃâubasens ge nē xamariron nî ǀguia sī ǁî-aitsama mâbasensa.  ǃNorasa ûib ǃoan ni ǀguitsē oasa.
P20	The translated text here should be the questions.
