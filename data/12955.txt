title:	Namicha Baayyee Dheeraa
author:	Siyane Aniley and Mulualem Daba
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Afaan Oromo
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/12955.png
desc:	Namicha bayyee dheeraa mana isaa gadhisee bosona keessa jiraachuu jalqabe.

page_text:

P1	Qottoon isaa bayyee gabaabdu dha.
P2	Balballi mana isaa bayyee gabaabdu dha.
P3	Sireeen isaa baayyee gabaabdu dha.
P4	Biskileettiin isaa bayyee gabaabdu dha.
P5	Namichi kun baayyee dheeraa dha!
P6	Qottoo dheeraa hojjate.
P7	Balbala dheeraa hojjate.
P8	Siree isaa baayyee dheeraa godhe.
P9	Biskileettii dheeraa bitate.
P10	Barcuma baayyee dheeraa irra taa'e. Fallanaa baayyee dheeraa taheen nyaata isaa nyaate.
P11	Mana isaa gadhiisee,  bosona keessa umrii dheera jiraate.
