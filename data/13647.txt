page_text:

P1	Hiseera hya luŋumula. Nahisi mwaana gesunga ohwagama engo.
P2	During holiday time,  we do a lot of work and play. Muluŋumula, hu hola emirimu emyeene emingi era hwa gudula.
P3	Muhwana ni Nandi be nyuha mumugamba enyo baŋaa engoho ebiryo, bangala amagi ate batenya ekwi.
P4	Muhwana ni Bbaaba wuwe baŋira eŋombe ohwaaya.
P5	Maama, Muhwana ni Nandi batiina ohuhyalira nguhuhwa yawe e' Mulandu.
P6	They go to the bus stop and wait for the bus to take them.
P7	Nguhu gasanyuha nyoo ohuboona hu bejuhulu baabe! Bamuŋirilaho obulabo.
P8	Sehulu yesi gasanyuha nyoo ohubona hu bejuhulu baabe!
P9	Nguhu ali hutegehera abageni e'hyomumusi.
P10	Nandi ni Muhwana babeŋuye enyeeni n'omucere ehyo mumusi.
P11	Ehyegulogulo, Muhwana gatiina ohugudula omupiira naba meri babe.
P12	Nandi geyunga hu basooma ehyegulo.
P13	Huludaalo olusembayo olw'oluŋumula, Muhwana ni Nandi batiina hu mukolo gwabaana.
