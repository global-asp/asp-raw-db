page_text:

P1	This was a child Elijah who never used to listen to whatever he was told.
P2	One day he was with his mother and they we're going somewhere, the boy saw a mango tree which he climbed and started to pick mangoes.
P3	When the mother saw he had collected a lot she told him what he had collected was enough but he refused.
P4	Then the mother left him so when he was eating his mangoes the lion came and ate him
