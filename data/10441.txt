page_text:

P1	Nele Lekishon. Eipoto Lekishon oldia lenye.
P2	Eiriamari shoo. Eirowuaja enkolong'.
P3	Neinepu pokirare esumash we ngure.
P4	Eshomo aing'oru enkare.
P5	Neiminie inkishu.
P6	Eishirita Lekishon.
P7	Eloito aang' woldia lenye.
P8	Alda! Noona nkishu tiang'!
