title:	Inyang and the Bird
author:	Simon Ipoo
artist:	Wiehan de Jager, Jemma Kahn, Catherine Groenewald, Kathy Arbuckle and Rijuta Ghate
lang:	English
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	A story of a Turkana herdboy who spent many days hungry away from home.

page_text:

P1	Inyang was a herd's boy.
P2	One day….
P3	… he was very hungry.
P4	He got a bird.
P5	And sent it home.
P6	The bird flew.
P7	And landed on the hut.
P8	People heard the bird.
P9	Food was cooked.
P10	They took food.
P11	Found him under the tree.
P12	The cows had gone.
P13	They fed him.
P14	His father went for the cows.
P15	Inyang ate enough.
P16	Inyang and the cows were taken home.
P17	The bird had saved Inyang!
