desc:	Umbhali wale ndaba osemncane ukhathazeke kakhulu ngomndeni wakhe. (Indaba nemifanekiso kwaqanjwa engqungqutheleni yomhlangano wokufundisana eyayigqugquzelwe abasebenzi bezengqondo baseSophiatown eGoli ngamaholide kaJuni 2019).

page_text:

P1	Umama wayekhuluma nocingo.
P2	Wathola umlayezo wokuthi ugogo akasekho.@@Wayekhala kakhulu.
P3	Ngadabuka kakhulu.@@Sonke saqala sakhala.@@Ugogo wayengeve esithanda, noma sesilahlwe yibo bonke abantu.
P4	Zonke izingane zakhe zafika ukuzomngcwaba.@@Sathandaza ukuthi kungenzeki lutho olubi kuwo wonke umndeni.
