page_text:

P1	Fati ekedo etok aboikpa ami ake@  afageke nte utin.@  Aye ama ama ndi sag imam, ayong adomo ukeme@  adi nam nagha edoko aye.
P2	Usen kied, Fati asagha ye ete ye eka amo aka iyem@  ifia.
P3	Fati ama sie ama adi wam@  eka ye ette amo, ado mkpo ike waka se emam@  ado awak se ese. @  Nte Inuen, Ebok@  ye ikpa eyong.
P4	Eka amo akebo,@  "Fati kpang utong! amekeme@  adi yem ifia mi  ayem ko."@  "Ado Fati, mbok se nte aka yung kpeme idem."
P5	Aye aboro "oh!, eka."
P6	Ikpeneke eka ye ette@  amo ema eneke enana ufang.
P7	Ema ekid ikpo ifia.@  @@  Ema ekid ntuk ifia.
P8	Fati nko ama akene anana ufang.@  @@  Anye ama akid etuk ifia.@   @  Anye ama akid ntuk ifia.
P9	Ndien Fati ese ayung.@  @@  Anye akid ndadad@  inuen ke ayan mbied.
P10	Udiana, Fati ama ase isong.@  Anye akid brang nnuene ke ntong@  afang.
P11	Fati akid uyaya mfang. Mfang ado nko ama ayama@  ayung aduk ayin.@  Anye ama asin nnun ubok kied@  ke uyai mfang ado.
P12	Fati abo, "Ayem ibioto@  mfang."@  KE edi bob ayin ntaad, uyaya@  mfang ado atongo isang!@  Fati abo, "ayem idoho@  uyaya mfang. Awawa uduk ikot@  ado ami."
P13	Fati asio nkpo,@  "Ejji!"@  Anye ama afehe usob-usob ke yem eka ye ette@  amo.
P14	Fati akid ette amo asio nkpo,@  asio nkpo "uduk ikod! Uduk@  ikod!"@  Ette abo "ke mmo?@  Ke mmo?@  Fati abo, "ko! Ko!@  "
P15	Ndien ette amo akama eto abene@  awawa uduk ikot ado@  Awawa uduk ikot@  ado afeh asop.
P16	Ette amo abo Fati,@  nkpo unanma.@  @@  Yak ibene eka ikidoko ibanha@  awawa uduk ikot ado.
P17	Eka amo abo "Fati,esit anen ke awawa iduk ikot ike udomo fien."
P18	"Ado ini mfen, mbok se nte aka, yung neke kpeme."@  Ndien utid ikido dro.
