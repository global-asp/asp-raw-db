desc:	Arach yali mugunha gha mwaami ku matwaale age Quarolopint. Yali mulungi inho nga buli musaadha mu matwaale ayendha kwefuunza bulungi bwe aye okwebonagho nhobulolobavubwaletala bulungi okubaotinomubi asingayo.

page_text:

P1	Mumatwaale ga Quarolopint, mwali mu inhe bantu asinga yo bulungi ayetabwa.
P2	Abasaadha abalangira mumatwaale balaga obwetaavu obwamaanhi mu Arach.
P3	Basndhana beenhenabeenhe olwokuwangula omutima gwe. Aye byonabyoona byagwa.
P4	Bbaabba gha Arach, Ojok, nga aghandhi abuuza Arach lwaaki tiyalaga kwendha.
P5	Ayenga airamu, ''Bbaabba, ku basaadha abo ezira asaanha bulungi bwange.''
P6	Obulungi n'omuwendho gwange bisaana bakyerupe.
P7	Emikuzi n'emikuzi, Arach yeyongeire mubumantaavu bwa okudheema abasaadha.
P8	Kale abasaadha beyongera yo era babayiza abakazi mwitaka eryo.
P9	Emyaaka dhabita, era nga Arach akaali nekilowoozo ekyo kufumbirwa kyerupe.
P10	Bakagwa be babayiza mumaka ageisanhu. Yenga akaali.
P11	Buli myeeka bwegyeyongera nokukulakwe kutyo, aye okweraga tikwemwikiriza.
P12	''Ndhi mukuluinho okufuna abaana, ay'era tiidha kubayiza abasaadha bano''.
P13	Arach yatuuka emyeeka anha era tiyafuna musaadha omukyerupe.
P14	Yafuuka ekyokusekererwa eri abaghala kukyaalo.
P15	Bamutwaala obulungi nga obutagasa.
P16	Ghazira yayendhe kumwefananiza kuba yatwaribwa nga ekikolimo.
P17	Bbaabba we nga'omwami, yenka neyamwendha.
P18	Ekyembi yafa era Arach yaasigala yenka.
P19	Yaliranga buli lunaku olwokusekererwa n'abantu.
P20	Ekyomukisa omusaadha omukaire kukyaalo yamubayiza.
P21	Yamala gaikiriza kuba yabuzibwaaku ebintu byonabyona mukufa kwabbaabba we.
P22	Yakitegeera nti bwaba tafumbirwa aidha kubonaabona.
P23	Arach yabonabonezebwa n'omusaadha omukaire.
