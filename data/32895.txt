desc:	Amai banobhancha Thabo kululamisa chipo chibhodo chamegulu babe.

page_text:

P1	Amegulu bamuzha kuno vakaja. Nokudalo banosobuzila Thabo mandongo nemichelo yeshango, zumanga ne lemoni umwenshaji. @@Thabo umuda kupa amegulu babe chinhu chibhodo neebo.
P2	lwhalo ingaba ibhodo, Koga amegulu bangatubula kubala?@@Ngana wabenda kuchintolo walowana chinhu chibuya.@@"lzha tilulamise chikwama," Thabo wakaleba emwemweteka. "Chikwama chikulwana chonkwakwa wejini."
P3	Mai banobhisa uncheka wabakabatenga kunchintolo. Wakawala chisakulo akatanga kucheka.@@Potanga mabhazo mabili makulwana mpambi.
P4	Kojota mabili matukutuku kupa bumba.@@Thabo wakapeta pamwempela namabhazo mabili.
P5	Bano cheka kusi zwifwapi zwibili zwilefu. Yezwi ndizo zwichasimisa chikwama. @@Thabo wakapeta kojoba amai betola chisakulo akale. Baka cheka zwifwapi zuna zulefu mpabi.
P6	Zubili zuchafumilwa pamwempela kululamisa chisakulo. @@Yechi chichapa chikwama isimba lilophamijwa lubili.
P7	Feno amai banobhisa unshina wofhuma. @@Unoshanganisa zwifwapi zose kululamisa chikwama chamegulu. @@Unopa Thabo unomwemweta eti, "Taboka mai chikwama chonkwakwa chamegulu!"
