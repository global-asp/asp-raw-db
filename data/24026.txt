page_text:

P1	Hakaa hariyo omwoojo wubakweeta Abeeri.
  Akekorera ekigaari. 
  

  Abeeri akaba ateine muvuji w’ekigaari kye
  kisya. Yajira mwiine nyina Meri ati, “Ninyenda omuvuji wekigaari kyanje, bambe
  mpereza dolle yawe. Neebasa kushitama omukigaari.”
P2	Konka Meri yagira ati, “Ngaaha, dolle yange ningyenda.
  “Meri kuuyazibeire Abeeri kutwaara dolle, Abeeri yaagira ekiniga kyyeinji
  munonga. Yakwaata dolle na Meri yamunyurura. Atyo Meri yanyurura omukono gwa
  dolle ogwaakabiri. Abeeri yanyurura gumwe, Meri yakwaata ogundi. Omukono gwa
  dolle gwacweeka.
P3	Meri yarira nataragaza owa nyina naagira ati “Reeba
  maama,Abeeri yanyurura omukona gwa dolle yangye yagucwa. Yaba nayenda ngu
  dolle yangye yagucwa. Yaba nayenda ngu dolle yangye eshitame omu kigaari kye
  kisya konka na’ninyenda kujizaanisa.
  

  Nyina yamugira ati “Abeeri tiyaagira micwe.”
P4	Maama yatekyereza okwarayegyese mutabani weeye
  obutakwaata aha byokuzaanisa bya mwiine nyina. Yabwena eki arakore. 
  

  Yaaza owa munywaani we omushabo yamubuuza, “Ninyenda
  onyambe bambe.” Omushaho yamugarukamu aati, “Nkuyambe nta munywaani wangye.”
P5	Maama yamugiira ati, “Mutabani wangye Abeeri
  areesire emicwe mibi ebiro ebi. Yacwa omukono gwa dolle ya mwiine nyina. Ekyo
  tayine kukikora. 
  

  Nkyakara ninkumugira ngu areete dolle
  ojigaruzeho omukono.”
P6	“Bambe mugire ngu ayine kusharura kworaheze
  kukora dolle. Tayine sente, mbweenu omugire ngu ayozye emotokoya yaawe enkuru
  empango ekukira kuba eyine omucuucu.” Nyiina nikwe yajizire. 
  

  Munywani yasheka nagira ati “Nimba nyine kuza
  ow’omushaho.”
P7	Nyina wa Abeeri yagaruka omunju ye. Yabuuza
  Abeeri ati, “K’okuba orweire nooza nkahi?” Abeeri yamugarukamu ati “Nimba
  nyine kuza ow’omushaho.”
  

  Nyina wa Abeeri yamugira ati, “dolle
  wajifutaza, mbweenu oyine kujitwaara ow’omushaho.”
P8	Abeeri atyo yatwaara dolle ow’omushaho. “dolle
  eji eri omubusaasi bwamaani. Maama yangira ngu njireete aha. Mushaho, nobaasa
  kujiheereza omukono musya?”
  

  Omushaho yeikyiriza kureberera dolle. Akabaasa
  kukora omukono musya.
P9	Omushaho yagira Abeeri ati, “Dolle eyine
  omukono musya. Noteekwa wanshashura. Oyine sente zingahi?”
  

  Abeeri yagira ati, “Bambe mushaho, tinyine
  nangwa. Tinkubaasa kushshurira omurimo guwaakora.
P10	Omushaho yamugira ati, “Toyine sente, reeba
  emotoka yangye empaango eyine omucuucu mwinji. Jinyogyze obwe nikwo
  washashura.
  

  Abeeri yabwena ebucketi y’amizi n’akeenda
  kabweneire. Akatuuraho nayzya emotoka y’omucuucu egyo.
P11	Your translated textAtyo Abeeri yagaruza dolle
  owa Meri. Akashemererwa yagira Abeeri ati, "Ori munyanyazi murungi. Webare
  kugaruzaho dolle yanye."
  

  "Mwiine maw, nsasira kugira ekiniga nkareetera
  otashemererwa." Nikwo Abeeri yamugarukiremu.
P12	Kuruga obunaku obwe, Abeeri taragarukiremu
  kutwara ebintu bya mwiine nyina. Kandi yagyezaho obutamugirira kiniga. 
  

  Abeeri kuyeijukire obweire buyamazire nayoza
  egyo emotoka y'omushaho empango kugyeihaho oburofa. Yareba ngu tikwo ayine
  kushashurira kigira ekiniga n'okwaata ebintu.
