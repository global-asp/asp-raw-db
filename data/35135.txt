page_text:

P1	Gogogo! Gogogoi!@@Pagonhi rekumberi panogogodzwa zvine simba
P2	Sharnice anobva atanga kufara. "Ndiani?", anobvunza.  "Mhamha, tine muenzi!"
P3	"Zvakanaka," mai vake vanopindura. "Vanofarira kudai vari Mai Magadzire. Vavhurire gonhi."
P4	Sharnice anovhura gonhi achinyemwerera. "Titambire mhamha, pindai zvenyu. Mhamha vangu vari kuuya manje manje kuzova nemi." Mai Magadzire vanosekerera Sharnice.
P5	"Ndikutsvagirei chekunwa here?" Sharnice anobvunza.@@"Aiwa, ndakaguta Sharnice. Waita hako," vanopindura mai Magadzire
P6	"Maswera sei, Mai Magadzire," vanodaro Mai Sithole, mai vaSharnice. "Ndine hurombo ndakumisaika."@@"Aaa, hazvina kana basa shamwari, Sharnice ndiye ainditandadza."
P7	Mai vaSharnice vanonyemwera, "Hongu, Sharnice anogona kubata vaenzi. Ndinodada naye zvekuti," vakaudza Mai Magadzire.
