page_text:

P1	Tortoise and Squirrel need money.
P2	They make plates to sell at the market.
P3	Tortoise sees a fight at the market.
P4	Squirrel and Mole are fighting.
P5	"Leave my friend alone!" shouts Tortoise.
P6	Mole bites Tortoise on the nose.
P7	His broken nose hurts.
P8	Tortoise has a crooked nose because he rushed to fight.
