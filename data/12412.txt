desc:	A story about meeting new friends

page_text:

P1	My name is Simo.
P2	Meet my new friends.
P3	Zizo,  Lele,  Sisa and Ayanda.
P4	Zizo loves football.
P5	Lele loves to swim.
P6	Sisa loves to play hide and seek.
P7	Ayanda loves reading.
P8	I enjoy everything my friends love.
P9	I will play football with Zizo.
P10	Sometimes I swim with Lele.
P11	Or even play hide and seek with Sisa.
P12	Come on,  make new friends and have fun.
P13	Play football together.
P14	Swim together.
P15	Play hide and seek together.
P16	Or even read together.
