page_text:

P1	Ormwalimui,  Ormwalimui,  Ormwalimui,  kayiolo entoki nikisuma,  Kisuma iyie nanu ajo keetai irankin opasha.
P2	Ore ole dukuya naa olodo anaa orsarge. Ore ensati ena anyoni naa kedoo.
P3	Orsiteti anaa ormachungwai. Ore olkarasha liabori le natasat na kesiteti.
P4	Ormwalimui,  Ormwalimui kayio niche,  intasho matolimu. Osikitoi anaa enmpira ormwalimui.
P5	Olonyori anaa inkujit. Ore intapuka naa olchani o nkujit naa kenyori.
P6	Orpus anaa oloigange. Ore olmarinda le naa keipus.
P7	Orpile olerai. Ore ildonyo tenapisha naa kipile. Olerai anaa boo o kuna ajijik.
P8	Ketai sii oloibor o lorok. Ore enshati ena anyoni naa kerok eibor.
P9	Ormwalimui,   Ormwalimui ira sidai anaa irankin.
