title:	Nabulira ntya mu kiya
author:	Enocha Tasingika
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lusoga

desc:	Omusulube bamusuubiza ogulabo ogubba ku mazaalibwa ge ago omukaaga. Nhonereza ebyaligho.

page_text:

P1	Ku kyalo kyange kuliku embuzi. Gheswireku akata okuva mu bibuga ebibba.
P2	Maama ghange akoba, ''Elliabu, tuli ne ekirabo kyo ku mazaalibwa go.
P3	Maama, senga ni nze twalindirira e bbaasi.
P4	Na tyama ku bisambi bya maama. Naghongera olugendo lwona lwona.
P5	Twatuuka mu kiya. Nabona namunkanga ya bbululu enkalamu.
P6	Omudaala ogundi gwaliku e bibala. Nabuuza maama ''biki bino?''
P7	Maama yankoba, ''Dhino apo.''  Nasaba, nguliraaku ndala.''
P8	Nerabiire omukono gwa maama ghange.  Ndhuubye nga apo yange.
P9	Bwenamala okulya, Nali zenka. Nainhama okubbolooga.
P10	Omukazi yantwala aghaali abaana abandi.
P11	Nali ndhuuba nti abaana boona batanibwa mu kiya.
P12	Omusaadha ya buuza  ''Eliabu aligha?'' Na mwekwekaku.
P13	Eiroboozi lya maama ghange lye namanhirira lya yeta, ''Eliabu!'' Nasanhuka
P14	Songa ghange ya sikulayo namunkanga enkalamu. ''Yiyo!'' bwe yankoba.
