desc:	The story was written during the 2020 International Day of the Girl Child in Bauchi. Its the story of a girl called Batina. Batina has wicked friends that lured her to the river and almost killed her by drowning but she was rescued.

page_text:

P1	There was a girl called Batina. Her two friends were Katola and Mbane
P2	One day, they went to the river to fetch water.
P3	"Let's take our bath before going home", they told her.
P4	Binta agreed. "Go in first, we will join you", they said.
P5	They pushed her into the deep river and ran away.
P6	Binta started screaming for help. A fisherman came and helped her.
P7	Katala and Mbakana went home. They lied that Batina drowned in the river.
P8	They were shocked to see Batina back.  Batina forgave them. They promised not to be wicked again.
