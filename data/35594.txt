page_text:

P1	We have a big beautiful flower in our school.
P2	We love our flower, it is very beautiful.
P3	Why is our flower very beautiful? because it is planted in our school garden and we tender it all the time.
P4	The bright colours of the flower beautifies our school.
P5	Every body in our community began to pluck and replant our big beautiful flower in their houses. Our community is now very beautiful  because  of our big beautiful flower.
