desc:	Luno lufumo olwogera kungeri yobulamu bwekyalo Namayira.

page_text:

P1	Ekyaalo kyange kyeetebwa Namayira.
P2	Amaadhi bwegatoonha,kiba kyakiragala inho.
P3	Bwetuba mwidhembe,bbaabba atutwaala okulambula ebisolo ebiri okumpi.
P4	Mwikumiro ly'ebisolo tubonayo empologoma n'endhovu.
P5	Ebisolo biba ghalala mwidhembe.
P6	Abalambuuzi boona baidha mwikumiro ly'ebisolo okubibona.
P7	Tulina ente mukyalo kyaife.Enhume yaife etiisa abaana.
P8	Ngonza inho okusiiga ebifananhie nga mmaze okukola emirimo.
P9	Lulala nasiiga ekifananhie kya puusi yaife jebeeta Kairu.
P10	Wadhaiga mukagwa ghange.Ayendha inho ebifananhie byange.
P11	Lata akoba ati okulya omusaayi gw'ente kikuuma obutonde bw'omubiri bukalamu
P12	Balirwaana batweyungaku okulyaku.
P13	Tulina entaama n'ebisolo ebindhi mukyaalo kyaife.
P14	Bwewabaagho ekyeeya,tighaba mmere.
P15	Buli kunco nhwa buugi memale naaja kwisomero.
P16	Nhinha bakwaga bange kamaala b'enzaana nabo.
