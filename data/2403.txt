title:	Mboso Ikumea
author:	Stephen Musyoka
artist:	Mlungisi Dlamini
lang:	Kikamba
level:	First words

thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/2403.png
desc:	This story is about planting a bean seed and what happens when you water and look after it.

page_text:

P1	Mui umeaa mbee.
P2	Umeaa ulikite muthangani.
P3	Na indi kitina kyumea kimeaa kyelekele sua.
P4	Matu ma mbee makumila.
P5	Mumea wa mboso uyaasava.
P6	Na ukyumya malaa. Malaa mautwika mboso.
P7	Ve mbeu mbingi nthini wa muthande wa mboso.
