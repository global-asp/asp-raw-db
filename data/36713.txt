P1	Once apon a time a girl named Sonya asked how can you climb a tree.But no one answered.
P2	Her perants said we don't know but listen to your heart.
P3	She found a tree and started climbing up.
P4	A woman saw her and a man and said come down but she refused.
P5	A girl named Esther came to save her so she come down now  and she jumped down Esther cached her.
P6	Wile they where walking they where talking and Esther told Sonya to never climb a tree and she said okay the end.
