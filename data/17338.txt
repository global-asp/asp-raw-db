title:	Ikhaya Lezilwane Elikhethekile
author:	Pumla mdontswa and Zanele Zuma
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	isiZulu
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/17338.png
desc:	Indaba imayelana nokufika kwezilwane ezihlengwa zisengabantwana esikhungwini sezintandane. Lezi zilwane ezincane zinakekelwa ngumuntu okhethekile nethimba lakhe.

page_text:

P1	Leli yikhaya lezilwane eziyizintandane.
P2	Ngenyanga edlule bekumatasatasa kuleli khaya. UNdlovana ube ngowokuqala ukufika.   Wayezacile futhi elusizi.
P3	Kodwa ngokukhulu ukushesha uNdlovana wanonophala. Waqala ukudlala nezinye izilwane.
P4	Ezinsukwini ezimbalwa,   kwathi cababa indiza enophephela emhlane kuleli khaya.
P5	Ngabe ilethe bani namhlanje? Ha! Omunye undlovana?
P6	UNdlovana wayembeswe ngengubo yokulala. Wayenezinsuku ezinhlanu ezelwe. Umgcini wamncelisa ibhodlela.
P7	Vum,  vum,  vum! Ngubani-ke lowo? Ha! Yiloli elilethe uNdlulamithi.
P8	UNdlulamithi  wayenezinyanga ezinhlanu. Wayebuthakathaka emva kohambo lwakhe olude.
P9	Ngenxa yentamo yakhe ende,  umgcini kwakufanele agibele esihlahleni ukuze akwazi ukumncelisa.
P10	Ekupheleni kwenyanga kwa gcina sekufike izintandane zamawundlu engwe. Zazintathu,  uKopi,  uKipi,  noKeji.
P11	Umgcini nabasizi bakhe babezithanda zonke lezi zilwane,  ngisho nalezo ezigangile.
P12	Abantu bakuleli khaya babesebenza kanzima kodwa ngothando. Babefisa ukuthi ngelinye ilanga lezi zintandane ziyokwazi ukuzinakekela ngokwazo,  zibuyele emakhaya azo emvelo.
