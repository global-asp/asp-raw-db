page_text:

P1	Faanana  n'amaka ge  baviira Idhindha,  ekibuga ekyakanhigo  einho  mu  Yuganda. Faanana musomi  gha kya kusatu.  Gha  mutima mulungi  ate mugezigezi.
P2	Faanana  ayenda  ebisolo  bye.  Ali  ni puusi, enkoko   ibiri,  embuzi  ne ngiibwa.  Amala  ebiseera  bye  ne bisolo  bino. Abigheegheeta  n'okuzaanha azaanha nabyo.
P3	Olunaku lulala, Faananayali  mukisaawe ekyo ku isomero  ni  mikwano gye, yabona omwana nga   akuba akagiibwa.

"leaki  bali   kubonereza  engiibwa?"  yeebuuza  mwene.
P4	Yaalekera okuzaanha yaaja yaabaana abaali  bakuba engiibwa amabaale.

Mu kusooka, mikwano gye baali ti bali kutegeera kili  kugya mu maiso, era baamusimbaku.  
"Mulekere  okukuba amabaale," Faanana bwe yakaabuka.
P5	Abaana abandi baalumuka. 

Faanana yagema engiibwa eibiri dhe baali  bakubye. Yaabona ebighundu ku maghagha. Yasalagho  okutwaala ebinhonhi byombi  eka abiidhandhabe.
P6	Yaaliisa engiibwa. Olweigulo yaakobera abeeghaka  kiki kye yabona ni kye yakola emisana, ne ngeri ye yayambamu engiibwa.
P7	Kunco, Faanana n'abazaire be baaja mu irwaliro  baafuna obulezi obwe ngiibwa dhaibwe.


nga  wabiseegho  ennhaku  ntono  inho, ebighundu  eby'engiibwa  byaghona. Faanana  yaba  musagambu inho.
P8	Faanana yaakoba mikwano gye, "nhenda ebisolo. Ebisolo.  Ebisolo bya mugaso ye tuli ate mikwano  gyaife. Tuteekwa okubikuuma n'okubirabirira."
