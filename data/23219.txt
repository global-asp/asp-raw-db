page_text:

P1	Kong! Kong! Ejai itwan yeni okongi ekek lo ngaren.
P2	Iyalama Alake."Ingaibo ngin," ingisit ya. "Toto, ijatar so ipejonon!"
P3	"Aso, obongokini ngesi toto ke." Amit Mrs Hassan keda ike mukeru. Ongaik kesi ekek.
P4	Abu Alake ongar ekek imuriemurai. "Iyalama aanyun toto, iboikin ocholong. Ebunit toto apak a na edit." Ochamuni Mrs Hassan, ijulankin aboilia kede ike koku.
P5	"Omat ber akipi nu toto.Ainak idio bore iche kere?ingisi Alake."mam ipodokit engo Alake,Iyalama."obongokini Mrs.Hassan
P6	"Aparan najokan Mrs.Hassan,"obala toto kenge Alakeewuta alomun Odiro."Itin engo kanu aitilor ijo""Mam ipodo apapero,Isialamikit iso Alake ejok."
P7	Imuriemuraikini ngesi toto kenge Alake."Ebo"Obongokini."Ejeni Alake idar ipejok ejok noi i bore yen apogar ngo kanu angesi "
