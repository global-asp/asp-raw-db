desc:	Omuti ogwe gukayombera kiki ahari ebishisha bantu.

page_text:

P1	izoba rimwe kiki akasitama ahansi yogwe omuti anyigyire kandi atinire.
P2	kuyabire aryaho yahurira iraka rikuruga enyuma yee.yahinduka yashanga omutwe gukumushekyeramu.akaguruka atinire kandi ashobirwe.
P3	omuti gukatandika kuraa.kiki akagyira obwoba yabuza omuti ahabwakyi gukurira."ahabwakyi norira muti we?omuti gwagarukamu ngu ahabwokuba abantu nibabiota burizoba nokubatema.
P4	kiki akashasha aha bwomuti kandi yaguraganisa okugukuma na banyabuzare ahari  abazigu.
P5	kiki na ogwe muti bakanwana.boona bakashemererwa ahabwokunwana
P6	kiki akanjura omukazi we nabana be owomuti ogukugamba.mbwenu bakaba abanwani barungi.kiki kuyabire afuna obwire abumara naganira nomuti.
P7	singa ebishani ebiri omu kagano
