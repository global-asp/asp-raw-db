title:	Impanuka ikomeye

desc:	Abana babiri babonye impanuka ikomeye cyane ibakura umutima.

page_text:

P1	Nge na mushiki wange twarimo twitemberera ku muhanda.
P2	Tubona igikamyo kinini kimanuka mu muhanda ucuramye cyane.
P3	Muri ako kanya, tubona imodoka ntoya yiruka cyane izamuka aho mu muhanda.
P4	Umushoferi w'igikamyo yari yarangaye. Yari arimo kuvugira kuri terefoni.
P5	Muri ako kanya icyo gikamyo kiba kigonze ya modoka ntoya!
P6	Hashize akanya, imodoka ya ambilasi na polisi biba birahageze.
P7	Ndetse hahita hagera n'izindi modoka ebyiri zije gukurura izakoze impanuka.
P8	Ibyo byose byabaye tubireba. Byaduteye ubwoba bwinshi!
