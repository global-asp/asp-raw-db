title:	Ondoongi no Hove
author:	Hamukoto Efraim
artist:	Salim Kasamba
lang:	Oshikwanyama
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/37048.png
desc:	Okahokololo kombinga yOhove hailongo neeghono osho yoo Oondongi ina eedunge.

page_text:

P1	Omunafalama okwa li ena Ohove yakulupa.
P2	Osho yoo Ondoongi.@@Ohove no Ndoongi ovali ookaume.
P3	Komatango, Ohove oya lombwela Ondoongi, "Onda loloka nena."
P4	Ohove oya lombwela Ondoongi, "Ina ndi fuwapo nena."
P5	Ondoongi oya nyamukula, "Ame onda longa unene shidulife pwoove."
P6	Efiku lala ndula, Ohove oya tya, "Efiku lonena okwa li lii."
P7	Ondoongi oya tya kuye, "Ngeenge Omunafalama okwe uya, tyaa, 'Moo!' Ote kuefa."
P8	Efiku lala ndula, Ohove oya kwena, 'Moo!'@@Omunafalama okwe mu efa afuwepo.
P9	Omunafalama okwa kufa Ondoongi i kapulule epya.
P10	Ondoongi oya longa neenghono efiku alishe. @@Okwa li aloloka unene.
P11	Ondoongi oya tya, "Omunafalama okwa ti ote ku dipaya uninge ombelela."
P12	Ohove oya tya, "Oha ndilongo mongula. Paife ondi udite xwepo."
