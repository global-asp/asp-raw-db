title:	Ungrateful Jackal

desc:	Rabbit saves Jackal from a difficult situation. But instead of fulfilling his promise to reward Rabbit

page_text:

P1	Hungry Jackal was hunting.
P2	He chased a mouse under a big rock.
P3	As Jackal dug, the rock fell on his paw.
P4	Rabbit heard Jackal calling and ran to see what happened.
P5	Rabbit agreed to help Jackal because Jackal promised him dinner.
P6	Rabbit pushed the rock until it rolled off Jackal’s paw.
P7	Rabbit asked for his reward.

Jackal threatened to eat him!
P8	Jackal and Rabbit found an old man to judge their case.
P9	Jackal threatened to eat the old man too!
P10	The man said, “Let me see the rock before I decide.”
P11	Jackal and Rabbit took the man to the rock.
P12	Jackal explained how the rock fell on his paw.
P13	The man asked Rabbit to show how he pushed the rock.
P14	Rabbit pushed until the rock was back on Jackal’s paw.
P15	The man said, “Now it is fair. Let us go home.”
P16	The man and Rabbit went. 

Jackal was left trapped.
