page_text:

P1	Lowoozaamu ku  olunaku  lulala  singa  tubaaku  n'akabaga  ku  lubalama  lw'enhandha  Nalubaale?
P2	Lowoozaamu ku  olunaku singa  buli  muntu asobola  okusoma era  nga  n'ebitabo  birigho ebya buli  lulimi?
P3	Lowoozaamu ku olunaku lwendizaala omwana  wange  asooka.
P4	Lowoozaamu ku  olunaku singa  abantu  boonaboona  baba mu  mirembe nga baganda  baibwe  ni  banhinaibwe.
P5	Olowoozaamu  olunaku  nga  nninza okutambulira  ku  maadhi.
P6	Lowoozaamuuku olunaku singa mughala wo  okufuuka  omukulembeze ow'eighanga.
P7	alowoozaamu  ku  olunaku  singa  oli kumpi  kuteebwa  mu  magombe era  ghaira  mu  bulamu.
P8	Lowoozaamu ku  olunaku  singa buli  muntu  afuna  amaghagha ni  baguluka okutuuka  y'omutonzi.
P9	Lowoozaamu  ku  olunaku  singa ensi yoonayoona  ebwikiibwaku kapeti.
