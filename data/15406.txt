title:	Be Nitie Ng'at Machal Koda?

desc:	Wuoro ka be nitiere an machielo kamoro e piny

page_text:

P1	Awuoro ka be nitie moro machal koda kamoro e piny.
P2	Maneno ka an.
P3	To wuoyo machal koda.
P4	To nigi yier mong'inre ka mara.
P5	Ng'at manigi wang'a,  ita.
P6	Man gi nyaka nyiero na.
P7	Manyalo timo gima anyalo timo.
P8	To ok nyal timo gima ok anyal.
P9	Aseng'iyo mang'iyo kuonde te mondo ayud an morro. Asedhi piny e bungu marango e tok yiende.
P10	Narango aluora,  malo gi piny.
P11	E such ng'ato ka ng'ato.
P12	Nokanyal yudo machal koda kamoro amora kata achiel.
P13	Nitie mana an achiel e piny ka! An makende,  oh en adier!
P14	Kata kamano ok en an kende,  be ineno?Nitie mana in achiel kende!
