page_text:

P1	Silo the goat lives in Sagini village.
P2	She likes stealing fruits and vegetables from Mallam Tanko's farm.
P3	She steals and eats them all up.
P4	Everytime she steals from Malam Tanko's farm, she overfeeds herself.
P5	One day, Malam Tanko caught her stealing. He told her that he would give her some seeds to grow her own farm if she promises to change.
P6	His behaviour really suprised her so she decided to stop stealing immediately. She told him that she was sorry and will never steal again.
P7	From that day, she didn't steal from Mallam Tanko's farm again.
P8	She planted her own fruits and vegetables to eat and share with others.
