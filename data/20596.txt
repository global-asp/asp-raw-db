desc:	Kigana me obedo kwong chwo adek kodi nyako achieli maju keng kisoma to ju kadho dwaaro winyini iluli.

page_text:

P1	obedo ndir achieli, oyido nitye chwo mathindho adek kodi nyako achieli. ido jujye jubedo ichalo achieli ma ilwongo ni Nakwiga.
P2	oyido jusoma isomero ma ilwongo ni Busolwe Glory of God primary school.
P3	idhoyo munyo jukadho isomero oyido jutera winjo ka winyini juwero
P4	ndelo acheili, chwo adek kodi nyako achieli jokeng kadho isomero to jukadho dwaar iluli mani chengin
P5	winyo ochako weri ni, wii lwongani arachi; ani aki rachi *4.
P6	aber tokiparo ni arachi alabedo marachi igike. to nyithidho me jumako winyo no to judoki ngine pecho.
P7	munyo jo tundo pecho to ju yango winyo no to ju chamo.
P8	yawere mere jujye jutho. rupiri winyo oyido nigi butwa.
