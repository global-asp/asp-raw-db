desc:	The story is about insects meeting to discuss starting school and selected the tall  tree where  the bee lived to be their school. Some insects struggled to climb up to attend and the clever helpful spider used its web as a ladder to help our.

page_text:

P1	The insects came together near the river to talk about school. @@The spider, the locust, the bee, the ant and the butterfly came to the meeting.
P2	The insects chose the tall tree where the bee lived to be their school. @@The locust said, "I will be your teacher."
P3	Monday came, Teacher Locust rang the school bell. @@All insects ran to the tree.
P4	The bee flew up the tree. @@Then the butterfly followed.
P5	Teacher Locust and the ant could not climb up to the tree. @@The spider watched them struggling as he waited for his turn to climb.
P6	The clever spider thought quickly and made a web from the bottom to the top of the tree. @@The ant and Teacher Locust climbed up the tree using the spider web.
P7	All insects thanked the clever spider for helping. @@Then, school started at the top of the tree.
P8	Teacher Locust taught them how to save food for winter. @@The bee thanked the spider with honey.
