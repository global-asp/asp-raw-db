P1	John was looking for a new shirt his old shirts are all filthy dirty and muddy.
P2	He searched for hours but It was too hard to find a shirt just one little shirt all he wanted was a shirrrrrrrt.
P3	He asked his friend Ava to help him but it was no use it wasn't enough to find a shirt for him.
P4	He found this Orange shirt but he didn't like it it was just Orange and it had nothing on it plus it wasn't his size.
P5	Finally he found the perfect shirt for him it was his size he loved the designing on it it looks so amazing So he tried it on.
P6	Ava tried it one too But then they both turned into animals then the whole world started to become animals What are we gonna do John said I have no idea said Ava.
P7	Then all the city and the universe started becoming animals there was nothing left Only animals not even buildings Where standing up tall in their places.
P8	Ava and John look around for clues to find out the cure So that day can Be free from being animals.
P9	They checked a barn they checked The grass They checked everywhere but could not find a cure
P10	But then Steffy and Dave arrived they saved   Ava and JohnThey were the ones who had the cure.
P11	Then Ava and John were safe and not animals anymore.
P12	Then they decided to throw throw the shirt away even though John really liked it it was what had to be done.
