page_text:

P1	I am Ana Maria Namata.
P2	My dad is Mr Agaba Ali.
P3	My mum is Ananga Asha. She cares and loves so much.
P4	Dad wants apples and ants.
P5	Angela and Agatha play with toy cats and rabbits.
P6	Male is a tall man.
P7	Apples are packed in a basket.
P8	Namata,  Cathy,  Sara, Ali and Aba sat together.
P9	Mummy taught me words with sound Aa.
P10	Namagala is a prayerful girl.
P11	Asaba and Akello learnt about a train.
