title:	Dog and Crocodile

desc:	Dog finds some eggs on the river shore

page_text:

P1	Dog found eggs near a river. He asked,  "Which animal left eggs here?"
P2	He put the eggs in his bag.
P3	Dog took the eggs home. He put them near the fire.
P4	Crocodile asked Dog,  "Have you seen my eggs?" Dog said,  "No."
P5	Crocodile asked every animal,  "Have you seen my eggs?"
P6	The eggs hatched one by one.
P7	Dog gave the young crocodiles little food.
P8	Crocodile heard the young crocodiles crying.
P9	Crocodile beat Dog with her tail. Dog ran away.
P10	Crocodile chased Dog.
P11	Dog cried,  "I am sorry. I did not know they were your eggs."
P12	Crocodile forgave Dog. She went to swim with her children.
