title:	Uko Amabara Yabaye Umwe mu Kirere
author:	Jean Paul Harelimana
artist:	Offei Tettey Eugene
lang:	Kinyarwanda

desc:	Umwana muto w umukobwa yigishije murumuna we impamvu imikororombya iba mu kirere.

page_text:

P1	Umunsi umwe nyuma y'imvura nyinshi, Keza yitegereje mu kirere abonamo amabara atandukanye, kandi bwaribwo bwa mbere ayabona.
P2	Yaribwiye ati: "Mbega amabara meza!" Hari umuntu wakoresheje imbaraga ze asiga aya amabara mu kirere akoresheje uburoso nkuko Papa ajya abigenza asiga amazu.
P3	Yirukankiye kwa Mukuru we aravuga ati: "Kaliza, reba! Hari umuntu wadusigiye mu kirere amabara akigira kiza cyane!
P4	Kaliza yaravuze ati: Oya! Uriya ni umukororombya. Uracyari umwana, nge ndi mukuru, urumva rero ko nzi impamvu uri mu kirere. Reka ngusobanurire neza."
P5	Kaliza yajyanye Keza ahaba inyamaswa, aravuga ati: "Imukororombya ntiwagaragara kuberako ibyana by'intare bikivuka cyangwa se inzovu aribwo zikivuka."
P6	Yongeye kujyana Keza mu busitani bw'indabo aramubwira ati: "Ntizigaragara kuberako ibinyugunyugu byatwaye amabara yazo mu bicu."
P7	Yeretse  Keza igitabo arangije aramubwira ati: Ntabwo amabara agaragara kuberako imvubu yirukanse n'imbara igatera ubwoba amabara agahungira mu kirere.
P8	Nzi neza impamvu imikororombya iboneka. Byose mama yarabimbwiye, nange ndabigusobanurira.@@Kaliza yafashe Keza akaboko berekeza ku kibuga cy' umupira.
P9	"Imikororombya igaragara mu kirere kubera abana bameze nkanje nawe. Umunsi umwe, amabara yabonekaga hasi avuye mu kirere agakunda ko bayareba.
P10	"Yabonaga abana bafite uruhu rwera n'abafite urwirabura kandi bakababona kimwe. Bagatekereza bati, mbega ukuntu ari byiza cyane kubona aba bana!"
P11	Ayo mabara yajyanye mu kirere atekereza ibyo gukora afata umanzuro ati: "Niba dushaka guhora tugaragara neza kandi dushimishije, tugomba kwishyira hamwe nk' itsinda."
P12	Kuva ubwo amabara yishyize hamwe maze amurika cyane uko ashoboye. Yizeye kuza beza cyane nk'abana yabonye, aribo bameze nkawe nanjye.
