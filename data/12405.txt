desc:	A girl gets stuck to a rock and her mother builds a fence around her for protection. They agree that the girl will listen for her mothers singing before opening the door in the fence. Greedy Hyena has his own plans.

page_text:

P1	Atabo iyo Akiru waxeey aadeen wabiga si ay biyo uga soo dhaansadaan.
P2	Marki ay guriga usoo laabanayeen waxeey ku nasteen dhagax weeyn.
P3	Atabo waxuu isku dayay inuu dhagaxii ka kaco oo gurigii aado lakin dhagaxii weeynaa ayaa ku dhagay.
P4	Akiru waxuu ucararay guriga si uu hooyadii ugu soo sheego.
P5	Markii ay hooyadii timaaday meeshii dhagaxa,  waxa ay aragtay Atabo oo dhagaxii saaid ugu dhagay.
P6	Hooyadii waxey tiri Atabo,  "waxaan kuu sameenayaa deer oo ku hareersan dhagaxa,  si uu kaaga difaaco xayawaanada duur joogta ah."
P7	Kadib waxeey sameesay deerkii,  oo ay usameesay hal albaab oo ay kasoo gali jirtay.
P8	Lakin waraabe ayaa udhuuman jiray oo dhageesan jiray markeey Atabo hooyadii timaado.
P9	Waraabihii waxuu isku taxluujiyay inuu barto heesta.
P10	Xayawaanihii kale ayaa waxeey ku dhaheen waraabihii waxaad wabiga kasoo laqdaa taqsi si aad uheesto heestan si fican.
P11	Waraabihii markale ayuu banaanka taqsi kasoo laqay oo uu isku dayay hadana in uu heeso heestii,  lakin albaabkii ma furmin hadana. - Ugu dambeestii waraabihii banaanka fog ayuu taqsi kasoo laqay.
P12	Waraabihii waxuu soo aaday in uu heeso heestii,  ugu dambeestii na albaabkii wuu furmay. Waraabihiina Atabo ayuu cunay.
P13	Markii ay hooyadii timaaday meeshii Atabo ku jiray,  waa ay uheestay,  uheestay oo uheestay lakin waxbo ma furmin.
P14	Hooyadii dab weyn ayee shiday oo waxeey iskugu yeertay xayawaanadi oo dhan. Waxeey tiri,  xayawaankii cunay Atabo dabkaan ayuu ku dhacayaa oo uu ku gubanayaa.
P15	Xayawaanadii oo dhan waa eey ka boodeen dabkii,  ilaa laga soo gaaro dawaco.
P16	Dawacadii waa eey ka booday dabkii lakin seenta ayee ka gubatay. Dabacadii xanuun ayee la ooysay.
P17	Uga dambeestii waraabihi ayaa lasoo gaaray. Waa uu booday lakin dabki ayuu ku dhax dhacay.
P18	Kadibna xayawaanadii oo dhan hees kale ayee uheeyseen: "Ata kalio,  Anikanyamit ikon koku,  Achakakin nakim na,  Chi. Gi. Gir."
