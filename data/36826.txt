P1	One day, there was lion who like cleanliness and hygiene only. But he doesn't likes that bacteria should come. Others also not like bacteria. But hunter loves bacteria to attack animals.
P2	The baba came and he saw a snake in danger. Snake said " I fall sick because of  bacteria had attacked me". The baba was shocked about bacteria and was very scared  of bacteria.
P3	After 10 days, teacher was checking bacteria in bob's hand and adam saw a machine and he asked " what is this?". Teacher said " this microscope a machine which used to check bacteria".
P4	Adam sees bacteria through microscope , he  saw the bacteria were big in size, as he came to know that bacteria in our body can make us sick. Adam understands the bacteria clearly and knows that it can cause diarrhoea in our body.
P5	Then he was angry because hunter was producing bacteria in this world that's why he strokes hunter gently. And said " you are the bacteria producer and you are only producing bacteria to animals and humans who are eating crabs can get this.
