desc:	UDuma uneminyaka elishumi. Uluntu lwangakowabo luchaphazelwe kakhulu yintsholongwane ye-Corona. Udade kaDuma

page_text:

P1	Incwadi yabantwana base Afrika yonke jikelele
P2	Igama lam nguDuma. Ndineminyaka elishumi.@@Ndihlala nodade wethu uZintle oneminyaka elishumi nantathu. @@Sihlala noMama, uTata, nobhuti wethu uWandile oneminyaka emithandathu, kunye nenja yethu, uBhubesi.
P3	Oko kuvele intsholongwane ye-Corona, ubomi bethu butshintshile.@@Intsholongwane incinci injalo ingasigulisa kakhulu xa@singalumkanga.
P4	Ndilusizi kuba andikwazi ukudlala nezihlobo zam.@@NoWandile naye ulusizi ngoba akasaziboni izihlobo zakhe.@@Kwaye noZintle naye ukhathazekile kuba akwasakwazi ukwabelana neetshomi zakhe ngamafutha okuhombisa umlomo.
P5	UMama ungumcoci sibhedlele edolophini.@@UTata yena uthengisa iziqhamo kunye nemifuno edolophini.@@Xa besemsebinzini abazali bethu sihoywa nguZintle ekhaya.
P6	Ummelwane wethu, esimbiza Makhulu, mdala kakhulu kwaye unesifo sentliziyo.@@Akukho mntu ovunyelweyo ukusondela kuye, ngokuba le ntsholongwane iyingozi kakhulu ebantwini abadala.@@Kwaye asifuni ukumgulisa.
P7	UMama uye adlule kuMakhulu xa ephuma emsebenzini, amphathele iziqhamo.@@Uye afike azishiye emnyango iziqhamo zikaMakhulu, kuba engenakungena endlini.
P8	UZintle uvuka kwangentseni ayokukha amanzi empompeni yasengingqini.@@Abanye abantu basuke benxibe iimaski, abanye bengazinxibanga.@@UZintle usebenzize iqhiya yakhe awazenzela yona, njenge maski.
P9	Qho ngexesha lesidlo sangokuhlwa, umama uyasikhumbuza:@@"Nxibani iimaski ezicocekileyo ntsuku zonke. Hlambani izandla zenu ngesepha kangangemizuzwana engamashumi amabini. Gqumani umlomo xa nikhohlela."@@"Kulungile mama!" sivume.
P10	1. Hlamba izandla zakho ngamanzi.@2. Sebenzisa isepha ukuhlamba izandla zakho.@3. Hlamba imizuzwana engamashumi amabini, okanye bala ukususela kwi-1 uyotsho kuma-20 ucothisisa.@4. Pula izandla zakho ngamanzi.@5. Sula izandla zakho ngetawuli ecocekileyo, okanye ngethishu.@6. Izandla zakho zicocekile!
