desc:	An elder from Turkana teaches us about the importance of palm trees.

page_text:

P1	Let me tell you about the palm tree.
P2	This tree lives when everything else is dry.
P3	The shade from a palm tree is always cool.
P4	We eat fruit from the palm tree.
P5	We use palm leaves to thatch our houses.
P6	We make brooms and mats from palm leaves.
P7	We use palm tree seeds for fuel.
P8	Do you see why the palm tree is important?
