desc:	Byantaka yali nakasugha akatono. Yeegomba ensugha enene. Yaalikozeeki? Yakozesa omutwegwe yaafuna kyeyayenda.

page_text:

P1	Byantaka yali n'ente n'akasugha akatono ak'amaadhi.
P2	yaaja yeyazikwa ensugha enene egha muliraanwaghe.
P3	Byantaka yata akasugha akatono mu nnene eya muliraanwaghe.
P4	Yatwala ensugha dhombi egha muliranwa.
P5	"Ensughayo yazaala" Byantaka bweyakoba muliraanwaghe.
P6	Byantaka yaja era yairamu okweyazikwa ensugha.
P7	Tiyagiizaayo. Muliranwa yaidha okubuuza.
P8	Byantaka yakoba ati "Yafa. Mbaire ndhikwidha kukukobera.'
P9	Muliraanwa yawoigana,"Tiwulizangaku ku nsugha efa!"
P10	Byantaka yairamu, "Mukagwa ghange, buli kintu kizaala kifa"
P11	Muliraanwa yaja mu kooti okughaabira Byantaka.
P12	Omulamuzi yaakoba ati,"Buli kintu ekigheera ekizaala kiteekwa okifa.
P13	Byantaka yafuna ensugha enene aye muliraanwaghe yaaviiramu agho.
