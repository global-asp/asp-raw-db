title:	Mosuwetsana Akinyi
author:	Tseliso Masolane
artist:	Vusi Malindi
lang:	Sesotho (S.Africa)
level:	First sentences

desc:	Pale e kgutshwane e bonolo ka mosuwetsana ya ratehang le seo a re rutang sona.

page_text:

P1	Enwa ke mosuwetsana wa ka. Lebitso la hae ke Mosuwetsana Akinyi. O nthata haholo.
P2	Mosuwetsana Akinyi o re ruta ditlhaku le ditumanotshi. Ke kgona ho bitsa ditlhaku a,  e,  i,  o,  u.
P3	Mosuwetsana Akinyi o re rutile mebala kgubedu, tala, tshehla, le ntsho.
P4	Nakong ya ho robala, Mosuwetsana Akinyi o re binela  ho fihlela re robala. O re binela dipina tsa ho re koietsa.
P5	Pina ya ho koietsa e binwa tjena: Boroko ba kgauta  bofa mahlo a hao Pososelo tseo emetseng ha o tsoha Robalang baratuwa ba battle, le se lle Mme ke tla le binela pina e monate e koietsang.
P6	Mosuwetsana Akinyi o tseba dipale tse ngata. O re phetela dipale tse fapaneng letsatsi ka leng la beke.
P7	Ka Mantaha, o re phetela dipale tsa  banna ba bahale.
P8	Ka Labobedi, o re phetela dipale tsa basadi ba bahale.
P9	Ka Laboraro, o re phetela dipale ka mefuta ya dipalangwang.
P10	Ka Labone, o re phetela dipale ka temo.
P11	Mme ka Labohlano, Mosuwetsana Akinyi o re kopa ho phetela barutwana ba bang ka phaposing dipale tsa rona.
P12	Mosuwetsana wa rona ke mosuwetsana wa mantlha lefatsheng! Ha ke hola, ke batla ho tshwana le Mosuwetsana Akinyi.
