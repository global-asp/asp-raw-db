page_text:

P1	There live a man called Apenti Puku who lived in a small village. Because he could make snakes follow him, people called him the snake man. He was a very wise and  brave  man.
P2	One day,  as he was walking he came across a fox who was in a coat and holding a stick in his hand. Apenti said, "hello young fox how are you doing? The fox said I am fine but who are you?I am Apenti Puku .
P3	I am popularly  known as the snake man. Snake man! Said the fox. If you're a  snake man then show me what you can do.
P4	So Apenti Puku  did what the fox said. He controlled the snake. If he turned his hand to the left side ,the snake will also turn it's body to the left. The fox was amazed. He said truly you are a snake man.
