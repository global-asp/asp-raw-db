title:	Tshimange tsho ṱalifhaho
author:	Tshedza  Tlhako
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Vho Kio vha na tshimange tsho ṱalifhaho tshine tsha vhidzwa Safi.

page_text:

P1	Vho Kio vha dzula muvhunduni wahashu.@@Vho Kio vha na tshimange tsho no vhidzwa Safi.
P2	Musi Vho Kio vha tshi ya mushumoni, vha sia muṋango wo vulea.
P3	Safi tshi dzula muṋangoni uri tshi ṱhogomele nnḓu.
P4	Musi mmbwa ya muhura i tshi sendela, Safi tshi a i shushedza.
P5	Mmbwa i a shavha.
P6	Musi muhura a tshi khokhonya muṋangoni, Safi tshi@vha tsho ḓilugiselela u mu veta.
P7	Muhura u humela murahu nahone a shavha.
P8	Musi Safi tshi na nḓala, tshi vala muṋango.
P9	Safi tshi ḽa zwiḽiwa zwatsho.
P10	Nga murahu, Safi tshi namela sofa nahone tsha eḓela,@"Purr! Purr! Purr!"
P11	Musi Vho Kio vha tshi vhuya, Safi tshi a ḓionyolosa tsho takala nahone tsha vha ṱanganedza hayani.
P12	Vho Kio na Safi vha dzula fhasi, nahone vhuvhili havho vha vhala gurannḓa.
