page_text:

P1	Long ago, in the small village of Kitete, lived a small boy called Kintu.
P2	Kintu loved bananas very much, and he used to eat them every day.
P3	One day, a monkey came  and stole Kintu's bananas.He decided to chase it
P4	The monkey ran into the forest, and Kintu followed it
P5	The monkey swung up onto a branch, and started singing a song about sharing
P6	When Kintu heard the song, he decided to share the bananas with the monkey.
P7	The monkey was so happy, and decided to come down from the tree.
P8	Kintu shared the bananas with the monkey, and they ate them in the forest.
P9	After sharing the bananas, Kintu and the monkey became good friends.
