page_text:

P1	Endiga eyakoowa obulamu bwe'kibuga.
Wandiga yabeeranga mu kibuga. Yakoowa obulamu bwe'kibuga era yasalawo agende mu nsiko.
Mu kubo lye, yasisinakana wampisi eyali amaze ennaku nnyingi nga talya.
P2	Yabuuza wampisi nga bwa'ddamu nga bwa'ddamu," ow'oluganda wampisi, oli otya? Ow'oluganda wampisi, oly'otya? Wampisi yaddamu wandiga," tombuuzabuuza. Kolawo ekintu okomye enjala yange."
P3	Wandiga bweyawulira kino, yatandika okuduka. Mbagirawo, yatuuka ku mpukuya wampologoma. Wampisi yali adduka bwa'muvaako emabega. Wampisi yatuuka ku mpuku era oluvanyuma  yavumbeera nga bwa'yiwa enfuufu mu maaso ga wampologoma.
P4	Wampologoma yagamba wampisi," tetusobola kusanga mirembe mu mpuku zaffe? Oyiwa enfuufu mu maaso gaffe!"
Wampisi yaddamu," kabaka, sikitegezeeza eky'okukuyisaamu amaaso! Ndi kugobererabugoberezi mmere yange!"
P5	Wampologoma yagamba,"nange oli mmere gy'endi, wampisi. N'akugobye? Gwe aze gyendi. Tonnenya oluvannyuma."
P6	Olwo wampologoma n'agamba wandiga," lwaki waze mu nsiko?"
Wandiga yaddamu,"n'aze kubanga ndi kizuuzi."
P7	Wampologoma yagamba,"tegeka eddagala ly'ekinnaansi okulaga nti ddala oli kizuuzi." Wandiga yamuddamu,"eddagala lyange  lya bbeeyi."
Wampologoma yamugamba,"ekyo si kizibu, kyonna kye ligula. Tewali kintu kiyinza kunnemerera."
P8	Wandiga yagamba,"eddagala g'ematu ga wampisi,"
Wampologoma yasalako amatu ga wampisi era n'agawa wandiga.
Wandiga yateeka amatu ga wampisi mu kita ky'omubisi era n'akiwa wampologoma.
P9	Wampologoma yalya amatu ga wampisi era n'alisanga nga liwooma. Yabuuza wandiga,"kisoboka nze okufuna ekutu ery'okubiri?"
P10	Wandiga yagamba,"ye, kisoboka kabaka wange."
Kati wampologoma n'asalako ekkutu lya wampisi ery'okubiri era n'alireetera wampologoma.
wandiga yaliteeka mu kita ky'omubisi era n'akiwa wampologoma.
P11	Olwo wampologoma n'agamba wandiga,"kiba kisoboka okufuna eddiba?" Wandiga n'addamu,"ndowooza kisoboka kabaka wange." Wampisi bweyawulira ekyo n'atya okufa era n'adduka. Wampologoma n'adduka nga bw'amuvaako emabega.
P12	"wandiga yagamba,"obwo bw'ebuba obulamu mu nsiko awo kisingako nze n'enzirayo ewaka."
