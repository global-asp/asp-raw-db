desc:	A dog that keeps on remembering stuff that happened to her brother

page_text:

P1	One day there was a dog that had  three friends the doggies name was fluffball
P2	She remembered the day when her brother had to run away from a chicken
P3	She thought about it for such a long time it was night time she took her friends over to the house for dinner
P4	After dinner she went to take her friends to the horse stable
P5	But then she thought about it again where chicken was chasing everyone they were so terrified
P6	Then she thought about it for the last time
P7	Then she went to howl the moon then she went home
P8	The end of the dogs sister
