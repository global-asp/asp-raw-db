page_text:

P1	Ngenye imini mna nodade wethu sasihamba ecaleni kwendlela.
P2	Ilori enkulu yayisihla endulwini.
P3	Imoto yayinyuka iqhina ngesantya esiphezulu.
P4	Umqhubi wayethetha kwimfono-mfono yakhe. Wayengajonganga endleleni.
P5	Ilori yantlitheka emotweni.
P6	Inqwelo yezigulane namapolisa zeza kunceda.
P7	Iilori ezinkulu zafika. Enye yathatha ilori le intlithekileyo. Enye yathatha imoto.
P8	Sabona yonke into kwaye sasothukile.
