desc:	Wankoko ni Napupu bali nokusindanwa okwomukwanho. Lulala wankoko abulaku katonho okufiirwa yakola ekyobusiru.

page_text:

P1	Wankoko  ni  Napupu  baali  bamukwanho. Aye  bali  nga  basindanwa.  Lulaala  baasalagho  okuzanha  omupira  okubonha  ani  eyali  omuzanhi  omulungi.
P2	Bagya  mukisaghe  eky'omupira  batandika  omuzanho  gwaibwe. Wankoko  yali  mwangu,  aye Napupu  yali  mwangu  inho.  Wankoko  yagusamba  ghala, aye  Napupu  yagukuba  ghala  inho. Wankoko  yanhiiga.
P3	Basalagho  okukuba  peneti. ukusoka  Napupu  n'eyali  omugemi  gha  golo. Wankoko  yateeba  golo  ndhala  yonka. Kabili  kyali  kisera  kya  wankoko  okukugira  golo.
P4	Napupu  yakuba  omupira  era  yaateba.  Napupu  yafula  omupira  era  yateeba.Napupu  yakubisa  omutwe  omupira    yateeba.  Napupu  yateeba  golo  itanu!
P5	Wankoko  yali  mukaghu  inho  era  yagobwa.  yalemererwa  bubi. Napupu  yatandiika  okuseka  kubanga  mukwanho  gwe  yali  ali  kwekola  eby'obusiru.
P6	Wankoko  yali  munhivu  inho  era yayasama  omunwa  gwe  inho  yaamira  Napupu.
P7	Wankoko  yaali  atambula  kujja  ka,  yayaganha  Maama Napupu. Maama  Napupu  yaabuza, "obweineku  omwanha  ghange?"  Wankoko  tiyakoba  kintu  kyonakyona.  Maama  Napupu  yerarikira  inho.
P8	Agho  maama  Napupu  yaghulira  akalobozi  akatono. "nhamba  maama"  akalobozi  kaalira. Eirobozi  lyava  munda  munkoko.
P9	Maama  Napupu  yaghoweiganha, "Kozesa  amanhi  go  agendaghulo  mwana  wange!"
Napupu  asobola  okukola  omuka  omubi  n'okulega  okubi. Wankoko  yatandika  okulwaala.
P10	Wankoko  yegoga. Yaamira  yaamala  yafuudha.  agho  yeniza  era  yakoghola.  Era  yakoghola.  Napupu  yaghunha  bubi.
P11	Wankoko  yakoghola  paka  bwe  yakoghola  Napupu.  Maama  Napupu  bekululira  kumuti  okwekweka.
P12	Okuva  kwolwo,Bawankoko  ni Banapupu  bali  bampalana.
