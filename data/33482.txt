desc:	Indaba ye ngwenya ehlakaniphile eyayi zama ukudla imfene ehlakaniphile.

page_text:

P1	kwasuka sukela,ingwenya ayiyihlezi elangeni ngase mvuleni.Imfene yafika base baya bingelelana
P2	Imfene yabuza ingwenya kuthi ihlala kuphi.Ingwenya yaphendula yathi 'khona lapha emanzini"imfene ya mangala.Mfene wabuza ngwenya kuthi injani impilo yase manzini?imnanadi nah?
P3	Yaphendula ingwenya,"impilo emanzini imnandi"wena ingabe ihlulakuphi?yaphendula imfene @ingihlala ezihlahleni ngidla nezithelo nama cembe"wena udlani?
P4	Ngwenya yaphendula,ngidla inyama.Besekavele aqabanga indlela yokubhamba imfene ayidle.Wabuza umfene "uyakwazi kubhukuda?"
P5	Waphendula umfene,"cha cha! angikwazi."Ingwenya yathi, "woza ngikufundise ukubhukuda"Imfene yathi, "cha anginaNtshisakalo"
P6	Ngwenya yaqamba amanga yathi,"sesingaba ngani ungesabi ngizokugibeza emhlane wami".Imfene yagibela emhlani ngwenya baqala babukuda.
P7	Mabafika phakathi kwemvula,ingwenya yabuza imfene,"uzizwa njan ukuba phakathi kwamanzi?unethezekile?"Yaphendula imfene,"kuyamangaza futhi ngijabulile"
P8	Ngwenya yaphefumula wathi kwi mfene,"Malume wam uyagula kakhulu.asikatholi umuthi wokumelapha"
P9	Imfene yathuka kakhulu yaze yaqhaqhazela.Yazi buza kuthi izasinda kanjani kule ngwenya.
P10	Imfene yafikelwa yiqinga.Wathi ku Ngwenya,"ungakhathazeki."Ngizoninika inhliziyo yam uyinika umalume wakho.Izomulapha"
P11	Ngwenya yaqhazeka.Yathi,ngempela?unganginikeza inhliziyo yakho ukuze ngelaphe umalume wam?" imfene yathi "yebo singaba ngani nje"
P12	Emva kweskhashana imfene yathi "senglibele inhliziyo yam eshlahleni,masbuyele siyoyilanda'
P13	Ngwenya yaqala yangabaza,yabuza yathi unqinisekile uzonginika yona uma uyithola eshlahleni?" imfene yathi "yebo ngizoyi letha,futhi sihambe siyofakashela no malume wakho"
P14	Ngwenya yavuma yabuya emuva emhlabathini.Mabafika emhlabathini imfene yaqcuma yahlika emhlani weNgwenya
P15	Seyime esihlahleni,imfene yathi "sala kahle mngani wam,ngiyabonga ngokungivakashisa emvuleni.
P16	ngwenya yathukuthela,"manje ungicambele amanga?uzobuya nayo yini indliziyo yakho?imfene yaphendula,@bengikhona kuhlala emhlani wakho ngoba inhliziyo yam vele beyikimi2.
