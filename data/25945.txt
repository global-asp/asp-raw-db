title:	Mbwali mubitirivu

desc:	Emboozi eno edhuba eti engeri bwe twiza einhuma abaana

page_text:

P1	Mu ganda ghanga alwa gho okulambala. Nsituka mangu, kuba nga ndhi mubitirivu!
P2	Nze aye yanhika mu kasana.
P3	"Oli kyankyo kyange ekyo omunyenye," kateme ne yakoba.
P4	Nali newulira bulirunaku, tye taga buyambi.
P5	Ti fayo ku maadhi againhogoga, oba ku saabuni ayoza engoye.
P6	Kateme andhidhusa, "tiwerabira linho lyo." Ndhiramu, "mbe, ti nze!"
P7	Ghe mara okwo oza, nhamusa dhaadha ni senga. Mbendeza olunaku olulungi.
P8	Gho neyambaza mwene. "ndhi munene kati, Kateme," nakoba.
P9	Nsobola okwingala gho amapesa gange ne engaito dhange okudhisiba.
P10	Nfayo inho okubona nga muganda ghange ategera buli ekiba ku insomero.
P11	Mu kibina nkola bulungi buli mu ngeri.
P12	Nkola ebintu bino byona byona buli lunaku. Aye kintu kyensinga kwendha, no okuzaaha no oku zaanha.
