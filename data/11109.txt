P1	Ubaba uSaucepan nobaba uPot bebabangani abakhulu begodu bebahlala bathabile iminyaka eminengi.
P2	Ngelinye ilanga ubaba uSaucepan watjela ubaba uPot bonyana bakhambe bayokukweriyela umnganabo omunye ubaba uPlate.
