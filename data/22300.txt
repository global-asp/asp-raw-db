title:	Kinyo ke kí-iì yiìn é bo m lày é way
author:	Beatrice Tafon Takeh and Mbinkar Emmanuel Keelen
artist:	Catherine Groenewald
lang:	Lámnsoʼ
level:	First words

desc:	Á kàʼ wán woo lumèn e fómo wo ghaʼán wóo sheʼrí kishiíy ke vé-eè dzəən wun ke kí meen ji ntuùfú. Yi-iì koóy ka?

page_text:

P1	Bvə́y si yii dzə́ e věr ntèʼ, a  yì dzə̀ kwaʼda fo ŋkà se laʼ.
P2	Màmé yóŋ jii, Elli, vèr kér fómo wò ji a sì á viyá ntuùfú.
P3	Vèr à màmé wom, à fər və, nèn a shiy mótù.
P4	M dzə́ʼə́m e màmé kibìy, e nèn a buuné buunén.
P5	Vèr mèé e way, m yén elíkovtà wùmòʼ a wù boŋkìrì, kiʼ blù.
P6	Kibàn kímóʼ kí-iì kér afrur. M nèn dzə́r jii, "Víne dzə̀ vinée?"
P7	Wù yòŋ jii, "Apə̀r." M lón jii, "Kíi wò, yún kimòʼón e fò mo."
P8	M nèn màʼtì kiwó ke màmé, e nèn kù a kuumé a dzə̀ ápər wom.
P9	M màysì kfə̀r ápə̀r, e yèn a m sì vèr mòʼón, nèn kèʼ a dzə̀ tóŋ.
P10	Wiíy wùmòʼ lí mo, e dù sho fo wón ámóʼó-oò dzəə̀n.
P11	M nèn siì tòŋ tóŋén. Mò-oò kwaʼtì jii, a yii fiìnè a wón é way.
P12	Wìr wùmòʼ wiy e dzə̀r jii, "Elli bèé?" M laáy fo wù yén mo.
P13	M nèn yù a màmé wom jaŋ mo jii, "Elli-ií." Wúné bóŋ mo féyì.
P14	Fər màmé fəə́r elíkovtà, a wù boŋkìrì, e yòŋ jii, "Si wo bèn."
