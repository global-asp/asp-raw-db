id:	34301
title:	Mpho  ya makhulu
author:	Ndivhuho Mutsila
artist:	Sibongile Mnkandla
lang:	Tshivenḓa
level:	Longer paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Mma vha thusa Thabo kha u itela makhulu wawe mpho ya tshipentshela.

page_text:

P1	Makhulu vha khou ḓa u dala. Tshifhinga tshoṱhe Thamba vha mu ḓela na nḓuhu na mitshelo ya ḓaka kana tshikoli na bvani, kana zwiṅwe-vho. @@Thamba u khou tama u ṋea-vho Makhulu tshiṅwe tshithu tsha tshipentshela.
P2	Bugu yo vha i tshi nga vha takadza, fhedzi Makhulu vha a kona u vhala naa?@@Arali a nga ya mavhengeleni, a nga wana tshiṅwe tshithu tshavhuḓi?@@"Kha ri vha itele bege," hu amba Thamba a tshi ṅwethuwa. "Bege khulwane yo khwaṱhaho ya sila ḽa denimi ḽa muvhala wa lutombo."
P3	Mme a Thamba vha bvisa sila ḽe vha renga vhengeleni. Vha ḽi ṱharamudza vha tala phetheni vha thoma u gera.@@Tsha u thoma, vhurumbu vhuvhili vhuhulwane nahone ho aṱamaho.
P4	Vha tevhedza nga zwipiḓa zwiṱuku zwi no ḓo thusa kha tshivhumbeo.@@Thamba a zwi peta na vhuḽa vhurumbu vhuvhili. A ṱalela mme awe vha tshi tala iṅwe phetheni hafhu.
P5	Vha gera zwipiḓa zwilapfu zwivhili zwine zwa ḓo ita tshiraho tsha bege. Zwi ḓo ita uri bege iyi i khwaṱhe vhukuma.
P6	Vha gera mikungelo yo aṱamaho na u lapfa.@@Vhulabi vhuvhili vhu ḓo rungelelwa ha ita mukungelo muthihi. Hezwi zwi ḓo ita uri bege i khwaṱhe kavhili.
P7	Mme awe vha mbo ḓi bvisa mutshini wa u runga.@@Vha rungelela zwipiḓa zwoṱhe vha tshi ita bege ya Makhulu.@@Vha mbo i ṋea Thamba we a mwemwela a ri, "Ndo livhuwa Mma, vho runga bege khulukhulu ya Makhulu!"
