desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	Ka letsatsi le leng tlou engoe eile ea ea ho oa noa metsi.
P2	Eaba ho ba le giraffe tse peli li eo noa metsi le tsona.
P3	Ho ne ho ena le tse tharo tse tona le linonyana tse'ne. Kaofela hoa tsona line line linoa metsi.
P4	Likhants'i tse hlano le likolobe tse ts'eletseng line li eo noa metsi.
P5	Linqankane tse suping le lithlapi tse robeli line lile ka metsing.
P6	Li Qoakha tse robong line lilo noa mestsi.
P7	Tau e'nogoe ea kena hoea noa metsi.
P8	Ke mang ea senatla se seholo,  aka tsoelang pele ka ho noa metsie?
