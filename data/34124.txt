title:	Imvubu kanye nelufudvu

desc:	Bangani lababili labatsandzanako labahlala babonke ngasosonke sikhatsi

page_text:

P1	Lufudvu ne mvubu bebabangani labakhulu bebahlala bajabulile ngaso sonke sikhatsi.
P2	Bekutsi njalo ekuseni lufudvu luye emfuleni luyodlala nemngani walo mvubu.
P3	Kwatsi ngalelinye lilanga basadlala emfuleni imvubu lencane yancwela  emfuleni.
P4	Lufudvu lwamemeta lwatsi sitanibo! Sitanibo! Nembala lwaluthola lusito.
P5	Kwachamuka bantfu labahlala ngasemfuleni bagijima  batawusita imvubu le ncwela emfuleni.
P6	Imvubu yalutfola lusito babuya bajabula bangani lababili imvubu yabuye yasho nekutsi iyabonga.
P7	Kwatsi ngalelinye lilanga umntfwana wemvubu  wacela unina kutsi amfundzise kubhkuda .
P8	Umnfwana wemvubu nelufudvu besebahlala babhukusha njalo futsi bebahlala bajabulile .
