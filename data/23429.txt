desc:	Umugabo Ututa kukintu yananiwe kubika imyaka

page_text:

P1	cyera habayeho umugabo witwa Joel mumudugudu wa Aya
P2	Nti yigeze yita kukubika imyaka yokuzabatunga mubihe biri imbere
P3	Ntakintu yasaguye mu kigega
P4	Ibigori byarahonze kandi biruma
P5	Inkubi y'umuyaga yasenye igisenge ki kigega
P6	Abana be badwaniraga ubukukumba byasigaraga mu masafuriya
P7	Mama wabo yaricaraga akarira yibaza icyo azagaburira abanabe undi munsi
P8	Papa wabo nawe yicaye hasi y,urubaraza atekereza kubihe biri imbere.
