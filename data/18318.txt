desc:	Zwine na takalela u ita ndi zwifhio? (Bilingual Tshivenda and English.)

page_text:

P1	Ndi takalela u fhufha khadi na khonani dzanga. I enjoy skipping with my friend.
P2	Ndi takalela u ṅwala maḽeḓere kha bodo. I enjoy writing letters on the board.
P3	Ndi takalela u vhala bugu ire na zwifanyiso. I enjoy reading books with pictures.
P4	Ndi takalela u ḽa mitshelo yo fhambananaho. I enjoy eating different fruit.
P5	Ndi takalela u thusa hayani. I enjoy helping at home.
P6	Ndi takalela u thetshelesa dzinganea dza mme anga. I enjoy listening to my mother's stories.
P7	Ndi takalela u eḓela mbeteni muhulu. I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.
P8	Zwi a ntakadza u ita izwi zwithu. Inwi vho ni takadzwa nga u ita mini? I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?
