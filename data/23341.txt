page_text:

P1	Omuhara muto akatumwa abazeire beye owa shwekuruweye obaire arataha enyima yihamba yagyenda ashemeirirwe.
P2	Kuhahikyire aharubajju gwihamba yabugana embuzi lero yamagyira gu agyende ayetonzire habwokuba ariho orikyiko gwe ebi nyamwishwa omukibira
P3	Kuyahikyire omukibira, yabugana ebinyamwishwa bwigyi hihi nomuhanda lero yayiruka yayiruka aratera enduru
P4	Kuyarugeire omwihamba, yabugana ebinyonyi bwigyi birikutera enduru. Omuhara muto yayiruka arikutera enduru. Shwekuru we yamuhurira.
P5	Shwekuruwe yagambira omukazi weye ngu ogwe nimuhara wabo. Yagyenda jjuba nebwa yeye lero yamushaga yaruhire.
P6	Kubahukyire omuka yatahamu yaramusya abandi bana lero yarya. Kuyamazire kurya yagamba ahabwamubaho ahamuhanda.
P7	Kuyamazire kubagambira ebyabireho lero nokubona obuhabuzi ngu atariraba omwihamba hateine owayine yagyenda ashemeirerwe yaza kuzana nabandi bana.
P8	Tarashubire kuraba omwihamba ariweka.
