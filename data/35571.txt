page_text:

P1	My name is Hafsat. I go to school. I like to learn.
P2	Amina is my friend. She is not in school.
P3	"Why don't you go to school? I asked her one day.
P4	" I am an orphan. My uncle does not have money"
P5	"I will tell my father. He may help you".
P6	"Daddy, my friend doesn't go to school. She is an orphan".
P7	My father told Amina's uncle; " I will help with her fees".
P8	Now Amina and I go to school together. She is happy.
