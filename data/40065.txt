page_text:

P1	A day finally came which everyone was eagerly waiting for.Everyone was filled with spam of joy hoping that all will be fine.
P2	We quickly prepared ourselves and rushed to the dinning table to have breakfast.My mom susan,dad Martin,sister Rebecca and brother Daniel were the people ready for the journey.
P3	We gathered all our necessities required for the day and ready to start the journey.Wow surely the day was blessed by our forefathers.Everyone was anxious to witness the goodness of the day.
P4	Our moods were filled with estacy.By and by we got near the occasion reception and in the hint of a second we were all there.As the crowd sang and danced,a time came when the bride and the groom arrived in the reception.
P5	The bride and groom came dancing towards the reception, where the cutting of the cake was to be done.A time came when they had to present the cake to each other and the parents.The groom did it in a different way which made the crowd happy.
P6	The ushers guided us to where the food was served.We all cued to have the meal.The food was well presented and deliciously prepared.The place was full of songs and dances.
P7	Soon we had a gang shot thrice and a scarely man came demanding that we should lie down all else we will all die.My heart was biting so fast that you would see my clothes move from my body.
P8	The man came closer to the bride and commanded her to confess or else he will take her life.The lady was confused to what she was to confess about.Before she could say a word the stranger shot her to death.
P9	Everyone was shouting and screaming for help and no one was there to help.The groom cried uncontrollably didn't know what to do.The family quickly rashed the bride to the hospital but she was already dead.The occasion ended very painfully.
