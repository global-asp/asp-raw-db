page_text:

P1	Luuka 11:1-10
Yesu  yali  nga  alikusabira  mu  kifo. Bweyamala, omulala  ku  begeresaabe  yaakoba  ati, "mukama  waife, twegerese  bwe  basaba  nga  Yowaana bwe  yasomesa  abeegeresaabe." Agho  yaabakoba  ati, "bwosaba, okobanga  oti, "bbaabba lata, eriina lyo  lisaakibwe nga  obwakyabazinga  bwo  bwidhe. Otuwe eky'okulya  buliidho,  otusoniwe ebyonoono  byaife  nga  ife  bwetusonigha  abatuyona, totutwala  mu  kukemebwa."
P2	Aye yaabakoba  ati:

Singa  omulala  kwimwe aba  n'omukwano, ghaaja  yaali  mu  bwire  ghagati  gha mukoba, "mukwano  gwange,  nnamba yo  emigaati  esatu, kubanga  mukwano  gwange aidhye  yaabitiraku  ghano  nga  aja  mu  lugendo lwe,  ate  nzira kya kumugha."
P3	Agho aidha  kulowookeza  munda  mule, "tonfaaku. Olwigi  lwigale, nze  n'abaana  bange  tulambaire. Tisobola  kusituka  kukugha  kintu  kyonakyona."
P4	Nkukobere, ni  bwekinaaba  nga  omusadha  munda  mule  nga  taasituke  nampa kintu  kyonakyona olwokuba  mukwano  gwange, ate  kubanga  okulemeraku  okw'omusaadha  aidha  kusituka  amughe  buli  kyayenda.
P5	"Kale  mbakobera:
Musabe, mwidha  kugheebwa; munoonie, mwidha kufuna; mukonkone, mwidha  kwigulibwagho.
Oyo  yeenayeena  asaba agheebwa, era anoonia afuna n'oyo  akonkona  aigulibwagho."
