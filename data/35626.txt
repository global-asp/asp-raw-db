page_text:

P1	Amina is nine years old, she is in primary school,and she lives in Kano. Amina does not participate in any class or school activities. One day the headmistress announced that she go will round the school primeses.
P2	The headmistress shared all responsibilities to all pupils, and Amina was in the singing group ,The teacher incharge of the singing group discovered that Amina was not good in singing and she threw her out of the singing group.
P3	Amina cried and she told the headmistress that she was sent out of the singing group. The headmistress enquired why she was sent out and she told her that she was not good in singing. The headmistress re-assigned her to the tailoring.
P4	The headmistress took her to the tailoring class and introduced her to the teacher. When Amina started the tailoring class, the tailor also discovered that Amina could not do any tailoring work a and threw her out of the class.
P5	The headmistress decided to give Amina a simple work to do, and Amina agreed,the headmistress taught her how to save money and take care of the money Amina was excited and learned so much. The  headmistress gave her a book to record .
P6	At the end of the month, Amina came to the headmistress with two collection boxes. The headmistress asked her to throw the old box away but she didn't throw it because she like it.
P7	On the day of paying savings, Amina dropped the old box in the headmistress office and put some dry leave and stones inside. A thief came and stole the box and thought he had taken money box.
P8	The headmistress did not know that Amina exchanged the collection box and wondered how Amina got the box stole box . Amina told her that she exchange the box before it was stolen.
P9	Amina told the headmistress that the collection box with money was intact and the headmistress was very proud of Amina and she became a super girl.
