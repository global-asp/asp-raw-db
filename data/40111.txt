desc:	Jan loves her friend but she wants a new friend. What happens when she meets a butterfly?

page_text:

P1	Jan grew up in with loving friends. She played with them all day long. She was never tired of playing with them and she always looked for new things to play with. One day she met a butterfly.
P2	She met a butterfly and made it her new Friend. She really loved the Butterfly and played with it all day.
P3	Jan loved the Butterfly so much that she became one of them. @The butterfly gave her the powers to fly by giving her butterfly wings.
