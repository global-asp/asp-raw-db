desc:	Parents on tour in the United States of America forget their baby at home in Uganda. What happened is the story to tell.

page_text:

P1	Once upon a time, there lived a boy in Nakivubo village in Uganda. The boy had his father and mother with one brother and a sister.
P2	One day, the parents planed for a tour in the United States of America. The time for going to America reached and the boy was teased by His sister and he got angry.
P3	So he wanted to do something so terrible. You know what, he wanted to kill his sister! 
So his mother decided to lock him up in his bedroom and she did.
P4	Unfortunately, in the morning when they were leaving for America, they forgot about him! And when he woke up, he went on calling on his mother, "Mother! Mother! Mother! Mother!" 
And no one could answer because they had all gone.
P5	When his mother was in the aeroplane about to reach America, she remembered that she had left her son locked up in the bedroom!
P6	When they reached USA, she told her husband, "Well, you can go on but I am going back to get my son I left locked up in the bedroom. So she took the next plane back to Uganda.
P7	They were very rich at home and had heard of bad people who wanted to steal their property at home.
P8	The robber came. Fortunately,the boy had already planned on how to protect the home. So when the robbers came he had dug a pit and they fell into it.
P9	He called police. By the time the police reached, his mother had also arrived. And he ran to his mother.
P10	The police removed the robbers from the pit and carried them to the police station for imprisonment.
