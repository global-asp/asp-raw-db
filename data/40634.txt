desc:	This story is a simple tale of a boy and his mom or any child and their mom which have a great relationship  and they  go on adventures  together  its about a mother and childs  love for each  other.

page_text:

P1	Mama loves me!@ My mama wakes me up in the morning,  and gives me a glass of milk ,and then I get my favorite fruit.Can you tell what it is?
P2	My mama gives me a nice cool bath,and i use my  imagination. I am bathing in a pond oh look mama there is an alligator! .Can you name the other animals that are in the pond
P3	My mama is my teacher,  she taught me my ABC and 123.
P4	My mama cooks us yummy food, and she let's me help .I love when mamma cooks for me it makes me feel  so swell . But when my Dadda cooks for us there's always a yummy smell
P5	My mamma takes me on my daily outdoor adventure. I imagine im in the jungle, and see the jungle king. I see all the monkeys,lions and gazelles.
P6	And now it's time to say good night, I'm safely tucked away my mama sings a lullaby and I'm off to Lala land.
