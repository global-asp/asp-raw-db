title:	Tima Ssunguda Tima
author:	Namee and Wanyenze Lillian and Robinah
artist:	Wiehan de Jager

desc:	Kumuyembe kwakwa asi asi we shisaala isi sunguda akonele. Kumulundi kwenen ukwo

page_text:

P1	Kumuyembe kwakwa asi is abe sunguda akonele.
P2	Sunguda awulira likoona, “Tima sunguda tima!”
P3	Ingkhoho yananiha khutima nga yawulira shesi sunguda aloma.
P4	Baboolera imbwa nayo bari, “Sunguda awulire ishindu shikwa
			amala watimma.”
P5	Imbwa yananiha khutiima ni sunguda ni ingogkho.
P6	Imbwa yabolerera indogoyi iri, “Sunguda awuliire ishindu
			shikwa atima.”
P7	Indogoyi nayo yananiha khutiima ni tsindaala.
P8	Ipunda yereba tsashe, “Mulikhutima shi?” Indogoyi yeramu
			iri, “ Nasse nakhumanya tawe.”
P9	Ipunda yananikha khutima nitsashe.
P10	Ipunda yabolera ingafu, “Sunguda awuliire ishindu shikwa
			watima.”
P11	Ingafu yeyemberera nayo yananiha khutima.
P12	Ingafu yabolera pussi, “Sunguda awuliire ishindu shikwa
			watima.”
P13	Pussi naye wananikha khutiima ni bisoolo bindi.
P14	Bakaanana umusinde.
			“Wakhumanya shibelewo ta?” wareba aryo.
