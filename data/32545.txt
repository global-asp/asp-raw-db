desc:	Eko okondo wolongo wa pami

page_text:

P1	Akiko loshi lomotshi, olongo omotshi konangaka mbidjase l'anto. Ko nde konangaka kanga wadende tsho tenda anto.
P2	Loshi lomotshi, aktshu loswe omotshi etale da nde toka lodu, etale lasukanyi wande.
P3	Akatshoka pindju tshe ndo aka kalolaka la dikolo d'efula.
P4	Dikolo dimotshi, olongo wapami aka kalola la dikolo defula ko lawondo akenaka kene kakatsheka olongo wa pami.
P5	Lawondo aka kokola lo lokuke la lodu.
P6	Womoto aka kanyiye ate, omemi kamboya, konde dihola lokuke. Ko lawondo mbofumbwela ko mbole.
P7	Wonya waka kalala olongo wa pami langelo, nde akenyi da lawondo ambole wadende. Takinde ladikambo da mbita.
P8	Tshe kene nde konanga mbidjase suke l'anto.
