title:	A farmer  and his neigbour
author:	Rahila Bawa saeed
artist:	Isaac Okwir and Adonay  Gebru
lang:	English
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/35620.png
desc:	The story teaches us to not be cunning to our fellow humans. It could backfire on anyone that tries to be cunning to his or her neighbours.

page_text:

P1	There live  a farmer who needed water for his farm,  so he bought  a well  from his neigbour.
P2	How ever the  neigbour was cunning  and refused to let  the farmer to take water from his well
P3	When the farmer asked why  the neigbour replied  I sold the    well to you       not the water
P4	The farmer went to the village head ,he called them and asked  the neigbour. why he was preventing the farmer from fetching water from the well.
P5	"I sold the well not the water so he cannot take my water", the cunning man said.
P6	The  village head said "Alll that sounds  fine to me however if you have sold  the well but  the water is yours, thenot yout have no businessential keepino your water in his well, take out the water immediately.
P7	"If not the water will hereafter belong to the owner of the well". The cunning neigbour  realising that he has been beaten at his own game and thought a lesson, he apologized and  left.
