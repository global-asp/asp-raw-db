page_text:

P1	Nusha thought that she wasn't very good at anything.
P2	But one day, Nusha found out that she could fly.
P3	She flew up trees to pick coconuts.
P4	She flew to school when she was late.
P5	She flew along the beach on Saturdays.
P6	She flew to her grandma's house on Sundays.
P7	She flew to the market because it was quicker.
P8	Everyone asked her how she did it.
P9	But Nusha didn't know how she did it.@@She just did.
P10	One day she met a boy who could fly too.
P11	They started to go out together.
P12	Maybe one day they got married, and had children.
