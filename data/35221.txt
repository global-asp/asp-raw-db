desc:	How do you cope with feeling alone during lockdown?

page_text:

P1	I'm tired of staying home all day.@@I miss my friends. @@I wish we could go outside and play.
P2	All I hear about is the coronavirus!@@I wish it would go away!
P3	Let's play with our toys.@@Mum said if we stay home, God will help keep us safe.
P4	Mr. Khumalo is sick. He had to go to the hospital.@@Let's pray for him right now.
P5	Lord Jesus,@Please heal Mr. Khumalo. @@Protect us from the coronavirus.@@Thank you for being with us when we are alone and afraid.@Amen.
P6	"Even when I must walk through the darkest valley, I fear no danger, for you are with me."@@Psalm 23:4
