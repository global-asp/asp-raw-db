desc:	This story talks about different hairstyles.

page_text:

P1	Thuli ena nzwĩĩ nguvĩ.
P2	Anna ena nzwĩĩ ndaasa.
P3	Kathy nĩwe wĩna Nzwĩĩ ndaasa kwĩ angĩ.
P4	Zama nũsukĩte nzwĩĩ yake.
P5	Nau ena kĩng'ee.
P6	Nzanele nũsanũĩte nzwĩĩ yake.
P7	Thambo nũtilĩte nzwĩĩ yake.
P8	Themba nĩwenzete nzwĩĩ yake.
