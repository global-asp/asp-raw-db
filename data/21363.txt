desc:	Littafin yin kidaya

page_text:

P1	Kyanwa nawa ne? babu kyanwa ko guda. Ba kyanwa
P2	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa daya ce. Bakar kyanwa na iya cin jan nama.
P3	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa biyu ne. Fara da ruwan toka. Farar kyanwa da ruwan toka suke wasa a cikin furanni da kuma kan dabe.
P4	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa uku. Toka-toka, madara-madara dabbare-dabbare. Suna wasa sama da kasa kan bango.
P5	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa hudu. Kyanwa hudu abokan Paul ne.
P6	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa biyar. Kyanwa biyar na ta wasan kai-kawo a dakin hutawa. Ina son haka
P7	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa shida. Kyanwa shida suna iya zama tare su kalli fim
P8	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa bakwai. Kyanwa bakwai na wasa a tsakar gida.
P9	Kyanwa nawa ne? Kyanwa takwas. Kyanwa basa son karnuka.
P10	'Yar kyanwa ta makale a kan bishiya! Paul ya ce "zan ceto ki".
P11	"inna ko mu ajiye shi? saura daya tal". Kyanwa nawa ne? Inna tace kyanwa tara sun yi yawa.
