title:	The Boy Who Played
author:	Annet Ssebaggala
artist:	Kathy Arbuckle, Mlungisi Dlamini, Catherine Groenewald and Benjamin Mitchley
lang:	English
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0

desc:	Joe was sent for sugar and bread but he was attracted by different things which led him to loose the money for shopping.

page_text:

P1	Aunt Merane gave some money to Joe. Go Go to the shop. Buy sugar and bread.
P2	Joe picked an old car Tyre. He ran fast with to the shop.
P3	Before reaching the shop, he saw the ball. He forgot the tyre. He picked the ball and kicked it.
P4	He saw a flying bird. He wanted to catch it.
P5	He saw the cows. He stopped and started singing to them.
P6	"Where is Joe? Asked Jessica" "He is just late but he is coming." Aunt Merane said.
P7	Uncle Don sat with baby Tara. He wanted to have breakfast before going for work.
P8	Joe saw a puppy. He ran with it around the fields.
P9	He got tired. He sat down and checked his pocket. The money was not there.
P10	He ran home without sugar and bread.
P11	"Joe,  Where is sugar and bread? Where is the money?'' Asked Aunt Merane."
P12	Am very sorry Aunt Merane. I played too much and lost the money.
