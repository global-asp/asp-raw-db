page_text:

P1	What would you do? If you were alone in the world?
P2	What would you do? If what you thought was a rock was actually a crocodile?
P3	What would you do? If the crocodile let you ride on his back?
P4	What would you do? If the crocodile threatened to eat you?
P5	What would you do? If you were forced to stay up the tree for days?
P6	What would you do? If you didn't know which part of the water the crocodile was hiding?
P7	What would you do? If the only way to go home is by crossing the river?
P8	What would you do? If when you are half-way,  you see two huge crocodiles coming after you?
