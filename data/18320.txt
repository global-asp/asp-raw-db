title:	Kholomo Yashu Pendo
author:	Ramikosi Gudani
artist:	Rob Owen
lang:	Tshivenda
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 3.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/18320.png
desc:	Tshiṱori tsha kholomo i funeswaho muḓini.

page_text:

P1	Uyu ndi Ndalo.
P2	Ndalo u takadzwa nga u vhala bugu.
P3	Iyi ndi Pendo.
P4	Pendo i takalela u ḽa kharotsi.
P5	Ndalo u lisa Pendo a tshi vhuya tshikoloni.
P6	U nwisa pendo maḓi o kunaho.
P7	Baba vha ṋea Pendo thoro.
P8	Baba vha hama Pendo ḓuvha na ḓuvha.
P9	Pendo i bvisa mafhi manzhi.
P10	Baba vha ita na u rengisa mafhi a Pendo.
P11	Ndalo u nwa mafhi maswa ḓuvha na ḓuvha.
P12	Ndalo uri "Ndi a livhuwa Pendo!"
