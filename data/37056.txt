page_text:

P1	Onda limbililwa ngoo ngeenge opena tuu omunhu umwe afange mounyuni.
P2	Ha monika afange.
P3	Hapopi afange.
P4	Ye okuna ekalo lafa lange.
P5	Omunhu umwe ena omesho ange nomakutwi ange.
P6	Ha yolo afange.
P7	Ou hadulu okuninga osho andi dulu okuninga.
P8	Ye ita dulu okuninga osho ita ndi dulu okuninga.
P9	Onda tala tala apa na penya okumona omunhu afange. @@Onda ya ponhele yo kutulumukwa ndele handi tale konima yomiti.
P10	Onda tala apeshe, pombanda nopedu.
P11	No koipala aishe.
P12	Ina ndimona nande omunhu afange ponhele keshe.
P13	Aame ashike ndili mounyuni! @@Ame onda pumba, oh oshoshili!
P14	Ashike haame andike, kushiwete? @@Oove ashike!
