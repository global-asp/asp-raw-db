page_text:

P1	Nyani ipaka kang?
P2	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei dir kop ekitanda?@@Emam, epudorit nikalapatan akai.
P3	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei kidiama akabati a?@@Emam, eyei kop achelif.
P4	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei dir yaaye ekicholong aa?@@Emam, eyei nikalapatan a amana.
P5	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei dir diyete echap aa?@@Emam, eporoporoe anikalapatan edirisha.
P6	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei dir tooma aturee aa?@@Emam, eyei tooma abokis.
P7	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Eyei dir nikalapatan a amana?@@Emam, eyei yaaye nakaten.
P8	Nyani ipaka kang?@@Nyeen kane!@@Edukit alotooma akabati naka!
