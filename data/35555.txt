title:	Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?

artist:	Vusi Malindi

desc:	Se mba a wanye u a lu a inja tsembele heen. Doo u mbayev vea nenge inja i dedoo ken a na.

page_text:

P1	Yayeri wan u injaa ka a nder tughtugh. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P2	Ka a lu a iwuese sha ciu tugh mbu mbu kar la.@@ Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P3	Ka a sôr gambe tsembelee. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P4	Ka a sugh mbamaren nav mbakiriki man mbatamen. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P5	Ka a ôô ishigh nagh sha chahur i kwer.  @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P6	Ka a kile anyi sha tine i agenga.@ @Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P7	Ka a ôô iyol sha mngerem ma ndôhôl.@ @Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P8	Ka a hua yundufun i nyian fuee. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P9	Ka a wa akôv a il. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P10	Ka a yem makeranta sha Mbala fefa. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P11	Ka a time sha Akaa-a-gba-Aôndo, Akaa-a-taregh man Ayenge. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
P12	Ka a wa atsam, a vine amar shi a gbidye bol u sha angahar. @@Se pine ayol ase ser, "Kwagh u Yayeri gande nena?"
