page_text:

P1	Kate is my good friend. We share many things at school.
P2	Sun is our good friend. He gives us light during the day.
P3	Moon and stars are good friends. They bring light at night.
P4	Parents are good friends They take care of the family.
P5	Teachers train children at school. They are good friends.
P6	They show much love and care.
P7	Rain gives us much water.
P8	He keeps the plants healthy. He is a good friend.
P9	Doctors and nurses treat sick people. They are friends indeed.
P10	Cow gives us milk,  meat and cheese. We love her so much.
P11	Chickens are good friends. They give proteins to our bodies.
