page_text:

P1	A wani lokaci mai tsawo, dukkanin dabbobi sukan yi magana.@@Sauro da mahaifiyarsa sun rayu a wani gida.
P2	Kodayaushe mahaifiyar sauron takan bar shi a gida in za ta je nemo abinci.
P3	Wata rana, bayan mahaifiyar ta fita, shi kuwa ɗan ƙaramin sauron yana son fita waje.
P4	Bayan mahaifiyar ta dawo, sai tarar cewa ɗanta ya fita waje.
P5	Ta yi matuƙar damuwa, saboda shi kaɗai ya fita.@@Kwatsam sai ga ƙaramin sauron ya dawo gida.
P6	Cikin matuƙar farin ciki, sai ya ce baba, mutane suna murna in sun gan ni. @@Har suna ma min tafi idan na tashi cikin sauri na wuce ta wajensu.
P7	Mamar sai ta ji tsoro ta ce.@@"Ka ce mutane suna murna kuma suna yi maka tafi ko?"
P8	Mahaifiyar sai ta yi masa bayani ta ce, "Ba wai murnan ganin ka suke ba." Kawai so suke su muttsuke ka. @@Ta gargaɗe shi ta ce, "Mutane ba sa taɓa murna da ganin sauro."
