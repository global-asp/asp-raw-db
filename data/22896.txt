page_text:

P1	Oguni Billy. Ne' mbuzi eyi 'Ishe Jani yamuhiire nekyi rabo. Nobu Billy ayiine amaryo, Jani namukunda munonga. Atwire nazara abana beine amagara maruji abu Jani ne 'Ishe barikuguza.
P2	Nyimwazyo Billy ajiree aheeru omu rubuga n 'abana be. Jani ekyi tikyirikumu 'shemeza ahabwokuba abana banguhi kwiba narishi okuribwa 'embwa.
P3	Billy ariire ebitabo birimu waka eyi Ann abiire akoziire. Ann ajiziire ekyiniga kujirangu omushomesa nazakumutera. Atiire Billy omujere gwa amaani. Ekyi 'jirise Jani ekyiniga. Yayesibirana omukyishenje kye kuzaana na dore zee.
P4	Jani ayiine obwoba kujira ngu ishe nazakuguza Billy ahabwekyi yakora. Konka, omwami Tom, ishe tarikwenda kunyiiza omutabani kuguza embuzi ye. Araganise kurinda mpaka abaana bakuziire kunjira ngu ahareze Jani emwe.
P5	Tushemerirwe kubanka Jani na Ann. Tushemerirwe kurebera abikoko kurunji. Ebikoko nebyo omugasho ahabwokuba nibituha emere kandi nibiturinda aba juugu.
