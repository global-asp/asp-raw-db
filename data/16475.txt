desc:	Abafana ababini balahlekelwa ziinkomo. Bahambe bayokuzikhangela. Ngorhatya

page_text:

P1	Kudala,  kwakukho abafana ababini ababelahlekelwe ziinkomo. Bazikhangela iinkomo kwade kwarhatyela.
P2	Kusarhatyele njalo,  babona isibane komnye umzi. Bavumelana ngokuyocela indawo yokulala apho.
P3	Baya kulo mzi bankqonkqoza. Lwavuleka ucango baze bangena. Abafana zange babone mntu. Kodwa beva ilizwi libabulisa.
P4	Lathi ilizwi,  "Ndiyintwala. Vulani imbiza nitye. Vulani la mbiza yomdongwe nisele amarhewu."
P5	Yaze ke intwala yanxiba ulusu lwayo yaphuma.
P6	Abafana batya,  basela,  baze babulela. Bemka kulo mzi.
P7	Besahamba njalo,  babanengcinga embi. Bathi,  "Hayi,  ayinokwenzeka into yokuba siphiwe ukutya yintwala." Ekugqibeleni bavumelana ukuba bajike bayotyumza le ntwala.
P8	Babuyela kulamzi baze bayityumza intwala. Ngephanyazo intwala yanyamalala,  yaze nendlu yanyamalala! Bazifumana bengaphandle ethafeni.
