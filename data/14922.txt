page_text:

P1	Kiro kimu wakame akaba nagendera harubaju rw'enyanja.
P2	N'ensere ekaba eroho,  negenda kwemaramara nokulya ebinyansi ebirungi.
P3	Ensere etarole ngu wakame ekaba eroho kandi yarubata ekigere kyayo etakigenderire. Wakame yatokera haiguru kandi yatandika nokukunga,  "Lwe nsere! Tolikurora ngu orubasire ekigere kyange?"
P4	Nsere yasaba ekiganyiro hali wakame,  "Nganyira munywani wange. Tinkuboine. Caali nganyira!" Baitu wakame atahulirize kandi yatoka,  "Okikozire okigenderire! Ekiro ekindi noija kurora! Noija kusasura!"
P5	Wakame akagenda kuserra omurro kandi yagamba,  "Genda,  oyokye Nsere obwararuga omumaizi kugenda kulya ebinyansi andubasirege."
P6	Hanyumaho,  nsere akaba nalya ebinyansi haraho ne Nyanja. "Whoosh!" omurro gwarugwamu omwika. Omwika gwatandika kwokya obwoya bw'ensere.
P7	Nsere yatandika kurra kandi nairuka nagenda mumaizi. Obwoya bwayo bukaba buhyeriire omumurro.
P8	Nikyo habwaki enseretegenda hara namaizi habwokutina kuhyera mumurro. Wakame ekasemererwa muno obuyahulire nsere ahire,  yagamba,  "Musaswize!"
