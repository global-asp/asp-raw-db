title:	Embeba N'ekapa
author:	Bukheye Mulongo Christopher
artist:	Jacob Kono
lang:	Lunyole
level:	First words

desc:	Embeba engesi etwegeresa yiiti omusigu anahwereteresaho otehwa ohuba sitende ohu mudulumaho.

page_text:

P1	Ahale, ekapa n'embeba jaali ja muhago.
P2	Embeba jatagiha ohugotanga polapola.
P3	Embeba esaahulu yaloma yiiti, "Ekapa jiri hutulyanga."
P4	Embeba ekasi jaloma enyana jiiti, "Mwegenderese. Mutatulanga yi bulafu."
P5	Embeba josijosi jegenderesa.

Jabanga mu maloŋo gaajo.
P6	Ekapa enjala n'ejiri bubi jabuusa jiiti, "Embeba josijosi jiri ŋeena?"
P7	Ekapa jasalaŋo ohuhyalira embeba jitegehe obugole.
P8	Jaloma jiiti, "Muganye omuhaana weenywe mulala afumbirwe omuseere weefe."
P9	Embeba nga jiroma ekapa jiiti, "Mwenda hutulya."
P10	Olufanyuma embeba jefugirira obugole ohubaaŋo.
P11	Ekapa jagobolayo ewaajo ni jehomba.
P12	Huludaalo lw'ohugatibwa embeba jaali njegenderesa.
P13	Ekapa joola nijiri hwemba olwembo lw'ohugatibwa.
P14	Olwembo ni luloma luuti, "Mubaŋambe hubalye."
P15	Embeba esaahulu nga yemba yiiti, "Mudulume, mbeba, mudulume."
P16	Embeba esaahulu ng'eroma ekapa yiiti, "Sihuli ba muhago."
