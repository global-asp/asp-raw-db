desc:	Lule ni Bwonso bali n okukola mu nte edhendhawulo. Ente yali etyaime kumpi nekidiba ekyamaadhi ekyokukyalo.

page_text:

P1	Agho olwatuuka  nga  ku kyalo  ebaayo  omulenzi   ayayetebwanga Lule.
P2	Lule yali mukwano gwa Bwonso. Baali  ba  ku muliraano.
P3	Lulala baaja okunona amaadhi.
P4	Aye bwe baali bali kumpi okutuuka ku kidiba, baabona  Wante nga alambaire agho. Baayiya amagezi ge babe  babitemu okuzaanhaku naye.
P5	Agho Lule yaatandiika okutiisatiisa Wante. "Muuu, muuu, muuuu!" Yaakikola mu maloboozi  ag'omwanguka.
P6	Okutuusa Wante bwe yacuuka naabalingaku. Mu butali bugenderevu, Bwonso yaatya muuku. Lule  yaakizuula ati Bwonso afunhyemu okutya.
P7	Lule yaakoba, "Tuje ye Wante ono tumukube. Twidha kulumuka era tuje tusene amaadhi. "Lule,  ntiiremuuku!" Bwonso bwe yaakoba mu kutya  okwamaani.
P8	Lule yaasokayo. Bwonso yaaja mpolampola. Baagezaaku okugikuba, aye tiyaseetukaku ghe yali.
P9	Lule yaakoba, "Bwonso, sigala ghano. Nje nnone  amaadhi." Bwonso yaikiriza.
P10	Lule yaasenera amaadhi mu kalobo akasooka.
P11	Bwe yali ali kusenera mu kalobo akandi,  Wante  yaasituka. Yaabbedha Lule n'eiyiga irala, yaamukasuka mu nkoto ye.
P12	Lule yaayasikana, "woowe, wooowe, woooowe!" Bwonso Bwonso yaatoolera okuseka obutarekera.
P13	Wante yaakoba, "Mmoo, mmooo, mmooooooo!"
P14	Nga  Bwonso amaze okufa enseko, yaabita  mu  bwangu yaaja ghaka. Bwe yatuuka eyo, yaayagaana  Mbwali nga ali kweya  luya.
P15	Yaamukoba, "mama mama,  Wante etwaire Lule!" Aye Bwonso gali gamubiseemu.
P16	Mbwali  yaalekula  ekyeyo  baatoolera  okutaasa  Luule. 
Olowooza  biki  ebyairirira?
