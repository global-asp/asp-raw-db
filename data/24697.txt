desc:	Peddy is a young girl and her parents are afraid for her because she is lazy in doing work at home even at school. She wakes up very late and does not do anything before going to school but still ends up sitting under trees. Peddys teachers have tried to discipline her but she does not change her ways. Peddys parents dont like the way Peddy conducts herself when sent to do some duty.

page_text:

P1	Peddy is a lazy girl who wakes up very late.
P2	She goes to school very late.
P3	Peddy likes sitting under the tree when she is late for class.
P4	Her mother always warns Peddy about her laziness but she does  not change.
P5	When sent to the field Peddy comes back very late.
P6	Peddy's laziness made her mother angry.
