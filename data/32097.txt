id:	32097
title:	Bumɗo Giɗɗo Pinndi
author:	Kulsumi Iisa
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi
lang:	Fulfulde Mbororoore
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/32097.png
desc:	Habaru ɓiɗɗo debbo bumɗo mo yiɗi yahugo ngesa ngam e mo yiɗi pinndi.

page_text:

P1	Kannde danyaama bumɗo. Gite maako meeɗaayi yiʼugo danyooɓe maako, koo sakiraaɓe maako. O anndi geese maɓɓe ngam e mo meemaɗe.
P2	Nyalaande feere Kannde yahi ngesa haako. E mo yiɗi nanugo urngol pinndi belngol. E mo yiɗi meemugo pinndi ndin.
P3	Kannde yiɗi raarugo pinndi koo nde goʼo tan. O wiʼi nder ɓernde maako, "Pinndiiji e mbooɗi."
P4	Koo ndeye nyalaande Kannde yahay ngesa. E mo anndi laawol.
P5	Pab, ndunngu riggi, o tijji dow. Kannde wiʼi, "Sey mi dogga, mi hoota, taaʼ mi towra!"
P6	Ndiyam yoofake. Kannde morti, yani. Hoore maako fiyi hayre.
P7	Nde Kannde hebti hakkiilo muuɗum, o nani yimɓe haade maako. O ƴami, "Ko waɗi?"
P8	Baaba maako wiʼi, "A yanii a fiyii hoore maaɗa." Inna wiʼi, "En ngetta Allah nde a jamo."
P9	Kannde noddi inɗe sakiraaɓe bee danyooɓe maako fuu. Nden o wiʼi, "Inna, Baaba, ngaddaneeyam pinndi." Koo moye hayɗini.
P10	Minyiraawo maako waddanimo pinndi. Kannde jogiindi wiʼi, "Pinndi e wooɗii."
P11	Yimɓe maako dardartiri. Inna maako ƴami, "Kannde, a yiʼii pinndi ndin na?"
P12	Kannde jogii pinndi ndin, moosi wi'i, "Pinndi ndin e wooɗi yiʼugo, onon maa on woɗɓe yiʼugo."
