title:	Selemo

desc:	Dikgaitsedi tse di neng di dira ditshingwana tsa malomo fa motseng.

page_text:

P1	Go ne go le mosetsana mongwe a bidiwa Lethabo. One a kwa motseng mongwe o bidiwa Watervaal. Mosetsana yo one a na le kgaitsadie yo o neng a rata malomo.
P2	Kgaitsadie Lethabo e bong Mmathapelo o ne rotloetsa Lethabo ka gale gore ba ja le tshingwana ya malomo. Lethabo o ne a sena kgatlhego epe.
P3	E ne e re fa Lethabo a eta kwa ditsaleng tsa gagwe le ba losika a ba jalele malomo mme fa a ka boa a sa dira jwalo o ne a utlwa botlhoko.
P4	Lethabo o ne a tshoganyetsa ke bolwetse a tlhokofala.Ditsala, badiri ka ene le ba losika ba ne ba mo paka jaaka motho yoo o neng a rata malomo e bile mongwe le mongwe a bolela fa a mo jaletse  malomo fa a ntse a tshela.
P5	Morago ga lobakanyana Mmathapelo a simolola go tlhoafalela kgaitsedie e bong Lethabo. O ne a seka dikeledi a sa itse gore a direng eng. O ne a tlela ke leano la go lema  tshingwana ya malomo go mo gopola le go itshidisa ka yona.
P6	Lethabo a simolola go epa mosima wa go tshela makgapetla a merogo le maungo go dira mosutelo. A simolola go jwala malomo. Fa ba lelapa ba fetsa gotlhapa, tsaya metsi a o a a dirisa go nosetsa tshingwana ya gagwe ya Malomo.
P7	Letsatsi lengwe le lengwe a ya kwa tshingwaneng go e lekola le go nosetsa mme pelo ya gagwe e ne ya simolola go kgatlhegela malomo jaaka kgaitsadie.
P8	Baagisani ba ne ba tla go kopa dijalo tsa malomo gore ba ye go jala.Mo motsaneng wa Watervaal le gompieno go nale dikgaisano tsa di tshingwana tsa malomo.
