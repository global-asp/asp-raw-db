P1	Imagine a day. When we could have a picnic on the shores of Lake Victoria.
P2	Imagine a day. When everyone can read and there are books in every language.
P3	Imagine a day. When I shall give birth to my first baby.
P4	Imagine a day. When all people in the world live peacefully as brothers and sisters.
P5	Imagine a day. When I can walk on water.
P6	Imagine a day. When your daughter becomes a president.
P7	Imagine a day. When you are about to be put in a grave and then you come back to life again!
P8	Imagine a day. When people develop wings to fly,  and reach where their creator is.
P9	Imagine a day. When all the ground is covered with a carpet across the whole world.
