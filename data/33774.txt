page_text:

P1	Sasihlala nogogo nomama.
P2	Abazala bami oChristina, uThandi, uJunior nomalumekazi babehlala nathi.
P3	Ugogo wayesifundisa imidlalo.@@Kwakumnandi kakhulu.
P4	UJunior nogogo bahamba bayohlala eMalawi.
P5	Umalumekazi noChristina nabo bahamba bayohlala eMalawi.
P6	Kwalandela uThandi naye wayohlala eMalawi.
P7	Kwakudabukisa kakhulu lokhu.@@Akekho owazosala nathi.
P8	Kodwa akukubi kakhulu, ngoba umama ngisahlala naye lapha.
