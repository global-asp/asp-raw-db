desc:	Sipho goes to town with his sister and she buys him new shoes.

page_text:

P1	Elosi Sipho ka lokatokeng lore.
P2	Ite nakatokeng ng'iworui alodukan. Iloit Sipho.
P3	Ite nakatokeng ng'iworui alodukan. Iloit Sipho.
P4	Arai ng'amuk nagielara.
P5	Kitetemik ng'esi.
P6	Agielaki nakatokeng ng'esi ng'amuk napesiet epira.
P7	Nyonte ibunyatar kesi akinang nawi?
P8	A ekeny alajokon abu tanang naro napieset epira eroko esa nyedauna. Enapit ng'esi ng'amuk keng nakiteat napesiet epira.
