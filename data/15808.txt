title:	Ya Tukupanda Mun'gunda
author:	Alberto Mateus Chaibo
artist:	Silva Afonso
lang:	Ciyawo
level:	First paragraphs
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/15808.png
desc:	A-Luwisa akwete masengo gakutenda ga pacikola.  Mama vawo adipelece kwakamucisya.  A-Luwisa akudijiganya cici kwa mama vawo?

page_text:

P1	  Luwisa: "Ngwete masengo ganguti ndende ga pacikola."   Mama: "Komboleka nankamusye?"
P2	  Luwisa: "Ana cici tupandile mun'gunda-mu?"   Mama: "Yinangwa."
P3	  Luwisa: "Ana cici tukudyaga mu yinangwa-mu?"   Mama: "Tukudyaga njiga ni masamba gakwe."
P4	  Luwisa: "Ana cici sooni tukupandaga mun'gunda?"   Mama: "Tukupandaga ntesa."
P5	  Luwisa: "Ana ntesa wudi njiga?"   Mama: "Ngava. Jidi mbeju ja jikutyocela mu wuluva."
P6	  Luwisa: "Ana cici sooni tukupandaga mun'gunda?"   Mama: "Tukupandaga yimanga."
P7	  Luwisa: "Tukudyaga yimanga yevisi."   Mama: "Sooni tukutwaga kuti wukopoce wutandi. Wele wutandi-wo,  tuwugaga wugadi."
P8	  Luwisa: "Ana sooni cici tukupandaga mun'gunda?"   Mama: "Tukupandaga mbwanda sya ngunde syasidi cenene."
P9	  Luwisa: "Tukupandaga yicindu yejinji mun'gunda."   Mama: "Pele tuveje vana ndavi jose-pe yakudya yambone!"
