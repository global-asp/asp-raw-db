title:	Wangoko na Wakinyanyangolho
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere and Jenipher  Kabugho
artist:	Magriet Brink
lang:	Lukhonzo
level:	First words
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0

desc:	Wangoko na wakinyanyangolho bawithe omukaghu owembaghane. Kiro kighuma wangoko mwayibulhabulha ahabwerisingwa nikwa akole ekindu ekyobukiru.

page_text:

P1	Wangoko na wakinyanyangolho
  babya bira.
P2	Mubayasatha omupira.
P3	Wangoko niyo mulhambi
  we’golo.
P4	Wakinyanyangolho mwateba esyagolo
  ithanu.
P5	Wangoko mwayibulhabulha.
P6	Wangoko mwalya wakinyanyangolho.
P7	Wangoko mwabanangana namama
  wa wakinyanyangolho.
P8	“Nyitsune mama” wakinyanyangolho mwalira.
P9	Wangoko mwayowa inimukoni.
P10	Wakinyanyangolho akasiha nabi.
P11	Wangoko mwasala wakinyanyangolho.
P12	Ebinyanyangolho mubyalwaho mubyathibitha.
  Esyangoko nebinyanyangolho
  mubabya banyanzighu.
