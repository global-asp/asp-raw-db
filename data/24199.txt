title:	Entaama eyaruhire amagara go' mukibuga
author:	Ajuna David
artist:	Kenneth Boyowa Okitikpi

desc:	Buri mbera eyorikweshanga mu

page_text:

P1	Entaama ekaba netaaha omu kibuga. Ekarugaho yaruha amagara ge'kibuga nikwe kusharaho kugaruka omukishaka.

Niho yabuganiire empisi eyabire emazire ebiro bingi etari kurya.
P2	Ekaramusya empiisi emirundi mingi,"owishemwe Mpisi, nogambaki? Oriyo ota?

Wampisi yamugarukamu  ati,"Otankandamusya munonga. Gira ekiwakora okumara enjara."
P3	Wantama obu yahurire eki yatandika kwiruka.
Akahendera ahikire aha mwiina gwe enjojo. 

Manya, empisi ekaguma emukuratire.

Empisi ekahendera ehikire aha mwiina kandi yayemerera nikwo kushuka echu chu omumeisho ga wantale.
P4	Ekichunchu kikagambira empisi,"konka,titukabasa kugira obusinge omu maka geitu? Obayo notambika echu chu omu meisho giitu!"

Empisi yagarukamu eti,"Omukama,tina kimanyisa oku kurabya mu amaisho. Ndiyo nindonda eky'okurya kyange!"
P5	Entale yagarukamu eti,"Hona shi niwe kori emere yangye. Hona buziima nakwirukangaho? Tiwe wayereta. Otakanyekwasa amuheru."
P6	Entare ekagambire entama eti,"ahabwenki wiza omukishaka?"

Entaama yagarukamu eti,"Nija ahabwokuba ndi omutambizi."
P7	Ekichunchu kyagira ki ti,"tebekanisa ebishaka okuhamya nku okwoli omutambizi."Entama yagarukamu eri kugira,"Omubazi gwangye niguserwa."

Entare yagarukamu,"Ekyo tikirimu kabi,ebbeeyi tiyo nshonga nkuru. Tihariho ekitarikubasika aharinye."
P8	Entama yagiira eti,"Omubazi no'kuttu kwe'mpisi." Entare yasharaho okuttu kwe'mpisi yakuha entama. 
Entama eka kuteka omu ki kyebe kirimu obwokyi nikwe ku kikwasa entare.
P9	Entare ekarya okuttu kwe'mpisi yashanga mu obunuzi.
Ekabuuza entama eti,"nimbisa kurya no'kwakabiri?"
P10	Entama ekagarukamu eri kugira eti," Yeego nikibasika omukama wangye." Entare nikwo yashazireho okuttu kwe'mpisi okundi yakukwasa entama. Entama ekakwata okuttu kwe'mpisi yagutta omu kikebe kirimu obwoki nikwo kukikwasa entare.
P11	Entare ekagambira entama eti,"nikibasaka kutunga aha ruhu?" Entama yagarukamu,"nintekateka ngu nakyo nikibaasika omukama wangye." Empisi obu yahurire ebi yatiina kufa, nikwe kwiruka. Entare ekamwirukangaho.
P12	"Amagara go'mukishaka kugarabe gari gati, kikabire kirungi naye garukira omuka." Entama nikwe yagiziire.
