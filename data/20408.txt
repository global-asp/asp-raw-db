id:	20408
title:	Nseete Ayamba Kaghemba
author:	Cornelius Wambi Gulere
artist:	Wiehan de Jager
lang:	Lusoga
level:	First sentences
lic:	Creative Commons: Attribution 4.0
thumb:	http://africanstorybook.org/illustrations/covers/20408.png
desc:	Eitaka ikalu era eige lili nhonta. Lija ku mwiga okunhwa era amaadhi galyeyagho.

page_text:

P1	Lwali lunaku lusu lwa kyeya mu kibira ekikalu.
P2	Nseete yali azira
  maadhi okumala enaku kamaala.
P3	"Nnhenda ku eitondo lya maadhi, ghaire nga liviire
  ku ikoola, Nseete bwe yalira.
P4	Aye ghaire musulo
  gukaze.
P5	"Bwentafuna maadhi, ndha
  kufa," Nseete bwe yalira. "Nteekwa okugya ku mwiga gwennaghuliraku."
P6	Omwiga mwidhuvu era muguumuufu.
  Gwidha kukweyaawo gukutwaale, Wandaada omukalabakalaba bweyalabula.
P7	Aye Nseete lyali ne nhonta mbi. "Ndi kugya kufa bwentaanhwe ku maadhi." Era yaaja okunoonia omwiga
P8	Your translated text
P9	Yavunviiga mu isubi eighanvu
  ni ku nsaga enkalu.
P10	Yatambula paka bwe yawulira
  amaadhi agavuudhumuka. Gwali mwiga. Yawulira amayengo.
P11	Nseete yamalaku akaseera
  nga ali kugudha ku maadhi amainhogovu.
P12	Yaaba isanhufu inho
  yalema kubona iyengo nga liidha.
P13	Nseete yagezaaku okubaka
  eisubi agho nga libitagho lili kulela. Aye amaadhi gaamutwaala.
P14	Aani anannhamba. Yaayeta.
P15	Yanguya oniine kuno,
  Kaghemba bwe yamukoba, nga agemye olusaga
  ku munhwa gwe.
P16	Your translated text
P17	Nseete yasalagho ati, "Ndidha kuba ghano paka
  nga neeyanziiza  Kghemba." Ndidha kuteega paka lwa anaira okunhwa.
P18	Lwali lulala nga Nseete ali kukuuma, abalenzi babiri baidha ku mwiga. Baali ne empandha
  dhaibwe.
P19	"Kaghemba keelu,
  kaidha ghano okunhwa amaadhi." Abalenzi bwe baakoba. "Twidha kukaliira ekyeigulo."
P20	Your translated text
P21	Tiyinza kuganha balenzi
  bano kwita Kaghemba ono.
P22	Mangu ago, Kaghemba omweru yaidha nga aguluka okuva ku muti okwidha okunhwa.
P23	Your translated text
P24	Nseete ali ne ekiloghoozo.
P25	Yaatuumira ku kigere ekyomulenzi mulala. Nseete yaaluma omulenzi oyo einho nga bwasobola.
P26	Omulenzi yaatuuma. "Ouchi!" Yaalenkaana.
P27	Kaghemba omweru yaatabulwa era yaagulukagho yeekweka.
P28	Atyo Nseete bwe yeebaza Kaghemba omweru olwokumughonia.
