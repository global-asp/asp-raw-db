desc:	In the wild at a waterhole we count the animals coming to drink. What do you think happens when the lion comes to drink?

page_text:

P1	One elephant went to drink water.
P2	Two giraffes went to drink water.
P3	There are three buffaloes and four birds. They are all going to drink water.
P4	Five impalas and six wild hogs are going to drink water.
P5	Seven frogs and eight fish are in the water.
P6	Nine zebras are going to drink water.
P7	One lion came to drink water.
P8	Who is brave enough to continue drinking?
