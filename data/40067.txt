page_text:

P1	Once upon a time,in a village called Matilu,there lived a girl who loved going to school to study.The school she went was called Jabli Christian School.
P2	Bellol loved going to church .She was good to  teachers,fellow pupils,her parents and siblings.The neighbours loved her because of her good behaviours.
P3	Bellol was the last born among her three siblings.During the long holidays, she'd visit her grandparents and  help them by doing the house chores, fetching water in the river and also cooking.
P4	As days went by Bellol continued to put more effort in her studies. As she was approaching his end term she worked extra hard.She became position one in her class at the end of the term.
P5	Finally she  sat for her exams and by her results she had clearly passed scoring 417 marks,which made her position 1 out of forty nine pupils.She had qualified to go to a national school.Her family,teachers and pupil's went into celebration
P6	Bellol received a letter of admission of her dream National school .She continued working hard and at O level passed her exams.Later joined university and pursued her dream caarrer.She finally became a doctor.
