page_text:

P1	A little girl was sent by their parents to her grandfather who has lived  behind the forest she went herself with happiness.
P2	When she reached near the forest she met with a careless goat and talked to her to go carefully because there is a meeting  of animals in the forest.
P3	When she reached in the middle of the forest, she found many animal near the way and she run and run and making noise.
P4	When she went out of the forest  she met also many birds together making noise. The little girl run and making more noise. Her  grandfather heard her.
P5	Her grandfather talked to her wife that is their granddaughter. He went to see her quickly with his dog and find her tired.
P6	When they reached at home she entered and greet other children then she took food.After  eating she talked about what happened across the way.
P7	After telling what happened and getting advice of not passing in the forest without another one she went happily to play  with  other children.
P8	She didn't  pass again in the forest without anyone.
