page_text:

P1	She had a big red belt round her waist.
P2	In her hand,  she carried a shiny little orange handbag.
P3	She wore a pair of shiny orange high-heeled shoes.
P4	She stopped and touched her long hair,  which was held together in the middle with a hairpin.
P5	As she fixed her hairpin,  her handbag fell down. "Ouch," I said feeling bad for her.
P6	The lady bent to pick up her handbag.
P7	I saw long orange earrings as she cleaned the dust off her handbag.
